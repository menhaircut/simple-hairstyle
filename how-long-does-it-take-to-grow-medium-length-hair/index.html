How Long Does It Take To Grow Medium Length Hair

Written by Aprilia Oct 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Medium length hair is a popular hairstyle that many people aspire to have. However, the process of growing out your hair can be frustrating, especially if you’re not seeing results as quickly as you’d like. In this post, we’ll explore how long it takes to grow medium length hair and provide tips on how to speed up the process.

Description

Medium length hair is typically defined as hair that falls between the shoulders and the collarbone. This length of hair is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. However, growing out your hair to this length can take time and patience.

How to Grow Medium Length Hair

There are a few things you can do to help your hair grow faster and healthier:

Eat a Balanced Diet

What you eat can have a big impact on the health of your hair. Be sure to eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals. Foods like eggs, nuts, and leafy greens are all great options.

Take Care of Your Scalp

A healthy scalp is key to healthy hair growth. Be sure to keep your scalp clean and moisturized. You can use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a scalp treatment once a week.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and slow down growth. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible. If you must use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Step by Step Process

Here is a step-by-step process for growing medium length hair:

Step 1: Start with a Healthy Foundation

Before you start growing out your hair, make sure it’s in good condition. Get a haircut to remove any split ends, and start using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Be Patient

Growing out your hair takes time, so be patient. It typically takes about six months to a year to grow out medium length hair.

Step 3: Get Regular Trims

While it may seem counterintuitive, getting regular trims can actually help your hair grow faster. Trimming off split ends can prevent breakage and keep your hair healthy.

Step 4: Experiment with Styles

As your hair grows, experiment with different styles to keep things interesting. You can try braids, updos, and half-up styles.

Tips for Faster Hair Growth

Here are a few additional tips for growing your hair faster:

Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help increase blood flow to the hair follicles, which can promote hair growth. Try massaging your scalp for a few minutes each day.

Use a Hair Growth Treatment

There are a variety of hair growth treatments available, including serums and supplements. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin and keratin.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids can cause tension on the hair follicles, which can slow down growth. Try to avoid wearing tight hairstyles for extended periods of time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to grow medium length hair?

It typically takes about six months to a year to grow out medium length hair.

What can I do to make my hair grow faster?

Eating a balanced diet, taking care of your scalp, and avoiding heat styling can all help your hair grow faster. Additionally, massaging your scalp, using a hair growth treatment, and avoiding tight hairstyles can also promote hair growth.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

Once you’ve grown out your medium length hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips:

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair moisturized and prevent damage. Try using a treatment once a week.

Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Try wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF when you’re spending time in the sun.

In conclusion, growing out medium length hair takes time and patience. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can help it grow faster and healthier. Remember to be patient and experiment with different styles as your hair grows.

What Short Hair Style Suits A Round Face

Written by Oliver Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, it can be tricky to find the perfect short hairstyle that flatters your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss what short hair style suits a round face, how to achieve it, step-by-step tips, and hair care. So let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by soft, curved lines, and a wide forehead and cheekbones. The length and width of the face are almost the same, and the chin is usually round. The key to finding the perfect short hair style for a round face is to create the illusion of length and slimness.

The ideal short hair style for a round face is one that adds height and volume to the crown of the head while keeping the sides of the hair close to the face. A long bob, a pixie cut with volume at the crown, or a layered bob are all great options that flatter a round face.

How to

The first step to getting the perfect short hair style for a round face is to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting hair for this face shape. Next, choose a style that suits your personality and lifestyle. A short hair style requires regular maintenance, so make sure you're ready for the commitment.

When it comes to styling your hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the crown of your head. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the roots to create height. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create soft waves or curls that frame your face.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect short hair style for a round face:

  1. Find a stylist who is experienced in cutting hair for a round face.
  2. Choose a short hair style that flatters your features.
  3. Use a volumizing mousse or spray on damp hair.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the roots to create height.
  5. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create soft waves or curls that frame your face.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hair style for a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as blunt bobs or straight-across bangs.
  • Choose a style with layers, which help to create movement and texture.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair styles for a round face:

Q: Can I wear a short bob with a round face?
A: Yes, but make sure the bob is layered and angled, with longer pieces in the front to slim the face. Q: Should I have bangs with a short hair style for a round face?
A: Side-swept bangs can be flattering, but avoid straight-across bangs, which can make the face appear wider. Q: Can I wear my hair straight with a short hair style for a round face?
A: Yes, but add volume at the crown to create the illusion of length.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair style looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, which can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your style and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, there are many short hair styles that flatter a round face, but the key is to add height and volume to the crown of your head while keeping the sides close to your face. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve the perfect short hair style for your face shape and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny!

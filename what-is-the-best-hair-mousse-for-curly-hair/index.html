What Is The Best Hair Mousse For Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 05, 2023 · 5 min read

Curly hair can be both beautiful and frustrating to style. It can be tough to find the right products that work with your curls without weighing them down or causing frizz. That’s where hair mousse comes in. Mousse is a lightweight, foam product that can help define and enhance your curls. But with so many options on the market, how do you know what is the best hair mousse for curly hair? In this article, we’ll break down everything you need to know to choose the perfect mousse for your curly locks.

Description

Hair mousse is a styling product that comes in a foam form. It’s used to add volume, hold, and definition to hair. Mousse is typically applied to damp hair and then blow-dried or air-dried to set the style. When it comes to curly hair, mousse can help enhance and define your curls without weighing them down or causing frizz. The right mousse can make all the difference in your curly hair routine.

How To Choose The Best Hair Mousse For Curly Hair

When choosing a hair mousse for curly hair, there are a few key things to look for:

1. Hold Level

The hold level of a mousse refers to how much it can control your hair. For curly hair, you want a mousse with a medium hold. This will help define your curls without weighing them down or causing stiffness.

2. Moisture Level

Curly hair tends to be on the drier side, so you want to choose a mousse that adds moisture to your hair. Look for a mousse that contains nourishing ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, or glycerin.

3. Non-Sticky Formula

Avoid mousse that leaves your hair feeling sticky or crunchy. Look for a mousse that dries to a soft, touchable finish.

Step By Step Guide To Using Hair Mousse For Curly Hair

Here’s how to use hair mousse for curly hair:

1. Start With Clean, Damp Hair

Wash your hair and towel-dry it until it’s damp but not soaking wet.

2. Apply Mousse

Squeeze a golf ball-sized amount of mousse into your palm. Rub your hands together to distribute the product evenly. Then, scrunch the mousse into your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

3. Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and prevent frizz. If you don’t have a diffuser, you can air-dry your hair instead.

4. Finish With Hair Oil

Once your hair is dry, apply a few drops of hair oil to your palms. Rub your hands together and then scrunch the oil into your hair to add extra shine and moisture.

Tips For Using Hair Mousse For Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your hair mousse:

1. Don’t Over-Apply

Using too much mousse can weigh down your curls, so start with a small amount and add more as needed.

2. Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunching your hair as you apply mousse can help enhance your curls and prevent frizz.

3. Choose The Right Technique

There are different techniques for applying mousse to curly hair. Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Does Hair Mousse Do?

Hair mousse adds volume, hold, and definition to hair. It’s commonly used to enhance curls and create a bouncy, textured look.

Can You Use Hair Mousse Every Day?

Yes, you can use hair mousse every day. However, be careful not to over-apply as this can weigh down your hair.

What’s The Difference Between Hair Mousse And Gel?

Hair mousse is a foam product that adds volume and hold to hair. Gel is a thicker, more viscous product that provides a stronger hold.

Hair Care For Curly Hair

Using the right hair products is important, but it’s also essential to take care of your curly hair in other ways. Here are a few tips:

1. Don’t Over-Wash

Curly hair tends to be drier than other hair types, so avoid washing it too often. Aim for once or twice a week and use a sulfate-free shampoo.

2. Use A Wide-Tooth Comb

Avoid using a brush on your curly hair as this can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

3. Sleep On A Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and breakage. Silk is smoother than cotton, so it won’t rough up your hair while you sleep.

In conclusion, the best hair mousse for curly hair is one that provides a medium hold, adds moisture, and has a non-sticky formula. Using mousse to enhance your curls can be a game-changer in your hair care routine. Remember to start with clean, damp hair and apply the mousse evenly throughout your hair. Experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for you. And don’t forget to take care of your curly hair in other ways, like using a wide-tooth comb and sleeping on a silk pillowcase.

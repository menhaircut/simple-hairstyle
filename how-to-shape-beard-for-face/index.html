How To Shape Your Beard For Your Face Shape

Written by Aprilia Jun 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your beard looking unkempt and shapeless? It's time to learn how to shape your beard for your face shape. In this article, we will cover the basics of beard shaping and provide step-by-step instructions for achieving a well-groomed beard. We will also include tips for maintaining your beard and keeping it healthy. Let's get started!

How to Shape Your Beard

The first step in shaping your beard is to determine your face shape. This will help you determine the best beard style for your face. There are six basic face shapes: round, square, oval, diamond, heart, and oblong. Once you've determined your face shape, you can choose the best beard style to complement it.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you want to create the illusion of a longer face. To do this, you should keep your beard shorter on the sides and longer on the chin. This will help elongate your face and create a more angular look.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you want to soften your jawline. To do this, you should grow your beard longer on the sides and shorter on the chin. This will create a more oval shape and soften your features.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! You can pretty much rock any beard style you want. However, to enhance your features, you should keep your beard trimmed and well-groomed.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you want to create the illusion of a wider chin. To do this, you should keep your beard longer on the chin and shorter on the sides. This will help balance out your features.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you want to balance out your wider forehead with a wider jawline. To do this, you should grow your beard longer on the sides and shorter on the chin. This will create a more balanced look.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, you want to create the illusion of a wider face. To do this, you should keep your beard shorter on the sides and longer on the chin. This will help widen your face and create a more balanced look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Shaping Your Beard

Now that you know your face shape and the best beard style for it, it's time to shape your beard. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash your beard with a beard shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Comb your beard in the direction of growth.
  3. Trim your beard with clippers or scissors to the desired length.
  4. Using a beard trimmer, define the neckline and cheek line.
  5. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs.
  6. Apply beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and healthy.

Tips for Maintaining Your Beard

Now that you've shaped your beard, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your beard regularly to keep it looking neat.
  • Use a beard comb or brush to keep your beard smooth and tangle-free.
  • Apply beard oil or balm daily to keep your beard soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using regular shampoo on your beard as it can dry it out.

FAQs

How long should my beard be?

The length of your beard depends on your personal preference and face shape. However, most beard styles look best when they are kept between 1-3 inches in length.

How often should I trim my beard?

You should trim your beard every 2-4 weeks to keep it looking neat and well-groomed.

Can I use regular shampoo on my beard?

No, regular shampoo can dry out your beard. It's best to use a beard shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for beards.

Conclusion

Shaping your beard for your face shape doesn't have to be difficult. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a well-groomed beard that complements your features. Remember to maintain your beard by trimming it regularly and using beard oil or balm to keep it healthy. Follow these tips, and you'll have a beard that's the envy of all your friends!

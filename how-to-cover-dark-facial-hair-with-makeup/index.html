How To Cover Dark Facial Hair With Makeup

Written by Bardi Aug 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with dark facial hair that makes you feel self-conscious? Makeup can be a great solution to cover up any unwanted hair on your face. In this article, we’ll be discussing how to cover dark facial hair with makeup in a few simple steps. Let’s get started!

Description

Dark facial hair can be a common problem for many people, especially women. It can make you feel self-conscious and affect your confidence. While there are many ways to remove facial hair, not everyone is comfortable with those methods. That’s where makeup comes in. It can be a great way to cover up any unwanted hair and help you feel more confident about your appearance.

How to Cover Dark Facial Hair with Makeup

Here are the steps to follow when covering dark facial hair with makeup:

Step 1: Cleanse Your Face

The first step is to cleanse your face thoroughly. This will help remove any dirt, oil, or makeup residue that may be on your skin. Use a gentle cleanser that is suitable for your skin type and rinse with warm water.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Next, apply a foundation that matches your skin tone. Use a foundation brush or sponge to apply the product evenly. Make sure to cover the entire face, including the areas where you have dark facial hair. This will help create a smooth and even base for the rest of your makeup.

Step 3: Use a Color Corrector

If you have particularly dark facial hair, you may want to use a color corrector before applying your foundation. Green color correctors can help neutralize any redness, while peach or orange correctors can cancel out any blue or purple tones. Apply the corrector to the areas where you have dark hair and blend it in with a brush or sponge.

Step 4: Conceal the Hair

Next, use a concealer that matches your skin tone to cover up the dark hair. Apply the concealer to the areas where you have hair and blend it in with a brush or sponge. Make sure to blend it well so that it doesn’t look obvious.

Step 5: Set with Powder

Finally, set your makeup with a translucent powder. This will help keep your makeup in place and prevent it from smudging or rubbing off. Use a powder brush to apply the powder evenly to your face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when covering dark facial hair with makeup:

  • Choose a foundation that is long-lasting and transfer-resistant to ensure that it stays in place throughout the day.
  • Make sure to blend your makeup well to prevent any harsh lines or uneven patches.
  • Use a small brush to apply concealer to ensure that you cover only the areas where you have hair.
  • Consider using a setting spray to help your makeup last even longer.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about covering dark facial hair with makeup:

What kind of foundation should I use?

You should use a foundation that matches your skin tone and is suitable for your skin type. Look for a long-lasting and transfer-resistant formula to ensure that it stays in place throughout the day.

What color corrector should I use?

If you have redness around your facial hair, use a green color corrector. If you have blue or purple tones, use a peach or orange corrector. Apply the corrector before your foundation.

How do I blend my concealer?

Use a small brush to apply concealer to the areas where you have hair. Blend it in with a brush or sponge, making sure to blend it well so that it doesn’t look obvious.

Hair Care

While makeup can be a great way to cover up dark facial hair, it’s important to take care of your hair as well. Consider talking to a dermatologist or esthetician about the best ways to remove facial hair safely and effectively. They may recommend methods like laser hair removal, electrolysis, or threading.

Remember, everyone’s skin is different, so it’s important to find the method that works best for you. In the meantime, use makeup to cover up any dark facial hair and feel confident in your appearance.

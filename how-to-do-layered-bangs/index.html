How To Do Layered Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you thinking of getting a new hairstyle? Layered bangs are a great way to change up your look and add some dimension to your hair. In this article, we'll walk you through how to do layered bangs, step-by-step. From sectioning your hair to cutting your bangs, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Section Your Hair
  • How to Cut Your Bangs
  • Tips for Maintaining Layered Bangs
  • FAQ About Layered Bangs
  • Hair Care for Layered Bangs

How to Section Your Hair

The first step in creating layered bangs is to section your hair. This will ensure that your bangs are cut evenly and at the correct length. Here's what you need to do:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. You want your hair to be clean and free of tangles before you start cutting.
  2. Use a comb to create a section at the front of your head. This section should be about 2-3 inches wide.
  3. Clip the rest of your hair back so that it's out of the way.

Once you've sectioned your hair, you're ready to move on to the next step.

How to Cut Your Bangs

Now that you've sectioned your hair, it's time to cut your bangs. Here's what you need to do:

  1. Start by combing your bangs straight down. Make sure they're even and that there are no tangles.
  2. Take a small section of hair between your fingers. This section should be about the width of your finger.
  3. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. This will be your guide for the rest of your bangs.
  4. Continue to take small sections of hair and cut them straight across. Make sure you're following the guide you created in step 3.
  5. Once you've cut all of your bangs, use a comb to check that they're even. If there are any uneven sections, trim them until they're even.

Congratulations, you've just cut your own layered bangs!

Tips for Maintaining Layered Bangs

Now that you have your new hairstyle, here are some tips for maintaining your layered bangs:

  • Get regular trims to keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Use a flat iron or round brush to style your bangs.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.

With these tips, your layered bangs will look great for weeks to come.

FAQ About Layered Bangs

Here are some frequently asked questions about layered bangs:

How often should I trim my layered bangs?

You should trim your layered bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

What face shape is best suited for layered bangs?

Layered bangs look great on almost any face shape. They can help soften a square face or add dimension to a round face.

Hair Care for Layered Bangs

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair with layered bangs:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips, your hair will stay healthy and your layered bangs will look great.

Now that you know how to do layered bangs, it's time to give them a try! With a little practice, you'll be able to create a new hairstyle that's perfect for you. Happy cutting!

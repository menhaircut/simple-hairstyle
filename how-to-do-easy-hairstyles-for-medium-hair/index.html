Easy Hairstyles For Medium Hair How To Do Them

Written by Eveline Nov 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have medium-length hair and want to switch up your look without spending hours in front of the mirror, you're in luck! There are plenty of easy hairstyles for medium hair that you can do yourself at home. In this article, we'll walk you through some simple yet stylish hairstyles that will take your locks from drab to fab in no time.

Table of Contents

  • Section 1: Description
  • Section 2: How to
  • Section 3: Step by Step
  • Section 4: Tips
  • Section 5: FAQ
  • Section 6: Hair Care

Description

Medium-length hair is versatile and easy to style, making it a popular choice for many women. Whether you're heading to the office or a night out on the town, there are plenty of easy hairstyles that you can do yourself at home. From messy buns to sleek ponytails, the possibilities are endless.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles themselves, let's go over some basic styling tips. First, make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start. If you're using any heat styling tools, be sure to apply a heat protectant spray beforehand. And don't forget to have some hair elastics, bobby pins, and hairspray on hand!

Step by Step

1. Messy Bun

To achieve a messy bun, gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic, securing it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands around your face for a tousled look.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

To create a half-up half-down hairstyle, gather the top half of your hair and secure it with an elastic. For a more polished look, wrap a small section of hair around the elastic to conceal it.

3. Sleek Ponytail

To achieve a sleek ponytail, brush your hair back and gather it into a high ponytail. Use a small amount of gel or pomade to smooth down any flyaways. Secure the ponytail with an elastic and wrap a small section of hair around the elastic to conceal it.

4. Braided Crown

For a romantic look, try a braided crown. Part your hair down the middle and create two Dutch braids (braids that sit on top of your head) on either side. Wrap each braid around the back of your head, securing them with bobby pins.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your medium-length hair:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories, like headbands or barrettes, to add some flair to your look.
  • If you're short on time, try a simple low ponytail or a messy bun.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with texture. Try adding some waves or curls with a curling iron or straightener.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling medium-length hair:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go several days without washing. Try to find a balance that works for you.

Q: Can I still style my hair if it's thin?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of hairstyles that work well for thin hair, such as layered cuts or voluminous blowouts.

Hair Care

To keep your medium-length hair looking its best, be sure to follow a regular hair care routine. This should include washing and conditioning your hair with products that are appropriate for your hair type, as well as using a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Try to avoid using too much heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair over time.

With these easy hairstyles for medium hair, you'll be able to switch up your look in no time. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, these hairstyles are sure to impress. So grab your hair elastics and bobby pins, and get ready to transform your locks!

Do Wigs Cause Bald Spots

Written by Daniel Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wigs are a popular accessory for many people, whether they are looking to change up their style or are experiencing hair loss. However, there is a common concern that wearing wigs can lead to bald spots. In this article, we will explore whether or not wigs cause bald spots and provide tips on how to care for your hair while wearing wigs.

Table of Contents

  • What Causes Bald Spots?
  • Do Wigs Cause Bald Spots?
  • How to Prevent Bald Spots While Wearing Wigs
  • Tips for Caring for Your Hair While Wearing Wigs
  • Frequently Asked Questions

What Causes Bald Spots?

Bald spots can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, stress, and medical conditions. In some cases, wearing tight hairstyles or using harsh chemicals on the hair can also contribute to bald spots.

Do Wigs Cause Bald Spots?

While wearing wigs can contribute to hair loss, it is not necessarily the cause of bald spots. Bald spots can occur when the hair follicles become damaged or stop producing hair. Wearing wigs that are too tight or that pull on the hair can contribute to hair follicle damage, which can lead to bald spots over time.

It is important to note that not all wigs are created equal. High-quality wigs that are properly fitted and made with gentle materials are less likely to cause hair damage or bald spots. Additionally, taking breaks from wearing wigs and allowing the scalp to breathe can help prevent hair damage and promote healthy hair growth.

How to Prevent Bald Spots While Wearing Wigs

To prevent bald spots while wearing wigs, it is important to choose wigs that are the correct size and made with gentle materials. Consider investing in a wig cap to protect the hair and scalp from the wig's material. Additionally, avoid wearing wigs for extended periods of time and take breaks to allow the scalp to breathe.

Tips for Caring for Your Hair While Wearing Wigs

Wearing wigs can be a great way to protect your hair from daily styling and damage. However, it is important to care for your hair while wearing wigs to prevent hair loss and bald spots.

Here are some tips for caring for your hair while wearing wigs:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to promote healthy hair growth
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hot styling tools on your hair
  • Use a wig cap to protect your hair and scalp from the wig's material
  • Take breaks from wearing wigs to allow the scalp to breathe
  • Consider investing in a high-quality wig made with gentle materials

Frequently Asked Questions

Can wearing wigs cause hair loss?

Wearing wigs can contribute to hair loss if the wigs are too tight or pull on the hair. It is important to choose wigs that are properly fitted and made with gentle materials to prevent hair damage.

How long can I wear a wig?

It is recommended to take breaks from wearing wigs to allow the scalp to breathe. Avoid wearing wigs for extended periods of time and remove the wig at night to give the scalp a break.

Should I wear a wig cap?

Wearing a wig cap can help protect the hair and scalp from the wig's material. It can also help prevent the wig from slipping or moving around on the head.


In conclusion, wigs do not necessarily cause bald spots, but wearing wigs that are too tight or made with harsh materials can contribute to hair damage and loss. To prevent bald spots and care for your hair while wearing wigs, choose high-quality wigs that are properly fitted and made with gentle materials. Additionally, take breaks from wearing wigs and care for your hair regularly to promote healthy hair growth.

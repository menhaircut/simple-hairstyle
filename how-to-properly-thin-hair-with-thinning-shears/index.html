How To Properly Thin Hair With Thinning Shears

Written by Frank Oct 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your thick and unruly hair? Thinning shears might be the solution to your problem. Thinning shears are scissors with teeth that can help reduce the bulk of your hair without sacrificing the length. In this article, we will teach you how to properly thin hair with thinning shears.

Description

Thinning shears are a great tool for people with thick hair. They are designed to remove weight from the hair without changing the overall length. Thinning shears have teeth on one side and a straight blade on the other. The teeth allow you to cut the hair at different lengths, making it look more natural.

How to

When using thinning shears, it's important to remember to only cut a little bit of hair at a time. If you cut too much hair at once, you risk creating a choppy or uneven look.

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start using thinning shears, you need to prepare your hair. Wash and dry your hair, then comb it to remove tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any spots.

Step 3: Start Thinning

Hold a section of your hair with your non-dominant hand and use the thinning shears to cut into the hair. Start at the middle of the section and work your way down to the ends.

Keep in mind that you should only cut a little bit of hair at a time. You can always go back and thin more if needed.

Step 4: Blend

After thinning a section of hair, use regular scissors to blend the cut hair with the uncut hair. This will ensure that your hair looks natural and not choppy.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using thinning shears:

  • Start with dry hair
  • Use sharp thinning shears
  • Only thin the ends of your hair
  • Don't cut too much hair at once
  • Blend the cut hair with the uncut hair

FAQ

Can thinning shears damage your hair?

Thinning shears can damage your hair if they are not used properly. That's why it's important to only cut a little bit of hair at a time and to blend the cut hair with the uncut hair.

How often should you thin your hair?

How often you should thin your hair depends on the thickness and length of your hair. Generally, you should only thin your hair every few months.

Can you use thinning shears on curly hair?

Yes, you can use thinning shears on curly hair. However, it's important to be careful not to thin too much hair, as this can cause the curls to lose their shape.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after using thinning shears. Use a good conditioner to keep your hair hydrated, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

In conclusion, thinning shears can be a great tool for people with thick hair. By following these tips and steps, you can properly thin your hair without sacrificing the length.

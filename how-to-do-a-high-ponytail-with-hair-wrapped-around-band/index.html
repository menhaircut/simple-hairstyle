How To Do A High Ponytail With Hair Wrapped Around Band

Written by Robby Sep 19, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring ponytail? Why not try a high ponytail with hair wrapped around the band? This versatile hairstyle is perfect for both casual and formal occasions. In this article, we'll take you through the step-by-step process of achieving this look. But before we get started, here's a quick table of contents to help you navigate the article:

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a High Ponytail with Hair Wrapped Around Band
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect High Ponytail
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step-by-Step Guide

Before we dive into the steps, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Elastic hair band
  • Hairbrush
  • Hair spray
  • Bobby pins (optional)

Now let's get started with the steps:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Make sure your hair is completely smooth before moving on to the next step.

Step 2: Tie Your Hair into a High Ponytail

Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic hair band. Make sure the ponytail is tight and secure.

Step 3: Create a Gap in the Hair Above the Elastic Band

Using your fingers, create a small gap in the hair above the elastic band. Make sure the gap is big enough for you to tuck your ponytail through later.

Step 4: Tuck the Ponytail Through the Gap

Gently tuck the ponytail through the gap you created in the previous step. Make sure the ponytail is completely tucked through and the elastic band is no longer visible.

Step 5: Wrap a Section of Hair Around the Elastic Band

Take a small section of hair from the bottom of the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band. Use bobby pins to secure the hair in place if necessary.

Step 6: Secure the Hair with Hair Spray

Once you've wrapped the hair around the elastic band, use hair spray to secure it in place. This will help keep your high ponytail looking neat and tidy all day.

Tips for a Perfect High Ponytail

Here are a few tips to help you achieve a perfect high ponytail with hair wrapped around the band:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less visible.
  • For a sleeker look, use a comb to smooth any bumps or flyaways.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, try teasing the hair at the crown of your head before tying it into a ponytail to add volume.
  • If you have thick or curly hair, use a hair straightener to smooth out any frizz before tying it into a ponytail.

FAQs

Q: How do I make my high ponytail stay up?

A: To make your high ponytail stay up, use a hair tie that is tight enough to hold your hair in place but not so tight that it hurts. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose hairs or to hold the hair tie in place.

Q: How do I prevent my hair from getting tangled in the elastic band?

A: To prevent your hair from getting tangled in the elastic band, make sure your hair is completely smooth before tying it into a ponytail. You can also use a detangling spray or serum to help prevent tangles.

Hair Care Tips

Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind when styling your hair into a high ponytail:

  • Don't tie your hair into a ponytail when it's wet as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you're using a hair straightener or curling iron to style your hair.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps as these can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Don't tie your hair into a tight ponytail for extended periods of time as this can cause tension headaches and damage to your hair.

And there you have it - a step-by-step guide on how to do a high ponytail with hair wrapped around the band. We hope you found this article helpful and that you're now ready to try out this stylish and versatile hairstyle for yourself!

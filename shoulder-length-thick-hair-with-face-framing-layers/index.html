Shoulder Length Thick Hair With Face Framing Layers The Perfect Haircut For 2023

Written by Oliver Sep 16, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking for a fresh new haircut for 2023, you might want to consider shoulder length thick hair with face framing layers. This style is perfect for those with thick hair who want to add some dimension and movement to their locks. In this blog post, we'll go over everything you need to know about this trendy haircut, from how to get it to how to take care of it.

Description

The shoulder length thick hair with face framing layers is a haircut that features layers that frame the face and add volume to the hair. This style works best on those with thick hair, as the layers help to add movement and texture to the hair. The length of the hair is kept at shoulder level, making it a versatile style that can be worn up or down.

How to Get It

If you're interested in getting this haircut, the first step is to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting layers. You'll want to bring in some inspiration photos to show your stylist what you're looking for. They will then work with you to determine the best length and layer placement for your hair type and face shape. Once the cut is complete, your stylist may also add some styling product to help enhance the layers and add volume.

Step by Step

1. Find an experienced stylist
2. Bring in inspiration photos
3. Work with stylist to determine length and layer placement
4. Cut hair to desired length and add layers
5. Style with product to enhance layers and add volume

Tips

If you've decided to go for the shoulder length thick hair with face framing layers, there are a few tips to keep in mind to make sure your hair stays healthy and looking great:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type

FAQ

Is this style suitable for all face shapes?

While this style is generally flattering on most face shapes, it's always best to consult with a stylist before making any drastic changes to your hair. They can help determine the best layer placement for your face shape.

Can this style be worn curly or straight?

Yes! This style can be worn curly or straight, making it a versatile option for those who like to switch up their look.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

To keep your layers looking fresh, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length thick hair with face framing layers looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This means using high-quality shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heated styling tools too often, and getting regular trims. You can also experiment with different styling products to enhance your layers and add volume.

Overall, the shoulder length thick hair with face framing layers is a trendy and versatile haircut that's perfect for 2023. Whether you're looking to add some dimension to your locks or just want a fresh new look, this style is definitely worth considering!

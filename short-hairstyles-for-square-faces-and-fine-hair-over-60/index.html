Short Hairstyles For Square Faces And Fine Hair Over 60

Are you over 60 with a square face and fine hair? Don't fret! There are plenty of short hairstyles that can help you look stunning and feel confident. In this article, we'll be sharing some of the best short hairstyles for square faces and fine hair over 60. So, let's dive right in!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline and a broad forehead. If you have fine hair, you may struggle to find hairstyles that flatter your face shape while adding volume to your hair. However, short hairstyles can work wonders for square faces and fine hair over 60.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a square face and fine hair over 60, it's important to consider your hair texture, face shape, and personal style. A professional hairstylist can help you determine the best haircut for you.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best short hairstyles for square faces and fine hair over 60:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is an excellent choice for square faces with fine hair. It's a low-maintenance haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair. Ask your hairstylist for a layered pixie cut that's shorter on the sides and longer on top. You can also add some highlights to create dimension.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that works well for square faces and fine hair. Ask your hairstylist for a chin-length bob that's layered and textured. You can add some waves or curls to create volume and movement.

Short Shag

A short shag is a trendy hairstyle that looks great on square faces with fine hair. Ask your hairstylist for a shaggy bob that's layered and textured. You can also add some bangs to frame your face.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for square faces and fine hair over 60:

  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and adds volume to your hair.
  • Consider your hair texture and choose a haircut that works well with it.
  • Ask your hairstylist for advice on the best haircut for you.
  • Experiment with different styles and hair products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What is the best short hairstyle for a square face and fine hair over 60?

The best short hairstyle for a square face and fine hair over 60 depends on your personal style and hair texture. A pixie cut, bob cut, or short shag are all excellent choices.

How do I choose the right haircut for my face shape?

Consult with a professional hairstylist who can help you determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair texture.

Hair Care

When maintaining a short hairstyle for square faces and fine hair over 60, it's important to use the right hair care products. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. Use a lightweight hair oil or serum to add shine and control frizz. And don't forget to schedule regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

With these tips and suggestions, you can rock a chic and stylish short hairstyle that flatters your square face and fine hair over 60. So, go ahead and book that appointment with your hairstylist today!

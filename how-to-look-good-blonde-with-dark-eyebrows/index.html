How To Look Good Blonde With Dark Eyebrows

Written by Eveline Jul 29, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking to go blonde but worried that your dark eyebrows won't match? Fear not, because with the right techniques and products, you can rock the blonde look with confidence. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about how to look good blonde with dark eyebrows.

Description

Going blonde is a dramatic change and can be a daunting experience. It can be especially challenging if you have naturally dark eyebrows, as they can clash with your new hair color. However, with a few simple tricks, you can make sure that your eyebrows complement your blonde locks.

How to

The first step in achieving a blonde look with dark eyebrows is to determine the right shade of blonde for your skin tone. This is crucial because choosing the wrong shade can make your eyebrows look even darker and more pronounced. Once you have the right shade, you can start to work on your eyebrows.

Step by Step

Step 1: Lighten your eyebrows

The easiest way to make your dark eyebrows match your blonde hair is to lighten them. You can do this by using a brow lightening kit, which you can find at your local drugstore. Follow the instructions on the kit carefully and be sure to patch test beforehand.

Step 2: Fill in your eyebrows

Once your eyebrows are lightened, you may find that they look sparse. To combat this, use a brow pencil or powder to fill them in. Choose a shade that is slightly lighter than your natural eyebrow color to keep the look natural.

Step 3: Use a brow gel

To keep your eyebrows in place, use a clear or tinted brow gel. This will help to set your eyebrows and prevent them from smudging or fading throughout the day.

Tips

- Be patient. Achieving the perfect blonde look with dark eyebrows may take some trial and error.

- Consider getting your eyebrows professionally tinted. This can help to match them to your new hair color.

- Use a highlighter to accentuate your brow bones and bring attention to your eyebrows.

FAQ

Q: Will bleaching my eyebrows damage them?

A: Bleaching your eyebrows can cause some damage, so it's important to follow the instructions carefully and patch test beforehand. You can also apply a nourishing eyebrow oil after the treatment to help soothe any irritation.

Q: Can I dye my eyebrows to match my hair color?

A: It's not recommended to dye your eyebrows at home, as it can be dangerous if the dye gets into your eyes. It's best to leave this to the professionals.

Hair Care

Going blonde can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to take care of it properly. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You should also use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock a blonde look with dark eyebrows. Remember to take care of your hair and eyebrows properly to maintain the look.

How To Thin Medium Length Thick Hair

Written by Oliver Sep 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling with thick hair that is difficult to manage? If you have medium length hair that is thick, you may feel like there is no way to tame it. However, there are ways to thin your hair and make it more manageable. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to thin medium length thick hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Thin Medium Length Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions

How to Thin Medium Length Thick Hair

Thinning your hair is the process of removing some of the bulk and weight from your hair. This can be done in a variety of ways, including using thinning shears, razor cutting, or texturizing techniques.

Tools You Will Need:

  • Thinning Shears
  • Regular Scissors
  • Hair Comb
  • Hair Clips

Step-by-Step Instructions:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any product buildup and make your hair easier to work with.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use your hair clips to section your hair into manageable sections. This will make it easier to thin your hair evenly.

Step 3: Use Thinning Shears

Hold your hair out straight and use the thinning shears to cut into your hair at a 45-degree angle. Start at the ends and work your way up towards the middle of your hair shaft. Repeat this process on each section of your hair.

Step 4: Blend Your Hair

Use your regular scissors to blend your hair by cutting into the ends of your hair. This will help to create a more natural and even look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as you normally would, using your favorite styling products. Your hair should look lighter and more manageable.

Tips for Thinning Medium Length Thick Hair

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid over-thinning.
  • Don't use thinning shears on wet hair as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Make sure to blend your hair evenly to avoid any choppy or uneven sections.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut to thin your hair.

FAQs

Can I thin my hair at home?

Yes, you can thin your hair at home using thinning shears and regular scissors. However, it is important to be cautious and start with small sections of hair to avoid over-thinning.

Will thinning my hair damage it?

Thinning your hair can cause damage if not done correctly. It is important to use proper tools and techniques to avoid any damage or breakage.

How often should I thin my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and how quickly it grows. However, it is recommended to thin your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain a manageable and healthy look.

Hair Care for Thinning Medium Length Thick Hair

Thinning your hair can make it more manageable, but it is also important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid any damage or breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair when styling.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a more manageable and healthy look for your medium length thick hair. Remember to be cautious and start with small sections of hair to avoid over-thinning. If you're unsure or want a professional opinion, consider getting a haircut from a stylist who specializes in thinning hair.

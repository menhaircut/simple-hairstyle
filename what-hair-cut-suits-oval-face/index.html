What Hair Cut Suits Oval Face A Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Jun 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of getting a haircut that doesn't quite suit your face shape? Well, if you have an oval face, you're in luck! With its balanced proportions and versatility, there are plenty of haircuts that can suit an oval face shape. In this guide, we'll go through the different types of haircuts that are perfect for an oval face shape in 2023.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Haircut for an Oval Face
  3. Step by Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Description

Before we dive into the different types of haircuts that suit an oval face shape, let's first understand what an oval face shape is. An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, with a face length that is approximately one and a half times the width of the face.

An oval face shape is considered the most versatile face shape, as it can pull off a variety of haircuts and styles. So, whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there are plenty of haircuts that can flatter your face shape.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for an Oval Face

When choosing a haircut for an oval face shape, the goal is to create balance and proportion. You want to avoid haircuts that add too much volume to the top of your head or make your face look longer. Instead, opt for haircuts that accentuate your features and bring out your best qualities.

Some of the best haircuts for an oval face shape include:

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are perfect for oval face shapes, as they can help balance out the proportions of your face. A pixie cut, for example, can help highlight your cheekbones and jawline, while a bob can add volume and texture to your hair.

Medium Haircuts

Medium haircuts are also a great option for an oval face shape. A lob or a shoulder-length cut can help frame your face and bring out your best features. You can also add layers to your hair to add texture and movement.

Long Haircuts

If you prefer longer hair, don't worry! There are plenty of long haircuts that can suit an oval face shape. A long layered cut can help add volume and texture to your hair, while a side-swept fringe can help balance out your face shape.

Step by Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

Now that you know what types of haircuts suit an oval face shape, it's time to get the perfect haircut. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect haircut for your oval face shape:

  1. Find inspiration: Take a look at different haircuts and styles that suit an oval face shape. Save pictures or create a mood board to bring to your hairstylist.
  2. Choose a stylist: Choose a hairstylist who has experience working with oval face shapes. They can help guide you through the process and suggest haircuts that will suit your face shape.
  3. Consultation: During your consultation, discuss your hair goals and the different haircuts you're interested in. Your hairstylist can then advise you on which haircuts will work best for your face shape and hair texture.
  4. Cut and style: Once you've decided on a haircut, sit back and relax while your hairstylist works their magic. They will cut and style your hair to perfection.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Maintaining your haircut is just as important as getting the perfect cut. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Regular trims: Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and prevent split ends.
  • Use the right products: Use hair products that suit your hair type and texture. This will help your hair look its best and maintain its shape.
  • Protect your hair: Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.

FAQs

What face shape suits a bob?

A bob can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, heart, and square. When choosing a bob for an oval face shape, opt for a chin-length bob or a long bob that falls just above the shoulders.

Can an oval face shape pull off bangs?

Yes! Bangs can look great on an oval face shape. Opt for side-swept bangs or curtain bangs that will help balance out your face shape.

What is the best haircut for fine hair?

The best haircut for fine hair is a layered cut. Layers can help add volume and texture to fine hair, making it look fuller and thicker.

Hair Care for an Oval Face Shape

Now that you have the perfect haircut for your oval face shape, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for an oval face shape:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Deep condition: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid heat styling: Try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet: Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll have the perfect haircut for your oval face shape in no time. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

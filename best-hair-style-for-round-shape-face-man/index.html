Best Hair Style For Round Shape Face Man

Are you a man with a round face shape and looking for the best hair style that would suit you perfectly? Look no further, as we have got you covered! In this article, we will be discussing the best hair style for round shape face man and provide you with step by step guide on how to achieve it.

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

A round face shape is characterized by fullness in the cheeks and a rounded jawline. The best hair style for a round shape face man is one that adds height and volume to the top of the head, while keeping the sides short. This helps to elongate the face and create a more angular appearance.

How To

The best way to achieve the perfect hair style for round shape face man is by consulting with a professional hairstylist. They will be able to advise you on which hair style would suit your face shape and help you achieve it. However, if you prefer to do it yourself, follow the steps outlined below:

Step by Step

1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product such as mousse or gel.

3. Use a hairdryer and a round brush to create volume at the top of your head.

4. Use a trimmer or scissors to trim the sides and back of your hair to a shorter length.

5. Style your hair using a comb or your fingers to create the desired look.

6. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

- Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your head as this will only make your face appear rounder.

- Opt for hairstyles that have some length on top as this will help to create the illusion of a longer face.

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Q: Can I style my hair in a messy way?

A: Yes, you can. In fact, messy hairstyles can look great on men with round face shapes as they help to create a more relaxed and casual look.

Q: Should I avoid bangs?

A: Yes, it is best to avoid bangs as they tend to make the face appear shorter and rounder.

Hair Care

It is important to take good care of your hair to maintain a healthy and attractive appearance. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, avoid using too much heat on your hair, and trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, the best hair style for round shape face man is one that adds height and volume to the top of the head while keeping the sides short. Consult with a professional hairstylist or follow the steps outlined in this article to achieve the perfect look. Remember to take good care of your hair to maintain a healthy and attractive appearance.

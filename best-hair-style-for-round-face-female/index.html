Best Hair Style For Round Face Female How To Choose The Perfect Haircut

Written by Daniel Aug 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a round-faced female looking for the perfect hairstyle? Look no further! In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about choosing the best hair style for round face female. Follow our tips and tricks to find the perfect haircut that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.

Description

A round face shape is characterized by fullness in the cheeks and a rounded chin. Your goal in choosing a hairstyle should be to elongate the face and create a more angular appearance. This can be achieved by adding volume and height at the crown, as well as creating angles and layers around the face.

When choosing a hairstyle for round face female, it’s important to consider your hair type and texture. Fine hair may require different styling techniques than thick or curly hair. Your lifestyle and personal preferences should also be taken into account, as you want a style that is both flattering and easy to maintain.

How to Choose the Best Hair Style for Round Face Female

The first step in choosing the best hair style for round face female is to determine your face shape. This can be done by measuring the length and width of your face, as well as the distance between your forehead and chin.

Once you’ve determined that you have a round face shape, you can begin to look for styles that flatter your features. Some popular options include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Volume at the crown
  • A-line bob
  • Messy updo

When choosing a hairstyle, keep in mind that you want to create the illusion of length and angles. Avoid styles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs.

Step by Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Haircut

Follow these steps to find the perfect haircut for your round face shape:

  1. Measure your face to determine your face shape.
  2. Research and gather inspiration for styles that flatter round faces.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine what style will work best for your hair type and texture.
  4. Try on different styles using virtual makeover tools or by testing out different hairstyles with clips or bobby pins.
  5. Select a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural features.

Tips and Tricks for Styling Round Face Female Hair

Once you’ve found the perfect haircut, follow these tips and tricks to style your hair for maximum impact:

  • Add volume at the crown to elongate the face.
  • Use side-swept bangs to create angles around the face.
  • Avoid adding width to the face with blunt or straight-across bangs.
  • Layer hair around the face to create an elongated appearance.
  • Try out different parting styles to see what flatters your face shape.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, or barrettes to add interest to your hairstyle.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid styles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs. Stick to styles that create angles and elongate the face.

What is the best hair length for a round face?

Long hair is a great option for round faces, as it creates length and elongates the face. Shorter styles can also work, as long as they create angles and layers around the face.

How can I add volume to my hair?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a root-boosting spray or mousse. Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume at the crown.

Hair Care Tips for Round Face Female Hair

Follow these tips to keep your round face female hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect hair style for round face female and keep your locks looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to consult with a hairstylist if you’re unsure about what style will work best for your face shape and hair type. Happy styling!

How To Cut Short Hair Brad Mondo A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you thinking of cutting your short hair but don't know where to start? Look no further than Brad Mondo's expert guide on how to cut short hair. In this article, we'll break down his expert techniques and give you step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect short haircut.

Description

Brad Mondo is a celebrity hairstylist and YouTube personality known for his expert tips and techniques on all things hair. If you're looking to cut your short hair, his guide is the perfect place to start. Whether you're going for a pixie cut or a bob, Brad Mondo's techniques will help you achieve the perfect look.

How to

To start, you'll need a few key tools. These include sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It's also important to have a clear idea of the type of cut you want before you begin. Once you have your tools and vision, follow these steps:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: front, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 2: Cut the Back

Beginning with the back section, use your comb to separate a small section of hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and use your scissors to cut straight across. Work your way up the back of your head, cutting each section of hair to the desired length.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the sides of your head. Again, separate a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair to the desired length, working from the bottom up.

Step 4: Cut the Front

Finally, move on to the front of your head. Use your comb to separate a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair to the desired length, working from the center outwards.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind as you cut your short hair:

  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Trim small sections of hair at a time to avoid cutting off too much.
  • Keep your comb and scissors clean to prevent snagging and tangles.
  • If you're unsure about a particular section of hair, err on the side of caution and cut less rather than more.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about cutting short hair:

Q: How often should I cut my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I cut my own short hair?

A: Yes, you can cut your own short hair with the right tools and techniques. However, if you're unsure or don't feel confident, it's always best to consult with a professional hairstylist.

Hair Care

After cutting your short hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are a few key tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect short haircut just like Brad Mondo. Remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and consult a professional if you're unsure. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to master the art of cutting short hair.

What Hairstyles Suit A Round Face Shape

Written by Bardi Nov 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face shape, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a bit tricky. You want a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your features, without making your face appear even rounder. In this article, we’ll explore the best hairstyles for round faces and provide some tips for keeping your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  2. Hairstyles for Round Faces
  3. Tips for Styling Round Face Shape Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care for Round Face Shapes

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into hairstyles, it’s important to determine your face shape. To do so, pull your hair back and take a look in the mirror. Round faces typically have soft, curved jawlines and a similar width and height measurement. You may also have fuller cheeks and a rounded forehead.

Hairstyles for Round Faces

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for a round face shape, the goal is to create the illusion of length and angles. Here are some hairstyles to consider:

Long Layers

Long, layered hairstyles are ideal for round face shapes. The layers help to create the illusion of length, while the length keeps the face from appearing wider. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, which can make the face appear even rounder.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to create angles around the face, making it appear longer and more angular. Avoid straight, blunt bangs, which can make the face appear even rounder.

Updos with Height

Updos with height at the crown can help to elongate the face and create a more angular shape. Avoid flat, sleek styles, which can make the face appear even rounder.

Loose Waves

Loose waves can help to create movement and dimension around the face, making it appear longer and more angular. Avoid tight curls, which can make the face appear even rounder.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob that is longer on one side can help to create angles around the face, making it appear longer and more angular. Avoid bobs that are all one length, which can make the face appear even rounder.

Tips for Styling Round Face Shape Hair

Once you’ve found the perfect hairstyle, here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add lift and height at the crown.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, which can make the face appear even rounder.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying to create volume and lift.
  • Consider using a straightening iron to create angles and movement around the face.
  • Use a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or texturizing spray, to add texture and volume.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face shape?

Avoid blunt cuts, heavy bangs, and hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face. These styles can make the face appear even rounder.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a round face shape?

A center part can make a round face shape appear even rounder. Consider a side part instead to create angles around the face.

Should I avoid short hairstyles if I have a round face shape?

Short hairstyles can be flattering on round face shapes, as long as they are cut in a way that creates angles and movement around the face. Consider an asymmetrical bob or a short layered cut.

Hair Care for Round Face Shapes

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straightening irons and curling wands, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to help prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to keep your hair looking its best.

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can enhance your features and create a flattering look for your round face shape.

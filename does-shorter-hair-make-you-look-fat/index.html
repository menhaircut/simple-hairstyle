Does Shorter Hair Make You Look Fat

Written by Robby Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, many people believe that shorter hair can make you look fat. But is this really true? In this article, we will explore this common perception and determine if there is any truth to it. We will also provide some tips on how to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape and body type.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Short Hair

Description

One of the most common reasons people believe that shorter hair can make you look fat is because it draws attention to your face. In contrast, longer hair can elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. However, this does not necessarily mean that all short hairstyles will make you look fat.

Another reason people believe that shorter hair can make you look fat is because it can accentuate certain features, such as your cheeks and chin. If you have a round face or double chin, some short hairstyles may not be the most flattering for you.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The key to choosing the right hairstyle is to consider your face shape and body type. There are many different face shapes, including round, square, oval, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has its own unique features, and certain hairstyles can accentuate or minimize these features.

For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head. Instead, try a hairstyle that adds height to the top of your head, such as a pixie cut or a short bob with layers. If you have a square face, you may want to soften your features with a hairstyle that has soft layers or curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right hairstyle:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Research hairstyles that are flattering for your face shape.
  3. Consider your hair texture and thickness.
  4. Think about your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your body type. If you have a larger body type, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to your head.
  • Use accessories to add interest to your hair, such as headbands, clips, or scarves.
  • Experiment with different styles to see what works best for you.
  • Consider your skin tone when choosing a hair color.

FAQs

Q: Does shorter hair make you look fat?

A: Not necessarily. The key to choosing the right hairstyle is to consider your face shape and body type. There are many short hairstyles that can be flattering for different face shapes and body types.

Q: What hairstyles are best for a round face?

A: Hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, such as a pixie cut or a short bob with layers, can be flattering for a round face.

Hair Care for Short Hair

If you decide to go for a shorter hairstyle, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to maintain your hairstyle.

Conclusion

In conclusion, while some people believe that shorter hair can make you look fat, this is not necessarily true. The key to choosing the right hairstyle is to consider your face shape and body type, and to experiment with different styles to see what works best for you. With the right hairstyle and hair care, you can look and feel your best!

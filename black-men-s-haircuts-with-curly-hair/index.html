Black Men s Haircuts With Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 08, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a black man with curly hair looking for a new haircut? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the best haircuts for black men with curly hair, how to style them, and how to take care of your curls.

Table of Contents

  • Types of Haircuts for Black Men with Curly Hair
  • How to Style Your Haircuts

  • Hair Care Tips for Black Men with Curly Hair

Types of Haircuts for Black Men with Curly Hair

There are many different haircuts that work well with black men's curly hair. Some popular options include:

High Top Fade

The high top fade is a classic haircut that has been popular for decades. It involves cutting the sides of the hair short while leaving a longer section on top. This longer section can be styled into a rounded shape, giving the haircut its signature look.

Short Curls

If you prefer a shorter haircut, consider a style that keeps your curls cropped close to your head. This look is easy to maintain and can be styled with a bit of product for a defined, polished finish.

Long Curls

If you have longer hair, consider growing out your curls for a more dramatic look. This style requires a bit more maintenance, but the end result can be stunning.

How to Style Your Haircuts

Regardless of which haircut you choose, styling curly hair can be a bit challenging. Here are some tips to help you achieve the look you want:

Use Products

Using the right products can make a huge difference in how your curls look and feel. Look for styling products that are designed for curly hair, such as curl creams, gels, and mousses.

Don't Overwash Your Hair

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important not to overwash it. Try to limit washing your hair to every other day or every few days, and use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make your curls look frizzy. Instead, embrace your natural texture and use styling products to enhance your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Curls

Here's a simple step-by-step guide to styling your curls:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a quarter-sized amount of curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly through your hair.
  4. Scrunch your hair gently with your hands to encourage your curls to form.
  5. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your curls gently.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

In addition to styling your curls, there are a few things you can do to keep them looking and feeling their best:

Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help keep your curls looking neat and tidy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Cotton pillowcases can cause friction and lead to frizz and breakage. Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help keep your curls looking smooth and healthy.

Avoid Brushing Your Hair

Brushing curly hair can cause it to frizz and lose its shape. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when necessary.

FAQ About Black Men's Haircuts with Curly Hair

What haircut is best for black men with curly hair?

The best haircut for black men with curly hair depends on your personal style and preferences. Some popular options include the high top fade, short curls, and long curls.

How do you style curly hair for black men?

To style curly hair for black men, use curl-enhancing products and avoid heat styling tools. Scrunch your hair gently with your hands to encourage your curls to form, and let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Hair Care Tips for Black Men with Curly Hair

Finally, here are some general hair care tips for black men with curly hair:

Moisturize Your Hair

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important to keep your curls hydrated. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as relaxers and texturizers. These treatments can damage your hair and make it more difficult to maintain your curls.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help keep your hair looking and feeling healthy. Focus on eating plenty of fruits and vegetables, lean protein, and healthy fats.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect haircut and style for your curly hair. Remember to take care of your curls by using the right products and avoiding heat styling, and your hair will look and feel its best.

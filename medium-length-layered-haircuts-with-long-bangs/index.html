Medium Length Layered Haircuts With Long Bangs A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and versatile haircut that will enhance your natural beauty? Look no further than medium length layered haircuts with long bangs. This trendy style is perfect for those who want to add volume, texture, and movement to their hair while keeping it at a manageable length. In this guide, we will cover everything you need to know about medium length layered haircuts with long bangs, including how to get the look, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

Medium length layered haircuts with long bangs are characterized by layers of varying lengths throughout the hair, which create movement and texture. The layers are typically cut at angles to add depth and dimension to the style. Long bangs are also incorporated into the haircut, which frame the face and add a touch of femininity. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish and trendy.

How to

If you're considering getting a medium length layered haircut with long bangs, it's important to find a skilled hairstylist who can create the look you want. Here's how to get the look:

Step 1: Consultation

Before your haircut, schedule a consultation with your hairstylist to discuss the style you want. Bring photos of the haircut you like and be clear about your expectations.

Step 2: Cut the Layers

The hairstylist will start by cutting the layers of your hair, starting from the bottom and working their way up. They will use scissors or a razor to create texture and movement.

Step 3: Cut the Bangs

Once the layers are complete, the hairstylist will cut the long bangs. They will usually be cut at an angle to blend seamlessly with the rest of the layers.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once the haircut is complete, the hairstylist will style your hair using a blow dryer, curling iron, or straightener to create the desired look. They may also use styling products to add volume and texture.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling your medium length layered haircut with long bangs:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume.
  • Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture to your hair.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length layered haircuts with long bangs:

Q: Will this haircut work for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How often do I need to get this haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with this haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with this haircut. The layers and long bangs will add texture and movement to your updo.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length layered haircut with long bangs looking healthy and stylish, it's important to follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip the natural oils from your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

In conclusion, medium length layered haircuts with long bangs are a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can suit any hair type and face shape. By following the tips and advice in this guide, you can achieve a stylish and low-maintenance haircut that will enhance your natural beauty.

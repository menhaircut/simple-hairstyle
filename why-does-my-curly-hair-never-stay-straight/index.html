Why Does My Curly Hair Never Stay Straight

Written by Luffy Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you might have tried to straighten it at some point. Whether you used a flat iron or a chemical treatment, you might have noticed that your hair never stays straight for very long. In this article, we will explore the reasons behind this frustrating phenomenon and provide some tips on how to keep your hair straight for longer.

  • What Causes Curly Hair to Revert to Its Natural State?

What Causes Curly Hair to Revert to Its Natural State?

Curly hair is naturally more resistant to being straightened than straight hair. This is because curly hair has a different structure than straight hair. Each hair follicle has a curved shape, which causes the hair to curl. When you straighten your hair, you are essentially breaking the bonds that hold the hair in its curly shape. However, these bonds are not completely broken, which is why your hair might revert back to its natural state over time.

Another reason why curly hair might revert back to its natural state is humidity. Moisture can cause the hair to curl up again, even if you have straightened it. This is because curly hair is more porous than straight hair, which means it can absorb more moisture. If you live in a humid climate, it might be difficult to keep your hair straight for long periods of time.

How to Straighten Curly Hair

There are several ways to straighten curly hair, including:

  • Using a flat iron
  • Using a chemical treatment, such as a keratin treatment
  • Using a blow dryer and round brush

Each of these methods has its own pros and cons. Using a flat iron is quick and easy, but it can also damage your hair if you use it too frequently or at high temperatures. Chemical treatments can provide longer-lasting results, but they can also be expensive and time-consuming. Using a blow dryer and round brush is a more natural method, but it can also take longer and might not provide the same level of straightness as other methods.

Step-by-Step Guide to Straightening Curly Hair

If you decide to use a flat iron to straighten your hair, here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair until it is completely dry.
  4. Section your hair into small sections.
  5. Starting at the roots, clamp the flat iron onto a small section of hair and glide it down to the ends.
  6. Repeat this process on all sections of hair.
  7. Apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Keeping Your Hair Straight

If you want to keep your hair straight for longer, here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after straightening it.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause it to revert back to its natural state.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction on your hair while you sleep.
  • Avoid humidity and moisture as much as possible.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my hair if it is damaged?

A: It depends on the extent of the damage. If your hair is severely damaged, it might not be able to withstand the heat from a flat iron or other straightening methods. It is best to consult with a professional stylist to determine the best course of action for your hair.

Q: How often should I straighten my hair?

A: It is best to limit the use of heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair over time. If you do decide to straighten your hair, try to do it no more than once a week.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it is important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently.

With the right care and styling techniques, you can keep your curly hair looking beautiful and healthy.

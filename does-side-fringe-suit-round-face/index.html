Does Side Fringe Suit Round Face

Written by Aprilia Jul 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will be discussing whether a side fringe suits a round face shape. Round faces are characterized by soft curves, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you may be wondering if a side fringe will flatter your features. We will be exploring this topic in-depth, providing you with tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice to help you make an informed decision.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A side fringe, also known as a side-swept fringe, is a hairstyle where the hair is parted to one side and cut into a fringe that falls across one eyebrow. Side fringes can be long or short and can be styled in a variety of ways. They are a popular hairstyle choice as they can frame the face, add texture to a hairstyle, and can help to camouflage a high forehead.

Round faces are often described as being cute, youthful, and feminine. However, they can also be a challenge when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. Round faces tend to lack definition, and a side fringe can help to create the illusion of angularity and add dimension to the face.

How to

Before deciding if a side fringe is right for you, it is essential to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and looking at your face shape in the mirror. If your face is as wide as it is long and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you likely have a round face shape.

If you have a round face shape, a side fringe can be a flattering hairstyle choice. However, it is essential to consider your hair type, face structure, and lifestyle when deciding on a side fringe.

Step by step

If you have decided that a side fringe is right for you, here is a step-by-step guide on how to style one:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Blow-dry your hair straight, using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  3. Part your hair to one side, using a comb to create a straight line.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the side with less hair and comb it forward.
  5. Cut the hair to the desired length, using hairdressing scissors.
  6. Repeat on the other side, making sure to cut the hair to the same length.
  7. Use a styling product, such as a texturizing spray, to add definition to the fringe.
  8. Style the rest of your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your side fringe:

  • Choose a fringe length and thickness that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Use a styling product, such as a hair oil, to keep your fringe looking smooth and shiny.
  • Get regular trims to keep your fringe looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening, to change up your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about side fringes and round face shapes:

Q: Will a side fringe make my face look slimmer?

A: Yes, a side fringe can help to create the illusion of angularity and add dimension to the face, making it appear slimmer.

Q: Can I wear a side fringe if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a side fringe with curly hair. However, it is important to choose a fringe length and thickness that suits your hair type and face shape.

Q: How often should I trim my side fringe?

A: It is recommended to trim your side fringe every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

To keep your side fringe looking its best, it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, using a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, a side fringe can be a flattering hairstyle choice for those with a round face shape. By following our tips and step-by-step guide, you can achieve a stylish and on-trend look that flatters your features. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep your side fringe looking its best.

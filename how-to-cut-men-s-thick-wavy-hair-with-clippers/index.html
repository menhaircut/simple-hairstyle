How To Cut Men s Thick Wavy Hair With Clippers

Written by Oliver Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man with thick, wavy hair, you know how difficult it can be to maintain. It can be hard to find a haircut that works with your hair type, and even harder to find a barber who knows how to cut it properly. However, cutting your hair with clippers at home can save you time and money. In this article, we'll teach you how to cut men's thick wavy hair with clippers.

Description

Men's thick wavy hair can be challenging to cut, but with the right tools, it's a breeze. Clippers are a popular choice for cutting men's hair because they're easy to use and give a clean, even cut. They're also affordable, which means you can save money on haircuts by doing it yourself at home. In this guide, we'll show you how to use clippers to cut men's thick wavy hair.

How to

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

The first step in cutting men's thick wavy hair with clippers is to choose the right clippers. Look for clippers with adjustable blades, as these will allow you to cut hair at different lengths. You'll also want to choose clippers that are powerful enough to cut through thick hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and give you a more accurate result. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for thick, wavy hair, and towel dry your hair until it's mostly dry.

Step 3: Decide on a Hair Length

Decide on the length you want your hair to be before you start cutting. You can use a comb and scissors to trim the ends of your hair, or you can use the clippers to cut your hair to a uniform length. Make sure to choose a guard that will give you the desired length.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair at the back of your head, working your way toward the front. Use the clippers to cut your hair to the desired length, making sure to move the clippers in the direction of the hair growth. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can result in an uneven cut.

Step 5: Blend Your Hair

Once you've cut your hair to the desired length, use the clippers to blend your hair. Start at the bottom of your hairline and work your way up, using the clippers to gradually blend the hair at the back of your head with the hair at the top of your head.

Tips

  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while you're cutting your hair.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if you want a shorter cut.
  • Be patient and take your time while cutting your hair.
  • Use scissors to trim any stray hairs after you've finished cutting your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I use clippers to cut my hair if it's wet?

A: It's not recommended to cut your hair with clippers when it's wet, as this can result in an uneven cut. Make sure your hair is dry before you start cutting.

Q: Can I use the same clippers for my beard?

A: Yes, most clippers come with attachments that allow you to trim your beard as well.

Hair Care

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often. Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know how to cut men's thick wavy hair with clippers, you can save time and money by doing it yourself at home. Follow these steps and tips for a clean, even cut every time.

What Type Of Bangs Are Good For Round Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Jul 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Round faces have a softness and curviness that can make them appear youthful and cute. However, they can also make the face appear wider and fuller. One way to balance out a round face is by getting the right type of bangs. In this article, we'll discuss what type of bangs are good for round faces, how to style them, and some tips for maintaining them.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Type of Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Bangs for Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Bangs
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Bangs

Description:

When it comes to choosing the right type of bangs for round faces, you want to look for styles that elongate the face and create the illusion of a more angular shape. Bangs that are too short or too blunt can emphasize the roundness of the face, while bangs that are too long or too wispy can overwhelm the face. The key is finding the right balance.

How to Choose the Right Type of Bangs:

The best type of bangs for round faces are those that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This creates a diagonal line that elongates the face and draws attention away from the roundness. Some good options to consider include:

  • Side-swept bangs: These bangs are angled to one side and blend seamlessly into the rest of the hair. They can be long or short, depending on your preference.
  • Curled bangs: If you have naturally curly hair, consider adding some curls to your bangs. This will create movement and texture, which can help balance out a round face.
  • Layered bangs: Layers can add dimension and depth to your hair, which can help create the illusion of a more angular face shape. Consider adding some layers to your bangs for a more textured look.
  • Asymmetrical bangs: These bangs are shorter on one side and longer on the other, creating an angled look that can help elongate the face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Bangs for Round Faces:

Once you've chosen the right type of bangs for your round face, it's important to know how to style them. Follow these steps for a flawless look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off your bangs and clip the rest of your hair out of the way.
  3. Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fingertips and use it to shape your bangs.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs:

Keeping your bangs looking their best requires some maintenance. Here are a few tips to help you keep them looking great:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the right length.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting too oily between washes.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them look greasy.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about what type of bangs are good for round faces:

  • Q: Can I still get blunt bangs if I have a round face?
  • A: While blunt bangs can be challenging to pull off with a round face, it's not impossible. Consider getting longer, angled bangs that frame your face instead.
  • Q: How long should my bangs be?
  • A: The length of your bangs will depend on your face shape and personal preference. However, as a general rule, aim for bangs that hit at or below your eyebrows.
  • Q: Can I still wear side-swept bangs if I have a short forehead?
  • A: Absolutely! Side-swept bangs are a great option for round faces with short foreheads, as they create the illusion of length.

Hair Care for Bangs:

Taking care of your bangs is just as important as styling them. Here are a few tips to help you keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage it.
  • Consider using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz.

By following these tips and choosing the right type of bangs for your round face, you can create a look that's both flattering and stylish. Whether you opt for side-swept bangs, layered bangs, or asymmetrical bangs, you're sure to turn heads wherever you go.

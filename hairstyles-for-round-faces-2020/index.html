Hairstyles For Round Faces 2020

Are you tired of the same old hairdo and looking for a change? Well, if you have a round face, then you are in luck! There are numerous hairstyles that can enhance your features and make you look stunning. In this blog post, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for round faces in 2020. So, let's get started!

Description

A round face is characterized by a full cheek and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, then you should look for hairstyles that add length and volume to your face. A good hairstyle can create an illusion of a more elongated face and make your features look more defined. There are several hairstyles that you can try out, whether you have long or short hair.

How to Choose a Hairstyle for Round Faces

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, it is essential to consider the length and texture of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Choose hairstyles that add length to your face, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, and high ponytails.
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face look rounder, such as blunt bobs, full fringes, and chin-length haircuts.
  • Experiment with different hair textures, such as curls, waves, and straight hair, to find the best look for your face shape.

Step by Step Guide to Hairstyles for Round Faces

Here are some popular hairstyles for round faces:

Long Layers

Long layers can add volume and dimension to your hair, making your face appear longer. Here's how to get the look:

  1. Part your hair in the middle or on the side.
  2. Ask your hairstylist for long layers that frame your face.
  3. Curl the ends of your hair using a curling iron or a straightener.
  4. Apply hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can create an illusion of a longer face and make your features look more defined. Here's how to get the look:

  1. Part your hair on the side.
  2. Ask your hairstylist for long, side-swept bangs that cover one side of your forehead.
  3. Blow-dry your hair and bangs using a round brush.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair serum to add shine and control frizz.

High Ponytail

A high ponytail can pull your hair away from your face, making your features look more defined. Here's how to get the look:

  1. Brush your hair back and gather it into a high ponytail.
  2. Secure the ponytail with an elastic band.
  3. Tease the crown of your hair to add volume.
  4. Spray hairspray to hold the ponytail in place.

Tips for Maintaining Hairstyles for Round Faces

Here are some tips to keep your hairstyle looking fresh and stylish:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent hair damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

You should avoid hairstyles that make your face look rounder, such as blunt bobs, full fringes, and chin-length haircuts.

Can I wear a pixie cut with a round face?

Yes, you can wear a pixie cut with a round face. However, make sure to choose a style that adds volume and texture to your hair.

Do bangs look good on round faces?

Yes, bangs can look good on round faces. Side-swept bangs can create an illusion of a longer face and make your features look more defined.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Here are some hair care tips for round faces:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid pulling or tugging.

In conclusion, there are several hairstyles that can enhance the features of a round face. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and high ponytails are just a few examples of hairstyles that can make your face appear longer and more defined. Remember to choose a style that suits your hair type and texture, and maintain it with regular trims and proper hair care. With the right hairstyle, you can look and feel your best!

How To Cut A Rounded Bob Haircut Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a modern, stylish haircut that's both easy to maintain and versatile, the rounded bob haircut is an excellent choice. This haircut is suitable for all hair types and face shapes, making it a popular choice among women of all ages. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting a rounded bob haircut in a step-by-step manner.

Description

The rounded bob haircut is a classic hairstyle that has been in fashion for decades. It's a timeless look that can be customized to suit any face shape and hair type. The haircut is characterized by a rounded shape at the back and sides, with the hair being shorter at the back and gradually getting longer towards the front. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that looks chic and stylish.

How to Cut a Rounded Bob Haircut: Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Preparation

Before you start cutting, you need to prepare your tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Also, make sure that the hair is clean and dry.

Step 2: Sectioning

Divide the hair into sections using the comb. Start by creating a middle part, then divide the hair into four sections: two in the front and two at the back. Clip each section separately.

Step 3: Cutting the Back

Start cutting the back section of the hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length. Make sure to cut in a straight line, and be careful not to cut too much hair at once. Cut the hair in small sections, working from the bottom up.

Step 4: Cutting the Sides

Once you've finished cutting the back, move on to the sides. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length, making sure to follow the rounded shape of the bob. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, and cut in small sections.

Step 5: Cutting the Front

Finally, move on to the front sections of the hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length. Make sure to cut in a straight line and follow the rounded shape of the bob.

Step 6: Blending

Once you've finished cutting the hair, use the scissors to blend the hair at the sides and back. This will create a more natural look and prevent any harsh lines.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect rounded bob haircut:

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need. You can always cut more if necessary, but it's hard to undo a bad haircut.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're unsure about cutting your hair yourself. A professional stylist can give you advice on the best way to achieve the look you want.
  • Make sure to cut the hair in small sections. This will help you get a more even and natural-looking cut.

FAQ

Can I cut a rounded bob haircut on my own?

Yes, you can cut a rounded bob haircut on your own, but it's advisable to seek professional help if you're unsure about cutting your hair yourself.

Is the rounded bob haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the rounded bob haircut is suitable for all face shapes. It can be customized to suit any face shape by adjusting the length and shape of the cut.

How often should I trim my rounded bob haircut?

It's recommended that you trim your rounded bob haircut every six to eight weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your rounded bob haircut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot styling tools too often. Regular trims will also help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, cutting a rounded bob haircut is a simple process that can be done at home with the right tools and a little patience. With our step-by-step guide and helpful tips, you can achieve a stylish and modern look that will suit any occasion.

Which Curling Wand Is Best For Beach Waves

Written by Christine Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to achieve that effortless, beachy wave look, you might be wondering which curling wand is best for the job. With so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to figure out which one will give you the perfect waves you're after. In this article, we'll break down the best curling wands for beach waves, as well as some tips and tricks for achieving the look.

Description

Beach waves are a popular hairstyle that can be worn all year round. The look is effortless and natural, as if you've just spent a day at the beach. Beach waves are loose and tousled, with a slight wave that starts at the mid-lengths of your hair and continues to the ends. This look is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, yet stylish hairstyle.

How to Achieve Beach Waves

Before we dive into the best curling wands for beach waves, let's go over how to achieve the look. Here are the steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections, starting at the bottom.
  4. Using a curling wand, wrap each section of hair around the wand, leaving the ends out.
  5. Hold for a few seconds, then release.
  6. Repeat until all sections of hair are curled.
  7. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to gently loosen the curls into waves.
  8. Finish with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to enhance the beachy look.

Now that you know how to achieve beach waves, let's talk about the best curling wands to use.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Beach Wave

Here are some additional tips and tricks for achieving the perfect beach wave:

  • Use a smaller barrel for tighter waves, and a larger barrel for looser waves.
  • Alternate the direction of the curls to create a more natural look.
  • Don't curl the ends of your hair for a more relaxed, beachy look.
  • Use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to enhance the beachy look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different curling wands and techniques to find what works best for you.

  • Can I achieve beach waves with a straightener?

Hair Care for Beach Waves

It's important to take care of your hair when you're styling it with heat. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and moisturized:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Avoid using high heat settings on your curling wand.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

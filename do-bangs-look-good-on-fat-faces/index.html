Do Bangs Look Good On Fat Faces

Written by Bardi Jun 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but worried if it will look good on your round or fat face? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with round or chubby faces have concerns about getting bangs, but the good news is that there are several ways to make it work. In this article, we will discuss how to rock the bangs with a fat face, from choosing the right style to maintaining it.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for a Fat Face

The key to finding the perfect bangs for your round or chubby face is to choose a style that will balance out your features. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the right bangs:

Consider Your Face Shape

There are several face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart-shaped, and more. Knowing your face shape can help you determine which type of bangs will look best on you. For a round or chubby face, opt for longer, side-swept bangs that will elongate your face and create a slimming effect. Blunt or straight across bangs can make your face appear wider.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in choosing the right bangs. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to avoid blunt bangs as they can be difficult to manage. Instead, opt for softer, side-swept bangs that will blend in with the rest of your hair. If you have fine hair, you can try blunt bangs to add volume and texture.

Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing bangs, it's also important to consider your lifestyle. If you're always on the go and don't have time to style your hair every day, opt for low maintenance bangs that can be easily styled with a quick blowout or air-dry. If you're willing to put in more effort, you can try more high maintenance bangs such as thick, blunt bangs that require regular trims and styling.

Step by Step Guide to Get Bangs for a Fat Face

Now that you have an idea of what type of bangs will look best on a round or chubby face, here's a step-by-step guide to getting them:

Step 1: Choose Your Bangs Style

As mentioned earlier, choose a bangs style that will balance out your features. Side-swept bangs that are longer at the sides will help elongate your face, while blunt bangs can make your face appear wider.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

Consult with your hairstylist to determine which bangs style will suit you best. They can also help you decide on the right length and thickness of the bangs.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before getting your bangs. Comb your hair straight down and use hair clips to section off the hair that will become your bangs.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Using hair-cutting scissors, your hairstylist will cut your bangs starting from the center of your forehead and working outwards. Make sure to communicate with your hairstylist throughout the process to ensure you get the desired length and thickness.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Once your bangs are cut, you can style them as desired. Use a round brush to blow-dry them or let them air-dry. You can also use a straightener to add some texture or curl them with a curling iron.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs on a Fat Face

Getting bangs is one thing, but maintaining them can be a challenge. Here are some tips to keep your bangs looking great:

Trim Your Bangs Regularly

Regular trims are essential to keeping your bangs looking neat and tidy. Visit your hairstylist every four to six weeks to get your bangs trimmed.

Blow-Dry Your Bangs

Blow-drying your bangs with a round brush can help them look polished and smooth. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Keep Your Bangs Clean

Wash your bangs regularly to keep them clean and grease-free. You can also use dry shampoo in between washes to keep them looking fresh.

FAQ About Bangs for a Fat Face

Q: Can blunt bangs work on a fat face?

A: Blunt bangs can make a round or chubby face appear wider, so it's best to opt for longer, side-swept bangs that will create a slimming effect.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with bangs?

A: Absolutely! Bangs can be styled with a ponytail, bun, or any other updo. Just make sure to smooth them down with a brush or hairspray to keep them in place.

Q: Will bangs make my face look shorter?

A: If you have a round or chubby face, side-swept bangs that are longer at the sides can actually help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs on a Fat Face

Use a Heat Protectant

Before blow-drying or styling your bangs, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and breakage.

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days to keep it healthy and hydrated.

Invest in Good Quality Hair Products

Using high-quality hair products such as shampoo, conditioner, and styling products can make a big difference in the health and appearance of your hair.

Final Thoughts

Getting bangs with a round or chubby face can seem daunting, but with the right style and maintenance, anyone can rock this look. Remember to choose a style that will balance out your features, consult with your hairstylist, and maintain your bangs regularly for a polished and chic look.

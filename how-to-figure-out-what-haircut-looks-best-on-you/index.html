How To Figure Out What Haircut Looks Best On You

Written by William Nov 28, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of getting a haircut that doesn't suit you? It's frustrating to spend money on a haircut that you don't like. The solution is to figure out what haircut looks best on you. In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect haircut for your face shape, hair type, and personal preferences.

Table of Content

  1. Step by Step
  2. Tips
  3. FAQ
  4. Hair Care

Step by Step

The following steps will help you find the perfect haircut:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect haircut is to determine your face shape. Your face shape will help you determine which hairstyles will look best on you. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. You can determine your face shape by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

The second step is to consider your hair type. Your hair type will determine which haircuts will work best for you. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. You should also consider the thickness and texture of your hair.

Step 3: Research Hairstyles

The third step is to research hairstyles that will work for your face shape and hair type. You can find inspiration from magazines, social media, or by asking your hairstylist for recommendations. Make sure to find pictures of hairstyles that you like and that will work for your face shape and hair type.

Step 4: Consult with a Hairstylist

The fourth step is to consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you determine which hairstyles will work best for your face shape and hair type. They can also provide you with recommendations based on your personal preferences.

Step 5: Try Different Hairstyles

The final step is to try different hairstyles. Once you have determined which hairstyles will work best for your face shape and hair type, try them out. You can experiment with different lengths, textures, and styles until you find the perfect haircut.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you find the perfect haircut:

  • Bring pictures of hairstyles that you like to your hairstylist.
  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a haircut.
  • Be open to trying new hairstyles.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for recommendations.
  • Take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircuts for a round face are those that add volume and height to the crown of the head. Examples include long layers, a side-swept bang, or a layered bob.

What is the best haircut for thin hair?

The best haircuts for thin hair are those that add volume and texture. Examples include a layered bob, a pixie cut, or a short shaggy cut.

What is the best haircut for curly hair?

The best haircuts for curly hair are those that allow the curls to bounce and move freely. Examples include a long layered cut, a curly shag, or a curly bob.

Hair Care

Once you have found the perfect haircut, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is right for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great and maintain your perfect haircut.

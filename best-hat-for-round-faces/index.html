Best Hats For Round Faces

Written by Petter Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, it can be challenging to find the perfect hat that suits your face shape. But do not worry, we have got you covered! In this guide, we'll show you the best hats for round faces, and how to style them.

Description

Round faces have soft, circular features, with the width and length of the face in proportion. The cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the chin is rounded. The goal when choosing a hat for a round face is to add some length and balance the width of the face.

How to choose the best hat for a round face

There are several things to consider when choosing the best hat for a round face:

  • Look for hats with a brim that is wider than your face, as it will add length to your face and balance the width.
  • Avoid hats that are too round or too flat, as they will make your face appear even rounder.
  • Choose hats that have a high crown or a slanted brim, as they will elongate your face.
  • Look for hats that have some height, such as fedoras or cowboy hats, as they will create the illusion of a longer face.

Step by step guide to choose the best hat for a round face

Here is a step-by-step guide to choosing the best hat for a round face:

  1. Determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width and length of your face.
  2. Choose a hat with a wider brim than your face. A hat with a brim that is wider than your face will add length and balance the width of your face.
  3. Avoid hats that are too round or too flat. Hats that are too round or too flat will make your face appear even rounder.
  4. Choose hats with a high crown or a slanted brim. Hats with a high crown or a slanted brim will elongate your face.
  5. Look for hats with some height. Hats with some height, such as fedoras or cowboy hats, will create the illusion of a longer face.
  6. Try on different hats. Experiment with different styles and shapes to find the perfect hat for your face shape.

Tips for styling hats for round faces

Here are some tips for styling hats for round faces:

  • Pair your hat with a long necklace or earrings to create a vertical line.
  • Avoid wearing hats that sit too low on your forehead, as they will make your face appear shorter.
  • Wear your hat at an angle to create an asymmetrical look and add some length to your face.
  • Choose hats in darker colors, as they will create a slimming effect.

FAQs

What type of hat should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hats that are too round or too flat, as they will make your face appear even rounder.

What type of hat should I choose if I have a round face?

Choose hats that have a wider brim than your face, a high crown, and some height, such as fedoras or cowboy hats.

What color hat is best for a round face?

Choose hats in darker colors, as they will create a slimming effect.

Hair care tips when wearing hats

Wearing hats can cause hair damage and breakage, but there are ways to protect your hair when wearing hats:

  • Avoid wearing hats that are too tight, as they can cause hair breakage and damage.
  • Choose hats made with soft and breathable materials, such as cotton or wool.
  • Wear a silk or satin scarf under your hat to protect your hair from friction.
  • Avoid wearing hats for extended periods, as they can cause excessive sweating and oil buildup on the scalp.

Now that you know the best hats for round faces, you can confidently choose the perfect hat that flatters your face shape. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun accessorizing your outfits!

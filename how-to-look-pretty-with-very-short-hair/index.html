How To Look Pretty With Very Short Hair

Written by Robby Jul 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have very short hair, you might sometimes feel like it limits your options when it comes to styling and looking pretty. But the truth is, there are plenty of ways to make short hair look cute and stylish. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for how to look pretty with very short hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

The first step in looking pretty with very short hair is to find a style that works for you. This might mean experimenting with different cuts and lengths until you find one that flatters your face shape and complements your personal style.

Once you have a style that you like, there are a few things you can do to make it look even better. One option is to add some texture to your hair with products like texturizing spray or pomade. This can help give your hair some volume and movement, which can make it look more interesting and stylish.

Another option is to play around with accessories. Headbands, hair clips, and scarves can all be used to add some extra flair to your short hair. Just be sure to choose accessories that complement your outfit and don't overwhelm your hair.

Step by Step

If you're not sure how to style your short hair, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a texturizing spray or pomade to add some texture and volume.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  4. If you want to add some extra flair, try adding an accessory like a headband or hair clip.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for looking pretty with very short hair:

  • Experiment with different partings to find one that flatters your face shape.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add some dimension to your hair.
  • If you're feeling self-conscious about your short hair, remember that confidence is key. Own your look and don't worry about what other people think.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if it's very short?

A: Yes! There are plenty of ways to style short hair in an updo. Try a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style.

Q: Do I need to use special products for short hair?

A: Not necessarily. However, texturizing sprays and pomades can be helpful for adding volume and texture to short hair.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your short hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, which can damage short hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily look pretty with very short hair. Remember, the key is to find a style that works for you and to have confidence in your look. So go ahead and rock that short hair with pride!

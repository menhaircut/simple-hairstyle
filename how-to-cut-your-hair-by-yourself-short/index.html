How To Cut Your Hair By Yourself Short A Diy Guide

Written by Christine Oct 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you have never done it before. However, with the right tools, technique, and a little confidence, you can easily achieve a fresh, new look without leaving the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will guide you through a step-by-step process on how to cut your hair by yourself short.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Hair by Yourself Short
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

How to Cut Your Hair by Yourself Short

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some basic tools you'll need to cut your hair by yourself:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers (if you want to go shorter)
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Mirror (preferably a full-length one)
  • Patience and confidence

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your tools ready, let's get started on cutting your hair by yourself short:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. It's best to wash and condition your hair before cutting it. Make sure your hair is completely dry to ensure accurate cutting.
  2. Section your hair. Use your comb to create a middle part, then section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Use sectioning clips to keep the hair in place.
  3. Start with the back sections. Start with one of the back sections and comb it straight down. Use your fingers to measure the desired length and cut the hair in a straight line.
  4. Move to the front sections. Repeat the same process for the front sections, making sure the length is even on both sides.
  5. Trim the ends. Once you have cut the length, go over the ends of your hair and snip off any split ends or uneven pieces.
  6. Blend the layers (optional). If you want to add more dimension to your hair, use your scissors to cut small pieces of hair at an angle to create layers. Make sure to blend them in with the rest of your hair for a natural look.
  7. Use clippers (optional). If you want to go even shorter, use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach your desired length. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.
  8. Check your work. Use your mirror to check the back and sides of your hair. Make any necessary adjustments.
  9. Style your hair. Once you're satisfied with the length and shape of your hair, style it as desired.
  10. Clean up. Use a broom or vacuum to clean up any hair that fell on the floor or surfaces.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting your own hair:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting your own hair can be a time-consuming process, but it's worth it in the end.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. If you're not confident in your abilities, ask a friend or family member to assist you.
  • Remember that hair grows back. If you make a mistake, it's not the end of the world.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your hair by yourself short:

  • How do I know what length to cut my hair?
  • Can I cut my hair wet or does it need to be dry?
  • What if I make a mistake?
  • Should I use clippers for a shorter look?
  • How often should I cut my own hair?

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain the length and health of your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and length.
  • Apply a nourishing hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a fresh, new look without leaving your home. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips for cutting your hair by yourself short, and you'll be on your way to a fabulous new 'do!

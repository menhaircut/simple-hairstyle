FAQ

How often should I dye my hair?

It is recommended to wait at least four to six weeks before dyeing your hair again. This will give your hair enough time to recover from the previous dyeing process. Over-dyeing your hair can cause damage and breakage.

How can I prevent my hair color from fading?

You can prevent your hair color from fading by using color-safe shampoo and conditioner. Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip away the color. You can also use a color-protecting spray or serum to protect your hair from UV rays and other environmental factors.

What should I do if I don't like my new hair color?

If you don't like your new hair color, you can either wait for it to fade or visit a salon to have it professionally removed or corrected. Do not try to dye your hair again immediately, as this can cause further damage.

Hair Care Tips

Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind after dyeing your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Protect your hair from UV rays and other environmental factors
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

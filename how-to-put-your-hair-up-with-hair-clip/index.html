How To Put Your Hair Up With Hair Clip A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your hair getting in the way while you work or exercise? One solution is to use a hair clip to put your hair up. In this guide, we'll show you how to put your hair up with a hair clip in just a few easy steps. Whether you have short or long hair, this technique will work for you. Read on to find out how!

How to Put Your Hair Up with Hair Clip

First, let's gather our supplies. You'll need a hair clip, a comb, and some hair ties or bobby pins. Now, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to put your hair up.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into two parts. Use your comb to make a part down the middle of your head, from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Then, use the comb to make a part from each ear to the middle part. You should now have four sections of hair.

Step 3: Twist Your Hair

Take one section of hair and twist it tightly. Then, wrap the twisted hair around itself to create a bun shape. Use a hair tie or bobby pin to secure the bun in place. Repeat this step for the remaining sections of hair.

Step 4: Secure with a Hair Clip

Take your hair clip and use it to secure the buns in place. Place the clip over the buns, making sure it is secure and doesn't slip. You may need to adjust the buns slightly to get them to sit perfectly under the clip.

Tips for Putting Your Hair Up with Hair Clip

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect hair clip hairstyle:

  • Use a hair clip that matches your hair color for a more subtle look.
  • If you have thick hair, use larger hair clips to ensure they can hold all of your hair.
  • For a more secure hold, use bobby pins to secure the buns before adding the hair clip.
  • Experiment with different bun sizes and positions to find the perfect look for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I use this technique on short hair?

A: Yes, you can use this technique on short hair. Instead of twisting the hair into a bun, you can twist it into a knot and secure it with a hair tie or bobby pin.

Q: How long will this hairstyle last?

A: This hairstyle should last all day, but you may need to adjust the hair clip occasionally to ensure it stays in place.

Hair Care Tips

Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Don't use too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

Now that you know how to put your hair up with a hair clip, you're ready to tackle any task with ease. Whether you're working, exercising, or just want to change up your look, this hairstyle is quick, easy, and stylish. Give it a try today!

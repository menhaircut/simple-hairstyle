What To Ask A Barber For A Fade The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the barber and leaving with a haircut that's not quite what you wanted? One of the most popular hairstyles for men nowadays is the fade, but it can be confusing to know exactly what to ask your barber for. In this guide, we'll break down everything you need to know to get the perfect fade haircut.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Ask for a Fade
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Getting the Perfect Fade
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Fade

Description

A fade is a short hairstyle that starts with longer hair on top and gradually fades to shorter hair on the sides and back of the head. The fade can be high, medium, or low depending on how high up the sides and back of the head you want the fade to start. This hairstyle is popular among men of all ages and styles, from classic to trendy.

How to Ask for a Fade

When you're at the barber, it's important to communicate exactly what you want. Here are some phrases you can use to ask for a fade:

  • "Can I get a fade on the sides and back?"
  • "I want a high/medium/low fade."
  • "Can you fade the sides and back down to a #2/#1 clipper length?"
  • "I'd like a skin fade."

It's important to be specific about the length you want the fade to start and end at, as well as the clipper length you want the sides and back to be faded down to.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a fade:

  1. Decide on the type of fade you want: high, medium, or low.
  2. Determine the clipper length you want the sides and back to be faded down to.
  3. Communicate your desired fade to the barber.
  4. The barber will start by cutting the hair on top to the desired length.
  5. The barber will then use clippers to fade the sides and back down to the desired length.
  6. The barber will blend the fade into the hair on top using scissors or clippers.
  7. The barber will clean up the neckline and sideburns.
  8. The barber will style the hair on top as desired.
  9. Check the haircut in the mirror and communicate any adjustments you want.
  10. Leave the barber shop looking fresh with your new fade haircut!

Tips for Getting the Perfect Fade

Here are some tips for getting the perfect fade:

  • Bring a picture of the type of fade you want to show the barber.
  • Communicate clearly and be specific about the length and type of fade you want.
  • Trust your barber's expertise and let them make recommendations based on your hair type and face shape.
  • Consider getting a fade with a beard trim for a complete grooming experience.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a fade:

  • Will a fade work with all hair types? Yes, a fade can work with all hair types, but the length and type of fade may vary depending on your hair texture and thickness.
  • How often do I need to get a fade? This depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the fade grows out. On average, you may need to get a fade every 2-4 weeks to maintain the look.
  • Can I style my hair on top differently with a fade? Yes, you can style your hair on top however you like with a fade. Popular styles include a slick back, a side part, or a textured crop.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Fade

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your fade:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Use a styling product to keep your hair on top looking fresh and styled.
  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups and maintenance.
  • Consider getting a beard trim to keep your facial hair looking neat and groomed.

Now that you know what to ask a barber for a fade, you can confidently walk into your next haircut appointment and leave with the perfect fade haircut. Remember to communicate clearly with your barber, trust their expertise, and maintain your fade with proper hair care.

How To Hide Facial Hair Naturally Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who struggles with facial hair, you might feel like you're constantly trying to hide it. While there are plenty of ways to get rid of facial hair, not everyone wants to go through the hassle of shaving, waxing or plucking. Luckily, there are some natural ways to hide facial hair that will help you feel more confident and comfortable in your own skin.

How to Hide Facial Hair Naturally

Here are some natural remedies that can help minimize the appearance of facial hair:

Use Makeup

One of the easiest and most effective ways to hide facial hair is by using makeup. You can use a color corrector to neutralize any shadows, and then apply a concealer or foundation on top. Be sure to blend well and use a powder to set everything in place.

Try Turmeric

Turmeric is a natural anti-inflammatory and can help reduce the appearance of facial hair. Mix 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder with 2 tablespoons of milk and apply it to your face. Leave it on for 20 minutes and then rinse off with lukewarm water.

Use Oatmeal

Oatmeal is a gentle exfoliant that can help remove dead skin cells and hair. Mix 1 tablespoon of oatmeal with 1 tablespoon of honey and apply it to your face. Leave it on for 15-20 minutes and then rinse off with warm water.

Try Sugar Waxing

Sugar waxing is a natural way to remove facial hair. Mix 2 cups of sugar with 1/4 cup of lemon juice and 1/4 cup of water. Heat the mixture in a saucepan until it turns into a thick paste. Let it cool and then apply it to your face in the direction of hair growth. Place a strip of cloth over the wax and pull it off in the opposite direction of hair growth.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to hiding facial hair naturally:

  1. Start by washing your face with a gentle cleanser to remove any dirt or oil.
  2. If you're using makeup, apply a color corrector to neutralize any shadows.
  3. Apply a concealer or foundation on top of the color corrector.
  4. Blend well and use a powder to set everything in place.
  5. If you're using a natural remedy, mix the ingredients and apply to your face.
  6. Leave the remedy on for the recommended amount of time.
  7. Rinse off with lukewarm water.
  8. If you're using sugar waxing, apply the wax in the direction of hair growth.
  9. Place a strip of cloth over the wax and pull it off in the opposite direction of hair growth.
  10. Finish by applying a moisturizer to your face.

Tips for Hiding Facial Hair

Here are some tips to help you hide facial hair:

  • Use a good quality concealer or foundation.
  • Blend well to avoid any harsh lines.
  • Use a color corrector to neutralize any shadows.
  • Exfoliate regularly to remove dead skin cells and hair.
  • Use a moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated.
  • If you're using a natural remedy, be sure to patch test first to avoid any allergic reactions.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hiding facial hair naturally:

Is it safe to use natural remedies to hide facial hair?

Yes, natural remedies are generally safe to use. However, it's important to patch test first to avoid any allergic reactions.

How long do the effects of makeup last?

The effects of makeup can last for several hours, depending on the type of makeup and your skin type.

How often should I exfoliate my face?

It's recommended to exfoliate your face 1-2 times per week to remove dead skin cells and hair.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair can also help minimize the appearance of facial hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

With these tips and tricks, you can feel more confident and comfortable in your own skin. Try out different natural remedies and find what works best for you. Remember to always patch test first and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.

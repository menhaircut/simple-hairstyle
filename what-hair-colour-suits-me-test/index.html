What Hair Colour Suits Me Test Find The Perfect Shade For You

Written by Daniel Nov 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair color and looking for a change? Or are you a newbie in the world of hair coloring and wondering what hair color suits you best? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you on how to take a "what hair color suits me test" and find the perfect shade for your skin tone, eye color, and personality.

Description

The "what hair color suits me test" is a popular online quiz that helps you determine which hair color is most flattering for you. It takes into consideration various factors such as skin tone, eye color, natural hair color, and lifestyle. The test usually consists of a series of questions that you need to answer truthfully to get accurate results. You can find these tests on various websites or hair color brands.

How to Take the Test

Taking the "what hair color suits me test" is easy and fun. All you need is a few minutes and a device with an internet connection. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose a Website or Hair Color Brand

Search for "what hair color suits me test" on Google or any other search engine, and you will find numerous websites or hair color brands that offer this quiz. Choose the one that appeals to you the most or has good reviews.

Step 2: Answer the Questions

Once you have selected the website or brand, click on the link to the test. It will usually take you to a page where you need to answer a series of questions. The questions can range from your skin tone, eye color, natural hair color, lifestyle, and even personality. Be honest while answering the questions as it will help you get accurate results.

Step 3: Get the Results

Once you have answered all the questions, click on the submit button. The website or brand will then analyze your answers and give you the results. The results will usually include a range of hair colors that are most flattering for you based on your answers.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you get the best results from the "what hair color suits me test":

  • Take the test in natural light to get accurate results.
  • Consider your lifestyle before selecting a hair color. If you have a busy schedule, choose a low maintenance color.
  • Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color.
  • Consider your natural hair color before selecting a shade. If you have dark hair, choose a shade that is not too light.
  • Consult a hair color specialist if you are unsure about the results.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "what hair color suits me test":

Q: How accurate are the results of the test?

A: The results of the test are usually accurate if you answer the questions truthfully and follow the tips mentioned above. However, it is always best to consult a hair color specialist if you are unsure about the results.

Q: Can I take the test more than once?

A: Yes, you can take the test as many times as you want to try different answers and get different results.

Q: Can I trust the results of the test?

A: The results of the test are based on your answers and the algorithms used by the website or brand. While they can be a good starting point, it is always best to consult a hair color specialist before making any drastic changes to your hair.

Hair Care

Now that you have found the perfect hair color for you, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its color and shine. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for colored hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair frequently as it can fade the color.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and dyes that can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to take a "what hair color suits me test," go ahead and try it out. Who knows, you might discover a new hair color that suits you perfectly!

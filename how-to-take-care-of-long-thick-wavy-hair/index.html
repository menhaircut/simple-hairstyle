Long thick wavy hair is a beautiful asset to have, but it takes a lot of effort to keep it healthy and looking good. In this article, we'll go through some tips and tricks on how to take care of your long thick wavy hair in a way that will keep it looking beautiful and healthy.

How to Take Care of Long Thick Wavy Hair

Long thick wavy hair requires a lot of care and attention to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly is important to keep it clean and healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for long thick wavy hair. Avoid using hot water as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb

When your hair is wet, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle it. This will prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

3. Use a heat protectant

If you use heat styling tools, such as a flat iron or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

4. Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy. Aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

5. Don't over brush your hair

Brushing your hair too much can lead to breakage and damage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and only brush it when necessary.

6. Use a deep conditioner

Using a deep conditioner once a week will help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to take care of your long thick wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for long thick wavy hair. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.

Step 2: Detangle your hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

Step 3: Apply a heat protectant

If you plan on using heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage.

Step 4: Style your hair

Style your hair as desired using heat styling tools or air-dry it.

Step 5: Apply a deep conditioner

Apply a deep conditioner to your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy. Leave it on for 5-10 minutes before rinsing it out.

Tips for Long Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your long thick wavy hair:

1. Use a silk pillowcase

A silk pillowcase will help prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

2. Avoid using hot styling tools

Hot styling tools can damage your hair over time. Try to limit your use of these tools and let your hair air-dry whenever possible.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

4. Eat a healthy diet

Eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals will help keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner?

A: No, it's recommended to use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for long thick wavy hair.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

How To Wear Your Hair For Basketball Tips And Tricks

Written by William Sep 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a basketball player, you know how important it is to have your hair out of your face while you're on the court. Not only can long hair be distracting, but it can also get in the way of your vision and impact your performance. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks for how to wear your hair for basketball so you can focus on the game and not your hair!

How to

1. Braids or Cornrows

Braids or cornrows are a great option for basketball players. They keep your hair out of your face and minimize the amount of sweat that gets trapped in your hair. Plus, they look stylish and can be customized with different designs and patterns.

2. Ponytail or Bun

A simple ponytail or bun is another option for basketball players. Make sure to use a hair tie that won't slip or break during the game. You can also use bobby pins to secure any flyaways or loose strands.

3. Headband

A headband is a great accessory for basketball players. It keeps your hair out of your face and absorbs sweat to prevent it from dripping down your forehead. Choose a headband that's comfortable and won't slip during the game.

Step by Step

1. Brush your hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and brushed. This will help prevent tangles and make it easier to work with.

2. Decide on a style

Choose a hairstyle that's comfortable and won't distract you during the game. Braids, cornrows, ponytails, buns, and headbands are all great options.

3. Secure your hair

Once you've chosen a style, secure your hair with a hair tie or headband. Make sure it's tight enough to stay in place during the game, but not so tight that it hurts your head.

4. Add finishing touches

If you have any flyaways or loose strands, use bobby pins to secure them in place. You can also add hairspray or gel to keep your hair in place throughout the game.

Tips

1. Use hair products

Hair products like hairspray or gel can help keep your hair in place during the game. Make sure to choose products that won't leave residue or make your hair feel sticky.

2. Choose comfortable accessories

Accessories like headbands should be comfortable and not too tight. Avoid using accessories that are heavy or distracting.

3. Practice your hairstyle

Practice your hairstyle before the game to make sure it's comfortable and won't fall apart during the game.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair down for basketball?

A: It's not recommended to wear your hair down for basketball. It can get in the way of your vision and impact your performance.

Q: Can I wear a hat for basketball?

A: Hats are not allowed during basketball games. Stick to hairstyles that are comfortable and won't distract you during the game.

Q: How do I prevent sweat from getting in my hair?

A: Use a headband to absorb sweat and keep it from dripping down your forehead. You can also use hair products like dry shampoo to absorb sweat and oil.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you play basketball regularly. Make sure to wash your hair regularly and use conditioner to keep it soft and healthy. Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can wear your hair comfortably and stylishly for basketball. Remember to choose a hairstyle that's comfortable and won't distract you during the game. Practice your hairstyle before the game to make sure it's secure and won't fall apart. And don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong!

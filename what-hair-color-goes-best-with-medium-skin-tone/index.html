What Hair Color Goes Best With Medium Skin Tone

Written by Petter Oct 20, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hair color but not sure what shade will complement your medium skin tone? Look no further! In this article, we’ll explore the best hair colors for medium skin tones, as well as provide tips on how to care for your newly colored locks.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium skin tones fall in the middle of the spectrum, typically ranging from olive to caramel. When it comes to choosing a hair color, you want to select shades that complement your skin tone and bring out its natural warmth and radiance.

While there are many hair colors that can work with medium skin tones, some shades tend to stand out more than others. In general, warm and rich hues tend to work best, such as golden blonde, honey brown, and copper red.

How To

When choosing a hair color, it’s important to consider your skin’s undertones. If you have warm undertones, look for shades with golden or red tones. If you have cool undertones, opt for hues with ash or blue tones.

Additionally, consider your personal style and maintenance preferences. Do you want a low-maintenance color that doesn’t require frequent touch-ups, or are you willing to commit to a high-maintenance shade that requires regular salon visits?

Step By Step

Here are some general guidelines for selecting the best hair color for your medium skin tone:

  1. For warm undertones, try shades like golden blonde, honey brown, caramel, or copper red.
  2. For cool undertones, consider ash brown, platinum blonde, or burgundy.
  3. Avoid colors that are too light or too dark, as they can wash out your complexion.
  4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.
  5. Consult with a professional colorist to help you find the perfect shade.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your newly colored locks:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the color.
  • Protect your hair from heat styling and UV rays with a heat protectant spray.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your locks healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair color for medium skin tones:

What hair colors should I avoid with a medium skin tone?

Avoid colors that are too light or too dark, as they can wash out your complexion. Additionally, steer clear of shades with cool undertones if you have warm undertones, and vice versa.

Can I go blonde with a medium skin tone?

Absolutely! Just make sure to choose a warm, golden blonde shade that complements your skin tone.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair after coloring to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid hot tools that can damage and fade your color.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or UV-protectant spray.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep split ends at bay.

With these tips in mind, you’re ready to choose the perfect hair color for your medium skin tone. Whether you opt for a warm, rich hue or a cool, edgy shade, you’ll be sure to turn heads with your stunning new locks!

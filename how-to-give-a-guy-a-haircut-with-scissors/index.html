How To Give A Guy A Haircut With Scissors The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Giving a guy a haircut with scissors can be a daunting task, especially if you're new to it. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can give him a professional-looking haircut at home. In this article, we'll go through a step-by-step guide on how to give a guy a haircut with scissors, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

How to Give a Guy a Haircut with Scissors

Before we get started, here are the tools you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clip
  • Spray bottle
  • Mirror
  • Haircutting cape or towel

Once you have these tools, you're ready to start.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to give a guy a haircut with scissors:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Start by washing the guy's hair with shampoo and conditioner. Dry it with a towel or blow dryer until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Use a comb to section the hair into four parts: the top, back, and sides. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start with the top section of the hair. Use the comb to lift a small section of hair and cut it with the scissors. Work your way from the front to the back, cutting small sections of hair at a time.

Step 4: Move to the Sides and Back

Once you've finished cutting the top section, move on to the sides and back. Use the same technique of lifting small sections of hair and cutting them with the scissors.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've finished cutting all the sections, use the scissors to blend the hair together. This will give the haircut a more natural look.

Step 6: Trim the Hairline

Use the scissors to trim the hairline around the neck and ears. Be careful not to cut too much hair off.

Step 7: Check the Haircut

Use a mirror to check the haircut from all angles. If there are any uneven spots, use the scissors to trim them until the haircut looks even.

Step 8: Clean Up

Once you're satisfied with the haircut, remove the hair clip and cape or towel. Use a brush or comb to clean up any loose hairs.

Tips for Giving a Guy a Haircut with Scissors

Here are some tips to keep in mind when giving a guy a haircut with scissors:

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut slowly. It's better to cut too little hair than too much.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle. This will give the haircut more texture.
  • Always cut the hair when it's dry. Wet hair can be harder to cut evenly.
  • Use the mirror to check the haircut from all angles.
  • If you're not sure about a particular section, don't cut it. It's better to leave it for a professional.

FAQs

Can I use regular scissors to cut hair?

No, you should use scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. Regular scissors can be too dull and can damage the hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want to keep your hair. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner regularly.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and techniques, you can give a guy a haircut with scissors like a pro. Remember to take your time, start with small sections of hair, and use the mirror to check your work. Happy cutting!

How To Cut Short Hair In Back Of Head A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly going to the hair salon to get your short hair trimmed? Why not try cutting your hair at home? In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to cut short hair in the back of your head. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-quality haircut from the comfort of your own home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Short Hair in Back of Head
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Short Hair
  • FAQs

How to Cut Short Hair in Back of Head

Cutting the back of your head can be a little tricky, but with the right technique, it can be done easily. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before starting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a fine-toothed comb, hair clips, and a handheld mirror. Make sure your scissors are sharp to ensure a clean cut.

Step 2: Wet Your Hair

Wet your hair thoroughly to make it easier to cut. Use a spray bottle or run your hair under the sink. Make sure your hair is evenly damp.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Use the comb to create a horizontal parting across the back of your head, from one ear to the other. Clip the top section of hair up and out of the way.

Step 4: Cut the Bottom Section of Hair

Take a small section of hair from the bottom and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut it to your desired length. Remember to cut small sections at a time to avoid any mistakes.

Step 5: Work Your Way Up

Continue cutting small sections of hair and working your way up. Make sure each section is even and straight before moving on to the next one. If you need to, use the comb to help guide you and keep your hair straight.

Step 6: Check Your Progress

Use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head periodically. Make sure everything is even and straight.

Step 7: Blend the Sides

Once you have finished cutting the back, unclip the top section of hair and blend the sides into the back using the same technique. Use the comb to create a diagonal parting and cut small sections of hair at a time.

Step 8: Final Check

Use the handheld mirror again to check your work. Make sure everything is blended and even.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair

Cutting short hair can be challenging, but these tips will help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up.
  • Use a fine-toothed comb to keep your hair straight.
  • Don’t cut your hair too short; you can always cut more later.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check your progress.
  • Take your time and don’t rush.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your own hair at home. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a great haircut.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don’t panic. You can always cut more hair to fix it. If you’re really unsure, it’s best to visit a hair salon.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it’s recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have cut your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut at home. Remember to take your time, start with small sections of hair, and use the right tools. Happy cutting!

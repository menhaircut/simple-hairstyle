Men's Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top - How to Style

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish hairstyle that's low-maintenance and versatile, then the men's hairstyle short sides long top is a great option. This hairstyle has been popular for years and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to style this haircut, some tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions about it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Style

To achieve the men's hairstyle short sides long top, you'll need to have short hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. Here are the steps to style it:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product that works for your hair type. We recommend a matte finish pomade or wax.
  3. Using a comb, create a side part on the longer top section of your hair.
  4. Using a hair dryer and a round brush, blow dry your hair in the direction that you want it to go. This will add volume and texture to your hair.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to style it in the desired shape. You can play around with the texture and volume to find the perfect look for you.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your men's hairstyle short sides long top:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • If you have thick hair, consider using a texturizing spray to add more volume and texture.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Try using a salt spray to add a beachy, tousled look to your hair.

FAQ

1. Who can pull off the men's hairstyle short sides long top?

Anyone can pull off this hairstyle as long as they have the right hair type and face shape. It works best for those with thick or wavy hair and oval or square faces.

2. How do I decide on the length of the sides and top?

It's best to consult with your hairstylist to find the right length for your hair type and face shape. However, a good rule of thumb is to have the sides cut to a #2 or #3 and the top to around 3-5 inches.

3. Can I wear this hairstyle to a formal event?

Yes, you can! This hairstyle can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion. You can slick back the longer top section for a more formal look or leave it messy for a casual event.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, like bleach or hair dye.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the men's hairstyle short sides long top with confidence and style. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you!

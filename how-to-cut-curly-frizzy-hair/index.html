How To Cut Curly Frizzy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly frizzy hair, you know just how difficult it can be to manage. One way to make your hair more manageable is by cutting it. However, cutting curly hair can be tricky, especially if you want to avoid the dreaded triangle shape. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to cut curly frizzy hair, along with tips and FAQs to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Curly Frizzy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Hair
  • FAQs

How to Cut Curly Frizzy Hair

When it comes to cutting curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid cutting your hair when it's wet. This can cause your hair to look shorter than you intended once it dries. Instead, cut your hair when it's dry and in its natural state. Second, you want to use the right tools. A pair of sharp scissors and a spray bottle filled with water are essential.

Before you start cutting, decide on the length you want. It's always better to start with a longer length and work your way down. You can always cut more if needed, but you can't add length back once it's gone.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your curly frizzy hair:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair. Separate the top half of your hair from the bottom half by creating a part from ear to ear.
  2. Clip the top section of your hair out of the way.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the bottom half and twist it.
  4. Use your scissors to snip off the ends of the hair that stick out from the twist.
  5. Continue twisting and cutting small sections of hair until you've finished the bottom half of your hair.
  6. Release the top half of your hair and repeat steps 3-5.
  7. Check your work by letting your hair fall naturally and making any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

Cutting curly hair can be tricky, but these tips will help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Cut your hair when it's dry and in its natural state.
  • Start with a longer length and work your way down.
  • Twist small sections of hair before cutting to avoid the dreaded triangle shape.
  • Cut in small sections to avoid taking off too much hair at once.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my curly hair myself?

A: Yes, you can cut your curly hair yourself, but it's important to follow the right steps and use the right tools.

Q: How often should I cut my curly hair?

A: It's recommended to cut your curly hair every 3-4 months to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Cutting your curly frizzy hair is just one step in achieving healthy, beautiful locks. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, or use a heat protectant spray if you must.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can cut your curly frizzy hair with confidence. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll have beautiful, healthy hair in no time.

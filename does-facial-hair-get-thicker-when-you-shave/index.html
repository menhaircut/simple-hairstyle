Does Facial Hair Get Thicker When You Shave

Written by Bardi Jun 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Facial hair has become a popular trend among men in recent years. However, it's not uncommon for many men to have concerns when it comes to grooming their facial hair. One of the most common questions that men ask is whether facial hair gets thicker when you shave. In this blog post, we will explore this question in detail and provide you with all the information you need to know.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

There is a common misconception that shaving causes facial hair to grow back thicker and darker. This is not entirely true. Facial hair growth is determined by genetics, and shaving does not affect the thickness or color of the hair that grows back. However, there are a few reasons why some men may believe that their facial hair grows back thicker after shaving.

How to

If you're looking to groom your facial hair, whether it be a beard, mustache, or goatee, there are a few things you need to keep in mind:

  • Choose the right grooming tools (razor, trimmer, scissors)
  • Decide on the style you want
  • Take care of your skin and hair

Step by step

Here are a few steps you can follow to groom your facial hair:

  1. Wash your face with warm water and a mild cleanser to remove any dirt and oil.
  2. Use a trimmer or scissors to trim your facial hair to the desired length.
  3. Use a razor to shave any unwanted hair around the edges of your beard or mustache.
  4. Apply a moisturizer or beard oil to keep your skin and hair hydrated.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when grooming your facial hair:

  • Use a sharp razor or trimmer to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.
  • Trim your facial hair regularly to maintain the desired length.
  • Avoid using too much pressure when shaving to prevent skin irritation.
  • Apply a warm towel to your face before shaving to soften the hair and open up your pores.
  • Use a beard comb to detangle your hair and distribute beard oil evenly.

FAQ

Does shaving make facial hair grow back thicker?

No, shaving does not make facial hair grow back thicker. Hair growth is determined by genetics and hormones.

Why does facial hair appear thicker after shaving?

Facial hair may appear thicker after shaving because the ends of the hair shafts are cut off, creating a blunt edge.

How often should I groom my facial hair?

The frequency of grooming your facial hair depends on the style you want and how fast your hair grows. It's recommended to trim your hair every two to three weeks to maintain the desired length.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy facial hair. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo to remove any dirt and oil.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft.
  • Avoid using hot tools or harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in protein and vitamins to promote hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

In conclusion, shaving does not make facial hair grow back thicker. Hair growth is determined by genetics and hormones. However, proper grooming and hair care are essential for maintaining healthy facial hair. By following the tips and steps outlined in this blog post, you can achieve the desired style and keep your facial hair looking its best.

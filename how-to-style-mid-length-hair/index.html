How To Style Mid Length Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jun 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and easy-to-style haircut, mid length hair might be the perfect choice for you. With the right techniques and products, you can create a variety of looks that suit your personality and lifestyle. In this article, we'll show you how to style mid length hair in simple steps that you can follow at home. Let's get started!

Description: What is Mid Length Hair?

Mid length hair refers to hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. It's a popular haircut for both men and women, as it's easy to maintain and can be styled in many ways. Mid length hair is also versatile, as it allows you to experiment with different textures, layers, and colors.

How to: Styling Mid Length Hair

Step 1: Start with clean and dry hair

Before you start styling your mid length hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as this can damage your hair and make it frizzy.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant

If you plan to use hot tools such as a curling iron or straightener, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help prevent heat damage and keep your hair healthy.

Step 3: Choose your styling tools

Depending on the look you want to achieve, you can use different styling tools such as a brush, comb, curling iron, straightener, or hair dryer. Make sure you use high-quality tools that won't damage your hair.

Step 4: Decide on your hairstyle

There are many hairstyles you can create with mid length hair, such as beachy waves, sleek and straight, messy bun, or half-up half-down. Decide on the hairstyle that suits your mood and occasion.

Step 5: Start styling

Now it's time to start styling your mid length hair. Follow these tips to achieve your desired look:

  • For beachy waves: Use a curling iron or hair wand to create loose waves. Use your fingers to separate the curls and apply a texturizing spray for a natural look.
  • For sleek and straight: Use a straightener to smooth out your hair. Apply a smoothing serum or oil to tame frizz and add shine.
  • For messy bun: Pull your hair back into a loose bun and secure with bobby pins. Use your fingers to pull out a few strands for a messy look.
  • For half-up half-down: Take the top section of your hair and pull it back into a ponytail or bun. Leave the rest of your hair down or create loose waves.

Tips for Styling Mid Length Hair

Here are some additional tips that can help you style your mid length hair:

  • Invest in high-quality styling tools that won't damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what suits you best.
  • Don't be afraid to use hair accessories such as headbands, clips, or scrunchies to add a pop of color or texture to your hair.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Mid Length Hair

Here are some common questions people have about mid length hair:

How often should I cut my mid length hair?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your mid length hair.

Is mid length hair suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, mid length hair can be flattering for all face shapes. However, you may want to consult with your hairstylist to find the best mid length haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Can I dye my mid length hair?

Yes, you can dye your mid length hair. However, it's important to use high-quality hair dye and follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

To keep your mid length hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as this can damage your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Apply a heat protectant before using hot tools.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to hydrate your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your mid length hair in many ways that suit your personality and lifestyle. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair!

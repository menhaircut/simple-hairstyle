Is Layer Cut Good For Thick Hair

Written by Bardi Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Layer cut is a popular haircut style that has been around for decades, and it's not hard to see why. Layer cut is versatile, and it can work for any hair type, including thick hair. But is layer cut good for thick hair? In this blog post, we will explore the benefits of layer cut for thick hair, how to cut layers, and hair care tips to maintain healthy and beautiful hair.

Benefits of Layer Cut for Thick Hair

Thick hair can be challenging to manage, especially if you have long hair. Layer cut can help to reduce the weight of your hair, making it easier to style and manage. Here are some benefits of layer cut for thick hair:

  • Layer cut can add volume and movement to thick hair.
  • Layer cut can remove the bulkiness of thick hair, making it easier to style.
  • Layer cut can enhance the natural texture of thick hair.
  • Layer cut can create a layered look that flatters the face shape.

Overall, layer cut can make thick hair look more stylish and manageable.

How to Cut Layers

Cutting layers on thick hair requires some skills, but it's not impossible to do it yourself. Here's how to cut layers on thick hair:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections, starting from the back.
  3. Take a section of hair, and comb it straight.
  4. Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, and slide your fingers down to the length you want to cut.
  5. Cut the hair with a sharp pair of scissors.
  6. Move on to the next section of hair until you've cut all the layers.
  7. Blend the layers by combing your hair and trimming any uneven parts.

It's important to note that cutting layers on thick hair requires patience and practice. If you're not confident in cutting your hair, it's best to go to a professional hairstylist.

Tips for Layer Cut on Thick Hair

Here are some tips for achieving the best layer cut on thick hair:

  • Choose a layer cut that flatters your face shape.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Cut small sections of hair at a time to avoid mistakes.
  • Cut your hair when it's dry to see how the layers will look.
  • Don't cut too much hair in one go to avoid a drastic change.

Following these tips can help you achieve a beautiful and natural layer cut on thick hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about layer cut for thick hair:

Can layer cut make thick hair look thinner?

Yes, layer cut can reduce the bulkiness of thick hair, making it look thinner and more manageable.

How often should I cut my hair when I have a layer cut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you want to maintain the layers. Generally, it's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

What's the best way to style layered hair?

Layered hair can be styled in various ways, depending on your hair type and preference. You can try blow-drying your hair with a round brush to add volume, curling your hair with a curling iron for a romantic look, or straightening your hair for a sleek and modern look.

Hair Care Tips for Layer Cut on Thick Hair

Here are some hair care tips to maintain healthy and beautiful hair after a layer cut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when you do use heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Following these hair care tips can help you maintain healthy and beautiful hair after a layer cut.

Conclusion

Layer cut can be a great option for those with thick hair. It can add volume, movement, and texture to your hair, making it more manageable and stylish. By following the tips and hair care advice in this blog post, you can achieve a beautiful and natural layer cut on your thick hair.

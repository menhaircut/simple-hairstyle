What s The Best Eyeshadow For Over 60

Written by Luffy Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 60, you may be wondering what the best eyeshadow is for you. As we age, our skin changes and our makeup needs change as well. In this article, we will discuss what the best eyeshadow is for over 60 and provide tips on how to apply it.

Description

As we age, our eyelids can become droopy, and our skin can become dry and creased. This can make applying eyeshadow more challenging, and some products can make the problem worse. The best eyeshadow for over 60 is one that is moisturizing, long-lasting, and has a matte finish.

How to Choose the Best Eyeshadow for Over 60

When choosing an eyeshadow, it's important to consider your skin type, eye color, and personal style. For those over 60, it's best to choose an eyeshadow with a matte finish, as shimmery or glittery products can highlight fine lines and wrinkles. Neutral shades like browns, grays, and taupes are a safe choice and can be worn every day. If you want to add some color, choose shades that complement your eye color, such as purples for green eyes or blues for brown eyes.

Step by Step: How to Apply Eyeshadow for Over 60

1. Start by applying a primer to your eyelids. This will help the eyeshadow stay in place and prevent creasing.

2. Choose your eyeshadow shades and apply the lightest shade all over your lid.

3. Apply a mid-tone shade to your crease to add depth and dimension.

4. Use a darker shade to define your outer corner and add a subtle smoky effect.

5. Blend the colors together with a fluffy brush to create a seamless look.

6. Finish with a coat of mascara to define your lashes.

Tips for Applying Eyeshadow on Mature Skin

1. Use a primer to smooth out the texture of your eyelids.

2. Choose matte eyeshadows to avoid emphasizing fine lines and wrinkles.

3. Use a light hand when applying eyeshadow to avoid tugging on the delicate skin around your eyes.

4. Use a fluffy brush to blend the colors together for a seamless look.

5. Use a light-reflecting concealer to brighten your under-eye area and make your eyes appear more awake.

FAQs

What eyeshadow colors should I avoid?

Avoid bright and bold colors that can be overwhelming on mature skin. Stick to neutral shades like browns, grays, and taupes.

What type of eyeshadow is best for mature skin?

Matte eyeshadows are best for mature skin, as shimmery or glittery products can highlight fine lines and wrinkles.

How do I prevent my eyeshadow from creasing?

Use a primer before applying eyeshadow to prevent creasing. You can also set your eyeshadow with a translucent powder to make it last longer.

Hair Care Tips for Over 60

As we age, our hair can become thinner and drier. To keep your hair looking healthy and youthful, it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage your hair. Instead, opt for heatless styling methods like air-drying or using foam rollers. Finally, make sure to get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and prevent split ends.

Overall, choosing the right eyeshadow for over 60 can be challenging, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and youthful look. Remember to choose matte shades, use a primer, and blend your eyeshadow for a natural-looking finish. And don't forget to take care of your hair as well, to complete your overall look.

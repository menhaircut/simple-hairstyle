How To Do Easy Hairstyles For Little Girls

Written by Eveline Aug 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As a parent, you want your little girl to look her best, and that includes having a cute hairstyle. However, with busy schedules and limited hairstyling skills, it can be challenging to come up with easy and quick hairstyles for your little girl. In this article, we will provide you with some simple yet stylish hairstyles that you can do for your little girl.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, here are some tips to make hairstyling easier for you and your little girl:

  • Make sure your little girl's hair is clean and tangle-free before styling it.
  • Use hair accessories such as ribbons, clips, and headbands to add some flair to the hairstyles.
  • Be patient and take breaks if needed. Hairstyling can be a fun bonding activity for you and your little girl.

Step by Step

Braided Pigtails

Step 1: Part your little girl's hair down the middle and divide it into two sections.

Step 2: Take one section and divide it into three strands.

Step 3: Begin braiding by crossing the right strand over the middle strand, then the left strand over the new middle strand.

Step 4: Continue braiding until you reach the end of the hair and secure the braid with a hair tie.

Step 5: Repeat steps 2-4 for the other section of hair.

Half-Up Bun

Step 1: Take the top section of your little girl's hair and secure it with a hair tie.

Step 2: Twist the hair around the hair tie to create a bun.

Step 3: Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.

Top Knot

Step 1: Gather all of your little girl's hair at the top of her head and secure it with a hair tie.

Step 2: Twist the hair around the hair tie to create a knot.

Step 3: Use bobby pins to secure the knot in place.

Tips

  • Add some fun accessories such as glitter hairspray or temporary hair color to make the hairstyle unique.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the ones that work best for your little girl's hair type and length.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a hairstylist or friend who is skilled in hairstyling.

FAQ

What if my little girl has short hair?

There are still plenty of hairstyles that you can do for short hair, such as a pixie cut or a bob. You can also add some accessories to create a more playful look.

How often should I wash my little girl's hair?

It depends on your little girl's hair type and how active she is. Generally, washing her hair once or twice a week should be sufficient.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your little girl's hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your little girl's hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Encourage your little girl to eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.

With these easy hairstyles and tips, you and your little girl can have fun experimenting with different looks while keeping her hair healthy and beautiful.

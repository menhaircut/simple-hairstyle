How To Wear Curly Hair To An Interview

Written by Aprilia Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. It's beautiful, unique, and makes a statement. But, it can also be difficult to manage and style, especially when it comes to job interviews. So, if you're wondering how to wear curly hair to an interview, this guide is for you. Here, we'll go over some helpful tips and tricks to make sure your hair looks professional, polished, and interview-ready.

How to Wear Curly Hair to an Interview

When it comes to wearing curly hair to an interview, the key is to keep it simple and professional. You want to present yourself as someone who is polished, put together, and ready to take on any challenge. Here are some tips to help you achieve that:

1. Determine your hair type

Before you can style your hair, you need to know what type of curly hair you have. There are several different types of curly hair, ranging from loose waves to tight coils. Knowing your hair type will help you choose the right products and styling techniques for your hair.

2. Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape

Not all hairstyles work for all face shapes. So, before you choose a hairstyle for your interview, consider your face shape. If you have a round face, a long, layered cut with side-swept bangs can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a shoulder-length cut with soft, face-framing layers can soften your features.

3. Keep it simple

While curly hair can be fun to style and experiment with, it's best to keep it simple for an interview. Stick to classic styles, like a low bun or a sleek ponytail, that won't distract from your qualifications or skills.

4. Choose the right products

Curly hair requires different products than straight hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, like a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner, a leave-in conditioner, and a curl-defining gel or cream.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve a polished and professional look for your interview:

1. Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. Rinse thoroughly and gently squeeze out any excess water.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle and moisturize your curls. Use a wide-toothed comb to distribute the conditioner evenly.

3. Style your hair

Choose a simple and classic hairstyle, like a low bun or a sleek ponytail. Use a curl-defining gel or cream to define your curls and hold your style in place.

4. Finish with hairspray

Finish your style with a light mist of hairspray to keep everything in place. Avoid using too much, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep in mind when styling your curly hair for an interview:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your curls.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce frizz and maintain your curls overnight.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair down for an interview?

A: While it's possible to wear your hair down for an interview, it's generally recommended to wear it up in a simple and classic style, like a low bun or a sleek ponytail. This will help keep your hair out of your face and prevent distractions.

Q: What if my hair is too short to put up?

A: If your hair is too short to put up, consider using a headband or hair clips to keep it out of your face. Just make sure the accessories you choose are simple and professional.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, like curling irons and straighteners, too often.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to help keep it moisturized.
  • Don't brush your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when they're wet.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, like bleach and hair dye, as these can damage your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can wear your curly hair with confidence to your next interview. Remember to keep it simple, choose a classic style, and use products designed for curly hair to achieve a polished and professional look.

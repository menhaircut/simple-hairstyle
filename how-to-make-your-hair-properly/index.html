How To Make Your Hair Properly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 09, 2023 · 4 min read
How to Make Your Hair Properly | Step-by-Step Guide | Hair Care Tips | FAQ

Having a good hair day can make you feel confident and beautiful. But how do you achieve that perfect look? In this post, we’ll guide you through the process of making your hair properly. From prepping your hair to styling it, we’ll cover everything you need to know. So, let’s get started!

Prepping Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, it’s important to prep it properly. Here’s what you need to do:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt, oil, and product buildup.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair. This will help protect your hair from heat damage caused by styling tools.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently detangle your hair. This will help prevent breakage and make it easier to style your hair later on.

Wetting Your Hair

Depending on the hairstyle you want to achieve, you may need to wet your hair before styling it. Here’s how:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties.

Step 2: Wet Your Hair

Using a spray bottle or the showerhead, wet your hair section by section. Be careful not to soak your hair too much.

Brushing Your Hair

Brushing your hair is an important step in making it properly. Here’s what you need to do:

Step 1: Choose the Right Brush

Choose a brush that’s suitable for your hair type. For example, if you have thick, curly hair, use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

Starting at the ends, gently brush your hair in a downward motion. Gradually work your way up to the roots. This will help prevent breakage and make your hair easier to style.

Blow Drying Your Hair

If you want to achieve a smooth, sleek look, you’ll need to blow dry your hair. Here’s what you need to do:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair section by section. Make sure to keep the blow dryer moving and use a low to medium heat setting to avoid heat damage.

Straightening Your Hair

If you want to straighten your hair, you’ll need to use a flat iron. Here’s how:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties.

Step 2: Straighten Your Hair

Starting at the roots, clamp a section of hair with the flat iron and slowly glide it down to the ends. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Curling Your Hair

If you want to curl your hair, you’ll need to use a curling iron. Here’s how:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties.

Step 2: Curl Your Hair

Starting at the roots, wrap a section of hair around the curling iron and hold it for a few seconds. Release the curl and repeat this process for each section of hair.

Finishing Touches

Once you’ve styled your hair, you can add some finishing touches to complete the look. Here are some options:

Option 1: Apply Hair Oil

Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your hair to add shine and reduce frizz.

Option 2: Apply Hair Spray

Apply a light hold hair spray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.

Q: How can I prevent heat damage?

A: Use a heat protectant spray or cream before using hot tools, and avoid using them too often.

Q: What should I do if my hair is dry and damaged?

A: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week and avoid using hot tools too often.

We hope this guide has helped you learn how to make your hair properly. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful!

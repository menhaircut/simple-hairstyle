How To Change Your Hair Color In Animal Crossing New Horizons

If you're an avid Animal Crossing New Horizons player, you'll know that your character's appearance is everything. You want to make sure that your character looks their best, so why not change up their hair color? In this article, we'll be showing you how to change your hair color in Animal Crossing New Horizons.

Description

Animal Crossing New Horizons is a life simulation video game where you play as a customizable character living on a deserted island. One of the most exciting things about the game is the ability to change your character's appearance, including their hair color. This article will guide you through the process, step-by-step.

How To

Changing your hair color in Animal Crossing New Horizons is a simple process. Follow these steps to get started:

Step 1: Unlock the Able Sisters Shop

The first step to changing your hair color is to unlock the Able Sisters shop on your island. The Able Sisters shop is a clothing store that sells a variety of items, including hair color kits. To unlock the Able Sisters shop, you need to have at least one clothing item in your inventory and have played the game for at least seven days.

Step 2: Purchase a Hair Color Kit

Once you've unlocked the Able Sisters shop, head over and purchase a hair color kit. The hair color kit costs 3,000 bells and comes in a variety of colors. Choose the color you want and purchase it.

Step 3: Use the Hair Color Kit

Now that you have your hair color kit, it's time to use it. Head over to a mirror or vanity and interact with it. Select the "Change Look" option and choose "Hairstyle." From there, select "Change Color" and choose the hair color kit you purchased. Your character's hair will now be the color you selected.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to changing your hair color in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

  1. Unlock the Able Sisters shop
  2. Purchase a hair color kit
  3. Use the hair color kit at a mirror or vanity

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when changing your hair color in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

  • Make sure to purchase the correct hair color kit. Once you use it, you won't be able to change your hair color again until you purchase another kit.
  • Experiment with different hair colors to find the perfect one for your character.
  • Remember that your character's hair will grow over time, so you'll need to visit a mirror or vanity to change your hair color again.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hair color in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

Can I change my hair color without purchasing a kit?

No, you need to purchase a hair color kit from the Able Sisters shop to change your hair color.

Can I change my hair color more than once?

Yes, you can change your hair color as many times as you want, but you'll need to purchase a new hair color kit each time.

Do I need to visit a mirror or vanity to change my hair color?

Yes, you need to visit a mirror or vanity to change your hair color.

Hair Care

Changing your hair color in Animal Crossing New Horizons is a fun way to switch up your character's appearance. However, it's important to take care of your character's hair to keep it looking healthy. Here are a few tips:

  • Visit a mirror or vanity every few days to check on your character's hair growth and change the color if necessary.
  • Use hair care products, such as shampoo and conditioner, to keep your character's hair looking healthy.
  • Avoid using too many hair color kits in a short period of time, as this can damage your character's hair.

Changing your hair color in Animal Crossing New Horizons is a simple process that can make a big difference in your character's appearance. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to keep your character looking their best.

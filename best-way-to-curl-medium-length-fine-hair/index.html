Best Way To Curl Medium Length Fine Hair

Written by Christine Sep 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curling your hair can instantly transform your look and add volume to your fine hair. If you have medium length fine hair, you may find it challenging to curl your hair without losing the volume or damaging your hair. In this blog post, we will guide you to the best way to curl medium length fine hair in a relaxed, easy-to-understand language. Here is the table of content for this article:

Description

Medium length fine hair is fragile and delicate, which makes it more prone to damage from heat styling tools. Additionally, the curls may not hold for long due to the lack of volume and texture. Therefore, it is crucial to choose the right curling tool and technique to get the desired curls without damaging your hair.

How to Curl Medium Length Fine Hair

The best way to curl medium length fine hair is by using a curling iron or wand with a small barrel size. The smaller the barrel size, the tighter the curls. Additionally, it is essential to use heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage and frizz.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to curl your medium length fine hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Towel dry your hair and detangle it with a wide-tooth comb.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent heat damage.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections using clips.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand. Hold the hair for 10-15 seconds, then release.
  5. Repeat the process for all the sections of hair, alternating the direction of the curls for a more natural look.
  6. Once you have curled all the sections of hair, use a wide-tooth comb to separate the curls gently.
  7. Finish the look with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips to Get the Best Curls

Here are some tips to get the best curls for your medium length fine hair:

  • Choose a curling iron or wand with a small barrel size (3/4 inch to 1 inch).
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage and frizz.
  • Divide your hair into small sections for more defined curls.
  • Alternate the direction of the curls for a more natural look.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to separate the curls gently.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling medium length fine hair:

Q: Can I curl my hair without heat?

A: Yes, you can curl your hair without heat using overnight curls or braids. However, the curls may not be as defined or long-lasting as heat-styled curls.

Q: How often should I curl my hair?

A: It is best to limit the use of heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair. You can curl your hair once or twice a week, depending on your hair's health and condition.

Q: How can I make my curls last longer?

A: You can make your curls last longer by using a hairspray, avoiding touching your hair too much, and sleeping on a silk pillowcase.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some tips to take care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry to prevent frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Limit the use of heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve the best curls for your medium length fine hair without causing damage or losing volume.

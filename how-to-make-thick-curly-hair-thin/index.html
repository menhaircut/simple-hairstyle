How To Make Thick Curly Hair Thin A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Dec 02, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to manage. It can take hours to dry and style, and it can be hard to find products that work with your hair type. But what if you could make your thick curly hair thin? In this guide, we’ll show you how to do just that.

Description

Thick curly hair can be beautiful, but it can also be a challenge to style and maintain. If you’re looking for a way to make your hair more manageable, thinning it out can be a great option. By reducing the volume of your hair, you can make it easier to style and cut down on drying time.

How to Make Thick Curly Hair Thin

There are a few different methods for thinning out thick curly hair. Here’s a step-by-step guide to the most effective options:

Method 1: Use Thinning Scissors

Thinning scissors are a great tool for thinning out thick curly hair. Here’s how to use them:

  1. Wash and dry your hair as you normally would.
  2. Section off your hair into small sections.
  3. Take a section of hair and hold it straight up.
  4. Use the thinning scissors to cut into the hair, taking small snips at a time.
  5. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair until you’ve achieved your desired level of thinness.

Method 2: Try a Chemical Treatment

If you’re looking for a more permanent solution, a chemical treatment may be the way to go. Here’s what you need to know:

  1. Research different chemical treatments and choose one that’s right for your hair type.
  2. Make an appointment with a professional stylist to have the treatment done.
  3. Follow the stylist’s instructions for aftercare to ensure that your hair stays healthy and strong.

Tips for Making Thick Curly Hair Thin

Here are a few additional tips for thinning out thick curly hair:

  • Use a hair straightener to help smooth out your hair and reduce volume.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look even thicker.
  • Consider getting a shorter haircut to help reduce volume.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few common questions about thinning out thick curly hair:

Q: Will thinning out my hair damage it?

A: Thinning out your hair should not cause any damage if done properly. However, if you use a chemical treatment, it’s important to follow the aftercare instructions to prevent any damage.

Q: How often should I thin out my hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and how quickly it grows. You may only need to thin out your hair once or twice a year, or you may need to do it more frequently.

Q: Can I thin out my hair at home?

A: While you can use thinning scissors at home, it’s best to have a professional stylist perform a chemical treatment to ensure that your hair stays healthy and strong.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Finally, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

With these tips and techniques, you can make your thick curly hair thin and more manageable. Whether you choose to use thinning scissors or a chemical treatment, be sure to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.

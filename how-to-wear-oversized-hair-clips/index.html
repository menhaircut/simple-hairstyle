How To Wear Oversized Hair Clips A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Aug 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Oversized hair clips have become a popular hair accessory trend in recent years. They are versatile, stylish, and can add a touch of glam to any outfit. However, many people are unsure of how to wear oversized hair clips without looking over the top. In this guide, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to wear oversized hair clips and make them work for you.

How to Wear Oversized Hair Clips

Oversized hair clips come in different styles, shapes, and colors. The best way to wear them is to find one that complements your hair color, outfit, and personal style. Here are some tips on how to wear oversized hair clips:

1. Wear Your Hair Down

One of the easiest ways to wear an oversized hair clip is to wear your hair down. This way, the clip can serve as a statement piece and add some interest to your hairstyle. Place the clip on one side of your head, near your temple, for an effortless look.

2. Half-Up, Half-Down

Another way to wear an oversized hair clip is to pull back the top half of your hair and secure it with the clip. This style is perfect for those who want to keep their hair out of their face but still show off the clip.

3. Low Ponytail

For a more understated look, try wearing an oversized hair clip with a low ponytail. This style is perfect for those who want to show off the clip without it being the main focus of the hairstyle.

4. Updo

If you’re feeling adventurous, try wearing an oversized hair clip with an updo. This style is perfect for special occasions and can add some glamour to your look.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to wear an oversized hair clip:

Step 1: Choose Your Clip

Choose an oversized hair clip that complements your hair color, outfit, and personal style.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

Decide on how you want to wear your hair – down, half-up, half-down, low ponytail, or updo.

Step 3: Position the Clip

Place the clip on one side of your head, near your temple, or wherever you want it to be.

Step 4: Secure the Clip

Secure the clip in place by gently pushing it into your hair. For added security, use bobby pins to hold the clip in place.

Tips for Wearing Oversized Hair Clips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing oversized hair clips:

1. Keep it Simple

Oversized hair clips are statement pieces, so it’s best to keep the rest of your outfit and accessories simple.

2. Mix and Match

Don’t be afraid to mix and match different hair accessories, such as headbands and hair ties, with your oversized hair clip.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Try wearing your oversized hair clip with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can oversized hair clips be worn with short hair?

A: Yes, oversized hair clips can be worn with short hair. Simply position the clip in a way that works best for your hair length.

Q: Can oversized hair clips be worn to work or school?

A: Yes, oversized hair clips can be worn to work or school. However, it’s best to keep the rest of your outfit and accessories simple to avoid looking too over the top.

Q: Can oversized hair clips damage my hair?

A: No, oversized hair clips should not damage your hair if used properly. However, it’s important to avoid pulling the clip too tightly or using it on wet hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy while wearing oversized hair clips, make sure to:

1. Use a Good Hairbrush

Use a good quality hairbrush to detangle your hair before using the clip.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.

3. Use a Heat Protectant

If you do use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray or cream to protect your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear oversized hair clips and make them work for you. Whether you’re going for a casual or formal look, oversized hair clips can add some interest and glamour to any hairstyle.

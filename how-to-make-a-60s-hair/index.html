How To Make A 60S Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

The 60s was a decade of bold fashion statements and iconic hairstyles. From the bouffant to the beehive, women in the 60s knew how to make a statement with their hair. If you want to recreate the classic 60s look, here's a step-by-step guide on how to make a 60s hair.

Description

The 60s hair is all about volume and height. It's a bold and statement-making hairstyle that's perfect for parties or special occasions. This hairstyle is characterized by bouffants, beehives, and big curls. It's a timeless look that's still popular today.

How to Make a 60s Hair

If you want to make a 60s hair, here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add extra body and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Apply volumizing mousse

To add even more volume to your hair, apply a volumizing mousse to your roots. This will help your hair stay lifted and voluminous throughout the day.

Step 3: Tease your hair

The key to making a 60s hair is teasing your hair. Take small sections of hair and backcomb them at the roots using a teasing brush. This will create volume and height at the crown of your head.

Step 4: Smooth the top layer

Once you've teased your hair, smooth the top layer using a boar bristle brush. This will help to hide any teasing and create a more polished look.

Step 5: Pin your hair

To create a beehive, gather the teased hair at the crown of your head and pin it in place using bobby pins. You can also use a hair rat to create more volume and shape.

Step 6: Curl your hair

To create big, bouncy curls, use a curling iron or hot rollers on the rest of your hair. Make sure to curl your hair away from your face for a more flattering look.

Step 7: Finish with hairspray

To make sure your 60s hair stays in place all day, finish with a strong hold hairspray. This will help to lock in the volume and keep your curls looking bouncy.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make the perfect 60s hair:

  • Use a teasing brush to create more volume and texture
  • Invest in a hair rat to create a more defined beehive
  • Use hot rollers for a more natural and bouncy curl
  • Finish with a shine spray for a more polished look

FAQ

Q: Can I make a 60s hair with short hair?

A: Yes! You can still create a 60s-inspired hairstyle with short hair. Instead of a beehive, you can create a bouffant by teasing the hair at the crown of your head and pinning it in place.

Q: Can I make a 60s hair without teasing my hair?

A: Teasing is a key element in making a 60s hair, but if you don't want to tease your hair, you can still create a similar look by using hot rollers or a curling iron to create big, bouncy curls.

Hair Care

To keep your 60s hair looking its best, make sure to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a dry shampoo to add extra texture and volume between washes.

Always use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair, and try to limit your use of hot tools to prevent damage.

Finally, make sure to deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and moisturized.

How To Know If A Haircut Will Look Good On Me

Written by Frank Jul 23, 2023 · 4 min read
How to know if a haircut will look good on me

Before getting a haircut, it’s important to consider whether it will suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we’ll provide tips on how to know if a haircut will look good on you.

Table of Contents

  • Consider your face shape
  • Think about your hair type
  • Consider your personal style
  • Step-by-step guide to choosing a haircut
  • Tips for getting a haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair care after a haircut

Consider your face shape

The shape of your face plays a big role in determining which haircut will look good on you. There are several face shapes, including round, square, oval, heart, and diamond. Each of these shapes has unique features that can be accentuated or minimized with the right haircut.

For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for a haircut that adds height and length, such as a short back and sides with a longer top.

Think about your hair type

The texture and thickness of your hair can also affect which haircut will look good on you. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid very short haircuts, as they can make your hair look even thinner. Instead, opt for a medium-length cut with layers to add volume.

If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a shorter haircut to make your hair more manageable. A layered bob or pixie cut can help reduce the weight of your hair and make it easier to style.

Consider your personal style

Your personal style should also play a role in choosing a haircut. If you prefer a more classic look, a short, neat haircut may be the best choice for you. If you’re more adventurous, you may want to try a bold, edgy haircut with shaved sides or a bright color.

Step-by-step guide to choosing a haircut

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you choose a haircut that will look good on you:

  1. Consider your face shape
  2. Think about your hair type
  3. Consider your personal style
  4. Look for inspiration – browse magazines, social media, or consult with a hairstylist
  5. Take into account your lifestyle – will you be able to maintain the haircut?
  6. Make an appointment with a hairstylist – communicate your preferences and ask for their recommendations
  7. Try on the haircut – use a virtual makeover tool or try a wig to get a better idea of how the haircut will look on you
  8. Make the decision – choose a haircut that you feel comfortable and confident with

Tips for getting a haircut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut:

  • Bring a photo – showing your hairstylist a photo of the haircut you want can help them understand your preferences
  • Communicate your preferences – be clear about what you want and don’t want
  • Be open to suggestions – your hairstylist may have recommendations based on your hair type and face shape
  • Consider the maintenance – choose a haircut that you will be able to maintain on a daily basis

FAQs

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and the length of your haircut. Generally, short haircuts need to be trimmed more often than longer haircuts.

Q: Can I pull off a short haircut?

A: Short haircuts can look great on a variety of face shapes and hair types. Consult with a hairstylist to determine if a short haircut is right for you.

Hair care after a haircut

After getting a haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain the look. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to maintain the shape of your haircut

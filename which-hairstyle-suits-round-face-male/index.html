Which Hairstyle Suits Round Face Male

Written by William Jul 18, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering which hairstyle will suit you best. It can be a challenge to find the right style that complements your face shape while also staying on-trend. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best hairstyles for round face males.

Description

Round faces have a circular shape, with wide cheekbones and a rounded jawline. If you have a round face, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face shape and adds some angles. This will give the illusion of a more oval face shape.

How to

The first step in choosing a hairstyle for a round face is to identify your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they’re roughly the same width, you likely have a round face.

Once you’ve identified your face shape, you can begin looking for hairstyles that suit your features. Some of the best hairstyles for round face males include:

1. Short Haircuts

Short haircuts can work well for round face males, especially if they’re styled with some height on top. This adds some angles to the face and elongates the overall shape. A textured crop or buzz cut can also work well.

2. Side Part

A side part can help to create the illusion of a more oval face shape. This style works best with medium-length hair that’s been swept to one side. You can add some texture with a matte pomade or wax.

3. Undercut

An undercut hairstyle involves shaving the sides and back of the head while leaving the top longer. This style can work well for round face males, as it adds some height and volume to the crown. You can style the top with a side-swept fringe or some messy texture.

4. Fringe

A fringe can work well for round face males, especially if it’s a choppy, textured style. This adds some angles to the face and draws attention to the eyes. A fringe can be styled with a side part or worn straight down.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face male:

Step 1: Identify your face shape.

Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline to determine if you have a round face.

Step 2: Research hairstyles.

Look for hairstyles that complement your face shape, such as short haircuts, side parts, undercuts, and fringes. Save some inspiration photos to show your barber or stylist.

Step 3: Consult with a professional.

Visit a barber or stylist who can help you choose the right hairstyle for your face shape and hair type. They can also recommend products to keep your hair looking its best.

Step 4: Experiment with styling.

Once you’ve found the perfect hairstyle, experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you. You can try adding some texture, volume, or hold with different products.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face male:

  • Choose a style that adds some height and volume to the crown of the head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as these can make the face appear rounder.
  • Add some texture or layers to your hair to create the illusion of angles.
  • Consider getting a beard or goatee to add some definition to the jawline.

FAQ

What hairstyles should round face males avoid?

Round face males should avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as these can make the face appear rounder. They should also avoid hairstyles with rounded edges, as these don’t add any angles to the face.

Can round face males wear a side part?

Yes, round face males can wear a side part. This style can help to create the illusion of a more oval face shape, especially if it’s paired with some height on top.

What products should round face males use?

Round face males should use products that add texture, volume, or hold to their hair. This can include pomades, waxes, or texturizing sprays. They should also use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep their hair healthy and strong.

Hair Care

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, round face males should also take good care of their hair. This can include using a quality shampoo and conditioner, avoiding harsh chemical treatments, and getting regular trims to maintain the shape of the style.

They should also protect their hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like blow dryers or straighteners. Finally, they should avoid pulling or tugging on their hair, as this can lead to breakage and damage.

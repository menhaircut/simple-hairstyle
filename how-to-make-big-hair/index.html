How To Make Big Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Frank Jun 29, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking to make a statement with your hair? Do you want to add volume and body to your locks? If so, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll show you how to make big hair in relaxed English language. From simple tips to step-by-step instructions, we've got everything you need to know about creating voluminous, bouncy locks. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Big Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Big Hair

How to Make Big Hair

There are several ways to make big hair, and we've listed some of the most popular ones below:

  • Teasing or backcombing
  • Using hot rollers
  • Blow-drying with a round brush
  • Using a volumizing mousse or spray
  • Getting a perm or body wave

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the different ways to make big hair, let's go through a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If you have fine or limp hair, consider washing it with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and work it through your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and body.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use hot rollers or a curling iron to create loose curls.
  5. Tease or backcomb your hair at the crown to create more lift.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for making big hair:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to add volume without frizz.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in braids to create natural waves.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • If you're getting a perm or body wave, make sure to go to a reputable stylist.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making big hair:

Q: Can I make big hair if I have fine or thin hair?

Yes, you can! Just make sure to use volumizing products and avoid weighing down your hair with too much product.

Q: How long will my big hair style last?

It depends on your hair type and the products you use, but most styles should last at least a day or two.

Q: Is it bad to tease or backcomb my hair?

Teasing or backcombing can cause damage to your hair over time, so it's best to use this technique sparingly.

Hair Care for Big Hair

If you want to maintain your big hair style, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause hair loss.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to make big hair, it's time to try it out for yourself! Whether you're going for a glamorous look or just want to add some volume to your everyday style, these tips and tricks will help you achieve the look you want. So go ahead and experiment with different techniques and products, and have fun with your new big hair!

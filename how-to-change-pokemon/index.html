How To Change Pokemon A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 02, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of battling with the same old Pokemon? Do you want to switch things up and try out a new team? Changing Pokemon is a simple process that can be done in just a few easy steps. In this guide, we'll show you how to change Pokemon in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Open your Pokemon menu
  • Step 2: Choose the Pokemon you want to switch
  • Step 3: Select the new Pokemon
  • Tips for Changing Pokemon
  • FAQs

Step 1: Open your Pokemon menu

The first step in changing your Pokemon is to open your Pokemon menu. You can do this by clicking on the Pokeball icon in the bottom center of your screen. This will bring up a menu with several options, including "Pokemon" and "Items". Click on "Pokemon" to view your current team.

Step 2: Choose the Pokemon you want to switch

Next, you'll need to choose the Pokemon you want to switch out. Click on the Pokemon you want to replace, and you'll see a list of options appear on the right side of your screen. One of these options will be "Switch". Click on this option to move to the next step.

Step 3: Select the new Pokemon

Now it's time to choose the new Pokemon you want to add to your team. You can do this by scrolling through your list of Pokemon and selecting the one you want to add. Once you've selected your new Pokemon, click on the "Switch" button to finalize the change.

Tips for Changing Pokemon

  • Make sure you have a strong and well-rounded team. It's important to have a mix of different types of Pokemon to be prepared for any situation.
  • Consider the type advantages and disadvantages when choosing your new Pokemon. For example, if you're facing a water-type gym leader, you may want to choose a grass or electric-type Pokemon to have an advantage.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different Pokemon. Trying out new Pokemon can be a fun and rewarding experience.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing Pokemon:

Can I change my Pokemon during a battle?

No, you cannot change your Pokemon during a battle. You'll need to wait until the battle is over to make any changes to your team.

How many Pokemon can I have on my team?

You can have up to six Pokemon on your team at a time. If you catch more than six Pokemon, you'll need to choose which ones you want to keep on your team and which ones you want to store in your PC.

What happens to the Pokemon I switch out?

The Pokemon you switch out will be stored in your PC. You can access your PC by visiting a Pokemon Center or by using the PC function in your menu.

Conclusion

Changing your Pokemon is a simple process that can be done in just a few easy steps. By following our guide, you'll be able to switch up your team and try out new Pokemon in no time. Remember to consider your team's strengths and weaknesses and don't be afraid to experiment with different Pokemon. Happy battling!

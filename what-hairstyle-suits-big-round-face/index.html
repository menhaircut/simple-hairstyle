Tips on Choosing a Hairstyle for Big Round Face

Before we dive into the different hairstyles that will suit your big round face, let's talk about some tips on how to choose a hairstyle that will flatter your face shape.

1. Look for hairstyles with volume on top

Hairstyles with volume on top will help elongate your face and make it look slimmer. Avoid hairstyles that add volume on the sides as this will only make your face look wider.

2. Side-swept bangs

Side-swept bangs will help create angles on your face, making it look less round. Avoid blunt bangs or center-parted bangs as this will only emphasize the roundness of your face.

3. Long layers

Long layers will help create movement and texture on your hair, making your face look slimmer. Avoid blunt cuts or chin-length hair as this will only make your face look wider.

4. Embrace your natural texture

If you have curly or wavy hair, embrace it! Curly or wavy hair will add volume and dimension to your hair, making your face look slimmer. Avoid straightening your hair too often as this will only make it look limp and flat.

How to Achieve Different Hairstyles for Big Round Face

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, let's talk about how to achieve different hairstyles that will suit your big round face.

1. Long and Layered

To achieve this hairstyle, ask your stylist for long layers that start at your chin or lower. This will help frame your face and create movement on your hair. You can also add side-swept bangs to create angles on your face.

2. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, go for a pixie cut! This hairstyle will help elongate your neck and create angles on your face. Ask your stylist to add texture on top to create volume, and keep the sides short to avoid adding width to your face.

3. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob with side-swept bangs is a classic hairstyle that will suit your big round face. Ask your stylist for a chin-length bob with long layers and side-swept bangs. This will help elongate your face and create angles on your face.

4. Half-Up Half-Down

If you want to keep your hair down but still want to create volume on top, go for a half-up half-down hairstyle. Tease the crown of your hair and secure it with bobby pins. Leave the rest of your hair down to create movement and texture.

FAQs on Hair Care for Big Round Face

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or you sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more often. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day or every three days.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from getting frizzy?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid rubbing your hair with a towel after showering. Instead, gently pat it dry. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid brushing it when it's wet. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to keep your hair moisturized.

Q: Should I use heat styling tools on my hair?

A: Heat styling tools can damage your hair if used too often. If you need to use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using them on wet hair. Use the lowest temperature setting that works for your hair type and avoid using them every day.

Q: How can I make my hair look fuller?

A: To make your hair look fuller, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Blow-dry your hair upside down to create volume on top. Use a round brush when blow-drying to create movement and texture. You can also use a dry shampoo or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

Q: How can I maintain my hairstyle?

A: To maintain your hairstyle, get regular trims to avoid split ends. Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and hairstyle. Avoid touching your hair too often as this can transfer oil and dirt from your hands to your hair.

Now that you know what hairstyle suits big round face, it's time to experiment and find the one that works best for you. Remember, confidence is key! Own your look and rock it with pride.

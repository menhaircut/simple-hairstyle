How To Do Medium Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Medium hairstyles are perfect for those who want to change their look without going too drastic. It's a versatile length that can be styled in various ways, whether you want it straight, wavy, or curly. In this article, we'll show you how to do medium hairstyles in easy-to-follow steps. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Medium Hairstyles
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Medium Hairstyles
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Medium Hairstyles

How to Do Medium Hairstyles

Before we start, it's essential to know the different types of medium hairstyles. Here are some of the most popular ones:

  • Bob
  • Lob (Long Bob)
  • Shoulder-length cut
  • Layered cut
  • Side-swept bangs

Each of these styles can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. Now, let's move on to the steps on how to do medium hairstyles.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, dry hair

The first step is to wash and dry your hair. You can air-dry your hair or use a blow dryer. Make sure your hair is completely dry before styling it.

2. Apply heat protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

3. Choose your styling tool

Decide which styling tool you want to use. You can use a straightener, curling iron, or wand. Depending on the tool you choose, the technique will vary.

4. Section your hair

Section your hair into manageable parts using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to style each section without getting tangled.

5. Style your hair

Start styling your hair section by section. Depending on the tool you're using, follow the instructions carefully. For example, if you're using a curling iron, wrap your hair around the barrel, hold it for a few seconds, and release.

6. Finish with hairspray

Once you've styled all sections of your hair, finish with a light hairspray. This will hold the style in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Medium Hairstyles

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing medium hairstyles:

  • Invest in quality styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant to keep your hair healthy.
  • Don't use too much hairspray as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what suits you best.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping natural oils from your hair.

Q: How can I make my hair look thicker?

A: Use volumizing products such as mousse or dry shampoo to add texture and body to your hair.

Q: Can I use a straightener to create curls?

A: Yes, you can use a straightener to create curls. Simply twist your hair around the straightener and pull downwards to create a curl.

Hair Care for Medium Hairstyles

Keeping your hair healthy is essential for maintaining any hairstyle. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated for healthy hair.

Now that you know how to do medium hairstyles, it's time to experiment with different styles and find what suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.

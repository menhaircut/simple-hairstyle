What Style Of Glasses Suit A Fat Face A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, finding the perfect pair of glasses can be a daunting task. With so many styles available, it's hard to know where to start. But fear not, we've got you covered. In this blog post, we'll be sharing our top tips on what style of glasses suit a fat face, so you can find the perfect pair that flatters your features.

Description

When it comes to choosing glasses for a round face, the key is to look for styles that balance out the proportions of your face. This means opting for frames that are wider than your cheekbones and have angular or geometric shapes.

How to Choose the Right Glasses for a Fat Face

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right glasses for a fat face is to determine your face shape. To do this, take a look at yourself in the mirror and note the widest part of your face. If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face and your jawline is rounded, you have a round or chubby face shape.

Step 2: Look for Wide Frames

When it comes to choosing glasses for a round face, it's important to look for frames that are wider than your cheekbones. This will help to balance out the proportions of your face and create a more flattering look. Look for frames that have a width of at least 130mm.

Step 3: Choose Angular or Geometric Shapes

In addition to choosing wide frames, it's also important to look for frames that have angular or geometric shapes. These shapes will help to create definition and angles, which will help to balance out the softness of your face. Avoid frames that are too round or circular, as these can make your face look even rounder.

Step 4: Consider the Size of the Frames

The size of the frames is also an important factor to consider when choosing glasses for a round face. Look for frames that are proportionate to your face size. Frames that are too small can make your face look larger, while frames that are too large can overwhelm your features.

Tips for Choosing the Right Glasses for a Fat Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing glasses for a round or chubby face:

  • Opt for frames with a high bridge, as this will help to create more space between the lenses and your cheeks.
  • Avoid frames that have a lot of detail or embellishments, as these can draw attention to the roundness of your face.
  • Choose frames that have a thin or lightweight construction, as these will help to balance out the proportions of your face.
  • If you want to wear oversized frames, look for ones with sharp angles or square shapes.

FAQ

What type of glasses should I avoid if I have a fat face?

Avoid glasses that are too small or too round, as they can make your face look even rounder. Additionally, avoid glasses with a lot of detail or embellishments, as these can draw attention to the roundness of your face.

Can I wear oversized glasses if I have a fat face?

Yes, you can wear oversized glasses if you have a fat face. Look for frames with sharp angles or square shapes to balance out the proportions of your face.

What is the best color for glasses for a fat face?

When it comes to color, it's best to choose a frame color that complements your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, opt for frames in warm colors like brown, gold, or tortoise. If you have a cool skin tone, opt for frames in cool colors like black, silver, or blue.

Hair Care Tips for a Fat Face

In addition to choosing the right glasses, there are also some hair care tips that can help to flatter a fat face:

  • Choose a hairstyle with layers or angles, as this will help to create definition and structure.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as these can make your face look even rounder.
  • Opt for hairstyles with volume at the crown, as this will help to create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face.
  • Consider getting bangs, as they can help to create angles and draw attention to your eyes.

With these tips in mind, you're sure to find the perfect pair of glasses that flatters your features and complements your personal style. Remember to choose frames that are wide, angular, and proportionate to your face size, and consider hair care tips that can help to create definition and structure. Happy shopping!

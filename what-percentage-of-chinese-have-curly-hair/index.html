What Percentage Of Chinese Have Curly Hair

Written by Frank Aug 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curly hair has always been a topic of interest among people. We all have different hair types, and it's fascinating to know how many people have curly hair in different parts of the world. Today, we are going to talk about what percentage of Chinese have curly hair.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Curly hair is not very common among Chinese people. According to research, only about 2% of Chinese people have naturally curly hair. This is because most Chinese people have straight hair due to genetics. However, with the increasing popularity of perming and hair styling, more and more Chinese people are embracing curly hair.

How to

If you are interested in finding out if you have curly hair, you can do a simple test. Take a strand of your hair and wet it. Then, gently pull it straight. If your hair curls up again when you let go, you have curly hair. If it stays straight, you have straight hair.

Step by step

To test if you have curly hair, follow these simple steps:

  1. Take a strand of your hair.
  2. Wet it with water.
  3. Gently pull it straight.
  4. Let go.

If your hair curls up again when you let go, you have curly hair.

Tips

If you have curly hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Don't wash your hair every day.
  • Use a conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hair:

Q. Is curly hair genetic?

A. Yes, curly hair is genetic.

Q. Can you make straight hair curly?

A. Yes, you can make straight hair curly with a perm or other styling techniques.

Q. What causes curly hair?

A. Curly hair is caused by the shape of the hair follicle. If the follicle is oval, the hair is more likely to be curly. If it's round, the hair is more likely to be straight.

Hair Care

If you have curly hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt instead.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol.

Now that you know what percentage of Chinese have curly hair, you can appreciate how unique your hair type is. Whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

What Type Of Haircut Suits For Oval Face

Written by Petter Oct 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect haircut for your oval face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles that suit oval faces. From short to long, curly to straight, we have got you covered.

Description

An oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile face shape. If you have an oval face, then you are lucky because you can pull off almost any haircut. However, there are certain styles that can enhance your features and make you look even more stunning.

How to Choose the Right Haircut?

Before choosing a haircut, it is important to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal preferences. Your hairdresser can help you choose the right haircut based on your face shape, but here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for oval face:

  • Choose a haircut that compliments your face shape and enhances your features.
  • Avoid haircuts that add too much volume around the face.
  • Consider your hair texture and choose a style that works well with it.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some popular haircuts that suit oval faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for oval face shapes because they enhance your features and add volume to your hair. Start by parting your hair in the middle and adding long layers to the ends. This will create a soft and feminine look.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is perfect for oval faces because it adds volume to the top of your head and creates a flattering shape. Ask your hairdresser to add layers to the top of your head and keep the sides short. This will create a modern and chic look.

3. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another great option for oval faces because it adds volume to the back of your head and creates a flattering shape. Ask your hairdresser to add layers to the ends of your hair and keep the length above your shoulders. This will create a classic and sophisticated look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for oval face:

  • Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long.
  • Avoid haircuts that add too much volume around the face.
  • Consider your hair texture and choose a style that works well with it.

FAQ

Q. Can oval faces pull off bangs?

A. Yes, oval faces can pull off bangs. However, it is important to choose the right type of bangs that suit your face shape. Side-swept bangs are a great option for oval faces because they add volume to the sides of your face.

Q. Can oval faces pull off short haircuts?

A. Yes, oval faces can pull off short haircuts. However, it is important to choose a style that adds volume to the top of your head and creates a flattering shape. Pixie cuts and bob cuts are great options for oval faces.

Q. Can oval faces pull off curly hair?

A. Yes, oval faces can pull off curly hair. However, it is important to choose a style that works well with your hair texture. Layered haircuts are a great option for curly hair because they add volume and create a flattering shape.

Hair Care

To maintain your haircut, it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Now that you know what type of haircut suits for oval face, it's time to book an appointment with your hairdresser and try out a new style. Remember to choose a style that compliments your face shape and enhances your features.

How To Know If You Will Look Good With Curtain Bangs

Written by Bardi Sep 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking about getting curtain bangs but not sure if they will suit you? Curtain bangs have been trending for quite some time now, and they look perfect on some people. However, they may not be suitable for everyone. In this article, we will guide you on how to know if you will look good with curtain bangs.

Table of Contents

  2. Analyze Your Hair Texture
  3. Look at Your Forehead Size

Consider Your Face Shape

Curtain bangs are versatile and can suit most face shapes. However, some face shapes can pull them off better than others. For example, curtain bangs look great on oval and heart-shaped faces. On the other hand, people with round faces should be careful with curtain bangs as they can make the face appear wider.


Analyze Your Hair Texture

Curtain bangs work well with most hair textures, but they look best on hair that is straight or slightly wavy. If you have curly or thick hair, curtain bangs may require more maintenance to keep them looking good.


Look at Your Forehead Size

If you have a small forehead, you may want to avoid curtain bangs as they can make it appear smaller. However, if you have a larger forehead, curtain bangs can help balance out your face.


Check Your Hair Length

Curtain bangs look best on mid-length to long hair. If you have short hair, it may not be possible to get curtain bangs as they require a certain length to achieve the desired look.


Think About Your Lifestyle

Curtain bangs require regular maintenance, such as trimming, styling, and blow-drying. If you have a busy lifestyle and don't have time to style your hair regularly, curtain bangs may not be the best option for you.


Consider Your Hair Styling Habits

If you prefer to wear your hair up most of the time, curtain bangs may not be the best choice for you. Curtain bangs tend to fall forward, and if you constantly have to push them back, they can become annoying.


Factor in Your Hair Color

Curtain bangs can look great on most hair colors, but they tend to stand out more on lighter shades. If you have dark hair, you may want to consider adding some highlights to your bangs to make them pop.


Examine Your Face Features

Curtain bangs can highlight your facial features, such as your eyes and cheekbones. If you have prominent features that you want to showcase, curtain bangs can help draw attention to them.


Try Them On Virtually

If you're still unsure if curtain bangs will suit you, you can try them on virtually. There are many apps and websites that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles, including curtain bangs. This can give you a good idea of what you would look like with curtain bangs.


FAQs

Q: Can I get curtain bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can get curtain bangs if you have curly hair, but it may require more maintenance to keep them looking good.


Q: Will curtain bangs suit me if I have a round face?

A: It depends on the length and thickness of the bangs. If they are too short or too thick, they can make your face appear wider. However, if they are the right length and thickness, they can balance out your face shape.


Q: Can I wear my curtain bangs up?

A: Yes, you can wear your curtain bangs up, but they may require some styling to keep them in place.


Hair Care

Once you've decided to get curtain bangs, it's essential to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Regular trimming and styling are necessary to maintain the shape and length of your bangs. You can also use a dry shampoo to keep them looking fresh in between washes.


Now that you know how to know if you will look good with curtain bangs, you can confidently make the decision to get them or not. Remember, it's all about finding the right length, thickness, and shape that will suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

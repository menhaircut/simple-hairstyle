What Hair Colour Suits My Skin Tone Quiz

Written by Aprilia Aug 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to change up your hair colour but not sure what shade will suit your skin tone? Take our quiz to find out which hair colour will make you look and feel your best.

Description

Choosing the right hair colour for your skin tone can be a daunting task. With so many shades to choose from, it's hard to know where to start. Our quiz is designed to help you find the perfect hair colour for your skin tone.

How to

To take the quiz, simply answer the following questions:

  1. What is your skin tone?
  2. What is your natural hair colour?
  3. What is your eye colour?
  4. Do you prefer warm or cool colours?
  5. What is your personal style?

Once you have answered these questions, we will provide you with a hair colour recommendation based on your answers.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your skin tone

The first step in determining which hair colour will suit you best is to determine your skin tone. There are three main types of skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral.

Warm skin tones have a yellow or golden undertone, while cool skin tones have a pink or blue undertone. Neutral skin tones have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

Step 2: Consider your natural hair colour

Your natural hair colour can also play a role in determining which hair colour will suit you best. If you have warm undertones in your skin, warm hair colours like golden blonde or copper red will complement your skin tone. If you have cool undertones, cool hair colours like ash blonde or platinum blonde will suit you best.

Step 3: Look at your eye colour

Your eye colour can also help you determine which hair colour will suit you best. If you have blue or green eyes, cool hair colours will complement your eyes. If you have brown eyes, warm hair colours will make your eyes pop.

Step 4: Decide between warm and cool colours

Some people prefer warm colours, while others prefer cool colours. If you prefer warm colours, go for shades like golden blonde or copper red. If you prefer cool colours, go for shades like ash blonde or platinum blonde.

Step 5: Consider your personal style

Your personal style should also be taken into consideration when choosing a hair colour. If you have a bold, edgy style, you may want to consider a bright, bold hair colour. If you have a more classic style, a more natural hair colour may suit you best.

Tips

  • Always test a hair colour before committing to it.
  • Consider the maintenance of the hair colour before choosing it.
  • Ask your hair stylist for their opinion.
  • Consider the season when choosing a hair colour.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair a different colour if it doesn't suit my skin tone?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair a different colour if you're not happy with the results. However, it's important to remember that dyeing your hair can damage it, so it's best to consult with a professional hair stylist before making any drastic changes.

Q: Can I dye my hair a colour that's not recommended for my skin tone?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair any colour you like. However, if you want to look your best, it's best to choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen your new hair colour, it's important to take care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for coloured hair to keep your new colour looking vibrant. Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the colour. Use a heat protectant when using hot tools on your hair, as heat can also damage coloured hair.

Now that you know what hair colour suits your skin tone, it's time to book an appointment with your hair stylist and get ready to rock your new look!

The Best Long Haircuts For Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having a round face can be both a blessing and a curse when it comes to choosing the perfect haircut. On one hand, it can mean that you have a naturally youthful and soft appearance, but on the other hand, it can be challenging to find a haircut that flatters your face shape. If you have long hair and a round face, you're in luck! In this article, we'll be highlighting the best long haircuts for round face and giving you tips on how to style and maintain your gorgeous locks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Long Haircuts for Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide on Styling Long Haircuts for Round Face
  • Tips on Maintaining Long Haircuts for Round Face
  • FAQs on Long Haircuts for Round Face
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Haircuts for Round Face

How to Choose the Best Long Haircuts for Round Face

When it comes to choosing the best long haircuts for round face, it's essential to consider the length, layers, and texture of your hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for long haircuts that elongate your face, such as layers that start at your chin or below.
  • Choose haircuts with side-swept bangs to create an illusion of length.
  • Consider adding volume to the crown of your head to create a more oval shape.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your cheekbones or chin, as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Experiment with different textures, such as waves or curls, to add dimension to your hair and face.

Step-by-Step Guide on Styling Long Haircuts for Round Face

Now that you've chosen the perfect long haircut for your round face, it's time to style it! Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to your roots to create lift.
  3. If you have curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural texture.
  4. If you have straight hair, use a round brush to create volume and curl at the ends.
  5. After blow-drying your hair, use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or curls, depending on your preference.
  6. Finish off your look with a spritz of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips on Maintaining Long Haircuts for Round Face

Keeping your long haircut looking fresh and stylish requires some maintenance. Here are some tips:

  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage, and always use a heat protectant before styling.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it appear flat.
  • Invest in a high-quality brush and comb to detangle your hair without causing breakage.

FAQs on Long Haircuts for Round Face

What are the best long haircuts for round face?

The best long haircuts for round face are those that add length and volume to your face, such as layered cuts that start at your chin or below, and side-swept bangs.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face. Just make sure to create volume at the crown of your head to elongate your face.

How often should I trim my long haircuts for round face?

You should trim your long haircuts for round face every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Long Haircuts for Round Face

Maintaining healthy and beautiful hair requires proper care. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, the best long haircuts for round face are those that add length, volume, and dimension to your hair and face. By following our tips on choosing, styling, and maintaining your long haircut, you'll be sure to turn heads and feel confident in your appearance.

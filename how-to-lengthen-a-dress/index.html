How To Lengthen A Dress A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 17, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a dress that is too short or you want to add some length for a more modest look, you may be wondering how to lengthen a dress. Fortunately, there are several easy ways to do this without spending a lot of money or time. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to add length to your dress so you can wear it with confidence.

How to Lengthen a Dress

Before we get started, here are some things you’ll need:

  • The dress you want to lengthen
  • Fabric to match the dress
  • Scissors
  • A sewing machine or needle and thread
  • Pins

Step 1: Measure the Length

The first step is to measure how much length you want to add to the dress. Try the dress on and determine how much longer you want it to be. Measure from the hemline up to where you want the new hemline to be.

Step 2: Choose the Fabric

Choose fabric that matches the dress as closely as possible. This will ensure that the new material blends in seamlessly with the original fabric. You can use the same fabric as the dress if you have extra, or you can purchase new fabric.

Step 3: Cut the Fabric

Cut a strip of fabric that is the length you measured in step one plus an inch or two for the hem. The width of the fabric strip should be the same width as the dress hem. Cut the strip of fabric in a straight line.

Step 4: Pin the Fabric

Pin the strip of fabric to the bottom of the dress, right sides together. Pin all the way around the dress, making sure to keep the fabric strip even with the hemline of the dress.

Step 5: Sew the Fabric

Using a sewing machine or needle and thread, sew the fabric strip to the dress. Sew as close to the hemline as possible, and make sure to backstitch at the beginning and end of the seam. Trim any excess fabric.

Step 6: Hem the Dress

Turn the dress right side out and fold the new fabric strip under. Pin in place and sew a new hemline. You can use a straight stitch or a zigzag stitch, depending on your preference.

Tips for Lengthening a Dress

  • Make sure to choose fabric that matches the dress as closely as possible.
  • Use a sewing machine if possible for a stronger seam.
  • Try the dress on before hemming to make sure the length is right.

FAQ

Q. Can I lengthen a dress without sewing?

A. Yes, there are several no-sew options for lengthening a dress, such as adding lace or ruffles to the hemline. However, these options may not be as secure as sewing.

Q. How long does it take to lengthen a dress?

A. Depending on your sewing skill level, it can take anywhere from 30 minutes to a few hours to lengthen a dress.

Q. Can I lengthen a dress that is too small?

A. It is possible to add length to a dress that is too small, but it may be more difficult. You may need to add fabric to the sides of the dress as well as the hemline.

Hair Care

When lengthening a dress, it’s important to also take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools on your hair.
  • Avoid using tight hair elastics that can cause breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair stays healthy and beautiful while you rock your newly lengthened dress.

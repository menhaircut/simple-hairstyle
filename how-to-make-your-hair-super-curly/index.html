How To Make Your Hair Super Curly

Written by Christine Aug 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight or wavy hair and want to try something new? Look no further than super curly hair! It may seem intimidating, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve voluminous and bouncy curls. Here’s how:

Description

Super curly hair is a popular trend that has been seen on runways, red carpets, and social media. It involves tight, defined curls that add volume and texture to your hair. This look can be achieved on any hair type, from straight to wavy to curly, and can be done with or without heat.

How To

There are several methods to achieve super curly hair, but here are some of the most popular:

Method 1: Heat Styling

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.

3. Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties.

4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron or wand.

5. Hold for 10-15 seconds, then release the curl.

6. Repeat on all sections of your hair.

7. Once all curls are done, use your fingers to separate them and create volume.

8. Finish with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Method 2: No Heat

1. Start with clean, damp hair.

2. Apply a curl enhancing cream or mousse to your hair.

3. Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties.

4. Take a small section of hair and twist it around your finger, starting at the root and working your way down to the ends.

5. Secure the twist with a bobby pin or hair tie.

6. Repeat on all sections of your hair.

7. Once all twists are done, let your hair air dry completely.

8. Remove the bobby pins or hair ties and use your fingers to separate the curls and create volume.

9. Finish with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving super curly hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is naturally oily, wash it the night before and let it air dry overnight.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant spray or curl enhancing cream to your hair to protect it from heat damage or to enhance your curls.

Step 3: Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to curl or twist your hair.

Step 4: Use a curling iron or wand to curl your hair, or twist your hair around your finger and secure with a bobby pin or hair tie.

Step 5: Once all curls or twists are done, let your hair cool completely before removing the pins or ties.

Step 6: Use your fingers to separate the curls and create volume.

Step 7: Finish with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect super curly hair:

- Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

- Don’t curl or twist your hair too tightly, as it can damage your hair and create an unnatural look.

- Use a curl enhancing cream or mousse to enhance your natural curls.

- Don’t brush or comb your hair after curling or twisting, as it can ruin the curls.

- Use a hairspray to hold the curls in place, but don’t apply too much as it can weigh down your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve super curly hair on short hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve super curly hair on any length of hair.

Q: Will heat styling damage my hair?

A: Heat styling can damage your hair if not done properly. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray and don’t apply too much heat to your hair.

Q: Can I achieve super curly hair without using any products?

A: It’s possible, but using a curl enhancing cream or mousse can help enhance your curls and make them last longer.

Hair Care

To maintain your super curly hair, make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Avoid using too many styling products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

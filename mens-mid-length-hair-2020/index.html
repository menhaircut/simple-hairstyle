Mens Mid Length Hair 2020 A Guide To The Latest Hairstyle Trends

Written by Bardi Oct 01, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a new hairstyle to try out in 2023, why not consider mid-length hair? This versatile length has been popular for a few years now, and it shows no signs of going away anytime soon. In this blog post, we’ll take a look at some of the latest trends for mens mid length hair 2020, and give you tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Mens Mid Length Hair 2020
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Mid Length Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

Description

Mid-length hair is defined as hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. This length is perfect for those who want to try something different than the traditional short or long hairstyles. Mid-length hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from messy and textured to sleek and polished. It’s also a great option for those with curly or wavy hair, as it allows for more movement and definition than shorter styles.

How to Style Mens Mid Length Hair 2020

There are many different ways to style mid-length hair, depending on your personal preference and hair type. Here are a few popular options:

1. Textured and Messy

This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look. Simply apply a texturizing product to damp hair and let it air dry. Use your fingers to tousle the hair and create texture.

2. Slicked Back

This style is polished and sophisticated. Apply a medium hold gel or pomade to damp hair and comb it back away from the face. Use a blow dryer to create extra volume and hold.

3. Surfer Waves

This style works well for those with wavy or slightly curly hair. Apply a sea salt spray to damp hair and scrunch it with your hands. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance the waves.

Step by Step Guide

To achieve any of the above styles, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair
  2. Apply your chosen styling product
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to create the desired shape
  4. Blow dry or air dry, depending on the look you want
  5. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product, if desired

Tips for Maintaining Mid Length Hair

Here are a few tips to keep your mid-length hair looking healthy and stylish:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Invest in quality styling products

FAQs

1. Is mid-length hair suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, mid-length hair is versatile enough to suit all face shapes. However, you may want to consult with your hairstylist to find the best length and style for your specific features.

2. Can I still style my mid-length hair if it’s thin?

Absolutely! Texturizing products and volumizing sprays can help create the illusion of thicker hair. Talk to your stylist about the best products for your hair type.

3. How often should I wash my mid-length hair?

This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If your hair is dry or curly, you can probably get away with washing it every few days.

Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

Here are a few tips for keeping your mid-length hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Protect your hair from heat damage
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair

In conclusion, mens mid length hair 2020 is a versatile and stylish option for those looking to change up their hairstyle. Whether you prefer a messy, textured look or a sleek, polished style, there are many options to choose from. By following these tips for styling and caring for your hair, you can achieve a look that’s both fashionable and healthy.

What Faces Suit Bangs A Guide

Written by Frank Nov 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking about getting bangs but not sure if they will suit your face shape? Bangs can be a great addition to any hairstyle, but it’s important to choose the right type of bangs that will complement your facial features. In this guide, we will discuss what faces suit bangs and provide tips for choosing the perfect style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Bangs
  • Tips for Maintaining Bangs
  • FAQs about Bangs
  • Hair Care Tips for Bangs

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right bangs for your face shape is to determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. Once you know your face shape, you can choose the type of bangs that will complement your features:

Round Face:

If you have a round face, you should choose bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Avoid blunt bangs and heavy bangs that are shorter than your eyebrows.

Oval Face:

For those with an oval face shape, you can pretty much rock any type of bangs. However, if you have a high forehead, you may want to go for full bangs that cover your forehead.

Square Face:

If you have a square face, you should choose bangs that are soft and wispy. Side-swept bangs are a great option as they help to soften the angles of your face. Avoid blunt bangs and straight-across bangs that will emphasize your square jawline.

Heart-Shaped Face:

If you have a heart-shaped face, you should choose bangs that are side-swept and tapered. This will help to balance out a wider forehead and narrower chin. Avoid blunt bangs and heavy bangs that will make your forehead appear even wider.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Bangs

If you’re thinking about cutting your own bangs, here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with dry, clean hair
  2. Section off the hair that will become your bangs
  3. Use a comb to create a straight line for your bangs
  4. Use a pair of sharp scissors to cut the hair
  5. Start with small snips and work your way up
  6. Check your progress in the mirror and adjust as needed
  7. Style your bangs as desired

Tips for Maintaining Bangs

Once you’ve got your bangs, here are some tips for maintaining them:

  • Wash your bangs daily to prevent them from becoming greasy
  • Blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to lay
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent oil buildup

FAQs about Bangs

Q: Can I pull off bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can! However, it’s important to choose the right type of bangs for your curl pattern. Wispy bangs or side-swept bangs work well with curly hair.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: It’s recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with bangs?

A: Yes, you can! Simply pull your bangs back with the rest of your hair or leave them out for a cute, effortless look.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Here are some general hair care tips for keeping your bangs looking their best:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Invest in a good dry shampoo to extend the life of your bangs between washes
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to keep your bangs shiny and frizz-free

Now that you know what faces suit bangs, you can confidently choose the perfect style for your features. Whether you opt for full bangs or side-swept bangs, bangs can be a fun and stylish addition to any hairstyle.

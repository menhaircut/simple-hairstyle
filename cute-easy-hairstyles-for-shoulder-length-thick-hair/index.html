Cute Easy Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Thick Hair

Written by Oliver Nov 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hair routine? Do you want to switch up your look without spending hours in front of the mirror? Look no further! Here are some cute and easy hairstyles for shoulder length thick hair that you can rock on any occasion.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length hair is versatile and easy to style, but when you add thickness to the mix, it can be a bit more challenging. Fortunately, with the right tools and techniques, you can create stunning looks that will turn heads. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, these cute hairstyles will help you achieve your desired style with ease.

How to

The key to achieving these cute hairstyles is to have the right tools and products on hand. Some of the essential items include:

  • Brush or comb
  • Hair elastics
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray or styling product
  • Curling iron or straightener (optional)

Step by step

1. Half-up Half-down

Begin by brushing your hair and creating a part in the middle. Take a section of hair from each side of your head and pull it back, securing it with an elastic band. You can leave the rest of your hair down or curl it for a more polished look.

2. Low Ponytail

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band. For a more polished look, wrap a small section of hair around the elastic band and pin it in place.

3. Messy Bun

Pull your hair back into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

4. French Braid

Begin by creating a part on one side of your head. Take a small section of hair near your forehead and divide it into three sections. Begin braiding your hair by crossing the outer sections over the center section. As you continue braiding, pick up additional sections of hair and incorporate them into your braid. Secure the end of your braid with an elastic band.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or straightener to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Dry shampoo can help add volume to your hair and extend the life of your style.

FAQ

1. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have thick hair, you may be able to go longer between washes. Generally, it's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. How can I prevent my hair from getting tangled?

Brush your hair regularly and use a detangling spray or conditioner. Avoid sleeping with your hair down, as this can cause tangles.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, it's important to use the right products and follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools frequently.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated.

With these cute and easy hairstyles for shoulder length thick hair, you can switch up your look in no time. Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to keep it healthy and looking its best.

How Do You Know If A Haircut Will Look Good

Written by Daniel Aug 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Getting a new haircut can be a big decision. After all, your hair is a big part of your identity, and you want it to look its best. But how do you know if a haircut will look good on you? In this article, we’ll give you some tips on how to figure out if a haircut will flatter your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Table of Contents

  1. Determining Your Face Shape
  2. Considering Your Hair Type
  3. Matching Your Personal Style
  4. Haircut Ideas for Different Face Shapes
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips

Determining Your Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a haircut is your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and long. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. Take note of the widest part of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face.

Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can look for haircut ideas that will flatter your features. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid cuts that add volume to the sides of your head. Instead, opt for a long layered cut that adds height to your crown and lengthens your face.

Considering Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when choosing a new haircut is your hair type. Do you have thick or thin hair? Is it straight or curly? Do you have any problem areas, such as frizz or split ends? All of these factors can influence the type of haircut that will look best on you.

For example, if you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut that thins out your hair and adds movement. If you have thin hair, you may want to avoid blunt cuts that can make your hair look flat and lifeless.

Matching Your Personal Style

Of course, your personal style is also an important factor to consider when choosing a new haircut. Do you prefer a classic, timeless look, or do you like to stay on top of the latest trends? Are you comfortable with a dramatic change, or do you prefer to stick with a more subtle update?

Ultimately, the best haircut for you is the one that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Consider your personal style and how a new haircut will fit into your overall aesthetic.

Haircut Ideas for Different Face Shapes

Here are some haircut ideas for different face shapes:

  • Round face: long layered cut, side-swept bangs
  • Square face: angled bob, layered shag
  • Oval face: blunt lob, pixie cut
  • Heart face: shoulder-length layers, side-parted bob
  • Diamond face: chin-length bob, textured layers
  • Long face: blunt bangs, shoulder-length waves

FAQs

How do I know if a short haircut will look good on me?

If you’re considering a short haircut, look for photos of people with similar face shapes and hair types. You can also try on wigs or use virtual makeover apps to get an idea of how a short haircut will look on you.

What should I do if I don’t like my new haircut?

If you’re not happy with your new haircut, talk to your stylist about what you don’t like and how it can be fixed. Most stylists want their clients to be happy with their hair, so they should be willing to make adjustments.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your new haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat tools on a daily basis
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and dyes that can damage your hair

By following these tips and considering your face shape, hair type, and personal style, you can choose a haircut that will make you look and feel your best.

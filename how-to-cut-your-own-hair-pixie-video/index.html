How To Cut Your Own Hair Pixie Video A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts every few weeks? Do you want to try something new and daring with your hair? Cutting your own hair into a pixie cut might be the perfect solution for you. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own hair pixie video, including tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look. So, let's get started!

  • Description
  • How to Cut Your Own Hair Pixie Video
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is typically cut to be around ½ inch to 3 inches in length. It is a bold and daring style that is perfect for those who want to make a statement with their hair. While it can be intimidating to cut your own hair, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful pixie cut in the comfort of your own home.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Pixie Video

Cutting your own hair can seem daunting, but it is actually quite simple. All you need is a few basic tools and some patience. In this section, we will go over the tools you will need and the basic steps to cutting your own hair into a pixie cut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you begin cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers with guards
  • Comb and hair clips
  • Mirror

Once you have gathered your tools, follow these steps:

  1. Start with dry, clean hair. Comb your hair straight down and section off the top portion of your hair with hair clips.
  2. Using the clippers with a guard, start at the nape of your neck and work your way up, cutting your hair to the desired length.
  3. Remove the guard from the clippers and use them to trim the hair around your ears and neckline.
  4. Release the top section of your hair and comb it straight down. Using the scissors, cut the hair to the desired length, starting from the back and working your way forward.
  5. Trim the hair around your ears and neckline with the scissors.
  6. Check your cut in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect pixie cut:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut shorter as you gain more confidence.
  • Use hair clips to section off your hair and make it easier to cut.
  • Take breaks between cutting to give your arms and hands a rest.
  • Invest in high-quality scissors and clippers for the best results.
  • Watch tutorials on YouTube or other video platforms to see the process in action.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair into a pixie cut:

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your pixie cut.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. Hair grows back and mistakes can be fixed. Take a break and come back to it later, or seek the help of a professional stylist to correct any mistakes.

Can I dye my hair after cutting it into a pixie cut?

Yes, you can dye your hair after cutting it into a pixie cut. However, it is recommended to wait a few days after cutting to allow your hair to recover before applying any chemicals.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair into a pixie cut, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to add shine and tame any frizz.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your pixie cut.

Cutting your own hair into a pixie cut can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and unique hairstyle that reflects your personality. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to make mistakes. Happy cutting!

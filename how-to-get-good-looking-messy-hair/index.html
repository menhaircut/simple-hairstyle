How To Get Good Looking Messy Hair

Written by Aprilia Aug 09, 2023 · 3 min read

In this article, we will discuss how to achieve the perfect messy hair look. This is a popular hairstyle that is easy to achieve with the right techniques and products. We will cover everything from how to style your hair to how to care for it. Let’s get started!

How to Get Good Looking Messy Hair

Messy hair is a popular style that can be achieved with the right techniques and products. Here are the steps to follow:

1. Start with Clean Hair

The first step to achieving good looking messy hair is to start with clean hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. This will help to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Add Texture

The next step is to add texture to your hair. This can be done by using a texturizing spray or powder. Apply the product to your hair and work it in with your fingers. This will help to create volume and texture.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to create volume and texture. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you blow dry it.

4. Use a Styling Product

Use a styling product to help hold the messy look in place. This can be a pomade or wax. Apply the product to your hair and work it in with your fingers. This will help to create texture and hold the style in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to achieving good looking messy hair:

Step 1:

Start with clean hair.

Step 2:

Add texture to your hair with a texturizing spray or powder.

Step 3:

Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment.

Step 4:

Apply a styling product to hold the messy look in place.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Messy Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy hair:

Tip 1:

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2:

Use a diffuser attachment when blow drying your hair to create volume and texture.

Tip 3:

Apply a styling product to hold the messy look in place.

FAQs

Q: Can messy hair be achieved with short hair?

A: Yes, messy hair can be achieved with short hair. Use a pomade or wax to create texture and hold the style in place.

Q: Can messy hair be achieved with curly hair?

A: Yes, messy hair can be achieved with curly hair. Use a diffuser attachment when blow drying your hair to create volume and texture.

Hair Care Tips for Messy Hair

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining healthy, good looking messy hair:

Tip 1:

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2:

Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.

Tip 3:

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect messy hair look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

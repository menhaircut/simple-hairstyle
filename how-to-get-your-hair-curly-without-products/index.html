How To Get Your Hair Curly Without Products

Written by Oliver Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally straight hair, getting your hair curly can be a daunting task. However, with the right techniques and methods, you can achieve bouncy curls without using any products. In this article, we will show you how to get your hair curly without products using simple and easy-to-follow steps.

How to

There are several ways to get your hair curly without using any products. Here are some methods you can try:

Method 1: Sleeping with Braids

One of the easiest ways to get your hair curly without using products is by sleeping with braids. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's slightly damp.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section tightly.
  3. Secure the ends of each braid with a hair tie.
  4. Sleep with the braids in and remove them in the morning.
  5. Use your fingers to gently separate the curls and apply a small amount of hair oil to add shine.

By sleeping with braids, you can achieve beautiful, natural-looking curls without using any heat or products.

Method 2: Using Rollers

Another way to get your hair curly without using products is by using rollers. Here's how:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's slightly damp.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and roll each section onto a roller.
  3. Secure the rollers in place with hair clips.
  4. Leave the rollers in for a few hours or overnight.
  5. Remove the rollers and use your fingers to gently separate the curls.

Using rollers is a great way to get bouncy, voluminous curls without damaging your hair with heat or products.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • For tighter curls, use smaller braids or rollers.
  • For looser curls, use larger braids or rollers.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.
  • Don't brush or comb your hair after removing the braids or rollers to avoid ruining the curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting your hair curly without products:

Can I use this method on all hair types?

Yes, these methods can be used on all hair types, but the results may vary depending on your hair's texture and thickness.

How long will the curls last?

The curls can last for a few days, depending on how well you maintain them.

Can I use heat styling tools after using these methods?

Yes, you can use heat styling tools after using these methods, but make sure to use a heat protectant to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes foods rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and achieve beautiful, natural-looking curls without using any products.

Getting your hair curly without products is easy and can be done using simple methods like sleeping with braids or using rollers. By following the steps and tips in this article, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls without damaging your hair with heat or chemicals.

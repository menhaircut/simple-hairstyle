How To Get More Volume In Your Hair With A Straightener

Getting more volume in your hair is a common desire among women. While many people believe that only a curling iron can achieve the desired volume, a straightener can also be used to create volume. In this blog post, we will explain how to get more volume in your hair with a straightener.

How to Get More Volume in Your Hair with a Straightener

Here are the steps to get more volume in your hair with a straightener:

1. Choose the Right Straightener

Not all straighteners are created equal. If you want to create more volume in your hair, you need to choose a straightener that has a curved edge. This will allow you to create waves in your hair, which will add volume. Additionally, choose a straightener that has adjustable heat settings so that you can lower the heat to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Prep Your Hair

Before you start using the straightener, you need to prep your hair. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair, and then apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots. This will help to add volume to your hair.

3. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections. The smaller the sections, the easier it will be to straighten your hair and create volume. Use hair clips to keep the sections in place.

4. Straighten Your Hair

Take one section of hair and clamp the straightener near the roots. Twist the straightener inwards and slide it down the length of your hair. This will create a slight wave in your hair, which will add volume. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.

5. Finish Your Look

Once you have straightened all sections of your hair, use a volumizing hairspray to set your style. You can also use a teasing comb to tease the roots of your hair to add even more volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting more volume in your hair with a straightener:

Step 1: Choose the right straightener

Choose a straightener that has a curved edge and adjustable heat settings.

Step 2: Prep your hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair, and then apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips.

Step 4: Straighten your hair

Take one section of hair and clamp the straightener near the roots. Twist the straightener inwards and slide it down the length of your hair. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.

Step 5: Finish your look

Use a volumizing hairspray to set your style. You can also use a teasing comb to tease the roots of your hair to add even more volume.

Tips for Getting More Volume

Here are some additional tips for getting more volume in your hair:

  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume.
  • Use a volumizing dry shampoo to add texture to your hair.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume.
  • Use a volumizing powder at your roots to add lift.

FAQs

Can I use a straightener to curl my hair?

Yes, you can use a straightener to curl your hair. Simply clamp the straightener near the roots, twist the straightener inwards, and slide it down the length of your hair. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.

What is the best temperature to use with a straightener?

The best temperature to use with a straightener depends on your hair type. If you have fine or thin hair, use a lower temperature setting to prevent damage. If you have thick or coarse hair, use a higher temperature setting to get the best results.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a straightener or curling iron.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair to prevent damage.

By following these tips and using a straightener with a curved edge, you can achieve more volume in your hair. Remember to prep your hair, section it properly, and use a volumizing hairspray to finish your look. With a little practice, you can create a voluminous hairstyle that will turn heads.

How To Cut Hair With Scissors And Comb Beginners

Written by Petter Aug 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Cutting hair with scissors and a comb is a basic skill that every hairdresser should master. If you are a beginner, you might find it intimidating at first, but with the right tools and techniques, you can easily achieve a professional-looking haircut. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut hair with scissors and comb beginners, step by step.

How to Cut Hair with Scissors and Comb Beginners

Step 1: Preparing the Hair

The first step is to prepare the hair by washing and drying it thoroughly. Comb the hair to remove any tangles or knots. Then, section the hair using hair clips into four parts: the top, sides, and back.

Step 2: Choosing the Right Scissors and Comb

Choosing the right scissors and comb is crucial in achieving a good haircut. For beginners, it is recommended to use a basic pair of scissors and a wide-tooth comb. The scissors should be sharp and comfortable to hold, while the comb should have long and sturdy teeth.

Step 3: Trimming the Hair

Start trimming the hair from the back section, using the comb to hold the hair in place and the scissors to cut. Trim the hair slowly and carefully, making sure to cut only a small amount at a time. Repeat this process for each section.

Step 4: Blending the Hair

After trimming each section, use the comb to blend the hair by combing it back and forth. This will create a seamless transition between the different sections of the hair.

Step 5: Finishing the Haircut

Once you have trimmed and blended all sections, use scissors to create layers, if desired. Finish the haircut by trimming the bangs, if any, and checking for any uneven areas. Use a mirror to check the haircut from different angles.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Scissors and Comb Beginners

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Trim the hair slowly and carefully, cutting small amounts at a time.
  • Comb the hair before and after trimming to ensure evenness.
  • Use a mirror to check the haircut from different angles.
  • Practice on mannequin heads or willing friends and family members before cutting clients' hair.

FAQ

Can I cut hair with regular scissors?

Regular scissors are not recommended for cutting hair, as they are not sharp enough and may cause split ends. It is best to use a pair of scissors specifically designed for cutting hair.

What is the best way to hold the scissors?

Hold the scissors with your thumb and ring finger inside the handles and your index and middle fingers outside. This will give you more control and precision when cutting.

Hair Care after Cutting

After cutting the hair, it is important to care for it properly. Wash and condition the hair regularly, and use a leave-in conditioner to prevent split ends. Avoid using heat-styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair. Trim the hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the haircut's shape.

Cutting hair with scissors and comb beginners may seem daunting at first, but with practice and patience, you can master this skill. Remember to choose the right tools, take your time, and follow the steps carefully. Happy cutting!

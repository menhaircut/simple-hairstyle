Women s Short Haircut Styles A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Nov 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short haircuts have been a popular trend among women for many years. They are easy to maintain, require less styling time, and can be customized to suit your face shape and personality. In this article, we will discuss the different women's short haircut styles, how to achieve them, step-by-step guides, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned pro, there's something for everyone in this guide.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that has been popularized by celebrities such as Emma Watson and Halle Berry. This style is perfect for women who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks chic and stylish. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back while leaving longer layers on top. This will create a textured, edgy look that can be styled in various ways. The pixie cut is perfect for women with oval, heart or square-shaped faces.

How to Achieve the Pixie Cut

To achieve the pixie cut, you will need to follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  2. Dry your hair using a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair.
  3. Use a styling product such as a pomade or wax to style your hair in the desired style. You can add texture by using your fingers to separate the hair and create a messy look.

Tips for the Pixie Cut

  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Use a hairspray to hold your style in place.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your style.

FAQs about the Pixie Cut

  • Is the pixie cut suitable for all face shapes?
  • Can I style my pixie cut in different ways?
  • How often do I need to get a trim to maintain my pixie cut?

How to Care for the Pixie Cut

To care for your pixie cut, you will need to:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your style.

Bob Cut

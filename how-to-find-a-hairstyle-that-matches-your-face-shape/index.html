How To Find A Hairstyle That Matches Your Face Shape

Written by Daniel Nov 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle, it's important to take your face shape into consideration. The right hairstyle can enhance your best features and downplay any less desirable ones. In this article, we'll show you how to find a hairstyle that matches your face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle
  3. Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding a hairstyle that matches your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Here's how to determine which face shape you have:

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded jawline and forehead. The width of the forehead is slightly wider than the cheekbones and the jawline is gently curved.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide hairline. The width and length of the face are almost equal.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong, angular jawline and a square hairline. The width of the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost equal.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape is wider at the forehead and temples and narrows down to a point at the chin. The cheekbones may be high and prominent.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has a narrow hairline and chin, with wide cheekbones.

Oblong Face Shape

An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide, with a straight forehead and a long, narrow jawline.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle

Once you've determined your face shape, follow these steps to find your perfect hairstyle:

  1. Consider your hair texture and thickness. Some hairstyles work best with certain hair textures and thicknesses.
  2. Think about your personal style. Do you prefer a classic or trendy look?
  3. Look for inspiration. Browse magazines or online galleries for hairstyles that catch your eye.
  4. Talk to your stylist. Your stylist can help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and fits your lifestyle.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that enhances your best features.
  • Avoid hairstyles that emphasize any less desirable features.
  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, choose a low-maintenance hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment. Hair grows back, so if you don't like your new hairstyle, it's not permanent.

FAQs

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It's not uncommon for people to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, choose a hairstyle that flatters the most prominent features of your face.

What if I don't like my face shape?

Remember that there is no "perfect" face shape. Embrace your unique features and choose a hairstyle that enhances them.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found your perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the look. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools, like straighteners and curling irons, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and fits your personal style. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain the look and keep it healthy.

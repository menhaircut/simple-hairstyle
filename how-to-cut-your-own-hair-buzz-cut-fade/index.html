How To Cut Your Own Hair Buzz Cut Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Dec 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts every few weeks? With the right tools and techniques, you can easily give yourself a buzz cut fade at home. In this guide, we will walk you through the process of cutting your own hair, from choosing the right clippers to perfecting your fade.

Tools You Will Need

Before you start cutting your hair, you will need to gather the following tools:

  • Clippers with guards of different lengths
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair trimmer
  • Mirror
  • Cape or towel to cover your shoulders
  • Hair clips

Make sure all your tools are clean and in good working condition. If your clippers are dull or your scissors are rusty, it's time to invest in new ones.

Preparing Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. Comb it out to remove any tangles or knots. Then, use hair clips to section off the top and sides of your hair.

You can also use a mirror to check if you missed any areas.

Buzzing Your Hair

Attach the shortest guard to your clippers and start buzzing your hair from the bottom. Use an upward motion and go against the direction of hair growth. Work your way up to the sides and back of your head.

Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that were missed by the clippers.

Cutting the Top

Now it's time to cut the top of your hair. Remove the hair clips and use scissors to trim the hair to your desired length.

You can also use your clippers with a longer guard to trim the top, but be careful not to cut too much.

Fading the Sides

Attach a longer guard to your clippers and start fading the sides of your head. Start with the longest guard and work your way down to the shortest one.

Use an upward motion and fade the hair gradually. Make sure to create a smooth transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides.

Blending the Fade

Use your hair trimmer to blend the fade even more. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a flicking motion to create a seamless blend.

Check the mirror frequently to make sure the fade looks even on both sides.

Cleaning Up

Once you're finished cutting your hair, use a comb and scissors to trim any stray hairs that were missed.

Remove the hair clips and cape or towel and shake off any loose hairs.

Tips for a Perfect Buzz Cut Fade

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to the shortest one to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Take your time and check the mirror frequently to make sure the fade looks even on both sides.
  • Use a hair trimmer to create a seamless blend between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair without clippers?

You can use scissors, but it will take longer and may not give you the same precision as clippers.

What should I do if I make a mistake?

If you accidentally cut too much hair, you may need to shave your head or wait for it to grow back. If you're not happy with the results, you can always go to a professional stylist to fix it.

Hair Care After the Cut

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.

Trim your hair every few weeks to maintain your buzz cut fade. You can also experiment with different lengths and styles to keep things fresh.

With these simple steps, you can easily give yourself a buzz cut fade at home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll have a great-looking haircut in no time.

How To See What Hair Colour Suits Me A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking of changing your hair colour but don't know where to start? Choosing the right hair colour can be overwhelming, especially with so many options available. However, with a few simple steps, you can determine what hair colour suits you best. In this guide, we'll show you how to see what hair colour suits you in just a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour
  • Step 3: Determine Your Natural Hair Colour

  • Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining what hair colour suits you is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell if they are blue or green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Warm Skin Tones

If you have a warm skin tone, you will look best in warm hair colours like golden blondes, coppery reds, and rich browns.

Cool Skin Tones

If you have a cool skin tone, you will look best in cool hair colours like ash blondes, cool browns, and burgundy reds.

Neutral Skin Tones

If you have a neutral skin tone, you can wear both warm and cool hair colours.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also play a role in determining what hair colour suits you best. Generally, if you have blue or green eyes, cool hair colours will look best on you. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warm hair colours will look best on you.

Step 3: Determine Your Natural Hair Colour

When choosing a hair colour, it's important to consider your natural hair colour. If you have dark hair, it may be difficult to achieve a light blonde colour without damaging your hair. If you have light hair, you may be able to achieve a bold colour without as much damage.

Step 4: Choose the Right Shade

Once you have determined your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour, it's time to choose a shade. If you are unsure, it's best to start with a subtle change. For example, if you have dark brown hair, you may want to try a lighter brown colour before going blonde.

Step 5: Try a Virtual Makeover

If you're still unsure what hair colour suits you best, try a virtual makeover. Many websites and apps allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hair colours. This can give you a better idea of what you will look like with a certain hair colour.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you don't have a lot of time to maintain your hair, choose a low-maintenance colour.
  • Choose a colour that complements your wardrobe. If you wear a lot of warm colours, choose a warm hair colour.
  • Consult with a professional. A hair stylist can help you choose a colour that suits you and your lifestyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I touch up my hair colour?

This depends on the type of colour you choose and how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Will colouring my hair damage it?

Colouring your hair can cause damage, especially if you choose a drastic change. However, there are ways to minimize damage, such as using a deep conditioner and avoiding heat tools.

Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent fading.
  • Avoid heat tools as much as possible.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy.

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, but with these tips, you can determine what hair colour suits you best. Remember to consider your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour, and don't be afraid to consult with a professional. With the right hair colour, you can feel confident and beautiful.

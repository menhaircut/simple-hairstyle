Does Long Hair Suit Round Faces Male

Written by Daniel Nov 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you are a male with a round face, you might be wondering if long hair is a good look for you. Many people have different opinions on the matter, but the truth is that long hair can work well for men with round faces, as long as it is styled correctly. In this article, we will discuss how to make long hair work for you, whether you have a round face or not.

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Having a round face can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that suits you. However, long hair can be a great option if you know how to style it properly. Long hair can help to elongate your face and balance out your features, making your face look less round. It can also add volume and texture to your hair, which can make it look thicker and healthier.

How To

When considering a long hairstyle, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First of all, you should think about the shape of your face. Men with round faces should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of their head, as this can make their face look even rounder. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, which will help to elongate your face.

Another factor to consider is your hair type. If you have thick, curly hair, a longer style might work well for you. However, if you have thin or fine hair, a shorter style may be more suitable. You should also think about how much time and effort you are willing to put into styling your hair. Long hair requires more maintenance than shorter styles, so you should be prepared to invest time and energy into keeping it looking good.

Step by Step

If you decide to go for a long hairstyle, here are some steps you can follow to make it work for you:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height to the top of your head, such as a pompadour or quiff.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, such as a mullet or shag.
  • Consider getting layers cut into your hair to add texture and movement.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and use it to blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go.
  • Use a styling product, such as wax or pomade, to add hold and definition to your hair.
  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for trims and touch ups.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make long hair work for you:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Accessorize with hats, headbands, or bandanas to add interest to your look.
  • Be patient – growing out your hair takes time, so don't expect to see results overnight.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long hair and round faces:

Q: Can men with round faces pull off a ponytail?

A: Yes, men with round faces can wear a ponytail, but it's important to make sure that it is styled correctly. A low, loose ponytail can help to elongate your face, while a high, tight ponytail can make your face look even rounder.

Q: Is it better to part your hair on the side or in the middle?

A: For men with round faces, it is generally better to part your hair on the side, as this can help to create the illusion of a longer face. However, if you prefer a middle part, you can still make it work by adding height to the top of your head.

Hair Care

Long hair requires more maintenance than shorter styles, so it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and strong. Here are some tips for keeping your long hair in good condition:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair straighteners or curling irons, too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

In conclusion, long hair can work well for men with round faces, as long as it is styled correctly. By following the tips and guidelines outlined in this article, you can create a look that suits you and complements your features. Remember to be patient and experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.

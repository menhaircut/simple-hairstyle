Types of GHD Curlers

GHD offers four different types of curlers: the Classic Curl Tong, the Creative Curl Wand, the Soft Curl Tong, and the Curve Classic Wave Wand. Each of these curlers has its own unique features and is designed for different types of curls.

The Classic Curl Tong is a traditional curling iron that is perfect for creating tight, defined curls. The Creative Curl Wand, on the other hand, is a tapered wand that is ideal for creating loose, beachy waves. The Soft Curl Tong is designed to create voluminous, bouncy curls, while the Curve Classic Wave Wand is perfect for creating glamorous, Old Hollywood-style waves.

How to Use a GHD Curler

Using a GHD curler is relatively simple, although it can take some practice to get the perfect curl. To use a GHD curler, start by selecting the appropriate heat setting for your hair type. Then, section your hair and start curling from the bottom, working your way up to the top of your head. Hold each curl for a few seconds before releasing it, and use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using a GHD Curler

Here is a step-by-step guide to using a GHD curler:

  1. Start by selecting the appropriate heat setting for your hair type.
  2. Section your hair and clip up the top layers.
  3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curler, starting at the base.
  4. Hold the curl for a few seconds, then gently release it.
  5. Repeat this process on the rest of your hair, working your way up to the top of your head.
  6. Once all of your hair is curled, use your fingers to gently tousle the curls for a natural look.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Achieving Perfect Curls with a GHD Curler

Here are some tips for achieving perfect curls with a GHD curler:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage to your hair.
  • Don't hold the curler on your hair for too long, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Experiment with different types of curls to find the style that works best for you.
  • Use a comb or brush to gently comb through your curls for a more natural look.
  • Invest in a high-quality curling iron, like those offered by GHD, for the best results.

FAQ about GHD Curlers

How do I choose the right GHD curler for my hair?

The right GHD curler for your hair depends on the type of curls you want to achieve. The Classic Curl Tong is best for tight, defined curls, while the Creative Curl Wand is ideal for loose, beachy waves. The Soft Curl Tong is designed to create voluminous, bouncy curls, while the Curve Classic Wave Wand is perfect for creating glamorous, Old Hollywood-style waves.

Can I use a GHD curler on wet hair?

No, it's not recommended to use a GHD curler on wet hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a curling iron to avoid damage and breakage.

How do I clean my GHD curler?

Wait for your GHD curler to cool down completely before cleaning. Use a soft, damp cloth to wipe down the barrel and plates, and avoid using any abrasive cleaners or tools that could scratch the surface.

Hair Care Tips when Using a GHD Curler

Using a GHD curler can be a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. However, it's important to take care of your hair to minimize damage and breakage. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when using a GHD curler:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage to your hair.
  • Wait for your hair to cool down before brushing or combing through it.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using a curling iron on wet hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these hair care tips and choosing the right GHD curler for your needs, you can achieve beautiful, salon-worthy curls at home. Whether you prefer tight, defined curls or loose, beachy waves, GHD has a curler that can help you achieve the look you want.

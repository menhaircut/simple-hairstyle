Description

Oval face shape is the most versatile and balanced face shape for men. If you have an oval face shape, you can try out a wide range of hairstyles that complement your face shape. In this guide, we will explore the best hairstyles for oval face shape men, how to determine your face shape, step-by-step styling guide, tips for maintaining your hairstyle, FAQs, and hair care tips.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Note down the measurements.
  4. Determine the shape of your face based on the following:
  • Oval: Forehead and jawline are about the same width, with a slightly rounded chin.
  • Round: Forehead and jawline are about the same width, with a rounded chin.
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are about the same width, with a square chin.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones and jawline, with a pointed chin.
  • Diamond: Cheekbones are wider than the forehead and jawline, with a pointed chin.
  • Triangle: Jawline is wider than the forehead and cheekbones, with a pointed chin.

Hairstyles for Oval Face Shape Men

Men with oval face shape can try out the following hairstyles:

Slicked Back

The slicked-back hairstyle is a classic and timeless hairstyle that looks great on men with oval face shape. To achieve this hairstyle, you need to apply some pomade or wax to your hair and comb it back.

Side Part

The side part is a simple and elegant hairstyle that works well for all occasions. To achieve this hairstyle, you need to create a deep side part and comb your hair to the side.

Crew Cut

The crew cut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that looks great on men with oval face shape. To achieve this hairstyle, you need to cut your hair short on the sides and back and leave it slightly longer on top.

Taper Fade

The taper fade is a modern and stylish hairstyle that looks great on men with oval face shape. To achieve this hairstyle, you need to fade your hair on the sides and back and leave it longer on top.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair (pomade, wax, gel, etc.).
  4. Comb your hair to the desired style.
  5. Blow dry your hair to set the style (optional).
  6. Apply a finishing product (hairspray, matte paste, etc.) to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a finishing product to hold your hairstyle in place and prevent frizz.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

What is the most flattering hairstyle for oval face shape men?

The most flattering hairstyle for oval face shape men is the one that complements your face shape and personality.

Can oval face shape men have long hair?

Yes, oval face shape men can have long hair, but it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape.

What kind of styling products should I use for my hair?

You should use a styling product that suits your hair type and the desired hairstyle. Pomade, wax, gel, and mousse are some of the popular styling products for men.

Hair Care for Men with Oval Face Shape

Here are some hair care tips for men with oval face shape:

  • Avoid using hot tools (hair straighteners, curling irons, etc.) too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Get a scalp massage to improve blood circulation and promote hair growth.

Hope this guide helps you find the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape. Remember, confidence is key, so wear your hairstyle with pride!

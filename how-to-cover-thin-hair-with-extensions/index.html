How To Cover Thin Hair With Extensions

Written by Robby Aug 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have thin hair and are looking for a way to add volume and length, hair extensions are a great option. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to cover thin hair with extensions. We will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and answers to frequently asked questions, as well as hair care advice to ensure your extensions stay looking their best.

How to Cover Thin Hair with Extensions

Before you begin, it is essential to choose the right type of extensions for your hair. There are many different types of extensions available, including clip-ins, tape-ins, sew-ins, and micro-link. Clip-ins are the best choice for covering thin hair as they are easy to install and remove and do not damage your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cover thin hair with extensions:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair to make sure it is clean and free of any product buildup.
  2. Blow-dry your hair and part it in the middle.
  3. Take a small section of hair at the back of your head, just above the nape of your neck, and clip in the first extension.
  4. Repeat the process, working your way up to the crown of your head, leaving a gap of about 1 inch between each extension.
  5. Once you reach the crown of your head, clip in the final extension.
  6. Using a brush, blend your natural hair with the extensions to create a seamless look.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when covering thin hair with extensions:

  • Choose extensions that match your natural hair color and texture.
  • Clip in the extensions close to the scalp for a more natural look.
  • Use a hair straightener or curling iron to style your hair and blend it with the extensions.
  • Do not sleep with your extensions in as this can cause tangling and damage to your natural hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about covering thin hair with extensions:

Do hair extensions damage your natural hair?

No, if you choose the right type of extensions and take care of them properly, they should not damage your natural hair.

How long do hair extensions last?

The lifespan of hair extensions depends on the type of extensions and how well you take care of them. Clip-in extensions can last up to a year with proper care.

Can you swim with hair extensions?

It is best to avoid swimming with hair extensions as chlorine and saltwater can damage them. If you do swim, make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly with fresh water afterwards.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to ensure your extensions stay looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Brush your hair and extensions regularly with a soft-bristle brush to prevent tangling.
  • Do not use hot tools on the extensions as this can damage them.
  • Wash your hair and extensions with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products as they can weigh down the extensions.

With these tips, you can easily cover thin hair with extensions and achieve a fuller, more voluminous look. Remember to choose the right type of extensions and take care of them properly to ensure they last as long as possible.

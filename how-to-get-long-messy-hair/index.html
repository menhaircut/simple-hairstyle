How To Get Long Messy Hair A Guide For Effortlessly Cool Locks

Written by Oliver Jul 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a laid-back and low-maintenance hairstyle that's still chic and stylish, messy long hair is the perfect choice. This look is all about texture, volume, and a carefree attitude. Whether you have naturally wavy hair or straight locks, you can achieve a long messy hairstyle with a few simple tricks and products. In this article, we'll show you how to get long messy hair step by step, share some tips and tricks, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Prep Your Hair for Messy Styling

Before you start styling your long messy hair, you need to prepare it properly. First, wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create a good base for your hairstyle. Then, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools. You can also apply a texturizing spray or mousse to add some grip and hold to your hair.

How to Create Messy Waves for Long Hair

Messy waves are a key element of the long messy hairstyle. To create this look, start by sectioning your hair into two parts: top and bottom. Then, use a curling iron or a wand to create loose waves in each section. Don't worry about making the waves too perfect or uniform - the messier, the better. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural, tousled look.

How to Add Volume to Long Messy Hair

Volume is another important aspect of the long messy hairstyle. To add volume to your hair, start by blow-drying your roots upside down. This will create a lift at the roots and give your hair more body. You can also use a volumizing powder or spray to add even more volume and texture to your hair.

How to Finish Your Long Messy Hairstyle

To finish your long messy hairstyle, you can use a few different products depending on your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, a dry shampoo or a texturizing spray can help add volume and hold. If you have thick or curly hair, a serum or oil can help tame frizz and add shine. You can also use a hairspray to hold your style in place and prevent flyaways.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Long Messy Hair

Long messy hair is a low-maintenance hairstyle, but that doesn't mean you can neglect your hair care routine. Here are some tips and tricks for keeping your long messy hair looking its best:

  • Don't wash your hair too often - this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look flat and lifeless.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to brush your hair gently and avoid tangles.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to prevent tangling and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep split ends at bay and promote healthy growth.

Frequently Asked Questions about Long Messy Hair

Here are some answers to common questions about long messy hair:

Q: Can I get long messy hair if I have short hair?

A: If you have short hair, you can still achieve a messy look with the help of hair extensions or a wig.

Q: Can I wear long messy hair to a formal event?

A: Long messy hair can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. To make it more formal, you can add some accessories like a hairpin or a headband, or style it into an updo.

Q: Is long messy hair suitable for all hair types?

A: Long messy hair can work for most hair types, but it may require different products and techniques depending on your hair's texture and thickness.

Hair Care Tips for Long Messy Hair

To keep your long messy hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips for long messy hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out and becoming brittle.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect long messy hair look that's effortless yet stylish. So embrace your natural texture, let your hair down, and rock that messy look with confidence!

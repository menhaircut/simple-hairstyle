How To Hold Hair In Humidity Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Oct 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Humidity can wreak havoc on your hair, leaving it frizzy, unmanageable and just plain unruly. If you're struggling to keep your hair looking sleek and smooth in humid weather, you're not alone. Fortunately, there are plenty of tips and tricks you can use to hold hair in humidity and keep it looking great no matter what the weather throws your way.

Description

Humidity can be a real nightmare for anyone with hair, particularly if you have long, thick or curly locks. The moisture in the air can cause your hair to swell, frizz and lose its shape, leaving you with a bad hair day that seems to last forever. But with the right techniques and products, you can learn how to hold hair in humidity and keep it looking fabulous, no matter what the weather has in store.

How to Hold Hair in Humidity

The key to holding hair in humidity is all about preparation. You need to make sure your hair is properly hydrated and nourished before you even step outside. This means using the right products, avoiding excessive heat styling and protecting your hair from the elements. Here are some tips to help you get started:

Step 1: Use Moisturizing Products

One of the best ways to keep your hair looking smooth and frizz-free in humid weather is to use moisturizing products. Look for shampoos and conditioners that contain natural oils, like coconut or argan oil, as these will help to hydrate and nourish your hair, leaving it more resilient to humidity. You can also use leave-in conditioners or hair masks to give your hair an extra boost of moisture.

Step 2: Avoid Heat Styling

If you're prone to using heat styling tools like hair dryers, straighteners or curling irons, you might want to cut back during humid weather. Heat styling can dry out your hair, making it more susceptible to frizz and flyaways. Instead, opt for air-drying your hair or using low heat settings on your styling tools.

Step 3: Protect Your Hair

When you're out and about in humid weather, it's important to protect your hair from the elements. Wear a hat or scarf to shield your hair from the sun, wind and rain, and avoid touching your hair too much as this can lead to frizz. You can also use products like hair serums or oils to create a barrier against humidity.

Tips for Holding Hair in Humidity

Now that you know the basics of how to hold hair in humidity, here are some additional tips to help you keep your hair looking fabulous no matter what the weather:

Tip 1: Use a Humidity-Blocking Hairspray

Hairspray can be your best friend when it comes to holding hair in humidity. Look for a hairspray that specifically blocks out moisture, as this will help to keep your hair looking smooth and sleek. Apply the hairspray to your hair before you head outside, and touch up throughout the day as needed.

Tip 2: Try a Keratin Treatment

Keratin treatments are a great way to help tame frizz and make your hair more resistant to humidity. These treatments infuse your hair with keratin, a protein that helps to strengthen and smooth the hair shaft. Talk to your hair stylist about getting a keratin treatment, or try an at-home version for a quick fix.

Tip 3: Embrace Braids and Updos

If all else fails, embrace braids and updos as your go-to hairstyles for humid weather. Braids and updos can help to keep your hair off your face and neck, reducing the chances of frizz and flyaways. Plus, they can be an easy and stylish way to switch up your look.

FAQs

Q: Can I still use heat styling tools in humid weather?

A: Yes, you can still use heat styling tools in humid weather, but it's important to use them sparingly and on low heat settings to avoid drying out your hair.

Q: What's the best way to protect my hair from humidity?

A: The best way to protect your hair from humidity is to use moisturizing products, avoid excessive heat styling, and wear a hat or scarf to shield your hair from the elements.

Q: How often should I touch up my hairspray during humid weather?

A: It depends on the intensity of the humidity and the thickness of your hair. As a general rule, you should touch up your hairspray every few hours to maintain its hold.

Hair Care for Humid Weather

Humid weather can be tough on your hair, but with the right care and attention, you can keep your locks looking fabulous no matter what the weather is like outside. Remember to use moisturizing products, avoid excessive heat styling, and protect your hair from the elements. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to hold hair in humidity and enjoy sleek, smooth and frizz-free locks all year round.

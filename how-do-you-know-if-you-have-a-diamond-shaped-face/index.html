How Do You Know If You Have A Diamond Shaped Face

Written by Petter Jul 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you ever wonder what your face shape is? Knowing your face shape can help you determine what hairstyles, makeup, and accessories would best suit you. One of the face shapes that people often overlook is the diamond-shaped face. In this article, we will discuss how to identify a diamond shaped face and what hairstyles and makeup work best for this face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Step by Step Guide

Description

A diamond-shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead and jawline, with the widest part of the face being the cheekbones. This face shape is often considered to be the rarest of all the face shapes. It is also known as the "inverted triangle" shape because of its wider cheekbones and narrower forehead and jawline.

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you may also have a pointed chin and a high forehead. This face shape is often associated with a more angular and sharp appearance, but it can also be softened with the right hairstyles and makeup techniques.

How To Identify a Diamond Shaped Face

Identifying your face shape can be done in a few simple steps:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Use a tape measure or ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Write down these measurements.

Step 2: Analyze Your Measurements

Compare your measurements to each other. If your cheekbone measurement is the widest, you most likely have a diamond-shaped face.

Step 3: Look at Your Facial Features

Take a look at your facial features. If you have a pointed chin and a high forehead, this could indicate a diamond-shaped face.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you identify if you have a diamond-shaped face:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Use a tape measure or ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Write down these measurements.

Step 2: Calculate Your Facial Ratios

Divide the width of your cheekbones by the width of your forehead. If the result is greater than 1.5, you most likely have a diamond-shaped face.

Divide the length of your face (from your hairline to your chin) by the width of your face at the widest point (usually the cheekbones). If the result is greater than 1.5, you most likely have a diamond-shaped face.

Tips for Diamond Shaped Faces

Here are some tips for enhancing your diamond-shaped face:

  • Try to soften your angular features with hairstyles and makeup that add volume to the forehead and jawline.
  • Choose hairstyles that add width to the forehead and jawline, such as side-swept bangs, layers, and curls.
  • Contour your face with makeup to create a more oval appearance.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the cheekbones, as this can make your face appear wider.

FAQs

Q: Can a diamond-shaped face wear short hairstyles?

A: Yes, diamond-shaped faces can wear short hairstyles, but it's important to choose a style that doesn't add volume to the cheekbones.

Q: Can a diamond-shaped face wear a center part?

A: Yes, diamond-shaped faces can wear a center part, but it's important to add volume to the forehead and jawline to balance out the wider cheekbones.

Hair Care and Styling for Diamond Shaped Faces

When it comes to hair care and styling for diamond-shaped faces, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the cheekbones.
  • Choose hairstyles that add width to the forehead and jawline, such as side-swept bangs, layers, and curls.
  • Use volumizing products to add volume to the forehead and jawline.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can elongate the face even more.

With the right hairstyles and makeup techniques, you can enhance your diamond-shaped face and create a more balanced and flattering appearance. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find the ones that work best for you.

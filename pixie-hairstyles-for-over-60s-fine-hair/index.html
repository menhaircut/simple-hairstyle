Pixie Hairstyles For Over 60S Fine Hair Embrace Your Age With Style

Written by Bardi Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 60 and searching for a new hairstyle to suit your fine hair? Look no further than the pixie cut! This short and sassy hairstyle is perfect for mature women who want to embrace their age with style. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the perfect pixie cut for your hair type, face shape, and personal style. We'll also provide step-by-step instructions for achieving the look, as well as tips for maintaining it. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Perfect Pixie Cut for Your Hair Type

Before making the chop, it's important to consider your hair type. Fine hair is typically delicate and lacks volume, so you'll want to choose a style that adds texture and body. Here are some pixie cuts that work well for fine hair:

The Classic Pixie

The classic pixie cut is a timeless look that works well for all ages. It's short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. This style adds volume and texture to fine hair, making it appear thicker and fuller.

The Layered Pixie

The layered pixie cut is another great option for fine hair. It features choppy layers throughout the hair, adding movement and volume. This style is perfect for those who want a more modern and edgy look.

The Side-Swept Pixie

The side-swept pixie cut is a softer and more feminine style than the classic or layered pixie. It features longer layers on one side of the head, swept across the forehead. This style adds volume to fine hair and frames the face beautifully.

How to Achieve the Perfect Pixie Cut

If you've decided on a pixie cut, it's important to find a stylist who specializes in short haircuts. You'll want to bring in pictures of the style you want, as well as any concerns or questions you may have. Here are step-by-step instructions for achieving the perfect pixie cut:

Step 1: Shampoo and Dry Hair

Start by shampooing and drying your hair. It's important to have clean, dry hair before cutting.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Next, section the hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. Use clips or hair ties to keep each section in place.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Using scissors or clippers, cut the sides and back of the hair to the desired length. This will vary depending on the style you've chosen.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Next, cut the top of the hair to the desired length. Use thinning shears to add texture and volume to fine hair.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Finally, style the hair using a styling product, such as wax or pomade. Use your fingers to create texture and volume.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Once you've achieved the perfect pixie cut, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, as these can damage fine hair

FAQ: Pixie Hairstyles for Over 60s Fine Hair

Q: Will a pixie cut make my fine hair look thinner?

A: No, a pixie cut can actually make fine hair appear thicker and fuller by adding texture and volume.

Q: Is a pixie cut easy to maintain?

A: Yes, a pixie cut is easy to maintain with regular trims and the right hair care routine.

Q: Can I still style my pixie cut for special occasions?

A: Yes, there are many ways to style a pixie cut for special occasions, such as adding accessories, braids, or curls.

Hair Care: Embrace Your Age with Style

Choosing a new hairstyle can be daunting, but with the right guidance, you can find the perfect look to suit your hair type, face shape, and personal style. A pixie cut is a great option for mature women with fine hair who want to embrace their age with style. By following these tips and maintaining a healthy hair care routine, you can keep your pixie cut looking great for years to come. So why not take the plunge and try a pixie cut today?

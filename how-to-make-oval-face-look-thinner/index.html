How To Make Oval Face Look Thinner Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jun 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having a round and chubby face? Do you want to make your oval face look thinner and more defined? If the answer is yes, then you have come to the right place! In this article, we will share with you some tips and tricks on how to make your face look slimmer and more angular.

Description

An oval face is often considered to be the perfect face shape as it is balanced and symmetrical. However, if you have a rounder face, it can be challenging to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Fortunately, there are several ways to make your oval face appear thinner, including makeup, hairstyles, and facial exercises.

How to Make Oval Face Look Thinner

Step 1: Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades to create shadows and depth on the face. By creating the illusion of shadows, you can make your face appear slimmer and more defined. To contour your face, use a matte bronzer or a contouring powder and apply it to the areas that you want to appear slimmer, such as your temples, jawline, and cheekbones.

Step 2: Hairstyles

The right hairstyle can also make a significant difference in the appearance of your face. A layered haircut with soft waves or curls can add volume and movement to your hair, creating the illusion of a slimmer face. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a blunt bob or a straight fringe.

Step 3: Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help to tone and shape your face, creating a more defined and angular look. Try exercises such as the fish face, cheek lifts, and jaw releases to target the muscles in your face and neck.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your oval face look thinner:

  • Avoid heavy or thick bangs that cover your forehead.
  • Use a highlighter on the high points of your face to create a more defined look.
  • Wear glasses with a thick frame to add angles to your face.
  • Avoid wearing your hair in a high ponytail or bun as it can make your face look rounder.
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to keep your skin looking fresh and radiant.

FAQ

1. How long does it take to see results from facial exercises?

It can take several weeks or months to see the results of facial exercises. However, with consistent practice, you can expect to see a more toned and defined face.

2. Can contouring make my face look too thin?

Contouring is a makeup technique that can create the illusion of shadows and depth on the face. However, it is essential to use a light hand and blend the product well to avoid looking too harsh or unnatural.

Hair Care

Along with the tips mentioned above, taking care of your hair is also essential to make your face look slimmer. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage your hair and make it look flat.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and smoothness to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your oval face look thinner and more defined. Remember to be patient and consistent as it may take some time to see the results. With a little effort and dedication, you can achieve the desired look and feel confident in your skin.

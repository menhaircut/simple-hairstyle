Short Bob Hairstyles For Thin Hair Over 60 A Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over the age of 60 and have thin hair, finding a hairstyle that suits you can be a challenge. Short bob hairstyles are a great option for those with thin hair as they add volume and texture. In this guide, we will discuss the best short bob hairstyles for thin hair over 60, how to achieve them, and tips on maintaining them.

Description

A short bob hairstyle is a haircut that features hair that is cut straight around the head at jaw-level or above. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways such as straight, curly, or with bangs. Short bob hairstyles for thin hair over 60 are designed to add volume and texture to thin hair, making it appear fuller and more youthful.

How to Achieve Short Bob Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 60

Before achieving a short bob hairstyle, it is important to consult with a hairstylist to determine which style would best suit your face shape and hair type. Once you have decided on a style, follow these steps to achieve your new look:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for thin hair. This will help to add volume and thickness to your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to pat your hair dry until it is damp. Avoid rubbing your hair as this can cause breakage and damage.

Step 3: Apply a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to add volume and lift to your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Using a flat iron or curling iron, style your hair as desired. Short bob hairstyles can be worn straight or curled, depending on your preference.

Tips for Maintaining Short Bob Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 60

Short bob hairstyles require maintenance in order to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short bob hairstyle:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add thickness and texture to your hair
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or color treatments
  • Keep your hair hydrated by drinking plenty of water and using a leave-in conditioner

FAQ

What is the best short bob hairstyle for thin hair over 60?

The best short bob hairstyle for thin hair over 60 is one that adds volume and texture to your hair. A layered bob with choppy ends is a great option for those with thin hair.

Can I wear my short bob hairstyle straight or curly?

Yes, you can wear your short bob hairstyle straight or curly. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways.

How often should I visit my hairstylist for trims and touch-ups?

You should visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for trims and touch-ups. This will help to keep your short bob hairstyle looking its best.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair in order to maintain a healthy and youthful appearance. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with products that are designed for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as this can cause damage
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and nutrients that promote healthy hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated

With these tips, you can achieve a short bob hairstyle that is perfect for thin hair over 60. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to determine which style would best suit your face shape and hair type, and to maintain your new hairstyle with regular trims and touch-ups.

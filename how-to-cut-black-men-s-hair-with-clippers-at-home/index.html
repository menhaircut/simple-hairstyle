How To Cut Black Men s Hair With Clippers At Home

Written by Petter Jul 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a black man looking to save some money and cut your hair at home, using clippers can be an excellent option. Cutting your hair with clippers can be quick, easy, and give you a fresh look. In this article, we'll show you how to cut black men's hair with clippers at home, step by step. We'll also provide you with some tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice.

Step 1: Gather the Right Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the right tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Clipper guards in different sizes
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hand mirror
  • Cape or towel to keep hair off your clothes

Make sure your clippers are clean, oiled, and sharp. Dull clippers can pull your hair and make the cut uneven.

Step 2: Choose Your Clipper Guard

Clipper guards come in different sizes, from 1/16 inch to 1 inch. The larger the guard, the longer the hair will be. If you're not sure which guard to use, start with a larger guard and work your way down. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's cut.

Step 3: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut, and it can also cause the clippers to rust.

Step 4: Start Cutting Your Hair

Start with the largest guard and work your way down. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it with the clippers. Make sure you're cutting against the direction of hair growth for a closer cut. Use the scissors to trim the hair around the ears and the back of the neck.

Step 5: Clean Up and Style

Use the hand mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the cut is even. Use the smaller guards to trim around the edges and create a fade. You can also use the scissors to create a textured look. Once you're done, clean up any loose hairs and style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Black Men's Hair with Clippers at Home

  • Start with a larger guard and work your way down.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it with the clippers.
  • Cut against the direction of hair growth for a closer cut.
  • Use the scissors to trim the hair around the ears and the back of the neck.
  • Check the back of your head with a hand mirror to make sure the cut is even.
  • Use the smaller guards to trim around the edges and create a fade.
  • Use the scissors to create a textured look.
  • Clean your clippers after each use and oil them regularly.
  • Don't cut your hair too short, as this can cause razor bumps and ingrown hairs.
  • Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want it to be. For most people, cutting their hair every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Q: How do I prevent razor bumps?

A: Use a clean, sharp razor and shave with the grain of the hair. Apply a moisturizing lotion or aftershave to soothe the skin.

Q: How can I style my hair after I cut it?

A: Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you. You can try using pomade, gel, or wax to create a slicked-back look or a textured look.

Hair Care Tips for Black Men

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and blow dryers, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like relaxers and dyes, as they can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can cut your hair with clippers at home and achieve a fresh, clean look. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

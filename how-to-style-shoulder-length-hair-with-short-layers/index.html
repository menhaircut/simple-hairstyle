How To Style Shoulder Length Hair With Short Layers

Written by William Aug 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will give you a fresh and trendy look? If you have shoulder length hair with short layers, you have many styling options available to you. In this article, we will show you how to style your shoulder length hair with short layers in a relaxed and effortless way. From messy waves to sleek updos, we have got you covered. Keep reading to find out more!

Description

Shoulder length hair with short layers is a popular hairstyle that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Short layers add texture and volume to your hair, making it look fuller and more youthful. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to achieve a casual yet stylish look without spending too much time or effort on their hair. With the right styling tips and techniques, you can create a variety of hairstyles that will suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair with Short Layers

Here are some easy and stylish ways to style your shoulder length hair with short layers:

1. Messy Waves

To achieve this look, start by applying a texturizing spray or mousse to your damp hair. Use a curling iron to create loose waves, and then tousle your hair with your fingers to create a messy, beachy look. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the waves in place. This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out or a beach vacation.

2. Sleek Ponytail

If you want a more polished look, try a sleek ponytail. Start by straightening your hair with a flat iron or blow dryer. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head, and secure it with a hair tie. Use a toothbrush or a comb to smooth down any flyaways or baby hairs. This hairstyle is perfect for a formal event or a day at the office.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

This hairstyle is a combination of a ponytail and loose hair. Start by gathering the top half of your hair into a ponytail, and secure it with a hair tie. Leave the bottom half of your hair loose, and add some loose waves using a curling iron. This hairstyle is perfect for a romantic date night or a casual brunch with friends.

4. Braided Updo

If you want to add some texture and dimension to your short layers, try a braided updo. Start by creating a loose braid on one side of your hair, and secure it with a hair tie. Use bobby pins to pin the braid to the back of your head, and then create a messy bun with the rest of your hair. This hairstyle is perfect for a wedding or a formal event.

Step by Step

Here are the step by step instructions for each hairstyle:

Messy Waves

  1. Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to your damp hair
  2. Use a curling iron to create loose waves
  3. Tousle your hair with your fingers to create a messy, beachy look
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the waves in place

Sleek Ponytail

  1. Straighten your hair with a flat iron or blow dryer
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head
  3. Secure it with a hair tie
  4. Use a toothbrush or a comb to smooth down any flyaways or baby hairs

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail
  2. Secure it with a hair tie
  3. Leave the bottom half of your hair loose
  4. Add some loose waves using a curling iron

Braided Updo

  1. Create a loose braid on one side of your hair
  2. Secure it with a hair tie
  3. Use bobby pins to pin the braid to the back of your head
  4. Create a messy bun with the rest of your hair

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your shoulder length hair with short layers:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools
  • Experiment with different hair accessories such as headbands, hair clips, and hair ties
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair
  • Try different parting styles to create a new look

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling shoulder length hair with short layers:

1. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash your hair once or twice a week. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. How can I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using a volumizing spray or mousse, backcombing your hair at the roots, or blow-drying your hair upside down. You can also try a layered haircut or a textured hairstyle to add volume and movement to your hair.

3. How can I prevent frizz?

You can prevent frizz by using a smoothing serum or oil, avoiding over-styling your hair with heat tools, and using a diffuser when blow-drying your hair. You can also try using a satin pillowcase and avoiding rubbing your hair with a towel when drying it.

Hair Care

Lastly, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for maintaining your shoulder length hair with short layers:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in protein and vitamins to promote healthy hair growth

Now that you know how to style your shoulder length hair with short layers, it's time to try out some new hairstyles. Whether you prefer a messy, beachy look or a sleek and polished style, there are many options available to you. With a little bit of practice and patience, you can create a hairstyle that will make you feel confident and stylish.

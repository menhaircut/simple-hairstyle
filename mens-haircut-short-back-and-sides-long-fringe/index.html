Mens Haircut Short Back And Sides Long Fringe

Written by Bardi Oct 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut that's easy to maintain, then the mens haircut short back and sides long fringe is the perfect choice for you. This haircut has become increasingly popular in recent years and is perfect for all hair types and face shapes. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this trendy haircut, including how to get it, hair care tips, and FAQs.

Description

The mens haircut short back and sides long fringe is a style that features short hair on the back and sides of the head, with a longer fringe at the front. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it a popular choice for men of all ages. It is also known as the "undercut" or "modern quiff" and is often paired with a beard or stubble to create a rugged and masculine look.

How To

If you're interested in getting a mens haircut short back and sides long fringe, the first step is to find a skilled barber who can create the look you want. It's important to bring a picture of the style you're after to ensure you and your barber are on the same page.

During the haircut, the barber will use clippers to create a short back and sides, leaving the hair on top longer. They will then trim and shape the fringe to your desired length and style. Once the haircut is complete, the barber will style the hair with a product of your choice, such as pomade or wax.

Step by Step

1. Find a skilled barber who can create the mens haircut short back and sides long fringe.

2. Bring a picture of the style you want to ensure you and your barber are on the same page.

3. During the haircut, the barber will use clippers to create a short back and sides and leave the hair on top longer.

4. The barber will trim and shape the fringe to your desired length and style.

5. Once the haircut is complete, the barber will style the hair with a product of your choice.

Tips

1. Use a quality hair product to maintain the style and hold the fringe in place.

2. Visit your barber regularly to keep the haircut looking fresh and sharp.

3. Experiment with different styles and lengths to find the one that works best for you.

4. Consider pairing the mens haircut short back and sides long fringe with a beard or stubble to create a rugged and masculine look.

FAQ

Q: Will this haircut suit me?

A: The mens haircut short back and sides long fringe is a versatile style that works for all hair types and face shapes. However, it's important to consult with your barber to find the best length and style for your specific features.

Q: How often do I need to visit the barber to maintain this haircut?

A: It's recommended to visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.

Q: Can I style this haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, the mens haircut short back and sides long fringe is a versatile style that can be styled in many different ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured.

Hair Care

To maintain the mens haircut short back and sides long fringe, it's important to use a quality hair product that provides hold and texture. It's also recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and hydrated. Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage the hair and prevent it from holding its shape.

Overall, the mens haircut short back and sides long fringe is a stylish and modern choice that's perfect for men of all ages. With the right care and maintenance, this haircut can keep you looking sharp and stylish for weeks to come.

How To Wear A Beanie Correctly Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Nov 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Beanies have become a popular fashion accessory in recent years. They’re not only stylish, but they also keep you warm during the colder months. However, not everyone knows how to wear a beanie correctly. This can lead to an awkward and uncomfortable look. In this article, we’ll cover how to wear a beanie correctly, step by step, so you can rock this accessory with confidence.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Beanie
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Beanie
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Wear a Beanie

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let’s first discuss the basics of wearing a beanie:

  • Choose the right beanie: The first step to wearing a beanie correctly is to choose the right one. Make sure it fits your head snugly without being too tight. Look for a beanie made of soft, comfortable material such as wool or cotton.
  • Consider your face shape: Beanies come in different styles and shapes. Consider your face shape when choosing a beanie. For example, if you have a round face, go for a beanie with a slouchy top to elongate your face.
  • Think about your outfit: When wearing a beanie, think about your outfit as a whole. Consider the colors and patterns you’re wearing and choose a beanie that complements them.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, let’s dive into the step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by brushing your hair back: Before putting on a beanie, brush your hair back to avoid any awkward bumps or lumps.
  2. Place the beanie on your head: Place the beanie on your head, making sure it’s pulled down snugly but not too tight.
  3. Adjust the beanie: If your beanie has a slouchy top, adjust it to your liking. You can push it back for a more relaxed look or pull it down for a more snug fit.
  4. Style your hair: If you have longer hair, you can style it in a messy bun or low ponytail to avoid any awkward bumps under the beanie. If you have shorter hair, simply tuck it behind your ears.

Tips for Wearing a Beanie

Here are some additional tips for wearing a beanie:

  • Experiment with different styles: Beanies come in different styles, including slouchy, cuffed, and pom-pom. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Accessorize: Beanies look great with other accessories such as scarves and gloves. Consider adding these to your outfit to complete the look.
  • Wear with confidence: The most important tip for wearing a beanie is to wear it with confidence. If you feel good in it, others will notice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you wear a beanie with short hair?

Absolutely! If you have short hair, simply tuck it behind your ears before putting on the beanie.

How do you wear a beanie without messing up your hair?

Brush your hair back before putting on the beanie to avoid any awkward bumps or lumps. If you have longer hair, style it in a messy bun or low ponytail.

Can you wear a beanie with glasses?

Yes, you can! Just make sure the beanie isn’t too tight around your head, as this can cause discomfort when wearing glasses.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing a beanie can cause your hair to become static and flat. Here are some tips to avoid this:

  • Use a volumizing spray: Before putting on the beanie, use a volumizing spray to add some lift to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair: If you have time, blow-dry your hair before putting on the beanie. This will help prevent flatness.
  • Use dry shampoo: If your hair becomes oily or flat after wearing a beanie, use dry shampoo to add some texture and lift.

Now that you know how to wear a beanie correctly, it’s time to rock this accessory with confidence. Remember to choose the right beanie, consider your face shape and outfit, and wear it with confidence. Happy styling!

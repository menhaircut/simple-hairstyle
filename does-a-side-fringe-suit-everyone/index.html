Does A Side Fringe Suit Everyone

Written by Aprilia Aug 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking of getting a side fringe but wondering if it would suit you? Well, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll be answering the age-old question - does a side fringe suit everyone? We’ll be giving you tips on how to choose the right side fringe for your face shape, how to maintain it and even some frequently asked questions about side fringes. So, let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Side Fringe for Your Face Shape

When it comes to choosing the right side fringe, it’s all about your face shape. Here are some tips to help you:

Round Face

If you have a round face, go for a side fringe that is longer and more tapered towards the ends. This will help elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

Square Face

For those with a square face, opt for a side fringe that is longer and slightly curved. This will soften your face and make it look more feminine.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, a side fringe that is shorter and more blunt will suit you best. This will help balance out your face and draw attention to your eyes.

Oval Face

Lucky you! Those with an oval face can pretty much pull off any side fringe style. So, feel free to experiment with different lengths and textures.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Cut a Side Fringe

If you’re feeling brave and want to cut your own side fringe, here’s a step-by-step guide:

Step 1:

Wash and blow-dry your hair as you normally would. Make sure it’s completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2:

Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape. The point of the triangle should be where you want your fringe to start and the base of the triangle should be at the top of your head.

Step 3:

Comb the hair straight down and hold it between your index and middle finger. Make sure it’s taut but not too tight.

Step 4:

Using a sharp pair of scissors, cut across the hair in a straight line. For a more textured look, you can point-cut into the hair.

Step 5:

Repeat on the other side, making sure both sides are even.

Tips for Maintaining Your Side Fringe

Now that you have your side fringe, here are some tips to help you maintain it:

- Trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh. - Use a round brush to blow-dry your fringe in the direction you want it to sit. - If you have naturally curly hair, use a straightener to smooth out your fringe. - Use a small amount of hair oil to prevent frizz and add shine.

FAQ About Side Fringes

1. How long should my side fringe be?

It depends on your face shape and personal preference. Generally, a longer side fringe that hits just below the eyebrows is the most flattering.

2. Can I wear a side fringe if I have curly hair?

Yes! Just make sure to straighten it out with a flat iron if you want a more polished look.

3. Will a side fringe make my hair look thinner?

No, a side fringe can actually make your hair look thicker and more voluminous.

Hair Care Tips for Side Fringes

If you want to keep your side fringe looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness. - Avoid using heat tools too often as they can damage your hair. - Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny. - Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

In conclusion, does a side fringe suit everyone? The answer is yes, as long as you choose the right style for your face shape. With the right cut and maintenance, a side fringe can be a flattering and stylish addition to any hairstyle. So, go ahead and give it a try!

How to Style Curly Hair Round Face

The key to styling curly hair round face is to create height and volume at the crown of your head. This helps elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. Here are some hairstyles that work well for round-faced women with curly hair:

1. Long Layers

Long layers help elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. This hairstyle looks great when paired with side-swept bangs.

2. Curly Bob

A curly bob is a great option for women with round faces. This hairstyle looks best when the length is chin-length or shorter.

3. Half Up Half Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for women with curly hair round face. This hairstyle creates height at the crown of your head, which helps elongate your face.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to draw attention away from your cheeks. This hairstyle looks great when paired with long layers.

Step-by-Step Guide

Hereâs a step-by-step guide on how to style curly hair round face:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle and moisturize your curls.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to help define your curls. Use a curl-enhancing cream, gel, or mousse.

Step 4: Diffuse Your Hair

Diffuse your hair using a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment. This helps create volume at the crown of your head.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using one of the hairstyles mentioned above.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair Round Face

Here are some additional tips for styling curly hair round face:

1. Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid using heavy products that weigh down your curls. Instead, opt for lightweight products that enhance your curls without making them look greasy or crunchy.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls. This helps prevent breakage and damage.

3. Sleep on Satin

Sleep on a satin pillowcase or wrap your hair in a satin scarf to help prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair if you have a round face. However, keep in mind that straight hair can make your face appear wider.

Q: What hair color looks best on round-faced women with curly hair?

A: Warm-toned hair colors like caramel, honey, and auburn look best on round-faced women with curly hair.

1. Donât Overwash Your Hair

Donât wash your hair every day. Overwashing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage.

2. Deep Condition Regularly

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to help prevent damage.

Best Fade For Big Head A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Sep 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you someone with a big head and looking for the perfect fade hairstyle? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best fade for big head and provide you with tips and tricks to maintain your new hairstyle. Let's dive in!

Description

A fade is a trendy and stylish haircut that has been around for decades. A fade involves tapering the hair from the sides and back of the head, creating a gradient effect. This haircut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance and versatile hairstyle. However, for those with a big head, finding the perfect fade can be a bit tricky.

How to

Before getting a fade, it's essential to choose the right style that suits your head shape. For those with a big head, a high fade or a drop fade is perfect. A high fade involves tapering the hair from the temples to the top of the head, while a drop fade tapers the hair from the temples to the back of the head. These styles create an illusion of a smaller head and elongate the face.

Once you've chosen the right style, it's time to visit your barber. When getting a fade, make sure to communicate your preferences clearly to your barber. Show them pictures of the style you want and discuss the length and tapering of the hair.

After getting the perfect fade, it's essential to maintain it. Regular visits to the barber and trimming the hair every few weeks can keep your fade looking sharp. Using a good quality hair product can also help in maintaining the style and keeping your hair healthy.

Step by step

1. Choose the right style - a high fade or a drop fade.

2. Communicate your preferences clearly to your barber.

3. Maintain the fade by visiting the barber regularly and trimming the hair every few weeks.

4. Use a good quality hair product to keep your hair healthy and maintain the style.

Tips

- Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals.

- Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

- Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and affect the style.

FAQ

Q. Can I get a fade if I have curly hair?

A. Yes, you can get a fade with curly hair. However, make sure to communicate your preferences to your barber and choose the right style that suits your hair type.

Q. Can I maintain a fade at home?

A. While it's always best to visit your barber regularly, you can maintain your fade at home by using a good quality hair product and trimming the hair every few weeks.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is essential in maintaining any hairstyle, including a fade. To keep your hair healthy, make sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals, and use a good quality hair product to maintain the style. Additionally, avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and affect the style.

In conclusion, a fade is a great hairstyle for those with a big head. By choosing the right style, communicating your preferences to your barber, and maintaining the style regularly, you can rock the best fade for big head. Follow our tips and tricks, and you'll have a stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle in no time!

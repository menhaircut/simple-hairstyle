How Do I Choose A Hairstyle For My Face

Are you tired of sporting the same hairstyle and want to switch things up? Choosing a new hairstyle can be daunting, especially if you don't know what suits your face shape. But don't worry, we have got you covered. In this article, we will guide you on how to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape.

How To Choose a Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

The first step to choosing a hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To find out your face shape, pull your hair back and take a photo of your face straight on. Then, look at the photo and compare it to the descriptions below:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! Almost any hairstyle will suit you. However, it's best to avoid haircuts that cover your forehead as they can make your face appear shorter.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, go for hairstyles that add height and length to your face. A long bob or a layered haircut that falls just below your chin will work wonders. Avoid blunt bangs and chin-length haircuts.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, choose hairstyles that soften your jawline. Soft waves and curls work great on this face shape. Avoid straight hairstyles and blunt bangs.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, go for hairstyles that balance your forehead and jawline. A side-swept bang or a chin-length bob will work wonders. Avoid voluminous hairstyles that add height to your crown.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, choose hairstyles that soften your cheekbones. A side-swept bang or a layered bob will work great. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your crown.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, go for hairstyles that add width to your face. A short bob or a mid-length haircut with layers will work wonders. Avoid hairstyles that add height to your crown.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing a Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, here are some steps to follow when choosing a hairstyle:

  1. Find inspiration. Look for hairstyles that you like and think would suit you. You can browse through magazines, social media, or consult with your hairstylist.
  2. Consider your lifestyle. Choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, go for a low-maintenance hairstyle.
  3. Think about your hair texture. Choose a hairstyle that works with your hair texture. If you have curly hair, go for a haircut that enhances your curls.
  4. Consult with your hairstylist. Your hairstylist is an expert and can guide you on what hairstyle will suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Tips for Choosing a Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Don't be afraid to experiment. Hair grows back, so don't be afraid to try new things.
  • Consider your skin tone. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone.
  • Accessorize your hair. Use hair accessories to add some personality to your hairstyle.
  • Take care of your hair. Healthy hair is key to any great hairstyle. Use hair care products that suit your hair type and avoid heat styling too often.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

A long bob or a layered haircut that falls just below your chin works great for a round face shape.

Can I pull off short hair?

Short hair works great for people with oval and heart-shaped faces. However, anyone can pull off short hair as long as the haircut suits their face shape.

What should I consider when choosing a haircut?

You should consider your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style when choosing a haircut.

Hair Care for Your New Hairstyle

After choosing a new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling too often and use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep split ends at bay.
  • Use hair masks and oils to keep your hair hydrated.

Choosing a new hairstyle can be overwhelming, but with the right guidance, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and suits your lifestyle. Remember to experiment and have fun with your hair!

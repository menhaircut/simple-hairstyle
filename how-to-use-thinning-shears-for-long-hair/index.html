How To Use Thinning Shears For Long Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have long hair that is thick, you may find it difficult to manage or style. One solution to this problem is to use thinning shears. Thinning shears are scissors that have teeth on one blade and a smooth blade on the other. They are designed to remove some of the hair's thickness, making it easier to manage and style. In this article, we will discuss how to use thinning shears for long hair, including tips and tricks to get the perfect style.

Description of Thinning Shears

Thinning shears are scissors with one blade that has teeth on it, and the other blade is smooth. They are used to remove some of the bulk from the hair, making it easier to style and manage. The teeth on the blade are designed to remove some of the hair while leaving some of it behind. This creates a more natural look and prevents the hair from looking too thin.

How to Use Thinning Shears for Long Hair

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before using thinning shears, you should wash and dry your hair. This will help you see the natural texture of your hair and make it easier to cut. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using thinning shears.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to cut the hair evenly and prevent any mistakes. Start at the bottom of your hair and work your way up.

Step 3: Hold the Shears Correctly

Thinning shears are held differently than regular scissors. Hold the scissors with the teeth pointing towards the hair and the smooth blade pointing away from the hair. This will help you remove the right amount of hair without creating any gaps or holes.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Start at the ends of the hair and work your way up. Use the thinning shears to remove some of the hair. You can use the shears in a few different ways, such as cutting horizontally or diagonally. Make sure you are cutting evenly and not removing too much hair.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut the hair, use a comb to blend the hair. This will ensure that the hair looks natural and not too thin. You can also use your fingers to blend the hair if you prefer.

Tips for Using Thinning Shears for Long Hair

Here are some tips to help you use thinning shears for long hair:

  • Start at the bottom of the hair and work your way up.
  • Cut the hair in small sections.
  • Hold the shears correctly.
  • Blend the hair after cutting.
  • Use the right type of thinning shears for your hair type.

FAQ

What are the benefits of using thinning shears?

Thinning shears can help reduce the bulk of thick hair, making it easier to manage and style. They can also create a more natural look and prevent the hair from looking too thin.

Can thinning shears damage the hair?

Thinning shears can damage the hair if they are not used correctly. It is important to use the right type of thinning shears and to cut the hair in small sections. It is also important to blend the hair after cutting to prevent any gaps or holes.

How often should I use thinning shears?

You can use thinning shears as often as you like, but it is important not to overdo it. Cutting too much hair with thinning shears can make the hair look too thin and unnatural.

Hair Care

Using thinning shears can help make managing and styling long hair easier. However, it is important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a heat protectant when using hot tools, and using a deep conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Thinning shears can be a great tool for managing long hair. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect style without damaging your hair. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and techniques, and your hair will look beautiful and healthy.

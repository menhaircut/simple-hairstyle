Best Hairstyle For V Shaped Face Male

Written by Eveline Oct 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a v shaped face, then choosing the right hairstyle can be a bit tricky. A v shaped face has a wider forehead and narrower chin, which can make some hairstyles look unflattering. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best hairstyles for v shaped face male.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The best hairstyle for v shaped face male is one that complements the shape of their face. This means that it should balance out the wider forehead and narrower chin. Hairstyles that work well for v shaped face males include those that add volume to the chin area and those that have longer hair on the sides.

How to

To find the best hairstyle for your v shaped face, you should consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style. You should also take into account the shape of your face and the features you want to highlight. A good hairstylist can help you choose the best hairstyle for your face shape.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can follow to find the best hairstyle for your v shaped face:

  1. Identify your face shape - take a photo of yourself and trace the outline of your face to see if it is a v shape.
  2. Research hairstyles - look online for hairstyles that work well for v shaped face males.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist - bring in photos of the hairstyles you like and ask for their opinion on which would work best for you.
  4. Try out different hairstyles - once you've decided on a hairstyle, try it out and see how it looks on you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your v shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short on the sides, as this can make your face look even wider.
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the chin area, such as a pompadour or quiff.
  • Consider growing out your hair on the sides to balance out your face shape.
  • Experiment with different hair products to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear a buzz cut with a v shaped face?

A: Yes, you can still wear a buzz cut with a v shaped face, but make sure to keep some length on the sides to balance out your face shape.

Q: What is the best way to style a pompadour?

A: Use a high-hold pomade or wax and comb your hair back and up, creating a voluminous pompadour on top.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Visit a hairstylist regularly to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can enhance your v shaped face and look your best.

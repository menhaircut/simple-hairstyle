Simple Wedding Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Robby Jul 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a simple yet elegant hairstyle for your wedding day? If you have medium length hair, you’re in luck! There are plenty of beautiful styles that are perfect for your big day. In this article, we’ll share some of our favorite simple wedding hairstyles for medium length hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Choose the Right Wedding Hairstyle for Medium Length Hair

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it’s important to consider a few things when choosing your wedding hairstyle. Here are a few tips:

  • Think about the overall theme and vibe of your wedding. Your hairstyle should complement your dress and the overall aesthetic of your wedding.
  • Consider your face shape and hair texture. Certain hairstyles will look better on certain face shapes and hair types.
  • Think about the weather and location of your wedding. If you’re getting married outdoors in the middle of summer, you may want to opt for a hairstyle that can withstand the heat.

Simple Wedding Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair: Step by Step

Hair Down with Soft Waves

For a simple and effortless look, consider wearing your hair down with soft waves. This style works well for medium length hair because it adds volume and texture without overwhelming your face. Here’s how to achieve this look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Curl small sections of hair with a curling iron or wand, alternating the direction of the curls.
  4. Gently brush out the curls with your fingers to create soft waves.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Low Chignon

A low chignon is a classic and elegant hairstyle that looks great on medium length hair. Here’s how to create this look:

  1. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins.
  3. Use a comb to gently tease the hair at the crown of your head for added volume.
  4. Spray with a light hold hairspray to keep everything in place.

Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down hairstyle is a great option if you want to show off your length while still keeping your hair out of your face. Here’s how to achieve this look:

  1. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and pull it back, securing with bobby pins.
  2. Curl the remaining hair in loose waves.
  3. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Simple Wedding Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

  • Don’t be afraid to add accessories, such as a crown or hair pins, to elevate your hairstyle.
  • If you’re wearing a veil, make sure your hairstyle is compatible with it.
  • Consider booking a trial with your hairstylist to try out different styles before your big day.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hairstyle for a summer wedding?

For a summer wedding, opt for a hairstyle that’s heat-resistant and won’t wilt in the heat. A low chignon or a simple updo would be a great choice.

How do I make my medium length hair look thicker?

Using volumizing products, such as mousse or root lift spray, can help add volume and thickness to your hair. You can also try teasing your hair at the crown for added volume.

Hair Care Tips for Your Wedding Day

To ensure your hair looks its best on your wedding day, it’s important to take care of it leading up to the big day. Here are a few tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.

With these simple wedding hairstyles for medium length hair, you’ll be able to find the perfect style for your big day. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful, and don’t forget to take care of your hair leading up to your wedding day.

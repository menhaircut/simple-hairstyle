Does Short Hair Make You Look Fatter Male Myth Or Fact

Written by Oliver Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair has been a popular hairstyle among men for years. But, there's a myth that short hair makes you look fatter. Is it true or just a myth? In this blog post, we'll get to the bottom of it and answer the question: does short hair make you look fatter male?

Description

The myth that short hair makes you look fatter has been around for years. Some people believe that shorter hair draws more attention to the face and neck, making the face appear wider and the neck shorter. But, is there any truth to this myth?

How to

There are a few things to consider when it comes to whether or not short hair makes you look fatter. Here are some factors to keep in mind:

Face Shape

One of the biggest factors that affect whether or not short hair makes you look fatter is your face shape. If you have a round face, shorter hair may make your face look wider. However, if you have a more angular face, short hair can actually enhance your features and make your face appear slimmer.

Hairstyle

The hairstyle you choose can also affect whether or not short hair makes you look fatter. A hairstyle that is too short or too tight to the scalp can make your face appear wider. On the other hand, a style with longer layers and more volume can balance out your face shape.

Body Type

Another factor to consider is your body type. If you have a larger body frame, shorter hair may make your face look wider. However, if you have a smaller frame, short hair can make you appear more proportionate.

Step by Step

If you're considering a shorter hairstyle but are worried about looking fatter, here are some steps you can take:

Consult with a Hairstylist

A professional hairstylist can help you choose a hairstyle that is flattering for your face shape and body type. They can also suggest styles that will make you look slimmer and more proportionate.

Experiment with Different Styles

Try out different styles and see what works best for you. Don't be afraid to try something new and be open to suggestions from your hairstylist.

Accessorize

Accessories like hats, scarves, and glasses can help balance out your face shape and make you appear slimmer.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Stay hydrated and maintain a healthy diet to keep your body in shape.
  • Exercise regularly to stay fit and healthy.
  • Choose clothing that flatters your body type.
  • Choose hairstyles that complement your features.

FAQ

Does short hair make you look older?

Short hair can make you look more mature, but it doesn't necessarily make you look older. It all depends on the style and how it suits your face shape and features.

Does short hair make you look more professional?

Short hair can give off a more polished and professional look, but it also depends on the style and how it's maintained.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your hair.

Conclusion

So, does short hair make you look fatter male? The answer is: it depends. Your face shape, body type, and hairstyle all play a role in whether or not short hair will make you look fatter. By consulting with a hairstylist and experimenting with different styles, you can find a haircut that flatters your features and makes you feel confident.

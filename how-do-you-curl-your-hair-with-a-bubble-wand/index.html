How Do You Curl Your Hair With A Bubble Wand

Written by Eveline Jul 16, 2023 · 4 min read

If you want to achieve bouncy and playful curls, then using a bubble wand is the way to go! This hair styling tool is designed to create wavy and voluminous curls that are perfect for any occasion. If you're new to using a bubble wand or want to improve your technique, then this guide is for you. In this blog post, we'll teach you how to curl your hair with a bubble wand step by step and provide some tips and tricks to ensure that your curls look flawless every time.

Description

A bubble wand is a hair styling tool that has a unique barrel shape. Instead of the typical round barrel, a bubble wand has a barrel that is made up of small bubbles. These bubbles create different-sized sections of hair to curl, resulting in a more textured and natural look. Bubble wands come in different sizes, so you can choose the one that works best for your hair length and desired curl size.

How to Curl Your Hair with a Bubble Wand

Now that you know what a bubble wand is, it's time to learn how to use it. Follow these steps to achieve bouncy and playful curls:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before curling your hair with a bubble wand, it's important to prep your hair. Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. This will protect your hair from heat damage and make it easier to style. Once your hair is dry, brush it to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, divide your hair into sections. The number of sections you create will depend on your hair thickness and the size of your bubble wand. If you have thick hair, create more sections, and if you have thin hair, create fewer sections. Secure each section with a hair clip or elastic band.

Step 3: Heat Up Your Bubble Wand

Turn on your bubble wand and let it heat up. It's important to wait until the wand is fully heated before using it to curl your hair. Most bubble wands have an indicator light that will tell you when it's ready to use.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Now it's time to start curling your hair. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the bubble wand. Hold the wand in place for 5-10 seconds, depending on your hair type and desired curl size. Release the hair from the wand and repeat until all sections of your hair are curled.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've curled your hair, finish with a light hairspray. This will help to set your curls and keep them in place throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your bubble wand:

  • Start with a lower heat setting and increase if necessary to avoid heat damage
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair
  • Wrap each section of hair around the bubble wand in the same direction for a more uniform look
  • Leave the ends of your hair out of the wand for a more natural look
  • After curling your hair, let it cool for a few minutes before brushing it out

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling your hair with a bubble wand:

Is a bubble wand suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a bubble wand can be used on all hair types. However, if you have very fine hair, you may need to use a lower heat setting to avoid damage.

How long should I hold the bubble wand on my hair?

Hold the bubble wand on your hair for 5-10 seconds, depending on your hair type and desired curl size. If you want tighter curls, hold the wand on your hair for a longer period.

Can I use a bubble wand on wet hair?

No, it's not recommended to use a bubble wand on wet hair. Make sure your hair is dry before using the wand to avoid damage.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair when using heat styling tools. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray
  • Don't use heat styling tools on wet hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day
  • Deep condition your hair regularly
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Conclusion

Curling your hair with a bubble wand is a fun and easy way to achieve bouncy and playful curls. By following these steps and tips, you'll be able to create flawless curls that will last all day. Remember to take care of your hair by using a heat protectant spray and deep conditioning regularly. Happy styling!

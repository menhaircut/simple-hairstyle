Description

The mens hairstyle short sides longer top has been one of the most popular haircuts for men in recent years, and its popularity shows no signs of slowing down in 2023. This style features short or faded sides with longer hair on top, providing a versatile look that works well for a variety of hair types and face shapes. With the right styling products and techniques, you can easily achieve this trendy and stylish look.

How To Achieve This Look

To achieve the mens hairstyle short sides longer top, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in this type of cut. They will be able to create the necessary fade on the sides and leave the length on top. Once you have the basic cut, you'll need to style your hair to achieve the desired look.

Styling Products

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the mens hairstyle short sides longer top:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply the styling product of your choice to the longer hair on top.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style the longer hair into the desired shape.
  4. If necessary, use a blow dryer to add volume to the longer hair on top.
  5. Finish with hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

To keep your mens hairstyle short sides longer top looking great, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for touch-ups.
  • Choose styling products that work well with your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look you like best.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down the hair and make it greasy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens hairstyle short sides longer top:

What face shapes work well with this style?

The mens hairstyle short sides longer top works well with a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces.

Can this style be worn with curly hair?

Yes, this style can be worn with curly hair. However, you may need to use different styling products and techniques to achieve the desired look.

How often should I visit my barber or stylist for touch-ups?

You should visit your barber or stylist for touch-ups every 4-6 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking great with the mens hairstyle short sides longer top, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid wearing hats or other accessories that can flatten your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve the mens hairstyle short sides longer top with ease and confidence.

Best Mousse To Hold Curls In Straight Hair Uk

Written by Frank Aug 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair falling flat every time you try to curl it? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best mousse to hold curls in straight hair in the UK. Whether you have short or long hair, these mousses will help you achieve the perfect curls that will last all day. Read on for our top picks and tips for using them.

Best Mousse to Hold Curls in Straight Hair

1. TRESemme Thermal Creations Volumizing Mousse - This mousse is perfect for creating long-lasting curls in straight hair. It provides heat protection and volume, which is essential for maintaining curls throughout the day.

2. Got2b Volumaniac Bodifying Mousse - This mousse is perfect for creating voluminous curls in straight hair. It provides a strong hold that lasts all day and is perfect for those with thin or fine hair.

3. L'Oreal Paris Studio Pro Boost It Volume Mousse - This mousse provides a strong hold and volume while protecting hair from heat damage. It is perfect for creating bouncy curls that last all day.

How to Use Mousse to Hold Curls in Straight Hair

Before using mousse, it is important to start with clean, dry hair. Apply a small amount of mousse to your palm and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Next, use a curling iron or wand to create your desired curls. Once you have curled your entire head, apply another small amount of mousse to your hands and scrunch it into your curls. This will help to define and hold your curls in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Mousse for Curls

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Apply a small amount of mousse to your palm and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

3. Use a curling iron or wand to create your desired curls.

4. Once you have curled your entire head, apply another small amount of mousse to your hands and scrunch it into your curls.

5. Allow your hair to cool completely before touching it.

Tips for Using Mousse to Hold Curls in Straight Hair

1. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Don't use too much mousse, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help dry your curls without disrupting them.

4. For tighter curls, use a smaller curling iron or wand. For looser curls, use a larger barrel.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use mousse on wet hair?

A: Yes, you can use mousse on wet hair. In fact, this can help to add volume and hold to your curls.

Q: How often should I wash my hair when using mousse?

A: It is best to wash your hair every other day when using mousse to hold curls. This will help to prevent product buildup and keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care for Maintaining Curls

1. Avoid brushing your hair once you have styled it, as this can disrupt your curls.

2. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and maintain your curls overnight.

3. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to keep it looking shiny and healthy.

4. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends, which can make your hair look frizzy and dull.

In conclusion, the best mousse to hold curls in straight hair in the UK are the TRESemme Thermal Creations Volumizing Mousse, Got2b Volumaniac Bodifying Mousse, and L'Oreal Paris Studio Pro Boost It Volume Mousse. Follow our tips and step-by-step guide for using mousse to achieve long-lasting, bouncy curls. Don't forget to take care of your hair by using hair care products and techniques that promote healthy, shiny hair.

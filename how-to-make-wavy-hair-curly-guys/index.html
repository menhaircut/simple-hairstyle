How To Make Wavy Hair Curly Guys

Written by Aprilia Sep 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your natural wavy hair and want to try something new? Do you want to make your wavy hair curly without any hassle? Well, you have come to the right place! In this article, we will guide you on how to make wavy hair curly for guys. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Wavy Hair Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Wavy Hair Curly
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Make Wavy Hair Curly

Before we dive into the process of making wavy hair curly, it is essential to understand the difference between wavy and curly hair. Wavy hair has a slight S-shape and a loose curl pattern, whereas curly hair has a more defined curl pattern with tighter curls.

Now, if you have wavy hair and want to make it curly, you have two options. You can either use heat styling tools or opt for natural methods. Heat styling tools can damage your hair in the long run, so we recommend trying natural methods first.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make wavy hair curly naturally:

  1. Begin by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and frizzy.
  2. Next, towel dry your hair and apply a curl-enhancing product. You can use a curl cream, gel, or mousse. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  3. With your hair still damp, divide it into small sections and twist each section into a tight coil. Secure the ends with a hair tie or clip.
  4. Leave your hair twisted for a few hours or overnight. The longer you leave it, the more defined your curls will be.
  5. Once your hair is completely dry, gently unravel the twists and finger comb your hair to separate the curls. Avoid using a brush or comb as it can cause frizz.
  6. Finally, apply a light hold hairspray to set your curls in place.

Tips for Making Wavy Hair Curly

Here are some tips to help you make wavy hair curly:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair in the long run.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural curls.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much as it can cause frizz.
  • Invest in a satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent hair breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about making wavy hair curly:

Can I make wavy hair curly without using any products?

Yes, you can make wavy hair curly without using any products. Simply twist your hair into tight coils and leave them for a few hours or overnight. However, using a curl-enhancing product can help enhance your curls and make them more defined.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, curly hair should be washed once or twice a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair using a flat iron or a blow dryer. However, excessive heat can damage your hair, so make sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol as it can dry out your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

We hope this article has helped you understand how to make wavy hair curly for guys. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you will soon have bouncy, defined curls!

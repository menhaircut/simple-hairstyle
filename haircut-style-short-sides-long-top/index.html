Haircut Style Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Oct 12, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're looking for a haircut that's both trendy and versatile, the short sides long top hairstyle might be just what you need. This haircut style has been popular for several years now, and it's not going away anytime soon. In this article, we'll show you how to get this haircut, provide some tips on how to style it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get a Short Sides Long Top Haircut
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Sides Long Top Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Sides Long Top Haircut

How to Get a Short Sides Long Top Haircut

The short sides long top haircut is all about having shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. This creates a nice contrast and allows you to style your hair in different ways. Here's what you need to know:

Choose a Style

Before you can get this haircut, you need to decide which version of the style you want. Some popular options include the undercut, fade, and taper. Each has its own unique characteristics, so it's important to choose the one that suits your face shape and personal style.

Find a Good Barber

Once you know which style you want, it's time to find a good barber or hairstylist. Look for someone who has experience with this type of haircut and has positive reviews from previous clients.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a short sides long top haircut:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products. This will make it easier for the barber to work with.

Step 2: Discuss Your Style with Your Barber

Talk to your barber about the style you want and ask for their input. They may have suggestions on how to tweak the style to better suit your face shape or hair type.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

The barber will typically start with the sides, using clippers or scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. They may use different guard sizes to create a fade or taper effect.

Step 4: Move to the Top

Once the sides are done, the barber will move on to the top. They may use scissors or a razor to create texture and shape the hair. Make sure to communicate with your barber about how much length you want to keep on the top.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once the haircut is done, it's time to style your hair. Use a pomade or wax to create your desired look, whether it's a slicked-back style or a messy, textured look.

Tips for Styling Short Sides Long Top Haircut

Here are a few tips to help you style your short sides long top haircut:

Use the Right Products

Choose a product that works well with your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Pomades and waxes are good options for creating texture and hold.

Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to try different styles with your short sides long top haircut. You can slick it back for a more formal look or create a messy, textured look for a more casual style.

Get Regular Trims

Make sure to get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut. This will prevent it from looking unkempt or losing its shape.

FAQs

Q: How long does a short sides long top haircut last?

A: The length of time a haircut lasts depends on how fast your hair grows, but typically, you'll need to get a trim every four to six weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Q: Can this haircut work for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut can work for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: What face shape does this haircut suit?

A: The short sides long top haircut can work for most face shapes, but it's especially flattering on oval and square faces.

Hair Care Tips for Short Sides Long Top Haircut

Here are a few hair care tips to keep your short sides long top haircut looking its best:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Make sure to wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.

Avoid Over-Styling

While it's fun to experiment with different styles, avoid over-styling your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you use heat styling tools like a blow dryer or flat iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the short sides long top haircut with confidence. Remember to communicate with your barber about the style you want and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.

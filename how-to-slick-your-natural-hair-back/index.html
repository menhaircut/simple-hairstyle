How To Slick Your Natural Hair Back

Are you looking for a sleek and stylish hairstyle that can be worn on any occasion? Slicking your natural hair back might be the perfect solution for you. This hairstyle is versatile, low maintenance, and easy to achieve. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to slick your natural hair back, step by step. We will also provide you with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to ensure that you get the perfect slicked-back look.

  • How to Slick Your Natural Hair Back

How to Slick Your Natural Hair Back:

Slicking your natural hair back is a simple and quick process. Here are the steps that you need to follow:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you start the process, make sure that your hair is clean and free from any product buildup. You can use a clarifying shampoo to remove any excess oil or dirt from your hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply Styling Gel or Pomade

Apply a small amount of styling gel or pomade to your hair. You can use your fingers to spread the product evenly throughout your hair. Make sure that you apply the product from the roots to the tips of your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a fine-toothed comb, start combing your hair back from the front of your head. Make sure that you comb your hair in a smooth and even motion. Keep combing until your hair is slicked back to your desired style.

Step 4: Secure Your Hair

Once your hair is slicked back, you can use a hair tie or bobby pins to secure it in place. Make sure that you place the hair tie or bobby pins at the back of your head, near the nape of your neck.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Once your hair is secured, you can use a small amount of hairspray to keep it in place. You can also use a toothbrush to smooth out any flyaways or frizz.

Step by Step Guide:

Here is a step by step guide to help you slick your natural hair back:

  1. Start with clean hair
  2. Apply styling gel or pomade
  3. Comb your hair back
  4. Secure your hair
  5. Finish with hairspray and toothbrush

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back:

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Use a fine-toothed comb to ensure that your hair is slicked back evenly
  • Apply the styling gel or pomade in small amounts to avoid product buildup
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best
  • Use a hair tie or bobby pins to secure your hair in place
  • Use a toothbrush to smooth out any flyaways or frizz

FAQs:

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking your natural hair back:

Q: Can I slick my hair back if it is short?

A: Yes, you can slick your hair back even if it is short. However, you may need to use more product to keep it in place.

Q: How often should I wash my hair when slicking it back?

A: You should wash your hair every 2-3 days when slicking it back. This will help to avoid product buildup and keep your hair healthy.

Q: Can I slick my hair back if it is curly?

A: Yes, you can slick your hair back even if it is curly. However, you may need to use more product to keep it in place.

Hair Care Tips:

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair stays healthy and beautiful, even when slicked back.

Conclusion:

Slicking your natural hair back is a simple and stylish hairstyle that can be worn on any occasion. By following the steps and tips provided in this article, you can achieve the perfect slicked-back look. Remember to take care of your hair by following our hair care tips to keep it healthy and beautiful. With a little practice and patience, you can rock this hairstyle with confidence!

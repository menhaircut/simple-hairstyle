Best Bangs For Round Face Small Forehead

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that don't seem to suit your round face with a small forehead? Well, you're in the right place! In this article, we'll be discussing the best bangs for round face small forehead, how to style them, and some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and gorgeous. Let's dive in!

Description

When it comes to finding the best bangs for round face small forehead, the goal is to create an illusion of length and balance. Bangs that are too short or too blunt can make your face look even rounder, while long and wispy bangs can make your forehead appear even smaller. The key is to find a style that complements your face shape and highlights your best features.

How to Choose the Best Bangs for Round Face Small Forehead

Before you decide on a specific style of bangs, it's important to consider your face shape and hair type. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for side-swept or asymmetrical bangs that are longer on one side than the other. This will create the illusion of length and balance.
  • Avoid blunt or straight-across bangs that end at or above your eyebrows, as they can make your face look rounder.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, consider getting longer, layered bangs that blend in with the rest of your hair.
  • If you have straight hair, you can experiment with different lengths and textures, such as choppy or wispy bangs.

Best Bangs for Round Face Small Forehead

Here are some of the best bangs for round face small forehead:

  • Side-swept bangs that are longer on one side
  • Asymmetrical bangs that are longer on one side
  • Wispy bangs that are longer and textured
  • Long, layered bangs that blend in with the rest of your hair

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Bangs

Once you've chosen the best bangs for your face shape, it's important to know how to style them properly. Here is a step by step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Part your hair down the middle or on one side
  3. Blow-dry your bangs straight using a round brush
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz
  5. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and prevent flyaways

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Here are some tips to keep your bangs looking fresh and stylish:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them at the right length
  • Wash your bangs separately from the rest of your hair to prevent them from getting greasy too quickly
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them oily and limp
  • Use dry shampoo to refresh your bangs in between washes

FAQs

What if I have a small forehead and a round face?

If you have a small forehead and a round face, you can still rock bangs! Just make sure to choose a style that is longer on one side or textured, as this will create the illusion of length and balance.

Can I wear blunt bangs with a round face and small forehead?

While blunt bangs can look great on some people, they may not be the best choice for a round face with a small forehead. Blunt bangs that end at or above the eyebrows can make your face look even rounder.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Bangs

Here are some hair care tips to keep your bangs healthy and looking their best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF

By following these tips and choosing the best bangs for round face small forehead, you can achieve a stylish and flattering hairstyle that highlights your best features. Remember to take good care of your hair and have fun experimenting with different styles!

Thick Hair Cut Straight Across A Guide To Get The Perfect Look

Written by Bardi Aug 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of dealing with your thick hair? Do you want a new haircut that can help you manage your hair easily? Look no further than a straight across cut! This classic cut can give you a chic and stylish look while making your hair more manageable. In this guide, we'll show you how to get the perfect straight across haircut for your thick hair.

Description

The straight across cut is a simple yet timeless haircut that involves cutting hair in a straight line across the ends. This cut is perfect for those with thick hair as it can help reduce the volume and weight of your hair. It's also a low-maintenance cut that requires minimal styling.

How to Get a Straight Across Cut for Thick Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in getting a straight across cut is deciding on the length you want. The length will depend on your personal preference and the thickness of your hair. If you have very thick hair, you may want to consider a shorter length to reduce the volume of your hair.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Next, divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair Straight Across

Using sharp scissors, cut your hair straight across each section. Make sure to keep the scissors at a 90-degree angle to get a straight cut. You can also use a comb to help guide your scissors.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting your hair, check for evenness. Make sure that the length of your hair is consistent across all sections. If you notice any unevenness, you may need to make small adjustments.

Tips for Maintaining Your Straight Across Cut

Now that you have your straight across cut, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy

FAQ

Q: Is a straight across cut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a straight across cut can work well for all face shapes. However, it's important to choose the right length that flatters your face shape.

Q: Can I style my straight across cut in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your straight across cut in different ways, such as adding layers or bangs.

Q: How often do I need to get a trim?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your cut.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Thick hair requires special care to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness
  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent stripping natural oils
  • Use a boar bristle brush to distribute natural oils and reduce frizz
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair

With these tips, you can keep your thick hair healthy and looking great!

Conclusion

A straight across cut can be a great option for those with thick hair. It's a classic and low-maintenance cut that can make your hair more manageable. By following our guide, you can get the perfect straight across cut for your hair. Remember to maintain your cut properly and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.

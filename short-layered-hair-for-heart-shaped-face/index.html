Short Layered Hair For Heart Shaped Face

Written by Aprilia Jun 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you know how important it is to find the right hairstyle that flatters your unique facial features. Short layered hair is a great option for heart shaped faces, as it can help balance out a wider forehead and narrower chin. In this article, we’ll discuss how to achieve the perfect short layered hairstyle for heart shaped faces.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A short layered hairstyle is a great option for heart shaped faces, as it can help to balance out the wider forehead and the narrower chin. Adding layers to your hair can also help to add volume and texture, creating a more youthful and playful look. Short layered haircuts can range from a classic bob to a messy pixie cut, depending on your personal style and preferences.

How To

The first step in achieving the perfect short layered hairstyle for a heart shaped face is to consult with your hairstylist. They can help you determine the right length and style that will flatter your unique facial features. They may also recommend adding in layers or bangs to help balance out your face shape.

Choose the Right Length

When choosing the length of your short layered haircut, it’s important to consider your face shape. For heart shaped faces, a chin-length bob or a short pixie cut can work well. Shorter hairstyles help to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, while also adding volume to your hair.

Add Layers

Adding layers to your short haircut can help to create movement and texture, while also balancing out your face shape. Layers can be added throughout your hair, or concentrated near the crown of your head to add volume.

Consider Bangs

Bangs can be a great addition to a short layered haircut for heart shaped faces. Side-swept bangs can help to balance out a wider forehead, while also drawing attention to your eyes.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieving a short layered hairstyle for a heart shaped face:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the right length and style for your face shape.
  2. Choose a chin-length bob or a short pixie cut.
  3. Add layers throughout your hair, or concentrate them near the crown of your head for added volume.
  4. Consider adding side-swept bangs to balance out a wider forehead.
  5. Keep your hair well-maintained with regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and voluminous.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your short layered haircut:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products, such as pomade or wax, to create different looks.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume and movement.

FAQ

What is a heart shaped face?

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and a narrower chin. The face may also have a pointed chin and a prominent jawline.

What short layered hairstyles work best for heart shaped faces?

Chin-length bobs and short pixie cuts work well for heart shaped faces. Adding layers throughout the hair can also help to create movement and balance out the face shape.

How do I style a short layered haircut?

You can style a short layered haircut with a variety of products, such as pomade, wax, or hairspray. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume and movement.

Hair Care

To keep your short layered haircut looking fresh and voluminous, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and voluminous.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it look limp.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect short layered hairstyle for your heart shaped face. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the right length and style for your unique facial features, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different products and styling techniques.

Best Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Faces

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles that will flatter your face shape and accentuate your features.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The best hairstyles for heart shaped faces are those that balance out the width of the forehead and create the illusion of a wider jawline.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for your heart shaped face, it is important to consider your hair texture, length, and personal style. Some of the best hairstyles for heart shaped faces include:

1. Long layers

Long layers create movement and volume around the chin, which can balance out the wider forehead. This style is best for those with long hair and a straight or wavy texture.

2. Side-swept bangs

Side-swept bangs draw attention to the eyes and create the illusion of a wider forehead. This style is best for those with medium to long hair and a straight or wavy texture.

3. Bob haircut

A chin-length bob can balance out the wider forehead and create the illusion of a wider jawline. This style is best for those with straight or wavy hair.

4. Pixie cut

A short pixie cut can create the illusion of a wider jawline and draw attention to the cheekbones. This style is best for those with a straight or wavy hair texture.

Step by Step

Here are the step by step instructions for achieving some of the best hairstyles for heart shaped faces:

1. Long layers

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  4. Create long layers around the chin using scissors or a razor.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray.

2. Side-swept bangs

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush.
  4. Create side-swept bangs by parting your hair to the side and blow drying them in the same direction.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray.

3. Bob haircut

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush.
  4. Cut your hair to chin length using scissors or a razor.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray.

4. Pixie cut

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Cut your hair to a short length using scissors or clippers.
  4. Create texture using a texturizing spray.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair if you have a heart shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate the width of your forehead, such as blunt bangs.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Use a curling iron to create soft waves or curls around the chin.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or slicked back, as they can accentuate the narrowness of your chin.

FAQ

What if I have curly or thick hair?

Long layers or a chin-length bob are still great options for those with curly or thick hair. Talk to your stylist about the best way to achieve these hairstyles with your specific hair type.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

A low ponytail or a side ponytail can be a great option for those with heart shaped faces. Avoid wearing your hair in a high ponytail, as it can accentuate the width of your forehead.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, or use a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for heart shaped faces, you can confidently choose a new style that will flatter your features and make you feel great!

Which Short Haircut Suits For Round Face Female

Written by Aprilia Nov 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a round-faced female looking for the perfect short haircut? Look no further! In this article, we'll go over the best short haircuts that suit a round face shape. From the classic bob to the trendy pixie cut, we've got you covered.

Description

First, let's talk about what a round face shape looks like. A round face is characterized by a circular shape with the same width and length. The cheeks are often full, and the jawline is soft. The goal when choosing a short haircut is to create angles and length to balance out the roundness of the face.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Length: The shorter the hair, the more exaggerated the roundness of the face can appear. Aim for a length that hits just below the chin or longer.
  • Layers: Layers can help create volume and angles, which are essential for balancing out a round face shape.
  • Bangs: Bangs can also help create angles and length. Side-swept bangs can be especially flattering for a round face.

Step by Step

Now, let's dive into the best short haircuts for a round face:

1. The Bob

A classic bob is a great option for a round face. Aim for a length that hits just below the chin and add layers to create volume and angles. Side-swept bangs can also help elongate the face.

2. The Lob

If you're not quite ready for a bob, a lob (long bob) is a great option. Aim for a length that hits just below the collarbone and add layers to create volume and angles. Side-swept bangs can also help elongate the face.

3. The Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a daring option for a round face, but it can also be incredibly flattering. Aim for a cut with lots of layers and texture to create volume and angles. Side-swept bangs can also help elongate the face.

Tips

When styling your short haircut, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid styles that are too flat or sleek, as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to create volume and texture in your hair.
  • Try styling your hair with a deep side part to create angles.

FAQ

What if I have curly hair?

Curls can be incredibly flattering for a round face. Aim for a cut that is chin-length or longer and add layers to create volume and texture. Consider adding side-swept bangs to elongate the face.

What if I have thin hair?

A bob or lob can be a great option for thin hair, as it can help create the illusion of volume. Consider adding layers to create texture and volume.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your short haircut, here are a few tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create volume and texture in your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect short haircut for your round face shape. Remember to have fun with your hair and experiment with different styles!

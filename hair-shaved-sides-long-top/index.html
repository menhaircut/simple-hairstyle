Hair Shaved Sides Long Top A Trendy Hairstyle In 2023

If you're looking for a versatile and edgy hairstyle, the hair shaved sides long top might be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's easy to see why. With a bold and daring look, it's perfect for those who want to make a statement.

Description

The hair shaved sides long top hairstyle is characterized by a shaved or closely cropped undercut on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. The length of the hair on top can vary, but it's usually long enough to be styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, pompadour, or messy and textured.

This hairstyle is popular among both men and women, and it can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. It's also a great option for those who want to experiment with different hair colors or highlights, as the contrast between the shaved sides and long top can create a dramatic effect.

How to Achieve the Hair Shaved Sides Long Top

The hair shaved sides long top hairstyle can be achieved with the help of a professional hairstylist, or you can do it yourself with the right tools and techniques. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers or a razor
  • Hair scissors
  • Styling products, such as pomade or wax

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the hair shaved sides long top hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to work with and style.
  2. Using clippers or a razor, shave or closely crop the hair on the sides and back of your head. The length of the hair should be about half an inch or less.
  3. Blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top, making sure there are no harsh lines or uneven patches.
  4. Using hair scissors, trim the hair on top to the desired length. You can choose to leave it long and textured, or slick it back or style it into a pompadour.
  5. Apply styling products, such as pomade or wax, to hold the hair in place and add shine and texture.

Tips for Maintaining the Hair Shaved Sides Long Top

Here are some tips for maintaining the hair shaved sides long top hairstyle:

  • Visit a hairstylist regularly to keep the hair on the sides and back trimmed and neat.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair on top healthy and nourished.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Try different hairstyles and variations of the hair shaved sides long top, such as adding bangs or a side part.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the hair shaved sides long top hairstyle:

Can women pull off the hair shaved sides long top?

Yes, women can definitely pull off the hair shaved sides long top hairstyle. It's a great way to add edge and personality to your look, and it can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

Is the hair shaved sides long top difficult to maintain?

It depends on the length of the hair on top and the desired style. If you prefer a messy and textured look, it can be relatively low maintenance, but if you want a slicked back or pompadour style, it may require more styling and upkeep.

What hair types are best suited for the hair shaved sides long top?

The hair shaved sides long top hairstyle can work for a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, it's important to choose a style and length that suits your hair type and texture.

Hair Care Tips for the Hair Shaved Sides Long Top

Here are some hair care tips for the hair shaved sides long top hairstyle:

  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair on top healthy and nourished.
  • Protect the hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or coloring products on the hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

The hair shaved sides long top hairstyle is a bold and daring choice that can add personality and edge to your look. With the right tools, techniques, and styling products, you can achieve and maintain this trendy hairstyle with ease.

Description

Long hair is beautiful, but if it's lacking volume, it can be frustrating to style. Luckily, there are ways to cut long hair to give it more volume. In this article, we'll discuss the steps you can take to achieve voluminous long hair, including a step-by-step guide, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

How to Cut Long Hair to Give it Volume

The trick to cutting long hair for volume is to create layers. Layers add texture and movement to hair, which creates the appearance of more volume. Here's how to cut long hair to give it volume:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is wet or damp, it will be harder to judge where to cut and how much to cut. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup that may weigh your hair down.

2. Section your hair

Divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

3. Start cutting

Starting with the back sections, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use scissors to cut the hair at a slight angle, starting about two inches from the bottom. Continue cutting small sections of hair, gradually working your way up to the crown of your head.

4. Cut the front sections

Move on to the front sections of your hair, repeating the same process. When cutting the front sections, make sure to angle the scissors slightly towards your face to create face-framing layers.

5. Blend the layers

Once you've finished cutting all four sections, use the scissors to blend the layers together. This will create a seamless, natural look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting long hair for volume:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup that may weigh your hair down.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Start cutting

Starting with the back sections, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use scissors to cut the hair at a slight angle, starting about two inches from the bottom. Continue cutting small sections of hair, gradually working your way up to the crown of your head.

Step 4: Cut the front sections

Move on to the front sections of your hair, repeating the same process. When cutting the front sections, make sure to angle the scissors slightly towards your face to create face-framing layers.

Step 5: Blend the layers

Once you've finished cutting all four sections, use the scissors to blend the layers together. This will create a seamless, natural look.

Tips for Cutting Long Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting long hair for volume:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Angle the scissors slightly when cutting to create layers.
  • Don't cut too much off the length of your hair. Focus on creating layers instead.
  • Always cut in a well-lit area.

FAQs

Can cutting long hair give it more volume?

Yes, cutting long hair to create layers can add volume and texture.

How often should I cut my hair to maintain volume?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain volume and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my own hair to add volume?

It's possible to cut your own hair, but it's recommended to see a professional stylist for best results.

Hair Care after Cutting Long Hair

After cutting your long hair for volume, it's important to take care of it to maintain the look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add more volume at the roots.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look flat.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the layers and prevent split ends.

Cutting long hair to give it volume can be a great way to change up your look and make your hair more manageable. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve voluminous long hair that's easy to style and maintain.

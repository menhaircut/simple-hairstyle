Mens Haircut Fade Side Part How To Achieve It

Written by William Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a classic and stylish haircut, then the mens haircut fade side part is the perfect choice for you. This haircut is perfect for any occasion and can be customized to suit your personal style. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve the mens haircut fade side part, step by step, and provide you with some tips on how to care for your hair after the cut.

Description

The mens haircut fade side part is a classic haircut that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back and leaving it longer on the top. The hair is then styled by creating a side part, which gives the haircut a clean and sophisticated look. The fade on the sides and back of the head creates a seamless transition from short to long hair, making it a popular choice for men of all ages.

How to Achieve the Mens Haircut Fade Side Part

Step 1: Choose the Right Hair Length

The first step in achieving the mens haircut fade side part is to choose the right hair length. The hair on the sides and back should be cut short, while the hair on the top should be left longer. The length of the hair on top will depend on your personal style and preference, but generally, it should be longer than the hair on the sides and back.

Step 2: Create a Side Part

Once the hair has been cut to the right length, the next step is to create a side part. Use a comb to create a straight part on one side of your head, and then use your fingers to push the hair to the other side. This will create a clean and neat look.

Step 3: Create the Fade

The final step in achieving the mens haircut fade side part is to create the fade. This involves gradually cutting the hair shorter as it gets closer to the sides and back of the head. The fade can be done using clippers or scissors, depending on your preference.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect mens haircut fade side part:

  • Choose a skilled and experienced barber to ensure that the haircut is done correctly.
  • Use a good quality hair product, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair after the cut.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths and styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut fade side part:

What face shape is best for the mens haircut fade side part?

The mens haircut fade side part is suitable for most face shapes, but it works particularly well for men with oval or square faces.

How often should I get a trim?

It is recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.

Hair Care

After getting the mens haircut fade side part, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and nourished.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to hydrate and strengthen your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as this can damage the hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

By following these tips, you can maintain your mens haircut fade side part and keep it looking stylish and fresh.

Wedding Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair With Veil

Written by Frank Jul 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a bride-to-be with medium length hair and wondering what hairstyle to choose for your wedding day? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of beautiful wedding hairstyles for medium length hair with veil. Whether you're going for a classic or modern look, these hairstyles will make you look stunning on your big day.

Description

Medium length hair can be styled in various ways to create a beautiful bridal look. Adding a veil to the hairstyle can add an extra touch of elegance and sophistication. It's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape, dress, and veil.

How to

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Apply some heat protectant spray to avoid any damage from heat styling tools. Here are some wedding hairstyles for medium length hair with veil:

1. Half Up Half Down with Loose Waves

This classic hairstyle is perfect for medium length hair. Create loose waves with a curling iron and leave some hair out in the front. Take the top half of your hair and create a small bun or twist, securing it with bobby pins. Add your veil above the bun or twist.

2. Low Bun with Side Swept Bangs

If you want a more formal look, try a low bun with side swept bangs. Brush your hair back into a low ponytail and twist it into a bun. Leave some hair out in the front to create side swept bangs. Place your veil on top of the bun.

3. Braided Updo

If you want a bohemian or rustic look, try a braided updo. Create a low bun and braid the remaining hair. Wrap the braid around the bun and secure it with bobby pins. Add your veil above the bun.

Step by Step

Here are the step by step instructions for creating a half up half down hairstyle with loose waves:

  1. Section your hair into two parts.
  2. Curl the bottom half of your hair with a curling iron.
  3. Create a small bun or twist with the top half of your hair.
  4. Spray some hairspray to hold the bun or twist in place.
  5. Place your veil on top of the bun or twist.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a wedding hairstyle for medium length hair with veil:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape, dress, and veil.
  • Do a trial run of the hairstyle before the wedding day.
  • Use hair accessories, such as flowers or jewels, to add an extra touch of elegance.
  • Use a strong hold hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place throughout the day.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wedding hairstyles for medium length hair with veil:

1. How do I choose a wedding hairstyle?

Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape, dress, and veil. Do a trial run of the hairstyle before the wedding day.

2. How do I secure my veil to my hair?

Use bobby pins or a comb to secure the veil to your hair. Place the veil above or below the hairstyle, depending on the style you choose.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair leading up to your wedding day. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get a trim every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip the natural oils from your hair.

Now that you have some ideas for wedding hairstyles for medium length hair with veil, it's time to start experimenting and finding the perfect hairstyle for your big day. Remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

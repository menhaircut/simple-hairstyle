How To Put Hair Up In High Ponytail

Written by Luffy Jul 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Putting your hair up in a high ponytail is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's perfect for any occasion, whether you're going to work, hitting the gym, or going out with friends. This easy-to-follow guide will show you how to put your hair up in a high ponytail in just a few simple steps.

Table of Content

  • How to Put Hair Up in High Ponytail
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Step by Step

Step 1: Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. It's best to use a wide-tooth comb or brush to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 2: Use a hair tie to tie your hair in a high ponytail. You can use your hands to gather your hair or a brush to create a smooth finish.

Step 3: Secure the hair tie tightly to prevent your ponytail from falling down. You can also use a second hair tie for added security.

Step 4: Use a hair spray or hair gel to smooth out any flyaways and create a sleek look. You can also use a hairbrush to create a more polished look.

Tips

Tip 1: Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.

Tip 2: Add some volume to your ponytail by teasing the crown of your head before tying your hair up.

Tip 3: Use a hair accessory such as a ribbon or scrunchie to add some flair to your ponytail.

FAQ

Q: How do I prevent my hair tie from slipping?

A: To prevent your hair tie from slipping, use a hair tie that has a strong grip or texture. You can also use bobby pins to secure your ponytail in place.

Q: Can I put my hair up in a high ponytail if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can still put your hair up in a high ponytail if you have short hair. Use bobby pins to secure any loose hairs and create a polished finish.

Hair Care

Putting your hair up in a high ponytail can cause stress on your hair and scalp. To prevent damage, make sure to use a hair tie that is gentle on your hair and avoid tying your hair up too tightly. It's also important to take breaks from wearing your hair up to allow your scalp and hair to rest.

By following these simple steps and tips, you can easily put your hair up in a high ponytail and create a chic and stylish look. Whether you're running errands or heading to a special event, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.

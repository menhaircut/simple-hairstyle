Can You Cut Thick Curly Hair Short A Guide To Short Haircuts For Curly Hair

Written by William Jun 26, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you may be wondering if it's possible to pull off a short haircut. The answer is yes, but there are a few things you need to keep in mind before making the chop. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know about cutting thick curly hair short, including how to do it, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Thick Curly Hair Short
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Thick Curly Hair Short
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

Description

Thick curly hair can be challenging to manage, but cutting it short can make it easier to style and maintain. Short haircuts can also help reduce frizz and make your curls more defined. However, it's essential to choose the right cut for your hair type and face shape to ensure that it looks flattering and stylish.

How to Cut Thick Curly Hair Short

Cutting thick curly hair short requires a bit of skill and knowledge. Here are some things to keep in mind before you start:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your face shape.
  • Consider the texture and density of your hair.
  • Find a stylist who has experience cutting curly hair.
  • Be prepared to invest in quality styling products and tools.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut thick curly hair short:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair, and use a wide-tooth comb to detangle it.
  3. Section your hair into four parts: top, front, and two sides.
  4. Starting at the back of your head, use hair clippers to cut your hair to the desired length.
  5. Move to the sides and front, using scissors to cut your hair to the desired length and shape.
  6. Blend the top section with the sides and front, using scissors to create a seamless transition.
  7. Use styling products to define your curls and add volume.
  8. Finish with a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to set your curls.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Thick Curly Hair Short

Here are some tips to ensure that your short haircut looks great and is easy to maintain:

  • Choose a cut that works with your natural texture and curl pattern.
  • Consider a layered cut to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles, such as a pixie cut or a bob.
  • Invest in quality styling products and tools designed for curly hair.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and maintenance.

FAQs

Q: Can you cut thick curly hair short without damaging it?

A: Yes, as long as you use the right techniques and tools, cutting thick curly hair short should not cause damage.

Q: Which short haircuts work best for thick curly hair?

A: Short layered haircuts, pixie cuts, and bobs work well for thick curly hair, but it's essential to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture.

Q: How often should I get my short curly hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short curly hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to set your curls.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and defined.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut thick curly hair short and enjoy a stylish, low-maintenance look. Remember to choose a cut that works for your hair type and face shape, invest in quality styling products and tools, and visit your stylist regularly for maintenance.

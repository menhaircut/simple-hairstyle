Am I Too Old For Purple Hair

Written by Robby Aug 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Are you considering dying your hair purple but wondering if you're too old for it? The truth is, there is no age limit when it comes to hair color. You can rock whatever color you want at any age!

However, there are some things to consider before making the leap to purple hair. For example, if you work in a conservative profession, such as law or finance, you may want to think twice about a bold hair color.

In this article, we'll explore how to dye your hair purple, step by step instructions, tips, and more. We'll also address some common questions about dying your hair and how to take care of it.

How to

Before you dye your hair purple, you'll need to bleach it first. Bleaching your hair will remove the natural pigments, so the purple dye will show up more vibrant.

Here's how to bleach your hair:

  1. Prepare your hair by washing and drying it thoroughly.
  2. Apply the bleach mixture to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  3. Leave the bleach in for the recommended amount of time, which will depend on your hair type and the brand of bleach you're using.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.

Once your hair is bleached, you can then apply the purple hair dye. Follow the instructions on the packaging carefully, and leave the dye in for the recommended amount of time.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to dye your hair purple:

  1. Choose the right shade of purple for your skin tone.
  2. Prepare your hair by washing and drying it thoroughly.
  3. Apply the purple hair dye to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Leave the dye in for the recommended amount of time.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  6. Apply conditioner to your hair and leave it in for a few minutes.
  7. Rinse your hair again with cool water.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you maintain your purple hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent the color from fading.
  • Avoid using heat-styling tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time in the sun, as UV rays can also cause the color to fade.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about dying your hair purple:

1. Will purple hair damage my hair?

Any hair dye has the potential to damage your hair, but if you take proper care of it and use high-quality products, you can minimize the damage.

2. How often will I need to touch up my roots?

This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. On average, you may need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential when you have colored hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent the color from fading.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can cause the color to fade more quickly.

In conclusion, you're never too old to dye your hair purple! Follow these tips and take proper care of your hair, and you'll be rocking your new hair color in no time.

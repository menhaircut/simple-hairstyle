How To Put Short Hair In A Bun Guys A Step By Step Guide

Short hair doesn't mean you can't rock a bun. Whether you're going for a sleek look or a messy one, a bun is a versatile hairstyle that can work for any occasion. In this guide, we'll show you how to put short hair in a bun guys in just a few easy steps.

How to Put Short Hair in a Bun Guys

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some of the tools you'll need to create a bun with short hair:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hair elastic
  • Hair spray (optional)

Now that you have your tools ready, let's get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Hair

Start by gathering all of your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a hair elastic to secure the ponytail in place.

Step 2: Twist Your Hair

Next, twist your hair around the hair elastic to create a bun shape. Keep twisting until your hair is tight enough to stay in place.

Step 3: Secure the Bun

Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Make sure to insert the pins in different directions to create a secure hold.

Step 4: Optional: Style the Bun

If you want a sleeker look, you can use hair spray to smooth down any flyaways. If you prefer a messy look, you can use your fingers to loosen up the bun and create a more relaxed style.

Tips for Putting Short Hair in a Bun Guys

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect bun:

  • Use a hair elastic that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Don't be afraid to use more bobby pins than you think you need to create a secure hold.
  • If you have shorter layers around your face, you can leave them out for a more relaxed look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I put short hair in a bun without a hair elastic?

No, a hair elastic is necessary to create the base of the bun. Without it, your hair will not stay in place.

Can I create a bun with really short hair?

Yes, you can create a mini bun with very short hair. Simply twist a small section of hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a bobby pin.

Hair Care for Short Hair Guys

While short hair is low-maintenance, it's still important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy. Here are some tips for hair care for short hair guys:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner every other day.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to put short hair in a bun guys in no time. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, a bun is a great hairstyle option for any occasion.

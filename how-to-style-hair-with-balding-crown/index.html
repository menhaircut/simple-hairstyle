How To Style Hair With Balding Crown

Written by Robby Oct 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As we age, hair loss is a common problem, and many men experience balding crowns. However, that doesn't mean you can't style your hair in a way that looks good and boosts your confidence. In this article, we will guide you on how to style hair with balding crown, providing you with tips and tricks to achieve a stylish look.

Description

Balding crown or thinning hair at the crown is a common problem that affects many men. It can be caused by various factors, including genetics, age, and lifestyle choices. It can be challenging to style hair with balding crown, but it's not impossible. There are various ways you can style your hair to cover up the balding area, making your hair look fuller and thicker.

How to Style Hair with Balding Crown

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in styling hair with balding crown is choosing the right haircut. A shorter haircut is often the best option as it can make the hair look thicker and fuller. Avoid long hairstyles that can highlight the balding area. A buzz cut, crew cut, or a short textured cut can work well for thinning hair.

Step 2: Use Hair Products

Using hair products can help give your hair volume and texture, making it look thicker and fuller. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. Apply a styling product such as a hair clay or paste to add texture and hold. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have chosen the right haircut and used the right products, it's time to style your hair. Use a blow dryer to add volume to your hair. Use a brush or your fingers to style your hair away from the balding crown. You can also use a hair dryer and a round brush to add volume to the hair at the crown.

Tips

  • Avoid combing your hair over the balding crown as it can make it look obvious.
  • Use a hairdryer to add volume to your hair.
  • Use a hair clay or paste to add texture and hold.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.
  • Consider getting a hair transplant if you want a more permanent solution to balding crown.

FAQ

Can I use hair extensions to cover up my balding crown?

Yes, hair extensions can be used to cover up balding crown. However, it's not a permanent solution, and it can be expensive.

Can I use hair fibers to conceal my balding crown?

Yes, hair fibers can be used to conceal balding crown temporarily. It's a quick and easy solution, but it will need to be reapplied regularly.

Is a hair transplant a good option for balding crown?

A hair transplant is a more permanent solution for balding crown. However, it can be expensive and may require multiple sessions.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential, especially if you have balding crown. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. Avoid using hot tools such as a straightener or curling iron, as they can damage your hair. Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Conclusion

Styling hair with balding crown can be challenging, but with the right haircut, products, and styling techniques, you can achieve a stylish look that boosts your confidence. Use the tips and tricks we have provided in this article to style your hair and feel confident again.

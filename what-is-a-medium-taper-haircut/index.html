What Is A Medium Taper Haircut

Written by Bardi Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, a medium taper might be the perfect choice for you. A taper is a type of haircut that gradually changes in length from the top of the head to the neckline. While a taper can be short or long, a medium taper is a popular choice for those who want a classic and stylish look.

Description

A medium taper haircut is a style where the hair is longer on top and gradually gets shorter towards the neckline. The sides are also tapered, creating a smooth and seamless transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back. This creates a neat and polished appearance that's suitable for both casual and formal occasions.

How to

If you're interested in getting a medium taper haircut, here's what you can expect during your appointment:

Step 1: Consultation

The first step is to talk to your hairstylist about the type of medium taper you want. They will ask about your preferences and suggest the best style for your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Preparation

Your hairstylist will then prepare your hair by washing and drying it. They will also section off your hair to ensure a precise and even cut.

Step 3: Cutting

Starting at the top of your head, your hairstylist will cut your hair to the desired length. They will then use clippers to taper the sides and back, gradually cutting the hair shorter as they move down.

Step 4: Finishing

Once the cutting is done, your hairstylist will use a comb and scissors to blend the layers and create a seamless transition. They will also trim any stray hairs and style your hair to your liking.

Tips

To maintain your medium taper haircut, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your hair looking sharp and polished.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax to add texture and hold.
  • Experiment with different partings and styling techniques to switch up your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium taper haircuts:

What's the difference between a taper and a fade?

While both tapers and fades involve cutting the hair shorter towards the neckline, a fade is a more drastic change in length. A fade starts with very short hair at the bottom and gradually gets longer towards the top, creating a gradient effect. A taper, on the other hand, is a more subtle transition.

Is a medium taper suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a medium taper can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. Your hairstylist will be able to adapt the style to your hair texture and face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

In conclusion, a medium taper haircut is a stylish and versatile choice for those who want a classic look that can be dressed up or down. It's suitable for all hair types and requires minimal maintenance, making it a convenient option for busy individuals. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can keep your medium taper looking sharp and polished for weeks to come.

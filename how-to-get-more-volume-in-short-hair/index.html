How To Get More Volume In Short Hair

Short hair is versatile and easy to maintain, but sometimes it can lack volume. If you have short hair and want to add more volume, there are several ways to achieve it. In this article, we will discuss how to get more volume in short hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get More Volume in Short Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Adding Volume to Short Hair
  4. FAQ

How to Get More Volume in Short Hair

There are several ways to add volume to short hair:

  • Blow-drying
  • Using volumizing products
  • Teasing
  • Backcombing

All of these methods can help give your hair more volume, but it's important to use them correctly and not damage your hair in the process.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to add volume to short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair upside down using a round brush to lift your roots.
  4. Tease the crown of your head for extra volume.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the volume.

Remember not to overdo it with the teasing and backcombing, as this can damage your hair and make it difficult to style in the future.

Tips for Adding Volume to Short Hair

Here are some additional tips for adding volume to short hair:

  • Choose a layered haircut to add texture and volume.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a volumizing dry shampoo between washes to keep your roots lifted.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about adding volume to short hair:

Q: Can I add volume to short hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can use volumizing products and backcombing to add volume without using heat.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly it gets oily. Generally, every 2-3 days is sufficient for most people.

Hair Care for Short Hair

It's important to take care of your short hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage.

By following these tips and using the methods described above, you can add more volume to your short hair and keep it looking great.

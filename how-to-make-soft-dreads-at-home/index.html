How To Make Soft Dreads At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read
Soft dreads are a popular hairstyle that can be achieved at home with the right tools and techniques. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look that still has a touch of elegance. In this article, we will guide you through the process of making soft dreads at home.

Description

Soft dreads are a type of dreadlock hairstyle that is made by using a crochet hook to create loops in the hair. The loops are then twisted and interlocked to form the dreads. The end result is a hairstyle that looks like twisted ropes and has a soft, fluffy texture.

How to Make Soft Dreads at Home

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make soft dreads at home:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start making your soft dreads, you will need to gather your supplies. You will need a crochet hook, hair clips, a spray bottle filled with water, and hair products such as oil or gel.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or product residue. Then, condition your hair to make it easier to work with.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use hair clips to section your hair into small, manageable sections. The smaller the sections, the tighter and more defined your dreads will be.

Step 4: Start Crocheting

Take a small section of hair and use the crochet hook to create a loop. Then, pull the loop through the hair until the dread is formed. Repeat this process with the rest of your hair sections.

Step 5: Twist and Interlock

Once you have created your loops, twist each dread to give it a more defined look. Then, use the crochet hook to interlock the dreads by pulling one dread through another.

Step 6: Repeat

Repeat the process of crocheting, twisting, and interlocking until all of your hair is in dreads.

Tips

- Use a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp while you work. This will make it easier to crochet and interlock your dreads. - Apply hair products such as oil or gel to your hair before you start crocheting. This will help to keep your dreads looking neat and defined. - Be patient! Making soft dreads takes time and practice. Don't be discouraged if it takes a few tries to get it right.

FAQ

Q: Can I make soft dreads on my own?

A: Yes! With the right tools and techniques, you can make soft dreads at home.

Q: How long does it take to make soft dreads?

A: The time it takes to make soft dreads depends on the length and thickness of your hair. On average, it can take anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Q: Do I need to have natural hair to make soft dreads?

A: No. Soft dreads can be made on all hair types, including relaxed and chemically treated hair.

Hair Care

To keep your soft dreads looking good, you will need to take proper care of them. Here are some tips: - Wash your dreads regularly with a residue-free shampoo. - Avoid using heavy products such as wax or heavy oils, as these can cause buildup and damage to your dreads. - Cover your dreads with a silk scarf or bonnet at night to protect them while you sleep. - If you notice any loose or frayed dreads, use a crochet hook to re-tighten them.

Conclusion

Making soft dreads at home is a fun and creative way to switch up your hairstyle. With a little patience and practice, you can achieve a look that is both stylish and low-maintenance. Follow our step-by-step guide and remember to take good care of your dreads for long-lasting results.

How To Get Waves In Short Hair Black Guys

Written by Bardi Aug 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a black guy with short hair and wondering how to get waves? Look no further, because in this article we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect waves in your short hair. Whether you’re going for a low-cut, fade, or even a buzz cut, these tips will help you get the waves you desire.

  • How to Get Waves in Short Hair Black Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Waves
  • FAQ

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to achieving waves in your short hair is to start with clean hair. Make sure you wash your hair thoroughly, removing any build-up or oils that may be present. Use a clarifying shampoo to get rid of any excess product.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

After washing your hair, use a brush to brush your hair in the direction you want your waves to form. This will help to train your hair to form into the wave pattern you desire.

Step 3: Apply Wave Cream or Pomade

Apply a wave cream or pomade to your hair, using a small amount at first and adding more as needed. Make sure to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair, using a brush to help spread it evenly.

Step 4: Brush Your Hair Again

After applying the wave cream or pomade, brush your hair again in the direction you want your waves to form. This will help to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair and encourage your hair to form into waves.

Step 5: Use a Durag

Once you have brushed your hair into the wave pattern you desire, use a durag to hold your hair in place. Wear the durag for at least 30 minutes to an hour to help set the waves in place.

Step 6: Repeat as Needed

Repeat this process daily or every other day, depending on your hair type and the wave pattern you desire. Over time, your hair will begin to form into waves more easily and you will be able to achieve the perfect wave pattern.

Tips for Maintaining Your Waves

Here are some tips to help you maintain your waves:

  • Brush your hair regularly, especially before applying wave cream or pomade.
  • Wear a durag at night to help keep your waves in place while you sleep.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and prevent it from forming into waves.
  • Try to avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can disrupt the wave pattern.

FAQ

How long does it take to get waves in short hair?

The time it takes to get waves in short hair varies depending on your hair type and how often you follow the wave training process. It can take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months to achieve the perfect wave pattern.

What type of wave cream or pomade should I use?

It is best to use a wave cream or pomade that is specifically designed for black hair. Look for products that are lightweight and provide a natural-looking hold.

Can I still get waves if I have a low-cut or fade?

Yes, you can still get waves in your hair even if you have a low-cut or fade. The process is the same, but you may need to adjust the brush and durag technique to fit the length of your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any build-up or oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as relaxers or dyes, on your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect waves in your short hair as a black guy. Remember to be patient and consistent in your wave training process, and you will see results over time.

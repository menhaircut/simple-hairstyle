How To Deal With Severe Hair Breakage

Written by Aprilia Oct 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Dealing with severe hair breakage can be a frustrating and daunting experience. Hair breakage can occur due to various reasons such as heat damage, chemical treatments, over-styling, and lack of proper hair care. However, with the right hair care routine and a few essential tips, you can effectively deal with severe hair breakage and restore your hair's health.

Description

Hair breakage occurs when the hair strands become weak and brittle, leading to split ends and breakage. This can be caused by various factors such as excessive use of heat styling tools, chemical treatments, over-processing, and lack of proper hair care. Severe hair breakage can also be a sign of underlying health issues such as thyroid problems or hormonal imbalances.

How to Deal with Severe Hair Breakage

Dealing with severe hair breakage requires a combination of proper hair care routine and lifestyle changes. Here are a few essential tips to help you deal with severe hair breakage:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends, which can lead to severe hair breakage. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends from traveling up the hair shaft.

2. Moisturize Your Hair Regularly

Dry hair is more prone to breakage than moisturized hair. Therefore, it's essential to moisturize your hair regularly using a good quality hair moisturizer or natural oils such as coconut oil, avocado oil, or olive oil. Apply the moisturizer or oil to your hair and scalp, then cover your hair with a shower cap for at least 30 minutes before shampooing and conditioning.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools such as flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers can cause severe hair breakage. If you must use heat styling tools, use them on low heat settings and apply a heat protectant to your hair before styling.

4. Use Gentle Hair Products

Harsh hair products can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it prone to breakage. Therefore, it's essential to use gentle hair products that are free of sulfates, parabens, and alcohol. Look for hair products that are specifically formulated for damaged or brittle hair.

5. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet rich in vitamins, minerals, and protein can help promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair breakage. Therefore, it's essential to eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and healthy fats.

6. Protect Your Hair at Night

While you sleep, your hair can rub against your pillow, causing friction, which can lead to hair breakage. Therefore, it's essential to protect your hair at night by wrapping it in a silk scarf or sleeping on a silk pillowcase.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you deal with severe hair breakage:

  • Avoid over-styling your hair
  • Avoid using harsh hair dyes or bleach
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated

FAQs

1. What causes severe hair breakage?

Severe hair breakage can be caused by various factors such as excessive use of heat styling tools, chemical treatments, over-processing, and lack of proper hair care. It can also be a sign of underlying health issues such as thyroid problems or hormonal imbalances.

2. Can severe hair breakage be reversed?

With the right hair care routine and lifestyle changes, severe hair breakage can be reversed. However, it may take some time to see significant results.

3. Can diet affect hair breakage?

A healthy diet rich in vitamins, minerals, and protein can help promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair breakage.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to prevent severe hair breakage. Follow these simple hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to moisturize your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet
  • Avoid using tight hair accessories that can pull on your hair

By following these simple tips, you can effectively deal with severe hair breakage and restore your hair's health. Remember, patience is key when dealing with severe hair breakage, and it may take some time to see significant results. However, with the right hair care routine and a few essential tips, you can achieve healthy, beautiful hair.

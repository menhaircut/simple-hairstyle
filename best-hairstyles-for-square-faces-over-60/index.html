Best Hairstyles For Square Faces Over 60

Written by Petter Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square face shape and you're over 60, you might be wondering what hairstyles will flatter your features. Square faces have a strong jawline and forehead, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that softens these areas. In this article, we'll go over the best hairstyles for square faces over 60, as well as some tips on how to care for your hair as you age.

Description

Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular features. As we age, our skin loses elasticity and our hair can become thinner and more brittle. This means it's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters our features and is easy to maintain. The best hairstyles for square faces over 60 are those that soften the jawline and forehead, while adding volume and texture to the hair.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Square Faces Over 60

When choosing a hairstyle for a square face over 60, it's important to consider the length, texture, and color of your hair. Here are some tips for choosing the best hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head, such as a layered bob or a pixie cut with longer layers on top.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can emphasize the squareness of your face.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair, as they can soften the jawline and add movement to your hair.
  • Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and brings out your best features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair if you have a square face over 60:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. If you have bangs, blow-dry them using a flat brush to smooth them out.
  5. If you have short hair, use a styling wax or pomade to add texture and definition.
  6. If you have longer hair, curl the ends using a curling iron or hot rollers.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to set your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

As we age, our hair can become thinner and more brittle. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to support hair growth.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for square faces over 60:

What are the best haircuts for square faces over 60?

The best haircuts for square faces over 60 are those that soften the jawline and forehead, while adding volume and texture to the hair. Some examples include a layered bob, a pixie cut with longer layers on top, or a shoulder-length cut with layers.

What hair colors look best on square faces over 60?

The best hair colors for square faces over 60 are those that complement your skin tone and bring out your best features. Some examples include warm browns, caramel highlights, and honey blonde.

How can I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using a volumizing mousse or spray at the roots, blow-drying your hair using a round brush, and using a curling iron or hot rollers to curl the ends.

Hair Care for Square Faces Over 60

As we age, our hair requires more care and attention to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your hair if you have a square face over 60:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to support hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the best hairstyle for your square face over 60, you can look and feel your best as you age. Remember to care for your hair and treat it with the love and attention it deserves!

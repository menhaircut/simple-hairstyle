Why Do I Have Asian Hair

Written by Christine Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever wondered why your hair has certain characteristics that resemble those of Asian hair? If you're not of Asian descent, it can be confusing and even frustrating to deal with hair that doesn't seem to fit the typical mold of your own ethnic group. But fear not! In this article, we'll explore the reasons why you might have Asian hair and what you can do to care for it.

Description

Asian hair is typically straight, thick, and shiny. It is also known for being resistant to chemical treatments, such as perms or color, and can be difficult to style without the use of heat tools. People of Asian descent also tend to have a lot of hair follicles per square inch, which contributes to the thickness and density of their hair.

If you don't have Asian ancestry, but your hair fits this description, there are a few reasons why this might be the case:

Genetics

One of the most common reasons why someone might have Asian hair is genetics. Hair type and texture are determined by a variety of genes, and it's possible that you inherited a combination of genes that resulted in hair that resembles Asian hair. This is especially likely if you have family members with similar hair.

Environmental Factors

Another reason why your hair might resemble Asian hair is due to environmental factors. For example, if you live in a humid climate, your hair may become straighter and thicker due to the moisture in the air. Similarly, if you live in an area with hard water, the minerals in the water can make your hair appear shinier and smoother.

Styling and Care

The way you style and care for your hair can also contribute to its characteristics. If you frequently use heat tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, your hair may become straighter or more textured. Additionally, if you use certain hair products or treatments, such as keratin treatments or Japanese straightening, your hair may become shinier and smoother.

How to Care for Asian Hair

If you have Asian hair, or hair that resembles Asian hair, it's important to care for it properly in order to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

Use Gentle Shampoos

Asian hair can be prone to dryness, so it's important to use gentle shampoos that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients, such as coconut oil or shea butter.

Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair no more than every other day, and consider using dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat tools on your hair, such as flat irons or curling irons, it's important to use a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage. Apply the product to your hair before using heat tools, and be sure to use a lower heat setting to avoid overheating your hair.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are important for keeping your hair healthy and preventing split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, or as needed to keep your hair looking its best.

FAQ

Can I Color My Asian Hair?

Yes, you can color Asian hair, but it's important to be cautious when doing so. Asian hair can be resistant to color, so it may take longer for the color to take hold. Additionally, the chemicals in hair dye can be harsh on your hair, so it's important to use a gentle dye and to follow up with deep conditioning treatments.

Can I Get a Perm If I Have Asian Hair?

While it is possible to get a perm if you have Asian hair, it can be difficult to achieve the desired result. Asian hair is typically resistant to chemical treatments, so it may take longer for the perm to take hold. Additionally, perming can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to use a gentle perm solution and to follow up with deep conditioning treatments.

Hair Care

Caring for Asian hair, or hair that resembles Asian hair, can be a challenge, but it's important to take the time to care for your hair properly in order to keep it healthy and looking its best. By using gentle shampoos, avoiding over-washing, using heat protectant, getting regular trims, and taking care when using chemical treatments, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great.

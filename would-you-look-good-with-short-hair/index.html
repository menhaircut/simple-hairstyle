Would You Look Good With Short Hair How To Decide

Written by Bardi Nov 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Short hair is a bold and trendy style that can transform your look. However, deciding whether or not to cut your hair can be a daunting task. In this article, we will explore how to determine if short hair is right for you and provide tips on how to care for your new style.

Description

Short hair can be a liberating and refreshing change for many people. It can accentuate your facial features and give you a youthful appearance. However, it’s important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style before making the big chop.

How to Decide

Before deciding to cut your hair, consider the following factors:

Face Shape

Your face shape is an important factor when deciding on a haircut. Short hair can look great on oval, heart-shaped, and square faces. However, if you have a round or long face, a longer haircut may be more flattering.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also affect how your short haircut looks. If you have fine, straight hair, a short bob or pixie cut can add volume and dimension. If you have thick, curly hair, a short cut can be difficult to manage and may require more styling time.

Personal Style

Your personal style should also be taken into consideration when deciding on a haircut. If you prefer a more edgy or modern look, a short haircut may be perfect for you. However, if you prefer a more classic or conservative style, a longer haircut may be a better fit.

Step by Step

If you’ve decided to take the plunge and cut your hair short, follow these steps:

1. Find Inspiration

Look for inspiration photos of short haircuts that you like. Consider the length, texture, and style of the cuts that catch your eye.

2. Consult with a Stylist

Visit a reputable stylist and discuss your desired haircut. They can provide insight on what styles will look best on you based on your face shape and hair texture.

3. Prepare Your Hair

Before your appointment, make sure to wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier for your stylist to work with and will ensure a more accurate cut.

4. Cut Your Hair

Trust your stylist and let them work their magic. Make sure to communicate any concerns or preferences during the process.

5. Style Your Hair

After your haircut, your stylist can show you how to style your new look. Ask for tips on how to maintain your style at home.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you care for your short hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and style. This can help enhance your natural texture and prevent damage.

2. Protect Your Hair

Use heat protectant products before using styling tools and avoid using excessive heat. This can help prevent damage and breakage.

3. Get Regular Trims

Short haircuts require more frequent trims to maintain their shape and prevent split ends. Schedule regular appointments with your stylist.

FAQ

1. Will short hair make me look older?

No, short hair can actually give you a more youthful appearance by accentuating your facial features.

2. Will short hair work with my hair texture?

Short hair can work with any hair texture, but it’s important to choose a style that will work with your natural texture.

3. Will short hair be difficult to style?

Short hair can be easier to style than longer hair because there is less hair to work with. However, it may require more frequent styling to maintain its shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

4. Avoid Excessive Heat

Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as this can cause damage and breakage.

By following these tips and taking the time to consider whether short hair is right for you, you can achieve a bold and beautiful new look that enhances your natural beauty.

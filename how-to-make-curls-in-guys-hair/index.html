How To Make Curls In Guys Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curls have always been a popular hairstyle among men. They can add volume, texture, and style to any haircut. However, not all guys have naturally curly hair, which is why knowing how to make curls in guys hair is essential. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve perfect curls in your hair.

Description

Before we dive into the steps, let's go over some basic information about curls in guys hair. There are different types of curls, including loose waves, tight curls, and spiral curls. The type of curl you want to achieve will depend on your hair type, length, and the styling products you use. Additionally, it's important to note that making curls in guys hair can be time-consuming and requires some practice. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a head full of bouncy, defined curls.

How to Make Curls in Guys Hair

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step in making curls in guys hair is to wash and condition your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. If you have dry or damaged hair, consider using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it gently.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before using any heating tools, it's essential to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will prevent your hair from getting damaged due to the heat. Apply the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Use a Curling Iron

Now it's time to use a curling iron to create curls in your hair. Choose a curling iron that suits your hair length and the type of curl you want to achieve. Start by dividing your hair into sections and then wrap each section around the curling iron, holding it for a few seconds before releasing it. Repeat this for all sections of your hair.

Step 4: Apply a Hair Spray

Once you have created curls in your hair, apply a hairspray to hold them in place. Choose a hairspray that provides a strong hold but does not make your hair stiff or sticky. Spray the hairspray evenly over your hair, holding the can approximately 6 inches away from your head.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you achieve perfect curls in your hair:

  • Choose a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline coating as it will distribute heat evenly and prevent hair damage.
  • Use a curling iron with a smaller barrel if you want to create tight curls and a larger barrel for loose waves.
  • Do not use too much hair spray as it can make your hair stiff and difficult to style.
  • Always use a heat protectant before using any heating tool on your hair.
  • Practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs

Can I make curls in my hair without a curling iron?

Yes, you can make curls in your hair without a curling iron. You can use a hair straightener, a curling wand, or even braid your hair and leave it overnight to create curls.

How long do curls last in guys hair?

The duration of curls in guys hair depends on various factors such as hair type, length, and the products used. Generally, curls can last for a day or two, but you can prolong their duration by using a strong hold hairspray.

How often should I make curls in my hair?

Making curls in your hair can be damaging, so it's best to avoid doing it too often. You can make curls once or twice a week, depending on your hair type and condition.

Hair Care

Making curls in guys hair can be damaging, which is why it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid using heating tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and dyes on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and health.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and shiny, even after making curls in it.

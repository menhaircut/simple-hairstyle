Does Pixie Cut Suit Round Face Your Ultimate Guide To Pixie Cuts

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle but afraid to try something new? Well, a pixie cut may just be the perfect solution for you! But wait, does a pixie cut suit a round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered with this ultimate guide to pixie cuts.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that typically features short layers on top and longer layers on the bottom. It can be styled in a variety of ways and is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle.

Now, when it comes to round faces, the answer is yes, a pixie cut can definitely suit a round face. In fact, a pixie cut can help to elongate and balance out a round face shape.

How to Get a Pixie Cut

Before you make the leap and get a pixie cut, there are a few things to consider. First, think about your hair texture and face shape. Pixie cuts work best for those with straight or wavy hair and can be customized to suit any face shape.

Next, find a reputable stylist who specializes in short haircuts. They will be able to give you personalized advice on what type of pixie cut will work best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Pixie Cut

If you're ready to take the plunge and get a pixie cut, here is a step-by-step guide to help you prepare:

  1. Find inspiration photos of the type of pixie cut you want to get.
  2. Book a consultation with a stylist to discuss your hair type and face shape.
  3. Prepare your hair by washing and drying it the day before your appointment.
  4. Bring your inspiration photos to your appointment and discuss them with your stylist.
  5. Sit back and let your stylist work their magic!

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Once you've gotten your pixie cut, there are a few things you can do to keep it looking great:

  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality pair of scissors to trim any split ends.
  • Visit your stylist for regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

FAQs

Will a pixie cut make my face look rounder?

No, a pixie cut can actually help to elongate and balance out a round face shape.

Will a pixie cut suit my hair type?

Pixie cuts work best for those with straight or wavy hair, but can be customized to suit any hair type.

How often should I get my pixie cut trimmed?

We recommend getting your pixie cut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and stylish.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

When it comes to hair care for pixie cuts, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

Now that you know all about pixie cuts and how they can suit a round face, are you ready to take the plunge and try out this trendy hairstyle? Go ahead and book that appointment with your stylist today!

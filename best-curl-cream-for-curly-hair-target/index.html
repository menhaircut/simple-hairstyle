How to Choose the Best Curl Cream for Your Curly Hair

Before you start browsing the aisles at Target, it's important to know what to look for in a curl cream. Here are some key factors to consider:

Hair Type

Not all curl creams are created equal. Depending on your hair type (i.e. loose curls, tight coils, thick or fine strands), you'll want to choose a curl cream that's specifically formulated for your hair type.

Ingredients

Look for curl creams that are free of sulfates, parabens, and other harmful ingredients. Instead, opt for products with natural ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and jojoba oil.

Hold

Do you want a curl cream that provides a strong hold or a more natural look? This will depend on your personal preference and the type of hairstyle you're going for.

Best Curl Creams for Curly Hair at Target

Now that you know what to look for in a curl cream, here are some of the best options available at Target:

1. SheaMoisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie

This curl cream is perfect for those with thick, curly hair. It provides a strong hold and helps define curls while also moisturizing and reducing frizz.

2. Cantu Shea Butter for Natural Hair Coconut Curling Cream

This curl cream is great for those with fine or thin curls. It's lightweight and provides a natural-looking hold without weighing down your hair.

3. OGX Locking + Coconut Curls Decadent Creamy Mousse

If you're looking for a mousse instead of a traditional cream, this product is a great option. It provides a light hold and helps enhance curls while also fighting frizz.

How to Use Curl Cream

Now that you've found the perfect curl cream for your hair, here's how to use it:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are specifically formulated for curly hair.

Step 2: Apply Curl Cream

Apply a small amount of curl cream to your damp hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired, either by air-drying or using a diffuser on low heat. Avoid touching your hair too much while it dries to prevent frizz.

Tips for Caring for Curly Hair

In addition to using the right curl cream, here are some other tips for caring for your curly hair:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Curly hair is naturally drier than straight hair, so you don't need to wash it as often. Aim to wash your hair no more than twice a week.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage and damage.

3. Sleep on a Satin Pillowcase

Satin pillowcases are gentler on your hair than cotton, which can cause frizz and breakage.

FAQ

What is the Best Curl Cream for Fine Hair?

The Cantu Shea Butter for Natural Hair Coconut Curling Cream is a great option for those with fine or thin curls.

Can You Use Curl Cream on Wet Hair?

Yes, curl cream can be applied to wet hair to help define curls and reduce frizz.

How Often Should You Use Curl Cream?

You can use curl cream as often as needed, but be sure not to overuse it as this can lead to buildup and weighed-down hair.

Final Thoughts

With the right curl cream and hair care routine, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls. Head to Target and try out some of our recommended curl creams to find the perfect one for your hair type and needs. And don't forget to follow our tips for caring for your curly hair!

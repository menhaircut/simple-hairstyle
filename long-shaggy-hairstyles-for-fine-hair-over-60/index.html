Long Shaggy Hairstyles For Fine Hair Over 60

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair loses its natural thickness and volume, and this can be particularly challenging for those with fine hair. However, with the right haircut, you can still achieve a stylish and youthful look. One of the best options for fine hair over 60 is the long shaggy hairstyle. In this blog post, we will discuss how to achieve this look, step-by-step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions (FAQ), and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The long shaggy hairstyle is a perfect option for those with fine hair over 60. This haircut features layers throughout the hair, adding volume and texture. It is a versatile style that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. The shaggy layers frame the face, softening any fine lines and wrinkles, creating a more youthful appearance.

How to

The long shaggy hairstyle is achieved by adding layers throughout the hair. It is important to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting fine hair to avoid any damage. When choosing a length, consider your face shape and lifestyle. A longer style may be more manageable, but a shorter style can add more volume.

Step by step

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to create volume.
  3. Using a comb, part your hair down the middle.
  4. Starting at the back of your head, take small sections of hair and cut them at a slight angle, creating layers.
  5. Continue cutting layers throughout the hair, making sure to blend them together.
  6. Once all the layers are cut, use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to the hair.
  7. Style your hair as desired, using a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or straighten the hair.
  8. Finish the look with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Choose a stylist who is experienced in cutting fine hair to avoid any damage.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to create volume.
  • Cut the layers at a slight angle to create movement and volume.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to the hair.
  • Style your hair as desired, using a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or straighten the hair.
  • Finish the look with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

FAQ

What face shape is best for the long shaggy hairstyle?

The long shaggy hairstyle is a versatile style that can be adapted to suit any face shape. However, it is particularly flattering for those with round or oval faces.

Can the long shaggy hairstyle be worn with bangs?

Yes, the long shaggy hairstyle can be worn with or without bangs. If you have a high forehead, consider adding bangs to soften your features.

Can the long shaggy hairstyle be styled for formal occasions?

Yes, the long shaggy hairstyle can be styled for formal occasions. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or straighten the hair. Add a hair accessory such as a headband or hairpin to dress up the look.

Hair Care

To maintain the long shaggy hairstyle, it is important to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to create volume. Cut the layers regularly to maintain the style. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to the hair, and style your hair with a curling iron or flat iron as desired.

Overall, the long shaggy hairstyle is a perfect option for those with fine hair over 60. It is a versatile style that can be dressed up or down, and it adds volume and texture to the hair. Use the tips and step-by-step instructions provided to achieve a stylish and youthful look that will make you feel confident and beautiful.

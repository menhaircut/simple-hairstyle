Why Does My Face Look Fat With Short Hair

Written by Daniel Jul 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting a short haircut but afraid that it will make your face look fat? You are not alone. Many women are hesitant to cut their hair short because they fear it will not flatter their face shape. In this article, we will explore why short hair can sometimes make your face look fuller and what you can do about it.

Why Does My Face Look Fat with Short Hair?

There are a few reasons why short hair can make your face look fuller. Firstly, when you have long hair, it can create the illusion of a slimmer face because it elongates your features. When you cut your hair short, however, your face is more exposed, and this can make your cheeks, chin, and jawline appear wider or rounder than they actually are.

Another reason why short hair can sometimes make your face look fat is that it draws attention to your facial features. A shorter haircut can highlight your cheeks, chin, and jawline, which can be great if you have defined features, but not so great if you have a rounder face shape.

How Does Your Face Shape Affect How Short Hair Looks?

When it comes to short haircuts, your face shape plays a crucial role in determining what style will flatter you. If you have a round face shape, for example, you should avoid blunt cuts that end at your jawline because they will only emphasize the roundness of your face. Instead, opt for a layered or angled cut that adds height and length to your face.

If you have a square face shape, you should avoid straight, blunt cuts that end at your chin because they can make your face look boxy. Instead, go for a layered or textured cut that softens your features.

How Can the Style of Your Haircut Affect Your Face Shape?

The style of your haircut can also affect how fat your face looks. As mentioned earlier, blunt cuts that end at your jawline or chin can make your face look wider or rounder than it actually is. On the other hand, layered or textured cuts can add movement and dimension to your hair, which can make your face appear slimmer.

Does the Length of Your Hair Affect How Fat Your Face Looks?

Yes, the length of your hair can also affect how fat your face looks. Short haircuts that end above your chin or ears can make your face look wider because they emphasize your cheeks and jawline. On the other hand, longer haircuts that end below your chin can elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

Tips for Choosing a Short Haircut to Flatter Your Face Shape

Here are some tips to help you choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape:

  • If you have a round face shape, go for a layered or angled cut that adds height and length to your face.
  • If you have a square face shape, opt for a layered or textured cut that softens your features.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your jawline or chin if you want to make your face appear slimmer.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create the illusion of dimension and movement.

FAQs

Q: Can short hair make my face look longer?

A: Yes, a longer haircut can elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Q: What kind of short haircut is best for a round face shape?

A: A layered or angled cut that adds height and length to your face is best for a round face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you take care of your short hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Visit your stylist regularly to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Now that you know why your face may look fat with short hair and how to choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape, you can confidently make the chop. Remember to take care of your hair properly to keep it looking healthy and stylish.

How To Style My Super Short Pixie Cut

Written by Frank Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and ready for a drastic change? A super short pixie cut might be just what you need! Not only is it a bold statement, but it also requires minimal styling and maintenance. In this article, we'll discuss how to style your super short pixie cut and provide some helpful tips to keep it looking fabulous.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style My Super Short Pixie Cut
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling a Super Short Pixie Cut

How to Style My Super Short Pixie Cut

The key to styling a super short pixie cut is to embrace the natural texture and shape of your hair. With the right products and techniques, you can create a chic and effortless look that's perfect for any occasion.

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Once your hair is dry, you can begin styling.

2. Apply a styling product.

To add texture and hold to your hair, apply a styling product like a wax or pomade. Rub a small amount between your fingertips and distribute evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and ends.

3. Use your fingers to style.

One of the great things about a super short pixie cut is that you don't need any fancy tools to style it. Simply use your fingers to shape your hair in the desired direction. If you want to add volume, use your fingers to lift your hair at the roots.

4. Finish with hairspray.

To keep your hair in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Hold the can about 10 inches away from your head and spray evenly.

Step by Step Guide

If you're new to styling a super short pixie cut, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

1. Wash and dry your hair.

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and use a blow dryer on low heat to dry it completely.

2. Apply a styling product.

Apply a small amount of wax or pomade to your fingertips and distribute evenly throughout your hair.

3. Use your fingers to style.

Use your fingers to shape your hair in the desired direction. If you want to add volume, use your fingers to lift your hair at the roots.

4. Finish with hairspray.

Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Styling a Super Short Pixie Cut

Here are some additional tips to help you style your super short pixie cut:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to play with texture and volume.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Accessorize with headbands or hair clips to add some flair.

FAQs

Q: Will a super short pixie cut work for my face shape?

A: A super short pixie cut can work for many different face shapes, but it's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine what will look best on you.

Q: How often should I get my super short pixie cut trimmed?

A: You should get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your pixie cut.

Hair Care for a Super Short Pixie Cut

A super short pixie cut requires minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips to help:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

Now that you know how to style your super short pixie cut, it's time to rock your new look with confidence. With the right products and techniques, you can create a chic and effortless style that's perfect for any occasion.

Where To Find A Barber In Witcher 3 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Oct 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're playing Witcher 3 and want to change your character's hairstyle, you might be wondering where to find a barber. Luckily, there are several locations in the game where you can get a fresh cut or trim. In this guide, we'll go over where to find a barber in Witcher 3 and give you some tips on how to get the most out of your haircuts.

How to Find a Barber in Witcher 3

Firstly, it's important to note that not all barbers in the game offer the same services. Some will only let you change your hairstyle, while others will also let you trim your beard or shave your head. Here are some of the places where you can find a barber in Witcher 3:

1. Novigrad

Novigrad is one of the biggest cities in Witcher 3, and it's home to several barbershops. You can find them in various parts of the city, including Hierarch Square and the Bits. One of the best barbershops in Novigrad is The Golden Sturgeon, which is located near the harbor.

2. Oxenfurt

Oxenfurt is another city where you can find a barber. The barbershop is located near the main square, and it's called The Kingfisher.

3. Skellige

If you're in Skellige, you can find a barber in Kaer Trolde. The barbershop is located near the marketplace, and it's called The Trolled Man's Tavern.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Haircut in Witcher 3

Now that you know where to find a barber in Witcher 3, here's a step-by-step guide to getting a haircut:

Step 1: Find a Barbershop

As we mentioned earlier, there are several barbershops in Witcher 3. Choose one that's convenient for you and head there.

Step 2: Talk to the Barber

Once you're in the barbershop, talk to the barber to see what services they offer. You can ask them to change your hairstyle, trim your beard, or shave your head.

Step 3: Choose Your Style

After you've talked to the barber, they'll present you with a list of hairstyles to choose from. Pick one that you like and confirm your choice.

Step 4: Pay the Barber

Once the barber has finished your haircut, you'll need to pay them. The cost of the haircut will depend on the barbershop and the services you chose.

Tips for Getting the Most Out of Your Haircuts

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your haircuts in Witcher 3:

1. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to try out different hairstyles and see what works best for your character. It's a great way to change up your look and keep things fresh.

2. Don't Forget about Your Beard

If you're playing as Geralt, you have a beard that grows over time. Make sure to keep it trimmed and neat by visiting a barber regularly.

3. Save Your Game before Getting a Haircut

Before you get a haircut, make sure to save your game. This way, if you don't like the new style, you can reload your save and try again.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Change My Hairstyle at Any Time?

Yes, you can visit a barber at any time to change your hairstyle or trim your beard.

How Much Does a Haircut Cost?

The cost of a haircut will depend on the barbershop and the services you choose. It can range from a few crowns to several hundred.

Can I Shave My Head?

Yes, some barbershops will allow you to shave your head if you want a completely bald look.

Hair Care in Witcher 3

Aside from getting haircuts and trimming your beard, there's not much else you can do in terms of hair care in Witcher 3. However, it's important to keep your character clean and well-groomed to avoid negative effects like lowered charisma.

That's everything you need to know about where to find a barber in Witcher 3. Whether you're looking for a new style or just need to tidy up your beard, there are plenty of barbershops to choose from. Happy styling!

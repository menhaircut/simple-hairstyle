Short Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair And Oval Face A Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Aug 11, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick wavy hair and an oval face, you might sometimes find it difficult to style your hair. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll be discussing the best short hairstyles for thick wavy hair and oval face, as well as how to style them and take care of your hair. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Short Hair for Thick Wavy Hair and Oval Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Short hairstyles are perfect for people with thick wavy hair and oval face because they can help to frame your face and tame your waves. This type of hair is known for its volume and texture, which can make it challenging to style. However, with the right cut and styling techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and effortless look.

How to Style Short Hair for Thick Wavy Hair and Oval Face

The first step in styling short hair for thick wavy hair and oval face is to choose the right haircut. You want to choose a cut that will complement your face shape and enhance your natural texture. Some popular short haircuts for this hair type include:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob cut
  • Layered cut
  • Asymmetrical cut

Once you have chosen your haircut, you can start to style your hair. One of the easiest ways to style short hair for thick wavy hair and oval face is to use a styling product that will enhance your natural texture. You can use a mousse, gel, or spray to add volume and definition to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style short hair for thick wavy hair and oval face:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for thick and wavy hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product of your choice.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.
  4. Use a curling iron to define your curls if needed.
  5. Finish off your look with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Short Hair for Thick Wavy Hair and Oval Face

Here are some tips for styling short hair for thick wavy hair and oval face:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and frizz-free.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Try different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits your face shape and personal style.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for thick wavy hair and oval face?

The best haircut for thick wavy hair and oval face is one that complements your face shape and enhances your natural texture. Some popular short haircuts for this hair type include the pixie cut, bob cut, layered cut, and asymmetrical cut.

Can I straighten my thick wavy hair?

Yes, you can straighten your thick wavy hair using a flat iron or a straightening brush. However, it’s important to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

How can I prevent frizz in my thick wavy hair?

You can prevent frizz in your thick wavy hair by using a leave-in conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and using a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair. You can also try using a serum or oil to tame any flyaways or frizz.

Hair Care for Short Hair for Thick Wavy Hair and Oval Face

To keep your short hair looking its best, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care for short hair for thick wavy hair and oval face:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for thick and wavy hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can achieve beautiful and effortless short hair for thick wavy hair and oval face. Experiment with different styles and products to find the ones that work best for you, and don’t be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice.

How To Cut Baby Bangs On Girl A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a cute and stylish way to switch up your little girl's hairstyle, baby bangs might just be the answer. Baby bangs are short, straight bangs that sit just above the eyebrows, giving your child a playful and youthful look. But cutting baby bangs on a girl can be tricky, especially if you have no experience. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to cut baby bangs on a girl, as well as tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect look.

How to Cut Baby Bangs on Girl

Cutting baby bangs on a girl requires some careful attention, but it's not impossible to do at home. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Once you have your supplies ready, you can follow these steps to cut baby bangs on your little girl:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by wetting your child's hair with the spray bottle. This will make it easier to see where the bangs should fall.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape. The base of the triangle should be at the top of the forehead, and the point should be in the middle of the head.
  3. Clip the rest of the hair back and out of the way.
  4. Using the comb, comb the hair in the triangle forward and down over your child's forehead.
  5. Using your scissors, make a small cut in the middle of the triangle, about 1/4 inch above the eyebrows.
  6. Cut small sections of hair at a time, working your way out to the corners of the triangle.
  7. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you reach the desired length for the bangs.
  8. Use the comb to check that the bangs are even on both sides.
  9. Trim any stray hairs or uneven sections.
  10. Style the bangs as desired.

Tips for Cutting Baby Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect baby bangs:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way out to the ends of the hair.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as you can always trim more later.
  • Use a comb to measure the length of the bangs and make sure they're even on both sides.
  • If your child is fidgety, consider having someone hold her still or distract her with a toy or game.
  • For best results, cut the bangs when your child's hair is dry and styled as usual.

FAQs

Q: How short should baby bangs be?

A: Baby bangs should sit just above the eyebrows, and be no shorter than 1/4 inch above the brow line.

Q: Can I cut baby bangs on curly hair?

A: Yes, you can cut baby bangs on curly hair, but it may be more difficult to achieve a straight and even look. Consider straightening the hair before cutting.

Q: How often should I trim baby bangs?

A: It depends on how fast your child's hair grows, but you may need to trim the bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length and shape.

Hair Care for Baby Bangs

Once you've cut your child's baby bangs, you'll want to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips for baby bangs:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy.
  • Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to keep the bangs in place.
  • Use a soft-bristled brush or comb to style the bangs.
  • Don't use hot styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, on the bangs.
  • Trim any stray hairs or split ends as needed to maintain the shape of the bangs.

Cutting baby bangs on a girl can be a fun and rewarding experience, as long as you take your time and follow these steps and tips. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect look for your little girl.

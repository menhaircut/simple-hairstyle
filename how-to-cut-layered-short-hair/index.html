How To Cut Layered Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting your own hair can be an intimidating task, especially when it comes to layered short hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, it can be done efficiently and affordably. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps to cut layered short hair, as well as provide tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to ensure you achieve the desired look.

How to Cut Layered Short Hair

Layered short hair is a versatile style that can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. Before you begin, it's important to gather the necessary tools: scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and allowing it to air dry or blow dry. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use hair clips to section your hair into manageable sections. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Hold the bottom layer between your index and middle fingers and cut straight across. Repeat this step for each section, gradually moving up towards the top layer.

Step 4: Cut the Top Layer

Use the same technique to cut the top layer, but cut at an angle to create layers.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Use the scissors to blend the layers together, removing any harsh lines.

Tips for Cutting Layered Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting layered short hair:

  • Start with small cuts and gradually work your way up.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't cut too much at once, as it can be difficult to fix.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you, especially if it's your first time cutting your own hair.

FAQs

Can I cut my own layered short hair?

Yes, it's possible to cut your own layered short hair with the right tools and techniques.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length and shape.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always go to a professional stylist to fix it or try to fix it yourself by blending the layers together.

Hair Care Advice

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the desired style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as it can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the desired length and shape.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Cutting layered short hair can be a fun and rewarding experience, as long as you have the right tools and follow the proper techniques. With a little practice, you can achieve the desired look and save money on salon visits.

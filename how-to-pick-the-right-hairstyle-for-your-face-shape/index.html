How To Pick The Right Hairstyle For Your Face Shape

Written by Luffy Oct 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you ever find yourself scrolling through Pinterest or Instagram, admiring hairstyles you wish you could pull off? You're not alone! But before making a drastic change to your hair, it's important to consider your face shape. Not all hairstyles are created equal, and what may look great on one person may not flatter your unique features. In this article, we'll break down how to pick the right hairstyle for your face shape, so you can rock a look that suits you best.

Description

Choosing a hairstyle that flatters your face shape can be a game-changer. It can enhance your features, balance out proportions, and even make you look younger or more mature. By taking a few minutes to determine your face shape and the hairstyles that suit it best, you'll have a better idea of what to ask for at your next salon appointment.

How to Pick the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. There are six main face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Once you've determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that accentuates your best features and balances out any proportions that may be off. Here are some general guidelines for each face shape:

Round Face Shape

A round face shape is characterized by soft, rounded features with a width and length that are roughly equal. If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that adds length and definition to your face. This can be achieved with a side-swept bang, layered haircut, or a hairstyle that's longer than chin-length.

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and jaw. Lucky for you, an oval face shape is considered the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. You can pull off just about any haircut or style, from a pixie cut to long, flowing locks.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape is characterized by a defined jawline and an equally wide forehead, cheekbones, and jaw. If you have a square face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that softens your features. This can be achieved with a side part, soft layers, or a wispy, textured haircut.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape is wider at the temples and narrows at the chin, with a pointy chin and a high hairline. If you have a heart face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that balances out your features. This can be achieved with long, side-swept bangs, a layered bob, or a pixie cut with volume at the crown.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and jawline, with wider cheekbones. If you have a diamond face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that adds width to your forehead and jawline while minimizing the width of your cheekbones. This can be achieved with a side-swept bang, voluminous curls, or a layered bob.

Triangle Face Shape

A triangle face shape is wider at the jawline and narrows at the forehead. If you have a triangle face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that balances out your features. This can be achieved with a side part, long layers, or a textured bob.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape and the hairstyles that suit it best, here's a step by step guide for choosing the right hairstyle:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Research hairstyles that flatter your face shape
  3. Consider your hair type and texture
  4. Talk to your hairstylist about the best haircut and style for you
  5. Bring pictures or examples of hairstyles you like
  6. Be open to suggestions from your hairstylist
  7. Maintain your hairstyle with regular trims and styling
  8. Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to find what works best for you

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on styling
  • Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how different hairstyles look on you
  • Don't be afraid to try something new, but also be realistic about what will work for you
  • Take care of your hair with regular washing, conditioning, and styling
  • Use heat protectant products when using hot tools

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape:

Q: Can I still wear a hairstyle that doesn't suit my face shape?

A: Of course! Ultimately, the most important thing is that you feel confident and happy with your hair. However, choosing a hairstyle that flatters your face shape can enhance your natural beauty and make styling easier.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy ends and prevent breakage.

Hair Care for Your New Hairstyle

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your new look:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, or use a heat protectant product
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine with a hat or swim cap
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle wet hair
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can seem daunting, but with a little research and guidance from your hairstylist, you can find a look that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories to find what works best for you.

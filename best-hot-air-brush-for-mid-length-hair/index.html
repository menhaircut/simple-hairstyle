Best Hot Air Brush For Mid Length Hair Your Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a way to style your mid length hair without having to visit the salon every time, a hot air brush might just be the perfect solution for you. But with so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best hot air brush for mid length hair.

In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about hot air brushes and help you find the perfect one for your mid length hair.

What is a Hot Air Brush?

A hot air brush is a styling tool that combines a hair dryer and a round brush in one. It blows hot air while you style your hair with the brush, making it easier to create volume, curls, and waves.

How to Choose the Best Hot Air Brush for Mid Length Hair

Here are some factors to consider when choosing the best hot air brush for mid length hair:

Barrel Size

The barrel size of the hot air brush will determine the size of the curls or waves you can create. For mid length hair, a barrel size of 1.5 to 2 inches is recommended.

Heat Settings

Look for a hot air brush with multiple heat settings, so you can adjust the temperature according to your hair type and texture.

Bristles

The bristles of the hot air brush should be gentle on your hair and scalp. Look for brushes with natural bristles or those made of nylon.

Brand

Choose a hot air brush from a reputable brand with good customer reviews.

How to Use a Hot Air Brush on Mid Length Hair

Follow these steps to use a hot air brush on your mid length hair:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair until it's about 80% dry.

Step 2: Apply heat protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to protect it from heat damage.

Step 3: Divide your hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips, starting from the bottom.

Step 4: Start styling

Take a section of hair and wrap it around the barrel of the hot air brush, starting from the root. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat until you've styled all sections of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Using a Hot Air Brush on Mid Length Hair

Here are some tips to get the most out of your hot air brush:

- Use a heat protectant to prevent heat damage. - Start with a low heat setting and gradually increase if needed. - Use a comb to detangle your hair before styling. - Hold the brush vertically for more volume. - Use the cool shot button to set your style in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a hot air brush on wet hair?

It's not recommended to use a hot air brush on wet hair, as it can cause damage. Make sure your hair is at least 80% dry before using a hot air brush.

Can I use a hot air brush every day?

It's not recommended to use a hot air brush every day, as it can cause heat damage to your hair. Use it once or twice a week and give your hair a break in between.

How do I clean my hot air brush?

Make sure your hot air brush is unplugged and cool before cleaning. Use a damp cloth to wipe the bristles and barrel, and remove any hair or debris.

Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

To keep your mid length hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

- Wash your hair every 2-3 days. - Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. - Apply a hair mask once a week. - Avoid using hot tools every day. - Trim your hair every 8-12 weeks to prevent split ends.

With the right hot air brush and proper hair care, you can easily style your mid length hair at home and achieve salon-worthy results.

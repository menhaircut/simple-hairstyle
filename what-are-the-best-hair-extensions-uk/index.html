Description

Hair extensions are a great way to add volume and length to your hair. They come in different types, textures, and colors to suit your needs. In the UK, there are many hair extensions available in the market, but which one is the best for you?

In this guide, we will help you choose the best hair extensions UK that will suit your hair type and lifestyle. We will also provide you with tips on how to care for your hair extensions to make them last longer.

How to Choose the Right Hair Extensions

Choosing the right hair extensions can be overwhelming, especially if you are new to it. Here are some factors that you need to consider:

1. Hair Type

The first thing that you need to consider is your hair type. Hair extensions come in different textures, such as straight, wavy, and curly. Choose the texture that matches your natural hair so that the extensions will blend seamlessly.

2. Length and Volume

Determine the length and volume that you want to achieve. Hair extensions come in different lengths, from 14 inches to 26 inches. The volume will depend on the number of wefts that you will use.

3. Quality

Choose hair extensions that are made of high-quality hair. Remy hair is a popular choice because the cuticles are intact, and the hair is soft and shiny. Synthetic hair is cheaper, but it does not look as natural as human hair.

4. Attachment Method

There are different attachment methods for hair extensions, such as clip-ins, tape-ins, sew-ins, and micro-ring. Choose the attachment method that is comfortable for you and will not damage your natural hair.

Step-by-Step Guide in Applying Hair Extensions

Once you have chosen the right hair extensions, it is time to apply them. Here is a step-by-step guide:

1. Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before applying the extensions. Make sure that your hair is free from tangles and knots.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small parts using a comb. Clip the top part of your hair, so it will not interfere with the application of the extensions.

3. Attach the Extensions

Attach the extensions to your hair using your chosen attachment method. Make sure that the wefts are evenly distributed and not too close to your scalp.

4. Blend the Extensions

Blend the extensions with your natural hair using a comb or your fingers. Style your hair as desired.

Tips in Maintaining Your Hair Extensions

Here are some tips to make your hair extensions last longer:

1. Brush Your Hair Extensions

Brush your hair extensions regularly using a soft-bristled brush. Start at the ends and work your way up to avoid tangling the hair.

2. Wash Your Hair Extensions

Wash your hair extensions every two weeks using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Rinse them thoroughly and let them air dry.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling wands, on your hair extensions. If you need to use them, apply a heat protectant spray first.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long do hair extensions last?

Hair extensions can last up to six months, depending on how well you take care of them.

2. Can I color my hair extensions?

It is not recommended to color your hair extensions because they have already been treated. If you need to change the color, it is best to buy new hair extensions.

Hair Care Tips for Your Hair Extensions

Here are some additional hair care tips for your hair extensions:

1. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Use a silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent tangling of your hair extensions while you sleep.

2. Braid Your Hair Extensions

Braid your hair extensions before going to bed to prevent tangling and breakage.

3. Trim Your Hair Extensions

Trim your hair extensions regularly to get rid of split ends and keep them looking healthy.

Choosing the best hair extensions UK can be challenging, but with the right information, you can make an informed decision. Follow these tips and maintain your hair extensions regularly to make them last longer.

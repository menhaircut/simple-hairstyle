What Haircut For Fat Face The Ultimate Guide

Description

Having a round or fat face can be a challenge when it comes to finding the right haircut. The wrong cut can make your face look even rounder and chubby. That's why it's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and enhances your features. In this article, we'll guide you through what haircut for fat face, including tips on how to choose the best style for you and how to maintain it.

How to

When picking a hairstyle for a round face, you should aim to add height and volume to your hair. This will help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Avoid styles that are too flat or too short, as they can make your face look wider. Layered haircuts are perfect for round faces, as they create movement and texture.

Short Haircuts

If you want to go for a shorter haircut, choose a bob or a pixie cut. Both styles can look great on a round face if done right. A bob that falls just below the chin is perfect for adding length and creating a more angular look. A pixie cut with volume on top can also help elongate your face.

Medium-Length Haircuts

If you prefer medium-length hair, go for a layered cut that falls just below your shoulders. This will create the illusion of a longer face and add movement to your hair. You can also add side-swept bangs to soften your features.

Long Haircuts

If you want to keep your long hair, go for a layered cut with face-framing layers that start at your chin. This will create a more angular look and draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

Step by step

Here are the steps to follow when getting a haircut for a fat face:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Show your hairstylist pictures of hairstyles you like and discuss what would work best for you.
  3. Choose a style that adds height and volume to your hair.
  4. Avoid styles that are too flat or too short.
  5. Consider adding layers to create movement and texture.
  6. Maintain your haircut with regular trims to keep it looking fresh.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a fat face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use volumizing products to add height to your hair.
  • Avoid slicked-back styles, as they can make your face look wider.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face. Just make sure to add volume at the crown of your head to elongate your face.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

A: No, you don't have to avoid bangs if you have a round face. Side-swept bangs can actually help soften your features and create a more angular look.

Q: Can I get a bob if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can get a bob if you have a round face. Just make sure it falls below your chin and has some layers to create movement.

Hair Care

Maintaining a healthy head of hair is essential to making any haircut look great. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for a fat face, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your hairstyle.

Long On Top Short On Sides Wavy Hair A Trendy Look For Men

Are you tired of your boring haircut and want to go for something trendy and stylish? Then, long on top short on sides wavy hair can be a perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has gained immense popularity among men in recent years. It's a versatile haircut that works well for both formal and casual occasions. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle.

Description

The long on top short on sides wavy hair is a classic men's hairstyle that has been around for decades. It's a popular haircut that never goes out of style. The hairstyle features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides and back. The top part can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured, depending on your preference. The wavy texture adds a natural and effortless look to the hairstyle, making it perfect for any occasion.

How To Get Long on Top Short on Sides Wavy Hair

If you're looking to get long on top short on sides wavy hair, you can achieve it by following these steps:

Step 1: Find a Good Hairstylist

The first step to getting this hairstyle is finding a good hairstylist who has experience in cutting men's hair. Look for a hairstylist who understands your hair type and can suggest the best haircut based on your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

The next step is to decide on the length of your hair. The top part should be longer than the sides and back. You can choose the length depending on your preference and face shape.

Step 3: Create the Waves

The wavy texture is what makes this hairstyle unique. You can create the waves by using a curling iron or a hair straightener. Wrap a section of your hair around the curling iron or hair straightener and hold it for a few seconds. Repeat the process until you achieve the desired waves.

Step By Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style long on top short on sides wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair, leaving it clean and fresh.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel, as it can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair, such as mousse or styling cream. This will help to define the waves and hold the hairstyle in place.

Step 4: Create the Waves

Use a curling iron or hair straightener to create the waves. Take small sections of your hair and wrap them around the curling iron or hair straightener. Hold it for a few seconds and release. Repeat the process until you've created waves throughout your hair.

Step 5: Style the Top

Style the top part of your hair as desired, such as slicked back or messy. Use a comb or your fingers to create the desired look.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the look with a hairspray to hold the hairstyle in place. Choose a hairspray that provides a natural hold and doesn't make your hair look stiff or greasy.

Tips To Maintain Long on Top Short on Sides Wavy Hair

To maintain long on top short on sides wavy hair, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid over-drying.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length and shape of the hairstyle.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Q: Can I get long on top short on sides wavy hair if I have straight hair?

A: Yes, you can create the waves using a curling iron or hair straightener, even if you have straight hair.

Q: Can I style long on top short on sides wavy hair for a formal occasion?

A: Yes, this hairstyle is versatile and can be styled for both formal and casual occasions.

Hair Care Tips For Long on Top Short on Sides Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your long on top short on sides wavy hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip the natural oils from your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as hair bleach or hair dye, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and health.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a hat or scarf.

Long on top short on sides wavy hair is a trendy and stylish look for men. It's a versatile haircut that works well for any occasion. By following the above tips and steps, you can achieve this hairstyle and maintain it for a long time. So, go ahead and try this look today!

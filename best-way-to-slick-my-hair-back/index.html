Best Way To Slick My Hair Back

Written by Robby Jun 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a classic and timeless hairstyle, slicking your hair back might just be the perfect choice. This style has been around for centuries and is still popular today. Not only does it look sleek and sophisticated, but it's also practical for keeping hair out of your face. However, achieving the perfect slick back can be a tricky process. In this article, we will discuss the best way to slick your hair back in a relaxed English language.

Description

Slicking your hair back involves using a styling product to smooth your hair into a neat and tidy style. The finished look should have a glossy and polished finish. This hairstyle is perfect for both men and women and can be worn in a casual or formal setting.

How to Slick Your Hair Back

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to slick your hair back:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step is to start with clean and dry hair. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and make sure it's completely dry before moving onto the next step.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product of your choice to your hair. A pomade or gel is ideal for slicking your hair back. Use a small amount and work it through your hair from root to tip. Make sure to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Take a fine-toothed comb and comb your hair back. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way to the back of your head. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction you want it to lay.

Step 4: Smooth Your Hair

Use a bristle brush to smooth your hair down. Use gentle strokes to smooth out any bumps or kinks in your hair. Make sure to brush your hair in the direction you want it to lay.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your slicked back hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold your hair in place and prevent any flyaways.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  • Use a small amount of styling product to avoid a greasy look.
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Use a fine-toothed comb to ensure your hair is evenly distributed.
  • Smooth out any bumps or kinks with a bristle brush.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to finish off your style.

FAQs

1. Can I slick my hair back if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still slick your hair back if you have short hair. Use a small amount of styling product and a comb to smooth your hair back.

2. Will slicking my hair back damage it?

No, slicking your hair back will not damage it. However, overuse of styling products can lead to product buildup, which can be damaging to your hair.

3. How long will a slicked back hairstyle last?

A slicked back hairstyle can last all day with the right styling products and techniques.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

To keep your hair healthy when sporting a slicked back hairstyle, make sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much styling product, as this can lead to product buildup. Additionally, you may want to consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know the best way to slick your hair back, you can confidently rock this classic and sophisticated style. Remember to use the right products and techniques to achieve the perfect slicked back look.

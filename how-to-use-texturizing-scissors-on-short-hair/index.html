How To Use Texturizing Scissors On Short Hair

Written by Bardi Sep 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to add texture and volume to your short hair? Texturizing scissors may be the solution you need! These scissors are designed to create texture and movement in hair without removing too much length. In this article, we will show you how to use texturizing scissors on short hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Texturizing Scissors on Short Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Texturizing Scissors on Short Hair
  • FAQs

Description

Texturizing scissors are a type of hair cutting tool that are used to create texture and movement in hair. They have teeth-like blades that remove some of the hair while leaving other strands intact. This creates a more natural, lived-in look that is perfect for short hairstyles.

How to Use Texturizing Scissors on Short Hair

Before using texturizing scissors on your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. It is also important to have a clear idea of the style you want to achieve.

Hold the texturizing scissors at a slight angle and start cutting small sections of hair. Be sure to only cut a little bit at a time, as you can always go back and cut more if needed.

As you cut, keep the scissors moving in a back-and-forth motion to create a textured effect. Be sure to work evenly throughout your hair to avoid any uneven patches.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to use texturizing scissors on short hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Decide on the style you want to achieve.
  3. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  4. Hold the texturizing scissors at a slight angle.
  5. Start cutting small sections of hair.
  6. Keep the scissors moving in a back-and-forth motion.
  7. Work evenly throughout your hair.
  8. Check your progress as you go.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Using Texturizing Scissors on Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when using texturizing scissors on short hair:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Be sure to work evenly throughout your hair to avoid any uneven patches.
  • Use a comb to help guide your scissors and create a more even cut.
  • Don't cut too much at once, as it is easier to take off more hair than to add it back.
  • Take breaks as needed to avoid hand fatigue.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about using texturizing scissors on short hair:

How often should I use texturizing scissors on my hair?

This depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Some people use texturizing scissors every time they get a haircut, while others only use them occasionally.

Can texturizing scissors be used on all hair types?

Yes, texturizing scissors can be used on all hair types, but they may not work as well on very curly or coarse hair.

Can I use regular scissors instead of texturizing scissors?

Regular scissors can be used to cut hair, but they will not create the same textured effect as texturizing scissors.

Hair Care After Using Texturizing Scissors on Short Hair

After using texturizing scissors on your short hair, it is important to take good care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to use texturizing scissors on short hair, you can achieve the textured, voluminous look you've been dreaming of. Remember to take your time and work carefully, and you'll have a beautiful new hairstyle in no time!

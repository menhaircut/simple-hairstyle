Can You Get Faux Locs With Short Hair

Written by William Sep 26, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have short hair, you might be wondering if it’s possible to get faux locs. The good news is that the answer is yes! Faux locs are a great way to switch up your look and give your hair a break from styling. In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know about getting faux locs with short hair.

Description

Faux locs are a protective hairstyle that involves wrapping synthetic hair around your natural hair. This process creates the illusion of real locs without the commitment or maintenance. Faux locs can be done with various lengths of hair, including short hair.

How To

The process of getting faux locs with short hair is similar to getting them with longer hair. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Hair

Choose the type of hair you want to use for your faux locs. Synthetic hair is the most popular choice, but you can also use human hair. Make sure to choose a hair texture that matches your natural hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small parts. The smaller the sections, the more natural your faux locs will look. Use a rat-tail comb to section your hair.

Step 3: Wrap Your Hair

Take a small section of your natural hair and wrap it around the synthetic hair. Use a crochet hook to pull the synthetic hair through the section of your natural hair. Then, wrap the synthetic hair around the natural hair to create a faux loc. Repeat this process until all of your hair is wrapped.

Step 4: Set Your Faux Locs

Once all of your hair is wrapped, set your faux locs by dipping them in hot water. This will help the synthetic hair hold its shape.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the best faux locs with short hair:

  • Choose a hair texture that matches your natural hair.
  • Section your hair into small parts for a more natural look.
  • Use a crochet hook to pull the synthetic hair through your natural hair.
  • Dip your faux locs in hot water to set them.

FAQ

Can I get faux locs with relaxed hair?

Yes, you can get faux locs with relaxed hair. Just make sure your hair is healthy enough to handle the process.

How long do faux locs last?

Faux locs can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks, depending on how well you take care of them.

Can I wash my faux locs?

Yes, you can wash your faux locs. Use a gentle shampoo and avoid rubbing your scalp too hard.

Hair Care

When you have faux locs, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep them looking fresh. Here are a few tips:

  • Keep your scalp moisturized with oil.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your locs.
  • Wrap your hair at night to prevent frizz.

Now that you know you can get faux locs with short hair, it’s time to switch up your look! Whether you’re looking for a temporary change or a long-term protective style, faux locs are a great option.

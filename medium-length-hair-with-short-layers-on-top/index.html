Medium Length Hair With Short Layers On Top A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Nov 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both stylish and low-maintenance, consider medium length hair with short layers on top. This versatile haircut can be adapted to suit a variety of face shapes and hair types, and it's easy to style at home with just a few simple tools and products. In this article, we'll take you through everything you need to know about medium length hair with short layers on top, including how to get the look, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

Medium length hair with short layers on top typically features hair that falls between the chin and the shoulders, with shorter layers cut around the crown of the head. This creates volume and texture at the top of the head, while the longer length provides flexibility for styling. This haircut works particularly well for those with fine or thin hair, as the layers can add the illusion of thickness and body. However, it can also be adapted for those with thicker or curlier hair, with the layers helping to reduce weight and prevent excessive bulkiness.

How to Get the Look

The first step to getting medium length hair with short layers on top is to find a hairstylist who is experienced in this type of cut. Bring a few photos of the style you're looking for to your appointment, and be prepared to discuss your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle with your stylist. They will then be able to tailor the cut to suit your individual needs and preferences.

Once you've had your haircut, you can style your hair at home using a few basic tools and products. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are tailored to your hair type. Towel-dry your hair, then apply a heat protectant spray and blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and smoothness. You can then use a curling wand or straightener to add waves or curls, or leave your hair straight for a sleeker look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting medium length hair with short layers on top:

  1. Find a hairstylist who is experienced in this type of cut.
  2. Bring photos of the style you're looking for to your appointment.
  3. Discuss your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle with your stylist.
  4. Have your hair cut to the desired length, with shorter layers cut around the crown of the head.
  5. Wash and condition your hair with products that are tailored to your hair type.
  6. Towel-dry your hair, then apply a heat protectant spray.
  7. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and smoothness.
  8. Use a curling wand or straightener to add waves or curls, or leave your hair straight for a sleeker look.
  9. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your medium length hair with short layers on top:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that are tailored to your hair type to keep your hair looking healthy and nourished.
  • Invest in a good heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.
  • Experiment with different styling products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to find the ones that work best for your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.
  • Play around with different partings and hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to change up your look.

FAQ

Is medium length hair with short layers on top suitable for all face shapes?

While this haircut can be adapted to suit a variety of face shapes, it may not be the best choice for those with very round or square faces, as the layers can accentuate these features. However, your hairstylist will be able to advise you on the best way to tailor the cut to flatter your face shape.

Can I still wear my hair up with this haircut?

Absolutely! The longer length at the back of the haircut provides flexibility for styling, so you can still wear your hair up in a variety of styles, such as a ponytail or bun.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows, but as a general rule, it's a good idea to get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length hair with short layers on top looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with products that are tailored to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too frequently, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging at your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock medium length hair with short layers on top like a pro. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance everyday style or something a little more glam, this haircut is sure to become your new go-to. So why not book an appointment with your hairstylist today and give it a try?

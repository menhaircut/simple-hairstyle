How To Shave Back And Sides Of Head A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and clean look, shaving the back and sides of your head can be a great option. However, it can also be a bit intimidating if you've never done it before. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step-by-step to help you achieve a perfectly shaved head.

Description

Shaving your head can be a great way to change up your look, especially if you're tired of dealing with hair every day. However, shaving can be a bit tricky, especially when it comes to the back and sides of your head. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a clean and polished look that will have you feeling confident and stylish.

How to Shave Back and Sides of Head

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you get started, you'll need to gather all the tools you'll need. This includes a quality razor, shaving cream or gel, a mirror, and a towel. You may also want to use a trimmer or clippers to trim your hair down before you begin shaving.

Step 2: Trim Your Hair

Using your trimmer or clippers, trim your hair down to a manageable length. This will make it easier to shave and will help prevent clogging your razor.

Step 3: Apply Shaving Cream

Apply a generous amount of shaving cream or gel to the back and sides of your head. Make sure to cover all the areas you'll be shaving.

Step 4: Begin Shaving

Using your razor, begin shaving the back of your head, starting at the nape of your neck and working your way up. Use slow, even strokes and be careful not to apply too much pressure. Rinse the razor frequently to prevent clogging.

Step 5: Move to the Sides

Once you've finished shaving the back of your head, move on to the sides. Use the same slow, even strokes and be careful not to nick your ears or scalp. Rinse the razor frequently.

Step 6: Rinse and Repeat

After you've finished shaving, rinse your head thoroughly with warm water. Check for any missed spots and touch up as needed.

Tips for Shaving Back and Sides of Head

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best possible shave:

  • Use a quality razor to prevent irritation and ingrown hairs.
  • Shave in the shower to help soften your hair and make it easier to shave.
  • Take your time and use slow, even strokes.
  • Rinse your razor frequently to prevent clogging.
  • Apply a moisturizing lotion or oil after shaving to help prevent irritation and keep your scalp smooth and hydrated.

FAQ About Shaving Back and Sides of Head

Is it safe to shave your head?

Yes, it is safe to shave your head as long as you use the proper tools and techniques. Make sure to use a quality razor and shaving cream or gel, and be careful not to apply too much pressure or shave too close to your scalp.

How often should I shave my head?

How often you shave your head is up to you and depends on your personal preference. Some people prefer to shave every day, while others shave once a week or less frequently.

Will shaving my head make my hair grow back thicker?

No, shaving your head will not make your hair grow back thicker. This is a common myth. However, shaving can make your hair appear thicker because the ends of the hair are blunt instead of tapered.

Hair Care After Shaving Back and Sides of Head

After you've finished shaving, it's important to take care of your scalp to prevent irritation and keep your skin healthy. Here are a few tips:

  • Apply a moisturizing lotion or oil to your scalp to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid exposure to the sun and wear a hat if you're going to be outside for an extended period.
  • Be gentle when combing or brushing your hair to prevent irritation.
  • Consider using a scalp scrub or exfoliating shampoo to help remove dead skin cells and prevent ingrown hairs.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a perfectly shaved head that looks and feels great. Remember to take your time and be careful, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and looks.

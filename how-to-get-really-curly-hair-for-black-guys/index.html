How To Get Really Curly Hair For Black Guys

Written by Robby Jul 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Do you want to add some curls to your hair? As a black guy, it can be challenging to achieve those perfect curls, but it's not impossible. In this article, we will show you how to get really curly hair for black guys with some easy steps and tips.

Description

Curly hair is trendy, and everyone wants to have it. If you're a black guy, having curly hair can be a little challenging because of your hair texture. But don't worry, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve those perfect curls.

How to

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to achieving curly hair is to wash your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair's natural oils. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water and make sure to massage your scalp to promote blood circulation.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft. Make sure to apply it evenly to your hair, concentrating on the ends.

Step 3: Apply a Curl Enhancer

Next, apply a curl enhancer to your hair. A good curl enhancer will define your curls and add volume. Make sure to apply it evenly to your hair, concentrating on the ends.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser to dry your hair. A diffuser will help to enhance your curls and prevent frizz. Start by using low heat and low speed and gradually increase the temperature and speed as your hair dries.

Step 5: Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, scrunch it to enhance your curls. Use a small amount of hair oil to add shine and definition to your curls.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before washing it.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair because it can cause frizz. Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt instead.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair because it can cause damage. If you must use them, make sure to use a heat protectant.
  • Get a regular trim to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Can I achieve curly hair if I have a relaxer?

Yes, you can still achieve curly hair even if you have a relaxer. Follow the same steps, but make sure to use products that are suitable for relaxed hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair once or twice a week, depending on your hair type and lifestyle. Over-washing can strip your hair's natural oils, causing it to become dry and brittle.

Can I use a hair mask to enhance my curls?

Yes, you can use a hair mask to enhance your curls. Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and defined. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair's natural oils. Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft. Use a curl enhancer to define your curls and add volume. Use a diffuser to dry your hair and prevent frizz. Scrunch your hair to enhance your curls and add shine with a small amount of hair oil.

With these easy steps and tips, you can achieve really curly hair for black guys. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and defined.

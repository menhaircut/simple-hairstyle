Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Face Short Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face and short hair, it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a broad forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow chin. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces with short hair.

Description

The heart-shaped face is one of the most popular face shapes among women. It is characterized by a broad forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow chin. Women with a heart-shaped face tend to have a lot of hair, making it easier to style their hair in a variety of ways. Short hair is a great option for women with a heart-shaped face, as it can help to balance out the proportions of the face.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a heart-shaped face with short hair, it is essential to consider the proportions of your face. The goal of any hairstyle should be to balance out the features of the face and create a harmonious look. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

Tips

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of the head to balance out the broad forehead.
  • Opt for a style that has layers or texture to soften the jawline and chin.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make the face appear unbalanced.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle, as they can help to balance out the proportions of the face.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you.

Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Face Short Hair

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, here are some of the best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces with short hair:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for heart-shaped faces. It adds volume to the top of the head, which balances out the broad forehead. The short layers also help to soften the jawline and chin.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another classic hairstyle that flatters heart-shaped faces. The length should be just above the chin to draw attention away from the narrow chin. The layers in the bob also help to soften the jawline and chin.

3. Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered hairstyle that works well for heart-shaped faces. The layers add volume to the top of the head, which balances out the broad forehead. The layers also soften the jawline and chin.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great addition to any short hairstyle for a heart-shaped face. They help to balance out the proportions of the face and draw attention away from the narrow chin.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair in a center part with a heart-shaped face?

A: It is generally not recommended to wear a center part with a heart-shaped face, as it can make the forehead appear even broader. Instead, opt for a side part or side-swept bangs.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a blunt cut with a heart-shaped face?

A: A blunt cut is not recommended for a heart-shaped face, as it can make the face appear even more unbalanced. Instead, opt for a layered or textured cut.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking its best, be sure to take good care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Be sure to also get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces with short hair, you can confidently choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Remember to consider the proportions of your face and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Men s Haircut Long On Top Fade The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut that's easy to maintain, then the men's haircut long on top fade is the perfect choice for you. This versatile hairstyle has been popular for many years, and it's still going strong in 2023. In this guide, we'll show you how to get the perfect long on top fade haircut, as well as some tips and tricks to keep it looking great.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Men's Haircut Long on Top Fade
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

The men's haircut long on top fade is a hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. The hair on the top is usually styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. The fade is a gradual tapering of the hair from the top to the sides and back, creating a clean and sharp look. This haircut is perfect for men who want a stylish and modern look that's easy to maintain.

How to Get a Men's Haircut Long on Top Fade

The first step in getting a men's haircut long on top fade is to find a skilled and experienced barber. This is important because a good haircut can make all the difference in how you look and feel. Once you've found a barber you trust, you can discuss the specific details of the haircut, such as the length of the hair on top, the type of fade, and any styling preferences.

Choosing the Right Length

One of the most important parts of a men's haircut long on top fade is the length of the hair on top. This can vary depending on your personal style and preferences, but generally, the hair should be long enough to style and short enough to be easily managed. Your barber can help you decide on the best length based on your face shape, hair texture, and overall style.

Choosing the Right Fade

Another important aspect of the long on top fade is the type of fade you choose. There are several different types of fades, including high fades, low fades, and skin fades. Your barber can help you choose the best type of fade based on your hair type and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a men's haircut long on top fade:

  1. Decide on the length of the hair on top and the type of fade you want
  2. Show your barber a picture of the style you want
  3. Sit back and relax while your barber works their magic
  4. After the haircut is finished, ask your barber for styling tips and product recommendations

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you maintain your men's haircut long on top fade:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean
  • Invest in a good styling product, such as pomade or wax, to help you achieve your desired look
  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups and trims
  • Experiment with different styles and looks to keep your haircut fresh and interesting

FAQ

How often should I get a men's haircut long on top fade?

You should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your long on top fade.

What type of styling product should I use?

The type of styling product you use will depend on your hair type and desired look. Pomade and wax are good choices for most hair types and styles.

Can I style my long on top fade in different ways?

Yes, you can experiment with different styles and looks to find the one that suits you best.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain a healthy and stylish long on top fade:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can dry out your hair and scalp
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat styling, as it can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and trendy men's haircut long on top fade that's sure to turn heads. So why wait? Book an appointment with your barber today and get ready to look and feel your best!

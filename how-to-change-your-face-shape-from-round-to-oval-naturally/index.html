How To Change Your Face Shape From Round To Oval Naturally

Written by Aprilia Sep 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your round face shape and want to achieve an oval face shape naturally? You're in luck! In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to change your face shape from round to oval naturally. So, let's get started!

Description

Having a round face shape can somewhat limit your hairstyle choices and make you feel self-conscious. Fortunately, there are natural ways to change your face shape from round to oval. These methods involve facial exercises, diet, and haircuts. By following these tips, you can achieve an oval face shape, which is considered more aesthetically pleasing.

How To

To change your face shape from round to oval, there are several things you can do:

Facial Exercises:

Facial exercises are one of the best ways to change your face shape naturally. These exercises can help tone and strengthen your facial muscles, which can lead to a more defined and oval face shape. Some of the best facial exercises to try include:

  • Chin Lifts
  • Cheek Puffs
  • Eye Circles
  • Smiling Fish Face

Try to incorporate these facial exercises into your daily routine for best results.

Diet:

Your diet can also play a role in changing your face shape. Eating a healthy and balanced diet can help reduce overall body fat, which can lead to a slimmer and more defined face shape. Make sure to incorporate plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein into your diet.

Haircuts:

Your haircut can also make a significant difference in the appearance of your face shape. Opt for haircuts that are longer on the top and shorter on the sides. This will create the illusion of a longer and more oval face shape. Avoid haircuts that are too short or too round, as they can accentuate a round face shape.

Step-by-Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to change your face shape from round to oval naturally:

Step 1: Incorporate Facial Exercises

Start incorporating facial exercises into your daily routine. Try to do these exercises at least once a day for best results.

Step 2: Modify Your Diet

Modify your diet to include plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein. Avoid processed and high-fat foods.

Step 3: Choose the Right Haircut

Choose a haircut that is longer on the top and shorter on the sides to create the illusion of a longer and more oval face shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you change your face shape from round to oval naturally:

  • Drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated and healthy.
  • Get enough sleep to help reduce overall body fat.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they can both contribute to a round face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can facial exercises really change your face shape?

A: Yes, facial exercises can help tone and strengthen your facial muscles, which can lead to a more defined and oval face shape.

Q: How long does it take to see results?

A: Results may vary depending on the individual, but it can take several weeks to several months to see noticeable changes.

Q: Can changing my diet really help change my face shape?

A: Yes, a healthy and balanced diet can help reduce overall body fat, which can lead to a slimmer and more defined face shape.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to maintain your new oval face shape. Make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and style. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it brittle. Finally, visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your new haircut and style.

Conclusion

Changing your face shape from round to oval naturally may take some time and effort, but the results are worth it. By incorporating facial exercises, modifying your diet, and choosing the right haircut, you can achieve a more defined and aesthetically pleasing oval face shape. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain your new look. Good luck!

Does Short Hair Look Good On Round Faces

Written by William Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and want to try out a new hairstyle? If you have a round face, you might be wondering if short hair would look good on you. Well, the answer is yes! Short hair can look great on round faces, and in this article, we will discuss how to pull it off.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair can be a great option for those with round faces. Short haircuts can help to elongate the face and create the illusion of a slimmer face. However, not all short haircuts are created equal. It's important to find a haircut that flatters your face shape and features.

How To

When deciding on a short haircut for your round face, you should consider your hair texture, facial features, and personal style. A good hairstylist can help you choose a cut that will flatter your face shape and bring out your best features.

Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can play a big role in how a short haircut will look on you. If you have thin hair, a short bob or pixie cut can make your hair appear fuller. If you have thick hair, a layered cut can help to thin out your hair and make it more manageable.

Choose a Cut That Flatters Your Features

When choosing a short haircut for your round face, you should consider your facial features. If you have a prominent forehead, a side-swept bang can help to balance out your face. If you have a small chin, a chin-length bob can help to elongate your face.

Step By Step

Once you have decided on a short haircut, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some steps to follow to keep your short hair looking great:

  1. Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and lift.
  3. Use a styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  4. Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut for your round face:

  • Opt for cuts that add height and volume to the crown of your head.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider adding layers to your haircut to create movement and texture.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a side part or messy waves, to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my round face look even rounder?

A: Not necessarily. A well-chosen short haircut can actually help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Q: What are some good short haircuts for round faces?

A: Some good short haircuts for round faces include pixie cuts, bobs, and layered cuts. A good hairstylist can help you choose a cut that will flatter your face shape and features.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for keeping your short hair looking great. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

In conclusion, short hair can look great on round faces. The key is to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and features, and to maintain it properly with regular trims and styling. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can rock a stylish short haircut and feel confident in your appearance.

What Are Round Layers Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Round layers curly hair is a hairstyle that features voluminous curls with layers cut in a rounded shape. This style is perfect for those with naturally curly hair who want to add more shape and definition to their locks. It’s a versatile style that can be worn in many different ways, from loose and free-flowing to tightly coiled and defined.

How to achieve round layers curly hair

The key to achieving round layers curly hair is to start with healthy, well-moisturized hair. This means using a good shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair. After washing, apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep your curls defined and frizz-free.

Next, you’ll want to begin cutting your hair into layers. It’s important to have a professional stylist do this for you, as they will be able to create the perfect shape for your face and hair type. They may use a technique called “dry cutting,” which involves cutting your hair when it’s dry to ensure that the layers fall in the right place.

Once your layers are cut, you can begin styling your hair. You can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help enhance your curls and add more volume. You may also want to use a curling iron or wand to define your curls even further.

Step by step guide to achieving round layers curly hair

Step 1: Start with clean, moisturized hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair, and apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls defined and frizz-free.

Step 2: Get your hair cut into layers

Visit a professional stylist to get your hair cut into layers. They may use a dry cutting technique to ensure that the layers fall in the right place.

Step 3: Style your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls and add more volume. You can also use a curling iron or wand to define your curls even further.

Tips for maintaining round layers curly hair

To maintain your round layers curly hair, it’s important to use the right products and techniques. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls defined and frizz-free
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls
  • Avoid using brushes or combs on your curls, as this can cause breakage and frizz
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your layers
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair

Frequently Asked Questions about round layers curly hair

What is the best way to style round layers curly hair?

The best way to style round layers curly hair is to use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls and add more volume. You can also use a curling iron or wand to define your curls even further.

How often should I trim my round layers curly hair?

You should trim your round layers curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your layers.

Can I achieve round layers curly hair at home?

We recommend visiting a professional stylist to get your hair cut into layers, as they will be able to create the perfect shape for your face and hair type. However, you can still style your hair at home using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer and a curling iron or wand.

Hair care for round layers curly hair

It’s important to take good care of your hair to keep your round layers curly hair looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls defined and frizz-free
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls
  • Avoid using brushes or combs on your curls, as this can cause breakage and frizz
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your layers
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve and maintain beautiful, healthy round layers curly hair.

How To Style Medium Length Layered Haircut Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of sporting the same old hairstyle? Maybe it's time to switch things up and try a medium length layered haircut. Not only is this cut versatile, but it's also easy to style. In this blog post, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to style a medium length layered haircut.

How to Style a Medium Length Layered Haircut

A medium length layered haircut is perfect for those who want to add volume, texture, and movement to their hair. Here are some ways you can style your medium length layered haircut:

  • Beachy Waves
  • Sleek and Straight
  • Messy Bun
  • Half-Up Half-Down
  • Braids

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the different ways to style your medium length layered haircut, here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve each look:

Beachy Waves

  1. Apply a heat protectant on your hair.
  2. Part your hair in the middle or on the side.
  3. Curl your hair using a curling iron or a wand. Make sure to curl away from your face.
  4. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.
  5. Apply a sea salt spray for that beachy texture.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Sleek and Straight

  1. Apply a heat protectant on your hair.
  2. Part your hair in the middle or on the side.
  3. Use a straightening iron to straighten your hair.
  4. Apply a shine serum for that sleek finish.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Messy Bun

  1. Apply a texturizing spray on your hair.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  3. Twist your hair and wrap it around the base of the ponytail to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Part your hair in the middle or on the side.
  2. Take the top section of your hair and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Tease the crown of your head for added volume.
  4. Leave the rest of your hair down.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Braids

  1. Part your hair in the middle or on the side.
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side and braid it.
  3. Secure the braid with a clear elastic band.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. You can leave the rest of your hair down or tie it up in a ponytail or bun.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind when styling your medium length layered haircut:

  • Invest in a good quality heat protectant to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Use a hairspray that provides a flexible hold to avoid that stiff and crunchy look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I still style my medium length layered haircut if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can still style your medium length layered haircut if you have thin hair. In fact, layering can add volume and texture to thin hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more frequently. If you have dry hair or color-treated hair, you may want to wash your hair less often to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Medium Length Layered Haircut

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and beauty of your medium length layered haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style your medium length layered haircut and keep it looking fabulous. Don't be afraid to experiment with different looks and have fun with your hair!

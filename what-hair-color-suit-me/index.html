What Hair Color Suits Me Tips And Tricks To Find Your Perfect Shade

Written by Eveline Sep 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair color? Do you want to try something new but don't know where to start? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll go over how to find the perfect hair color for you. From warm to cool tones, we'll help you choose a shade that will make you look and feel your best.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step to finding the perfect hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral.

Cool Skin Tones

If you have cool skin tones, your skin will have pink, red, or blue undertones. Your veins will appear blue or purple, and you might burn easily in the sun. Cool skin tones look best with hair colors that have cool tones, like ash blonde or cool brown.

Warm Skin Tones

If you have warm skin tones, your skin will have yellow or golden undertones. Your veins will appear green, and you might tan easily in the sun. Warm skin tones look best with hair colors that have warm tones, like honey blonde or golden brown.

Neutral Skin Tones

If you have neutral skin tones, your skin will have a mix of cool and warm undertones. Your veins will appear blue-green, and you might tan or burn in the sun. Neutral skin tones can pull off both warm and cool hair colors, so feel free to experiment.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

Once you've determined your skin tone, it's time to choose the right hair color. Here are some tips:

Consider Your Natural Hair Color

If you want a subtle change, consider a hair color that's only a few shades lighter or darker than your natural hair color. This will give you a new look without being too drastic.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Consider how much time and money you're willing to spend on hair care. If you're not willing to spend a lot of time and money, consider a low-maintenance hair color, like balayage or ombre.

Take Inspiration from Celebrities

If you're unsure about what hair color you want, take inspiration from your favorite celebrities. Look for someone with a similar skin tone and eye color, and see what hair color they're rocking.

FAQs About Choosing the Right Hair Color

Q: Can I dye my hair if it's damaged?

A: It's not recommended to dye damaged hair. If your hair is damaged, focus on repairing it with hair masks and treatments before dyeing it.

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if you have sensitive skin. Look for hair dyes that are labeled as "sensitive" or "gentle."

Q: How often do I need to touch up my hair color?

A: It depends on the type of hair color you choose. If you choose a permanent hair color, you'll need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks. If you choose a semi-permanent hair color, you'll need to touch up your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Once you've chosen your perfect hair color, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

Use a Color-Safe Shampoo

Regular shampoos can strip your hair of its color. Use a color-safe shampoo to help preserve your hair color.

Avoid Hot Tools

Hot tools like curling irons and straighteners can damage your hair and fade your hair color. If you must use hot tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This will help preserve your hair color and keep it looking fresh.

In conclusion, finding the perfect hair color can be a daunting task, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect shade for you. Remember to consider your skin tone, lifestyle, and inspiration from celebrities. With the right hair color and proper hair care, you'll be able to rock your new look with confidence.

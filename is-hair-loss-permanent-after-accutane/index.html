Is Hair Loss Permanent After Accutane

Written by William Sep 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Accutane is a popular medication used to treat severe acne. While it can be very effective in treating acne, one of the side effects that many people worry about is hair loss. In this article, we'll take a look at whether hair loss is permanent after Accutane, and what you can do to prevent it.

What Causes Hair Loss After Accutane?

Accutane works by reducing the amount of oil produced by the sebaceous glands in the skin. This can cause the hair to become dry and brittle, which can lead to hair loss. Additionally, Accutane can also affect the hair follicles, causing them to become damaged and leading to hair loss.

Is Hair Loss Permanent After Accutane?

The good news is that hair loss is usually not permanent after Accutane. In most cases, the hair will start to grow back once you stop taking the medication. However, it may take several months for the hair to grow back fully.

How to Prevent Hair Loss After Accutane

If you're concerned about hair loss after Accutane, there are a few things you can do to help prevent it:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is designed for dry or damaged hair. Avoid using hot water, as this can further dry out your hair and scalp.

2. Avoid heat styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers. These tools can further damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

3. Eat a healthy diet

Eat a diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, especially those that are important for hair health, such as B vitamins, vitamin D, and iron.

4. Take hair supplements

Consider taking hair supplements that are designed to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. Some popular supplements include biotin, vitamin E, and omega-3 fatty acids.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take for hair to grow back after Accutane?

A: It can take several months for hair to grow back fully after Accutane. However, in most cases, the hair will start to grow back within a few weeks of stopping the medication.

Q: Can Accutane cause permanent hair loss?

A: While hair loss is a common side effect of Accutane, it is usually not permanent. In most cases, the hair will start to grow back once you stop taking the medication.

Hair Care Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help keep your hair healthy and prevent hair loss:

1. Don't wash your hair too often

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it more prone to breakage. Aim to wash your hair no more than every other day.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb

When combing wet hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling and breaking your hair.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

In conclusion, hair loss after Accutane is usually not permanent. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can help prevent hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.

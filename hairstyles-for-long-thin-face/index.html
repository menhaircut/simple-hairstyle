Hairstyles For Long Thin Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Oct 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a long thin face, choosing a hairstyle can be a little tricky. You want to find a style that flatters your face shape and brings out your best features. In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks for finding the perfect hairstyle for long thin face. We'll also be sharing some hairstyles that are perfect for this face shape. Let's get started!

Description

A long thin face is characterized by a face shape that is longer than it is wide. This face shape can be a little challenging when it comes to choosing a hairstyle because you want to choose a style that doesn't make your face look even longer. The key is to find a hairstyle that adds width to your face and balances out your features.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for long thin face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Look for styles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight.
  • Consider bangs to shorten the appearance of your face.
  • Avoid center parts.

Hairstyles for Long Thin Face

The Bob

The bob is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for long thin face. It adds volume to the sides of the face and gives the appearance of a wider face. You can choose from a variety of bob styles, including the classic bob, the inverted bob, and the lob.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short and sassy style that can work really well for long thin face. It adds volume to the top of the head and makes the face look wider. This style is also very low maintenance and perfect for those who don't have a lot of time to spend on their hair.

The Shag

The shag is a layered hairstyle that is perfect for long thin face. It adds volume to the sides of the face and gives the appearance of a wider face. This style is also very versatile and can be worn in a variety of ways.

The Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to shorten the appearance of a long face. They add volume to the sides of the face and give the appearance of a wider face. You can choose from a variety of side-swept bangs, including the wispy bangs and the blunt bangs.

Step by Step Guide to Style Your Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair for long thin face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the sides of your face.
  3. If you have bangs, blow-dry them to the side to shorten the appearance of your face.
  4. Use a curling iron to add loose curls to your hair.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair in a center part?

A: It's best to avoid center parts if you have a long thin face. Center parts can make your face look even longer.

Q: What if I have thin hair?

A: If you have thin hair, you can still wear the hairstyles we've mentioned. Just be sure to use volumizing products to add volume to your hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle for long thin face can be a little tricky, but with the tips and tricks we've shared in this article, you'll be able to find the perfect style for you. Whether you choose the bob, the pixie cut, the shag, or side-swept bangs, these styles will add volume to the sides of your face and give the appearance of a wider face. Remember to maintain your hairstyle with regular visits to your hairstylist and using the right products to keep your hair healthy and voluminous.

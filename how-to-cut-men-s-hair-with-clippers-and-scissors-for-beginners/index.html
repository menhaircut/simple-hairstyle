How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers And Scissors For Beginners

Written by William Nov 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are new to cutting men's hair or want to learn how to cut hair with clippers and scissors, this guide is for you. In this article, we will go over how to cut men's hair with clippers and scissors for beginners, step by step. We will also provide tips and answer some frequently asked questions to help you get started.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Cutting men's hair with clippers and scissors can be intimidating, especially if you don't have any experience. However, with the right tools, techniques, and a little bit of practice, you can learn how to cut men's hair like a pro. This guide will teach you the basics of cutting hair with clippers and scissors, as well as provide some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How To

The first step in cutting men's hair with clippers and scissors is to gather the right tools. You will need a good quality pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and hair clips or ties to section off the hair. You may also want to invest in a cape or towel to protect the client's clothing.

Clippers

When choosing clippers, look for a model that is designed for professional use. They should have a powerful motor, multiple speed settings, and come with a variety of guard sizes. The guard sizes range from 1/16 of an inch to 1 inch and are used to determine the length of the hair. The larger the guard, the longer the hair will be.

Scissors

Scissors are used for cutting the hair around the ears, neckline, and for blending the hair. Look for a pair of professional hair cutting scissors that have a sharp blade and comfortable grip.

Comb

A comb is used to section off the hair and to create a clean part. Look for a comb that has a long tail, which can be used to section off the hair and to create a straight line.

Hair Clips or Ties

Hair clips or ties are used to hold the hair in place while you work on other sections. They are also used to section off the hair and to make it easier to work with.

Step by Step

Step 1: Prep the Hair

Before you start cutting, it's important to prep the hair. Start by washing and drying the hair. Then, comb through it to remove any tangles or knots. Section off the hair using clips or ties, leaving the hair on top of the head for last.

Step 2: Cut with Clippers

Start with the largest guard and work your way down to the smaller ones as needed. Use the larger guard to cut the sides and back of the head. Use a shorter guard to blend the hair around the ears and neckline. Use a comb to create a straight line and to make sure the hair is even.

Step 3: Cut with Scissors

Use scissors to cut the hair on the top of the head, around the ears, and neckline. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Use scissors to blend the hair and to create a natural look.

Step 4: Clean Up

Use clippers and scissors to clean up any stray hairs and to make sure the cut looks even. Use a comb to check for any uneven areas and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to the shorter ones to avoid cutting the hair too short.
  • Use a comb to create a straight line and to make sure the hair is even.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid mistakes. Take your time and make small cuts.
  • Use scissors to blend the hair and create a natural look.
  • Practice on a mannequin head or a willing volunteer before cutting someone's hair for the first time.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with clippers and scissors?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers and scissors. However, it can be difficult to cut the back of your head and to create a balanced look. It's best to have someone help you or to visit a professional barber or stylist.

How often should I cut my hair?

How often you should cut your hair depends on the length and style. Shorter haircuts may need to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks, while longer styles may only need to be cut every 6-8 weeks. Talk to your stylist or barber for personalized advice.

How do I care for my hair after a haircut?

After a haircut, it's important to shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and clean. Use a hairbrush or comb to style your hair and avoid using too much heat or styling products.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. In addition to regular haircuts, it's important to shampoo and condition your hair regularly. Use a hairbrush or comb to style your hair and avoid using too much heat or styling products. Talk to your stylist or barber for personalized advice on how to care for your hair.

Cutting men's hair with clippers and scissors can be a fun and rewarding skill to learn. With the right tools, techniques, and practice, you can create a variety of styles and looks. Remember to take your time, use a comb to create a straight line, and practice on a mannequin head or willing volunteer before cutting someone's hair for the first time. Good luck!

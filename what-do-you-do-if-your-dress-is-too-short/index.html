What Do You Do If Your Dress Is Too Short

Written by Petter Aug 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our article on what to do if your dress is too short. This is a common problem that many women face, especially when shopping online or trying on clothes at home. We understand how frustrating it can be to find the perfect dress, only to realize that it is too short. That's why we have put together this guide to help you solve this problem.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

If you have a dress that is too short, there are several things you can do to make it work for you. The first thing you should do is assess the situation. Is the dress just a little too short, or is it so short that you can't wear it at all? If the dress is just a little too short, you may be able to make it work with the right accessories. If the dress is too short to wear, you may need to return it or exchange it for a longer one.

How To

There are several ways to make a dress that is too short work for you. Here are some options:

Hem it

The most obvious solution to a dress that is too short is to hem it. If you have basic sewing skills, you can hem the dress yourself. If you don't know how to sew, you can take the dress to a tailor and have it hemmed professionally. Just make sure that the hemline is even and that the dress looks good on you after it has been hemmed.

Layer it

If the dress is just a little too short, you can layer it over leggings or tights. This will not only make the dress longer, but it will also add some warmth to your outfit. You can also layer the dress over a longer shirt or blouse for a more casual look.

Add a slip

If the dress is too short, you can add a slip underneath it. This will not only make the dress longer, but it will also add some volume to the skirt. You can choose a slip that matches the color of the dress or go for a contrasting color for a more fun look.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for hemming a dress that is too short:

  1. Try on the dress and determine how much you need to hem it. Mark the hemline with a piece of chalk or a pin.
  2. Turn the dress inside out and fold the hem up to the desired length.
  3. Pin the hem in place and try on the dress again to make sure the length is right.
  4. Sew the hem in place, either by hand or with a sewing machine.
  5. Trim any excess fabric and turn the dress right side out.

Tips

  • If you don't want to hem the dress, you can also try wearing it with high heels or wedges to make it appear longer.
  • Make sure to choose the right slip for your dress. A slip that is too short or too long will not look good.
  • You can also try wearing a belt with the dress to make it appear longer.

FAQ

Q: Can I return a dress that is too short?

A: It depends on the store's return policy. Check with the store to see if you can return or exchange the dress.

Q: Can I wear a dress that is too short to a formal event?

A: It's not recommended. A dress that is too short may be inappropriate for a formal event.

Q: Can I wear a dress that is too short to work?

A: It depends on your workplace's dress code. If your workplace allows for casual dress, you may be able to wear a dress that is too short with leggings or tights.

Hair Care

When wearing a dress that is too short, it's important to pay attention to your hair. A messy bun or ponytail can make your outfit look more casual, while a sleek updo can make it look more formal. Choose a hairstyle that complements the style of the dress and the occasion you are wearing it for.

We hope this guide has helped you figure out what to do if your dress is too short. Remember, there are several ways to make a short dress work for you, whether it's by hemming it, layering it, or adding a slip. With the right accessories and hairstyle, you can rock any dress, no matter how short it is!

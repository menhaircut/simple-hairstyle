How To Trim Your Beard Round Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you know the struggle of finding the right beard style. You want to make sure your facial hair complements your face shape and doesn't make it look even rounder. That's where trimming comes in. In this article, we'll show you how to trim your beard round face in the most straightforward way possible.

  • How to Trim Your Beard Round Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Trimming Your Beard

How to Trim Your Beard Round Face

Before you start trimming your beard, you need to decide on the length and shape you want. For a round face, we recommend a shorter length on the sides and a longer length on the chin. This will help elongate your face and make it look less round. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Beard

Before trimming, make sure your beard is clean and dry. Use a beard shampoo to wash and then pat it dry with a towel.

Step 2: Comb Your Beard

Use a beard comb to detangle your facial hair. This will help you see any tangles or knots that need to be trimmed.

Step 3: Trim the Sides

Start by trimming the sides of your beard. Use a trimmer with a shorter guard and trim downwards. Follow your jawline and stop at the bottom of your earlobe.

Step 4: Trim the Chin

Next, trim the hair on your chin. Use a trimmer with a longer guard and trim downwards. Follow the shape of your chin and stop a few inches below your lower lip.

Step 5: Blend the Sides and Chin

Now it's time to blend the sides and chin. Use a trimmer with a longer guard and trim upwards. This will help create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 6: Trim the Mustache

Finally, trim your mustache. Use a small pair of scissors and trim any stray hairs that hang over your upper lip.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a summary of the steps:

  1. Wash and dry your beard
  2. Comb your beard
  3. Trim the sides
  4. Trim the chin
  5. Blend the sides and chin
  6. Trim the mustache

Tips for Trimming Your Beard

Trimming your beard can be tricky, especially if you're not used to it. Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Invest in a good quality trimmer with different guard lengths
  • Take it slow and trim a little at a time
  • Use a comb to help guide the trimmer
  • Trim in a well-lit area to ensure you don't miss any spots
  • Use scissors to trim any stray hairs

FAQs

What beard styles suit a round face?

Shorter lengths on the sides and longer lengths on the chin work well for a round face. You can also try a goatee or a soul patch.

How often should I trim my beard?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. Generally, every two weeks is a good time frame to trim your beard.

Beard Care Tips

Trimming your beard is just one part of beard care. Here are some other tips to keep your facial hair looking its best:

  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and hydrated
  • Don't over-wash your beard as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Brush or comb your beard daily to prevent tangles and knots
  • Use a beard conditioner to keep your beard looking healthy
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote beard growth

Trimming your beard can be a daunting task, but by following these simple steps, you can achieve a well-groomed beard that complements your round face. Remember to take it slow, invest in a good quality trimmer, and practice good beard care habits.

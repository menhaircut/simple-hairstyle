Will A Graduated Bob Suit Round Face

Written by William Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering if a graduated bob will suit you. The answer is yes! A graduated bob is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, making it a great option for those with round faces. In this article, we'll explore how to get a graduated bob that flatters your face shape, as well as some tips for styling and caring for your new haircut.

Description

A graduated bob is a haircut where the hair is shorter in the back and longer in the front. This creates a graduated effect, hence the name. The length of the bob can vary, from a short pixie cut to a longer shoulder-length bob. The key to a good graduated bob is to have the shortest layer hit at or above the nape of the neck, with the front layers gradually getting longer.

How to Get a Graduated Bob

If you're interested in getting a graduated bob, the first step is to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting this type of haircut. Once you've found a stylist you trust, talk to them about your face shape and what type of bob will flatter it the most. They may suggest a longer bob if you have a round face, as this will help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

When getting a graduated bob, it's important to keep in mind that the shorter layers may require more maintenance than a traditional bob. You'll need to get regular trims to keep the shape of the cut, so be prepared to visit the salon every 6-8 weeks.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a graduated bob:

Step 1: Consultation

Discuss your face shape and desired length with your stylist.

Step 2: Shampoo and Condition

Your stylist will shampoo and condition your hair to prepare it for cutting.

Step 3: Sectioning

Your stylist will section your hair and begin cutting the shortest layer at the nape of your neck.

Step 4: Graduation

Your stylist will continue cutting the longer layers, creating a graduated effect.

Step 5: Finishing

Your stylist will finish the cut and style your hair to your liking.

Tips for Styling a Graduated Bob

Once you have your graduated bob, there are many ways to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.
  • Blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction of where it falls to create volume and texture.
  • Add waves or curls with a curling iron or hot rollers.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Use a flat iron to create a sleek, straight look.

FAQ

Q: Will a graduated bob make my face look rounder?

A: No, a graduated bob can actually help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer, especially if the longest layer is at or below your chin.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a graduated bob?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a graduated bob. Try a messy bun or a low ponytail to show off the layers.

Q: How do I care for my graduated bob?

A: To care for your graduated bob, use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type. You may also want to use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after getting a graduated bob. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your graduated bob.

In conclusion, a graduated bob can definitely suit a round face. By finding the right stylist and discussing your face shape, you can get a bob that flatters your features and is easy to style and care for. With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking your new haircut in no time!

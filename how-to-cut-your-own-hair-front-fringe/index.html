How To Cut Your Own Hair Front Fringe Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts? Want to learn how to cut your own hair front fringe? You're in luck! In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of cutting your own front fringe. From how to section your hair to tips on how to style your new fringe, we've got you covered. So, grab your scissors and let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Section Your Hair
  2. How to Cut Your Hair Front Fringe
  3. Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair

How to Section Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to section it properly. Follow these steps to section your hair:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it.
  2. Using a comb, part your hair down the middle.
  3. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and clip it away.
  4. Take another section of hair from the top of your ear to the crown of your head and clip it away.
  5. You should be left with the front section of your hair. This is the section you'll be cutting.

How to Cut Your Hair Front Fringe

Now that you've sectioned your hair, it's time to cut your front fringe. Follow these steps:

  1. Take a small section of hair from the front of your hairline. This will be the length of your fringe.
  2. Using your scissors, cut the hair straight across. Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length.
  3. Once you've cut the first section, take another section of hair and use the first section as a guide for the length.
  4. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you've cut all of the front section of your hair.
  5. Remove the clips and style your new fringe.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own hair:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Use the first section of hair as a guide for the length of the rest of your hair.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet as it will shrink when it dries.
  • Always use sharp scissors.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair:

Can I cut my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your hair if it's curly. However, keep in mind that curly hair shrinks when it dries, so be careful not to cut off too much.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended that you cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my own hair if I have layers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair if you have layers. Just be sure to use the longest layer as a guide for the rest of your hair.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a nourishing hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

And there you have it! A step-by-step guide on how to cut your own hair front fringe. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to save money and have a fresh new look.

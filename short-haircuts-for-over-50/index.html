Short Haircuts For Over 50 A Guide To Choosing The Perfect Style

Written by Petter Sep 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our hair tends to lose its volume and thickness. This is why many women over 50 opt for short haircuts. Short hair not only looks stylish and trendy but it is also easy to maintain. In this article, we will guide you through the different types of short haircuts for over 50, how to choose the perfect style, and tips for maintaining your new haircut.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Haircut for Over 50

Choosing the perfect short haircut can be a daunting task, especially if you have had long hair for most of your life. Here are a few tips to help you choose the perfect haircut:

Consider your face shape

The first thing you need to consider when choosing a short haircut is your face shape. Different face shapes suit different haircuts. For example, if you have a round face, a pixie cut will suit you best.

Think about your lifestyle

You also need to consider your lifestyle when choosing a short haircut. If you have an active lifestyle, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling.

Consult with your stylist

Your stylist is the best person to advise you on the perfect haircut. They will consider your face shape, lifestyle, and hair type to recommend the best haircut for you.

Short Haircut Styles for Over 50

Now that you know how to choose the perfect short haircut, let's take a look at some of the most popular styles for women over 50:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless classic that never goes out of style. It is a short, layered haircut that is perfect for women with round faces.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another classic short haircut that is perfect for women over 50. It is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways.

Layered Cut

The layered cut is perfect for women with thin hair. It adds volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker.

Step by Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut

Getting a short haircut can be nerve-wracking, especially if you have never had short hair before. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you through the process:

Step 1: Research

Take some time to research different short haircuts for over 50. Look for styles that suit your face shape and lifestyle.

Step 2: Consult with your stylist

Book an appointment with your stylist and discuss your options. Your stylist will advise you on the best haircut for you.

Step 3: Prepare for the cut

Wash your hair the day before your appointment and avoid using any styling products. This will make it easier for your stylist to cut your hair.

Step 4: Get the cut

Sit back, relax, and let your stylist work their magic. Remember, hair grows back, so don't panic if you don't like the cut at first.

Step 5: Style your new haircut

Your stylist will show you how to style your new haircut. Follow their instructions and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Now that you have your new haircut, here are a few tips to help you maintain it:

Keep it trimmed

Short haircuts require more frequent trims to maintain their shape. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

Use the right products

Use products that are specifically designed for short hair. This will help you style your hair and maintain its shape.

Experiment with different styles

Short haircuts are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Can I still have a short haircut if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can. In fact, a short layered cut can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller.

Will a short haircut make me look older?

No, a short haircut can actually make you look younger and more vibrant. It all depends on the style you choose.

Do I need to use special products for short hair?

Yes, it is recommended that you use products that are specifically designed for short hair. These products will help you style your hair and maintain its shape.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips:

Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

Avoid using too much heat

Excessive heat can damage your hair. Avoid using hot tools such as hairdryers and straighteners on a daily basis.

Use a nourishing hair mask

Use a nourishing hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you are now ready to rock your new short haircut. Remember, confidence is key, and a good haircut can make all the difference.

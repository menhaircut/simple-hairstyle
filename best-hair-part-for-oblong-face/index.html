Best Hair Part for Oblong Face

If you have an oblong face, choosing the right hair part can make a big difference in the way you look. In this article, we will discuss the best hair part for oblong face and provide you with tips and tricks to style your hair in a way that flatters your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oblong face is longer than it is wide and has a straighter jawline. The best hair part for oblong face is a side-part that falls slightly off-center. This helps to create the illusion of width and balance out the length of the face. A center-part, on the other hand, can make the face look even longer.

How To

To achieve the best hair part for oblong face, start by blow-drying your hair in the direction you want it to go. If you're going for a side-part, blow-dry your hair to the opposite side and then flip it over to the other side. This will create volume and help keep the hair in place. Use a comb or your fingers to create the part, making sure it's slightly off-center. Finish with hairspray to keep the part in place.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the best hair part for oblong face:

  1. Blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction of the part you want
  2. Flip your hair to the other side to create volume
  3. Create the part with a comb or your fingers
  4. Make sure the part is slightly off-center
  5. Finish with hairspray to keep the part in place

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair with the best hair part for oblong face:

  • Avoid a center-part
  • Add layers to create volume
  • Try a side-swept fringe to soften the face
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down for added volume

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair part for oblong face:

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, you can still achieve the best hair part for oblong face. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls and create volume. Follow the same steps for creating the part.

What if I have a short haircut?

If you have a short haircut, you can still create the best hair part for oblong face. Use a styling product like wax or pomade to create texture and volume, then use a comb to create the part.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is important for achieving the best hair part for oblong face. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type and use a heat protectant when blow-drying your hair. Avoid using too much heat or styling products, as this can damage your hair over time.

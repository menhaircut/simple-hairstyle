Is an Undercut a Fade? - A Comprehensive Guide in 2023

Description

Many people get confused when it comes to differentiating between an undercut and a fade. Although they may look similar at first glance, they are two distinct hairstyles. An undercut is when the hair on the sides and back of the head is shaved or cut very short, while the hair on top is left longer. On the other hand, a fade is when the hair gradually fades from short to long, typically on the sides and back of the head.

How to

To achieve an undercut, you will need to have a clipper or razor to shave the sides and back of your head. You can also ask a professional stylist to do it for you. To get a fade, you will need to have the sides and back of your hair cut gradually shorter using clippers or scissors. It is best to go to a professional stylist for this as it requires precision and skill.

Step by Step

How to get an undercut

1. Decide on the length you want for the top of your hair.

2. Section off the top of your hair and tie it up out of the way.

3. Use clippers or a razor to shave the sides and back of your head. Start with a higher guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length.

4. Use scissors to blend the top of your hair with the shaved sides and back.

5. Style your hair on top as desired.


How to get a fade

1. Decide on the length you want for the top of your hair.

2. Determine where you want the fade to start and use clippers to cut the hair shorter at that point.

3. Gradually work your way down to a shorter guard, blending the hair as you go.

4. Use scissors to blend the top of your hair with the faded sides and back.

5. Style your hair on top as desired.

Tips

1. Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting.

2. Start with a higher guard and work your way down gradually to avoid cutting too much too quickly.

3. Go to a professional stylist if you are unsure or want a more precise cut.

FAQ

Q: Can you have both an undercut and a fade?

A: Yes, you can have an undercut with a fade on the sides and back of your head.


Q: How often should I get my undercut or fade trimmed?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows, but typically every 2-4 weeks.

Hair Care

Both undercuts and fades require regular maintenance to keep them looking sharp. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools. You can also use a pomade or wax to style your hair on top and keep it in place.

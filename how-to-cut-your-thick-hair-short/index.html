How To Cut Your Thick Hair Short A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick hair, you know how challenging it can be to manage. It can take forever to style, and it can be difficult to find the perfect haircut. One solution to both of these problems is to cut your hair short. Not only will it be easier to manage, but it can also be a trendy and chic look. In this article, we will guide you through the steps on how to cut your thick hair short.

  • How to prepare
  • Step-by-step guide on how to cut your thick hair short
  • Tips for cutting your thick hair short
  • FAQs

How to Prepare

Before you start cutting your thick hair short, there are a few things you need to prepare:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Towel or cape
  • Spray bottle filled with water

It is also important to decide on the length you want for your hair. You can use a ruler or a measuring tape to measure the length. You can also use a photo of the hairstyle you want as a reference.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Cut Your Thick Hair Short

Now that you have prepared everything you need, it's time to start cutting your hair. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Before cutting your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Shampoo and condition your hair as you normally would, then dry it with a towel or a hair dryer.

Step 2: Section your hair

Use a comb to divide your hair into sections. Start with the top section and clip it away from the rest of your hair. Then, divide the sides and the back into sections and clip them as well.

Step 3: Start cutting

Start with the top section. Use your comb to lift a section of hair and hold it between your index and middle fingers. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way down to the sides and the back.

Step 4: Check for evenness

Once you have finished cutting all the sections of your hair, check to make sure it is even. Use your comb to comb through your hair and check for any uneven spots. If you find any, trim them with your scissors.

Step 5: Style your new haircut

Once you are satisfied with the length and the evenness of your hair, you can style it as you like. Use a hairbrush or your fingers to style your new haircut.

Tips for Cutting Your Thick Hair Short

Here are some tips to make cutting your thick hair short easier:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Use hair clips to keep the sections of hair separated and organized.
  • Use a spray bottle filled with water to dampen your hair, which can make it easier to cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to, especially if you have a lot of hair to cut.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting thick hair short:

Can I cut my hair short myself?

Yes, you can cut your thick hair short yourself, but it is important to be patient and take your time. You can also ask a friend or family member to help you.

What is the best haircut for thick hair?

There are many great haircuts for thick hair, including the bob, the pixie cut, and the layered cut. It depends on your face shape and personal style.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 6 to 8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care after Cutting Your Thick Hair Short

After cutting your thick hair short, it is important to take care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, which can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to cut your thick hair short, you can give yourself a new look that is both stylish and manageable. Remember to take your time, be patient, and follow the steps in this guide. Good luck!

Short Haircut For Square Face Woman How To Find The Perfect Style

Written by Christine Jun 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a square face, you may find it challenging to find the right haircut that flatters your features. A square face typically has a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a wide chin, which can make it look boxy. However, with the right haircut, you can soften your angles and create a more balanced look. In this article, we'll show you how to choose the best short haircut for square face women, and offer some tips on how to care for your new style.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Haircut for Square Face Women

Choosing the right haircut for your face shape is crucial to enhancing your natural beauty. When it comes to square faces, you want to create an illusion of length to balance out your features. The best way to do that is to go for a short haircut that is either layered or asymmetrical. Here are some of the best short haircuts for square face women:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that can work wonders for square faces. It's a low-maintenance style that can be customized to suit your face shape. A layered pixie cut can add volume to the crown and soften your jawline. You can also add side-swept bangs to create an asymmetrical look.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that can be adapted to suit any face shape. For square faces, a chin-length bob with layers can create a flattering frame for your face. You can also add texture to your bob with choppy layers or curls.

Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut is a bold and edgy style that can work well for square faces. It can create an illusion of length by having one side longer than the other. You can go for a short asymmetrical bob or a pixie cut with an asymmetrical fringe.

Step by Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut for Square Face Women

Getting a short haircut can be nerve-wracking, especially if you're used to having long hair. However, with the right guidance, you can make the transition smoothly. Here's a step by step guide to getting a short haircut for square face women:

Step 1: Find Inspiration

Before you head to the salon, it's important to have a clear idea of the type of short haircut you want. Look for inspiration in magazines, blogs, and social media. Compile a few pictures of the styles you like and show them to your hairstylist.

Step 2: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Once you have a few hairstyle options, book a consultation with your hairstylist. They can advise you on the best style for your face shape and hair type. They can also offer suggestions on how to style your new cut.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Before you get your haircut, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

Step 4: Get Your Haircut

Once you're in the salon, your hairstylist will shampoo and condition your hair. They will then cut your hair according to the style you've chosen. Don't be afraid to speak up if you're not happy with the cut.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

After your haircut, your hairstylist will style your hair. Ask them for tips on how to recreate the look at home. They may recommend using a specific hair product or styling tool.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut for Square Face Women

Once you've got your short haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and texture. Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

Tip 1: Use the Right Hair Products

Choose hair products that are specifically designed for short hair. Use a lightweight shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh down your hair. You can also use a styling product to add texture and hold.

Tip 2: Schedule Regular Trims

Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape. Schedule a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Tip 3: Experiment with Styling

Short haircuts can be versatile, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. You can try a sleek, straight look or add texture with curls or waves.

FAQ: Short Haircut for Square Face Women

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look even squarer?

A: No, a short haircut can actually soften your angles and create a more balanced look. The key is to choose a haircut that is either layered or asymmetrical.

Q: Can I still wear my short hair in a ponytail?

A: Yes, you can still wear your short hair in a ponytail. However, you may need to use hair accessories to keep your hair in place.

Q: Will a short haircut require a lot of maintenance?

A: Short haircuts can be low-maintenance, but they do require regular trims to maintain their shape. You may also need to use styling products to keep your hair in place.

Conclusion

A short haircut can be a great way to enhance your natural beauty, especially if you have a square face. By choosing the right haircut and taking care of your hair, you can create a fresh and stylish look. Use the tips in this article to find the perfect short haircut for square face women and enjoy your new style!

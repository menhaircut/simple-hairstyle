Should Eyebrows Match Hair Color Everything You Need To Know

Written by Bardi Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering whether your eyebrows should match your hair color? It's a common question, and the answer depends on your personal preference and style. In this blog post, we'll explore the topic in depth and provide you with tips and advice on how to achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How to Match Your Eyebrows to Your Hair Color
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Matching Your Eyebrows to Your Hair Color
  5. FAQs

Description

First things first, let's discuss why some people prefer to match their eyebrows to their hair color. The main reason is that it creates a cohesive and polished look. When your eyebrows and hair color match, it gives the impression that you put effort into your appearance, which can boost your confidence and self-esteem.

However, there's no hard and fast rule that says your eyebrows must match your hair color. In fact, many people prefer to have a slight contrast between the two, as it can add depth and dimension to your overall look. Ultimately, the decision is up to you and your personal style.

How to Match Your Eyebrows to Your Hair Color

If you decide that you want to match your eyebrows to your hair color, the first step is to determine your hair color category. There are three categories: warm, cool, and neutral. Warm hair colors include shades of red, orange, and yellow, while cool hair colors include shades of blue, green, and purple. Neutral hair colors fall somewhere in between.

Once you've determined your hair color category, you can choose a brow product that matches your hair color. For warm hair colors, opt for a brow product with a reddish or golden undertone. For cool hair colors, choose a brow product with an ashy undertone. For neutral hair colors, you can go for a brow product with a neutral undertone.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to match your eyebrows to your hair color:

  1. Determine your hair color category.
  2. Choose a brow product that matches your hair color.
  3. Fill in your eyebrows using the brow product.
  4. Blend the product using a spoolie brush.
  5. Set your eyebrows with a clear brow gel.

Tips for Matching Your Eyebrows to Your Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect match:

  • Start with a lighter hand and build up the product gradually.
  • Use short, feathery strokes to mimic the look of natural hairs.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match brow products to achieve the perfect shade.
  • Consider the shape of your eyebrows when filling them in - different shapes may require different techniques.
  • Use a highlighter or concealer to clean up any mistakes or smudges.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about matching eyebrows to hair color:

Should I match my eyebrows to my natural hair color or my dyed hair color?

If you dye your hair regularly, it's best to match your eyebrows to your current hair color. However, if you prefer your natural hair color, you can match your eyebrows to that instead.

What if I have highlights or lowlights in my hair?

If you have highlights or lowlights in your hair, you can choose to match your eyebrows to your overall hair color or to the specific highlights/lowlights. It's up to you and your personal preference.

What if my hair color changes frequently?

If you change your hair color frequently, you may want to invest in a brow product that is versatile and can be adjusted to match different shades. Alternatively, you can choose to have a slight contrast between your eyebrows and hair color, as it can still look cohesive and stylish.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools (such as curling irons and straighteners) too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and strength.

Matching your eyebrows to your hair color can be a great way to enhance your overall look. However, it's important to remember that there are no strict rules - ultimately, it's up to you and your personal style. Use the tips and advice in this blog post to achieve the perfect match and take care of your hair for healthy, vibrant locks.

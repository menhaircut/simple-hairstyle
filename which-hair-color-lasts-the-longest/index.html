Types of Hair Color

There are three main types of hair color: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent. Temporary hair color lasts for only a few washes and is a good option if you want to experiment with a new color without committing to it. Semi-permanent hair color lasts for several weeks and gradually fades away. Permanent hair color, as the name suggests, is designed to last until your hair grows out.

Which Hair Color Lasts the Longest?

Of the three types of hair color, permanent hair color lasts the longest. This is because it contains chemicals that penetrate the hair shaft and permanently change the color of your hair.

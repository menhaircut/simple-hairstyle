How To Cut Men s Short Hair With Scissors At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the barbershop every few weeks? Want to learn how to cut your own hair at home? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we will walk you through how to cut men's short hair with scissors at home. From the tools you'll need to the step-by-step process, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Tools You'll Need

Before we dive into the actual cutting process, let's talk about the tools you'll need. Here's what you'll need to get started:

  • Haircutting scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips (optional)
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape to catch hair clippings

How to Cut Men's Short Hair with Scissors: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will give you a better idea of how your hair naturally falls. Be sure to dry your hair completely before starting to cut.

Step 2: Determine the Length You Want

Use a comb to comb your hair straight down. Determine the length you want to cut off and use your fingers or a hair tie to mark the spot.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Using your scissors, start cutting your hair slowly and carefully. It's always best to start with small snips and work your way up. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.

Step 4: Use Clippers (Optional)

If you want an even shorter cut, you can use clippers to help. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you achieve your desired length.

Step 5: Blend the Sides and Back

When cutting the sides and back, be sure to blend the hair so it looks natural. Use your comb to help guide you and make small snips until you achieve the desired look.

Step 6: Check Your Progress

After you've finished cutting, use a mirror to check your progress. Look for any uneven spots or areas that need additional trimming.

Step 7: Finishing Touches

Once you're happy with your cut, use your scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven spots. You can also use clippers to clean up the neckline and sideburns.

Tips for Cutting Men's Short Hair with Scissors at Home

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use sharp scissors
  • Take your time
  • Start with small snips and work your way up
  • Blend the sides and back for a natural look
  • Check your progress using a mirror

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair at home?

Yes, you can! With the right tools and a little patience, you can achieve a great haircut at home.

What type of scissors should I use?

Use haircutting scissors, which are specifically designed for cutting hair. Avoid using regular household scissors, as these can damage your hair.

Do I need clippers?

No, clippers are optional. If you want a shorter cut, you can use clippers to help. If not, you can achieve a great cut with just scissors.

How to Take Care of Your Hair After Cutting It

Now that you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth
  • Avoid using hot tools, like blow dryers or straighteners, too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking fresh

Cutting men's short hair with scissors at home is a great way to save money and achieve a great haircut. With the right tools and a little patience, you can get the look you want without ever leaving your house. Happy cutting!

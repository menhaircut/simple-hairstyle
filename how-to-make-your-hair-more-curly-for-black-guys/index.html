How To Make Your Hair More Curly For Black Guys

Written by Robby Jul 01, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for ways to get more defined and curly hair? If you're a black guy with relaxed hair, you might find it challenging to achieve the curly look. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to make your hair more curly for black guys.

Description

Curly hair is a fashion statement that never goes out of style. But for black guys with relaxed hair, it can be challenging to get the perfect curls. Relaxing your hair can cause damage to your curls, and it can take time and effort to get them back. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve defined curls that will make you look and feel great.

How to Make Your Hair More Curly for Black Guys

Step 1: Use the Right Products

The first step to getting more defined and curly hair is to use the right products. Look for products that are designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products. These products will help define your curls and keep them looking healthy.

Step 2: Moisturize Your Hair

Moisturizing your hair is essential to getting defined and healthy curls. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends where hair tends to be drier.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

A diffuser is a great tool to help define your curls. Attach it to your blow dryer and use it on a low heat setting. Flip your head upside down and use the diffuser to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go. This will help define your curls and give them more bounce.

Step 4: Try Twist Outs

Twist outs are a great way to get defined curls. After washing and conditioning your hair, twist small sections of your hair around your finger, making sure to twist tightly. Leave the twists in overnight and unravel them in the morning. You'll be left with defined curls that will last for days.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making your hair more curly:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as a regular comb can cause breakage.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and cause it to become dry.
  • Protect your hair while sleeping by using a silk or satin pillowcase.

FAQ

Q: Can I get curly hair if I have relaxed hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve curly hair even if you have relaxed hair. Follow the steps we've outlined above and use products that are designed for curly hair to help define your curls.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is essential to getting defined and curly hair. Make sure to use products that are designed for your hair type, moisturize your hair regularly, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. With the right care, you can achieve healthy and defined curls that will make you look and feel great.

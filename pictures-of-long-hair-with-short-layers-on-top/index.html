Pictures Of Long Hair With Short Layers On Top A Trendy And Versatile Hairstyle

Written by William Jun 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a fresh and stylish haircut that can add volume and movement to your long locks? Then, you might want to try long hair with short layers on top. This haircut features longer strands on the bottom and shorter layers on the crown, which creates a flattering and dynamic effect. Moreover, you can customize the length and shape of the layers to suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences. In this article, we will show you some inspiring pictures of long hair with short layers on top, as well as tips on how to get, style, and maintain this trendy hairstyle.

How to Get Long Hair with Short Layers on Top

If you want to get long hair with short layers on top, you should go to a professional hair stylist who has experience in this type of haircut. They will assess your hair type, thickness, and length, as well as your face shape and features, to determine the best layering technique for you. Generally, the stylist will section your hair into horizontal layers and use scissors or a razor to cut the top layer shorter than the bottom one. They may also add some face-framing layers or bangs to enhance your facial structure and add dimension to your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some basic steps that your stylist may follow to give you long hair with short layers on top:

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry it until it is slightly damp.
  2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and part it in the middle or on the side, depending on your preference.
  3. Divide your hair into sections, starting from the nape of your neck and moving upwards.
  4. Clip the top layers away and focus on the bottom ones first.
  5. Cut the bottom layer straight across or at a slight angle, depending on the desired length.
  6. Release the next layer and use it as a guide to cut the top layer shorter.
  7. Blend the layers together by using a feathering or point-cutting technique.
  8. Trim any flyaways or split ends and check the symmetry and balance of the layers.
  9. Style your hair with a blow dryer, curling iron, or straightener, and add some volumizing or texturizing products to enhance the layers.

Tips for Long Hair with Short Layers on Top

Here are some useful tips to make the most of your long hair with short layers on top:

  • If you have fine or thin hair, opt for shorter and choppier layers that can add fullness and movement to your hair.
  • If you have thick or heavy hair, choose longer and more gradual layers that can reduce the bulk and weight of your hair.
  • If you have a round or square face, go for longer layers that can elongate your face and soften your features.
  • If you have an oval or heart-shaped face, try shorter layers that can frame your face and highlight your cheekbones and eyes.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, embrace the texture and volume of your hair by adding some layers that can define and shape your curls.
  • If you have straight or sleek hair, create some dimension and movement by adding some layers that can break up the monotony of your hair.

FAQ about Long Hair with Short Layers on Top

Q: Who can wear long hair with short layers on top?

A: Long hair with short layers on top can suit many different hair types, face shapes, and lifestyles. However, it is recommended for people who have medium to long hair and want to add some volume, shape, and movement to their hair. It can also work for people who want a low-maintenance and versatile hairstyle that can be styled in different ways.

Q: How often should I trim my long hair with short layers on top?

A: It depends on your hair growth rate and the desired length and shape of your layers. Generally, you should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the health and appearance of your hair. You can also ask your stylist for advice on how often to trim your hair based on your specific needs and goals.

Q: Can I color my long hair with short layers on top?

A: Yes, you can color your hair to enhance the beauty and uniqueness of your long hair with short layers on top. However, you should choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and natural hair color, and avoid over-processing your hair with harsh chemicals. You should also use color-safe and nourishing hair products to keep your hair shiny, healthy, and vibrant.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair with Short Layers on Top

To keep your long hair with short layers on top looking its best, you should follow some basic hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle and sulfate-free shampoo that can cleanse your hair without stripping its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair with a hydrating and nourishing conditioner that can soften and detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers, curling irons, or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair and make it brittle.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray or serum before using hot tools to minimize the damage and protect your hair from heat.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to brush your hair gently and avoid pulling or breaking your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes that can weaken and damage your hair, and opt for natural and gentle hair treatments instead.
  • Eat a balanced and healthy diet that can provide your hair with the essential nutrients such as protein, vitamins, and minerals that can promote hair growth and strength.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water and avoiding alcohol and caffeine that can dehydrate your hair and scalp.

With these tips and tricks, you can enjoy the beauty and versatility of long hair with short layers on top, and create a stunning and personalized hairstyle that reflects your personality and style. Whether you want a casual and tousled look or a sleek and polished one, you can achieve it with this trendy and timeless haircut.

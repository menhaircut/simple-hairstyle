How To Choose A Short Hairstyle For Your Face Shape

Written by Christine Jul 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're considering getting a short hairstyle, it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape. Not all short hairstyles are created equal, and what looks great on one person may not work as well on another. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of choosing a short hairstyle that flatters your face shape, so you can look and feel your best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose a Short Hairstyle for Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing a Short Hairstyle

Introduction

Short hair can be an incredibly stylish and low-maintenance option, but it's important to choose a style that suits your face shape. A great short haircut can enhance your best features and draw attention away from any areas you're not as confident about. Whether you have a round, square, oval, or heart-shaped face, there's a short hairstyle that will flatter your features.

How to Choose a Short Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a short hairstyle is to determine your face shape. This can be done by measuring the width and length of your face and noting any prominent features, such as your cheekbones or jawline. Once you've identified your face shape, you can start looking for short hairstyles that will complement your features.

If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a short hairstyle that adds height and lengthens your face. A layered bob or pixie cut with volume at the crown can help elongate your face and make it appear more oval-shaped.

If you have a square face, you'll want to soften your features with a short hairstyle that has layers or texture. A side-swept pixie cut or a tousled bob can help add movement and soften your jawline.

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off almost any short hairstyle. Consider a sleek bob or a textured pixie cut to show off your balanced features.

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to choose a short hairstyle that balances out your forehead and jawline. A layered bob or a choppy pixie cut with bangs can help draw attention away from your forehead and create a more balanced look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow when choosing a short hairstyle for your face shape:

  1. Determine your face shape by measuring the width and length of your face and noting any prominent features.
  2. Research short hairstyles that complement your face shape.
  3. Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a style.
  4. Bring pictures of your desired hairstyle to your hair stylist.
  5. Ask your stylist for their professional opinion on which short hairstyle would look best on you.
  6. Take into account your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair each day.
  7. Book your appointment and enjoy your new short hairstyle!

Tips for Choosing a Short Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair's natural texture and thickness when choosing a style. Some short hairstyles may require more maintenance than others.
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair each day. If you're low-maintenance, choose a style that doesn't require a lot of upkeep.
  • Bring pictures of short hairstyles you like to your stylist. This will give them a better idea of what you're looking for.
  • Consider experimenting with different hair colors and highlights to add dimension to your short hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new! Short hair can be a fun and versatile option that allows you to express your personal style.

FAQs

What if I have a long face?

If you have a long face, you'll want to choose a short hairstyle that adds width and volume to your face. A curly bob or a layered pixie cut with volume at the sides can help balance out your features.

Can I still wear bangs with a short hairstyle?

Absolutely! Bangs can be a great addition to a short hairstyle and can help balance out your features. Consider a side-swept fringe or choppy bangs to add interest to your short haircut.

How often will I need to get my short hair trimmed?

Short hair typically needs to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style. Talk to your stylist about the best maintenance schedule for your specific short hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires some special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Consider using a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and hold to your short hairstyle.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your short haircut.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for your hair.

Choosing a short hairstyle that complements your face shape can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips and consulting with your hair stylist, you can find the perfect short haircut that makes you look and feel your best.

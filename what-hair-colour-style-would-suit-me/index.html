What Hair Colour Style Would Suit Me

Written by Luffy Jun 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and looking to switch things up? Choosing a new hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what will suit you. In this article, we'll go through some tips on how to determine what hair colour style would suit you best.

Description

Before we get started, it's important to understand that there are many factors that can affect what hair colour will suit you. These include your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. It's also important to consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance you're willing to do on your hair.

How To

One way to determine what hair colour will suit you is to look at your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, such as yellow or peach, then warm hair colours like golden blonde or copper may suit you. If you have cool undertones, such as pink or blue, then cool hair colours like ash blonde or platinum may be a better fit.

Another factor to consider is your eye colour. If you have blue or green eyes, then warm hair colours like strawberry blonde or caramel may make your eyes pop. If you have brown eyes, then cool hair colours like dark chocolate or espresso may complement your eyes.

It's also important to consider your natural hair colour when choosing a new hair colour. If you have dark hair, then going too light may not suit you. Similarly, if you have light hair, then going too dark may be too drastic of a change.

Step By Step

1. Determine your skin tone. You can do this by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, then you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, then you have cool undertones.

2. Consider your eye colour. Warm hair colours may suit blue or green eyes, while cool hair colours may suit brown eyes.

3. Think about your natural hair colour. Going too light or too dark may not be the best fit for you.

4. Consult with a hair stylist. They can help you determine what hair colour will suit you best based on your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour.

Tips

- If you're not ready to commit to a new hair colour, try a temporary hair colour or highlights to see how it looks on you.

- Consider your lifestyle when choosing a new hair colour. If you don't have a lot of time to maintain your hair, then a low-maintenance hair colour may be the best fit for you.

FAQ

Q: What hair colour will suit me if I have pale skin?

A: If you have pale skin, then cool hair colours like platinum or ash blonde may suit you. Warm hair colours like copper or golden blonde may make your skin look washed out.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: While it's possible to dye your hair at home, it's recommended that you consult with a professional hair stylist. They can ensure that the hair colour is applied correctly and that it will suit you.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a new hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its vibrancy. Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for coloured hair, and avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curlers too often. Additionally, protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

Choosing a new hair colour can be a fun way to switch up your look. By considering your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour, you can determine what hair colour style would suit you best.

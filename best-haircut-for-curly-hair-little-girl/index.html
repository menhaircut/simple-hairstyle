Best Haircut For Curly Hair Little Girl

Written by Bardi Jun 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Curly hair can be difficult to manage, especially for little girls who love to play around and have fun. As a parent, you want your child to look her best and be comfortable with her hair. Choosing the right haircut is essential to give your little girl the confidence to embrace her natural curls. In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for curly hair little girl, how to do it, step by step, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQs

Description

The best haircut for curly hair little girl is a layered cut that enhances the natural curl pattern and adds volume to the hair. Layers help to distribute the weight of the hair evenly, which makes it easier to manage and style. The length of the hair should be kept at shoulder-length or shorter to prevent tangling and breakage. A shorter haircut also adds bounce to the curls and makes them look more defined.

How to

When choosing a haircut for your little girl's curly hair, it's important to consider her face shape and hair type. It's also a good idea to bring pictures of the desired haircut to the stylist to ensure that the final result is what you envisioned. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Choose the right stylist

Not all stylists are familiar with cutting curly hair, so it's important to find someone who has experience with this hair type. Look for a stylist who specializes in curly hair or ask for recommendations from friends and family.

2. Consider the face shape

The haircut should flatter your little girl's face shape. Round faces look best with layered haircuts that add volume to the top of the head. Oval faces can pull off most haircuts, but a shoulder-length cut with long layers is a good option. Square faces look great with a layered bob that hits just below the chin.

3. Embrace the natural curl pattern

The best haircut for curly hair little girl should enhance the natural curl pattern. Avoid straightening or relaxing the hair, as this can cause damage and make the hair harder to manage. Instead, opt for a layered cut that adds definition to the curls.

Step by step

Here's how to achieve the best haircut for curly hair little girl:

1. Wash and detangle the hair

Start by washing the hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Gently detangle the hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.

2. Section the hair

Section the hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back. Clip each section to keep it out of the way.

3. Cut the hair

Starting at the back, take a small section of hair and cut it at an angle. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way around the head. Be sure to cut the hair at an angle to create layers.

4. Blend the layers

Once all the hair has been cut, go back and blend the layers by cutting small sections of hair at a time. This will create a seamless look and prevent any choppy edges.

5. Style the hair

Apply a curl-enhancing product to the hair and scrunch it with your hands. Let the hair air-dry or use a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer to dry the hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid brushing curly hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to the hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer to dry the hair without causing frizz.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for curly hair?

The best haircut for curly hair is a layered cut that enhances the natural curl pattern and adds volume to the hair.

How often should I cut my little girl's curly hair?

Curly hair should be trimmed every 8-12 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the haircut.

Can I straighten my little girl's curly hair?

Straightening curly hair can cause damage and make the hair harder to manage. It's best to embrace the natural curl pattern and opt for a layered haircut that enhances it.

Hair Care

Maintaining curly hair requires a little extra care. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash the hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing the hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to the hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim the hair every 8-12 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the haircut.

In conclusion, the best haircut for curly hair little girl is a layered cut that enhances the natural curl pattern and adds volume to the hair. It's important to choose the right stylist, consider the face shape, and embrace the natural curl pattern. With these tips and a little extra care, your little girl's curly hair will look beautiful and healthy.

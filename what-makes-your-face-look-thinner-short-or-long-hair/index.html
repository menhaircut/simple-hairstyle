What Makes Your Face Look Thinner Short Or Long Hair

Written by Daniel Jul 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you often wonder what haircut would suit your face shape and make it look thinner? Whether you have a round, square, heart, or oval face, choosing the right hairstyle can enhance your features and make your face appear slimmer. In this article, we’ll explore what makes your face look thinner: short or long hair.

Description

Our face shape is determined by the structure of our bones, which cannot be changed. However, we can create an illusion of a slimmer face by choosing the right hairstyle. Short haircuts tend to make the face look more angular and defined, while long hair can soften the features and create a more oval shape.

How to

If you’re considering a new haircut to make your face look thinner, here are some tips to keep in mind:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before choosing a haircut, it’s important to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and examining the outline of your face in the mirror. There are four main face shapes:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Oval

Each shape has its own characteristics and ideal haircuts to enhance them.

Step 2: Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape

Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can choose a haircut that flatters your features. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round face: A short, angular haircut with layers can create the illusion of a slimmer face.
  • Square face: Soft, layered haircuts that frame the face can soften the angles and make the face appear less boxy.
  • Heart-shaped face: Long, layered haircuts that frame the face can balance out a wider forehead and narrow chin.
  • Oval face: This face shape can pull off many different haircuts, but a long, layered style can enhance the symmetry of the face.

Step 3: Consider your hair texture and thickness

When choosing a haircut, it’s important to consider your hair texture and thickness. Some haircuts may not work well with curly or fine hair, for example. Your hairstylist can help you choose a style that works best for your hair type.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut to make your face look thinner:

  • Avoid heavy bangs that cover the forehead, as they can make the face look wider.
  • Consider adding layers to create movement and texture.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to see what flatters your face shape.
  • Accessorize with earrings or a necklace to draw attention away from the face.

FAQ

Does short hair make your face look thinner?

Short haircuts tend to create an angular shape, which can make the face look slimmer. However, it’s important to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair type.

Does long hair make your face look thinner?

Long hair can soften the features and create a more oval shape, which can make the face appear slimmer. Again, it’s important to choose a haircut that works with your face shape and hair type.

Can bangs make your face look thinner?

Heavy bangs that cover the forehead can make the face look wider, so it’s best to avoid them if you’re trying to create the illusion of a slimmer face. However, side-swept or wispy bangs can be flattering for some face shapes.

Hair Care

Whether you choose a short or long haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

Overall, choosing the right haircut can enhance your features and make your face appear slimmer. Whether you prefer short or long hair, consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style to find a haircut that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

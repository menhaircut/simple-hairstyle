How To Thicken Thin Fine Hair

Written by William Nov 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thin fine hair, you may feel like there's nothing you can do to make it look thicker and fuller. But there are actually several ways to add volume and thickness to your hair. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for how to thicken thin fine hair.

Description

Thin fine hair can be frustrating to deal with, but with the right techniques and products, you can add volume and thickness to your locks. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for how to thicken thin fine hair.

How to

There are several ways to thicken thin fine hair, including:

1. Use volumizing products

Look for shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are designed to add volume and thickness to hair. These products usually contain ingredients like proteins and polymers that help to plump up the hair shaft.

2. Blow-dry your hair upside down

To add volume to your hair, blow-dry it upside down. This will help to lift the roots and create more volume at the crown of your head.

3. Use a round brush

When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to add even more volume. Wrap sections of hair around the brush and blow-dry in an upward motion to create lift and volume.

4. Try a volumizing mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair before blow-drying to add even more volume and thickness.

5. Use a thickening spray

A thickening spray can help to add volume and thickness to your hair without weighing it down. Apply the spray to your roots and then blow-dry your hair upside down for maximum volume.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo. Look for a product that contains ingredients like proteins and polymers that help to add volume and thickness to hair.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing conditioner

Apply a volumizing conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Avoid applying the conditioner to your roots, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

Step 3: Towel-dry your hair

After washing your hair, gently towel-dry it to remove excess water.

Step 4: Apply a volumizing mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to add volume and thickness to your hair.

Step 5: Blow-dry your hair upside down

Flip your head upside down and use a blow-dryer to dry your hair. Use a round brush to add even more volume, wrapping sections of hair around the brush and blow-drying in an upward motion.

Step 6: Use a thickening spray

After blow-drying your hair, apply a thickening spray to your roots. This will help to add even more volume and thickness to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for how to thicken thin fine hair:

1. Avoid heavy products

Avoid using heavy products like oils and serums, as these can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

2. Get regular trims

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look thinner.

3. Use a clarifying shampoo

Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup from your hair. This will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent it from looking flat and lifeless.

FAQ

Q: Can thin fine hair be thickened?

A: Yes, there are several ways to thicken thin fine hair, including using volumizing products, blow-drying your hair upside down, and using a thickening spray.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have thin fine hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day if you have thin fine hair. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it look even thinner.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important if you want to keep it looking healthy and thick. Here are some tips for how to take care of thin fine hair:

1. Use a heat protectant

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like blow-dryers and flat irons. This will help to protect your hair from damage and keep it looking healthy.

2. Avoid tight hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids, as these can pull on your hair and make it look thinner.

3. Eat a healthy diet

Eating a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals can help to keep your hair looking thick and healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can add volume and thickness to your thin fine hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

How To Make Your Hair Wavy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a change in your hair routine, making your hair wavy is a great option. It's a timeless and effortless look that can be achieved with just a few simple steps. In this article, we will guide you through the process of making your hair wavy and provide some helpful tips to ensure your new style lasts all day.

How to Make Your Hair Wavy

Making your hair wavy can be done in several different ways, depending on your hair type and the tools you have available. Below are some of the most popular methods:

Method 1: Braids

One of the simplest ways to make your hair wavy is by braiding it. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into three sections.
  3. Braid each section tightly and secure with a hair tie.
  4. Leave the braids in for several hours or overnight.
  5. Remove the hair ties and gently finger comb your hair to separate the waves.

Tip: The tighter the braids, the tighter the waves will be.

Method 2: Curling Iron

If you prefer more defined waves, a curling iron may be the way to go. Follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair and clip away the top layers.
  3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds.
  4. Release the hair from the curling iron and repeat with the remaining sections.
  5. Once all sections are curled, use your fingers to gently separate the waves.

Tip: Use a heat protectant spray before using the curling iron to prevent damage to your hair.

Method 3: Salt Spray

If you want a more natural, beachy look, a salt spray can help you achieve it. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a salt spray to your hair, working from the roots to the ends.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create waves.
  4. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Tip: Use a lightweight salt spray to avoid weighing down your hair.

Tips for Making Your Waves Last

Once you've achieved the perfect waves, you'll want to make sure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips to help:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the waves in place.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to maintain the waves overnight.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.

FAQ

Can I make my hair wavy without heat?

Yes, braiding your hair is a great way to make it wavy without using heat.

How long will my waves last?

This will depend on your hair type and the method you use. Waves created with a curling iron may last longer than those created with a salt spray, for example.

Can I make my hair wavy if it's naturally straight?

Yes, you can still make your hair wavy even if it's naturally straight. It may take a bit more effort and the waves may not last as long, but it's definitely possible.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Wavy hair can be prone to dryness and frizz, so it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to make your hair wavy in no time. Experiment with different methods and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to mix things up to keep your style fresh and exciting.

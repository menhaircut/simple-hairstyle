What Rollers Are Best For Thick Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick hair, you know that finding the right styling tools can be a challenge. When it comes to rollers, it's important to choose the right size and material for your hair type. In this article, we'll discuss the best rollers for thick hair and provide tips on how to use them.

Description

Thick hair requires rollers that can hold and shape the hair without causing damage or breakage. The best rollers for thick hair are those that are large in size and made of high-quality materials that can withstand the weight and volume of the hair.

How to Choose the Right Rollers for Thick Hair

When choosing rollers for thick hair, consider the following factors:

Size

Large rollers are best for thick hair as they can hold more hair and create bigger, bouncier curls. Look for rollers that are at least 2 inches in diameter.

Material

The material of the rollers is also important. Ceramic and tourmaline rollers are best for thick hair as they heat up quickly and evenly, reducing the risk of damage to the hair.

Step by Step Guide: How to Use Rollers on Thick Hair

Follow these steps to use rollers on thick hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Towel dry your hair until it is damp but not dripping wet. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to roll your hair and ensure that every section is evenly curled.

Step 3: Roll Your Hair

Starting at the ends of your hair, wrap each section around the roller, rolling it up towards your scalp. Secure the roller in place with a clip or pin.

Step 4: Set Your Rollers

Leave the rollers in your hair for at least 30 minutes to allow the curls to set. You can speed up the process by using a hair dryer or sitting under a hooded dryer.

Step 5: Remove the Rollers

Once the curls have set, remove the rollers from your hair. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to gently separate the curls and create your desired style.

Tips for Using Rollers on Thick Hair

Here are some tips to get the best results when using rollers on thick hair:

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant Spray

Always use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage when using rollers.

Tip 2: Don't Overload Your Rollers

Make sure you don't overload your rollers with too much hair as this can cause them to slip out or not curl properly.

Tip 3: Use the Right Size Rollers

Choose large rollers that are at least 2 inches in diameter to ensure that you get big, bouncy curls.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

In addition to using the right rollers, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using high heat when styling your hair and always use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Overall, choosing the right rollers for thick hair is essential for achieving beautiful, bouncy curls. Use large rollers made of high-quality materials and follow the steps and tips outlined in this article to get the best results.

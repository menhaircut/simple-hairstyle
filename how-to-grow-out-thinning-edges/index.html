How To Grow Out Thinning Edges Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jun 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Thinning edges can be a frustrating and embarrassing issue for many people. Whether it's due to over-styling, harsh chemicals, or genetics, thinning edges can affect anyone. But the good news is that there are ways to grow out thinning edges and restore your hairline. In this article, we'll provide you with some tips and tricks to help you grow out your thinning edges.

How to Grow Out Thinning Edges: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Identify the Cause of Thinning Edges

The first step in growing out thinning edges is to identify the underlying cause. If the cause is due to over-styling or harsh chemicals, then it's important to stop these practices immediately. If the cause is genetics or a medical condition, then you may need to seek professional help.

Step 2: Adopt a Healthy Hair Care Routine

A healthy hair care routine is essential for growing out thinning edges. This includes washing your hair regularly with a mild shampoo, deep conditioning your hair, and avoiding heat styling tools. Additionally, it's important to protect your hair from damage by wearing a satin or silk scarf to bed.

Step 3: Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help to increase blood flow to the hair follicles and promote hair growth. Use your fingers to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

Step 4: Use Natural Oils

Natural oils such as castor oil, coconut oil, and olive oil can help to moisturize your scalp and promote hair growth. Apply a small amount of oil to your scalp and massage it in using your fingers.

Step 5: Take Supplements

Taking supplements such as biotin, vitamin E, and iron can help to promote hair growth and strengthen your hair. Consult with your doctor before taking any supplements.

Tips for Growing Out Thinning Edges

Here are some additional tips to help you grow out your thinning edges:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles such as braids and weaves
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as relaxers and hair dyes
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat styling tools
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to grow out thinning edges?

A: Growing out thinning edges can take anywhere from a few months to a year or more, depending on the underlying cause and how well you take care of your hair.

Q: Can I use hair growth products to grow out my thinning edges?

A: Hair growth products such as minoxidil can help to promote hair growth, but it's important to consult with your doctor before using any products.

Q: Can stress cause thinning edges?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss and thinning edges. It's important to manage your stress levels to prevent hair loss.

Hair Care for Thinning Edges

Proper hair care is essential for growing out thinning edges. Here are some additional tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles
  • Wear a satin or silk scarf to bed
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

By following these tips and tricks, you can grow out your thinning edges and restore your hairline. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and consult with a professional if you have any concerns.

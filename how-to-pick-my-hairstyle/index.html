How To Pick My Hairstyle Tips Steps And Faqs

Written by Aprilia Aug 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing a hairstyle can be overwhelming, especially when you have no idea where to start. With so many options available, it's easy to feel lost and confused. However, picking the perfect hairstyle is essential. Your hairstyle can enhance your beauty and boost your confidence. In this post, we'll guide you through the process of picking the right hairstyle for you. Here's what we'll cover:

Tip 1: Consider Your Face Shape

Knowing your face shape is the first step to picking the right hairstyle. There are six common face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each shape suits different hairstyles, so it's essential to know your face shape to choose a flattering hairstyle. Here's a quick guide:

  • Oval: Almost any hairstyle suits this face shape.
  • Round: Hairstyles that add height and length to the face, such as layered cuts and side-swept bangs.
  • Square: Soft, wavy hairstyles and long layers work well with this face shape.
  • Heart: Pixie cuts, side-swept bangs, and chin-length bobs look great on this face shape.
  • Diamond: Chin-length bobs, side-swept bangs, and layered cuts work well with this face shape.
  • Oblong: Short hairstyles, such as bobs and pixie cuts, add width to the face, making it look more balanced.

Tip 2: Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle plays a crucial role in picking the perfect hairstyle. If you're always on the go, you need a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires little upkeep. On the other hand, if you have more time to spare, you can opt for a more elaborate hairstyle. Consider your daily routine, job, and hobbies when picking your hairstyle.

Tip 3: Consider Your Hair Type and Texture

Your hair type and texture also affect which hairstyles will work best for you. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to avoid short hairstyles that require frequent straightening. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut that adds volume and texture. Consider your hair's natural texture and thickness when picking your hairstyle.

Step 1: Research and Gather Inspiration

Before you visit your stylist, gather inspiration for your ideal hairstyle. Browse social media, magazines, and websites to find hairstyles that appeal to you. Save pictures and create a mood board to bring to your stylist. This will help your stylist understand your vision and recommend the best hairstyle for you.

Step 2: Consult with a Professional Stylist

Consulting with a professional stylist is crucial when picking your hairstyle. Your stylist can help you determine which hairstyles will suit your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. They can also recommend the best haircuts and styling products to achieve your desired look. Don't be afraid to ask questions and voice your concerns.

Step 3: Try On Different Hairstyles Virtually

If you're still unsure which hairstyle to choose, try on different hairstyles virtually. There are many apps and websites that allow you to upload a picture of yourself and see how different hairstyles will look on you. This can give you a better idea of how the hairstyle will suit your face shape and features.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style you choose. Generally, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle's shape and length.

Q: Can I change my hairstyle if I don't like it?

A: Yes, you can always change your hairstyle. However, keep in mind that drastic changes may require time to adjust. Consider making gradual changes to your hairstyle to avoid shock.

Q: How do I style my hair at home?

A: Your stylist can recommend the best styling products for your hair type and style. Follow their instructions and use the recommended products to achieve your desired look. Practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've picked your hairstyle, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and length.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and dyes that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

With these tips, steps, and FAQs, you'll be able to pick the perfect hairstyle for you. Remember, your hairstyle should enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and comfortable. Happy hairstyling!

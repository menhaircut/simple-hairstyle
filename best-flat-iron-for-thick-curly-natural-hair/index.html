Best Flat Iron For Thick Curly Natural Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 18, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick, curly, and natural hair, then finding the right flat iron can be a daunting task. You need a flat iron that can straighten your hair without damaging it or causing it to lose its natural texture. In this blog post, we will introduce you to the best flat iron for thick curly natural hair in 2023.

Description

The best flat iron for thick curly natural hair is one that has high heat settings, ceramic plates, and ion technology. These features ensure that your hair is straightened without causing any damage. The flat iron should also be wide enough to accommodate your hair thickness and length.

How to Choose the Best Flat Iron for Thick Curly Natural Hair

When choosing a flat iron, there are several factors to consider. These include:

Heat Settings

Choose a flat iron that has adjustable heat settings. This will allow you to customize the temperature to suit your hair type. For thick, curly, and natural hair, a temperature of 380-410°F is ideal.

Plate Material

Flat irons with ceramic plates are the best for thick curly natural hair. Ceramic plates distribute heat evenly, reducing the risk of heat damage. They also produce negative ions that help to eliminate frizz and static.

Plate Width

The plate width should be wide enough to accommodate your hair thickness and length. A wider plate will allow you to straighten your hair faster and more efficiently.

Price

The best flat iron for thick curly natural hair doesn't have to be expensive. However, investing in a high-quality flat iron will save you money in the long run.

Step by Step Guide to Using a Flat Iron on Thick Curly Natural Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use a flat iron on thick curly natural hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair moisturized.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair before using a flat iron. This will help to reduce the risk of heat damage.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to straighten your hair.

Step 4: Straighten Your Hair

Start straightening your hair from the roots to the tips. Use a comb to guide the flat iron and ensure that each section is straightened evenly.

Step 5: Finish with a Serum

Apply a serum to your hair to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Using a Flat Iron on Thick Curly Natural Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when using a flat iron on thick curly natural hair:

- Use a heat protectant to reduce the risk of heat damage. - Section your hair into small sections to make it easier to straighten. - Use a comb to guide the flat iron and ensure that each section is straightened evenly. - Use a serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a flat iron on wet hair?

A: No, you should never use a flat iron on wet hair. Wet hair is more susceptible to heat damage, and using a flat iron on wet hair can cause your hair to break.

Q: How often should I use a flat iron on my hair?

A: You should use a flat iron on your hair no more than once a week. Using a flat iron too often can cause heat damage and make your hair more prone to breakage.

Q: Can I straighten my hair without using a flat iron?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair without using a flat iron. You can use a blow dryer, a round brush, and a hair straightening brush to straighten your hair.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important, especially when using heat-styling tools like flat irons. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

- Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized. - Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy. - Use a heat protectant before using any heat-styling tools. - Avoid using heat-styling tools too often. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, the best flat iron for thick curly natural hair is one that has high heat settings, ceramic plates, and ion technology. Choosing the right flat iron and using it correctly will help you achieve straight hair without causing any damage. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.

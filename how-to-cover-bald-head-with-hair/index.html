How To Cover Bald Head With Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jul 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're dealing with hair loss or balding, you might be wondering how to cover your bald head with hair. While there are several options available, not all of them may work for you. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to cover bald head with hair and regain your confidence.

Description

Balding can be a frustrating and disheartening experience for many people. It can affect self-esteem and make you feel less confident. While there are several hair loss treatments available, not everyone wants to go down that route. Fortunately, there are ways to cover bald head with hair and feel more confident.

How to Cover Bald Head with Hair

There are several ways to cover bald head with hair. Here are some of the most popular options:

Hairpieces or Wigs

Hairpieces or wigs are the most popular option for covering bald head with hair. They come in different styles, colors, and lengths, and can be made from synthetic or real hair. You can choose a hairpiece or wig that matches your natural hair color and style, or try something new.

Scalp Micropigmentation

Scalp micropigmentation is a cosmetic tattooing technique that involves injecting small dots of pigment into the scalp to mimic hair follicles. It creates the illusion of a full head of hair and can be a great option for those who don't want to wear a wig or hairpiece.

Hair Transplant

A hair transplant is a surgical procedure that involves transplanting hair follicles from one part of the scalp to another. It can be a permanent solution to balding, but it can be expensive and may not be suitable for everyone.

Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are tiny fibers made from keratin that attach to the existing hair and create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair. They are easy to apply and can be a great temporary solution for covering bald spots.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cover bald head with hair:

Step 1: Choose Your Option

Decide which option you want to go for - hairpieces or wigs, scalp micropigmentation, hair transplant, or hair fibers.

Step 2: Consult with a Professional

Consult with a professional who can guide you on which option is best for you based on your hair type, budget, and lifestyle.

Step 3: Choose Your Style and Color

If you're going for a hairpiece or wig, choose a style and color that matches your natural hair. If you're going for scalp micropigmentation or hair fibers, choose a color that matches your natural hair color.

Step 4: Apply or Get the Treatment

Apply the hair fibers or hairpiece as per the instructions, or get the scalp micropigmentation or hair transplant treatment done by a professional.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as per your preference, and enjoy your new look!

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cover bald head with hair:

  • Choose an option that matches your natural hair style and color.
  • Consult with a professional before making a decision.
  • Invest in good quality hairpieces or wigs.
  • Take care of your hair and scalp to prevent further hair loss.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to cover bald head with hair:

Can I wear a hat or cap to cover my bald head?

Yes, you can wear a hat or cap to cover your bald head. However, it is not a permanent solution and may not be suitable for all occasions.

Is scalp micropigmentation painful?

Scalp micropigmentation is not usually painful, but some people may experience mild discomfort during the procedure.

Can I use hair fibers on wet hair?

No, hair fibers should be applied to dry hair for best results.

Hair Care

Whether you're using a hairpiece, wig, or hair fibers, it's important to take care of your hair and scalp to prevent further hair loss. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can cover bald head with hair and regain your confidence. Remember that there are several options available, so consult with a professional before making a decision.

Little Boy Haircuts Long On Top Short On Sides A Trendy And Practical Choice

If you're looking for a cool and low-maintenance hairstyle for your little boy, a long on top and short on sides haircut might be just the thing. This style has been popular for years and remains a top choice among parents and kids alike. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this look, provide tips on how to care for it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Get Little Boy Haircuts Long on Top Short on Sides

The long on top and short on sides haircut is a versatile style that can be tailored to suit your little boy's hair type, face shape, and personal style. To achieve this look, you'll need to keep the hair on the top of your child's head longer while trimming the sides and back shorter. You can achieve this style with clippers or scissors, depending on your preference and skill level.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to give your child a little boy haircut long on top short on sides:

  1. Start by washing your child's hair and towel-drying it until it's damp.
  2. Use clippers or scissors to trim the sides and back of your child's hair to the desired length. For a classic look, use a #3 or #4 guard for the sides and back.
  3. Gradually blend the sides and top by using a longer guard or scissors to trim the hair around the crown.
  4. Use scissors to cut the hair on the top of your child's head to the desired length. You can leave it long and tousled or give it a more structured look by using a comb to create a side part or a quiff.
  5. Trim the hair around the ears and neckline to give the cut a clean, polished look.
  6. Style the hair with a bit of gel or pomade if desired.

It's important to note that every child's hair is different, so don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles until you find the perfect look for your little one.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Once you've achieved the perfect long on top short on sides cut, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking sharp. Here are some tips:

  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Teach your child how to brush and style his own hair to encourage independence and self-care.

FAQ

What face shapes suit little boy haircuts long on top short on sides?

The long on top and short on sides cut is a versatile style that can suit a range of face shapes. However, it tends to look best on boys with round or square faces, as it helps to create a more angular, defined look.

Can you achieve the long on top short on sides style with curly hair?

Absolutely! In fact, curly hair can add an extra dimension of texture and volume to this style. To achieve the look, simply leave the curls on top longer and use clippers or scissors to trim the sides and back.

Is the long on top short on sides style suitable for all ages?

Yes, this style can work for boys of all ages, from toddlers to teenagers. It's a classic, timeless look that never goes out of style.

Hair Care for Little Boys

Caring for your little boy's hair can be simple and fun with the right tools and products. Here are some tips:

  • Use a soft-bristled brush or comb to detangle the hair gently.
  • Wash the hair every 2-3 days with a mild, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep the hair hydrated and tangle-free.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or flat irons, as these can damage the hair.
  • Encourage your child to wear a hat or cap in the sun to protect his scalp and hair from UV damage.

With these tips and tricks, you can help your little boy look and feel his best with a trendy and practical long on top short on sides haircut.

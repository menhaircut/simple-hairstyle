Best Way To Wear Hair For Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Oct 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the right hairstyle for your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best ways to wear your hair to flatter your round face shape. From haircuts to styling tips, we’ve got you covered. So let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Introduction
  • Best Haircuts for Round Faces
  • Styling Tips for Round Faces
  • Hair Accessories for Round Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Introduction

Round faces are characterized by a soft, circular shape with fuller cheeks and a rounded chin. The best way to wear your hair for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles. This can be achieved through haircuts, styling, and accessories.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces

The right haircut can make all the difference when it comes to flattering a round face. Here are some haircuts to consider:

Long Layers

Long layers can help elongate the face and create the illusion of angles. Avoid blunt cuts and opt for layers that start at or below the chin.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob can add angles and dimension to a round face. The longer side of the bob should fall below the chin, while the shorter side should be above the chin.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help elongate the face and draw attention away from the cheeks. Avoid blunt bangs and opt for a wispy, layered look.

Remember to always consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape.

Styling Tips for Round Faces

In addition to haircuts, styling can also play a role in flattering a round face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Part Your Hair to the Side

A side part can help create the illusion of angles and elongate the face. Avoid center parts, as they can make the face appear rounder.

Use a Curling Iron

Curling your hair can add volume and dimension to a round face. Opt for loose, beachy waves instead of tight curls.

Avoid Volume at the Crown

Volume at the crown can add height to the face and make it appear rounder. Instead, focus on adding volume to the ends of your hair.

Hair Accessories for Round Faces

Accessories can also be a great way to flatter a round face. Here are some ideas:

Headbands

A thin headband can help create the illusion of angles and draw attention away from the cheeks.

Scarves

A scarf tied around the head can add height and dimension to a round face.

Statement Earrings

Statement earrings can help elongate the face and draw attention to the jawline.

FAQs

What hairstyles should be avoided for round faces?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the crown, such as bouffants or high ponytails. Blunt bangs and chin-length bobs should also be avoided.

Can short hair be flattering for round faces?

Yes, short hair can be flattering for round faces. Opt for a pixie cut or an asymmetrical bob to add angles and dimension.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Finally, here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

Avoid Heat Damage

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it appear dull and lifeless. Use heat protectant sprays and avoid using heat styling tools every day.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

A weekly deep conditioning treatment can help keep your hair soft and shiny. Look for treatments that contain ingredients like keratin and argan oil.

Conclusion

Flattering a round face can be achieved through the right haircut, styling, and accessories. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape, and use these tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

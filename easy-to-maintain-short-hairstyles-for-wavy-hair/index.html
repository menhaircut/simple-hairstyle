Description

Short hairstyles for wavy hair are perfect for those who want a low maintenance look that still looks stylish. These hairstyles are not only easy to maintain, but they also require minimal styling time, making them perfect for busy individuals who still want to look their best.

Whether you have fine or thick wavy hair, there are many styles to choose from that will flatter your face shape and enhance your natural waves. In this article, we will discuss some easy to maintain short hairstyles for wavy hair that you can try out for yourself.

How to

Before getting into the specific hairstyles, it is important to understand some basic tips for maintaining short wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Use a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or curl cream, to enhance your natural waves.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Step by Step

1. Bob with Side Swept Bangs

This classic bob with side swept bangs is a great option for those with wavy hair. It is easy to maintain and can be styled in under 10 minutes.

To achieve this look:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a lightweight styling product to enhance your waves, such as a mousse or curl cream.
  3. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a straightener to add a slight bend to the ends of your hair.
  5. Finish with a texturizing spray to add some definition to your waves.

This style can be worn straight or with a slight wave, making it versatile for any occasion.

2. Pixie Cut with Side Swept Bangs

If you're looking for a bold, edgy style, a pixie cut with side swept bangs is a great option. This hairstyle is easy to maintain and requires minimal styling time.

To achieve this look:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a lightweight styling product to enhance your waves, such as a mousse or curl cream.
  3. Blow dry your hair, using your fingers to scrunch your hair and enhance your waves.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray to add some definition to your waves.

This style is perfect for those who want a low maintenance, yet stylish look.

Tips

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands or hair clips for a fun, playful look.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

FAQ

What face shape is best for short hairstyles?

Short hairstyles can flatter any face shape, but it is important to choose a style that complements your unique features. For example, those with a round face may want to opt for a longer pixie cut with side swept bangs to elongate their face.

Can I wear short hairstyles with curly hair?

Absolutely! Short hairstyles can look great on those with curly hair, as they can enhance your natural texture and create a fun, playful look.

Hair Care

To keep your short wavy hair looking its best, it is important to maintain a healthy hair care routine. This includes using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoiding over-styling with heat tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Additionally, it is important to use a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or curl cream, to enhance your natural waves and prevent frizz. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.

By following these tips and trying out some of the easy to maintain short hairstyles for wavy hair discussed in this article, you can achieve a stylish, low maintenance look that is perfect for any occasion.

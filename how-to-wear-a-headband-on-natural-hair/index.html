How To Wear A Headband On Natural Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Headbands are a great way to add some flair to your natural hair while keeping it out of your face. However, not everyone knows how to wear a headband on natural hair without causing damage or discomfort. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to wear a headband on natural hair, as well as some tips and FAQs.

How to Wear a Headband on Natural Hair: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Headband

The first step to wearing a headband on natural hair is to choose the right headband. Not all headbands are created equal, and some may be too tight or too loose for your hair type. Look for headbands that are made with soft, stretchy materials like cotton or satin, and avoid those that are too tight or have hard plastic grips.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before you put on your headband, prep your hair by detangling and moisturizing it. This will make it easier to style and prevent breakage. Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair, and use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle any knots or tangles.

Step 3: Place the Headband

To place the headband, start by slipping it over your head and positioning it where you want it to sit. You can wear it closer to your hairline or further back on your head, depending on your preference. Make sure the headband is snug but not too tight, and adjust it if necessary.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once the headband is in place, you can start styling your hair. You can wear your hair down or in a ponytail or bun, depending on the look you’re going for. If you’re wearing your hair down, you can add some curls or waves for a more polished look. If you’re wearing a ponytail or bun, make sure the headband is covering your hairline and not slipping back.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your look in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help prevent frizz and keep your hair looking polished all day.

Tips for Wearing a Headband on Natural Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing a headband on natural hair:

  • Choose a headband that complements your hair type and style.
  • Don’t wear the headband too tight or for too long, as this can cause damage or discomfort.
  • Experiment with different styles, like wearing your hair down, in a ponytail, or in a bun.
  • Accessorize with earrings or other jewelry to complete the look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wear a headband on wet hair?

It’s best to avoid wearing a headband on wet hair, as this can cause breakage and damage. Wait until your hair is dry or damp before putting on a headband.

How often can I wear a headband on natural hair?

You can wear a headband on natural hair as often as you like, but it’s best to give your hair a break every now and then to prevent damage.

How do I prevent my headband from slipping?

To prevent your headband from slipping, make sure it’s snug but not too tight. You can also use bobby pins or hair clips to secure the headband in place.

Hair Care for Natural Hair

In addition to wearing a headband, it’s important to take proper care of your natural hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Moisturize your hair with a leave-in conditioner or oil.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, or use a heat protectant if you do.
  • Protect your hair at night with a satin or silk bonnet or pillowcase.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear a headband on your natural hair without causing damage or discomfort. Remember to choose the right headband, prep your hair, style it, and finish with hairspray for a polished look that lasts all day.

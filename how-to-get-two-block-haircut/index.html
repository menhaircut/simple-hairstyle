Get The Perfect Two Block Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and stylish haircut, the two block haircut might be the right choice for you. This trendy haircut has been popularized by K-Pop stars and has become a hit among men and women alike. In this guide, we will take you through everything you need to know to get the perfect two block haircut.

Description

The two block haircut is a short hairstyle that features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides and back. The hair on the top is cut to create a block shape, while the sides and back are tapered. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in many ways, from slicked back to messy.

How to Get the Two Block Haircut

Getting the two block haircut is not as difficult as it might seem. However, it is important to go to a professional hairstylist who has experience with this type of haircut. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hairstylist

Look for a hairstylist who has experience with the two block haircut. Ask for recommendations from friends or search online for reviews. Make sure to bring photos of the haircut you want to show your hairstylist.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length of the hair on the top. The length can vary depending on your personal style and the shape of your face. The hairstylist will help you decide on the right length.

Step 3: Cut the Top

The hairstylist will cut the hair on the top to create a block shape. They will use scissors or clippers to achieve the desired length and shape. The hair on the top should be longer than the hair on the sides and back.

Step 4: Taper the Sides and Back

The hairstylist will use clippers to taper the hair on the sides and back. The length will gradually decrease towards the bottom. The hairstylist will use different clipper sizes to create a smooth transition from the top to the sides and back.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Once the haircut is done, the hairstylist will style the hair according to your preferences. You can choose to slick it back, leave it messy, or add some texture. The hairstylist will recommend the right products to use.

Tips for Maintaining the Two Block Haircut

The two block haircut is easy to maintain with a few simple tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep the shape of the haircut.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy.
  • Style the hair using the right products and tools.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

What is the two block haircut?

The two block haircut is a short hairstyle that features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides and back. The hair on the top is cut to create a block shape, while the sides and back are tapered.

Is the two block haircut suitable for all hair types?

The two block haircut is suitable for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, it is important to consult with a hairstylist to determine if it is the right haircut for you.

How often should I get a trim?

It is recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep the two block haircut looking its best. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy. Avoid using too much heat on the hair and use a heat protectant before using styling tools. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

Getting a two block haircut can be a great way to change up your look and stay on-trend. Follow these steps, tips, and FAQs to get the perfect two block haircut that suits your personal style and face shape.

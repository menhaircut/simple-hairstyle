How To Regain Hair Volume Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Aug 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of having flat, lifeless hair? Do you want to add volume to your hair without spending a fortune? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to regain hair volume that you can use to achieve a fuller, more voluminous look.

Description

Many people struggle with hair volume, either because they have naturally fine hair, or because their hair has become damaged over time. But don’t worry – there are ways to regain hair volume and restore your hair’s natural bounce and body.

How to Regain Hair Volume

Step 1: Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

The right shampoo and conditioner can make all the difference when it comes to hair volume. Look for products that are designed to add volume to hair, and avoid heavy, moisturizing formulas that can weigh hair down. Clarifying shampoos can also be helpful, as they remove buildup that can contribute to flat, lifeless hair.

Step 2: Use a Volumizing Mousse or Spray

Volumizing mousses and sprays can help give your hair an instant boost of volume. Apply these products to your roots before blow-drying to help lift your hair and create a fuller look. Be sure to choose products that don’t leave a sticky or crunchy residue, as this can make hair look unnatural.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow-drying your hair upside down can help create volume at the roots, which is where hair tends to be the flattest. Use a round brush to lift your hair as you blow-dry, and focus on the roots to create lift and volume.

Step 4: Try a Volumizing Haircut

If you’re looking to regain hair volume, consider getting a haircut that’s specifically designed to add volume. Layers, choppy cuts, and blunt bobs can all help create the illusion of fuller hair. Talk to your stylist about which cut would be best for your hair type and face shape.

Tips for Regaining Hair Volume

Here are a few additional tips that can help you regain hair volume:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair, as this can help prevent breakage and damage
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as these can weigh hair down and make it look flat
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair
  • Eat a balanced diet that’s rich in vitamins and minerals that are essential for healthy hair, such as biotin, vitamin D, and iron

FAQ

What causes hair to lose volume?

Hair can lose volume for a variety of reasons, including genetics, aging, damage from heat styling or chemical treatments, and hormonal changes.

Can I regain hair volume if I have naturally fine hair?

Yes, there are many products and techniques that can help add volume to fine hair. The key is to choose the right products and styling techniques that work for your hair type.

Hair Care for Regaining Hair Volume

Regaining hair volume is not just about using the right products and styling techniques – it’s also about taking care of your hair from the inside out. Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy, voluminous hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often – aim for every 2-3 days
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause damage
  • Get enough sleep, exercise regularly, and manage stress, as all of these factors can impact hair health

By following these tips and tricks, you can regain hair volume and enjoy fuller, more voluminous hair. Remember, healthy hair starts with a healthy scalp, so be sure to take care of your hair from the inside out.

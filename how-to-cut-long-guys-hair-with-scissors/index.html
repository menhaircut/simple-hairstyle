How To Cut Long Guys Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of shelling out money for a haircut every few weeks? Or maybe you're looking for a new skill to impress your friends and family? Whatever your reason may be, cutting long guys hair with scissors is a great skill to learn. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut long guys hair with scissors, along with some tips and FAQ's to help you perfect your technique. Let's get started!

How to Cut Long Guys Hair with Scissors

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some of the tools you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle (optional)

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. This will help make the hair more manageable and easier to cut. If the hair is particularly tangled, you may want to consider using a detangling spray or conditioner. Next, section off the hair using hair clips. Start at the bottom and work your way up, clipping away the hair that you won't be cutting.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Before you start cutting, decide on the length you want to achieve. Keep in mind that it's always better to cut less than you think you need, as you can always go back and cut more later. Use the comb to separate the section of hair that you'll be cutting, and hold it between your fingers.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Using the scissors, start cutting the hair. Make sure to cut in a straight line, and use the comb to guide you. Take your time and be patient. It's better to make small cuts and go back to fix any mistakes, rather than making big cuts and having to start over.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the desired length, use the scissors to blend the hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a technique called point cutting. This involves cutting the hair at an angle, rather than straight across. This will help create a more natural look.

Step 5: Repeat on the Remaining Sections

Once you've finished one section, unclip the next section and repeat the process. Continue until you've cut all of the desired sections.

Step 6: Clean Up the Hair

Once you've finished cutting, use the comb to check for any stray hairs. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs and create an even look.

Tips for Cutting Long Guys Hair with Scissors

Here are some additional tips to help you perfect your technique:

  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can make it difficult to cut the hair cleanly and evenly.
  • Take breaks. Cutting hair can be tiring, so make sure to take breaks and rest your hands.
  • Practice makes perfect. Don't be discouraged if your first few haircuts don't turn out perfectly. Keep practicing and you'll get better with time.

FAQ's

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair. However, it can be difficult to cut the back of your head, so you may want to ask a friend or family member for help.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go back and fix it. If it's a small mistake, you may be able to trim it yourself. If it's a bigger mistake, you may want to consider going to a professional stylist to fix it.

Hair Care

Now that you've learned how to cut long guys hair with scissors, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you have all the information you need, it's time to start cutting! Remember to be patient and take your time. With practice, you'll soon be able to cut long guys hair with scissors like a pro.

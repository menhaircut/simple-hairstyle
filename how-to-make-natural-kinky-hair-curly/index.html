How To Make Natural Kinky Hair Curly

Written by Bardi Jul 19, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have natural kinky hair, you might be tired of the same old look. Luckily, there are ways to make your hair curly without using harsh chemicals or heat. In this article, we will show you how to make natural kinky hair curly using natural methods.

Description

Natural kinky hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right techniques, you can achieve beautiful curls without damaging your hair. This article will provide step-by-step instructions on how to make natural kinky hair curly using natural methods.

How to

There are several ways to make natural kinky hair curly. Here are some methods you can try:

Co-washing

Co-washing involves washing your hair with a conditioner instead of shampoo. This method helps to keep your hair moisturized, which is essential for achieving curls.

Twist-outs

Twist-outs involve twisting your hair into small sections and leaving them overnight. When you undo the twists in the morning, you will have beautiful curls.

Bantu knots

Bantu knots involve twisting your hair into small knots and leaving them overnight. When you undo the knots in the morning, you will have defined curls.

Flexi-rods

Flexi-rods are bendable rods that you can use to curl your hair. Simply wrap your hair around the rod and leave it in overnight.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make natural kinky hair curly:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a conditioner. This will help to keep your hair moisturized.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to work with.

Step 4: Apply a curl defining cream

Apply a curl defining cream to each section of your hair. This will help to define your curls.

Step 5: Twist or knot your hair

Twist or knot each section of your hair, depending on the method you are using.

Step 6: Let your hair dry

Leave your hair in the twists or knots overnight to let it dry.

Step 7: Undo the twists or knots

In the morning, undo the twists or knots to reveal your beautiful curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make natural kinky hair curly:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage
  • Avoid using heat on your hair
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making natural kinky hair curly:

Can I use heat to make my hair curly?

While you can use heat to make your hair curly, it is not recommended as it can be damaging to your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair once a week to keep it clean and moisturized.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential for making it curly. Here are some tips for taking care of your natural kinky hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week
  • Avoid using heat on your hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized

With these tips and techniques, you can make your natural kinky hair curly without using harsh chemicals or heat. Try out these methods and see which one works best for you.

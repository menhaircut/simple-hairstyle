Description

The high fade men's haircut has been a popular trend for years, but in 2021, it's still going strong. This haircut is characterized by short sides that gradually fade into longer hair on top. The fade can be high or low, depending on the individual's preference. High fades are popular because they create a bold and edgy look that is perfect for men who want to make a statement.

High fade men's haircuts can be customized to suit any face shape, hair type, and personal style. The versatility of this haircut makes it a go-to choice for men who want a low-maintenance but stylish look. Whether you prefer a classic or modern look, a high fade men's haircut can be tailored to fit your needs.

How to Get a High Fade Men's Haircut

Getting a high fade men's haircut is easy, but it's important to find a skilled stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. When you visit a stylist, be sure to bring pictures of the style you're interested in, as well as any specific instructions you may have. Here are the basic steps involved in getting a high fade men's haircut:

Step 1: Choose Your Fade

The first step in getting a high fade men's haircut is to choose your fade. A high fade starts at the temples and gradually fades as it moves towards the top of the head. You can choose a low, medium, or high fade depending on your preference.

Step 2: Decide on Hair Length

Once you've chosen your fade, you'll need to decide on the length of your hair on top. This will depend on your personal style and the look you're going for. You can choose a short, medium, or long length, or anything in between.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Once you've decided on your fade and hair length, your stylist will start cutting your hair. They'll use clippers to create the fade, gradually blending the short hair on the sides into the longer hair on top. They'll also trim the hair on top to your desired length.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

After your hair is cut, your stylist will style it to your liking. This may involve using products like gel or pomade to create a specific look. They may also use a blow dryer to add volume or texture.

Step by Step Guide

If you want to try cutting your own hair, here's a step-by-step guide on how to create a high fade men's haircut:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, you'll need to gather the right tools. You'll need clippers, scissors, a comb, and hair products like gel or pomade.

Step 2: Choose Your Fade

Decide on the type of fade you want. A high fade starts at the temples and gradually fades as it moves towards the top of the head.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Using clippers, cut the hair on the sides of your head to your desired length. Start at the bottom and work your way up, gradually blending the short hair into the longer hair on top.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Using scissors, trim the hair on top to your desired length. You can choose a short, medium, or long length.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, style it to your liking using gel or pomade. You can use a comb to create a specific look, or use your fingers to create a more textured style.

Tips for Maintaining a High Fade Men's Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining a high fade men's haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Style your hair using products like gel or pomade to create a specific look.
  • Use a comb or brush to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get my high fade men's haircut trimmed?

A: It's best to get a trim every four to six weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Q: Can I style my high fade men's haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your high fade men's haircut in different ways depending on your personal style and the occasion.

Q: Can anyone get a high fade men's haircut?

A: Yes, anyone can get a high fade men's haircut. However, it's important to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care for High Fade Men's Haircut

Keeping your hair healthy is important for maintaining a high fade men's haircut. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair without causing damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Overall, a high fade men's haircut is a stylish and versatile choice that can be customized to suit any taste. Whether you choose to visit a stylist or try cutting your own hair, be sure to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained to keep your haircut looking fresh.

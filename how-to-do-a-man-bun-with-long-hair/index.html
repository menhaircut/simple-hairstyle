How To Do A Man Bun With Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of your long hair getting in your face? Or, are you looking for a stylish way to manage your long locks? The man bun might just be the answer you're looking for. In this blog post, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to do a man bun with long hair, as well as tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Do a Man Bun with Long Hair

A man bun is a popular hairstyle for men with long hair. It's a simple and easy way to keep your hair out of your face, while still looking stylish. Here are the basic steps to doing a man bun:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to pull your hair back into a bun.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Using your hands, gather all of your hair at the back of your head. You can position the bun high or low, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair

Take an elastic hair tie and wrap it around the gathered hair. Make sure to wrap it tightly, so that the bun stays in place.

Step 4: Create the Bun

Twist the hair tie around your hair until it forms a bun. You can adjust the size and shape of the bun by pulling on the hair and adjusting the hair tie.

Step 5: Secure the Bun

Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair. You can also use hairspray to keep the bun in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to do a man bun with long hair:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Let Your Hair Dry Naturally

Avoid using a hair dryer, as it can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair dry naturally or use a towel to pat it dry.

Step 3: Apply Hair Products

If you want to add some texture or hold to your hair, you can apply some hair products, such as pomade or wax. Use a small amount and work it into your hair with your fingers.

Step 4: Brush Your Hair Back

Brush your hair back with a comb or brush. This will help to remove any knots or tangles, and make it easier to gather your hair into a bun.

Step 5: Gather Your Hair

Using your hands, gather all of your hair at the back of your head. You can position the bun high or low, depending on your preference.

Step 6: Tie Your Hair

Take an elastic hair tie and wrap it around the gathered hair. Make sure to wrap it tightly, so that the bun stays in place.

Step 7: Create the Bun

Twist the hair tie around your hair until it forms a bun. You can adjust the size and shape of the bun by pulling on the hair and adjusting the hair tie.

Step 8: Secure the Bun

Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair. You can also use hairspray to keep the bun in place.

Tips for Doing a Man Bun

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing a man bun:

  • Use an elastic hair tie that's strong and durable.
  • Don't tie your hair too tightly, as it can damage your hair and cause headaches.
  • Experiment with different bun shapes and sizes to find the one that suits you best.
  • Accessorize your man bun with a headband or a scarf.
  • Don't forget to take care of your hair by washing it regularly and using hair products that are suitable for your hair type.

FAQs

Q: Can I do a man bun if my hair is not long enough?

A: If your hair is not long enough, you can try a half-bun or a top knot instead.

Q: Can I wear a man bun to a formal event?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure to keep it neat and tidy.

Q: Can a man bun cause hair loss?

A: No, a man bun does not cause hair loss. However, tying your hair too tightly or pulling it back too often can cause hair breakage.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Long hair requires extra care and attention to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips for long hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can improve the health of your hair.

Now that you know how to do a man bun with long hair, you can try out this stylish and practical hairstyle. Just remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different bun shapes and sizes to find the one that suits you best.

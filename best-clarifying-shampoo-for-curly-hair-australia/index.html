Best Clarifying Shampoo For Curly Hair Australia

Written by Christine Nov 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it requires special care and attention. One of the most important aspects of curly hair care is choosing the right shampoo. A clarifying shampoo is particularly important for curly hair, as it helps to remove product buildup and impurities that can weigh down and damage your curls. In this article, we will discuss the best clarifying shampoo for curly hair in Australia, along with tips on how to use it and care for your curly locks.

What is Clarifying Shampoo?

Clarifying shampoo is a type of shampoo that is designed to remove buildup from your hair. This buildup can be caused by products like gels, mousses, and hairsprays, as well as hard water and environmental pollutants. Clarifying shampoo works by opening up the hair cuticle and removing the buildup, leaving your hair clean and refreshed.

How to Use Clarifying Shampoo on Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to use clarifying shampoo correctly to avoid damaging your curls. Follow these steps for best results:

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Start by wetting your hair thoroughly with warm water. Make sure your hair is completely saturated before applying the shampoo.

Step 2: Apply the Shampoo

Apply a small amount of clarifying shampoo to your scalp and massage it in gently with your fingertips. Be careful not to rub too hard or tangle your curls.

Step 3: Rinse

Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water. Make sure to rinse all the shampoo out of your hair, as leftover residue can cause further buildup.

Step 4: Condition

After using clarifying shampoo, it's important to condition your hair to replenish moisture. Use a moisturizing conditioner and apply it to the mid-lengths and ends of your hair. Avoid applying conditioner to your scalp, as this can weigh down your curls.

Tips for Using Clarifying Shampoo on Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when using clarifying shampoo on curly hair:

  • Use clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and keep your curls looking their best.
  • Be gentle when applying and rinsing the shampoo to avoid tangling and damaging your curls.
  • Follow up with a moisturizing conditioner to replenish moisture and prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using clarifying shampoo on color-treated hair, as it can cause fading.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls after washing, and avoid brushing your hair when it's wet.

FAQs about Clarifying Shampoo for Curly Hair

Q: Can I use clarifying shampoo on my curly hair every day?

A: No, it's not recommended to use clarifying shampoo on your curly hair every day, as it can strip your hair of natural oils and cause dryness. Use it once a week to remove buildup and keep your curls looking their best.

Q: Will clarifying shampoo make my curly hair frizzy?

A: Clarifying shampoo can strip your hair of natural oils, which can cause dryness and frizz. To prevent frizz, be sure to follow up with a moisturizing conditioner after using clarifying shampoo.

Q: Can I use clarifying shampoo on color-treated hair?

A: It's not recommended to use clarifying shampoo on color-treated hair, as it can cause fading. If you have color-treated hair, look for a clarifying shampoo that is specifically designed for colored hair.

Curly Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls and cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz.
  • Avoid touching your curls too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your curls looking their best.

Now that you know the best clarifying shampoo for curly hair in Australia and how to use it, you can keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to follow up with a moisturizing conditioner and use it once a week to remove buildup and keep your curls looking their best.

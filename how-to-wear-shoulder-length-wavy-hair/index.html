Description

Shoulder length wavy hair is a versatile and timeless style that can be worn in a variety of ways. Whether you're looking for a casual, beachy vibe or a sleek, polished look, shoulder length wavy hair can be styled to suit any occasion. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to wear shoulder length wavy hair with ease and achieve effortless style.

How to Wear Shoulder Length Wavy Hair

The key to wearing shoulder length wavy hair is to embrace its natural texture and work with it, rather than against it. Whether you have naturally wavy hair or you've created waves using a curling iron or wand, there are a few simple steps you can follow to achieve a stylish, effortless look.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and allow it to air dry or blow dry it with a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.

2. Apply a styling product

Apply a lightweight styling product, such as a texturizing spray or mousse, to enhance the natural texture of your waves and add volume and hold.

3. Part your hair

Choose a part that flatters your face shape and complements your overall look. A center part is a classic option, while a side part can add some asymmetry and interest.

4. Define your waves

Use a curling iron or wand to define any sections of hair that need more shape or curl. Use a larger barrel for looser waves or a smaller barrel for tighter curls.

5. Finish with hairspray

Spray a light hold hairspray over your hair to set your waves and add some extra hold and shine.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling to avoid breakage and damage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find one that works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Try out different parting techniques to see what flatters your face shape and overall look.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth to your waves.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my shoulder length wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less frequently, such as every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I wear my shoulder length wavy hair up?

A: Yes! Shoulder length wavy hair can be styled in a variety of updos, such as a messy bun or a half-up half-down style.

Q: Can I wear my shoulder length wavy hair straight?

A: Of course! You can straighten your hair using a flat iron or a blow dryer with a straightening attachment. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length wavy hair looking healthy and shiny, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Take a break from styling products and let your hair air dry every once in a while to give it a break.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these simple tips, you can keep your shoulder length wavy hair looking its best and wear it with confidence and style.

Men s Hair Long On Top Curly A Guide To Rocking The Look

Long hair on top with tight curls has become a popular hairstyle choice for men in recent years. Not only is it stylish, but it's also versatile and low maintenance. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to achieve the perfect men's hair long on top curly style and provide tips on how to care for your curls.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Look

How to Style Men's Hair Long On Top Curly

When it comes to styling men's hair long on top curly, the key is to embrace your natural curls and work with them. Here are some tips:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using a brush or comb to style your curls as this can cause them to lose shape and become frizzy.
  • Consider getting layers cut into your hair to add texture and movement to your curls.
  • Experiment with different partings and styling techniques to find what works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Look

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect men's hair long on top curly style:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using products that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair by gently scrunching it with a towel to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair, scrunching it with your hands as you go.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to style it into shape.
  6. If desired, apply a small amount of styling product to your hair to add definition and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

To keep your men's hair long on top curly looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day as this can strip it of its natural oils. Instead, aim to wash it every 2-3 days.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons as these can damage your curls.
  • Consider investing in a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's hair long on top curly:

Can you wear men's hair long on top curly in a professional setting?

Yes, you can. To make your curls look more polished, consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy. You can also use a small amount of styling product to add definition and hold.

What hairstyles work well with men's hair long on top curly?

Some popular hairstyles for men's hair long on top curly include the curly fringe, the curly quiff, and the messy curly top. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

How do I prevent my curls from becoming frizzy?

To prevent frizz, avoid using a brush or comb on your curls and instead use your fingers to style them. You can also use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and reduce frizz.

Hair Care for Men's Hair Long On Top Curly

To keep your men's hair long on top curly looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools.
  • Consider investing in a silk or satin pillowcase.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the men's hair long on top curly look with ease. Remember to embrace your natural curls and work with them to achieve the best results.

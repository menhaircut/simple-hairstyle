How To Thin Out Short Hair With Thinning Shears

Written by Eveline Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thinning out short hair can be a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. One of the best ways to achieve this look is by using thinning shears. They can help you remove some of the hair without sacrificing the length. In this article, we will be discussing how to thin out short hair with thinning shears.

Description

Thinning shears are scissors that have teeth on one side and a straight blade on the other. They are designed to help remove some of the bulk from your hair while maintaining the length. Thinning shears are perfect for people with thick hair who want to reduce the volume without cutting the hair too short.

How to

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Start by sectioning your hair into small parts, so it's easier to work with. It's important to use the right type of thinning shears for your hair type. Thinning shears come in different sizes and styles, so make sure you get the right one for your hair.

Step by step guide:

  1. Start by taking a small section of hair that you want to thin out. Hold it between your fingers at the point where you want to start thinning it.
  2. Take the thinning shears and hold them at a slight angle to the hair. Make sure the teeth of the shears are facing towards the hair.
  3. Close the shears on the hair and then slide them down towards the ends. You should only cut a small amount of hair at a time. Repeat this process until you have thinned out the entire section of hair.
  4. Repeat this process on all the sections of hair that you want to thin out. Make sure you don't overdo it, as you can end up with uneven hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use the right type of thinning shears for your hair type.
  • Don't overdo it, as you can end up with uneven hair.
  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start.
  • Hold the thinning shears at a slight angle to the hair for best results.

FAQ

What type of thinning shears should I use?

The type of thinning shears you use will depend on your hair type. If you have thick hair, you'll need thicker shears. If you have fine hair, you'll need thinner shears.

Can I use thinning shears on short hair?

Yes, you can use thinning shears on short hair. They are great for adding volume and texture to short hair without sacrificing the length.

How often should I thin out my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, you should thin out your hair every three to six months.

Hair Care

After you've thinned out your hair, it's important to take care of it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. Use a heat protectant spray if you're going to use heat styling tools. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Thinning out short hair with thinning shears is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. By following these tips and using the right type of thinning shears, you can achieve the perfect look. Remember to take care of your hair after you've thinned it out, so it stays healthy and looking great.

Is Hair Naturally Thinner On Top

Written by Frank Nov 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will discuss the common question of whether hair is naturally thinner on top or not. We will also provide tips and information on how to care for thinning hair on top. If you are experiencing hair loss or thinning, keep reading to learn more.

Description

Many people believe that hair is naturally thinner on top, and in some cases, this is true. As we age, our hair follicles can shrink, which can cause the hair shaft to become thinner. Additionally, genetics and hormones can play a role in hair thinning on the top of the head.

How to Determine if Your Hair is Thinning on Top

If you are concerned about hair thinning on top, there are a few things you can do to determine if this is the case. First, look at your hair in natural light to see if you can see your scalp through your hair. Second, part your hair in different ways to see if there is a noticeable difference in thickness on the top of your head compared to the sides and back. Finally, pay attention to how much hair you are shedding when you brush or wash your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Caring for Thinning Hair on Top

Step 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

When washing your hair, use a gentle shampoo that is specifically designed for thinning hair. Look for shampoos that contain ingredients like biotin, niacin, and caffeine, which can help to stimulate hair growth.

Step 2: Avoid Over-Styling

Over-styling your hair can lead to breakage and damage, which can make thinning hair look even thinner. Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, and try to let your hair air dry whenever possible.

Step 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy and balanced diet can help to promote hair growth and prevent thinning. Make sure to eat plenty of protein, fruits and vegetables, and healthy fats like those found in avocados and nuts.

Step 4: Consider Supplements

There are many supplements on the market that claim to help with hair growth and thickness. Some of the most popular supplements include biotin, vitamin D, and iron. Talk to your doctor before taking any new supplements to make sure they are safe for you.

Tips for Caring for Thinning Hair on Top

Here are some additional tips for caring for thinning hair on top:

  • Be gentle when brushing and combing your hair, and avoid pulling or tugging on it.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails, which can pull on the hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective hair product.
  • Consider using a volumizing hair product to add fullness to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can stress cause hair to thin on top?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair to thin and fall out. This is because stress can disrupt the hair growth cycle and cause more hair to enter the shedding phase.

Q: Are there any medications that can help with hair thinning on top?

A: Yes, there are medications like minoxidil and finasteride that are FDA-approved to help with hair loss and thinning. However, these medications can have side effects and should only be used under the guidance of a doctor.

Hair Care for Thinning Hair on Top

If you are experiencing hair thinning or loss on top, it is important to take steps to care for your hair and promote healthy growth. By using gentle hair products, eating a healthy diet, and avoiding over-styling, you can help to keep your hair looking its best. If you are concerned about hair loss or thinning, talk to your doctor to determine the underlying cause and the best course of treatment.

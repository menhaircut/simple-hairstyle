How To Cut Pixie Haircut With Clippers

Written by Petter Oct 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, edgy haircut, then a pixie cut might be the perfect choice for you. Pixie cuts are short, choppy haircuts that can be styled in a variety of ways. However, getting a pixie cut at a salon can be costly. So, why not try cutting your own hair with clippers? In this article, we'll explain how to cut a pixie haircut with clippers in easy-to-follow steps.

Description

A pixie haircut is a short, choppy haircut that typically features long bangs and layers. The haircut is named after the mischievous pixie fairy, and it's become popular among women of all ages. Pixie cuts can be styled in a variety of ways, including spiky, messy, or sleek. They are also low-maintenance and easy to care for, making them a great choice for busy women.

How to Cut Pixie Haircut with Clippers

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary supplies. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, a comb, hair clips, and a pair of scissors. You may also want to invest in a handheld mirror so you can see the back of your head.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Use hair clips to keep the sections separated.

Step 4: Start with the Sides

Begin cutting the sides of your hair. Use the clippers to trim the hair to your desired length. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to guide the clippers. Make sure to keep the clippers level with the comb to ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Move to the Back

Next, move to the back of your head. Use the same technique as before, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the cut is even.

Step 6: Finish with the Top

Finally, move to the top of your head. Use the scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. You can also use the clippers to create texture and layers. Use the comb to guide the clippers and create a choppy, edgy look.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard on the clippers and work your way down to a shorter guard to create different lengths.
  • Be patient and take your time. It's better to cut a little at a time than to cut too much and ruin the haircut.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. If you're having trouble cutting the back of your head, ask a friend or family member to assist you.

FAQ

Can I cut a pixie haircut with scissors?

Yes, you can cut a pixie haircut with scissors. However, using clippers can make the process easier and more precise.

How often should I trim my pixie haircut?

It's recommended to trim your pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care

Once you've cut your pixie haircut with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

With these easy steps, you can cut a pixie haircut with clippers and achieve an edgy, low-maintenance look. Remember to take your time and have fun with your new haircut!

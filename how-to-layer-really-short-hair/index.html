How To Layer Really Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Layering really short hair can add volume, texture, and dimension to your locks. If you're looking to add some oomph to your short hair, then layering is the way to go. In this article, we'll show you how to layer really short hair in a relaxed and easy-to-follow manner. Read on for our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Layer Really Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Layering Really Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Layered Short Hair

How to Layer Really Short Hair

Before we get started with the layering process, it's important to decide what kind of layers you want. Do you want short choppy layers or longer ones? Do you want a layered bob or a layered pixie cut? Once you've decided on the kind of layers you want, you can proceed with the following steps:

1. Wash your hair

The first step is to wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup and make your hair easier to work with.

2. Part your hair

Next, part your hair the way you normally do. This will help you see where the layers will fall and ensure that they are even on both sides.

3. Section your hair

Using clips or elastic bands, section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

4. Cut the first layer

Starting with the bottom layer, take a small section of hair and cut it to the desired length. Remember, you can always cut more hair later, so start with a small section.

5. Move on to the next layer

Once you've cut the first layer, move on to the next one. Take a small section of hair and cut it to the same length as the first layer. Continue this process until you've cut all the layers.

6. Check for evenness

Once you've cut all the layers, check for evenness. Use a mirror to ensure that the layers are even on both sides.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to layer really short hair:

  1. Wash your hair
  2. Part your hair
  3. Section your hair
  4. Cut the first layer
  5. Move on to the next layer
  6. Check for evenness

Tips for Layering Really Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when layering really short hair:

  • Start with a small section of hair and cut a little at a time.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Cut the layers at an angle for a more natural look.
  • Don't forget to check for evenness.
  • If you're unsure, it's best to go to a professional stylist.

FAQs

How often should I get my short hair layered?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the layers lose their shape. As a general rule, it's best to get your hair layered every 6-8 weeks.

Can I layer really short curly hair?

Yes, you can layer really short curly hair. However, it's important to go to a stylist who has experience working with curly hair to ensure that the layers are cut properly.

Can I layer really short hair at home?

Yes, you can layer really short hair at home. However, it's important to be careful and start with a small section of hair. If you're unsure, it's best to go to a professional stylist.

Hair Care for Layered Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep your layered short hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the layers.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week.

Layering really short hair can be a fun and easy way to add some volume and texture to your locks. With our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice, you'll be able to layer your short hair like a pro. Remember, if you're unsure, it's always best to go to a professional stylist.

What Is The Best Haircut For Square Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square face, you may have wondered what the best haircut is to flatter your features. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to decide what hairstyle is the best fit for your face shape. In this guide, we'll take a closer look at what a square face shape is, what hairstyles work best for this face shape, and some tips on how to care for your hair.

What is a Square Face Shape?

A square face shape is characterized by a strong, angular jawline and a square forehead. The width of the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all relatively similar in size.

How to Determine if You Have a Square Face Shape

To determine if you have a square face shape, measure your face from the hairline to the bottom of your chin. Then measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all of these measurements are relatively similar in size, you likely have a square face shape.

What is the Best Haircut for Square Face?

When it comes to finding the best haircut for square face, the goal is to soften the angles of the face and create balance. Here are some of the best haircuts for square face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers work wonders for square face shapes. They help to soften the angles of the face and add movement to the hair.

2. Soft Waves

Soft waves are another great option for square face shapes. They add volume to the hair and help to create a more rounded, balanced look.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add some softness to a square face. They help to break up the angular lines of the face and draw attention to the eyes.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can work well for square face shapes, especially if the hair is styled with volume at the crown. This adds height to the face and helps to balance out the strong jawline.

5. Bob Cut

A bob cut that hits just above the shoulders can also work well for square face shapes. The length helps to elongate the face, while the soft layers add movement and balance.

Tips for Caring for Square Face Hair

Now that you know what the best haircuts for square face are, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Deep conditioning treatments can help to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, which is important for all hair types, but especially for square face shapes.

2. Avoid Over-Processing Your Hair

Over-processing your hair can lead to damage and breakage, which can be especially noticeable with square face shapes. Be sure to avoid excessive heat styling and chemical treatments.

3. Protect Your Hair from Sun Damage

UV rays can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Be sure to protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protecting hair product.

FAQ

Q: Can I Wear My Hair in a Middle Part with a Square Face Shape?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a middle part with a square face shape. However, it's important to make sure that the hairstyle you choose balances out the strong angles of your face.

Q: Can I Wear Short Hair with a Square Face Shape?

A: Yes, you can wear short hair with a square face shape. In fact, a pixie cut can work well for this face shape. Just be sure to choose a style that adds volume and height to the face.

Q: What Color Should I Dye My Hair if I Have a Square Face Shape?

A: The color you choose to dye your hair will depend on your personal preferences and skin tone. However, it's generally a good idea to avoid overly harsh or contrasting colors, which can emphasize the angles of the face.

Conclusion

When it comes to finding the best haircut for square face, there are plenty of options to choose from. Long layers, soft waves, side-swept bangs, pixie cuts, and bob cuts can all work well for this face shape. Just be sure to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. With these tips, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle to flatter your square face shape.

The Right Haircut For Your Face Shape Male A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Oct 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle is essential to look your best. However, the perfect haircut is not just about following the latest trend. It should complement your face shape, hair type, and personality. In this article, we will discuss the right haircut for your face shape male, how to choose it, and how to maintain it.

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Face Shape Male

  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Description

Every face shape has its unique characteristics that determine the right haircut. Men's face shapes can be broadly categorized into four types:

  • Oval
  • Square
  • Round
  • Heart

The right haircut for your face shape male can enhance your features, make your face look more balanced, and create a more attractive appearance.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Face Shape Male

Here are some tips to help you choose the right haircut for your face shape male:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky because almost any hairstyle will suit you. You can try a short buzz cut or a longer hairstyle with layers. Avoid hairstyles that cover your forehead or eyes.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you should opt for a hairstyle that softens your strong jawline. A side parting with a medium length hairstyle or a textured crop will suit you best. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you should aim for a hairstyle that adds length to your face. A side parting with a medium length hairstyle or a messy top with short sides will suit you best. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you should aim for a hairstyle that balances your forehead and jawline. A side parting with a medium length hairstyle or a messy top with short sides will suit you best. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.

Step-by-Step Guide to Get the Right Haircut for Your Face Shape Male

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you get the right haircut for your face shape male:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. Based on these measurements, determine your face shape.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles

Once you know your face shape, research hairstyles that suit your face shape. Look for inspiration from magazines, social media, or your barber's portfolio.

Step 3: Consult with Your Barber

Consult with your barber or hairstylist to discuss the hairstyle that you want. Show them the pictures of the hairstyles that you like and ask for their advice on whether it will suit your face shape and hair type.

Step 4: Get the Haircut

Once you have decided on the hairstyle, get the haircut. Make sure to follow your barber's instructions on how to style and maintain your haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Style your hair using the right products, such as gel, wax, or pomade.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently, as it can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

What is the most popular men's hairstyle?

The most popular men's hairstyle is the undercut, which features short sides and a longer top.

How often should I get a haircut?

You should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut and promote healthy hair growth.

What is the best hairstyle for men with thinning hair?

The best hairstyle for men with thinning hair is a short textured crop or a buzz cut, as it creates an illusion of thicker hair.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Here are some hair care tips for men:

  • Choose a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair with hot water, as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid pulling or breaking it.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as it can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know the right haircut for your face shape male, it's time to book an appointment with your barber and get a new hairstyle that suits you best. Remember to maintain your haircut and follow our hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and stylish.

Face With Short Hair Tips And Tricks For A Stylish Look

Written by Aprilia Jul 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you thinking of getting a haircut and trying something new? Short hair can be a great option if you want a low-maintenance style that is both chic and edgy. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks for styling a face with short hair.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Face Shape

Before getting a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape. Different hairstyles suit different face shapes, and choosing the right one can make all the difference. Here are some tips to help you choose the right short haircut for your face shape:

Round Face

If you have a round face, you should go for a short haircut that adds height and volume to the crown. A layered pixie cut or a shaggy bob is a great option that will elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

Square Face

If you have a square face, you should choose a short haircut that softens your angular features. A layered bob or a side-swept pixie cut can help to soften your jawline and make your face look more feminine.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you should opt for a short haircut that balances out your features. A layered bob or a tousled pixie cut can help to add volume to the sides of your face and create a more balanced look.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you are lucky because almost any short haircut will suit you. You can go for a sleek bob or a messy pixie cut, and both will look great on you.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, you can create a stylish look in no time. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your short hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel-dry it.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to protect it from heat damage and to add moisture. This will also make your hair more manageable and easier to style.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush and blow-dry your hair in sections, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron

Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or create soft waves. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 5: Add Some Texture

Add some texture to your hair by using a texturizing spray or a pomade. This will give your hair a messy, tousled look that is perfect for short hair.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair is low-maintenance, but it still requires some care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

Tip 1: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short hair grows quickly, so it's important to trim it regularly to keep your style looking fresh. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Tip 2: Invest in Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products that are designed for short hair. Look for products that will add volume, texture, and hold to your hair.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair. Be sure to use a low heat setting and do not over-style your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is short hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: No, different hairstyles suit different face shapes. It's important to choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I style short hair without heat?

A: Yes, you can style short hair without heat by using texturizing sprays, pomades, and other styling products.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair is easy to care for, but it still requires some attention to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is formulated for your hair type. Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Condition Your Hair

Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy. Use a conditioner that is designed for short hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish short haircut that flatters your face shape and suits your personal style. Remember to take care of your hair and maintain it regularly to keep it looking its best.

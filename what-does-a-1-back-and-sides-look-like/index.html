What Does A 1 Back And Sides Look Like

Written by Daniel Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post about what a 1 back and sides haircut looks like. In this article, we will discuss this popular hairstyle, its features, and how it can be achieved. If you are interested in getting a 1 back and sides haircut, keep reading.

Description

A 1 back and sides refers to a haircut where the hair on the sides and back of the head is cut very short. The number 1 refers to the guard size of the clipper used to achieve this haircut. This means that the hair will be cut to about 1/8th of an inch or 3mm in length. The hair on the top of the head can be left longer or trimmed to the same length as the sides and back.

How to Achieve a 1 Back and Sides

If you want to achieve a 1 back and sides haircut, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to make sure it is clean and easy to work with.

Step 2: Trim the Sides and Back

Use clippers with a number 1 guard to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using an upward motion to create a fade.

Step 3: Trim the Top

Trim the hair on the top of the head to the desired length. You can use scissors or clippers with a longer guard for this step.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Use a blending clipper or scissors to blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top.

Step 5: Style the Hair

You can style your hair however you like once the cut is complete. Use a hair product like gel, wax, or pomade to create your desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a 1 back and sides haircut:

  • Bring a picture of the style you want to show your barber or stylist.
  • Make sure you go to a professional for the best results.
  • Ask your barber or stylist for advice on how to maintain your haircut.
  • Use a quality hair product to keep your hair looking its best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the 1 back and sides haircut:

How often should I get a 1 back and sides haircut?

You should get a 1 back and sides haircut every 2-4 weeks to maintain the length and shape of the cut.

Can I style my hair differently with a 1 back and sides?

Yes, you can style your hair differently with a 1 back and sides. You can use hair products to create different looks like slicked back, messy, or textured hairstyles.

Is a 1 back and sides suitable for all hair types?

A 1 back and sides can work for most hair types, but it may not be suitable for those with very curly or thick hair. It is best to consult with your barber or stylist to determine if this haircut is right for you.

Hair Care

To keep your 1 back and sides looking its best, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair from styling tools like blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners.
  • Use a hair product that works best for your hair type.
  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to maintain your haircut.

Now that you know what a 1 back and sides haircut looks like, you can decide if it is the right style for you. Remember to consult with your barber or stylist for advice on achieving and maintaining this haircut.

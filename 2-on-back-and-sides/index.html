2 On Back And Sides The Perfect Haircut For Men

Written by Bardi Jul 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a fresh new haircut that’s both trendy and timeless? Look no further than the “2 on back and sides” haircut! This versatile style is perfect for men of all ages and hair types, and it’s easy to maintain and style. In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know about the 2 on back and sides, from how to get the cut to how to care for your hair afterwards. Let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the 2 on Back and Sides
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

The 2 on back and sides is a classic haircut that involves cutting the hair to a length of 2 inches on the back and sides of the head, while leaving the hair on top longer. This creates a clean, polished look that’s both professional and stylish. The style is commonly paired with a side part or slicked back for a more formal look, or with a messy, textured top for a more casual look.

How to Get the 2 on Back and Sides

The 2 on back and sides can be achieved at any hair salon or barbershop. Simply ask your stylist or barber for a “2 on back and sides” haircut, and they’ll know exactly what you’re referring to. It’s important to communicate with your stylist about how you want the top of your hair to be cut, as this will vary depending on your personal style and preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting the 2 on back and sides:

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry it.
  2. Sit in the barber chair and explain to your stylist that you want a “2 on back and sides” haircut.
  3. Your stylist will begin by using clippers to cut your hair to a length of 2 inches on the back and sides of your head.
  4. Next, your stylist will use scissors to cut the top of your hair to your desired length and style.
  5. If you want a side part, your stylist will create a part and use a comb to style your hair accordingly.
  6. If you want a slicked back style, your stylist will use a comb and styling product to create the desired look.
  7. If you want a messy, textured top, your stylist will use texturizing shears or a razor to create the desired look.
  8. Your stylist will finish the cut by trimming any stray hairs and cleaning up the neckline and sideburns.
  9. Pay for your haircut and leave the salon feeling fresh and stylish!

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling your 2 on back and sides:

  • For a more formal look, use a comb and styling product to create a side part or slicked back style.
  • For a more casual look, use your fingers to create a messy, textured top.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the right one for your hair type and desired look.
  • Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about the 2 on back and sides:

How long does the haircut take?

The haircut typically takes about 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the complexity of the styling.

Can the style be adapted for curly or thick hair?

Yes! The 2 on back and sides can be adapted for any hair type, including curly or thick hair. Your stylist will adjust the cutting technique as needed to achieve the desired look.

Is the style easy to maintain?

Yes! The 2 on back and sides is a low-maintenance style that’s easy to maintain with regular trims and a good quality shampoo and conditioner.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after getting the 2 on back and sides:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

That’s everything you need to know about the 2 on back and sides! Whether you’re looking for a fresh new haircut or simply want to switch up your style, the 2 on back and sides is a perfect choice. With its timeless look and easy maintenance, this haircut is sure to become a staple in your grooming routine.

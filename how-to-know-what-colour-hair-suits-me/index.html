How To Know What Colour Hair Suits Me A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of your current hair colour and looking for a change? Choosing a new hair colour can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, it can be a fun and exciting experience. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to know what colour hair suits you.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Choosing the right hair colour can enhance your natural beauty and boost your confidence. However, it can be challenging to determine what colour suits you best. The right hair colour should complement your skin tone, eye colour, and personality.

How to

There are several factors to consider when choosing the right hair colour. Here are some tips on how to know what colour hair suits you.

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is a significant factor in determining the right hair colour for you. People with warm skin tones should opt for warm hair colours such as golden blonde, copper, and auburn. On the other hand, people with cool skin tones should go for cool hair colours such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, and burgundy.

Look at Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also help determine the right hair colour for you. For instance, people with blue or green eyes may find that blonde or light brown hair colours suit them best. Meanwhile, people with brown eyes may look great with dark hair colours such as black or dark brown.

Consider Your Personality

Your hair colour should also reflect your personality. If you have a bold and adventurous personality, you may want to try out vibrant hair colours such as pink or purple. However, if you prefer a more subtle look, natural hair colours such as brown or blonde may suit you better.

Step by Step

Here is a step by step guide on how to know what colour hair suits you.

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you likely have a neutral undertone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

Look at your natural eye colour to determine what hair colours will complement it.

Step 3: Look at Your Current Hair Colour

If you are unsure about what hair colour to choose, consider starting with a shade that is close to your current hair colour. This will make the transition more subtle and less dramatic.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

If you are still unsure about what hair colour suits you, consider consulting with a professional hair stylist. They can help you determine the right hair colour based on your skin tone, eye colour, and personality.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair colour.

  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hair colour. If you have a busy schedule, choose a low maintenance hair colour that requires minimal touch-ups.
  • If you have sensitive skin, opt for a hair colour that is free of harsh chemicals such as ammonia and peroxide.
  • Experiment with temporary hair colours before committing to a permanent change.

FAQ

What hair colour suits fair skin?

People with fair skin should opt for cool hair colours such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, and burgundy. These colours complement fair skin tones and make them stand out.

What hair colour suits dark skin?

People with dark skin should go for warm hair colours such as golden blonde, copper, and auburn. These colours complement dark skin tones and make them look radiant.

How often should I touch up my hair colour?

The frequency of touch-ups depends on the type of hair colour and how fast your hair grows. Generally, permanent hair colours should be touched up every 4-6 weeks, while semi-permanent hair colours can last up to 8 weeks.

Hair Care

After choosing the right hair colour, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its vibrancy and shine. Here are some hair care tips.

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to protect your hair colour.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to know what colour hair suits you, go ahead and experiment with different colours until you find the perfect shade that complements your unique features and personality.

How To Wear A Long Dress If You Are Short

Written by Frank Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long dresses are versatile and elegant pieces that can be worn on different occasions. However, if you are short, wearing a long dress can be challenging. You may feel overwhelmed and lost in the fabric, or the dress may look too long and awkward. But don't worry, by following these tips and tricks, you can wear a long dress confidently and elegantly.

Description

Wearing a long dress can be a bit tricky if you are short. You may feel like you are drowning in the fabric or like the dress is wearing you. However, with the right styling, you can make a long dress work for your body type. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to wear a long dress if you are short.

How to Wear a Long Dress if You are Short

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in wearing a long dress if you are short is to choose the right length. Look for dresses that are above the ankle or just below the knee. This length will show off your legs and make you look taller. Avoid dresses that are full length, as they will make you look even shorter.

Step 2: Choose the Right Fabric

The fabric of the dress can also make a difference in how it looks on you. Choose a lightweight fabric that drapes well and doesn't add bulk to your frame. Avoid heavy fabrics like velvet or brocade, as they can make you look shorter and wider.

Step 3: Choose the Right Style

When it comes to the style of the dress, choose something that is fitted at the waist and flares out at the bottom. This style will create an illusion of height and make you look taller. Avoid dresses that are too loose or too tight, as they can make you look shorter.

Step 4: Choose the Right Shoes

The shoes you wear with a long dress can also make a difference. Choose shoes with a heel, as they will add height and make you look taller. Avoid flats or shoes with ankle straps, as they can cut off your leg line and make you look shorter.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you wear a long dress if you are short:

  • Wear a belt to define your waist and create an illusion of height.
  • Avoid dresses with horizontal stripes, as they can make you look wider and shorter.
  • Choose dresses with vertical stripes or patterns, as they can make you look taller.
  • Accessorize with long necklaces or earrings to draw the eye up and create an illusion of height.
  • Avoid bulky accessories, as they can add weight and make you look shorter.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to wear a long dress if you are short:

Q: Can short people wear long dresses?

A: Yes, short people can wear long dresses. The key is to choose the right length, fabric, style, and shoes.

Q: What length should a long dress be for short people?

A: A long dress should be above the ankle or just below the knee for short people. This length will show off your legs and make you look taller.

Q: What shoes should short people wear with a long dress?

A: Short people should wear shoes with a heel with a long dress. This will add height and make you look taller.

Hair Care

When wearing a long dress, your hair can also play a role in how the outfit looks. Here are some tips for hair care when wearing a long dress:

  • Wear your hair up to elongate your neck and create an illusion of height.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your head, as they can make you look shorter.
  • Accessorize with headbands or hair clips to draw the eye up and create an illusion of height.

By following these tips and tricks, you can wear a long dress confidently and elegantly, even if you are short. Remember to choose the right length, fabric, style, and shoes, and accessorize appropriately. With the right styling, you can make a long dress work for your body type and look stunning on any occasion.

Ways To Wear Medium Length Hair Up

Written by Robby Sep 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for ways to style your medium length hair up? Look no further! In this article, we'll be sharing some easy and stylish ways to wear your hair up. Whether you're going to a formal event or just want to switch up your everyday look, these hairstyles are perfect for any occasion.

Description

Medium length hair can often be tricky to style, especially when you want to wear it up. However, there are plenty of options that will allow you to create a chic and effortless look. From messy buns to sleek ponytails, there are a variety of styles that can be achieved with medium length hair.

How to

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to make sure it's clean and dry. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it first to make the styling process easier. Once your hair is prepped, you can begin styling it up.

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic and effortless hairstyle that looks great on medium length hair. To achieve this look, simply gather your hair into a loose ponytail and twist it around itself until it forms a bun. Secure it in place with bobby pins and pull out a few strands to create a messy, undone look.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for those who want to wear their hair up but still show off their length. To create this look, simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip. Leave the bottom half of your hair down and styled as usual.

3. Sleek Ponytail

The sleek ponytail is a chic and polished look that works well on medium length hair. To achieve this look, brush your hair back into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. Use a flat iron to smooth down any flyaways or frizz and finish off the look with a spritz of hairspray.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair

2. Choose your desired hairstyle

3. Gather your hair into the appropriate style (bun, ponytail, half-up, etc.)

4. Secure your hair in place with bobby pins or a hair tie

5. Pull out a few strands to create a messy, undone look (if desired)

6. Finish off with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips

- Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair before styling it up

- Play around with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some extra flair to your hairstyle

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you

FAQ

Q: Can I wear these hairstyles to a formal event?

Yes! These hairstyles are versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. For a formal event, you may want to opt for a sleeker hairstyle, such as a low bun or a polished ponytail.

Q: Will these hairstyles work on all hair types?

Yes! These hairstyles can be adapted to work with different hair types, whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair. You may need to use different styling products or tools to achieve your desired look, but the basic styles can be achieved on any hair type.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure it looks and feels its best. Make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily style your medium length hair up for any occasion. Whether you prefer a messy bun or a sleek ponytail, these hairstyles are sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful.

Ways To Keep Your Hair Back Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Oct 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to keep your hair out of your face? Whether you're working out, running errands, or just trying to tame your tresses, keeping your hair back can be a challenge. Fortunately, there are plenty of ways to keep your hair back that are both stylish and practical. From headbands to bobby pins, here are some tips and tricks to help you keep your hair out of your face.

How to Keep Your Hair Back: Headbands

Headbands are a classic way to keep your hair back, and they come in all shapes and sizes. Whether you prefer a thick, wide headband or a thin, dainty one, there's a headband out there for you. One of the best things about headbands is that they can add a pop of color or pattern to your outfit, so they're both functional and fashionable. Headbands work best on hair that's shoulder-length or longer.

Step-by-Step: How to Wear a Headband

If you're new to wearing headbands, it can be a little tricky to figure out how to wear them. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose a headband that's the right size for your head. If it's too tight, it'll give you a headache; if it's too loose, it'll slip off.
  2. Put the headband on your head, positioning it where you want it to sit.
  3. Slide the headband back slightly so that it's sitting at the crown of your head.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and tuck it behind the headband.
  5. Repeat on the other side, tucking a small section of hair behind the headband.
  6. Adjust the headband as needed to make sure it's secure and comfortable.

How to Keep Your Hair Back: Bobby Pins

If headbands aren't your thing, bobby pins are another great option. Bobby pins are small and discreet, so they won't distract from your outfit. They're also versatile - you can use them to create all sorts of hairstyles, from messy buns to sleek updos. Bobby pins work best on hair that's chin-length or longer.

Step-by-Step: How to Use Bobby Pins

Using bobby pins is pretty straightforward, but there are a few tips and tricks that can help you get the most out of them. Here's how to use bobby pins:

  1. Gather the section of hair you want to pin back.
  2. Hold the section of hair in place with one hand.
  3. Use your other hand to slide the bobby pin into the hair, pushing it towards your scalp.
  4. Repeat with as many bobby pins as needed to secure the hair.
  5. Once the hair is secure, give it a gentle tug to make sure it's not going anywhere.

Tips for Keeping Your Hair Back

Here are a few more tips to help you keep your hair back:

  • Use hair ties that are gentle on your hair. Avoid using rubber bands, as they can cause breakage.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to see what works best for you. You might find that a messy bun is more comfortable than a sleek ponytail.
  • Invest in some dry shampoo. When your hair is clean, it's more likely to slip out of a hair tie or headband. Using dry shampoo can help give your hair some texture and grip.
  • Don't be afraid to accessorize. From hair clips to scrunchies, there are plenty of cute hair accessories that can help keep your hair back.

FAQ: Keeping Your Hair Back

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping your hair back:

Q: How can I keep my hair back when I'm working out?

A: Sweat can make it tough to keep your hair back, but there are a few things you can do. Use a headband to keep flyaways out of your face, and try securing your ponytail or bun with a hair tie and some bobby pins.

Q: What's the best way to keep short hair back?

A: If your hair is chin-length or shorter, bobby pins are your best bet. Use them to pin back any pieces that are falling in your face.

Q: Can I use hairspray to keep my hair back?

A: While hairspray can help keep your hair in place, it can also make it stiff and crunchy. If you're going to use hairspray, use it sparingly and choose a flexible hold formula.

Hair Care for Keeping Your Hair Back

Keeping your hair back can be tough on your locks, so it's important to take good care of them. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles for extended periods of time.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to keep your hair back in style. Whether you prefer headbands or bobby pins, there's a method that's right for you. And don't forget to take good care of your hair - healthy locks are the foundation for any great hairstyle.

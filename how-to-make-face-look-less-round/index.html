How To Make Your Face Look Less Round Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jul 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may be looking for ways to make it appear slimmer and more defined. Fortunately, there are several tips and tricks you can use to achieve this look. In this article, we’ll share some of our favorite methods for making your face look less round.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Face Look Less Round
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Your Face Look Less Round
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for a Slimmer Face

How to Make Your Face Look Less Round

There are several ways to make your face look less round:

1. Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades to create the illusion of shadows on your face. By applying contouring powder or cream to your cheekbones, jawline, and temples, you can create a more defined and sculpted look.

2. Highlighting

In addition to contouring, highlighting is another makeup technique that can help make your face look less round. By applying a light-colored highlighter to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and nose, you can create the illusion of height and dimension.

3. Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help strengthen your facial muscles and create a more defined appearance. Some exercises to try include cheek lifts, jawline exercises, and neck stretches.

4. Face Massage

A face massage can help improve circulation and reduce puffiness, which can make your face look less round. Use your fingers to gently massage your face in circular motions, focusing on your cheekbones, jawline, and temples.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to making your face look less round:

Step 1: Apply Contouring Products

Begin by applying a contouring powder or cream to the areas of your face where you want to create shadows. Use a brush or sponge to blend the product outwards, creating a natural-looking effect.

Step 2: Apply Highlighter

Next, apply a light-colored highlighter to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and nose. Use a brush or sponge to blend the product outwards, creating a natural-looking effect.

Step 3: Try Facial Exercises

Try incorporating facial exercises into your daily routine to help strengthen your facial muscles and create a more defined appearance. Cheek lifts, jawline exercises, and neck stretches are all great options.

Step 4: Massage Your Face

Finish off by massaging your face in circular motions with your fingers. Focus on your cheekbones, jawline, and temples to improve circulation and reduce puffiness.

Tips for Making Your Face Look Less Round

Here are some additional tips for making your face look less round:

1. Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Consider a long bob, a side-swept fringe, or a layered cut.

2. Wear Earrings

Wearing earrings can help draw attention to your neck and shoulders, creating the illusion of a longer, slimmer face.

3. Wear V-Neck Tops

Wearing V-neck tops can help elongate your neck and create a more defined collarbone, making your face appear slimmer.

FAQ

Q: Can losing weight help make my face look less round?

A: Yes, losing weight can help reduce the appearance of a round face. However, it’s important to remember that genetics also play a role in the shape of your face.

Q: Are there any foods that can help make my face look less round?

A: While there are no specific foods that can help make your face look less round, maintaining a healthy diet and drinking plenty of water can help reduce puffiness and improve skin health.

Hair Care Tips for a Slimmer Face

Here are some hair care tips to help make your face look slimmer:

1. Avoid Heavy Bangs

Heavy bangs can make your face appear shorter and rounder. Consider a side-swept fringe instead.

2. Try a Center Part

A center part can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer appearance.

3. Use Hair Products Wisely

Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make your face appear rounder. Use lightweight products instead.

By using these tips and tricks, you can help make your face look less round and achieve a more defined appearance. Whether you’re using makeup, facial exercises, or hair care techniques, there are plenty of ways to create the look you want.

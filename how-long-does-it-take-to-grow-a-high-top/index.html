How Long Does It Take To Grow A High Top

Are you wondering how long it takes to grow a high top? Well, the answer depends on various factors. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about growing a high top, including some tips, hair care routine, and frequently asked questions.

How to Grow a High Top

A high top is a hairstyle that requires hair to be grown on the top of the head while the sides are kept short. To grow a high top, you need to have patience and follow some basic hair care routine. Here are some tips that can help you grow a high top:

1. Avoid Haircuts

The first step to growing a high top is to avoid getting haircuts. You need to let your hair grow out for a few months before you can start shaping it into a high top. It is recommended that you wait at least three months before getting a haircut.

2. Use Hair Products

Using hair products can help you achieve the desired look for your high top. You can use hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place and give it a shiny look. Make sure to apply the product evenly and comb your hair to distribute it properly.

3. Get Regular Trims

Although you need to avoid haircuts, you still need to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy. Trimming the ends of your hair can prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down your hair growth.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to grow a high top:

1. Stop Getting Haircuts

As mentioned earlier, you need to stop getting haircuts and let your hair grow out for a few months.

2. Use Hair Products

Start using hair products to help style your hair as it grows out. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly.

3. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

4. Shape Your High Top

Once your hair has grown out, you can start shaping your high top. You can use clippers or scissors to trim the sides and back of your hair while leaving the top long.

Tips for Growing a High Top

Here are some additional tips that can help you grow a high top:

1. Be Patient

Growing a high top takes time and patience. Don't get discouraged if your hair is taking longer to grow than you expected.

2. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet can help promote hair growth. Make sure to include protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools such as curling irons or straighteners as they can damage your hair and slow down hair growth.

FAQs

Q. How long does it take to grow a high top?

A. It can take anywhere from six months to a year to grow a high top, depending on how fast your hair grows and how long you want your high top to be.

Q. Can I grow a high top if I have thin hair?

A. Yes, you can still grow a high top if you have thin hair. You can use hair products to add volume and thickness to your hair.

Q. Will my hair grow faster if I shave my head?

A. No, shaving your head does not make your hair grow faster. It only makes your hair appear thicker when it grows back.

Hair Care Routine for High Top

Here is a hair care routine that you can follow to keep your high top healthy:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair at least once a week to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Moisturize Your Hair

Use a hair moisturizer or leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent dryness.

3. Protect Your Hair

Avoid exposing your hair to harsh weather conditions such as extreme heat or cold. Wear a hat or use a hair scarf to protect your hair.

In conclusion, growing a high top requires patience and basic hair care routine. Follow these tips and you will have a stylish high top in no time!

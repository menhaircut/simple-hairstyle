How To Get Messy Pixie Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 30, 2023 · 5 min read

If you’re looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that’s both edgy and feminine, a messy pixie cut might be just what you need. This short haircut is perfect for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair while keeping things easy and breezy. In this article, we’ll show you how to get messy pixie hair in just a few simple steps.

Step 1: Choose the Right Pixie Cut

The first step in getting messy pixie hair is choosing the right cut. Pixie cuts come in many different styles, so it’s important to find one that complements your face shape and hair texture. Some popular pixie cuts include:

  • The classic pixie cut
  • The undercut pixie cut
  • The textured pixie cut
  • The messy pixie cut

For a messy pixie cut, you’ll want to opt for a style that has longer layers on top and shorter layers on the sides and back. This will allow you to create volume and texture in your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before styling your hair, it’s important to start with clean and conditioned hair. This will help your hair hold its shape and style better. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

Step 3: Apply a Texturizing Product

To create messy pixie hair, you’ll want to use a texturizing product. This will help add volume and texture to your hair. Apply a small amount of texturizing spray, mousse, or cream to your hair, focusing on the roots and ends.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using your fingers to lift and tousle your hair. This will help create volume and texture in your hair. Be sure to dry your hair completely, as damp hair can weigh down your style.

Step 5: Add Volume and Texture

Once your hair is dry, use a comb or your fingers to create volume and texture in your hair. You can use a comb to lift your hair at the roots and create volume, or use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, textured look.

Step 6: Use a Styling Product

To hold your messy pixie hair in place, use a styling product like hair wax, pomade, or gel. Apply a small amount of product to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends and the areas where you want more texture and definition.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish off your messy pixie hair with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your style in place and prevent flyaways.

Tips for Maintaining Messy Pixie Hair

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it harder to style.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to refresh and add volume to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your messy pixie hair looking its best.

FAQs

How often should I wash my messy pixie hair?

You should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

What styling products are best for messy pixie hair?

Texturizing sprays, mousses, and creams are great for adding volume and texture to your hair, while hair wax, pomade, and gel can help hold your style in place.

Can I still style my messy pixie hair if it’s growing out?

Absolutely! As your hair grows out, you may need to adjust your styling techniques to accommodate the longer length. Talk to your stylist about the best ways to style your hair as it grows out.

Hair Care for Messy Pixie Hair

Maintaining healthy, nourished hair is key to achieving and maintaining a messy pixie cut. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.
  • Apply a weekly hair mask to give your hair an extra boost of hydration.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it harder to style.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve a messy pixie cut that’s both stylish and low-maintenance. Whether you’re looking to add volume and texture to your hair or you simply want a new, edgy look, a messy pixie cut is a great option for any hair type or texture.

Easy Updo Hairstyles For Long Thin Hair

Written by Eveline Oct 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing your long thin hair down or in a simple ponytail? It’s time to try some easy updos that will add some style and sophistication to your look. In this article, we’ll show you some simple yet stunning hairstyles that you can try at home.

Description

Long thin hair can be a challenge to style, as it lacks volume and texture. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create beautiful updos that will make your hair look fuller and more voluminous. These hairstyles are perfect for special occasions or even for everyday wear.

How to

Before you start styling your hair, make sure that it’s clean and dry. Apply some volumizing mousse or spray to add some texture and hold to your hair. Here are some easy updos that you can try:

Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for long thin hair. To create this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure it with bobby pins and pull out a few strands to create a messy, undone look. This hairstyle is perfect for casual occasions or for a day out with friends.

Twisted Bun

The twisted bun is a simple yet elegant hairstyle that adds some sophistication to your look. To create this look, divide your hair into two sections and twist them together. Gather the twisted sections into a low bun and secure it with bobby pins. This hairstyle is perfect for formal occasions or for a night out.

Braided Updo

The braided updo is a beautiful hairstyle that adds some texture and detail to your look. To create this look, start by braiding a small section of hair at the front of your head. Pull the rest of your hair into a low ponytail and wrap the braid around the base of the ponytail. Secure it with bobby pins and pull out a few strands to create a soft, romantic look.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating these easy updo hairstyles:

Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands to create a messy, undone look.

Twisted Bun

  1. Divide your hair into two sections.
  2. Twist the two sections together.
  3. Gather the twisted sections into a low bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Braided Updo

  1. Braid a small section of hair at the front of your head.
  2. Pull the rest of your hair into a low ponytail.
  3. Wrap the braid around the base of the ponytail.
  4. Secure the braid and ponytail with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few strands to create a soft, romantic look.

Tips

Here are some tips for creating easy updo hairstyles for long thin hair:

  • Use volumizing products to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Tease your hair at the crown to add some volume to your updo.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Finish your updo with some hairspray to hold it in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy updo hairstyles for long thin hair:

Can I create these hairstyles on my own?

Yes, these hairstyles are easy to create on your own with some practice and patience.

Can I wear these hairstyles to work?

Yes, these hairstyles are versatile and can be worn to work or for any occasion.

What products should I use to create these hairstyles?

You can use volumizing mousse or spray, bobby pins, and hairspray to create these hairstyles.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your long thin hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these easy updo hairstyles for long thin hair, you can create beautiful and stylish looks that will make you feel confident and chic. Try them out today and see which one works best for you!

What Length Bob For Long Face A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a long face, finding the right haircut can be a bit challenging. However, a bob haircut can help you achieve balance and proportion. But what length bob should you go for? In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the perfect length bob for a long face shape.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to choosing the right bob length for a long face, you need to consider the length of your face and your hair texture. The ideal length is one that creates balance and proportion. A bob that hits just below the chin or collarbone is perfect for a long face shape. This length adds width to the face and creates an illusion of roundness.

How to

To determine the right length for your bob, you should consult with your hairstylist. They will take into account your face shape, hair texture, and personal style preferences. They can also help you choose the right type of bob, whether it be an A-line, inverted, or stacked bob.

Step by step

Here are the steps you can follow to get the perfect bob for a long face:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the right length and type of bob for your face shape.
  2. Decide on the type of bob you want, whether it be an A-line, inverted, or stacked bob.
  3. Ask your stylist to cut your hair to the desired length, making sure it hits just below the chin or collarbone.
  4. Style your hair using a round brush and blow dryer to add volume and texture.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or styling product to hold the look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a bob for a long face:

  • Avoid going too short, as it can elongate the face even more.
  • Add layers to create volume and movement.
  • Consider adding bangs to soften the face and add dimension.
  • Use styling products to add texture and hold the look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bobs for long faces:

  • Will a bob make my face look shorter? Yes, a bob can add width to the face and create an illusion of roundness, making the face look shorter.
  • Can I wear a bob with curly hair? Yes, a bob can work well with curly hair. However, you may need to add layers to prevent the hair from looking too bulky.
  • Should I go for a blunt or layered bob? A layered bob can add volume and movement, while a blunt bob can create a sleek and chic look. It depends on your personal style preferences.

Hair Care

To keep your bob looking its best, you should follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a heat protectant before styling with hot tools, and getting regular trims to maintain the shape of the bob. You can also use hair masks and treatments to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know what length bob for long face, you can confidently rock a chic and stylish bob haircut that flatters your face shape. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and follow a proper hair care routine to keep your bob looking its best.

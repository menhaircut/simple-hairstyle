Easy Hairstyles For Over 70 Simple And Stylish

Written by Petter Dec 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our hair becomes thinner, drier, and less manageable. But that doesn't mean we can't look stylish and chic! In this article, we'll explore easy hairstyles for over 70 that are simple, low-maintenance, and trendy. Whether you have short, medium, or long hair, there's a hairstyle that will suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Easy hairstyles for over 70 are those that require minimal time, effort, and styling tools. They are usually short or medium in length, and feature layers, bangs, or waves. The aim is to create volume, texture, and movement, without compromising on comfort and convenience. Some popular hairstyles for women over 70 include the pixie cut, the bob, the layered shag, and the wavy lob (long bob). These hairstyles are versatile, flattering, and easy to maintain, making them ideal for mature women who want to look youthful and stylish.

How to

To achieve an easy hairstyle for over 70, you need to follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose the right haircut

The first step in achieving an easy hairstyle for over 70 is to choose the right haircut. This should be based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. The most popular haircuts for women over 70 are the pixie cut, the bob, the layered shag, and the wavy lob. These haircuts are low-maintenance, versatile, and stylish.

Step 2: Use the right products

The second step in achieving an easy hairstyle for over 70 is to use the right products. This includes shampoo, conditioner, styling gel, and hairspray. You should choose products that are specifically designed for mature hair, as they will help to nourish, hydrate, and protect your hair from damage.

Step 3: Style your hair

The third step in achieving an easy hairstyle for over 70 is to style your hair. This can be done in a variety of ways, depending on your haircut and personal preference. Some popular styling options include blow-drying, curling, flat-ironing, and scrunching. You should choose a style that is easy to achieve and maintain, and that complements your face shape and hair texture.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some easy hairstyles for over 70:

The Pixie Cut

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling gel to add texture and volume.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift.
  4. Use a flat iron to create flicks or spikes at the ends.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

The Bob

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and add shine.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

The Layered Shag

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling gel to add texture and volume.
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, focusing on the crown and the ends.
  4. Use a flat iron to create flicks or waves at the ends.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for achieving and maintaining easy hairstyles for over 70:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair texture.
  • Use products that are specifically designed for mature hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling tools and techniques to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

What is the easiest hairstyle for over 70?

The easiest hairstyle for over 70 is the pixie cut. It is short, low-maintenance, and stylish.

What is the best haircut for over 70?

The best haircut for over 70 depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Some popular haircuts for women over 70 include the bob, the layered shag, and the wavy lob.

How can I make my hair look fuller and thicker?

To make your hair look fuller and thicker, you can try using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying your hair upside down, and using a texturizing spray or powder.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips for mature hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these easy hairstyles for over 70 and hair care tips, you can look and feel great at any age. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find the ones that work best for you. Remember, it's never too late to reinvent yourself and embrace your natural beauty!

How to Pick the Best Haircut for Your Face Shape

Picking the best haircut for your face shape is not as complicated as it seems. All you need to do is determine the shape of your face and find a hairstyle that complements it. Here's how you can do it:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in picking the best haircut for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six different face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the shape of your face and compare it to the six different face shapes.

Step 2: Pick a Hairstyle that Complements Your Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to pick a hairstyle that complements it. Here are some hairstyles that work best for each face shape:

  • Oval: Any hairstyle works well with an oval face shape.
  • Round: A layered haircut or a side-swept bangs haircut helps to elongate the face.
  • Square: A soft, layered haircut or a side-swept bangs haircut helps to soften the angles of the face.
  • Heart: A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs helps to balance out the face.
  • Diamond: A chin-length bob or a layered haircut with volume at the crown helps to soften the angles of the face.
  • Long: A long, layered haircut or a side-swept bangs haircut helps to add width to the face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to consider when picking the best haircut for your face shape:

  • Consider your hair texture when picking a hairstyle.
  • Use Pinterest or Instagram to find inspiration for your next haircut.
  • Consult with your hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair.
  • Keep in mind that your face shape can change over time, so be open to trying different hairstyles.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about picking the best haircut for your face shape:

Q: Can you change your face shape with a haircut?

A: No, you cannot change your face shape with a haircut. However, you can use a haircut to complement your face shape and enhance your features.

Q: What if I have multiple face shapes?

A: It's common for people to have a combination of two face shapes. In this case, choose a hairstyle that complements both face shapes.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style of your haircut. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Hair Care

After getting a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and health. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to pick the best haircut for your face shape, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and transform your hair into a masterpiece.

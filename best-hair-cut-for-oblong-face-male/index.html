Best Hair Cut For Oblong Face Male The Ultimate Guide

If you have an oblong face shape, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. Your face shape is longer than it is wide, with a prominent forehead and a narrow chin. However, with the right haircut, you can balance out your features and enhance your best features.

  • Best Haircuts for Oblong Face Males

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best haircut for your oblong face shape is to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Measure your face from your hairline to your chin and from cheek to cheek.
  2. If the length is longer than the width and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are about the same width, you have an oblong face shape.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for the best haircut to flatter your features.

Best Haircuts for Oblong Face Males

Here are some of the best haircuts for oblong face males:

Short and Textured

A short and textured haircut can help balance out your face shape. The texture adds volume and dimension, while the short length keeps the focus away from your forehead.

Side Parted

A side-parted haircut can also work well for oblong face males. The side part adds volume to the top of your head, while the length on the sides and back helps balance out your face shape.

Quiff

A quiff is another great haircut for oblong face males. The volume on top adds width to your forehead, while the shorter sides and back help balance out your face shape.

Undercut

An undercut is a trendy haircut that can work well for oblong face males. The short sides and back help balance out your face shape, while the longer length on top adds volume and dimension.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your oblong face shape, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Cleanse and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.

FAQs

What hairstyles should oblong face males avoid?

Oblong face males should avoid hairstyles that make their faces look longer, such as slicked-back hair or hairstyles with a center part.

Can oblong face males have long hair?

Oblong face males can have long hair, but it's important to keep the length balanced with the width of your face. Long hair that is too thin or wispy can make your face look even longer.

Hair Care for Oblong Face Males

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

With these tips and the right haircut, you can enhance your oblong face shape and look your best.

How To Remove Under Eye Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Under eye hair can be a pesky problem for many, but luckily there are ways to remove it safely and effectively. In this guide, we'll go over how to remove under eye hair and provide tips for maintaining smooth skin in that area.

How to Remove Under Eye Hair

There are a few methods for removing under eye hair, including:

1. Tweezing

Tweezing is a simple and effective method for removing under eye hair. To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Cleanse your face with a gentle cleanser and pat dry.
  2. Use a pair of sanitized tweezers to pluck the hairs one at a time.
  3. Always pluck in the direction of hair growth to avoid breakage.
  4. Apply a cold compress to soothe the area afterwards.

Tweezing can be painful, but it's a great option for small areas of hair.

2. Threading

Threading is a technique that involves using a twisted cotton thread to remove hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Cleanse your face and dry it well.
  2. Take a piece of cotton thread and twist it into a loop.
  3. Hold one end of the loop in your mouth and the other in your hand.
  4. Place the loop over the hairs and move it back and forth to remove them.
  5. Apply a cold compress to soothe the area afterwards.

Threading can be more time-consuming than tweezing, but it can remove hair more quickly and effectively.

3. Waxing

Waxing is a popular method for removing larger areas of hair, including under eye hair. Here are the steps:

  1. Choose a wax that's safe for use on the face.
  2. Cleanse your face and dry it well.
  3. Apply a thin layer of wax to the area with a spatula.
  4. Place a strip of cloth over the wax and press down firmly.
  5. Pull the strip of cloth off quickly in the opposite direction of hair growth.
  6. Apply a cold compress to soothe the area afterwards.

Waxing can be painful, but it's a great option for larger areas of hair.

Tips for Removing Under Eye Hair

Here are a few tips for safely and effectively removing under eye hair:

  • Always cleanse your face before removing hair to prevent infection.
  • Use a gentle touch when removing hair to avoid irritating the skin.
  • Apply a cold compress to soothe the area afterwards.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or products on the under eye area.
  • If you're unsure about how to remove under eye hair, seek the advice of a professional.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about under eye hair removal:

1. Is it safe to remove under eye hair?

Yes, it's safe to remove under eye hair as long as you use a safe and effective method.

2. Will removing under eye hair cause it to grow back thicker?

No, removing under eye hair will not cause it to grow back thicker. This is a common myth.

3. Can I use hair removal cream on under eye hair?

Hair removal cream is not recommended for use on the under eye area due to its harsh chemicals.

Hair Care for Under Eye Area

To keep the under eye area looking smooth and hair-free, it's important to practice good hair care habits. Here are a few tips:

  • Keep the area clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid rubbing or tugging at the skin around the eyes.
  • Use a gentle exfoliating scrub once a week to remove dead skin cells.
  • Avoid using heavy makeup or creams on the under eye area.
  • Always wear sunglasses when outside to protect the delicate skin from sun damage.

By following these tips, you can keep your under eye area looking smooth and hair-free.

Conclusion

Removing under eye hair can be a simple and effective way to keep the area looking smooth and hair-free. Whether you choose tweezing, threading, or waxing, it's important to use a safe and effective method and to practice good hair care habits. By following these tips, you can keep your under eye area looking its best.

Short Hairstyles For Round Face Boy The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Sep 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a boy with a round face and struggling to find the perfect short hairstyle that suits you? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the best short hairstyles for round face boys that will make you look sharp and stylish.

Description

A round face shape is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. It can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that complements the face shape, but with the right cut, you can accentuate your features and look great.

How to

The first step in getting a short hairstyle is to determine the shape of your face. Once you have identified that you have a round face, it's time to choose a haircut that flatters your features. Here are some of the best short hairstyles for round face boys:

1. Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic hairstyle that is easy to maintain and suits almost all face shapes. It is a short and simple hairstyle that is perfect for boys with round faces as it accentuates the facial structure.

2. Side Part

The side part is a timeless hairstyle that adds sophistication to any look. It works well with round faces as it creates an illusion of length and slims down the face.

3. Crew Cut

The crew cut is a popular hairstyle among boys as it is low maintenance and versatile. It is a short hairstyle that is tapered on the sides and back while leaving more length on top. This cut creates an angular look that softens the roundness of the face.

4. Undercut

The undercut is a trendy hairstyle that is perfect for boys who want to make a statement. It is a short cut with shaved sides and longer hair on top. This style complements round faces as it adds height and volume to the hair.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get a short hairstyle for round face boys:

Step 1: Consult with a hairstylist

Before getting any haircut, it is essential to consult with a hairstylist. They can recommend the best cut that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Choose a haircut

Once you have consulted with a hairstylist, it's time to choose a haircut that you like. You can ask the stylist for suggestions or show them a picture of the style you want.

Step 3: Maintain the hairstyle

Short hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep them looking sharp. Make sure to schedule regular appointments with your hairstylist to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for round face boys:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your facial features
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face
  • Stick to short and simple hairstyles
  • Maintain the hairstyle regularly

FAQ

What is the best short hairstyle for round face boys?

The best short hairstyle for round face boys is one that complements the facial features and adds length to the face. Some of the best hairstyles include the buzz cut, side part, crew cut, and undercut.

Can boys with round faces have long hair?

Yes, boys with round faces can have long hair. However, it is essential to choose a hairstyle that adds length to the face and avoids adding width.

How often should I maintain my short hairstyle?

Short hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep them looking sharp. It is recommended to schedule an appointment with your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the hairstyle.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential to keep your short hairstyle looking great. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather

Now that you have a list of the best short hairstyles for round face boys, it's time to choose the perfect cut for you. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and maintain your hairstyle regularly for the best results.

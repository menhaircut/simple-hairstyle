Short Hair With Bangs Heart Shaped Face The Perfect Combination In 2023

Written by Frank Nov 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking for a fresh, new look? Short hair with bangs might just be what you need to spice up your appearance. This hairstyle has been trendy for a while now, and it's no surprise why. It's versatile, easy to maintain, and can flatter almost any face shape.

What is a Heart Shaped Face?

Before we dive into the short hair with bangs hairstyle, let's define what a heart-shaped face is. A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. This face shape is also known as an inverted triangle shape, and it's considered one of the most challenging face shapes to style.

How to Determine if You Have a Heart Shaped Face?

To determine if you have a heart-shaped face, take a look at your face in the mirror. If your forehead is wider than your chin, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, then you have a heart-shaped face.

Short Hair with Bangs for Heart Shaped Face: How to Style?

Now that you know what a heart-shaped face is let's talk about how to style short hair with bangs for this face shape. The goal is to balance out the wide forehead and narrow chin, and the best way to do that is by adding volume to the top of your head and creating width around your jawline.

Step-by-Step Guide to Style Short Hair with Bangs for Heart Shaped Face

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair with bangs for heart-shaped faces:

  1. Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it until it's 80% dry.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections and use a round brush to blow-dry each section, starting from the roots to the ends.
  3. Once your hair is completely dry, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Focus on the top of your head to add volume.
  4. Apply a small amount of texturizing spray to your roots for extra lift.
  5. Part your hair on the side and create side-swept bangs. Make sure your bangs are long enough to cover your forehead but not too long that they cover your eyes.
  6. Finish the look with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Short Hair with Bangs Heart Shaped Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short hair with bangs for heart-shaped faces:

  • Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your forehead look even wider.
  • Side-swept bangs are the most flattering for heart-shaped faces.
  • Adding volume to the top of your head can balance out your face shape.
  • Loose waves are perfect for adding texture and dimension to your hair.
  • Texturizing spray can help create lift and volume to your roots.

FAQ about Short Hair with Bangs Heart Shaped Face

Q: Can I still style my short hair with bangs if I have a heart-shaped face?

A: Yes, absolutely! Short hair with bangs can be styled to flatter any face shape, including heart-shaped faces. The key is to add volume to the top of your head and create width around your jawline.

Q: How often do I need to trim my bangs?

A: It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them in shape and prevent them from getting too long.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with bangs?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with bangs. Just make sure to leave your bangs out and style them to the side for a chic look.

Hair Care for Short Hair with Bangs Heart Shaped Face

Short hair with bangs requires minimal maintenance, but it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it over time.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

Short hair with bangs heart shaped face is the perfect combination for a chic and trendy look. With the right styling techniques and hair care routine, you can rock this hairstyle with confidence.

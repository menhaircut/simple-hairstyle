Best Haircuts For Fat Faces Over 50

Are you over 50 and have a round face? Do you want to try out a new haircut that will flatter your face shape? You have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for fat faces over 50. We will also give you tips on how to care for your hair and maintain your hairstyle. Let's get started.

Description

As we age, our face shape changes, and we may develop a rounder face due to the loss of muscle tone and the accumulation of fat. A round face can be beautiful, but it can be challenging to find the right haircut that compliments it. The goal is to create the illusion of a slimmer face, and this can be achieved with the right hairstyle.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in choosing the right haircut is to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you want to avoid haircuts that make your face appear wider. Instead, you want to choose a haircut that elongates your face and creates the appearance of a slimmer face.

Some of the best haircuts for fat faces over 50 include:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. The layers should begin at the jawline and continue down to the ends of your hair. This will help to elongate your face and create a more flattering shape.

2. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob with side-swept bangs can be a great option for those with round faces. The side-swept bangs help to create the illusion of a longer face, while the bob adds volume to the crown of your head. This can help to balance out your face shape.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold choice, but it can be very flattering for those with round faces. The key is to add volume to the crown of your head and keep the sides short. This will help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Step by Step Guide to Getting a New Haircut

If you have decided to get a new haircut, here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

Step 1: Research

Research different hairstyles that are suitable for your face shape and age. Look for inspiration from magazines, social media, and celebrities.

Step 2: Consultation

Schedule a consultation with your hairstylist. Bring pictures of the hairstyles you like and discuss your face shape, lifestyle, and hair texture. Your hairstylist will be able to recommend the best haircut for you.

Step 3: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair before your appointment. This will help your hairstylist to see your hair's natural texture and condition.

Step 4: Communication

During the haircut, communicate with your hairstylist. Let them know if you want more volume, less volume, or a different length.

Step 5: Styling

After your haircut, your hairstylist will style your hair. Ask them for tips on how to maintain your hairstyle at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.

2. Use a Blow Dryer

Blow-dry your hair after washing it. Use a round brush to add volume to your hair.

3. Regular Trims

Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQs

Q: Can I still get a short haircut if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still get a short haircut if you have a round face. The key is to add volume to the crown of your head and keep the sides short. This will help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Q: What type of bangs are suitable for a round face?

A: Side-swept bangs are suitable for a round face. They help to create the illusion of a longer face.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair. This will protect your hair from heat damage.

3. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils. Wash your hair every other day or every third day.

Now that you know the best haircuts for fat faces over 50, you can confidently book an appointment with your hairstylist. Remember to take care of your hair and maintain your hairstyle with the tips we have provided.

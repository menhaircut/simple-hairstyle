Do Bangs Suit Small Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old look and want to try something new with your hair? Bangs are a great way to switch things up and add some personality to your style. However, if you have a small face, you might be wondering if bangs will suit you. In this guide, we’ll explore the do’s and don’ts of bangs for small faces, so you can make an informed decision before taking the plunge.

Description

Bangs are a popular hairstyle that involves cutting the hair across the forehead. They have been in fashion for decades and come in many different styles, from blunt to side-swept to wispy. However, not all bangs are created equal, and some styles may not be suitable for those with small faces.

How to Determine if Bangs Suit Small Faces

Before deciding on bangs for your small face, it’s important to consider a few things. First, you need to determine the shape of your face. Small faces can come in many shapes, such as oval, round, heart-shaped, or square. Each face shape has different features that should be taken into account when choosing a suitable bang style.

Next, you need to consider your hair type and texture. Some bang styles may not work well with certain hair types or textures. For example, if you have curly hair, a blunt bang may not be the best option as it can be difficult to style and may not sit properly.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Bangs for Small Faces

Step 1: Determine your face shape. This can be done by looking in the mirror and studying the contours of your face. You can also use an online face shape calculator to help you determine your face shape.

Step 2: Consider your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, a wispy or layered bang may work better than a blunt bang. If you have thick hair, a heavier bang may be more suitable.

Step 3: Look for inspiration. Browse online or in magazines for pictures of bangs that you like. Keep in mind that what looks good on someone else may not necessarily look good on you.

Step 4: Consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you choose the right bang style for your face shape and hair type. They can also give you tips on how to style and maintain your bangs.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs on Small Faces

Once you’ve chosen the perfect bang style for your small face, it’s important to take care of them to keep them looking great. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your bangs:

  • Keep them trimmed. Regular trims will help keep your bangs looking neat and tidy.
  • Blow-dry them properly. Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to go.
  • Use styling products. A small amount of hair gel or mousse can help keep your bangs in place.
  • Avoid touching them too much. Constantly touching your bangs can make them greasy and unkempt.

FAQs

Q: What bang style is best for small faces?

A: The best bang style for a small face depends on your face shape and hair type. A professional hairstylist can help you choose the right style.

Q: Can I wear bangs if I have a small forehead?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs even if you have a small forehead. Just make sure to choose a style that doesn’t cover too much of your forehead.

Q: Will bangs make my face look smaller?

A: Bangs can help balance out your features and make your face look more proportionate. However, the wrong bang style can make your face look smaller, so it’s important to choose the right style for your face shape.

Hair Care for Bangs on Small Faces

Bangs require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are a few hair care tips to help you keep your bangs healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your bangs clean and free of oil and dirt.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your bangs and make them look dry and frizzy.
  • Use a conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair and bangs moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Remember, choosing the right bang style for your small face can make a big difference in your overall look. By following these tips and consulting with a professional hairstylist, you can find the perfect bangs to suit your style and personality.

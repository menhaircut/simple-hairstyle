Best Hairstyles For Square Faces 2021

Written by Bardi Sep 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a square face shape, you are in luck because there are plenty of hairstyles that will flatter your features. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for square faces in 2021. Whether you have long hair, short hair, or something in between, we've got you covered!

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and a forehead that is the same width as the cheekbones. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for a square face is to soften the angles and create balance.

How To

The first step in choosing a hairstyle for a square face is to determine your hair type. Do you have straight, wavy, or curly hair? Next, consider the length of your hair. Do you prefer short hair or long hair? Once you have determined your hair type and length, you can begin to explore different hairstyles that will flatter your square face shape.

Step by Step

1. Determine your hair type and length.

2. Explore different hairstyles for square faces.

3. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

4. Consult with a hairstylist if you are unsure about what hairstyle to choose.

Tips

When choosing a hairstyle for a square face, consider adding layers to soften the angles of your face. Bangs can also be a great option for square faces, as they can help to balance out the width of the forehead. If you have long hair, consider adding waves or curls to add volume and movement.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a bob with a square face?

A: Yes, a bob can be a great option for square faces. Consider adding layers or side-swept bangs to soften the angles of your face.

Q: Should I avoid straight hair if I have a square face?

A: Not necessarily. Straight hair can work well with a square face, but be sure to add layers or waves to soften the angles of your face.

Hair Care

Regardless of your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair. This means washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using heat protectant when styling with heat tools, and getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for square faces in 2021, it's time to head to the salon and try out a new look! Remember to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

How To Cut Curly Men s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting curly hair can be a daunting task, especially if you are not familiar with the particularities of curly hair. It requires a lot of attention to detail and a good understanding of the hair's texture and natural movement. In this article, we will show you how to cut curly men's hair with scissors, providing you with tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions.

How to Cut Curly Men's Hair with Scissors

Before we get started, let's take a look at the tools you will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Spray bottle
  • Cutting cape or towel

Step 1: Preparation

Start by preparing your client's hair. Make sure it is clean and dry, and detangle it using a wide-tooth comb. If necessary, spray the hair lightly with water to make it more manageable.

Step 2: Sectioning

Next, section the hair. Divide it into four parts: two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 3: Cutting

Take one section at a time and start cutting. Use your fingers to guide the scissors, and cut the hair at an angle. Make sure to leave some length, as curly hair tends to shrink when it dries.

Step 4: Blending

Once you have cut all four sections, blend the hair using scissors or thinning shears. This will help to create a seamless transition between the different lengths.

Step 5: Finishing

To finish, remove the hair clips and comb through the hair. Check for any unevenness or stray hairs, and trim them as necessary. Style the hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Curly Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Make sure your scissors are sharp. Dull scissors can damage the hair.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to cut a little bit at a time than to take off too much and regret it later.
  • Use your fingers to guide the scissors, rather than a comb. This will help you to create a more natural-looking cut.
  • Don't cut curly hair when it's wet. Curly hair tends to look shorter when it's wet, so cutting it when it's dry will give you a better idea of the final result.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut curly hair with clippers?

While it is possible to cut curly hair with clippers, scissors are usually a better option. Clippers can cause the hair to look too uniform and can result in an uneven cut.

How often should I cut curly hair?

Curly hair tends to grow more slowly than straight hair, so you may be able to wait longer between cuts. However, it's generally recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep the hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help your clients take care of their curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing curly hair when it's dry. This can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle the hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define the curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair and cause it to lose its natural curl pattern.
  • Protect the hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

Conclusion

Cutting curly men's hair with scissors requires patience, skill, and attention to detail. By following the steps outlined in this article and using the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and natural-looking cut that your clients will love. Remember to communicate with your clients throughout the process and ask for their feedback to ensure that you are meeting their expectations.

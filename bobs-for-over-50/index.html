Bobs For Over 50 A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

Written by Oliver Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 50 and looking for a flattering and low-maintenance hairstyle, a bob might be the perfect choice. This classic cut is versatile and can suit a variety of face shapes and hair types. In this guide, we'll explore different bobs for over 50, how to style them, and hair care tips to keep your locks looking healthy and vibrant.

Why Choose a Bob?

There are many reasons why bobs are a popular choice for women over 50. Firstly, they are easy to maintain and style. A bob can be a wash-and-go style, requiring minimal effort to look good. Secondly, bobs are versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. Finally, bobs can make you look younger and more vibrant, which is always a plus!

How to Choose the Right Bob

Before you decide on a bob, consider your face shape and hair type. Bobs can be tailored to suit different face shapes, such as round, square, oval, or heart-shaped. Similarly, there are bobs for different hair types, such as fine, thick, curly or straight hair. Your stylist can recommend the best bob for you based on these factors.

Popular Bobs for Over 50

Here are some popular bobs for women over 50:

  • The classic bob: This is a chin-length bob with a straight cut.
  • The layered bob: This bob has layers to add volume and movement.
  • The angled bob: This bob is longer in the front and shorter at the back, creating an angled shape.
  • The shaggy bob: This bob has choppy layers and a tousled look.
  • The curly bob: This bob is cut to showcase natural curls.

How to Style a Bob

Bobs are easy to style and maintain. Here are some tips:

  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and tame frizz.
  • For a more casual look, let your hair air-dry and use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture.

Bob Hair Care Tips

To keep your bob looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hydrating conditioner to moisturize your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as heat can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your bob and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQ

Can I wear a bob if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can wear a bob if you have thin hair. Opt for a layered bob to add volume and fullness to your hair.

What face shape is best suited for a bob?

Bobs can suit a variety of face shapes, but they are particularly flattering for those with a heart-shaped or oval face.

How often should I get my bob trimmed?

It's recommended to get your bob trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Can I still wear a bob if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can wear a curly bob. Opt for a layered cut to showcase your natural curls.

What products should I use to style my bob?

Use a round brush, flat iron or curling iron, and a texturizing spray or hair oil to style your bob.

Conclusion

Bobs are a timeless and versatile hairstyle that can suit women of all ages, including those over 50. With the right cut, styling, and hair care, you can rock a bob that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural beauty. Talk to your stylist to find the perfect bob for you and embrace this chic and low-maintenance style!

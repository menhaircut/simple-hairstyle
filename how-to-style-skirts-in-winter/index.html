How To Style Skirts In Winter Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jul 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Winter may not be the season that comes to mind when you think of skirts, but they can be a versatile addition to your cold-weather wardrobe. With the right styling, you can wear skirts all winter long. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks for how to style skirts in winter.

How to Style Skirts in Winter

When it comes to styling skirts in winter, the key is to layer. Here are some steps you can follow to create a stylish winter outfit with a skirt:

Step 1: Choose the Right Skirt

Not all skirts are created equal. Some are better suited for winter weather than others. Look for skirts that are made from thick, warm materials like wool or tweed. Midi and maxi skirts are also great options for winter as they offer more coverage than shorter skirts.

Step 2: Layer with Tights or Leggings

When it’s cold outside, you’ll want to keep your legs covered. Tights or leggings are a great way to do this while still showing off your skirt. Choose thicker tights or leggings for extra warmth.

Step 3: Add a Sweater or Cardigan

To keep warm and add some texture to your outfit, layer a sweater or cardigan over your top. You can also opt for a turtleneck or mock neck top for extra warmth.

Step 4: Finish with Boots or Booties

The right footwear can make or break your winter outfit. Boots or booties are the perfect finishing touch to a winter skirt outfit. Look for styles with a low heel or flat sole for comfort and ease of walking in snow or slush.

Tips for Styling Skirts in Winter

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling skirts in winter:

Tip 1: Experiment with Different Lengths

Don’t be afraid to try different skirt lengths in winter. Midi and maxi skirts offer more coverage, but shorter skirts can be just as stylish when paired with tights or leggings.

Tip 2: Mix Textures

Winter is the perfect time to experiment with different textures. Pair a wool skirt with a silk blouse or a velvet skirt with a knit sweater for a cozy yet chic look.

Tip 3: Play with Colors

Winter doesn’t mean you have to stick to a neutral color palette. Add a pop of color to your outfit with a bright skirt or patterned tights.

FAQs about Styling Skirts in Winter

Q: Can I wear a skirt without tights in winter?

A: It’s not recommended to wear a skirt without tights in winter as it can leave you feeling cold and exposed.

Q: What kind of boots should I wear with a skirt in winter?

A: Look for boots or booties with a low heel or flat sole for comfort and ease of walking in snow or slush.

Hair Care for Winter Skirt Outfits

When wearing a skirt in winter, you’ll want to make sure your hair is styled to keep you warm and comfortable. Here are some tips for winter hair care:

Tip 1: Keep Your Hair Moisturized

Winter weather can be drying, so it’s important to keep your hair moisturized. Use a deep conditioner once a week and avoid over-washing your hair.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from the Cold

Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the cold and wind. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to provide extra protection and moisture.

Tip 3: Embrace Braids and Updos

Braids and updos are great hairstyles for winter skirt outfits as they keep your hair off your face and neck, while also adding some texture and interest to your overall look.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock a skirt in winter with confidence and style. Remember to layer, experiment with different textures and colors, and keep your hair protected and moisturized. Stay warm and stylish this winter season!

Men s Haircut Fade Medium Top How To Achieve The Look

Description

The men's haircut fade medium top is a popular hairstyle that has been around for years. This style features short hair on the sides and back, gradually blending into longer hair on top. The hair on top can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. This versatile haircut is perfect for men who want a stylish yet low-maintenance look.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the men's haircut fade medium top, you will need to visit a professional barber or hairstylist. They will use clippers to create a fade on the sides and back, gradually blending the shorter hair into the longer hair on top. The length of the hair on top can vary depending on your preference, but typically ranges from 2 to 4 inches.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide for achieving the men's haircut fade medium top:

  1. Consult with your barber or hairstylist to determine the length and style of the hair on top.
  2. Ask for a fade on the sides and back, starting with a longer guard and gradually decreasing to a shorter guard.
  3. Have the hair on top cut to the desired length and style. This may involve cutting layers or adding texture.
  4. Once the cut is complete, your barber or hairstylist will use a comb and scissors to blend the hair on top with the fade on the sides and back.
  5. Style your hair as desired using a pomade, wax, or gel.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

To maintain the men's haircut fade medium top, consider the following tips:

  • Visit your barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Invest in a good styling product that is suitable for your hair type and desired style.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQs

How long does the men's haircut fade medium top last?

The length of time the haircut lasts depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you get it trimmed. On average, the style can last between 4-6 weeks.

Is the men's haircut fade medium top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the style is suitable for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Can I style the hair on top differently each day?

Yes, the hair on top can be styled in various ways, allowing you to change up your look as desired.

Hair Care for Men

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for achieving and maintaining the men's haircut fade medium top. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage the hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

By following these tips and getting regular trims, you can maintain a healthy and stylish men's haircut fade medium top.

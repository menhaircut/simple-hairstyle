How To Keep Hair Slicked Back In A Ponytail

Written by Eveline Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a classic, sleek hairstyle that's easy to achieve, look no further than the slicked back ponytail. This look is perfect for any occasion, from a day at the office to a night out with friends. However, keeping your hair slicked back in a ponytail can be tricky, especially if your hair is prone to slipping out of place. In this article, we'll show you how to keep your hair slicked back in a ponytail with ease.

  • How to Keep Hair Slicked Back in a Ponytail
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Keeping Your Hair in Place

How to Keep Hair Slicked Back in a Ponytail

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the tools you'll need to achieve this look. Here's what you'll need:

  • Comb or brush
  • Hair tie
  • Hair gel or pomade

Now, let's get started!

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is wet, it will be more difficult to achieve a sleek look. Blow dry your hair if necessary.

Step 2: Apply hair gel or pomade

Next, apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hands and rub them together. Then, starting at the roots, work the product through your hair. Make sure to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair, as this will help to keep it slicked back.

Step 3: Brush your hair back

Using a comb or brush, brush your hair back away from your face. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Step 4: Secure your hair with a hair tie

Once your hair is brushed back, use a hair tie to secure it in a ponytail. Make sure to pull the hair tie tight, as this will help to keep your hair in place.

Step 5: Apply more product if necessary

If you notice any flyaways or if your hair starts to slip out of place, apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hands and work it through your hair again. This will help to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Keeping Your Hair in Place

Now that you know how to achieve a slicked back ponytail, here are some tips for keeping your hair in place all day:

  • Use a strong hair tie to secure your ponytail.
  • Apply hair gel or pomade to your hair before brushing it back.
  • Use a comb or brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  • Apply more product throughout the day if necessary.

FAQs

Can I achieve a slicked back ponytail with curly hair?

Yes, you can achieve a slicked back ponytail with curly hair. However, you may need to use more product to keep your hair in place.

How do I prevent my hair tie from slipping out of place?

To prevent your hair tie from slipping out of place, make sure to pull it tight when securing your ponytail. You can also try using a hair tie with a rubber grip to keep it in place.

Hair Care Tips

Keeping your hair healthy is important for achieving a sleek, polished look. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the right tools and products, you can easily achieve a sleek, polished look with a slicked back ponytail. Whether you're headed to work or out for a night on the town, this hairstyle is perfect for any occasion.

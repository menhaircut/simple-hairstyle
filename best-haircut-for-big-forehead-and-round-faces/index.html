Best Haircut For Big Forehead And Round Faces

Written by Bardi Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of hiding your big forehead and round face behind your hair? If yes, then you are in the right place. In this article, we will share the best haircuts that will flatter your face shape and highlight your best features. Read on to discover the perfect haircut for big forehead and round faces.

Description

Having a big forehead and round face can be a challenge when it comes to choosing the right haircut. You want to find a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your best features. The good news is that there are plenty of haircuts that can do just that. From bangs to layers, we've got you covered.

How to

Before we dive into the best haircuts for big forehead and round faces, it's essential to understand your face shape. To determine if you have a round face, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all relatively equal, then you have a round face shape. If your forehead is wider than your cheekbones and jawline, then you have a heart-shaped face.

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to choose the right haircut. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Step by Step

  • Choose a haircut that adds height to your face, such as a bob or a pixie cut.
  • Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a blunt cut or a one-length bob.
  • Consider adding bangs to your haircut to help balance out your face shape.
  • Layers can also help add dimension to your hair and create a flattering frame for your face.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Tips

When choosing a haircut for big forehead and round faces, it's essential to consider your hair type. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a layered haircut to help thin out your hair. If you have fine hair, a blunt cut may be a better option to add volume to your hair.

It's also important to consider your lifestyle when choosing a haircut. If you have a busy schedule, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling. If you have more time to spend on your hair, you may want to choose a haircut that allows for more styling options.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a big forehead?

The best haircut for a big forehead is one that adds volume to the crown of your head, such as a bob or a pixie cut. Adding bangs can also help to balance out your face shape.

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that adds height to your face, such as a layered bob or a pixie cut. Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face.

Hair Care

Once you have found the perfect haircut for your big forehead and round face, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hairline and make your forehead look even bigger.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep it looking its best.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut for big forehead and round faces can be a challenge, but with the right tips and advice, it's possible to find a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your best features. Remember to consider your hair type, lifestyle, and face shape when choosing a haircut, and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

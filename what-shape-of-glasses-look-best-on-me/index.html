What Shape Of Glasses Look Best On Me

Description

Choosing the right pair of glasses can be a daunting task, especially if you are not sure what shape of glasses look best on you. With so many different styles, shapes, and colors to choose from, it can be overwhelming to find the perfect pair of glasses that not only look good but also suit your personality and lifestyle.

In this article, we will guide you through the process of determining your face shape and choosing the right glasses that will compliment your features and enhance your overall appearance. We will also provide you with some tips and tricks on how to care for your hair when wearing glasses.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right glasses is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the shape of your face and compare it to the descriptions below:

  • Round face: The width and length of your face are almost equal, with a rounded jawline and cheekbones.
  • Oval face: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded forehead and jawline.
  • Square face: Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, with an angular jawline.
  • Heart face: Your forehead is wider than your chin, with a pointed chin.
  • Diamond face: Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, with a pointed chin and forehead.
  • Triangle face: Your jawline is wider than your forehead, with a pointed chin.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Glasses

Now that you have determined your face shape, it's time to choose the right glasses that will compliment your features and enhance your overall appearance. Follow these steps to find the perfect pair:

  1. Choose glasses that contrast with your face shape. If you have a round face, choose glasses with angular frames. If you have a square face, choose glasses with round frames.
  2. Choose glasses that are the right size for your face. The frames should not be too wide or too narrow for your face.
  3. Choose glasses that compliment your skin tone and hair color. If you have warm skin tones, choose glasses with warm colors such as brown or gold. If you have cool skin tones, choose glasses with cool colors such as black or silver.
  4. Choose glasses that compliment your style and personality. If you are a professional, choose glasses with a classic and timeless style. If you are more adventurous, choose glasses with a bold and unique style.
  5. Try on different styles and shapes of glasses to see what looks best on you. Don't be afraid to ask for opinions from friends and family.

Tips for Choosing the Right Glasses

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right glasses:

  • Choose glasses that are comfortable to wear. The frames should fit snugly on your nose and ears without pinching or sliding.
  • Choose glasses that are durable and easy to maintain. Look for glasses made from materials such as titanium or acetate.
  • Choose glasses that provide the right amount of protection for your eyes. Look for glasses with UV protection and anti-reflective coatings.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right glasses:

What shape of glasses are best for round faces?

Glasses with angular frames such as rectangular or square frames are best for round faces. These frames will help to create a more defined and angular appearance.

What shape of glasses are best for oval faces?

Oval faces can wear almost any shape of glasses, but rectangular or square frames are particularly flattering.

What shape of glasses are best for square faces?

Glasses with round or oval frames are best for square faces. These frames will help to soften the angular lines of the face.

What shape of glasses are best for heart-shaped faces?

Glasses with bottom-heavy frames such as aviator or cat-eye frames are best for heart-shaped faces. These frames will help to balance out the width of the forehead and the narrowness of the chin.

Hair Care Tips for Glasses Wearers

Here are some tips and tricks for caring for your hair when wearing glasses:

  • Choose a hairstyle that compliments your glasses. If you have long hair, consider tying it back in a ponytail or bun to keep it out of the way of your glasses.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from getting flattened against your head by your glasses.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent your hair from getting damaged.

By following these tips and tricks, you can choose the right glasses that will compliment your features and enhance your overall appearance. Remember to take your time and try on different styles and shapes of glasses to find the perfect pair for you.

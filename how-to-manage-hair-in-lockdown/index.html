How To Manage Hair In Lockdown

Written by Christine Sep 14, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you struggling to manage your hair during the lockdown? You are not alone. Many people are finding it difficult to take care of their hair without regular salon appointments. However, with some simple tips and tricks, you can keep your hair healthy and beautiful even in the lockdown.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Manage Hair in Lockdown
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Managing Hair in Lockdown
  4. FAQs

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to manage your hair during the lockdown:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly is essential to keep it clean and healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Rinse your hair thoroughly and avoid using hot water as it can damage your hair.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools

Heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons can damage your hair. Avoid using them as much as possible. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray and don’t use them on wet hair.

3. Trim your hair regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy. You can do it yourself or ask someone in your household to help you. Use sharp scissors and cut small sections at a time.

4. Use natural hair masks

You can make natural hair masks at home using ingredients like avocado, banana, and honey. Apply the mask to your hair and leave it on for half an hour before washing it off. This will nourish your hair and make it shiny and healthy.

5. Avoid tight hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails as they can pull on your hair and cause damage. Opt for loose hairstyles instead.

Tips for Managing Hair in Lockdown

Here are some additional tips for managing your hair during the lockdown:

  • Brush your hair regularly to distribute natural oils and prevent tangles.
  • Avoid using too much hair products as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz.
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a balanced diet to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.
  • Try different hairstyles to keep your hair looking fresh and stylish.

FAQs

Can I dye my hair during the lockdown?

You can dye your hair at home using a DIY dye kit. However, it’s important to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test first to avoid any allergic reactions.

How often should I wash my hair during the lockdown?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage.

Can I cut my own hair during the lockdown?

You can cut your own hair during the lockdown, but it’s important to be careful and use sharp scissors. You can also ask someone in your household to help you.

Hair Care During Lockdown

Lockdown can be a challenging time for hair care, but with these simple tips and tricks, you can keep your hair healthy and beautiful. Remember to wash your hair regularly, avoid heat styling tools, trim your hair, use natural hair masks, and avoid tight hairstyles. Additionally, follow our tips for managing hair in lockdown and don’t forget to drink plenty of water and eat a balanced diet to keep your hair healthy from the inside out. With these tips, you can look and feel your best during the lockdown.

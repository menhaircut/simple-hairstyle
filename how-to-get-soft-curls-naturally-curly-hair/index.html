How To Get Soft Curls Naturally Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools to achieve soft curls in your naturally curly hair? It's time to embrace your curls and learn how to get soft curls naturally. In this article, we will guide you through the process of achieving beautiful, soft curls without damaging your hair.

Description

Soft curls are a great way to add volume and bounce to your hair. Many people with naturally curly hair struggle with achieving soft curls without using heat styling tools. However, there are ways to get soft curls naturally without damaging your hair. Follow these simple steps to achieve soft, bouncy curls that last all day.

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can be harsh on curly hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for curly hair to enhance your natural curls.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your curls and provide moisture. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the conditioner evenly through your hair.

Step 3: Use a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair to enhance your curls' natural shape and add definition. Choose a product that is lightweight and won't weigh down your curls.

Step 4: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Diffusing your hair helps to enhance your curls' natural shape and reduce frizz. Use a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with your hands as you dry it.

Step 5: Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your hands to scrunch your curls gently. This will help to enhance your curls' natural shape and create soft, bouncy curls.

Tips for Getting Soft Curls Naturally Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve soft curls without damaging your hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can be damaging to curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can be drying to curly hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.

FAQs

What is the best way to enhance natural curls?

The best way to enhance natural curls is to use a curl-enhancing product and diffuse your hair with a hair dryer. Scrunching your hair with your hands can also help to enhance your curls' natural shape.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's best to wash curly hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for curly hair to enhance your natural curls.

Can I use heat styling tools on my curly hair?

While it's best to avoid heat styling tools, you can use them occasionally. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Soft Curls Naturally Curly Hair

To keep your hair looking and feeling healthy, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for caring for soft curls naturally curly hair:

  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can be damaging to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.

By following these simple steps and tips, you can achieve soft, bouncy curls that last all day without damaging your hair. Embrace your natural curls and enjoy beautiful, healthy hair!

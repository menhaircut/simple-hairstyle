Male Hairstyles Long On Top A Guide To Effortless Cool

Written by Daniel Aug 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, high-style haircut, you can't go wrong with a male hairstyle long on top. This versatile look can be dressed up or down, and it works well for all hair types and face shapes. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect long-on-top hairstyle, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A male hairstyle long on top typically features longer hair on the top of the head, with shorter hair on the sides and back. This creates a stylish contrast that can be worn in a variety of ways. For example, you might slick back the longer hair for a more polished look, or leave it tousled for a more relaxed vibe.

How To

When it comes to achieving the perfect male hairstyle long on top, there are a few key steps you'll need to follow.

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step is to decide how long you want your hair to be on top. This will depend on your personal style and your hair type. If you have thick hair, you might be able to get away with longer hair on top, while those with fine hair may need to keep it shorter.

Step 2: Decide on the Style

Next, you'll need to decide on the style you want. Do you want a slicked-back look, or do you prefer a more casual, tousled style? Do you want to add some texture to your hair, or keep it smooth and polished? These are all decisions you'll need to make before you begin cutting or styling your hair.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Once you've decided on the length and style you want, it's time to cut your hair. If you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, you may want to visit a barber or stylist. Be sure to communicate exactly what you want, and bring photos if possible.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Finally, it's time to style your hair. Depending on the look you're going for, you may need to use products like wax, pomade, or mousse to achieve the right texture and hold. Experiment with different products until you find the one that works best for your hair type and style.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your male hairstyle long on top:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different products until you find the right one for your hair type.
  • If you're not sure how to achieve a certain style, ask your barber or stylist for advice.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about male hairstyles long on top:

What face shapes look best with this hairstyle?

Male hairstyles long on top can work well for all face shapes, but they tend to be particularly flattering for those with square, diamond, or oval faces.

How often should I get my hair cut?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your style. Generally, you'll want to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Can I achieve this look with curly hair?

Absolutely! In fact, curly hair can add a lot of texture and volume to a male hairstyle long on top. Just be sure to use products that are designed for curly hair to keep your curls looking their best.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a male hairstyle long on top. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • If you have dandruff or an itchy scalp, use a medicated shampoo to treat the condition.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect male hairstyle long on top in no time. Whether you're going for a polished, professional look or a laid-back, casual vibe, this versatile style is sure to turn heads.

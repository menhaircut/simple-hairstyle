Male Haircut Fade Sides A Complete Guide

Written by William Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut, the male haircut fade sides is a great option. This style is characterized by short hair on the sides that gradually fades into longer hair on the top. It's a versatile style that can be customized to suit your individual preferences. In this post, we'll take a look at how to get this style, some tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Get a Male Haircut Fade Sides

The male haircut fade sides can be achieved in a few simple steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step is to decide how short you want the sides of your hair to be. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. Typically, the sides are buzzed or faded with clippers, starting with a longer guard and gradually decreasing to a shorter guard.

Step 2: Blend the Hair

Once the sides are cut to the desired length, it's time to blend the hair. Using clippers or scissors, gradually blend the shorter hair on the sides into the longer hair on the top. This will create a smooth and gradual transition between the two lengths.

Step 3: Style the Top

Finally, style the hair on the top of your head to your liking. This can be done with a comb or your fingers, using a product like pomade or wax to add texture and hold.

Tips for Maintaining a Male Haircut Fade Sides

Now that you've got your male haircut fade sides, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking sharp. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Get Regular Touch-Ups

To maintain the fade, you'll need to get regular touch-ups. Depending on how fast your hair grows, this could be every 2-4 weeks. Don't wait too long between touch-ups, as this can create an uneven look.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For a matte finish, try a clay or pasty pomade. For a shiny finish, try a wax or gel.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Damage

Protect your hair from damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like a blow dryer or straightener. Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and cause dryness and breakage.

FAQs About Male Haircut Fade Sides

Q: How long does it take to get a male haircut fade sides?

A: The length of time it takes to get a male haircut fade sides will depend on the length of your hair and the complexity of the style. It could take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour.

Q: Is the male haircut fade sides suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, the male haircut fade sides can be customized to suit all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: Can I style my male haircut fade sides in different ways?

A: Yes, the male haircut fade sides is a versatile style that can be styled in many different ways. You can slick it back, part it to the side, or add volume to the top.

Hair Care for Male Haircut Fade Sides

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove dirt and oil. Avoid using hot water, as this can cause dryness and damage to your hair.

Tip 2: Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair. Look for a mask that contains ingredients like argan oil or coconut oil for best results.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. A trim every 6-8 weeks is usually sufficient.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to get and maintain a stylish male haircut fade sides that suits your individual style and personality.

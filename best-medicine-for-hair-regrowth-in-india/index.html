3. Biotin

Biotin is a vitamin that is essential for healthy hair, skin, and nails. It is available in supplement form and can help improve hair thickness and growth. Biotin supplements are safe for both men and women and are available over-the-counter.

4. Ketoconazole

Ketoconazole is an antifungal medication that is used to treat dandruff and other scalp conditions. It may also help with hair growth by reducing inflammation and improving scalp health. Ketoconazole is available in shampoo form and can be used in combination with other hair regrowth medications.

Saw palmetto is a natural supplement that is commonly used to treat hair loss. It works by blocking the production of DHT, a hormone that contributes to hair loss. Saw palmetto supplements are safe for both men and women and are available over-the-counter.

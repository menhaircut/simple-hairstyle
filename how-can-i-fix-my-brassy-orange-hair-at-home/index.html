How Can I Fix My Brassy Orange Hair At Home

Written by William Sep 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling with brassy orange hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people experience this problem after dyeing their hair blonde or using lightening treatments. Fortunately, you don't need to go to the salon to fix it. In this article, we'll show you how to fix your brassy orange hair at home.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Fix Brassy Orange Hair at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Blonde Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

Brassy orange hair is a common problem that occurs when blonde hair turns yellow or orange. This can happen due to various reasons, including exposure to sunlight, chlorine in swimming pools, hard water, and using the wrong shampoo or conditioner. Fortunately, you can fix this problem at home by using toners, purple shampoos, and other hair care products.

How to Fix Brassy Orange Hair at Home:

Before you start fixing your brassy orange hair, it's essential to understand your hair type and the cause of the problem. Once you have identified the cause, you can choose the right product to fix it. Here are some of the best ways to fix brassy orange hair at home:

1. Use a Toner:

A toner is a hair product that helps neutralize brassy tones and add shine to your hair. You can use a toner at home by mixing it with a developer and applying it to your hair. There are many toners available in the market, such as Wella Color Charm T18, which is ideal for removing orange and yellow tones from blonde hair.

2. Purple Shampoo:

Purple shampoo is another effective way to fix brassy orange hair. It contains purple pigments that help neutralize yellow and orange tones in your hair. You can use purple shampoo once or twice a week to maintain your blonde hair's color and prevent it from turning brassy.

3. Hair Mask:

A hair mask is a deep conditioning treatment that nourishes your hair and restores its natural shine. You can use a hair mask once a week to maintain your blonde hair's color and prevent it from turning brassy. There are many hair masks available in the market, such as Olaplex No. 3 Hair Perfector, which is ideal for repairing damaged hair and preventing breakage.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Here's a step-by-step guide to fixing your brassy orange hair at home:

Step 1: Identify the Cause of the Problem:

Before you start fixing your hair, it's important to identify the cause of the problem. This will help you choose the right product to fix it.

Step 2: Choose the Right Product:

Once you have identified the cause of the problem, choose the right product to fix it. You can use toners, purple shampoos, and hair masks to fix your brassy orange hair.

Step 3: Apply the Product:

Follow the instructions on the product label to apply it to your hair. For toners and hair masks, you may need to mix them with a developer or water before applying. For purple shampoo, lather it on your hair and leave it for a few minutes before rinsing.

Step 4: Rinse:

Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water to remove the product.

Step 5: Dry and Style Your Hair:

Dry and style your hair as usual. You should notice a significant difference in the color and texture of your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Blonde Hair:

Here are some tips for maintaining blonde hair and preventing it from turning brassy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid exposure to sunlight and chlorine
  • Use a heat protectant before using styling tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

FAQ:

Q: Can I fix my brassy orange hair at home?

A: Yes, you can fix your brassy orange hair at home by using toners, purple shampoos, and hair masks.

Q: How often should I use purple shampoo?

A: You can use purple shampoo once or twice a week to maintain your blonde hair's color and prevent it from turning brassy.

Q: Can toners damage my hair?

A: Toners do not damage your hair if used correctly. However, you should follow the instructions on the product label and avoid overusing them.

Hair Care:

It's essential to take good care of your hair to prevent it from turning brassy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with cool water
  • Avoid using hot styling tools
  • Use a heat protectant before using styling tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

By following these tips and using the right hair care products, you can fix your brassy orange hair at home and maintain your blonde hair's color and shine.

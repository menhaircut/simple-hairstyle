Why Is My Hair Curly On The Sides

Description

Have you ever wondered why your hair is curly on the sides? This is a common question among people, especially those who have naturally curly hair. The answer to this question lies in the science of hair follicles and genetics.

Curly hair is caused by a combination of genetics and the shape of the hair follicle. The shape of the hair follicle determines the shape of the hair shaft and ultimately the texture of the hair. Straight hair has round hair shafts, while curly hair has oval or elliptical hair shafts.

When it comes to curly hair on the sides, this is usually caused by the way the hair grows out of the follicle. Hair follicles on the sides of the head tend to be more oval-shaped, which causes the hair to grow out in a curly pattern. This is why many people have straight hair on the top of their head and curly hair on the sides.

How to

If you want to enhance your curly hair on the sides, there are a few things you can do. Here are some tips:

1. Use the right products

Curly hair requires special care and attention. Use products that are designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products. These products will help define your curls and keep them looking their best.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage curly hair and cause it to lose its shape. Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons. Instead, let your hair air dry and use a diffuser to dry it gently.

3. Get regular trims

Regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This is especially important for curly hair, which tends to be more prone to breakage and damage.

Step by Step

If you want to embrace your curly hair on the sides, here is a step-by-step guide:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo. Be sure to massage your scalp well to stimulate blood flow and promote healthy hair growth.

2. Condition your hair

Apply a curl-enhancing conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while the conditioner is still in.

3. Dry your hair

Gently squeeze excess water out of your hair with a towel. Do not rub your hair, as this can cause frizz. Use a diffuser to dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down.

4. Style your hair

Apply a curl-enhancing styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or gel. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and define your curls. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry it gently.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for embracing your curly hair:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage
  • Try a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair
  • Experiment with different styling methods to find what works best for your hair

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair on the sides?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair with heat styling tools. However, this can damage your hair over time and cause it to lose its natural curl pattern.

Q: Is it possible to change the texture of my hair?

A: No, the texture of your hair is determined by genetics and cannot be changed. However, you can use styling products and techniques to enhance your natural texture.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, as this can cause breakage
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can embrace your curly hair on the sides and feel confident and beautiful every day.

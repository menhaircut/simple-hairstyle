Hairstyles For Over 50 With Glasses And Round Face

Written by Oliver Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this blog post, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for women over 50 with glasses and a round face. As we age, our hair tends to change in texture, volume, and style. It can be challenging to find the right haircut that flatters our face shape and complements our glasses. But don't worry, we have got you covered! Here are some hairstyles that will make you look amazing.

Description

Women over 50 with glasses and a round face tend to have softer facial features, and their hair may have lost some volume and texture. The right haircut can help to balance out the roundness of the face and add volume to the hair. The goal is to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and enhances your glasses, rather than hiding them.

How To

The first step in finding the right hairstyle is to consider your face shape. Round faces tend to have softer features and a wider forehead and cheekbones. The goal is to create height and volume on the top of the head to elongate the face. You can achieve this with a layered cut or a short bob.

Next, consider your glasses. If you have bold, statement glasses, you may want to opt for a simpler, more classic hairstyle. If you have more understated glasses, you can experiment with bolder haircuts.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the best hairstyles for women over 50 with glasses and a round face:

Short Bob

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Create a deep side part.
  3. Cut the hair into a short bob, with the longest pieces grazing the chin.
  4. Add layers to the top of the head to create volume.
  5. Style with a round brush and blow dryer to create lift and volume.

Shoulder-Length Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Cut the hair into shoulder-length layers.
  3. Add layers to the top of the head to create volume.
  4. Style with a curling iron to create loose waves.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for women over 50 with glasses and a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. A mid-length cut is the most flattering for round faces.
  • Add layers to the top of the head to create height and volume.
  • Avoid blunt cuts, which can make the face appear wider.
  • Consider adding bangs to frame the face.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for women over 50 with glasses and a round face:

What is the most flattering haircut for a round face?

A layered cut or a short bob with volume on the top of the head is the most flattering for a round face.

Can I wear bangs with glasses?

Yes, bangs can be a great way to frame the face and complement glasses. Consider side-swept bangs or wispy bangs.

What should I avoid when choosing a hairstyle?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, blunt cuts, and hairstyles with no volume on the top of the head.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, be sure to wash and condition regularly and use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Avoid using heat tools too frequently, and always use a heat protectant spray. Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Congratulations, you are now armed with all the information you need to find the perfect hairstyle for women over 50 with glasses and a round face. Remember, the most important thing is to find a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

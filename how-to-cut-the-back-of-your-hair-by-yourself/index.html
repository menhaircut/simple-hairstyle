How To Cut The Back Of Your Hair By Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon to get your hair cut? Are you looking for a way to save money and time? Then cutting your own hair may be the solution for you. However, cutting the back of your hair can be tricky. It's hard to see what you're doing and it's easy to make mistakes. But don't worry, with a little guidance and practice, you can learn how to cut the back of your hair by yourself.

Description

In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut the back of your hair by yourself. We'll also give you some tips on how to make the process easier and answer some frequently asked questions about cutting hair. Lastly, we'll discuss some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need a few tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror. You'll also need to decide on the length you want to cut your hair. It's best to start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will give you a better idea of how your hair falls.

2. Comb your hair straight back and use the handheld mirror to look at the back of your head.

3. Use the comb to separate a section of hair at the desired length you want to cut.

4. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and slide your fingers down to the desired length. Make sure your fingers are parallel to the floor.

5. Use the scissors to cut the hair in a straight line. Be careful not to cut your fingers or cut the hair too short.

6. Repeat steps 3-5 for the rest of the hair on the back of your head. Make sure to use the previous section of hair as a guide for the next section.

7. Once you've finished cutting the back of your hair, use the handheld mirror to check for any uneven areas or missed spots. You can also ask a friend or family member to check for you.

Tips

1. Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed. It's easier to cut more hair than to wait for it to grow back.

2. Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.

3. Cut small sections of hair at a time to ensure evenness.

4. Don't rush the process. Take your time and be patient.

FAQ

Q: Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

A: It's best to use scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. Regular scissors may not be sharp enough and can damage your hair.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Q: What if I make a mistake?

A: Don't panic! Mistakes happen. If you cut your hair too short, you can always wait for it to grow back or go to a salon to fix it.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.

- Don't wash your hair too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of natural oils.

- Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Cutting the back of your hair by yourself may seem intimidating, but with these tips and guidance, you can do it! Remember to take your time, be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if needed. With practice, you'll be a pro in no time.

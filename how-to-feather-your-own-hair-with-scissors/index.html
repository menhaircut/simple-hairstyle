How To Feather Your Own Hair With Scissors

Written by Robby Sep 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a simple way to change up your hairstyle, feathering your own hair with scissors is a great option. Feathering adds softness and movement to your hair, making it look more voluminous and textured. In this blog post, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to feather your own hair with scissors, as well as tips on how to maintain your new style.

How To Feather Your Own Hair With Scissors

Before getting started, make sure you have a pair of sharp scissors and a comb. It's also important to work on dry, clean hair to ensure accuracy. Here's how to feather your own hair with scissors:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into at least four parts: two on the sides and two in the back. Use hair clips to secure each section, leaving only the bottom layer of hair loose.

Step 2: Hold and Cut

Take a small section of hair from the bottom layer and hold it out at a 45-degree angle. Use your scissors to cut into the hair, creating small, angled cuts. Be sure to cut only a small amount at a time to avoid any mistakes.

Step 3: Repeat

Continue to cut small sections of hair at a time, working your way up to the top of your head. Be sure to keep your cuts angled and consistent throughout each section.

Step 4: Blend

Once you've finished cutting all of your hair, use your comb to blend the feathered layers together. This will help create a more seamless look.

Tips For Feathering Your Own Hair With Scissors

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when feathering your own hair with scissors:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up gradually.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go.
  • Be patient and take your time to avoid any mistakes.
  • Consider watching tutorials or seeking advice from a professional stylist if you're unsure.

FAQ: Feathering Your Own Hair With Scissors

Can I feather my own hair with regular scissors?

While it's possible to use regular scissors, it's recommended to use hair-cutting scissors for the best results.

How often should I feather my hair?

This depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style.

Will feathering damage my hair?

Feathering does involve cutting your hair, so it's important to take care of it properly. Be sure to use a quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Hair Care After Feathering

After feathering your own hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain your new style. Here are some tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style.

Now that you know how to feather your own hair with scissors, it's time to give it a try! Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to seek advice from a professional stylist if needed. With a little practice, you'll be able to create a beautiful feathered hairstyle that you can rock with confidence.

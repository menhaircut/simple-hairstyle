How To Slick Back Thick Hair Mens A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your thick hair getting in the way of your sleek hairstyle? Slicking back your hair is a great way to achieve a polished and sophisticated look. In this guide, we’ll show you how to slick back thick hair mens in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Slick Back Thick Hair Mens
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Back Thick Hair Mens
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Slick Back Thick Hair Mens

Description

Slicking back thick hair mens involves using a hair product such as pomade, gel, or wax to sleek back the hair. This hairstyle works well for men with thick hair who want to achieve a polished and professional look. With the right technique and products, you can achieve a stylish slicked-back hairstyle that will last all day.

How to Slick Back Thick Hair Mens

The first step in slicking back your hair is choosing the right hair product. Pomade or wax works best for thick hair as they provide a strong hold without making your hair look greasy. Gel can also be used but may not work well for thick hair as it can make your hair look crunchy.

Once you’ve chosen the right product, follow these steps:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Take a small amount of your chosen product and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  3. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, starting from the back and working your way forward.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to slick back your hair, starting from the hairline and working your way back.
  5. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways with your fingers or a comb.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to set the style in place.

Tips for Slicking Back Thick Hair Mens

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Don’t use too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Use a comb with wide teeth to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Try blow-drying your hair before applying the product to make it easier to slick back.
  • Don’t be afraid to add a bit of volume to the front of your hair for a more dynamic look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking back thick hair mens:

Can I slick back my hair if it’s thin?

Yes, you can slick back thin hair as well. However, you may need to use less product and be more gentle when combing your hair to avoid damaging it.

Can I slick back my hair if it’s curly?

Yes, you can slick back curly hair as well. However, you may need to use more product and a stronger hold to keep your curls in place.

How often should I wash my hair when slicking it back?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days when slicking it back. This will prevent product buildup and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care for Slick Back Thick Hair Mens

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair when slicking it back:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Don’t forget to wash your hair regularly to prevent product buildup and keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily slick back your thick hair mens and achieve a stylish and professional look. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the one that works best for you. Happy styling!

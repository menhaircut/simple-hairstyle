What Hairstyles Do Guys Like The Most

Written by Luffy Jun 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, women often wonder what men find attractive. While it's true that everyone has their own preferences, there are certain styles that tend to be popular among men. In this article, we'll explore some of the hairstyles that guys like the most and provide tips on how to achieve them.

The Long and Wavy Look

Long, wavy hair is a classic look that never goes out of style. Men tend to find this style feminine and alluring, as it gives off a carefree and natural vibe. To achieve this look, start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing mousse. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume, then use a curling iron to create loose waves. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your waves in place.

The Classic Bob

The bob is a timeless hairstyle that men love. It's a low-maintenance style that's easy to style and maintain. The classic bob is typically chin-length and straight, but variations like the lob (long bob) and A-line bob (shorter in the back, longer in the front) are also popular. To achieve this look, start by shampooing and conditioning your hair. Blow-dry your hair straight, then use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or flyaways. Finish with a shine serum to give your hair a glossy finish.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a bold and daring hairstyle that men find attractive. It's a short, cropped style that highlights your facial features and gives you a confident, edgy look. To achieve this look, start by cutting your hair short and adding layers to create texture. Use a pomade or wax to style your hair, then finish with a hairspray to keep it in place.

The Half-Up, Half-Down

The half-up, half-down hairstyle is a romantic and feminine look that men love. It's a versatile style that can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion. To achieve this look, start by curling your hair with a curling iron. Section off the top half of your hair and secure it with a clip or hair tie. Leave the bottom half down and tousle it with your fingers to create loose waves. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your hair in place.

The High Ponytail

The high ponytail is a simple and easy hairstyle that men find attractive. It's a classic look that's perfect for busy days or when you're in a rush. To achieve this look, start by brushing your hair back and securing it with a hair tie at the crown of your head. Use a comb to smooth out any bumps or flyaways, then finish with a hairspray to keep your ponytail in place.

The Messy Bun

The messy bun is a casual and effortless hairstyle that men love. It's a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still looking stylish. To achieve this look, start by pulling your hair back into a high ponytail. Twist your hair into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy, tousled look. Finish with a hairspray to keep your bun in place.

The Side-Swept Bangs

The side-swept bangs are a flirty and feminine hairstyle that men find attractive. They add a touch of glamour to any look and can be worn with any hair length or texture. To achieve this look, start by sectioning off the front of your hair. Use a flat iron to create a slight bend in your bangs, then sweep them to one side. Finish with a hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

The Layered Cut

The layered cut is a versatile and modern hairstyle that men find attractive. It adds texture and movement to your hair and can be styled in a variety of ways. To achieve this look, start by getting a haircut with layers. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or straighten your hair for a sleeker look. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

The Beachy Waves

The beachy waves are a relaxed and effortless hairstyle that men love. They give off a carefree, summery vibe and can be worn with any hair length or texture. To achieve this look, start by washing your hair and applying a texturizing spray. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create waves, then blow-dry your hair with a diffuser. Finish with a hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What hairstyles do guys find most attractive?

Guys tend to find long, wavy hair, the classic bob, the pixie cut, and the half-up, half-down hairstyle attractive.

2. How do I choose a hairstyle that will suit me?

Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle. It's also a good idea to consult with a hairstylist for their expert opinion.

3. How often should I wash my hair?

It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.

4. How can I protect my hair from damage?

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, use a heat protectant spray before styling, and deep condition your hair regularly.

5. How can I make my hair look thicker?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume, and consider getting layers cut into your hair.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyles that guys find attractive, ranging from long and wavy to short and edgy. The key is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable. By following the tips and techniques provided in this article, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle that will leave guys swooning.

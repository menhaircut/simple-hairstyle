How Do I Know If A Hair Color Will Suit Me

Written by Oliver Jul 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering changing your hair color but not sure if it will suit you? With so many different hair colors and styles available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one for you. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide to help you determine if a hair color will suit you.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How To

Before you decide to dye your hair, it's important to consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. These factors will help you determine which hair color will suit you best. Here are some tips to help you:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone

Knowing your skin tone is essential when choosing a hair color that suits you. There are three main skin tone categories: warm, cool, and neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell if they're blue or green, you have a neutral skin tone.

2. Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining which hair color will suit you best. If you have blue or green eyes, warm hair tones such as golden blonde, copper, and auburn will complement your eyes. If you have brown eyes, cool tones such as ash brown or platinum blonde will look great.

3. Look at Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color will also play a part in choosing the right hair color. If you have warm undertones in your hair, such as red or orange, warm hair colors such as golden blonde or copper will suit you best. If you have cool undertones, such as blue or violet, cool hair tones such as ash blonde or platinum blonde will look great.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step process to help you determine which hair color will suit you:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on your wrist to determine your skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Think about your eye color and which hair colors will complement it best.

Step 3: Look at Your Natural Hair Color

Consider your natural hair color and which hair colors will complement it best.

Step 4: Choose Your Hair Color

Based on your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color, choose a hair color that complements all three.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, choose a low-maintenance hair color.
  • Try on wigs or use a virtual hair color app to see what the hair color will look like on you before dyeing your hair.
  • Consult with a professional hair colorist to help you choose the right hair color for you.

FAQ

What hair colors suit warm skin tones?

Warm hair colors such as golden blonde, copper, and auburn suit warm skin tones.

What hair colors suit cool skin tones?

Cool hair colors such as ash blonde or platinum blonde suit cool skin tones.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hair color you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the natural oils from your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

By following these tips and using our step-by-step guide, you can determine which hair color will suit you and keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Oval Face Shape Hairstyles For Indian Men Without Beard

If you're an Indian man with an oval face shape and prefer a clean-shaven look, then you might be wondering what are the best hairstyles that suit your face shape. In this article, we'll explore some of the most popular and trendy hairstyles for oval face shape male Indians without a beard.

  2. How To

Description

Oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile and balanced face shape for men. This means that oval-faced men can pull off almost any hairstyle without much effort. However, it's important to keep in mind that different hairstyles can enhance or detract from your facial features.

If you're an Indian man with an oval face shape and prefer a clean-shaven look, then you should consider hairstyles that accentuate your jawline and forehead while maintaining a balanced look. Some of the best hairstyles for oval face shape male Indians without a beard include the slicked-back undercut, side part, buzz cut, and textured crop.

How To

The key to pulling off any hairstyle is to find the right balance between your hair type, facial features, and personal style. Here are some tips on how to choose the best hairstyle for your oval face shape:

  • Consider your hair type: Different hairstyles work better on different hair types. For example, a slicked-back undercut looks great on thick and straight hair, while a textured crop suits curly and wavy hair.
  • Accentuate your features: Choose a hairstyle that enhances your facial features. For example, a side part can highlight your cheekbones and jawline, while a buzz cut can showcase your forehead.
  • Personal style: Your hairstyle should reflect your personal style and fashion sense. Choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Step By Step

Here's how to achieve some of the most popular hairstyles for oval face shape male Indians without a beard:

Slicked-back undercut

  1. Apply pomade or styling cream to damp hair.
  2. Comb your hair back and away from your forehead.
  3. Use a hairdryer to set your hair in place.
  4. Trim the sides and back with clippers or scissors.

Side part

  1. Apply pomade or styling cream to damp hair.
  2. Create a deep side part using a comb.
  3. Comb the longer side of your hair over the top of your head.
  4. Trim the sides and back with clippers or scissors.

Buzz cut

  1. Use clippers with a guard to achieve your desired length.
  2. Trim the sides and back with a shorter guard.
  3. Blend the hair on top with scissors.

Textured crop

  1. Apply styling cream or wax to damp hair.
  2. Use your fingers to create texture and volume on top.
  3. Trim the sides and back with clippers or scissors.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for oval face shape male Indians without a beard:

  • Consult with a professional hairstylist: A hairstylist can help you find the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.
  • Experiment with different styles: Don't be afraid to try different hairstyles until you find the one that suits you best.
  • Maintain your hair: Regular shampooing, conditioning, and trimming can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about oval face shape hairstyles for male Indians without a beard:

What is the best hairstyle for oval face shape male Indians without a beard?

The best hairstyle for oval face shape male Indians without a beard depends on your hair type, facial features, and personal style. Some popular options include the slicked-back undercut, side part, buzz cut, and textured crop.

Can oval-faced men pull off any hairstyle?

Oval-faced men have the most versatile face shape and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that enhances your facial features and personal style.

Do I need to use styling products to achieve these hairstyles?

Styling products such as pomade, wax, and cream can help you achieve and maintain these hairstyles. However, you can also achieve some of these styles without styling products by using a comb or your fingers.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy and stylish hair requires regular hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly: Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly: Regular trimming can prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools such as flat irons and hairdryers can damage your hair. Use them sparingly and always use a heat protectant.
  • Eat a balanced diet: A diet rich in vitamins and minerals can promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can showcase your oval face shape and personal style with confidence.

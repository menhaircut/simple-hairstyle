How To Choose The Right Hair Colour For Indian Skin Tone

Written by Luffy Jun 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hair colour for Indian skin tone can be a daunting task, especially with the plethora of hair colours available in the market. However, with a little bit of research and understanding of your skin tone, you can easily choose the perfect hair colour that will enhance your overall look. In this article, we will guide you on how to choose the right hair colour for Indian skin tone.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Indian skin tone varies from light to dark, and it is important to choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone. While choosing the right hair colour, you should consider factors such as your skin undertone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. Choosing the wrong hair colour can make you look dull and washed out. Hence, it is important to do your research and consult with a hair expert before making a decision.

How to

The first step in choosing the right hair colour for Indian skin tone is to determine your skin undertone. Indian skin tones can have warm, cool, or neutral undertones. Warm undertones have more yellow or golden hues, while cool undertones have more pink or blue hues. Neutral undertones have a mix of warm and cool undertones. You can determine your skin undertone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones; if they appear green, you have warm undertones; if they appear both blue and green, you have neutral undertones.

Step by Step

Once you have determined your skin undertone, you can choose a hair colour that complements it. For warm undertones, choose hair colours that have golden or caramel hues, such as honey blonde or copper. For cool undertones, choose hair colours that have ashy or platinum hues, such as ash blonde or platinum blonde. For neutral undertones, choose hair colours that have a mix of warm and cool hues, such as chocolate brown or auburn.

It is also important to consider your eye colour when choosing the right hair colour. For brown eyes, warm hair colours such as golden blonde or copper can enhance the natural beauty of your eyes. For blue or green eyes, cool hair colours such as platinum blonde or ash brown can make your eyes stand out.

Tips

Before you commit to a hair colour, do a strand test to ensure that the colour suits your skin tone. You can also try temporary hair colour sprays or wigs to see how the colour looks on you. If you have dark hair and want to go lighter, it is recommended to do it gradually to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it is recommended to consult with a hair expert before doing so. Follow the instructions on the hair dye box carefully and do a strand test before applying the dye to your entire head.

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if you have sensitive skin, but it is recommended to do a patch test before applying the dye to your entire head. Use a hair dye that is free from ammonia and other harsh chemicals.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it is important to take good care of it to maintain the colour and prevent damage. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for coloured hair. Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Choosing the right hair colour for Indian skin tone can be a fun and exciting experience. With these tips and guidelines, you can easily choose the perfect hair colour that will enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and beautiful.

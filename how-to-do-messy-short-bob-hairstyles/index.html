How To Do Messy Short Bob Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 08, 2023 · 3 min read

Description

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will add some edge to your look? Look no further than the messy short bob! This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, yet stylish look. In this blog post, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect messy short bob hairstyle.

How To

To achieve the perfect messy short bob hairstyle, you will need a few things. First, you will need a good quality hairbrush or comb, a hairdryer, and some styling products such as texturizing spray, dry shampoo, and hairspray. You will also need a set of hair scissors if you plan on cutting your own hair.

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it is slightly damp. Apply some texturizing spray to add some volume and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume. Use your fingers to tousle your hair as you dry it to create a messy look.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

If you plan on cutting your own hair, use a pair of hair scissors to cut your hair to the desired length. Start by cutting the hair at the back of your head, working your way to the sides and front.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry and cut to the desired length, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add some texture and volume to your hair. Use your fingers to mess up your hair and create a messy look. Finish off with some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

- If you are not comfortable cutting your own hair, visit a professional stylist to have it done. - Play around with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type. - Use a hair straightener or curling iron to add some waves or curls to your hair for a different look.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have a messy short bob? A: It depends on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. Generally, you can go 2-3 days between washes. Q: Can I still style my hair if I have fine hair? A: Yes! Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add some volume to your hair before styling.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, make sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid washing your hair too often as this can strip your hair of its natural oils. Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your hair and try to limit the use of these tools as much as possible to avoid damaging your hair. Finally, visit a professional stylist every few months to get a trim and keep your hair looking its best.

Conclusion

The messy short bob is a great hairstyle for those who want a low-maintenance, yet stylish look. By following these simple steps, you can achieve the perfect messy short bob hairstyle in no time. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

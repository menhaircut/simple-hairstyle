Do I Have A Square Face Find Out Here

Are you wondering if you have a square face shape? This is a common concern for many people who want to choose the right hairstyle or makeup that flatters their face shape. In this article, we will discuss how to determine if you have a square face, what hairstyles and makeup suit this face shape, and tips for taking care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine If You Have a Square Face
  • Hairstyles for Square Faces
  • Makeup for Square Faces
  • Tips for Taking Care of Your Hair
  • FAQs

How to Determine If You Have a Square Face

A square face shape is defined by a wide forehead, strong jawline, and a square chin. To determine if you have a square face, follow these steps:

  1. Measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. They should all be around the same width.
  2. Look at your chin. If it is square and angular, you likely have a square face.
  3. Check if your face has a flat or straight hairline.
  4. Look for sharp angles and straight lines in your facial features.

If most of these characteristics match your face, then you likely have a square face shape.

Hairstyles for Square Faces

When it comes to hairstyles for square faces, the goal is to soften the jawline and create some roundness in the face. Here are some hairstyles to consider:

  • Long layers: This adds movement and texture to the hair, softening the jawline and creating a more feminine look.
  • Side-swept bangs: These draw attention away from the jawline and create a more oval shape.
  • Soft waves: Waves add volume and create a more rounded shape in the face.
  • Bob with layers: A layered bob can add volume to the top and sides of the head, balancing out the strong jawline.

Makeup for Square Faces

Makeup can also help to create a more rounded and feminine look for square faces. Here are some makeup tips:

  • Contouring: Use a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to contour the jawline and create a softer look.
  • Blush: Apply blush to the apples of the cheeks to add some roundness to the face.
  • Soft eyeshadow: Choose eyeshadow shades that have a soft, rounded edge to create a more feminine look.
  • Fuller lips: Use a lip liner to create a fuller, more rounded shape for the lips.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Hair

Regardless of your face shape, taking care of your hair is important for maintaining healthy, beautiful locks. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray or serum.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and lead to dryness.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

FAQs

1. Can I still wear a center part with a square face?

Yes, you can wear a center part with a square face. However, you may want to add some volume to the top of your head to balance out the strong jawline.

2. What kind of earrings suit square faces?

Earrings that are round or curved can help to soften the angles of a square face. Hoops, teardrops, and chandelier earrings are good choices.

3. Can I still wear a pixie cut with a square face?

A pixie cut can work with a square face, but it is important to add some volume to the top and sides of the head to balance out the jawline.

In conclusion, if you have a square face, don't worry! There are plenty of hairstyles and makeup techniques that can help to flatter your face shape. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can look and feel your best every day.

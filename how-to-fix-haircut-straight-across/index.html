How To Fix A Haircut Straight Across

Written by Bardi Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've recently had a haircut that was done straight across, you may be feeling frustrated with the look. Luckily, there are several ways to fix this issue and achieve a more flattering style. In this article, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to fix a haircut straight across, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to ensure your new style looks great.

  • How to Fix a Haircut Straight Across
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Fixing a Haircut Straight Across
  • FAQs

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are the steps you can take to fix a haircut that is too straight across:

1. Trim the Ends

The first step is to trim the ends of your hair. Use scissors or clippers to cut about half an inch off the ends. This will help to create some texture and movement in your hair, which will make it look less blunt.

2. Add Layers

Next, you'll want to add some layers to your hair. You can do this by cutting your hair at an angle, starting at the bottom and working your way up. This will help to create some volume and movement in your hair.

3. Style Your Hair

Once you've trimmed the ends and added some layers, you can style your hair to create the look you want. You can use a curling iron or straightener to create waves or curls, or you can use products like mousse or hairspray to add volume and hold.

Tips for Fixing a Haircut Straight Across

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when fixing a haircut that is too straight across:

1. Work with a Professional

If you're not comfortable cutting your own hair or fixing a bad haircut, it's always best to work with a professional stylist. They can help you to achieve the look you want and ensure that your hair looks great.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. Look for products that are designed to add volume, texture, or hold, depending on the look you want to achieve.

3. Experiment with Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and looks until you find the one that works best for you. Try out different hairstyles and products to see what looks best with your hair type and face shape.

FAQs

Q: Can I fix a haircut straight across on my own?

A: Yes, you can fix a bad haircut on your own, but it's always best to work with a professional stylist if you're not comfortable doing it yourself.

Q: How long will it take to fix a haircut straight across?

A: The length of time it takes to fix a bad haircut will depend on the severity of the cut and the steps you need to take to fix it. It could take anywhere from a few minutes to a few hours.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your hair looking great after fixing a bad haircut, here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Use Quality Products

Using quality products can help to keep your hair looking and feeling healthy. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and needs.

2. Limit Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Limit your use of heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners, and always use a heat protectant product.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

By following these tips and steps, you can fix a haircut that is too straight across and achieve a more flattering style. Remember to work with a professional stylist if you're not comfortable doing it yourself, and always use quality hair care products to keep your hair looking and feeling its best.

How To Get Back Old Hair After Straightening A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you've recently straightened your hair, you might be missing your old curls or waves. While straight hair can be beautiful, it's also important to embrace your natural texture. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to get back old hair after straightening. From how to, step by step, tips, FAQ, and hair care, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Back Old Hair After Straightening
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Getting Your Old Hair Back
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Natural Texture

How to Get Back Old Hair After Straightening

The first step in getting your old hair back is to stop straightening it. If you continue to use heat on your hair, it will be difficult to return to your natural texture. You should also avoid using any products that contain alcohol or silicone, as these can dry out your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

Next, you'll want to start deep conditioning your hair on a regular basis. This will help to restore moisture and nourishment to your hair, which is essential for healthy curls or waves. You can use a deep conditioning treatment once a week or more often if your hair is particularly dry.

Another important step is to start using a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and damage to your hair. You should also consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to help define your natural texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get back your old hair:

  1. Stop using heat on your hair
  2. Avoid products that contain alcohol or silicone
  3. Deep condition your hair once a week
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  5. Use a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to define your natural texture

Tips for Getting Your Old Hair Back

Here are some additional tips to help you get your old hair back:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get your old hair back after straightening?

The time it takes to get your old hair back will vary depending on the condition of your hair and how often you straightened it. It can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months to fully restore your natural texture.

Can you speed up the process of getting your old hair back?

While you can't speed up the process of getting your old hair back, you can take steps to promote healthy hair growth and restore moisture to your hair. This includes deep conditioning, using a wide-tooth comb, and avoiding heat and damaging products.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Natural Texture

Once you've successfully restored your natural texture, it's important to maintain it. Here are some hair care tips to help you do just that:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can dry it out
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using heat on your hair as much as possible
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

With these tips and tricks, you can get back your old hair after straightening and maintain your natural texture for years to come.

How To Sleep On Curly Hair Without Messing It Up

Written by Petter Sep 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Sleeping on curly hair can be a nightmare for those who want to maintain their curls without damaging them. Waking up with tangled, frizzy hair is not the best way to start your day. But fear not, we have compiled a step-by-step guide on how to sleep on curly hair without messing it up.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Sleep on Curly Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Sleeping on Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Sleep on Curly Hair

The first step to sleeping on curly hair without messing it up is to understand your hair type. Curly hair needs special care, and that includes sleeping on the right pillowcase and in the right position.

Pillowcase

Choose a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz. Cotton pillowcases can absorb moisture from your hair, making it dry and frizzy. Silk or satin pillowcases are also gentle on your skin and prevent wrinkles.

Sleeping Position

Sleeping on your back is the best position for curly hair as it prevents tangling and flattening of the curls. Avoid sleeping on your stomach or side as it can cause friction and lead to frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to sleep on curly hair without messing it up:

  1. Wash your hair before going to bed.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or a styling product to your hair.
  3. Detangle your hair gently using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  4. Pineapple your hair by gathering it on top of your head and securing it with a scrunchie or a silk scarf.
  5. Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf or bonnet to protect it from friction and keep it in place.
  6. Go to bed and sleep on your back.
  7. When you wake up, remove the scarf or bonnet and gently shake out your hair.
  8. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to style your curls as desired. Do not use a brush as it can cause frizz.

Tips for Sleeping on Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you sleep on curly hair without messing it up:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase and scarf or bonnet.
  • Try sleeping on a silk or satin sheet to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much as it can cause frizz.

FAQs

Q. How often should I wash my curly hair?

A. It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people with curly hair can go a week without washing, while others may need to wash their hair every other day. Experiment to find out what works best for you.

Q. Can I sleep on wet hair?

A. It is not recommended to sleep on wet hair as it can cause breakage and damage. It is best to let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair before going to bed.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair requires a little extra effort, but the results are worth it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, bouncy curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can dry out your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or a styling product to your hair while it is still wet.
  • Do not rub your hair vigorously with a towel to dry it. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out the excess water.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Follow these tips and our step-by-step guide on how to sleep on curly hair without messing it up, and you will wake up with beautiful, healthy curls every morning.

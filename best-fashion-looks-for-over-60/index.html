Best Fashion Looks For Over 60 How To Look Stylish And Elegant

Written by Aprilia Oct 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on the best fashion looks for over 60. If you’re looking for ways to update your wardrobe and style, then you’ve come to the right place. We’ve put together some top tips and advice on how to look stylish and elegant no matter what your age. From clothing to accessories and hair care, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Clothing
  • Accessories
  • Hairstyles
  • Tips for Looking Your Best
  • FAQs

Clothing

When it comes to fashion for over 60, comfort is key. Opt for comfortable and breathable fabrics such as cotton, linen, and silk. Loose-fitting clothing such as tunics, maxi dresses, and wide-leg pants are also great options.

For more formal occasions, consider tailored blazers, pencil skirts, and classic dresses. Don’t be afraid to experiment with colors and prints, but stick to classic styles that will stand the test of time.

Accessorize with statement jewelry such as chunky necklaces and bracelets, and add a pop of color with a bright scarf or handbag.

Accessories

Accessories are a great way to add some personality and style to your outfit. Invest in a good quality handbag that can hold all your essentials, and opt for classic styles such as a leather tote or crossbody bag.

Scarves are another versatile accessory that can be worn in a variety of ways. Tie one around your neck, wear it as a headscarf, or use it as a belt to add some color and interest to your outfit.

When it comes to shoes, comfort is once again key. Opt for comfortable and supportive footwear such as loafers, sneakers, and sandals with a low heel. Avoid high heels and shoes with a narrow toe box that can be uncomfortable and cause foot problems.

Hairstyles

A great hairstyle can make all the difference when it comes to looking and feeling your best. For over 60s, shorter hairstyles are often a better option as they are easier to manage and can be more flattering.

Consider a bob or pixie cut that frames your face and highlights your best features. If you prefer longer hair, opt for a layered cut that adds texture and movement.

When it comes to hair color, consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth. Avoid drastic changes such as going from dark to blonde, as this can be damaging to your hair and skin.

Tips for Looking Your Best

Here are some additional tips for looking your best over 60:

  • Stay active and maintain a healthy lifestyle. Exercise regularly and eat a balanced diet to keep your skin and body in good condition.
  • Invest in good quality skincare products to keep your skin looking youthful and radiant.
  • Get regular check-ups with your doctor and dentist to maintain your overall health and wellbeing.
  • Stay up-to-date with fashion trends, but don’t be afraid to stick to classic styles that suit your body type and personal style.

FAQs

Q: What colors are best for over 60s?

A: Bright colors such as red, pink, and orange can be flattering on over 60s, but it’s also important to stick to classic neutrals such as black, navy, and beige.

Q: Can over 60s wear skinny jeans?

A: Yes, over 60s can wear skinny jeans, but it’s important to choose a style that is comfortable and flattering. Look for stretchy denim and a high-waisted style that will smooth and flatter your figure.

Q: What kind of jewelry is best for over 60s?

A: Statement jewelry such as chunky necklaces and bracelets can be a great way to add some personality and style to your outfit. Opt for classic styles such as pearls, diamonds, and gold that will stand the test of time.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It’s recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Conclusion

We hope you’ve found our guide on the best fashion looks for over 60 helpful. Remember, fashion is all about expressing your personal style and feeling confident and comfortable in your own skin. With these tips and advice, you can look stylish and elegant no matter what your age.

What Kind Of Curly Hair Do I Have

Written by Bardi Oct 24, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you may have wondered what kind of curls you have. Knowing your curl type can help you choose the right products and styling techniques for your hair. In this article, we'll help you determine what kind of curly hair you have and provide tips for caring for your curls.

Description

Curly hair comes in many different types, ranging from loose waves to tight coils. The most commonly used system for categorizing curl types is the Andre Walker Hair Typing System, which divides curls into four categories based on the tightness of the curl:

  • Type 1: Straight hair
  • Type 2: Wavy hair
  • Type 3: Curly hair
  • Type 4: Coily hair

Within each category, there are subcategories that further define the curl type. For example, Type 3 hair can range from loose, bouncy curls to tight corkscrew curls.

How to Determine Your Curl Type

The best way to determine your curl type is to examine your hair when it's in its natural state. Here's what to look for:

  • Type 2: Your hair has an S-shaped wave pattern and is generally easy to manage.
  • Type 3: Your hair has a definite curl pattern, with curls ranging from loose to tight. Your hair may frizz easily and require more moisture.
  • Type 4: Your hair has a tight coil pattern and is often very fragile. It requires a lot of moisture and careful handling to avoid breakage.

Once you've determined your curl type, you can use this information to choose the right products and styling techniques for your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some steps you can follow to care for your curly hair:

  1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  2. Apply a moisturizing conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it's still wet.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls.
  5. Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  6. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.
  7. Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  8. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with UV protection.
  9. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  10. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can dry out your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Try using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • If you have Type 4 hair, consider using protective styles like braids or twists to give your hair a break from styling.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hair:

What causes curly hair?

Curly hair is caused by the shape of the hair follicle. People with curly hair have follicles that are more oval-shaped, which causes the hair to grow in a spiral pattern.

How can I make my curls more defined?

You can make your curls more defined by using a curl-defining product like a curl cream or gel. Apply the product to wet hair and scrunch your curls with your hands to encourage definition.

What's the best way to dry curly hair?

The best way to dry curly hair is to air-dry it or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. Avoid using a regular towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz.

Hair Care

Caring for curly hair can be challenging, but with the right products and techniques, you can keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to choose products that are specifically designed for your curl type, and experiment with different styling techniques until you find what works best for you.

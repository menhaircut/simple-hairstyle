What Type Of Hair Color Suits Me Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you planning to change your hair color but unsure of what type of hair color suits you? Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially when you're not familiar with the different options available. But don't worry, we've got you covered!

Description

Your hair color is an essential aspect of your overall look. It can make or break your appearance, so it's crucial to choose the right one that flatters your skin tone, eye color, and features. Hair color can also boost your confidence and make you feel more beautiful and attractive. However, choosing the right hair color can be tricky, especially if you're not familiar with the options available. That's why we've created this ultimate guide to help you find the perfect hair color that suits you.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

Choosing the right hair color requires careful consideration of several factors, including your skin tone, eye color, natural hair color, and personal preferences. Here's how to choose the right hair color:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a crucial role in determining the right hair color for you. There are three main skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. Warm skin tones have golden or yellow undertones, while cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of warm and cool undertones. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If you can't tell, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also influence the right hair color for you. For instance, if you have blue or green eyes, warm hair colors like gold, copper, or honey can make your eyes pop. If you have brown or hazel eyes, cool hair colors like ash or platinum can complement your eyes.

Step 3: Look at Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also help you choose the right hair color. If you have dark hair, going too light can make you look washed out. If you have light hair, going too dark can make you look older. It's best to stay within two shades of your natural hair color for a more natural look.

Step 4: Consider Your Personal Preferences

Ultimately, the right hair color is the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Consider your personal preferences, such as your lifestyle, fashion sense, and personality. Do you prefer a bold, edgy look or a soft, natural look? Do you want a low-maintenance or high-maintenance hair color?

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • Consider your skin tone, eye color, natural hair color, and personal preferences.
  • Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your features.
  • Stay within two shades of your natural hair color for a more natural look.
  • Consider your lifestyle, fashion sense, and personality when choosing a hair color.
  • Experiment with temporary hair color or highlights before committing to a permanent hair color.

FAQ

What hair color suits warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colors, such as golden blonde, caramel, honey, or copper.

What hair color suits cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colors, such as ash blonde, platinum, or burgundy.

What hair color suits neutral skin tones?

Neutral skin tones can pull off both warm and cool hair colors. You can experiment with different shades to find the one that suits you best.

Hair Care Tips

After coloring your hair, it's essential to take good care of it to maintain its vibrancy and health. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading and damage.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip the color and natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools as they can damage the hair and fade the color.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Now that you know what type of hair color suits you, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and transform your look. Remember to choose a hair color that makes you feel confident and beautiful, and take good care of it to maintain its vibrancy and health.

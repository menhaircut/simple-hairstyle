Does The Hair Grow Back Understanding Hair Growth And Relaxing

Written by Daniel Sep 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to hair, one of the most common questions is, "Does the hair grow back?" This question is especially relevant to those who have undergone hair relaxing. Hair relaxing is a process that involves using chemicals to straighten curly or kinky hair. While it can give you the desired look, it can also cause damage to your hair. In this article, we'll explore how hair grows, the effects of relaxing on hair growth, and tips on how to care for your hair after relaxing.

  • Effects of Relaxing on Hair Growth

How Hair Grows

Before we dive into the effects of relaxing on hair growth, it's important to understand how hair grows. Hair growth is a cyclical process that occurs in three phases:

  • Anagen: This is the growth phase, where hair actively grows from the follicle. This phase can last anywhere from 2-7 years, depending on genetics and other factors.
  • Catagen: This is the transitional phase, where hair stops growing and the follicle shrinks. This phase lasts for about 2-3 weeks.
  • Telogen: This is the resting phase, where the hair follicle remains inactive for about 3 months. After this phase, the hair falls out and the cycle starts again.

On average, hair grows about half an inch per month. However, this can vary depending on factors such as age, genetics, and health.

Effects of Relaxing on Hair Growth

Relaxing can have a significant impact on hair growth. When the chemicals in the relaxer break down the protein bonds in the hair, it can cause the hair to become weak and brittle. This can lead to breakage and hair loss.

If you experience hair loss after relaxing, it's important to note that it may not be permanent. In many cases, the hair will grow back once it's properly cared for. However, it may take some time for the hair to grow back to its original length and thickness.

It's also worth noting that relaxing can affect hair growth differently depending on your hair type. Those with thinner hair may experience more hair loss and slower regrowth, while those with thicker hair may have an easier time regrowing their hair.

How to Care for Your Hair After Relaxing

If you've recently relaxed your hair and are experiencing hair loss or slow regrowth, there are steps you can take to care for your hair and encourage growth:

  1. Moisturize: Relaxed hair is often dry and prone to breakage. To keep your hair healthy, it's important to moisturize regularly. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.
  2. Avoid heat: Heat can further damage relaxed hair, so it's important to avoid using heat-styling tools as much as possible. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.
  3. Trim regularly: Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can lead to slower hair growth. Aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.
  4. Eat a balanced diet: Proper nutrition is important for hair growth. Make sure your diet includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.

FAQs

Q: Will my hair grow back after relaxing?

A: In most cases, yes. However, it may take some time for the hair to grow back to its original length and thickness.

Q: How long does it take for hair to grow back after relaxing?

A: This can vary depending on factors such as hair type, genetics, and how well you care for your hair. Generally, it can take several months to a year for hair to grow back fully.

Q: Can I relax my hair if I have thinning hair?

A: It's generally not recommended to relax your hair if you have thinning hair, as relaxing can cause further damage and hair loss.

Q: Can I color my hair after relaxing?

A: It's generally recommended to wait at least two weeks after relaxing before coloring your hair. This will give your hair time to recover and minimize the risk of further damage.

In conclusion, the answer to the question "does the hair grow back?" is generally yes, even after relaxing. However, it's important to take proper care of your hair to encourage healthy growth. If you're experiencing hair loss or slow regrowth, try incorporating some of the tips mentioned above into your hair care routine.

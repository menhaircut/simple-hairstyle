Medium Length Men s Hair Short Sides A Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to change up your hairstyle? Consider the trendy medium length men's hair with short sides. This style can be versatile and low maintenance. In this guide, we'll go over how to achieve this look, tips, and hair care.

Description

The medium length men's hair with short sides is a popular style among men. The hair on top is left longer, while the sides and back are trimmed shorter. This style can be customized to fit your face shape and hair type. It's a great option for those looking for a low maintenance yet stylish look.

How To

To achieve the medium length men's hair with short sides, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consult with a Barber or Stylist

Before making any hair changes, it's essential to consult with a barber or stylist to ensure that the style fits your face shape and hair type. They can also recommend products to maintain the style.

Step 2: Grow Out Your Hair

Grow out the hair on top to a medium length, typically around 2-3 inches. The sides and back should be trimmed shorter, around 1-2 inches.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Style your hair with a styling product of your choice, such as a pomade or wax. Use your fingers or a comb to create your desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when rocking medium length men's hair with short sides:

  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep your hair in place throughout the day.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the short sides and back.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specific to your hair type to keep it healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length men's hair with short sides:

Can I achieve this look with curly hair?

Absolutely! Curly hair can add texture and volume to this style.

How often should I style my hair?

It depends on your hair type and the product you use. Some products can last all day, while others may need to be reapplied throughout the day.

Will this style work with a receding hairline?

This style can be customized to fit any hairline, consult with a barber or stylist to find the best option for you.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any hairstyle. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specific to your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair frequently
  • Get regular trims to maintain healthy hair
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental pollutants

In conclusion, the medium length men's hair with short sides is a stylish and low maintenance option for men. It can be customized to fit any face shape and hair type. Follow these tips and hair care to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

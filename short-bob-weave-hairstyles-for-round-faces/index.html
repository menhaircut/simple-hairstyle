Short Bob Weave Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Christine Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect hairstyle that will complement your round face? Look no further than the short bob weave hairstyle. This versatile and trendy hairstyle is perfect for those with round faces, and it is easy to maintain as well. In this article, we will show you how to achieve this look and provide tips for hair care.

Description

A short bob weave hairstyle is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that is perfect for those with round faces. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous. This hairstyle is achieved by attaching hair extensions to your natural hair, giving you the length and fullness you desire. The length of the weave can vary, from chin-length to shoulder-length, depending on your preference.

How to Achieve the Look

Before you can achieve the short bob weave hairstyle, you will need to gather the necessary materials, including hair extensions, a weaving needle, thread, and scissors. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Once your hair is clean and dry, comb it out to remove any tangles. If you have short hair, you may need to braid it to make it easier to attach the extensions.

Step 2: Attach the Extensions

Using a weaving needle and thread, attach the hair extensions to your natural hair. Start at the back of your head and work your way up to the top. Be sure to sew the extensions tightly to your hair to prevent them from slipping or falling out.

Step 3: Cut and Style

Once the extensions are attached, use scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. You can then style your hair as desired, whether that be straight, curly, or wavy.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Bob Weave Hairstyle

Now that you have achieved the short bob weave hairstyle, it is important to take proper care of it to ensure that it lasts. Here are some tips for maintaining your new look:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Wrap your hair at night to prevent tangling and breaking.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the short bob weave hairstyle:

Q: How long does a short bob weave hairstyle last?

A: A short bob weave hairstyle can last up to 6-8 weeks, depending on how well you take care of it.

Q: Can I color my short bob weave hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can color your short bob weave hairstyle, but it is best to have a professional do it to avoid damaging the extensions.

Q: Can I wear my short bob weave hairstyle in a ponytail?

A: Yes, you can wear your short bob weave hairstyle in a ponytail, but be sure to use a hair tie that is gentle on your hair to prevent breakage.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important, especially if you are wearing a weave. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly, as this can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With proper care and maintenance, your short bob weave hairstyle can last for weeks, giving you a stylish and trendy look that complements your round face.

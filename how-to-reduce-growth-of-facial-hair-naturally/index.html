Reducing Growth Of Facial Hair Naturally Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Nov 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Facial hair growth can be a hassle, especially for women. While there are various methods to remove facial hair, such as waxing, shaving, and laser hair removal, there are also natural ways to reduce facial hair growth. In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks on how to reduce growth of facial hair naturally.

Table of Contents

  • How to Reduce Growth of Facial Hair Naturally
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Facial Hair Reduction

How to Reduce Growth of Facial Hair Naturally

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, it's essential to understand why facial hair grows. Hormonal imbalances, genetics, and medications are the primary culprits of excessive facial hair growth. While it may be challenging to control hormonal imbalances, there are natural ways to reduce facial hair growth.

The following are some ways to reduce facial hair growth naturally:

  • Spearmint tea
  • Turmeric
  • Sugar and lemon
  • Papaya
  • Egg white mask
  • Lavender oil

Step by Step Guide

Here's how to use the natural remedies mentioned above:

Spearmint Tea

Drink 2-3 cups of spearmint tea per day. Spearmint tea contains anti-androgen properties that reduce testosterone levels, which is a hormone responsible for facial hair growth.

Turmeric

Create a paste by mixing 2 tablespoons of turmeric with water. Apply the paste to your face and leave it on for 20-30 minutes. Rinse your face with warm water. Turmeric has anti-inflammatory and anti-bacterial properties that can reduce hair growth.

Sugar and Lemon

Mix 2 tablespoons of sugar with 1 tablespoon of lemon juice. Apply the mixture to your face in the opposite direction of hair growth. Leave it on for 15-20 minutes and rinse your face with warm water. Sugar and lemon have exfoliating properties that can remove dead skin cells and reduce hair growth.

Papaya

Mix 1 tablespoon of papaya paste with 1 tablespoon of turmeric. Apply the mixture to your face and leave it on for 20-30 minutes. Rinse your face with warm water. Papaya contains an enzyme called papain that can break down hair follicles and reduce hair growth.

Egg White Mask

Whisk an egg white and apply it to your face. Leave it on for 15-20 minutes and rinse your face with warm water. Egg white contains a protein called albumin that can tighten pores and reduce hair growth.

Lavender Oil

Mix 1-2 drops of lavender oil with 1 tablespoon of carrier oil, such as coconut oil or olive oil. Massage the mixture into your face and leave it on for 15-20 minutes. Rinse your face with warm water. Lavender oil has anti-androgen properties that can reduce hair growth.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to reduce facial hair growth naturally:

  • Take a zinc supplement to balance hormones.
  • Exfoliate your face regularly to remove dead skin cells.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your face.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet.
  • Exercise regularly to reduce stress levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will these natural remedies completely remove my facial hair?

A: No, these remedies may reduce hair growth, but they will not completely remove facial hair.

Q: How long will it take to see results?

A: Results may vary, but it may take 4-6 weeks to see a noticeable difference.

Q: Are these remedies safe?

A: Yes, these remedies are generally safe, but it's always best to do a patch test before using any new product.

Hair Care for Facial Hair Reduction

In addition to the natural remedies and tips mentioned above, it's crucial to take care of your facial hair to reduce growth. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Trim your facial hair regularly.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your facial hair.
  • Moisturize your face to prevent ingrown hairs.
  • Use a gentle cleanser to avoid irritation.
  • Avoid touching your face too often.

Conclusion

Reducing facial hair growth naturally may take some time and effort, but it's worth it in the end. By implementing these tips and tricks and taking care of your facial hair, you can reduce excessive growth and feel more confident in your own skin.

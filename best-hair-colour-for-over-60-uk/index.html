Best Hair Color For Over 60 Uk Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Nov 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for the best hair color for over 60 UK? As we age, our hair naturally loses pigment and becomes gray. However, this doesn't mean that you can't still have beautiful, vibrant hair. In fact, there are many hair colors that can enhance your look and make you feel more confident. In this article, we will discuss the best hair color for over 60 UK, how to choose the right color, and how to take care of your hair after coloring.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

Choosing the right hair color can be tricky, especially as we age. Here are some tips to help you find the best hair color for over 60 UK:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a crucial role in determining which hair color will look best on you. If you have a warm skin tone, go for hair colors with warm undertones like golden blonde or chestnut brown. If you have a cool skin tone, go for hair colors with cool undertones like platinum blonde or ash brown.

Go for a Natural Look

If you're not used to coloring your hair, it's best to go for a natural look. Choose a hair color that's close to your natural hair color, or a shade or two lighter or darker. This will make the transition less noticeable and more flattering.

Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hair color, consider your lifestyle. If you're active and spend a lot of time outdoors, go for a hair color that won't fade easily, like a deeper shade of brown or black. If you're more laid back and spend most of your time indoors, you can experiment with brighter colors like red or blonde.

The Best Hair Colors for Over 60 UK

Now that you know how to choose the right hair color, let's discuss the best hair colors for over 60 UK:

Warm Blonde

Warm blonde is a great hair color for over 60 UK because it adds warmth and brightness to your face. It's a great choice if you have a warm skin tone and want a natural look. You can go for shades like honey blonde, golden blonde, or caramel blonde.

Chestnut Brown

Chestnut brown is a rich, warm hair color that looks great on over 60 UK women. It's a great choice if you have a cool skin tone and want a natural look. You can go for shades like light chestnut brown, medium chestnut brown, or dark chestnut brown.

Ash Blonde

Ash blonde is a cool-toned hair color that looks great on over 60 UK women with cool skin tones. It's a great choice if you want a natural look and don't want to stray too far from your natural hair color. You can go for shades like light ash blonde, medium ash blonde, or dark ash blonde.

How to Take Care of Your Hair After Coloring

After coloring your hair, it's important to take proper care of it to maintain the color and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and prevent fading. These products are specially formulated to protect your hair color and keep it looking vibrant.

Avoid Hot Styling Tools

Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair and cause your color to fade faster. If you do use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This will help your hair color look more vibrant and last longer.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it's best to go to a professional if you're not experienced. They can help you choose the right color and ensure that it's applied evenly.

Q: How often should I color my hair?

A: It depends on the color and how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should color your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the color and prevent fading.

Q: Can I still dye my hair if I have gray hair?

A: Yes, you can still dye your hair if you have gray hair. In fact, many hair colors are specifically formulated to cover gray hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the best hair color for over 60 UK can be challenging, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find the perfect shade to enhance your look. Remember to consider your skin tone, go for a natural look, and consider your lifestyle when choosing a hair color. After coloring your hair, take proper care of it to maintain the color and prevent damage. With the right care, your hair color can look vibrant and beautiful for years to come.

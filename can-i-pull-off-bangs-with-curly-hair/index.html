Can I Pull Off Bangs With Curly Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair and are considering getting bangs, you might be wondering if you can pull them off. The answer is yes! Bangs can look great on curly hair, but there are a few things to consider before making the cut.

Description

Curly hair can be tricky to work with, but bangs can add some dimension and style to your look. However, because curly hair tends to be more voluminous, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape and hair texture.

How to

When deciding on the right type of bangs for your curly hair, it's important to consider your face shape. Some bangs styles work better on certain face shapes than others. For example, if you have a round face, you might want to opt for longer, side-swept bangs. If you have a square face, you might want to try soft, wispy bangs.

Another thing to consider is your hair texture. If you have tight, coily curls, you might want to avoid blunt bangs, as they can be difficult to style. Instead, opt for curly bangs that are cut to blend in with the rest of your hair.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow if you're considering getting bangs for your curly hair:

  1. Figure out your face shape.
  2. Research different types of bangs and which ones work best for your face shape.
  3. Consult with a stylist who has experience cutting curly hair.
  4. Consider getting a trial run of bangs before committing to a full cut.
  5. Invest in styling products that will help you maintain your bangs and keep them looking great.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your curly bangs:

  • Invest in a good diffuser attachment for your hair dryer.
  • Use a curl cream or gel to define your curls and keep them from frizzing.
  • Avoid brushing your bangs, as this can cause them to lose their shape.
  • Use bobby pins or hair clips to pin back your bangs if they start to get in your eyes.
  • Get regular trims to keep your bangs looking fresh.

FAQ

Will bangs make my curly hair look even bigger?

It's possible that bangs could make your hair look bigger, but it depends on the type of bangs you get. Soft, wispy bangs tend to blend in with the rest of your hair, while blunt bangs can add more volume. Talk to your stylist about the best type of bangs for your hair type and face shape.

Should I cut my own bangs?

We don't recommend cutting your own bangs, especially if you have curly hair. It's easy to make mistakes and end up with uneven or unflattering bangs. Instead, find a stylist who has experience cutting curly hair and can give you the best advice on what type of bangs will work for you.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming too dry.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Be gentle when detangling your hair, as curly hair is more prone to breakage.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.

Overall, you can definitely pull off bangs with curly hair! Just make sure to consider your face shape and hair texture before making the cut, and invest in the right styling products to keep your bangs looking great.

Should I Dye My Hair Buzzfeed A Guide For 2023

Written by Robby Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking about changing up your hair color? Perhaps you've come across a Buzzfeed quiz that's got you thinking about dyeing your hair. Before you make any decisions, it's important to consider a few things first. In this guide, we'll provide you with tips and advice on whether or not you should dye your hair, as well as how to do it properly and how to take care of your newly dyed locks.

Why Do You Want to Dye Your Hair?

Before you dye your hair, it's essential to understand why you want to do it in the first place. Are you bored with your current hair color? Are you looking for a change? Do you want to express your personality through your hair color? Or are you trying to cover up gray hairs?

Understanding your motivations can help you make the right decision about whether or not you should dye your hair. If you're doing it for the right reasons, then go for it! But if you're doing it for the wrong reasons, it's best to hold off and think about it some more.

The Buzzfeed Quiz

If you've stumbled upon a Buzzfeed quiz that asks whether or not you should dye your hair, it's important to take the results with a grain of salt. While these quizzes can be fun, they shouldn't be the sole factor in your decision-making process.

Instead, use the results of the quiz as a starting point for your research. Look into different hair colors, styles, and techniques to get a better idea of what you want. And remember, ultimately, the decision to dye your hair should be yours and yours alone.

How to Dye Your Hair

If you've decided to dye your hair, the next step is to figure out how to do it properly. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose Your Color

Consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color when choosing a new shade. You can also consult with a hairstylist for professional advice.

Step 2: Gather Your Supplies

You'll need hair dye, gloves, a bowl, a brush, and a towel to protect your clothes and surfaces.

Step 3: Prep Your Hair

Wash your hair and let it dry completely before applying the dye. Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline and ears to prevent staining.

Step 4: Apply the Dye

Follow the instructions on the hair dye box carefully. Apply the dye evenly, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. Leave it on for the recommended time, then rinse it out thoroughly.

Tips for Dyeing Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Do a patch test before dyeing your hair to check for allergic reactions.
  • Don't wash your hair for two days before dyeing it to allow your natural oils to protect your scalp.
  • Apply the dye in a well-ventilated area to avoid inhaling fumes.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prolong the life of your hair color.

Hair Care After Dyeing

After dyeing your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to prevent damage and fading. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after dyeing it.
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to protect your hair color.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent further damage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.

FAQ

Is it okay to dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but make sure to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test beforehand.

Will dyeing my hair damage it?

Dyeing your hair can cause damage if not done properly or if done too frequently. Make sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner and avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.

How often should I touch up my roots?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. Generally, touch-ups are needed every 4-6 weeks.

The Bottom Line

Deciding whether or not to dye your hair is a personal choice. If you're doing it for the right reasons and take the necessary precautions, it can be a fun way to express yourself and change up your look. Just remember to take care of your hair properly after dyeing it to prevent damage and fading.

How To Get Side Bangs To Lay Flat A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have side bangs, you know how frustrating it can be when they don't lay flat. It can ruin the perfect hairstyle and make you look unkempt. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to get your side bangs to lay flat with our step-by-step guide.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Side Bangs to Lay Flat
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Keeping Side Bangs Flat
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Side Bangs

How to Get Side Bangs to Lay Flat

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some of the tools you'll need to get your side bangs to lay flat:

  • Flat Iron
  • Hair Clips
  • Hairspray
  • Comb or Brush

Now that you have your tools ready, let's get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow dry it before you begin.

Step 2: Clip Your Bangs

Clip your side bangs away from the rest of your hair. This will make it easier to flat iron them.

Step 3: Heat Up Your Flat Iron

While your flat iron heats up, apply a heat protectant spray to your side bangs. This will help prevent damage from the heat.

Step 4: Flat Iron Your Bangs

Take a small section of your side bangs and place it between the flat iron plates. Slowly glide the flat iron down the section of hair, starting at the roots and moving towards the ends. Repeat this process until all of your side bangs are flat ironed.

Step 5: Spray with Hairspray

Once you've flat ironed your side bangs, spray them with a light hold hairspray. This will help keep them in place.

Step 6: Comb or Brush Your Bangs

Using a comb or brush, gently comb or brush your side bangs to smooth out any bumps or kinks.

Tips for Keeping Side Bangs Flat

Here are some tips for keeping your side bangs flat throughout the day:

  • Apply a dry shampoo to your bangs to absorb any oil or sweat.
  • Avoid touching or playing with your side bangs throughout the day.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your side bangs in place.

FAQs

Q: How often should I flat iron my side bangs?

A: It's best to limit the amount of heat you apply to your hair, so try to only flat iron your side bangs once a week.

Q: Can I use a straightening brush instead of a flat iron?

A: Yes, you can use a straightening brush to flat iron your side bangs. Just be sure to follow the manufacturer's instructions.

Hair Care for Side Bangs

To keep your side bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent oil buildup.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using any heated styling tools.
  • Trim your side bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them at the right length.

By following these tips and using our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to get your side bangs to lay flat and look great all day long.

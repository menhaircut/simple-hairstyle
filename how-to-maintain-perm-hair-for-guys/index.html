How To Maintain Perm Hair For Guys

Written by Frank Nov 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a guy with perm hair and looking for ways to maintain it? You’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll discuss some tips and tricks on how to take care of your perm hair to ensure it stays healthy and stylish. Read on to learn more about how to maintain perm hair for guys.

How to Perm Hair for Guys

Before we dive into how to maintain perm hair for guys, let’s first discuss how to get a perm. A perm is a chemical process that adds curls or waves to straight hair. The process involves applying a chemical solution to the hair, which reshapes the hair follicles.

To get a perm, you’ll need to visit a licensed hairstylist who specializes in perms. The stylist will assess your hair type and decide which type of perm will work best for you. There are two types of perms: alkaline and acid. Alkaline perms are stronger and better for coarse or resistant hair, while acid perms are gentler and better for fine or damaged hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Maintaining Perm Hair for Guys

Now that you have a perm, it’s important to take care of it to ensure it stays healthy and stylish. Here’s a step-by-step guide to maintaining perm hair for guys:

Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for permed hair. These products will help maintain the shape of your curls and prevent frizz. Be sure to massage the shampoo and conditioner into your hair gently, avoiding rubbing or tugging.

Step 2: Towel Dry

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz and damage your curls. Instead, use a soft, absorbent towel to blot your hair gently.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to your hair while it’s still damp. These products will help define your curls and keep them in place. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Air Dry or Diffuse

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. A diffuser will help distribute heat evenly and prevent frizz. Avoid using a regular hair dryer without a diffuser, as this can cause your curls to become frizzy and damaged.

Step 5: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, on your permed hair. These tools can cause your curls to become frizzy and damaged. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting.

Tips for Maintaining Perm Hair for Guys

Here are some additional tips for maintaining perm hair for guys:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using hair elastics or tight headbands that can break your hair.
  • Don’t wash your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swimming cap.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your curls.

FAQs about Maintaining Perm Hair for Guys

Q: How often should I wash my permed hair?

A: It’s best to wash permed hair every two to three days. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Q: Can I color my permed hair?

A: Yes, you can color your permed hair. However, it’s best to wait at least two weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair. Be sure to use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your color from fading.

Q: How long does a perm last?

A: A perm can last anywhere from three to six months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of it.

Remember to be gentle with your hair and avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools. With a little bit of care and attention, your perm hair will look great for months to come.

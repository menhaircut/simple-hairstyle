How To Look Better Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, it's natural to want to look our best. But what does it mean to look good over 60? There are many factors to consider, including skincare, hair care, makeup, and wardrobe choices. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for looking and feeling your best in your golden years.

Table of Contents

  • Skincare
  • Hair Care
  • Makeup
  • Wardrobe
  • Tips and Tricks

Skincare

As we age, our skin becomes thinner, drier, and more prone to wrinkles and age spots. To keep your skin looking its best over 60, it's essential to establish a good skincare routine. Here are some tips:

Cleanse and Moisturize

Start by cleansing your skin twice a day, using a gentle cleanser that won't strip your skin of its natural oils. Follow up with a moisturizer that's specially formulated for aging skin. Look for ingredients like hyaluronic acid, retinol, and vitamin C, which can help hydrate and brighten your skin.

Protect Your Skin from the Sun

One of the most significant contributors to aging skin is sun damage. Always wear sunscreen with an SPF of at least 30, and reapply every two hours if you're outside. Wear a hat and sunglasses to protect your face and eyes from the sun's harmful rays.

Hair Care

As we age, our hair may become thinner, drier, and more prone to breakage. To keep your hair healthy and looking its best over 60, follow these tips:

Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Look for shampoos and conditioners that are formulated for aging hair. These products will contain ingredients that can help strengthen your hair, add moisture, and improve its texture.

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your hair look dull and lifeless. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks, or as needed.

Makeup

Makeup can help enhance your natural beauty and give you a more youthful appearance. Here are some tips for applying makeup over 60:

Use a Primer

A primer can help smooth out your skin's texture and create a more even base for your makeup. Look for a primer that's formulated for aging skin, with ingredients like peptides, hyaluronic acid, and antioxidants.

Choose the Right Foundation

Look for a foundation that's lightweight and moisturizing, with a dewy finish. Avoid matte or heavy foundations, which can settle into fine lines and wrinkles.

Wardrobe

Your clothing choices can have a significant impact on how you look and feel. Here are some tips for building a wardrobe that flatters your body and age:

Invest in Quality Basics

Invest in quality basics like a well-fitting pair of jeans, a classic blazer, and a white button-down shirt. These pieces can be dressed up or down and will never go out of style.

Choose Flattering Silhouettes

As we age, our bodies may change, and it's essential to choose clothing that flatters our new shape. Look for silhouettes that accentuate your best features and downplay any areas of concern.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for looking your best over 60:

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Get enough sleep to help your body repair and regenerate
  • Exercise regularly to maintain muscle tone and boost circulation
  • Practice good posture to look taller and more confident
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest fashion and beauty trends

FAQs

What's the best way to prevent wrinkles?

The best way to prevent wrinkles is to protect your skin from the sun, avoid smoking, and establish a good skincare routine that includes cleansing, moisturizing, and using anti-aging products.

How can I make my hair look thicker?

You can make your hair look thicker by using volumizing shampoos and conditioners, blow-drying your hair upside down, and using styling products like mousse or volumizing spray.

What's the best way to apply makeup over 60?

The best way to apply makeup over 60 is to start with a primer, followed by a lightweight foundation, concealer, and powder. Use a light hand when applying eye makeup and focus on enhancing your natural features.

What's a good way to update my wardrobe without spending a lot of money?

A good way to update your wardrobe without spending a lot of money is to focus on accessories like scarves, statement jewelry, and shoes. These items can add interest to your existing wardrobe and make your outfits feel fresh and new.

Looking your best over 60 is all about taking care of yourself and making intentional choices about your appearance. With a good skincare routine, hair care, makeup, and wardrobe choices, you can look and feel your best at any age.

How to Choose the Best Short Hair for Round Face Asian 2020

When choosing a short hairstyle for round face Asian 2020, there are a few things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Face Shape

The first thing to consider is your face shape. Round faces tend to have fuller cheeks and a wider forehead and chin. To balance out your face, choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume at the crown and has some length at the sides.

Think About Your Hair Texture

The next thing to consider is your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a layered bob or a pixie cut with lots of texture can add volume and movement to your hair. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or a shaggy cut can help thin out your hair and create a more flattering shape.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your hair is also important. A short bob that hits just below the chin can be very flattering for round faces. A pixie cut with longer layers at the top can add height and elongate the face. Avoid very short cuts that hug the head too closely, as these can make the face appear even rounder.

How to Style Short Hair for Round Face Asian 2020

Short hair for round face Asian 2020 can be styled in many different ways, depending on your personal style and hair type. Here are some ideas to get you started:

Add Some Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help create the illusion of volume and movement. Use a texturizing spray or mousse to add some grit and tousle your hair with your fingers for a messy, bedhead look.

Create Some Height

Adding height at the crown can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Use a volumizing mousse or a root-lifting spray to add some lift to your hair. You can also backcomb the hair at the crown for extra height.

Experiment with Accessories

Adding accessories can help jazz up your short hair and make it look more stylish. Try adding a hairband, a hairpin, or a scarf to your hair for a pop of color and texture.

Step by Step Guide to Getting Short Hair for Round Face Asian 2020

Here's a step by step guide to getting short hair for round face Asian 2020:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

Choose the short hair style that best suits your face shape and hair texture. Bring a picture to your stylist to help explain the look you want.

Step 2: Go for a Consultation

Before you make the chop, go for a consultation with your stylist. They can give you advice on the best cut and style for your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Make the Cut

Once you've agreed on a style, it's time to make the cut. Sit back, relax, and let your stylist work their magic.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

After your cut, your stylist will style your hair to show you how to get the look at home. Take note of the products and tools they use, and ask any questions you may have.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Round Face Asian 2020

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hair for round face Asian 2020:

Get Regular Trims

Short hair needs regular trims to keep it looking sharp and well-maintained. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your style in shape.

Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically formulated for short hair, such as texturizing sprays, volumizing mousses, and pomades. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs about Short Hair for Round Face Asian 2020

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: Short hair can actually have a slimming effect on round faces, as long as you choose the right cut and style.

Q: Can I still style my short hair in different ways?

A: Yes, short hair can be very versatile and can be styled in many different ways, from messy and textured to sleek and polished.

Q: Will short hair be easy to maintain?

A: Short hair can be easier to maintain than long hair, as it requires less washing, conditioning, and styling time.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair for Round Face Asian 2020

Here are some hair care tips for short hair for round face Asian 2020:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and fresh. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.

Avoid Over-Washing

Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness and breakage.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray. Sun damage can cause dryness and breakage.

Short hair for round face Asian 2020 can be a great way to update your look and feel more confident. By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short hairstyle for your face shape and hair type, and keep it looking great all year round.

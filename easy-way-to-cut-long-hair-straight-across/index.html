Easy Way To Cut Long Hair Straight Across Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have long hair, you might be looking for a way to give it a trim and maintain its length. Cutting your hair straight across is one of the easiest ways to do this. But, going to a salon every time can be quite expensive, and doing it at home can seem daunting. However, with a little bit of practice and a few tricks up your sleeve, you can easily cut your long hair straight across at home. In this guide, we’ll take you through a step-by-step process of cutting your hair straight across, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Cut Long Hair Straight Across – Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

The first step is to prepare your hair for the cut. Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it. If your hair is tangled, use a detangling brush or comb to remove any knots. Divide your hair into two equal parts and tie them into two ponytails at the back of your head.

Step 2: Cut the First Ponytail

Take one of the ponytails and comb it straight down towards the front of your face. Hold the hair between your fingers at the desired length that you want to cut. Use a pair of sharp scissors to cut across the hair in a straight line. Make sure you cut the hair in one clean sweep and avoid sawing or hacking at it. Repeat the same process with the other ponytail.

Step 3: Check for Evenness

After you have cut both ponytails, check your hair for evenness. If there are any uneven strands, use the scissors to trim them until you have a straight line. To check, part your hair in the middle and compare the two sides. If you notice any unevenness, trim the hair until the two sides are even.

Step 4: Check the Length

Once you have trimmed your hair, check the length to ensure that it is what you want. If you feel that the hair is still too long, you can repeat the process by tying your hair into another ponytail and cutting it at the desired length.

Step 5: Finish the Cut

Once you are happy with the length, remove the ponytails and let your hair fall free. Use a mirror to check the back of your head and trim any stray hairs until you have a straight line.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Straight Across

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect straight cut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid frayed ends.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet as it may appear shorter when dry.
  • Trim your hair gradually if you're not sure about the length. You can always cut more if it's too long.
  • Have a friend or family member help you if you're not confident in doing it alone.

FAQs

Q. How often should I cut my hair?

A. It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, it's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair growth.

Q. Should I cut my hair when it's wet or dry?

A. It's best to cut your hair when it's dry as wet hair may appear shorter when it dries.

Q. Can I cut my hair straight across if it's layered?

A. Yes, you can still cut your hair straight across if it's layered. Just make sure you cut each layer at the same length.

Hair Care after Cutting Your Hair Straight Across

After you've cut your hair straight across, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and length. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat-styling tools like curling irons or straighteners as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and maintain its length.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

Cutting your hair straight across can be a simple and easy way to maintain its length and keep it looking healthy. With these tips and step-by-step guide, you can easily do it at home and save yourself a trip to the salon.

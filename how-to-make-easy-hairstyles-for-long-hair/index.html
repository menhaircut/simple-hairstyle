How To Make Easy Hairstyles For Long Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's beautiful and versatile, allowing for a variety of hairstyles. On the other hand, it can be overwhelming and time-consuming to maintain. If you're looking for easy hairstyles for long hair that you can do at home, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll show you some simple and stylish hairstyles that you can create in minutes.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

The first step to creating easy hairstyles for long hair is to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a comb, a brush, bobby pins, hair ties, and possibly some hair spray or gel. Once you have your tools ready, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles.

One of the easiest hairstyles for long hair is a simple ponytail. To create this look, start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Then, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band. You can leave your ponytail straight or add some texture by curling the ends with a curling iron.

Another easy hairstyle for long hair is a messy bun. To create this look, start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place, leaving some loose pieces around your face for a messy, effortless look.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating easy hairstyles for long hair:

Simple Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck.
  3. Secure your hair with an elastic band.
  4. Curl the ends of your ponytail with a curling iron, if desired.

Messy Bun

  1. Pull your hair back into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Leave some loose pieces around your face for a messy, effortless look.

Tips

Here are some tips for creating easy hairstyles for long hair:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some variety to your hairstyles.
  • Don't be afraid to add some texture to your hair, whether that's through curling or braiding.
  • If you're short on time, dry shampoo can be a lifesaver for refreshing your hair and adding some volume.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for long hair:

Q: What are some easy hairstyles for long hair that don't require heat styling?

A: Braids are a great option for creating easy hairstyles without heat styling. You can try a simple three-strand braid or experiment with more complex braids, such as fishtails or Dutch braids.

Q: How can I make my ponytail look more interesting?

A: You can add some interest to a simple ponytail by wrapping a small section of hair around the elastic band to conceal it. You can also try a high ponytail or a side ponytail for a different look.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your long hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining long hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner before swimming.

With these easy hairstyles for long hair and hair care tips, you'll be able to keep your hair looking its best without spending hours in front of the mirror. Whether you're heading to work or out for a night on the town, these hairstyles are sure to impress.

Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Faces with Widows Peak

If you have a heart shaped face with a widows peak, you may find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that flatters your face. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the best hairstyles for heart shaped faces with widows peak, as well as tips and tricks to help you care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin. A widows peak is a V-shaped point in the hairline in the center of the forehead. Heart shaped faces with widows peak tend to have a more prominent forehead, which can make it challenging to find a hairstyle that balances the face's proportions.

How to

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for heart shaped faces with widows peak is to choose a style that draws attention away from the forehead and towards the chin. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

1. Side-swept bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for heart shaped faces with widows peak. They help to balance out the forehead and draw attention towards the chin. Opt for long, wispy bangs that blend seamlessly into the rest of your hair.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that flatters heart shaped faces with widows peak. Keep the length just below the chin and add some layers to create movement and texture. This will help to soften the forehead and balance out the face's proportions.

3. Long layers

Long layers are another excellent option for heart shaped faces with widows peak. They help to create volume and movement around the face, which can help to balance out the forehead. Keep the layers soft and subtle to avoid drawing attention to the forehead.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you style your hair if you have a heart shaped face with widows peak:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before styling. This will help to ensure that your hair looks its best and that your hairstyle lasts throughout the day.

2. Choose your hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and widows peak. Refer to the tips above for inspiration.

3. Style your hair

Use a curling iron, straightener, or other styling tool to create your desired look. Be sure to use heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

4. Finish with hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray to help it last throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face with widows peak:

1. Consider your hair texture

Your hair texture can have a significant impact on how your hairstyle looks. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter hairstyle with layers to add volume. If you have thick hair, a longer hairstyle with fewer layers may be more flattering.

2. Consult with a hairstylist

If you're unsure which hairstyle is right for you, consider consulting with a hairstylist. They can help you choose a style that flatters your face shape and widows peak and provide tips for styling and caring for your hair.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a heart shaped face with widows peak?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the forehead, such as heavy bangs or high ponytails. These styles can make the forehead appear even larger and draw attention away from the chin.

Can I still wear my hair up if I have a heart shaped face with widows peak?

Absolutely! Just be sure to avoid styles that pull the hair back tightly from the forehead, as this can accentuate the widows peak. Instead, opt for loose, tousled updos that frame the face.

Hair Care

Finally, it's essential to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is gentle on your hair and scalp. Look for products that are free from harsh chemicals, such as sulfates and parabens.

2. Protect your hair from heat

Use heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

3. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face with widows peak. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best!

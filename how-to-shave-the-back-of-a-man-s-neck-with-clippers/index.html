How To Shave The Back Of A Man s Neck With Clippers

Written by Robby Jul 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Shaving the back of a man's neck with clippers can be a little tricky, but with the right technique and tools, it can be done easily and quickly. In this article, we will discuss step by step how to shave the back of a man's neck with clippers, along with some tips and FAQs to help you achieve a clean and smooth shave.

How to

Before we dive into the steps, let's first start with the tools you will need:

  • Clippers
  • Clipper guard
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Towel
  • Shaving cream or gel (optional)
  • After-shave lotion or balm (optional)

Step by Step

1. Start by attaching the clipper guard to the clippers. Choose the appropriate guard size depending on how short or long you want the hair to be.

2. Use the comb to lift the hair on the back of the neck. This will make it easier to trim the hair evenly.

3. Turn on the clippers and start trimming the hair from the base of the neck upwards. Use long, smooth strokes and go against the direction of hair growth for a closer shave.

4. Continue trimming the hair until you reach the hairline at the top of the neck. Be careful not to cut too high or you may end up with a bald spot.

5. Use the mirror to check for any missed spots or uneven areas. If necessary, go over those areas again with the clippers.

6. Once you are satisfied with the results, use the towel to wipe away any loose hair.

7. If you prefer, you can apply shaving cream or gel to the shaved area to soothe the skin and prevent irritation.

8. Lastly, apply after-shave lotion or balm to moisturize and protect the skin.

Tips

- If you are new to shaving your own neck, it may be helpful to have someone assist you.

- Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure you are getting an even shave.

- Don't rush. Take your time and go slowly to avoid any nicks or cuts.

- Clean your clippers regularly to keep them in good condition.

FAQ

Q: Should I shave my neck before or after a shower?

A: It is recommended to shave after a shower or bath as the warm water will soften the hair and make it easier to trim.

Q: What do I do if I accidentally cut myself while shaving?

A: Rinse the area with cold water and apply pressure with a clean towel or tissue to stop the bleeding. Apply a small amount of antiseptic to prevent infection.

Hair Care

After shaving, it is important to take care of your hair and skin to prevent irritation and ingrown hairs. Use a moisturizing lotion or balm to soothe the skin and keep it hydrated. Avoid touching the shaved area with dirty hands or clothing to prevent infection. Lastly, keep your clippers clean and well-maintained to avoid any damage to your hair or skin.

By following these simple steps and tips, you can easily shave the back of a man's neck with clippers and achieve a clean and smooth shave. Happy shaving!

Men s Hair Long On Top Pushed Back A Relaxed Look

Written by Christine Oct 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and easy-to-maintain hairstyle, then men's hair long on top pushed back is the perfect option for you. This classic hairstyle is versatile and can be worn in a variety of different ways, making it an ideal choice for any occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect long-on-top look and provide you with some tips for keeping your hair looking great.

Description

The men's hair long on top pushed back hairstyle is characterized by long hair on the top of the head that is pushed back and away from the face. This style is typically shorter on the sides and back, creating a contrast between the long hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides. The hair on top can be styled in many different ways, including slicked back, messy, or even spiked.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're interested in getting the men's hair long on top pushed back look, there are a few things you'll need to do. First, you'll need to grow your hair out to the desired length. Typically, the hair on top should be at least six inches long, but it can be longer if you prefer. Once your hair is long enough, you'll need to visit a stylist or barber to have it cut and styled.

The stylist or barber will typically trim the sides and back of your hair while leaving the hair on top longer. They may also add layers or texture to the hair on top to give it some volume and movement. Once your hair has been cut and styled, you can use products like pomade or wax to keep it in place and create the desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the men's hair long on top pushed back look:

  1. Grow your hair out to the desired length.
  2. Visit a stylist or barber to have your hair cut and styled.
  3. Use products like pomade or wax to keep your hair in place.
  4. Style your hair by pushing it back away from your face.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

To keep your men's hair long on top pushed back hairstyle looking great, there are a few tips you'll need to follow:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to style your hair and remove any tangles or knots.
  • Use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to help set your hair in place.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What face shapes suit this hairstyle?

The men's hair long on top pushed back hairstyle can suit a variety of different face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. It's important to work with a skilled stylist or barber who can help you choose the right length and style for your face shape.

Can this hairstyle be worn with a beard?

Yes, the men's hair long on top pushed back hairstyle can be worn with a beard. In fact, many men choose to pair this hairstyle with a well-groomed beard or mustache for a stylish and masculine look.

How often should I get my hair cut?

How often you need to get your hair cut will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. As a general rule, most men will need to get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length and shape.

Hair Care

To keep your men's hair long on top pushed back hairstyle looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. This means using a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy, avoiding over-washing your hair, and using a hairbrush or comb to style your hair and remove any tangles or knots. Additionally, you should use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to help set your hair in place and avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Overall, the men's hair long on top pushed back hairstyle is a classic and versatile look that can be worn in a variety of different ways. Whether you prefer a slicked back, messy, or spiked look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.

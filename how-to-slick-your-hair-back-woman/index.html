How To Slick Your Hair Back Woman A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 28, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

If you're looking for a chic and sophisticated hairstyle, slicking your hair back is the way to go. This hairstyle has been around for decades and has been a go-to look for women who want to look polished and put-together. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to slick your hair back woman, along with tips and tricks to make it easier.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Slick Your Hair Back Woman
  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Slick Your Hair Back Woman

Slicking your hair back is a simple, yet elegant hairstyle that can be done in a few minutes. Here's what you'll need:

  • Brush or comb
  • Hair gel or pomade
  • Hair spray
  • Hair tie (optional)

Now that you have your tools ready, here's how to slick your hair back woman:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with dry and detangled hair. Use a brush or comb to get rid of any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Apply hair gel or pomade to your hair, starting from the roots to the ends. Make sure you distribute the product evenly to avoid any clumps.

Step 3: Use a comb to slick your hair back. Start from the hairline and work your way down to the nape of your neck. Make sure your hair is smooth and free of any bumps or lumps.

Step 4: If you have long hair, you can tie it into a low ponytail or bun. This will help keep your hair in place and prevent any flyaways.

Step 5: Finish off with a hairspray to set your hairstyle in place. Spray a generous amount of hairspray all over your hair, making sure to get the roots and ends.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make slicking your hair back easier:

  • Use a strong hold hair gel or pomade to keep your hairstyle in place all day.
  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing mousse before applying hair gel to add some texture and volume to your hair.
  • If you have curly hair, blow dry your hair straight before applying hair gel to make it easier to slick back.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to distribute the hair gel evenly and to smooth out any bumps or lumps.

FAQs

Q: Can I slick my hair back if I have a fringe?

A: Yes, you can. Use a comb to slick back your hair and leave your fringe down. You can also slick back your fringe and secure it with bobby pins.

Q: Can I slick my hair back if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can. Use a hair gel or pomade and a comb to slick back your hair. You can also use bobby pins to secure any flyaways.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're using hair gel or pomade on a daily basis. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly to get rid of any product buildup.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

Slicking your hair back is a simple and elegant hairstyle that can be done in a few minutes. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a polished and put-together look that's perfect for any occasion. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips and tricks to make it easier, and remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

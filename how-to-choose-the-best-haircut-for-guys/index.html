How To Choose The Best Haircut For Guys

Written by Christine Aug 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, it's important to choose a haircut that not only looks great, but also works well with your hair type and face shape. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for choosing the best haircut for guys.

Table of Contents

  1. Know Your Hair Type
  2. Determine Your Face Shape
  3. Types of Haircuts for Guys
  4. Tips for Getting the Perfect Haircut
  5. Hair Care Tips for Men
  6. FAQ

Know Your Hair Type

The first step in choosing the best haircut for guys is to know your hair type. This includes the texture, thickness, and natural style of your hair. Some common hair types include:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Thick
  • Thin

Understanding your hair type can help you choose a haircut that will work well with your natural hair and require minimal styling.


Determine Your Face Shape

Your face shape is another important factor to consider when choosing a haircut. Some common face shapes include:

  • Oval
  • Square
  • Round
  • Heart-shaped

Different haircuts can help enhance or downplay certain features of your face, so it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape.


Types of Haircuts for Guys

There are many different types of haircuts for guys, each with their own unique style and personality. Some popular types include:

  • Crew cut
  • Buzz cut
  • Pompadour
  • Quiff
  • Slicked back
  • Side part
  • Undercut
  • Long hair

Each of these haircuts can be customized to fit your hair type and face shape, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles.


Tips for Getting the Perfect Haircut

Once you've decided on a haircut, it's important to find a skilled barber or stylist who can bring your vision to life. Here are some tips for getting the perfect haircut:

  • Bring pictures or references of the style you want
  • Communicate clearly with your barber or stylist
  • Be open to their professional suggestions
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair

Remember, a great haircut is all about finding the right balance between style and practicality.


Hair Care Tips for Men

After you've gotten your new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking and feeling its best. Here are some hair care tips for men:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

With the right hair care routine, you can keep your new haircut looking great for weeks to come.


FAQ

What is the best haircut for guys with thin hair?

A short, textured haircut can help create the illusion of thicker hair for guys with thin or fine hair.

What is the best haircut for guys with curly hair?

A layered haircut can help control the volume and shape of curly hair, while also showcasing its natural texture.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's generally recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.


Choosing the best haircut for guys can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your hair type, face shape, and personal style, you can find a haircut that not only looks great, but also makes you feel confident and stylish.

Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50 A Complete Guide

Written by William Nov 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our hairstyle preferences change. Many women over 50 prefer shoulder-length hairstyles because they are easy to manage, versatile, and flattering. If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle that complements your features can be challenging. In this article, we will explore the best shoulder length hairstyles for round faces over 50, along with tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

As we age, our hair loses its natural luster and thickness. Shoulder length hairstyles for round faces over 50 can help you look elegant and sophisticated without requiring a lot of maintenance. These hairstyles are perfect for women who want a fresh, modern look that is easy to style. They are also perfect for women who want to add volume and texture to their hair.

How To

The key to a great shoulder length hairstyle is to find one that flatters your face shape. If you have a round face, you should choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and adds height. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for your round face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. Round faces have soft, curved lines and are typically as wide as they are long. If you are unsure about your face shape, you can use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

Shoulder length hair is perfect for round faces because it adds height and elongates the face. You can also add layers to your hair to create texture and volume.

Step 3: Choose the Right Style

The right style for a round face is one that adds height to the top of the head and creates the illusion of length. You can achieve this by adding layers to your hair, curling the ends, or wearing your hair in an updo.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for your round face:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Use a towel to dry your hair gently.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or styling cream. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a sleek, smooth look. You can also use a diffuser attachment to create more defined curls or waves.

Step 4: Add Layers

If you want to add volume and texture to your hair, you can add layers. Ask your hairstylist to add layers that frame your face and add height to the top of your head.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair by curling the ends or wearing it in an updo. You can also add accessories, such as hair clips or headbands, to complete your look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you care for your shoulder length hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

FAQ

What are the best shoulder length hairstyles for round faces over 50?

The best shoulder length hairstyles for round faces over 50 are those that add height to the top of the head and create the illusion of length. You can achieve this by adding layers to your hair, curling the ends, or wearing your hair in an updo.

How can I care for my shoulder length hair?

You can care for your shoulder length hair by using a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, avoiding hot tools, using a heat protectant spray, and trimming your hair regularly.

Hair Care

As we age, our hair becomes thinner and more fragile. It is essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Take supplements, such as biotin and vitamin D, to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy, vibrant, and beautiful.

In conclusion, shoulder length hairstyles for round faces over 50 can help you look elegant and sophisticated without requiring a lot of maintenance. By choosing the right length and style, you can create a look that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

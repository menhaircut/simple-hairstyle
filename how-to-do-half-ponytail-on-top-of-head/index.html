How To Do Half Ponytail On Top Of Head A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to try something new and trendy? If yes, then a half ponytail on top of the head is the perfect hairstyle for you! This hairstyle is simple, easy to do, and looks great on any hair length or texture. In this article, we will guide you on how to do a half ponytail on top of the head in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Half Ponytail on Top of Head
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Perfect Half Ponytail on Top of Head
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Do Half Ponytail on Top of Head

A half ponytail on top of the head is a simple hairstyle that involves pulling half of your hair up and securing it with an elastic band. To achieve this look, you will need:

  • Elastic Band
  • Brush or Comb

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Brush or Comb Your Hair

Start by brushing or combing your hair to remove any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your fingers, section off the top half of your hair. Make sure to leave some hair at the front to frame your face.

Step 3: Tie the Half Ponytail

Pull the section of hair you have just separated to the top of your head and secure it with an elastic band. Make sure that the elastic band is tight enough to hold the hair in place but not too tight that it hurts.

Step 4: Tease the Ponytail

If you want to add some volume to your ponytail, tease it with a comb or brush. Gently back-comb the hair at the base of the ponytail to create a fuller look.

Step 5: Finish the Look

Lastly, you can add some hairspray or hair oil to give your hair some shine and hold. You can also add some hair accessories like clips or pins to decorate your half ponytail.

Tips for Perfect Half Ponytail on Top of Head

Here are some tips to make your half ponytail on top of the head look perfect:

  • Make sure to brush or comb your hair before starting to avoid any knots or tangles.
  • Try to leave some hair at the front to frame your face and make the look more flattering.
  • Use an elastic band that matches your hair color to avoid it being visible.
  • Tease the ponytail if you want to add some volume to your hair.
  • Use hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I do a half ponytail on top of the head with short hair?

Yes, you can! You may need to use bobby pins to secure the hair in place.

Can I do a half ponytail on top of the head with curly hair?

Yes, you can! You may need to use some hair gel or mousse to tame down the frizz.

Hair Care for Half Ponytail on Top of Head

Here are some tips to take care of your hair when doing a half ponytail on top of the head:

  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly to prevent hair breakage or hair loss.
  • Use a good quality elastic band that is gentle on your hair.
  • Avoid tying your hair in a half ponytail on top of the head too often to prevent hair damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Now that you know how to do a half ponytail on top of the head, you can rock this trendy hairstyle anytime, anywhere. Follow our step-by-step guide, use our tips, and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Happy styling!

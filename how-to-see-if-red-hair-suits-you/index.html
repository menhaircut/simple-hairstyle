How To See If Red Hair Suits You

Written by Robby Jun 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you thinking about going red? Red hair is a bold and beautiful choice, but it's not for everyone. If you're not sure whether red hair will suit you, don't worry. In this article, we'll show you how to determine if red hair is the right choice for you.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in determining whether red hair suits you is to identify your skin tone. People with warm skin tones typically look best in shades of red that have orange or golden undertones, while those with cool skin tones look best in shades of red with blue or purple undertones.

Another thing to consider is your natural hair color. If you have very dark hair, it can be difficult to achieve a vibrant red shade without damaging your hair. On the other hand, if you have light hair, you may be able to achieve a beautiful red shade without much effort.

Finally, think about your personal style. Red hair is a bold choice that can make a statement, so make sure it fits with your overall aesthetic. If you're more of a minimalist, for example, a bright red shade might not be the best choice for you.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to determine whether red hair suits you:

  1. Determine your skin tone.
  2. Consider your natural hair color.
  3. Think about your personal style.
  4. Look at pictures of people with similar skin tone and natural hair color who have gone red.
  5. Try on a wig or use a virtual hair color app to see how you would look with red hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when considering red hair:

  • If you're not sure about going all-in with red hair, start with highlights or ombre.
  • Consider consulting with a professional colorist to get their opinion on whether red hair would suit you.
  • Choose a shade of red that complements your skin tone and natural hair color.
  • Invest in high-quality hair care products to keep your red hair looking vibrant and healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about red hair:

Will red hair suit me if I have pale skin?

Yes, red hair can look beautiful on people with pale skin. Just make sure to choose a shade of red that complements your skin tone.

Can I go red if I have dark hair?

It's possible, but it may require bleaching your hair first, which can be damaging. Consider consulting with a professional colorist before making the leap to red.

How can I keep my red hair looking vibrant?

Invest in high-quality hair care products designed for color-treated hair, and avoid washing your hair too frequently. Also, consider getting regular touch-ups to keep your red hair looking fresh.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for caring for red hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the color and cause it to fade.
  • Use a color-depositing conditioner or hair mask to refresh the color between touch-ups.
  • Avoid heat styling tools when possible, as excessive heat can cause the color to fade.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to determine if red hair suits you, you can make an informed decision about whether to take the plunge. Whether you go for a subtle strawberry blonde or a bold cherry red, red hair can be a beautiful and transformative choice.

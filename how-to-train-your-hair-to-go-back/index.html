How To Train Your Hair To Go Back

Written by Bardi Nov 05, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of your hair always getting in your face? Are you looking for a way to style your hair that is both practical and stylish? Look no further! In this article, we will teach you how to train your hair to go back. Follow our step-by-step guide and you'll have a sleek and sophisticated hairstyle in no time.

Table of Contents

  • How to Train Your Hair to Go Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Success

How to Train Your Hair to Go Back

Training your hair to go back is a simple process that requires patience and persistence. The key is to train your hair to grow in the direction that you want it to go. By following these steps, you'll be able to achieve a sleek and sophisticated hairstyle that is both practical and stylish.

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step in training your hair to go back is to start with clean hair. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup. This will make it easier to style your hair and will also help to prevent breakage.

Step 2: Use a Comb

Using a comb, gently comb your hair back in the direction that you want it to go. Start from the front and work your way back, making sure to comb your hair in a smooth and even motion. Be sure to use a comb with wide teeth to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as gel or pomade, to your hair. This will help to hold your hair in place and will also give it a sleek and polished look. Be sure to use a product that is appropriate for your hair type and texture.

Step 4: Repeat Daily

Repeat this process daily, combing your hair back in the same direction each time. Over time, your hair will start to grow in the direction that you are training it to go. This process may take a few weeks or even a few months, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps for a foolproof way to train your hair to go back:

  1. Start with clean hair
  2. Gently comb your hair back in the direction that you want it to go
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product
  4. Repeat daily

Tips for Success

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Be patient – training your hair to go back takes time
  • Use a comb with wide teeth to avoid damaging your hair
  • Choose a styling product that is appropriate for your hair type and texture
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • If you have trouble keeping your hair in place, try using a hair clip or bobby pin to hold it back while it dries

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about training your hair to go back:

How long does it take to train your hair to go back?

The amount of time it takes to train your hair to go back depends on how quickly your hair grows. It may take a few weeks or even a few months to achieve the desired result.

What kind of styling product should I use?

You should use a styling product that is appropriate for your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, use a lightweight product such as mousse or gel. If you have thick or coarse hair, use a heavier product such as pomade or wax.

Can I still wear my hair down?

Yes, you can still wear your hair down. Training your hair to go back simply gives you more styling options and helps to keep your hair out of your face.

Hair Care for Your New Style

Now that you've trained your hair to go back, it's important to take good care of it to maintain your new style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy

By following these tips, you'll be able to maintain your sleek and sophisticated hairstyle for weeks to come.

Training your hair to go back is a simple process that can give you a stylish and practical hairstyle. By following our step-by-step guide and using the right products, you'll be able to achieve the look that you want in no time. Remember to be patient and persistent, and your hair will soon be trained to go back.

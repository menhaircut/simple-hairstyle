How Do I Know What Hair Colour Will Suit Me

Are you planning to try a new hair color but not sure which one to choose? Picking the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you are experimenting with a new color for the first time. In this blog post, we will help you figure out how to know what hair color will suit you best.

How to Know What Hair Color Will Suit Me

Before you take the plunge and dye your hair, you need to consider a few factors that could affect how the color looks on you. These factors include your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. Here are some tips to help you decide which hair color will suit you best:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a vital role in determining which hair color will suit you best. People with warm skin tones, for example, look best in warm hair colors like golden blonde, caramel, and copper. Those with cool skin tones, on the other hand, look great in cooler shades like ash blonde, platinum, and burgundy.

2. Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color should also be a consideration when choosing a hair color. If you have blue or green eyes, for example, you can opt for warmer shades like honey blonde or auburn. Brown-eyed people, on the other hand, look great in almost any hair color, but warm shades like caramel and chestnut can make their eyes pop.

3. Think About Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color should also be a factor when choosing a hair color. If you have dark hair, going blonde may not be the best option as it can be damaging to your hair. Similarly, if you have light hair, going too dark can be a shock to your system. Opt for a shade that is no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural hair color.

Step-by-Step Process to Choose the Right Hair Color

Now that you know what factors to consider when choosing a hair color, here is a step-by-step process to help you choose the perfect color:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Figure out whether you have a warm or cool skin tone. You can do this by looking at the veins on your wrist. If your veins are blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If your veins are green, you have a warm skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Take a look at your eye color and consider what shades will complement it best. Generally, cool shades like ash blonde and platinum look great with blue or green eyes, while warm shades like honey blonde and auburn look great with brown eyes.

Step 3: Think About Your Natural Hair Color

Consider your natural hair color and think about what shades will complement it best. Going too light or too dark can be damaging to your hair, so opt for a shade that is no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural color.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

If you are still unsure which hair color to choose, consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine which shades will compliment your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color best.

Tips to Consider When Choosing the Perfect Hair Color

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the perfect hair color:

1. Be Realistic

Be realistic about what hair color will look best on you. If you have dark hair, going platinum blonde may not be the best option. Similarly, if you have light hair, going too dark can be a shock to your system.

2. Start Small

If you are experimenting with a new hair color for the first time, start small. Opt for a subtle change like adding highlights or lowlights before going for a full dye job.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hair color. If you have a busy schedule, opt for a low-maintenance color that won't require frequent touch-ups.

FAQs About Choosing the Right Hair Color

1. Can I Change My Hair Color If I Don't Like It?

Yes, you can always change your hair color if you don't like it. However, keep in mind that constantly dyeing your hair can be damaging, so it's best to wait a few weeks in between dye jobs.

2. Will a New Hair Color Damage My Hair?

Dyeing your hair can be damaging, especially if you go too light or too dark. However, using high-quality hair care products and getting regular trims can help minimize damage.

3. How Often Should I Touch Up My Hair Color?

The frequency of touch-ups depends on the color and your natural hair growth. Generally, you should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hair color.

Hair Care Tips to Maintain Your New Hair Color

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your new hair color:

1. Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Using color-safe shampoo and conditioner can help prevent your hair color from fading. Look for products that are specifically formulated for color-treated hair.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can be damaging to your hair and can cause your hair color to fade faster. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This can help your hair color look more vibrant and last longer.

Choosing the right hair color can be a challenging process, but by considering your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color, you can find a shade that complements you perfectly. Remember to be realistic, start small, and consult with a professional if you are unsure. With these tips, you'll be able to find the perfect hair color in no time!

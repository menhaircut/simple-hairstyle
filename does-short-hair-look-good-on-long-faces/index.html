Does Short Hair Look Good On Long Faces

Written by Eveline Jun 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having long hair? Do you have a long face and wonder if short hair would look good on you? If so, you're not alone. Many people with long faces struggle to find the right hairstyle that complements their facial features. In this article, we'll answer the question, "does short hair look good on long faces?" and provide you with tips on how to find the perfect short hairstyle for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long faces are characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and narrow features. Many people with long faces have a hard time finding the right hairstyle that flatters their facial features. Short hair can be a great option for those with long faces, but it's important to choose the right style that complements your face shape.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a long face, it's important to consider your face shape and the features that you want to highlight. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect short hairstyle:

Finding the Right Length

The length of your hair can greatly affect how it looks on your face. If you have a long face, you want to avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can make your face look even longer. Short hairstyles that hit at or above the chin are a great option for those with long faces.

Choosing the Right Cut

The right cut can make all the difference when it comes to a short hairstyle for a long face. You want to choose a cut that helps balance out your facial features. A layered bob or a pixie cut with volume on top can help add width to your face and balance out your long chin.

Adding Texture

Adding texture to your short hairstyle can also help balance out your facial features. A choppy or layered cut can add volume and movement to your hair, helping to soften your long face. Texturizing sprays or pomades can also help add definition and texture to your hair.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to find the perfect short hairstyle for your long face:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Research short hairstyles that flatter your face shape.
  3. Choose a short hairstyle that complements your facial features.
  4. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the right length and cut for your hair.
  5. Add texture to your hair to help balance out your facial features.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for a long face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short.
  • Consider getting bangs to help balance out your facial features.
  • Experiment with different textures and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use styling products to help add volume and movement to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair make my face look even longer?

A: Not necessarily. The right short hairstyle can actually help balance out your facial features and make your face appear shorter.

Q: Should I get bangs?

A: Bangs can be a great option for those with long faces, as they can help balance out your facial features. However, not all bangs are created equal. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the right type of bangs for your face shape.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use styling products to help add volume and texture. If you're using heat tools to style your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Now that you know the answer to the question, "does short hair look good on long faces?" you can confidently choose a short hairstyle that flatters your facial features. Remember to consider your face shape and the features that you want to highlight, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and textures. With the right short hairstyle, you can accentuate your best features and feel confident in your own skin.

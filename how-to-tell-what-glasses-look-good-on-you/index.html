How To Tell What Glasses Look Good On You

Written by Aprilia Jul 15, 2023 · 5 min read

Do you want to know how to choose the right glasses that suit your face shape and style? In this article, we'll give you some tips on how to tell what glasses look good on you.

Determining Your Face Shape

Before choosing the right glasses, you need to know your face shape. There are five basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, and triangle.

To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Take a look at the overall shape of your face.
  3. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  4. Note the length of your face from the top of your forehead to your chin.

Now that you know your face shape, you can choose glasses that complement your features.

Choosing the Right Frame Style

The key to finding the right glasses is to choose a frame that balances your face shape. Here are some tips for choosing the right frame style for your face shape:

  • Round faces look best with angular or rectangular frames.
  • Oval faces can wear almost any frame style.
  • Square faces look best with round or oval frames.
  • Heart-shaped faces look best with bottom-heavy frames or rimless styles.
  • Triangle faces look best with cat-eye or oval frames.

Additionally, you should consider the size of the frame in relation to your face. Small frames can make your face look larger, while large frames can overwhelm a small face.

Considering Your Skin Tone and Hair Color

Another factor to consider when choosing glasses is your skin tone and hair color. Here are some tips for choosing the right color:

  • Cool skin tones look best with silver, black, blue, and pink frames.
  • Warm skin tones look best with gold, brown, red, and orange frames.
  • Blondes look best with warm-colored frames, while brunettes look best with cool-colored frames.
  • Redheads look best with green or blue frames.

However, these are just guidelines. Ultimately, you should choose a color that you feel comfortable wearing.

Reflecting Your Personality

Glasses are not just a functional accessory – they can also reflect your personality. Here are some tips for choosing glasses that match your style:

  • If you have a classic style, choose traditional frames in neutral colors.
  • If you have an edgy style, choose frames with bold shapes and colors.
  • If you have a feminine style, choose frames with delicate details or cat-eye shapes.
  • If you have a sporty style, choose lightweight frames that stay put during physical activity.

Ultimately, you should choose glasses that make you feel confident and comfortable.

Trying on Glasses

Once you have an idea of the frame style, color, and size you want, it's time to try on glasses. Here are some tips for trying on glasses:

  • Bring a friend or family member for a second opinion.
  • Try on several different styles and colors.
  • Make sure the glasses fit your face comfortably and don't slide down your nose.
  • Look at yourself from different angles to see how the glasses look from all sides.

If you're shopping for glasses online, some websites offer virtual try-on tools that allow you to see how the glasses look on your face.

Getting a Prescription

If you need prescription glasses, you'll need to get a prescription from an optometrist or ophthalmologist. Here are some tips for getting a prescription:

  • Get your eyes checked annually to ensure your prescription is up-to-date.
  • Choose a reputable eye doctor with good reviews.
  • Bring your current glasses and any relevant medical records to your appointment.
  • Ask your doctor any questions you have about your prescription or eye health.

Maintaining Your Glasses

Once you have your glasses, it's important to take care of them to keep them in good condition. Here are some tips for maintaining your glasses:

  • Clean your glasses regularly with a microfiber cloth and lens cleaner.
  • Avoid using paper towels or tissues, as they can scratch the lenses.
  • Store your glasses in a case when you're not wearing them.
  • Avoid leaving your glasses in hot or cold temperatures, as extreme temperatures can damage the frames or lenses.

FAQs

What if I have a unique face shape?

If you have a unique face shape, don't worry – there are glasses that will suit you. Look for frames that balance your features, and don't be afraid to try on different styles.

What if I don't like wearing glasses?

If you don't like wearing glasses, you can consider contact lenses or corrective surgery. However, glasses are a stylish and practical accessory that can enhance your look.

Can I wear glasses with any outfit?

Yes, glasses are versatile and can be worn with any outfit. However, you may want to consider coordinating the colors of your glasses and outfit for a cohesive look.

Hair Care Tips for Glasses Wearers

If you wear glasses, you may notice that they can affect your hairstyle. Here are some tips for styling your hair with glasses:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that cover your eyes or compete with your glasses.
  • Consider bangs or layers to frame your face and balance your glasses.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and glasses to find the perfect combination.

With these tips, you can choose glasses that look good on you and feel confident wearing them.

Hair Care

The first step to styling thick frizzy curly hair is to take care of it properly. This means using the right shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, as they will help to define your curls and reduce frizz.

You should also avoid using hot tools on your hair as much as possible, as they can cause damage and make your hair even frizzier. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls gently.

Finally, make sure you get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

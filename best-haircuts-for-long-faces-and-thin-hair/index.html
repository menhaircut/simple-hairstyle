Best Haircuts For Long Faces And Thin Hair

If you have a long face and thin hair, you may find it challenging to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will share with you the best haircuts for long faces and thin hair in 2023. So, let's dive in!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

A long face is characterized by a high forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. If you have thin hair, it means that your hair lacks volume and thickness. The combination of these two features can make it challenging to find the right haircut that flatters your face shape and adds volume to your hair.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for your face shape and hair type is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing the two. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long face shape.

Next, consider your hair type. If you have thin hair, you want a haircut that adds volume and thickness. This can be achieved with layers, bangs, and textured cuts.

Step by step

Here are the best haircuts for long faces and thin hair in 2023:

  1. Long layers: This haircut adds volume and movement to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker. The layers should start at your cheekbones and continue down to the ends of your hair.
  2. Blunt bob: A blunt bob is a classic haircut that adds volume to your hair. The length of the bob should be just above your shoulders, and the ends should be cut bluntly.
  3. Textured pixie: This haircut adds volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker. The hair is cut short on the sides and back and left longer on top.
  4. Side-swept bangs: Bangs can add volume to your hair, and side-swept bangs can flatter your face shape. The bangs should start at your temples and be swept to the side.
  5. Layered lob: A layered lob is a long bob that adds volume and movement to your hair. The layers should start at your chin and continue down to the ends of your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut for long faces and thin hair:

  • Avoid haircuts that are too long, as they can make your face look even longer.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and texture.
  • Use volumizing products, such as mousse or root lift spray, to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy products, such as pomades or waxes, as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a long face?

The best hairstyle for a long face is one that adds width and volume to your hair. This can be achieved with layers, bangs, and textured cuts.

What is the best haircut for thin hair?

The best haircut for thin hair is one that adds volume and thickness to your hair. This can be achieved with layers, bangs, and textured cuts.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips for long faces and thin hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles, such as ponytails or braids, as they can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

With these tips and haircuts, you can rock your long face and thin hair with confidence in 2023!

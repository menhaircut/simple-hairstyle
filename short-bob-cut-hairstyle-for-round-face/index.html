Short Bob Cut Hairstyle For Round Face A Complete Hair Guide

Written by Frank Jun 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a round-faced beauty looking for a new hairstyle that flatters your features? Look no further than the short bob cut. This timeless haircut is versatile, low-maintenance, and perfect for those with round faces. In this blog post, we'll take you through everything you need to know about getting and maintaining the perfect short bob cut hairstyle for round face.

Description

The short bob cut is a classic haircut that has been popular for decades. This hairstyle is defined by its short length, typically falling above the shoulders, and its straight, blunt cut. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, including straight, curled, or textured. For those with round faces, the short bob cut is an excellent choice because it helps to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

How to Get the Short Bob Cut

Getting the short bob cut hairstyle for round face is relatively easy, but it's essential to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting hair for round faces. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose Your Bob Length

Before you head to the salon, decide on the length of your bob. A short bob cut falls anywhere between chin-length to just above the shoulders. Choose a length that flatters your features and complements your personal style.

Step 2: Discuss with Your Stylist

When you get to the salon, discuss your desired length and style with your stylist. They will help you decide on the best cut for your face shape and hair texture.

Step 3: Cut and Style

Your stylist will then cut your hair to the desired length and style it according to your preferences. They may use a razor or scissors to create texture and movement in your hair.

Tips for Maintaining the Short Bob Cut

The short bob cut is a low-maintenance hairstyle, but it still requires some care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short bob cut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Avoid overwashing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

What face shapes suit a short bob cut?

A short bob cut suits most face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on those with round faces. It helps to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

Can I style my short bob cut in different ways?

Yes, the short bob cut is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways, including straight, curled, or textured.

How often should I get my short bob cut trimmed?

It's recommended to get your short bob cut trimmed every six to eight weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Short Bob Cut

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your short bob cut looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid damaging it.

Conclusion

The short bob cut hairstyle for round face is a classic and versatile haircut that's perfect for those with round faces. It's easy to maintain, and there are many different ways to style it. Whether you choose a chin-length bob or a longer bob that falls just above the shoulders, you're sure to love this timeless hairstyle.

Shave Side Of Head Woman Black A Bold And Edgy Hair Trend

Written by Christine Jun 12, 2023 · 4 min read

The shave side of head woman black trend has been around for a few years now, but it still remains one of the most popular and edgy hairstyles for women. This look has been seen on celebrities like Rihanna, Cassie, and Keke Palmer, and it's not hard to see why it's so popular. The shaved side adds a bold and edgy element to any hairstyle, while the longer hair on the other side provides a feminine and glamorous touch. If you're thinking about trying this trend, read on for a step-by-step guide, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Description

The shave side of head woman black look involves shaving one side of your head, while leaving the other side longer. This can be done with clippers or a razor, depending on your preference. The length of the longer side can vary, but it's usually kept around shoulder-length or longer. This look can be customized with different hair colors, textures, and styles, making it a versatile trend that can be adapted to suit any individual.

How to Achieve the Shave Side of Head Woman Black Look

If you're interested in trying the shave side of head woman black trend, here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose Your Side

Decide which side of your head you want to shave. This is a personal preference, but many people choose to shave the side that's opposite their part. This creates a more dramatic effect and allows the longer hair to fall naturally on the other side.

Step 2: Gather Your Supplies

You'll need clippers or a razor, a comb, and hair clippers. Make sure you have all the necessary supplies before you begin.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section off the hair on the side you want to keep long. Clip it up and out of the way so it doesn't get in the way while you're shaving.

Step 4: Start Shaving

Using clippers, start shaving the side of your head that you want to be shaved. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one until you achieve the desired length. Take your time and be careful not to nick your skin.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair on the shaved side with the longer hair on the other side. Use scissors or clippers to create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. You can leave it down, pull it back into a ponytail, or try different braids and updos.

Tips for Maintaining the Shave Side of Head Woman Black Look

Here are a few tips to help you maintain your shave side of head woman black look:

- Keep the shaved side trimmed regularly to maintain the desired length. - Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. - Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth. - Experiment with different hairstyles and hair accessories to change up your look.

FAQs

Is the shave side of head woman black look suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the shave side of head woman black look can be adapted to suit any hair type. However, it's important to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best approach for your hair type and texture.

Will shaving one side of my head damage my hair?

No, shaving one side of your head will not damage your hair. However, it's important to take proper care of your hair to keep it healthy and hydrated.

Can I still wear my hair up with a shave side of head woman black look?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a shave side of head woman black look. Experiment with different updos and braids to create new styles.

Hair Care Advice

Here are a few hair care tips to keep your shave side of head woman black look looking its best:

- Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. - Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to combat dryness and frizz. - Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair. - Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective hair product.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the shave side of head woman black look with confidence and style. Whether you're looking for a bold and edgy look or just want to switch up your hairstyle, this trend is sure to turn heads and make a statement.

Easy Hairstyles For Medium Hair How To Achieve Them

Written by William Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will be discussing some easy hairstyles for medium hair that you can do yourself at home. These hairstyles are perfect for those who want to add some variety to their daily look without spending too much time or effort on their hair. Whether you have a special occasion coming up or just want to switch things up, these hairstyles will help you achieve the look you want. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to know the basics of styling medium hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Invest in some good quality hair products such as hairspray, mousse, and hair oil to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you.

Step by Step

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for medium hair. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure with an elastic band.
  3. Twist your hair and wrap it around the elastic band, securing it with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out some pieces of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair out of their face but still show off their length. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it into a small ponytail with an elastic band.
  3. Fluff up the ponytail to create volume.
  4. Take a small piece of hair from the underside of the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band to hide it.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Fishtail Braid

The fishtail braid is perfect for those who want a boho-chic look. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into two equal sections.
  3. Take a small piece of hair from the outside of the left section and cross it over to the inside of the right section.
  4. Repeat on the other side, taking a small piece of hair from the outside of the right section and crossing it over to the inside of the left section.
  5. Continue the pattern until you reach the end of your hair and secure with an elastic band.
  6. Pull out some pieces of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  7. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Experiment with different hair accessories such as hair clips, headbands, and scrunchies to add some extra flair to your hairstyles.
  • Don't forget to take care of your hair by using a good quality shampoo and conditioner and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Try out different hairstyles on different days to keep things interesting.

FAQ

1. What products do I need to achieve these hairstyles?

You will need a good quality hairspray, mousse, and hair oil to help you achieve your desired look.


2. How long will these hairstyles last?

These hairstyles should last all day if you use enough hairspray to hold them in place.


3. Can I achieve these hairstyles if I have fine hair?

Yes, these hairstyles should work for all hair types, but you may need to use more product to achieve the desired look.


Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.

We hope this article has given you some inspiration for easy hairstyles for medium hair. Remember to experiment and have fun with your hair, and don't be afraid to try new things!

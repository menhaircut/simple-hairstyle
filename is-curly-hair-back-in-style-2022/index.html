Is Curly Hair Back In Style 2022

Written by Robby Sep 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Curly hair has been a trend since the early 2000s, but it seems to be making a comeback in 2022. Celebrities and influencers alike have been sporting their natural curls, and it's not just for women. Men are also embracing their curly locks. In this blog post, we'll explore whether curly hair is back in style for 2022 and how to embrace your natural curls.

Description

Curly hair has always been a topic of discussion in the fashion industry. Some years it's in style, and other years it's not. However, in 2022, curly hair is definitely back in style. Celebrities like Zendaya, Tracee Ellis Ross, and Ariana Grande are rocking their natural curls, and it's inspiring others to do the same. The curly hair trend is not just for women, but men are also embracing their curls.

How to Embrace Your Natural Curls

If you have curly hair, you might be wondering how to embrace your natural curls. Here are a few tips:

1. Find the Right Haircut

The right haircut can make a world of difference for curly hair. If you have long hair, consider getting layers to add volume and shape. If you have short hair, consider getting a pixie cut or a bob that accentuates your curls.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right products is essential for maintaining healthy, bouncy curls. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, like curl creams or gels. Avoid using products that contain sulfates or alcohol, which can dry out your hair and cause frizz.

3. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important not to overwash your hair. Washing your hair every other day or every three days can help maintain the natural oils in your hair, which can prevent frizz and dryness.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to embracing your natural curls:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and free of any product buildup. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, like a curl cream or gel, to wet hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Let Your Hair Air Dry

Air drying your hair is the best way to maintain your natural curls. Avoid using a hairdryer or towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, let your hair air dry naturally.

4. Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, scrunch it to enhance your curls. Use your hands or a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to scrunch your hair and add volume.

5. Finish with Hair Spray

Finish off your curly hair look with a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips to Maintain Your Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to maintain your curly hair:

1. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help reduce frizz and prevent hair breakage. Silk is a smooth fabric that won't tug or pull on your hair, which can cause damage.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

3. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 8-12 weeks.

FAQs

1. Can I Embrace My Natural Curls if I Have Straight Hair?

If you have straight hair, you can still embrace your natural curls. There are many ways to create curls, like using a curling iron or getting a perm. However, it's important to note that these methods can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to use them sparingly.

2. Can I Still Straighten My Hair if I Have Curly Hair?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair if you have curly hair. However, it's important to use a heat protectant spray and avoid straightening your hair too often, as it can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a bit more care than straight hair. Here are a few tips for taking care of curly hair:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help keep your curls hydrated and healthy. Look for a treatment that is specifically designed for curly hair.

2. Avoid Brushing Your Hair

Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.

3. Use a Satin or Silk Scarf

Using a satin or silk scarf to wrap your hair at night can help reduce frizz and prevent hair breakage. It's also a great way to maintain your curls overnight.

Conclusion

Curly hair is definitely back in style for 2022. Whether you have natural curls or want to create them, there are many ways to embrace this trend. With the right haircut, styling products, and hair care routine, you can achieve healthy, bouncy curls that will turn heads.

Short Haircuts For Long Faces 2021 A Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a long face, you may be wondering what the best short haircuts are for your face shape. Fortunately, there are plenty of options that can flatter your features and accentuate your natural beauty. In this guide, we'll explore some of the top short haircuts for long faces in 2021, as well as tips for styling and caring for your new 'do.

Description

A long face is characterized by a narrow, elongated shape that's longer than it is wide. To balance out this shape, it's important to choose a haircut that adds width and volume to the sides of the face. Short haircuts are ideal for long faces, as they can help create the illusion of a wider, more balanced face shape.

How To

The first step in choosing a short haircut for a long face is to consider your hair type and texture. Some cuts may work better for fine hair, while others are better suited for thick or curly hair. Once you've determined your hair type, you can start exploring different styles and lengths.

Step by Step

1. Start by gathering inspiration. Look for photos of short haircuts on people with similar face shapes and hair types to yours.

2. Consult with a hairstylist. A professional can help you choose a cut that flatters your features and works with your hair type and texture.

3. Consider your lifestyle. If you're busy and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance cut.

4. Experiment with different styling techniques. Play around with styling products, tools, and techniques to find a look that works for you.

Tips

When choosing a short haircut for a long face, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Choose a cut that adds width to the sides of the face, such as a bob or layered pixie cut.
  • Avoid cuts that are too long or too short, as these can elongate the face even more.
  • Add volume and texture with styling products and techniques.
  • Consider adding bangs to help balance out the face shape.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair short if I have a long face?
A: Absolutely! Short haircuts can be incredibly flattering for long faces, as long as you choose the right style for your features.

Q: What are some good short haircuts for long faces?
A: Some of the best short haircuts for long faces include bobs, pixie cuts, and layered cuts with volume at the sides.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen your new short haircut, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using styling tools like flat irons or curling wands.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With the right short haircut and some simple hair care tips, you can rock a stylish and flattering look that highlights your natural beauty.

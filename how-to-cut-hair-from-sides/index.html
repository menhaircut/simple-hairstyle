How To Cut Hair From Sides A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a fresh new look or just want to trim your hair, cutting the sides of your hair is a great place to start. It's a simple process that you can do at home with the right tools and technique. In this article, we'll show you how to cut hair from sides step-by-step.

How to Cut Hair from Sides

Cutting hair from sides is a simple process that requires only a few tools. Before you start, make sure you have the following:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your hair from sides:

  1. Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly. Wet hair can be difficult to cut.
  2. Part your hair down the middle and clip one side away from the other.
  3. Starting at the bottom of your hair, comb a small section straight down and hold it between your fingers.
  4. Trim the ends of the hair with the scissors. Start with a small amount and trim more if necessary.
  5. Move up the section and continue to trim the hair until you reach the top.
  6. Repeat on the other side.
  7. Check both sides in the mirror to ensure they are even. If not, make adjustments as necessary.
  8. Blend the sides together by combing the hair straight down and trimming any longer pieces.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Hair from Sides

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Practice on a small section of hair before cutting the entire side.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to trim a little bit at a time.
  • For a more professional look, consider using thinning shears to blend the hair together.

FAQs

How often should I cut my hair from sides?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your haircut. Generally, it's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

Can I cut my hair from sides with regular scissors?

While it's possible to cut your hair with regular scissors, it's not recommended. Hair cutting scissors are designed to be sharper and more precise, which will give you better results.

Hair Care After Cutting Hair from Sides

After cutting your hair from sides, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to cut your hair from sides like a pro. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll be rewarded with a fresh new look.

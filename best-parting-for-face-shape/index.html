Best Parting For Face Shape The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a simple way to change up your hairstyle, changing your parting can make a big difference. But did you know that the best parting for your face shape can actually enhance your features and make you look more polished? In this article, we'll go over the best parting for each face shape and give you some tips for making the most of your new look.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best parting for each face shape, it's important to determine what your face shape actually is. Here's a quick guide to help you:

  • Round: Your face is nearly as wide as it is long, with soft curves and a rounded chin.
  • Square: Your face is nearly as wide as it is long, with a strong jawline and angular features.
  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with soft curves and a slightly rounded chin.
  • Heart: Your face is wider at the forehead and temples, with a narrow chin.
  • Diamond: Your face is widest at the cheekbones, with a narrow forehead and chin.

Best Parting for Round Face

If you have a round face, you'll want to create the illusion of length and angles. The best parting for you is a deep side part or an off-center part.

By creating a diagonal line across your forehead, you'll create the illusion of length and add some angles to your face. Avoid center parts, as they can make your face look rounder.

Best Parting for Square Face

If you have a square face, you'll want to soften your strong jawline and create some angles. The best parting for you is a side part or a center part.

By creating a diagonal line across your forehead with a side part, you'll soften the angles of your face. Alternatively, a center part can create some symmetry and balance out your features.

Best Parting for Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! Your face shape is well-balanced and symmetrical, so any parting will work well for you.

Best Parting for Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to create some balance between your forehead and chin. The best parting for you is a side part or a center part.

By creating some symmetry with a center part, you'll balance out your features. Alternatively, a side part can create some angles and draw attention away from your forehead.

Best Parting for Diamond Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you'll want to create some balance between your forehead and chin. The best parting for you is a side part or a center part.

By creating some symmetry with a center part, you'll balance out your features. Alternatively, a side part can create some angles and draw attention away from your forehead.

Tips for Choosing the Right Parting

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing the best parting for your face shape:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness. Some partings may be more flattering for fine hair, while others may work well for thick hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to see what looks best on you.
  • Don't be afraid to switch up your parting from day to day!

FAQ

What if I have a different face shape?

While the face shapes we've covered are the most common, there are a few other face shapes out there. If you're not sure what your face shape is, try taking a selfie and drawing a line around your face. This can help you see your face shape more clearly.

Can I part my hair in the middle if I have a round face?

While a center part can work for some people with round faces, it's generally not the most flattering option. A deep side part or an off-center part will create more angles and length, making your face appear slimmer.

Can I change my parting if I have a specific hairstyle?

Depending on your hairstyle, you may be limited in terms of where you can part your hair. However, you can still experiment with different partings within those limitations to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Parting

Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind when parting your hair:

  • Use a comb to create a precise parting.
  • Switch up your parting from day to day to prevent breakage.
  • Use a volumizing product at the roots to create more lift and volume.
  • Don't pull your hair too tightly when creating a parting, as this can cause hair loss over time.

Now that you know the best parting for your face shape, it's time to experiment and find your new favorite look!

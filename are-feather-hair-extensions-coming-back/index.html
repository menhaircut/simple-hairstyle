Are Feather Hair Extensions Coming Back In 2023

Written by Bardi Aug 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Feather hair extensions were a popular hair trend a few years back, but are they making a comeback in 2023? If you're curious about whether feather hair extensions are back in style, you've come to the right place. In this blog post, we'll discuss everything you need to know about feather hair extensions, including how to wear them, where to buy them, and how to take care of them.

Description

Feather hair extensions are small feathers that are attached to your hair with a small clamp or bead. They're usually made from real feathers, but synthetic options are also available. Feather hair extensions come in a variety of colors, patterns, and lengths, so you can choose the ones that best suit your style.

How to Wear Feather Hair Extensions

Wearing feather hair extensions is easy and straightforward. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Feathers

Choose feathers that match your hair color and style. You can also mix and match different feathers to create a unique look.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section the area of your hair where you want to attach the feather. Use a comb or hair clip to separate the hair.

Step 3: Attach the Feather

Thread the bead or clamp through the feather and then slip it onto the sectioned hair. Use pliers to clamp the bead closed. Repeat the process with as many feathers as you want.

Tips for Wearing Feather Hair Extensions

Here are some tips to help you rock your feather hair extensions:

  • Choose feathers that complement your hair color and length.
  • Don't overdo it. A few feathers are enough to make a statement.
  • Don't wash your hair for at least 24 hours after attaching the feathers.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the feathers.
  • Remove the feathers gently to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about feather hair extensions:

Are feather hair extensions safe?

Yes, feather hair extensions are safe to wear. However, you should be careful not to attach too many feathers to your hair, as this can cause damage.

How long do feather hair extensions last?

Feather hair extensions can last up to 8 weeks, depending on how well you take care of them.

Can I wash my hair with feather hair extensions?

Yes, you can wash your hair with feather hair extensions, but you should avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to help you take care of your feather hair extensions:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the feathers.
  • Don't sleep on your feathers.
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid damaging the feathers.

Conclusion

So, are feather hair extensions coming back in 2023? The answer is yes! Feather hair extensions are a fun and easy way to add some flair to your hair. With the right feathers and proper care, you can enjoy your feather hair extensions for weeks to come.

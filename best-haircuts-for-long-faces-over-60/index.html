Best Haircuts For Long Faces Over 60

Description

As we age, our hair tends to thin out and lose its volume. This is especially true for those with long faces over 60. However, just because your hair is thinning and your face is elongated doesn't mean you can't rock a fabulous haircut.

In this article, we'll share with you some of the best haircuts for long faces over 60. Whether you prefer a short, medium, or long hairstyle, we've got you covered.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Long Faces Over 60

First, it's important to determine your face shape. If you have a long face, you'll want to choose a haircut that creates the illusion of width. This can be achieved by adding layers, bangs, or volume to the sides of your face.

When choosing a haircut, it's also important to consider your hair type and texture. Thin, fine hair will require a different cut than thick, coarse hair. A good hairstylist will be able to help you choose a haircut that works best for your hair type and face shape.

Best Haircuts for Long Faces Over 60

Short Haircuts

A short haircut can be a great option for those with long faces over 60. A pixie cut, for example, can add volume to the top of your head and create the illusion of width. A bob or lob can also work well, especially if you add layers or side-swept bangs.

Medium Haircuts

If you prefer a medium-length haircut, consider a shoulder-length cut with layers or a side-swept fringe. This will add volume to the sides of your face and create the illusion of width.

Long Haircuts

Long hair can still work for those with long faces over 60, but it's important to keep it well-maintained. Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, while side-swept bangs can help balance out your face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide for the Best Haircuts for Long Faces Over 60

1. Determine your face shape.

2. Consider your hair type and texture.

3. Choose a haircut that adds volume or width to the sides of your face.

4. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

5. Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims and using the right hair care products.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.

2. Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and prevent frizz.

3. Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

4. Consider getting a color treatment to add depth and dimension to your hair.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for a long face over 60?

The best haircut for a long face over 60 is one that adds volume or width to the sides of your face. This can be achieved with layers, bangs, or volume at the crown of your head.

Can I still have long hair with a long face over 60?

Yes, you can still have long hair with a long face over 60. However, it's important to keep it well-maintained with regular trims and the right hair care products.

What hair care products should I use for long hair over 60?

You should use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, a hair serum or oil, and a heat protectant if you use hot styling tools. It's also important to get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Long Faces Over 60

1. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

2. Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

3. Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

4. Consider getting a deep conditioning treatment once a month to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

5. Eat a healthy, balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can look and feel your best with long faces over 60.

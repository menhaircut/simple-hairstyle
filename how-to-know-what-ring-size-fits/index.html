How To Know What Ring Size Fits A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 16, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you planning to buy a ring for yourself or your loved one but don't know what size to get? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps on how to know what ring size fits. From measuring your finger size to using a ring size chart, we've got all the information you need. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Measure Your Ring Size
  • Using a Ring Size Chart
  • Tips for Finding the Right Ring Size
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Measure Your Ring Size

Before you go out and buy a ring, the first thing you need to do is measure your finger size. Here are the steps:

  1. Get a piece of string or paper and wrap it around the base of your finger.
  2. Mark the point where the string/paper overlaps.
  3. Measure the length of the string/paper from the starting point to the overlapping point in millimeters.
  4. Use the chart below to determine your ring size based on the measurement.

Here's a quick tip: measure your finger size at the end of the day when your fingers are slightly larger due to swelling.

Using a Ring Size Chart

If you already have a ring that fits you perfectly, you can use it to determine your ring size using a ring size chart. Here's how:

  1. Get a ruler and measure the inside diameter of the ring in millimeters.
  2. Use the chart above to determine your ring size based on the inside diameter measurement.

Tips for Finding the Right Ring Size

Here are some tips to help you find the right ring size:

  • Measure your finger size at the end of the day when your fingers are slightly larger due to swelling.
  • If you're between sizes, always go for the larger size to ensure a comfortable fit.
  • If you're buying a wide band ring, you may need to go up half a size to accommodate the width.
  • If you're buying a ring as a surprise gift, try to borrow a ring from the recipient's jewelry box and use it to determine their ring size.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I'm still unsure of my ring size?

If you're still unsure of your ring size, you can visit a jewelry store and ask them to measure your finger size for you. Alternatively, you can order a ring sizer online and measure your finger size at home.

Do ring sizes vary by country?

Yes, ring sizes vary by country. The chart above shows the ring sizes for the US, UK, and EU. If you're buying a ring from a different country, make sure to check their ring size chart.

Can I resize a ring if it doesn't fit?

Yes, most rings can be resized by a jeweler. However, some rings with intricate designs may be difficult to resize without damaging the design. It's always best to check with the jeweler first before purchasing a ring.

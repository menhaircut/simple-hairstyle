What To Tell Your Barber For Waves A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 20, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of having a plain and boring hairstyle? Do you want to switch things up and achieve a wavy look? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll be discussing what to tell your barber for waves, so you can achieve the hairstyle of your dreams. Read on to learn more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Waves are a popular hairstyle that involves creating a pattern of waves in the hair. This can be achieved through brushing, using products, and getting a haircut that’s designed for waves. To achieve this look, you’ll need to communicate with your barber effectively so they can help you achieve the desired results.

How To

Before you head to the barber, it’s important to know what you want. If you’re not sure what kind of waves you want, do some research beforehand. Look at pictures of hairstyles you like and bring them with you to the barber. This will give them an idea of what you’re looking for.

Step-by-Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on what to tell your barber for waves:

  1. Ask for a short to medium length haircut
  2. Specify that you want a haircut that’s designed for waves
  3. Ask for a low or mid fade
  4. Request that your hair be cut at an angle
  5. Ask your barber to use a wave brush to create the pattern
  6. Ask your barber to apply a wave pomade or cream to hold the waves in place

By following these steps, you’ll be well on your way to achieving the wavy hairstyle you’ve always wanted.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut for waves:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before getting it cut
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your barber questions
  • Be honest about what you want and don’t want
  • Take care of your hair by using products designed for waves

FAQ

Q: How often should I get my hair cut for waves?

A: It’s recommended that you get a haircut every 2-3 weeks to maintain the wave pattern.

Q: Can I achieve waves without getting a haircut?

A: While it’s possible to achieve waves without getting a haircut, it’s much easier to do with a haircut that’s designed for waves.

Hair Care

Once you’ve achieved the wavy hairstyle you want, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain the look. Use products designed for waves, such as wave pomade or cream, to hold the waves in place. Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause the waves to flatten. Use a wave brush to keep the pattern in place and avoid using a regular brush or comb, as this can disrupt the pattern.

By following these tips and communicating effectively with your barber, you’ll be able to achieve the wavy hairstyle of your dreams. Good luck!

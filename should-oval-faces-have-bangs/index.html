Description

Oval faces are characterized by their balanced proportions and gently rounded shape. Those with oval faces have a forehead that is slightly wider than their chin, and their cheekbones are typically high and well-defined. Bangs can be a great way to add some texture and volume to the hair, but not all bangs will work for oval faces.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in deciding whether or not to get bangs is to determine your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back away from your face. Look at the shape of your face and compare it to the following descriptions:

  • Oval: Face length is greater than the width of the cheekbones, and forehead is wider than the jawline.
  • Round: Prominent cheekbones and a rounded chin.
  • Square: Prominent jaw and forehead, with roughly equal width and length.
  • Heart: Wide forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow jawline.
  • Diamond: Narrow forehead and jawline, with wide cheekbones.

Can oval faces pull off blunt bangs?

Yes, oval faces can definitely pull off blunt bangs! Just make sure to consider your forehead size and hair texture before making the decision.

What types of bangs should oval faces avoid?

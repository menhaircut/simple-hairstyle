Is It Haram To Braid Your Hair With Yarn

Written by Eveline Dec 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Braiding hair is a popular hairstyle choice among women, and it can be done using various materials such as natural hair, synthetic hair, and even yarn. However, for Muslim women, the question arises whether it is haram to braid hair with yarn. In this blog post, we will explore this topic in detail and provide you with all the necessary information to make an informed decision.

Description

Yarn braids are a popular hairstyle among African American women, and it involves braiding natural hair with yarn instead of extensions. The yarn is wrapped around the hair in a spiral pattern, creating a unique and beautiful look. However, for Muslim women, there is a debate about whether it is haram or not.

How to

If you are interested in trying out yarn braids, here are the steps to follow:

Step 1:

Wash and condition your hair thoroughly, and then blow-dry it.

Step 2:

Divide your hair into small sections and braid it tightly.

Step 3:

Cut the yarn into equal lengths, and then wrap it tightly around each braid.

Step 4:

Secure the ends of the yarn with a knot, and then trim any excess.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when braiding your hair with yarn:

  • Use high-quality yarn that is soft and won't cause any irritation to your scalp.
  • Be gentle when braiding your hair, as tight braids can cause hair breakage and damage.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent dryness.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about braiding hair with yarn:

Q: Is it haram to braid your hair with yarn?

A: There is no clear answer to this question, as it depends on the interpretation of Islamic law. Some scholars argue that using yarn to braid hair is haram, while others believe it is permissible as long as the yarn is not made from silk or gold.

Q: Can yarn braids damage your hair?

A: Yes, yarn braids can cause hair breakage and damage if they are braided too tightly or left in for too long. It is important to be gentle when braiding your hair and to take breaks between styles to allow your hair to rest.

Hair Care

Whether you decide to braid your hair with yarn or not, it is important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy growth.

In conclusion, whether it is haram to braid your hair with yarn is a controversial topic, and it ultimately depends on your personal beliefs and interpretations of Islamic law. However, if you do decide to try out this hairstyle, make sure to take good care of your hair and be gentle when braiding it.

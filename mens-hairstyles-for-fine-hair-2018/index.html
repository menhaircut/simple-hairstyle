Mens Hairstyles For Fine Hair 2018 A Guide For Men With Fine Hair

Written by Eveline Jul 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man with fine hair, you're not alone. Many men face the challenge of finding the perfect hairstyle to suit their hair type. But with the right haircut and styling products, you can make your fine hair work for you. In this article, we'll discuss the best mens hairstyles for fine hair in 2018, and provide tips on how to achieve and maintain the look you want.

Description

When it comes to mens hairstyles for fine hair, it's all about creating the illusion of volume and thickness. You want a hairstyle that adds texture and movement to your hair, while still looking stylish and modern. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can achieve a full and voluminous look that will make your fine hair look its best.

How to

The first step in achieving the perfect mens hairstyle for fine hair is to choose the right haircut. A shorter haircut can help to create the illusion of thicker hair by removing any straggly ends and creating more volume at the crown of the head. A classic buzz cut or crew cut is a great option for men with fine hair, as it requires minimal styling and can be easily maintained at home.

If you prefer longer hair, opt for a layered cut that adds movement and texture to your hair. Ask your barber to add layers around the crown of your head, which will help to create the illusion of fuller hair. Avoid heavy, blunt cuts that can make your hair look flat and lifeless.

Step by Step

Once you have the right haircut, it's time to style your hair to perfection. Start by applying a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to your hair to add body and texture. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and then blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting, using your fingers to create volume and lift at the roots.

Next, apply a volumizing mousse or styling cream to your hair, focusing on the roots and crown of your head. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, creating texture and movement. Finish off with a light-hold hairspray to set your style in place.

Tips

When it comes to styling mens hairstyles for fine hair, there are a few tips and tricks that can make all the difference. First, avoid using heavy, greasy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Instead, opt for lightweight products that add texture and volume to your hair.

Second, avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look dry and lifeless. Instead, try washing your hair every other day, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh and clean.

FAQ

What are the best mens hairstyles for fine hair in 2018?

The best mens hairstyles for fine hair in 2018 are those that create the illusion of volume and thickness. Shorter haircuts like the buzz cut and crew cut are great options, as they require minimal styling and can be easily maintained at home. Longer haircuts with layers around the crown of the head can also add movement and texture to fine hair.

What styling products are best for mens hairstyles for fine hair?

The best styling products for mens hairstyles for fine hair are lightweight products that add texture and volume to your hair. Volumizing mousses and styling creams are great options, as they create the illusion of fuller hair without weighing it down. Hairsprays with a light hold are also great for setting your style in place without making your hair look stiff or crunchy.

Hair Care

To keep your fine hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair, and avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair. Try to wash your hair every other day, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh and clean. And don't forget to visit your barber regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your hair looking sharp and stylish.

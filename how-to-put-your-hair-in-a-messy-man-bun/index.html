How To Put Your Hair In A Messy Man Bun

Written by Frank Sep 08, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion, a messy man bun is a great option. Not only is it stylish and trendy, but it's also practical and comfortable. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to put your hair in a messy man bun.

Description

A messy man bun is a casual hairstyle that involves pulling your hair back into a bun at the crown of your head. The key to achieving the messy look is to leave some hair loose and tousled, which gives the hairstyle a relaxed, effortless vibe.

How to

Here are the steps to follow to put your hair in a messy man bun:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before attempting to put it into a man bun. This will make it easier to manage and style.

Step 2: Brush your hair back

Use a brush or comb to brush your hair back and away from your face. Make sure to get rid of any tangles or knots.

Step 3: Gather your hair at the crown of your head

Use your hands to gather your hair at the crown of your head, as if you were about to make a ponytail. However, instead of pulling all of your hair through the elastic, leave some strands loose to create a messy look.

Step 4: Twist your hair

Take the hair that you've gathered and twist it around itself, creating a bun shape. Use your fingers to keep the bun in place.

Step 5: Secure with an elastic

Once you've twisted your hair into a bun shape, use an elastic to secure it in place. Make sure to wrap the elastic around the bun several times to ensure that it's secure.

Step 6: Pull out some strands

To achieve the messy look, pull out some strands of hair from the bun. You can also tug on the bun itself to loosen it up and make it look more casual.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when putting your hair in a messy man bun:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color for a more seamless look.
  • Experiment with different bun shapes to find the one that works best for you.
  • Add some texture to your hair by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.
  • Don't worry too much about making your bun perfect – a messy look is the goal!

FAQ

How can I make my messy man bun stay in place?

Use a strong hair tie and bobby pins to secure your bun in place. You can also use hairspray to set the style.

Can I wear a messy man bun to a formal event?

While a messy man bun is typically a casual hairstyle, it can be dressed up for a formal event. Use a comb to smooth down any flyaways and make the bun neater.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent tangles.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and nutrients to promote healthy hair growth.

That's it – now you know how to put your hair in a messy man bun! Give it a try and see how it looks on you.

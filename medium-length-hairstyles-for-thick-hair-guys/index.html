Medium Length Hairstyles For Thick Hair Guys

Written by Aprilia Sep 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Thick hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it may give you the chance to experiment with different hairstyles, it can also be challenging to manage. If you're a guy with thick hair, you might find it difficult to find the right hairstyle that suits your hair type. One popular option for men is medium length hairstyles. In this article, we'll be discussing the best medium length hairstyles for guys with thick hair.

Description

Medium length hairstyles are perfect for guys who want to keep their hair long enough to style but not too long that it becomes unmanageable. These hairstyles are versatile and can be styled in various ways, making them perfect for any occasion. For guys with thick hair, medium length hairstyles provide the right balance of volume and texture.

How to

To achieve the perfect medium length hairstyle, you need to follow a few simple steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will ensure that your hair is clean, smooth, and easy to manage.
  2. Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and texture. Make sure that your hair is completely dry before you start styling it.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair wax, pomade, or gel to your hair, depending on the level of hold and shine you want to achieve.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair in the desired direction. You can create a messy, textured look or a sleek, polished style.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray or hair serum to hold the style in place and add shine.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style three of the best medium length hairstyles for guys with thick hair:

1. The Messy Quiff

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to your hair, focusing on the top and front sections.
  3. Use your fingers to create a messy, textured look by pulling your hair upwards and towards the back.
  4. Use a comb or brush to smooth down the sides and back.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. The Side Part

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of gel or wax to your hair, focusing on the top and front sections.
  3. Use a comb to create a side part, and comb the hair on the longer side towards the back.
  4. Use your fingers to create a textured look by pulling the hair on the shorter side upwards and towards the back.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. The Textured Crop

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair wax to your hair, focusing on the top and front sections.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to create a messy, textured look by pulling your hair in different directions.
  4. Use a pair of scissors to trim any excess hair around the ears and neckline.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray or hair serum to hold the style in place and add shine.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling medium length hairstyles for guys with thick hair:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and manageable.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot styling tools.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different products and styling techniques to find the perfect look.
  • Visit a professional stylist for regular trims and maintenance.

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for guys with thick hair?

The best hairstyles for guys with thick hair are those that provide volume and texture, such as medium length hairstyles, layered haircuts, and textured crops.

How do I style medium length hair?

To style medium length hair, start by washing and blow-drying your hair, then apply a small amount of hair product and use a comb or your fingers to style your hair in the desired direction.

What hair products should I use for medium length hair?

The best hair products for medium length hair are those that provide hold and texture, such as hair wax, pomade, and gel. Choose a product that suits your hair type and desired style.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy, strong, and manageable, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Get enough sleep and exercise regularly to reduce stress and promote hair growth.
  • Visit a professional stylist for regular trims and maintenance.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style medium length hairstyles for guys with thick hair. Whether you prefer a messy quiff, a side part, or a textured crop, there's a hairstyle out there that will suit your style and personality.

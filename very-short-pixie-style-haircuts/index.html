Very Short Pixie Style Haircuts How To Make A Bold Statement With Your Hair

Written by Petter Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a bold and daring hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd, then a very short pixie style haircut might be just what you need. This trendy hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years, and for good reason. Not only is it a low-maintenance style, but it's also versatile and can be customized to suit your personal style. In this article, we'll tell you everything you need to know about very short pixie style haircuts, from how to get the look to how to take care of your new hairstyle.

Description: What is a Very Short Pixie Style Haircut?

A pixie cut typically refers to a short hairstyle that's cropped close to the head. However, a very short pixie style haircut takes it a step further by being even shorter, often with layers and textured ends. This style can be worn with or without bangs, and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style.

How to: Getting a Very Short Pixie Style Haircut

If you're ready to take the plunge and get a very short pixie style haircut, here's what you need to know:

1. Find a reputable stylist

Look for a stylist who has experience with very short hairstyles, and who you can trust to give you the cut you want. Bring in pictures of the style you're interested in, and be clear about what you want.

2. Consider your face shape

Very short pixie style haircuts can be customized to suit your face shape, so think about what will work best for you. For example, if you have a round face, you might want to opt for a style with more height on top to elongate your face.

3. Prepare for upkeep

While very short pixie style haircuts are low-maintenance, they do require regular upkeep. You'll need to get regular trims to keep the style looking sharp, and you may need to touch up the color more frequently to keep it looking fresh.

Step by Step: How to Style a Very Short Pixie Style Haircut

Styling a very short pixie style haircut is simple and easy. Here's how to do it:

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry until it's damp but not dripping wet.

2. Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or pomade, to your hair to add texture and hold.

3. Use your fingers to style

Use your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape, adding volume on top and smoothing the sides and back.

4. Finish with hairspray

Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips: How to Maintain a Very Short Pixie Style Haircut

Maintaining a very short pixie style haircut is easy with these tips:

1. Get regular trims

To keep your haircut looking sharp, get regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

2. Protect your hair from damage

Protect your hair from damage by using a heat protectant spray when using hot tools, and avoiding excessive heat styling.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQ: Common Questions About Very Short Pixie Style Haircuts

Q: Will a very short pixie style haircut suit me?

A: Very short pixie style haircuts can be customized to suit any face shape and personal style. Talk to your stylist to find a look that will work for you.

Q: Is a very short pixie style haircut low-maintenance?

A: Yes, very short pixie style haircuts are low-maintenance and easy to style.

Q: Can I still style my hair if it's very short?

A: Yes, very short pixie style haircuts can still be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and smooth to textured and messy.

Hair Care: Products to Use for a Very Short Pixie Style Haircut

To keep your very short pixie style haircut looking its best, use these hair care products:

1. Shampoo and conditioner

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Styling product

Use a styling product, such as a mousse or pomade, to add texture and hold to your hair.

3. Hairspray

Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

A very short pixie style haircut is a bold and daring hairstyle that's perfect for those who want to make a statement with their hair. With the right cut, styling techniques, and hair care products, you can rock this look with confidence. Talk to your stylist today to find out if a very short pixie style haircut is right for you.

Shoulder Length Haircut With Long Layers A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look In 2023

Written by William Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to update your hairstyle for 2023, consider getting a shoulder length haircut with long layers. This versatile haircut can be styled in a variety of ways and is perfect for those who want to add some volume and movement to their hair. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about getting a shoulder length haircut with long layers, including how to style it, tips for maintenance, and more.

Description

A shoulder length haircut with long layers is a hairstyle that features layers that start at the collarbone and gradually get longer towards the ends. This type of haircut is perfect for those who want to add some volume and movement to their hair without going too short. Long layers are also great for those who want to add some dimension to their hair, as they can create the illusion of depth and texture.

How to Get a Shoulder Length Haircut with Long Layers

If you're interested in getting a shoulder length haircut with long layers, the first step is to find a good hair stylist who is experienced in creating this type of haircut. When you go in for your appointment, be sure to bring in some reference photos to show your stylist what you're looking for. From there, your stylist will work with you to create a customized version of the haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture.

During the haircut, your stylist will start by cutting your hair to the desired length. From there, they will create long layers using a technique called point cutting, which involves cutting at an angle to create a soft, textured look. Your stylist may also use a razor to create even more texture and movement.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Shoulder Length Haircut with Long Layers

One of the best things about a shoulder length haircut with long layers is that it's incredibly versatile. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your new haircut:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can blow dry your hair with a round brush to add some volume and movement.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant

Before you use any hot tools on your hair, be sure to apply a heat protectant to prevent damage.

Step 3: Curl your hair

Using a curling iron, curl your hair in sections. For a natural look, alternate the direction of the curls.

Step 4: Add some texture

To add even more texture and movement to your hair, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finally, finish off your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining a Shoulder Length Haircut with Long Layers

To keep your shoulder length haircut with long layers looking its best, here are a few tips:

  • Regular trims: To maintain the shape of your haircut, be sure to get regular trims every 6-8 weeks.
  • Use a deep conditioner: Long layers can be prone to dryness, so be sure to use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid over-styling: While a shoulder length haircut with long layers is versatile, it's important not to over-style your hair. Too much heat and product can damage your hair over time.

FAQ

Is a shoulder length haircut with long layers suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Can I still wear my hair straight with a shoulder length haircut with long layers?

Absolutely! This haircut can be styled straight, curly, or anything in between.

Do I need to use a lot of product to style my hair?

Not necessarily. A little bit of texturizing spray or dry shampoo can go a long way in adding volume and movement to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect shoulder length haircut with long layers in 2023. Whether you're looking to add some volume and movement to your hair or simply want a fresh new look, this versatile haircut is sure to turn heads.

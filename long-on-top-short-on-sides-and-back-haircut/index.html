Long On Top Short On Sides And Back Haircut A Timeless Look

Written by Daniel Sep 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is both classic and trendy? Look no further than the long on top short on sides and back haircut. This versatile style has been popular for decades and continues to be a go-to for men of all ages. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this iconic cut, from how to achieve it to tips for maintaining it.

Description

The long on top short on sides and back haircut is exactly what it sounds like: longer hair on the top of the head, with shorter hair on the sides and back. This creates a contrast in length that is both stylish and practical. The longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, textured style. The shorter sides and back make this cut low-maintenance and easy to manage.

How To Achieve The Look

The long on top short on sides and back haircut can be achieved in a few different ways. If you have longer hair, you can simply have the sides and back trimmed shorter by a barber or hairstylist. If you have shorter hair, you may need to grow it out on top before you can achieve the desired length contrast. Either way, it is important to communicate your desired style to your stylist or barber to ensure they understand the look you are going for.

Step By Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the long on top short on sides and back haircut:

  1. Decide on the length of hair you want on top and the length you want on the sides and back.
  2. Communicate this to your stylist or barber.
  3. Have the sides and back of your hair trimmed to the desired length.
  4. Style the longer hair on top as desired.

Tips For Maintaining The Look

The long on top short on sides and back haircut is a low-maintenance style, but there are a few things you can do to keep it looking its best:

  • Regularly trim the sides and back to maintain the length contrast.
  • Use a hair product that works well with your hair type to style the longer hair on top.
  • Consider getting a touch-up every few weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long on top short on sides and back haircut:

Is this style suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this style can work for most hair types, but it may require different styling techniques depending on the texture and thickness of your hair.

Can I still have a part with this style?

A side part can work well with this style, but it is important to communicate this to your stylist or barber to ensure they cut the hair accordingly.

Can I wear this style to formal events?

Yes, this style can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. For formal events, consider using a stronger hold product to keep the longer hair on top in place.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hairstyle, and the long on top short on sides and back haircut is no exception. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair health.

The long on top short on sides and back haircut is a timeless style that can work for any man. With a little maintenance and the right styling products, you can keep this look fresh and stylish for years to come.

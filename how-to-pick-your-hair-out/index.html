How To Pick Your Hair Out A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Aug 15, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to add some volume and texture to your hair, picking it out might just be the solution you need. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of how to pick your hair out, as well as provide some useful tips and tricks for maintaining your hair's health. So, let's get started!

How to Pick Your Hair Out

The process of picking your hair out involves using a comb or pick to shape your hair into a fuller, more voluminous style. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you begin picking out your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will help you achieve the best results and prevent any damage to your hair.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

Using a wide-toothed comb, gently detangle your hair from the ends to the roots. This will help prevent any breakage or damage to your hair during the picking process.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using hair clips or elastics. This will make it easier to pick out your hair and ensure you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Pick Your Hair Out

Starting at the roots, use a comb or pick to gently lift and shape your hair. Work your way through each section, picking out your hair until you achieve your desired look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've picked out your hair, you can style it as desired. You can create a high, voluminous afro or a more natural-looking style. The choice is yours!

Tips for Picking Your Hair Out

Here are some useful tips to keep in mind when picking out your hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or pick to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't pick your hair out too often, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized and prevent dryness.
  • Be gentle when picking out your hair, as rough handling can lead to breakage and damage.

FAQ About Picking Your Hair Out

Can I pick my hair out if it's been chemically treated?

Yes, you can still pick out your hair if it's been chemically treated. However, be gentle and take extra care to prevent any damage or breakage.

Is it necessary to use a pick to pick out my hair?

No, you can also use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to pick out your hair. The important thing is to be gentle and avoid rough handling.

How often should I pick out my hair?

It's best to pick out your hair no more than once or twice a week to prevent damage and breakage.

Hair Care Tips for Picking Your Hair Out

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great when picking it out:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can pick it out to achieve a fuller, more voluminous look without damaging your strands.

Conclusion

Picking your hair out is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. By following the steps and tips outlined in this guide, you can achieve a beautiful, healthy-looking style that's sure to turn heads. So go ahead and give it a try!

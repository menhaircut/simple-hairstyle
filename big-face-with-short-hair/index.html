Big Face With Short Hair How To Rock The Look In 2023

Written by Frank Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair has always been a popular choice among women, but for those with a big face, it can be a bit of a challenge to find the perfect hairstyle. However, with the right cut and style, a big face with short hair can be a winning combination. In this article, we'll provide you with tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to help you rock the big face with short hair look in 2023.

Description: What is a Big Face with Short Hair?

A big face with short hair is a hairstyle that features a short haircut on a person with a larger facial structure. The goal is to create a balanced look that accentuates the face and brings out its best features. Women with a big face may feel self-conscious about their features, but a short hairstyle can help to create a more confident and assertive look.

How to: Getting the Big Face with Short Hair Look

Step 1: Find the Right Cut

The first step in getting the big face with short hair look is to find the right cut. A shorter cut can help to balance out the face and make it appear more proportional. A bob or pixie cut are both great options, as they are short but still versatile.

Step 2: Consider the Shape of Your Face

When choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to consider the shape of your face. If you have a round face, a short bob with layers can help to elongate the face. If you have a square face, a short pixie cut with bangs can soften the angles of your face.

Step 3: Find a Good Stylist

Once you've found the right cut and considered the shape of your face, it's important to find a good stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Look for a stylist who is experienced in working with short hair and has a good understanding of how to create a look that is flattering for a big face.

Tips for Rocking the Big Face with Short Hair Look

Now that you've got the big face with short hair look, here are some tips to help you rock it:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Accessorize with earrings or statement necklaces to draw attention away from your face.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Try different parting styles to find what works best for your face shape.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look even bigger?

A: No, a short haircut can actually help to make your face appear more proportional. The key is to find the right cut that works for your face shape.

Q: Can I still style my short hair in different ways?

A: Absolutely! Short hair can be surprisingly versatile. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Big Face with Short Hair

Just because you've got short hair doesn't mean you can skip out on hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Invest in a quality hair mask to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant when you do.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

With the right cut, styling, and care, a big face with short hair can be a winning combination. Follow these tips and you'll be rocking the look in no time.

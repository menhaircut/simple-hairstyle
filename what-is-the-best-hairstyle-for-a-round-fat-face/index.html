The Best Hairstyle For A Round Fat Face

Written by Bardi Jun 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that will flatter your round fat face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyle options for those with a round fat face. We will cover everything from how to choose the right style to tips for maintaining your new look.

Description

A round fat face has full cheeks and a soft jawline. The goal when choosing a hairstyle is to create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. This can be achieved by adding height and volume to the crown of the head while keeping the sides of the hair close to the face.

How to Choose the Right Style

When choosing a hairstyle for a round fat face, it's important to consider the length and texture of your hair. Shorter hairstyles tend to work better as they help to create the illusion of a longer face. If you prefer longer hair, consider adding layers to create movement and volume.

Additionally, textured hairstyles can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Consider adding waves or curls to your hair to add volume and dimension.

Step by Step

1. Determine the length and texture of your hair.

2. Choose a shorter style or add layers for movement and volume.

3. Add texture to your hair with waves or curls.

4. Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect style for your face shape.

Tips

1. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs.

2. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

3. Use volumizing products to add height and lift to the crown of your head.

4. Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or sleek as they can make your face appear rounder.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round fat face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs or slicked-back styles.

Can I still have long hair if I have a round fat face?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a round fat face. Consider adding layers to create movement and volume.

Hair Care

Once you have found the perfect hairstyle for your round fat face, it's important to maintain it with proper hair care. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture. Additionally, consider using a heat protectant when using hot styling tools such as curling irons or straighteners.

Regular trims are also important to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle for a round fat face can be challenging, but with the right style, you can create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. Consider your hair length and texture, and consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect style for you. Remember to maintain your new style with proper hair care, and enjoy your new, flattering look!

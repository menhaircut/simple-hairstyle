Short Hairstyles For Thinning Hair On Top Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Sep 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thinning hair is a common problem among men, and it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that suits your personality and covers up the hair loss. In this blog post, we will be discussing the best short hairstyles for men with thinning hair on top. We have also included tips on how to take care of your hair and maintain your hairstyle.

Description

Thinning hair on top can be caused by various factors such as genetics, stress, and medical conditions. It can affect a man's confidence and self-esteem. However, a good short hairstyle can help cover up the hair loss and make you look stylish and confident.

Short hairstyles are ideal for men with thinning hair as they create an illusion of fuller hair. They are easy to maintain and require less styling time compared to long hairstyles. Short hairstyles also suit all face shapes and add a touch of sophistication to your look.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle

When choosing a short hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some short hairstyles that work well for men with thinning hair on top:

  • The buzz cut: This is a popular short hairstyle that involves cutting the hair to a uniform length using clippers. It is easy to maintain and suits all face shapes.
  • The crew cut: This hairstyle features short hair on the sides and slightly longer hair on top. It is ideal for men with round or square faces.
  • The textured crop: This is a trendy short hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and leaving it slightly longer on top. It is perfect for men with wavy or curly hair.
  • The side-parted comb-over: This is a classic short hairstyle that involves parting the hair on the side and combing it over to one side. It is ideal for men with thinning hair on top.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Short Hairstyle

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect short hairstyle:

  1. Choose the right hairstyle based on your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Show your hairstylist a picture of the hairstyle you want.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair short on the sides and longer on top.
  4. Style your hair using a matte hair product such as a clay or paste.
  5. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair in the desired direction.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or a hair wax to keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction to add volume to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for men with thinning hair on top:

  • Q: Can I still style my hair if I have thinning hair on top?
  • A: Yes, you can still style your hair. Short hairstyles work well for men with thinning hair as they create an illusion of fuller hair.
  • Q: How often should I get a haircut?
  • A: It is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Q: What hair products should I use for my short hairstyle?
  • A: You can use a matte hair product such as a clay or paste to style your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Thinning Hair

Here are some hair care tips for men with thinning hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as hair dyes and relaxers as they can weaken your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Short hairstyles are a great option for men with thinning hair on top. They are easy to maintain and can help cover up the hair loss. Follow our tips and hair care advice to achieve the perfect short hairstyle and maintain healthy hair.

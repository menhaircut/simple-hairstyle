What Fringe Suits A Square Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Nov 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square face, you may be wondering what kind of fringe (or bangs, as they're known in the United States) would suit your face shape best. A square face is characterized by a jawline that's almost as wide as the forehead, with a strong, angular jaw. To balance out your features and soften your face shape, a well-chosen fringe can work wonders. Here's everything you need to know about what fringe suits a square face.

How to Choose the Best Fringe for a Square Face

When it comes to choosing a fringe for a square face, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want to create the illusion of a softer, more oval-shaped face. This means avoiding styles that accentuate your angular jawline or make your face look even more square. Instead, opt for fringes that add height to your forehead and create a more rounded shape.

Another thing to consider is the thickness and length of your fringe. A thick, blunt fringe can emphasize your strong features, so it's best to choose a lighter, wispy fringe that falls just below your eyebrows. This will help to soften your face and create a more balanced look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Fringe

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the right fringe for your square face:

  1. Consider your hair type: If you have thick, curly hair, a heavy, blunt fringe may work well for you. If you have fine, straight hair, a wispy fringe may be a better choice.
  2. Think about your lifestyle: If you're always on the go and don't have much time to style your hair, a low-maintenance fringe that can be swept to the side may be best.
  3. Look at your face shape: If you have a square face, avoid fringes that are too short or too blunt. Opt for a wispy fringe that falls just below your eyebrows.
  4. Consider your personal style: Do you prefer a classic, polished look, or something more edgy and textured? Your personal style can help guide your choice of fringe.

Tips for Maintaining Your Fringe

Once you've chosen the right fringe for your square face, it's important to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your fringe:

  • Trim your fringe regularly to keep it at the right length.
  • Blow-dry your fringe with a round brush to create a smooth, polished look.
  • Use a lightweight, non-greasy styling product to keep your fringe in place.
  • Avoid touching or brushing your fringe too much, as this can make it look greasy or frizzy.
  • If you have bangs, consider washing them separately from the rest of your hair to keep them looking fresh.

FAQs About Fringes for Square Faces

Here are some frequently asked questions about fringes for square faces:

What kind of fringe should I avoid if I have a square face?

Avoid fringes that are too short or too blunt, as these can accentuate your strong jawline.

What's the best way to style a wispy fringe?

To style a wispy fringe, blow-dry it with a round brush and use a lightweight, non-greasy styling product to keep it in place.

What if I don't like the way my fringe looks?

If you're not happy with your fringe, don't be afraid to go back to your stylist and ask for a different style. It may take a bit of trial and error to find the perfect fringe for your face shape and personal style.

Final Thoughts

Choosing the right fringe for a square face can be a bit tricky, but with a bit of guidance, you can find a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Whether you opt for a wispy fringe or a more heavy, blunt style, be sure to keep your fringe maintained and styled to perfection.

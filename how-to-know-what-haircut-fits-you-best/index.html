How To Know What Haircut Fits You Best A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a change in your hairstyle but aren't sure what haircut will suit you best, you're not alone. Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this guide, we'll go through the steps to help you determine the best haircut for your face shape, hair type, and personality, and answer some frequently asked questions to help you make an informed decision.

Table of Contents:

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type and Texture
  • Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle and Personality
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right haircut is determining your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and looking in the mirror to see the overall shape of your face. There are several face shapes, including:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Pear

Round face: If your face is round, choose a haircut that adds length and height to your face, such as a long layered cut or a side-swept bang.

Oval face: An oval face is considered the ideal face shape, and most haircuts will suit this face shape. You can try a bob, a pixie cut, or even long beachy waves.

Square face: For a square face, you want to soften your features with a haircut that has soft layers or waves. Avoid blunt cuts that accentuate your jawline.

Heart face: If you have a heart-shaped face, try a side-swept bang or a layered haircut that frames your face. Avoid short or blunt cuts that accentuate your forehead.

Diamond face: A diamond face shape is rare, and you can choose from a variety of haircuts, including a pixie cut or a bob. You can also experiment with a middle part or side-swept bangs.

Pear face: For a pear-shaped face, choose a haircut that adds volume to your crown and balances your jawline. A layered cut or a long bob would work well for this face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type and Texture

Another important factor to consider when choosing a haircut is your hair type and texture. If you have thin or fine hair, you may want to avoid long haircuts as they can make your hair look even thinner. Instead, opt for a shorter cut with layers or a pixie cut. If you have thick hair, you can choose from a variety of haircuts, including a bob or long layers.

Your hair texture is also important to consider. If you have curly hair, you may want to choose a haircut that enhances your curls, such as a layered cut or a curly bob. If you have straight hair, you can experiment with a variety of haircuts, including a blunt cut or a long layered cut.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle and Personality

When choosing a haircut, it's important to consider your lifestyle and personality. If you're always on the go and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, choose a low-maintenance haircut that can be styled quickly and easily, such as a bob or a pixie cut. If you're more adventurous and love to experiment with your hair, try a bold haircut, such as a shaved head or a bold hair color.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right haircut:

  • Bring pictures to your hairstylist to show them the exact look you're going for.
  • Consider your hair's natural texture and choose a haircut that works with it, not against it.
  • Think about the maintenance required for your chosen haircut and make sure it fits in with your lifestyle.
  • Ask your hairstylist for their professional opinion on what haircut would suit your face shape and hair type best.

FAQs

What haircut should I get for a round face?

A long layered cut or a side-swept bang can add length and height to a round face.

What haircut should I get for thick hair?

A bob or long layers work well for thick hair.

What haircut should I get for curly hair?

A layered cut or a curly bob can enhance your curls.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen your haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and fresh. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips, you'll be able to choose the right haircut for your face shape, hair type, and personality. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.

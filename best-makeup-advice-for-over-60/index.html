Best Makeup Advice For Over 60 How To Look Your Best In 2023

Written by Luffy Jun 09, 2023 · 3 min read

As we age, our skin changes and so does our makeup routine. If you are over 60, you may be wondering how to update your makeup to look your best. In this article, we’ll provide you with the best makeup advice for over 60, including tips, step by step instructions, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Apply Makeup for Over 60
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Makeup Tips for Over 60
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Advice for Over 60

How to Apply Makeup for Over 60

When applying makeup for over 60, less is more. You want to enhance your natural beauty, not cover it up. Start with a clean, moisturized face and apply a primer to help your makeup last longer. Use a light foundation that matches your skin tone and blend it well. Avoid using heavy powder as it can settle in fine lines and wrinkles.

Next, add a touch of color to your cheeks with a cream blush. Use a light hand and blend well. For your eyes, use neutral shades that complement your skin tone. Avoid using dark colors as they can make your eyes look smaller. Finally, add a touch of lip color in a shade that complements your skin tone.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide for applying makeup for over 60:

  1. Cleanse your face and apply moisturizer
  2. Apply a primer to help your makeup last longer
  3. Use a light foundation that matches your skin tone and blend it well
  4. Add a touch of color to your cheeks with a cream blush
  5. Use neutral shades for your eyes
  6. Apply a light coat of mascara to your lashes
  7. Add a touch of lip color in a shade that complements your skin tone

Makeup Tips for Over 60

Here are some makeup tips for over 60:

  • Use a light hand when applying makeup
  • Choose neutral shades that complement your skin tone
  • Avoid using heavy powder as it can settle in fine lines and wrinkles
  • Use cream blush instead of powder to add a touch of color to your cheeks
  • Avoid using dark colors on your eyes as they can make them look smaller
  • Choose a lip color that complements your skin tone

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about makeup for over 60:

Can I still wear eyeliner?

Yes, you can still wear eyeliner. However, choose a soft pencil in a neutral shade and apply it lightly.

Do I need to wear foundation?

No, you don’t need to wear foundation. If you prefer, you can use a tinted moisturizer or BB cream instead.

Should I use a setting spray?

Yes, a setting spray can help your makeup last longer. Choose one that is lightweight and doesn’t feel heavy on your skin.

Hair Care Advice for Over 60

As we age, our hair can become thinner and drier. Here are some hair care tips for over 60:

  • Use a gentle shampoo that doesn’t contain harsh chemicals
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and manageable
  • Avoid using heated styling tools as they can damage your hair
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair texture
  • Add some highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair

By following these makeup and hair care tips, you can look your best at any age. Remember, the key is to enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your own skin.

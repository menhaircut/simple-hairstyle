How To Control Hair Fall After Delivery In Tamil

Written by Petter Jun 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a new mom experiencing hair fall after delivery? Don't worry, it's a common postpartum symptom. In this article, we will discuss how to control hair fall after delivery in Tamil. Follow these simple steps and tips to get your luscious locks back!

Description

After delivery, most women experience hair fall due to hormonal changes. This is a natural process, and the good news is that it is temporary. With proper care and attention, you can control hair fall and promote hair growth.

How to Control Hair Fall After Delivery in Tamil

Here are some steps to control hair fall after delivery in Tamil:

1. Oil Your Hair Regularly

Oil your hair regularly with coconut oil, castor oil, or almond oil. Massage your scalp gently with the oil and leave it overnight. This will nourish your hair and promote hair growth.

2. Use a Mild Shampoo

Use a mild shampoo to wash your hair. Avoid using hot water as it can damage your hair. Rinse your hair with cold water to lock in the moisture and promote hair growth.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair dry naturally or use a hair dryer on a cool setting.

4. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals like iron, zinc, and vitamin C. This will nourish your hair and promote hair growth. Include foods like eggs, spinach, nuts, and fruits in your diet.

5. Manage Stress

Manage your stress levels as stress can also lead to hair fall. Practice yoga, meditation, or any other relaxation technique to reduce stress levels.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these step by step guide to control hair fall after delivery in Tamil:

Step 1: Oil your hair regularly

Apply coconut oil, castor oil, or almond oil to your hair and massage your scalp gently. Leave it overnight and wash your hair with a mild shampoo the next day.

Step 2: Use a mild shampoo

Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and rinse with cold water to lock in the moisture.

Step 3: Avoid heat styling

Avoid using heat styling tools and let your hair dry naturally or use a hair dryer on a cool setting.

Step 4: Eat a balanced diet

Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to nourish your hair.

Step 5: Manage stress

Manage your stress levels through yoga, meditation, or any other relaxation technique.

Tips

Here are some tips to control hair fall after delivery in Tamil:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and breakage.

2. Avoid tight hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids as they can pull on your hair and lead to hair fall.

3. Use a wide-toothed comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair and avoid breakage.

4. Don't tie your hair when wet

Don't tie your hair when wet as it can lead to breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to control hair fall after delivery in Tamil:

1. Is hair fall after delivery normal?

Yes, hair fall after delivery is normal due to hormonal changes.

2. How long does hair fall last after delivery?

Hair fall after delivery usually lasts for a few months and is temporary.

3. Can hair fall after delivery be prevented?

Hair fall after delivery can be controlled with proper care and attention.

4. Can I use hair products to control hair fall after delivery?

Yes, you can use hair products specifically designed to control hair fall after delivery.

Hair Care

Here are some additional hair care tips to promote hair growth:

1. Use a hair mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and promote hair growth.

2. Don't over-wash your hair

Don't over-wash your hair as it can strip it of its natural oils and lead to hair fall.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with SPF.

Follow these steps and tips to control hair fall after delivery in Tamil and get your beautiful locks back!

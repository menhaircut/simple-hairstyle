Women s Short Haircuts 2020 Over 60 A Guide To Looking Fabulous

If you are over 60 and looking for a new haircut, you are in the right place! Short haircuts are a popular choice for women over 60 because they are easy to maintain and can make you look younger. In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for women over 60 in 2020. You will also find tips on how to care for your new haircut and answers to frequently asked questions.

  • How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Women Over 60

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Women Over 60

When choosing a short haircut, it is essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the best short haircut for women over 60:

  • Consider your face shape: If you have a round face, you may want to choose a short haircut with layers to add volume and height. If you have a square face, a short bob with side-swept bangs can soften your features. If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any short haircut.
  • Think about your hair texture: If you have fine hair, a layered bob can add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a pixie cut can help you manage your hair better.
  • Consider your personal style: Do you prefer a classic or edgy look? Are you willing to style your hair every day, or do you want a low-maintenance haircut?

Step by Step Guide for Getting the Perfect Short Haircut

Once you have chosen the best short haircut for women over 60, it is time to get it done. Here is a step by step guide:

  1. Find a reputable hairstylist who has experience cutting short hair.
  2. Show them a picture of the haircut you want, or describe it in detail.
  3. Trust their professional opinion and let them make adjustments based on your face shape and hair texture.
  4. Ask for styling tips and product recommendations.
  5. Book your next appointment to maintain your new haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products, such as a volumizing mousse or a texturizing spray.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as blow-drying and using a flat iron.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best short haircut for women over 60 with fine hair?

A layered bob or a pixie cut can add volume and movement to fine hair. Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make your hair look flat.

Can women over 60 wear a pixie cut?

Yes, women over 60 can wear a pixie cut. However, it is essential to consider your face shape and hair texture before getting a pixie cut.

Do short haircuts make you look younger?

Short haircuts can make you look younger because they can add volume and height to your hair, which can make your face look more lifted.

Hair Care Tips for Women Over 60

As we age, our hair can become thinner and drier. Here are some hair care tips for women over 60:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling wands, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals, such as bleach and hair dye, as they can damage your hair.

Now that you know everything there is to know about short haircuts for women over 60, it is time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and get a fabulous new haircut!

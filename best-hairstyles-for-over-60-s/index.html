Best Hairstyles For Over 60S A Guide To Flattering And Age Appropriate Looks

Written by Luffy Aug 23, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle that flatters your face shape, suits your lifestyle, and enhances your natural beauty, you're in the right place. In this guide, we'll share some of the best hairstyles for over 60s, along with tips, tricks, and hair care advice to help you look and feel your best.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Over 60s

When it comes to choosing the best hairstyle for over 60s, there are several factors to consider:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair texture and thickness
  • Your lifestyle and personal style
  • Your hair care routine

It's important to choose a hairstyle that complements the shape of your face and enhances your natural features. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume at the crown to elongate your face. If you have a square face, soft layers and wispy bangs can soften your features and add dimension.

Your hair texture and thickness can also influence your hairstyle choices. If you have thin or fine hair, shorter hairstyles can add volume and fullness. If you have thick hair, longer styles can showcase your beautiful locks.

Your lifestyle and personal style are also important factors to consider. If you're active and on-the-go, shorter hairstyles that require minimal styling may be a better choice. If you prefer a more polished and sophisticated look, longer hairstyles with layers and curls may be more your style.

Finally, your hair care routine is crucial to maintaining the health and vitality of your hair. Make sure to choose a hairstyle that works with your hair care routine and doesn't require too much maintenance or styling.

Short Hairstyles for Over 60s

Short hairstyles can be a great choice for over 60s, as they're low-maintenance, easy to style, and can add volume and fullness to fine or thin hair. Here are some of the best short hairstyles for over 60s:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob haircut
  • Cropped cut
  • Layered cut
  • Short shag haircut

Short hairstyles can be styled with a variety of techniques, including blow-drying, curling, and flat ironing. They're also easy to maintain, as regular trims can keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Medium-Length Hairstyles for Over 60s

Medium-length hairstyles can offer the best of both worlds for over 60s, as they're versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Here are some of the best medium-length hairstyles for over 60s:

  • Shoulder-length bob
  • Wavy lob
  • Layered haircut
  • Medium shag hairstyle

Medium-length hairstyles can be styled with loose waves, curls, or straightened for a sleek and polished look. They can also be pinned up or braided for a more formal occasion.

Long Hairstyles for Over 60s

Long hairstyles can be a beautiful and elegant choice for over 60s, as they can showcase healthy and shiny hair. Here are some of the best long hairstyles for over 60s:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Beachy waves
  • Sleek and straight
  • Braided updo

Long hairstyles can be styled with loose waves, curls, or straightened for a sleek and polished look. They can also be pinned up or braided for a more formal occasion.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining Your Hair

Whether you choose a short, medium, or long hairstyle, there are some tips and tricks to keep in mind to ensure your hair looks and feels its best:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and fullness to fine or thin hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Invest in a good-quality hairbrush and comb to detangle and style your hair without causing damage or breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as braids, buns, or twists, to add variety and interest to your hairstyle.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair if I'm over 60?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at any age! However, it's important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. Consider consulting with a professional stylist to determine the best hair color for you.

Q: Should I cut my hair short if I'm over 60?

A: Short hairstyles can be a great choice for over 60s, as they're low-maintenance and can add volume and fullness to fine or thin hair. However, the best hairstyle for you depends on several factors, including your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Consider consulting with a professional stylist to determine the best haircut for you.

Q: How can I keep my hair healthy as I age?

A: To keep your hair healthy and shiny as you age, it's important to maintain a healthy diet, drink plenty of water, and avoid excessive heat styling or chemical treatments. Regular trims and using high-quality hair care products can also help keep your hair looking and feeling its best.

Hair Care Tips for Over 60s

In addition to choosing the best hairstyle for over 60s, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for over 60s:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals, such as B vitamins, iron, and zinc, which are important for healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing a flattering and age-appropriate hairstyle, you can look and feel your best at any age!

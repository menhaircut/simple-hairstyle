How To Style Curly Hair After Sleeping

Written by Christine Jun 21, 2023 · 3 min read

Waking up with curly hair can either be a blessing or a curse. Sometimes, you wake up with perfectly defined curls and other times, it can be a frizzy mess. Styling curly hair after sleeping can be a daunting task, but not impossible. In this article, we’ll be discussing how to style curly hair after sleeping and some tips to make it easier.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Curly Hair After Sleeping
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Style Curly Hair After Sleeping

The first step to styling curly hair after sleeping is to not brush it. Brushing curly hair can cause it to become frizzy and lose its definition. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your curls.

Next, dampen your hair with water or a leave-in conditioner. This will help to revive your curls and make them easier to manage. You can also use a curl refresher spray for extra definition.

Once your hair is damp, apply a styling product such as a curl cream, gel, or mousse. This will help to hold your curls in place and prevent frizz. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.

Finally, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and prevent them from becoming frizzy. Alternatively, you can air dry your hair, but this may take longer.

Step by Step

Here’s a step by step guide on how to style curly hair after sleeping:

  1. Do not brush your hair.
  2. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  3. Dampen your hair with water or a leave-in conditioner.
  4. Apply a styling product such as a curl cream, gel, or mousse.
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair or air dry.

Tips

Here are some tips to make styling curly hair after sleeping easier:

  • Sleep with a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin hair scarf to protect your curls while sleeping.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to your ends before sleeping to prevent dryness.
  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel to prevent frizz.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling curly hair after sleeping:

How do I refresh my curls in the morning?

You can refresh your curls in the morning by dampening your hair with water or a leave-in conditioner and applying a curl refresher spray.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should wash your curly hair once or twice a week to prevent dryness and breakage.

What is the best way to dry curly hair?

The best way to dry curly hair is by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer or air drying.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and defined curls. Here are some tips for taking care of curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.

Styling curly hair after sleeping can be tricky, but with the right techniques and products, it can be easy and effortless. Remember to embrace your curls and enjoy the process of styling them.

Is Short Hair Good For Oval Faces

Written by Aprilia Aug 31, 2023 · 5 min read

Short haircuts have been a popular trend among women for decades. It’s no surprise why many women are opting for shorter haircuts. They are easy to style, requires less maintenance, and can accentuate your facial features. However, the question remains, is short hair good for oval faces?

Description

Oval faces have a balanced and symmetrical appearance, with a slightly rounded jawline and high cheekbones. This face shape is versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, not all short haircuts are suitable for oval faces.

The key to choosing the right short haircut for oval faces is to find a style that complements your facial features. Short haircuts that highlight your cheekbones, eyes, and jawline can enhance your natural beauty and make your face look more proportionate.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Oval Face

There are various short haircuts that you can choose from, but not all of them are suitable for oval faces. Here are some tips to help you choose the right short haircut for your oval face:

  • Consider your hair texture: Short haircuts work well for oval faces, regardless of hair texture. However, you need to choose a style that complements your hair texture. For example, if you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a pixie cut that adds volume to your hair.
  • Look for styles that frame your face: Short haircuts that frame your face can accentuate your cheekbones and jawline, making your face look more proportionate. Bob haircuts and asymmetrical cuts work well for oval faces.
  • Avoid styles that add height to your crown: Short haircuts that add height to your crown can make your face look longer, which is not ideal for oval faces. Instead, opt for styles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Consider your lifestyle: Short haircuts require less maintenance, but you still need to style them regularly. Choose a style that fits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Oval Faces

Styling short hair for oval faces is relatively easy, and you can achieve different looks with minimal effort. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling short hair for oval faces:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair to add texture and volume.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the sides of your face.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, starting from the roots to the tips.
  5. Add some hairspray to keep your hair in place.
  6. You can also add some accessories, such as headbands or hairpins, to add some flair to your hairstyle.

Tips to Maintain Short Hair for Oval Faces

Short haircuts require less maintenance, but you still need to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips to maintain short hair for oval faces:

  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it in shape.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.

FAQs

Q: Can oval faces pull off a pixie cut?

A: Yes, oval faces can pull off a pixie cut, but you need to choose the right style that complements your facial features.

Q: Will a bob haircut suit oval faces?

A: Yes, bob haircuts work well for oval faces as they can accentuate your cheekbones and jawline.

Q: Do short haircuts require less maintenance?

A: Yes, short haircuts require less maintenance than long haircuts, but you still need to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short haircuts are easy to maintain, but you still need to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Don't over wash your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it in shape.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as it can damage your hair.

In conclusion, short haircuts can work well for oval faces, but you need to choose the right style that complements your facial features. Bob haircuts and asymmetrical cuts work well for oval faces, and you should avoid styles that add height to your crown. With proper care and maintenance, you can enjoy a stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle that accentuates your natural beauty.

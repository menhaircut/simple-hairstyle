How Many Mm Is A 2 Carat Princess Cut Diamond

When it comes to buying a diamond, it is important to know the 4Cs: carat, clarity, color, and cut. One of the most popular cuts is the princess cut. This cut has a square or rectangular shape with pointed corners and is known for its brilliance and fire.

Description

A 2 carat princess cut diamond is a popular choice for engagement rings and other jewelry. The size of the diamond is measured in carats, which is a unit of weight. One carat is equal to 0.2 grams, and a 2 carat diamond weighs 0.4 grams. The size of the diamond is also measured in millimeters (mm), which is the measurement of the width and length of the diamond.

How to Measure a Princess Cut Diamond

To measure a princess cut diamond, you will need a ruler or a digital caliper. A digital caliper is more accurate and can measure to the nearest hundredth of a millimeter.

Step-by-Step Guide

To measure a princess cut diamond:

  1. Place the diamond on a flat surface with the pointed corners facing up.
  2. Measure the length of the diamond from one corner to the opposite corner.
  3. Measure the width of the diamond from the other corner to the opposite corner.
  4. Add the length and width measurements together to get the total measurement in millimeters. For example, if the length is 5.5 mm and the width is 5.5 mm, the total measurement is 11 mm.

It is important to note that the measurements may vary slightly depending on the depth and symmetry of the diamond.

Tips for Buying a Princess Cut Diamond

When buying a princess cut diamond, it is important to consider the 4Cs: carat, clarity, color, and cut. The carat weight determines the size of the diamond, while the clarity and color affect the diamond's appearance. The cut is the most important factor as it determines the diamond's brilliance and fire.

It is also important to buy from a reputable jeweler who provides a certificate of authenticity and offers a return policy.

FAQs

Q: How much does a 2 carat princess cut diamond cost?

A: The cost of a 2 carat princess cut diamond depends on the quality of the diamond and the jeweler. On average, a 2 carat diamond can range from $10,000 to $50,000.

Q: Is a 2 carat princess cut diamond too big for an engagement ring?

A: The size of the diamond depends on personal preference and budget. A 2 carat diamond is considered a large diamond and may be too big for some people. It is important to consider the recipient's style and taste when choosing a diamond.

Q: Is a princess cut diamond more expensive than a round cut diamond?

A: The cost of a diamond depends on the quality of the diamond and the cut. Generally, a round cut diamond is more expensive than a princess cut diamond due to the amount of rough diamond that is lost during the cutting process.

Knowing how many mm is a 2 carat princess cut diamond is important when buying a diamond. By considering the 4Cs and buying from a reputable jeweler, you can ensure that you are getting the best diamond for your money.

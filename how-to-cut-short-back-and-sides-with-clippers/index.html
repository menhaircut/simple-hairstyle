How To Cut Short Back And Sides With Clippers

Written by Frank Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the barbershop? Do you want to learn how to cut your own hair? One of the most popular hairstyles for men is the short back and sides. It's a simple, classic look that never goes out of style. In this article, we'll show you how to cut short back and sides with clippers in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

Description

The short back and sides haircut is a style where the hair is shorter on the back and sides and longer on the top. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways. This haircut is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance style that still looks great.

How to Cut Short Back and Sides with Clippers

Here are the steps to cut short back and sides with clippers:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

Before you start cutting, you need to make sure you have the right tools. Choose a good quality clipper that's designed for cutting hair. You'll also need a set of guards, which are the plastic attachments that fit onto the clipper and determine the length of the hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair thoroughly and dry it completely before you start cutting. Wet hair is more difficult to cut and can cause the clipper to pull or snag the hair.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Attach the appropriate guard to the clipper and start cutting the sides. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using an upward motion. Be sure to blend the hair evenly to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Next, move to the back of the head and repeat the same process. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using an upward motion. Again, be sure to blend the hair evenly to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Remove the guard from the clipper and use the bare blade to trim the hair on the top of the head. Use scissors to trim any longer hair that needs to be blended with the sides and back.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Using a trimmer, clean up the neckline and sideburns. Be sure to create a natural-looking hairline that follows the natural curve of the neck.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure everything looks even.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look.

FAQ

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim the hair shorter to even it out. It's better to start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.

Can I cut my hair wet?

No, it's best to cut your hair dry. Wet hair is more difficult to cut and can cause the clipper to pull or snag the hair.

How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look.

Hair Care

After you finish cutting your hair, be sure to wash it again to remove any loose hair. Apply a small amount of hair oil or pomade to add shine and style.

In conclusion, cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking short back and sides haircut at home. Just remember to take your time and start with a longer guard to avoid any mistakes. Happy cutting!

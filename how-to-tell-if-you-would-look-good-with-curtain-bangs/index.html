How To Tell If You Would Look Good With Curtain Bangs

Written by Aprilia Jun 19, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you thinking of getting curtain bangs but not quite sure if they would suit you? Curtain bangs have been trending for quite some time now, and it's easy to see why. They're versatile, low maintenance, and frame the face beautifully. However, not everyone can pull off this look. In this article, we'll show you how to tell if you would look good with curtain bangs.

1. Determine Your Face Shape

The first thing you need to consider is your face shape. Curtain bangs look best on oval, heart, and square face shapes. If you have a round face, this look may not be the best for you as it can make your face appear even rounder. If you're not sure what your face shape is, take a selfie and trace the outline of your face. You can also ask your hairstylist to help you determine your face shape.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Another factor to consider is your hair texture. Curtain bangs work best on hair that's medium to thick and straight to wavy. If you have thin or fine hair, this look may not be the best for you as the bangs can appear too wispy. If you have curly hair, curtain bangs may be difficult to manage as they tend to curl up.

3. Take Your Forehead Size into Account

If you have a larger forehead, curtain bangs can be a great way to balance out your features. However, if you have a smaller forehead, this look may not be the best for you as it can make your forehead appear even smaller. Take a look at your forehead in the mirror and decide if you want to draw attention to it or not.

4. Look at Your Eye Shape

Your eye shape can also play a role in whether or not curtain bangs will suit you. If you have large, round eyes, this look can be very flattering. However, if you have small, almond-shaped eyes, this look may not be the best for you as it can make your eyes appear even smaller.

5. Determine Your Hair Type

Another factor to consider is your hair type. Curtain bangs work best on hair that's healthy and well-maintained. If your hair is damaged or prone to breakage, this look may not be the best for you as the bangs can make your hair look even more damaged. If you have a lot of split ends, it's best to get a trim before getting curtain bangs.

6. Consider Your Face Proportions

When getting curtain bangs, it's important to consider your face proportions. If you have a small face, you may want to opt for shorter bangs that don't overpower your features. If you have a larger face, longer bangs can help balance out your features.

7. Take Your Skin Tone into Account

Your skin tone can also play a role in whether or not curtain bangs will suit you. If you have warm undertones, you may want to opt for bangs that are more golden or caramel in color. If you have cool undertones, bangs that are more ashy or platinum may suit you better.

8. Think About Your Lifestyle

Finally, you should consider your lifestyle when deciding if curtain bangs are right for you. If you lead an active lifestyle or don't have much time to style your hair, this look may not be the best for you as it requires regular maintenance. However, if you have the time and patience to style your hair, curtain bangs can be a great way to switch up your look.

Tips for Getting Curtain Bangs

If you've decided that curtain bangs are right for you, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Bring pictures of the look you want to your hairstylist
  • Ask your hairstylist for their opinion on whether or not curtain bangs will suit you
  • Be prepared for regular maintenance, including trims every 4-6 weeks
  • Invest in a good quality hair straightener to style your bangs
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your bangs

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Once you've gotten your curtain bangs, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips for curtain bangs:

  • Wash your bangs every 2-3 days to keep them clean and prevent oil buildup
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs as they can weigh them down
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a hair straightener on your bangs
  • Consider getting a silk pillowcase to prevent your bangs from getting tangled while you sleep

With these tips in mind, you can confidently decide whether or not curtain bangs are right for you. Remember, it's all about finding a look that suits your individual features and personal style. Good luck!

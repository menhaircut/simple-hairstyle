Best Hairstyle For Round Face Small Forehead The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face and a small forehead, finding the right hairstyle can be challenging. You want a style that will flatter your face shape and minimize the appearance of your forehead. In this post, we'll explore the best hairstyles for round faces with small foreheads, including how to achieve them, tips for styling, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Round Face Small Forehead
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

Description

A round face with a small forehead is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a short forehead. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to create the illusion of length and balance. You want to draw attention away from your cheeks and create the appearance of a longer face. The right hairstyle can do just that.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Round Face Small Forehead

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face with a small forehead, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Opt for hairstyles that create height at the crown of your head to elongate your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider.
  • Choose hairstyles that frame your face and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.
  • Avoid heavy bangs that cover your forehead, as this can make your forehead appear even smaller.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some of the best hairstyles for round faces with small foreheads:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great choice for those with round faces and small foreheads. They create movement and texture while drawing attention to your eyes and cheekbones. To achieve this look:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Create a deep side part.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves throughout your hair.
  • Apply a texturizing spray for added volume and hold.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great choice for those with small foreheads. They add dimension and frame your face while drawing attention away from your forehead. To achieve this look:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Create a deep side part.
  • Section off a small section of hair at your hairline.
  • Trim your bangs to the desired length, angling them slightly downward.
  • Use a flat iron to add a slight curve to your bangs.
  • Sweep your bangs to the side and secure with a bobby pin.

Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a simple and elegant hairstyle that works well for those with round faces and small foreheads. It creates the illusion of length and draws attention to your neck and collarbone. To achieve this look:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Create a deep side part.
  • Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  • Secure with a hair tie.
  • Take a small section of hair from the underside of your ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to conceal it.

Tips for Styling

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair if you have a round face with a small forehead:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it appear flat.
  • Invest in a high-quality curling iron or wand to create loose waves and add volume to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add interest to your hairstyle.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a bob if I have a round face and small forehead?

A: Yes, a bob can work well for those with round faces and small foreheads. Opt for a longer bob that hits just below your chin and has layers to create movement and texture.

Q: Should I avoid wearing my hair up if I have a round face and small forehead?

A: No, you can still wear your hair up if you have a round face and small forehead. Try a high ponytail or a top knot to create the illusion of length.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Advice

Maintaining healthy hair is important for achieving the best hairstyle for your face shape. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily or dry.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can flatter your face shape and highlight your best features. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you!

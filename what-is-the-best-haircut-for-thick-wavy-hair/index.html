What Is The Best Haircut For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Oliver Jun 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know that it can be both a blessing and a curse. While your hair may look voluminous and full of life, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Fortunately, there are plenty of haircut options that can help you make the most of your thick wavy locks. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best haircuts for thick wavy hair, as well as tips and tricks for maintaining your hair's health and style.

Description

When it comes to haircuts for thick wavy hair, there are a few key factors to consider. First and foremost, you'll want to choose a haircut that complements your natural texture and enhances your hair's volume and movement. You'll also want to consider your face shape and personal style, as well as the level of maintenance required for each haircut option.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Thick Wavy Hair

When choosing a haircut for thick wavy hair, it's important to keep in mind that not all haircuts are created equal. Some haircuts may enhance your natural texture and volume, while others may make your hair look flat and lifeless. Here are some tips to help you choose the best haircut for your thick wavy hair:

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape can play a big role in determining which haircut will look best on you. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for a longer haircut that elongates your face and draws attention away from your cheeks. If you have a square face, a layered haircut can soften your features and add movement to your hair.

Think About Your Lifestyle

When choosing a haircut, it's important to consider your lifestyle and the level of maintenance required. If you're someone who likes to spend a lot of time styling your hair, you may be able to handle a more high-maintenance haircut. However, if you're someone who prefers a low-maintenance routine, you'll want to choose a haircut that doesn't require a lot of styling or upkeep.

Choose a Haircut That Complements Your Natural Texture

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a haircut for thick wavy hair is your natural texture. Some haircuts may enhance your natural waves and curls, while others may make your hair look flat and lifeless. For example, a layered haircut can add movement and volume to your hair, while a blunt cut can weigh your hair down and make it look limp.

Step by Step: Best Haircuts for Thick Wavy Hair

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut, let's take a look at some of the best options for thick wavy hair:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for thick wavy hair, as they can add movement and volume without sacrificing length. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long, face-framing layers that start at your cheekbones and blend seamlessly into the rest of your hair. This cut works well with both natural waves and curls, and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Beachy Waves

If you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that enhances your natural waves and curls, a beachy waves cut may be the perfect option for you. This cut features long, tousled layers that are designed to mimic the look of beachy waves. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long layers that start at your chin and blend seamlessly into the rest of your hair.

Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob is a great option for thick wavy hair, as it can add volume and movement without overwhelming your face. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a blunt cut that falls just below your shoulders. This cut works well with both natural waves and straightened hair, and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair's Health and Style

Once you've chosen the perfect haircut for your thick wavy hair, it's important to know how to maintain your hair's health and style. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking its best:

Use the Right Products

Using the right products is key to maintaining your hair's health and style. For thick wavy hair, you'll want to use products that enhance your natural texture and add volume and movement. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil, shea butter, and keratin, which can help nourish and strengthen your hair.

Avoid Overwashing

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. To maintain your hair's health and style, try to wash your hair no more than two or three times a week. When you do wash your hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims is important for maintaining the health and style of your hair. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks, or as needed to maintain your desired length and style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for thick wavy hair:

Can I wear bangs with thick wavy hair?

Yes, you can wear bangs with thick wavy hair. However, it's important to choose a bang style that complements your natural texture and face shape. For example, side-swept bangs can add movement and volume to your hair, while blunt bangs can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.

What is the best way to style thick wavy hair?

The best way to style thick wavy hair is to embrace your natural texture and enhance it with the right products and styling techniques. For example, you can enhance your natural waves and curls by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer or scrunching your hair with a curl enhancer. You can also add volume and movement to your hair by using a volumizing mousse or spray.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining the health and style of your thick wavy hair:

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. To maintain your hair's health and style, try to avoid heat styling as much as possible. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature as low as possible.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can cause your hair to become dry and damaged. To protect your hair from the sun, wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when you're spending time outdoors.

Hydrate Your Hair from the Inside Out

Drinking plenty of water and eating a healthy diet can help hydrate your hair from the inside out. Aim to drink at least eight glasses of water a day and eat plenty of fruits and vegetables that are rich in vitamins and minerals.

Conclusion

When it comes to choosing the best haircut for thick wavy hair, it's important to consider your natural texture, face shape, and personal style. Whether you opt for long layers, a beachy waves cut, or a shoulder-length bob, there are plenty of options that can enhance your hair's volume and movement. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish for years to come.

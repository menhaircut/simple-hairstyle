What Face Shape Does Curtain Bangs Look Best On

Written by Daniel Aug 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're thinking about getting curtain bangs, it's important to know what face shape they will look best on. Curtain bangs have been a popular hairstyle for quite some time now, and it's not hard to see why. They add a touch of femininity and softness to any hairstyle, and they're easy to maintain. But before you get them done, you need to know what face shape they will look best on.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get Curtain Bangs
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a style of bangs that are parted in the middle, framing the face on both sides. They are longer than traditional bangs, and they create a curtain-like effect. This style of bangs works best on hair that is shoulder-length or longer, as it can be difficult to achieve the desired effect on shorter hair.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

If you're interested in getting curtain bangs, it's important to go to a stylist who has experience with this style of bangs. They will be able to assess your face shape and recommend the best length and style for your hair.

Step by Step Guide

1. Find a stylist who has experience with curtain bangs.

2. Discuss what length and style of curtain bangs will look best on your face shape.

3. Have your hair washed and blown dry.

4. Your stylist will section off the hair for the bangs and cut them to the desired length.

5. They will then create the curtain effect by cutting the hair at an angle, creating a V-shape.

6. Your stylist will then style your hair to show off your new curtain bangs.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

1. Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length.

2. Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to go.

3. Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs if they become unruly.

4. Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh between washes.

FAQ

Q: What face shape does curtain bangs look best on?

A: Curtain bangs work best on oval, heart, and square face shapes.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with curtain bangs?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with curtain bangs. Simply pin them back or tuck them behind your ears.

Q: Are curtain bangs high maintenance?

A: No, curtain bangs are not high maintenance. They are easy to style and maintain with regular trims.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

4. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Now that you know what face shape curtain bangs look best on, you can confidently go to your stylist and ask for this trendy hairstyle. With regular trims and proper hair care, you can enjoy your new curtain bangs for months to come.

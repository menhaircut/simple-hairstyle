Best Hairstyles For Square Face With High Forehead A Guide

Written by Petter Nov 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square face with a high forehead, you may be wondering what hairstyles will suit you best. In this article, we'll provide you with some helpful tips and ideas to help you find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Square faces typically have a strong jawline and a wide forehead. If you also have a high forehead, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that balances out the proportions of your face. The goal is to create the illusion of a longer face shape, which can be achieved with the right haircut and styling techniques.

How To

The best hairstyles for square faces with high foreheads are those that add height and volume to the top of the head while creating softness around the jawline. This can be achieved with a number of different haircuts, including:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Soft waves
  • Curly hair
  • A pixie cut with volume on top

Step by Step

Here's how to achieve some of the best hairstyles for square faces with high foreheads:

Long Layers

Long layers can be flattering for square faces with high foreheads, as they create movement and texture around the face. Here's how to achieve this look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Use a curling iron to add some loose waves to the ends of your hair
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair
  4. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to create volume
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your layers in place

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to soften a square face shape, while also drawing attention away from a high forehead. Here's how to achieve this look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair
  3. Part your hair to the side
  4. Take a small section of hair near your hairline and comb it forward
  5. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add a slight wave to your bangs
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a square face with a high forehead:

  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your forehead appear even larger
  • Consider getting highlights or balayage to add some dimension to your hair
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back, as they can emphasize a strong jawline
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you

FAQ

What should I avoid when choosing a hairstyle for a square face with a high forehead?

Avoid blunt bangs, slicked-back hairstyles, and hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

What are the best hairstyles for a square face with a high forehead?

The best hairstyles for this face shape include long layers, side-swept bangs, soft waves, curly hair, and pixie cuts with volume on top.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

Absolutely! Just make sure to add some volume to the top of your head to balance out your face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some lift to your roots. If you have curly hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls looking defined and frizz-free. Additionally, be sure to get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and ideas, you're sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your square face with a high forehead. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!

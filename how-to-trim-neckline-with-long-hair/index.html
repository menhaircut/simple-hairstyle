Step-by-Step Guide

  2. Using a comb, part your hair down the middle of the back of your head. This will create two sections of hair.
  3. Take one section of hair and comb it straight down, so that it is lying flat against your back.
  4. Using your scissors, trim the hair at the nape of your neck, just above your shirt collar. Be sure to cut straight across, rather than at an angle.
  5. Repeat this process on the other side of your head.
  6. If you want, you can use clippers to create a more defined neckline. Simply trim along the bottom edge of your hairline, using a guard to prevent cutting too much hair.
  7. Once you have finished trimming your neckline, comb your hair back into place and check for any uneven spots. If you notice any areas that need to be trimmed further, simply repeat the process until you are satisfied with the results.

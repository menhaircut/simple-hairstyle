How To Make Oval Face Look Round Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of your oval-shaped face and want to make it look rounder? Don't worry; we have got you covered. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to make oval face look round. From haircuts to makeup, we will cover everything you need to know to achieve the perfect round face shape. Let's get started!

Haircuts for Oval Face

One of the easiest ways to make your oval face look rounder is by choosing the right haircut. Here are some haircuts that can help you achieve the round face shape:

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a short haircut that ends just above the chin. This haircut can make your face look fuller and rounder.

Layered Cut

A layered cut adds volume to your hair and creates an illusion of a round face. Make sure to add layers around your cheekbones to achieve the desired result.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short haircut that can make your face look fuller and rounder. This haircut is perfect for those who want to experiment with short hair.

Remember to avoid haircuts that add height to your hair, such as a high ponytail, as they can elongate your face even more.


Makeup for Oval Face

Makeup can also play a crucial role in making your oval face look rounder. Here are some makeup tips that you can follow:

Contouring

Contouring can help create an illusion of a rounder face shape. Use a contour powder or cream to create shadows under your cheekbones and jawline to make them look more prominent.

Blush

Blush can add a flush of color to your cheeks, making your face look rounder. Apply blush on the apples of your cheeks and blend it outwards towards your temples.

Lipstick

Wearing a bright lipstick can draw attention to your lips and away from your face's elongated shape. Choose a bold color that complements your skin tone.

Remember to avoid applying too much highlighter on your cheekbones as it can elongate your face even more.


Hairstyling Tips for Oval Face

Hairstyling can also help you achieve a rounder face shape. Here are some hairstyling tips that you can follow:

Side-Parted Hair

A side-parted hairstyle can add volume to one side of your face, making it look rounder. Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume to your hair.

Bangs

Adding bangs to your hairstyle can create an illusion of a shorter forehead, making your face look rounder. Choose side-swept bangs or blunt bangs, depending on your preference.

Curls

Curls can add volume to your hair and create an illusion of a rounder face. Use a curling iron or rollers to create soft curls that frame your face.

Remember to avoid slicked-back hairstyles as they can elongate your face even more.


Facial Hair for Oval Face

Facial hair can also help you achieve a rounder face shape. Here are some facial hair tips that you can follow:

Beard

A beard can add volume to your chin and jawline, making your face look rounder. Choose a beard style that complements your face shape and maintain it regularly.

Moustache

Wearing a moustache can draw attention to your lips and away from your face's elongated shape. Choose a moustache style that complements your face shape.

Remember to avoid wearing a goatee as it can elongate your chin even more.


Accessories for Oval Face

Accessories can also help you achieve a rounder face shape. Here are some accessories that you can wear:

Round Sunglasses

Wearing round sunglasses can create an illusion of a rounder face. Choose sunglasses that have a round frame and avoid angular frames.

Hats

Wearing hats can add volume to your hair and create an illusion of a rounder face. Choose hats that have a round shape, such as a fedora or a beanie.

Remember to avoid wearing earrings that are too long or angular as they can elongate your face even more.


FAQs

1. Can I make my oval face look round without getting a haircut?

Yes, you can make your oval face look round without getting a haircut. You can follow the makeup, hairstyling, and facial hair tips mentioned above to achieve the desired result.

2. Can I wear any kind of hat to make my face look rounder?

No, you should choose hats that have a round shape, such as a fedora or a beanie, to make your face look rounder.

3. Can I wear angular earrings with an oval face?

No, you should avoid wearing earrings that are too long or angular as they can elongate your face even more.


Hair Care Tips for Oval Face

Lastly, here are some hair care tips that you can follow to maintain healthy and voluminous hair:

Regular Trimming

Regular trimming can help you maintain the shape and volume of your hair. Visit the salon every 6-8 weeks to get a trim.

Deep Conditioning

Deep conditioning can help you maintain the moisture and shine of your hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as a straightener or a curling iron, too often as they can damage your hair and make it look flat. Use heat protectant products before using any heat styling tool.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your oval face look rounder and achieve the desired result. Remember to choose the haircuts, makeup, hairstyling, and facial hair styles that complement your face shape and personality. Good luck!

How To Do Easy Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Frank Jun 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same hairstyle every day? Do you want to spice up your look but don't know how? Look no further! In this guide, we will show you how to do easy hairstyles for medium length hair that are perfect for any occasion. From braids to buns, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, let's talk about the basics of styling medium length hair. First, it's important to invest in quality styling tools such as a straightener, curling iron, and hair dryer. These tools will help you achieve the look you want. Second, use products that work for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing spray. If you have curly hair, use a curl-enhancing product. Lastly, don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles. Practice makes perfect!

Step by Step

The Messy Bun

This is a great hairstyle for those days when you don't have time to wash your hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail.
  3. Secure with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

The Half-Up Half-Down

This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out. Here's how to do it:

  1. Gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail.
  2. Secure with a hair tie.
  3. Tease the crown of your head for added volume.
  4. Let the rest of your hair flow freely.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

The Braided Crown

This hairstyle is perfect for a wedding or formal event. Here's how to do it:

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side and braid it.
  3. Wrap the braid around the top of your head, securing it with bobby pins.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your medium length hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Don't overuse products, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Have fun with accessories such as headbands and hair clips.
  • Experiment with different partings to create a new look.

FAQ

Q: Can I still do these hairstyles if I have short hair?

A: Some of these hairstyles may work for short hair, but they may need to be modified slightly. For example, the braided crown may not work if your hair is too short to wrap around your head.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and the products you use. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair is crucial to maintaining healthy locks. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.

With these easy hairstyles for medium length hair, you'll be able to switch up your look in no time. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles. Happy styling!

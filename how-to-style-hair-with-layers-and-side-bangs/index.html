How to Style Hair with Layers and Side Bangs

The first step in styling hair with layers and side bangs is to make sure your hair is clean and dry. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more body and texture to your hair. After washing, blow dry your hair and use a round brush to lift and add volume to your roots.

Next, section your hair into layers and use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls. Make sure to curl your hair away from your face to create a natural-looking wave. Once youâve curled your hair, use your fingers to separate the curls and create more volume.

To style your side bangs, use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways. Then, use a small amount of styling wax or pomade to create a piecey, textured look. You can also use a hair spray to keep your bangs in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling hair with layers and side bangs:

What products should I use to style my hair with layers and side bangs?

You should use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, a round brush, a curling iron, a flat iron, styling wax or pomade, and hair spray.

How often should I wash my hair if I have layers and side bangs?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

Can I style my hair with layers and side bangs if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can style your hair with layers and side bangs if you have thin hair. Just make sure to use volumizing products and avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

  • Get regular trims to maintain your layers and side bangs.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

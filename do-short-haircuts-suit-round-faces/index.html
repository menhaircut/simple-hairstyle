Do Short Haircuts Suit Round Faces

Written by Oliver Oct 26, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you one of those people who have a round face and want to try a short haircut but not sure if it would suit you? Well, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss whether short haircuts suit round faces or not. Let’s dive in!

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts can look fantastic on round faces if done correctly. The key is to find the right cut that flatters your face shape. A short haircut can make your face appear slimmer and more defined, enhancing your features and bringing out your best facial attributes. However, if the cut is not done correctly, it can make your face look even rounder.

How To

When deciding on a short haircut for a round face, consider the length, volume, and shape of the cut. Short haircuts that are chin-length or shorter, with volume on top and tapered sides, can work well on round faces. Another option can be a short bob or pixie cut with longer layers around the face. These cuts create the illusion of length and balance out the proportions of the face.

Step By Step

If you have decided to go for a short haircut, here are some steps you can follow:

  1. Research different short haircuts that suit round faces.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which cut would work best for you.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in a way that creates the illusion of length and balances out your facial features.
  4. Maintain your short haircut by getting regular trims and using styling products that add volume and texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short haircut for a round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin level as they can make your face look rounder.
  • Consider adding layers to your haircut to create movement and texture.
  • Use styling products like volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Try side-swept bangs to elongate your face.

FAQ

Q. Can people with round faces get a short haircut?

A. Yes, people with round faces can get a short haircut if done correctly.

Q. What are some short haircuts that suit round faces?

A. Short bobs, pixie cuts with longer layers around the face, and chin-length cuts with volume on top and tapered sides can work well on round faces.

Q. How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

A. It is recommended to get your short haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and shape.

Hair Care

After getting a short haircut, it is essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your short haircut.

In conclusion, short haircuts can suit round faces if done correctly. Consider the length, volume, and shape of the cut, and consult with your hairstylist before making a decision. Follow the steps and tips outlined in this article to get the perfect short haircut for your round face. Take care of your hair to keep it healthy and stylish. Happy styling!

Perfect Haircut For Round Chubby Face Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Nov 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of not being able to find the perfect haircut for your round chubby face? Look no further! In this guide, we will give you tips, step-by-step instructions, and answer frequently asked questions to help you achieve the perfect haircut for your face shape.

Description

A round chubby face often has full cheeks and a rounded chin, creating a soft and youthful appearance. However, finding a haircut that complements this face shape can be challenging. The perfect haircut for a round chubby face should elongate the face and create the illusion of a slimmer, more defined jawline.

How to

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect haircut:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before choosing a haircut, determine your face shape. This will help you choose a style that will complement your features. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. If you have a round face, your face will be as wide as it is long.

Step 2: Choose the right length

The length of your hair can make a big difference in how your face looks. For a round chubby face, a longer length is recommended as it will elongate the face. Avoid short hairstyles that fall above the chin, as they can make the face appear wider.

Step 3: Add layers

Adding layers to your haircut can create the illusion of a slimmer face. Layers should start at the jawline or below and be cut at an angle to create a tapered effect.

Step 4: Create volume

Creating volume at the crown of the head can also elongate the face. Use a round brush when blow-drying to create volume, and avoid flat or sleek hairstyles.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a round chubby face:

  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make the face appear wider
  • Consider side-swept bangs, as they can create a more defined jawline
  • Avoid straight hairstyles, as they can make the face appear wider
  • Consider curly or wavy hairstyles, as they can add volume and dimension to the hair

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round chubby face?

The best haircut for a round chubby face is one that elongates the face and creates the illusion of a slimmer jawline. A longer length with layers and volume at the crown is recommended.

What hairstyles should I avoid with a round chubby face?

Avoid short hairstyles that fall above the chin, blunt bangs, and straight hairstyles. These can make the face appear wider.

Can I still have bangs with a round chubby face?

Avoid blunt bangs, but consider side-swept bangs as they can create a more defined jawline.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any haircut. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with heat tools
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect haircut for your round chubby face. Remember to choose a style that complements your features and maintain healthy hair to keep your new haircut looking its best.

How To Cut Long Layers In Medium Length Hair Yourself

Written by Oliver Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of paying a visit to the salon every time you want to get a haircut? Well, you don't have to anymore. With the right tools and techniques, you can easily cut your long layers in medium length hair yourself. In this article, we will guide you through the process step by step, including tips and hair care advice.

Description

Long layers in medium length hair are a great way to add volume and movement to your hair. They are also perfect for those who want to keep their hair length but still want to add some style to it. Cutting your own hair might seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve salon-quality results at home.

How to

To cut long layers in medium length hair yourself, you will need the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips

Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Once your hair is clean, towel-dry it and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back. Clip each section to keep it separate.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start with the back section of your hair. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the comb to comb through the section of hair and make sure it's smooth. Then, use the scissors to cut the hair at an angle. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Move to the Sides and Front

Once you've finished the back section, move on to the sides and front. Repeat the same process of taking small sections of hair and cutting them at an angle.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting all the sections of your hair, check your work in the mirror. If you notice any unevenness, go back and trim the sections until you're satisfied with the results.

Tips

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean and even cut.
  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Always cut at an angle to create the layered effect.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid mistakes.
  • If you have curly hair, make sure to cut it when it's dry to avoid unevenness.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair as long as you have the right tools and techniques. Cutting your own hair might seem daunting, but with practice, you can achieve salon-quality results.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I cut layers in short hair?

Yes, you can cut layers in short hair. However, it's recommended to go to a professional stylist for a more precise cut.

Hair Care

To maintain your long layers in medium length hair, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily cut long layers in medium length hair yourself and achieve salon-quality results. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.

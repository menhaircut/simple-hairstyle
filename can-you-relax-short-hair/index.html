Can You Relax Short Hair

Written by Aprilia Oct 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog about relaxing short hair! If you're here, you're probably wondering if it's possible to relax short hair. The answer is yes, it is possible, and we're here to guide you through the process. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about relaxing short hair, from how to do it to how to take care of your newly relaxed locks.

Description

Relaxing short hair involves chemically straightening the hair to make it more manageable and easier to style. The process involves applying a relaxer cream to the hair, which breaks down the protein bonds in the hair shaft, allowing it to be reshaped. The result is straight, smooth hair that is easier to style and maintain.

How to

Before relaxing short hair, it's important to prepare your hair and scalp. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. Next, section your hair into four parts and apply petroleum jelly to your scalp and hairline to protect your skin from the relaxer cream. Then, apply the relaxer cream to your hair, starting at the back and working your way towards the front.

Leave the relaxer cream on your hair for the recommended amount of time, usually between 10-20 minutes. Be sure to monitor your hair during this time to avoid overprocessing, which can cause damage to your hair and scalp. After the recommended time, rinse the relaxer cream out of your hair with warm water and apply a neutralizing shampoo to stop the chemical process.

Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare your hair and scalp

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. Section your hair into four parts and apply petroleum jelly to your scalp and hairline to protect your skin from the relaxer cream.

Step 2: Apply the relaxer cream

Apply the relaxer cream to your hair, starting at the back and working your way towards the front. Be sure to follow the instructions on the package for the recommended amount of time.

Step 3: Rinse and Neutralize

Rinse the relaxer cream out of your hair with warm water and apply a neutralizing shampoo to stop the chemical process.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you relax short hair:

  • Be sure to follow the instructions on the relaxer cream package carefully.
  • Avoid overprocessing your hair, which can cause damage.
  • Use a neutralizing shampoo to stop the chemical process and prevent further damage.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and strong.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about relaxing short hair:

1. Can you relax short hair at home?

Yes, you can relax short hair at home, but it's important to follow the instructions on the relaxer cream package carefully to avoid damaging your hair.

2. How often should you relax short hair?

You should wait at least 6-8 weeks between relaxer treatments to avoid overprocessing your hair.

3. Can you color relaxed hair?

Yes, you can color relaxed hair, but it's important to wait at least two weeks after relaxing your hair before coloring it.

Hair Care

After relaxing short hair, it's important to take care of your newly relaxed locks. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, strong hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, or use them sparingly and with a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to relax short hair, you can achieve the smooth, straight look you've been dreaming of. Just be sure to follow the instructions carefully and take good care of your hair afterwards. Happy styling!

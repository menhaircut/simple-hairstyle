How Is Hair Type Determined Genetically

Written by Bardi Oct 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you ever wonder why your hair is curly while your siblings have straight hair? Or why you have frizzy hair while your parents have smooth hair? Hair type is determined genetically, and it's fascinating to learn how genes play a role in our hair type. In this article, we will dive into the details of how hair type is determined genetically and what it means for your hair care routine.

How to Determine Your Hair Type

Your hair type is determined by the shape of your hair follicle. There are three types of hair follicles – straight, wavy, and curly. The shape of your hair follicle is determined by genetics, and it cannot be changed.

The easiest way to determine your hair type is to look at the shape of your hair when it's wet. If your hair is straight, it will dry straight. If your hair is wavy or curly, it will dry with a wave or curl. You can also determine your hair type by looking at the texture of your hair. Straight hair has a smooth texture, while wavy and curly hair have a rough texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to determine your hair type:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Do not use any styling products on your hair.
  3. Allow your hair to air dry.
  4. Observe the shape and texture of your hair.
  5. Identify your hair type – straight, wavy, or curly.

It's important to note that some people have a combination of hair types. For example, you may have straight hair at the roots and curly hair at the ends. In this case, it's best to identify the dominant hair type.

Hair Care Tips for Different Hair Types

Once you have identified your hair type, it's important to tailor your hair care routine accordingly. Here are some hair care tips for different hair types:

  • Straight Hair: Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Wavy Hair: Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to define your waves. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your waves.
  • Curly Hair: Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated. Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls.

It's also important to note that hair type can change over time due to hormones, aging, and other factors. It's important to reassess your hair type periodically and adjust your hair care routine accordingly.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair type:

Can I Change My Hair Type?

No, hair type is determined genetically, and it cannot be changed. However, you can use styling products and tools to temporarily change the appearance of your hair.

What Causes Frizzy Hair?

Frizzy hair is caused by a lack of moisture in the hair. It's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner and to avoid heat styling tools that can dry out your hair.

What Causes Hair Loss?

Hair loss can be caused by genetics, hormones, stress, and other factors. It's important to consult with a dermatologist or trichologist if you are experiencing hair loss.

In conclusion, hair type is determined genetically, and it's fascinating to learn how genes play a role in our hair type. By identifying your hair type and tailoring your hair care routine accordingly, you can keep your hair healthy and beautiful. Remember to reassess your hair type periodically and adjust your hair care routine accordingly.

Best Hairstyle For Round Face 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying out various hairstyles that don't seem to complement your round face shape? Do you want to know which hairstyle is best suited for your face type? Look no further because we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the best hairstyle for round face 2020 to help you rock a gorgeous look.

Description

When it comes to hairstyle, one size doesn't fit all. The shape of your face plays a significant role in determining which hairstyle will look best on you. Round face shapes are characterized by a soft, circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The aim of a hairstyle for round face is to create an illusion of a longer and slimmer face.

How to:

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face is to create angles that elongate the face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, such as a voluminous topknot or a high ponytail.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that emphasize the roundness of the face. Instead, opt for layers that create movement and texture.
  • Side-swept bangs or a deep side part can create a flattering asymmetrical look that slims the face.
  • Long, face-framing layers that start below the chin can create an elongated look.
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, such as a messy bun or a half-up hairstyle.

Step by Step:

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the best hairstyle for round face 2020:

  1. Start by determining your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and looking at the shape of your face in the mirror.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape. You can consult with your stylist or use online resources to find inspiration.
  3. Ensure that your hair is healthy and well-maintained. Regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and protective styling can help keep your hair in top condition.
  4. Use styling products that add volume and texture to your hair. This can help create the illusion of a longer face shape.
  5. Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to find the perfect look for you. Don't be afraid to try something new!

Tips:

Here are some additional tips to help you rock the best hairstyle for round face 2020:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle. You want to feel comfortable and confident in your new look.
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle. Different hair types require different styling techniques and products.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, scarves, or hair clips to add some extra flair.
  • Be patient with your hair. It may take some trial and error to find the perfect hairstyle, but it's worth the effort.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for round face 2020:

Q: What are the best hairstyles for a round face?

A: The best hairstyles for a round face are those that add height and angles to the face, such as a voluminous topknot, a high ponytail, or long face-framing layers.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Blunt cuts, chin-length bobs, and center parts should be avoided if you have a round face. These styles can emphasize the roundness of the face and create an unflattering look.

Q: Can I still wear short hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes! Short hair can be flattering on a round face if it's styled correctly. Consider a pixie cut with choppy layers or a short bob with angled bangs.

Hair Care:

To maintain healthy and beautiful hair, it's essential to follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant spray first.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and nourished.
  • Protect your hair from environmental damage and UV rays by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

In conclusion, finding the best hairstyle for round face 2020 is all about creating angles that elongate the face. With the right hairstyle and a little bit of confidence, you can rock any look you desire. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Happy styling!

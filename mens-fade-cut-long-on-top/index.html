The Mens Fade Cut Long On Top A Complete Guide For 2023

Written by Frank Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy hairstyle that's easy to maintain and versatile, the mens fade cut long on top is a great option. This style has been popular for a few years now, and it's not going away anytime soon. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know about the mens fade cut long on top, including how to get the look, tips for styling, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get the Look
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Advice

Description

The mens fade cut long on top is a hairstyle that features shorter hair on the sides and back that gradually gets longer as it reaches the top of the head. The hair on top can be left long enough to style in a variety of ways, from messy and textured to slicked back and smooth. This style is popular because it's versatile and works well for a range of face shapes and hair types.

How to Get the Look

If you're interested in getting a mens fade cut long on top, the first step is to find a skilled barber who can help you achieve the look you want. When you go in for your appointment, bring in pictures of the style you're interested in, and be prepared to discuss your hair type and any concerns you have.

Hair Type

The mens fade cut long on top works well for a variety of hair types, from straight to curly. However, if you have particularly thick or coarse hair, you may want to consider getting some of the bulk removed with thinning shears or a razor.

Face Shape

The mens fade cut long on top can work well for a range of face shapes, from round to square. However, if you have a particularly long or narrow face, you may want to consider a slightly shorter style on top to balance out your features.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a mens fade cut long on top:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head to your desired length. A number 2 or 3 guard is a good starting point.
  3. Blend the hair on the sides and back into the hair on top using a clipper with a longer guard, such as a number 4 or 5.
  4. Use scissors to cut the hair on top to your desired length. Be sure to leave enough length to style, but not so much that it looks unkempt.
  5. Texturize the hair on top using thinning shears or a razor, if necessary.
  6. Style your hair using your preferred product, such as pomade or wax.

Tips for Styling

Here are a few tips for styling your mens fade cut long on top:

  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create texture and volume on top.
  • If you want a slicked back look, apply product to damp hair and use a comb to smooth it back.
  • If you want a messy, textured look, apply product to dry hair and use your fingers to create volume and movement.

FAQs

Is the mens fade cut long on top a low-maintenance hairstyle?

Yes, the mens fade cut long on top is a relatively low-maintenance hairstyle. However, you'll still need to visit your barber every few weeks for touch-ups and to keep the fade looking sharp.

Can the mens fade cut long on top work for all hair types?

Yes, the mens fade cut long on top can work for a variety of hair types, from straight to curly. However, if you have particularly thick or coarse hair, you may need to have some of the bulk removed to make styling easier.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your mens fade cut long on top looking sharp, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as a hair dryer or straightener, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Visit your barber every few weeks for touch-ups and to keep the fade looking sharp.

In conclusion, the mens fade cut long on top is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that can work well for a range of face shapes and hair types. With the right barber, styling products, and hair care routine, you can keep this look looking sharp and stylish for years to come.

How To Cut Simple Layers In Long Hair

Written by Daniel Jul 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

If you have long hair, adding layers can transform your look and give your hair more volume and movement. Cutting simple layers in long hair is an easy way to refresh your hairstyle without sacrificing length. In this article, we’ll cover how to cut simple layers in long hair step-by-step, as well as some tips and frequently asked questions.

How to

The first step to cutting simple layers in long hair is to gather the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair.

Next, start by parting your hair in the middle and dividing it into two equal sections. Clip one side of your hair out of the way, and work on the other side.

Step 1: Divide Your Hair

Take a small section of hair from the top of your head and comb it straight up. Hold it between your index and middle fingers, and cut the ends off with your scissors. This will be your guide for the rest of your layers.

Step 2: Cut the First Layer

Take another section of hair from the top layer, just below the guide section. Comb it straight up, and cut the ends off at the same length as the guide. This will create your first layer.

Step 3: Continue Cutting Layers

Repeat step 2 with the remaining top sections of your hair, working your way down towards the back of your head. Make sure to use the guide section as a reference for each subsequent layer.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

Once you have finished cutting the top layers of your hair, unclip the bottom section and repeat steps 1-3. When you have finished cutting all of your layers, use your scissors to blend the ends of your hair together.

Tips

  • When cutting layers, it’s always better to start with less hair and work your way up. You can always cut more hair, but you can’t put it back!
  • Remember to cut each section of hair at the same angle to create even layers.
  • If you’re not confident in your hair cutting skills, it’s always best to seek the help of a professional.

FAQ

How often should I cut my hair if I have layers?

It’s recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.

Can I cut layers in curly hair?

Yes, you can cut layers in curly hair! However, it’s important to cut curly hair when it’s dry, as wet hair can stretch and appear longer than it actually is.

Can I cut layers in short hair?

Yes, you can cut layers in short hair! However, the process may be slightly different depending on the length of your hair and the style you’re going for.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

Cutting simple layers in long hair is an easy way to update your hairstyle and give your hair more volume and movement. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve beautiful layers at home. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful!

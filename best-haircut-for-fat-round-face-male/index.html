Best Haircut For Fat Round Face Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Aug 27, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face with a bit of extra weight, finding the right haircut can be a daunting task. But don't worry, we have got you covered! In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on the best haircut for a fat round face male.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to finding the perfect haircut for a fat round face male, the key is to choose a style that will help elongate the face and draw attention away from the roundness. It's important to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face and instead opt for ones that add height to the top.

How to

The best way to find the perfect haircut for your face shape is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They will be able to analyze your face shape and suggest styles that will flatter your features. However, if you prefer to do your own research, there are a few things to keep in mind when choosing a haircut.

Consider the Length

Short haircuts tend to work well for round faces as they can help create the illusion of a longer face shape. However, if you prefer longer hair, consider adding layers to add height and volume to the top of the head.

Think About the Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to draw attention away from the roundness of the face. Opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on one side than the other, as they can help elongate the face.

Choose the Right Texture

The texture of your hair can also play a role in how well a certain haircut will work for your face shape. If you have thick, wavy hair, a longer, layered cut may work well. If you have fine hair, a shorter, textured cut may be more flattering.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of the best haircuts for a fat round face male:

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic, low-maintenance haircut that can work well for round faces. Here's how to achieve it:

  • Start by cutting your hair to an even length all over using clippers.
  • Gradually reduce the length of the clippers until you achieve your desired length.
  • Use scissors to trim any stray hairs around the ears.

The Side Part

The side part is a versatile haircut that can work well for a variety of face shapes. Here's how to achieve it:

  • Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  • Apply a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to your hair.
  • Use a comb to create a side part.
  • Style your hair using the product, making sure to add height to the top of the head.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best haircut for a fat round face male:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as bowl cuts or mushroom cuts.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and texture.
  • Try out different hairstyles using virtual try-on tools before committing to a cut.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair products to achieve your desired style.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a fat round face male?

The best haircut for a fat round face male is one that adds height to the top of the head and draws attention away from the roundness of the face. Short haircuts with layers and side-swept bangs can work well.

Should I avoid certain hairstyles if I have a fat round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as bowl cuts or mushroom cuts. These can make the face appear rounder.

Hair Care

To keep your haircut looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often.
  • Use a styling product that suits your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut for your fat round face male. Remember, it's all about finding a style that flatters your features and makes you feel confident!

Is Revlon One Step Hair Dryer And Volumizer Worth The Hype

Written by Aprilia Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who's always on the lookout for hair styling tools that can make your life easier, then you've probably heard of the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer. This hair dryer and volumizer combo has been making waves in the beauty community, and for good reason. In this article, we're going to take a closer look at this product to help you decide if it's worth investing in.

Description

The Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is essentially a hair dryer and round brush in one. It's designed to help you achieve a salon-quality blowout at home, without having to use multiple tools. The brush features a unique oval shape that's meant to help smooth out your hair while also adding volume and bounce. It also has a ceramic coating that's meant to help distribute heat evenly, reducing the risk of heat damage.

How to Use

Using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is actually pretty simple. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to help reduce the risk of heat damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties.

Step 3: Start Styling

Start by taking a section of hair and placing the brush at the root. Slowly glide the brush down the length of your hair, twisting it slightly as you go. Repeat this process until you've styled all of your hair.

Step 4: Finish with a Blast of Cool Air

Once you've finished styling, switch the dryer to the cool setting and blast your hair with cool air for a few seconds. This will help set your style and reduce frizz.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer:

  • Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to reduce the risk of heat damage.
  • Section your hair into manageable sections for easier styling.
  • Use the low heat setting to reduce the risk of heat damage.
  • Finish with a blast of cool air to set your style and reduce frizz.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer:

Is the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer good for all hair types?

Yes, the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is designed to work on all hair types, including thick, curly, and coarse hair.

Can I use the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on wet hair?

No, it's best to use the dryer on towel-dried hair to prevent damage.

How long does it take to dry and style hair with the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer?

It typically takes around 10-15 minutes to dry and style hair with the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer.

Hair Care

While the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer can be a great tool for achieving a salon-quality blowout at home, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using the highest heat setting on your hair dryer.
  • Take breaks from heat styling to give your hair a chance to recover.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish your hair.

Overall, the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer can be a great investment for anyone who wants to achieve a salon-quality blowout at home. Just be sure to use it properly and take care of your hair to prevent damage.

Shoulder Length Hairstyles With Bangs A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Oct 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Shoulder length hairstyles with bangs are a popular choice among women who want to add a touch of style and elegance to their hair. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about these versatile hairstyles, including how to style them, care for them, and more.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length hairstyles with bangs are a great way to add some personality to your hair without sacrificing length. From blunt bangs to side-swept fringe, there are plenty of options to choose from. Shoulder length hair can be worn curly, straight, or somewhere in between, making it a versatile choice for any occasion.

How To

If you're considering a shoulder length hairstyle with bangs, there are a few things you should know. First, make sure the length is right for your face shape and style. Second, choose the type of bangs that will flatter your face shape and features. Finally, decide on the style you want, whether it's a blunt cut or something more textured.

Step by Step

If you're not sure how to style shoulder length hair with bangs, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair until it's about 80% dry.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  4. Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting with the bangs.
  5. Once the bangs are dry, move on to the rest of your hair, working in sections.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten any sections that need it.
  7. Apply a small amount of hair oil to add shine and tame flyaways.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling shoulder length hairstyles with bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron to ensure your hair stays straight and sleek.
  • Use a small amount of hair oil to add shine and tame flyaways.
  • Try different types of bangs to see which one flatters your face shape the most.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hairstyles with bangs:

What face shapes are best suited for shoulder length hairstyles with bangs?

Shoulder length hairstyles with bangs can be flattering on a variety of face shapes, including oval, round, and heart-shaped faces.

What type of bangs should I choose?

The type of bangs you choose will depend on your face shape and features. Blunt bangs can be flattering on round faces, while side-swept fringe can soften the look of a square jawline.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length hairstyle with bangs looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type, and avoid washing your hair every day. Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with heat tools, and apply a small amount of hair oil to add shine and tame flyaways.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create and maintain beautiful shoulder length hairstyles with bangs. Whether you're looking for a sleek and sophisticated look or something more playful and fun, there's a shoulder length hairstyle with bangs that's perfect for you.

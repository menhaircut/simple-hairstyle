What Haircut Suits An Oblong Face

Written by Bardi Aug 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oblong face, you may find it challenging to determine what hairstyle can flatter your features and bring balance to your face shape. An oblong face is longer than it is wide, with a straight jawline and a forehead that is the same width as the cheekbones. Fortunately, there are many hairstyles that can suit an oblong face, and we've got you covered with some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect one for you.

Description

An oblong face shape is also known as a rectangular face shape. This face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face, with a straight jawline and a forehead that is the same width as the cheekbones. People with an oblong face shape tend to have high foreheads and prominent chins, and their cheeks are usually not very full. The best hairstyles for an oblong face are those that add width to the face, creating the illusion of a more oval shape.

How to Choose a Haircut for an Oblong Face?

When choosing a haircut for an oblong face shape, the goal is to add width to the face and create a more oval shape. This can be achieved through various hairstyles, including layers, bangs, and waves. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for an oblong face shape:

1. Layers

Layers can add volume and texture to the hair, which can help balance out an oblong face shape. The layers should start at cheekbone level or higher, and they should be cut in a way that adds width to the face.

2. Bangs

Bangs can also help balance out an oblong face shape by adding width to the forehead area. Side-swept bangs are a great option for oblong faces, as they create a diagonal line that helps break up the length of the face.

3. Waves

Wavy hairstyles can add volume and texture to the hair, which can help balance out an oblong face shape. Loose waves or beachy waves are a great option for oblong faces, as they create a soft, feminine look that can help make the face appear wider.

Step by Step Guide for Choosing a Haircut for an Oblong Face

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the perfect haircut for your oblong face shape:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine whether you have an oblong face shape. You can do this by measuring your face and comparing the length to the width. If your face is longer than it is wide, with a straight jawline and a forehead that is the same width as the cheekbones, then you have an oblong face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

Next, consider your hair type. Thin or fine hair may benefit from layers or bangs, while thick hair may require more weight to prevent it from becoming too bulky.

Step 3: Determine Your Hair Length

Decide on the length of your hair. Short hairstyles can add volume and texture, while longer hairstyles may require more layers or waves to add width to the face.

Step 4: Consult with a Stylist

Consult with a stylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type. They may recommend specific hairstyles or techniques to help create the illusion of a more oval face shape.

Tips for Styling Hair for an Oblong Face

Here are some tips for styling hair for an oblong face shape:

1. Avoid Center Parts

Avoid center parts, as they can make the face appear even longer. Instead, opt for a side part to create the illusion of a wider face.

2. Add Volume

Add volume to the hair with layers or waves to help balance out an oblong face shape. Backcombing or teasing the hair at the crown can also help create the illusion of a wider face.

3. Avoid Straight Styles

Avoid straight styles that can make the face appear even longer. Instead, opt for hairstyles with texture, such as waves or curls.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for an oblong face?

The best haircut for an oblong face is one that adds width to the face, creating the illusion of a more oval shape. This can be achieved through various hairstyles, including layers, bangs, and waves.

Do bangs suit an oblong face?

Yes, bangs can suit an oblong face shape by adding width to the forehead area. Side-swept bangs are a great option for oblong faces, as they create a diagonal line that helps break up the length of the face.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face?

Avoid hairstyles that make the face appear even longer, such as straight styles or center parts. Instead, opt for hairstyles with texture, such as waves or curls, and styles that add width to the face, such as layers or bangs.

Hair Care for an Oblong Face

Regular hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair and preventing damage. Here are some tips for hair care for an oblong face:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip the hair of its natural oils. Look for shampoos that are formulated for your hair type and avoid shampoos that contain harsh chemicals, such as sulfates.

2. Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and prevent breakage. Use a conditioner that is formulated for your hair type and apply it from mid-length to the ends of your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use heat protectant products before using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners. This can help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. This can also help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent damage from occurring.

Conclusion

If you have an oblong face shape, there are many hairstyles that can suit your features and bring balance to your face shape. Consider adding layers, bangs, or waves to your hair, and consult with a stylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type. Remember to take care of your hair by using gentle products, conditioning regularly, and protecting it from heat.

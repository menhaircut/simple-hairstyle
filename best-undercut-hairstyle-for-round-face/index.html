Best Undercut Hairstyle For Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Sep 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that just don't seem to suit your round face? Worry not, because we have the perfect solution for you - the undercut hairstyle! This versatile haircut works wonders for those with a round face, as it helps to create a more defined and structured look. In this article, we will be discussing the best undercut hairstyles for round faces, along with tips on how to style and maintain them.

Description

The undercut hairstyle is characterized by short, shaved sides and a longer top. This style has been a popular trend in recent years, and for good reason - it looks great on almost everyone, especially those with a round face. The shorter sides help to elongate the face, while the longer top adds volume and dimension.

How to Get the Best Undercut Hairstyle for Round Face

The first step to getting the perfect undercut hairstyle for your round face is to find a skilled hairstylist who has experience with this style. Once you have found the right stylist, you can discuss your desired look and they can advise you on the best cut and style for your face shape.

When it comes to the undercut hairstyle, there are a few variations that work particularly well for those with a round face:

  • The Classic Undercut: This style features short, shaved sides and a longer, slicked-back top. It works particularly well for those with thick, straight hair.
  • The Textured Undercut: This style features choppy, textured layers on top, and shorter, tapered sides. It works well for those with wavy or curly hair.
  • The Side-Swept Undercut: This style features longer, side-swept bangs and shorter, tapered sides. It works well for those with fine, straight hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Undercut Hairstyle

To style your undercut hairstyle, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as a volumizing mousse or pomade, to your hair.
  4. Blow dry your hair, using your fingers to create volume and texture.
  5. Style your hair as desired, using a comb or your fingers to create a messy, textured look.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Undercut Hairstyle

To keep your undercut hairstyle looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers about the best undercut hairstyle for round face:

Q: Will an undercut hairstyle make my face look even rounder?

A: No, when done correctly, an undercut hairstyle can actually help to elongate and define your face.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with an undercut?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair in a ponytail or bun with an undercut hairstyle. Just make sure to leave enough length on top to work with.

Q: Can I get an undercut if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes, an undercut can actually help to create the illusion of thicker hair by adding volume and texture.

Hair Care for Undercut Hairstyles

To keep your undercut hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, strong hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the best undercut hairstyle for round face with confidence and style!

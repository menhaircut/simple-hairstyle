What To Do When You Start Losing Your Hair

Written by Petter Jul 11, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you starting to notice more hair falling out than usual? Losing hair can be an alarming experience, but it doesn't have to be the end of the world. In this article, we'll go over what to do when you start losing your hair and how to take care of your hair to prevent further hair loss.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine if You're Losing Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide on What to Do When You Start Losing Hair
  • Tips on How to Prevent Hair Loss

How to Determine if You're Losing Hair

Everyone loses hair every day. It's normal to lose anywhere from 50 to 100 hairs per day. However, if you notice an increase in hair falling out or bald patches, then it's time to take action.

Here are some signs that you're losing hair:

  • You see more hair on your pillow when you wake up in the morning.
  • You notice more hair in your shower drain or on your brush when you style your hair.
  • You can see your scalp more easily than before, especially around the crown of your head.

Step-by-Step Guide on What to Do When You Start Losing Hair

If you're experiencing hair loss, don't panic. Here's a step-by-step guide on what to do:

Step 1: Determine the Cause of Your Hair Loss

The first step is to determine the cause of your hair loss. There are many reasons why people lose hair, including genetics, stress, hormonal changes, and certain medical conditions. Talk to your doctor to rule out any medical conditions that may be causing your hair loss.

Step 2: Change Your Diet

Eating a healthy diet is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure you're getting enough protein, iron, and vitamins A, C, and E. These nutrients are essential for healthy hair growth.

Step 3: Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can help prevent further hair loss. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and saw palmetto. These ingredients can help strengthen hair and promote healthy hair growth.

Step 4: Consider Hair Loss Treatments

If you're experiencing significant hair loss, you may want to consider hair loss treatments. There are many options available, including over-the-counter treatments like minoxidil and prescription medications like finasteride.

Tips on How to Prevent Hair Loss

Preventing hair loss is easier than you might think. Here are some tips:

  • Reduce stress in your life.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and avoid over-styling your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of protein, iron, and vitamins A, C, and E.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption.

FAQs

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss. Stress can disrupt the hair growth cycle, leading to increased hair shedding.

Q: Can I prevent hair loss?

A: Yes, you can prevent hair loss by eating a healthy diet, reducing stress, and using the right hair products.

Q: Is hair loss normal?

A: Yes, everyone loses hair every day. It's normal to lose anywhere from 50 to 100 hairs per day.

Hair Care for Healthy Hair

Finally, taking care of your hair is essential for healthy hair growth. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle wet hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair.

Remember, losing hair can be a scary experience, but it doesn't have to be. By taking care of your hair and taking steps to prevent hair loss, you can have healthy, beautiful hair for years to come.

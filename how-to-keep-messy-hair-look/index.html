How To Keep Messy Hair Look A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of trying to keep your hair perfectly styled all the time? Do you love the effortless, messy look but don't know how to achieve it? Look no further! In this guide, we will teach you how to keep that messy hair look without sacrificing style. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to achieve the perfect messy hair look.

Step 1: Start with the Right Haircut

The first step in achieving the perfect messy hair look is to start with the right haircut. This means getting a cut that will allow your hair to naturally fall into a messy, textured style. Some great haircuts for this look include a shag, layers, or a choppy bob.

Remember, the goal is to have a messy look, so don't worry if your haircut isn't perfectly even or if some pieces are longer than others.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right products is key to achieving that messy, textured look. Start by using a shampoo and conditioner that will add volume and texture to your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like sea salt or clay.

Next, use a lightweight styling product like a mousse or a hair wax. These products will help to add texture and hold to your hair without weighing it down.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair is a crucial step in achieving the perfect messy hair look. Start by rough drying your hair with your hands to remove any excess moisture. Then, use a round brush to lift and add volume to your hair as you blow dry.

Don't worry about getting your hair perfectly smooth or straight. The goal is to add texture and volume to your hair.

Step 4: Use a Texturizing Spray

Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray to add even more texture and hold to your hair. Spray the product onto your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths.

Use your hands to scrunch and tousle your hair as you apply the product. This will help to create that effortless, messy look.

Step 5: Add Volume with a Dry Shampoo

If your hair is looking a little flat, use a dry shampoo to add some volume and texture. Spray the product onto your roots and use your fingers to work it into your hair.

Not only will this help to add volume, but it will also help to absorb any excess oil and refresh your hair.

Step 6: Mess It Up

Now it's time to mess it up! Use your hands to tousle and separate your hair, creating that messy, textured look.

Don't be afraid to be a little rough with your hair. The more you mess it up, the better it will look.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish off your messy hair look with a light hold hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place without making it look too stiff or crunchy.

Spray the product onto your hair from a distance, holding the can about 6 inches away from your head. Use your hands to scrunch and tousle your hair as you apply the product.

Tips to Keep in Mind

  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush. This will help to preserve the natural texture of your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If your hair is naturally curly or wavy, embrace it! Messy hair looks great on all hair types.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair to maintain the messy look?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to maintain the messy look. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look flat and lifeless.

Q: Can I achieve the messy hair look without blow drying my hair?

A: Yes! While blow drying can help to add volume and texture to your hair, you can still achieve the messy look without it. Simply use a texturizing spray and mess up your hair with your hands.

Hair Care for Messy Hair

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. This means using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible, and getting regular trims to keep your ends healthy.

You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and nourished.

Final Thoughts

The messy hair look is all about embracing your natural texture and creating an effortless, undone style. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve this look in no time. So go ahead, embrace the messiness and rock that messy hair look!

How To Cut Your Own Hair Straight Brad Mondo Style

Written by Aprilia Oct 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to cut your own hair straight Brad Mondo style? Brad Mondo is a popular hairstylist who has gained popularity due to his unique style and techniques. If you're tired of paying for expensive salon visits or are unable to visit a salon, cutting your own hair is a great option. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut your own hair straight Brad Mondo style.

Step by Step Instructions

Before you start cutting your own hair, here are some things you will need:

  • Sharp Hair Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair Clips
  • Mirror

Once you have these items, follow these steps to cut your own hair straight Brad Mondo style:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

It's essential to wash your hair before cutting it. This will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Brush and Section Your Hair

Brush your hair thoroughly and section it into four parts. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start by cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Be careful not to cut too much at once.

Step 4: Cut The Top Layer

Once you have finished cutting the bottom layer, move on to the top layer. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle. This will give your hair a layered look.

Step 5: Check Your Hair

After you have finished cutting your hair, check it in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments and trim any uneven sections.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own hair:

  • Start with small cuts and trim your hair gradually.
  • Use a comb to guide your scissors and ensure a straight cut.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, or you might end up with uneven sections.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring and stressful.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair:

Can I Cut My Own Hair?

Yes, anyone can cut their own hair. However, it's essential to be patient and take your time.

What If I Mess Up?

If you mess up, don't worry. Hair grows back, and you can always visit a salon to fix any mistakes.

How Often Should I Cut My Hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own hair, it's essential to take care of it to maintain its health and shape. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

With these easy steps and tips, you can cut your own hair straight Brad Mondo style. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new techniques. Cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience!

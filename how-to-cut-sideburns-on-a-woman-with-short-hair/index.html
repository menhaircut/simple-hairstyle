How To Cut Sideburns On A Woman With Short Hair

Written by Robby Jun 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Short hair is a popular trend among women in recent years. It's low-maintenance, stylish, and versatile. One of the challenges of having short hair is managing sideburns. Sideburns can add a touch of elegance to your short hairstyle or make it look messy. In this article, we will discuss how to cut sideburns on a woman with short hair.

Description

Sideburns are the strips of hair that grow from the temples to the ears. They can be long, short, or shaved. Sideburns are a crucial part of short haircuts for women. They can frame your face, balance your features, and even make your hair look thicker. However, sideburns can also be challenging to maintain. They can grow unevenly, curl, or stick out. Cutting your sideburns correctly is essential to make your short hair look polished and stylish.

How to Cut Sideburns on a Woman with Short Hair

Step 1: Prepare Your Tools

The first step in cutting sideburns is to prepare your tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Make sure your scissors are clean and sharp. Dull scissors can damage your hair and create split ends.

Step 2: Wet Your Hair

Wet your hair and sideburns with water. Wet hair is easier to cut and style. You can use a spray bottle or run your hair under the faucet. Use a towel to dry your hair gently. Don't rub your hair with the towel, as it can create frizz and tangles.

Step 3: Comb Your Sideburns

Use a comb to separate your sideburns from the rest of your hair. Comb your sideburns straight down and trim any hair that sticks out. Make sure both sideburns are even in length and thickness. You can use hair clips to hold the rest of your hair away from your face.

Step 4: Cut Your Sideburns

Hold your scissors at a slight angle and cut your sideburns from the bottom up. Don't cut straight across, as it can create a blunt and unnatural look. Cut only a small amount of hair at a time and check your progress in the mirror. You can always trim more if needed, but you can't add hair that you've already cut.

Step 5: Check Your Symmetry

Check your sideburns for symmetry. Stand in front of a mirror and compare the length and shape of both sideburns. Adjust any uneven parts by trimming them with your scissors. Make sure your sideburns are not too long, as they can overpower your face and make your hair look unbalanced.

Tips

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up.
  • Use a comb to guide your scissors and create a natural-looking shape.
  • Don't rush the process, take your time to ensure precision.
  • Trim your sideburns every few weeks to maintain their shape and length.
  • If in doubt, seek professional help from a hairstylist.

FAQ

Can I cut my sideburns with a razor?

It's not recommended to cut your sideburns with a razor, as it can create an uneven and jagged look. Use scissors instead, as they provide more control and precision.

How long should my sideburns be?

The length of your sideburns depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences. As a general rule, your sideburns should be in line with the middle of your ear and the corner of your eye.

How often should I trim my sideburns?

You should trim your sideburns every few weeks to maintain their length and shape. If you have fast-growing hair, you may need to trim them more often.

Hair Care

Cutting your sideburns is just one part of maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. To keep your short hair in good condition, you should also follow a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and harsh weather conditions.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

By following these tips, you can keep your short hair and sideburns looking their best.

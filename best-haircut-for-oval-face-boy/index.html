The Best Haircut For Oval Face Boy A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Sep 29, 2023 · 5 min read

If you are a boy with an oval-shaped face, you may feel overwhelmed when it comes to choosing the right haircut for your face shape. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for oval face boys, including how to choose the right style, step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice. So, let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Oval Face Boy

Before we dive into the best haircuts for oval face boys, let's discuss how to choose the right one. The key is to find a style that will balance your facial features and complement your face shape.

Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a haircut that adds width to the forehead and temples, such as a side-parted fringe or a quiff.
  • Avoid haircuts that add height to the top of the head, as this can elongate the face even more.
  • Consider a haircut that has some texture or layers to add dimension to your face.
  • Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear too round or too narrow.

With these tips in mind, let's take a look at the best haircuts for oval face boys.

Best Haircuts for Oval Face Boy

There are several haircuts that work well for oval face boys. Here are some of our favorites:

The Side-Parted Fringe

The side-parted fringe is a classic haircut that works well for oval face boys. It adds width to the forehead and temples, which balances out the face shape. To achieve this look, ask your barber to cut your hair to about two inches on top and tapered on the sides. Then, part your hair to the side and style it with a bit of pomade or wax.

The Quiff

The quiff is another great haircut for oval face boys. It adds height to the front of the head, which balances out the face shape. To achieve this look, ask your barber to cut your hair to about three or four inches on top and tapered on the sides. Then, style your hair upwards and back with a bit of pomade or wax.

The Messy Textured Crop

The messy textured crop is a low-maintenance haircut that works well for oval face boys. It adds texture and dimension to the hair, which complements the face shape. To achieve this look, ask your barber to cut your hair to about an inch on top and tapered on the sides. Then, style your hair with your fingers and a bit of texturizing spray.

Step-by-Step Guide to Get the Perfect Haircut

Now that you know the best haircuts for oval face boys, let's discuss how to get the perfect haircut. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose the right haircut for your face shape, considering the tips we discussed earlier.
  2. Find a reputable barber or hairstylist who has experience with your chosen haircut.
  3. Communicate your desired haircut clearly to your barber or hairstylist.
  4. Sit still and relax while your hair is being cut.
  5. Ask your barber or hairstylist for styling tips and product recommendations.
  6. Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims and using the right hair care products.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims every four to six weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use the right hair care products for your hair type and desired style.
  • Wash your hair regularly but not too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to nourish your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for oval face boys:

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, you can still rock any of the haircuts we mentioned earlier. Just make sure to find a barber or hairstylist who has experience cutting curly hair and knows how to work with your texture.

What if I want a shorter or longer haircut?

You can still choose a shorter or longer haircut, as long as it balances out your face shape. For example, a buzz cut or a long, layered cut can work well for oval face boys if done right.

What if I don't like my new haircut?

If you don't like your new haircut, don't panic. You can always ask your barber or hairstylist to make some adjustments or try a different style next time. It may take some trial and error to find the perfect haircut for your face shape and style.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Boy

Finally, here are some general hair care tips for oval face boys:

  • Choose a shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type and scalp.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and other elements by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your face shape, you can achieve a stylish and flattering look that suits your personality and lifestyle. So, go ahead and try out one of the haircuts we discussed earlier, and enjoy your new look!

Bob Cut For Round Face 2021 How To Get The Perfect Look

Written by Oliver Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face and are looking for a stylish haircut to flatter your features, the bob cut is an excellent option. This classic hairstyle has been around for decades, but it has evolved over the years to suit different face shapes and hair types. In this article, we will guide you through everything you need to know about getting a bob cut for a round face in 2021.

Description

The bob cut is a short to medium-length hairstyle that typically reaches the chin or shoulders. It is characterized by its straight lines and even length, which creates a clean and polished look. The bob cut has been popularized by celebrities such as Victoria Beckham, Rihanna, and Taylor Swift, and it has become a staple hairstyle in the fashion industry.

How to

If you want to get a bob cut for a round face in 2021, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist

Before getting a bob cut, it is essential to consult with your hairstylist to determine which type of bob cut will suit your face shape and hair type. A professional hairstylist will be able to advise you on the best length, texture, and style for your hair.

Step 2: Choose the right length

For a round face, a bob cut that reaches the chin or shoulders is the most flattering. This length will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Avoid bob cuts that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear wider.

Step 3: Opt for layers

Layered bob cuts are ideal for round faces, as they add volume and texture to the hair. Layers can also frame the face and draw attention to your best features. Ask your hairstylist to add layers to your bob cut to create a more dynamic look.

Step 4: Consider bangs

Bangs can complement a bob cut and enhance your facial features. For a round face, opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that are longer on the sides. These types of bangs will lengthen your face and create a more balanced look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when getting a bob cut for a round face in 2021:

  • Choose a bob cut that suits your hair type and texture. If you have thin or fine hair, a layered bob cut can add volume and texture. If you have thick or curly hair, a blunt bob cut can create a sleek and polished look.
  • Use styling products to enhance your bob cut. Adding a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray can give your hair more body and movement.
  • Regularly trim your bob cut to maintain its shape and length. A bob cut that is too long or uneven can make your face appear wider.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a bob cut for a round face in 2021:

1. Will a bob cut make my face look rounder?

No, a bob cut can actually elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Choose a bob cut that is chin-length or longer to flatter your features.

2. Can I wear a bob cut if I have curly hair?

Yes, a bob cut can look great on curly hair. Opt for a blunt bob cut to create a sleek and polished look, or choose a layered bob cut to add volume and texture.

3. How often should I trim my bob cut?

You should trim your bob cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and length. Regular trims can also prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

To keep your bob cut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals or alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

By following these tips and steps, you can get the perfect bob cut for your round face in 2021. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and choose a bob cut that flatters your features and suits your hair type and texture. With the right care and maintenance, your bob cut can look stylish and chic for months to come.

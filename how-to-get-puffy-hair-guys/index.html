How To Get Puffy Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

If you're a guy who wants to add some volume to your hair, you're in luck. There are several ways to get puffy hair that look great and are easy to achieve. In this article, we'll cover some tips and tricks to help you get the puffy hair you want.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Puffy Hair Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Getting Puffy Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Puffy Hair

How to Get Puffy Hair Guys

There are several ways to get puffy hair for guys. Here are some of the most popular:

  • Blow-drying your hair upside down
  • Teasing your hair with a comb or brush
  • Using a volumizing spray or mousse
  • Getting a perm

Each of these methods has its own pros and cons, so you'll need to experiment to find which one works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you want to get puffy hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing spray or mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  3. Flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you dry it.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a comb or brush to tease your hair at the roots. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down.
  5. When you've teased your hair to your desired level of puffiness, use a light hairspray to hold it in place.

Remember, the key to getting puffy hair is to add volume at the roots. So, focus on lifting your hair up from the scalp as you dry and tease it.

Tips for Getting Puffy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the puffy hair you want:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to tease your hair. This will help prevent breakage and damage.
  • Don't over-tease your hair. Too much teasing can cause your hair to become tangled and difficult to manage.
  • Use a light hand when applying hairspray. Too much hairspray can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different volumizing sprays and mousses to find one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Can I get puffy hair if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can still get puffy hair with short hair. Focus on teasing your hair at the roots to add volume.

Q: Will teasing my hair damage it?

A: Teasing your hair too much can cause damage and breakage. Be gentle and use a wide-tooth comb or brush to prevent damage.

Q: How long will my puffy hair last?

A: Your puffy hair should last all day with the help of some hairspray. However, if you have particularly fine or straight hair, you may need to touch up throughout the day.

Hair Care for Puffy Hair

If you want to keep your puffy hair looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. Blow-dry your hair on a low setting and use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you should be able to get the puffy hair you want. Remember to experiment with different methods to find what works best for your hair type. And, as always, take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.

How to Know What is My Face Shape | Tips, Step by Step Guide

If you've ever wondered what your face shape is, you're not alone. Knowing your face shape can help you choose the right hairstyle, glasses, and makeup, but it can be difficult to determine on your own. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to figure out what your face shape is and give you tips on how to style your hair based on your face shape.

Step 1: Measure Your Face

The first step in determining your face shape is to measure your face. You'll need a flexible tape measure or a piece of string and a ruler. Measure the following:

  • Forehead: Measure from the peak of one eyebrow arch to the peak of the opposite eyebrow arch.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jawline starts to angle up.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting and ending at the pointiest part below the outer corner of each eye.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Write down each of these measurements.

Step 2: Determine Your Jawline

Look at your jawline and determine its shape. Is it round, square, or pointed? Write down your jawline shape.

Step 3: Measure Your Forehead

Look at the measurement you took of your forehead. Is it wider than your cheekbones or narrower? Write down whether your forehead is wider or narrower than your cheekbones.

Measure Your Cheekbones

Look at the measurement you took of your cheekbones. Are they the widest part of your face or narrower than your forehead? Write down whether your cheekbones are the widest part of your face or not.

Tips for Styling Your Hair Based on Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, you can use it to choose the right hairstyle for you. Here are some tips:

  • If you have a round face, go for hairstyles that add height and lengthen your face.
  • If you have a square face, go for hairstyles with soft edges that round out your face.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, go for hairstyles that add width to the bottom of your face.
  • If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any hairstyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I can't determine my jawline shape?

If you're having trouble determining your jawline shape, ask a friend or family member for their opinion.

I have a combination of face shapes. What should I do?

If you have a combination of face shapes, go with the one that is most dominant.

Can my face shape change?

Your face shape can change slightly over time due to aging or changes in weight, but the overall shape should remain the same.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, you can use it to choose the right hair care routine for you. Here are some tips:

  • If you have a round face, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • If you have a square face, avoid hairstyles that are too short or have severe angles.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.
  • If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any hairstyle.

Knowing your face shape can help you make informed decisions about your hair and makeup. Follow these steps to determine your face shape and use the tips to choose the perfect hairstyle for you.

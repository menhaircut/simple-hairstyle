Will I Look Younger With A Fringe

Written by Bardi Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to refresh your look, you might be considering getting a fringe. But will a fringe really make you look younger? In this article, we'll explore the answer to this question and provide you with some useful tips on how to get the most out of your new hairstyle.

Description

A fringe, also known as bangs, is a hairstyle where the hair is cut and styled to fall over the forehead. Fringes come in a variety of styles, from blunt and straight to side-swept and wispy. They can be cut at different lengths and angles to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to Get a Fringe

If you're considering getting a fringe, here are some steps you can follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Fringe Style

Before you get a fringe, it's important to choose the right style that will suit your face shape and hair type. You can consult with your hairdresser or look online for inspiration. Some popular fringe styles include:

  • Straight and blunt fringe
  • Side-swept fringe
  • Wispy fringe
  • Curtain fringe

Step 2: Get a Professional Haircut

Once you've chosen your fringe style, it's time to get a professional haircut. A qualified hairdresser will be able to cut your fringe to the right length and angle, and style it to suit your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Maintain Your Fringe

To keep your fringe looking its best, you'll need to maintain it regularly. This may involve trimming it every few weeks, washing and conditioning it, and blow-drying it in the right direction.

Tips for Looking Younger with a Fringe

If you want to look younger with a fringe, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a fringe style that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Opt for a shorter fringe that sits above your eyebrows.
  • Go for a fringe with layers or texture to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe that creates a diagonal line across your face, which can help to make your face appear slimmer and more youthful.
  • Pair your fringe with a fresh, modern outfit to complete your youthful look.

FAQ

Will a Fringe Really Make Me Look Younger?

A fringe can make you look younger by drawing attention to your eyes and cheekbones, which are associated with youthfulness. However, the effect will depend on the style of fringe you choose and how well it suits your face shape and hair type.

What Face Shapes Suit a Fringe?

Most face shapes can suit a fringe, as long as the fringe is cut and styled to suit the face shape. For example:

  • A blunt or straight fringe can suit a round face by creating the illusion of length.
  • A side-swept fringe can suit a heart-shaped face by softening the forehead and drawing attention to the eyes.
  • A wispy fringe can suit an oval face by adding texture and movement to the hair.

Hair Care Tips

When you have a fringe, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a heat protectant when blow-drying or styling your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and split ends.
  • Trim your fringe regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Conclusion

If you're considering getting a fringe, it's worth trying out different styles and consulting with a hairdresser to find the right look for you. A fringe can be a great way to refresh your look and add some youthful energy to your appearance. With the right care and maintenance, your fringe can be a stylish and flattering addition to your hairstyle.

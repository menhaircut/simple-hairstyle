How To Wear Sports Headbands With Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Oct 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your short hair getting in your face while you work out? Do you want to add some style to your gym look? Then it's time to invest in a sports headband! But if you have short hair, you may be wondering how to wear sports headbands without looking like you're wearing a child's hair accessory. Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to wear sports headbands with short hair, so you can look and feel your best during your next workout.

How to Wear Sports Headbands with Short Hair: Step by Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Headband

When it comes to sports headbands, there are many different styles to choose from. Look for headbands that are specifically designed for sports, as they will be made from moisture-wicking materials that will keep you cool and dry. You'll also want to choose a headband that is the right size for your head. Headbands that are too tight can cause headaches, while headbands that are too loose will slip off during your workout.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before you put on your headband, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you have long bangs, you can pin them back with bobby pins so they don't get in your way. You can also apply a bit of hairspray or gel to your hair to help keep it in place.

Step 3: Put on the Headband

Slide the headband over your head, positioning it so that it sits right at your hairline. Make sure the headband is snug but not too tight. You can adjust the headband as needed to make sure it stays in place.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now that your headband is in place, you can style your hair however you like. If you have short hair, you can leave it down or pull it back into a ponytail or bun. You can also add some texture to your hair by using a texturizing spray or salt spray.

Tips for Wearing Sports Headbands with Short Hair

Tip 1: Experiment with Different Styles

There are many different styles of sports headbands to choose from, so don't be afraid to try out different styles to see which one works best for you. Some headbands are wider and more padded, while others are thinner and more streamlined. You can also try out different colors and patterns to add some personality to your gym look.

Tip 2: Use Bobby Pins

If you have short hair that tends to slip out of your headband, you can use bobby pins to keep it in place. Simply slide a bobby pin through your hair and into the headband to secure it.

Tip 3: Wash Your Headband Regularly

Since sports headbands are designed to absorb sweat, they can get pretty gross after a few workouts. To keep your headband clean and fresh, wash it regularly in cold water with a mild detergent. Hang it up to air dry.

FAQ: Sports Headbands with Short Hair

Q: Can I wear a sports headband if I have really short hair?

A: Yes! Even if you have a buzz cut, you can wear a sports headband to keep sweat out of your eyes.

Q: Can I wear a sports headband if I have fine hair?

A: Absolutely! Just make sure to choose a headband that is not too tight, as it can cause breakage and damage to fine hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

While sports headbands can help keep your hair out of your face during workouts, it's important to take care of your hair outside of the gym as well. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy and looking its best:

- Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

- Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

- Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swim cap.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear sports headbands with short hair like a pro. So go ahead and add some style to your gym look while keeping your hair out of your face!

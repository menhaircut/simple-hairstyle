How To Do Shaping Around The Face For A Flattering Look

Written by Bardi Sep 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Shaping around the face can be a great way to update your look and enhance your natural features. In this article, we will guide you through the process of shaping around the face with simple steps and tips.

Description

Shaping around the face is a technique where the hair is cut to frame and enhance the features of the face. It can be done with different hair lengths and styles, from short pixie cuts to long layers. This technique can also help balance out the facial proportions and create a flattering look.

How to

Step 1: Consultation

The first step in shaping around the face is to consult with your stylist. Discuss your desired look, your face shape, and your hair type to determine the best style for you. Your stylist can also recommend the right products and tools to maintain your new hairstyle.

Step 2: Preparation

Before cutting, prepare your hair by washing and drying it thoroughly. Use a heat protectant spray if you plan to use heat styling tools. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and make sure it is free of knots.

Step 3: Sectioning

Section your hair by dividing it into four parts: the top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to secure each section as you work on the others.

Step 4: Cutting

Start cutting the hair from the back and work your way to the front. Use your fingers or a comb to guide the scissors as you cut. Cut small sections at a time and check your progress in the mirror as you go. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once or you may end up with an uneven cut.

Step 5: Finishing

Once you have finished cutting, style your hair as desired. Use a hairbrush, curling iron, or straightener to create the look you want. Finish with a hairspray or styling product to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape.
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when selecting a style.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • Check your progress in the mirror frequently.
  • Use heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Maintain your new hairstyle with regular trims and proper hair care.

FAQ

Q: Will shaping around the face work for all hair types?

A: Yes, shaping around the face can be done on all hair types, but the style may vary depending on your hair texture and thickness.

Q: How often should I get a trim to maintain the shape?

A: It is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and keep split ends at bay.

Q: Can I do shaping around the face myself?

A: It is best to leave this technique to a professional stylist to ensure a proper and even cut.

Hair Care

To maintain your new hairstyle, it is important to follow a proper hair care routine. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture. Avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals on your hair as they can damage it. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and minimize breakage. Protect your hair from the sun and heat styling tools by using a UV protectant spray and heat protectant spray. Finally, get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

Shaping around the face can be a great way to update your look and enhance your natural features. Follow these simple steps and tips to achieve a flattering hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.

Short Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50 With Glasses A Guide To Flattering And Chic Haircuts

Written by William Jun 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a woman over 50 with a round face and glasses? Are you looking for a chic and flattering short hairstyle that complements your features and enhances your beauty? Look no further than our guide to short hairstyles for round faces over 50 with glasses. From pixie cuts to bob styles, we've got you covered with expert tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to keep your locks looking their best.

Description: Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50 with Glasses

As we age, our hair changes in texture, color, and thickness. For women over 50 with round faces and glasses, finding a short hairstyle that flatters their face shape and complements their frames can be challenging. However, with the right cut and styling techniques, you can achieve a chic and youthful look that accentuates your features and expresses your personality.

How to: Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Round Face and Glasses

When selecting a short hairstyle for your round face and glasses, consider the following factors:

Face Shape

Round faces have soft curves and a wider width than length. To elongate your face and create a more angular appearance, opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown and sides, such as pixie cuts, layered bobs, and asymmetrical styles. Avoid blunt cuts and center parts that accentuate the roundness of your face.

Glasses Frame

Your glasses frame can also influence your hairstyle choice. If you have thick or bold frames, opt for short hairstyles that balance the weight and width of your glasses, such as layered bobs, shaggy pixies, and textured crops. If you have thin or delicate frames, go for simple and classic cuts that frame your face without overwhelming your glasses, such as sleek bobs, blunt cuts, and side-swept bangs.

Hair Texture and Color

Your hair texture and color can also affect the way your short hairstyle looks on you. If you have fine or thin hair, choose cuts that add volume and texture, such as choppy layers, curls, and waves. If you have thick or coarse hair, opt for styles that reduce bulk and weight, such as tapered bobs, pixie cuts, and buzzed sides. If you have gray or silver hair, embrace your natural color and enhance it with highlights, lowlights, or balayage.

Step by Step: How to Achieve Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50 with Glasses

Follow these steps to achieve some of the most popular short hairstyles for round faces over 50 with glasses:

Pixie Cut

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: crown, sides, and nape.
  3. Use a clipper or scissors to trim the sides and nape to the desired length, leaving the crown longer.
  4. Blend the layers with scissors and texturizing shears.
  5. Style the crown with a blow dryer and round brush, adding volume and texture.
  6. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to define the ends and create a tousled look.
  7. Put on your glasses and enjoy your chic and edgy pixie cut!

Layered Bob

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: crown, sides, and nape.
  3. Use a razor or scissors to create layers and texture, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  4. Blend the layers with scissors and texturizing shears.
  5. Style the bob with a blow dryer and round brush, flipping the ends out or under.
  6. Apply a small amount of serum or oil to add shine and control frizz.
  7. Put on your glasses and enjoy your chic and sophisticated layered bob!

Asymmetrical Style

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: crown, sides, and nape.
  3. Use a clipper or scissors to create an asymmetrical shape, with one side longer than the other.
  4. Blend the layers with scissors and texturizing shears.
  5. Style the longer side with a blow dryer and round brush, adding volume and texture.
  6. Apply a small amount of mousse or gel to keep the hair in place.
  7. Put on your glasses and enjoy your chic and daring asymmetrical style!

Tips: How to Maintain and Care for Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50 with Glasses

Here are some tips to keep your short hairstyle looking its best:

  1. Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to maintain the shape and texture of your cut.
  2. Use high-quality hair products that suit your hair type and style, such as volumizing shampoo, texturizing spray, and finishing cream.
  3. Avoid over-washing and over-styling your hair, as it can damage and dry out your locks.
  4. Protect your hair from heat and sun exposure by wearing a hat or using a thermal protectant spray.
  5. Experiment with different accessories, such as headbands, barrettes, and scarves, to add variety and interest to your hairstyle.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50 with Glasses

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face with glasses?

The best short hairstyle for a round face with glasses depends on your personal preferences, hair type, and glasses frame. Some popular choices include pixie cuts, layered bobs, and asymmetrical styles.

How do I know if a short hairstyle will suit my round face and glasses?

You can try on different wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to visualize how a short hairstyle will look on you. You can also consult with your stylist or friends for their opinions and recommendations.

Can I color my short hair if I have glasses?

Yes, you can color your short hair if you have glasses. However, you should choose a color that complements your skin tone and glasses frame, and maintain regular touch-ups and conditioning treatments to keep your color vibrant and healthy.

Hair Care: The Importance of Maintaining Healthy and Beautiful Hair

Short hairstyles for round faces over 50 with glasses can be trendy and flattering, but they also require proper care and maintenance to keep your hair healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining optimal hair health:

  1. Eat a balanced and nutritious diet that includes vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth and strength, such as biotin, iron, and zinc.
  2. Drink plenty of water and avoid excessive alcohol and caffeine consumption, which can dehydrate your hair and scalp.
  3. Protect your hair from environmental stressors, such as pollution, wind, and humidity, by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  4. Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation and promote hair growth.
  5. Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage and weaken your hair, and opt for natural and gentle hair care products.

By following these hair care tips and styling techniques, you can achieve short hairstyles for round faces over 50 with glasses that are chic, flattering, and easy to maintain. Remember to embrace your natural beauty and personality, and have fun experimenting with different styles and accessories!

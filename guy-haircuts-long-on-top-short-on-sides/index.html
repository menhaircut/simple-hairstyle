Guy Haircuts Long On Top Short On Sides A Relaxed Guide For Your Next Haircut

Written by Frank Jun 29, 2023 Â· 2 min read

Are you looking for a fresh and stylish haircut? If so, you may want to consider the popular trend of guy haircuts long on top short on sides. This style has been around for a while, but it is still a trendy and fashionable option for men of all ages. In this guide, we will discuss everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, maintenance tips, and more. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Haircut
  • FAQ About Guy Haircuts Long on Top Short on Sides
  • Hair Care for This Haircut

How to Get the Haircut

The first step to getting a guy haircut long on top short on sides is to find a skilled hairstylist who has experience with this style. You can ask your friends, family, or colleagues for recommendations or search online for hairstylists in your area. Once you find a hairstylist, schedule a consultation to discuss the style and see if it will suit your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

During the consultation, you should also bring a few pictures of the haircut you want to show your hairstylist. This will help them understand your vision and create a customized haircut that will suit your needs and preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get a guy haircut long on top short on sides:

  1. Wash and dry your hair before the haircut to ensure it is clean and tangle-free.
  2. Sit in the hairstylist's chair and discuss the style you want.
  3. Section your hair into three parts: top, sides, and back.
  4. Using scissors or clippers, trim the sides and back of your hair to your desired length.
  5. Blend the sides and top using scissors or clippers to create a smooth transition.
  6. Cut the top of your hair to the desired length, leaving it longer than the sides and back.

    7. Tips for Maintaining the Haircut

    Here are some tips for maintaining your guy haircut long on top short on sides:

    • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for touch-ups and maintenance.
    • Use high-quality hair care products, such as shampoo, conditioner, and styling products, to keep your hair healthy and strong.
    • When styling your hair, use a comb or brush to create the desired shape and hold.
    • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair greasy or weighed down.

    FAQ About Guy Haircuts Long on Top Short on Sides

    What face shape is best for this haircut?

    This haircut is versatile and can suit most face shapes, but it is particularly flattering for oval or square-shaped faces.

    What hair type is best for this haircut?

    This haircut can work well for most hair types, including straight, wavy, or curly hair.

    Can I style my hair in different ways with this haircut?

    Yes, you can style your hair in different ways with this haircut, such as slicked back, messy, or textured.

    Hair Care for This Haircut

    To keep your hair healthy and strong with this haircut, it is important to follow a hair care routine that includes shampooing, conditioning, and nourishing your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

    • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
    • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or straighteners, too often, as this can damage your hair.

      • In conclusion, a guy haircut long on top short on sides is a stylish and trendy option for men who want a versatile and low-maintenance haircut. By following the tips and steps outlined in this guide, you can get the perfect haircut for your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. So, go ahead and book your appointment with a skilled hairstylist and get ready to rock this trendy haircut!

How To Get Korean Like Hair In 2023 A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 16, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a fan of K-dramas or K-pop, you've probably noticed how beautiful and healthy Korean hair looks. From their soft waves to their sleek straight hair, Korean celebrities are known for their flawless locks. But how can you get Korean like hair? In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve that beautiful Korean hair in relaxed English language.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Korean Like Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Maintain Korean Like Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Routine for Korean Like Hair

How to Get Korean Like Hair

There are several ways to achieve Korean like hair, including haircuts, styling, and hair care. Here are some tips on how to achieve that perfect Korean hair:

  • Choose the right haircut: Korean hairstyles are known for their soft layers and often have no harsh lines. You can opt for a bob, a shoulder-length cut, or a long layered cut to achieve that soft and feminine look.
  • Get a perm: Korean perms are popular for their soft and natural waves. You can get a digital perm or a cold perm to achieve those soft curls.
  • Straighten your hair: Korean hair is also known for its sleek and straight texture. You can straighten your hair using a flat iron or get a hair rebonding treatment.
  • Use hair accessories: Hair accessories such as hairpins, headbands, and bows are popular in Korea. They can add a touch of cuteness and femininity to your hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve Korean like hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. It's important to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  2. Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair. You can use a Korean hair mask or make a DIY mask using natural ingredients like avocado, honey, and egg.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and bounce to your hair.
  4. If you have curly or wavy hair, you can use a hair straightener to achieve that sleek and straight Korean hair.
  5. If you have straight hair, you can use a curling iron to achieve those soft and natural waves.
  6. Add hair accessories like hairpins, headbands, or bows to add a touch of cuteness and femininity to your hairstyle.

Tips to Maintain Korean Like Hair

Here are some tips to maintain your Korean like hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like a flat iron or curling iron every day. This can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to the ends of your hair to keep it moisturized and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like hair dyes or bleach. This can damage your hair and make it dry and dull.
  • Get a regular trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting Korean like hair:

  • Can I get Korean like hair if I have naturally curly hair? Yes, you can get Korean like hair even if you have naturally curly hair. You can use a hair straightener to achieve that sleek and straight Korean hair.
  • Can I get Korean like hair if I have short hair? Yes, you can get Korean like hair even if you have short hair. You can opt for a bob or a shoulder-length cut to achieve that soft and feminine look.
  • Do I need to use Korean hair products to get Korean like hair? No, you don't need to use Korean hair products to get Korean like hair. You can use any hair products that suit your hair type and texture.

Hair Care Routine for Korean Like Hair

Here's a hair care routine for Korean like hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and bounce to your hair.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, you can use a hair straightener to achieve that sleek and straight Korean hair.
  • If you have straight hair, you can use a curling iron to achieve those soft and natural waves.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to the ends of your hair to keep it moisturized and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools like a flat iron or curling iron every day.
  • Get a regular trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, getting Korean like hair is easy with the right haircut, styling, and hair care routine. Follow these tips and you'll be on your way to achieving that beautiful and healthy Korean hair.

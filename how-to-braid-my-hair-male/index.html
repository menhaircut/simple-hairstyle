How To Braid Your Hair As A Male

Written by Christine Sep 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a male looking to try out braided hairstyles? Braids are a great way to switch up your look and add some texture to your hair. However, it can be a bit intimidating to braid your own hair, especially if you've never done it before. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps on how to braid your hair as a male.

Table of Contents

  • How to Braid Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Braiding Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Braids

How to Braid Your Hair

Before we get started, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a comb, hair ties, and possibly some hair gel or wax, depending on your hair type.

There are a few different types of braids you can try, including:

  • Cornrows
  • Box braids
  • Fishtail braids
  • French braids

The technique for each braid is slightly different, but the basic steps are the same.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by combing your hair to remove any tangles.

2. Decide where you want to start your braid. For cornrows, you'll typically start at the front of your head and work your way back, while for box braids you'll usually start at the nape of your neck.

3. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and divide it into three equal sections.

4. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the new middle section.

5. Continue braiding in this pattern, adding more hair to each section as you go. For cornrows, you'll want to add hair from each side of the section as you braid, while for box braids you'll add hair from underneath the section.

6. Once you've braided all the way down to the end of your hair, secure it with a hair tie.

Tips for Braiding Your Hair

1. Practice makes perfect! Don't be discouraged if your first few attempts at braiding don't turn out quite right. Keep practicing and you'll get better.

2. Use hair gel or wax to help keep your hair in place while you braid.

3. Don't braid your hair too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

4. Experiment with different types of braids to see which ones you like best.

FAQs

Q: How long does my hair need to be to braid it?

A: It depends on the type of braid you're trying to do. For cornrows and box braids, your hair should be at least a few inches long. For French braids and fishtail braids, your hair can be shorter.

Q: Can I braid my hair if it's curly?

A: Absolutely! In fact, braids can be a great way to show off your curls.

Hair Care for Braids

1. Keep your scalp clean and moisturized by using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

2. Don't leave your braids in for too long, as this can cause damage to your hair.

3. Be gentle when taking your braids out. Use a detangling spray or conditioner to help loosen any knots.

4. Massage your scalp regularly to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to braid your hair as a male with confidence. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and have fun with it!

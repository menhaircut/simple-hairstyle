Ways To Wear Big Hair Clips

Written by Aprilia Aug 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on ways to wear big hair clips. Hair clips have been a part of hair styling for a long time. However, oversized hair clips have become a trend in recent years. If you have been wondering how to incorporate big hair clips into your hairstyle, then you are in the right place. This article will discuss different ways to wear big hair clips, step-by-step guides, tips, and FAQs.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wear Big Hair Clips
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Wearing Big Hair Clips

How to Wear Big Hair Clips

Big hair clips are an excellent way to add a touch of glam to any hairstyle. They are perfect for any occasion, from weddings to casual outings. Below are ways to wear big hair clips:

Half-Up Half-Down Style

The half-up half-down style is a classic hairstyle that big hair clips can enhance. To achieve this style, take a section of hair from the front and clip it with a big hair clip. This style works best with loose waves or straight hair.

Bun Styles

Big hair clips can also be used to create different bun styles. The clips can be used to hold the bun in place or to add some sparkle to the hairstyle.

Ponytail Styles

Ponytail hairstyles can also be enhanced with big hair clips. You can use the clips to hold the hair in place or to decorate the ponytail.

Step-by-Step Guide

Below is a step-by-step guide on how to wear big hair clips:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clip

There are different types of big hair clips available in the market. It is essential to choose the right clip that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair depending on the style you want to achieve. For example, for a half-up half-down style, section the front part of your hair.

Step 3: Secure Your Hair

Secure the sectioned hair with a big hair clip. Make sure the clip is firmly in place.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style the rest of your hair according to the style you want to achieve.

Step 5: Add Finishing Touches

Add any finishing touches such as hairspray to hold the hairstyle in place.

Tips for Wearing Big Hair Clips

Below are some tips for wearing big hair clips:

  • Choose a clip that matches your outfit
  • Use a clip that complements your hair color
  • Experiment with different hairstyles
  • Secure the clip firmly in place

FAQs

Below are some frequently asked questions about wearing big hair clips:

What is the best way to wear big hair clips?

The best way to wear big hair clips is to experiment with different hairstyles. You can wear them with a half-up half-down style, bun styles, and ponytail styles.

Can big hair clips be worn with any hair type?

Yes, big hair clips can be worn with any hair type. However, it is essential to choose a clip that suits your hair type.

Hair Care Tips

Below are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair frequently
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

With these tips and tricks, you can rock big hair clips with confidence. Whether you are going for a casual or formal look, big hair clips can elevate your hairstyle to the next level.

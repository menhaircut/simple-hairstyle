What Haircut Makes Your Face Look Slimmer

Written by Luffy Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about the shape of your face? Do you want to know what haircut can make your face look slimmer? You're in luck! In this article, we will discuss the different hairstyles that can help you achieve a slimmer-looking face.

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the different hairstyles that can make your face look slimmer, it's important to understand what makes a face appear rounder. A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. To create the illusion of a slimmer face, you want to create angles and elongate your facial features.

How To

The first step in achieving a slimmer-looking face is to choose the right haircut. The best haircuts for a round face are those that create angles, add height, and elongate your features. Here are some hairstyles to consider:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help create the illusion of a slimmer face by adding volume and movement to your hair. Long layers can also help elongate your facial features, making your face appear longer and slimmer.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help create angles and add height to your face. When styling your bangs, be sure to sweep them to the side to create a diagonal line across your forehead, which can help make your face appear slimmer.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can help create angles and add height to your face, which can help make your face appear slimmer. When getting a pixie cut, be sure to ask your stylist to add layers to the top of your hair to create volume and movement.

Step By Step

Now that you know what hairstyles can make your face look slimmer, here are some step-by-step instructions to help you achieve the look:

1. Long Layers

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Part your hair in the center.
  3. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to your hair.
  4. Apply a volumizing spray to the roots of your hair.
  5. Tease the crown of your hair to add height.
  6. Spray your hair with hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Part your hair to the side.
  3. Blow dry your bangs to the side to create a diagonal line across your forehead.
  4. Curl the ends of your hair to add movement.
  5. Apply a volumizing spray to the roots of your hair.
  6. Spray your hair with hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Pixie Cut

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume.
  4. Use a flat iron to add texture to your hair.
  5. Spray your hair with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

When choosing a haircut to make your face look slimmer, be sure to consider your hair type and face shape. The right haircut can help enhance your natural features and create a more flattering look.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs or a bob with straight ends. These hairstyles can make your face appear wider and rounder.

How can I make my face look slimmer without cutting my hair?

You can create the illusion of a slimmer face by using contouring makeup techniques. Apply a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to the sides of your face and blend it in to create a more angular look.

Hair Care

To maintain your new hairstyle and keep your hair healthy, be sure to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a gentle shampoo, conditioning regularly, and using a heat protectant when styling with heat tools.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut can make a big difference in the appearance of your face. By creating angles and elongating your features, you can achieve a slimmer-looking face and feel more confident in your appearance.

How To Get Rid Of Semi Permanent Red Hair Dye

Written by Frank Jul 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your red hair dye and want to change your hair color? You might be wondering how to get rid of semi permanent red hair dye without damaging your hair. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to remove your red hair dye and get your hair back to its original color.

Description

Semi permanent hair dye is a great way to temporarily change your hair color. However, it can be tough to remove once it has been applied. Red hair dye, in particular, is challenging to remove as it tends to stain the hair more than other colors. To remove semi permanent red hair dye, you will need patience and the right products.

How to Get Rid of Semi Permanent Red Hair Dye

Step 1: Clarify Your Hair

The first step in removing semi permanent red hair dye is to clarify your hair. Clarifying shampoos are designed to remove buildup and product residue from your hair. Use a clarifying shampoo to wash your hair thoroughly.

Step 2: Apply a Color Remover

Once your hair is clean, you can apply a color remover. Color removers are designed to break down the hair dye molecule and remove the color from your hair. Follow the instructions on the color remover carefully and apply it to your hair.

Step 3: Rinse Your Hair

After the color remover has been applied, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water. Make sure all the color remover has been removed.

Step 4: Apply a Hair Mask

Color removers can be harsh on your hair, so it's essential to apply a hair mask to restore moisture to your hair. Leave the hair mask on for at least 10 minutes, then rinse it out with warm water.

Tips

  • Be patient. Removing semi permanent red hair dye can take time.
  • Use a color remover specifically designed for semi permanent hair dye.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair as it can strip the color further.
  • Avoid heat styling your hair until it has fully recovered from the color removal process.

FAQ

Can I remove semi permanent red hair dye without using a color remover?

While it is possible to remove semi permanent red hair dye without using a color remover, it will be more challenging and time-consuming. A color remover is designed to break down the hair dye molecule and remove the color from your hair quickly.

Will removing the red hair dye damage my hair?

Using a color remover can be harsh on your hair, so it's essential to follow up with a hair mask to restore moisture to your hair. If you have damaged or fragile hair, it's best to consult a professional before attempting to remove the red hair dye.

Hair Care

After removing the red hair dye, it's essential to take care of your hair to prevent further damage. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid heat styling your hair as much as possible.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask at least once a week to restore moisture to your hair.

With these tips, you can safely and effectively remove semi permanent red hair dye and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

How To Cut A Bob Weave Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, a bob weave might be the perfect option for you. The good news is that you don't have to go to a salon and spend a lot of money to get this look. In this article, we'll show you how to cut a bob weave yourself, with easy-to-follow steps and helpful tips.

Table of Contents

  2. Preparation
  3. Sectioning Your Hair

  5. Blending Your Weave
  6. Styling Your Bob Weave

Materials You'll Need

Before you start cutting your bob weave, make sure you have the following materials:

  • A mirror
  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips or hair ties
  • Spray bottle with water
  • Straightening or curling iron (optional)

Preparation

The first step in cutting your bob weave is to prepare your hair. Make sure it's clean and dry, and detangle it with a comb. If you have curly or wavy hair, you might want to straighten it first to get a more accurate cut. You can also add some water to your hair with a spray bottle to make it easier to work with.

Sectioning Your Hair

Next, you need to section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips or ties to keep each section separate. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly and avoid mistakes.

Cutting Your Bob Weave

Now it's time to start cutting your bob weave. Take the first section of your hair and comb it straight. Use your scissors to cut it at the desired length, starting from the back and moving forward. Remember to cut a little bit at a time, and check your progress in the mirror frequently.

Repeat this process with the other sections of your hair, making sure to cut them evenly. If you want a more layered look, you can also cut the top layers slightly shorter than the bottom ones.

Blending Your Weave

Once you've cut your bob weave, you might notice that it looks a bit uneven or choppy. To create a more natural look, you need to blend your weave with your natural hair. Take a comb and gently blend the two together, using a scissor to trim any stray hairs if necessary.

Styling Your Bob Weave

Now that you've cut and blended your bob weave, you can style it any way you like. You can straighten it with a flat iron for a sleek look, or curl it with a curling iron for a more voluminous effect. You can also add some hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for a Perfect Bob Weave

  • Start with a longer length than you want, as you can always trim more later.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it will look shorter when it dries.
  • Take your time and check your progress frequently in the mirror.
  • If you're not sure about cutting your hair yourself, ask a friend or family member to assist you.

FAQs

Can I cut a bob weave on my own?

Yes, you can cut a bob weave yourself with the right tools and technique. Just make sure to take your time and follow the steps carefully.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my bob weave?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim more hair or go to a salon for a professional fix. Just remember to start with a longer length than you want.

How often should I cut my bob weave?

You should aim to cut your bob weave every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Your Bob Weave

To keep your bob weave looking its best, you need to take good care of it. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and shiny.

Conclusion

Cutting a bob weave yourself might seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and technique, it's actually quite simple. Just remember to take your time, follow the steps carefully, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve a stylish and low-maintenance haircut that you'll love.

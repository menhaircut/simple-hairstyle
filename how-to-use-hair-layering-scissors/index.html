How To Use Hair Layering Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Layering your hair is a great way to add volume, texture, and shape to your locks. If you’re looking to give your hair a little extra something, hair layering scissors are the perfect tool for the job. In this article, we’ll go through everything you need to know about how to use hair layering scissors. From how to hold them to the different hair layering techniques, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Use Hair Layering Scissors
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Using Hair Layering Scissors
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Layered Hair

How to Use Hair Layering Scissors

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let’s go over the basics. Hair layering scissors are specialized scissors designed to cut hair at an angle, creating layers. They’re sharp and precise, making them ideal for creating a variety of layering techniques.

When using hair layering scissors, you want to hold them at an angle. The angle will depend on the technique you’re using, but generally, you’ll want to hold the scissors at a 45-degree angle. This angle allows you to create soft, blended layers.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, let’s go through the step-by-step process of using hair layering scissors:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

It’s important to start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see the different layers as you cut them.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into manageable pieces using hair clips. This will make it easier to see what you’re doing and ensure that you’re cutting each section evenly.

Step 3: Choose your technique

There are several different techniques you can use with hair layering scissors. Some popular techniques include point cutting, slide cutting, and razor cutting. Choose the technique that best suits your hair type and desired look.

Step 4: Hold the scissors at an angle

Hold the scissors at a 45-degree angle, or the angle recommended for your chosen technique.

Step 5: Cut the hair

Using the hair layering scissors, cut the hair at the desired angle. Be sure to cut in small sections, and don’t cut too much at once. It’s always better to cut less and go back for more if needed.

Step 6: Blend the layers

Once you’ve cut all the sections, use a comb or your fingers to blend the layers together. This will create a seamless, natural look.

Tips for Using Hair Layering Scissors

Now that you know how to use hair layering scissors, here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with small sections of hair, especially if you’re new to layering.
  • Use a comb to help guide the scissors and create even, precise cuts.
  • Be gentle with the scissors – they’re sharp and can easily damage your hair if used incorrectly.
  • If you’re not sure which technique to use, ask your hairdresser for advice.

FAQs

Here are some common questions people have about using hair layering scissors:

Q: Can I use regular scissors to layer my hair?

A: While you can technically use regular scissors to layer your hair, hair layering scissors are specifically designed for the job. They’re sharper and more precise, making it easier to create the desired look.

Q: How often should I layer my hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type, length, and desired look. Generally, you can layer your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Q: Can I layer my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can layer your hair at home. However, if you’re not experienced with hair cutting, it’s best to start with small sections and go slow.

Hair Care Tips for Layered Hair

Now that you’ve layered your hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for layered hair:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to use hair layering scissors, it’s time to give your hair a little extra something. Whether you’re going for a subtle change or a dramatic transformation, hair layering scissors are a great tool to have in your hair care arsenal.

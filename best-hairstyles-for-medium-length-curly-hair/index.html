Best Hairstyles For Medium Length Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Aug 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to style, especially when it comes to medium length hair. However, with the right hairstyle, you can embrace your natural texture and show off your curls. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for medium length curly hair.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium length curly hair is defined as hair that falls between the chin and shoulder length. This hair type can be challenging to style because it tends to be frizzy and difficult to manage. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a variety of hairstyles that showcase your curls.

How to

The first step in styling medium length curly hair is to find a haircut that works with your natural texture. Layers are a great option for curly hair because they help to define the curls and reduce frizz. When styling, it's important to use products specifically designed for curly hair. This includes a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner, as well as a styling product like mousse or gel.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating the best hairstyles for medium length curly hair:

1. Curly Lob

To create a curly lob, start by washing and conditioning your hair with a curl-enhancing product. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment, scrunching your hair as you go. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any loose curls. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

For a half-up half-down hairstyle, start by parting your hair down the middle. Take the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie. Leave the rest of your hair down to showcase your curls.

3. Messy Bun

To create a messy bun, pull your hair back into a low ponytail. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun, securing with bobby pins. Pull a few pieces of hair out around your face for a soft, romantic look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling medium length curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush, which can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help hydrate and define your curls.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for medium length curly hair?

The best haircut for medium length curly hair is one with layers, which help to define the curls and reduce frizz. A lob or shoulder-length cut with long layers is a great option.

What products should I use on my medium length curly hair?

You should use products specifically designed for curly hair, such as a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner, as well as a styling product like mousse or gel.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your medium length curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush, which can cause frizz.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help hydrate and define your curls.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can embrace your natural curls and show off your medium length curly hair.

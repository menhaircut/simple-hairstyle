How To Do An Undercut Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to try? An undercut haircut might be the one for you! This style has been popular for several years now, and it shows no signs of going out of fashion anytime soon. An undercut haircut is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways. It is characterized by short or shaved sides and a longer top. In this article, we'll show you how to do an undercut haircut in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

  • How to Do an Undercut Haircut

Description

The undercut haircut is a trendy hairstyle that is characterized by shaved or short sides and a longer top. This hairstyle is suitable for both men and women, and it can be styled in many ways. The undercut haircut is popular because it is easy to maintain and gives a modern and edgy look.

How to Do an Undercut Haircut

To do an undercut haircut, you will need some essential tools such as clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. You can either do this on your own or ask for the help of a friend. Here's how to do an undercut haircut:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash your hair thoroughly and dry it with a towel. Make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products.
  2. Decide on the length of the top and sides of your hair. Use the clippers to shave the sides of your hair to the desired length. You can start with a longer guard and gradually move to a shorter guard to achieve a faded look.
  3. Use scissors to trim the top of your hair to your desired length. You can choose to have a textured or blunt cut.
  4. Blend the sides and top by using the clippers to taper the hair around the edges.
  5. Use a comb to check the length of your hair and trim any uneven parts.
  6. Style your hair as desired. You can use a hair product to give your hair volume and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing an undercut haircut:

  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head to ensure that your haircut is even.
  • Start with a longer guard when cutting the sides of your hair and gradually move to a shorter guard to achieve a faded look.
  • When trimming the top of your hair, use scissors to create texture and movement.
  • Use hair products to give your hair volume and hold.
  • Do not cut your hair too short on the sides as this can result in an unbalanced look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about an undercut haircut:

1. Can I do an undercut haircut on curly hair?

Yes, you can. However, it might be more challenging to achieve a clean and even look due to the natural texture of curly hair. It is best to ask for the help of a professional hairstylist.

2. How often should I trim my undercut haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, it is recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and shape of your haircut.

3. Can I style my undercut haircut in different ways?

Yes, you can. An undercut haircut is versatile, and you can style it in various ways such as slicked back, messy, or textured.

Hair Care

After doing an undercut haircut, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Apply hair oil or serum to keep your hair moisturized and smooth.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and style.

Now that you know how to do an undercut haircut, it's time to try it out! Remember to follow the steps carefully and take your time. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and modern haircut that suits your personality and lifestyle.

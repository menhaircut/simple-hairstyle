What Does A Long Pixie Haircut Look Like

Written by Daniel Oct 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking of getting a new haircut but not sure which one to go for? A long pixie haircut might be the perfect choice for you. In this article, we will discuss what a long pixie haircut looks like, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

A long pixie haircut is a shorter version of a bob haircut but longer than a traditional pixie cut. It usually ends just above the ears, and the layers create movement and texture. The longer length makes it easier to style and can be a great option for those who want a low-maintenance haircut.

How to Get a Long Pixie Haircut

If you are interested in getting a long pixie haircut, it is important to find a stylist who is experienced in this type of haircut. Bring a picture of the haircut you want and discuss it with your stylist. They will be able to advise you on the best length and style for your face shape and hair type.

During the haircut, your stylist will use scissors to create layers and texture. They may also use a razor to add even more texture. The final result will be a haircut that is easy to style and maintain.

Step by Step Guide to Style a Long Pixie Haircut

Styling a long pixie haircut is simple and easy. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or wax, to your hair.
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, lifting the roots to create volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to create loose waves or curls.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

With a long pixie haircut, you can create a variety of looks, from sleek and polished to messy and textured. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the style that works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining a Long Pixie Haircut

Maintaining a long pixie haircut is easy and requires minimal effort. Here are some tips to help you keep your haircut looking great:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.

FAQ

1. Is a long pixie haircut suitable for all face shapes?

A long pixie haircut can be flattering on most face shapes, but it is important to consult with your stylist to find the best length and style for your features.

2. Can I style a long pixie haircut in different ways?

Yes, a long pixie haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and polished to messy and textured.

3. Do I need to use special products to maintain a long pixie haircut?

Using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair healthy and shiny, but you don't need to use any special products to maintain a long pixie haircut.

Hair Care

A long pixie haircut is easy to maintain and requires minimal effort. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny, and avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain the shape of your haircut, and experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the style that works best for you.

How To Style Women s Hair With Undercut

Written by Eveline Jun 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle? An undercut may be just what you need! This trendy hairstyle involves shaving the hair on the sides and back of your head, leaving a longer layer on top. It's a versatile look that can be styled in many ways, but it can be intimidating to figure out how to style women's hair with undercut. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice for styling your undercut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Before we dive into the steps for styling your undercut hair, make sure you have the following items on hand:

  • Hair styling products (e.g. mousse, gel, hairspray)
  • A comb or brush
  • A blow dryer (optional)
  • A flat iron or curling wand (optional)

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's right for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product of your choice. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, focusing on the longer top layer.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to part your hair on the side or in the middle, depending on your preference. Comb the longer layer of hair forward and the shorter layers on the sides and back of your head down.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

There are many ways to style your undercut hair, depending on your mood and the occasion. Here are a few ideas:

  • Slicked Back: Apply a generous amount of hair gel or mousse to your hair and comb it back away from your face. Use a blow dryer to set the style in place.
  • Messy Bun: Pull your hair back into a low bun, leaving some loose strands around your face. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.
  • Beach Waves: Use a curling wand or flat iron to create loose waves in your hair. Apply a texturizing spray to enhance the beachy look.
  • Braids: Create a braid on one side of your head, starting from the longer top layer and incorporating the shorter layers on the side. Secure the end of the braid with a hair elastic or bobby pin.

Tips for Styling Undercut Hair

Here are a few tips to help you style your undercut hair like a pro:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and desired style.
  • Use a comb or brush to create a smooth, polished look, or use your fingers for a more textured, messy look.
  • Don't be afraid to accessorize your undercut with hair clips, headbands, or hats.
  • If you're feeling adventurous, consider adding some color to your hair to make your undercut stand out even more.

FAQs

Can I still wear my hair up with an undercut?

Absolutely! Depending on the length of your hair, you can pull it back into a ponytail, braid, or bun. You can also use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair in place.

Do I need to shave my head to get an undercut?

No, you don't have to shave your entire head to get an undercut. You can have your hairdresser shave just the sides and back, leaving the top layer longer.

Will an undercut work for my hair type?

An undercut can work for many hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and thick. Talk to your hairdresser about whether an undercut is right for you.

Hair Care for Undercut Hair

Maintaining an undercut requires some extra hair care, since the shorter layers on the sides and back of your head can grow out quickly. Here are a few tips:

  • Visit your hairdresser regularly to keep the shorter layers trimmed.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, since they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to nourish your hair and add shine.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently style your undercut hair in a variety of ways. Have fun experimenting with different looks and finding the ones that suit you best!

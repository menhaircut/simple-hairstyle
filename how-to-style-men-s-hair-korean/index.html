How To Style Men s Hair Korean A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Oct 12, 2023 · 2 min read

If you're interested in Korean pop culture or simply want to switch up your hairstyle, you might be wondering how to style men's hair Korean. Not to worry – we've got you covered! In this article, we'll go over the steps to achieve a Korean-inspired hairstyle, as well as tips and FAQs to help you along the way.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Men's Hair Korean
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Korean Hair
  • FAQs About Styling Men's Hair Korean
  • Hair Care for Korean-Inspired Hairstyles

How to Style Men's Hair Korean

Korean hairstyles for men are often characterized by a mix of edgy and trendy styles. From textured crops to sleek undercuts, there are a variety of hairstyles to choose from. However, most Korean-inspired hairstyles share a few key features, such as:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Textured layers
  • Short sides with longer top

Keep these features in mind when deciding which Korean hairstyle you want to try. From there, you can customize the style to fit your hair type and personal preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieve a Korean-inspired hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If you have thick or curly hair, you may want to blow dry it straight or use a flat iron.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as pomade or hair wax, to the top of your hair. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair and create texture.
  3. Create side-swept bangs by combing your hair to one side. Use a hair dryer or flat iron to set the style in place.
  4. If desired, use a trimmer or scissors to shape your hair on the sides and back. Most Korean hairstyles have shorter sides with a longer top.

Remember, every hairstyle is unique and you may need to adjust the steps to fit your hair type and length. Don't be afraid to experiment and find what works best for you!

Tips for Styling Korean Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve your desired Korean hairstyle:

  • Choose a product that matches your hair type. For example, if you have thin hair, use a lightweight product like a hair gel. If you have thick hair, opt for a stronger hold product like a wax or clay.
  • Use a comb or brush to create a clean part for the side-swept bangs.
  • If you're not sure which Korean hairstyle to try, consider bringing a photo to your stylist for inspiration.
  • Don't forget to maintain your hairstyle by getting regular trims and using hair care products that are suited for your hair type.

FAQs About Styling Men's Hair Korean

What is the most popular Korean hairstyle for men?

The most popular Korean hairstyle for men is the textured crop. This style features short sides with a choppy, textured top that can be styled in a variety of ways.

Can I style Korean hair without using heat tools?

Yes, you can achieve a Korean-inspired hairstyle without using heat tools. Use a styling product like a pomade or wax to create texture and shape your hair with your fingers.

How often should I get a haircut for a Korean-inspired hairstyle?

Most Korean-inspired hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep their shape. Plan on getting a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your style looking fresh.

Hair Care for Korean-Inspired Hairstyles

To keep your Korean-inspired hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suited for your hair type.
  • Limit the use of heat tools, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with SPF.

Following these hair care tips can help keep your Korean-inspired hairstyle looking healthy and vibrant.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a Korean-inspired hairstyle that suits your personal style. Remember to experiment and have fun with your hair, and don't be afraid to switch things up!

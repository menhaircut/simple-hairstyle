How To Trim Men s Hair On Top With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the barbershop every month? Learning how to trim men's hair on top with scissors is a great way to save money and maintain your hair at the same time. In this guide, we'll go over the steps to achieve a great haircut from the comfort of your own home.

Description

Trimming men's hair on top with scissors is a simple process that can be done by anyone with the right tools and techniques. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to achieve a professional-looking haircut in no time. The key to success is to take your time and follow the steps carefully.

How to Trim Men's Hair on Top with Scissors

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a good pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here's how to trim men's hair on top with scissors:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to wash it thoroughly. This will remove any dirt or product buildup and make it easier to cut. Rinse your hair with warm water and shampoo, then rinse again with cold water to close the hair cuticles.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Once your hair is clean and damp, use a comb to detangle it. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an even result. Use a wide-toothed comb to remove any knots, then switch to a finer-toothed comb to smooth out your hair.

Step 3: Create a Guide

To create a guide for your haircut, start by sectioning your hair. Use your comb to create a part in the middle of your head, then split your hair into two sections. Take a small section of hair from the front and comb it straight up. Use your fingers to hold the hair in place, then cut it straight across to create a guide. Repeat this process on the other side.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Once you have a guide, it's time to start cutting. Use your comb to lift a section of hair, then cut it using your scissors. Work your way around your head, cutting small sections of hair at a time. Make sure to cut your hair evenly and to use your guide to ensure that your hair is the same length all around.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

As you cut your hair, make sure to check your progress in the mirror. This will help you to see if you've missed any spots and to make sure that your hair is even. If you notice any uneven areas, go back and trim them carefully.

Step 6: Blend the Sides

Once you've cut the top of your hair, it's time to blend the sides. Use your comb to lift a section of hair from the side of your head, then use your scissors to trim it. Continue to do this, working your way around your head, until the sides are blended with the top.

Step 7: Clean Up the Edges

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's time to clean up the edges. Use your scissors to trim any stray hairs around your ears and neck. You can also use clippers to clean up the back of your neck if needed.

Tips for Trimming Men's Hair on Top with Scissors

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best possible result when trimming men's hair on top with scissors:

  • Always start with clean, damp hair
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time
  • Use your mirror to check your progress as you go
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're not sure what to do

FAQ

Can I use regular household scissors to cut my hair?

No, it's important to use scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair. Regular household scissors can damage your hair and lead to split ends.

How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go back and trim the area carefully. If you're not sure what to do, ask a friend or family member for help.

Hair Care

After you've finished trimming your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions

With these tips, you'll be able to trim men's hair on top with scissors like a pro. Remember to take your time, follow the steps carefully, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.

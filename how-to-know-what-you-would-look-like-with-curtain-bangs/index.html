How To Know What You Would Look Like With Curtain Bangs

Written by Daniel Sep 05, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you wondering if curtain bangs would suit your face shape and hair texture? Curtain bangs are a trendy hairstyle that can add a touch of elegance and sophistication to your look. However, before you commit to getting curtain bangs, it's essential to know what you would look like with them.

Table of Contents

  • How to Know What You Would Look Like with Curtain Bangs
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Curtain Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

How to Know What You Would Look Like with Curtain Bangs

The easiest way to know what you would look like with curtain bangs is by trying them out virtually. You don't have to commit to cutting your hair to see if curtain bangs would suit you. Here are some ways to know what you would look like with curtain bangs:

1. Use a Virtual Try-On Tool

You can use a virtual try-on tool to see what you would look like with curtain bangs. These apps allow you to upload a photo of yourself and apply different hairstyles, including curtain bangs. Some popular virtual try-on tools for hairstyles include:

  • ModiFace
  • Hairstyle Makeover
  • Virtual Makeover

Using these virtual try-on tools can help you visualize how curtain bangs would look on you. However, keep in mind that the results may not be 100% accurate, so it's best to use them as a reference only.

2. Try on a Wig

If you're not ready to commit to cutting your hair, you can try on a wig with curtain bangs. This is a more realistic way to see what you would look like with curtain bangs since you can see yourself in real-time.

You can buy a wig with curtain bangs or create one yourself by cutting a wig to the desired length. This way, you can try on different styles and lengths of curtain bangs to see what suits you best.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to try on curtain bangs virtually:

Step 1: Choose a Virtual Try-On Tool

Choose a virtual try-on tool that allows you to try on different hairstyles, including curtain bangs. Some popular options include ModiFace, Hairstyle Makeover, and Virtual Makeover.

Step 2: Upload Your Photo

Upload a clear photo of yourself to the virtual try-on tool. Make sure the photo is well-lit and shows your face clearly.

Step 3: Apply Curtain Bangs

Choose the curtain bangs option in the virtual try-on tool and adjust the length and thickness to your liking. Some tools may also allow you to adjust the color of the curtain bangs to match your hair.

Step 4: Save and Share

Once you're happy with your new look, save the photo and share it with your friends and family to get their opinion.

Tips for Choosing the Right Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to help you choose the right curtain bangs for your face shape and hair texture:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

Curtain bangs can look great on any face shape, but some styles may suit your face shape better than others. If you have a round face, go for longer curtain bangs that frame your face. If you have a square face, choose curtain bangs that are wispy and tousled.

2. Think About Your Hair Texture

Curtain bangs can work with any hair texture, but some styles may require more maintenance than others. If you have thick hair, consider getting curtain bangs that are longer and more layered. If you have fine hair, go for shorter curtain bangs that are more blunt.

3. Consult with a Hairstylist

If you're not sure which style of curtain bangs would suit you best, consult with a hairstylist. They can give you personalized advice based on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

FAQs

1. Can I try on curtain bangs in a salon?

Yes, you can ask your hairstylist to show you what you would look like with curtain bangs using a virtual try-on tool or by trying on a wig.

2. How long do curtain bangs take to grow out?

Curtain bangs can take anywhere from three to six months to grow out, depending on the length and thickness of your bangs.

3. Are curtain bangs high maintenance?

Curtain bangs require some maintenance to keep them looking their best. You may need to trim them every four to six weeks to prevent them from getting too long.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Here are some hair care tips to keep your curtain bangs looking their best:

1. Blow Dry Your Bangs

Blow dry your curtain bangs with a round brush to give them volume and shape. This will also prevent them from looking flat and oily.

2. Use Dry Shampoo

Use dry shampoo to keep your curtain bangs looking fresh between washes. This will also help absorb excess oil and prevent your bangs from looking greasy.

3. Trim Your Bangs Regularly

Trim your curtain bangs every four to six weeks to prevent them from getting too long. This will also help maintain their shape and prevent them from looking uneven.

By following these tips, you can keep your curtain bangs looking their best and make sure they suit your face shape and hair texture.

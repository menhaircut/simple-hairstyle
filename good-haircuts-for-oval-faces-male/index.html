Good Haircuts For Oval Faces Male A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that suits your oval face shape? Well, you’re in luck! We have compiled a complete guide on good haircuts for oval faces male. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know, from how to determine your face shape to the best hairstyles for oval faces. Let’s get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for oval faces, it’s essential to determine your face shape. Here are the steps you can follow:

  1. Stand in front of the mirror.
  2. Pull your hair back, so your face is fully visible.
  3. Take a close look at the shape of your face.

There are six common face shapes, including oval, square, round, heart, diamond, and oblong. If your face is longer than it is wide, and your jawline is slightly narrower than your forehead, then you have an oval face shape.

The Best Haircuts for Oval Faces Male

Men with oval faces are fortunate in that they can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, some haircuts look better than others. Here are some of the best haircuts for oval faces male:

1. Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are perfect for oval faces, as they accentuate the face shape's natural symmetry. You can opt for a buzz cut, crew cut, or a classic taper fade. These haircuts are low maintenance and require little styling.

2. Medium Length Haircuts

Medium length haircuts look great on oval faces, as they balance the face's features. You can try a textured crop, side-swept fringe, or a messy quiff. These hairstyles offer versatility and can be styled in different ways.

3. Long Haircuts

Long haircuts also suit oval faces well, as they add volume and dimension. You can try a layered cut, beach waves, or a top knot. These hairstyles require more maintenance and styling but are worth the effort.

Step by Step Guide to Get the Perfect Haircut

Now that you know the best haircuts for oval faces male, it’s time to get the perfect haircut. Follow these steps:

  1. Find a reputable barber or stylist.
  2. Bring a picture of the haircut you want or describe it in detail.
  3. Ask for their opinion on what haircut would suit your face shape.
  4. Make sure to communicate any concerns or preferences you have.
  5. After the haircut, ask for styling tips and product recommendations.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you’ve got the perfect haircut, it’s essential to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a suitable shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use styling products that suit your hair type and style.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

FAQs

What if I don’t like the haircut?

If you’re not happy with the haircut, don’t be afraid to speak up. Most barbers and stylists are willing to make adjustments or offer a refund if you’re not satisfied.

Can I try different hairstyles for oval faces?

Yes, you can try different hairstyles for oval faces to see what works best for you. Experiment with different lengths, textures, and styles.

What if I have thinning hair?

If you have thinning hair, opt for a shorter haircut or a textured style that adds volume. You can also use hair products that add thickness and body.

Hair Care for Oval Faces Male

Good haircuts for oval faces male are just the starting point. To keep your hair looking its best, it’s essential to take care of it. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of nutrients and vitamins that promote hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get enough sleep to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss.
  • Use hair products that suit your hair type and style.

With these tips and good haircuts for oval faces male, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect hairstyle for your face shape. Remember to communicate with your barber or stylist and take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance.

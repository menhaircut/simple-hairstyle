Description

Short pixie haircuts have been a popular hairstyle for a while now. This hairstyle is low maintenance and perfect for people who are always on-the-go. However, sometimes you might find yourself wanting to switch up the style or fix your pixie cut. In this guide, we will show you how to fix short pixie hair easily and quickly.

How to Fix Short Pixie Hair

Fixing short pixie hair can be a bit tricky because of its length. However, there are a few ways to do it. You can add texture, volume, or even change the parting. Here are a few tips:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. Make sure your hair is damp but not soaking wet.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair. This will add texture and volume to your hair.

Step 3: Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair. Make sure to lift the roots of your hair while blow-drying to add volume.

Step 4: Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture to your hair. You can add curls or waves to your hair to create a messy look.

Step 5: Change the parting of your hair. If you usually have a side parting, try a middle parting. This will give your hair a new look.

Step 6: Use a hair wax or pomade to add texture and hold to your hair. Apply a small amount of the product to your fingertips and work it through your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Short Pixie Hair

Short pixie haircuts require minimal maintenance. However, here are a few tips to keep your hair looking great:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and help your hair retain moisture.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my short pixie hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. However, it's generally recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I color my short pixie hair?

A: Yes, you can. However, it's important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care for Short Pixie Hair

Short pixie haircuts require minimal hair care. However, here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent hair breakage.

By following these tips and steps, you can easily fix short pixie hair and keep it looking great.

How To Air Dry Fine Hair For Volume A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of relying on heat styling tools to add volume to your fine hair? Air drying is a great alternative that can give you the volume you crave without damaging your hair with excessive heat. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to air dry fine hair for volume step by step. We'll also provide tips and answer some FAQs to help you achieve your best hair yet.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Air Dry Fine Hair for Volume
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Air Drying Fine Hair
  4. FAQs about Air Drying Fine Hair
  5. Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

How to Air Dry Fine Hair for Volume

The key to air drying fine hair for volume is to start with clean, damp hair. Here's how to do it:

1. Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

First, wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Be sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid any product buildup that could weigh your hair down.

2. Apply a Volumizing Product

Apply a volumizing product to your hair to help give it some lift. This could be a mousse, root lifter, or volumizing spray.

3. Use a Microfiber Towel to Dry Your Hair

Gently blot your hair with a microfiber towel to remove any excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz.

4. Part Your Hair and Clip it Up

Part your hair as you normally would and clip it up. This will help your hair dry evenly and prevent any weird kinks or bends.

5. Let Your Hair Air Dry

Allow your hair to air dry completely. This may take a while, so be patient. You can speed up the process by using a fan or sitting outside in the sun.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to air drying fine hair for volume:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly.

2. Apply a Volumizing Product

While your hair is still damp, apply a volumizing product to your roots. This could be a mousse, root lifter, or volumizing spray.

3. Use a Microfiber Towel to Dry Your Hair

Gently blot your hair with a microfiber towel to remove any excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz.

4. Part Your Hair and Clip it Up

Part your hair as you normally would and clip it up. This will help your hair dry evenly and prevent any weird kinks or bends.

5. Use a Diffuser Attachment

If you have curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help enhance your natural texture.

6. Let Your Hair Air Dry

Allow your hair to air dry completely. This may take a while, so be patient. You can speed up the process by using a fan or sitting outside in the sun.

7. Shake Out Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, shake it out gently to give it some volume. You can also scrunch your hair with your hands to help enhance any natural waves or curls.

Tips for Air Drying Fine Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you air dry your fine hair for volume:

  • Avoid brushing your hair while it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to gently detangle your hair after washing.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your ends to help prevent split ends.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help enhance any natural waves or curls.

FAQs about Air Drying Fine Hair

Can I air dry my hair overnight?

Yes, you can air dry your hair overnight. However, if you have fine hair, you may want to avoid sleeping on wet hair, as this can cause breakage and frizz.

What should I do if my hair feels greasy after air drying?

If your hair feels greasy after air drying, try using a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil. You can also try washing your hair less frequently to help regulate your scalp's oil production.

Can I use a hair oil on my fine hair?

Yes, you can use a hair oil on your fine hair to help add shine and prevent frizz. Just be sure to use a lightweight oil and apply it sparingly to avoid weighing your hair down.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for your fine hair:

  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Be gentle when brushing and styling your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any product buildup.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve voluminous, healthy-looking hair without the use of heat styling tools. Happy air drying!

Short Hairstyles For Thick Hair Over 50 With Glasses

Written by Petter Sep 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for a stylish new haircut that suits your thick hair and glasses? Look no further than this guide to short hairstyles for women over 50 with glasses. From pixie cuts to bobs, we've got you covered with the latest trends and expert tips.

Description

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it provides volume and texture, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Glasses can add another element to consider when choosing a haircut, as they can affect the overall balance and shape of your face. Short hairstyles are a great option for women over 50 with thick hair and glasses, as they are low-maintenance, youthful, and can be customized to suit your unique features.

How to

The first step in choosing a short hairstyle for thick hair over 50 with glasses is to consider your face shape and glasses style. Round faces can benefit from angular cuts, while square faces can soften with layers. If you have a high forehead, try a side-swept fringe. If your glasses have a bold or colorful frame, consider a simple, classic cut to balance the look.

Another important factor to consider is your hair texture. Thick hair can be straight, wavy, or curly, and each texture requires a different approach to cutting and styling. For example, a pixie cut can work well for straight hair, while a layered bob can enhance waves or curls.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some popular short hairstyles for thick hair over 50 with glasses:

Pixie Cut:

1. Start with dry, clean hair

2. Use scissors or clippers to cut the hair short all over, leaving longer pieces on top

3. Use a comb to lift and texturize the hair on top

4. Apply a styling product, such as wax or pomade, to create definition and hold

Layered Bob:

1. Start with dry, clean hair

2. Use scissors to cut the hair into layers, starting at the back and working towards the front

3. Use a razor or scissors to create texture and movement

4. Use a round brush and blow dryer to style the hair, curling the ends under

Tips

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect short hairstyle for thick hair over 50 with glasses:

- Choose a haircut that complements your glasses and face shape

- Consider using highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair

- Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny

- Experiment with styling products to find the one that works best for your hair texture and style

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear a bob if I have thick hair?

A: Yes, a layered bob can be a great option for thick hair, as it can help to reduce bulk and add movement.

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. Short haircuts can be youthful and modern, especially when paired with bold glasses and accessories.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle for thick hair over 50 with glasses:

- Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner

- Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too frequently

- Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to nourish and protect your hair

- Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and tidy

With these tips and ideas, you're sure to find the perfect short hairstyle for your thick hair over 50 with glasses. Whether you opt for a classic bob or a trendy pixie cut, remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

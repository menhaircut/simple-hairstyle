Haircuts For Round Faces Over 50 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 24, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have a round face and are over 50, you might be wondering what kind of haircut would suit you best. Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll go through some of the best haircuts for round faces over 50, as well as tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

As you age, your face shape can change, and it’s important to choose a haircut that flatters your features. If you have a round face, you want to choose a haircut that creates angles and adds height to your face. This can help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

How To

When you’re choosing a haircut for a round face over 50, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to choose a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head. This can be achieved with layers or a volumizing product. Second, you want to choose a haircut that has angles, such as a bob or a layered cut. This can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Step-by-Step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a haircut for a round face over 50:

  1. Consult with your stylist to find a haircut that suits your face shape and personal style.
  2. Ask for layers or a volumizing product to add height to your hair.
  3. Ask for angles, such as a bob or a layered cut, to create the illusion of a slimmer face.
  4. Maintain your haircut with regular trims to keep it looking fresh.

Tips

When getting a haircut for a round face over 50, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Avoid straight, blunt cuts, as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension in your hair.
  • Use a volumizing product to add height to your hair.
  • Consider using a round brush when styling your hair to create volume at the roots.

FAQ

What kind of haircut should I get if I have a round face over 50?

You should choose a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head and has angles, such as a bob or a layered cut.

What should I avoid when getting a haircut for a round face over 50?

You should avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as well as straight, blunt cuts.

How can I care for my hair after getting a haircut for a round face over 50?

You can maintain your haircut with regular trims and use volumizing products to add height to your hair.

Hair Care

When caring for your hair after getting a haircut for a round face over 50, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather with a hat or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect haircut for your round face over 50 and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Does Short Hair Make You Look Fatter

Written by Daniel Sep 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Short hair is a popular hairstyle among women, and there are many reasons why women choose to cut their hair short. However, some women worry that short hair will make them look fatter. In this article, we will explore this topic and answer the question: does short hair make you look fatter?

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Many women believe that short hair makes them look fatter, but this is not necessarily true. The length of your hair does not affect your body shape or size. However, the way you style your hair can affect the overall appearance of your face and body.

If you have a round face, you may want to avoid short hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. On the other hand, if you have a long face, you may want to choose a short hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face to balance out your features.

The key to finding the right short hairstyle is to choose a style that complements your face shape and features. This will help you look your best and feel confident in your appearance.

How to

If you are considering cutting your hair short, here are some tips to help you choose the right style:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

Before choosing a short hairstyle, consider your face shape. Different hairstyles flatter different face shapes. If you have a round face, you may want to choose a short hairstyle that adds height to the crown of your head. If you have a square face, you may want to choose a short hairstyle that softens your features.

2. Think About Your Hair Texture

Short hairstyles work best with certain hair textures. If you have curly hair, you may want to choose a short hairstyle that embraces your natural texture. If you have straight hair, you may want to choose a short hairstyle that adds texture and volume.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

Short hairstyles require more maintenance than long hairstyles. If you are someone who likes to wash and go, a short hairstyle may not be the best option for you. However, if you are willing to spend time styling your hair each day, a short hairstyle can be a great option.

Step by Step

If you have decided to cut your hair short, here are the steps to follow:

1. Choose Your Style

Research different short hairstyles and choose the one that best suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

2. Find a Good Stylist

Find a stylist who specializes in short hairstyles. Look at reviews and ask for recommendations from friends and family.

3. Communicate With Your Stylist

Communicate your desired style to your stylist. Bring pictures and be clear about what you want.

4. Maintain Your Hair

Maintain your short hair by getting regular trims and using products that are suited to your hair type and texture.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right short hairstyle:

  • Consider the length of your neck when choosing a short hairstyle.
  • Choose a short hairstyle that complements your hairline.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to see how different short hairstyles look on you.
  • Consider getting bangs to complement your short hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair.

FAQ

Does short hair make your face look fatter?

No, short hair does not make your face look fatter. However, certain short hairstyles can add volume to the sides of your face, which can make your face appear wider. If you have a round face, you may want to choose a short hairstyle that adds height to the crown of your head to balance out your features.

Does short hair make your body look fatter?

No, short hair does not make your body look fatter. The length of your hair does not affect your body shape or size. However, the way you style your hair can affect the overall appearance of your face and body.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Get regular trims to maintain your short hairstyle.
  • Use products that are suited to your hair type and texture.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

In conclusion, short hair does not make you look fatter. The key to finding the right short hairstyle is to choose a style that complements your face shape and features. With the right style and maintenance, short hair can be a great option for any woman.

How Short Can Your Hair Be To Get Cornrows

Written by Daniel Nov 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're thinking about getting cornrows, one of the first things you might be wondering is how short your hair can be before it's too short for the style. The answer depends on a few factors, including the length of your hair and the skill of your stylist. In this article, we'll take a closer look at how short your hair can be for cornrows and provide some tips for getting the best results.

Description

Cornrows are a popular braiding style that involve weaving your hair into tight, narrow rows that lay flat against your scalp. The style has been around for centuries and is often associated with African culture. It's a low-maintenance style that can last for several weeks with proper care, making it a popular choice for people with all hair types.

How To

The length of your hair will play a big role in whether or not you can get cornrows. Generally, your hair should be at least two inches long to get the style. This is because the braids need enough hair to grip onto in order to stay in place. If your hair is shorter than two inches, you may not be able to get cornrows.

However, if your hair is longer than two inches, you can still get cornrows even if it's very short. Your stylist will simply create smaller braids that are closer together. This can create a more intricate and detailed pattern that looks great on shorter hair.

Step by Step

If you're thinking about getting cornrows, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Make sure your hair is at least two inches long.
  2. Choose a style that you like and show it to your stylist.
  3. Have your stylist section your hair into rows and begin braiding.
  4. Once all of the braids are complete, make sure to take care of your hair to keep the style looking fresh.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you're getting cornrows:

  • Make sure to moisturize your scalp and hair before getting the braids. This can help prevent breakage and keep your hair healthy.
  • Wear a satin or silk scarf or bonnet at night to protect your hair while you sleep.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on the braids, as this can damage your hair or cause the braids to come loose.
  • Don't leave the braids in for too long. Generally, you should only keep them in for six to eight weeks to avoid damaging your hair or scalp.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cornrows:

Can you get cornrows if you have thin hair?

Yes, you can get cornrows if you have thin hair. However, your stylist may need to create smaller braids or use extensions to achieve the desired look.

Can you get cornrows if you have a sensitive scalp?

Yes, you can get cornrows if you have a sensitive scalp. However, you may need to take extra care when washing and styling your hair to avoid irritation.

How long do cornrows last?

Cornrows can last for up to eight weeks with proper care. However, you may need to touch them up every two to four weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential if you want your cornrows to look their best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Moisturize your hair and scalp regularly.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down or cause buildup.
  • Be gentle when styling your hair to avoid damaging the braids or causing breakage.

Now that you know how short your hair can be to get cornrows, you can decide if this style is right for you. With the right stylist and proper care, cornrows can be a beautiful and low-maintenance hairstyle that you'll love.

Should I Have My Hair Up Or Down For Wedding

Written by Bardi Oct 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Choosing the perfect hairstyle for your wedding day can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's easy to feel overwhelmed. One of the most common questions brides ask themselves is whether they should have their hair up or down for their wedding. In this article, we'll explore both options, provide tips, and answer some frequently asked questions to help you decide.

Table of Contents

  • How to Decide
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wedding Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Decide

Deciding whether to have your hair up or down for your wedding ultimately comes down to personal preference and the overall style and theme of your wedding. Here are some factors to consider:

Wedding Dress Style

Your wedding dress style can play a big role in deciding whether to have your hair up or down. If you have a strapless dress, an updo can help accentuate your neckline and show off your shoulders. If your dress has intricate back detailing, wearing your hair up can help showcase it.

Outdoor Wedding

If you're having an outdoor wedding, the weather can play a big role in your hair decision. If it's going to be hot and humid, an updo can help keep you cool and prevent your hair from frizzing. If it's going to be windy, wearing your hair down may not be the best option, as it can get in your face and obscure your vision.

Face Shape

Your face shape can also play a role in the decision to have your hair up or down. If you have a round face, an updo can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. If you have a square face, wearing your hair down can help soften your features.

Personal Style

Ultimately, your personal style should play a big role in your hair decision. If you're someone who loves wearing their hair down and feels most comfortable that way, go for it! If you love the look of an updo and want something more formal, that's a great option too.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you've decided to wear your hair up for your wedding, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If you're washing your hair the day of your wedding, use a lightweight shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from feeling weighed down.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from hot tools.
  3. Curl your hair using a curling iron or hot rollers. This will help create texture and volume.
  4. Tease the crown of your hair to create more volume.
  5. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic.
  6. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  7. Spray your hair with a strong hold hairspray to keep it in place.
  8. Add any hair accessories or veils as desired.
  9. Finish with a final spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips for Wedding Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when deciding whether to have your hair up or down for your wedding:

  • Consider a trial run with your hairstylist to make sure you love the final look.
  • Take your wedding dress and accessories into account when deciding on a hairstyle.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products to keep your hair looking and feeling its best.
  • Don't forget about your hair color and highlights when deciding on a hairstyle.
  • If you're having an outdoor wedding, consider bringing along a touch-up kit with hairspray, bobby pins, and a comb.

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I wear my hair up or down for a beach wedding?

If you're having a beach wedding, wearing your hair up can help keep you cool and prevent your hair from getting tangled in the wind. However, wearing your hair down can look beautiful and romantic, so it ultimately comes down to personal preference.

What if I have short hair?

If you have short hair, an updo may not be an option. However, you can still create a beautiful and elegant look by adding accessories like a jeweled headband or a flower crown.

Can I wear my hair half up and half down?

Yes! Wearing your hair half up and half down is a great option if you can't decide between an updo and wearing your hair down.

Hair Care Tips

Whether you decide to wear your hair up or down for your wedding, it's important to take care of your hair leading up to the big day. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools like curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Consider getting a professional hair treatment like a keratin treatment to help smooth and strengthen your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote strong, healthy hair.

Ultimately, whether you decide to have your hair up or down for your wedding comes down to personal preference and what makes you feel most comfortable and confident. With these tips and tricks, you're sure to look and feel amazing on your special day.

Short Haircut For Square Face Female

Written by Bardi Aug 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a woman with a square face shape and looking for a new hairstyle? You've come to the right place! In this article, we will guide you on how to choose the best short haircut for square face female. We'll share tips, how-to's, and answer frequently asked questions about this topic. Let's dive in!

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and a forehead that's almost as wide as the cheekbones. The goal of choosing a short haircut for square face female is to soften the jawline and create balance with the rest of the face. The right haircut can emphasize your best features and make you look and feel confident.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Square Face Female

The first step in choosing a short haircut for square face female is to determine your face shape. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a haircut that flatters your features. Here are some tips to help you choose the right short haircut for square face female:

1. Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can affect how your haircut looks on you. If you have thin hair, you may want to choose a shorter haircut with layers to add volume. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a sleek bob or a pixie cut that will help you manage your hair.

2. Look for Softening Elements

When choosing a short haircut for square face female, look for styles that have softening elements such as layers, curls, or waves. These elements will soften your facial features and create balance. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs that will emphasize your square jawline.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a short haircut for square face female that matches your lifestyle. If you're always on the go and don't have much time to style your hair, choose a low-maintenance style that can easily be styled with minimal effort.

Step by Step Guide for Short Haircut for Square Face Female

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get a short haircut for square face female:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a short haircut for square face female is to determine your face shape. Use a mirror to look at your face and identify the shape.

Step 2: Choose a Short Haircut

Once you know your face shape, choose a short haircut that flatters your features. Consult with your hair stylist for their opinion and advice.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before your haircut appointment. This will make it easier for your stylist to work with your hair.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Your stylist will cut your hair according to the haircut you've chosen. They may use scissors or clippers depending on the style.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

After your haircut, your stylist will style your hair according to your preference. You can also ask for tips on how to style your hair at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut for Square Face Female

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut for square face female:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking fresh. Visit your hair stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

2. Use the Right Hair Products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and style. Ask your stylist for recommendations.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

FAQs

Q: What is the best short haircut for square face female?

A: The best short haircut for square face female is one that softens the jawline and creates balance with the rest of the face. Look for styles that have softening elements such as layers, curls, or waves.

Q: Can I wear bangs with a square face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with a square face shape. However, avoid heavy bangs that will emphasize your square jawline. Opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that will soften your features.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look wider?

A: No, a short haircut will not make your face look wider. A short haircut can emphasize your best features and create balance with the rest of the face.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Avoid Over-Shampooing

Avoid over-shampooing your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Wash your hair every other day or as needed.

3. Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. Look for a hair mask that is suitable for your hair type.

That's it for our guide on short haircut for square face female. We hope you found this article helpful in choosing the right haircut for your face shape. Remember to consult with your hair stylist for their opinion and advice. Happy styling!

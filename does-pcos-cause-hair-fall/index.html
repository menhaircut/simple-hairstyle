Does Pcos Cause Hair Fall A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome (PCOS) is a common hormonal disorder that affects women of reproductive age. It is characterized by the presence of multiple cysts in the ovaries, irregular periods, and high levels of androgens or male hormones. One of the most distressing symptoms of PCOS is hair fall. In this article, we will explore the link between PCOS and hair fall, and provide some tips on how to manage this condition.

What Causes Hair Fall in PCOS?

Hair fall is a common symptom of PCOS. The exact cause of hair fall in PCOS is not fully understood, but it is believed to be due to the hormonal imbalances that occur in this condition. High levels of androgens or male hormones can cause the hair follicles to shrink, resulting in thinner hair and eventually hair fall. In addition, PCOS can also lead to insulin resistance, which can further exacerbate hair fall.

How to Diagnose PCOS?

If you are experiencing hair fall and suspect that you may have PCOS, it is important to see a doctor for a proper diagnosis. Your doctor may perform a physical exam, blood tests, and an ultrasound to check for cysts in your ovaries. They may also ask about your menstrual history and any other symptoms you may be experiencing.

How to Manage Hair Fall in PCOS?

Managing hair fall in PCOS can be challenging, but there are steps you can take to help reduce your symptoms. One of the most effective ways to manage hair fall in PCOS is to manage your hormonal imbalances. This can be done through medications such as birth control pills, which can help regulate your menstrual cycle and reduce androgen levels. Other medications such as metformin may also be prescribed to help manage insulin resistance.

In addition, there are several lifestyle changes you can make to help manage your PCOS symptoms. These include maintaining a healthy weight, exercising regularly, and following a healthy diet that is low in sugar and processed foods. You may also want to consider taking supplements such as biotin, which can help improve hair health.

Step-by-Step Guide to Hair Care for PCOS

If you have PCOS and are experiencing hair fall, it is important to take extra care of your hair. Here are some steps you can take to help reduce hair fall and improve hair health:

Step 1: Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type. Look for products that are gentle and free from harsh chemicals that can further damage your hair.

Step 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as blow dryers, curling irons, and flat irons, as these can damage your hair and make hair fall worse.

Step 3: Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to gently detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots. Avoid using a brush, as this can cause hair breakage.

Step 4: Massage Your Scalp

Massage your scalp using circular motions to help stimulate blood flow and promote healthy hair growth.

Step 5: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a scarf to protect your hair from the sun, as UV rays can damage your hair and make hair fall worse.

PCOS and Hair Transplant

If you have PCOS and are experiencing severe hair fall that is not responding to treatment, you may want to consider hair transplant surgery. This procedure involves transplanting hair follicles from one part of your scalp to another to help restore hair growth. However, it is important to consult with a doctor before undergoing this procedure.

FAQs

Q: Does PCOS cause permanent hair loss?

A: PCOS can cause permanent hair loss if left untreated for a long period of time. However, with proper treatment and management, hair fall can be reduced and hair growth can be restored.

Q: Can hair fall due to PCOS be reversed?

A: Hair fall due to PCOS can be reversed with proper treatment and management. This may include medications, lifestyle changes, and hair care tips.

Q: Can biotin help with hair fall due to PCOS?

A: Biotin can help improve hair health and reduce hair fall, but it should be used in conjunction with other treatment options for PCOS.

Q: Is hair transplant surgery a safe option for PCOS patients?

A: Hair transplant surgery can be a safe and effective option for PCOS patients who are experiencing severe hair fall. However, it is important to consult with a doctor before undergoing this procedure.

Conclusion

PCOS can cause hair fall, but with proper treatment and management, this symptom can be reduced and hair growth can be restored. If you are experiencing hair fall and suspect that you may have PCOS, it is important to see a doctor for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan.

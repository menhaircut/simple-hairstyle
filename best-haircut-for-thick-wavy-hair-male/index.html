Best Haircut For Thick Wavy Hair Male How To Get The Perfect Look

Written by Bardi Oct 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your thick wavy hair that seems unmanageable? Do you want to know the best haircut for thick wavy hair male? Look no further because we’ve got you covered!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Perfect Haircut?
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Maintain Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse at the same time. On one hand, it adds volume and texture to your hair, and on the other hand, it can be difficult to manage without the right haircut. The best haircut for thick wavy hair male is one that balances the thickness and waviness of your hair, without making it look too heavy or bulky.

How to Get the Perfect Haircut?

The first step to getting the perfect haircut for thick wavy hair male is to find a stylist who understands the unique challenges of your hair type. Look for someone who has experience with thick wavy hair and can recommend the right style for your face shape and hair texture.

When it comes to the actual haircut, there are several options to choose from:

1. Short Textured Crop

This is a popular style for men with thick wavy hair. The short length keeps the hair from getting too heavy, while the textured layers add volume and movement.

2. Side Part with Tapered Sides

This classic style is perfect for thick wavy hair. The side part adds structure to the hair, while the tapered sides keep it from looking too bulky.

3. Long Wavy Layers

If you prefer longer hair, consider adding layers to your thick wavy hair. This will add movement and texture, while keeping the overall look soft and natural.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect haircut for thick wavy hair male:

Step 1: Choose the Right Stylist

Look for a stylist who has experience with thick wavy hair and can recommend the right style for your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Discuss Your Options

Talk to your stylist about the different haircut options available for your hair type. Consider factors like length, texture, and face shape.

Step 3: Decide on a Style

Once you’ve discussed your options, decide on a style that works best for you. Keep in mind that the best haircut for thick wavy hair male is one that balances the thickness and waviness of your hair.

Step 4: Maintain Your Hair

Once you’ve got the perfect haircut, it’s important to maintain your hair with regular trims and styling. Ask your stylist for tips on how to style and care for your hair at home.

Tips to Maintain Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your thick wavy hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Choose shampoos and conditioners that are designed for thick wavy hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like keratin and argan oil, which help to strengthen and nourish your hair.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage. Try to limit your use of blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons, and use heat protectant products when you do.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It’s recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Q: Can I still get a fade with thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can still get a fade with thick wavy hair. However, it’s important to find a stylist who has experience with this hair type to avoid any mistakes.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips for men with thick wavy hair:

1. Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it more difficult to manage. Try to wash your hair every other day, or even less frequently if possible.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

A wide-tooth comb is gentler on your hair than a brush and can help to detangle your hair without causing damage.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids can pull on your hair and cause breakage. Opt for looser hairstyles or use a soft scrunchie to tie your hair back.

In conclusion, the best haircut for thick wavy hair male is one that balances the thickness and waviness of your hair, without making it look too heavy or bulky. With the right stylist and the right style, you can achieve the perfect look for your hair type.

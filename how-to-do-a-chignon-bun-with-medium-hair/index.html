How To Do A Chignon Bun With Medium Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Sep 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for a chic and elegant hairstyle that you can wear on a special occasion or a night out? Look no further than the chignon bun. This classic updo is versatile, easy to create, and looks great on medium-length hair. In this guide, we'll show you how to do a chignon bun with medium hair step by step, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a Chignon Bun with Medium Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Creating the Perfect Chignon Bun
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Healthy and Beautiful Hair

How to Do a Chignon Bun with Medium Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's take a look at what you'll need to create a chignon bun with medium hair:

  • Brush or comb
  • Hair elastics
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray

Now, let's get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create a chignon bun with medium hair:

  1. Brush or comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair elastic.
  3. Divide the ponytail into two sections.
  4. Twist each section around the base of the ponytail, creating two separate buns.
  5. Secure the buns with bobby pins.
  6. Take the remaining hair that's hanging loose and twist it around the base of the buns.
  7. Secure the twisted hair with bobby pins.
  8. Spray your hair with hairspray to hold the style in place.

And that's it! You now have a beautiful chignon bun with medium hair.

Tips for Creating the Perfect Chignon Bun

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect chignon bun:

  • Use a hair donut or sock bun to create a fuller and more voluminous chignon bun.
  • Add accessories such as flowers or hairpins to enhance the look.
  • Create a messy chignon bun for a more relaxed and casual look.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to create a seamless look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about chignon buns:

  • Can I create a chignon bun with short hair?
  • Yes, you can create a chignon bun with short hair by using a hair donut or sock bun to add volume and length.
  • Is a chignon bun suitable for a formal event?
  • Yes, a chignon bun is a classic and elegant hairstyle that's perfect for formal events and occasions.
  • How long does it take to create a chignon bun?
  • It usually takes around 10-15 minutes to create a chignon bun, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy and Beautiful Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your medium hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can create a beautiful chignon bun with medium hair and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

So there you have it, a complete guide on how to do a chignon bun with medium hair. Whether you're attending a formal event or just want to add some elegance to your everyday look, the chignon bun is a perfect choice. Give it a try and see how it can transform your hair into a beautiful work of art.

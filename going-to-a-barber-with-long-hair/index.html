Going To A Barber With Long Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have long hair and you're planning on going to a barber, you might be feeling a bit nervous. After all, barbers are known for their short haircuts and fades, which might not be exactly what you're looking for. But don't worry, with the right approach, you can still get a great haircut that suits your long locks. In this post, we'll go over some tips and tricks for going to a barber with long hair.

How to Prepare for Your Barber Visit

Before you head to the barber, there are a few things you should do to prepare. Here are some tips:

1. Research Barbers in Your Area

Not all barbers are created equal, and not all of them have experience cutting long hair. Do some research and find a barber who has experience working with long hair. Look at reviews online and see what other customers have said about their experience.

2. Know What You Want

Before you go to the barber, have a clear idea of what you want. Bring in pictures or examples of the type of haircut you're looking for. This will help your barber understand your vision and give you the best possible result.

3. Wash Your Hair

Make sure to wash your hair before you go to the barber. This will make it easier for them to work with and will ensure that your hair looks its best.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Haircut with Long Hair

Now that you're prepared, here's a step-by-step guide to getting a haircut with long hair:

1. Consultation

The first step is to have a consultation with your barber. This is where you'll discuss what you want and your barber will give you their professional opinion. Be open to their suggestions, as they might have ideas that you haven't considered.

2. Cutting

Once you've agreed on a plan, your barber will start cutting your hair. They'll likely use scissors instead of clippers, as this will give them more control over the length and shape of your hair.

3. Styling

After your hair is cut, your barber will style it to your liking. They might use products like gel or pomade to give your hair volume and hold.

Tips for Taking Care of Long Hair After a Haircut

Once you've gotten your haircut, it's important to take care of your long hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

1. Use Quality Hair Products

Invest in high-quality hair products that are designed for long hair. This will help keep your hair healthy and strong.

2. Brush Your Hair Regularly

Brush your hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush specifically designed for long hair.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your hair looking its best, make sure to trim it regularly. This will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about going to a barber with long hair:

1. Can I Still Get a Fade with Long Hair?

Yes, it's possible to get a fade with long hair. However, it might not be as dramatic as it would be with shorter hair.

2. Should I Cut My Hair Before Going to the Barber?

No, you don't need to cut your hair before going to the barber. Your barber will be able to work with your long hair and give you a great haircut.

3. How Often Should I Get My Long Hair Cut?

It's recommended that you get your long hair cut every 8-12 weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently go to a barber with long hair and get a great haircut that suits your style. Just remember to communicate clearly with your barber and take good care of your hair afterwards.

How To Cut Curly Hair Bangs At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be tricky to manage, especially when it comes to cutting bangs. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect curly hair bangs at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting curly hair bangs step by step, provide you with tips and answer frequently asked questions. Whether you're looking to switch up your style or save some money on salon visits, this guide has got you covered.

How to Cut Curly Hair Bangs at Home

Cutting curly hair bangs at home may seem daunting, but it's actually easier than you think. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Prepare Your Tools

Before you start cutting your curly hair bangs, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a fine-tooth comb, a spray bottle filled with water, and hair clips to section off your hair.

Step 2: Wet Your Hair

Next, wet your hair with the spray bottle. Make sure your hair is damp but not soaking wet. This will make it easier to cut and help you achieve a more accurate cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using the hair clips, section off the hair you want to cut into bangs. You can choose how thick or thin you want your bangs to be, but make sure the sections are even on both sides.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Hold the first section of hair between your fingers and comb it straight down. Then, using the scissors, cut the hair at an angle, following the shape of your face. Be careful not to cut too much at once, as curly hair tends to shrink when it dries. Repeat this step for each section of hair.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After you've cut your curly hair bangs, release the sections and check your work in the mirror. If you need to make any adjustments, repeat the previous steps until you're happy with the results.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curly hair bangs:

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut a little at a time.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Make sure your hair is wet or damp to achieve a more accurate cut.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short, as curly hair tends to shrink when it dries.
  • When in doubt, leave it to the professionals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my curly hair bangs dry?

While it's possible to cut curly hair bangs dry, it's easier and more accurate to cut them when your hair is wet or damp.

How often should I cut my curly hair bangs?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs grow out. Generally, you should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Should I use thinning shears on my curly hair bangs?

It's not recommended to use thinning shears on curly hair, as they can cause frizz and damage the hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Bangs

Now that you've cut your curly hair bangs, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

By following these tips and the steps outlined in this guide, you can achieve the perfect curly hair bangs at home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and if you're unsure, it's always best to leave it to the professionals.

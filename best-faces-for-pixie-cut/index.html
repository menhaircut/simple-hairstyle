Best Faces For Pixie Cut How To Choose The Perfect Style For You

Written by Eveline Nov 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're thinking of getting a pixie cut, congratulations! This short and stylish haircut has been a favorite among women for years. But before you head to the salon, it's important to consider what face shape suits a pixie cut. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the best faces for pixie cut and give you tips on how to style and care for your new 'do.

What is a Pixie Cut?

A pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that sits above the ears and usually has a longer top. It's a low-maintenance and versatile cut that can be styled in many ways, including messy, sleek, or textured.

Best Faces for Pixie Cut

While pixie cuts can look great on anyone, certain face shapes are particularly suited to this style. Here are the best faces for pixie cut:

Oval Face

The oval face shape is considered the most versatile when it comes to haircuts. A pixie cut can highlight your cheekbones and jawline, making this face shape an ideal candidate for a short cut.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, a pixie cut can balance out a wider forehead and narrow chin. To soften the look, ask your stylist to add some wispy bangs or layers around the face.

Square Face

A square face shape has a strong jawline and angular features. A pixie cut can soften these features and add some femininity to your look. Opt for a style with some volume on top to balance out the squareness of your face.

Round Face

If you have a round face, a pixie cut can elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Add some height on top to create the illusion of a longer face.

How to Style a Pixie Cut

Styling a pixie cut is easy and fun. Here are some tips on how to style your new 'do:

Use the Right Products

To keep your pixie cut looking its best, use lightweight products that won't weigh down your hair. A texturizing spray or pomade can add some volume and texture to your hair.

Experiment with Different Styles

A pixie cut is versatile and can be styled in many ways. Try a sleek and polished look for the office, or a messy and textured style for a night out.

Accessorize

Accessories can add some extra flair to your pixie cut. A headband, scarf, or hair clip can add some color and personality to your look.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cut

Taking care of your pixie cut is essential to maintain its shape and style. Here are some hair care tips for pixie cut:

Trim Regularly

To keep your pixie cut looking fresh and stylish, make sure to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat tools on your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and breakage.

Wash and Condition Regularly

Keep your pixie cut clean and healthy by washing and conditioning your hair regularly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

FAQ

Can anyone pull off a pixie cut?

Yes! A pixie cut can look great on anyone, regardless of face shape or hair type. It's all about finding the right style that flatters your features.

Will a pixie cut make my hair look thinner?

Not necessarily. A pixie cut can actually make your hair look thicker and fuller, especially if you add some layers or volume on top.

How do I know if a pixie cut is right for me?

The best way to know if a pixie cut is right for you is to consult with your stylist. They can help you find the perfect style that suits your face shape and hair type.

Conclusion

A pixie cut is a stylish and low-maintenance haircut that can look great on anyone. By considering the best faces for pixie cut and following our styling and hair care tips, you can rock this short and chic hairstyle with confidence. So go ahead, make the chop, and embrace your pixie cut!

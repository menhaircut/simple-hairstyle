How To Keep Healthy Curls Tips For Relaxed Hair

Written by Bardi Aug 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have naturally curly hair that has been chemically relaxed, it can be challenging to keep your curls healthy and bouncy. However, with the right hair care routine, you can maintain your relaxed curls and keep them looking their best. In this blog post, we'll share tips and tricks for keeping your curls in great condition, so read on to find out more.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Keep Your Curls Healthy
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Maintaining Relaxed Curls
  3. Tips for Healthy Relaxed Curls

How to Keep Your Curls Healthy

Keeping your curls healthy starts with a good hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you maintain your relaxed curls:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can help keep your curls healthy and prevent damage.

Step-by-Step Guide to Maintaining Relaxed Curls

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you maintain your relaxed curls:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help moisturize it.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.
  4. Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair to help define your curls.
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair.
  6. Avoid touching your hair while it's drying to prevent frizz.

Following this routine regularly can help you maintain healthy, defined curls.

Tips for Healthy Relaxed Curls

Here are some additional tips to help you keep your relaxed curls healthy:

  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment on your hair once a week to help keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair elastics or tight hairstyles, as this can cause breakage.

By following these tips, you can help keep your relaxed curls healthy and looking their best.

FAQs about Relaxed Curls

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry.

Q: Can I use a flat iron on my relaxed curls?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my curls?

A: Avoid touching your hair too much, and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair.

Best Products for Relaxed Curls

Here are some of the best products for maintaining healthy, relaxed curls:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Curl-defining cream or gel
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Heat protectant spray

By using these products regularly, you can help keep your relaxed curls looking their best.

Conclusion

With the right hair care routine and products, you can maintain healthy, bouncy curls even if you have chemically relaxed hair. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this blog post, you can keep your curls in great condition and prevent damage. So go ahead and rock those gorgeous curls with confidence!

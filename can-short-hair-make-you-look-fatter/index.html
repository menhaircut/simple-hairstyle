Can Short Hair Make You Look Fatter

Written by Luffy Jul 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Short hair is in trend these days, and many women are opting for this style. It’s chic, stylish, and easy to maintain. However, some women are hesitant to try this look because they believe it can make them look fatter. The question is, can short hair make you look fatter? In this blog post, we will discuss this topic in detail and provide you with tips to make the most out of your short hair.

Description

Many women believe that short hair makes their face look rounder and their body appear fuller. This is because long hair can be used to create an illusion of a slimmer face and a more elongated body. Short hair, on the other hand, does not offer the same opportunity to create such illusions. However, this is not always the case.

How To

If you are considering getting a short haircut, there are a few things you can do to ensure that it does not make you look fatter. Firstly, choose the right haircut that complements your face shape. Secondly, style your hair in a way that flatters your face and body shape. Lastly, wear clothes that complement your haircut.

Step by Step

Step 1: Choose the right haircut

The right haircut can make a huge difference in how your face and body look. If you have a round face, opt for a haircut that adds height to your crown and has layers that fall around your face. This will create an illusion of a slimmer face. If you have a square face, go for a haircut that softens your jawline.

Step 2: Style your hair in a way that flatters your face and body shape

Styling your hair in the right way can also make a difference in how you look. If you have a round face, avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous on the sides as they can make your face look wider. Instead, style your hair in a way that adds height to your crown.

Step 3: Wear clothes that complement your haircut

The clothes you wear can also make a difference in how you look. If you have a short haircut, opt for clothes that create an illusion of height. This could be high-waisted pants, long flowy skirts, or dresses that fall just above the knee.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a short haircut:

1. Choose a haircut that complements your face shape.

2. Style your hair in a way that flatters your face and body shape.

3. Wear clothes that complement your haircut.

4. Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous on the sides.

FAQ

Q. Can short hair make you look fatter?

A. Short hair can make you look fatter if it is not styled properly. However, if you choose the right haircut and style your hair in a way that flatters your face and body shape, short hair can make you look slimmer.

Q. What is the best short haircut for a round face?

A. The best short haircut for a round face is one that adds height to your crown and has layers that fall around your face.

Hair Care

No matter what your haircut is, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy:

1. Wash your hair regularly.

2. Condition your hair after washing.

3. Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type.

4. Avoid using heat styling tools too often.

5. Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

Conclusion

Short hair can make you look fatter if it is not styled properly. However, with the right haircut and styling, short hair can make you look slimmer and more stylish. Remember to choose a haircut that complements your face shape, style your hair in a way that flatters your face and body shape, and wear clothes that complement your haircut. Follow these tips, take care of your hair, and you’ll be rocking your short hair in no time!

How To Make A Short Dress Longer With Lace

Written by Eveline Aug 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having to pass up on cute short dresses because they're too short? Well, fear not! With a little bit of lace and some basic sewing skills, you can easily transform a short dress into a longer one. In this article, we'll show you how to make a short dress longer with lace in just a few simple steps.

Description

Adding lace to a short dress is a great way to give it a more feminine and romantic look. Plus, it's a fun DIY project that can save you money and make you feel like a fashion designer. With the right tools and materials, you can easily transform a short dress into a longer one that's perfect for any occasion.

How to

Step 1: Choose the Right Dress

The first step in making a short dress longer with lace is to choose the right dress. Look for a dress that fits you well and has a hemline that falls just above your knees. This will give you enough room to add the lace without making the dress too long.

Step 2: Choose the Right Lace

Next, choose the right lace for your dress. Look for a lace that matches the color and style of your dress. You can choose from a variety of laces, including cotton, polyester, or even lace trim. Make sure the lace is wide enough to cover the length of the hemline.

Step 3: Measure and Cut the Lace

Measure the length of your dress from the hemline to the desired length you want to add. Then, add about 1 inch to the measurement to account for hemming. Cut the lace to the desired length using fabric scissors.

Step 4: Hem the Lace

Using a sewing machine or by hand, hem the lace by folding the bottom edge up 1/4 inch and then again 1/4 inch. Sew the hemline in place using a straight stitch.

Step 5: Attach the Lace to the Dress

With a needle and thread, attach the lace to the hemline of the dress by sewing the lace to the inside of the dress. Make sure to sew the lace as close to the hemline as possible to create a seamless look.

Step 6: Finish the Hemline

Once the lace is attached, fold the hemline up 1/4 inch and then again 1/4 inch. Using a sewing machine or by hand, sew the hemline in place using a straight stitch.

Tips

  • Use a lightweight lace to avoid adding too much weight to the dress.
  • Make sure to iron the dress and the lace before sewing to create a seamless look.
  • Choose a lace that matches the style of the dress, such as a floral or scalloped lace.

FAQ

Can I use a different fabric instead of lace?

Yes, you can use a different fabric, such as chiffon or tulle, to add length to your dress. Just make sure the fabric matches the style and color of your dress.

Do I need to be an expert seamstress to add lace to my dress?

No, you don't need to be an expert seamstress. This project is great for beginners who want to learn basic sewing skills. Just make sure to take your time and follow the steps carefully.

Hair Care

Now that you've learned how to make a short dress longer with lace, it's time to think about your hair. For a romantic and feminine look, try a loose updo or soft curls. Add a flower crown or hair accessory for a bohemian touch. Remember to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using too much heat on your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily transform a short dress into a longer one with lace. Whether you're going to a wedding, a party, or just a casual outing, this DIY project is a great way to add some extra style to your wardrobe.

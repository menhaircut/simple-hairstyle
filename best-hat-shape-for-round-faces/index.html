Best Hat Shape For Round Faces

Written by Bardi Aug 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of trying on hats that just don't flatter your face shape? Well, if you have a round face, you're in luck because there are certain hat shapes that will complement your features. In this article, we'll be discussing the best hat shape for round faces, how to choose the right hat, and some tips for hair care when wearing hats.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hat
  • Tips for Wearing Hats with Round Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips When Wearing Hats

How to Choose the Right Hat

When it comes to choosing the right hat for a round face, you want to look for styles that will elongate your face and give the illusion of length. Here are some hat shapes that work well:

Fedora

A fedora is a timeless hat that looks great on most face shapes, including round faces. The key is to choose a fedora with a taller crown and a wider brim to balance out your features.

Bucket Hat

A bucket hat is a great option for those who want a more casual look. Look for bucket hats with a sloping brim to elongate your face and add some definition to your features.

Newsboy Cap

A newsboy cap is another classic hat that looks great on round faces. Look for caps with a slimmer profile and a slightly curved brim to add some height to your face.

Wide-Brimmed Sun Hat

If you're looking for a hat to wear in the summer, a wide-brimmed sun hat is a great choice. Look for hats with a wide brim and a structured crown to balance out your features.

Remember, the key is to look for hats that add height and definition to your face. Avoid hats with a round crown or brim as they will only accentuate your round face.

Tips for Wearing Hats with Round Faces

Now that you know what hat shapes work best for round faces, here are some tips for wearing them:

  • Wear your hat slightly back on your head to elongate your face.
  • Avoid hats that sit too low on your forehead as they will only make your face appear rounder.
  • Consider wearing your hair in a ponytail or updo to add some height to your face.
  • Accessorize your hat with a scarf or statement jewelry to draw attention away from your face shape.

FAQs

Q: Can round faces wear beanies?

A: Yes, round faces can wear beanies. Look for beanies with a slouchy fit and avoid wearing them too far down on your forehead.

Q: Can round faces wear berets?

A: Yes, round faces can wear berets. Look for berets with a slightly slimmer profile and a structured crown to add some height to your face.

Q: Can round faces wear baseball caps?

A: Yes, round faces can wear baseball caps. Look for caps with a slightly curved brim and avoid wearing them too far down on your forehead.

Hair Care Tips When Wearing Hats

If you're wearing a hat, it's important to take care of your hair to avoid damage and breakage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid wearing hats too tight as they can cause hair breakage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid wearing hats for extended periods of time as they can cause your hair to become flat and greasy.
  • Consider using dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

With these tips and hat shape suggestions, you'll be sure to find the perfect hat to complement your round face. Just remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you!

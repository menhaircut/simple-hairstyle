How To Cut A Pixie Haircut At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that's chic and stylish, a pixie cut is a great option. And what's even better is that you can cut it at home! In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to cut a pixie haircut at home, including tips on how to achieve the perfect cut and how to take care of your hair afterwards.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cut a Pixie Haircut at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Cut
  • FAQs

How to Cut a Pixie Haircut at Home

The first step in cutting your own pixie haircut at home is to gather all the necessary tools. You'll need:

  • Haircutting scissors
  • Clipper
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Water spray bottle

Once you have these tools, you're ready to start cutting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own pixie haircut:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  3. Start with the back section and use the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length. Use a comb to guide the clippers and create an even cut.
  4. Move to the sides and repeat the process, using the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length.
  5. Next, move to the top section and use the scissors to create the desired length and texture. Start by cutting the hair in the middle and work your way towards the sides.
  6. Once you've cut all the hair to the desired length, use the scissors to create layers and texture. This will give your pixie cut more movement and dimension.
  7. After you've finished cutting, use the comb to blend any uneven areas and trim any stray hairs.
  8. Finish by styling your hair with a hair product of your choice.

Tips for a Perfect Cut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect pixie cut:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Use the right tools and make sure they're sharp.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and be patient.
  • Use the comb to measure the length and make sure both sides are even.
  • When cutting the top section, use the point-cutting technique to create texture and movement.

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about cutting a pixie haircut at home:

Q: Can I cut a pixie haircut with regular scissors?

A: No, you need to use haircutting scissors to achieve the best results.

Q: How often should I trim my pixie haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows, but generally every 4-6 weeks is recommended.

Q: What if I make a mistake while cutting?

A: Don't panic! You can always trim a little more to even things out or visit a professional stylist to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care After the Cut

Once you've cut your pixie haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Visit a professional stylist for regular trims to maintain your pixie haircut.

With these tips, you'll be able to cut a perfect pixie haircut at home and keep it looking great. Happy cutting!

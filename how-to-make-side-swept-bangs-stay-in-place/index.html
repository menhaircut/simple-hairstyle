How To Make Side Swept Bangs Stay In Place A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have side swept bangs, you know how frustrating it can be when they won't stay in place. Whether you have fine hair, thick hair, or something in between, there are a few tricks you can use to keep your bangs looking fabulous all day long. In this article, we'll show you how to make side swept bangs stay in place with a step-by-step guide, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Make Side Swept Bangs Stay in Place

There are a few different methods you can use to keep your side swept bangs in place. Here are some of the most effective:

Method #1: Use Hairspray

One of the easiest ways to keep your side swept bangs in place is to use hairspray. Here's how:

  1. After washing and drying your hair, use a round brush to blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to go.
  2. Spray your bangs with hairspray, holding the can about 8-10 inches away from your head.
  3. Use your fingers to gently smooth your bangs into place.

This method works best if you use a light to medium hold hairspray. You don't want to use anything too heavy, as it can weigh your hair down and make your bangs look greasy.

Method #2: Blow Dry with a Round Brush

If you don't want to use hairspray, you can try blow drying your bangs with a round brush. Here's how:

  1. After washing your hair, use a towel to gently pat your bangs dry.
  2. Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to go.
  3. Once your bangs are dry, use your fingers to gently smooth them into place.

This method works best if you have thick or coarse hair. If you have fine hair, you may need to use a bit of hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Method #3: Use a Flat Iron

If you have curly or wavy hair, you can use a flat iron to straighten your bangs and keep them in place. Here's how:

  1. After washing your hair, blow dry your bangs with a round brush.
  2. Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  3. Once your bangs are straight, use your fingers to gently smooth them into place.

Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron. You don't want to damage your hair!

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the different methods you can use to keep your side swept bangs in place, let's go through a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash your hair and pat your bangs dry with a towel.
  2. Blow dry your bangs with a round brush, using the direction you want them to go.
  3. Spray your bangs with hairspray or use a flat iron to straighten them, depending on your hair type.
  4. Use your fingers to gently smooth your bangs into place.
  5. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your bangs in place all day.

Tips for Keeping Your Bangs in Place

Here are a few additional tips to help you keep your side swept bangs in place:

  • Don't touch your bangs too much throughout the day. The oils from your fingers can make your bangs look greasy and weigh them down.
  • If you have fine hair, try using a dry shampoo to add some texture and volume to your bangs.
  • If your bangs start to look greasy throughout the day, use a bit of baby powder or cornstarch to absorb the oil.
  • If you're working out or doing any activities that will make you sweat, pull your bangs back with bobby pins or a headband.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping side swept bangs in place:

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can probably get away with washing it every few days.

What type of hairspray should I use?

You should use a light to medium hold hairspray. Avoid anything too heavy, as it can weigh your hair down and make your bangs look greasy.

How do I keep my bangs out of my face when I'm working out?

You can use bobby pins or a headband to keep your bangs in place. Make sure to use ones that match your hair color so they blend in.

Hair Care Tips for Side Swept Bangs

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your side swept bangs looking their best:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as it can cause damage and split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your side swept bangs looking fabulous all day long!

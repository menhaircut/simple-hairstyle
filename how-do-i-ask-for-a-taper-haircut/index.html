How Do I Ask For A Taper Haircut

Written by Bardi Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that will give you a fresh and stylish look? A taper haircut could be the perfect choice for you. Whether you're going for a classic or modern look, a taper haircut is versatile and suits all hair types. But before you go to the barber, you might be wondering, "How do I ask for a taper haircut?" In this blog post, we'll guide you through the process of asking for a taper haircut in relaxed English language.

  • How to Ask for a Taper Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Ask for a Taper Haircut

Asking for a taper haircut is easy, and you don't need to use any technical terms or jargon. You can simply ask your barber for a taper haircut or a low fade haircut with a gradual decrease in hair length towards the neckline. Here are some ways to ask for a taper haircut:

  • "I want a taper haircut with a fade."
  • "Can you give me a low fade with a taper on the sides and back?"
  • "I'd like my hair short on the sides and longer on top, with a taper at the back."

The key is to communicate clearly with your barber and explain the style you want. You can also bring a picture of the style you prefer to show your barber.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're still unsure about how to ask for a taper haircut, follow this step-by-step guide:

  1. Decide on the length and style of your hair. Taper haircuts can range from short to long, and you can choose a classic or modern style.
  2. Show your barber a picture or describe the style you want. Be specific about the length and the tapering effect you desire.
  3. Your barber will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair with clippers or scissors. The hair will be tapered gradually to the neckline.
  4. The top of your hair will be cut to the desired length, and your barber will blend it with the sides and back.
  5. Your barber may use a razor or trimmer to create a clean line at the neckline and around the ears.
  6. After the haircut, your barber will style your hair with a product that suits your hair type and desired look.

Remember to communicate with your barber throughout the process to ensure you're getting the style you want.

Tips for Getting a Perfect Taper Haircut

To get the best results from your taper haircut, follow these tips:

  • Find a skilled and experienced barber who understands your hair type and desired style.
  • Bring a picture or describe the style you want clearly and concisely.
  • Communicate with your barber throughout the process to ensure you're getting the style you want.
  • Use hair styling products that suit your hair type and desired look.
  • Maintain your haircut regularly by scheduling appointments with your barber every 4-6 weeks.

FAQs About Taper Haircut

Here are some frequently asked questions about taper haircuts:

What is a taper haircut?

A taper haircut is a style where the hair gradually decreases in length towards the neckline. It can be combined with a fade or a classic scissor cut.

What's the difference between a taper and a fade?

A taper involves gradually decreasing the length of the hair towards the neckline, while a fade involves tapering the hair to a shorter length using clippers.

How long does a taper haircut last?

A taper haircut can last up to 4-6 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it.

Can taper haircuts suit all hair types?

Yes, taper haircuts can suit all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair.

Hair Care After Taper Haircut

After getting a taper haircut, you need to take care of your hair to maintain the style and health of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, as they can damage your hair and affect the shape of your taper haircut.
  • Use hair styling products that suit your hair type and desired look.
  • Maintain your haircut regularly by scheduling appointments with your barber every 4-6 weeks.

By following these tips, you can keep your taper haircut looking fresh and stylish.

In conclusion, asking for a taper haircut is easy, and you don't need to be a hair expert or use complicated terms. Simply communicate with your barber and describe the style you want, and they'll take care of the rest. By following our step-by-step guide and tips, you can get the perfect taper haircut that suits your hair type and desired look.

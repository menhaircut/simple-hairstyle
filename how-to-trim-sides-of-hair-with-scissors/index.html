How To Trim Sides Of Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Trimming the sides of your hair may seem like a daunting task, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a fresh look without having to visit a salon. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps on how to trim sides of hair with scissors in a relaxed language. We will also provide you with some tips and tricks to ensure that you get the best results.

How to Trim Sides of Hair with Scissors

Before we begin, let’s go over the tools you will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clip or ponytail holder

Once you have these tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and making sure it is clean and dry. Wet hair is difficult to cut, so make sure your hair is completely dry before you begin.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to separate the top section of your hair from the sides. Clip or tie back the top section so that it is out of the way while you work on the sides.

Step 3: Determine the Length

Decide on the length you want for your hair. If you’re not sure, start with a longer length and work your way down.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Take a small section of hair from the side and comb it straight down. Hold the section between your fingers and use the scissors to trim the ends. Work your way around the sides, taking small sections and trimming them to the desired length.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you have finished cutting, use a mirror to check your work. Make sure both sides are even and adjust as necessary.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Use the scissors to blend the hair on the sides with the hair on the top. This will create a seamless transition from the sides to the top.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once you’re happy with the length and blend, style your hair as desired. You can use a hairdryer, curling iron, or straightener to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Trimming Sides of Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way down. It’s easier to cut more hair than to add it back.
  • Take small sections of hair and trim them slowly. This will help you avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go. This will help you ensure that both sides are even.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for help. If you’re not confident in your ability to trim your hair, ask a friend or family member to assist you.

FAQ

Can I trim the sides of my hair without scissors?

While scissors are the best tool for trimming hair, you can use clippers if you prefer a shorter cut.

How often should I trim the sides of my hair?

It depends on your hair growth rate and the length of your hair. As a general rule, it’s recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired length.

Hair Care

Regular trimming is important for maintaining healthy hair. It helps to remove split ends and prevent breakage. In addition to trimming, make sure to take good care of your hair by using a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoiding excessive heat styling, and protecting your hair from the sun.

Now that you know how to trim sides of hair with scissors, give it a try and see how it turns out. With a little practice, you’ll be able to achieve a fresh look whenever you want.

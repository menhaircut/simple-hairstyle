Does Picking Your Hair Make It Curly

Written by William Jul 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who's always been fascinated by the beauty of curly hair, you might have heard several myths about how to make your straight hair curly. One of the most popular myths is that picking your hair can make it curly. But is that really true? In this article, we'll explore the truth behind this myth and give you some tips on how to achieve curly hair.

Description

The myth that picking your hair can make it curly has been around for many years. This myth suggests that by picking your hair, you can create small curls or waves in your hair. Some people even believe that picking your hair regularly can permanently change the texture of your hair, making it curly. However, this myth is not entirely true.

How to

Picking your hair alone cannot make it curly. However, there are some techniques you can use to achieve curly hair. Here are some methods you can try:

Method 1: Use Curling Products

One of the easiest ways to achieve curly hair is by using curling products. You can use curling creams, gels, or mousses to create curls in your hair. Simply apply the product to your hair, scrunch your hair, and let it air dry. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance the curls.

Method 2: Use Heat Styling Tools

You can also use heat styling tools like curling irons or wands to create curls in your hair. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to protect your hair from heat damage.

Method 3: Try a Perm

If you want a more permanent solution for curly hair, you can try getting a perm. A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the texture of your hair, making it curly or wavy. However, perms can be damaging to your hair, so make sure to consult with a professional stylist before getting a perm.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving curly hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

Step 2: Apply Curling Product

Apply a curling product to your hair. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair to create curls. Use your hands to scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Let your hair air dry. Avoid using a hairdryer as it can cause frizz.

Step 5: Enhance Your Curls

If you want to enhance your curls, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer. Simply attach the diffuser to your hairdryer and use it to dry your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips for achieving curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before applying any products.
  • Avoid brushing your hair as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about picking your hair and curly hair:

1. Can picking your hair make it curly?

No, picking your hair alone cannot make it curly. However, there are other methods you can use to achieve curly hair.

2. Is it bad to pick your hair?

Picking your hair can cause damage to your hair and scalp. It can also cause frizz and breakage.

3. Can you make your hair permanently curly?

Yes, you can make your hair permanently curly by getting a perm. However, perms can be damaging to your hair, so make sure to consult with a professional stylist before getting a perm.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and frizz-free. Here are some tips for taking care of curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol as they can dry out your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, curly hair without picking your hair. Remember, picking your hair can cause damage and frizz, so it's best to avoid it altogether.

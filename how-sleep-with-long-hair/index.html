There are a few ways to sleep with long hair, depending on your hair type and personal preference. Here are some of the most popular methods:

If you have curly hair, you may want to consider piling your hair on top of your head to sleep. This helps to keep your curls intact and prevent them from getting flattened during the night. You can use a soft scrunchie or a hair clip to hold the pile in place.

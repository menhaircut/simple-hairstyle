How Do I Know What Hairstyle Would Suit Me? Tips and Tricks

Figuring out what hairstyle would suit you can be a daunting task. With so many options and trends to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. However, with a few tips and tricks, finding the perfect hairstyle for you can be a stress-free process. Here are some ways to help you determine what hairstyle would suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Considering Your Hair Type
  • Personal Style and Preferences
  • Getting the Haircut
  • Hair Care Tips

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for you is to determine your face shape. Your face shape plays a significant role in determining what hairstyles would look best on you. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back from your face. Look at the shape of your face and compare it to the six basic face shapes. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that flatter your features.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you want to create the illusion of length in your face. Hairstyles that work well for a round face shape are those that add height and volume to the crown of your head. Long, layered hairstyles that fall below the chin are also great options as they elongate the face.

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is considered the ideal face shape as it is versatile and can pull off many different hairstyles. If you have an oval face shape, you can experiment with a variety of hair lengths and styles.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong jawline and an equally broad forehead. Hairstyles that work well for a square face shape are those that soften the angles of the face. Soft, layered hairstyles that fall around the collarbone are great options.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape features a wide forehead and a narrow chin. Hairstyles that work well for a heart face shape are those that add volume to the chin area. Long, layered hairstyles that fall below the collarbone are great options.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and chin with wide cheekbones. Hairstyles that work well for a diamond face shape are those that add volume to the forehead and chin areas. Long, layered hairstyles with side-swept bangs are great options.

Oblong Face Shape

An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide and has a prominent chin and forehead. Hairstyles that work well for an oblong face shape are those that add width to the face. Short to medium-length hairstyles with volume at the sides are great options.

Considering Your Hair Type

Another factor to consider when determining what hairstyle would suit you is your hair type. Your hair type can influence the way a hairstyle looks on you and how easy it is to maintain. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Here are some hairstyle options for each hair type:

Straight Hair: Sleek bob, blunt cut, layered long hair

Wavy Hair: Beachy waves, layered shag, long layers

Curly Hair: Curly bob, layered cut, side-swept bangs

Coily Hair: Tapered cut, wash and go, twist-out

Personal Style and Preferences

Finally, when determining what hairstyle would suit you, consider your personal style and preferences. Your hairstyle should be an expression of your personality and should make you feel confident and comfortable. Think about the colors, textures, and accessories that you enjoy wearing and how they can be incorporated into your hairstyle. Your hairstylist can also offer suggestions based on your personal style and preferences.

Getting the Haircut

Once you have determined the perfect hairstyle for you, it is time to get the haircut. It is essential to communicate with your hairstylist and show them pictures of the hairstyle you want. Be honest about your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle, so your stylist can create a hairstyle that is customized for you. Don't be afraid to ask questions or provide feedback during the haircutting process.

Hair Care Tips

After getting a new haircut, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and style. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh environmental factors.

FAQs

How often should I get a haircut?

It is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

How do I choose the right hair color?

When choosing a hair color, consider your skin tone and personal style. It is also important to consult with a professional hairstylist to find the right shade for you.

What should I do if I don't like my new haircut?

If you are unhappy with your new haircut, communicate with your stylist and provide specific feedback. They may be able to make adjustments to the haircut to better suit your preferences.

Figuring out what hairstyle would suit you can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your face shape, hair type, personal style, and preferences, you can find the perfect hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Remember to communicate with your hairstylist and take care of your hair to maintain its shape and style.

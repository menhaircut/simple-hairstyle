Best Hairstyle For Thick Hair Male The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thick hair is a blessing and a curse at the same time. While it adds volume and texture to your hair, it can also be difficult to style and manage. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we will share the best hairstyle for thick hair male, along with tips and tricks to keep your hair looking great.

Description

Thick hair is characterized by a high density of hair strands on the scalp. This type of hair tends to be strong and resilient, but it can also be prone to frizz and tangling. Men with thick hair have a wide range of hairstyle options, from short and sleek to long and wavy.

How to

The best way to style thick hair is to work with its natural texture. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for thick hair. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and manageable.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as a mousse or a gel, to add volume and hold.
  3. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while shaping it with your fingers. If you want a sleek look, use a comb to create a side part.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to keep your style in place throughout the day.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for the best hairstyle for thick hair male:

Short and Sleek

  1. Ask your barber for a short haircut, such as a buzz cut or a crew cut. This will help to reduce the volume of your hair and make it easier to style.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your hair and use a comb to create a side part.
  3. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while shaping it with your fingers.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to keep your style in place.

Medium Length and Textured

  1. Ask your barber for a medium-length haircut, such as a layered cut or a textured crop.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your hair and use your fingers to create texture and volume.
  3. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while shaping it with your fingers.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to keep your style in place.

Long and Wavy

  1. Ask your barber for a long haircut, such as a shoulder-length cut or a shaggy cut.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to your hair and use your fingers to create waves and texture.
  3. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while shaping it with your fingers.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your thick hair looking great:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for thick hair?

The best haircut for thick hair depends on your personal style and preferences. Short haircuts, such as buzz cuts and crew cuts, are great for reducing the volume of your hair, while medium-length and long haircuts are ideal for showcasing your hair's natural texture and volume.

How do I style thick hair?

To style thick hair, it's best to work with its natural texture. Use a styling product, such as a mousse or a gel, to add volume and hold, and use your fingers or a comb to shape your hair while blow-drying it.

How do I keep my thick hair healthy?

To keep your thick hair healthy, use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for thick hair, avoid using hot tools that can damage your hair, get regular trims to prevent split ends, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your thick hair looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for thick hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat when blow-drying your hair.

By following these tips and using the best hairstyle for thick hair male, you can keep your hair looking great and feeling healthy.

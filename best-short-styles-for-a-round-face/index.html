Best Short Styles For A Round Face A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Aug 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of always having the same hairstyle? Do you have a round face and struggle to find the perfect short style? Look no further! In this guide, we'll go over the best short styles for a round face in 2023. From bobs to pixies, we've got you covered.

Description

Having a round face can make it challenging to find the right hairstyle. You want to choose a style that will complement your face shape and enhance your features. Short hairstyles are a great option as they can make your face appear slimmer and highlight your best features. In this guide, we'll go over the best short styles for a round face that will leave you feeling confident and stylish.

How-To

Before we dive into the different short styles, it's important to know your face shape. To determine if you have a round face, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all similar in width, you have a round face. Once you know your face shape, it's time to choose a short style that will flatter it.

Step-by-Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape
Step 2: Look at different short styles that complement a round face
Step 3: Choose a style that you love and is easy to maintain
Step 4: Visit a hairstylist for a consultation and to get your hair cut

Tips

When choosing a short style, consider the texture and thickness of your hair. A layered bob can add volume to fine hair, while a pixie cut can work well with thick hair. Another tip is to add some texture to your hair with highlights or lowlights. This can add dimension and make your face appear slimmer.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have bangs with a round face?

A: Yes! Bangs can work well with a round face, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Q: Can I still have long hair with a round face?

A: Absolutely! While short hairstyles can work well with a round face, long hairstyles can also be flattering. Consider adding layers to your hair to add movement and frame your face.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any hairstyle. To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

There are many short styles that can work well with a round face. From bobs to pixies, there's a style out there for everyone. It's important to choose a style that you love and feel confident in. Remember to consult with a hairstylist to ensure the style you choose is right for you.

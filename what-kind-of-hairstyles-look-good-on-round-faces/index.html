What Kind Of Hairstyles Look Good On Round Faces

Written by Robby Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your round face? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people have round faces and finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But fear not, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll explore what kind of hairstyles look good on round faces and provide some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by a wider forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles. The key is to avoid hairstyles that make your face look wider and rounder. Look for styles that add height and length to your face.

How To

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your round face is to determine your face shape. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all similar in width, then you have a round face. Once you know your face shape, it's time to start exploring different hairstyles that will flatter your features.

Step by Step

Here are some steps you can follow to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face:

  1. Start by looking at hairstyles that add height and volume to your crown. This will help elongate your face and create the illusion of length.
  2. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or blunt. These styles can make your face look wider and rounder.
  3. Consider adding layers to your hair. Layers can create movement and dimension, which can help balance out the roundness of your face.
  4. Try a side-swept bang. This can help create angles and draw attention away from the roundness of your face.
  5. Consider a long bob or "lob" hairstyle. This length can help elongate your face while still providing volume and movement.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or flat. These styles can make your face look wider and rounder.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair. This can create depth and dimension, which can help balance out the roundness of your face.
  • Try a side part. This can create the illusion of length and draw attention away from the roundness of your face.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and lengths. It may take some trial and error to find the perfect look, but it will be worth it in the end.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or blunt. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or flat.

What hairstyles look good on round faces?

Hairstyles that add height and volume to your crown, such as a long bob or layers, can help elongate your face and create the illusion of length. A side-swept bang can also help create angles and draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for your round face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to hydrate and nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect hairstyle for your round face. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

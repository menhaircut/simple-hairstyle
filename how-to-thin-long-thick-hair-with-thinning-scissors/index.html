How To Thin Long Thick Hair With Thinning Scissors

Written by Robby Nov 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it may look beautiful and voluminous, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Thinning scissors are a great tool for those with thick hair who want to achieve a more manageable and lightweight style. In this article, we will cover how to thin long thick hair with thinning scissors, including tips, steps, and hair care advice.

Description

Thinning scissors are a type of hair cutting scissors that have teeth on one or both blades. These teeth help to remove some of the hair from the section being cut, resulting in a thinner and more lightweight look. Thinning scissors are a great tool for those with thick hair, as they can help to reduce the weight of the hair and make it more manageable.

How to Thin Long Thick Hair with Thinning Scissors

Before you begin, it is important to make sure that your hair is clean and dry. You will also need a pair of thinning scissors, which can be purchased at most beauty supply stores or online. Follow these steps to thin your long thick hair with thinning scissors:

Step 1: Section your hair

Start by sectioning your hair into small sections. The size of the sections will depend on the thickness of your hair. If you have very thick hair, you may need to work with smaller sections. Use hair clips or ties to keep the sections separate.

Step 2: Hold the scissors correctly

Hold the thinning scissors with the teeth facing upwards, and the regular scissors with the blades facing downwards. This will help to ensure that you are using the correct scissors for each section of hair.

Step 3: Cut the hair

Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the thinning scissors to make small cuts along the length of the hair. Be sure to only cut a small amount of hair at a time, as thinning scissors can be very powerful. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you have achieved the desired level of thinness.

Step 4: Blend the hair

After you have thinned each section of hair, use the regular scissors to blend the hair together. This will help to create a more natural and seamless look.

Tips

- Use caution when using thinning scissors, as they can be very sharp and powerful.

- If you are unsure about how to use thinning scissors, it may be best to seek the help of a professional hairstylist.

- Remember to only cut a small amount of hair at a time, as thinning scissors can be very powerful.

FAQ

Q: How often should I thin my long thick hair with thinning scissors?

A: This will depend on the individual and their hair type. Some people may only need to thin their hair once every few months, while others may need to do it more frequently.

Q: Can thinning scissors be used on all hair types?

A: Thinning scissors are typically best suited for those with thick hair. Using them on thin or fine hair can result in a choppy and uneven look.

Hair Care

After thinning your long thick hair with thinning scissors, it is important to take care of your hair to ensure that it remains healthy and strong. Here are a few tips:

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it is less likely to cause damage.

Thinning scissors can be a great tool for those with long thick hair who want to achieve a more manageable and lightweight style. By following these tips and steps, you can safely and effectively thin your hair at home. Remember to take care of your hair to ensure that it remains healthy and strong.

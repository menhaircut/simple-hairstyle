Why Are My Ends Straight After A Perm

Written by Frank Jul 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have recently gotten a perm and noticed that the ends of your hair are straight, you may be wondering why this has happened. This article will explain why your hair ends may be straight after a perm, and what you can do to fix it.

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that is used to add curls or waves to your hair. The process involves applying a solution to your hair that breaks down the protein bonds in your hair, allowing it to be reshaped. The solution is then rinsed out, and your hair is styled into the desired shape.

How To

If you have noticed that the ends of your hair are straight after a perm, there are a few things that you can do to fix it. Here are some steps that you can take:

Step 1: Get a Trim

If your hair ends are straight, it may be because they are damaged or split. Getting a trim can help to remove the damaged ends and give your hair a fresh start. This will also help to prevent further damage and breakage.

Step 2: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment can help to restore moisture to your hair and prevent further damage. Look for a treatment that is specifically designed for permed hair, as this will help to protect your curls and keep them looking great.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

If you want to add some volume and curl to your hair, try using a curling iron or hot rollers. This can help to create the look that you want and add some texture to your hair. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you prevent your hair ends from becoming straight after a perm:

  • Make sure to get your hair trimmed regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair products that can cause damage to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Why are my ends straight after a perm?

A: Your ends may be straight after a perm because they are damaged or split. This can cause the hair to break off or become weaker, resulting in straight ends.

Q: Can I fix straight ends after a perm?

A: Yes, you can fix straight ends after a perm by getting a trim, using a deep conditioning treatment, and styling your hair with heat styling tools.

Q: How often should I get a perm?

A: You should wait at least six months before getting another perm to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

Perms can be a great way to add some texture and volume to your hair, but it is important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Make sure to get your hair trimmed regularly, use a deep conditioning treatment, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. With the right care, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

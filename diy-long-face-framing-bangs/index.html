Diy Long Face Framing Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for an affordable way to change up your hairstyle, adding long face framing bangs can make a big impact. Not only do they highlight your facial features, but they also add a touch of glamour to any look. In this article, we'll show you how to create DIY long face framing bangs from the comfort of your own home. Here's what we'll cover:

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long face framing bangs are a great way to add some excitement to your hair, without having to make any drastic changes. They work well for any face shape and can be styled in various ways. The best part is that you can create long face framing bangs at home, without having to make an appointment at the salon.

How To

The first step to creating DIY long face framing bangs is to choose the right length. Depending on how dramatic you want your bangs to be, you can choose to cut them at eyebrow level or slightly longer. Once you've decided on the length, it's time to gather your tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips

Step-by-Step

Now that you have your tools, it's time to get started on creating your long face framing bangs. Here are the steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Section off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. Use hair clips to secure the rest of your hair out of the way.
  3. Using your comb, section off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. Make sure the section is straight and even.
  4. Hold the section of hair between your fingers, and carefully cut it to the desired length. Remember to cut less than you think you need, as you can always trim more later.
  5. Repeat the previous steps until you've cut all of your hair into long face framing bangs.
  6. Once you've cut your hair, use your comb to blend your bangs into the rest of your hair. You can also use your scissors to trim any uneven pieces.
  7. Style your bangs as desired. You can straighten them with a flat iron, curl them with a curling iron, or leave them natural.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when creating long face framing bangs:

  • Start with small cuts, and work your way up. You can always trim more hair, but you can't add it back.
  • Make sure your hair is dry before cutting it. Wet hair can appear longer than it actually is, which can lead to uneven cuts.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends and frayed edges.
  • Don't cut too close to your eyebrows, as this can make your bangs appear too short.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about long face framing bangs:

Can I create long face framing bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes! Long face framing bangs can work well with curly hair. Just make sure to cut them a little longer than you would if you had straight hair, as curly hair appears shorter when dry.

How often should I trim my long face framing bangs?

It's best to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Will long face framing bangs work for me if I have a round face?

Yes! Long face framing bangs can actually help to elongate a round face.

Hair Care

Now that you have your long face framing bangs, it's important to take care of them. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling your bangs with heat tools.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days, and use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your bangs and make them appear greasy.
  • Trim any split ends as soon as you notice them, to keep your bangs looking fresh.

Creating DIY long face framing bangs can be a fun and easy way to switch up your hairstyle. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a glamorous and stylish look in no time.

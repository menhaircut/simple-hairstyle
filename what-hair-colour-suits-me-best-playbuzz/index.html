Are you looking for a new hair colour but not sure what suits you best? Look no further! Playbuzz has a fun quiz that can help you determine what hair colour suits your skin tone, eye colour, and personality. In this article, we will guide you on how to take the quiz and provide tips on how to maintain your new hair colour. Let's get started!

Description

Playbuzz is a website that offers various quizzes, including one that determines what hair colour suits you best. The quiz asks questions about your skin tone, eye colour, and personality to suggest the perfect hair colour for you. It's a fun and easy way to discover a new look without the hassle of trial and error.

How to

First, go to the Playbuzz website and search for the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" quiz. Click on the quiz and begin answering the questions truthfully. The quiz will ask about your skin tone, eye colour, and personality. After answering all the questions, the quiz will suggest a hair colour that suits you best.

Step by Step

Step 1: Go to the Playbuzz website
Step 2: Search for "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" quiz
Step 3: Click on the quiz and start answering the questions
Step 4: Answer all the questions truthfully
Step 5: The quiz will suggest a hair colour that suits you best

Tips

When taking the quiz, it's important to answer the questions truthfully. Don't try to manipulate the answers to get a certain hair colour. Trust the quiz and have fun with it. Also, consider your lifestyle and maintenance level before choosing a hair colour. Some colours require more upkeep than others.

FAQ

Q: Is the quiz accurate?

A: The quiz is based on general guidelines and may not be 100% accurate. However, it can provide a good starting point for choosing a hair colour.

Q: Can I change my hair colour after taking the quiz?

A: Of course! The quiz is just a suggestion. You can choose any hair colour you desire.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair colour?

A: It depends on the hair colour and how fast your hair grows. Generally, touch-ups are needed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After choosing your new hair colour, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading. Avoid excessive heat styling and always use a heat protectant. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Choosing a new hair colour can be overwhelming, but Playbuzz's "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" quiz can make it fun and easy. Remember to answer the questions truthfully and consider your lifestyle before making a decision. And don't forget to take care of your new hair colour with proper maintenance. Happy colouring!

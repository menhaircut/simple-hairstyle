Long Hair For Oblong Face Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you an oblong-faced male wondering if long hair is a good fit for you? Look no further! This guide will provide you with all the information you need to confidently grow out your locks and rock them like a pro.

Description

Long hair can be a great look for oblong-faced males, as it helps to balance out the proportions of the face. However, not all hairstyles will suit an oblong face shape, so it's important to choose the right one. This guide will offer tips and tricks for choosing the perfect long hairstyle to suit an oblong face shape, as well as advice on how to care for and maintain long hair.

How to

The first step in achieving long hair as an oblong-faced male is to commit to growing it out. This may take some time, but with patience and a few tricks, you'll have luscious locks in no time.

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before choosing a long hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape, with a forehead, cheekbones, and jawline that are all roughly the same width.

Step 2: Choose the right hairstyle

When it comes to choosing a long hairstyle for an oblong face shape, it's important to consider volume and texture. A hairstyle with layers and volume at the top can help to balance out the length of the face, while adding texture can create the illusion of width.

Step 3: Grow it out

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle, it's time to commit to growing out your hair. This may take several months or even a year, but with regular trims and proper care, you'll have healthy and long locks in no time.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when growing out long hair as an oblong-faced male:

  • Regular trims are important to keep split ends at bay and maintain healthy hair.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Consider using hair products like mousse or hairspray to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about long hair for oblong-faced males:

Q: Will long hair make my face look longer?

Not necessarily. With the right hairstyle and grooming techniques, long hair can actually balance out the length of an oblong face shape.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with an oblong face shape?

Absolutely! Experiment with different updos, like messy buns or half-up styles, to find the one that suits you best.

Q: How often should I trim my hair when growing it out?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential when growing out long hair as an oblong-faced male. Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to achieving the perfect long hairstyle for your oblong face shape. Happy growing!

What Kind Of Bangs For My Face Shape A Guide To Flattering Fringes

Written by Aprilia Dec 03, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're thinking about getting bangs but aren't sure what style will suit your face shape, you're not alone. With so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to figure out what kind of bangs for your face shape will flatter your features best. But don't worry – in this guide, we'll break down the different types of bangs and help you find the perfect match for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Face Shapes
  • Types of Bangs
  • How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape
  • Tips for Styling Your Bangs
  • FAQs

Face Shapes

Before we dive into the different types of bangs, let's first identify your face shape. There are six main face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Long

Take a look at yourself in the mirror and determine which shape best describes your face. Once you know your face shape, you can use this guide to find the right bangs for your features.

Types of Bangs

There are several types of bangs to choose from, including:

  • Straight Across Bangs
  • Side-Swept Bangs
  • Curly Bangs
  • Blunt Bangs
  • Wispy Bangs
  • Tapered Bangs
  • Micro Bangs

Each of these styles can be customized to suit your face shape and personal preferences. Let's take a look at which bangs work best for each face shape.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape

Round Face

If you have a round face, you want to avoid bangs that are too short or too blunt. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that angle down towards the cheekbones. This will help elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

Oval Face

Lucky you – oval faces can pull off just about any type of bangs! Consider trying straight across bangs for a dramatic look, or side-swept bangs to add some softness to your features.

Square Face

Soft, rounder bangs work best for square faces. Try wispy or tapered bangs that curve around the forehead and blend in with the rest of your hair. Avoid blunt or straight across bangs, as they can make your face appear too boxy.

Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to balance out your features by drawing attention away from the forehead. Side-swept bangs are perfect for this, as they create a diagonal line that flatters the face. Avoid blunt or straight across bangs, which can make the forehead appear larger.

Diamond Face

Diamond faces are characterized by high cheekbones and a narrow forehead. To balance out your features, try side-swept bangs that angle downwards towards the middle of your face. Avoid blunt or heavy bangs, which can make your face appear too top-heavy.

Long Face

If you have a long face, you want to avoid bangs that are too short or too blunt. Instead, try side-swept bangs that angle downwards towards the cheekbones. This will help create the illusion of a shorter face.

Tips for Styling Your Bangs

Once you've found the perfect bangs for your face shape, it's important to know how to style them. Here are a few tips:

  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or cowlicks.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what looks best on you.

FAQs

Can I pull off bangs if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Curly bangs can look amazing on the right person. Just make sure to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture.

Can I cut my own bangs?

We don't recommend it. Cutting bangs requires a certain level of skill and precision. It's best to leave it to a professional stylist.

What if I don't like my bangs?

Don't worry – bangs grow out! If you're not happy with your new fringe, talk to your stylist about options for growing them out or changing the style.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Now that you have your perfect bangs, it's important to take care of them. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your bangs every 2-3 days to keep them looking fresh.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to keep your bangs from getting greasy.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as oils from your hands can make them look greasy.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect bangs for your face shape and rock them with confidence. Happy styling!

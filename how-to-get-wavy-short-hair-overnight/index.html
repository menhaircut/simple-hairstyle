How To Get Wavy Short Hair Overnight A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having the same straight and boring hair every day? Do you want to switch up your look with some effortless waves? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we will be providing you with an easy-to-follow guide on how to get wavy short hair overnight. So, let's get started!

How to Get Wavy Short Hair Overnight

Whether you have naturally straight hair or even if your hair is already slightly wavy, you can achieve beautiful waves overnight. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to getting wavy short hair overnight is to start with clean hair. Make sure to wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to ensure that your hair is healthy and nourished.

Step 2: Apply Some Hair Mousse

The next step is to apply some hair mousse to your damp hair. Mousse will help to add volume and hold to your hair, making it easier to create waves. Make sure to distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Now, divide your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on the thickness of your hair. If you have thick hair, you may want to create more sections. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 4: Twist Each Section

Take each section of hair and twist it away from your face. The tighter you twist, the tighter the waves will be. Make sure to twist all sections in the same direction.

Step 5: Secure the Twists

Once you have twisted all sections, secure them in place with hair ties or bobby pins. Make sure that the twists are tight and secure, so they don't come undone while you sleep.

Step 6: Sleep on It

Now that your hair is all twisted up, it's time to get some beauty sleep. When you wake up in the morning, carefully remove the hair ties or bobby pins and gently unravel the twists.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Now that your hair has some beautiful waves, it's time to style it. Use your fingers to gently separate the waves and create a tousled look. You can also use a texturizing spray to add some extra volume and texture to your hair.

Tips for Getting Wavy Short Hair Overnight

Here are some additional tips to help you get beautiful waves overnight:

  • Use a silk pillowcase to reduce hair breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and dry it out.
  • If you have naturally curly hair, you may not need to twist your hair as tightly to achieve waves.
  • Experiment with the number of sections and the tightness of the twists to find the perfect wave for your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get wavy short hair overnight without using hair mousse?

While hair mousse can help to add volume and hold to your hair, you can still achieve waves without it. Instead, you can try using a curl-enhancing cream or spray.

Can I sleep with wet hair to get wavy short hair overnight?

No, it's not recommended to sleep with wet hair, as it can cause hair breakage and frizz. Make sure to dry your hair before twisting and securing it for the night.

Hair Care for Wavy Short Hair

Once you've achieved beautiful waves in your short hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and nourished. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for wavy or curly hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve beautiful waves in your short hair overnight. So, go ahead and give it a try!

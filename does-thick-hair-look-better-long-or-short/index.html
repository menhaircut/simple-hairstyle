Does Thick Hair Look Better Long Or Short

Written by Frank Aug 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having thick hair but can't decide whether to keep it long or cut it short? You're not alone. Many people with thick hair struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that suits them. In this article, we will discuss whether thick hair looks better long or short.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care
  7. Conclusion

Description

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it can look voluminous and beautiful, but on the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. When it comes to choosing between long and short hair, there are a few things to consider:

  • Your face shape
  • Your lifestyle
  • Your personal style
  • The texture of your hair

These factors can all affect whether long or short hair will look better on you.

How to

The first step in deciding whether to keep your thick hair long or short is to determine your face shape. Different face shapes suit different hairstyles, so it's important to choose a style that complements your features.

Next, consider your lifestyle. If you are always on the go and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, a shorter haircut may be more practical. If you have more time to spend on your hair, a longer style may be a better fit.

Your personal style is also important. If you prefer a more classic or conservative look, a shorter hairstyle may be more appropriate. If you like to experiment with bold and trendy looks, a longer hairstyle may be a better fit.

Finally, consider the texture of your hair. Thick hair can be straight, wavy, or curly, and each of these textures will look different in a long or short style. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which length will work best with your hair texture.

Step by step

Here are the steps to follow when deciding whether to keep your thick hair long or short:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Consider your lifestyle
  3. Think about your personal style
  4. Take your hair texture into account
  5. Consult with your hairstylist

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when deciding whether to keep your thick hair long or short:

  • Consider the maintenance required for each style
  • Look for inspiration in magazines or online
  • Try out different styles with hair extensions or wigs
  • Consult with your hairstylist to determine which style will work best for you

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about whether thick hair looks better long or short:

Q: Does thick hair look better long or short?

A: It depends on your face shape, lifestyle, personal style, and hair texture.

Q: What length is best for thick hair?

A: The best length for thick hair depends on the factors mentioned above. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which length will work best for you.

Q: Can thick hair be thinned out?

A: Yes, thick hair can be thinned out by a hairstylist using special techniques and tools.

Hair Care

Whether you decide to keep your thick hair long or short, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant before using heat styling tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized

Conclusion

In conclusion, whether thick hair looks better long or short depends on a variety of factors. It's important to consider your face shape, lifestyle, personal style, and hair texture when deciding which length to choose. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which style will work best for you, and take care of your hair properly to keep it healthy and beautiful.

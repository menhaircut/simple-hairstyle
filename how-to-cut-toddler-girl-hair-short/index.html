How To Cut Toddler Girl Hair Short A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're a mom of a toddler girl, you may be considering cutting her hair short. Short hair is not only easy to maintain but also looks adorable on little ones. However, cutting your toddler's hair can be a daunting task. Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this blog post, we'll guide you through how to cut toddler girl hair short in easy steps. So, let's get started!

How to Cut Toddler Girl Hair Short

Cutting a toddler girl's hair short can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, you can do it at home. Here's what you'll need:

Tools You'll Need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clippers (optional)
  • Hair ties
  • Spray bottle
  • Cape or towel

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your toddler girl's hair short:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Wash and condition your toddler's hair. Dry it with a towel and comb it to remove any tangles. Spray some water on the hair to make it damp.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Divide the hair into sections using hair ties. Start with the top section and secure it with a hair tie.

Step 3: Cut the Top Section

Using scissors, cut the top section to the desired length. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across.

Step 4: Cut the Side Sections

Unclip the side sections and comb them straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle towards the back.

Step 5: Cut the Back Section

Unclip the back section and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair by combing it and checking for any unevenness. Use scissors to trim any excess hair.

Step 7: Clean Up

Remove the hair ties and comb the hair to remove any loose hair. Clean up around the ears and neck using hair clippers or scissors.

Tips for Cutting Toddler Girl Hair Short

Here are some tips to make the process of cutting toddler girl hair short easier:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid pulling or tearing the hair.
  • Start with a longer length and work your way down gradually.
  • Keep a spray bottle handy to keep the hair damp.
  • Use hair clippers to trim the hair around the ears and neckline.
  • Take breaks if your toddler gets fussy or restless.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting toddler girl hair short:

1. How often should I cut my toddler girl's hair?

You should cut your toddler girl's hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the style and keep it healthy.

2. What if I make a mistake while cutting my toddler girl's hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your toddler girl's hair, try not to panic. You can always take her to a professional hairdresser to fix it.

3. How can I make my toddler girl sit still while cutting her hair?

You can distract your toddler girl by giving her a toy or a snack. You can also play her favorite music or video to keep her entertained.

Hair Care for Toddler Girl Hair

Here are some tips to take care of your toddler girl's short hair:

  • Wash her hair every two to three days with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep her hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or flat irons on her hair.
  • Trim her hair regularly to avoid split ends.

That's it! Follow these steps to cut toddler girl hair short and give your little one a cute and stylish look. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don't worry if it's not perfect the first time. Keep trying, and you'll get better with time. Good luck!

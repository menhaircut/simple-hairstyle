How To Blow Dry Wavy Frizzy Hair Straight

Written by Petter Sep 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post about how to blow dry wavy frizzy hair straight. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you know how difficult it can be to tame frizz and achieve a sleek, straight look. Blow drying your hair straight is a great way to achieve the look you want, but it can be tricky if you don't know what you're doing. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to blow dry wavy frizzy hair straight.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Blow Dry Wavy Frizzy Hair Straight
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Blowing Dry Wavy Frizzy Hair Straight

Step-by-Step Guide

Before we get started, it's important to note that the key to achieving a straight blowout is to start with clean, wet hair. Here's how to blow dry wavy frizzy hair straight:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Shampoo and condition your hair with products that are designed to tame frizz and add moisture. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water, but don't rub your hair vigorously as this can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from the heat of the blow dryer. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start with the bottom section and work your way up. This will make it easier to blow dry your hair straight and ensure that each section gets enough heat.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, start at the roots and blow dry your hair in a downward motion, following the brush with the nozzle of your blow dryer. Make sure to keep the brush taut and use a high heat setting. Repeat this process for each section of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Cool Air

Once you've blown dry all of your hair, switch to the cool air setting on your blow dryer and blow dry your hair for an additional 30 seconds to set the style.

Tips for Blowing Dry Wavy Frizzy Hair Straight

Blow drying wavy frizzy hair straight can be a challenge, but with these tips, you'll be able to achieve a sleek, straight look:

  • Use a high-quality blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to help distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz.
  • Invest in a good round brush with natural bristles to help smooth your hair and reduce frizz.
  • Don't blow dry your hair when it's soaking wet. Use a towel to gently squeeze out excess water before blow drying.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before blow drying.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while blow drying as this can cause frizz.

FAQs

Q: Can I blow dry my hair straight without using a round brush?

A: While a round brush is helpful for achieving a sleek, straight look, you can still blow dry your hair straight without one. Simply use your fingers to guide your hair as you blow dry it in a downward motion.

Q: How often should I blow dry my hair?

A: Blow drying your hair too often can cause damage and dryness. Try to limit blow drying to once or twice a week and use a heat protectant product to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to help keep your wavy frizzy hair looking its best:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often as they can cause damage.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as this can strip it of natural oils. Try to wash your hair every other day or every two days.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help tame frizz and add shine.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

We hope this article has been helpful in teaching you how to blow dry wavy frizzy hair straight. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a sleek, straight look that will last all day. If you have any additional tips or tricks, feel free to share them in the comments below.

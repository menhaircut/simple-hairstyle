How To Get Natural Curls For Boys A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curls are no longer just for girls. Boys with curly hair can rock a natural, effortless look that is both stylish and trendy. However, getting the perfect curls can be a challenge. That's why we've put together a complete guide on how to get natural curls for boys. From how to, step by step, tips, FAQ, and hair care, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Natural Curls for Boys
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Getting Natural Curls for Boys

How to Get Natural Curls for Boys

Getting natural curls for boys starts with the right haircut. You need to have a good length of hair for curls to show up. A short haircut will not give you the desired curls. Once you have the right haircut, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it dry and frizzy. Use a conditioner to add moisture to your hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product to your damp hair. You can use a curl cream, gel, or mousse. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage the curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Use a low heat setting and scrunch your hair as you dry it.

Step 4: Finish with a Hair Spray

Finish off with a hair spray to hold the curls in place. Use a light hold spray to avoid making your hair stiff. Avoid touching your hair too much to prevent frizz.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get natural curls for boys:

  1. Get the right haircut
  2. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo
  3. Apply a curl enhancing product
  4. Scrunch your hair with your hands or diffuser attachment
  5. Finish off with a hair spray

Tips for Getting Natural Curls for Boys

Here are some tips that can help you get natural curls for boys:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair as it can cause frizz
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair
  • Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage the curls
  • Use a light hold hair spray to avoid making your hair stiff

FAQs

Q: Can I get natural curls if I have straight hair?

A: It's possible to get natural curls even if you have straight hair. However, you may need to use a curling iron or get a perm to achieve the desired look.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and frizzy.

Q: Can I use a hair dryer without a diffuser attachment?

A: Yes, you can use a hair dryer without a diffuser attachment. However, it may cause frizz and make your curls less defined.

Hair Care for Boys with Natural Curls

Here are some tips on how to take care of your natural curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to add moisture to your hair

Getting natural curls for boys is easy with the right haircut and products. Follow our guide, and you'll have the perfect curls in no time. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

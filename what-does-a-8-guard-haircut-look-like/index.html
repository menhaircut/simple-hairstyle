What Does A 8 Guard Haircut Look Like A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to get a new haircut, you might be wondering about the different guard sizes and what they mean. One of the most popular guard sizes is the 8 guard, which leaves 1 inch of hair on the scalp. In this article, we'll go over what a 8 guard haircut looks like, how to achieve it, and some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking great.

Description

A 8 guard haircut is a medium-length style that leaves 1 inch of hair on the scalp. It's perfect for those who want a bit of length but still want to keep their hair relatively short. This guard size can be used to create a variety of styles, including buzz cuts, crew cuts, and textured cuts. It's also a popular choice for those who want to grow out their hair gradually.

How to Achieve a 8 Guard Haircut

If you're looking to achieve a 8 guard haircut, you'll need to have the right tools. You'll need a set of clippers with an 8 guard attachment, a comb, and scissors. Here's how to achieve this style:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Once your hair is clean, towel dry it or use a hair dryer to get it completely dry. This will make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Attach the 8 Guard to Your Clippers

Next, attach the 8 guard to your clippers. Make sure it's securely attached before you start cutting.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin at the sides of your head, moving the clippers up towards the top of your head. Use the comb to lift the hair as you go, making sure to cut evenly. Repeat this process on the back of your head and the other side.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the hair with the 8 guard, use scissors to blend the hair on the top of your head with the shorter hair on the sides and back. This will create a natural-looking transition between the different lengths.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as desired using a hair product of your choice. You can use a comb or your fingers to create texture and volume.

Tips for Maintaining a 8 Guard Haircut

To keep your 8 guard haircut looking great, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep the length consistent
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated

FAQ

How long does a 8 guard haircut last?

A 8 guard haircut can last anywhere from 4-6 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Can I achieve a 8 guard haircut at home?

Yes, you can achieve a 8 guard haircut at home with the right tools and technique. However, if you're not confident in your ability to cut your own hair, it's best to go to a professional.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is key to maintaining a 8 guard haircut. Make sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type. If you have dry hair, consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Finally, get regular trims to keep the length consistent and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

A 8 guard haircut is a versatile and stylish choice for those who want a bit of length without going too short. By following the steps above and maintaining your hair properly, you can achieve a great-looking haircut that will last for weeks. Whether you're looking for a buzz cut, crew cut, or textured cut, the 8 guard is a great option to consider.

Is Short Hair Good For Oval Faces Male

Written by Bardi Sep 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you been contemplating getting a short haircut but unsure if it would suit your oval face shape? Well, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about whether short hair is good for oval faces male, including tips and FAQs. So, let’s dive in!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Oval faces are the most versatile and balanced face shape, making it easy to pull off any hairstyle. If you have an oval face, you can experiment with both short and long hairstyles, as long as they complement your features. Short haircuts are excellent for oval faces because they highlight your facial features and give your face a more defined look. However, not all short haircuts work for oval faces, so you need to choose the right one.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for an oval face, you need to consider the length, texture, and style that suits you. Here are some tips to guide you:

1. Determine your face shape

Before choosing a short haircut, make sure you know your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face using a tape measure. An oval face typically has a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, with prominent cheekbones.

2. Consider your hair texture

Short haircuts work well for oval faces, regardless of hair texture. However, you need to consider your hair type when choosing a style. For example, if you have curly hair, you can opt for a short cut with more volume at the top to balance out your face shape.

3. Choose the right length

When it comes to short haircuts for oval faces, the length is crucial. You want to avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make your face appear longer. Instead, choose a haircut that falls just above the ears or at chin-length.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a short haircut for an oval face:

1. Consult with your barber

Before getting a short haircut, consult with your barber to determine the best style for your face shape and hair texture.

2. Bring reference pictures

Bring reference pictures of short haircuts you like to give your barber an idea of the style you want.

3. Consider maintenance

Short haircuts require frequent maintenance, so consider how often you are willing to visit the barber before getting a short haircut.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when getting a short haircut for an oval face:

1. Add volume

Adding volume to your hair can help balance out an oval face. Consider using volumizing products or getting a cut with more volume at the top.

2. Avoid bangs

Bangs can make an oval face appear shorter, so it’s best to avoid them if you have an oval face.

3. Consider your lifestyle

When choosing a short haircut, consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair. Choose a low-maintenance style if you have a busy schedule.

FAQ

Q: Can oval faces pull off any short hairstyle?

A: While oval faces can pull off most short hairstyles, it’s essential to choose a style that complements your features and hair texture.

Q: Should I choose a choppy or sleek short haircut for an oval face?

A: It depends on your personal style and hair texture. Choppy haircuts can add texture and volume, while sleek haircuts can give a more polished look.

Q: How often should I get a short haircut for an oval face?

A: Short haircuts require more maintenance than longer styles, so it’s recommended to visit the barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Hair Care

Short haircuts for oval faces require proper hair care to maintain their shape and texture. Here are some tips to keep your short haircut looking its best:

1. Use the right products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and style, such as volumizing products or styling gels.

2. Wash and condition regularly

Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner.

3. Style with care

When styling your hair, be gentle to avoid damaging the hair or altering the shape of your haircut. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair.

4. Protect from heat

Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can damage your hair. Use a heat protection spray when using heat styling tools.

In conclusion, short hair can look great on oval faces male, as long as you choose the right style and consider your hair texture and lifestyle. Remember to consult with your barber and maintain your hair with proper care and styling.

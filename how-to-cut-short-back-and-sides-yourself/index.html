How To Cut Short Back And Sides Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save money on haircuts or just want to try your hand at cutting your own hair, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll show you how to cut short back and sides yourself without the need for any professional help.

Description

Short back and sides is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. It's a low-maintenance cut that's perfect for men who want a stylish yet practical look. However, getting this haircut at a barbershop can be expensive, and you may not always get the desired result. By learning how to cut short back and sides yourself, you can save money and have more control over your hairstyle.

How to Cut Short Back and Sides Yourself

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, a mirror, and a towel. Make sure your clippers are fully charged or plugged in.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and then dry it with a towel. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an even result.

Step 3: Determine the Length

Decide on the length you want for your hair. If you're not sure, start with a longer length and gradually go shorter until you reach your desired length.

Step 4: Start with the Sides

Begin by using your clippers to trim the sides of your head. Use a longer guard for the top section of your sides and a shorter guard for the lower section. Start at the bottom and work your way up to the top, using the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Move to the Back

Next, use your clippers to trim the back of your head. Again, use a longer guard for the top section and a shorter guard for the lower section. Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Step 6: Trim the Top

Use your scissors to trim the top of your hair to your desired length. Start at the front and work your way to the back, using the comb to guide the scissors and ensure an even cut.

Step 7: Blend the Hair

Blend the sides and back of your hair with the top by using the clippers without a guard. Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure a smooth transition between the different lengths.

Step 8: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check the back and sides of your hair. If you notice any uneven areas, use your clippers or scissors to fix them.

Step 9: Clean Up

Use your clippers to clean up any stray hairs around your ears and neck. Brush away any loose hair with a towel.

Step 10: Style Your Hair

Apply a small amount of hair product to your hair and style it to your desired look.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter until you reach your desired length.
  • Use the comb to guide the clippers and scissors and ensure an even cut.
  • If you're not sure about a certain area, start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard.
  • Use a mirror to check the back and sides of your hair.

FAQ

Can I cut short back and sides myself?

Yes, you can cut short back and sides yourself with the right tools and technique.

What tools do I need to cut short back and sides myself?

You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, a mirror, and a towel.

What length should I start with?

Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter until you reach your desired length.

Hair Care

To maintain your short back and sides, you'll need to trim your hair every few weeks. Use a mirror to check for any uneven areas and use your clippers or scissors to fix them. Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner and apply a small amount of hair product to style it.

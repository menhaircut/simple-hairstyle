Videos On How To Cut Your Own Hair With Clippers If You Are A Woman

Written by Robby Sep 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending hundreds of dollars every year on haircuts? With the help of videos on how to cut your own hair with clippers, you can say goodbye to those expensive trips to the salon. This article will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your hair with clippers, along with some tips and FAQ to ensure you get the best results possible.

Description

Learning how to cut your own hair with clippers can save you a significant amount of money and time. With the help of these videos, you can achieve professional-looking results in the comfort of your own home. It's important to note that while these videos are specifically for women, anyone can use them to cut their hair with clippers.

How To

The first step in cutting your own hair with clippers is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a set of clippers, a comb, hair scissors, a mirror, and a towel to cover your shoulders. Once you have all the tools, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you get the best results possible.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guards

Most clippers come with different guards that determine the length of the hair. Choose the guard that corresponds to the length you want your hair to be.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Begin by trimming the sides of your hair. Use the clippers to trim the hair in an upward motion, starting from the bottom and working your way up to the top. Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Once you have finished trimming the sides, move on to the back of your head. Use the same technique as before, trimming the hair in an upward motion and using the comb to guide the clippers.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Finally, trim the top of your hair using the scissors. Take small sections of hair and trim them to the desired length. Use the comb to guide the scissors and ensure that you get an even cut.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results possible:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one until you get the desired length.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head and ensure that you have an even cut.
  • Go slowly and take your time. Rushing can result in an uneven cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair with clippers:

Can I cut my own hair with clippers if I have long hair?

Yes, you can still cut your own hair with clippers if you have long hair. However, you may need to use scissors to trim the ends and ensure a more even cut.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always cut your hair shorter or visit a professional stylist to fix the mistake.

Hair Care

After cutting your own hair with clippers, it's important to take care of it properly. Make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a leave-in conditioner to keep it healthy and shiny.

Cutting your own hair with clippers can be a daunting task, but with the help of these videos and tips, you can achieve professional-looking results in no time. Just remember to take your time and go slow, and you'll be sure to get the perfect cut every time.

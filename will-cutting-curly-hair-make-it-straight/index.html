Will Cutting Curly Hair Make It Straight A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Oct 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have curly hair, you may have wondered if cutting it would make it straight. The answer is no, cutting curly hair will not make it straight. However, there are certain cutting techniques that can be used to make your curls more manageable and appear less curly. In this article, we will explore those techniques and provide you with tips on how to care for your curly hair after a cut.

How to Cut Curly Hair

When cutting curly hair, it is important to keep in mind the shape of the curls. Curly hair can have a tendency to be bulky, so it is important to remove weight from the hair to create a more defined shape. This can be achieved through layering and thinning techniques.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning the hair
  2. Comb the hair while it is still wet
  3. Section the hair into four parts
  4. Begin cutting one section at a time
  5. Use thinning shears to remove bulk from the hair
  6. Create layers by cutting the hair at an angle
  7. Once all sections are cut, style the hair as desired

It is important to note that cutting curly hair is best left to a professional stylist. They will have the knowledge and expertise to create a shape that complements your curls.

Tips for Caring for Curly Hair After a Cut

Once you have had your curly hair cut, it is important to care for it properly to maintain the shape and health of your curls. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair after a cut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting curly hair:

Will cutting curly hair make it grow faster?

No, cutting curly hair will not make it grow faster. Hair growth is determined by genetics and overall health.

How often should curly hair be cut?

Curly hair should be cut every 8-12 weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Can I cut my own curly hair?

It is not recommended to cut your own curly hair. It is best to visit a professional stylist who specializes in cutting curly hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair can be challenging, but with the right products and techniques, it can be a breeze. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls

In conclusion, cutting curly hair will not make it straight, but it can make it more manageable and appear less curly. It is important to seek the help of a professional stylist who specializes in cutting curly hair to achieve the best results. With the right care and products, your curly hair can look its best.

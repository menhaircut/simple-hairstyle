How To See Which Hairstyle Suits Your Face

Choosing a hairstyle that suits your face shape can be challenging, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your face in no time.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a hairstyle that suits your face is determining your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Here's how to determine your face shape:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, your face will be longer than it is wide, with a rounded hairline and a gently curved jawline. This face shape is considered the most versatile, as most hairstyles will suit an oval face shape.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, your face will be as wide as it is long, with a rounded hairline and a circular jawline. Hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, such as a high ponytail, will help elongate your face.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, your face will be as wide as it is long, with a strong, square jawline and a straight hairline. Softening your jawline with layers or side-swept bangs can help balance your face shape.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, your face will be wider at the temples and narrow at the jawline, with a pointed chin. Soft, layered hairstyles that frame your face, such as beachy waves or a bob, will complement your face shape.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, your face will be widest at the cheekbones and narrow at the forehead and jawline. Short hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a pixie cut, will balance your face shape.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, your face will be longer than it is wide, with a straight hairline and a gently curved jawline. Hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as a bob with side-swept bangs, will help balance your face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide for Choosing a Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle that suits you. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Consider your hair texture and thickness. Some hairstyles work better with certain hair types than others.
  2. Think about your lifestyle. Choose a hairstyle that is easy to maintain and fits your daily routine.
  3. Look for inspiration. Browse magazines or online for hairstyles that catch your eye.
  4. Consult a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.
  5. Try it out. If you're unsure about a new hairstyle, try it out with a wig or a temporary hairstyle app before making the cut.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

  • Consider your skin tone. Certain hair colors will complement your skin tone better than others.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment. If you're feeling bold, try a completely different hairstyle than what you're used to.
  • Accessorize. Adding accessories such as headbands or hair clips can elevate your hairstyle.
  • Take care of your hair. Regular haircuts and a good hair care routine will keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

FAQs

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

It's common to have a combination of face shapes. Focus on the dominant face shape and choose a hairstyle that complements it.

What if I don't like any hairstyles that suit my face shape?

Ultimately, the choice is yours. Choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've chosen the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use hair masks and oils to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

Choosing a hairstyle that suits your face shape can be daunting, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for you. Remember to experiment and have fun with your hair!

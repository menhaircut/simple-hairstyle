How To Cut Long Hair Straight Across The Back

Written by Aprilia Jul 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have long hair, it can be challenging to maintain its length and keep it looking healthy. One way to do this is by cutting it straight across the back, which can give it a neat and tidy appearance. In this article, we will show you how to cut long hair straight across the back in relaxed English.

Description

Cutting long hair straight across the back may seem like a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. This method is suitable for those who want to maintain their hair's length while keeping it healthy and looking neat.

How To Cut Long Hair Straight Across the Back:

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also best to cut your hair when it's dry, so you can see how it will look once it's cut.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. This will help you see its natural texture and make it easier to cut. You can use a blow dryer or let your hair air dry.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a brush to detangle it. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into two equal sections, one on the left and one on the right. Use a hair tie or clip to secure each section in place.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Take one section of your hair and hold it straight in front of you. Use your scissors to cut it straight across, starting at the middle of your back and working your way down to the ends. Repeat this step with the other section of your hair.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

After you have cut both sections of your hair, check for evenness. If necessary, make small adjustments to ensure that your hair is straight and even across the back.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut your long hair straight across the back:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Cut your hair in a well-lit area, so you can see what you're doing.
  • Use a comb to measure the length of your hair before cutting it.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair straight across the back if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your hair straight across the back if it's curly. However, keep in mind that curly hair may shrink up when it dries, so it's best to cut it when it's dry.

How often should I cut my hair straight across the back?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want to maintain its length. You can cut it every 2-3 months or as needed.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always make small adjustments and trim any uneven ends. If you're unsure, it's best to seek the help of a professional hairdresser.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and health.

By following these tips and techniques, you can cut your long hair straight across the back and maintain its length and health. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and seek professional help if needed. Happy cutting!

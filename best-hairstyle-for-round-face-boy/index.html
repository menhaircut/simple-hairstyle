Best Hairstyle For Round Face Boy

Written by Petter Aug 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a boy with a round face? Do you struggle to find the best hairstyle that suits your face shape? Look no further, as we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyle for round face boy. We will guide you step by step, provide tips, and answer frequently asked questions. So, let's get started.

Description

Round face is a common face shape for boys. It is characterized by a circular shape with a similar length and width. The best hairstyle for round face boy is one that adds angles and height to the face, creating an illusion of a more elongated face. The right hairstyle can also highlight your best features and complement your personality.

How to

There are various hairstyles that you can try if you have a round face. Here are some of the best hairstyles for round face boy:

1. Short sides and long top

This hairstyle involves trimming the sides and back of your head while leaving the top longer. It adds height to your face and creates angles, making your face appear longer. You can style the top as you like, whether it's a messy look or a slicked-back style.

2. Side part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that suits all face shapes, including round faces. It involves creating a part on one side of your head and combing the hair to the opposite side. It adds angles to your face and gives a clean and polished look.

3. Fringe

A fringe is a great way to add angles to your face. It involves cutting your hair in a way that the front hair falls on your forehead. You can style the fringe as you like, whether it's a messy look or a straight cut.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for styling the best hairstyle for round face boy:

1. Short sides and long top

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Use a trimmer to trim the sides and back of your head.
  3. Leave the top longer and style it using a comb or your fingers.
  4. Apply a styling product if desired.

2. Side part

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Create a part on one side of your head using a comb.
  3. Comb your hair to the opposite side.
  4. Apply a styling product if desired.

3. Fringe

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Cut your hair in a way that the front hair falls on your forehead.
  3. Style the fringe as you like, whether it's a messy look or a straight cut.
  4. Apply a styling product if desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling the best hairstyle for round face boy:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds angles and height to your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face look rounder, such as a full fringe or a buzz cut.
  • Experiment with different styles and find the one that suits your personality.
  • Use a styling product to keep your hair in place and add texture.
  • Visit a hairstylist regularly to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for round face boy:

1. Can I have a buzz cut if I have a round face?

A buzz cut can make your face look rounder, so it's not the best hairstyle for round face boy. Instead, try a short sides and long top or a fringe to add angles to your face.

2. Can I have a full fringe if I have a round face?

A full fringe can make your face look rounder, so it's not the best hairstyle for round face boy. Instead, try a side part or a fringe that falls to the side to add angles to your face.

3. How often should I visit a hairstylist to maintain my hairstyle?

You should visit a hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and stylish look. Here are some hair care tips for boys with a round face:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair smooth and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as it can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as a flat iron or a curling iron, as it can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and health.

Now that you know the best hairstyle for round face boy, it's time to experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and stylish look. Good luck!

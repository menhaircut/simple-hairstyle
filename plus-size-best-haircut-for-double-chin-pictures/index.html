Plus Size Best Haircut For Double Chin Pictures How To Get The Perfect Look

Written by Eveline Nov 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect haircut to flatter your plus size figure and hide your double chin? Look no further. In this article, we will provide you with all the tips and tricks to get the best hairstyle for your face shape and size. Plus, we will show you some pictures of the best haircuts for double chin to inspire your next salon visit. Let's get started!

Description: Understanding Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the different haircuts that work best for double chin, it's important to understand your face shape. Plus size women come in all shapes and sizes, and the right haircut can make or break your look.

The most common face shapes are round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. For a round face shape, it's best to have a haircut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a more angular jawline. For an oval face shape, you can opt for a variety of haircuts, but it's best to avoid styles that add width to the face. For a square face shape, it's best to soften the angles with a layered haircut. Finally, for a heart-shaped face, it's best to avoid haircuts that add volume to the top of the head and instead focus on creating balance between the forehead and chin.

How to: Choosing the Right Haircut

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the right haircut for your double chin. The key is to create volume and movement around the face while drawing attention away from the jawline. Here are some of the best haircuts for double chin:

Bob Haircut

The bob haircut is a classic and timeless look that works for women of all ages and face shapes. For plus size women with double chin, a chin-length bob with layers around the face can create a slimming effect. Make sure that the bob hits at the right spot on your face - not too high, not too low - to elongate your face and draw attention away from the chin.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and daring look that can work wonders for women with double chin. The short length of the haircut draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones while creating a more defined jawline. Make sure to add layers to the top of the pixie cut to create volume and texture.

Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is perfect for women with double chin because it creates volume and movement around the face while drawing attention away from the chin. The layers should be around the face, not at the back of the head, to create a slimming effect. Make sure to ask your hairstylist to create long layers that frame your face.

Step-by-Step: Getting the Perfect Haircut

Now that you know the best haircuts for double chin, it's time to get the perfect haircut for your face shape and size. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Find Inspiration

Start by looking at the pictures of haircuts for double chin to find inspiration. You can find pictures online or in magazines. Make sure to choose a haircut that suits your face shape and size.

Step 2: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's time to consult with your hairstylist. Make sure to bring pictures of the haircut you want to show your hairstylist. They will be able to tell you if the haircut will work for your face shape and size.

Step 3: Trust Your Hairstylist

Your hairstylist is a professional who has years of experience in cutting hair. Trust their judgment and let them make small adjustments to the haircut to make it work for your face shape and size.

Tips: Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you have the perfect haircut, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Trim Your Hair Regularly

To maintain the shape of your haircut, it's important to trim your hair regularly. Make sure to schedule appointments with your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks.

Tip 2: Use the Right Hair Products

Use hair products that are designed for your hair type and texture. This will help to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often. This can cause damage and breakage. If you must use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

FAQ: Answering Your Questions

Question 1: Can I still have long hair if I have double chin?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have double chin. The key is to add layers around the face to create movement and volume. Make sure to avoid haircuts that add volume to the back of the head.

Question 2: Will bangs work for me if I have double chin?

Yes, bangs can work for women with double chin. The key is to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. Side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs can create a slimming effect.

Question 3: What hair color is best for women with double chin?

There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. The best hair color for women with double chin depends on your skin tone and personal style. Consult with your hairstylist to find the right hair color for you.

Hair Care: Taking Care of Your Hair

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and beautiful look. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.

Tip 2: Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair and keep it healthy and shiny.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

Conclusion

Getting the perfect haircut for double chin can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve the look you want. Remember to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and size, consult with your hairstylist, and maintain your haircut with the right hair products and care. With these tips, you'll be turning heads with your new haircut in no time!

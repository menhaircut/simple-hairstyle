How To Take Care Of Chemically Smoothened Hair

Written by Oliver Sep 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Chemical hair smoothening is a popular technique used to straighten and soften the hair. It involves the use of chemicals to break the bonds in the hair, making it more manageable and easy to style. However, chemically smoothened hair requires special care and attention to keep it healthy and prevent damage. In this article, we will discuss how to take care of chemically smoothened hair.

Description:

Chemically smoothened hair is hair that has been treated with a chemical process to make it straighter, shinier, and more manageable. The process involves the use of chemicals that break down the hair's natural bonds, making it easier to style. The result is hair that is straighter, smoother, and generally more manageable.

How to Take Care of Chemically Smoothened Hair:

Proper hair care is essential to maintain chemically smoothened hair. Here are some tips:

  1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair. Sulfates can strip the hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and damage.

  2. Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and blow dryers as much as possible. These tools can cause damage to chemically treated hair.

  3. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it is wet. This is gentler on the hair and helps prevent breakage.

  4. Avoid using hair dyes and chemicals on your hair, as they can cause damage and breakage.

  5. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

  6. Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine and salt water, as they can cause dryness and damage.

  7. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Here is a step-by-step guide to taking care of chemically smoothened hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

  2. Gently towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner.

  3. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.

  4. Allow your hair to air dry as much as possible.

  5. Avoid using heat styling tools, but if you must, use a heat protectant spray.

  6. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

  7. Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine and salt water, or wear a swim cap.

  8. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Tips for Maintaining Chemically Smoothened Hair:

Here are some additional tips for maintaining chemically smoothened hair:

  1. Avoid using tight hair ties or elastic bands, as they can cause breakage and damage.

  2. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf.

  3. Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause breakage and damage.

  4. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage.

  5. Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can cause dryness and damage.

FAQs:

Here are some frequently asked questions about taking care of chemically smoothened hair:

  • Q: Can I color my hair after getting it chemically smoothened?
    A: It is best to wait at least two weeks after getting your hair chemically smoothened before coloring it.

  • Q: How often should I wash my chemically smoothened hair?
    A: It is best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

  • Q: Can I go swimming with chemically smoothened hair?
    A: It is best to avoid chlorine and salt water, but if you must swim, wear a swim cap and rinse your hair with clean water afterwards.

Hair Care Products for Chemically Smoothened Hair:

Here are some recommended hair care products for chemically smoothened hair:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

  • Leave-in conditioner

  • Deep conditioning treatment

  • Heat protectant spray

  • Silk or satin pillowcase

By following these tips and using the right products, you can keep your chemically smoothened hair looking healthy and beautiful.

What Haircut Suits Fat Faces A Guide For 2023

Written by Bardi Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, it can be challenging to find the right haircut that suits your features. The goal is to create balance and proportion to enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we'll discuss various hairstyles that flatter fat faces, along with tips and tricks to maintain healthy hair. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Choose the Right Haircut for Fat Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Description

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to understand what a fat face looks like. A fat face is typically round with fuller cheeks, and the jawline is less defined. The goal is to create angles and length to slim down the face.

How To Choose the Right Haircut for Fat Faces

The best hairstyles for fat faces are those that create angles and length. Some popular styles include:

Layered Haircuts

Layered haircuts are great for creating movement and dimension. Longer layers that frame the face will help elongate the face and create angles. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin as they will make the face appear wider.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for adding length to the face. They can be blended into the layered haircut or worn as a statement piece. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs as they will make the face appear rounder.

Bob Haircuts

Bob haircuts are versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape. A longer bob that ends at the collarbone will help elongate the face. Add layers to create volume and movement. Avoid chin-length bobs as they will make the face appear wider.

Long Hairstyles

Long hairstyles are perfect for creating length and slimming down the face. Layers that frame the face will help create angles. Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous as they will add width to the face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you know what haircut suits fat faces, let's talk about how to style your hair. Follow these steps for a salon-worthy look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair in sections, starting at the roots and pulling the brush down to the ends.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to create your desired style.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Healthy hair is essential for any hairstyle to look its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for fat faces:

Q: Can I still wear short hair with a fat face?

A: Yes! Just make sure to choose a style that creates angles and length. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a fat face?

A: No, but make sure to choose side-swept bangs that add length to the face.

Q: How do I know if a hairstyle suits my face shape?

A: Consult with a hairstylist. They can recommend styles that complement your features.

Hair Care Products for Fat Faces

Here are some recommended hair care products for fat faces:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Light hold hairspray

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut that flatters your fat face. Remember to maintain healthy hair with the right products and styling tools. With a little effort, you can look and feel your best every day.

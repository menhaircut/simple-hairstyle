How To Cut Long Hair Boy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a parent or a hair stylist who wants to learn how to cut long hair boy? Cutting a boy's hair can be a challenging task, especially if you're not familiar with the proper techniques. In this post, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut long hair boy. We'll also share some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to ensure you achieve the best results.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Long Hair Boy
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cut Long Hair Boy

Before you start cutting a boy's hair, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and clips to hold the hair in place.

Start by washing the boy's hair and towel drying it. Comb the hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Decide on the Haircut

Before you start cutting, decide on the type of haircut you want to give the boy. Do you want a buzz cut or a layered cut? A buzz cut is easier to do but a layered cut will require more time and effort.

Section the Hair

Use clips to section the hair. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward. Use a comb to part the hair and clip the top section out of the way.

Cut the Hair

Take a small section of hair and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it in a straight line. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way around the head. When you're done with one section, unclip the next section and repeat the process.

Trim the Bangs

If the boy has bangs, trim them separately. Use the comb to part the hair and cut it in a straight line.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut long hair boy:

  1. Wash the boy's hair and towel dry it.
  2. Comb the hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  3. Decide on the type of haircut you want to give the boy.
  4. Use clips to section the hair.
  5. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight.
  6. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it in a straight line.
  7. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way around the head.
  8. Unclip the next section and repeat the process.
  9. Trim the bangs separately.
  10. Style the hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Long Hair Boy

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting long hair boy:

  • Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts.
  • Comb the hair regularly to ensure you cut it evenly.
  • Cut small sections of hair at a time to avoid making mistakes.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or if the boy becomes restless.
  • Watch tutorials or seek advice from experienced hair stylists.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long hair boy:

How often should I cut a boy's hair?

It depends on the type of haircut and how fast the boy's hair grows. A buzz cut may need to be trimmed every few weeks, while a layered cut may only need to be cut every few months.

Can I cut a boy's hair when it's wet?

Yes, you can cut a boy's hair when it's wet. Wet hair is easier to handle and cut evenly.

How do I clean the scissors after cutting hair?

Wipe the scissors with a clean cloth or tissue to remove any hair or debris. You can also use rubbing alcohol to disinfect the scissors.

Hair Care for Long Hair Boy

After cutting a boy's hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep the hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on the hair.
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect the hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a hair serum.

Cutting long hair boy may seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve great results. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips, and don't be afraid to seek advice from experienced hair stylists. With a little practice, you'll be able to cut long hair boy like a pro!

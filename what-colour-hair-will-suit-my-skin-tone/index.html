What Colour Hair Will Suit My Skin Tone

Written by Christine Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, especially when you're not sure which shade will suit your skin tone. Your skin tone plays a crucial role in determining which hair colour will look best on you. In this article, we will discuss what colour hair will suit your skin tone and provide you with tips on how to choose the perfect shade for your hair.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hair Colour
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hair Colour

Description

The first step in choosing the perfect hair colour for your skin tone is to determine your skin's undertone. Undertones are the subtle hues that lie beneath the surface of your skin. They can be warm, cool, or neutral. Once you have identified your undertone, you can choose a hair colour that will complement it.

If you have a warm undertone, you will look best in hair colours with warm tones, such as golden blonde or auburn. If you have a cool undertone, you will look best in hair colours with cool tones, such as ash blonde or cool brown. If you have a neutral undertone, you can pull off both warm and cool tones.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour

To determine your undertone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool undertone. If they appear green, you have a warm undertone. If you can't tell whether they are blue or green, you have a neutral undertone.

Another way to determine your undertone is to look at the jewellery you wear. If you look best in silver jewellery, you have a cool undertone. If you look best in gold jewellery, you have a warm undertone. If you look good in both, you have a neutral undertone.

Warm Undertones

If you have a warm undertone, you will look best in hair colours with warm tones. Some good options include:

  • Golden blonde
  • Red
  • Auburn
  • Caramel
  • Honey blonde

Cool Undertones

If you have a cool undertone, you will look best in hair colours with cool tones. Some good options include:

  • Ash blonde
  • Platinum blonde
  • Cool brown
  • Jet black
  • Blue-black

Neutral Undertones

If you have a neutral undertone, you can pull off both warm and cool tones. Some good options include:

  • Caramel
  • Champagne blonde
  • Warm brown
  • Cool brown
  • Black

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the perfect hair colour for your skin tone:

  1. Determine your skin's undertone.
  2. Choose a hair colour with tones that complement your undertone.
  3. Talk to your stylist about the shade you want and ask for their opinion.
  4. Consider the maintenance required for the hair colour you choose.
  5. Take into account your natural hair colour and how much of a change you want.
  6. Consider the season and how the hair colour will look with your wardrobe.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect hair colour for your skin tone:

  • Consider your eye colour. Certain hair colours can make your eyes pop.
  • Look at pictures of celebrities with similar skin tones to yours and see what hair colours they have tried.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to get an idea of how a hair colour will look on you.
  • Have a consultation with your stylist before making a decision.

FAQs

What hair colours should I avoid if I have a warm undertone?

If you have a warm undertone, you should avoid hair colours with cool tones, such as platinum blonde or blue-black. These colours can make your skin look sallow.

What hair colours should I avoid if I have a cool undertone?

If you have a cool undertone, you should avoid hair colours with warm tones, such as golden blonde or auburn. These colours can make your skin look ruddy.

Can I dye my hair a completely different colour than my natural hair?

Yes, you can dye your hair a completely different colour than your natural hair. However, keep in mind that drastic changes can be damaging to your hair and require more maintenance.

Hair Care Tips

After you have dyed your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to preserve your hair colour.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the colour.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair and fade the colour.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Choosing the perfect hair colour for your skin tone can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips and guidelines, you can find a shade that will make you look and feel your best.

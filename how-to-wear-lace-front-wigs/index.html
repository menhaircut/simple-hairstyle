How To Wear Lace Front Wigs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Dec 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're new to wearing wigs or just need a refresher on how to wear lace front wigs, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of putting on and styling your lace front wig for a natural and flawless look.

What is a Lace Front Wig?

A lace front wig is a type of wig that has a sheer lace panel along the front hairline. The lace allows for a natural-looking hairline and can be customized to match your skin tone.

How to Wear Lace Front Wigs

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before putting on your lace front wig, make sure your hair is clean and flat. If you have long hair, braid it or put it in a low bun to minimize bulkiness. If you have short hair, use a wig cap to keep your hair in place.

Step 2: Adjust the Cap

Place the wig cap over your head and adjust it so that it fits snugly. If your wig cap has adjustable straps, make sure to tighten or loosen them as needed.

Step 3: Cut the Lace

Carefully cut the lace along the front hairline with a pair of scissors. Be sure to leave a small amount of lace to blend with your skin.

Step 4: Apply Adhesive

Apply wig adhesive or tape along the hairline and press the lace into place. Use a blow dryer on cool setting to help the adhesive dry faster.

Step 5: Style Your Wig

Once your wig is securely in place, you can style it as desired. Use a wig brush or comb to gently detangle the hair and create your desired look.

Tips for Wearing Lace Front Wigs

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your lace front wig:

  • Use a wig cap to keep your natural hair in place
  • Don't use too much adhesive or tape
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on high heat
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to maintain its shape
  • Wash your wig regularly to keep it clean and fresh

FAQ

How long can I wear a lace front wig?

You can wear a lace front wig for up to six weeks at a time, but it's important to give your scalp a break in between wears.

Can I swim or shower with a lace front wig?

It's not recommended to swim or shower with a lace front wig, as water can damage the wig and loosen the adhesive.

Hair Care

To keep your lace front wig looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining your wig:

  • Use a wig shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair soft and shiny
  • Avoid using hot water or high heat when washing your wig
  • Hang your wig to dry on a wig stand
  • Store your wig in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear your lace front wig with confidence and style.

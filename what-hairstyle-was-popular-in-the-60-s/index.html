What Hairstyle Was Popular In The 60 S

Written by Frank Sep 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

The 1960s was a decade of change and revolution, and this was evident in the hairstyles that were popular during the time. From the beehive to the bouffant, the 60s was a time of experimentation and creativity when it came to hair. In this article, we will take a closer look at what hairstyle was popular in the 60's and how you can recreate it today.

Description

The 1960s was a decade that saw a lot of changes in fashion, music, and culture. It was a time when people were exploring new ideas and expressing themselves in different ways. One of the ways that people expressed themselves was through their hairstyles. The 60s saw a lot of experimentation with hair, from the short and sleek to the long and flowing.

How to

If you want to recreate the 60s hairstyle today, there are a few things that you can do. First, you will need to decide on the type of hairstyle that you want. Do you want a beehive or a bouffant? Once you have decided on the type of hairstyle that you want, you can start working on the details.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to create a beehive hairstyle:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into three parts: the crown, the sides, and the back.
  2. Tease the crown section of your hair to create volume.
  3. Smooth the crown section over the teased hair and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Take the sides of your hair and bring them up to meet the crown section, securing them with bobby pins.
  5. Smooth the back section of your hair up and over the sides and crown, securing it with bobby pins.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

If you want to create a bouffant hairstyle, follow these steps:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into three parts: the crown, the sides, and the back.
  2. Tease the crown section of your hair to create volume.
  3. Smooth the crown section over the teased hair and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Take the sides of your hair and pull them back, securing them with bobby pins.
  5. Take the back section of your hair and tease it to create volume.
  6. Smooth the teased hair back over the sides and crown, securing it with bobby pins.
  7. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips that you should keep in mind when creating a 60s hairstyle:

  • Use a rat tail comb to tease your hair and create volume.
  • Use bobby pins that match the color of your hair to keep the style in place.
  • Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.
  • Experiment with different types of accessories, such as headbands and scarves, to complete your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about 60s hairstyles:

What was the most popular hairstyle in the 60s?

The beehive and the bouffant were two of the most popular hairstyles in the 60s.

What products were used to create 60s hairstyles?

Hairspray and backcombing were two of the most common techniques used to create 60s hairstyles.

Can you wear a 60s hairstyle today?

Absolutely! 60s hairstyles are still popular today, and many people enjoy recreating them for special occasions or just for fun.

Hair Care

When it comes to creating 60s hairstyles, it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know what hairstyle was popular in the 60's, you can experiment with different styles and create your own unique look. Whether you opt for a beehive or a bouffant, these hairstyles are sure to turn heads and make a statement.

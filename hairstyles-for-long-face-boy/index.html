How to Choose the Perfect Hairstyle for a Long Face Boy

Choosing the right hairstyle for a long face boy can be a bit tricky, as you want to avoid styles that elongate the face even further. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add width to the face, such as those with layers, volume, or curls.
  • Avoid styles that are too slicked back or straight, as they can make the face appear longer.
  • Consider getting a fringe or side-swept bangs to break up the length of the face.
  • Try out different styles with your hairdresser to see what works best for your face shape.

Best Hairstyles for Long Face Boys:

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, here are some of the best haircuts for long face boys:

  • The classic crew cut
  • A messy textured look
  • A layered shaggy cut
  • A side-swept fringe
  • A short crop with a textured fringe
  • A medium-length cut with layers and volume

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Face Haircuts

Once you've chosen the perfect haircut for your long face, here's how to style it:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. If you have a fringe or side-swept bangs, use a flat iron to style them.
  5. Apply a styling product, such as a pomade, wax, or gel, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  6. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into the desired shape.
  7. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Long Face Hairstyles

Here are some tips to keep your long face hairstyle looking great:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray when styling.
  • Try out different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.

Frequently Asked Questions About Long Face Hairstyles

Can I still get a short haircut if I have a long face?

Absolutely! Just make sure to choose a style that adds width to your face, such as a textured crop or a layered cut.

What if I have curly hair?

Curls can actually be great for adding width to a long face. Consider a layered cut that enhances your natural curl pattern.

Should I avoid bangs altogether if I have a long face?

No, you don't have to avoid bangs completely. Just make sure to choose a style that is side-swept or layered to break up the length of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Long Face Boys

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your long face hairstyle looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, and always use a heat protectant spray when styling.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

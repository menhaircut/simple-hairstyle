What To Do When Your Dress Is Too Short

Written by Frank Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever put on a dress that you love, only to realize that it's too short? It can be frustrating and uncomfortable, especially if you're going to a formal event. But don't worry, there are several things you can do to make your dress more comfortable and appropriate for the occasion. In this article, we'll share some tips on what to do when your dress is too short.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Lengthen a Short Dress

There are several ways to lengthen a short dress:

1. Add a Slip

If your dress is just a little too short, you can add a slip underneath. This will add some length and prevent your dress from riding up. Choose a slip that matches the color of your dress and is made of a lightweight fabric, such as silk or chiffon.

2. Wear Leggings or Tights

If your dress is too short to wear on its own, you can pair it with leggings or tights. This will add some coverage and make your dress more appropriate for the occasion. Choose a pair of leggings or tights that match the color of your dress and are made of a thick, opaque material.

3. Add a Hem

If your dress is too short and you have some sewing skills, you can add a hem to lengthen it. Choose a fabric that matches the color and texture of your dress, and sew it onto the bottom of your dress. This will add some length and make your dress more comfortable to wear.

Step by Step Guide

If you're adding a hem to your dress, here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Measure the Length of Your Dress

Use a measuring tape to measure the length of your dress. Decide how much additional length you want to add, and add an inch or two to account for the hem.

Step 2: Choose a Fabric

Choose a fabric that matches the color and texture of your dress. Make sure the fabric is lightweight and comfortable to wear.

Step 3: Cut the Fabric

Cut the fabric to the desired length, making sure to add an inch or two for the hem.

Step 4: Sew the Fabric to Your Dress

Sew the fabric onto the bottom of your dress, making sure to fold the fabric under to create a clean hem. Use a sewing machine or hand stitch the fabric onto your dress.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when your dress is too short:

  • Choose shoes that complement your dress and make you feel comfortable.
  • Accessorize with jewelry, a scarf, or a belt to add some interest to your outfit.
  • If you're wearing a short dress to a formal event, make sure it's appropriate for the occasion.

FAQ

Q: What if I don't have any sewing skills?

A: If you don't know how to sew, you can take your dress to a tailor or seamstress. They can add a hem or make other alterations to make your dress more comfortable and appropriate for the occasion.

Q: Can I wear a short dress to a wedding?

A: It depends on the dress code and the formality of the wedding. If the wedding is black tie or formal, a short dress may not be appropriate. However, if the wedding is more casual, a short dress can be a great choice.

Hair Care

When wearing a short dress, it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your outfit. Here are some hairstyles to consider:

  • A low bun or chignon
  • Loose waves or curls
  • A sleek ponytail

Choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

When your dress is too short, it can be tempting to give up and choose a different outfit. But with these tips, you can make your dress more comfortable and appropriate for the occasion. Whether you're adding a slip, wearing leggings, or adding a hem, you can make your short dress work for you. Don't be afraid to experiment and find a solution that works best for you.

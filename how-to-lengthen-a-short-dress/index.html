How To Lengthen A Short Dress Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Oct 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you've ever bought a cute dress, only to realize that it's too short for your liking, you're not alone. Short dresses can be fun and flirty, but they're not always practical or appropriate for every occasion. Luckily, there are several ways to lengthen a short dress and make it more comfortable and versatile. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to do it.

How to Lengthen a Short Dress

There are different methods you can use to lengthen a short dress, depending on your preferences and skills. Here are some of the most common ways:

1. Add a Hem

If your dress has a straight or slightly flared hemline, you can add a hem to it to increase its length. You'll need a sewing machine, thread, and fabric that matches your dress. Cut the fabric into a strip that's slightly wider than the hemline and long enough to cover the desired length. Fold the strip in half lengthwise, with the wrong side facing out, and sew it to the bottom of the dress, aligning the raw edges. Turn the dress right side out and iron the hem flat.

2. Layer with Leggings or Tights

If you don't want to alter your dress permanently, you can wear leggings or tights underneath it to cover your legs and add some length. Choose a color that complements your dress and the occasion. For example, black leggings or tights can make a short dress look more formal, while patterned or colorful ones can make it look more playful.

3. Wear Over Jeans or Pants

If you want to make a short dress look casual or edgy, you can wear it over skinny jeans or pants. This will not only add length but also create a layered and textured look. Choose jeans or pants that fit well and complement your dress in terms of color and style. You can also add a belt to cinch the waist and define your silhouette.

4. Attach a Ruffle or Lace

If you want to add some feminine or romantic flair to your dress, you can attach a ruffle or lace to the hemline or neckline. This will not only lengthen the dress but also create a pretty and unique detail. You can use fabric glue, sewing machine, or hand sewing to attach the ruffle or lace. Choose a color and texture that complement your dress and your personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to lengthen a short dress using the hemming method:

Step 1: Measure the Length

Put on the dress and measure how much longer you want it to be. Add an inch or two to the measurement to account for the hem allowance.

Step 2: Cut the Fabric

Cut a strip of fabric that's slightly wider than the hemline and long enough to cover the desired length. Fold the strip in half lengthwise, with the wrong side facing out.

Step 3: Pin the Fabric

Pin the fabric to the bottom of the dress, aligning the raw edges. Make sure the fabric is evenly distributed and there are no wrinkles or bumps.

Step 4: Sew the Fabric

Use a sewing machine or hand sewing to sew the fabric to the dress, using a straight stitch or a zigzag stitch. Backstitch at the beginning and end to secure the thread.

Step 5: Trim the Excess Fabric

Trim the excess fabric to about Â¼ inch from the seam. Fold the hem down and iron it flat.

Tips for Lengthening a Short Dress

Here are some tips to keep in mind when lengthening a short dress:

  • Choose a fabric that matches your dress in terms of weight and texture.
  • Use sharp scissors and a good-quality thread to ensure a clean and durable hem.
  • Try on the dress with the added length before cutting the excess fabric to avoid any mistakes.
  • Experiment with different layering options, such as jackets, cardigans, or scarves, to create a new look.

FAQ

Can I lengthen a short dress without sewing?

Yes, you can use fabric glue, iron-on hemming tape, or safety pins to attach a strip of fabric to the hemline. However, these methods may not be as durable or precise as sewing.

How much should I add to the length of my dress?

It depends on your personal preference and the occasion. Generally, adding 1-2 inches to the length is a safe bet. However, if you're wearing the dress to a formal event or you want a more dramatic look, you can add more length.

Hair Care Tips

When wearing a short dress, it's important to pay attention to your hair as well. Here are some hair care tips to complement your look:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your dress and your face shape. For example, if you're wearing a strapless dress, you can opt for an updo to show off your shoulders and neck.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and body to your hair.
  • Accessorize with a headband, a hair clip, or a scarf to add some color and interest to your hairstyle.

Lengthening a short dress can be a fun and creative way to update your wardrobe and make the most of your clothes. Whether you choose to hem it, layer it, or decorate it, the possibilities are endless. Just remember to choose a method that suits your style and skill level, and have fun experimenting!

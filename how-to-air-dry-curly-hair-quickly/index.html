How To Air Dry Curly Hair Quickly Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jun 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending hours trying to dry your curly hair? Air drying can be a great option, but it can take forever if you don’t know the right techniques. In this post, we’ll share some tips and tricks to help you air dry your curly hair quickly.

Table of Contents

  • How to Air Dry Curly Hair Quickly
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Air Dry Curly Hair Quickly

The first step to air drying your curly hair quickly is to start with freshly washed hair. This will help your curls dry faster and prevent frizz. After washing your hair, gently squeeze out the excess water with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt. Avoid rubbing your hair with a regular towel, as this can cause frizz and damage.

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner or a curl cream to your hair to help define your curls and keep them moisturized. Be sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair. Then, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. This will help your curls dry evenly and prevent tangling.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to air dry your curly hair quickly:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair.
  2. Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out the excess water.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair.
  4. Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  5. Flip your hair over and scrunch it with your hands to encourage curl formation.
  6. Flip your hair back and let it air dry naturally.
  7. Once your hair is about 80% dry, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the drying process.
  8. Hold the diffuser about 6 inches away from your hair and use a low heat setting.
  9. Gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser until it is completely dry.
  10. Finish with a light hold hairspray or hair oil to add shine and tame frizz.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you air dry your curly hair quickly:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much while it’s drying, as this can cause frizz.
  • Try plopping your hair in a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt for 10-15 minutes to speed up the drying process.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the drying process if you’re in a hurry.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them take longer to dry.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and prevent breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about air drying curly hair:

Q: Can I air dry my curly hair overnight?

A: Yes, you can air dry your curly hair overnight, but be sure to protect your hair with a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

Q: How long does it take for curly hair to air dry?

A: It can take anywhere from 2-4 hours for curly hair to air dry, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Q: Can I use a blow dryer on my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can use a blow dryer on your curly hair, but be sure to use a diffuser attachment and a low heat setting to avoid causing damage and frizz.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to air drying your curly hair quickly, it’s important to take good care of your curls to keep them healthy and looking their best. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than once or twice a week to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can air dry your curly hair quickly and keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful.

Asian Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish hairstyle that can make you stand out from the crowd? If yes, then you must try the Asian hairstyle short sides long top. This hairstyle has become quite popular in recent years and is loved by people of all ages. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, tips, and hair care.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

The Asian hairstyle short sides long top is a modern and edgy haircut that features short buzzed sides and a longer top. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want to add some personality and style to their overall look. The long top can be styled in many different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or spiky, depending on your personal preference.

How to

If you want to get the Asian hairstyle short sides long top, you can follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose a Good Barber

It is essential to choose a good barber who has experience in cutting this type of hairstyle. You can ask for recommendations from friends or family members or read online reviews to find a reputable barber in your area.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

You need to decide on the length of your hair for the top and sides. Generally, the sides are buzzed or faded with clippers, and the top is left long enough to style. You can show your barber a picture of the style you want or ask for their suggestions based on your face shape and hair texture.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have the right haircut, you can style your hair in different ways using hair products such as pomade, wax, or gel. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get the Asian hairstyle short sides long top:

  1. Choose a good barber
  2. Decide on the length
  3. Clean the hair and scalp
  4. Cut the sides with clippers
  5. Cut the top with scissors
  6. Blend the sides and top together
  7. Style your hair with products

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain your Asian hairstyle short sides long top:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy
  • Apply hair products sparingly to avoid making your hair look greasy or heavy
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the shape and length of your hairstyle
  • Experiment with different styles to keep your look fresh and trendy

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the Asian hairstyle short sides long top:

Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit different face shapes by adjusting the length and style of the top.

What hair products should I use to style my hair?

You can use pomade, wax, or gel to style your hair, depending on your preferred look and hair type.

How often should I visit my barber?

You should visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hairstyle.

Hair Care

To keep your Asian hairstyle short sides long top looking its best, you need to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hairdryers or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with UV protection
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to provide your hair with the necessary nutrients and vitamins

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can rock the Asian hairstyle short sides long top with confidence and style.

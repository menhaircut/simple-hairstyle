What Haircut Suits Me Quiz A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Nov 03, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle but don't know where to start when it comes to choosing a new one? Look no further than the "what haircut suits me quiz"! This easy and fun quiz can help you determine the best haircut for your face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of taking the quiz and provide tips for maintaining your new 'do.

How to Take the Quiz

The "what haircut suits me quiz" is available online and can be accessed for free. Simply search for the quiz on your preferred search engine and click on the link to the quiz. The quiz will typically ask you a series of questions about your hair type, face shape, and personal style. Be sure to answer each question honestly and to the best of your ability for the most accurate results.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Search for the "what haircut suits me quiz" online.
Step 2: Click on the link to the quiz.
Step 3: Answer each question honestly and to the best of your ability.
Step 4: Receive your results and take note of the recommended haircuts.
Step 5: Book an appointment with a trusted hair stylist to discuss your results and choose the best haircut for you.
Step 6: Bring a photo of your desired haircut to your appointment to ensure clear communication with your stylist.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

Once you've chosen your new haircut, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking fresh and healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining your new 'do:

  • Invest in high-quality hair products that are suited to your hair type.
  • Visit your hair stylist regularly for trims to avoid split ends.
  • Avoid using heat-styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective hair product.
  • Consider getting a deep conditioning treatment every few months to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

What if I don't like my new haircut?

If you don't like your new haircut, don't panic! Hair grows back, and there are always ways to style your hair differently in the meantime. Speak to your stylist about any concerns you have and they may be able to make adjustments to your haircut.

Can I take the quiz multiple times?

Yes, you can take the quiz multiple times to gain a better understanding of which haircuts suit you best.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo that is suited to your hair type.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh your hair down and cause buildup.

Now that you know how to take the "what haircut suits me quiz" and how to maintain your new haircut, it's time to book that appointment and rock your new 'do with confidence!

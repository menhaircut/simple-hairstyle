How To Style Mid Length Hair Female Tips Tricks And Techniques

Written by Robby Jun 16, 2023 · 5 min read

If you’re looking for a versatile and trendy hairstyle, mid length hair is the way to go. It’s not too long and not too short, which means you can create a variety of hairstyles that work for casual and formal occasions. In this article, we’ll show you how to style mid length hair female, step by step, and offer tips and tricks to make your hair look its best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Mid Length Hair Female
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Mid Length Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

How to Style Mid Length Hair Female

Before we dive into the styling techniques, it’s important to note that mid length hair is a versatile length that can suit different face shapes, hair textures, and personal styles. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there’s a mid length hairstyle that can flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty.

The key to styling mid length hair is to experiment with different techniques and find what works for you. Here are some popular mid length hairstyles that you can try:

  • Layered haircut
  • Bob haircut
  • Shoulder-length haircut
  • Shag haircut
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Middle part
  • Ponytail
  • Braid
  • Curls
  • Straight hair

Now that you have an idea of the different mid length hairstyles, let’s move on to the step by step guide on how to style mid length hair female.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start with clean, washed hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type and texture.

2. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair before using any hot tools. This will prevent heat damage and keep your hair healthy.

3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will add volume and body to your hair, making it easier to style.

4. If you want to add curls or waves, use a curling iron or a flat iron to create loose curls or beachy waves. Alternatively, you can use a hair straightener to create sleek, straight hair.

5. Add texture and volume to your hair by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. This will give your hair a natural, tousled look.

6. If you want to create a ponytail or a braid, use a hair tie or an elastic band to secure your hair. You can also use bobby pins to create a half-up, half-down hairstyle.

7. Finish off with a hairspray or a hair oil to add shine and hold to your hair.

Tips for Styling Mid Length Hair

1. Use the right hair products for your hair type and texture. This will help you achieve the desired hairstyle and keep your hair healthy.

2. Experiment with different hairstyles and techniques to find what works for you. Don’t be afraid to try something new and step out of your comfort zone.

3. Invest in good quality hot tools, such as a curling iron, flat iron, and blow dryer. These tools will last longer and give you better results than cheap, low-quality tools.

4. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray or serum. This will prevent split ends, breakage, and dryness.

5. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks.

FAQ

Q: Can I style mid length hair if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can. In fact, mid length hair is a great length for curly hair because it’s long enough to showcase your curls but not too long that it weighs your curls down. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls, and add a curl defining cream or gel for extra hold.

Q: Can I style mid length hair if I have straight hair?

A: Yes, you can. Mid length hair is versatile and can suit different hair textures. If you have straight hair, you can add volume and body to your hair with a blow dryer and a round brush, or create loose curls with a curling iron or a flat iron.

Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

1. Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and breakage. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type and texture.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to detangle your hair when it’s wet. This will prevent breakage and split ends.

3. Apply a hair mask or a deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

4. Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and harsh weather by wearing a hat or a scarf.

5. Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day. Instead, air dry your hair or use low heat settings on your blow dryer and flat iron.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily style mid length hair female and create a variety of hairstyles that suit your personal style and occasion. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right hair products and following a regular hair care routine.

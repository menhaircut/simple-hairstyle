Hairstyles For 50 Year Old Woman With Thick Hair 2017 A Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a 50 year old woman with thick hair, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing some of the best hairstyles for 50 year old women with thick hair in 2017. So, whether you're looking for a short, medium, or long hairstyle, we've got something for you. Let's get started!

Description

As women age, their hair can start to thin out, but for those with thick hair, it can be a blessing and a curse. While thick hair can be voluminous and gorgeous, it can also be difficult to manage and style. That's why we've compiled this list of hairstyles that are perfect for 50 year old women with thick hair. These hairstyles are easy to maintain, stylish, and will make you feel confident and beautiful.

How to

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, let's talk about how to achieve them. First, you'll want to make sure your hair is healthy and well-maintained. This means getting regular trims, using a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding excessive heat styling. Once your hair is healthy, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles.

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles are perfect for women who want a low-maintenance and easy-to-style look. Here are some of our favorite short hairstyles for 50 year old women with thick hair:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob
  • Cropped layers

To style these hairstyles, simply add some texturizing spray or mousse to your hair and use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.

Medium Hairstyles

Medium hairstyles are versatile and can be worn in a variety of ways. Here are some of our favorite medium hairstyles for 50 year old women with thick hair:

  • Shoulder-length layers
  • Long bob
  • Wavy lob

To style these hairstyles, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls. You can also add some volumizing spray or mousse to give your hair some extra body and texture.

Long Hairstyles

If you have long hair, there are plenty of hairstyles that will work for you. Here are some of our favorite long hairstyles for 50 year old women with thick hair:

  • Long layers
  • Beachy waves
  • Straight and sleek

To style these hairstyles, use a straightening iron to create a sleek and polished look. Or, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or beachy curls. You can also add some shine serum or oil to give your hair a glossy finish.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving one of our favorite hairstyles for 50 year old women with thick hair:

The Long Bob

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  4. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls.
  5. Finish with a texturizing spray to give your hair some extra body and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use a texturizing spray or mousse to give your hair some extra body and texture.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for 50 year old women with thick hair:

Q: What is the best hairstyle for a 50 year old woman with thick hair?

A: The best hairstyle for a 50 year old woman with thick hair depends on her personal style and preferences. Some popular options include the pixie cut, bob, long layers, and beachy waves.

Q: How do I style my thick hair?

A: To style thick hair, use a texturizing spray or mousse to give your hair some extra body and texture. You can also use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls, or a straightening iron to create a sleek and polished look.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for keeping your thick hair healthy and strong:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to nourish your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment to give your hair some extra hydration and nourishment.

Does Platinum Hair Suit Everyone The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering going platinum blonde? This hair color has been trending for years, and many celebrities have rocked it with style. However, before you book an appointment with your stylist, you may be wondering if platinum hair suits everyone. In this guide, we will explore this question and provide you with tips and advice on how to achieve and maintain platinum hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve Platinum Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Platinum Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Platinum Hair

Description

Platinum hair is a very light shade of blonde that has a silvery-white appearance. It is achieved by bleaching the hair to remove its natural pigment and then toning it with a purple-based toner to neutralize any yellow or orange tones. Platinum hair suits people with fair skin tones and cool undertones. However, it is not suitable for everyone, and the process of achieving it can be damaging to the hair.

How to Achieve Platinum Hair

The process of achieving platinum hair involves bleaching the hair to remove its natural pigment. It is a complex process that should be done by a professional stylist. Bleaching can cause damage to the hair, so it is essential to prepare your hair before the process and take care of it after. Here are some steps to achieve platinum hair:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine if platinum hair suits you and to prepare your hair for the process.
  2. Bleach your hair to remove its natural pigment.
  3. Tone your hair with a purple-based toner to neutralize any yellow or orange tones.
  4. Deep condition your hair to restore moisture and prevent damage.
  5. Maintain your platinum hair by using purple shampoo and conditioner to prevent brassy tones.

Tips for Maintaining Platinum Hair

Maintaining platinum hair requires extra care and attention. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a purple shampoo and conditioner to prevent brassy tones.
  • Avoid heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to restore moisture.
  • Avoid swimming in chlorinated water that can cause damage and discoloration to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQs

Does platinum hair suit everyone?

No, platinum hair does not suit everyone. It looks best on people with fair skin tones and cool undertones. It can also suit people with warm undertones, but they may need to adjust their makeup and clothing to complement their hair color.

Will bleaching my hair damage it?

Yes, bleaching your hair can cause damage and breakage. It is essential to prepare your hair before the process and take care of it after by deep conditioning and avoiding heat styling tools.

Hair Care for Platinum Hair

Platinum hair requires extra care and attention to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to restore moisture.
  • Avoid heat styling tools that can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Avoid swimming in chlorinated water that can cause damage and discoloration to your hair.

In conclusion, platinum hair is a trendy and bold hair color that can suit many people. However, it is not suitable for everyone, and the process of achieving it can be damaging to the hair. Consult with a professional stylist to determine if platinum hair suits you and to prepare your hair for the process. Take care of your hair after the process by deep conditioning and avoiding heat styling tools. With proper care and attention, you can rock your platinum hair with style!

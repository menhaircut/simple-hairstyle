How To Tell If You Have An Oval Face

If you're wondering whether you have an oval face shape or not, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing the key characteristics of an oval face shape, how to determine if you have one, and the best hairstyles for oval faces.

Table of Contents:

  • Defining Oval Face Shape
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Characteristics of Oval Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Oval Faces

Defining Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is defined by having a face that is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and jawline. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and balanced of all the face shapes.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The easiest way to determine your face shape is to stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face onto the mirror using a washable marker. Alternatively, you can take a photo of yourself facing the camera directly with your hair pulled back, and then use a ruler to measure the length and width of different parts of your face.

Characteristics of Oval Face Shape

As mentioned earlier, an oval face shape is characterized by having a face that is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and jawline. In addition, people with oval faces have the following features:

  • A forehead that is slightly wider than the chin
  • Cheekbones that are high and prominent
  • A face that tapers to a narrow oval chin

Best Hairstyles for Oval Faces

People with oval faces are lucky as they can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, some of the best hairstyles for oval faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Blunt bob
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Wavy lob
  • Center-parted long hair

Tips for Oval Face Care

Here are some tips to help you take care of your oval face:

  • Wear sunscreen to protect your skin from sun damage
  • Moisturize your skin regularly to keep it hydrated
  • Exercise regularly to keep your face toned
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your skin looking healthy

FAQ

Q. Can oval face shape change over time?

A. No, your face shape is determined by your bone structure, which doesn't change over time.

Q. Is oval face shape the most common?

A. No, the most common face shape is round.

Q. Can oval face shape wear any type of glasses?

A. Yes, oval face shape can wear almost any type of glasses.

Now that you know the key characteristics of an oval face shape and how to determine if you have one, you can choose the best hairstyles and take care of your face shape accordingly. Remember to wear sunscreen, moisturize regularly, and drink plenty of water to keep your oval face looking its best!

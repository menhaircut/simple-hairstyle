Mens Medium Combed Back Hairstyles A Classic And Timeless Look

Written by Aprilia Nov 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both classic and timeless, then look no further than the mens medium combed back hairstyle. This style has been popular for decades and continues to be a go-to choice for men of all ages.

Description

The mens medium combed back hairstyle is a versatile look that can be achieved with a variety of hair types and lengths. This style is characterized by hair that is swept back away from the face and combed into place. It's a clean and polished look that's suitable for both casual and formal occasions.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the mens medium combed back hairstyle, you'll need medium-length hair that's at least three inches long on top. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then blow dry it until it's mostly dry. Use a comb to create a side part and comb your hair back away from your face. Apply a small amount of pomade or hair wax to your hands and work it through your hair, focusing on the front and sides. Use a comb to shape your hair into place, using your fingers to create volume and texture. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Wash and condition your hair
2. Blow dry your hair until it's mostly dry
3. Create a side part using a comb
4. Comb your hair back away from your face
5. Apply a small amount of pomade or hair wax to your hands
6. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the front and sides
7. Use a comb to shape your hair into place
8. Use your fingers to create volume and texture
9. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips for Maintaining the Style

To maintain your mens medium combed back hairstyle, you'll need to visit your stylist every four to six weeks for a trim. This will help keep your hair looking neat and tidy. You should also use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Finally, avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve the mens medium combed back hairstyle with short hair?

A: Unfortunately, this style requires medium-length hair that's at least three inches long on top. If your hair is too short, you won't be able to achieve the look.

Q: Is the mens medium combed back hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this style is versatile and can be adapted to suit a variety of face shapes. Talk to your stylist about the best way to adapt the style to suit your face shape.

Q: How do I choose the right pomade or hair wax?

A: Look for a product that's lightweight and provides a medium hold. You don't want anything too heavy or greasy, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Hair Care

The mens medium combed back hairstyle requires regular maintenance to keep it looking neat and tidy. Visit your stylist every four to six weeks for a trim, and use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

The mens medium combed back hairstyle is a classic and timeless look that's suitable for men of all ages. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve this versatile style and maintain it with ease.

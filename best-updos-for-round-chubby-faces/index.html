Best Updos For Round Chubby Faces A Complete Guide

If you have a round, chubby face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry - we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll share the best updos for round chubby faces that will flatter your features and make you feel confident and beautiful. From classic buns to trendy braids, there are many options to choose from. Let's dive in!

How to Choose the Right Updo

The key to finding the best updo for your round, chubby face is to look for styles that elongate your features and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose updos that add height to your crown and don't hug your face too tightly.
  • Avoid styles that are too sleek and flat, as they will only emphasize the roundness of your face.
  • Consider adding braids, twists, or other details to create texture and dimension.
  • Experiment with different parts and placements to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

Now that you know what to look for, let's explore some of the best updos for round chubby faces!

Step-by-Step Updo Tutorials

1. Classic Bun

The classic bun is a timeless updo that works well for all face shapes, including round, chubby faces. Here's how to create this elegant look:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure with an elastic band.
  2. Twist the ponytail until it coils around the base of the ponytail.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to tuck in any loose ends.
  4. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Messy Top Knot

The messy top knot is a chic and effortless updo that's perfect for casual occasions. Here's how to create this laid-back look:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure with an elastic band.
  2. Twist the ponytail until it coils around the base of the ponytail, leaving the ends sticking out.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins, pulling out a few strands to create a messy, undone look.
  4. Finish with a spritz of texturizing spray for added volume and texture.

3. Side Braid

A side braid is a romantic and feminine updo that flatters round faces by creating vertical lines. Here's how to create this bohemian look:

  1. Part your hair on one side and sweep it over your shoulder.
  2. Begin braiding your hair, starting at the nape of your neck and working your way down to the ends.
  3. Secure the braid with an elastic band and gently pull on the strands to loosen the braid and create volume.
  4. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Updo

Now that you've created the perfect updo, it's important to know how to maintain it throughout the day. Here are some tips to keep your hairstyle looking fresh and polished:

  • Use bobby pins and hairspray to secure any loose strands or flyaways.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause the style to come undone.
  • Carry a travel-sized hairspray in your purse for touch-ups throughout the day.
  • If your hair starts to fall flat, gently tease the roots with a comb to add volume.

FAQs about Updos for Round Chubby Faces

What type of hair is best for updos?

Updos work well on most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. If you have fine hair, consider using a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add body to your updo.

How do I make my updo last longer?

Use bobby pins and hairspray to secure your updo in place, and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day. You can also try using a hair net to keep your style in place.

Can I wear an updo with short hair?

Yes! There are many updos that work well with short hair, such as a messy bun or a braided crown. Look for styles that don't require a lot of length, and use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Locks

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it looks its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful locks:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and lead to breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the best updos for round chubby faces with confidence and style. Whether you're headed to a wedding, a night out with friends, or just want to switch up your everyday look, these updos are sure to flatter your features and make you feel beautiful!

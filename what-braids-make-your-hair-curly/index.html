What Braids Make Your Hair Curly

Written by Robby Sep 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat styling tools to curl your hair? Or are you looking for a low-maintenance way to add some texture and volume to your locks? Braids might just be the solution you're looking for. In this article, we'll explore what braids make your hair curly and provide some tips for achieving the perfect braid curls.

Types of Braids for Curly Hair

There are several types of braids that can create curly hair:

Fishtail Braids

Fishtail braids are created by dividing the hair into two sections and crossing small sections of hair over each other. This braid creates a tight, defined curl.

Dutch Braids

Dutch braids are created by crossing strands of hair under each other, rather than over. This braid creates a loose, wave-like curl.

French Braids

French braids are created by crossing strands of hair over each other, incorporating more hair into the braid as you go. This braid creates a tight, defined curl.

Twist Braids

Twist braids are created by twisting sections of hair together. This braid creates a loose, beachy wave.

How to Braid Your Hair for Curls

To achieve braid curls, start with clean, damp hair. Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as mousse or curl cream, to your hair before braiding.

Choose the type of braid you want to create and begin braiding your hair. Be sure to braid all the way to the ends of your hair to ensure that the curls are defined from root to tip.

Once your hair is braided, allow it to air dry completely. Once your hair is dry, carefully unravel the braids to reveal your curly locks.

Step-by-Step Guide to Braiding for Curls

Here is a step-by-step guide to creating braids for curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair
  3. Choose the type of braid you want to create
  4. Begin braiding your hair, making sure to braid all the way to the ends
  5. Allow your hair to air dry completely
  6. Unravel the braids to reveal your curly hair

Tips for Achieving Perfect Braid Curls

Here are some tips for achieving perfect braid curls:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product to help define your curls
  • Braid your hair tightly for more defined curls
  • Braid your hair loosely for more natural-looking waves
  • Allow your hair to air dry completely before unraveling the braids
  • For tighter curls, leave the braids in overnight

FAQs

Can I create braid curls with short hair?

Yes, you can create braid curls with short hair. However, you may need to use smaller braids to create the desired curl.

How long do I need to leave my braids in for curly hair?

The length of time you leave your braids in will depend on the desired curl. For looser waves, you can leave the braids in for a few hours. For tighter curls, you may need to leave the braids in overnight.

Hair Care for Braid Curls

To maintain your braid curls, be sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your curls from becoming dry and brittle.

Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls. Instead, opt for air-drying or diffusing your hair.

Be sure to use a deep conditioner or hair mask regularly to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

With the right technique and care, braid curls can be a low-maintenance way to add some texture and volume to your hair. Try out different braiding techniques and products to find the perfect braid curls for you.

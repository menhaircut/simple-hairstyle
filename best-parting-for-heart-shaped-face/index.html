Best Parting For Heart Shaped Face

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles and not being able to find the perfect one for your heart-shaped face? If yes, then this article is for you. In this blog post, we will be discussing the best parting for heart-shaped faces, how to do it, step-by-step guide, tips, FAQ, and hair care tips. So, let's get started.

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a broad forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The best parting for this face shape is a side parting. This style will help to balance out the forehead and chin, and make the cheekbones look more prominent. A side parting also helps to soften the overall look of the face.

How to Part Your Hair for Heart-Shaped Face

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's important to understand how to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  • Take a measuring tape and measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  • Compare the measurements and determine which area is the widest.
  • If your forehead is the widest, you have a heart-shaped face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape, here's how to part your hair for a heart-shaped face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Towel dry your hair and comb it thoroughly.
  2. Using a tail comb, create a straight parting on one side of your head. The parting should be in line with the highest point of your eyebrow arch.
  3. Apply some hair gel or serum to the parting to keep it in place.
  4. Blow-dry your hair on low heat while using a round brush to create volume.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten it further.
  6. Apply some hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Parting Your Hair for Heart-Shaped Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind while parting your hair for a heart-shaped face:

  • Make sure the parting is not too sharp or severe as it can make your forehead look even wider.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that suits you the best.
  • If you have short hair, opt for a side-swept fringe instead of a side parting.

FAQ

Q. Can I part my hair in the middle if I have a heart-shaped face?

A. It's not recommended to part your hair in the middle if you have a heart-shaped face. This style can make your forehead look even wider and your chin look narrower.

Q. Can I tie my hair in a ponytail if I have a heart-shaped face?

A. Yes, you can tie your hair in a ponytail if you have a heart-shaped face. Make sure to keep the ponytail low and to the side to balance out your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Heart-Shaped Face

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a regular basis as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to ensure your hair gets the necessary nutrients.

Conclusion:

Parting your hair in the right way can make a huge difference in the way your face looks. For a heart-shaped face, a side parting is the best option. With the help of this guide, you can now easily part your hair in the right way and enhance your facial features.

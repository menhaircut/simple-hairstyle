How To Get Hair To Grow Back From Traction Alopecia

Written by Robby Jul 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Traction alopecia is a type of hair loss that occurs when hair is pulled too tightly for a long time. This type of hair loss can be caused by tight hairstyles such as braids, weaves, and extensions. Traction alopecia can be a frustrating condition to deal with, but it is possible to get your hair to grow back. Here are some tips on how to get hair to grow back from traction alopecia.

Description

Traction alopecia is caused by damage to the hair follicles due to tension on the hair. This can lead to hair loss in the affected area. The condition is most commonly seen in people who wear tight hairstyles that pull on the hair, such as braids, weaves, and extensions. Traction alopecia can also be caused by wearing hats or helmets that are too tight, or by using hair tools that pull on the hair, such as curling irons and flat irons.

How to Get Hair to Grow Back from Traction Alopecia

Step by Step

Step 1: Identify the cause of the traction alopecia. If the cause is tight hairstyles, stop wearing them. If the cause is tight hats or helmets, stop wearing them. If the cause is hair tools, stop using them.

Step 2: Massage the affected area with a hair growth oil, such as castor oil or coconut oil. Massage the oil into the scalp and leave it on for at least an hour before washing it out.

Step 3: Use a scalp stimulant, such as peppermint oil or tea tree oil, to increase blood flow to the affected area. Apply the oil to the scalp and massage it in.

Step 4: Take a hair growth supplement, such as biotin or vitamin E. These supplements can help promote hair growth and strengthen the hair.

Step 5: Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals. This can help promote overall hair health and encourage hair growth.

Tips

Tip 1: Be patient. It can take several months for hair to grow back from traction alopecia.

Tip 2: Avoid tight hairstyles and hair tools that pull on the hair.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from damage by wearing a satin or silk scarf or bonnet at night.

FAQ

Q: Can traction alopecia be reversed?

A: Yes, traction alopecia can be reversed with proper hair care and treatment.

Q: How long does it take for hair to grow back from traction alopecia?

A: It can take several months for hair to grow back from traction alopecia.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for preventing and treating traction alopecia. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

- Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

- Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as relaxers and hair dyes.

- Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to detangle your hair.

- Avoid heat styling tools, such as curling irons and flat irons, as much as possible.

- Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips, you can get your hair to grow back from traction alopecia and maintain overall hair health. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you will see results.

What Is The Best Short Haircut For Thick Hair In 2023

Written by Aprilia Sep 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it's great to have voluminous locks, it can be tough to manage and style. Short haircuts can be a great solution for those with thick hair, but which one is the best? In this blog post, we'll explore the best short haircuts for thick hair in 2023, including a description, how to style it, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Advice

Description

The best short haircut for thick hair in 2023 is the pixie cut. This cut is super versatile and can be tailored to suit any face shape, hair texture, and personal style. The pixie cut is a short, layered haircut that's typically no longer than two inches in length. It's a great option for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that's easy to style.

Another great short haircut for thick hair is the bob. This classic haircut never goes out of style and can be customized to suit any face shape and hair texture. The bob is typically chin-length or shorter and can be styled straight, wavy, or curly.

How to Style

Styling a short haircut for thick hair is super easy. For a pixie cut, simply apply a styling cream or pomade to damp hair and blow dry using your fingers to create texture. For a bob, use a round brush to blow dry your hair straight or add some waves using a curling iron or flat iron.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style a pixie cut:

  1. Apply a styling cream or pomade to damp hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair using your fingers to create texture.
  3. If desired, use a flat iron to straighten any unruly pieces.
  4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray.

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style a bob:

  1. Apply a heat protectant to damp hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  3. If desired, use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or straighten your hair.
  4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling a short haircut for thick hair:

  • Use a lightweight styling cream or pomade to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair in the opposite direction to create volume and texture.
  • Invest in a good quality round brush for optimal results.
  • Experiment with different partings to see what suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for thick hair:

Q: Will a short haircut make my thick hair look thinner?

A: No, a short haircut won't make your hair look thinner. In fact, it can actually make your hair look fuller and more voluminous.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. Try a half-up, half-down style or a messy bun for a chic look.

Q: Do I need to use heat styling tools to style my short haircut?

A: No, you don't need to use heat styling tools to style your short haircut. You can create texture and volume using your fingers and a styling product.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some tips for caring for your short, thick hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to detangle your hair gently.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

In conclusion, the best short haircut for thick hair in 2023 is the pixie cut or bob. These cuts are versatile, easy to style, and can be customized to suit any face shape and hair texture. Remember to use lightweight styling products, experiment with different partings, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

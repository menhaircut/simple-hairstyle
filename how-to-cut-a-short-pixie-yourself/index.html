How To Cut A Short Pixie Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read
If you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish and sophisticated, a short pixie cut might be just what you need. But going to a salon every few weeks to maintain your cut can be expensive and time-consuming. That's why we've put together this step-by-step guide to help you cut a short pixie yourself at home.

Description

A short pixie cut is a very short hairstyle that typically features short layers on top and shaved or tapered sides and back. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look that still looks polished and put together.

How to Cut a Short Pixie Yourself: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies
Before you begin, make sure you have all the supplies you'll need. You'll need a good pair of scissors, clippers, a comb, and a mirror. Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair
Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you begin. Comb it out so it's tangle-free. Step 3: Section Your Hair
Use the comb to section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Clip each section out of the way. Step 4: Cut the Sides and Back
Starting with the sides and back, use the clippers to cut your hair to the desired length. Use a smaller guard for a shorter cut and a larger guard for a longer cut. Be sure to blend the sides and back so there are no harsh lines. Step 5: Cut the Top
Next, take down the top section and use the scissors to cut it to the desired length. Start at the front and work your way to the back. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle for a layered look. Step 6: Blend the Layers
Once you've cut the top section, use the scissors to blend the layers so they look natural and seamless. Step 7: Check Your Work
Use the mirror to check your work from every angle. Make any necessary adjustments. Step 8: Style Your Hair
Once you're happy with your cut, style your hair as desired. You can use a texturizing product to add volume and texture.

Tips for Cutting a Short Pixie Yourself

- Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter to avoid cutting too much off. - Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle for a layered look. - Blend the layers so they look natural and seamless. - Use a mirror to check your work from every angle.

FAQ

Can I cut a short pixie myself?
Yes, you can cut a short pixie yourself with the right tools and technique. What supplies do I need to cut a short pixie?
You'll need scissors, clippers, a comb, and a mirror. How often do I need to cut a short pixie?
It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep your cut. Generally, you'll need to cut it every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care for a Short Pixie Cut

To keep your short pixie cut looking fresh and polished, we recommend washing it every other day and using a texturizing product to add volume and texture. You can also use a dry shampoo to extend the time between washes. Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your cut.

Conclusion

Cutting a short pixie yourself might seem intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, it's definitely doable. Follow our step-by-step guide and you'll have a stylish and low-maintenance cut in no time! Remember to take your time, start with a longer length, and blend the layers for a natural look. Happy cutting!

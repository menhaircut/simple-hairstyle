How To Cut Your Own Hair In 11 Minutes A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending hours at the salon, waiting for your turn to get your hair cut? Do you want to save time and money by cutting your own hair at home? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will show you how to cut your own hair in 11 minutes. We will guide you through the process, provide you with tips, and answer frequently asked questions. So, let's get started.

How to Cut Your Own Hair in 11 Minutes

Before we begin, we need to make sure that you have the right tools for the job. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, a mirror, and a spray bottle filled with water. Once you have these tools, you can start cutting your hair.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to cutting your hair is to wash it. This will make it easier to cut and will ensure that you have a clean canvas to work with. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and rinse thoroughly.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Next, divide your hair into four sections: two at the front and two at the back. Use your comb to create a straight parting from the center of your forehead to the nape of your neck. Then, create another parting from one ear to the other. This will create four sections of hair.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start with the back sections of your hair. Take one section and comb it straight down. Hold it between your index and middle fingers and cut off the desired length. Repeat this process with the other section.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the side sections of your hair. Comb the hair straight down and hold it between your fingers. Cut off the desired length, making sure that both sides are even.

Step 5: Cut the Front

Finally, it's time to cut the front sections of your hair. Comb the hair forward and hold it between your fingers. Cut off the desired length, making sure that both sides are even.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you have finished cutting your hair, check for evenness. Use a mirror to see the back and sides of your head. Make any necessary adjustments until you are satisfied with the result.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Now that you have cut your hair, it's time to style it. Use a blow dryer and a styling product of your choice to create the look you want.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut your own hair in 11 minutes:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it will look shorter when it dries.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress.
  • Don't be too hard on yourself. Cutting your own hair takes practice, so don't worry if it's not perfect the first time.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair:

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair. It just takes practice and the right tools.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and the style you want. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every six to eight weeks.

How do I know how much to cut off?

Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's cut.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, you should use hair cutting scissors, as they are sharper and designed specifically for cutting hair.

Hair Care

Now that you have cut your hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair.

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy, beautiful hair.

Conclusion

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done quickly and easily. By following our step-by-step guide, you can cut your own hair in 11 minutes. Don't be too hard on yourself if it's not perfect the first time - practice makes perfect. And remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

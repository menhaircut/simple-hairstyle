Shoulder Length Hair Long Bangs A Timeless Hairstyle

Written by Luffy Oct 20, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, shoulder length hair with long bangs is a classic choice that never goes out of style. This versatile look can be worn in many different ways and is perfect for anyone who wants a chic and effortless look. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this hairstyle, and provide tips on how to maintain and care for it.

Description

Shoulder length hair with long bangs is a hairstyle that features hair that falls just below the shoulders, with long bangs that frame the face. The bangs can be styled in various ways, such as swept to the side, parted down the middle, or even cut into a curtain fringe. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look that still looks stylish and put-together.

How to Achieve This Look

To achieve the shoulder length hair with long bangs look, you will need to have hair that is at least shoulder length. If your hair is shorter, you can use hair extensions to add length. Next, you will need to decide on the type of bangs that you want. A good rule of thumb is to choose a bang style that complements your face shape.

Once you have your hair and bangs in place, you can style your hair in various ways. For a casual look, you can simply let your hair air dry and use a texturizing spray to add some volume and texture. If you want a sleeker look, you can blow-dry your hair straight and use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz. You can also add some waves or curls for a more romantic look.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the shoulder length hair with long bangs look:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and then rinse thoroughly.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Using a soft towel, gently pat your hair dry to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or texturizing spray. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a hair dryer, blow dry your hair until it is about 80% dry. Use a round brush to smooth out any tangles and add some volume to your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Using a flat iron, style your bangs in the desired direction. You can sweep them to the side, part them down the middle, or even cut them into a curtain fringe.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have styled your hair, finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining This Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your shoulder length hair with long bangs:

  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain the length.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQ

Q: Is shoulder length hair with long bangs suitable for all face shapes?

A: While shoulder length hair with long bangs can be flattering on many face shapes, it’s important to choose a bang style that complements your face shape. Consult with a hairstylist to find the right style for you.

Q: Can I style my hair in different ways with this hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can style your hair in various ways with this hairstyle. Try adding some waves or curls for a more romantic look, or wear your hair straight for a sleeker look.

Q: How often do I need to trim my hair?

A: It’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the length and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hairstyle, including shoulder length hair with long bangs. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

By following these tips, you can enjoy healthy, beautiful hair that looks great with your shoulder length hair with long bangs hairstyle.

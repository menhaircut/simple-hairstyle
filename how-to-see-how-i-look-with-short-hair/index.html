How To See How I Look With Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Aug 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering chopping off your long locks and going for a shorter hairstyle? Before making the big decision, you may want to see how you would look with short hair. Luckily, there are various ways to do so without committing to a new haircut just yet. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to see how you look with short hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to See How You Look with Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQ

How to See How You Look with Short Hair

There are various ways to see how you look with short hair, depending on how much effort and time you want to invest. Here are some options:

Use Virtual Makeover Tools

One of the easiest and quickest ways to see how you look with short hair is to use virtual makeover tools. These are online or mobile applications that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try different hairstyles, colors, and makeup looks. Some popular virtual makeover tools include:

  • Virtual Hairstyler
  • TAAZ Virtual Makeover
  • YouCam Makeup

Using virtual makeover tools is free and fun, but keep in mind that the results may not be 100% accurate or realistic.

Try a Wig

If you want a more realistic and hands-on approach, you can try wearing a wig that resembles the short hairstyle you have in mind. Wigs come in various styles, colors, and lengths, and you can easily find one that suits your face shape and skin tone. You can either buy a wig online or at a local wig shop, or borrow one from a friend.

Wearing a wig can give you a better idea of how you would look with short hair, but keep in mind that wigs may feel uncomfortable or itchy, and they require some maintenance and styling.

Photoshop Your Hair

If you have some Photoshop skills, you can also try editing a photo of yourself to make it look like you have short hair. You can use the crop, cut, and clone tools to remove your long hair and add a short hairstyle from a reference photo. This method requires some time and effort, but it can give you a more accurate and personalized result.

Step-by-Step Guide

Want to try the wig method? Here is a step-by-step guide on how to see how you look with short hair using a wig:

  1. Choose a wig that resembles the short hairstyle you have in mind. Look for wigs that match your natural hair color and texture, and try them on to see which one fits and looks best.
  2. Prepare your hair by brushing it and tying it into a low ponytail or bun. Use hair clips or bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  3. Put on a wig cap to keep your hair in place and create a smooth base for the wig.
  4. Put on the wig and adjust it to fit your head. Use the ear tabs and straps to secure the wig and make it feel comfortable.
  5. Style the wig using a wig brush or comb. You can use heat tools such as a flat iron or curling iron, but make sure to use a low heat setting and protect the wig from damage.
  6. Take a photo or look in the mirror to see how you look with short hair. Experiment with different angles and poses to get a better idea.
  7. Remove the wig and store it properly to keep it in good condition for future use.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make the most of your short hair trial:

  • Choose a wig that is made of high-quality synthetic or human hair, as these materials look and feel more natural.
  • Consider getting a wig with a lace front or monofilament cap, as these features create a more realistic hairline and scalp.
  • Take your time to adjust the wig and style it to your liking. Don't rush or force it, as this can damage the wig or your hair.
  • Experiment with different short hairstyles, such as bobs, pixie cuts, and shags. Don't be afraid to try something new and bold.
  • Ask for feedback from friends, family, or hairstylists to get a second opinion on your short hair look.

FAQ

Can I see how I look with short hair without cutting it?

Yes, you can use virtual makeover tools, try a wig, or edit a photo of yourself to see how you would look with short hair.

What is the best way to see how I look with short hair?

The best way depends on your preferences and resources. Virtual makeover tools are quick and easy, wigs are more realistic and hands-on, and Photoshop requires some skills and time.

Will a short hairstyle suit my face shape?

Short hairstyles can suit various face shapes, but it's important to choose a style that flatters your features and balances your proportions. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best short hairstyle for you.

Hair Care for Short Hair

If you decide to go for a short hairstyle, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Short hair tends to get greasy and flat faster than long hair, so don't skip washing.
  • Use styling products such as mousse, gel, or wax to add volume, texture, and hold to your hair. Apply them sparingly and evenly, and avoid overloading your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and avoid split ends. Short hair can grow out quickly and lose its structure, so regular trims are essential.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting on your styling tools. Avoid using them on wet or damp hair, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, clips, or scarves to add some fun and personality to your short hairstyle.

Now that you know how to see how you look with short hair, it's time to try it out and see if it's the right choice for you. Remember to have fun, be confident, and embrace your new look!

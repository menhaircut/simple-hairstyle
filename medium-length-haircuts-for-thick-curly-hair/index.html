Medium Length Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jul 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have thick curly hair, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that showcases your natural curls while also being manageable and easy to maintain. That's why medium length haircuts are a great option. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about medium length haircuts for thick curly hair, including how to choose the right style, tips for maintenance and care, and more.

How to Choose the Right Medium Length Haircut for Thick Curly Hair

When it comes to choosing a medium length haircut for thick curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your face shape: Different haircuts work better with different face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to go for a layered look that adds volume and height.
  • Think about your hair texture: Thick curly hair can be difficult to manage, so you'll want to choose a style that works with your natural texture. For example, if you have tight curls, a shorter layered cut may be best.
  • Choose a style that fits your lifestyle: If you're busy and always on the go, you may want a low-maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of upkeep. On the other hand, if you have more time to spend on styling, you may want a more intricate cut.

Some popular medium length haircuts for thick curly hair include:

  • Shoulder-length layers: This style works well for those with longer hair and looser curls. The layers help to add volume and movement, while still maintaining length.
  • Bob with bangs: A bob cut with bangs can be a great option for those with tighter curls. It's a low-maintenance style that still looks chic and stylish.
  • Long shag: For those with thicker, more voluminous curls, a long shag can be a great option. The layers help to add more movement and texture to the hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Medium Length Hair for Thick Curly Hair

If you're considering cutting your medium length hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide to get you started:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair until it's about 80 percent dry.
  3. Section your hair into four equal parts.
  4. Start cutting at the nape of your neck, cutting vertically to create a graduated bob shape.
  5. Move to the sides of your head and cut at a 45-degree angle, pulling the hair out and away from the head.
  6. Continue cutting the rest of your hair in sections, making sure to maintain the same length throughout.
  7. Once you're done cutting, style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining and Styling Medium Length Haircuts for Thick Curly Hair

To keep your medium length haircut looking great, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use the right products: Look for products that are specifically designed for thick curly hair, such as leave-in conditioners, curl creams, and styling gels.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools can be damaging to curly hair, so try to avoid using them as much as possible. If you do need to use a blow dryer, make sure to use a diffuser attachment.
  • Get regular trims: To keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends, make sure to get regular trims every 6 to 8 weeks.
  • Experiment with different styles: Don't be afraid to try out different styles and techniques to find what works best for you and your hair.

FAQs about Medium Length Haircuts for Thick Curly Hair

Q: Will a medium length haircut work for all types of thick curly hair?

A: No, different haircuts work better for different types of curly hair. It's important to choose a style that works with your natural texture.

Q: How do I prevent frizz in my thick curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz, make sure to use a leave-in conditioner, avoid heat styling tools, and try to let your hair air dry as much as possible.

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight with a medium length haircut for thick curly hair?

A: Yes, with the right styling tools and products, you can still straighten your hair even with a curly haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Hair

Finally, here are some general hair care tips that can help keep your thick curly hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair less frequently: Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair no more than 2 to 3 times a week.
  • Use a deep conditioner: A deep conditioner can help to nourish and moisturize your hair, leaving it soft and shiny.
  • Avoid brushing your hair: Brushing curly hair can lead to frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: UV rays can be damaging to hair, so make sure to wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when you're out in the sun.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect medium length haircut for your thick curly hair. Whether you go for layers, a bob, or a shag, remember to embrace your natural curls and have fun with your style!

Hairstyle For Face Shape Male Black Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jul 31, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a black man trying to figure out the best hairstyle for your face shape, you've come to the right place. In this article, we will go over some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect hairstyle that complements your facial features. From oval to square and everything in between, we've got you covered.

Description

For black men, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. But the first step in choosing the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which style will look best on you. By understanding your face shape, you can narrow down your options and choose a hairstyle that accentuates your best features.

How To Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. To do this, you will need to measure the length and width of your face. Here's how:

  1. Measure the distance across your forehead at the widest point (temples).
  2. Measure the distance across your cheekbones at the widest point.
  3. Measure the distance from your hairline to your chin.
  4. Measure the distance across your jawline at the widest point.

Once you have these measurements, you can determine your face shape. Here are the most common face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Diamond
  • Heart
  • Triangle

Step By Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the perfect hairstyle. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Research hairstyles that are best suited for your face shape.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that complements your features.
  3. Consult with your barber or hairstylist to determine if the hairstyle will work for your hair type and texture.
  4. Maintain your hairstyle by getting regular trims and using hair products that are best suited for your hair type.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that fits your personality and lifestyle.
  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one.
  • Use hair products that are best suited for your hair type and texture.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for black men:

What is the best hairstyle for an oval face?

For an oval face, the best hairstyle is one that adds volume to the top of the head while keeping the sides short. A buzz cut or a textured crop are great options.

What is the best hairstyle for a square face?

For a square face, the best hairstyle is one that adds volume to the top of the head while keeping the sides short. A textured crop or a slick back undercut are great options.

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

For a round face, the best hairstyle is one that adds height to the top of the head while keeping the sides short. A high top fade or a messy fringe are great options.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining a healthy head of hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals.
  • Use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape and accentuates your best features. Remember to always consult with your barber or hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair. With a little bit of effort and care, you can achieve a stylish and healthy head of hair.

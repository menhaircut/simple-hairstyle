How To Use Hair Toppers A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Nov 12, 2023 · 4 min read

If you are looking to add volume and length to your hair, hair toppers are the perfect solution for you. Hair toppers are easy to use and can help you achieve the perfect hair look in no time. In this article, we will guide you on how to use hair toppers and answer some frequently asked questions related to hair toppers.

What are Hair Toppers?

Hair toppers are hairpieces that are used to cover the top of the head and add volume and length to the hair. They are perfect for people who have thinning hair or hair loss. Hair toppers come in different sizes, shapes, and colors so you can choose the perfect one that matches your hair color and style.

How to Use Hair Toppers?

Using hair toppers is very easy and can be done in a few simple steps. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use hair toppers:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hair Topper

The first step in using hair toppers is to choose the right one for you. Hair toppers come in different sizes, shapes, and colors so make sure you choose the one that matches your hair color and style. You can either buy hair toppers online or from a hair salon.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before you use the hair topper, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can use a dry shampoo to remove any excess oil from your hair. You can also style your hair the way you want it to look before using the hair topper.

Step 3: Attach the Hair Topper

Now it’s time to attach the hair topper. Most hair toppers come with clips that you can use to attach them to your hair. Simply open the clips and attach them to your hair, starting from the front of your head and moving towards the back. Make sure the hair topper covers the top of your head and blends well with your natural hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once the hair topper is attached, you can style your hair the way you want it to look. You can use a curling iron, straightener, or any other styling tool to achieve the perfect look. Make sure you style the hair topper and your natural hair together to create a seamless look.

Tips for Using Hair Toppers

Here are some tips that can help you use hair toppers more effectively:

  • Choose the right size and shape of the hair topper that matches your hair type and style.
  • Use hair toppers on clean and dry hair for better results.
  • Clip the hair topper close to your scalp for a more natural look.
  • Blend the hair topper with your natural hair for a seamless look.
  • Style the hair topper and your natural hair together for a more natural look.

FAQs

Q: How long do hair toppers last?

A: Hair toppers can last for a few months to a year depending on the quality and how well you take care of them.

Q: Can I wear a hair topper while swimming?

A: It is not recommended to wear a hair topper while swimming as it can damage the hair topper and your natural hair.

Q: Can I dye my hair topper?

A: It is not recommended to dye your hair topper as it can damage the hair fibers and affect the quality of the hair topper.

Hair Care Tips for Hair Toppers

Here are some hair care tips that can help you maintain your hair topper:

  • Wash your hair topper regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Do not use hot water or blow dryers on your hair topper as it can damage the hair fibers.
  • Store your hair topper in a cool and dry place when not in use.
  • Brush your hair topper gently with a wide-toothed comb to avoid any tangles or knots.

Using hair toppers is a great way to add volume and length to your hair. By following these tips and guidelines, you can achieve a flawless look and maintain the quality of your hair topper. So go ahead and try out hair toppers for the perfect hair look!

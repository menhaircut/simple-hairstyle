Mens Hairstyles Short Sides Long Top A Guide To The Perfect Look

If you're looking for a versatile and trendy haircut, the mens hairstyles short sides long top might be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been around for a while now, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In fact, it has evolved and adapted to different styles and preferences, making it suitable for any man who wants to look stylish and modern.

Description

The mens hairstyles short sides long top is a haircut that features shorter length on the sides and longer length on the top. This combination creates a contrast that is both bold and stylish. The length on the top can vary depending on your preference, but it's usually at least two inches long. This hairstyle can be styled in many ways, making it perfect for any occasion.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the mens hairstyles short sides long top, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in this type of haircut. Make sure to bring a photo or a reference of the style you want to achieve. The barber will start by cutting the sides short using clippers or scissors, depending on your preference. The top will be left longer and layered to create texture and movement. The length and style of the top will depend on your preference and face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the Right Barber or Stylist

Not all barbers or stylists are experienced in mens hairstyles short sides long top, so make sure to choose one who is. Look for reviews and recommendations from friends or online to find the best one in your area.

Step 2: Bring a Photo or a Reference

Bringing a photo or a reference of the style you want will help the barber or stylist understand your preference and achieve the look you want.

Step 3: Communicate with Your Barber or Stylist

Communicate clearly with your barber or stylist about your preference, the length you want, and the style you want to achieve. This will avoid any miscommunication and ensure that you get the look you want.

Step 4: Cut the Sides Short

The barber or stylist will start by cutting the sides short using clippers or scissors, depending on your preference. The length will depend on your preference and face shape.

Step 5: Leave the Top Longer

The top will be left longer and layered to create texture and movement. The length and style of the top will depend on your preference and face shape.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once the haircut is done, you can style your hair in many ways. You can use hair products like pomade, wax, or gel to create different looks. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips

  • Choose a barber or stylist who is experienced in mens hairstyles short sides long top.
  • Bring a photo or a reference of the style you want to achieve.
  • Communicate clearly with your barber or stylist about your preference and the look you want to achieve.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that suits you best.
  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to maintain your haircut.

FAQ

What face shapes does the mens hairstyles short sides long top suit?

The mens hairstyles short sides long top suits many face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces.

How long should the top be?

The length of the top depends on your preference and face shape. However, it's usually at least two inches long.

Can I style my hair in different ways?

Yes, you can style your hair in many ways using hair products like pomade, wax, or gel. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Hair Care

Maintaining your mens hairstyles short sides long top requires some hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to maintain your haircut.

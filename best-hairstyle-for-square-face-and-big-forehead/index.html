Best Hairstyle For Square Face And Big Forehead

If you have a square face and a big forehead, don't worry! There are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter your features and make you look stunning. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for square faces and big foreheads, along with tips and tricks for hair care.

  • How to Choose the Best Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Description

If you have a square face shape, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width. This can make your face look strong and angular. However, a big forehead can also make your face appear longer and more oval-shaped.

To balance out your features, you want to look for hairstyles that soften your jawline and forehead. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing the best hairstyle for a square face and big forehead, you want to look for cuts that create volume at the crown of your head and soften your features. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height at the crown of your head. This will help balance out a big forehead.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight. This can make your face look even more angular.
  • Consider a layered haircut. Layers can add movement and softness to your face.
  • Avoid blunt bangs. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or a deep side part.
  • Consider adding texture to your hair with curls or waves. This can help soften your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Now that you've chosen the perfect haircut, it's time to style your hair. Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a flattering hairstyle for a square face and big forehead:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a root-lifting spray to your roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the hair at the crown of your head to add volume.
  4. If you have straight hair, use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves or curls.
  5. If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls.
  6. Finish with a hairspray that provides hold without weighing down your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your hair looking its best, here are some tips to follow:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling, which can damage your hair over time.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Invest in a good quality brush and comb to detangle your hair without causing breakage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for a square face and big forehead:

What is the best haircut for a square face and big forehead?

The best haircut for a square face and big forehead is one that adds volume at the crown of your head and softens your features. Consider a layered haircut with side-swept bangs or a deep side part.

Can I wear a center part with a square face and big forehead?

A center part can be tricky with a square face and big forehead, as it can make your face look even more angular. Stick to a side part or side-swept bangs for a more flattering look.

Should I avoid short haircuts with a square face and big forehead?

Short haircuts can work well with a square face and big forehead, as long as they add volume at the crown of your head. Consider a pixie cut or a bob with layers.

Hair Care for Square Faces and Big Foreheads

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.
  • Invest in a silk pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a flattering hairstyle for a square face and big forehead. Remember to choose a cut that adds volume at the crown of your head and softens your features, and take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

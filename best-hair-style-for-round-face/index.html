Best Hair Style For Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Nov 20, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face and you’re looking for the best hairstyle that will suit your face shape, you’ve come to the right place. In this blog post, we’ll be discussing the best hair style for round face, along with tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide forehead. The best hairstyle for this face shape is one that adds length to the face and makes it appear more oval. This can be achieved by adding volume and height to the crown of the head, and keeping the sides of the hair close to the face.

How to

The best way to determine the right hairstyle for your round face is to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair type, and personal style. However, there are some general guidelines that you can follow when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

Consider the length of your hair

The length of your hair can make a big difference in how it looks on a round face. Generally, medium to long hair works best for this face shape, as it adds length to the face. Short hair can also work, but it should be styled to add height to the crown of the head.

Choose the right layers

Layers can add texture and volume to your hair, but they should be placed strategically to avoid adding width to the face. Long layers that frame the face and taper at the ends can help to elongate the face, while short layers that start at the cheekbones can make the face look wider.

Consider bangs

Bangs can be a great way to add interest to your hairstyle and draw attention away from the roundness of your face. Side-swept bangs that fall at an angle can help to balance out the face, while blunt bangs that fall straight across can make the face appear shorter and wider.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving the best hair style for round face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then towel-dry it until it’s damp.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair and blow-dry it using a round brush to add height to the crown of the head.
  3. Part your hair to one side and use a flat iron to straighten the ends.
  4. If you have bangs, use a small round brush to blow-dry them and style them to one side.
  5. Finish your hairstyle with a light hold hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for achieving the best hair style for round face:

  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add depth and dimension.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too tight, as they can make the face appear wider.
  • If you have curly hair, embrace it and opt for a style that enhances your natural texture.
  • Accessorize your hair with a headband or hair clip to add interest and draw attention away from your face.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail, as long as you keep it high on the head to add height and length to the face.

Q: Should I avoid side parts if I have a round face?

A: No, you can wear a side part, as long as you keep the hair close to the face and add volume at the crown of the head.

Q: Can I wear my hair curly if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair curly, as long as you choose a style that elongates the face and doesn’t add width.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to achieve the best hair style for round face and look your best every day. Remember to consult with a professional stylist for personalized advice and recommendations.

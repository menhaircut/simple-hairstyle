What to Use to Tone Out Orange Hair - Tips and Tricks

If you've ever tried to lighten your hair and ended up with brassy, orange tones, you know how frustrating it can be. But don't worry, there are ways to tone out that orange and achieve beautiful, blonde locks. Here are some tips and tricks on what to use to tone out orange hair.

Description

Orange hair is a common problem when lightening hair, especially for those with dark hair. This happens when the underlying pigments in hair are exposed during the lightening process, resulting in orange tones. Toning hair helps to neutralize these unwanted tones and achieve the desired hair color.

How to Tone Out Orange Hair

There are several methods to tone out orange hair, including:

  • Using a toner: A toner is a great option for toning out orange hair. Look for a toner with a blue or purple base, as these colors help to neutralize orange tones. Apply the toner to damp hair and leave on for the recommended time before rinsing out.
  • Using a purple shampoo: Purple shampoo is another option for toning out orange hair. Use it like regular shampoo, focusing on the brassy areas. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing out. Be careful not to overuse purple shampoo, as it can dry out hair.
  • Using a hair mask: A hair mask with a blue or purple base can also help to tone out orange hair. Apply the mask to damp hair and leave on for the recommended time before rinsing out.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use toner to tone out orange hair:

  1. Wash hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Towel dry hair until it's damp.
  3. Mix the toner with developer, following the instructions on the package.
  4. Apply the toner to damp hair, focusing on the brassy areas.
  5. Leave the toner on for the recommended time, usually around 20-30 minutes.
  6. Rinse out the toner with cool water.
  7. Apply a deep conditioner to hair and leave on for a few minutes before rinsing out.

Tips for Toning Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when toning hair:

  • Don't leave toner on for too long, as it can result in over-toned hair.
  • Always follow the instructions on toner packaging.
  • Use a deep conditioner after toning to keep hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on toned hair, as it can cause damage.

FAQs

How often should I tone my hair?

It depends on your hair and how often you wash it. Generally, toning hair once a month is enough to maintain the desired color.

Can I tone my hair at home?

Yes, toning hair at home is possible with the right products and instructions. However, it's always best to consult a professional if you're unsure.

Can I use toner on colored hair?

Yes, toner can be used on colored hair to neutralize unwanted tones.

Hair Care

Toning hair can be drying, so it's important to take care of your hair afterwards. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep hair healthy and avoid using hot tools too often.

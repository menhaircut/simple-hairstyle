How To Hide Brows With Glue A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a way to completely transform your look or just want to experiment with different makeup styles, hiding your eyebrows can make a big difference. While there are many ways to do this, using glue is one of the most popular and effective techniques. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of how to hide brows with glue, as well as some tips and tricks to make the process as smooth as possible.

How to Hide Brows with Glue

Before we dive into the steps, let's go over some of the reasons why you might want to hide your eyebrows with glue. One of the most popular reasons is to create a blank canvas for makeup application, allowing you to experiment with different shapes and styles. It's also a popular technique for drag queens, cosplayers, and anyone looking to create a more dramatic look.

Now, let's get into the steps of how to hide brows with glue.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by brushing your eyebrows up with a spoolie brush to get them out of the way.
  2. Apply a thin layer of glue stick directly onto your eyebrows, making sure to cover them completely. You can use any type of washable glue stick, such as Elmer's, but avoid using permanent glue or other adhesives that could damage your skin or hair.
  3. Use a spoolie brush to smooth out the glue and make sure it's evenly distributed over your brows.
  4. Wait for the glue to dry completely. This can take anywhere from 2-5 minutes, depending on the thickness of the glue and the humidity in the air.
  5. Once the glue is dry, apply a second layer of glue stick over your brows. This will help to strengthen the hold and create a smoother surface for makeup application.
  6. Wait for the second layer of glue to dry completely before moving on to the next step.
  7. Apply a layer of powder foundation or setting powder over the glued area, using a fluffy brush. This will help to set the glue and create a smoother surface for makeup application.
  8. Use a concealer brush to apply a cream or liquid concealer over the glued area, blending it out towards the edges of your face. This will help to create a seamless transition between your skin and the glued area.
  9. Set the concealer with a layer of powder foundation or setting powder, using a fluffy brush.
  10. Once the glue is dry and set, you can apply your makeup as usual, creating any shape or style you desire!

Tips for Hiding Brows with Glue

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when hiding your brows with glue:

  • Make sure to use a washable glue stick, as permanent glue or adhesives could damage your skin and hair.
  • Don't apply too much glue, as this can create a lumpy or uneven surface. A thin, even layer is all you need.
  • Wait for the glue to dry completely before applying any makeup or setting powder. This will help to create a smoother surface and prevent any smudging or smearing.
  • When applying makeup over the glued area, use a light hand and blend carefully to create a seamless transition between your skin and the glued area.
  • If you're having trouble getting the glue to stick, try applying a layer of hairspray over your brows before applying the glue. This can help to create a stronger hold.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hiding brows with glue:

  • Is it safe to use glue on my eyebrows? Yes, as long as you use a washable glue stick and follow the steps carefully, it should be safe to use glue on your eyebrows.
  • Will hiding my brows with glue damage my hair? No, as long as you use a washable glue stick and avoid using permanent glue or other adhesives.
  • How long will the glue hold? The glue should hold for several hours, but it may start to wear off or become loose if you sweat or touch your face. You can always touch up the glue and makeup throughout the day if needed.

Hair Care Tips

While hiding your brows with glue can be a fun and exciting way to experiment with your look, it's important to take care of your hair and skin afterwards. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair and skin:

  • Use a gentle cleanser to remove any leftover glue or makeup, being careful not to scrub too hard or irritate your skin.
  • Apply a moisturizer or soothing balm to your skin to help reduce any redness or irritation.
  • Be gentle when brushing or styling your hair, as pulling too hard or using harsh products can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment to help restore any moisture lost during the glueing process.

Now that you know how to hide brows with glue, you're ready to experiment with different makeup styles and create a whole new look for yourself! Just remember to follow the steps carefully, use a washable glue stick, and take care of your hair and skin afterwards.

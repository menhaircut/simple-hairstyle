Medium Shaggy Hairstyles For Over 60 A Guide To Chic And Easy To Manage Hair

Written by Bardi Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a chic, easy-to-manage hairstyle, a medium shaggy cut may just be the perfect option for you. This versatile and low-maintenance style can work with any hair texture and face shape, making it a popular choice for women in their golden years. In this guide, we'll discuss everything you need to know about medium shaggy hairstyles for over 60, from how to get the look to how to care for your hair.

Why Choose a Medium Shaggy Cut?

There are several reasons why a medium shaggy cut is a great choice for women over 60. First and foremost, it's a low-maintenance style that requires minimal styling and upkeep. This is especially important for older women who may not have the time or energy to spend hours styling their hair each day.

Another benefit of a medium shaggy cut is that it can add volume and texture to thinning hair, a common issue among older women. The layers and choppy ends of a shaggy cut can create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair.

How to Get a Medium Shaggy Cut

The first step in getting a medium shaggy cut is to find a hairstylist who is experienced in this style. Look for someone who has a portfolio of shaggy cuts and can advise you on which length and layers will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Once you've found a stylist you trust, it's time to book your appointment. During your consultation, discuss your desired length and layers, as well as any concerns you may have about styling and maintenance. Your stylist will then shampoo and condition your hair before cutting it into the desired shape.

After your cut is complete, your stylist may recommend products or styling techniques to help you achieve your desired look. They may also schedule a follow-up appointment to ensure that your cut is growing out evenly and to make any necessary adjustments.

Styling Tips for Medium Shaggy Hair

One of the best things about a medium shaggy cut is that it can be styled in a variety of ways. Here are some tips for achieving different looks:

  • To create a casual, beachy look, apply sea salt spray to damp hair and scrunch with your hands. Let air dry or diffuse with a blow dryer.
  • To add volume and texture, apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair and blow dry with a round brush, focusing on the roots.
  • To create a sleek, polished look, smooth a small amount of smoothing serum over dry hair and use a flat iron to straighten.

FAQs About Medium Shaggy Hairstyles for Over 60

What face shapes work best with a medium shaggy cut?

A medium shaggy cut can work with any face shape, but it's especially flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

How often should I get my shaggy cut trimmed?

Most hairstylists recommend getting a shaggy cut trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and layers.

Can I still wear my hair up with a shaggy cut?

Absolutely! A shaggy cut can be styled in a variety of updos, from messy buns to sleek ponytails.

How to Care for Medium Shaggy Hair

To keep your medium shaggy hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Get regular trims to keep your cut looking fresh.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With the right cut, styling, and care, a medium shaggy hairstyle can be a chic and easy-to-manage option for women over 60. Whether you prefer a casual, beachy look or a sleek and polished style, a shaggy cut can be tailored to your individual preferences and needs.

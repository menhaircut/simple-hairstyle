How To Cut Hair At Home Short Back And Sides A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save some money and cut your hair at home, a short back and sides haircut is a great place to start. This classic men's haircut is relatively easy to do, and with a bit of practice, you'll be able to achieve professional-looking results. In this article, we'll go through the steps of cutting a short back and sides haircut, as well as some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

  • How to Cut Hair at Home Short Back and Sides
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cut Hair at Home Short Back and Sides

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need a few tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Mirror

Once you have your tools, you're ready to start cutting your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your hair at home:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, it will be more difficult to cut and may result in an uneven haircut.
  2. Put on your haircutting cape or towel to protect your clothes from hair clippings.
  3. Begin by using the clippers to cut the sides and back of your head. Use the #3 guard to start and adjust as needed. Start at the bottom and work your way up.
  4. Next, use the scissors to cut the hair on the top of your head. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. You can start longer and work your way shorter until you achieve the desired length.
  5. Blend the sides and top together using the scissors. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle, starting from the shortest point and blending it into the longer hair on top.
  6. Use the clippers to clean up the hairline around your ears and neck.
  7. Check your work in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get the best results when cutting your hair at home:

  • Practice makes perfect. Don't worry if your first few haircuts aren't perfect. With practice, you'll get better and better.
  • Start longer than you think you want. It's easier to cut more hair off than to put it back.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from different angles.
  • Invest in good quality clippers and scissors. They will make the process easier and give you better results.
  • Consider asking a friend or family member to help you with the back of your head.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair at home:

  • Can I cut my own hair?
    • Yes, you can cut your own hair. However, it's important to use the right tools and follow the proper technique.
  • What if I make a mistake?
    • If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go shorter or try to fix the mistake. If you're really unsure, consider going to a professional for help.
  • How often should I cut my hair?
    • It depends on your hair type and the style you're going for. Generally, a short back and sides haircut should be cut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care After the Cut

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain the style and keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as this can damage it.
  • Consider using a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to help hold the style in place.
  • Visit a professional stylist every few haircuts to keep your hair looking its best.

Cutting your hair at home can be a great way to save money and get a fresh new look. By following these steps and tips, you'll be able to achieve a short back and sides haircut that looks great and lasts. Good luck!

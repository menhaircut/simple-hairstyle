What Happens If You Cut Body Hair With Scissors

Written by Oliver Jul 09, 2023 · 5 min read

Cutting body hair with scissors is a common practice for many people. Some might do it to keep their hair neat and tidy, while others might do it as part of their grooming routine. However, have you ever wondered what happens if you cut body hair with scissors? In this blog post, we will explore the effects of cutting body hair with scissors and provide you with some tips on how to do it properly.

Description

Cutting body hair with scissors can have different effects on different people. Some people might experience irritation or discomfort, while others might not notice any difference at all. However, cutting body hair with scissors can also lead to ingrown hairs or infections if not done properly.

It is important to note that cutting body hair with scissors is not the same as shaving. While shaving removes hair from the skin's surface, cutting hair with scissors only shortens the hair without removing it entirely. As a result, the hair can still grow back and might require more frequent trimming.

How to Cut Body Hair With Scissors

Cutting body hair with scissors is a simple process that can be done at home. However, it is important to use the right tools and techniques to avoid any potential risks. Here are some tips on how to cut body hair with scissors:

Choose the Right Scissors

When cutting body hair, it is important to use scissors that are specifically designed for hair cutting. These scissors are sharp and have a straight edge that allows for precise cutting. Avoid using regular household scissors or kitchen scissors, as they can cause uneven cuts and can be dull or rusty.

Clean and Disinfect the Scissors

Before using the scissors, make sure to clean and disinfect them to prevent any infections. You can clean the scissors with soap and water, or you can use rubbing alcohol to disinfect them. Make sure to dry the scissors thoroughly before using them.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut body hair with scissors:

  1. Choose a well-lit area and stand in front of a mirror.
  2. Comb the hair in the direction of growth to remove any tangles and make it easier to cut.
  3. Hold the hair between two fingers and use the scissors to cut the hair in a straight line. Avoid cutting too close to the skin to prevent any nicks or cuts.
  4. Continue cutting the hair in small sections until the desired length is achieved.
  5. Check the hair for any uneven cuts and trim as necessary.
  6. When finished, clean and disinfect the scissors and wash the area with soap and water.

Tips for Cutting Body Hair With Scissors

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting body hair with scissors:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut shorter to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair away from the skin and create a guide for cutting.
  • Avoid cutting too close to the skin to prevent any nicks or cuts.
  • Use small, precise cuts to achieve an even length.
  • Trim hair in the direction of growth to avoid any damage to the hair follicle.

FAQs

Can cutting body hair with scissors cause ingrown hairs?

Yes, cutting body hair with scissors can cause ingrown hairs if not done properly. Ingrown hairs occur when the hair grows back into the skin instead of out of it, leading to inflammation and irritation. To prevent ingrown hairs, make sure to cut the hair in the direction of growth and avoid cutting too close to the skin.

Can cutting body hair with scissors cause infections?

Yes, cutting body hair with scissors can cause infections if the scissors are not clean or if the skin is cut. To prevent infections, make sure to clean and disinfect the scissors before and after use and avoid cutting too close to the skin.

Is it better to shave or cut body hair with scissors?

It depends on personal preference and the results you are looking for. Shaving removes hair from the skin's surface, while cutting hair with scissors only shortens the hair without removing it entirely. Cutting hair with scissors can be a good option for those who want to maintain a certain length or shape, while shaving is better for those who want a completely smooth look.

Hair Care After Cutting Body Hair With Scissors

After cutting body hair with scissors, it is important to take care of the hair to prevent any damage or irritation. Here are some tips for hair care after cutting body hair with scissors:

  • Moisturize the skin with lotion or oil to prevent dryness and irritation.
  • Avoid tight clothing or friction on the skin to prevent ingrown hairs.
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure to prevent sunburn or skin damage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or products on the skin or hair.

In conclusion, cutting body hair with scissors can be a safe and effective way to maintain hair length and shape. However, it is important to use the right tools and techniques to avoid any potential risks. By following these tips and taking care of your hair and skin after cutting body hair with scissors, you can achieve a neat and tidy look without any negative effects.

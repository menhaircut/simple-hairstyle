How To Hide A Bald Spot On Crown Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're experiencing baldness, you're not alone. According to the American Hair Loss Association, two-thirds of men experience some degree of hair loss by the time they're 35 years old. Furthermore, women can also experience hair loss as they age or due to hormonal changes. One common area where hair loss occurs is the crown. If you're wondering how to hide a bald spot on crown, you're in the right place. This article will provide you with tips and tricks that can help you cover up your bald spot and improve your overall confidence.

Description: Understanding Bald Spots on the Crown

A bald spot on the crown is an area of the scalp where hair is thinning or has completely fallen out. It can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, and age. Bald spots on the crown can be frustrating and affect your self-esteem, but there are ways to hide them.

How to Hide a Bald Spot on Crown: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose a Hairstyle

The first step in hiding a bald spot on the crown is to choose a hairstyle that works for you. A shorter haircut can help to make your hair look fuller, while longer hair can be styled to cover the bald spot. You can also consider getting a hairpiece or wig if you want to completely cover the bald spot.

Step 2: Use Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are a great option for covering up bald spots on the crown. They are made from natural keratin fibers that bind to your existing hair, making it look thicker and fuller. Simply sprinkle the fibers onto your scalp and gently pat them down. Hair fibers come in a variety of colors to match your natural hair color.

Step 3: Try Scalp Micropigmentation

Scalp micropigmentation is a non-invasive cosmetic procedure that involves tattooing tiny dots onto your scalp to create the illusion of hair. This procedure can be used to cover up bald spots on the crown and create a more defined hairline. However, it can be costly and requires touch-ups over time.

Tips for Hiding a Bald Spot on Crown

Here are some additional tips that can help you hide a bald spot on the crown:

  • Use volumizing hair products to make your hair look fuller
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can highlight the bald spot
  • Consider using a colored hair spray to cover up the bald spot
  • Wear hats or headbands to cover up the bald spot
  • Consult with a dermatologist or hair loss specialist for professional advice

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

What Causes Bald Spots on the Crown?

Bald spots on the crown can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, and age. Certain medical conditions and medications can also contribute to hair loss.

Can Bald Spots on the Crown be Reversed?

While there is no cure for baldness, there are treatments that can help to slow down or halt hair loss. These treatments include medications, hair transplant surgery, and scalp micropigmentation.

Is it Possible to Prevent Bald Spots on the Crown?

While it may not be possible to completely prevent bald spots on the crown, there are steps you can take to minimize their appearance. These include maintaining a healthy diet, avoiding harsh hair treatments, and managing stress levels.

Hair Care Tips for Bald Spots on the Crown

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you manage bald spots on the crown:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools, as they can damage hair
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep hair healthy
  • Brush hair gently to avoid pulling out hair
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles that can damage hair follicles

By following these tips and tricks, you can learn how to hide a bald spot on crown and improve your confidence. Remember that hair loss is a common issue, and there are many options available to help you manage it.

How To Get Rid Of Messy Hair In Royale High

Written by Oliver Nov 21, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

In the virtual world of Royale High, your avatar's hairstyle can make a big difference in your overall look. However, it's not uncommon for your hair to get messy after a long day of attending classes and exploring the game's various locations. If you're wondering how to get rid of messy hair in Royale High, you've come to the right place. In this blog post, we'll provide you with some tips and tricks to keep your virtual locks looking perfect.

Description

Messy hair can be a real hassle in Royale High. Not only does it detract from your overall appearance, but it can also make it difficult to participate in certain activities, like dancing or taking pictures. Fortunately, there are several ways to get rid of messy hair in the game.

How to Get Rid of Messy Hair in Royale High

If your hair is looking a little too disheveled for your liking, there are a few things you can do to fix it. Here are some tips:

Tip #1: Visit the Salon

The easiest way to get rid of messy hair in Royale High is to visit the salon. Inside the salon, you'll find a variety of hair styling tools that you can use to fix your hair. You can straighten it, curl it, or even add some highlights. Just be prepared to spend some diamonds, as the salon services aren't free.

Tip #2: Use a Hairbrush

If you don't want to spend any diamonds, you can always use a hairbrush to fix your hair. To do this, simply click on the "Inventory" button in the bottom right corner of your screen, and then click on the "Accessories" tab. From there, you can select a hairbrush and use it to smooth out any tangles or kinks in your hair.

Tip #3: Wear a Hat

If all else fails, you can always wear a hat to cover up your messy hair. There are plenty of cute hats available in the game, so you're sure to find one that matches your outfit.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're not sure how to implement the tips above, here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Open Your Inventory

Click on the "Inventory" button in the bottom right corner of your screen.

Step 2: Click on Accessories

Click on the "Accessories" tab to view all of the available hairbrushes and hats.

Step 3: Select a Hairbrush or Hat

Select a hairbrush to smooth out any tangles in your hair, or choose a hat to cover it up.

Step 4: Visit the Salon (Optional)

If you want to make more drastic changes to your hair, visit the salon and use the tools available to you there.

Tips for Hair Care in Royale High

While it's important to know how to get rid of messy hair in Royale High, it's even more important to take care of your hair in the first place. Here are some tips to keep your virtual locks looking healthy and beautiful:

Tip #1: Use Hair Products

Just like in real life, using hair products can make a big difference in the health and appearance of your virtual hair. Look for shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that suit your hair type.

Tip #2: Protect Your Hair

If you're going to be spending a lot of time in the sun or in water, be sure to protect your hair. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner to shield your hair from the elements.

Tip #3: Get Regular Trims

To keep your hair looking its best, get regular trims to remove split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ

Q: What if I don't have enough diamonds to visit the salon?

A: If you don't have enough diamonds to visit the salon, you can always use a hairbrush or wear a hat to cover up your messy hair.

Q: Can I change my hair color in Royale High?

A: Yes, you can change your hair color in the salon using diamonds.

Q: How often should I visit the salon?

A: It's up to you how often you want to visit the salon. Some players prefer to visit every day, while others only go occasionally.

Conclusion

Getting rid of messy hair in Royale High doesn't have to be a challenge. With the tips and tricks outlined in this blog post, you'll be able to keep your virtual locks looking perfect no matter where your adventures in the game take you. Just remember to take good care of your hair, and you'll always look your best.

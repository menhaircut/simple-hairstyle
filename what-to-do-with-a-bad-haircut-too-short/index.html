What To Do With A Bad Haircut Too Short Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Nov 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Getting a haircut is a great way to change your look and feel refreshed. However, sometimes things don't go as planned, and you end up with a bad haircut that's too short for your liking. If you find yourself in this situation, don't worry, there are still things you can do to make the most of your new hairdo. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on what to do with a bad haircut too short.

How to Fix a Bad Haircut Too Short?

Before you start panicking about your new haircut, take a deep breath and remember that hair grows back. There are also several things you can do to make your hair look better in the meantime. Here are some tips:

1. Style Your Hair Differently

One of the easiest ways to make a bad haircut look better is to style it differently. Experiment with different partings, use hair accessories like headbands, or try a new hair product to add texture or volume to your hair. You can also use a hair straightener or curling iron to create different styles.

2. Add Hair Extensions

If you're really unhappy with your new haircut, you can consider adding hair extensions. Hair extensions are a great way to add length and volume to your hair, and they come in a variety of styles and colors to match your natural hair. However, keep in mind that hair extensions can be expensive, and they need to be maintained regularly.

3. Get Regular Trims

One way to make your hair grow faster is to get regular trims. Although it may seem counterintuitive, trimming your hair regularly can actually help it grow longer and healthier. This is because split ends and breakage can slow down hair growth. So, make sure to schedule regular trims with your hairstylist to keep your hair healthy.

4. Embrace Your New Look

If all else fails, embrace your new look and rock it with confidence. Remember that a bad haircut doesn't define you, and it's just hair. You can always change your hair again in the future. So, wear your new haircut with pride and confidence.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Style a Bad Haircut Too Short?

If you're not sure how to style your new haircut, here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply Hair Product

Apply a hair product that's suited for your hair type, such as a mousse, gel, or serum. This will help add texture and hold to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair. Start by blow drying the roots, then move to the ends of your hair. Use the brush to create a side part or a center part, depending on your preference.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it using a hair straightener or curling iron. Create loose waves or curls, or straighten your hair for a sleek look. You can also use hair accessories like headbands or hair clips to add some flair to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the look with a hairspray to hold your hair in place. Choose a hairspray that's suited for your hair type and desired hold strength.

Tips to Take Care of a Bad Haircut Too Short

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type. Avoid using harsh chemicals or sulfates that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Too Often

Avoid heat styling your hair too often, as it can damage your hair and slow down hair growth. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth, such as vitamin C, biotin, and iron. Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQs: What to Do with a Bad Haircut Too Short?

Q: How long does it take for hair to grow back?

A: Hair grows an average of 0.5 inches per month, so it can take several months or even a year for your hair to grow back to its original length.

Q: Can I color my hair after a bad haircut?

A: It's best to wait at least a month before coloring your hair after a bad haircut to give your hair time to heal and grow.

Q: Should I cut my hair shorter to fix a bad haircut?

A: It's not recommended to cut your hair shorter to fix a bad haircut, as it can make the problem worse. Instead, try styling your hair differently or using hair extensions to add length and volume.

Conclusion

Getting a bad haircut too short can be frustrating, but it's not the end of the world. There are several things you can do to make the most of your new hairdo, including styling your hair differently, adding hair extensions, getting regular trims, and embracing your new look with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair by using gentle products, avoiding heat styling too often, and eating a healthy diet. With these tips and tricks, you can rock your new haircut and feel confident in your own skin.

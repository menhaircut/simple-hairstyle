Description

Short pixie style haircuts have been a popular trend for years, and they're not going away anytime soon. These haircuts are perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, edgy look that's easy to style and maintain. Short pixie style haircuts can be tailored to suit any face shape, hair texture, and personal style, making them a versatile option for anyone looking for a new haircut.

If you're considering a short pixie style haircut, you might be wondering what it will look like on you. That's where pictures come in handy. Seeing pictures of short pixie style haircuts can help you visualize what the haircut will look like on you and can also give you inspiration for how to style it.

How to Get a Short Pixie Style Haircut

If you're ready to take the plunge and get a short pixie style haircut, here's what you need to know:

  1. Find a reputable hairstylist who has experience cutting short hair.
  2. Bring pictures of the short pixie style haircut you want to your hairstylist.
  3. Discuss any concerns or questions you have with your hairstylist before they begin cutting your hair.
  4. Be prepared to maintain your haircut with regular trims and styling.

Choosing the Right Short Pixie Style Haircut for You

When choosing a short pixie style haircut, consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Some popular short pixie style haircuts include:

  • The classic pixie cut
  • The choppy pixie cut
  • The undercut pixie cut
  • The textured pixie cut

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting a Short Pixie Style Haircut

If you're feeling brave and want to cut your own short pixie style haircut, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the top of your hair with a clip.
  3. Use clippers or scissors to cut the sides and back of your hair to your desired length.
  4. Release the top section of your hair and use scissors to cut it to your desired length.
  5. Use scissors to texturize your hair and add movement.
  6. Style your hair using your preferred styling products.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Pixie Style Haircut

Short pixie style haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use styling products, like pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs about Short Pixie Style Haircuts

Will a short pixie style haircut suit my face shape?

Short pixie style haircuts can be tailored to suit any face shape, but it's important to choose the right style for your face shape. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for you.

What hair texture is best for a short pixie style haircut?

Short pixie style haircuts work well with all hair textures, but some styles may be easier to maintain with certain textures. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best style for your hair texture.

Can I still style my hair if it's short?

Absolutely! Short pixie style haircuts are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Short Pixie Style Haircuts

Short pixie style haircuts require some special care to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, like flat irons or curling irons, too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Whether you're looking for a dramatic change or just want to switch up your look, a short pixie style haircut might be just what you need. Use pictures and these tips to help you find the perfect haircut for you.

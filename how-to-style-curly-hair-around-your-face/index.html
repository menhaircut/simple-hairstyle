How To Style Curly Hair Around Your Face A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Sep 30, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it around your face. But, with the right techniques and products, you can create a beautiful and flattering look that highlights your natural curls. In this guide, we’ll show you how to style curly hair around your face in 2023, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Curly Hair Around Your Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Perfect Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Style Curly Hair Around Your Face

Styling curly hair around your face is all about enhancing your natural curls and creating a flattering shape that complements your features. Here are some tips to get you started:

  • Start with freshly washed hair to ensure your curls are defined and free from product buildup.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp hair to help define your curls and add moisture.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair and create a part that flatters your face shape.
  • Twist small sections of hair around your fingers to encourage curl formation and create a defined shape around your face.
  • Diffuse your hair on low heat to dry your curls and enhance their shape.
  • Finish with a lightweight hair oil or serum to add shine and tame frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Follow these steps for a flawless curly hairstyle:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair and create a part that flatters your face shape.
  4. Twist small sections of hair around your fingers to encourage curl formation and create a defined shape around your face.
  5. Diffuse your hair on low heat to dry your curls and enhance their shape.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use a lightweight hair oil or serum to add shine and tame frizz.

Tips for Perfect Curls:

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect curly hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different parting techniques to find the one that flatters your face shape.
  • Use a microfiber towel or T-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair while it dries to prevent disrupting the curl pattern.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling wands, as they can damage your curls.

FAQs:

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling curly hair around your face:

  • Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?
    A: It depends on your hair type and texture, but most people with curly hair can go 2-3 days between washes.
  • Q: What’s the best way to detangle curly hair?
    A: Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Q: How can I prevent my curls from getting frizzy?
    A: Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and add moisture, and avoid touching your hair while it dries.

Hair Care for Curly Hair:

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can dry out your curls.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and avoid using brushes or combs with small teeth.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling wands, as they can damage your curls.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering curly hairstyle that highlights your natural curls and enhances your features. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture, and don’t be afraid to try new things. With a little patience and practice, you can master the art of styling curly hair around your face.

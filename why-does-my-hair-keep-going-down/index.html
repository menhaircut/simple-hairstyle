If you have relaxed hair, you may have experienced the frustration of your hair going down over time. This can be caused by a variety of factors, from the products you use to the way you style your hair. In this article, we'll explore some of the common causes of hair going down and provide tips on how to keep your hair looking its best.

How to Prevent Your Hair from Going Down

Preventing your hair from going down requires a combination of proper hair care and styling techniques. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking voluminous and bouncy:

Step by Step Guide

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help lift your roots and add body to your hair.

2. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair, such as heavy oils or creams.

3. Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume to your roots.

4. Use a round brush when blow drying to add lift and bounce to your hair.

5. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, which can cause damage and breakage.

6. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Tips for Maintaining Volume in Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain volume in your hair:

  • Use a dry shampoo to help absorb excess oil and add volume to your roots.
  • Try using a volumizing mousse or spray to add body to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a messy bun or a high ponytail, to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid combing your hair too much, as this can cause it to go flat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why does my hair keep going down?

A: There are many reasons why hair can go down, including the use of heavy products, damage from heat styling, and genetics.

Q: How can I add volume to my hair?

A: Some ways to add volume to your hair include using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow drying your hair upside down, and using a round brush when blow drying.

Hair Care for Relaxed Hair

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your relaxed hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for relaxed hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray when heat styling.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or creams that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can help prevent your hair from going down and keep it looking voluminous and healthy.

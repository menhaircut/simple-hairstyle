What To Do For Hair Fall After Pregnancy A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling with hair fall after pregnancy? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many new moms experience hair fall due to hormonal changes and stress. However, with the right care and treatment, you can prevent hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about how to deal with hair fall after pregnancy.

Table of Contents

  1. Causes of Hair Fall After Pregnancy
  2. Hair Care Tips for Preventing Hair Fall
  3. Dietary Changes for Hair Growth
  4. Treatments for Hair Fall After Pregnancy
  5. FAQs

Causes of Hair Fall After Pregnancy

One of the main causes of hair fall after pregnancy is hormonal changes. During pregnancy, your body produces more estrogen, which prolongs the growth phase of your hair. This gives you thicker, healthier hair. However, after giving birth, your estrogen levels drop, and you may experience hair fall as a result.

Stress is another factor that can contribute to hair fall after pregnancy. Taking care of a newborn can be overwhelming and stressful, and this can affect your hair growth cycle.

Other factors that can contribute to hair fall after pregnancy include genetics, nutritional deficiencies, and medical conditions like thyroid problems.

Hair Care Tips for Preventing Hair Fall

Here are some hair care tips that can help prevent hair fall after pregnancy:

1. Be Gentle with your Hair

Avoid harsh chemical treatments and heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and lead to hair fall. Instead, use gentle, sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners, and let your hair air dry whenever possible.

2. Brush your Hair Gently

Avoid aggressive brushing, as this can pull out your hair and cause breakage. Use a wide-toothed comb or a boar bristle brush to detangle your hair gently.

3. Massage your Scalp

Massage your scalp gently with your fingertips to improve blood circulation and promote hair growth.

4. Use Hair Masks

Use hair masks made with natural ingredients like avocado, coconut oil, and honey to nourish your hair and prevent hair fall.

Dietary Changes for Hair Growth

Your diet can also play a role in preventing hair fall after pregnancy. Here are some dietary changes you can make to promote hair growth:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a diet rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and vitamin D. These nutrients can help promote healthy hair growth.

2. Drink Plenty of Water

Drink at least eight glasses of water a day to keep your body hydrated and promote healthy hair growth.

3. Consume Protein

Eat protein-rich foods like eggs, lean meats, and legumes to promote hair growth and prevent hair fall.

Treatments for Hair Fall After Pregnancy

If you're experiencing severe hair fall after pregnancy, you may want to consider these treatments:

1. Minoxidil

Minoxidil is a topical treatment that can help promote hair growth. It's available over-the-counter and can be applied to your scalp daily.

2. Hair Transplant

If you have significant hair loss, you may want to consider a hair transplant. This is a surgical procedure in which hair follicles are moved from one part of your scalp to another.

3. Hormone Therapy

If your hair fall is due to hormonal imbalances, hormone therapy may be an option. This involves taking medication to regulate your hormone levels and promote hair growth.

FAQs

1. How long does hair fall last after pregnancy?

Hair fall after pregnancy typically lasts for about six months to a year.

2. Can breastfeeding cause hair fall?

Yes, breastfeeding can cause hair fall due to hormonal changes.

3. Can stress cause hair fall?

Yes, stress can contribute to hair fall by disrupting your hair growth cycle.

Conclusion

Dealing with hair fall after pregnancy can be challenging, but with the right care and treatment, you can prevent hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to be gentle with your hair, eat a balanced diet, and consider treatments like minoxidil or hormone therapy if necessary. By following these tips, you can enjoy healthy, beautiful hair once again!

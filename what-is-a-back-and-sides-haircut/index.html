What Is A Back And Sides Haircut

Written by William Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A back and sides haircut is a classic men's hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short on the back and sides of the head while leaving the top longer. This style is also commonly referred to as a "short back and sides" or a "businessman's cut."

How to Get a Back and Sides Haircut

If you're interested in getting a back and sides haircut, here's what you need to know:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

There are a few variations of the back and sides haircut, so it's important to decide which one you like the best. The most common styles include a tapered fade, a classic side part, or a textured crop.

Step 2: Find a Barber

Once you've decided on a style, it's time to find a barber who can give you the cut you want. Look for a barber who has experience cutting men's hair and who can provide examples of their work.

Step 3: Communicate with Your Barber

When you go to the barber, be sure to communicate exactly what you want. Bring in pictures of the style you're interested in, and be clear about how short you want the back and sides to be.

Step 4: Maintain Your Haircut

Depending on how fast your hair grows, you may need to get a touch-up every few weeks to keep your back and sides looking neat and tidy. Be sure to use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy, and consider using a styling product to help you achieve the look you want.

Tips for a Perfect Back and Sides Haircut

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect back and sides haircut:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape
  • Keep your hair clean and well-maintained
  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep your hair in place
  • Consider getting a professional shave to keep your beard and sideburns looking neat

FAQ

Q: How short should the back and sides be?

A: The length of the back and sides can vary depending on your personal preference and the style you choose. Generally, the hair is cut to between 1/4 inch and 2 inches in length.

Q: Is a back and sides haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a back and sides haircut can work for all hair types, although the style may need to be adapted slightly depending on your hair's texture and thickness.

Q: How often do I need to get a touch-up?

A: This will depend on how fast your hair grows, but you may need to get a touch-up every 2-4 weeks to keep your back and sides looking neat.

Hair Care

To keep your back and sides looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage the hair
  • Use a high-quality styling product to help you achieve the look you want
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized

Conclusion

A back and sides haircut is a classic men's style that can work for a variety of face shapes and hair types. If you're interested in getting this style, be sure to communicate clearly with your barber and take good care of your hair to keep it looking great.

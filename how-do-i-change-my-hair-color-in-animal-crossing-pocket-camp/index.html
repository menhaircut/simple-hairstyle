How Do I Change My Hair Color In Animal Crossing Pocket Camp

Written by Bardi Jun 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your character's current hair color in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp? Do you want to switch things up and try a new look? Fortunately, changing your hair color in the game is a quick and easy process. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to change your hair color and answer some common questions you may have. Let's get started!

How to Change Your Hair Color in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp

Before we begin, it's important to note that you'll need to have unlocked the Able Sisters' shop in order to change your hair color. Once you've done that, follow these steps:

Step 1: Visit the Able Sisters' Shop

Head over to the Able Sisters' shop in your game. You can find it on the map by looking for the sewing machine icon. Once you're inside, talk to Mabel or Sable to access the changing room.

Step 2: Choose a New Hair Color

Inside the changing room, you'll be able to choose from a variety of hair colors. Use the arrows on the bottom of the screen to scroll through the options until you find one you like.

Step 3: Confirm Your Choice

Once you've selected your new hair color, Mabel or Sable will ask you to confirm your choice. If you're happy with your selection, choose "Yes" to finalize the change.

And that's it! Your character's hair color will now be updated to the new color you selected.

Tips for Choosing a New Hair Color

Now that you know how to change your hair color, you may be wondering which color to choose. Here are a few tips to help you make your decision:

  • Consider your character's skin tone. Some hair colors may look better with certain skin tones than others.
  • Think about your character's overall style. Do you want a natural look or something more bold and colorful?
  • Try out different colors in the changing room before making your final decision.

FAQ

Here are some common questions you may have about changing your hair color in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp:

Can I change my hair color more than once?

Yes! You can change your hair color as many times as you like.

Do I need to pay for a new hair color?

Yes, changing your hair color will cost you 3,000 Bells.

Will my hair color change in other Animal Crossing games?

No, changing your hair color in Pocket Camp will only affect your character's appearance in that game.

Hair Care

Now that you've changed your hair color, it's important to take care of your character's locks. Here are a few tips:

  • Visit Shampoodle in the city to get a haircut or style.
  • Use a mirror to check your character's hair and make any adjustments as needed.
  • Try on different hats and accessories to see how they look with your new hair color.

Changing your hair color in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp is a fun and easy way to switch up your character's appearance. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect new hair color in no time.

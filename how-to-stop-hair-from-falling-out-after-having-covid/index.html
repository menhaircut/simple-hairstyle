How To Stop Hair From Falling Out After Having Covid

Written by Daniel Oct 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss after recovering from Covid-19? This is a common problem that many people face after battling the virus. Hair loss can be a result of stress, medication, or a weakened immune system. However, there are ways to stop hair from falling out after having Covid. In this article, we will give you some tips and tricks to prevent hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.

Table of Contents:

  • Tip 1: Take Care of Your Scalp
  • Tip 2: Eat a Balanced Diet
  • Tip 3: Avoid Stress
  • Tip 4: Use Gentle Hair Products
  • Tip 5: Get Enough Sleep
  • FAQs

Tip 1: Take Care of Your Scalp

The first step in preventing hair loss is taking care of your scalp. This means keeping your scalp clean and moisturized. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Massage your scalp with your fingertips to increase blood circulation and promote hair growth. Avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals on your hair as they can damage your scalp and hair follicles.

Tip 2: Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a healthy and balanced diet is crucial for healthy hair growth. Make sure you consume enough protein, vitamins, and minerals that promote hair growth. Foods rich in biotin, iron, zinc, and vitamin D are great for your hair. Include foods such as eggs, nuts, spinach, and fish in your diet to promote healthy hair growth.

Tip 3: Avoid Stress

Stress can be a major contributor to hair loss. Try to avoid stress as much as possible by practicing relaxation techniques such as yoga, meditation, or deep breathing. Exercise regularly to release endorphins that reduce stress levels. Get enough rest and sleep to give your body time to recover and rejuvenate.

Tip 4: Use Gentle Hair Products

Using gentle hair products is important to prevent hair loss. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals such as sulfates, parabens, and alcohol. Opt for products that are natural and free from chemicals. Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid hair breakage.

Tip 5: Get Enough Sleep

Sleep is important for healthy hair growth. Lack of sleep can lead to stress and anxiety, which can cause hair loss. Aim to get at least 7-8 hours of sleep every night to promote healthy hair growth. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and damage.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take for hair to grow back after Covid?

A: It can take up to 3-6 months for hair to grow back after experiencing hair loss due to Covid.

Q: Can medication cause hair loss after Covid?

A: Yes, certain medications used to treat Covid-19 can cause hair loss as a side effect.

Q: Will hair loss after Covid be permanent?

A: In most cases, hair loss after Covid is temporary and hair will grow back with time and proper care.

By following these tips and tricks, you can prevent hair loss and promote healthy hair growth after recovering from Covid-19. Remember to take care of your scalp, eat a balanced diet, avoid stress, use gentle hair products, and get enough sleep. With time and patience, your hair will grow back healthy and strong.

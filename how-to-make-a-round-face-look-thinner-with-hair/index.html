How To Make A Round Face Look Thinner With Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may feel like your hair is working against you when it comes to making your face look thinner. However, with the right hairstyles and haircuts, you can use your hair to create the illusion of a slimmer face. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know to make a round face look thinner with hair.

How to Make a Round Face Look Thinner with Hair

The key to making a round face look thinner with hair is to create angles and length. This can be achieved through a variety of hairstyles and haircuts, including:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great way to create the illusion of length in a round face. By adding layers that start at the jawline and continue down, you can create a slimming effect. To really amp up the slimming effect, consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to elongate a round face by drawing attention to the cheekbones and eyes. Make sure to sweep your bangs to the side rather than straight across your forehead, as this can make your face look wider.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a great option for those with round faces. By keeping the hair short and close to the head, you can create a slimming effect. Consider adding layers to create volume and texture.

4. Bob with Angled Layers

A bob with angled layers can help to create the illusion of length and angles in a round face. By adding layers that are shorter in the back and longer in the front, you can create a slimming effect. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to making a round face look thinner with hair:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in making a round face look thinner with hair is to determine your face shape. This will help you to choose the right hairstyles and haircuts for your face shape.

Step 2: Choose Your Haircut

Choose a haircut that will help to create angles and length in your face. Consider long layers, side-swept bangs, a pixie cut, or a bob with angled layers.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you've chosen your haircut, it's time to style your hair. Use a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair. Consider using a straightening iron or curling iron to create waves or curls.

Step 4: Add Highlights or Lowlights

Adding highlights or lowlights to your hair can help to create dimension and texture. Consider adding lighter highlights around your face to draw attention to your cheekbones and eyes.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help make a round face look thinner with hair:

  • Consider parting your hair to the side rather than down the middle, as this can help to create angles in your face.
  • Avoid blunt bangs, as these can make your face look wider.
  • Consider using a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as these can make your face look wider.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as these can make your face look wider. Blunt bangs should also be avoided, as they can make your face look wider.

What hairstyles should I consider if I have a round face?

Consider hairstyles that create angles and length in your face, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, a pixie cut, or a bob with angled layers.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Make sure to wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type. Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and nourished. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can use your hair to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Experiment with different hairstyles and haircuts to find the perfect look for you.

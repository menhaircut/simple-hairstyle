What Does Layers Do To Curly Hair

Written by Frank Jul 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to style and maintain, but adding layers can give it a new life. In this article, we’ll explore what layers do to curly hair, how to add layers, and tips for taking care of your curly locks.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Add Layers
  • Step by Step
  • Tips for Taking Care of Curly Hair with Layers
  • FAQs
  • Curly Hair Care

Description

Adding layers to curly hair can help to create more volume, texture, and movement. Layers can also help to reduce the weight of your hair, which can make it easier to manage and style. By cutting layers, you can create a more natural-looking shape that complements your curls.

How to Add Layers

If you’re thinking about adding layers to your curly hair, it’s important to find a hairstylist who has experience cutting curly hair. Curly hair requires a different approach than straight hair, and a stylist who is not experienced in cutting curly hair may end up damaging your curls.

When adding layers to curly hair, it’s important to keep the layers long and subtle. Short layers can disrupt the curl pattern, which can result in frizz and an uneven appearance. Long layers, on the other hand, can help to enhance the natural shape of your curls and create a more flattering look.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to adding layers to curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair, then apply a curl-enhancing product to help define your curls.
  3. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties.
  4. Starting at the bottom of your hair, use a pair of scissors to cut the ends of your hair at a slight angle. Be sure to only cut a small amount of hair at a time.
  5. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way up to the top of your head.
  6. Once you’ve finished cutting the layers, style your hair as desired.

Tips for Taking Care of Curly Hair with Layers

Now that you’ve added layers to your curly hair, it’s important to take good care of your locks to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh.

FAQs

Q: Will layers make my curly hair look thinner?

A: No, layers can actually help to create the illusion of more volume and thickness in curly hair.

Q: Can layers help to reduce frizz in curly hair?

A: Yes, by reducing the weight of your hair, layers can help to prevent frizz and create a more defined curl pattern.

Curly Hair Care

Curly hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Consider using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help enhance your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

By following these tips and adding layers to your curly hair, you can create a beautiful, low-maintenance style that enhances your natural curls.

How To Wear A Beanie With Short Hair Guys

Written by Robby Aug 14, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you a guy with short hair wondering how to wear a beanie without looking silly? Look no further! In this article, we’ll show you how to rock a beanie with your short hair and look stylish and confident.

Description

Beanies are a great way to keep your head warm and add some style to your outfit. But if you have short hair, it can be a bit challenging to wear a beanie without looking like you’re trying too hard. In this article, we’ll give you some tips and tricks to help you wear a beanie with short hair like a pro.

How to Wear a Beanie with Short Hair Guys

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you wear a beanie with short hair:

  1. Choose the right beanie: Make sure the beanie fits your head comfortably and isn’t too tight or too loose.
  2. Brush your hair: Brush your hair before putting on the beanie to avoid any tangles or knots.
  3. Put on the beanie: Place the beanie on your head, making sure it covers your ears and sits comfortably on your head.
  4. Adjust the beanie: Adjust the beanie so that it sits slightly back on your head, exposing some of your forehead.
  5. Accessorize: Add some accessories like sunglasses, a watch, or a scarf to complete your look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you wear a beanie with short hair:

  • Choose a beanie color that complements your skin tone and hair color.
  • Experiment with different beanie styles like slouchy beanies or cuffed beanies.
  • Wear your beanie with confidence and own your style.
  • Don’t be afraid to mix and match patterns and colors.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a beanie with short hair:

Q: Can I wear a beanie with short hair?

A: Absolutely! Short hair looks great with a beanie.

Q: What kind of beanie should I wear with short hair?

A: You can wear any kind of beanie with short hair, but a slouchy beanie or a cuffed beanie tend to look the best.

Q: Should I tuck my hair into the beanie?

A: It’s up to you! Tucking your hair into the beanie can give you a cleaner look, but leaving your hair out can give you a more relaxed and casual look.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair when wearing a beanie. Here are some tips:

  • Don’t wear a beanie for too long as it can flatten your hair.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair as it can make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you can wear a beanie with short hair like a pro. So go ahead and rock that beanie with confidence!

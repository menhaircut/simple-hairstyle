How To Give Yourself A Fade Undercut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Why not try a fade undercut? This edgy and stylish haircut is perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd. Best of all, you can achieve this look from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we will show you how to give yourself a fade undercut.

What is a Fade Undercut?

A fade undercut is a haircut that involves shaving the sides and back of your head while leaving the top longer. The hair gradually fades from short to long, creating a stylish and unique look. This haircut is popular among men and women and can be customized to suit your personal style.

How to Give Yourself a Fade Undercut

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp while you work.

Step 2: Decide on Your Length

The first step in giving yourself a fade undercut is deciding on your desired length. The length of your hair will determine how short you need to cut the sides and back. Use your clippers to shave the sides and back of your head to your desired length.

Step 3: Create Your Fade

To create a fade, you will need to gradually blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back. Start with a longer guard on your clippers and work your way down to a shorter one. Use your clippers to create a fade line around the sides and back of your head.

Step 4: Blend the Top

Once you have created your fade line, it's time to blend the top. Use your scissors to trim any longer pieces of hair on top. Be sure to blend the top with the fade line you created earlier.

Step 5: Clean Up

After you have finished cutting your hair, it's time to clean up. Use your clippers to clean up any stray hairs around your neck and ears. You can also use your scissors to trim any uneven pieces.

Tips for Giving Yourself a Fade Undercut

- Start with a longer guard on your clippers and work your way down to a shorter one. - Use a mirror to check your progress as you go. - Take your time and be patient. It's better to go slow and get it right than to rush and make mistakes. - If you're unsure about any part of the process, ask a friend or family member for help.

FAQ

Can I give myself a fade undercut if I have long hair?

Yes, you can still achieve a fade undercut with longer hair. Just be sure to leave enough length on top to create the desired look.

How often do I need to touch up my fade undercut?

The frequency of touch-ups will depend on how quickly your hair grows. On average, you may need to touch up your fade every 2-4 weeks.

Hair Care

To maintain your fade undercut, you will need to keep it trimmed regularly. You can do this yourself or visit a stylist. It's also important to keep your hair healthy by using quality hair products and avoiding heat styling tools.

Congratulations! You have now learned how to give yourself a fade undercut. With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a stylish and edgy look that will turn heads wherever you go.

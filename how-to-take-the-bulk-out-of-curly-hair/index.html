How To Take The Bulk Out Of Curly Hair

Written by Frank Aug 08, 2023 ¬∑ 5 min read

Curly hair is a beautiful feature that many people are lucky enough to have. However, it can also be difficult to manage, especially when it comes to dealing with bulk. If you're tired of dealing with unruly curls that make your hair look bigger than it actually is, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll give you some tips on how to take the bulk out of curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Take the Bulk Out of Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Take the Bulk Out of Curly Hair

The key to taking the bulk out of curly hair is to manage your hair's texture. Curly hair tends to be thicker and coarser than straight hair, which can make it difficult to style. Here are some tips on how to take the bulk out of curly hair:

1. Get a Haircut

A good haircut can make a huge difference in managing curly hair. A professional stylist can help you choose a haircut that works with your curls and reduces the bulk. Look for a stylist who is experienced with curly hair to get the best results.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can help manage the texture of your hair and reduce bulk. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl-defining creams and gels. These products can help define your curls and prevent frizz.

3. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it more difficult to manage. Try to wash your hair every two to three days, or even less often if possible. When you do wash your hair, use a gentle shampoo that won't dry out your curls.

4. Use a Diffuser

A diffuser is a tool that attaches to your hair dryer and helps to distribute heat evenly through your curls. Using a diffuser can help to reduce frizz and bulk, and give your curls more definition. If you don't have a diffuser, you can also try air-drying your hair.

5. Try a Hair Mask

A hair mask can help to nourish your curls and reduce frizz. Look for a hair mask that is specifically designed for curly hair, and use it once a week for best results. You can also try using natural ingredients like coconut oil or avocado to make your own hair mask.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to take the bulk out of curly hair:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any buildup and prepare your hair for styling.

Step 2: Apply Products

Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair, using a small amount at a time. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and use it to dry your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using a low heat setting. Use your hands to scrunch your curls as you dry them.

Step 4: Finish with Oil

Apply a small amount of oil to your hair to add shine and reduce frizz. Use a lightweight oil like argan oil or jojoba oil, and apply it sparingly to avoid weighing down your curls.

Tips for Managing Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Don't rub your hair with a towel to dry it. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons or flat irons.
  • Avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your curls and make them look limp.

FAQs

Q: Can I take the bulk out of my curly hair without cutting it?

A: Yes, you can take the bulk out of your curly hair without cutting it by using the right products and styling techniques. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, and use a diffuser when drying your hair to help reduce bulk.

Q: What kind of haircut is best for reducing bulk in curly hair?

A: A layered haircut is a good choice for reducing bulk in curly hair. Ask your stylist to add long layers that will help to break up the bulk and give your curls more definition.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: You should wash your curly hair every two to three days, or even less often if possible. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it more difficult to manage.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair requires a little extra effort, but it's worth it to keep your curls looking their best. Here are some tips for hair care for curly hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't dry out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can take the bulk out of your curly hair and enjoy beautiful, defined curls that are easy to manage.

Women s Short Back And Sides Haircuts How To And Tips

Written by Eveline Oct 11, 2023 · 4 min read
Looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd? Women’s short back and sides haircuts may be just what you need. This hairstyle is becoming increasingly popular among women who want to make a statement with their hair. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about women's short back and sides haircuts, including how to get the look, tips for styling, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Get the Look

A short back and sides haircut for women involves shaving or cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head short, while leaving the hair on top longer. This style is often associated with a punk or edgy look but can be adapted to suit any style. Before getting a short back and sides haircut, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. This style works best on those with an oval or heart-shaped face and straight or wavy hair. If you have curly hair, it may be more difficult to achieve the desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're ready to take the plunge and get a short back and sides haircut, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the look: 1. Start by washing and drying your hair. 2. Section off the hair on top of your head and clip it up out of the way. 3. Use clippers to shave the hair on the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way shorter until you achieve the desired length. 4. Use scissors to blend the hair on top with the shorter sides and back. 5. Style your hair as desired using products such as pomade or wax.

Tips for Styling

Once you've got your short back and sides haircut, here are some tips to help you style it: - To add volume to the hair on top, use a round brush while blow-drying. - For a sleek and polished look, apply a small amount of pomade or wax to the hair on top and comb it back. - If you have a shorter cut, you can add texture to the hair on top by using a salt spray.

FAQ

Q: Will a short back and sides haircut suit me?
A: This style works best on those with an oval or heart-shaped face and straight or wavy hair. Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short back and sides haircut?
A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up. Use bobby pins to secure the hair on top into a ponytail or bun. Q: How often will I need to get my hair cut?
A: It will depend on how quickly your hair grows, but you will likely need to get it cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your short back and sides haircut looking its best, here are some hair care tips to keep in mind: - Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from becoming too dry. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and nourished. - Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair. In conclusion, a short back and sides haircut for women is a bold and edgy style that can be adapted to suit any look. By following the steps and tips outlined in this article, you can achieve the perfect short back and sides haircut and keep it looking its best. So why not give it a try?

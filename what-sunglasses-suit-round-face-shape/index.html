What Sunglasses Suit Round Face Shape A Guide For 2023

Written by Bardi Jun 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying on sunglasses that just don't seem to fit your round face shape? It can be frustrating to spend time and money on a pair of shades, only to find that they don't flatter your features. But fear not! With this guide, we'll walk you through the sunglasses that suit round face shapes, so you can find your perfect pair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round face shapes are characterized by soft, curved features and a lack of sharp angles. If you have a round face, you'll want to look for sunglasses that add definition and angles to your face. Avoid round or circular frames, as these can make your face appear even rounder.

Instead, opt for frames that are angular, square, or rectangular. These shapes will provide balance to your face and make it appear slimmer. Look for frames that are wider than they are tall, as this will help elongate your face.

How To

When shopping for sunglasses, keep in mind that the size of the frame should be proportional to the size of your face. If you have a smaller face, look for smaller frames. If you have a larger face, look for larger frames.

You'll also want to consider the color of the frames. Darker colors can be slimming, so black, brown, or tortoise shell frames are a good choice. Avoid bright colors or patterns, as these can draw attention to the roundness of your face.

Step By Step

1. Determine your face shape.

2. Look for sunglasses with angular, square, or rectangular frames.

3. Choose frames that are wider than they are tall.

4. Consider the size of the frames in proportion to your face.

5. Opt for darker frame colors.

Tips

When trying on sunglasses, take a close look at how they sit on your face. You want to make sure they don't rest on your cheeks or touch your eyebrows. If they do, they may not be the right fit for your face shape.

Another tip is to choose sunglasses with a high bridge. This will help lift the frames off your face and create more definition.

FAQ

What sunglasses should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid round or circular frames, as these can make your face appear even rounder.

Can aviator sunglasses suit a round face?

Yes, aviator sunglasses can suit a round face if they have angular frames and a high bridge.

What frame colors should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid bright colors or patterns, as these can draw attention to the roundness of your face.

Hair Care

If you have a round face, you may want to consider a hairstyle that adds angles and definition to your features. A layered cut with side-swept bangs can help elongate your face and create more balance.

When wearing sunglasses, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that cover your face, as this can hide the slimming effects of your shades. Opt for hairstyles that pull your hair away from your face, such as a sleek ponytail or messy bun.

Best Swimsuit For Large Bust And Hips Your Ultimate Guide

Summer is just around the corner, which means it's time to hit the beach! But for women who have a larger bust and hips, finding the perfect swimsuit can be a daunting task. You want something that is both comfortable and flattering, without sacrificing style. Luckily, there are plenty of options out there that cater to your body type. In this article, we'll guide you through the best swimsuits for large bust and hips, and give you tips on how to choose the perfect one for you.

How to Choose the Best Swimsuit for Large Bust and Hips

Before we dive into the different styles of swimsuits that are perfect for women with larger busts and hips, let's go over some tips on how to choose the best one for your body type.

1. Look for Supportive Features

When choosing a swimsuit, it's important to look for features that will provide support for your bust. Look for swimsuits with underwire, thick straps, or a built-in bra. These features will help keep everything in place while you're swimming, and prevent any wardrobe malfunctions.

2. Choose the Right Fabric

The fabric of your swimsuit can make a big difference in how it fits and feels. Look for swimsuits made from materials that are stretchy, but also provide enough support. Nylon and spandex are great options, as they are both durable and stretchy.

3. Focus on Flattering Your Body Type

When it comes to swimsuits, there is no one-size-fits-all solution. It's important to focus on finding a swimsuit that flatters your body type, rather than trying to fit into a certain style. High-waisted bottoms, for example, can be a great option for women with larger hips, as they provide coverage and support.

The Best Swimsuits for Large Bust and Hips

Now that you know what to look for in a swimsuit, let's take a look at some of the best styles for women with larger busts and hips.

1. One-Piece Swimsuits

One-piece swimsuits are a great option for women with larger busts and hips, as they provide plenty of coverage and support. Look for styles with built-in bras or underwire for extra support. Ruching and draping can also be flattering, as they help to create a more hourglass shape.

2. High-Waisted Bikinis

High-waisted bikinis are perfect for women with larger hips, as they provide coverage and support. Look for styles with a thicker waistband, as this will help to smooth out any bulges or bumps. Pair with a bikini top that has underwire or thick straps for added support.

3. Halter Tops

Halter tops are a great option for women with larger busts, as they provide plenty of support. Look for styles with thicker straps, as this will help to distribute the weight of your bust more evenly. Ruching and draping can also be flattering, as they help to create a more hourglass shape.

Tips for Caring for Your Swimsuit

Now that you've found the perfect swimsuit, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your swimsuit looking great all summer long:

1. Rinse Your Swimsuit After Each Use

After you've worn your swimsuit, rinse it with cool water to remove any chlorine or salt. This will help to prevent fading and damage to the fabric.

2. Hand Wash Your Swimsuit

Machine washing can be harsh on your swimsuit, so it's best to hand wash it instead. Use a mild detergent and cool water, and gently squeeze out any water before laying it flat to dry.

3. Avoid Sunscreen and Tanning Oils

Sunscreen and tanning oils can cause damage to your swimsuit, so it's best to avoid getting them on your swimsuit. Apply sunscreen or tanning oil before putting on your swimsuit, and wait for it to dry before putting it on.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the best swimsuit for a large bust?

The best swimsuit for a large bust is one that provides plenty of support. Look for swimsuits with underwire or a built-in bra, and thicker straps for added support.

2. What is the best swimsuit for larger hips?

The best swimsuit for larger hips is one that provides coverage and support. High-waisted bottoms can be a great option, as they provide coverage and help to smooth out any bulges or bumps.

3. How do I know if a swimsuit is the right size?

When trying on swimsuits, make sure to check the fit of the bust and hips. The swimsuit should be snug, but not too tight. You should also be able to move comfortably in it, without it riding up or falling down.

Choosing the best swimsuit for large bust and hips can be a challenge, but with these tips and styles, you'll be able to find the perfect one for you. Remember to focus on finding a swimsuit that flatters your body type, and take care of it properly to ensure it lasts all summer long.

How To Grow Thick Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of having thin and lifeless hair? Do you dream of having voluminous, thick and wavy locks? Well, you’re not alone! Many people struggle with achieving the hair of their dreams. But fear not, as we have compiled a comprehensive guide on how to grow thick wavy hair. Read on to find out how you can achieve your hair goals.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Thick, wavy hair is a popular hair type that many people aspire to have. It’s characterized by hair strands that are naturally wavy, with a thickness that gives it volume and body. However, genetics play a significant role in determining your hair type. If you don’t have naturally thick and wavy hair, don’t worry, as there are ways to achieve it. With the right hair care routine, products, and lifestyle changes, you can grow thick wavy hair that will make heads turn.

How to

Before we dive into the steps you can take to grow thick wavy hair, it’s essential to understand that it won’t happen overnight. Growing hair requires patience, commitment, and consistency. Here are some steps you can take to achieve your hair goals:

1. Nourish Your Hair from the Inside Out

What you eat affects the health of your hair. A diet that’s rich in vitamins, minerals, and protein can help nourish your hair from the inside out. Foods like salmon, nuts, and leafy greens are excellent sources of nutrients that promote hair growth.

2. Keep Your Scalp Healthy

Your scalp health plays a vital role in hair growth. A healthy scalp promotes healthy hair growth, while an unhealthy scalp can lead to hair loss. Keep your scalp clean and moisturized, and avoid using harsh chemicals that can damage your hair and scalp.

3. Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can help promote hair growth and prevent damage. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type, and avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.

4. Avoid Using Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and lead to breakage. Avoid using these tools as much as possible, and if you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

5. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends, which can lead to breakage and hair loss. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and promote growth.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to grow thick wavy hair:

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

Before you start any hair care routine, it’s essential to determine your hair type. This will help you choose the right products and styling techniques that work best for your hair.

Step 2: Develop a Hair Care Routine

Developing a hair care routine that works for your hair type is crucial to achieving your hair goals. This routine should include washing your hair regularly, conditioning, and styling techniques that promote hair growth.

Step 3: Use Hair Growth Products

Using hair growth products like biotin supplements, hair growth shampoos, and conditioners can help promote hair growth and thickness.

Step 4: Protect Your Hair

Protecting your hair from damage is essential to achieving thick wavy hair. Avoid using harsh chemicals, heat styling tools, and tight hairstyles that can cause damage.

Tips

Here are some additional tips you can follow to grow thick wavy hair:

  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote blood flow and encourage hair growth.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid hair breakage and frizz.

FAQ

Q: Can I grow thick wavy hair if I have naturally thin hair?

A: Yes, with the right hair care routine, products, and lifestyle changes, you can grow thick wavy hair, even if you have naturally thin hair.

Q: How long does it take to grow thick wavy hair?

A: Hair growth varies from person to person, but on average, hair grows about half an inch per month. Growing thick wavy hair takes time, patience, and consistency.

Q: Can I use hair extensions to achieve thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, hair extensions can be a temporary solution to achieving thick wavy hair. However, it’s essential to choose high-quality extensions that won’t cause damage to your natural hair.

Hair Care

Here are some additional hair care tips you can follow to promote hair growth and achieve thick wavy hair:

  • Choose a shampoo and conditioner that’s specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair dyes that can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to promote hair growth and prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and steps, you can achieve the thick wavy hair of your dreams. Remember, growing hair takes time, patience, and consistency, so don’t give up if you don’t see results right away. With the right hair care routine, products, and lifestyle changes, you can achieve your hair goals and feel confident in your hair.

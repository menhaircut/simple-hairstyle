Best Sunglasses for Heart Face Shape | 2023 Guide

Are you struggling to find the perfect pair of sunglasses for your heart-shaped face? Look no further! We have put together a guide to help you find the best sunglasses for your face shape. Read on to learn more!

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The best sunglasses for this face shape should balance out the width of the forehead and draw attention to the eyes. Look for sunglasses with rounded edges that are wider at the top and narrower at the bottom.

How to Find the Best Sunglasses for Heart Face Shape

The first step in finding the best sunglasses for heart face shape is to determine your face shape. This can be done by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have a heart-shaped face.

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to start shopping for sunglasses. Look for frames that are wider at the top and narrower at the bottom. Round or oval frames are a great choice, as they balance out the width of the forehead and draw attention to the eyes.

Step by Step Guide

  1. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Determine your face shape.
  3. Choose sunglasses with rounded edges that are wider at the top and narrower at the bottom.
  4. Try on different styles to find the perfect fit.
  5. Consider the color and material of the frames.

Tips and Tricks

  • Look for sunglasses with adjustable nose pads for a comfortable fit.
  • Consider polarized lenses to reduce glare and improve visibility.
  • Choose frames that complement your personal style.
  • Don't be afraid to try bold colors or patterns.

FAQs

What types of sunglasses should I avoid with a heart-shaped face?

Avoid sunglasses that are too small or too angular, as they can accentuate the narrowness of the chin.

Can I wear aviator sunglasses with a heart-shaped face?

Yes, aviator sunglasses can be a great choice for heart-shaped faces. Look for frames that are wider at the top and have a rounded shape.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, consider a hairstyle that adds volume to the chin area. Layers, side-swept bangs, and chin-length bobs can all be flattering. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too sleek, as they can emphasize the narrowness of the chin.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect pair of sunglasses for your heart-shaped face. Happy shopping!

Thick Hair Short Spiky Haircuts For Over 60 A Guide To Looking Fabulous

Written by Bardi Jul 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 60 and have thick hair? Do you want a haircut that's easy to maintain and looks great? Look no further than short spiky haircuts. In this article, we'll give you a guide to getting the perfect short spiky haircut for your thick hair. We'll cover everything from how to get the cut to how to care for your hair after the cut. Let's get started!

Description

Short spiky haircuts are a great option for women over 60 with thick hair. These haircuts are easy to maintain and can be styled in different ways to suit your style. The spiky look adds volume and texture to your hair, making it look more youthful and vibrant. Whether you have straight or curly hair, short spiky haircuts work well for all hair types.

How to Get the Cut

The first step to getting a short spiky haircut is to find a good stylist who has experience cutting thick hair. Look for a stylist who has worked with older clients and knows how to create a modern, edgy look. When you meet with your stylist, bring photos of spiky haircuts that you like, and discuss which style would work best for your hair type and face shape.

Your stylist will likely start by shampooing and conditioning your hair to make it easier to work with. They will then use scissors or clippers to cut your hair to the desired length. Next, they will use a texturizing technique to create the spiky look. This may involve using thinning shears or a razor to add texture to your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a short spiky haircut:

  1. Find a stylist who has experience cutting thick hair.
  2. Bring photos of spiky haircuts that you like and discuss which style would work best for you.
  3. Shampoo and condition your hair.
  4. Cut your hair to the desired length.
  5. Use texturizing techniques to create the spiky look.
  6. Style your hair using a gel or wax.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short spiky haircut:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Invest in a good styling product such as a gel or wax.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short spiky haircuts:

1. Will a short spiky haircut make me look younger?

Short spiky haircuts can make you look more youthful and vibrant. The spiky look adds volume and texture to your hair, which can help to create a more youthful appearance.

2. Can I style my short spiky haircut in different ways?

Yes, short spiky haircuts can be styled in different ways to suit your style. You can use a gel or wax to create different looks, such as a messy, tousled look or a sleek, polished look.

3. Will a short spiky haircut work for curly hair?

Yes, short spiky haircuts work well for curly hair. The spiky look adds texture and definition to your curls, making them look more defined and bouncy.

Hair Care

After getting a short spiky haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as hair dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

With these tips, you can keep your short spiky haircut looking fabulous for years to come.

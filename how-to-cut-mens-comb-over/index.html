How To Cut Men s Comb Over In Relaxed English Language

Written by Oliver Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a timeless hairstyle that exudes sophistication and style, the comb over is a perfect choice. It is a classic cut that can be styled in various ways, making it versatile enough to suit different personalities and preferences. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut men's comb over in relaxed English language, so you can achieve the desired look with ease.

Description

The comb over is a hairstyle that involves combing the hair to one side, creating a sleek and refined appearance. It is a popular choice among men because it is easy to style and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. The length of the hair on top of the head is usually longer than the sides, creating a contrast that accentuates the facial features.

How to Cut Men's Comb Over

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools, including a pair of scissors, clippers, and a comb. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash the hair thoroughly to remove any dirt or product buildup. Dry the hair using a towel or hairdryer until it is completely dry. This will make it easier to cut and style the hair.

Step 2: Determine the Length of the Hair

Use the clippers to trim the sides and back of the hair to the desired length. The length should be shorter than the hair on top of the head, creating a contrast. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard until you achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Cut the Top of the Hair

Use the scissors to cut the hair on top of the head. Start at the front and work your way to the back, cutting the hair to the desired length. Make sure the hair is evenly distributed on both sides.

Step 4: Create a Parting

Use the comb to create a parting on one side of the head. The parting should be slightly off-center, creating a natural look. Use the comb to smooth the hair on both sides of the parting.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Apply a small amount of styling product to the hair, such as pomade or wax. Use your fingers to style the hair to the desired shape. You can create a slicked-back look or a more textured look, depending on your preference.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect comb over:

  • Start with a longer guard when cutting the sides and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard until you achieve the desired length.
  • Use the scissors to cut the hair on top of the head, creating a more natural look.
  • Create a parting slightly off-center to create a natural look.
  • Apply a small amount of styling product to the hair to achieve the desired shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the comb over:

Q: What face shape is best for a comb over?

A: The comb over is suitable for most face shapes, but it works best for oval and square faces.

Q: How long should the hair be for a comb over?

A: The hair on top of the head should be long enough to comb over to one side, creating a contrast with the shorter sides.

Q: What styling product should I use for a comb over?

A: You can use pomade, wax, or gel to style the hair. Choose a product that suits your hair type and desired look.

Hair Care

To maintain the comb over, you should wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it smooth and shiny. Trim the hair every 4 to 6 weeks to keep it looking sharp. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant if you need to use a hairdryer or styling tool.

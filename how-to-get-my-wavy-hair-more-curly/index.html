How to Get My Wavy Hair More Curly

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, itâs important to understand the difference between wavy and curly hair. Wavy hair has a loose, S-shaped pattern that falls somewhere between straight and curly. Curly hair, on the other hand, has a tighter, more defined spiral pattern.

If you have wavy hair and want to make it more curly, there are several things you can do:

  • Use the right products
  • Change your styling routine
  • Try heat styling
  • Get a perm

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, letâs dive into the step-by-step guide for how to get your wavy hair more curly:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then gently towel dry until your hair is damp but not soaking wet.

Step 2: Apply a curl-enhancing product

Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel, to your hair. Be sure to distribute the product evenly from roots to ends.

Step 3: Scrunch your hair

Scrunch your hair by gently squeezing sections of it in an upward motion. This will help encourage your natural waves to turn into curls.

Step 4: Diffuse your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and scrunch your hair as you dry it.

Step 5: Finish with a holding product

Apply a holding product, such as hairspray or a setting spray, to your hair to help your curls hold their shape.

Tips for Enhancing Your Curls

Here are a few additional tips for enhancing your curls:

  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair, as this can disrupt your natural curl pattern.
  • Sleep with your hair in a pineapple or loose bun to help preserve your curls overnight.
  • Experiment with different curl-enhancing products to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair and disrupt your natural curl pattern.

FAQs

Here are a few frequently asked questions about how to get wavy hair more curly:

Can I get my wavy hair permanently curled?

Yes, you can get a perm to permanently curl your hair. However, perms can be damaging to your hair and may not work well on all hair types. Itâs important to consult with a professional stylist before getting a perm.

Can I use a diffuser on my straight hair?

Yes, you can use a diffuser on straight hair to add volume and texture. However, it may not create the same defined curls as it would on wavy or curly hair.

Hair Care Advice for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as it can dry it out and make it frizzy.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can disrupt your natural curl pattern. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze excess water out of your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when itâs wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to help keep it moisturized and frizz-free.

With these tips and tricks, you can enhance your natural waves and achieve a more defined, curly look. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques to find the ones that work best for your hair.

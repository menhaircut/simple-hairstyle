Description

An inverted triangle face shape is a facial structure that is wider at the forehead and narrower at the jawline, creating a triangular shape. This face shape is commonly seen in models and celebrities and is considered to be a less common face shape. It is also known as a heart-shaped face.

People with an inverted triangle face shape typically have a broad forehead, high cheekbones, a narrow jawline, and a pointed chin. This face shape is considered to be one of the most visually striking and attractive.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape is crucial in finding the right hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that will flatter your features. There are a few steps to follow in determining your face shape:

  1. Pull your hair back and away from your face
  2. Stand in front of a mirror and examine your face shape
  3. Compare your face shape to the following shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond
  4. Take note of the widest part of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face

Characteristics of an Inverted Triangle Face Shape

If you have an inverted triangle face shape, you'll notice the following characteristics:

  • A broad forehead
  • High cheekbones
  • A narrow jawline
  • A pointed chin

Step-by-Step Guide on Inverted Triangle Face Shape

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to highlight and flatter an inverted triangle face shape:

  1. Highlight your cheekbones: Use a blush or bronzer to add definition to your cheekbones, making them appear more prominent.
  2. Avoid adding volume to your forehead: Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to your forehead, such as bangs or high ponytails.
  3. Add volume to your chin: Use a hairstyle that adds volume to your chin, such as a bob or a layered haircut.
  4. Wear statement earrings: Statement earrings draw attention to your cheekbones and jawline, enhancing your features.
  5. Use a contouring kit: Use a contouring kit to add definition to your jawline, creating a more balanced look.

Tips for Inverted Triangle Face Shape

Here are some tips for people with an inverted triangle face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your forehead
  • Wear statement earrings to enhance your cheekbones and jawline
  • Use a contouring kit to add definition to your jawline
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your forehead look larger
  • Wear sunglasses with rounded frames to soften your features

FAQs About Inverted Triangle Face Shape

What hairstyles flatter an inverted triangle face shape?

Hairstyles that flatter an inverted triangle face shape include bobs, layered haircuts, and hairstyles that add volume to the chin area.

What accessories can I wear to enhance my features?

Statement earrings and sunglasses with rounded frames can enhance your cheekbones and soften your features.

Can makeup help balance an inverted triangle face shape?

Yes, contouring can help add definition to your jawline and balance your facial features.

Hair Care for Inverted Triangle Face Shape

When it comes to hair care for an inverted triangle face shape, it's important to choose hairstyles that flatter your features. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your forehead
  • Use a hairstyle that adds volume to your chin, such as a bob or a layered haircut
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your forehead look larger
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair

By following these tips and tricks, people with an inverted triangle face shape can enhance their features and feel confident in their appearance.

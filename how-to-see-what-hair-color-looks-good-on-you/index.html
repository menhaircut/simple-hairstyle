How To See What Hair Color Looks Good On You A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you thinking about changing your hair color but don't know which shade will suit you best? With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect hair color. However, with a few simple steps, you can find the right hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. In this guide, we'll show you how to see what hair color looks good on you.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding the perfect hair color is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone can be categorized into three types: cool, warm, and neutral. To find your skin tone, you can do a simple test by checking the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell whether they're green or blue, you have a neutral skin tone.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color for Your Skin Tone

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can choose a hair color that complements it. If you have a cool skin tone, consider shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, or burgundy. If you have a warm skin tone, consider shades like honey blonde, golden brown, or auburn. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can choose from a wide range of hair colors.

How to Test Hair Colors

Before committing to a hair color, it's important to test it first. You can do this by using temporary hair color sprays or chalks. These products allow you to see how a particular hair color looks on you without damaging your hair. You can also try on wigs or use virtual makeover apps to see how different hair colors look on you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you've found the perfect hair color, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair color:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using UV protection products
  • Get regular touch-ups to maintain your hair color

FAQs

What if I Have Multiple Skin Tones?

It's common to have multiple skin tones, especially if you have a lot of sun exposure. In this case, choose a hair color that complements the dominant skin tone.

Can I Dye My Hair at Home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it's important to follow the instructions carefully and choose a high-quality hair dye.

How Often Should I Touch Up My Hair Color?

It depends on the type of hair color and how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should touch up your hair color every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as it can strip away natural oils
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth

By following these tips, you can find the perfect hair color and maintain healthy, beautiful hair.

Description

In Pokemon Go, your avatar represents you in the game. You can customize your avatar's appearance, including their hairstyle, clothes, and accessories. Changing your hairstyle can be a fun way to mix things up and give your avatar a fresh look.

While changing your clothes and accessories is easy, changing your hairstyle requires a bit more effort. In order to change your hairstyle, you'll need to visit a special location in the game. But don't worry, we'll walk you through the process step-by-step.

How to Change Your Hairstyle in Pokemon Go

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, here are a few things you need to know:

  • You can only change your hairstyle once per day.
  • Changing your hairstyle costs 1,000 PokeCoins.
  • You can choose from a variety of hairstyles, but some are exclusive to certain genders.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to change your hairstyle in Pokemon Go:

  1. Open the game and tap on your avatar in the bottom left corner of the screen.
  2. Tap on the "Style" button.
  3. Scroll down to the "Hair" section and tap on it.
  4. Select the hairstyle you want from the available options.
  5. Confirm your selection and pay the 1,000 PokeCoins fee.
  6. Enjoy your new hairstyle!

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when changing your hairstyle:

  • Take your time choosing a hairstyle. You can only change it once per day, so make sure you're happy with your selection.
  • Some hairstyles are exclusive to certain genders. Make sure you're choosing a hairstyle that's available for your avatar's gender.
  • Consider your avatar's overall appearance when choosing a hairstyle. You want your hair to complement your outfit and accessories.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few common questions about changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go:

  • Can I change my hairstyle more than once per day? No, you can only change your hairstyle once per day.
  • How much does it cost to change my hairstyle? It costs 1,000 PokeCoins to change your hairstyle.
  • Can I choose any hairstyle I want? You can choose from a variety of hairstyles, but some are exclusive to certain genders.

Hair Care in Pokemon Go

Okay, so your avatar's hair isn't actually going to grow or get dirty in Pokemon Go. But that doesn't mean you can't take care of it!

When choosing your avatar's hairstyle, consider the following:

  • Does the hairstyle match your avatar's personality?
  • Does the hairstyle go well with your avatar's clothing and accessories?
  • Is the hairstyle easy to maintain?

By taking these factors into consideration, you can ensure that your avatar always looks their best in Pokemon Go.

That's it for our guide on how to change your hairstyle in Pokemon Go. We hope you found this article helpful. Happy gaming!

How To Style Curly Hair With Side Bangs Tips And Tricks

Curly hair with side bangs is a popular hairstyle that can look great on anyone. However, styling curly hair can be a challenge, especially when it comes to adding side bangs to the mix. In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to style curly hair with side bangs.

How to Style Curly Hair with Side Bangs

Styling curly hair with side bangs requires some work, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stunning look. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Start with clean hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and free of any product buildup. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair to keep your curls healthy and defined.

2. Apply styling product

Apply a styling product that's designed for curly hair to your damp hair. This will help define your curls and keep them in place. Choose a product that's lightweight and won't weigh down your hair, such as a curl cream or gel.

3. Blow-dry your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and prevent frizz. Use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

4. Style your side bangs

Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your side bangs. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs if necessary.

5. Finish with hairspray

Use a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling curly hair with side bangs:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a lightweight styling product to your damp hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair.
  4. Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your side bangs.
  5. Straighten your bangs with a flat iron if necessary.
  6. Apply a light hold hairspray to finish your look.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair with Side Bangs

Here are some tips to help you style your curly hair with side bangs:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before styling to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking sharp.

FAQs

Can I wear my side bangs curly?

Yes, you can wear your side bangs curly. To do this, simply style your bangs with a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer or let them air dry naturally.

How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair with side bangs?

To prevent frizz in your curly hair with side bangs, use a styling product that's designed for curly hair, avoid using heat styling tools too often, and sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair with Side Bangs

Here are some hair care tips to keep your curly hair with side bangs looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry out your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a beautiful and stylish look with curly hair and side bangs. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

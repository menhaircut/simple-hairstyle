How To Cut The Top Of Your Own Hair With Clippers

Written by Bardi Nov 06, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts or waiting in long lines at the salon? Cutting your own hair can be a great way to save time and money. In this article, we will discuss how to cut the top of your own hair with clippers.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Cutting the top of your own hair with clippers can be a bit intimidating at first, but with a little practice and patience, it can be done. Clippers are a great tool for a variety of haircuts, from buzz cuts to fades. They are relatively easy to use and come in a variety of sizes and shapes. By following the steps below, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, it’s important to have the right tools. You will need a set of clippers, a comb, and a mirror. It’s also a good idea to have a towel or cape to catch any hair clippings.

Step 1: Choose the right guard size

The guard is the attachment that goes on the clippers and determines the length of the hair. The number on the guard corresponds to the length of the hair in millimeters. For example, a #1 guard will leave the hair 1 millimeter long, while a #4 guard will leave the hair 4 millimeters long. Choose the guard size based on the length of the hair you want to leave on top.

Step 2: Wash and dry your hair

Before you start cutting your hair, it’s important to wash and dry it. This will help remove any dirt or product buildup and make it easier to cut.

Step 3: Comb your hair

Using a comb, comb your hair straight up from the scalp. This will help you see where the longer hair is and where you need to cut.

Step 4: Start cutting

Starting at the front of your head, place the clippers at the hairline and work your way back. Use the comb as a guide to help you cut the hair evenly. Move the clippers in an upward motion to cut the hair. Repeat this process until you have cut all the hair on top of your head.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut the top of your own hair with clippers:

  • Practice makes perfect. Don’t expect to get it perfect the first time.
  • Use a mirror to help you see the back of your head.
  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down if you want to go shorter.
  • Don’t cut your hair too short. You can always go shorter, but you can’t go longer.

FAQ

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. It can be a bit intimidating at first, but with a little practice and patience, it can be done.

Hair Care

After you have finished cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Conclusion

Cutting the top of your own hair with clippers can be a great way to save time and money. By following the steps and tips outlined in this article, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Remember to practice and be patient, and you’ll be a pro in no time!

How to Create Male Braids Hairstyles 2020 Short Hair

Creating male braids hairstyles 2020 short hair may seem daunting at first, but with a bit of practice, you'll be able to master the technique. Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a basic braid:

  1. Start by parting your hair into sections. The size of the sections will depend on how thick you want your braid to be.
  2. Take the section of hair closest to your forehead and divide it into three equal parts.
  3. Take the right section and cross it over the center section.
  4. Take the left section and cross it over the center section.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4, alternating between the right and left sections, until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Tie off the end of the braid with a hair elastic.

Once you have mastered the basic braid, you can experiment with different variations, such as the French braid, Dutch braid, and fishtail braid. Each of these braids has a unique look, and they can be combined with other braids to create intricate designs.

Types of Male Braids Hairstyles 2020 Short Hair

There are several different types of male braids hairstyles 2020 short hair that you can try. Here are some of the most popular:

The Man Bun Braid

This hairstyle involves braiding the hair at the back of the head and tying it into a man bun. It's a great option if you want to keep your hair out of your face but still want to maintain a stylish look.

The Side Braid

The side braid involves braiding a section of hair on one side of the head and tying it off at the end. It's a simple yet effective way to add some texture to your hair.

The Box Braid

The box braid involves braiding the hair into small, square-shaped sections. This style is popular among men with natural hair, as it can help to protect the hair and promote growth.

Tips for Maintenance

Maintaining male braids hairstyles 2020 short hair is crucial if you want to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your braids:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and keep your scalp healthy.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it difficult to braid.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid pulling your braids too tight, as this can cause tension and damage to your hair.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet at night to prevent frizz and preserve your braids.

FAQ

Can men with short hair wear braids?

Yes, men with short hair can definitely wear braids. In fact, braids can be a great way to add some texture and style to short hair.

How long do male braids hairstyles 2020 short hair last?

The length of time that male braids hairstyles 2020 short hair last will depend on several factors, including the type of braid and how well you take care of your hair. On average, braids can last anywhere from a few days to several weeks.

Do male braids hairstyles 2020 short hair require a lot of maintenance?

Male braids hairstyles 2020 short hair do require some maintenance, but it's not difficult to keep them looking their best. Regular washing and conditioning, as well as avoiding heavy styling products, can help to keep your braids looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential if you want to maintain healthy, strong hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, and protein.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Visit a hairstylist regularly to keep your hair looking its best.

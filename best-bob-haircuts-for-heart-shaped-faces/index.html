Best Bob Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Daniel Aug 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying to find the perfect haircut for your heart shaped face? Look no further than the classic bob haircut, which is both versatile and flattering for this face shape. In this article, we will explore the best bob haircuts for heart shaped faces, as well as provide tips on how to style and care for your new haircut.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Bob Haircut for Your Heart Shaped Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide for Styling Your Bob Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Bob Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Bob Haircut

Description

The heart shaped face is characterized by a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The bob haircut is perfect for this face shape because it helps to balance out the broad forehead and narrow chin, while also accentuating the cheekbones. There are several different types of bob haircuts that work well for heart shaped faces, including the classic bob, the long bob (also known as the "lob"), and the asymmetrical bob.

How to Choose the Best Bob Haircut for Your Heart Shaped Face

When choosing a bob haircut for your heart shaped face, it's important to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the best bob haircut:

  • If you have thin hair, consider a shorter bob haircut with layers to add volume.
  • If you have thick hair, consider a longer bob haircut with blunt ends to control the volume.
  • If you have a busy lifestyle, consider a low-maintenance bob haircut that requires minimal styling.
  • If you have a more formal or conservative personal style, consider a classic bob haircut with a blunt cut.
  • If you have a more edgy or trendy personal style, consider an asymmetrical bob haircut with a side-swept fringe.

Step-by-Step Guide for Styling Your Bob Haircut

Styling your bob haircut is easy and can be customized to suit your personal style. Here is a step-by-step guide for styling your bob haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  4. If you have a classic bob haircut, use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create a sleek finish.
  5. If you have an asymmetrical bob haircut, use a curling iron to add loose waves and texture.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bob Haircut

Maintaining your bob haircut is key to keeping it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips for maintaining your bob:

  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your haircut looking sharp.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair with hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about bob haircuts for heart shaped faces:

  1. Can I wear bangs with a bob haircut?
    Yes, you can wear bangs with a bob haircut. Side-swept bangs are particularly flattering for heart shaped faces.
  2. What hair color looks best with a bob haircut?
    The best hair color for a bob haircut depends on your skin tone and personal style. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect hair color for you.
  3. How do I know if a bob haircut will suit my face shape?
    Consult with your hairstylist to determine if a bob haircut will suit your face shape and hair texture.

Hair Care Tips for Your Bob Haircut

In addition to regular trims and deep conditioning treatments, there are several other hair care tips that can help keep your bob haircut looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair products that contain alcohol.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage.

With the right bob haircut and hair care routine, you can achieve a stylish and flattering look for your heart shaped face. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect bob haircut for you.

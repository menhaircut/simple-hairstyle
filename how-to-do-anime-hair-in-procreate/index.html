How To Do Anime Hair In Procreate A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're an artist who loves creating anime characters, then you know how important it is to get the hair just right. In this tutorial, we'll show you how to do anime hair in Procreate, the popular digital art app for the iPad. From sketching to coloring, we'll cover everything you need to know to create stunning anime hairstyles.

Description

Doing anime hair in Procreate can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, you can create amazing results. In this tutorial, we'll show you how to use Procreate's brushes, layers, and other features to create different types of anime hair, from spiky to curly.

How To

Here's how to do anime hair in Procreate:

Step 1: Sketch Your Character

Start by sketching your character's head and hair in Procreate. Use the sketching brush to create the basic shape of the hair, and then refine it until it looks the way you want.

Step 2: Add Layers

Once you're happy with your sketch, add a new layer for the hair. This will make it easier to color and add details without affecting the sketch underneath.

Step 3: Choose Your Brush

Procreate has a variety of brushes to choose from, including ones specifically designed for anime hair. Experiment with different brushes to find the one that works best for your style.

Step 4: Pick Your Colors

Now it's time to add color to your hair. Choose a base color and then add highlights and shadows to create depth and dimension. You can also use gradients and textures to add interest to your hair.

Step 5: Add Details

Finally, add details to your hair, such as individual strands or hair accessories. Use the eraser tool to clean up any mistakes or stray lines.

Step by Step

Step 1: Sketch Your Character

Start by sketching the basic shape of your character's head and hair. Use a light-colored brush to create a rough outline of the hair.

Step 2: Add Layers

Add a new layer for the hair. This will make it easier to color and add details without affecting the sketch underneath.

Step 3: Choose Your Brush

Choose a brush that works well for anime hair, such as the Studio Pen or Monoline brush. Experiment with different brushes to find the one that works best for you.

Step 4: Pick Your Colors

Choose a base color for your hair, and then add highlights and shadows to create depth and dimension. Use the color picker tool to choose complementary colors for your highlights and shadows.

Step 5: Add Details

Add individual strands of hair or hair accessories, such as ribbons or clips. Use the eraser tool to clean up any stray lines or mistakes.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create amazing anime hair in Procreate:

  • Experiment with different brushes to find the one that works best for your style.
  • Use the color picker tool to choose complementary colors for your highlights and shadows.
  • Add texture and gradients to create interest in your hair.
  • Use the eraser tool to clean up any mistakes or stray lines.

FAQ

1. How do I create spiky anime hair in Procreate?

To create spiky anime hair in Procreate, use a brush with a sharp point, such as the Studio Pen or Monoline brush. Use the eraser tool to create sharp edges and spikes.

2. How do I create curly anime hair in Procreate?

To create curly anime hair in Procreate, use a brush with a softer edge, such as the Soft Brush or Airbrush. Use the smudge tool to create a more natural, curly texture.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your digital hair, just like you would your real hair! Save your work often, and make sure to back up your files in case of any accidents. And don't forget to have fun and experiment with different styles and colors!

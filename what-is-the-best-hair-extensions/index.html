What Is The Best Hair Extensions In 2023

Written by Robby Oct 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of thin and lifeless hair? Do you want to add length and volume to your hair without waiting for it to grow? If your answer is yes, then hair extensions might be the perfect solution for you. However, with so many types of hair extensions available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one that suits your hair type and lifestyle. In this blog post, we will discuss the best hair extensions in 2023 and everything you need to know about them.

What are Hair Extensions?

Hair extensions are hairpieces that are attached to your natural hair to add length, volume and thickness. They can be made of synthetic or natural human hair. Hair extensions come in different types, lengths, textures and colors, which means you can choose the one that matches your natural hair.

Types of Hair Extensions

There are several types of hair extensions available in the market, each with its own pros and cons. Here are the most popular types:

  • Clip-in Hair Extensions
  • Tape-in Hair Extensions
  • Sew-in Hair Extensions
  • Micro Bead Hair Extensions
  • Pre-Bonded Hair Extensions

How to Choose the Best Hair Extensions?

Choosing the best hair extensions can be overwhelming, but here are some factors to consider:

  • Your Hair Type
  • Your Lifestyle
  • Your Budget
  • The Quality of the Hair Extensions

Clip-in Hair Extensions

Clip-in hair extensions are the most popular type of hair extensions because they are easy to use and affordable. They come in wefts that can be clipped onto your natural hair. Clip-in hair extensions can be made of synthetic or natural human hair. They are perfect for adding volume and length to your hair for special occasions or daily wear.

How to Apply Clip-in Hair Extensions?

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to apply clip-in hair extensions:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair horizontally.
  2. Clip the rest of your hair away from the section you are working on.
  3. Take a weft of clip-in hair extensions and place it under the section of your hair.
  4. Clip the weft onto your hair by snapping the clips shut.
  5. Repeat the process until you have achieved the desired volume and length.

Tips for Wearing Clip-in Hair Extensions

Here are some tips for wearing clip-in hair extensions:

  • Choose the right color that matches your natural hair.
  • Do not wear clip-in hair extensions while sleeping or showering.
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid damaging the extensions.
  • Remove the extensions before using heat styling tools.

Tape-in Hair Extensions

Tape-in hair extensions are semi-permanent hair extensions that are attached to your natural hair using double-sided tape. They are perfect for adding volume and length to your hair for a longer period of time. Tape-in hair extensions can be made of synthetic or natural human hair. They are less visible than other types of hair extensions because the tape is thin and flat.

How to Apply Tape-in Hair Extensions?

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to apply tape-in hair extensions:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair horizontally.
  2. Peel off the protective layer from the double-sided tape on the weft.
  3. Place the weft under the section of your hair.
  4. Press the weft onto your hair using a hair straightener.
  5. Repeat the process until you have achieved the desired volume and length.

Tips for Wearing Tape-in Hair Extensions

Here are some tips for wearing tape-in hair extensions:

  • Choose the right color that matches your natural hair.
  • Do not wash your hair for at least 48 hours after applying the extensions.
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid damaging the extensions.
  • Do not use oil-based products near the tape to avoid weakening the bond.

Sew-in Hair Extensions

Sew-in hair extensions, also known as weaves, are hair extensions that are sewn onto your natural hair using a needle and thread. They are perfect for adding volume and length to your hair for a longer period of time. Sew-in hair extensions are usually made of natural human hair. They are less damaging to your natural hair because they do not use heat or glue.

How to Apply Sew-in Hair Extensions?

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to apply sew-in hair extensions:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair horizontally.
  2. Braid your hair horizontally using cornrows.
  3. Sew the weft of hair extensions onto the cornrows using a needle and thread.
  4. Cut off the excess thread and hair.
  5. Repeat the process until you have achieved the desired volume and length.

Tips for Wearing Sew-in Hair Extensions

Here are some tips for wearing sew-in hair extensions:

  • Choose the right color that matches your natural hair.
  • Do not wash your hair for at least 48 hours after applying the extensions.
  • Do not scratch your scalp to avoid damaging the cornrows.
  • Remove the extensions after 6-8 weeks to avoid damaging your natural hair.

Micro Bead Hair Extensions

Micro bead hair extensions, also known as micro ring hair extensions, are hair extensions that are attached to your natural hair using tiny metal beads or rings. They are perfect for adding volume and length to your hair for a longer period of time. Micro bead hair extensions can be made of synthetic or natural human hair. They are less damaging to your natural hair because they do not use heat or glue.

How to Apply Micro Bead Hair Extensions?

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to apply micro bead hair extensions:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair horizontally.
  2. Thread a small section of your hair through a micro bead or ring.
  3. Insert the hair extension into the micro bead or ring.
  4. Use pliers to close the micro bead or ring tightly.
  5. Repeat the process until you have achieved the desired volume and length.

Tips for Wearing Micro Bead Hair Extensions

Here are some tips for wearing micro bead hair extensions:

  • Choose the right color that matches your natural hair.
  • Do not wash your hair for at least 48 hours after applying the extensions.
  • Do not use oil-based products near the micro bead or ring to avoid weakening the bond.
  • Remove the extensions after 6-8 weeks to avoid damaging your natural hair.

Pre-Bonded Hair Extensions

Pre-bonded hair extensions, also known as fusion hair extensions, are hair extensions that are attached to your natural hair using a keratin bond. They are perfect for adding volume and length to your hair for a longer period of time. Pre-bonded hair extensions can be made of synthetic or natural human hair. They are less damaging to your natural hair because they do not use heat or glue.

How to Apply Pre-Bonded Hair Extensions?

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to apply pre-bonded hair extensions:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair horizontally.
  2. Use a hot fusion tool to melt the keratin bond on the hair extension.
  3. Attach the melted bond onto your natural hair.
  4. Roll the bond between your fingers to secure it.
  5. Repeat the process until you have achieved the desired volume and length.

Tips for Wearing Pre-Bonded Hair Extensions

Here are some tips for wearing pre-bonded hair extensions:

  • Choose the right color that matches your natural hair.
  • Do not wash your hair for at least 48 hours after applying the extensions.
  • Do not use oil-based products near the keratin bond to avoid weakening the bond.
  • Remove the extensions after 6-8 weeks to avoid damaging your natural hair.

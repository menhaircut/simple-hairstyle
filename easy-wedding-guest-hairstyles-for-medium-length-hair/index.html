Easy Wedding Guest Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Robby Sep 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you attending a wedding soon and wondering how to style your medium length hair? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with easy and chic wedding guest hairstyles for medium length hair that you can easily recreate at home. Whether you prefer updos or down-dos, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

Description

Medium length hair is versatile and easy to style, making it perfect for wedding guests. From braids to buns, there are countless options to choose from. In this article, we will be sharing some of the best easy wedding guest hairstyles for medium length hair that will have you looking stunning at any wedding.

How to

The key to achieving easy wedding guest hairstyles for medium length hair is to keep it simple. You don't need to be a professional hairstylist to create beautiful hairstyles. All you need are some basic hair tools such as bobby pins, hair ties, and a curling iron.

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, and then blow-dry it straight or let it air dry naturally.

2. Decide on the hairstyle you want to create and gather all the necessary tools.

3. If you want to add some curls or waves to your hair, use a curling iron to create loose curls.

4. Use bobby pins to create a messy bun or a sleek bun at the back of your head.

5. Create a half-up, half-down hairstyle by pulling the top section of your hair back and securing it with a hair tie.

6. Add some volume to your hair by teasing the roots and spraying some hairspray.

7. Finish off your look with some hair accessories such as a flower crown, a hair clip, or a headband.

Tips

- If you have fine hair, use some volumizing mousse or dry shampoo to add some texture and volume.

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and hair accessories.

- Practice your chosen hairstyle before the wedding day to ensure you get it right.

FAQ

What are some easy wedding guest hairstyles for medium length hair?

Some easy wedding guest hairstyles for medium length hair include messy buns, sleek buns, half-up, half-down hairstyles, braids, and loose curls.

What hair accessories can I use?

You can use a flower crown, hair clips, headbands, or even a tiara for a more formal wedding.

How can I make my hair look fuller?

You can use volumizing mousse, dry shampoo, or tease the roots of your hair to add some volume.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair before and after styling it for a wedding. Make sure you use a heat protectant spray if you're using hot tools on your hair. Also, use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Now that you have some easy wedding guest hairstyles for medium length hair, you're ready to attend any wedding with confidence. Remember to keep it simple and don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and hair accessories. Happy styling!

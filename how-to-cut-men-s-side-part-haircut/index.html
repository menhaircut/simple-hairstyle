How To Cut Men s Side Part Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

The side part haircut has been a classic men's hairstyle for decades. It's a versatile and stylish cut that can work for any occasion. If you're a barber or just looking to cut your own hair, this guide will give you the steps to achieve the perfect side part.

Description

The side part haircut is characterized by a short back and sides, with longer hair on top that is parted to one side. It's a clean and classic cut that's perfect for any face shape. This style can be achieved with any hair type, but it's especially popular with straight or wavy hair.

How to

To cut a men's side part haircut, you'll need the following tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clip
  • Mirror

Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Prep the Hair

Start by washing and towel-drying the hair. Comb the hair straight down, and use the hair clip to section off the hair on top.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

Use the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard to achieve a fade. Blend the hair so there are no harsh lines.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Release the hair clip and comb the hair straight up. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Keep the top longer than the sides and blend the hair so there are no harsh lines.

Step 4: Create the Part

Using the comb, create a part on the side of the head. The part should be in line with the arch of the eyebrow.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to style the hair. Comb the hair to the side, following the part. Use your fingers to create texture and volume.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut shorter to avoid making any mistakes.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head as you cut.
  • Use a small amount of product to avoid a greasy or weighed-down look.
  • Use scissors to cut the hair on top for a more natural look.

FAQ

How often should I cut my hair for a side part?

It depends on how fast your hair grows, but typically every 4-6 weeks is recommended to maintain the style.

Can I achieve a side part with curly hair?

Yes, but it may require more styling product and effort to keep the hair in place.

Is the side part suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the side part is a versatile style that can work for any face shape.

Hair Care

To maintain your side part haircut, it's important to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often as they can damage the hair.

In conclusion, the side part haircut is a classic and versatile style that can work for any occasion. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve the perfect side part. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain the style.

How to

Using a guard is very easy. Simply attach the guard to the clipper and start cutting your hair. Make sure to move the clipper in the direction of hair growth and use a comb to guide the hair into the clipper blades. It is important to start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if you want a shorter haircut.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use a guard:

  1. Attach the guard to the clipper.
  2. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if needed.
  3. Move the clipper in the direction of hair growth.
  4. Use a comb to guide the hair into the clipper blades.
  5. Trim the hair evenly.
  6. Check for any missed spots and trim as needed.

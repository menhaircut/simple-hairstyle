Who Suits Short Bangs A Guide To Find The Perfect Look

Written by Aprilia Aug 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting short bangs but unsure if it will suit you? Short bangs can give you a fresh and bold look, but it's essential to understand which bangs will suit your face shape and hair texture. In this blog, we will guide you on who suits short bangs and how to get the perfect look.

  • Hair Texture

Face Shape

Short bangs can suit any face shape, but it's essential to choose the right style that compliments your features. If you have a round face, go for short bangs that are wispy and soft, as this will make your face look slimmer. For a square face, choose bangs that are choppy and textured, as this will soften your features. Heart-shaped faces can opt for blunt bangs that are shorter in the center and longer on the sides, as this will balance out the forehead and chin. Oval faces can experiment with any short bangs style, as this face shape is versatile.

Hair Texture

Short bangs can suit any hair texture, but it's essential to choose a style that works with your natural hair. If you have straight hair, go for blunt or choppy bangs, as this will add volume and texture. For curly hair, choose wispy or layered bangs, as this will blend in with your curls and give you a softer look. If you have fine hair, opt for blunt or textured bangs, as this will add thickness and volume.

How to Get Short Bangs

Getting short bangs can be intimidating, but it's crucial to trust a professional hairstylist to get the perfect look. Here's how to get short bangs:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist to choose the right style for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Wash your hair and blow-dry it straight.
  3. Section your hair and clip it away from your face, leaving only the section you want for your bangs.
  4. Cut your bangs slowly and gradually, starting from the center and moving towards the sides.
  5. Style your bangs using a round brush and blow dryer, or air dry them for a more natural look.

Tips for Styling Short Bangs

Short bangs can be challenging to style, but with the right techniques, you can achieve a chic and polished look. Here are some tips for styling short bangs:

  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to achieve a smooth and sleek look.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs for a more polished look.
  • Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to your bangs to add texture and definition.
  • Use bobby pins or hair clips to pin back your bangs when they grow out.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can everyone pull off short bangs?

Yes, short bangs can suit any face shape and hair texture, but it's crucial to choose the right style that compliments your features.

2. How often do I need to trim my short bangs?

It's best to trim your bangs every four to six weeks to maintain the shape and length.

3. Can I style my short bangs in different ways?

Yes, you can style your short bangs in various ways, such as side-swept, textured, or blunt. Experiment with different styles to find the perfect look for you.

Hair Care for Short Bangs

Short bangs require minimal maintenance, but it's essential to take care of them to keep them looking fresh and polished. Here are some tips for hair care for short bangs:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent your bangs from looking greasy.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your bangs between washes.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs, as this can damage your hair.

Short bangs can give you a bold and fresh look, but it's essential to choose the right style that suits your face shape and hair texture. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect short bangs look.

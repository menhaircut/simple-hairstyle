How To Waves Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and effortless hairstyle, then waves hair is the ultimate solution. It's a versatile style that suits every hair type, length, and face shape. However, creating waves in hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're a beginner. In this guide, we'll discuss how to waves hair and provide you with tips, tricks, and hair care advice to maintain your waves.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Waves Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Waves Hair

Waving hair is a simple process that requires only a few tools and products. Here's what you'll need:

  • A curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • Texture spray (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to create beautiful waves in your hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow dry it before starting.
  2. Apply heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from damage.
  3. Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections depends on the thickness of your hair. The thicker your hair, the more sections you'll need.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand, starting from the roots and moving towards the ends. Hold for a few seconds and release.
  5. Repeat step 4 for each section of hair, alternating the direction of the curls to create a natural wave pattern.
  6. Once all the sections are curled, lightly spray your hair with hairspray to hold the waves in place.
  7. If you want more texture, apply a texture spray to your hair and scrunch it with your fingers.
  8. Finally, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the waves and create a natural look.

Tips for Creating Waves Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when creating waves in your hair:

  • Choose the right curling iron or wand size for your hair length and desired wave size.
  • Don't hold the curling iron or wand on your hair for too long, as it can cause damage and burn your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage caused by heat styling.
  • Alternate the direction of the curls to create a natural wave pattern.
  • Use a texture spray to add more volume and texture to your waves.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about creating waves in hair:

Q: Can I create waves in my hair without a curling iron or wand?

A: Yes, you can create waves in your hair without heat styling tools. Try braiding your hair while it's wet and leaving it overnight. When you remove the braids in the morning, you'll have natural waves.

Q: How do I prevent my waves from falling out?

A: Use a strong hold hairspray to keep your waves in place. You can also try using a texturizing spray to add more grip to your hair.

Q: How often should I wave my hair?

A: You can wave your hair as often as you like, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and take breaks between styling to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Waves Hair

Maintaining your waves is essential to keep your style looking fresh and healthy. Here are some hair care tips for waves hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your waves from drying out.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as it can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and maintain your waves.

Now that you know how to waves hair, go ahead and try it out for yourself. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to create beautiful waves in no time.

Short Hair Cuts For Round Face 2021

Written by Aprilia Sep 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old haircut and looking for a change? If you have a round face, it can be challenging to find the perfect style that suits you. But don't worry, we have got you covered with the latest short hair cuts for round face 2021. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about short haircuts for round faces, including how to choose the right style, step-by-step guides, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Description

A round face is characterized by having full cheeks, a soft jawline, and a similar width and length. The key to finding the perfect short haircut is to create an illusion of length and to accentuate your best features. Short haircuts are an excellent option for round faces as they can help elongate your face and add volume to your hair.

How to Choose the Right Style

When choosing a short haircut, it is essential to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Here are some factors to keep in mind:

  • Length: Opt for a short bob, pixie cut, or lob with layers that hit below your chin to elongate your face.
  • Texture: If you have fine hair, go for a choppy bob or a layered pixie cut to add volume. If your hair is thick, try a blunt bob or a layered lob.
  • Side-swept bangs: Bangs that are swept to one side can help create the illusion of length and break up the roundness of your face.
  • Face-framing layers: Layers that start at your cheekbones or collarbone can help draw attention to your best features.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect short haircut for your round face:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts.
  2. Choose the right style based on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.
  3. Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow-dry it straight.
  4. Section your hair and start cutting from the back.
  5. Use a razor or scissors to add texture and layers.
  6. Style your hair using a curling wand or straightener.
  7. Apply a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short haircut for your round face:

  • Bring pictures of the style you want to your hairstylist for reference.
  • Use products that add volume to your hair, such as mousse or volumizing spray.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for round faces:

What is the best short haircut for a round face?

The best short haircut for a round face is one that creates an illusion of length and adds volume to your hair. Some popular options include a layered bob, a pixie cut, or a lob with face-framing layers.

Can you have bangs with a round face?

Yes, you can have bangs with a round face. Side-swept bangs that are longer on one side can help create the illusion of length and break up the roundness of your face.

How do you style short hair for a round face?

You can style short hair for a round face by using products that add volume, such as mousse or volumizing spray. You can also use a curling wand or straightener to create waves or curls that add texture and definition to your hair.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short haircut looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause breakage and split ends.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know everything about short haircuts for round faces, it's time to book your appointment with a hairstylist and try out a new style. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.

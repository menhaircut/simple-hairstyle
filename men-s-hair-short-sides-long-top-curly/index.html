Men s Hair Short Sides Long Top Curly A Trending Hairstyle In 2023

Written by William Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short sides long top curly is a hairstyle that has been trending among men for a while now. This hairstyle has been popular because of its versatility and ease of maintenance. It is a hairstyle that suits almost all face shapes and hair types. In this article, we will be discussing how to achieve the short sides long top curly hairstyle, tips on how to maintain it, and some FAQs about the hairstyle.

Description

The short sides long top curly hairstyle is a hairstyle where the sides are shaved or cut short, while the top is left long and curly. The hair on the top can be styled in different ways, such as a messy look, quiff, or slicked back. This hairstyle is a perfect choice for men who want a low maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish and trendy.

How to Achieve the Short Sides Long Top Curly Hairstyle

The short sides long top curly hairstyle is relatively easy to achieve. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Grow Out the Top

The first step in achieving the short sides long top curly hairstyle is to grow out the top. This will give you enough hair to work with when styling the hair on top. Depending on the length of your hair, this step may take a few weeks or months.

Step 2: Cut the Sides Short

The next step is to cut the sides short. This can be done using clippers or scissors. The sides can be cut to a length that suits your face shape and personal style. Some men prefer a skin fade, while others prefer a short buzz cut.

Step 3: Style the Top

Once the sides are cut short, it's time to style the hair on top. This can be done using a hair product such as pomade, wax, or gel. The hair on top can be styled into a quiff, messy look, or slicked back.

Tips for Maintaining the Short Sides Long Top Curly Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you maintain the short sides long top curly hairstyle:

Tip 1: Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products is crucial to maintaining the short sides long top curly hairstyle. Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair to help define the curls and reduce frizz.

Tip 2: Trim the Hair Regularly

Trimming the hair regularly will help keep the hairstyle looking neat and tidy. It's recommended to trim the hair every 4-6 weeks.

Tip 3: Protect the Hair from Damage

Curly hair is prone to damage, so it's essential to protect it. Use a heat protectant when using hot styling tools and avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on the hair.

FAQs about the Short Sides Long Top Curly Hairstyle

Q: What face shape does the short sides long top curly hairstyle suit?

A: The short sides long top curly hairstyle suits almost all face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on men with round or square faces.

Q: Can the short sides long top curly hairstyle be worn to work?

A: Yes, the short sides long top curly hairstyle can be worn to work. It can be styled neatly for a professional look or left messy for a more casual look.

Q: How long does the hair on top need to be for the short sides long top curly hairstyle?

A: The hair on top needs to be at least 2-3 inches long to achieve the short sides long top curly hairstyle.

Hair Care for the Short Sides Long Top Curly Hairstyle

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining the short sides long top curly hairstyle. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Shampoo and Condition Regularly

Shampoo and condition the hair regularly to keep it clean and moisturized. Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair.

Tip 2: Avoid Overwashing

Overwashing the hair can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz. It's recommended to wash the hair 2-3 times a week.

Tip 3: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Using a leave-in conditioner can help keep the hair moisturized and reduce frizz. Apply the conditioner to damp hair and style as desired.

Overall, the short sides long top curly hairstyle is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that is easy to achieve and maintain. With proper hair care and styling, this hairstyle can look great on any man.

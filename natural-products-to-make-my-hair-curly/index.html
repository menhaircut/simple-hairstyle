Natural Products To Make Your Hair Curly

Written by Frank Jul 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have straight hair but want to add some bounce and curl to your locks? Using natural products can help you achieve the curly hair of your dreams without damaging your hair with harsh chemicals. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best natural products to make your hair curly and how to use them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Make Your Hair Curly Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Keeping Your Curls Defined
  • FAQ about Natural Products for Curly Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Description

Natural products are a great choice for anyone who wants to add some curl to their straight hair. Unlike chemical treatments, natural products are gentle on your hair and scalp, making them a healthier option. Some of the best natural products to make your hair curly include:

  • Coconut oil
  • Aloe vera
  • Flaxseed gel
  • Shea butter
  • Apple cider vinegar

How to Make Your Hair Curly Naturally

Making your hair curly naturally is easy when you use the right products and techniques. Here are some tips to help you achieve curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and remove any knots
  • Apply your chosen natural product to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly throughout
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on low heat
  • Scrunch your hair as it dries to encourage curls
  • Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently separate your curls

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to making your hair curly naturally:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply your chosen natural product to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly throughout
  3. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties
  4. Take one section of hair and twist it tightly around your finger
  5. Repeat this process with all sections of hair
  6. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on low heat
  7. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently separate your curls
  8. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to prevent frizz

Tips for Keeping Your Curls Defined

Once you’ve achieved curly hair, you’ll want to keep your curls defined and bouncy. Here are some tips to help you maintain your curls:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated

FAQ about Natural Products for Curly Hair

Q: Can I use natural products on color-treated hair?

A: Yes, most natural products are safe to use on color-treated hair. However, it’s always a good idea to do a patch test first to make sure you don’t have an allergic reaction.

Q: How often should I use natural products to make my hair curly?

A: This will depend on your hair type and the product you’re using. Generally, you can use natural products to make your hair curly once or twice a week.

Q: Can I use natural products to make my hair curly if I have naturally curly hair?

A: Yes, you can use natural products to enhance your natural curls and add extra definition.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a little extra care to keep it healthy and bouncy. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

With these natural products and tips, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls without damaging your hair. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you and your hair type.

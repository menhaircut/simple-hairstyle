How To Use Pomade For Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to manage your thick hair? Do you find it difficult to style and keep it in place for long hours? If yes, you might want to try using pomade. Pomade is a styling product that can help you achieve a sleek and polished look for your hair. In this article, we will discuss how to use pomade for thick hair and provide you with some tips and tricks to make the most out of this product.

Table of Contents

  • How to Use Pomade for Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

How to Use Pomade for Thick Hair

Pomade is a versatile product that can be used to achieve various hairstyles, from slicked-back looks to textured and tousled styles. Here are some tips on how to use pomade for thick hair:

  • Start with a small amount of pomade and add more as needed.
  • Apply the product to dry or slightly damp hair.
  • Distribute the pomade evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the areas where you want to add texture or hold.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.
  • Finish with a hairspray or a finishing product to lock in the style.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to using pomade, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve a sleek and polished look:

  1. Start with clean, dry, or slightly damp hair. Avoid using pomade on wet hair as it may dilute the product and reduce its hold.
  2. Scoop a small amount of pomade onto your fingertips. If you have long hair, you may need a slightly larger amount.
  3. Rub the pomade between your palms to warm it up and soften it.
  4. Distribute the pomade evenly throughout your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the tips. Use your fingers or a comb to ensure even distribution.
  5. Style your hair as desired using a comb or your fingers. For a slicked-back look, comb your hair straight back. For a textured or tousled style, use your fingers to create volume and movement.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or a finishing product to lock in the style and add shine.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you make the most out of using pomade for thick hair:

  • Choose the right type of pomade for your hair. There are different types of pomade, such as water-based, oil-based, and clay-based. Water-based pomades are easy to wash out, oil-based pomades provide a stronger hold, and clay-based pomades add texture and volume.
  • Use a small amount of pomade to avoid a greasy or heavy look. You can always add more if needed.
  • Apply the pomade to the tips of your hair to create a more natural and textured look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about using pomade for thick hair:

Q: Can pomade damage my hair?

A: Pomade does not cause damage to your hair if used in moderation and washed out thoroughly. However, using too much pomade or leaving it in your hair for too long can cause buildup and clog your hair follicles.

Q: How often should I use pomade?

A: It depends on your hairstyle and personal preference. You can use pomade daily or as needed, but make sure to wash it out before going to bed to avoid buildup and clogged pores.

Q: Can I use pomade on wet hair?

A: It is not recommended to use pomade on wet hair as it may dilute the product and reduce its hold. Make sure your hair is dry or slightly damp before applying pomade.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Aside from using pomade, here are some hair care tips for thick hair:

  • Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and excess oil.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment to moisturize and nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as it can cause damage and dryness.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to use pomade for thick hair, it's time to try it out and see how it can transform your hairstyle. Remember to choose the right type of pomade for your hair, use it in moderation, and wash it out thoroughly to avoid buildup and clogged pores. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a sleek and polished look that lasts all day.

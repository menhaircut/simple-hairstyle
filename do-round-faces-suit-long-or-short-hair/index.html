Do Round Faces Suit Long Or Short Hair

Description

The question that often pops up in the minds of women with round faces is whether long or short hair suits them. The answer is both! It all depends on the hairstyle you choose and how you style it. A round face has soft, curved lines and a wide forehead and cheekbones. The key is to find a hairstyle that balances out the roundness of your face and elongates it to create an illusion of a more oval face shape.

How to

The first step is to identify your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and examining the shape of your face in the mirror. If your face is widest at the cheeks and ears with a rounded jawline, then you have a round face. Once you have identified your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that suit you.

Step by step

Step 1: Identify your face shape
Step 2: Look for hairstyles that balance out the roundness of your face
Step 3: Consider your hair texture and length
Step 4: Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best

Tips

1. Long layers can add length to your face and make it appear slimmer.
2. A side-swept fringe can also create an illusion of a more oval face shape.
3. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin as they can make your face look rounder.
4. If you prefer short hair, go for a pixie cut with volume on top to elongate your face.
5. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume at the roots and elongate your face.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a bob with a round face?
A: Yes, you can. However, make sure it is a longer bob that ends just above your shoulders and has layers to add volume and create an elongated look.

Q: Can I wear a center parting with a round face?
A: Yes, you can. However, make sure there is enough volume at the crown of your head to create an elongated look.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant spray when styling. Regular trims are also essential to keep split ends at bay and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

In conclusion, whether you have long or short hair, there is a hairstyle that will suit your round face. It is all about finding the right balance and creating an illusion of an elongated face shape. Use the tips mentioned above and experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

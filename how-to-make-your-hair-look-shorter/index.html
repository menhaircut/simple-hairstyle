How To Make Your Hair Look Shorter

Written by Frank Aug 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you want to try a shorter hairstyle without actually cutting your hair? There are various ways to make your hair look shorter without chopping off those precious locks. In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks on how to make your hair appear shorter without sacrificing its length.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Look Shorter
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Shorter-Looking Hair

How to Make Your Hair Look Shorter

There are several ways to make your hair look shorter without actually cutting it. Here are some of the most effective methods:

1. Try a Bob or Lob Hairstyle

A bob or lob (long bob) hairstyle can make your hair look shorter without actually cutting it. These hairstyles are perfect for those who want to try a shorter look but are not yet ready to commit to a drastic change. A bob or lob can be styled in various ways, such as straight, wavy, or curly.

2. Use Hair Extensions

Another way to make your hair appear shorter is by using hair extensions. You can use clip-in extensions to create a faux bob or lob, or you can use longer extensions to create the illusion of shorter hair. Make sure to blend the extensions well with your natural hair to achieve a seamless look.

3. Create Updos

Creating updos is another effective way to make your hair look shorter. You can create a messy bun or a sleek ponytail to give the illusion of shorter hair. You can also try braiding your hair and tucking the ends under to create a faux bob.

4. Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can also help create the illusion of shorter hair. You can use headbands, scarves, or hair clips to create a shorter-looking hairstyle. For example, you can use a headband to push your hair back and create the appearance of a shorter haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to create a faux bob:

  1. Start by curling your hair. This will help create texture and volume.
  2. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  3. Tuck the ends of your hair under and secure them with bobby pins.
  4. Loosen the hair at the crown of your head to create a more natural look.
  5. Use hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve a shorter-looking hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles and hair accessories to find what works best for you.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to create a messy, tousled look.

FAQs

Can I make my hair look shorter without cutting it?

Yes, you can make your hair look shorter without cutting it by trying different hairstyles, using hair extensions, or creating updos.

What is the best way to create a faux bob?

The best way to create a faux bob is by curling your hair, gathering it into a low ponytail, and tucking the ends under. You can also use bobby pins and hairspray to hold the style in place.

What type of hair accessories should I use to create a shorter-looking hairstyle?

You can use headbands, scarves, or hair clips to create a shorter-looking hairstyle. Experiment with different accessories to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Shorter-Looking Hair

If you want to maintain the illusion of shorter hair, it is important to take care of your locks. Here are some hair care tips for shorter-looking hair:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove product buildup.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

Now that you know how to make your hair look shorter, it’s time to experiment with different styles and techniques. Have fun and embrace your new look!

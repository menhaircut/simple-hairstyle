How to Style Hair for Oval Face Male - Tips and Tricks

Oval face is considered to be the most versatile face shape as it suits most hairstyles. However, finding the right hairstyle that complements your oval face is still important to enhance your overall appearance. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks on how to style hair for oval face male.

How to Style Hair for Oval Face Male

Before we jump into the different hairstyles for oval face male, it is important to identify your hair type and texture. Your hair texture can be straight, wavy, curly, or coily. Based on your hair type and texture, you can choose the right hairstyle that enhances your facial features.

For oval face male, the best hairstyle is the one that adds volume to the top and sides of the head while keeping the forehead and jawline visible. Here are some popular hairstyles for oval face male:

1. Short Quiff

The short quiff is a classic hairstyle that suits most face shapes, including oval face male. This hairstyle adds volume to the top while keeping the sides short. You can style this hairstyle with a matte finish or a shiny finish, depending on your preference.

2. Side Part

The side part is another versatile hairstyle that suits oval face male. This hairstyle involves combing the hair to one side and keeping the other side short. You can add some volume to the top to enhance your facial features.

3. Messy Fringe

The messy fringe is a popular hairstyle that suits oval face male. This hairstyle involves keeping the hair long at the front and styling it in a messy way. You can add some texture to your hair by using a hair product that provides a matte finish.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style your hair for oval face male:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt and oil from your hair and provide a clean base for styling.

2. Towel dry your hair

Use a towel to remove excess water from your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel as this can damage your hair.

3. Apply a hair product

Apply a hair product that suits your hair type and texture. You can choose from a range of hair products such as wax, pomade, gel, or mousse. Use a small amount of product at first and add more if required.

4. Style your hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair. Depending on the hairstyle you choose, you can add volume to the top or keep the sides short. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you the best.

Tips for Styling Hair for Oval Face Male

Here are some tips that can help you style your hair for oval face male:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top and sides of the head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that cover your forehead or jawline.
  • Choose a hair product that suits your hair type and texture.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best.
  • Visit a professional hairstylist for advice and guidance.

FAQs

1. Can oval face males have a buzz cut?

Yes, oval face males can have a buzz cut. However, it is important to keep some volume on the top to enhance your facial features.

2. Can oval face males have long hair?

Yes, oval face males can have long hair. However, it is important to keep the hair away from the forehead and jawline to avoid covering your facial features.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Male

Here are some hair care tips that can help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as this can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Visit a professional hairstylist for regular haircuts and trims.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your hair for oval face male and enhance your overall appearance. Remember to choose a hairstyle that suits your facial features and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you the best.

How To Cut Short Permed Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your permed hair and want to try something new? Cutting your hair short is a great way to refresh your look and add some volume to your hair. But cutting short permed hair can be tricky, especially if you've never done it before. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut short permed hair, as well as some tips and hair care advice to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Short Permed Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Short Permed Hair

Description

Permed hair is hair that has been chemically treated to create curls or waves. Cutting short permed hair involves cutting the hair to a shorter length while still maintaining the permed texture. This can be done at home or at a salon, but it's important to know the proper technique to avoid damaging your hair.

How to Cut Short Permed Hair

Cutting short permed hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done easily. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your short permed hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and clip each section to keep it out of the way.
  3. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Take a section of hair and comb it straight out from your head.
  4. Hold the hair between your fingers where you want to cut it. Use your scissors to cut the hair in a straight line.
  5. Move on to the next section of hair and repeat the process, keeping the length consistent.
  6. Once you've cut all the sections, check your hair in the mirror to make sure it's even.
  7. If you need to make any adjustments, do so carefully.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Short Permed Hair

Here are some tips to make the process of cutting short permed hair easier:

  • Start with dry hair to get a better idea of how it will look once it's cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends or damaged hair.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time to avoid mistakes.
  • Check your hair in the mirror frequently to make sure it's even.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once; it's better to cut a little bit at a time and adjust as needed.

FAQs

Can I cut my short permed hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your short permed hair at home, but it's important to follow the proper technique to avoid damaging your hair.

How often should I cut my short permed hair?

You should cut your short permed hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Should I cut my hair wet or dry?

It's best to cut your hair dry to get a better idea of how it will look once it's cut.

Hair Care Tips for Short Permed Hair

Here are some tips to keep your short permed hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hair.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as it can cause frizz and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your short permed hair at home and keep it looking healthy and stylish. Remember to take your time and follow the proper technique, and you'll have a great new look in no time!

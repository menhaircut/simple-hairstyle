How To Sleep With Very Short Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Jul 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have very short curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to maintain its shape and style, especially when you sleep. Tossing and turning at night can leave your hair looking frizzy, flat, or matted in the morning. But don't worry! With the right techniques and hair care routine, you can wake up with beautiful, bouncy curls every day. Here's how to sleep with very short curly hair:

Table of Contents

  • How to Sleep with Very Short Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Routine for Short Curly Hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Use a Silk or Satin Pillowcase

Swap out your cotton pillowcase for a silk or satin one. These materials are gentler on your hair and won't cause as much friction, which can lead to breakage, tangles, and frizz.

Step 2: Pineapple Your Hair

To preserve your curls, gather your hair at the top of your head and loosely secure it with a scrunchie. This technique, called "pineappling," helps keep your curls in place and prevents them from getting crushed while you sleep.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Before going to bed, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will help keep your curls moisturized and prevent them from getting tangled or frizzy.

Step 4: Sleep on Your Back

Try to sleep on your back instead of your sides or stomach. This will help prevent your curls from getting flattened or crushed while you sleep.

Step 5: Use a Silk or Satin Bonnet or Scarf

If you don't want to pineapple your hair, you can also use a silk or satin bonnet or scarf to protect your curls. Simply wrap your hair in the bonnet or scarf and secure it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining short curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using heat tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as much as possible.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair products that contain sulfates, alcohol, or fragrances.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my short curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with very dry hair may only need to wash their hair once a week, while others may need to wash it every other day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: Can I sleep with wet hair?

A: It's not recommended to sleep with wet hair, as it can lead to breakage and damage. If you must sleep with wet hair, use a microfiber towel or T-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water and then apply a leave-in conditioner.

Hair Care Routine for Short Curly Hair

Here's a sample hair care routine for short curly hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner once a week.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Pineapple your hair or use a silk or satin bonnet or scarf to protect your curls while you sleep.

With these tips and techniques, you can sleep with very short curly hair and wake up with beautiful, defined curls every day. Remember to be gentle with your hair and avoid harsh chemicals or heat tools as much as possible. Happy sleeping!

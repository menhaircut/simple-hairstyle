Which Hair Color Would Suit Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to change up your hair color, one of the biggest questions you may have is "which hair color would suit me?" With so many different colors and shades to choose from, it can be difficult to know where to start. In this article, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to choose the perfect hair color for your skin tone, eye color, and personal style.

Description

Choosing a new hair color can be an exciting experience, but it can also be overwhelming. With so many different colors and shades to choose from, it's important to take some time to consider which hair color would suit you best. In this article, we'll dive into the factors you should consider when choosing a new hair color, including your skin tone, eye color, and personal style. We'll also provide some tips on how to care for your new hair color once you've made the change.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

When choosing a new hair color, there are several factors to consider. The first and most important factor is your skin tone. Here's a quick guide to help you determine your skin tone:

  • If your skin has cool undertones, you may have pink, red, or bluish undertones.
  • If your skin has warm undertones, you may have yellow, peach, or golden undertones.
  • If your skin has neutral undertones, you may have a combination of cool and warm undertones.

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can start to consider which hair colors would suit you best. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have cool undertones, consider hair colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, cool brown, or black.
  • If you have warm undertones, consider hair colors like golden blonde, honey blonde, warm brown, or auburn.
  • If you have neutral undertones, you can generally pull off a wide range of hair colors.

It's also important to consider your eye color when choosing a new hair color. Here are some guidelines:

  • If you have blue eyes, consider hair colors like platinum blonde, ash blonde, or cool brown.
  • If you have green eyes, consider hair colors like warm brown, auburn, or golden blonde.
  • If you have brown eyes, you can generally pull off a wide range of hair colors.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you have a general idea of which hair colors would suit you based on your skin tone and eye color, here's a step by step guide to help you choose the perfect shade:

  1. Determine your skin tone using the guide above.
  2. Consider your eye color and use the guidelines above to narrow down your options.
  3. Look at pictures of people with similar skin tones and eye colors to see how different hair colors look.
  4. Consider your personal style and lifestyle. Are you willing to put in the time and effort to maintain a high-maintenance hair color, or do you prefer something low-maintenance?
  5. Consult with a hair colorist to get their professional opinion and advice.
  6. Try on wigs or use virtual hair color apps to get an idea of how different hair colors will look on you.
  7. Take the plunge and make the change!

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Once you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause the color to fade.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too frequently, as this can also cause the color to fade.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Consider getting regular touch-ups to keep your hair color looking fresh.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair after coloring it?

A: It's best to wait at least 48 hours after coloring your hair before washing it. After that, try to wash your hair no more than two or three times a week to avoid fading the color.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home, or should I go to a salon?

A: While it's possible to dye your hair at home, it's generally best to go to a salon to get professional results. A professional colorist will be able to help you choose the perfect shade and ensure that the color is applied evenly.

Q: Is it safe to dye my hair while pregnant?

A: It's generally considered safe to dye your hair while pregnant, but it's always a good idea to check with your doctor first. Some women may choose to avoid hair dye during pregnancy as a precaution.

Hair Care

Changing your hair color can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look, but it's important to take care of your hair properly to keep it healthy and looking its best. Be sure to use high-quality products designed for color-treated hair, and avoid using hot tools too frequently. With the right care and maintenance, your new hair color will look amazing for weeks to come!

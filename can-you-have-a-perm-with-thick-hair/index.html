Can You Have A Perm With Thick Hair Tips And How To For 2023

Written by Bardi Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of straight, flat hair and looking for a change? Perming your hair can add texture, volume, and bounce to your locks. However, if you have thick hair, you may be wondering if it's possible to get a perm. The answer is yes, you can have a perm with thick hair, but there are certain things you need to consider. In this blog post, we will discuss the how-to, tips, and hair care for perming thick hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How-to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the structure of your hair. It involves applying a solution to your hair, then wrapping it around rods and applying heat. The solution breaks down the bonds in your hair, allowing it to be reshaped. Perms can last for several months, and the results can vary from loose waves to tight curls.

How-to

Before getting a perm, you should consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine the best type of perm for your hair type and the look you want to achieve. They will also perform a strand test to ensure that your hair can handle the chemicals in the perm solution.

When perming thick hair, it's important to use the right size of rods. Larger rods will create looser waves, while smaller rods will create tighter curls. Your hairstylist will choose the appropriate size based on the length and thickness of your hair.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a perm with thick hair:

  1. Consult with a professional hairstylist
  2. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any build-up
  3. Wrap your hair around perm rods
  4. Apply the perm solution
  5. Process the perm solution for the recommended time
  6. Rinse the perm solution out of your hair
  7. Apply a neutralizer to your hair to set the curls
  8. Rinse your hair again and style as desired

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a perm with thick hair:

  • Choose a hairstylist who has experience perming thick hair
  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your newly permed hair to prevent damage
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a perm with thick hair:

Can you get a perm with natural thick hair?

Yes, you can get a perm with natural thick hair. However, it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist to ensure that your hair is healthy enough to handle the chemicals in the perm solution.

Can you get a perm if you have chemically relaxed hair?

If you have chemically relaxed hair, it's not recommended to get a perm. The chemicals in the perm solution can cause further damage to your hair.

How long does a perm last on thick hair?

A perm can last for several months on thick hair, depending on the type of perm and how well you take care of your hair.

Hair Care

After getting a perm with thick hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and maintain your curls. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your newly permed hair to prevent damage
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye

In conclusion, getting a perm with thick hair is possible, but it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist and take care of your hair afterward. By following the tips and hair care advice in this blog post, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls that last for months.

Can I Cut A Bob Yourself A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Nov 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh new hairstyle, a bob is a timeless and classic look. But, can you cut a bob yourself? The answer is yes, you can! In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting a bob at home. From what tools you'll need to hair care tips, we've got you covered.

How to Cut a Bob Yourself

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather the right tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Hair ties

Make sure your scissors are sharp, as dull scissors can cause split ends and uneven cuts.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. This will help you see the natural shape of your hair and make it easier to cut. If you have curly hair, make sure to straighten it first so you can see the length and shape more clearly.

Step 3: Part Your Hair

Part your hair down the middle and bring it forward, so it's in front of your shoulders. Make sure it's evenly parted, so your bob is cut symmetrically.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start by cutting the hair at the back of your head. Use the comb to section off a small amount of hair and hold it straight with your fingers. Cut the hair in a straight line, using the comb as a guide. Repeat this process for the rest of your hair, making sure to keep the cuts even and symmetrical.

Step 5: Cut the Front

Once you've cut the back, it's time to move onto the front. Use the same process as before, but make sure to leave your bangs longer than the rest of your hair. You can always trim them later.

Step 6: Trim the Bangs

If you want bangs, now is the time to trim them. Use the comb to section off the bangs and hold them straight with your fingers. Cut them straight across, or at an angle for a more textured look.

Tips for Cutting a Bob Yourself

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect bob:

  • Start with small cuts - you can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's cut.
  • Take breaks - cutting your own hair can be tiring, so take breaks if you need to.
  • Use a mirror - make sure to use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.
  • Keep your hair wet - wet hair is easier to cut and style.

FAQ

How often should I cut my bob?

You should cut your bob every 6-8 weeks to maintain the style and prevent split ends.

Can I cut a bob with curly hair?

Yes, you can cut a bob with curly hair. Just make sure to straighten it first so you can see the length and shape more clearly.

Can I cut a bob with short hair?

Yes, you can cut a bob with short hair. Just make sure to leave enough length to create the bob shape.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've cut your own bob, here are some tips to keep it looking its best:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the style.

Cutting a bob yourself can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect look from the comfort of your own home. Just remember to take your time, start with small cuts, and use a mirror to check your progress.

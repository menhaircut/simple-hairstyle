How To Cut A Textured Bob At Home Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and want to try out a textured bob, you might be hesitant to go to a salon due to the current pandemic. But don't worry, cutting a textured bob at home is easier than you think! In this article, we'll guide you through the process, step-by-step, and provide you with some tips and tricks to achieve the perfect textured bob.

How to Cut a Textured Bob at Home

Before we get started, here are the things you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Hair ties

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair ties or clips to secure the sections.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start with the back sections. Take one section at a time and comb it straight down. Hold your hair between your fingers and cut it in a straight line. You can cut it shorter or longer, depending on your preference. Repeat the process with the other back section.

Step 4: Cut the Front

Move on to the front sections. Take one section at a time and comb it straight down. Hold your hair between your fingers and cut it diagonally. This will create the textured look. Repeat the process with the other front section.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Blend the layers by combing your hair and cutting any uneven pieces. Make sure to check the back and sides to ensure that everything is blended nicely.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you're done cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can use a curling iron or straightener to create some waves or keep it straight.

Tips for Cutting a Textured Bob at Home

  • Start by cutting a little bit at a time. You can always cut more later, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair can stretch and you might end up cutting it shorter than you intended.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring and you don't want to rush it.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your hair if it's curly. Just make sure to cut it when it's dry and use the same steps as outlined above.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. However, if you're cutting your own hair, it's up to you to decide when you need a trim.

Hair Care

Cutting your hair at home can save you money and time. However, it's important to take care of your hair before and after cutting it. Make sure to use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage. You can also use a hair mask once a week to give your hair some extra love.

Now that you know how to cut a textured bob at home, it's time to grab your scissors and give it a try! Remember to take it slow and follow the steps carefully to achieve the perfect textured bob.

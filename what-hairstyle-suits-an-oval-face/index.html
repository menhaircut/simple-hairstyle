What Hairstyle Suits An Oval Face

Are you wondering what hairstyle suits an oval face? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the best hairstyles for oval-shaped faces. We will cover everything from how to determine if you have an oval face to tips on how to care for your hair. Let's get started!

  • Hairstyles for Oval Faces

How to Determine if You Have an Oval Face

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oval faces, let's first determine if you have an oval face. Oval faces are characterized by balanced proportions and a slightly curved jawline. Your forehead may be slightly wider than your chin, and your cheekbones are typically the widest part of your face.

To determine if you have an oval face, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. Then, measure the width of your face at the widest point (usually your cheekbones). If the length of your face is equal to about one and a half times the width of your face, you likely have an oval face.

Hairstyles for Oval Faces

Oval faces are versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles, from long and straight to short and curly. The key is to find a hairstyle that complements your features and highlights your face shape. Here are some of the best hairstyles for oval faces:

Long Layers

If you have long hair, long layers are a great option for oval faces. Layers add volume and movement to your hair, and they can help balance out your face shape. Ask your stylist for long layers that start below your chin.

Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that works well for oval faces. This style can be customized to suit your face shape, whether you prefer a short bob or a longer bob (also known as a lob). Opt for a blunt cut or add some layers for a softer look.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to frame your face and draw attention to your eyes. They work well for oval faces because they add width to your forehead and balance out your face shape. Ask your stylist for bangs that start at your cheekbones and sweep to the side.

Updo

If you're looking for a formal hairstyle, an updo is a great option for oval faces. Updos can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture, whether you prefer a sleek bun or a messy updo. Consider adding some volume at the crown of your head to balance out your face shape.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Now that you know which hairstyles work best for oval faces, here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and movement.
  • Invest in a good heat protectant to prevent damage from hot styling tools.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair texture.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Can oval faces pull off short hair?

Yes, oval faces can pull off short hair. Consider a pixie cut or a bob for a dramatic change.

What hair color looks best on oval faces?

Oval faces can pull off a variety of hair colors, from blonde to brunette to red. Consider your skin tone when choosing a hair color.

Can oval faces wear bangs?

Yes, oval faces can wear bangs. Side-swept bangs are a great option for oval faces.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Invest in a good shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day to prevent damage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

Now that you know what hairstyle suits an oval face, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist and try out some new looks! Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

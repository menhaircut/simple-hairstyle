How To Wear Long Hair At The Gym Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Nov 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your long hair getting in the way during workouts? Do you struggle to find hairstyles that keep your hair out of your face and off your neck while exercising? Look no further, because we have put together the ultimate guide on how to wear long hair at the gym. Read on for tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to keep your locks healthy and strong.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Wear Long Hair at the Gym
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Long Hair at the Gym
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Active Lifestyles

How to Wear Long Hair at the Gym

The first step to wearing long hair at the gym is to choose a hairstyle that fits your exercise routine. If you're doing cardio, opt for a style that keeps your hair off your neck and out of your face. For weightlifting, choose a hairstyle that keeps your hair secured and won't interfere with your movements. Some popular hairstyles for long hair at the gym include:

  • Ponytail
  • Braids
  • Bun
  • Headband

The key is to find a style that is comfortable and won't distract you from your workout.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to create a perfect gym hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. If you're doing a ponytail, gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with an elastic band.
  4. If you're doing braids, divide your hair into sections and braid each section. Secure with elastic bands at the bottom.
  5. If you're doing a bun, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure with bobby pins.
  6. If you're using a headband, place it on your head to keep your hair out of your face.
  7. Finish with hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Remember to choose a hairstyle that is comfortable and won't distract you from your workout.

Tips for Long Hair at the Gym

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when wearing long hair at the gym:

  • Use hair ties that won't break or damage your hair.
  • Apply dry shampoo to your roots to absorb sweat and oil.
  • Wear a sweatband to keep sweat from running down your face and into your hair.
  • Choose hairstyles that are easy to recreate and won't take too much time.
  • Avoid using metal clips or elastic bands that can damage your hair.

FAQs

Q: Should I wash my hair after every workout?

A: It's not necessary to wash your hair after every workout. Instead, try using dry shampoo to absorb sweat and oil.

Q: What should I do if my hair gets tangled during a workout?

A: Use a detangling brush or comb to gently remove tangles without damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Active Lifestyles

Active lifestyles can take a toll on your hair, but with the right care, you can keep your locks healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear your long hair at the gym with confidence and style. Remember to choose a hairstyle that is comfortable and won't distract you from your workout, and take good care of your hair to keep it strong and healthy.

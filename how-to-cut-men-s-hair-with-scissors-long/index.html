How To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors Long

Written by Daniel Jun 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts at the salon? Or maybe you just want to try your hand at cutting your own hair? Whatever the reason, cutting men's hair with scissors long is easier than you might think. In this article, we'll go over the steps to achieving a professional-looking haircut at home.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors Long
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before getting started, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clippers (optional). It's also helpful to have a mirror and a towel to keep the hair off the floor.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors Long

First, decide on the length you want your hair to be. It's helpful to have a picture or reference to guide you. Next, start by trimming the sides and back of your head. Use the hair clippers to create a shorter length, then blend the cut with scissors. For the top of the head, pull sections of hair straight up and cut across to create an even length.


It's important to take your time and cut small sections at a time. This will ensure an even cut and prevent any mistakes. If you're unsure about a certain section, it's better to leave it longer and come back to it later.

Step-by-Step

  1. Start by trimming the sides and back of your head with hair clippers.
  2. Blend the cut with scissors for a natural look.
  3. Pull sections of hair straight up from the top of your head.
  4. Cut across to create an even length.
  5. Take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  6. Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  7. Blend any uneven sections with scissors.
  8. Finish by trimming any stray hairs and cleaning up the neckline.
  9. Style as desired.
  10. Clean and sanitize your tools for future use.

Tips

  • Invest in a high-quality pair of scissors for the best results.
  • Use a comb to section off hair and create a guideline for cutting.
  • If you have curly hair, cut it dry to avoid any shrinkage.
  • Use hair clippers with different guard sizes to create a fade.
  • Don't forget to clean and sanitize your tools after each use.

FAQ

  1. How long should I leave my hair before cutting it again?

    2. It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you're going for. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

  2. Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

    3. It's best to cut hair when it's dry for the most accurate results. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and may result in uneven sections.

  3. What if I make a mistake?

    4. If you make a mistake, don't panic. It's always better to leave a section longer and come back to it later. You can also blend any uneven sections with scissors for a natural look.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's right for your hair type and avoid using too much product. Trimming your hair regularly will also help prevent split ends and maintain a healthy appearance.

By following these steps and tips, you'll be able to cut men's hair with scissors long and achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Remember to take your time, cut small sections at a time, and clean and sanitize your tools for future use.

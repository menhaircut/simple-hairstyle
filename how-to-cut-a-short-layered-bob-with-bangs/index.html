How To Cut A Short Layered Bob With Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both chic and low-maintenance, a short layered bob with bangs might be just what you need. This trendy hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years, and for good reason. Not only is it versatile and easy to style, but it also works well with most face shapes and hair types. In this article, we'll show you how to cut a short layered bob with bangs in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

Description

A short layered bob with bangs is a haircut that features layers throughout the hair, creating a voluminous and textured look. The bangs are cut straight across the forehead, adding a youthful and trendy touch. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add some dimension and movement to their hair while keeping it short and easy to maintain.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut a short layered bob with bangs:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 2: Section your hair

Use hair clips to section your hair into four parts: two on the sides, one in the back, and one at the top.

Step 3: Cut the back section

Start with the back section of your hair. Use your comb to create a straight line across the bottom of the section, then cut the hair below the line.

Step 4: Cut the side sections

Move on to the side sections of your hair. Use your comb to lift the hair up, then cut at an angle towards the back of your head.

Step 5: Cut the top section

Finally, move on to the top section of your hair. Use your comb to lift the hair up, then cut at an angle towards the back of your head.

Step 6: Cut the bangs

To cut the bangs, comb them forward and cut them straight across. Make sure to cut them slightly longer than you want them to be, as they will shrink up when they dry.

Step 7: Style your hair

Once you've cut your hair, style it to your liking. You can use a hair dryer, flat iron, or curling iron to create the look you want.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a short layered bob with bangs:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't undo a bad haircut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.
  • Wet your hair again if it starts to dry out during the cutting process.
  • Cut your hair in a well-lit area to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use hair products to enhance the texture and volume of your hair.

FAQ

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Can I cut my hair myself?

While it's possible to cut your own hair, it's not recommended unless you have experience and the right tools. It's always best to seek the help of a professional hairstylist.

What face shape works best with a short layered bob with bangs?

A short layered bob with bangs works well with most face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care

To keep your short layered bob with bangs looking its best, make sure to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. You can also use hair masks and treatments to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Cutting a short layered bob with bangs might seem intimidating at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it's actually quite easy. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips, and you'll be able to achieve a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle in no time.

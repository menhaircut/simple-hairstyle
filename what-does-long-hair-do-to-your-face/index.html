What Does Long Hair Do To Your Face

Written by Petter Jun 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair has always been a symbol of beauty and femininity. However, not many people realize the impact it can have on your face. In this article, we'll discuss how long hair affects your face, the different hairstyles that complement your face shape, and hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and shiny.

Description

Long hair can either enhance or detract from your facial features. It can also create an illusion of a longer or shorter face, depending on the hairstyle you choose. For example, if you have a round face, long hair can make it look slimmer, while a square face can benefit from layers that soften the angles.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The first step to choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes; oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and triangular. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that complements your features. Here are some tips:

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is considered the most versatile, which means you can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, a long layered haircut that draws attention to your cheekbones and eyes can enhance your features.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, a long layered haircut that falls below your chin can make your face look slimmer. You can also try a side-swept bang to create an illusion of a longer face.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape can benefit from soft layers that frame your face. You can also try a side part and long side bangs to soften the angles.

Heart Face Shape

A heart-shaped face can benefit from a chin-length bob or a long layered haircut that falls below your shoulders. You can also try a side part and side-swept bangs to draw attention to your eyes.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape can benefit from a long layered haircut that falls below your shoulders. You can also try a side part and side-swept bangs to soften your features.

Triangular Face Shape

A triangular face shape can benefit from a long layered haircut that falls to the middle of your back. You can also try a middle part and layers that start from your chin to create an illusion of a wider forehead.

Step by Step Guide

Once you have chosen the right hairstyle, here's a step by step guide to achieve it:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo that suits your hair type.
  2. Apply conditioner to the mid-lengths and ends of your hair.
  3. Gently towel-dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner or serum.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  5. If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment to define your curls.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to create your desired hairstyle.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Long Hair Care

Long hair requires special care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or ammonia.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using tight hairstyles that can cause breakage, such as tight ponytails or braids.

FAQs

Does long hair make your face look longer or shorter?

Long hair can create an illusion of a longer or shorter face, depending on the hairstyle you choose. For example, a long layered haircut that falls below your chin can make a round face look slimmer, while a chin-length bob can make a heart-shaped face look wider.

Can long hair cause breakouts?

Long hair can cause breakouts if it touches your face and transfers oil and bacteria. To prevent breakouts, keep your hair away from your face and wash it regularly.

How often should I wash my long hair?

You should wash your long hair every two to three days, depending on your hair type. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less often to prevent stripping it of natural oils.

Conclusion

Long hair can enhance your facial features and create a stunning look. However, it's essential to choose the right hairstyle that complements your face shape and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. By following these tips, you can achieve a gorgeous long hairstyle that makes you look and feel your best.

Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Receding Hairline A Guide For 2023

Written by Frank Nov 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a receding hairline, you may feel limited in your hairstyle options. However, the mens haircut short sides long top receding hairline has become a popular choice in recent years. This style can give the illusion of a fuller head of hair and is versatile enough to work for both formal and casual occasions. In this guide, we will go over how to achieve this style, tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Achieve the Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Receding Hairline

To achieve this style, you will need to have the sides of your hair cut short while leaving the top longer. The hair on the sides should be tapered, meaning it gradually gets shorter as it approaches the ear. The hair on top should be at least two inches long and styled in a way that covers the receding hairline.

When getting this haircut, it is essential to communicate with your barber or hairstylist. Let them know that you have a receding hairline and that you want a style that will work for it. They can give you advice on what will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.

2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a texturizing cream or pomade.

3. Use a comb to create a side part, and comb the hair on the top of your head forward.

4. Use a hair clip to hold the hair on top of your head in place while you trim the sides. Use clippers to gradually cut your hair shorter as you approach your ears.

5. Use scissors to cut the hair on top of your head to your desired length. Be sure to blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides.

6. Style your hair using a blow dryer and a comb or brush. If you want a more textured look, you can use your fingers to style your hair.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Receding Hairline

1. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

2. Regularly trim the sides to maintain the shape of your haircut.

3. Use a styling product that works for your hair type and the look you want to achieve.

4. Consider getting regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

Can I still achieve this style if I have thinning hair?

Yes, this style can work well for thinning hair. The longer hair on top can give the illusion of fuller hair, and the tapered sides can make your hair look thicker.

How often should I get this haircut?

It is recommended that you get this haircut every four to six weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

What styling products should I use?

The product you use will depend on your hair type and the look you want to achieve. A texturizing cream or pomade can help add texture and hold to your hair. A styling gel can help keep your hair in place, while a hairspray can help add volume and hold.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair to maintain the health and appearance of your style. Here are some tips:

- Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner.

- Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often.

- Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.

- Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

- Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

The mens haircut short sides long top receding hairline is an excellent option for those with a receding hairline. With the right cut and styling, you can achieve a look that is both stylish and flattering. Remember to communicate with your barber or hairstylist, take care of your hair, and have fun with your new style!

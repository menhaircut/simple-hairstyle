How to Cut Men's Top

The top cut is a popular men's hairstyle that involves cutting the hair on top of the head shorter than the sides and back. It can be worn in a variety of ways, from a messy, textured look to a sleek, polished finish. Here are the basic steps to cutting men's top:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to see the natural texture and shape of the hair, which is important when cutting the top. You can either wash and dry the hair yourself, or have the client do it before the appointment.

2. Section the Hair

Next, section the hair by dividing it into three parts: the top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to keep each section separate and in place while you work. Start with the top section, as this is the most important part of the cut.

3. Determine the Length

Decide how long you want the hair on top to be. This will depend on the client's preferences and the overall style you are trying to achieve. Use scissors or clippers to remove any excess length, starting at the back and working your way forward. Be sure to cut the hair evenly and in small sections to ensure a clean, even cut.

4. Blend the Sides and Back

Once you have cut the top to the desired length, it's time to blend the sides and back. Use scissors or clippers to remove any excess length, gradually working your way down to the desired length. Be sure to blend the sides and back evenly, so there are no harsh lines or uneven patches.

5. Texturize and Style

Finally, texturize the hair on top to create a more natural, tousled look. Use scissors to cut into the hair, creating small, random pieces that add texture and movement. Style the hair as desired using a styling product, such as pomade or wax.

Tips for Cutting Men's Top

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect men's top cut:

1. Use Sharp Scissors

Sharp scissors will make it easier to cut the hair evenly and accurately. Dull scissors can lead to uneven cuts and split ends.

2. Cut in Small Sections

Cutting the hair in small sections will help you achieve a clean, even cut. It also allows you to see the natural texture and shape of the hair, which is important when cutting the top.

3. Work Gradually

Don't try to cut too much hair at once. Work gradually, removing small amounts of hair at a time, until you achieve the desired length.

4. Use a Comb to Guide Your Cut

Using a comb to guide your cut will help you achieve an even, uniform length. Comb the hair straight up, and use the scissors or clippers to cut the hair at the desired length.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How Often Should You Cut Men's Top?

How often you should cut men's top depends on how fast the hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. On average, men should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their top cut.

2. What Products Should You Use to Style Men's Top?

There are a variety of products you can use to style men's top, including pomade, wax, gel, and hairspray. Choose a product that works well with your hair type and desired style.

3. Can You Cut Men's Top Yourself?

While it's possible to cut men's top yourself, we recommend going to a professional stylist to ensure a clean, even cut. If you do decide to cut your own hair, be sure to use sharp scissors and follow the steps outlined in this article.

Hair Care for Men's Top Cut

Maintaining your men's top cut is easy with a few simple hair care tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip the hair of its natural oils. Avoid using hot water, as this can also dry out the hair and scalp.

2. Condition Regularly

Conditioning the hair regularly will help keep it soft, smooth, and manageable. Choose a conditioner that works well with your hair type and desired style.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as these can damage the hair and cause split ends. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.

4. Trim Regularly

To maintain your men's top cut, trim the hair regularly every 4-6 weeks. This will help keep the shape and length of the cut looking fresh and polished.

Now that you know how to cut men's top, it's time to book your next appointment with a professional stylist. Follow these tips and hair care advice to keep your new look looking its best.

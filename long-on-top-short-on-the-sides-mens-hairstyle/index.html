Long On Top Short On The Sides Men s Hairstyle How To Achieve And Maintain It In 2023

If you're looking for a trendy men's hairstyle that's easy to maintain, the long on top short on the sides cut might just be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been around for a while now, and it's still going strong in 2023. In this article, we'll take a closer look at this popular hairstyle, and we'll give you some tips on how to achieve and maintain it.

Description

The long on top short on the sides men's hairstyle is exactly what it sounds like: the hair on top of the head is left long, while the hair on the sides and back is cut short. This hairstyle can be customized in many ways, with different lengths and styles for the top and sides. Some men choose to slick back the long hair on top, while others prefer a messy, textured look. This hairstyle is versatile, and it can be worn in many different settings, from casual to formal.

How to Achieve the Long on Top Short on the Sides Men's Hairstyle

The long on top short on the sides men's hairstyle is relatively easy to achieve, and it doesn't require much maintenance. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Lengths

The first step in achieving this hairstyle is to choose the right lengths for the top and sides of your head. The top should be long enough to style, but not so long that it becomes unmanageable. The sides and back should be short, but not so short that they look like a buzz cut. A good length for the sides is usually around a 2 or 3 on the clippers.

Step 2: Cut the Hair

The next step is to cut the hair to the chosen lengths. You can either do this yourself or go to a professional barber. If you're doing it yourself, make sure you have the right tools, including clippers, scissors, and a comb. Use the clippers to cut the sides and back to the desired length, and use the scissors to trim the hair on top to the right length.

Step 3: Style the Hair

Once the hair is cut to the right lengths, it's time to style it. The exact styling will depend on your personal preference and the length of your hair on top. Some men prefer a slicked-back look, while others prefer a messy, textured style. Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to achieve the desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to achieve the long on top short on the sides men's hairstyle:

Step 1: Choose the Right Lengths

Decide how long you want the hair on top to be, and choose a length for the sides and back. A good general rule is to keep the sides and back around a 2 or 3 on the clippers.

Step 2: Cut the Hair on the Sides and Back

Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach the desired length. Use the comb to guide the clippers, and make sure to cut in the direction of the hair growth.

Step 3: Cut the Hair on Top

Use scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length. Start by combing the hair straight up, and then trim the ends with the scissors. Use the comb to blend the top with the sides.

Step 4: Style the Hair

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to the hair on top. Style the hair in the desired way, whether it's slicked back or messy and textured.

Tips for Maintaining the Long on Top Short on the Sides Men's Hairstyle

Now that you have achieved the long on top short on the sides men's hairstyle, here are some tips for maintaining it:

  • Get regular trims to keep the hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and clean.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Is the long on top short on the sides men's hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit all face shapes, whether you have a round, square, or oval face.

What kind of styling product should I use?

The type of styling product you use will depend on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Pomade or wax are good options for most hair types.

How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the hair looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care for the Long on Top Short on the Sides Men's Hairstyle

Proper hair care is important if you want to keep the long on top short on the sides men's hairstyle looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and clean.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage the hair.
  • Avoid washing the hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair moisturized and healthy.

Overall, the long on top short on the sides men's hairstyle is a stylish and versatile option for men of all ages. With the right lengths and styling products, you can achieve a look that's perfect for your personal style.

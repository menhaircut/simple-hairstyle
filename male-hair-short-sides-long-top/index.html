Description

Male hair short sides long top has been a popular hairstyle for men for many years now, and it doesn't seem to be going away anytime soon. This look is characterized by having short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top that can be styled in a variety of ways.

The versatility of this hairstyle is one of the reasons why it's so popular. Depending on how you style it, it can be edgy and rebellious, or neat and professional. It's a great option for men who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish and put-together.

How to Achieve the Male Hair Short Sides Long Top Look

If you're interested in trying out this hairstyle for yourself, there are a few things you should know. First of all, it's important to find a good hairstylist who has experience with this type of cut. They'll be able to help you determine the best length and style for your hair type and face shape.

When it comes to styling the hair, there are a few different options. Some men prefer to keep the hair on top slicked back or parted to the side, while others like to add some texture and volume to the style. You can achieve this by using a pomade or wax to create a messy, tousled look.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you want to achieve the male hair short sides long top look at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any buildup and make your hair easier to style.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, focusing on the top section. You can use a pomade, wax, or gel, depending on the look you're going for.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape. You can slick it back, part it to the side, or add some texture and volume.
  5. Use a blow dryer to set the style in place.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Once you've achieved the male hair short sides long top look, it's important to maintain it. Here are a few tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep the sides and back of your hair short and tidy.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking and feeling great.

FAQ

Here are some common questions people have about male hair short sides long top:

Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

This hairstyle can work for most hair types, but it's best suited for men with straight or wavy hair. If you have very curly hair, it may be more difficult to achieve the desired look.

How often do I need to get a trim?

You'll need to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the short sides and back of your hair.

Can I style my hair differently every day?

Absolutely! That's one of the great things about this hairstyle – it's versatile enough to be styled in different ways depending on your mood or the occasion.

Hair Care for Male Hair Short Sides Long Top

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve and maintain the male hair short sides long top look with ease. Whether you're going for a sleek and sophisticated style or a messy, textured look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make a statement.

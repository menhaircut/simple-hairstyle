How To Do Underarm Makeup Tips Tricks And Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 18, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about your underarms? Do you want to learn how to do underarm makeup like a pro? Well, you’ve come to the right place! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to do underarm makeup, as well as some tips, tricks and FAQs to help you achieve flawless underarms.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Underarm Makeup
  • Tips for Applying Underarm Makeup
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Underarms

How to Do Underarm Makeup

Before we begin, it’s important to note that underarm makeup should only be used for special occasions and not as a daily routine. Also, make sure to use cosmetic products that are specifically designed for the underarm area.

Step 1: Clean and exfoliate your underarms to remove any dead skin cells and debris. This will help the makeup to adhere better and last longer.

Step 2: Apply a thin layer of primer to your underarms. This will help to smooth out any bumps or roughness, and create a base for the makeup to stick to.

Step 3: Apply a thin layer of foundation to your underarms. Use a shade that matches your skin tone, and blend it in well using a brush or sponge.

Step 4: Apply a thin layer of concealer to any areas that need extra coverage, such as dark spots or discoloration. Blend it in well with the foundation.

Step 5: Set your underarm makeup with a translucent powder. This will help to keep it in place and prevent it from smudging or rubbing off.

Step 6: Apply a light layer of shimmer or bronzer to your underarms, if desired. This will add a subtle glow and enhance your skin tone.

Step 7: Finish off with a setting spray to keep your underarm makeup in place all day or night.

Tips for Applying Underarm Makeup

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve flawless underarms:

  • Use a light hand when applying underarm makeup to avoid any streaks or smudging.
  • Choose products that are waterproof and sweat-resistant to avoid any mishaps.
  • Make sure to blend your underarm makeup well to avoid any harsh lines or patches.
  • Use a hair dryer on the cool setting to help set your underarm makeup in place.

FAQs

Is underarm makeup safe to use?

Yes, underarm makeup is safe to use as long as you use it properly and choose products that are specifically designed for the underarm area.

Can underarm makeup cause skin irritation?

It’s possible that underarm makeup can cause skin irritation, especially if you have sensitive skin. To avoid any problems, make sure to do a patch test before applying underarm makeup to your entire underarm area.

How long does underarm makeup last?

Underarm makeup can last up to 8 hours, depending on the products you use and how well you apply them.

Hair Care for Underarms

In addition to underarm makeup, it’s important to take care of your underarm hair to ensure that your skin looks and feels its best. Here are some tips for underarm hair care:

  • Shave or wax your underarm hair regularly to prevent ingrown hairs and irritation.
  • Exfoliate your underarms once a week to remove dead skin cells and prevent ingrown hairs.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or deodorants on your underarms that can cause irritation or allergic reactions.

Now that you know how to do underarm makeup like a pro, you can rock sleeveless tops and dresses with confidence. With a little practice and the right products, you can achieve flawless underarms that look and feel amazing. Happy glamming!

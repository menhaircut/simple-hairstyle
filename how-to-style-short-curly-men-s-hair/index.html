How To Style Short Curly Men s Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Short curly hair can be challenging to style, especially for men. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a great look that lasts all day. This guide will teach you how to style short curly men's hair step by step, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Style Short Curly Men's Hair

The first step to styling short curly hair is to start with clean, dry hair. Make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and reduce frizz.

Next, apply a styling product that is appropriate for your hair type. For short curly hair, a lightweight styling cream or gel can work well. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to work it in from root to tip.

Once you have applied the product, you can begin to style your hair. There are several different techniques that you can use, depending on the look that you are going for.

Curls + Volume

If you want to emphasize your curls and add some volume to your hair, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to enhance your natural curls while also adding volume and body.

To use a diffuser, start by flipping your head upside down and placing the diffuser at the roots of your hair. Turn the blow dryer on to a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with your hands. Continue to work your way through your hair, making sure to scrunch and lift as you go.

Sleek Look

If you prefer a sleeker, more polished look, you can use a flat iron to straighten your hair. This can help to tame frizz and create a smoother, more refined look.

To use a flat iron, start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair. Then, section your hair into small sections and run the flat iron over each section, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Textured Look

If you want to create a textured, messy look, you can use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray. These products can help to add texture and definition to your curls, while also providing a matte finish.

To use a texturizing spray, apply it evenly throughout your hair, making sure to work it in with your hands. Then, use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair, creating a messy, textured look.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to styling short curly men's hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product that is appropriate for your hair type.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls and add volume, or use a flat iron for a sleeker look.
  4. Apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray for a textured, messy look.
  5. Use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair, creating a messy, textured look.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or pomade to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Short Curly Men's Hair

Here are some tips for styling short curly men's hair:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls and add volume.
  • Use a flat iron for a sleeker, more polished look.
  • Apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray for a textured, messy look.
  • Use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair, creating a messy, textured look.
  • Finish with a hairspray or pomade to hold your style in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short curly men's hair:

Q: What products should I use to style short curly men's hair?

A: For short curly hair, a lightweight styling cream or gel can work well. You can also use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray for a textured, messy look.

Q: How often should I wash my short curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: Do I need to use a diffuser attachment on my blow dryer?

A: No, you don't need to use a diffuser attachment, but it can help to enhance your curls and add volume.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Men's Hair

Here are some hair care tips for short curly men's hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curly hair, as this can cause frizz.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a great look for your short curly hair. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

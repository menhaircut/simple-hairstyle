Best Short Haircuts For Oval Shaped Faces

Are you tired of searching for the perfect haircut that will flatter your oval-shaped face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for oval shaped faces, including how to style them and tips for hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step

Description

Oval shaped faces are considered to be the most versatile face shape, as they can pull off a wide range of haircuts. However, not every haircut is suitable for an oval shaped face. Short haircuts are a great option for those with oval shaped faces, as they can help to accentuate your features and show off your face shape.

How to

Before getting into the best short haircuts for oval shaped faces, it's important to understand how to determine if you have an oval face shape. An oval face shape is characterized by a balanced proportion of features, with a prominent forehead, high cheekbones, and a slightly narrow chin.

Once you've determined that you have an oval shaped face, it's time to choose your perfect haircut! When selecting a short haircut for an oval shaped face, it's important to consider your hair texture, as well as your personal style. Here are some popular short haircuts for oval shaped faces:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that is a great option for oval shaped faces. This haircut is characterized by short layers that are cut close to the scalp, and can be styled to create a variety of looks.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short haircut for oval shaped faces. This cut is characterized by a straight line at the bottom of the hair, and can be styled with bangs or without.

Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered haircut that is perfect for those with wavy or curly hair. This cut is characterized by long, choppy layers that create a messy, textured look.

Step by Step

Once you've chosen your perfect short haircut for your oval shaped face, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some simple steps to help you achieve the perfect look:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair as desired.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain your short haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the perfect one for your hair type.

FAQ

What is the most flattering haircut for an oval shaped face?

Short haircuts, such as the pixie cut, bob cut, and shag cut, are all flattering haircuts for oval shaped faces.

Can oval shaped faces pull off bangs?

Yes, oval shaped faces can pull off bangs. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. Side-swept bangs are a great option for those with oval shaped faces.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy, beautiful haircut. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in great condition:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get plenty of rest to help your hair grow and stay healthy.

Now that you know the best short haircuts for oval shaped faces, it's time to book your next haircut appointment! With the right haircut and styling, you can show off your beautiful oval shaped face and feel confident and stylish.

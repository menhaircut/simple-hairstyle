How Do Guys Slick Their Hair Back

Written by Bardi Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to achieve a sleek and stylish look? Slicked back hair is a popular choice for many men, but it can be tricky to get it just right. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how do guys slick their hair back, step by step. From choosing the right products to maintaining your sleek style, we've got you covered.

Description

Slicked back hair has been a classic look for men for decades. It's a versatile style that can be worn for any occasion, from a formal event to a casual night out. The key to achieving a polished look is to use the right products and techniques. With the right tools and a little bit of practice, you can master the art of slicking your hair back.

How To Slick Your Hair Back

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect slicked back look:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to achieving a sleek look is to start with clean hair. Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt and excess oil. This will help your hair to look shiny and healthy.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz. Instead, pat your hair dry and let it air dry for a few minutes.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply a small amount of pomade or hair gel to your hair. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair. Be sure to apply the product from the roots to the tips of your hair to ensure a sleek look.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a fine-tooth comb, start combing your hair back from your forehead. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction that you want it to go. Keep combing until your hair is slicked back and looks neat and tidy.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is slicked back, you can style it to your liking. You can leave it as is for a classic look or use your fingers to create a more textured look. Be creative and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back look:

  • Use a high-quality pomade or hair gel for best results
  • Apply the product evenly throughout your hair to avoid clumps
  • Choose a comb with fine teeth to get a sleek finish
  • Don't use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how do guys slick their hair back:

What products should I use to slick my hair back?

You can use either pomade or hair gel to slick your hair back. Pomade is ideal for a more natural look, while hair gel provides a stronger hold.

How often should I slick my hair back?

You can slick your hair back as often as you like. However, make sure to wash your hair regularly to avoid product buildup and damage.

Can I slick my hair back if I have curly or wavy hair?

Yes, you can still slick your hair back even if you have curly or wavy hair. However, you may need to use a stronger hold product to keep your hair in place.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

Now that you know how do guys slick their hair back, you can try this classic look for yourself. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and stylish look that's perfect for any occasion. So go ahead and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

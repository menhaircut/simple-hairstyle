How To Style Thick Wiry Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having thick, wiry hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks great when styled right, it can also be difficult to tame and style. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to style your thick, wiry hair so that you can look and feel your best every day.

How to Style Thick Wiry Hair

Styling thick, wiry hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, it can be done. Here are some tips on how to style your hair:

1. Start with a good haircut

A good haircut is the foundation for any great hairstyle. When getting a haircut, make sure to communicate with your stylist about your hair type and the look you want to achieve. A good stylist will be able to recommend a haircut that works with your hair type and face shape.

2. Use the right products

Using the right products can make a big difference when it comes to styling thick, wiry hair. Look for products that are designed for your hair type, such as thickening shampoos and conditioners, and styling products like gels and pomades.

3. Blow dry your hair

Blow drying your hair can help to tame it and make it more manageable. Use a round brush while blow drying to add volume and shape to your hair.

4. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Try out different haircuts, styles, and products until you find a look that you love.

Step by Step: How to Style Thick Wiry Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your thick, wiry hair:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz and damage.

Step 3: Apply styling product

Apply a styling product like gel or pomade to your hair, focusing on the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 4: Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.

Step 5: Style your hair

Style your hair using your fingers or a comb to create your desired look.

Tips for Styling Thick Wiry Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling thick, wiry hair:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it's less likely to cause breakage and damage.

2. Don't over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it more difficult to style. Try washing your hair every other day or every few days instead.

3. Use a leave-in conditioner

Using a leave-in conditioner can help to keep your hair moisturized and make it easier to style.

4. Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, making it easier to style.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my thick, wiry hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your thick, wiry hair using a flat iron. However, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using the flat iron on wet hair.

Q: Can I use hair spray on my thick, wiry hair?

A: Yes, you can use hair spray on your thick, wiry hair to help hold your style in place. Look for a hairspray that is designed for your hair type and use it sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair.

Hair Care for Thick Wiry Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your thick, wiry hair:

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

2. Avoid using hot tools too often

Using hot tools like curling irons and flat irons too often can damage your hair, making it more difficult to style. Try to limit your use of hot tools and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair, making it more difficult to style. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With the right techniques and products, styling thick, wiry hair can be easy and fun. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products until you find a look that you love.

Short Curly Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50

Written by Christine Aug 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we get older, our hair changes in texture and thickness, and our face shape may become more round. However, that doesn't mean we can't have stylish and flattering hairstyles. If you have a round face and are over 50, short curly hairstyles can be a great option. In this article, we'll provide you with a guide on how to choose the best short curly hairstyles for round faces over 50, as well as tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

Round faces are characterized by having fuller cheeks and a softer jawline. To balance out the roundness, it's important to choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the top of the head, while keeping the sides shorter. Short curly hairstyles can help achieve this look by adding texture and movement to the hair.

How to Choose a Short Curly Hairstyle for Round Faces Over 50

When choosing a short curly hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips:

Consider your face shape

For round faces, it's best to avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as blunt bobs or straight bangs. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to the top of the head, such as layered pixie cuts or curly bobs with side-swept bangs.

Consider your hair texture

If you have naturally curly hair, embrace it! Short curly hairstyles can showcase your natural texture and add volume to your hair. If you have straight or wavy hair, consider adding curls or waves with a curling iron or hot rollers.

Consider your lifestyle

Short curly hairstyles require some maintenance to keep their shape and volume. If you have a busy lifestyle and don't have time to style your hair every day, opt for a low-maintenance hairstyle, such as a curly pixie cut or a curly bob with layers.

Step by Step: How to Style Short Curly Hair for Round Faces Over 50

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style short curly hair:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for curly hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner to help prevent frizz and add moisture to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product, such as a curl defining cream or mousse, to damp hair. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, while scrunching the curls with your hands.

Step 3: Define your curls

Use a curling iron or hot rollers to define your curls and add volume to your hair. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron or hot rollers, holding for a few seconds before releasing.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light hairspray to hold the curls in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Short Curly Hair Care

Here are some tips for caring for your short curly hair:

Use products designed for curly hair

Curly hair requires more moisture than straight hair, so use products that are designed for curly hair to help nourish and define your curls.

Avoid over-washing your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz. Instead, try washing your hair every other day or using a dry shampoo in between washes.

Protect your hair while sleeping

Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from getting tangled and frizzy while you sleep. You can also wrap your hair in a silk scarf before going to bed.

FAQs

What are some short curly hairstyles for round faces over 50?

Some short curly hairstyles for round faces over 50 include layered pixie cuts, curly bobs with side-swept bangs, and curly pixie cuts with longer layers on top.

Can I dye my short curly hair?

Yes, you can dye your short curly hair. However, it's important to use products that are designed for curly hair and to avoid over-processing your hair, as this can lead to damage and dryness.

How often should I trim my short curly hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Overall, short curly hairstyles can be a great option for round faces over 50. By choosing the right hairstyle and using the proper hair care techniques, you can have stylish and healthy hair at any age.

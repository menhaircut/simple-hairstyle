How To Cut Ladies Short Hair During Lockdown

Written by Robby Sep 19, 2023 · 4 min read

With the pandemic still present, many people are still hesitant to go to salons for haircut. However, maintaining hair is important for everyone, especially for women. Short hair can be a relief during hot summer months and can also be a trendy style statement. So, if you are looking to cut your hair short during lockdown, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you out.

Description

Many women prefer to have short hair as it is easy to manage, comfortable and looks stylish. However, due to the pandemic, it is not possible for everyone to go to a salon for a haircut. Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with proper guidance and tools, it can be done easily. This blog post provides a detailed guide on how to cut short hair during lockdown.

How to Cut Ladies Short Hair During Lockdown

Before starting the haircut, it’s important to gather all the necessary tools such as sharp scissors, a comb, clips, and a handheld mirror. Also, it’s better to have someone who can assist you during the process.

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair and let it dry completely. This will help to remove any tangles and make it easier to cut. Also, make sure to comb through your hair to get rid of any knots.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips. Start by separating the top section of your hair from the bottom. Clip the top section on top of your head and leave the bottom section loose.

Step 3: Start cutting

Begin by trimming the ends of the bottom section of your hair. Hold a small section of hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Follow the same process for the rest of the hair in the bottom section.

Step 4: Cut the top section

Release the top section of your hair and comb it forward. Cut the hair in a straight line, starting from the front and working your way towards the back. Make sure to cut the hair evenly and gradually.

Step 5: Check for any unevenness

After finishing, it’s important to check for any unevenness in the haircut. Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your hair and make sure that both sides are equal in length.

Tips

- Always use sharp scissors to avoid any damage to your hair.
- Cut your hair in small sections to ensure evenness.
- Don’t cut your hair too short in the first attempt. You can always trim it further later.
- If possible, have someone assist you during the process.

FAQ

1. How do I know which short haircut will suit me?

You can search for different short hairstyles online or consult with a hairstylist for suggestions based on your face shape and hair texture.

2. Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, regular scissors are not recommended for cutting hair as they can damage the hair and cause split ends. Always use sharp scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair.

3. How often should I cut my short hair?

It depends on the individual’s hair growth rate and preference. However, it is generally recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Also, avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools.

With these simple steps and tips, cutting your short hair during lockdown can be a hassle-free experience. However, if you are not confident enough to do it yourself, it’s better to wait until it’s safe to visit a salon. Stay safe and take care of your hair!

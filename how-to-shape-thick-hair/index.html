How To Shape Thick Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Sep 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your thick hair looking bulky and unmanageable? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with styling their thick hair in a way that looks sleek and polished. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to shape thick hair and achieve a look that you'll love.

Table of Contents

  • How to shape thick hair
  • Step-by-step guide
  • Tips for shaping thick hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair care for thick hair

How to Shape Thick Hair

The key to shaping thick hair is to use the right tools and products. Here are some essentials:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Quality hair dryer
  • Round brush
  • Hair spray
  • Heat protectant

Once you have these tools, you can start shaping your hair.

1. Blow dry your hair

Start by blow drying your hair until it's about 80% dry. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair as you dry it. This will help to straighten your hair and make it easier to shape.

2. Section your hair

Section your hair into 2-3 inch sections, depending on the thickness of your hair. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

3. Use a round brush

Take one section of hair and use a round brush to shape it. Start at the roots and roll the brush down to the ends of your hair. Use a hair dryer to heat the brush as you roll it. This will help to set the shape of your hair.

4. Repeat

Repeat this process with each section of your hair, working from the bottom layers to the top layers. Use hair spray to hold the shape in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to shaping your thick hair:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Blow dry your hair until it's about 80% dry.
  4. Section your hair into 2-3 inch sections using hair clips.
  5. Take one section of hair and use a round brush to shape it.
  6. Start at the roots and roll the brush down to the ends of your hair.
  7. Use a hair dryer to heat the brush as you roll it.
  8. Repeat this process with each section of your hair.
  9. Use hair spray to hold the shape in place.
  10. Finish with a light mist of hair oil to add shine.

Tips for Shaping Thick Hair

Here are some tips to help you shape your thick hair:

  • Use a heat protectant to prevent damage.
  • Blow dry your hair until it's about 80% dry before shaping it.
  • Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb as you dry it.
  • Use a round brush to shape your hair.
  • Work in small sections to ensure that you shape your hair evenly.
  • Use hair spray to hold the shape in place.
  • Finish with a light mist of hair oil to add shine.

FAQs

Q: How often should I shape my thick hair?

A: This depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain it. Generally, you should shape your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Q: Can I shape my thick hair at home?

A: Yes, you can shape your thick hair at home. Just be sure to use the right tools and products, and follow our step-by-step guide.

Q: What if I make a mistake while shaping my hair?

A: Don't worry – mistakes happen. If you make a mistake while shaping your hair, you can always start over or try to fix it with some additional styling.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your thick hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to keep your hair healthy and strong.

By following these tips and tricks, you can shape your thick hair and achieve a look that you'll love. Remember to use the right tools and products, and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.

How to Style Medium Length Curly Hair Guys: Tips and Tricks

Curly hair is unique, and it can be challenging to manage, especially when it comes to styling. If you're a guy with medium length curly hair, you're in luck because there are several ways to style your hair to look great. In this article, we'll discuss how to style medium length curly hair guys and provide you with some tips and tricks to make your hair look its best.

How to Style Medium Length Curly Hair Guys

Before we dive into the styling process, it's essential to understand your hair type. Curly hair is classified into three types, ranging from loose waves to tight coils. Medium length curly hair is usually classified as type 3, which is tight curls. Knowing your hair type will help you choose the right products and styling techniques.

When it comes to styling medium length curly hair, the key is to keep it hydrated and use products that enhance your curls' natural texture. Here are some tips on how to style medium length curly hair guys.

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Your curls need moisture to look their best, so start by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain natural oils like coconut or argan oil, which will help nourish and hydrate your hair.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help lock in moisture. A leave-in conditioner will also help detangle your curls and reduce frizz.

3. Use a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product like a curl cream or gel to enhance your curls' natural texture. Apply the product to damp hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

4. Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair gently. A diffuser will help enhance your curls' definition and reduce frizz. Start by drying your hair on low heat and low speed, scrunching your curls with your hands as you go.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of styling medium length curly hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect curls.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product residue.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle it and lock in moisture. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly.

Step 3: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots. Use your fingers to scrunch your curls as you apply the product.

Step 4: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair gently. Start by drying your hair on low heat and low speed, scrunching your curls with your hands as you go.

Step 5: Finish with a Hair Spray

Finish your style with a light hairspray to help hold your curls in place. Use a flexible hold hairspray to avoid making your hair stiff or crunchy.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your medium length curly hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel to reduce frizz.
  • Try different curl-enhancing products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Don't overwash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

FAQ

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type, but most curly hair guys can get away with washing their hair once or twice a week. Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

What's the best way to detangle curly hair?

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots, gently separating any knots or tangles as you go.

Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and a flat iron with adjustable temperature settings. Straightening your hair too often can cause heat damage.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Guys

Curly hair requires extra care to look its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle and lock in moisture.
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Don't overwash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel to reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons too often, as this can cause heat damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your medium length curly hair to look its best. Remember to embrace your curls and experiment with different products and techniques to find the ones that work best for you.

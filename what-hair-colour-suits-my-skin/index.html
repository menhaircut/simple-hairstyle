What Hair Colour Suits My Skin

Written by Oliver Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking of changing your hair colour but not sure which shade would suit you best? The key to finding the perfect hair colour is to choose a shade that complements your skin tone. In this article, we will be discussing which hair colour suits your skin and how to choose the right shade for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hair Colour
  • Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Perfect Hair Colour
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

Description

Your skin tone is an important factor when choosing a hair colour. If you have warm undertones, you should opt for warm colours such as golden blondes, honey browns, and copper shades. If you have cool undertones, you should choose cool colours such as ashy blondes, cool browns, and burgundy shades. Neutral undertones can pull off both warm and cool shades.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour

There are a few ways to determine your skin tone:

  • Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If you can't tell, you likely have neutral undertones.
  • Take a look at the jewellery you wear. If you look better in gold, you have warm undertones. If you look better in silver, you have cool undertones. If you can wear both, you have neutral undertones.
  • Consider the colour of your eyes. If you have warm-toned eyes such as brown, hazel, or amber, you likely have warm undertones. If you have cool-toned eyes such as blue, green, or grey, you likely have cool undertones.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Perfect Hair Colour

Now that you know your skin tone, it's time to choose the perfect hair colour:

  • If you have warm undertones, opt for warm colours such as golden blondes, honey browns, and copper shades.
  • If you have cool undertones, choose cool colours such as ashy blondes, cool browns, and burgundy shades.
  • If you have neutral undertones, you can pull off both warm and cool shades. Try a neutral brown or blonde.
  • Consider your natural hair colour. If you're looking to go lighter, choose a shade that is no more than two shades lighter than your natural colour. If you're looking to go darker, choose a shade that is no more than two shades darker than your natural colour.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best shade for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour

Once you've chosen the perfect hair colour, it's important to maintain it:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as this can strip the colour.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons as they can also cause fading.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair a different colour if I have previously dyed it?

A: Yes, but it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist to avoid damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: It depends on the colour and how fast your hair grows. Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best schedule for touch-ups.

Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

If you've coloured your hair, it's important to take extra care of it:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach and ammonia.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid chlorine as it can cause discolouration.

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, but with these tips, you can find the shade that suits you best. Always consult with a professional hairstylist to ensure the best results.

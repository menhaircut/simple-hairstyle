How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers On Top

Written by William Jun 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to save money on haircuts or you just want to learn how to cut men's hair with clippers on top, you've come to the right place. Cutting hair at home with clippers is a great way to achieve a fresh, modern look without breaking the bank. In this article, we will walk you through the steps of cutting men's hair with clippers on top, and provide useful tips and tricks to help you achieve a professional-looking cut.

Description

Cutting men's hair with clippers on top is a simple process that involves using a clipper to trim the hair to a desired length. Clippers come with various guard sizes, which allow you to adjust the length of the hair you want to cut. This makes it easy to achieve a consistent and even cut every time. Additionally, clippers are easy to use, and with a little practice, you can quickly master the art of cutting hair at home.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers on Top

Before you begin cutting hair with clippers, make sure you have the following tools:

  • A pair of clippers with various guard sizes
  • A comb
  • A pair of scissors
  • A mirror

Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the hair is clean and free of any debris or product. Use a towel to dry the hair, and make sure it's completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Comb the Hair

Next, use a comb to comb the hair in the direction that it naturally falls. This will help you to see the natural parting of the hair and make it easier to cut.

Step 3: Select the Guard Size

Select the guard size that you want to use. The guard size will determine the length of the hair that you will cut. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to a shorter guard size until you achieve the desired length.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair with the clippers, working from the back of the head to the front. Use a steady hand and move the clippers in an upward motion against the direction of the hair growth. Avoid using too much pressure, as this can cause the clippers to cut the hair too short.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut the hair to the desired length, use the scissors to blend the hair. This involves cutting the hair at an angle to create a more natural-looking finish.

Step 6: Finish the Look

To finish the look, trim the hair around the ears and the back of the neck. Use the clippers to create a clean, even line around the edges.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting men's hair with clippers on top:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to a shorter guard size until you achieve the desired length.
  • Use a comb to comb the hair in the direction that it naturally falls.
  • Work in small sections to ensure that you achieve an even cut.
  • Use the scissors to blend the hair for a more natural-looking finish.
  • Trim the hair around the ears and the back of the neck to create a clean, even line.

FAQ

Can I cut men's hair with clippers if I've never done it before?

Yes, cutting men's hair with clippers is easy and straightforward. With a little practice, you can quickly master the art of cutting hair at home.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't worry. Hair grows back, and you can always try again. If you're not sure how to correct the mistake, consider visiting a professional hairstylist.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and well-nourished. Additionally, avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Cutting men's hair with clippers on top is a simple and affordable way to achieve a fresh, modern look. With the right tools and a little practice, you can quickly master the art of cutting hair at home. Follow these steps, tips, and tricks to achieve a professional-looking cut every time.

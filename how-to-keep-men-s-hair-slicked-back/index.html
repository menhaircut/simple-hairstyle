How To Keep Men s Hair Slicked Back

Written by Petter Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your hair falling out of place? Do you want to achieve a sleek and polished look that will turn heads? If so, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll go over how to keep men's hair slicked back in a relaxed and easy-to-understand way.

Description

Slicked-back hair has become a popular trend in men's hairstyles. Whether it's for a formal event or a casual day out, this style can elevate your look and give you a boost of confidence. However, keeping your hair slicked back can be a challenge, especially if you have thick or unruly hair. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and polished look that will last all day.

How to

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to keep men's hair slicked back:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and damp. You can either wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner or wet your hair with a spray bottle. Damp hair is easier to style and will hold its shape better than dry hair.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. This can be a pomade, gel, or wax, depending on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Use a small amount of product and evenly distribute it throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a fine-tooth comb, comb your hair back from the hairline to the crown of your head. Make sure your hair is evenly distributed and there are no clumps or bumps.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once your hair is combed back, you can style it to achieve the desired look. Use your fingers or a brush to smooth out any bumps or waves. You can also use a hairdryer to set the style in place.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To ensure your hair stays in place all day, finish with a light layer of hairspray. This will also add shine and volume to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when keeping men's hair slicked back:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style
  • Use a comb with fine teeth to ensure your hair is evenly distributed
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • If you have thick or unruly hair, consider using a hair straightener before styling
  • Be patient and take your time when styling your hair for the best results

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to keep men's hair slicked back:

What kind of styling product should I use?

This depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Pomade, gel, and wax are all popular options for slicked-back hairstyles.

How much product should I use?

Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing down your hair or making it look greasy. You can always add more if needed.

Can I achieve a slicked-back look with short hair?

Yes, you can! Use a small amount of product and comb your hair back from the hairline to the crown of your head for a stylish and polished look.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair slicked back requires proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair straighteners or curling irons too often
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and polished look that will turn heads. Whether you have short or long hair, thick or thin hair, you can rock a slicked-back hairstyle with confidence.

Hypoallergenic Low Maintenance Medium Sized Dogs A Perfect Companion For Busy People

Written by Aprilia Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a furry friend but don't have the time to groom them constantly? Do you or someone in your family have allergies and need a hypoallergenic dog? Look no further! Here are some of the best hypoallergenic low maintenance medium sized dogs that could be a perfect fit for you.

What is a Hypoallergenic Dog?

A hypoallergenic dog is a breed that produces fewer allergens compared to other breeds. This means that people who are allergic to dogs might still be able to live with a hypoallergenic dog without suffering too much from allergies.

How to Choose the Right Hypoallergenic Low Maintenance Medium Sized Dog

Choosing the right hypoallergenic low maintenance medium sized dog is crucial to ensure that you and your furry friend will be happy together. Here are some factors to consider:

Energy Level

If you have a busy lifestyle and cannot provide your dog with hours of exercise every day, then you should consider a low energy dog. Some hypoallergenic low maintenance medium sized dogs with low energy levels include:

  • Bolognese
  • Cavalier King Charles Spaniel
  • Chinese Crested
  • English Toy Spaniel

Coat Type

Choose a coat type that requires minimal grooming. Some hypoallergenic low maintenance medium sized dogs with minimal grooming requirements include:

  • Airedale Terrier
  • Bichon Frise
  • Poodle
  • Shih Tzu

Temperament

The temperament of a dog is a crucial factor when choosing a furry friend. Some hypoallergenic low maintenance medium sized dogs with friendly personalities include:

  • Basenji
  • Boston Terrier
  • Cairn Terrier
  • Scottish Terrier

Step by Step Guide on Taking Care of Your Hypoallergenic Low Maintenance Medium Sized Dog

Regular Vet Check-Ups

Regular vet check-ups are essential to ensure that your furry friend is healthy and happy. Schedule a vet appointment at least once a year or as advised by your veterinarian.

Feeding

Feed your dog with high-quality dog food that is appropriate for their age, size, and activity level. Always provide fresh water and avoid feeding them table scraps.

Exercise

Provide your furry friend with enough exercise to keep them healthy and happy. A 30-minute daily walk or playtime in the backyard is enough for low energy dogs, while high-energy dogs may need several hours of exercise per day.

Tips on Grooming Your Hypoallergenic Low Maintenance Medium Sized Dog

Bathing

Bathe your dog once every three months or as needed. Use a hypoallergenic dog shampoo to avoid irritating their skin.

Brushing

Brush your dog's coat once a week or as needed. Use a brush appropriate to their coat type to prevent matting and tangles.

Nail Trimming

Trim your dog's nails once every two weeks or as needed. Use a nail clipper appropriate to their nail size and avoid cutting the quick.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Difference Between a Hypoallergenic Dog and a Non-Hypoallergenic Dog?

A hypoallergenic dog produces fewer allergens compared to other breeds, while a non-hypoallergenic dog produces more allergens that can trigger allergies in some people.

Can Hypoallergenic Dogs Still Cause Allergies?

Yes, hypoallergenic dogs can still cause allergies, but they produce fewer allergens compared to non-hypoallergenic dogs.

Do Hypoallergenic Dogs Shed?

Yes, hypoallergenic dogs shed, but they shed less compared to non-hypoallergenic dogs.

Hair Care for Hypoallergenic Low Maintenance Medium Sized Dogs

Hair care for hypoallergenic low maintenance medium sized dogs may vary depending on their coat type. However, here are some general hair care tips:

  • Brush your dog's coat regularly to prevent matting and tangles.
  • Bathe your dog once every three months or as needed.
  • Trim your dog's nails once every two weeks or as needed.
  • Clean your dog's ears and teeth regularly to prevent infections and dental problems.

In conclusion, hypoallergenic low maintenance medium sized dogs can be a perfect companion for busy people who want to have a furry friend without spending too much time grooming them. Remember to choose the right breed, take care of them properly, and enjoy their company!

Short Hairstyles For Oval Faces Over 40

Are you looking for a new short hairstyle that will flatter your oval face and make you look younger? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for oval faces over 40. We will cover how to style your hair, step-by-step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care.

  2. How to

Description

Oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape, as they can pull off almost any haircut. However, if you are over 40, you might want a hairstyle that is not only flattering but also age-appropriate. Short hairstyles are an excellent option as they are easy to maintain and can make you look younger. In this article, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for oval faces over 40.

How to

Start by selecting a short hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair texture. Oval faces can pull off various hairstyles, including pixie cuts, bobs, and shoulder-length hair. Determine your hair texture, whether it is straight, wavy, or curly, and select a style that will work best for you.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style some short hairstyles for oval faces over 40:

Pixie Cut

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. Apply a hair serum or oil to protect your hair from heat damage.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  5. Apply a styling product to hold your hairstyle in place.

Bob Cut

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. Apply a hair serum or oil to protect your hair from heat damage.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  5. Use a curling iron to add some waves to your hair.
  6. Apply a styling product to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when selecting a short hairstyle for oval faces over 40:

  • Look for a style that complements your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consider the amount of time and effort you are willing to spend on styling your hair.
  • Choose a style that enhances your best features.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best short hairstyle for your face shape and hair texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for oval faces over 40:

Q: Can I pull off a pixie cut if I have an oval face?

A: Yes, you can! Oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape and can pull off almost any haircut, including pixie cuts.

Q: What is the best short hairstyle for oval faces over 40?

A: The best short hairstyle for oval faces over 40 depends on your face shape and hair texture. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best style for you.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hairstyle looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and free of split ends.

Now that you know the best short hairstyles for oval faces over 40 and how to style them, it's time to visit your hairstylist and try out a new look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.

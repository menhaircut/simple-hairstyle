Do Bridesmaids Hair Have To Match

Written by Frank Sep 08, 2023 · 4 min read

As bridesmaid, your hair plays a big role in your overall look on the wedding day. One question that often comes up is whether bridesmaids hair have to match. In this article, we’ll explore the answer to this question and provide tips on how to coordinate your hair with the rest of the bridal party.

Table of Content

  • What is the tradition?
  • How to Coordinate Bridesmaids Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Coordinating Bridesmaids Hair
  • Tips for Coordinating Bridesmaids Hair
  • FAQs

What is the tradition?

Traditionally, bridesmaids hair would match the bride’s hair. This was done to create a cohesive look among the bridal party. However, in recent years, this tradition has been relaxed and bridesmaids have more freedom to choose their own hairstyles.

How to Coordinate Bridesmaids Hair

While bridesmaids hair doesn’t have to match exactly, it should complement the overall bridal party look. Here are some tips on how to coordinate bridesmaids hair:

  • Choose a general theme or style for the bridal party hair
  • Consider the bride’s hairstyle and choose bridesmaids hair that will complement it
  • Take into account the bridesmaids’ hair length, texture, and color when choosing hairstyles
  • Consider the wedding dress style and overall theme when choosing hairstyles

Step-by-Step Guide to Coordinating Bridesmaids Hair

If you’re the bride or the maid of honor tasked with coordinating bridesmaids hair, here’s a step-by-step guide to help:

  1. Choose a general theme or style for the bridal party hair
  2. Discuss the theme with the bridesmaids and ask for their input and ideas
  3. Show the bridesmaids pictures or examples of the desired hairstyle
  4. Consider the bridesmaids’ hair length, texture, and color when choosing hairstyles
  5. Choose a hairstyle that complements the bride’s hairstyle and the overall bridal party look
  6. Make sure the chosen hairstyle is achievable for all bridesmaids
  7. Book appointments with hairstylists well in advance
  8. Do a hair trial before the wedding day to make sure the chosen hairstyles work for everyone
  9. On the wedding day, make sure the hairstylists have all the necessary tools and products
  10. Have touch-up kits on hand for any last-minute fixes

Tips for Coordinating Bridesmaids Hair

Here are some additional tips for coordinating bridesmaids hair:

  • Consider adding hair accessories to create a cohesive look
  • Make sure the chosen hairstyles are comfortable and won’t cause any discomfort or headaches
  • Communicate clearly with the bridesmaids about the chosen hairstyles and any expectations
  • Consider the weather and choose hairstyles that will hold up in different conditions
  • Choose hairstyles that will look good in both pictures and in person

FAQs

Do bridesmaids have to have the same hairstyle?

No, bridesmaids don’t have to have the same hairstyle. However, it’s important to coordinate bridesmaids hair to create a cohesive look among the bridal party.

Can bridesmaids choose their own hairstyles?

Yes, bridesmaids can choose their own hairstyles. However, it’s important to communicate with the bride and other bridesmaids to ensure that the chosen hairstyles complement the overall bridal party look.

Can bridesmaids wear their hair down?

Yes, bridesmaids can wear their hair down. However, it’s important to consider the overall bridal party look and choose hairstyles that complement the bride’s hairstyle and the wedding theme.

Hair Care Tips for Bridesmaids

Here are some hair care tips for bridesmaids:

  • Start preparing your hair well in advance of the wedding day
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage
  • Consider using hair masks or oils to keep your hair nourished
  • Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for your hair type and length

Overall, while bridesmaids hair doesn’t have to match exactly, it’s important to coordinate the hairstyles to create a cohesive look among the bridal party. By following these tips and guidelines, you can ensure that everyone looks their best on the wedding day.

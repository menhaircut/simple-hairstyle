What Diamond Shape Fits Your Personality

Written by William Aug 10, 2023 · 7 min read

Choosing the perfect diamond shape for your engagement ring or any other piece of jewelry can be a daunting task. With so many shapes available, it's easy to get overwhelmed. However, your choice of diamond shape can say a lot about your personality. In this blog post, we'll explore what diamond shape fits your personality so that you can make an informed choice.

Table of Contents

  • Classic Round
  • Funky Pear
  • Sophisticated Emerald
  • Edgy Princess
  • Romantic Heart

Classic Round

The classic round diamond shape is timeless and elegant. It's the most popular diamond shape and is perfect for the traditional, romantic person who values simplicity and tradition. The round shape is versatile and looks great in any setting, from solitaires to pave bands. If you're a classic person who values tradition, the round shape is the perfect choice for you.

How to Choose a Classic Round Diamond

When choosing a classic round diamond, look for one with excellent cut quality. The cut is the most important factor in a diamond's beauty, and a well-cut round diamond will have excellent fire and brilliance. Look for a diamond with a G color grade or higher and a clarity grade of VS2 or higher. These diamonds are high-quality and will look stunning in any setting.

Tips for Wearing a Classic Round Diamond

A classic round diamond looks great in any setting, from a simple solitaire to a pavé band. Pair it with a classic wedding band for a timeless look that will never go out of style. Wear it with a simple dress or outfit to let the diamond shine.

FAQs about Classic Round Diamonds

Q: What's the difference between a round cut and a brilliant cut?

A: A round cut refers to the shape of the diamond, while a brilliant cut refers to the way the diamond is cut to maximize its fire and brilliance.

Q: Are round diamonds more expensive than other shapes?

A: Round diamonds are typically more expensive than other shapes because they require more rough diamond to be cut away in the cutting process.

Funky Pear

The funky pear diamond shape is perfect for the person who wants something unique and out of the ordinary. The pear shape is a combination of the classic round shape and the elegant marquise shape, making it a perfect choice for someone who wants a more unusual diamond. If you're a creative, unconventional person who wants something different, the pear shape is the perfect choice for you.

How to Choose a Funky Pear Diamond

When choosing a funky pear diamond, look for one with excellent symmetry. The point of the diamond should be aligned with the center of the rounded end. Look for a diamond with a color grade of G or higher and a clarity grade of VS1 or higher. The pear shape is prone to bow-ties, so make sure to look for one with minimal bow-tie effect.

Tips for Wearing a Funky Pear Diamond

A funky pear diamond looks great in a unique setting, such as a halo or a split-shank band. Pair it with a wedding band that complements the shape of the diamond, such as a curved band. Wear it with a bold outfit to show off your individuality.

FAQs about Funky Pear Diamonds

Q: Are pear-shaped diamonds more fragile than other shapes?

A: Pear-shaped diamonds are not more fragile than other shapes. However, they do have a pointed end that can be prone to chipping if not properly cared for.

Q: Can a pear-shaped diamond be worn with a straight wedding band?

A: Yes, a pear-shaped diamond can be worn with a straight wedding band. However, a curved band may be more comfortable and complement the shape of the diamond more effectively.

Sophisticated Emerald

The sophisticated emerald diamond shape is perfect for the person who wants something sophisticated and elegant. The emerald shape has a rectangular shape with cut corners, and it's perfect for the person who wants a diamond that exudes class and sophistication. If you're a sophisticated, elegant person who values quality, the emerald shape is the perfect choice for you.

How to Choose a Sophisticated Emerald Diamond

When choosing a sophisticated emerald diamond, look for one with excellent clarity. The emerald shape has long flat planes, which can show inclusions more easily than other shapes. Look for a diamond with a color grade of H or higher and a clarity grade of VS2 or higher. The emerald shape is also prone to the bow-tie effect, so make sure to choose one with minimal bow-tie effect.

Tips for Wearing a Sophisticated Emerald Diamond

A sophisticated emerald diamond looks great in a classic setting, such as a solitaire or a three-stone ring. Pair it with a classic wedding band for a timeless look. Wear it with a formal outfit to show off its elegance.

FAQs about Sophisticated Emerald Diamonds

Q: What's the difference between an emerald cut and a step cut?

A: An emerald cut is a type of step cut, which refers to the way the diamond is cut with long, narrow facets that resemble steps.

Q: Can an emerald-shaped diamond be set in a halo setting?

A: Yes, an emerald-shaped diamond can be set in a halo setting. However, make sure to choose a halo setting with prongs that protect the corners of the diamond.

Edgy Princess

The edgy princess diamond shape is perfect for the person who wants something bold and modern. The princess shape has a square shape with pointed corners, and it's perfect for the person who wants a diamond that stands out from the crowd. If you're an edgy, modern person who values individuality, the princess shape is the perfect choice for you.

How to Choose an Edgy Princess Diamond

When choosing an edgy princess diamond, look for one with excellent cut quality. The princess shape is known for its fire and brilliance, so choose one with a cut grade of very good or higher. Look for a diamond with a color grade of H or higher and a clarity grade of VS2 or higher.

Tips for Wearing an Edgy Princess Diamond

An edgy princess diamond looks great in a modern setting, such as a tension setting or a split-shank band. Pair it with a wedding band that complements the sharp corners of the diamond, such as a channel-set band. Wear it with a bold outfit to show off its edgy style.

FAQs about Edgy Princess Diamonds

Q: Are princess-cut diamonds more expensive than other shapes?

A: Princess-cut diamonds are not more expensive than other shapes. However, they do require more rough diamond to be cut away in the cutting process, which can make them slightly more expensive.

Q: Can a princess-cut diamond be set in a vintage setting?

A: Yes, a princess-cut diamond can be set in a vintage setting. However, make sure to choose a setting that complements the modern shape of the diamond.

Romantic Heart

The romantic heart diamond shape is perfect for the person who wants something romantic and sentimental. The heart shape is a unique and rare shape that's perfect for the person who wants a diamond that symbolizes love and commitment. If you're a romantic, sentimental person who values love and connection, the heart shape is the perfect choice for you.

How to Choose a Romantic Heart Diamond

When choosing a romantic heart diamond, look for one with excellent symmetry. The two lobes of the heart should be symmetrical and well-proportioned. Look for a diamond with a color grade of G or higher and a clarity grade of VS1 or higher. The heart shape is also prone to the bow-tie effect, so make sure to choose one with minimal bow-tie effect.

Tips for Wearing a Romantic Heart Diamond

A romantic heart diamond looks great in a simple solitaire setting that lets the diamond shine. Pair it with a delicate wedding band for a romantic and sentimental look. Wear it with a romantic outfit to show off its heart shape.

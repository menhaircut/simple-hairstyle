How To Cut My Hair Medium Length A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 11, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle and cut your hair to a medium length? Cutting your own hair may seem daunting, but with the proper technique and tools, it can be done easily at home. In this blog post, we will guide you through the steps of cutting your hair to a medium length, provide tips to ensure a successful cut, and answer some common questions about hair care.

How to Cut My Hair Medium Length

Before you begin, gather the necessary tools: a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It’s also helpful to have a full-length mirror and good lighting. Start with clean, dry hair and follow these steps:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into four sections: two front sections and two back sections. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 2: Cut the Back Sections

Start with the back sections. Take one section and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers where you want to cut it, and cut straight across. Repeat with the other back section.

Step 3: Cut the Front Sections

Now move on to the front sections. Comb the hair forward and hold it between your fingers where you want to cut it. Cut straight across, using the back sections as a guide for length.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After cutting all four sections, check for evenness. Comb through your hair and look for any sections that are longer or shorter than the rest. Trim these sections as needed.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair

Here are some tips to ensure a successful cut:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length to avoid cutting off too much at once.
  • Use a comb to keep the hair taut and ensure a straight cut.
  • Don’t cut your hair when it’s wet, as it will appear longer than it actually is when dry.
  • Take breaks if your arms become tired to avoid mistakes.

FAQ About Hair Care

How Often Should I Cut My Hair?

It’s recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

How Can I Keep My Hair Healthy?

Regularly washing and conditioning your hair, using a heat protectant when styling with heat tools, and avoiding tight hairstyles can help keep your hair healthy.

Should I Cut My Hair at Home or Go to a Salon?

If you’re comfortable with cutting your hair yourself and have the proper tools, cutting your hair at home can save money and time. However, if you’re unsure or have a more complex hairstyle, it’s best to visit a salon to ensure a professional cut.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it’s important to care for it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type, and avoid using heat tools too frequently. Consider getting a hair mask or treatment to nourish and strengthen your hair. Remember to also schedule regular trims to maintain your new medium-length hairstyle.

Cutting your hair to a medium length can be a fun and exciting change. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a successful cut at home while also maintaining healthy hair.

What Hair Colour Will Suit Me Most A Comprehensive Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Oct 01, 2023 · 4 min read

If you've ever wondered what hair colour will suit you most, you're not alone. Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, with so many options available. But fear not, we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about finding the perfect hair colour for you in 2023. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the nitty-gritty of finding the perfect hair colour, let's talk about what we mean by "suitable" hair colour. The perfect hair colour for you is one that complements your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. It should also match your personality and lifestyle. Ultimately, the goal is to find a hair colour that enhances your natural beauty and makes you feel confident and comfortable.

How to

The best way to find the right hair colour for you is to consult with a professional hair colourist. They will take into account your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour to recommend the perfect shade for you. However, if you want to experiment with different hair colours at home, there are a few things you can do to make sure you choose the right one.

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair colour. There are three main skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. If you have warm undertones, opt for hair colours with warm tones such as golden blonde, copper, or caramel. If you have cool undertones, go for hair colours with cool tones like ash blonde, platinum, or black. If you have a neutral undertone, you can go for both warm and cool hair colours.

Think About Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also play a role in determining your ideal hair colour. For example, if you have blue eyes, blonde hair can make your eyes pop. If you have green eyes, red or auburn hair can complement your eye colour. If you have brown eyes, warm hair colours like caramel or honey can enhance your eyes.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also influence the hair colour you choose. If you're busy and don't have a lot of time to maintain your hair, choose a low-maintenance hair colour. If you're adventurous and love to try new things, consider bold and vibrant hair colours.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when choosing the right hair colour:

  1. Determine your skin tone
  2. Consider your eye colour
  3. Think about your lifestyle
  4. Consult with a professional hair colourist
  5. Try on different hair colours using virtual try-on tools
  6. Consider the upkeep and maintenance required for the hair colour
  7. Take the plunge and try a new hair colour!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hair colour:

  • Always choose a hair colour that complements your natural beauty
  • Try virtual try-on tools before committing to a hair colour
  • Consider the upkeep and maintenance required for the hair colour
  • Don't be afraid to consult with a professional hair colourist
  • Experiment with different hair colours to find your perfect shade

FAQ

What hair colour looks good on fair skin?

Blonde, light brown, and red hair colours tend to look good on fair skin.

What hair colour looks good on tan skin?

Warm hair colours like caramel, honey, and copper tend to look good on tan skin.

What hair colour looks good on dark skin?

Dark skin can look good with bold and vibrant hair colours like red, purple, or blue.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect hair colour for you, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the colour and prevent damage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat damage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use hair masks and treatments to nourish your hair

Now that you know everything about finding the perfect hair colour for you in 2023, it's time to take the plunge and try a new shade. Remember, the perfect hair colour is one that makes you feel confident and beautiful, so don't be afraid to experiment!

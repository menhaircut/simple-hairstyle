How To Get Fluffy Messy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you want to achieve that effortless, messy hair look? Well, you're in luck because we have the ultimate guide on how to get fluffy messy hair! This hair trend is all about embracing your natural hair texture and adding volume to create a playful and carefree look. Whether you have straight or curly hair, this guide will give you the tips and tricks you need to achieve the perfect messy hairdo.

How to Get Fluffy Messy Hair

Getting fluffy messy hair is all about creating volume and texture. Here are the steps you need to follow to achieve this look:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add volume and texture to your hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel-dry it.

Step 2: Apply a Texturizing Spray

Apply a texturizing spray to your damp hair. This will help create texture and give your hair a messy look. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and distribute the product evenly.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on a low heat setting. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair while blow-drying to create volume and texture.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Tease your hair with a comb or brush to create extra volume. Take small sections of your hair and backcomb them at the roots. This will help create the fluffy, messy look you're going for.

Step 5: Add a Hair Powder

Add a hair powder to your roots for extra volume and texture. This will help give your hair a lived-in, messy look. Use your fingers to work the powder into your roots.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look with a light-hold hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place and prevent it from falling flat. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair to create a messy look.

Tips for Getting Fluffy Messy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect fluffy messy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add more volume.
  • Use a dry shampoo or baby powder to absorb excess oil and add texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids or messy buns, to switch up your look.

FAQs About Getting Fluffy Messy Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting fluffy messy hair:

Is fluffy messy hair suitable for all hair types?

Yes! Fluffy messy hair can be achieved on all hair types, whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair.

How often should I wash my hair to maintain this look?

It's best to wash your hair every two to three days to maintain this look. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it harder to style.

Can I achieve this look without using heat styling tools?

Yes! You can achieve this look without using heat styling tools by braiding your hair overnight or using a hair roller set to create volume and texture.

Hair Care for Fluffy Messy Hair

It's essential to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and voluminous look. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using too many heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause damage.

Now that you know how to get fluffy messy hair, it's time to try it out for yourself! Follow our step-by-step guide and rock that playful, carefree look with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and voluminous look. Happy styling!

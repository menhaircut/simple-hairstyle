How to Choose Dresses That Look Good with Short Hair

Choosing dresses that look good with short hair can be a challenge, but it doesn't have to be. When deciding on a dress, consider the length, neckline, and overall style. Short hair allows for more versatility in dress styles, so have fun experimenting with different looks.

Length

The length of your dress is an important factor to consider when choosing a dress to wear with short hair. Generally, shorter dresses tend to look better with short hair, as they create a more balanced and proportional look. However, this doesn't mean you can't wear a longer dress. If you do opt for a longer dress, make sure it's fitted and hits just above or at the knee.

Neckline

The neckline of your dress is another important factor to consider. As a rule of thumb, V-neck and scoop necklines tend to be the most flattering for those with short hair, as they elongate the neck and draw attention to the face. Avoid high necklines, as they can make the neck appear shorter and take away from the overall look.

Style

When it comes to style, don't be afraid to experiment. Short hair allows for more versatility in dress styles, so have fun and try different looks. From flowy maxi dresses to fitted cocktail dresses, there's a style out there for everyone.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Dresses for Short Hair

Follow these simple steps to choose dresses that complement your short hair:

  1. Consider the length of your dress. Shorter dresses tend to look better with short hair.
  2. Choose a neckline that flatters your face and elongates your neck. V-neck and scoop necklines are generally the most flattering.
  3. Experiment with different dress styles, from flowy maxi dresses to fitted cocktail dresses.
  4. Don't be afraid to accessorize. A statement necklace or earrings can add some extra flair to your look.

Tips for Choosing Dresses That Complement Short Hair

Here are some additional tips for choosing dresses that look good with short hair:

  • Choose dresses with asymmetrical hemlines to create a more balanced look.
  • Opt for dresses with interesting patterns and textures to add some depth to your outfit.
  • When in doubt, choose a little black dress. It's a classic look that never goes out of style.
  • Don't forget about the power of accessories. A belt or scarf can add some extra dimension to your outfit.

Frequently Asked Questions About Dresses and Short Hair

Q: Can I wear a long dress with short hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a long dress with short hair. Just make sure the dress is fitted and hits just above or at the knee.

Q: What kind of neckline is best for short hair?

A: V-neck and scoop necklines tend to be the most flattering for those with short hair, as they elongate the neck and draw attention to the face.

Q: What kind of accessories should I wear with a dress and short hair?

A: Consider adding a statement necklace or earrings to add some extra flair to your outfit. A belt or scarf can also add some extra dimension.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Finally, here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair to avoid breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you have some tips and tricks for choosing dresses that look good with short hair, it's time to start experimenting with different styles. Remember to have fun and don't be afraid to try something new!

How Do Guys Keep Their Hair Back Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Keeping hair back is a common struggle for many guys, whether it's for work, sports, or just to look good. There are several ways to keep hair back, from simple hairstyles to hair accessories. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to keep your hair back, so you can look and feel your best.

Table of Contents

  • Hairstyles
  • Hair Accessories
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Hairstyles

One of the easiest ways to keep hair back is to style it in a way that keeps it out of your face. Here are some hairstyles to consider:

1. Ponytail

A ponytail is a classic hairstyle that works well for both short and long hair. Simply gather your hair at the back of your head and tie it with an elastic band. You can also leave some hair loose at the front to frame your face.

2. Bun

A bun is another classic hairstyle that works well for keeping hair back. Gather your hair into a ponytail, twist it into a bun, and secure it with bobby pins or an elastic band.

3. French Braid

A French braid is a stylish way to keep hair back while still looking good. Start by braiding your hair at the top of your head, and as you braid, add more hair from the sides to create a diagonal braid. Once you reach the nape of your neck, finish the braid with a regular braid and secure it with an elastic band.

Hair Accessories

If you don't want to style your hair in a certain way, you can also use hair accessories to keep it back. Here are some options to consider:

1. Headbands

Headbands are a simple way to keep hair back and add some style to your look. You can choose from a wide range of headbands, from thin and simple to wide and embellished.

2. Hats

Hats not only keep hair back, but they also protect your head from the sun. Choose from a variety of styles, from baseball caps to fedoras.

3. Hair Clips

Hair clips are a versatile option for keeping hair back. You can use them to clip back sections of hair or to create a half-up, half-down hairstyle.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying to keep your hair back:

1. Use Products

Using hair products like gel, pomade, or hairspray can help keep your hair in place. Apply a small amount of product to your hair and style it as desired.

2. Brush Your Hair

Brushing your hair can help keep it back and prevent it from falling into your face. Use a brush or comb to smooth your hair back, and use a hair tie or clip to secure it in place.

3. Choose the Right Accessories

Choose hair accessories that work well for your hair type and the occasion. If you're playing sports, a headband or hat may be a better option than a bun or ponytail.

FAQ

1. Can I keep my hair back without using products or accessories?

Yes, you can style your hair in a way that keeps it back without using products or accessories. Try a slicked-back hairstyle, or use a comb to create a side part.

2. What should I do if my hair keeps falling out of my ponytail?

If your hair keeps falling out of your ponytail, try using a thicker elastic band or wrapping it around your hair a few times to create a tighter hold. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose pieces.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and clean is essential for maintaining any hairstyle. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type.

2. Use a Hair Mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help nourish and strengthen your hair. Choose a mask that works well for your hair type and apply it to your hair for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair. If you need to use them, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

In conclusion, there are several ways to keep hair back, from simple hairstyles to hair accessories. By following these tips and tricks, you can find a method that works well for you and keeps you looking and feeling your best.

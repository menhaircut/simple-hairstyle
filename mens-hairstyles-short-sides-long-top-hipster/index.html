Mens Hairstyles Short Sides Long Top Hipster The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Nov 03, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a trendy and stylish hairstyle, then the mens hairstyles short sides long top hipster is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years and is a great way to express your individuality while still looking sharp and polished. In this guide, we’ll show you everything you need to know about this hairstyle, from how to get it to how to maintain it.

Description

The mens hairstyles short sides long top hipster is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in a number of ways. The basic idea is to keep the sides and back of your hair short, while leaving the top longer. This allows you to create a variety of looks, from slicked-back to messy and textured. The hipster style is all about individuality, so feel free to experiment with different styling techniques and products.

How to Get the Mens Hairstyles Short Sides Long Top Hipster

The first step in getting the mens hairstyles short sides long top hipster is to find a skilled barber or stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. It’s important to communicate your preferences clearly, so your stylist can create a customized cut that suits your face shape and hair type.

Tools You’ll Need

  • Clippers or scissors
  • Comb or brush
  • Hair product (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting the mens hairstyles short sides long top hipster:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

Before you start cutting, decide on the length you want for the sides and back of your hair. Generally, a length of 1-2 inches is ideal for the sides and back.

Step 2: Cut the Sides and Back

Using clippers or scissors, cut the sides and back of your hair to the desired length. Use a comb or brush to help guide your cutting.

Step 3: Blend the Sides and Top

Blend the sides and top of your hair using scissors or clippers. This will create a seamless transition between the shorter sides and the longer top.

Step 4: Style the Top

Style the top of your hair using your fingers or a comb. You can create a slicked-back look, a messy and textured look, or anything in between.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Hairstyles Short Sides Long Top Hipster

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens hairstyles short sides long top hipster:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Invest in a good styling product, such as pomade or wax, to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques and products to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: What face shapes suit the mens hairstyles short sides long top hipster?

A: The mens hairstyles short sides long top hipster suits most face shapes, but it’s especially flattering for those with oval or diamond-shaped faces.

Q: Can I wear the mens hairstyles short sides long top hipster to work?

A: Yes, the mens hairstyles short sides long top hipster can be a professional and polished look when styled correctly.

Q: How often do I need to get my hair cut for this style?

A: It’s recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.

Hair Care Tips for the Mens Hairstyles Short Sides Long Top Hipster

Here are some additional hair care tips for the mens hairstyles short sides long top hipster:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot styling tools.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the mens hairstyles short sides long top hipster, it’s time to take the plunge and try it out for yourself. Remember to communicate your preferences clearly to your stylist, experiment with different styling techniques and products, and get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best. With a little care and attention, you’ll be rocking this stylish and trendy look in no time.

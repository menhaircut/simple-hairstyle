What Does Thinning Shears Do To Hair

Written by Luffy Jul 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Thinning shears are a type of hair cutting tool that is used to remove bulk from hair by cutting out some strands while leaving others intact. They may also be used to create texture or to blend layers. In this article, we will explore what thinning shears do to hair and how to use them properly.

Description

Thinning shears are also known as texturizing shears or blending shears. They have two blades, one with teeth and one without. The teeth are designed to cut some of the hair while leaving some strands untouched. This creates a more natural look and removes bulk from the hair. Thinning shears can be used on all hair types, but they are particularly useful on thick, coarse, or curly hair.

How to Use Thinning Shears

Thinning shears should be used on dry hair. Wet hair can stretch and may not give an accurate representation of the final look. It is also important to use the correct technique. Thinning shears should be held at a 45-degree angle and should be opened and closed while moving them through the hair. The amount of hair to be removed should be determined before starting. It is best to start with a small amount and work in sections.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by sectioning the hair into manageable sections.
2. Hold the thinning shears at a 45-degree angle and open and close them while moving them through the hair.
3. Take small sections and work in a methodical manner.
4. Determine the amount of hair to be removed before starting.
5. Continue until the desired amount of hair has been removed.
6. Blend the hair by combing it with a regular comb.

Tips

1. Use thinning shears sparingly.
2. Do not use thinning shears on the ends of the hair, as this can create split ends.
3. Always use sharp thinning shears to avoid damaging the hair.
4. Do not use thinning shears on wet hair.
5. Do not use thinning shears on hair that has been chemically treated, as this can cause breakage.

FAQ

1. Will thinning shears make my hair look thinner?

Thinning shears remove bulk from the hair, which can make it look thinner. However, they do not actually change the thickness of the individual strands of hair.

2. Can thinning shears be used on any hair type?

Thinning shears can be used on all hair types, but they are particularly useful on thick, coarse, or curly hair.

3. Can thinning shears be used to create layers?

Thinning shears can be used to create texture or to blend layers, but they should not be used to create layers themselves.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair after using thinning shears. Use a good quality conditioner to keep the hair hydrated and healthy. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to the hair. It is also a good idea to get regular trims to keep the hair looking neat and healthy.

How Do You Cut Hair By Yourself? | Step by Step Guide | Hair Care Tips

How To Cut Your Own Hair:

Cutting your own hair can be a great way to save money and time. Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You will need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)

Step by Step Guide:

Follow these steps to cut your own hair:

  1. Wash your hair and let it dry completely.
  2. Part your hair down the middle and comb it straight.
  3. Start at the back and work your way forward, cutting small sections of hair at a time.
  4. Use the comb to hold the hair straight and cut at an angle, rather than straight across.
  5. Repeat this process on the other side of your head.
  6. Trim the ends by cutting upwards, rather than straight across.
  7. Trim any layers or bangs that you want.
  8. Use clippers to trim the sides and back, if desired.
  9. Finish by styling your hair as desired.

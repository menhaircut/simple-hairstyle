Mid Back Length Hairstyles How To Achieve The Look

Written by Christine Aug 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Mid back length hairstyles have become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. This versatile length can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to voluminous curls. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know to achieve the perfect mid back length hairstyle.

Description

Mid back length hairstyles refer to hair that falls between the shoulder blades and mid back. This length is perfect for those who want a hairstyle that is long enough to experiment with different styles, but not so long that it becomes difficult to manage.

How to Achieve Mid Back Length Hairstyles

There are a few different ways to achieve mid back length hairstyles. The most common method is to grow your hair out to the desired length over time. However, if you're looking for a quicker solution, you can also use hair extensions to add length and volume to your hair.

Growing Your Hair Out

To grow your hair out to mid back length, you'll need to be patient. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month, so it can take several months to a year to achieve the desired length. To speed up the process, you can try taking hair growth supplements or using hair growth products.

Using Hair Extensions

If you're looking for a quicker solution, hair extensions can be a great option. There are many different types of hair extensions to choose from, including clip-ins, sew-ins, and tape-ins. These extensions can be added to your natural hair to add length and volume.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mid Back Length Hair

Once you've achieved mid back length hair, there are many different ways to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving some popular mid back length hairstyles:

Sleek and Straight

To achieve a sleek and straight look, start by washing and blow drying your hair. Then, use a flat iron to straighten your hair in small sections. Finish with a smoothing serum to add shine and tame any flyaways.

Beachy Waves

To achieve beachy waves, start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's slightly damp. Then, apply a texturizing spray and scrunch your hair to create waves. Alternatively, you can use a curling iron to create loose waves.

Voluminous Curls

To achieve voluminous curls, start by washing your hair and blow drying it using a round brush to add volume. Then, use a curling iron to create curls, making sure to curl away from your face. Finish with a volumizing spray to add even more volume.

Tips for Maintaining Mid Back Length Hair

Once you've achieved mid back length hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining mid back length hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQ

What face shape is best for mid back length hairstyles?

Mid back length hairstyles can work for any face shape, but they are particularly flattering for those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Can I achieve mid back length hair if my hair is naturally thin?

Yes, you can still achieve mid back length hair if your hair is naturally thin. However, you may need to use hair extensions to add volume and thickness to your hair.

How often should I wash my mid back length hair?

It's recommended to wash your mid back length hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.

Hair Care Products for Mid Back Length Hair

Here are some hair care products that are great for mid back length hair:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Texturizing spray
  • Volumizing spray
  • Smoothing serum
  • Deep conditioning treatment

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect mid back length hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

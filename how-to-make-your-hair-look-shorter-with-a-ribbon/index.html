How To Make Your Hair Look Shorter With A Ribbon

Written by Petter Aug 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long hair but don't want to cut it off? Or maybe you just want to switch up your look for a day? Whatever the reason, using a ribbon to make your hair look shorter is a fun and easy way to change up your hairstyle. In this article, we will go over how to make your hair look shorter with a ribbon, step by step.

How to

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to style and keep the ribbon in place.

Step 2: Tie your hair into a low ponytail

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and tie it into a low ponytail. You can use a hair elastic or a scrunchie to secure the ponytail.

Step 3: Choose a ribbon

Choose a ribbon that complements your outfit or hair color. You can use a thin or thick ribbon, depending on your preference.

Step 4: Tie the ribbon around the ponytail

Take the ribbon and tie it around the base of the ponytail. Make sure it is tight enough to hold the hair in place, but not so tight that it is uncomfortable.

Step 5: Fan out the hair

Using your fingers, fan out the hair above the ribbon. This will create the illusion of a shorter hairstyle.

Step 6: Tuck in any loose strands

If you have any loose strands of hair, tuck them in above the ribbon. This will give your hairstyle a clean and polished look.

Step 7: Adjust the ribbon

Adjust the ribbon so that it sits where you want it to on your head. You can move it higher or lower depending on how short you want your hair to look.

Step 8: Finish with hairspray

Spray some hairspray over your hair to help keep it in place throughout the day.

Tips

  • Use a ribbon that matches your outfit to create a cohesive look.
  • Experiment with different ribbon widths to see what looks best on you.
  • If you have layered hair, use bobby pins to secure any shorter pieces that may stick out.

FAQ

Can I use any type of ribbon?

Yes, you can use any type of ribbon that you like. Just make sure it is long enough to tie around your ponytail.

How long will the hairstyle last?

The hairstyle should last throughout the day as long as you use enough hairspray to hold it in place.

Can I use this hairstyle on curly hair?

Yes, this hairstyle works on all hair types, including curly hair.

Hair Care

When you tie your hair with a ribbon, it is important to not tie it too tightly. This can cause breakage and damage to your hair. It is also important to use a scrunchie or ribbon that is gentle on your hair.

Before tying your hair with a ribbon, make sure your hair is well hydrated and moisturized. This will help prevent damage and breakage.

Lastly, take care when removing the ribbon from your hair. Gently untie it to prevent any hair from being pulled out or damaged.

Conclusion

Using a ribbon to make your hair look shorter is a fun and easy way to switch up your hairstyle. By following these simple steps, you can create a new look that is perfect for any occasion. Don't be afraid to experiment with different ribbons and styles to find what works best for you.

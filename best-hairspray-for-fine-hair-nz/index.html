Best Hairspray For Fine Hair Nz A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jul 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of using hairsprays that leave your fine hair limp and lifeless? Look no further because we have compiled a list of the best hairsprays for fine hair in NZ. Whether you want to add volume or hold your style in place, these hairsprays cater to all your needs. Keep reading to find out which hairspray suits your hair type best.

Description

Our list includes hairsprays that are specially designed for fine hair. These hairsprays not only add volume but also provide a firm hold without weighing down your hair. They are lightweight and do not leave any residue, making them ideal for everyday use. The hairsprays we have chosen are easily available in New Zealand and have received rave reviews from users.

How to

Before using any hairspray, it is essential to shampoo and condition your hair properly. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. Once your hair is dry, style it as desired. Hold the hairspray can about 12 inches away from your hair and spray lightly. For a stronger hold, spray more generously. Allow the hairspray to dry before touching your hair.

Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare your Hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and blow-dry it using a round brush to add volume. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

Step 2: Style your Hair

Once your hair is dry, style it as desired. Use a curling iron or straightener to add some texture to your hair. You can also use a volumizing mousse or spray to add extra volume.

Step 3: Apply Hairspray

Hold the hairspray can about 12 inches away from your hair and spray lightly. For a stronger hold, spray more generously. Allow the hairspray to dry before touching your hair.

Tips

  • Choose a hairspray that is specifically designed for fine hair.
  • Do not use too much hairspray as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Apply the hairspray in a well-ventilated area to avoid inhaling the fumes.
  • Do not touch your hair after applying the hairspray to avoid ruining the style.

FAQ

Q. Can hairspray damage my hair?

A. Hairspray can cause build-up on your hair, which can make it look dull and lifeless. However, using a clarifying shampoo once a week can help remove the build-up.

Q. Can I use hairspray on wet hair?

A. No, it is not advisable to use hairspray on wet hair. Hairspray works best on dry hair.

Q. Can I brush my hair after applying hairspray?

A. It is not recommended to brush your hair after applying hairspray as it can ruin the style. If you need to brush your hair, do it before applying the hairspray.

Hair Care

Using the right products for your hair type is essential for healthy-looking hair. Fine hair needs products that add volume without weighing it down. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. Avoid overusing styling products as they can cause build-up on your hair.

With our list of the best hairsprays for fine hair in NZ, you can now have the perfect hair day every day. Choose the hairspray that suits your hair type and style, and get ready to flaunt your beautiful hair.

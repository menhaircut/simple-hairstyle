FAQ

Can I straighten my hair without heat?

Yes, you can straighten your hair without heat by using a hair straightening brush or wrapping your hair around your head and securing it with bobby pins. However, these methods may not be as effective as using a flat iron and may not work for all hair types.

How often should I straighten my curly hair?

You should avoid straightening your curly hair every day, as this can cause damage. Instead, try to straighten your hair no more than once a week and give your hair a break in between.

What should I do if my hair gets damaged from straightening?

If your hair gets damaged from straightening, try using a deep conditioning treatment and avoid heat styling for a while. You may also want to consider getting a trim to remove any split ends.

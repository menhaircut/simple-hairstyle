The Trending Mens Hairstyle Long On Top Short Back And Sides

Written by Christine Jun 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

There is something about a man with a well-groomed haircut that gets the attention of everyone around him. One hairstyle that has been popular for years and continues to be trendy is the mens hairstyle long on top short back and sides. This hairstyle involves keeping the hair long on the top and cutting it short on the back and sides. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to style and take care of this hairstyle.

What is Mens Hairstyle Long on Top Short Back and Sides?

The mens hairstyle long on top short back and sides is a classic haircut that has been around for decades. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in different ways to suit different personalities and face shapes. The hair on the top can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. The back and sides are usually cut short, creating a sharp contrast with the long hair on the top. This hairstyle can be achieved with any hair type, whether straight, wavy, or curly.

How to Achieve the Mens Hairstyle Long on Top Short Back and Sides?

Getting the mens hairstyle long on top short back and sides is easy, and you can achieve it with the following steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step to achieving this hairstyle is choosing the right haircut. You need to visit a professional hairstylist who will cut the hair on the back and sides short and leave the hair on top long. The hairstylist will also layer the hair on top to create texture and volume.

Step 2: Style the Hair

Once you have the right haircut, you can style your hair in different ways to suit your personality and face shape. You can use hair products like wax, pomade, or gel to style the hair on top. You can also use a hairdryer to add volume to the hair.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Hairstyle Long on Top Short Back and Sides

To keep your hairstyle looking sharp and fresh, you need to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining the mens hairstyle long on top short back and sides:

Tip 1: Regular Trims

Regular trims will ensure that your hair stays in shape and maintains its sharp look. You should visit your hairstylist every four to six weeks for a trim.

Tip 2: Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products is crucial for maintaining this hairstyle. You should use products that provide a firm hold and add shine to the hair.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

You should protect your hair from environmental factors like the sun, wind, and pollution. You can wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair.

FAQs About Mens Hairstyle Long on Top Short Back and Sides

What face shape is suitable for this hairstyle?

This hairstyle is suitable for most face shapes, but it looks best on oval and square faces.

Can I achieve this hairstyle with curly hair?

Yes, you can achieve this hairstyle with curly hair. You need to ensure that the hairstylist layers the hair on top to create texture and volume.

What hair products should I use for this hairstyle?

You should use hair products like wax, pomade, or gel to style the hair on top.

Conclusion

The mens hairstyle long on top short back and sides is a classic hairstyle that continues to be popular today. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in different ways to suit different personalities and face shapes. With the right haircut and hair products, you can achieve this hairstyle and maintain it effortlessly.

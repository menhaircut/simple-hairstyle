How to Cut the Back of Your Short Hair Yourself

Short hair is a great way to show off your style and personality. However, going to the hair salon can be expensive and time-consuming. Luckily, with a few simple tools and techniques, you can easily cut the back of your short hair yourself. In this article, we will go through the steps of cutting the back of your short hair at home.

Description

Short hair can be a great way to show off your personality and style. However, maintaining short hair can be difficult and expensive. Going to the hair salon every few weeks can become a hassle, and it can be hard to find the time to do so. By learning how to cut the back of your short hair yourself, you can save time and money while still looking great.

How to

Tools You Will Need

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Handheld mirror

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to cut the back of your short hair yourself:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free from any tangles or knots.
  2. Section off the top half of your hair using hair clips. This will make it easier to focus on the back of your hair.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the back of your head using a comb. Hold the hair between your fingers and use your scissors to cut the hair to your desired length.
  4. Repeat this process, working your way up the back of your head. Be sure to cut small sections of hair at a time, as this will give you more control over the finished look.
  5. Once you have finished cutting the back of your hair, release the top half of your hair from the hair clips and blend the back with the top by trimming any uneven pieces.
  6. Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your hair for any missed spots or unevenness. Make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your hair for any missed spots.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it has been cut.

FAQ

Can I cut the back of my short hair myself?

Yes, with the right tools and techniques, you can easily cut the back of your short hair yourself.

What tools do I need to cut the back of my short hair?

You will need scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a handheld mirror.

How do I blend the back of my short hair with the top?

Release the top half of your hair from the hair clips and blend the back with the top by trimming any uneven pieces.

Hair Care

After cutting the back of your short hair yourself, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims will also help to keep your short hair looking fresh and stylish.

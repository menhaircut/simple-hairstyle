Best Short Hair For Oblong Faces

Written by Frank Aug 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for the perfect short haircut that will flatter your oblong face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for oblong faces, how to style them, tips for maintaining your new look, and more. Let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide, with a straight, narrow jawline and high forehead. To balance out your features and add width to your face, you'll want to choose a short haircut that adds volume to the sides.

How to:

When choosing a short haircut for an oblong face shape, you'll want to consider the length, layers, and texture of the cut. Shorter cuts that hit at or above the chin will help balance out your features and add width to the sides of your face. Layers can also add volume and movement to your hair, while textured cuts can help create the illusion of fullness.

Step by Step:

1. Start by finding inspiration photos of short haircuts that you like. Look for cuts that are chin-length or shorter, with layers or texture.
2. Schedule a consultation with a hairstylist who has experience working with oblong face shapes.
3. Discuss your hair type, lifestyle, and maintenance preferences with your stylist to determine the best short haircut for you.
4. Once you've chosen your new cut, ask your stylist to show you how to style it at home.

Tips:

1. Use volumizing products like mousse or dry shampoo to add fullness to your hair.
2. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as they can make your face appear even longer.
3. Consider adding bangs to your cut to help shorten your forehead and balance out your features.
4. To maintain your new cut, schedule regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ:

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a bob if I have an oblong face shape?
A: Yes, but make sure the bob hits at or above the chin and has layers to add volume to the sides of your face.

Q: What about pixie cuts?
A: Pixie cuts can work well with an oblong face shape, but make sure to add layers or texture to create fullness.

Hair Care:

To keep your short haircut looking its best, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Apply a heat protectant before using any hot tools, and use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair to avoid breakage. Finally, protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect short haircut to flatter your oblong face shape. Talk to your stylist, experiment with different styles, and have fun with your new look!

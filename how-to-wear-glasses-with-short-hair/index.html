How To Wear Glasses With Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Nov 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have short hair and wear glasses? Are you struggling to find the right hairstyle that complements your glasses? Don't worry; we've got you covered! In this article, we will give you tips and tricks on how to wear glasses with short hair. We will also answer some frequently asked questions and provide you with some hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Glasses with Short Hair
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Wear Glasses with Short Hair

Short hair can be challenging to style with glasses, especially if you're not used to wearing them. But don't let that discourage you from finding the perfect pair of glasses that suit your personality and style. Here are some tips on how to wear glasses with short hair:

1. Consider the Shape of Your Face

The first thing to consider when choosing glasses is the shape of your face. The glasses you choose should complement your face shape and enhance your features. For example, if you have a round face, choose glasses with angular frames to add definition. If you have a square face, choose glasses with round frames to soften your features.

2. Choose Glasses That Complement Your Hair Color

The color of your glasses should complement your hair color. If you have warm-toned hair, choose glasses with warm-toned frames. If you have cool-toned hair, choose glasses with cool-toned frames. This will help create a cohesive look.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing glasses. If you're active, choose glasses that are durable and can withstand wear and tear. If you spend a lot of time in front of a computer, choose glasses with blue light-blocking lenses to protect your eyes from digital eye strain.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know how to choose glasses that complement your short hair, let's look at how to wear them:

1. Start with Clean and Dry Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will make it easier to style your hair and keep your glasses in place.

2. Choose a Hairstyle That Complements Your Glasses

Choose a hairstyle that complements your glasses. For example, if you have thick-framed glasses, choose a hairstyle that's not too busy and won't compete with your glasses. If you have thin-framed glasses, choose a hairstyle that adds volume to your hair.

3. Use Hair Products to Keep Your Hair in Place

Use hair products to keep your hair in place. Hairspray, wax, and gel can help keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling onto your glasses.

4. Adjust Your Glasses

Adjust your glasses so that they sit comfortably on your nose. This will prevent them from sliding down your nose and interfering with your vision.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks on how to wear glasses with short hair:

1. Choose Glasses That Highlight Your Best Features

Choose glasses that highlight your best features. If you have beautiful eyes, choose glasses that draw attention to them. If you have high cheekbones, choose glasses that accentuate them.

2. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you. Don't be afraid to try something new – you might be surprised at how good you look with a new hairstyle.

3. Consider Your Skin Tone

Consider your skin tone when choosing glasses. If you have warm-toned skin, choose glasses with warm-toned frames. If you have cool-toned skin, choose glasses with cool-toned frames.

FAQ

1. Can I wear glasses with short hair?

Yes, you can wear glasses with short hair. Just make sure you choose glasses that complement your face shape, hair color, and lifestyle.

2. What hairstyles look good with glasses?

Hairstyles that add volume to your hair look good with glasses. Avoid hairstyles that are too busy and compete with your glasses.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair looking great:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

2. Use Hair Products Sparingly

Use hair products sparingly to avoid buildup and damage to your hair. Only use the amount you need to achieve the desired look.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Now that you know how to wear glasses with short hair, it's time to experiment with different hairstyles and glasses to find the perfect look for you. Don't be afraid to try something new – you might be surprised at how good you look!

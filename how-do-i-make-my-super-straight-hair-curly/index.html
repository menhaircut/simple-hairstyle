How Do I Make My Super Straight Hair Curly

Written by Frank Nov 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have super straight hair and want to add some volume and texture to your locks, you may be wondering how you can make your hair curly. Luckily, there are several methods you can try to achieve curly hair, whether you want loose waves or tight ringlets. Read on to discover how to make your super straight hair curly.

Description

Straight hair can be beautiful, but sometimes you may want to switch things up and add some curls to your locks. Curly hair is versatile and can add volume and texture to any hairstyle. However, if you have super straight hair, achieving curls can be a challenge. Don't worry, though, because there are several ways to make your hair curly without damaging your locks.

How to

There are several ways to make your super straight hair curly, including using heat styling tools, braiding your hair, using rollers, and applying curl-enhancing products. Here are some of the most effective methods:

Method 1: Heat Styling

If you want to achieve curls quickly and easily, heat styling tools are your best bet. You can use a curling iron, flat iron, or hot rollers to create curls. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Preheat your curling iron or flat iron to the desired temperature.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or flat iron, holding it for a few seconds.
  5. Release the section of hair and repeat until all of your hair is curled.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

You can also use hot rollers to create curls. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Heat up your hot rollers according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a hot roller.
  5. Repeat until all of your hair is wrapped around hot rollers.
  6. Leave the hot rollers in your hair for at least 20 minutes.
  7. Remove the hot rollers and gently finger comb your curls.
  8. Finish with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Method 2: Braiding

Braiding your hair is a great way to create curls without heat styling tools. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section tightly.
  4. Leave the braids in your hair overnight.
  5. In the morning, gently unravel the braids to reveal your curls.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Method 3: Using Rollers

Rollers are a classic way to create curls. Here's how to use them:

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and roll each section around a roller.
  4. Leave the rollers in your hair for at least 20 minutes.
  5. Remove the rollers and gently finger comb your curls.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Method 4: Using Curl-Enhancing Products

If you don't want to use heat styling tools or rollers, you can use curl-enhancing products to create curls. Here's how:

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as mousse or curl cream.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage curl formation.
  4. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide for making your super straight hair curly using heat styling tools:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Preheat your curling iron or flat iron to the desired temperature.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or flat iron, holding it for a few seconds.
  5. Release the section of hair and repeat until all of your hair is curled.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your super straight hair curly:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls.
  • Experiment with different methods to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to try new hairstyles and have fun with your curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making super straight hair curly:

Q: Can I make my hair curly without heat styling tools?

A: Yes, you can use braids, rollers, or curl-enhancing products to create curls without heat styling tools.

Q: Will curling my hair damage it?

A: Heat styling tools can damage your hair if you use them too often or at too high a temperature. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and don't overdo it with the heat.

Hair Care

To keep your curls looking their best, be sure to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Be gentle when detangling your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and methods, you can make your super straight hair curly and enjoy a new look with voluminous, textured locks.

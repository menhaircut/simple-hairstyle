Description

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the hair salon every time you need a haircut? With the help of Youtube, you can learn how to cut your own short curly hair from the comfort of your own home. Not only will you save money, but you'll also have the satisfaction of knowing you did it yourself.

In this post, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own short curly hair. We'll also provide you with tips, answer frequently asked questions, and give you advice on how to care for your hair after the cut.

How to

The first step in cutting your own short curly hair is to gather the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror that allows you to see the back of your head.

Next, you'll want to watch a few Youtube videos on how to cut short curly hair. This will give you an idea of what to do and what not to do during the cutting process.

Once you've familiarized yourself with the process, it's time to start cutting. It's important to start with small snips and work your way up. Remember, you can always cut more hair off, but you can't put it back on.

Step by Step

1. Wash and dry your hair

2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles

3. Section your hair into four parts using hair clips

4. Start with one section at a time and cut small snips

5. Continue cutting until you're happy with the length

6. Use a mirror to check the back of your head

7. Style your hair as desired

Tips

- Start with small snips and work your way up

- Use a mirror to check the back of your head

- Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it

- Practice makes perfect

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut my short curly hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, most people need a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I cut my short curly hair when it's wet?

A: It's best to cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair tends to shrink when it dries, which could result in a shorter cut than you intended.

Hair Care

After you've cut your short curly hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair

- Deep condition your hair once a week

Cutting your own short curly hair can be a bit intimidating at first, but with the help of Youtube and these tips, you'll be able to do it with confidence. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to try again if you're not happy with the results the first time.

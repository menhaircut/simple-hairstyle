How To Make My Curly Hair Wavy Naturally

Written by Bardi Jun 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your curly hair and want to switch things up? You may be wondering how to make your curly hair wavy naturally. Luckily, there are a few tips and tricks that can help you achieve the perfect wavy hair without using any heat or damaging products. In this article, we will go over the steps and hair care needed to make your curly hair wavy naturally.

How to

There are several ways to make your curly hair wavy naturally. Here are some steps you can follow:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Step 2: Apply a curl-defining product

After washing your hair, apply a curl-defining product to your hair while it's still damp. This will help to enhance your natural curls and define them.

Step 3: Twist your hair

Divide your hair into sections and twist them. You can either twist them loosely or tightly depending on the type of waves you want to achieve. Once you've twisted all your hair, let it dry naturally.

Step 4: Shake out your hair

Once your hair is completely dry, shake out the twists. This will help to loosen the curls and create waves.

Step 5: Finish with a hair spray

Finally, finish your hairstyle with a hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips that can help you achieve the perfect wavy hair:

- Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural curls. - Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair. - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. - Wear a silk or satin bonnet or pillowcase to avoid frizz and breakage while sleeping.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to make curly hair wavy naturally:

Q: Can I use a curling iron to make my curly hair wavy?

A: While you can use a curling iron to create waves, it's not recommended as it can damage your hair.

Q: How long will the waves last?

A: The waves will last for a few days, depending on your hair type and the products you use.

Q: Can I make my hair wavy if it's already straight?

A: Yes, you can make your hair wavy by following the same steps, but you may need to use more product to hold the waves in place.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy and beautiful wavy hair, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. - Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized. - Avoid using heat styling tools and harsh chemicals on your hair. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Conclusion

With these tips and tricks, you can make your curly hair wavy naturally without any heat or damaging products. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain healthy and beautiful waves.

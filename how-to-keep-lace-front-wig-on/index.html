How To Keep Lace Front Wig On A Comprehensive Guide

If you're a fan of wigs, you probably know that lace front wigs are some of the most popular wig styles in the market. But have you ever struggled to keep your lace front wig on? If you have, you're not alone. In this article, we'll discuss some tips and tricks on how to keep your lace front wig on securely and comfortably.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Lace Front Wig On
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Lace Front Wigs

How to Keep Lace Front Wig On

First things first, you need to make sure that your wig fits correctly. A properly fitting wig is crucial to keeping it on securely. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Measure Your Head

Use a flexible measuring tape to measure your head. Start by wrapping the tape around your hairline at the back of your neck, and then bring it around to the front of your hairline. Make sure to measure the widest part of your head, which is usually just above your ears and eyebrows.

Step 2: Choose the Right Wig Size

Once you have your head measurements, check the size chart of the wig you want to buy. Most wig brands offer different sizes, so make sure to choose the one that matches your head measurements.

Step 3: Adjust the Wig Straps

Most lace front wigs come with adjustable straps at the back. Adjust the straps to make sure the wig fits snugly on your head. You can also use wig clips or bobby pins to secure the wig further.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to keep your lace front wig on:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair and Scalp

Before putting on your wig, make sure your hair and scalp are clean and dry. You can also use a wig cap to keep your hair flat and secure under the wig.

Step 2: Put on the Wig

Put the wig on your head, starting from the front and pulling it all the way back. Adjust the wig straps and use wig clips or bobby pins to secure the wig further if needed.

Step 3: Style the Wig

Style your wig as desired. You can use heat styling tools, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray first to avoid damaging the wig fibers.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep your lace front wig on:

  • Use wig glue or tape for extra security. However, make sure to use them sparingly and avoid applying them directly to your scalp to prevent irritation.
  • Wear a wig grip band to keep the wig in place and prevent slipping.
  • Avoid overly tight hairstyles or braids that can pull on the wig and cause damage.
  • Store your wig properly when not in use to maintain its shape and quality.

FAQs

Q: How long can I wear my lace front wig?

A: It depends on the quality of the wig and how well you take care of it. Generally, lace front wigs can last up to six months with proper care and maintenance.

Q: Can I swim or shower with my lace front wig on?

A: It's not recommended to swim or shower with your wig on as it can cause damage to the wig fibers and adhesive. It's best to remove the wig before any water activities.

Hair Care for Lace Front Wigs

Proper hair care is essential to keep your lace front wig looking great and lasting longer. Here are some tips:

  • Use wig-specific shampoo and conditioner to clean your wig. Avoid using regular hair products as they can damage the wig fibers.
  • Detangle your wig gently using a wide-tooth comb or wig brush.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage the wig fibers. If you need to use them, use a heat protectant spray first.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape and prevent tangling.

Keeping your lace front wig on securely and comfortably is easy with these tips and tricks. Remember to choose the right wig size, adjust the straps, and use wig clips or bobby pins for extra security. With proper care and maintenance, your lace front wig can look great and last longer.

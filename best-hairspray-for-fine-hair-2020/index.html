Best Hairspray For Fine Hair 2020

Written by Daniel Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have fine hair, you know how frustrating it can be to find a hairspray that actually works without weighing your hair down or making it look greasy. Luckily, there are a variety of hairsprays on the market that are specifically designed for fine hair, and we've rounded up the best options of 2020 for you.

Description

Before we dive into the best hairsprays for fine hair, let's first talk about what makes fine hair different from other hair types. Fine hair is characterized by its thinness and lack of volume. This can make it difficult to style and keep in place, which is where hairspray comes in.

How to Choose the Best Hairspray for Fine Hair

When choosing a hairspray for fine hair, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Look for a lightweight formula that won't weigh your hair down.
  • Choose a hairspray that provides hold without making your hair look stiff or crunchy.
  • Consider a hairspray that has volumizing properties to help give your hair more body.

Top 5 Best Hairsprays for Fine Hair 2020

1. L'Oreal Paris Elnett Satin Hairspray

L'Oreal Paris Elnett Satin Hairspray is a cult favorite for a reason. It provides strong hold without making your hair look stiff or crunchy, and it's lightweight enough for fine hair. Plus, it has a fine mist that won't leave your hair feeling sticky or greasy.

2. Kenra Volume Spray 25

If you're looking for a hairspray that will give your fine hair more volume, look no further than Kenra Volume Spray 25. This hairspray provides strong hold while also adding fullness and body to your hair.

3. Bumble and Bumble Spray de Mode

Bumble and Bumble Spray de Mode is a versatile hairspray that works well for all hair types, including fine hair. It provides flexible hold without any stickiness or buildup, and it's easy to brush out at the end of the day.

4. Moroccanoil Luminous Hairspray

Moroccanoil Luminous Hairspray is a great option if you're looking for a hairspray that will add shine to your fine hair. It provides medium hold without any stiffness, and it has a lightweight formula that won't weigh your hair down.

5. Sebastian Shaper Zero Gravity Hairspray

Sebastian Shaper Zero Gravity Hairspray is a favorite among hairstylists for its ability to provide strong hold without any buildup. It's also lightweight and easy to brush out, making it a great option for fine hair.

Tips for Using Hairspray on Fine Hair

When using hairspray on fine hair, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a light hand when applying hairspray to avoid weighing your hair down.
  • Hold the can at least 6 inches away from your hair to prevent any buildup.
  • Avoid spraying too much hairspray in one area to prevent stiffness or crunchiness.

FAQ

What is the best hairspray for fine hair?

The best hairspray for fine hair depends on your specific needs, but some of the top options include L'Oreal Paris Elnett Satin Hairspray, Kenra Volume Spray 25, and Bumble and Bumble Spray de Mode.

Can hairspray make fine hair look greasy?

Hairspray can make fine hair look greasy if you use too much or if you use a formula that's too heavy for your hair type. Look for a lightweight hairspray that won't weigh your hair down or make it look greasy.

Hair Care for Fine Hair

In addition to using the right hairspray, there are a few other things you can do to take care of your fine hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help give your hair more body.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make it look flat.
  • Consider getting regular trims to help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Overall, finding the best hairspray for fine hair can be a game-changer when it comes to styling and keeping your hair in place. Consider trying out one of our top picks and see the difference it can make for your hair.

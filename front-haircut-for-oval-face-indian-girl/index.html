Front Haircut For Oval Face Indian Girl A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 16, 2023 · 4 min read

If you are an Indian girl with an oval face, then you are in luck! An oval face is considered the most versatile face shape, and you can experiment with a variety of hairstyles. However, if you want to highlight your best features and enhance your natural beauty, then a front haircut is the way to go. In this article, we will guide you on how to get a perfect front haircut for your oval face. Read on to learn more.

How to Get a Front Haircut for Oval Face Indian Girl

Getting a front haircut is not rocket science, but you need to keep certain things in mind. Here's how you can get a perfect front haircut for your oval face:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in getting a front haircut is to choose the right length. For an oval face, a shoulder-length haircut with layers framing your face is an excellent option. You can also opt for a short bob or a long layered cut. Choose a length that complements your face shape and highlights your features.

Step 2: Consult with your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist before getting a front haircut. Your hairstylist can suggest the best length and style for your face shape and hair type. They can also guide you on how to maintain your haircut and suggest the right products for your hair.

Step 3: Choose the Right Bangs

The right bangs can make all the difference in your haircut. For an oval face, side-swept bangs or layered bangs that fall on your forehead are the best options. Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face look shorter.

Step 4: Style your Hair

Once you get your front haircut, style your hair to enhance your features. You can blow-dry your hair, use a curling iron, or straighten your hair. Experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Maintaining your Front Haircut

Getting a front haircut is just the beginning – you need to maintain it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your front haircut:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQs

What is an oval face shape?

An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face. This face shape is considered the most versatile as it can pull off a variety of hairstyles.

What is a front haircut?

A front haircut is a hairstyle that frames your face and highlights your best features. It usually involves cutting your hair in layers and adding bangs to the front.

What are the best bangs for an oval face?

The best bangs for an oval face are side-swept bangs or layered bangs that fall on your forehead. Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face appear shorter.

Hair Care for Front Haircut

A front haircut requires proper hair care to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a hat or scarf.

Getting a front haircut for your oval face can enhance your natural beauty and highlight your best features. Follow our guide to get a perfect front haircut and maintain it with proper hair care. Experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you best.

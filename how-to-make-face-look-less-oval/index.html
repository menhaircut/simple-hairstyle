How To Make Your Face Look Less Oval

Written by Daniel Sep 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your face looking too oval? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people feel self-conscious about the shape of their face. Fortunately, there are ways to make your face look less oval without undergoing any invasive procedures. In this article, we'll provide you with tips and tricks on how to achieve a more balanced facial shape.

How to Make Your Face Look Less Oval

Step 1: Contouring

Contouring is a great way to create the illusion of a more oval face shape. Use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply the product to the areas of your face that you want to appear slimmer, such as your temples, cheekbones, and jawline. Blend the product well using a brush or sponge.

Step 2: Hairstyles

Your hairstyle can also make a difference in how oval your face appears. Avoid styles that are too long or too short. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head and have layers that fall around your face. Bangs can also work wonders for creating a more oval face shape.

Step 3: Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone and lift the muscles in your face, which can reduce the appearance of an oval face shape. Try exercises such as puckering your lips and holding for 5-10 seconds, or placing your index fingers on the outer corners of your eyes and pulling your skin back while squinting.

Tips for Making Your Face Look Less Oval

Tip 1: Use Lighter Shades

Using lighter shades of makeup on the areas of your face that you want to appear larger can help balance out an oval face shape. Apply a highlighter to your forehead, nose, and chin to create the illusion of a more balanced face shape.

Tip 2: Wear Statement Earrings

Wearing statement earrings can draw attention away from the oval shape of your face. Opt for earrings that are large and bold to create a distraction from your face shape.

Tip 3: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid hairstyles that are pulled back tightly, such as a high ponytail or bun. These styles can accentuate the oval shape of your face. Instead, opt for looser hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.

FAQ

Q: Can facial exercises really help reduce the appearance of an oval face shape?

While facial exercises can help tone and lift the muscles in your face, they may not work for everyone. Results may vary depending on your individual facial structure.

Q: Is contouring difficult to do?

Contouring can take some practice, but there are plenty of tutorials available online to help you get started. Start with a light hand and build up the product gradually to avoid applying too much.

Hair Care

Use Hair Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. Look for products that add volume and texture to your hair, such as volumizing mousse or texturizing spray.

Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons too often. Heat can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray beforehand.

Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a more balanced facial shape and feel confident in your appearance. Remember, everyone's face shape is unique and beautiful in its own way. Embrace your individuality and rock your oval face shape with pride!

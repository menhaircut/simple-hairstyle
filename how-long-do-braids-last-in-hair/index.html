How Long Do Braids Last In Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you planning to get braids but not sure how long they will last in your hair? Braids are a popular protective hairstyle that can last for a few days to several weeks, depending on various factors. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about how long do braids last in hair, including tips and tricks to make them last longer.

Table of Content

  1. How to braid hair
  2. How long do braids last
  3. Tips to make braids last longer

How to braid hair

Before we dive into how long braids last, let's first talk about how to braid hair. Braiding hair is a simple process that involves dividing the hair into three sections and crossing them over each other.

Here are the steps to braid hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide the hair into three equal sections.
  3. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  4. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Tie the end of the braid with a hair tie or elastic band.

Once you have learned how to braid hair, you can experiment with different braid styles, such as box braids, cornrows, and French braids.

How long do braids last

The lifespan of braids depends on several factors, such as the type of hair, the size of the braids, and the level of maintenance. Typically, braids can last anywhere from a few days to eight weeks.

Here is a breakdown of how long different types of braids can last:

  • Box braids: 4-6 weeks
  • Cornrows: 2-6 weeks
  • Twists: 4-8 weeks
  • Faux locs: 6-8 weeks

It's worth noting that leaving braids in for too long can cause damage to the hair, such as breakage and hair loss. Therefore, it's important to remove braids after the recommended time frame and give your hair a break before getting another set of braids.

Tips to make braids last longer

If you want to extend the lifespan of your braids, here are some tips to follow:

  • Wrap your hair with a silk scarf or bonnet at night to prevent frizz and tangling.
  • Avoid getting your braids wet, as this can cause them to unravel.
  • Apply a light oil to your scalp to keep it moisturized and prevent itchiness.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your braids, as this can cause tension on the hairline.

FAQs about braids

Here are some frequently asked questions about braids:

1. Can I wash my braids?

Yes, you can wash your braids, but it's important to be gentle and avoid scrubbing them too hard. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner and rinse your hair thoroughly.

2. Can I swim with braids?

It's not recommended to swim with braids, as chlorine and saltwater can damage the hair and cause them to unravel. If you must swim, wear a swim cap or cover your hair with a scarf.

3. Can I style my braids?

Yes, you can style your braids, but be gentle and avoid using heat styling tools. Opt for low-manipulation styles, such as buns and ponytails.

Hair care tips for braids

When wearing braids, it's important to take care of your hair and scalp to prevent damage and promote healthy hair growth. Here are some hair care tips for braids:

  • Keep your scalp clean and moisturized by applying a light oil, such as jojoba or almond oil.
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, as this can cause tension on the hairline.
  • Take breaks between getting braids to give your hair a chance to breathe and recover.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these hair care tips and taking proper precautions, you can enjoy braids for an extended period while maintaining healthy and strong hair.

Conclusion

Overall, braids can last for a few days to several weeks, depending on various factors. By following the tips and tricks mentioned in this article, you can make your braids last longer and keep your hair healthy and strong. Remember to take breaks between getting braids and give your hair the TLC it deserves.

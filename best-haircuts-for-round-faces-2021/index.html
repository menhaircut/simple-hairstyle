Best Haircuts For Round Faces 2021

Written by Christine Jun 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, it can be challenging to find the perfect haircut. You want a style that flatters your features and complements your face shape. Luckily, there are plenty of haircuts that work well for round faces. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for round faces in 2021.

Description

A round face is characterized by soft curves and a circular shape. The width and length of the face are nearly equal, and the cheekbones are typically the widest part of the face. When choosing a haircut for a round face, you want to create the illusion of length and add some angles to the face to balance out the roundness.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for a Round Face

When choosing a haircut for a round face, you want to consider a few factors:

  • Length: Longer hair can help elongate the face, while shorter hair can make it appear wider.
  • Layers: Layers can add movement and texture to the hair and help create the illusion of length.
  • Angles: Adding angles to the hair can help balance out the roundness of the face.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces 2021

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help elongate the face and create movement and texture. This style works well for both straight and curly hair.

2. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob with side-swept bangs can help create angles and balance out the roundness of the face. This style works well for straight hair.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can add angles to the face and create a bold, edgy look. This style works well for both straight and curly hair.

4. Lob

A lob, or long bob, can help elongate the face and create movement and texture. This style works well for both straight and curly hair.

Step by Step: How to Get the Best Haircut for a Round Face

1. Research different haircuts that work well for round faces.

2. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which style would work best for your hair type and face shape.

3. Bring pictures of the style you want to your appointment to show your hairstylist.

4. Ask your hairstylist to add layers or angles to the cut to help flatter your face shape.

5. Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools to avoid damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to keep your hair looking fresh and clean.

FAQs

What hairstyles should round faces avoid?

Round faces should avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob.

Can I wear short hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear short hair if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose a style that adds angles to the face, such as a pixie cut or a bob with side-swept bangs.

What hair color looks best on round faces?

There is no one hair color that looks best on round faces. It depends on your skin tone and personal preference. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which hair color would work best for you.

Hair Care for Round Faces

When caring for your hair, it's important to use products that are tailored to your hair type and texture. For round faces, it's also essential to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and complements your face shape. By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can create a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

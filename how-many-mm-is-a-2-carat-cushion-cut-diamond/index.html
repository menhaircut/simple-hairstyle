What is a cushion cut diamond?

A cushion cut diamond is a diamond that has a square or rectangular shape with rounded corners. It is one of the most popular diamond cuts because it has a timeless look and can highlight the diamond's clarity and brilliance.

How to measure a cushion cut diamond?

The size of a cushion cut diamond is measured in millimeters (MM). The length and width of the diamond are measured at the widest points on the top of the diamond, also known as the crown. The depth of the diamond is measured from the top of the diamond to the bottom, also known as the pavilion.

To get an accurate measurement of a cushion cut diamond's size, it's important to use a millimeter gauge or ruler. You can also ask a professional jeweler to measure the diamond for you.

How many MM is a 2 carat cushion cut diamond?

A 2 carat cushion cut diamond typically measures around 8.00-8.50 MM in length and width. However, the exact size of the diamond can vary based on the diamond's cut, depth, and other factors.

It's important to note that the size of a diamond does not always determine its value or beauty. The cut, color, and clarity of the diamond also play a significant role in its overall appearance and worth.

Tips for choosing a cushion cut diamond

When choosing a cushion cut diamond, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Look for a diamond with a high cut grade, as this will ensure maximum brilliance and sparkle.
  • Consider the diamond's color, as a more colorless diamond will appear brighter and more valuable.
  • Pay attention to the diamond's clarity, as any blemishes or inclusions can affect the diamond's appearance and value.
  • Choose a diamond with a shape and size that complements your personal style and preferences.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between a cushion cut and a princess cut diamond?

A cushion cut diamond has a square or rectangular shape with rounded corners, while a princess cut diamond has a square or rectangular shape with sharp corners. Cushion cut diamonds are known for their soft, romantic look, while princess cut diamonds have a more modern and geometric appearance.

What is the most popular size for a cushion cut diamond?

The most popular size for a cushion cut diamond varies based on personal preference and budget. However, 2 carat cushion cut diamonds are a popular choice for engagement rings and other fine jewelry.

What is the average cost of a 2 carat cushion cut diamond?

The cost of a 2 carat cushion cut diamond can vary greatly based on the diamond's color, clarity, and cut. On average, a 2 carat cushion cut diamond can cost anywhere from $8,000 to $50,000 or more.

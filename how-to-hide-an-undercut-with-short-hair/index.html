How To Hide An Undercut With Short Hair

Written by Bardi Sep 13, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of your undercut showing and want to hide it? You’re not alone. Whether you’re growing out your undercut or just want a new look, hiding it can be a challenging task. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll show you how to hide an undercut with short hair.

Description

An undercut is a hairstyle that involves shaving the hair underneath the top layer of hair. This creates a contrast between the short hair underneath and the longer hair on top. While undercuts are a trendy and popular hairstyle, they can be difficult to hide if you want to switch up your look or grow out your hair.

How to Hide an Undercut with Short Hair

To hide an undercut with short hair, you have a few options:

1. Part your hair differently

One way to hide your undercut is to part your hair differently. If you usually part your hair on the side, try parting it in the middle or on the opposite side. This will help cover up the shaved area and create a new look.

2. Use hair accessories

Hair accessories are a great way to hide an undercut. You can use headbands, scarves, or hair clips to cover up the shaved area. Just make sure to choose accessories that complement your hairstyle and outfit.

3. Style your hair differently

You can also hide your undercut by styling your hair differently. For example, you can try a messy bun or a top knot to cover up the shaved area. You can also use hair gel or pomade to slick back your hair and create a sleek look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to hide an undercut with short hair:

Step 1: Choose your method

Decide which method you want to use to hide your undercut. You can choose to part your hair differently, use hair accessories, or style your hair differently.

Step 2: Gather your tools

Depending on which method you choose, you may need hair accessories, hair gel, or pomade. Make sure to have everything you need before you start.

Step 3: Style your hair

If you’re going to style your hair differently, make sure to do it before you try to hide your undercut. Use hair gel or pomade to slick back your hair or create a messy bun or top knot.

Step 4: Part your hair differently

If you’re going to part your hair differently, use a comb to create a new part. You can part your hair in the middle or on the opposite side of your usual part.

Step 5: Use hair accessories

If you’re going to use hair accessories, choose ones that complement your hairstyle and outfit. You can use headbands, scarves, or hair clips to cover up the shaved area.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you hide your undercut:

1. Experiment with different hairstyles

Don’t be afraid to try different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you. You may need to experiment with different parts, hair accessories, or styling products to find the perfect look.

2. Use the right products

Make sure to use the right products for your hair type and hairstyle. For example, if you have curly hair, you may need to use a different styling product than if you have straight hair.

3. Get regular haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help you maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy. Make sure to schedule regular appointments with your hairstylist.

FAQ

Q: How long does it take to grow out an undercut?

A: It can take several months to a year to grow out an undercut, depending on how fast your hair grows and how long your undercut is.

Q: Can I still style my hair if I have an undercut?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair if you have an undercut. You may need to use different products or techniques to achieve the look you want.

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have an undercut?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if you have an undercut. Just make sure to follow the instructions on the hair dye carefully and consult with a hairstylist if you have any questions.

Hair Care

When hiding an undercut with short hair, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.

2. Use the right products

Use the right products for your hair type and hairstyle. This includes styling products, such as hair gel or pomade, as well as hair care products, such as shampoo and conditioner.

3. Protect your hair from damage

Avoid heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Now that you know how to hide an undercut with short hair, you can experiment with different hairstyles and looks. Whether you’re growing out your undercut or just want a new style, there are plenty of options available to you. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a look that’s uniquely yours.

What Can You Do With Shoulder Length Hair A Guide For 2023

Shoulder length hair is a versatile and popular hair length for many people. Whether you are growing out a shorter haircut or want to change up your look, shoulder length hair offers many styling options. In this guide, we will explore what you can do with shoulder length hair, including how to style it, tips for maintaining healthy hair, and frequently asked questions.

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair

Shoulder length hair can be styled in many different ways, depending on your personal preferences and hair type. Some popular styling options for shoulder length hair include:

  • Beachy waves
  • Straight and sleek
  • Braids and updos
  • Curls and volume

These styles can be achieved with the help of styling tools such as curling irons, straighteners, and hair dryers, as well as hair products like mousse, hairspray, and texturizing sprays.

Step-by-Step Guide for Styling Shoulder Length Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide for achieving some popular styles for shoulder length hair:

Beachy Waves

  1. Apply a texturizing spray to damp hair
  2. Part hair in the middle or to one side
  3. Take small sections of hair and wrap around a curling iron, curling away from the face
  4. Repeat until all hair is curled
  5. Run fingers through hair to loosen curls and create a more natural look
  6. Finish with a light hairspray

Straight and Sleek

  1. Apply a heat protectant to dry hair
  2. Section hair and use a flat iron to straighten each section
  3. Finish with a smoothing serum

Braids and Updos

  1. Choose a braid or updo style and gather necessary hair accessories
  2. Prepare hair by washing and conditioning, then blow dry
  3. Begin styling, using hair ties, bobby pins, and hairspray to secure the style
  4. Finish with a light hairspray

Curls and Volume

  1. Apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair
  2. Blow dry hair using a round brush to create volume
  3. Section hair and use a curling iron to create loose curls
  4. Run fingers through hair to loosen curls and create volume
  5. Finish with a light hairspray

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Shoulder Length Hair

Keeping your shoulder length hair healthy is important for achieving your desired styles and preventing damage. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-drying
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid using heat tools every day
  • Protect hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner
  • Trim hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to nourish hair from the inside out

Frequently Asked Questions About Shoulder Length Hair

How long does it take to grow shoulder length hair?

The average rate of hair growth is about half an inch per month, so it can take anywhere from 6 months to a year to grow shoulder length hair depending on your starting length.

Can I still wear a ponytail with shoulder length hair?

Yes, ponytails are still an option with shoulder length hair. You can wear a low, mid, or high ponytail depending on your preference.

Can I dye my shoulder length hair?

Yes, you can dye your shoulder length hair, but it is important to use hair dye that is gentle and does not contain harsh chemicals that can damage your hair. It is also important to follow the instructions carefully and use a deep conditioning treatment afterwards.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful shoulder length hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf at night to prevent breakage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair

With these tips and styling ideas, you can make the most of your shoulder length hair and keep it healthy and beautiful for years to come.

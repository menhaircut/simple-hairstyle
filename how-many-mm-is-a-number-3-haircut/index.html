How Many Mm Is A Number 3 Haircut

Written by Daniel Jul 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you wondering how many mm is a number 3 haircut? You’re not alone! This is a common question that many people ask when they visit the barbershop. The answer is simple, but it can vary depending on your hair type and the type of clipper being used. In this article, we’ll explain everything you need to know about a number 3 haircut, including the mm measurement, how to achieve the look, and tips for maintaining it.

How to Achieve a Number 3 Haircut

A number 3 haircut is a popular choice for men who want a short, clean-cut look without going too short. To achieve this hairstyle, your barber will use a clipper guard with a length of 3/8 inch or 9.5mm. This will leave your hair approximately 3/8 inch or 9.5mm long all over your head.

The number 3 haircut is versatile and can be styled in different ways depending on your preferences. You can opt for a classic buzz cut, a crew cut, or even a textured look by using scissors to cut the top of your hair slightly shorter than the sides and back.

Step by Step Guide to Getting a Number 3 Haircut

If you’re planning to get a number 3 haircut, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you prepare for your barbershop visit:

  1. Wash your hair thoroughly before your appointment. This will make it easier for your barber to cut your hair evenly.
  2. Bring a photo of the hairstyle you want to show your barber. This will help them understand your preferences and give you the look you desire.
  3. Ask your barber to use a number 3 clipper guard to achieve the desired length.
  4. If you want a textured look, ask your barber to use scissors to cut the top of your hair slightly shorter than the sides and back.
  5. When your haircut is finished, ask your barber for styling tips and product recommendations to help you maintain the look.

Tips for Maintaining a Number 3 Haircut

Now that you know how to achieve a number 3 haircut, it’s important to maintain it properly to keep it looking great. Here are some tips to help you do just that:

  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair at the desired length.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Apply a hair styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about a number 3 haircut:

What do I tell my barber if I want a number 3 haircut?

You can simply ask your barber for a number 3 haircut, and they will know what clipper guard to use to achieve the desired length.

What is the difference between a number 2 and number 3 haircut?

A number 2 haircut uses a clipper guard with a length of 1/4 inch or 6mm, while a number 3 haircut uses a clipper guard with a length of 3/8 inch or 9.5mm. This means that a number 3 haircut is slightly longer than a number 2 haircut.

Can I style my number 3 haircut differently?

Yes, a number 3 haircut can be styled in different ways depending on your preferences. You can opt for a classic buzz cut, a crew cut, or even a textured look by using scissors to cut the top of your hair slightly shorter than the sides and back.

Hair Care

It’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your number 3 haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get plenty of rest and exercise to reduce stress, which can lead to hair loss.

Now that you know how many mm is a number 3 haircut and how to achieve and maintain this popular hairstyle, you’re ready to visit the barbershop and get the look you desire. Remember to communicate clearly with your barber, take good care of your hair, and enjoy your new style!

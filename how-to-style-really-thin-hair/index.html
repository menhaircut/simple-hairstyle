How To Style Really Thin Hair Tips Tricks And Techniques

Written by Christine Jun 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have really thin hair, you may find it difficult to style it in a way that makes it look fuller and more voluminous. However, with the right techniques and products, it is possible to create a variety of hairstyles that will make your hair look thicker and more voluminous. In this article, we will provide you with tips, tricks, and techniques on how to style really thin hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Really Thin Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Really Thin Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Really Thin Hair

How to Style Really Thin Hair

The first step in styling really thin hair is to find the right haircut. A layered cut or a bob with layers can add volume and movement to your hair. Additionally, consider getting a fringe or bangs to frame your face and make your hair look fuller.

Once you have the right cut, you can use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. After washing and conditioning your hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray on your roots to add lift.

Blow-drying your hair upside down can also help add volume and create a fuller look. Use a round brush to lift your roots while blow-drying, and finish with a blast of cold air to set your style.

Products for Styling Really Thin Hair

When it comes to styling really thin hair, the right products can make all the difference. Here are some of the best products for styling really thin hair:

  • Volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Volumizing mousse or spray
  • Dry shampoo
  • Hair powder
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for styling really thin hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair, and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair upside down, using a round brush to lift your roots.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a dry shampoo or hair powder to add texture and volume.
  5. Style your hair as desired using a curling iron, straightener, or other styling tools.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Really Thin Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling really thin hair:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and keep your hair looking clean and fresh.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair, such as oils and serums.
  • Try using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create a more natural, tousled look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids, buns, and half-up, half-down styles, to add interest and dimension to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I still color my hair if it's really thin?

Yes, you can still color your hair if it's really thin. However, it's important to use a gentle, ammonia-free hair dye and to avoid overprocessing your hair.

Should I use a hair mask on my really thin hair?

Yes, using a hair mask once a week can help nourish and strengthen your hair, which can make it look and feel fuller. Look for a hair mask that contains ingredients such as keratin, biotin, and collagen.

Can I use a straightener on my really thin hair?

Yes, you can use a straightener on your really thin hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant spray and to avoid using the straightener on the same section of hair for too long.

Hair Care for Really Thin Hair

In addition to styling your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for really thin hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look even thinner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to avoid pulling or breaking your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider taking a hair supplement, such as biotin or collagen, to strengthen your hair and promote growth.

Styling really thin hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can create a variety of hairstyles that make your hair look fuller and more voluminous. Experiment with different cuts, styles, and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice.

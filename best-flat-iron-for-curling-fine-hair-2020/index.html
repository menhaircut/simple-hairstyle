Best Flat Iron For Curling Fine Hair 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of using a curling iron that damages your hair and leaves it looking lifeless? Look no further, we have the solution for you! In this guide, we will introduce you to the best flat iron for curling fine hair in 2020. We will cover everything from how to use it, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care. So, let's get started!

Description

Flat irons are a popular tool for straightening hair, but did you know that you can also use them to create beautiful curls? However, not all flat irons are created equal, and fine hair requires a specific type of flat iron that won't damage it. The best flat iron for curling fine hair in 2020 is one that is gentle on your hair while still providing long-lasting curls.

How to Choose the Best Flat Iron for Curling Fine Hair

Before we dive into the specifics of the best flat irons for curling fine hair, let's first talk about what to look for when choosing one.

1. Plate Material

The plate material is an essential factor when it comes to choosing a flat iron for curling fine hair. Ceramic or tourmaline plates are the best options, as they provide even heat distribution and reduce the risk of heat damage.

2. Plate Size

The plate size also matters when it comes to curling fine hair. Opt for a flat iron with a smaller plate size, as it will be easier to maneuver and create tighter curls.

3. Temperature Control

Temperature control is crucial when it comes to curling fine hair. Look for a flat iron that allows you to adjust the temperature to avoid using excessive heat, which can damage your hair.

Step by Step Instructions on How to Curl Fine Hair with a Flat Iron

Now that you have chosen the best flat iron for curling fine hair let's go over how to use it to achieve beautiful curls.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it is completely dry before using the flat iron to avoid heat damage.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before using the flat iron, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from the heat.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections, clipping them out of the way to make it easier to work with.

Step 4: Start Curling

Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron around it, making sure to start at the root. Twist the flat iron away from your face and slowly glide it down the hair shaft. Hold for a few seconds before releasing the hair.

Step 5: Repeat

Repeat this process until you have curled all of your hair. Remember to curl away from your face to achieve a more natural look.

Tips for Curling Fine Hair with a Flat Iron

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect curls:

1. Use a Smaller Section of Hair

Using a smaller section of hair will allow you to achieve tighter curls.

2. Don't Hold the Hair in the Flat Iron for Too Long

Leaving the hair in the flat iron for too long can cause heat damage. Hold for a few seconds before releasing the hair.

3. Use a Comb

Using a comb to separate the hair and create sections will make it easier to curl and achieve a more natural look.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I curl my hair with a flat iron?

Yes, you can curl your hair with a flat iron. However, you need to use a specific type of flat iron for fine hair to avoid damage.

2. How do I prevent heat damage when curling my hair with a flat iron?

Using a heat protectant and opting for a flat iron with temperature control can help prevent heat damage.

3. How long will the curls last?

The length of time the curls last will depend on your hair type and how well you maintain them. Using a hairspray or other styling product can help them last longer.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

Here are a few tips to help you take care of your fine hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Using a gentle shampoo will help prevent damage to your hair and keep it healthy.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Too Often

Using heat styling tools too often can damage your hair. Try to limit your use of flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers.

3. Use a Heat Protectant

Using a heat protectant before using heat styling tools can help prevent damage to your hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the best flat iron for curling fine hair in 2020 is essential to achieving beautiful, healthy curls. Remember to choose a flat iron with ceramic or tourmaline plates, a smaller plate size, and temperature control. Follow our step-by-step instructions and tips to achieve the perfect curls without damaging your hair. Lastly, take care of your hair by using a gentle shampoo, avoiding heat styling too often, and using a heat protectant.

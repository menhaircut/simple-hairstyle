What Kind Of Beard Suits A Round Face

Written by Bardi Aug 20, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

Are you a round-faced gentleman wondering what kind of beard will suit you best? Our guide will help you find the perfect beard style that complements your facial features. Read on for tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions.

Description: Round Faces and Beards

Round faces are characterized by their soft, curved jawline, and wide cheekbones. Beards can be a great way to add definition and balance to the face. However, not all beard styles are created equal. Certain styles can make a round face appear even rounder or unbalanced. It's important to choose a beard style that complements your unique facial features.

How To Choose a Beard Style for a Round Face

Choosing the right beard style for a round face can be tricky. Here are a few factors to consider:

Face Shape

When choosing a beard style, consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a style that adds length and definition to the face.

Hair Growth

Consider the thickness and texture of your facial hair. Some styles require more hair than others, so make sure you choose a style that works with your natural hair growth.

Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle and how much time and effort you're willing to put into maintaining your beard. Some styles require more upkeep than others.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing a Beard Style for a Round Face

Follow these steps to choose the perfect beard style for your round face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Take a close look at your face and determine your face shape. If you're unsure, ask a friend or a professional stylist for advice.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Growth

Consider the thickness and texture of your facial hair. Some styles require more hair than others, so make sure you choose a style that works with your natural hair growth.

Step 3: Look for Length

Choose a beard style that adds length to your face. This can help balance out the roundness of your face and create a more defined jawline.

Step 4: Avoid Full Beards

Avoid full beards that add volume to the cheeks. Instead, opt for a shorter beard style that adds length and definition to the face.

Step 5: Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different beard styles until you find the one that works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Beard

Once you've chosen the perfect beard style for your round face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are a few tips:

Regular Trimming

Regular trimming will help keep your beard looking neat and tidy. Invest in a good pair of clippers and trim your beard every few weeks.

Cleanliness

Keep your beard clean by washing it regularly with a good quality beard shampoo. This will help prevent dirt and oil buildup.

Moisturizing

Moisturize your beard regularly with beard oil or balm. This will help keep your beard soft and healthy.

FAQs

What kind of beard is best for a round face?

Shorter beard styles that add length and definition to the face are best for round faces. Avoid full beards that add volume to the cheeks.

What is the best way to maintain a beard?

The best way to maintain a beard is to regularly trim it, keep it clean, and moisturize it with beard oil or balm.

Can I still have a beard if I have a round face?

Absolutely! With the right beard style, a round face can look just as stylish and handsome as any other face shape.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your beard and the skin underneath it. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized. Apply beard oil or balm to keep it soft and healthy. Finally, trim it regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Short Layered Bob With Bangs Over 50 A Timeless Look

Written by Aprilia Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 50, you may be wondering if a short haircut is still appropriate for your age. The answer is yes, and a short layered bob with bangs is a timeless and trendy look that can work for women of all ages. In this article, we'll show you how to rock this haircut with style and confidence, no matter your hair type or face shape.

Description: What is a Short Layered Bob with Bangs?

A short layered bob with bangs is a haircut that features short layers at the back and sides, with longer layers at the top and front. The bangs can be blunt or side-swept, depending on your preference, and they can be cut straight across or angled to frame your face. This haircut is versatile and low-maintenance, making it a great choice for busy women who want to look chic without spending hours styling their hair.

How to: Getting a Short Layered Bob with Bangs Over 50

Getting a short layered bob with bangs over 50 is easy and straightforward. Here's how:

Step 1: Choose a Style

Before you go to the salon, decide on the style you want. Look at pictures online or in magazines, and choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type. Bring the picture to the salon so your stylist can see exactly what you want.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

When you arrive at the salon, consult with your stylist to make sure the style you've chosen is right for you. Your stylist will also be able to advise you on the best length and layers for your hair type and face shape.

Step 3: Cut and Style

Once you and your stylist have agreed on the style, it's time to cut and style your hair. Your stylist will start by cutting the back and sides of your hair into short layers, then add longer layers at the top and front. They will then cut your bangs to the desired length and style them to frame your face.

Tips: How to Rock a Short Layered Bob with Bangs Over 50

Here are some tips for rocking a short layered bob with bangs over 50:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add body to your hair and prevent it from falling flat.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a straightening iron to smooth out your bangs and create a polished look.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as pinning your bangs back or adding a headband for a playful look.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

FAQ: Short Layered Bob with Bangs Over 50

Q: Will a short layered bob with bangs make me look older?

A: No, a short layered bob with bangs can actually make you look younger and more vibrant. This haircut is versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type, so talk to your stylist about the best style for you.

Q: Can I wear a short layered bob with bangs if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, a short layered bob with bangs can actually make thin hair look fuller and more voluminous. Talk to your stylist about adding layers and texture to your hair to create more body.

Q: How often do I need to get a trim?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care: How to Maintain Your Short Layered Bob with Bangs Over 50

To maintain your short layered bob with bangs over 50, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from getting too greasy or weighed down.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straightening irons, too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to add shine and prevent frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a short layered bob with bangs over 50 with confidence and style. Remember to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type, consult with your stylist, and maintain your haircut with regular trims and proper hair care.

How To Make Face Look Wider With Makeup A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to make your face look wider with makeup? If so, you’ve come to the right place! This step-by-step guide will show you how to achieve a wider-looking face with just a few simple makeup tricks.

Description

Having a wide face can help balance out other features and give you an overall more symmetrical appearance. However, not everyone is naturally blessed with a wide face. Luckily, you can use makeup to create the illusion of a wider face in just a few easy steps.

How to Make Face Look Wider with Makeup

Step 1: Start with a clean face

Before you begin applying makeup, make sure your face is clean and moisturized. This will ensure that your makeup goes on smoothly and stays in place throughout the day.

Step 2: Apply primer

Next, apply a primer to your face. This will help create a smooth base for your makeup and help it last longer. Focus on the areas where you want to create the illusion of width, such as your cheekbones and temples.

Step 3: Use a darker foundation

Choose a foundation that is one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone. Apply it to your cheekbones, temples, and jawline, blending it outwards for a natural look. This will create the illusion of shadows, which can help make your face appear wider.

Step 4: Highlight your features

Use a highlighter to draw attention to the areas of your face that you want to appear wider. Apply it to the high points of your cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, and your forehead. This will create the illusion of height and width.

Step 5: Contour your cheeks

Use a contour powder to create the illusion of more prominent cheekbones. Apply it in the hollows of your cheeks, blending it upwards towards your temples. This will help create the illusion of width and balance out your features.

Step 6: Define your brows

Your eyebrows can help frame your face and make it appear wider. Use a brow pencil or powder to fill in any sparse areas and define their shape. Try to create a slightly arched shape to help widen your face.

Step 7: Add some color

Finally, add a pop of color to your cheeks with a blush. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone and apply it to the apples of your cheeks, blending it upwards towards your temples. This will help create a healthy, youthful glow and add to the illusion of width.

Tips

  • Use a fluffy brush to blend your makeup for a natural look.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your face look cakey and unnatural.
  • Choose makeup products that complement your skin tone and type.
  • Experiment with different shades and techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I use these techniques to make my face look slimmer?

A: Yes, you can use similar techniques to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Simply use lighter foundation and highlighter on the areas where you want to create the illusion of height, and use a darker contour powder on your jawline and temples.

Q: How often should I clean my makeup brushes?

A: It’s recommended to clean your makeup brushes at least once a week to prevent bacteria buildup and ensure that they work properly.

Hair Care

While makeup can certainly help create the illusion of a wider face, your hairstyle can also play a big role. Consider opting for a style that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as loose waves or a layered haircut. Avoid styles that pull your hair back tightly, as this can make your face look narrower.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily make your face look wider with makeup. With a little practice, you’ll be able to create a perfectly balanced, symmetrical look that enhances your natural beauty.

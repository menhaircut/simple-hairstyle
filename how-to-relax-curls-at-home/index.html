How To Relax Curls At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your curly hair and want to switch to a straighter look? Relaxing your curls at home is a great way to achieve the look you desire without breaking the bank. In this guide, we will take you through the process of relaxing your curls at home step-by-step. We will also provide tips and hair care advice to ensure your hair remains healthy and strong.

What is Hair Relaxing?

Hair relaxing is a process that chemically alters your hair structure to make it straighter. It involves the use of a relaxer, which is a chemical cream that breaks down the bonds that give your hair its natural curl pattern. The result is straighter, smoother hair.

How to Relax Curls at Home

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

The first step is to gather all the supplies you will need. Here is a list of items you will need:

  • Relaxer kit
  • Plastic gloves
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Clarifying shampoo
  • Neutralizing shampoo
  • Deep conditioner
  • Old t-shirt or towel

Make sure you read the instructions carefully before starting the process.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

The second step is to prep your hair. Start by applying petroleum jelly or a protective cream to your hairline, ears, and nape to prevent the relaxer from touching your skin.

Next, detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb. Make sure your hair is completely dry before applying the relaxer.

Step 3: Apply the Relaxer

Apply the relaxer to your hair, starting from the back and working your way to the front. Use the back of a comb to spread the relaxer evenly throughout your hair. Be sure to follow the instructions on the kit carefully and avoid leaving the relaxer on for too long.

Step 4: Rinse and Neutralize

Once the time is up, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water to remove the relaxer. Follow up with a neutralizing shampoo to stop the chemical process. Rinse your hair again and apply a deep conditioner to restore moisture.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

You can now style your hair as desired. However, avoid using heat styling tools for the first few days to allow your hair to recover.

Tips for Relaxing Curls at Home

Here are some tips to ensure you get the best results:

  • Avoid relaxing your hair too often as it can damage your hair
  • Use a clarifying shampoo before the relaxing process to remove any buildup on your hair
  • Use a deep conditioner after the process to restore moisture and prevent breakage
  • Protect your hairline, ears, and nape with petroleum jelly or a protective cream

FAQs

Can I relax my hair if it has been colored?

Yes, you can relax colored hair. However, it is important to wait at least two weeks after coloring before relaxing your hair to avoid damaging it.

How long does relaxed hair last?

Relaxed hair can last up to six to eight weeks before you need to touch up the roots.

Hair Care After Relaxing

After relaxing your hair, it is important to take care of it to prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a deep conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dye or bleach

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy, strong hair after relaxing your curls at home.

Conclusion

Relaxing your curls at home is a great way to switch up your look without breaking the bank. By following our step-by-step guide and taking care of your hair after the process, you can achieve healthy, straight hair. Remember to always read the instructions carefully and take the necessary precautions to prevent damage to your hair and skin.

Do Round Faces Look Good With Short Hair

Written by Oliver Jul 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may have wondered whether short hair would suit you. Short haircuts are becoming increasingly popular, but you might be concerned that they won't flatter your face shape. In this post, we'll explore the question "do round faces look good with short hair?" and provide you with tips and advice on how to choose the best short hairstyle for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyle for a Round Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Hair on a Round Face
  • FAQ

Description

Round faces are characterized by a circular shape and full cheeks. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a short hairstyle that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a more oval shape. While there are some short haircuts that may not flatter a round face, there are plenty of styles that will enhance your features and make you look amazing!

How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyle for a Round Face

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, you'll want to consider a few key factors. Firstly, you should look for styles that add height to your hair, as this will elongate your face. Secondly, you should look for styles that have layers or texture, as this will add volume and movement to your hair. Finally, you should consider your hair type and texture - some styles will work better with thick hair, while others may be better suited to fine hair.

Examples of Short Hairstyles for a Round Face

There are plenty of short hairstyles that work well on a round face. Here are a few examples:

  • Pixie cut with long side-swept bangs
  • Textured bob with layers
  • Asymmetrical bob with a side part
  • Fauxhawk with textured layers
  • Short shag with choppy layers

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to choose the best short hairstyle for a round face:

  1. Consider your face shape and decide which features you want to enhance.
  2. Look for short hairstyles that add height to your hair.
  3. Look for styles with layers or texture to add volume and movement.
  4. Take your hair type and texture into account.
  5. Consult with a stylist to find the best short hairstyle for you.

Tips for Styling Short Hair on a Round Face

Once you've chosen a short hairstyle for your round face, you'll want to make sure you style it in a way that flatters your features. Here are a few tips:

  • Use volumizing products to add height to your hair.
  • Add texture with a curling iron or texturizing spray.
  • Try a side part to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that can make your face look rounder.

FAQ

Can round faces wear short hair?

Yes, round faces can wear short hair! The key is to choose a style that elongates your face and adds height and volume to your hair.

What short hairstyles work best for a round face?

Short hairstyles with height, layers, and texture work best for a round face. Examples include pixie cuts, textured bobs, and asymmetrical bobs.

Should I consult with a stylist before getting a short haircut?

Yes, it's always a good idea to consult with a stylist before getting a short haircut. They can help you choose a style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair type and texture.

Hair Care for Short Hair on a Round Face

Short hair on a round face requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add height to your hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant before using hot tools to style your hair.
  • Try not to wash your hair every day to prevent stripping it of natural oils.

In conclusion, round faces can absolutely wear short hair! By choosing the right style and styling it in a way that flatters your features, you can look amazing with short hair. Remember to consult with a stylist and take your hair type and texture into account when choosing a short hairstyle.

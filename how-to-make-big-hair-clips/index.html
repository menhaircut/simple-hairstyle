How To Make Big Hair Clips A Diy Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 16, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on hair accessories? Why not make your own big hair clips at home? It’s easier than you think! In this article, we’ll show you how to create your own hair clips in just a few simple steps. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Make Big Hair Clips
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Big Hair Clips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Big hair clips are a popular hair accessory that can add a touch of elegance to any hairstyle. They come in different shapes, sizes, and colors, making them versatile for various occasions. Making your own big hair clips is not only cost-effective but also fun and creative. You can personalize them to match your style and mood. From floral designs to glittery embellishments, the options are endless.

How to Make Big Hair Clips

Making big hair clips is a simple process that requires minimal tools and materials. You can easily find these items at your local craft store or online. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Large hair clips
  • Ribbons or fabric
  • Glue gun or fabric glue
  • Scissors
  • Embellishments (optional)

Step by Step Guide

Follow these easy steps to make your own big hair clips:

  1. Cut a piece of ribbon or fabric that is long and wide enough to cover the hair clip.
  2. Apply glue to the hair clip and wrap the ribbon or fabric around it, making sure to cover the entire clip.
  3. Trim any excess ribbon or fabric.
  4. Add embellishments, such as flowers, buttons, or beads, to the hair clip using the glue gun or fabric glue.
  5. Let the glue dry completely before using.

Tips for Making Big Hair Clips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when making big hair clips:

  • Choose ribbon or fabric that is sturdy and won’t easily fray.
  • Experiment with different colors, patterns, and textures to create unique designs.
  • Use a glue gun for a stronger hold, especially if you’re adding heavy embellishments.
  • Be careful when using a glue gun to avoid burning yourself.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making big hair clips:

Q: How do I attach the hair clip to my hair?

A: Simply open the hair clip and slide it onto a section of your hair. Then, close the clip to secure it in place.

Q: Can I use other types of embellishments besides flowers and beads?

A: Yes, you can use anything that you think would look good on a hair clip, such as feathers, sequins, or rhinestones.

Q: How do I clean my hair clip?

A: Use a damp cloth or cotton swab to clean the surface of the hair clip. Avoid getting the glue or embellishments wet.

Hair Care Tips

While big hair clips can enhance your hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair too. Here are some tips:

  • Don’t leave hair clips in your hair for too long, as they can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a hair tie or scrunchie when tying your hair, as elastic bands can also damage your hair.
  • Keep your hair moisturized by using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can create your own big hair clips and rock them on any occasion. Happy crafting!

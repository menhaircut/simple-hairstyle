How To Install A Frontal Wig Step By Step

Written by Robby Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you new to wearing wigs or just looking for a better way to install your frontal wig? Look no further! In this guide, we will go over step by step how to properly install a frontal wig to achieve a natural and flawless look. We will also include tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to ensure your wig lasts and looks amazing. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Prepping Your Natural Hair
  • Step 2: Applying Wig Cap and Adhesive
  • Step 3: Placing the Frontal Wig
  • Step 4: Styling Your Frontal Wig
  • Tips for Installing a Frontal Wig
  • FAQs About Frontal Wig Installation
  • Hair Care Tips for Frontal Wig Maintenance

Step 1: Prepping Your Natural Hair

The first step to installing a frontal wig is to prepare your natural hair. This includes washing and conditioning your hair, and making sure it is completely dry. You can also braid your hair or put it in cornrows to make it easier to apply the wig cap.

Next, use a wig cap to cover your natural hair. Make sure it is secure and snug, but not too tight. You can also use an adhesive to help the wig cap stay in place.

Step 2: Applying Wig Cap and Adhesive

Once your natural hair is prepped and covered with a wig cap, it's time to apply the adhesive. There are many different types of adhesives, such as glue or tape, so choose one that works best for you. Apply the adhesive along the hairline where the wig will be placed.

Let the adhesive dry for a few seconds until it becomes tacky. This will help the wig stick better.

Step 3: Placing the Frontal Wig

Now it's time to place the frontal wig. Start by lining up the wig with your natural hairline. Use a comb to adjust any flyaways or baby hairs. Once the wig is in place, press down firmly along the hairline to ensure it is secure.

Once the wig is in place, adjust it as needed to make sure it looks natural and comfortable. You can also use a hair dryer on a low setting to help set the wig in place.

Step 4: Styling Your Frontal Wig

Finally, it's time to style your frontal wig! You can style it just like you would with your natural hair. You can use hot tools like curling irons or straighteners, or even try a new hairstyle. Be careful not to damage the wig with too much heat, and always use a heat protectant product.

And that's it! With these simple steps, you can install a frontal wig like a pro.

Tips for Installing a Frontal Wig

  • Choose an adhesive that works best for you and your skin type.
  • Make sure your natural hair is completely dry before applying the wig cap.
  • Use a comb or brush to blend your natural hair with the wig for a natural look.
  • Take your time and be patient when installing the wig to ensure it looks natural and secure.

FAQs About Frontal Wig Installation

Q. How long does a frontal wig last?

A. With proper care and maintenance, a frontal wig can last up to six months to a year.

Q. Can I swim or exercise with a frontal wig?

A. It is not recommended to swim or exercise with a frontal wig as it can cause damage to the wig and adhesive.

Q. Can I sleep in a frontal wig?

A. It is not recommended to sleep in a frontal wig as it can cause damage to the wig and your natural hair.

Hair Care Tips for Frontal Wig Maintenance

  • Wash your wig regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your wig too often to prevent damage.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to excessive heat or sunlight.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently install and maintain your frontal wig for a natural and flawless look. Happy styling!

How To Cut Straight Bangs Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to update your hairstyle, cutting straight bangs might just be the answer. Not only do they frame your face beautifully, but they can also give you a fresh, youthful look. In this article, we'll show you how to cut straight bangs at home with our easy-to-follow guide.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Straight Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Straight Bangs
  • FAQs

How to Cut Straight Bangs

Cutting straight bangs can seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, you can do it yourself at home. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and blow-drying your hair as you normally would. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and separate your bangs from the rest of your hair. Use hair clips to secure the rest of your hair away from your face.

Step 2: Determine Your Bangs' Length

Using your comb, section off your bangs to the desired length. If you're unsure, start with longer bangs and gradually cut them shorter. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't add length once you've cut it.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Hold the section of hair between your fingers and use your scissors to cut straight across. Start from the center and work your way out to the sides. Make sure to cut slowly and carefully to avoid any mistakes.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Release your hair from the clips and check your bangs in the mirror. If they're too long, you can always trim them a little more. If they're too short, you'll have to wait for them to grow out.

Tips for Cutting Straight Bangs

Here are a few extra tips to help you get the perfect straight bangs:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Cut your bangs when your hair is dry to avoid any shrinkage.
  • Start with longer bangs and gradually cut them shorter.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short. It's better to cut them a little longer and trim them later if needed.
  • Take your time and cut slowly and carefully to avoid any mistakes.

FAQs

Can I cut my bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can cut straight bangs even if you have curly hair. Just make sure to cut them when your hair is dry to avoid any shrinkage.

How often should I trim my bangs?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want your bangs to be. On average, you should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Can I style my bangs in different ways?

Absolutely! Straight bangs can be styled in different ways, such as swept to the side or pinned back.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

To keep your bangs looking their best, here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your bangs separately from the rest of your hair to avoid over-washing.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to give them volume.
  • Avoid touching and playing with your bangs to prevent them from becoming oily.
  • Use dry shampoo in between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh.

Cutting straight bangs at home can be a fun and easy way to switch up your hairstyle. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a salon-worthy look without ever leaving your house. Remember to take your time, cut slowly and carefully, and follow our tips for the best results. Happy cutting!

The Best Grade Of Human Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide to the best grade of human hair! Whether you’re looking to purchase hair extensions or a wig, it’s important to know which grade of hair will give you the most natural and beautiful results. In this article, we’ll discuss the best grade of human hair, its benefits, how to choose the right one, and how to care for it.

Table of Contents

  • What is the Best Grade of Human Hair?
  • Benefits of the Best Grade of Human Hair
  • How to Choose the Right Grade of Human Hair
  • How to Care for the Best Grade of Human Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Best Grade of Human Hair?

The best grade of human hair is virgin hair, which means it is completely unprocessed and has not been chemically treated in any way. Virgin hair comes from a single donor and has all of its cuticles intact, which gives it a natural and healthy look. It also has a soft and silky texture that is easy to style and maintain.

Benefits of the Best Grade of Human Hair

There are many benefits to using the best grade of human hair, including:

  • Natural look and feel
  • Soft and silky texture
  • Longevity and durability
  • Easy to style and maintain

Virgin hair is also more versatile than other grades of human hair, as it can be dyed, curled, straightened, and styled just like your natural hair.

How to Choose the Right Grade of Human Hair

When choosing the right grade of human hair, there are a few factors to consider:

  • Budget: Virgin hair is the most expensive grade of human hair, so it may not be suitable for everyone’s budget.
  • Style: If you’re looking for a specific style or texture, make sure to choose a grade of hair that can be easily styled to achieve the look you want.
  • Longevity: If you want to wear your hair extensions or wig for a long time, choose a grade of hair that is durable and can withstand regular use.

Ultimately, the best grade of human hair is one that fits your budget and style preferences while also being durable and easy to maintain.

How to Care for the Best Grade of Human Hair

To keep your virgin hair looking its best, it’s important to follow a few simple hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair extensions or wig regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as this can damage the cuticles and make your hair extensions or wig look dull and lifeless.
  • Store your hair extensions or wig in a cool, dry place to prevent tangling and matting.

With proper care, your virgin hair can last for up to a year or even longer.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between virgin hair and remy hair?

Virgin hair is completely unprocessed and has not been chemically treated in any way, while remy hair has been chemically treated to remove any impurities or imperfections. Remy hair can still be of high quality, but it may not be as natural-looking as virgin hair.

Can virgin hair be colored or bleached?

Yes, virgin hair can be colored or bleached just like your natural hair. However, it’s important to have a professional colorist or stylist do the job to avoid damaging the hair.

Can I use hair products on my virgin hair?

Yes, you can use hair products on your virgin hair, but make sure to choose products that are gentle and sulfate-free to avoid damaging the hair.

How often should I wash my virgin hair?

You should wash your virgin hair extensions or wig every 1-2 weeks to keep them clean and looking their best.

We hope this guide has been helpful in choosing the best grade of human hair for your needs. Remember to always take good care of your hair extensions or wig to keep them looking their best for as long as possible!

How To Have Good Looking Messy Hair A Complete Guide For 2023

Are you tired of spending hours styling your hair every morning? Do you crave a low-maintenance, effortlessly cool look? Look no further than messy hair!

In this complete guide, we'll cover everything you need to know to have good looking messy hair in 2023. From how to achieve the look to tips for maintaining it, we've got you covered.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Have Good Looking Messy Hair

Messy hair is all about creating a relaxed, effortless look. But don't be fooled, there is a method to the madness. Here are the steps to achieving that perfect "I woke up like this" look:

Step 1: Start with the Right Cut

The key to good looking messy hair starts with the right cut. You want a cut that is layered and textured, with longer layers on top and shorter layers on the sides and back. This will give you the volume and movement you need to achieve the messy look.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Next, you'll need to use the right products to achieve that perfectly messy look. Start with a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair. Then, use a matte finish styling paste or wax to add definition and hold.

Step 3: Embrace the Mess

The key to good looking messy hair is to embrace the mess. Use your fingers to tousle and scrunch your hair, creating a relaxed, messy look. Don't worry about getting it perfect, the imperfections are what make it look so cool.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, here's a step-by-step guide to achieving good looking messy hair:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Apply Texturizing Spray or Mousse

Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair, using your fingers to tousle and scrunch your hair as you dry it. This will create a relaxed, messy look.

Step 4: Apply Styling Paste or Wax

Once your hair is dry, apply a matte finish styling paste or wax to your hair. Use your fingers to tousle and scrunch your hair, creating the messy look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Messy Hair

Now that you have good looking messy hair, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great:

  • Don't wash your hair every day. Messy hair looks best when it's a little dirty, so try to wash your hair every other day.
  • Use dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using a brush or comb, as this will disrupt the texture of your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Embrace the imperfections. Messy hair looks best when it's a little imperfect, so don't worry about getting it perfect.

FAQ

What if I have short hair?

Messy hair works great on short hair too! Just make sure to ask your stylist for a textured, layered cut.

What if my hair is naturally straight?

You can still achieve the messy look with straight hair. Just make sure to use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care for Messy Hair

Messy hair requires a little extra care to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for caring for your messy hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it look dull.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and texture.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your cut and keep your hair looking healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve good looking messy hair in no time. So go ahead, embrace the mess and enjoy your effortlessly cool new look!

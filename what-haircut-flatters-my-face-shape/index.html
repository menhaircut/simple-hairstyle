What Haircut Flatters My Face Shape

Written by Oliver Aug 10, 2023 · 3 min read

When it comes to choosing a haircut, it’s important to take your face shape into consideration. Not all haircuts look great on every face shape, so finding the right one can make a big difference in how you look and feel. In this article, we’ll explore what haircut flatters your face shape, and provide tips on how to choose the perfect haircut for you.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Face Shape
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Face Shape
  4. Tips for Choosing the Perfect Haircut
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Description

Before we dive into the specifics of what haircut flatters your face shape, let’s take a look at the different face shapes. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has its own unique characteristics, and certain haircuts will look better on some face shapes than others.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right haircut for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are a few different methods for doing this, but one of the easiest is to take a selfie and draw an outline of your face on the photo using a photo editing app. Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can start looking for haircuts that flatter your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Face Shape

Here’s a step-by-step guide to finding your face shape:

  1. Take a selfie with your hair pulled back and your face fully visible.
  2. Open the photo in a photo editing app.
  3. Using the drawing tool, draw an outline around your face.
  4. Compare your outline to the six face shapes (oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong) to determine which one best matches your features.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Haircut

Once you’ve determined your face shape, there are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut:

  • For oval faces, almost any haircut will look great.
  • For round faces, choose a haircut that adds height to the top of your head and keeps the sides short.
  • For square faces, choose a haircut with soft layers around the face that add movement.
  • For heart-shaped faces, choose a haircut with a side-swept bang and layers that frame the face.
  • For diamond-shaped faces, choose a haircut with fullness at the top and bottom, and keep the sides and forehead narrow.
  • For oblong faces, choose a haircut with volume at the sides and soft layers around the face.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about what haircut flatters your face shape:

  • What if my face shape doesn’t fit neatly into one category?
  • Can I still wear a certain haircut even if it’s not recommended for my face shape?
  • How often should I get my haircut?
  • Is there a certain hair length that’s best for each face shape?

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you’ve found the perfect haircut for your face shape, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these tips and choosing a haircut that flatters your face shape, you can look and feel your best every day.

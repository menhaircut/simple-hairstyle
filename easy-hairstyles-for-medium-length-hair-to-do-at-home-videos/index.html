Description

Are you looking for easy hairstyles for medium length hair to do at home videos? Look no further! In this article, we will show you step-by-step tutorials on how to create different hairstyles that are perfect for medium length hair. These tutorials are easy to follow and can be done in the comfort of your own home, without having to go to a salon. We will also provide tips on how to care for your hair, so that it remains healthy and strong.

How to

In order to create these hairstyles, you will need a few basic tools such as a comb, hair ties, bobby pins, and a hairbrush. You may also want to have some styling products, such as hairspray or mousse, on hand to help hold the style in place.

Before you begin, make sure that your hair is clean and dry. You may also want to use a heat protectant spray if you plan on using heat styling tools such as a curling iron or straightener.

Step by Step

1. Half-up Half-down Ponytail

Start by dividing your hair into two sections, with the top section being slightly larger than the bottom section. Tie the top section into a ponytail, leaving the bottom section loose. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to hide it. Secure it with a bobby pin.

2. Messy Bun

Start by pulling your hair into a high ponytail. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape. Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to tuck in any stray hairs. For a messy look, pull out a few strands of hair from the bun to frame your face.

3. Beachy Waves

Start by dividing your hair into small sections. Using a curling iron, curl each section away from your face. Once you have curled your entire head, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more relaxed, beachy look.

Tips

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.

- Apply a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

- Use bobby pins that match your hair color to help hide them.

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I create these hairstyles on short or long hair?

A: These specific tutorials are designed for medium length hair, but some of the techniques can be adapted for shorter or longer hair.

Q: Do I need to use styling products to create these hairstyles?

A: Styling products can help hold the style in place, but they are not always necessary. Feel free to experiment and see what works best for your hair.

Hair Care

In order to keep your hair healthy and strong, it is important to follow a few basic hair care tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.

- Get regular trims to help prevent split ends.

With these easy hairstyles for medium length hair to do at home videos, you can have fun experimenting with different styles and looks. Remember to take care of your hair, and it will look beautiful and healthy for years to come.

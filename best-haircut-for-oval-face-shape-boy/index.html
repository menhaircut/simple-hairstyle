Best Haircut For Oval Face Shape Boy How To Choose The Perfect Style

Written by Bardi Aug 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a boy with an oval-shaped face and struggling to find the perfect haircut that flatters your face shape? Look no further! We've got you covered with the best haircut options that complement your facial features. In this article, we will guide you through the process of selecting the perfect hairstyle for your oval-shaped face. From short to long haircuts, we have got you covered. Let's dive in!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we get into the best haircut options, it's important to determine your face shape. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick. Once you have done this, step back and analyze the shape you have traced. An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin and cheekbones that are high and prominent.

Best Haircuts for Oval Face Shape Boy

1. Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are a great option for oval face shape boys as they help to accentuate the facial features. The buzz cut, crew cut, and French crop are some of the best options for boys with an oval face shape. These haircuts are low maintenance and easy to style, making them a great choice for boys who are always on the go.

2. Medium Length Haircuts

Medium length haircuts are another great option for boys with an oval face shape. The side-swept fringe, textured crop, and messy quiff are some of the best options for boys with medium length hair. These haircuts help to balance out the facial features and add volume to the hair, creating a fuller look.

3. Long Haircuts

Long haircuts are a great option for boys with an oval face shape who want to experiment with different hairstyles. The man bun, slicked-back hair, and layered haircut are some of the best options for boys with long hair. These haircuts help to elongate the face and add a touch of sophistication to the overall look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Haircut

Now that you know the best haircut options for an oval face shape boy, let's walk you through the step-by-step process of choosing the right haircut.

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

The first step in choosing the right haircut is to determine your hair type. Do you have straight, curly, or wavy hair? This will help you determine which haircut will flatter your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Consider Your Lifestyle

It's important to consider your lifestyle when choosing a haircut. If you're always on the go and don't have much time to style your hair, a low maintenance haircut like a buzz cut or crew cut may be the best option for you.

Step 3: Consult with a Hairstylist

Consulting with a hairstylist is always a good idea when choosing a new haircut. They can offer advice on which haircut will flatter your face shape and hair type and help you achieve the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you have chosen the perfect haircut, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Invest in quality hair care products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to achieve different looks.

FAQs

What are the best haircuts for oval face shape boys?

The best haircuts for oval face shape boys are short haircuts like the buzz cut and crew cut, medium length haircuts like the side-swept fringe and textured crop, and long haircuts like the man bun and slicked-back hair.

How do I determine my face shape?

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick. Once you have done this, step back and analyze the shape you have traced.

How often should I visit my hairstylist?

You should visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh. A good rule of thumb is to visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks.

Conclusion

Choosing the perfect haircut for an oval face shape boy can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. By following the step-by-step guide we have provided in this article and considering your lifestyle, hair type, and face shape, you can find the perfect haircut that flatters your facial features and complements your overall look. Remember to maintain your haircut with regular visits to your hairstylist and quality hair care products, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styling products to achieve different looks. Good luck!

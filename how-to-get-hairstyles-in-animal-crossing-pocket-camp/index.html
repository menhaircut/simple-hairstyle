How To Get Hairstyles In Animal Crossing Pocket Camp

Written by Petter Jul 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're a fan of Animal Crossing Pocket Camp, you know that customizing your character is a big part of the game. One way to do that is by changing up your hairstyle. But with so many options, it can be tough to figure out how to get the hairstyle you want. In this guide, we'll show you how to get hairstyles in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Hairstyles
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Get Hairstyles

There are a few ways to get new hairstyles in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp. Here are your options:

  • Buy them from the salon
  • Get them from events
  • Get them from fortune cookies

Let's take a closer look at each option.

Buy Them from the Salon

The easiest way to get new hairstyles is to buy them from the salon. Just head over to the salon and talk to Harriet. She'll show you all the hairstyles that are available for purchase. The prices range from 1,800 to 3,200 bells.

Get Them from Events

From time to time, there are events in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp that offer new hairstyles as rewards. Keep an eye out for these events and participate to get new hairstyles.

Get Them from Fortune Cookies

Fortune cookies are a special type of item in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp that can be purchased with Leaf Tickets. Sometimes, fortune cookies will contain new hairstyles as prizes. Keep an eye out for these and try your luck!

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting new hairstyles in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp:

  1. Open the game and go to your campsite
  2. Head to the salon
  3. Talk to Harriet to see the available hairstyles
  4. Choose the hairstyle you want and purchase it
  5. Or, participate in events or purchase fortune cookies to get new hairstyles

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting new hairstyles in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp:

  • Make sure you have enough bells or Leaf Tickets to purchase the hairstyle you want
  • Participate in events to get new hairstyles for free
  • Keep an eye out for fortune cookies that offer new hairstyles as prizes
  • Don't be afraid to switch up your hairstyle and try something new!

FAQ

Can I change my hairstyle whenever I want?

Yes! Once you purchase a hairstyle, you can switch between it and any other hairstyles you've purchased whenever you want.

Can I get hairstyles for free?

Yes! Sometimes, events will offer new hairstyles as free rewards. Keep an eye out for these and participate to get new hairstyles without spending any bells or Leaf Tickets.

Hair Care

Once you've got your new hairstyle, you'll want to take care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny
  • Try out different styling products to find what works for you
  • Visit the salon regularly to keep your hairstyle looking fresh

With these tips, you'll be able to get and maintain the perfect hairstyle in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp. Happy styling!

How To Do Anime Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a fan of anime, chances are you've admired the unique and eye-catching hairstyles of your favorite characters. From spiky hair to flowing locks, anime hair is often a key part of a character's identity. But how can you achieve those gravity-defying styles in real life? In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to create an anime-inspired hairstyle of your own.

How to: Anime Hair

Step 1: Choose Your Character

The first step to creating an anime hairstyle is to choose the character you want to emulate. Look for a character with a hairstyle that matches your own hair texture and length. If you have short hair, a spiky style like Naruto's might be a good choice. If you have long hair, you could try a braided style like Sailor Moon's.

Step 2: Gather Your Materials

Next, you'll need to gather the materials you'll need to create your hairstyle. Depending on the style you choose, you may need hair gel, hairspray, hair ties, bobby pins, a curling iron, or a straightening iron. Be sure to have everything you need on hand before you start styling.

Step 3: Prep Your Hair

Before you start styling, be sure to wash and dry your hair. If you're going for a spiky style, you may want to apply some hair gel to your damp hair to help it hold its shape. If you're going for a sleek style, you may want to straighten your hair with a flat iron.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now it's time to start styling! Follow the steps below based on the type of hairstyle you're going for:

Spiky Style

  1. Apply hair gel to your damp hair.
  2. Use your fingers to create spikes in your hair. You may need to use a comb to help shape the spikes.
  3. Use hairspray to help your spikes hold their shape.

Braided Style

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Create two braids, one on either side of your head.
  3. Secure the ends of the braids with hair ties.

Sleek Style

  1. Straighten your hair with a flat iron.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair oil to your hair to add shine.
  3. Use hairspray to help your hair stay in place.

Step 5: Add Accessories

Finally, you can add accessories to complete your anime-inspired look. Depending on the character you're emulating, you may want to add a headband, hair clip, or other accessory.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Anime Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect anime hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Practice your styling technique before you go out in public.
  • Use bobby pins to help secure your hair in place.
  • Don't be afraid to trim your hair to achieve the perfect length for your desired style.

Anime Hair FAQ

Here are some common questions about anime hair:

What makes anime hair so unique?

Anime hair is often exaggerated to help create a distinctive look for each character. This can include spiky styles, unnatural colors, and gravity-defying shapes.

Can I dye my hair to match an anime character?

Yes, you can dye your hair to match an anime character. Just be sure to choose a dye that is safe for your hair and consult a professional if you're not experienced with hair dyeing.

How do I keep my anime hairstyle in place all day?

Use a strong-hold hairspray to help your hair stay in place. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Hair Care for Anime-Inspired Styles

It's important to take care of your hair when styling it in an anime-inspired way. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools.
  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any excess product buildup.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Conclusion

With the right tools and techniques, you can create an anime-inspired hairstyle that's sure to turn heads. Whether you're going for a spiky style or a braided look, be sure to experiment until you find the perfect look for you. And don't forget to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong!

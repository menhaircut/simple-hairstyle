How To Make Men s Hair Gel At Home

Written by William Jun 12, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking for a way to save money on hair products or want to experiment with making your own, then making your own hair gel might be worth considering. Not only is it cost-effective, but you can also customize the ingredients to suit your hair type and preferences. In this article, we'll be discussing how to make men's hair gel at home.

How to Make Men's Hair Gel

Here's a simple recipe for making your own hair gel:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon unflavored gelatin
  • 1 teaspoon aloe vera gel
  • 1-2 drops of essential oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the water until it boils.
  2. Add the unflavored gelatin to the boiling water and stir until it dissolves completely.
  3. Remove the saucepan from heat and add the aloe vera gel and essential oil (if using) to the mixture.
  4. Stir the mixture thoroughly and let it cool for a few minutes.
  5. Pour the mixture into a clean container with a lid and refrigerate it for about an hour or until it solidifies.
  6. Once the mixture has solidified, use your fingers to scoop out a small amount and apply it to your hair.

Store the hair gel in the refrigerator when not in use and discard it after two weeks.

Tips for Making Men's Hair Gel

Here are some tips to keep in mind when making your own hair gel:

  • Experiment with different essential oils to find a scent that you like.
  • You can adjust the amount of gelatin to make the hair gel thicker or thinner.
  • Use a small amount of hair gel at first and add more as needed.
  • Cleanse your hair thoroughly before applying the hair gel to ensure that it adheres properly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is homemade hair gel safe?

Yes, homemade hair gel is safe as long as you use clean and fresh ingredients. However, it's recommended to discard the hair gel after two weeks to prevent bacteria growth.

Can I use other ingredients to make men's hair gel?

Yes, you can experiment with different ingredients such as flaxseed, honey, or aloe vera juice. However, the recipe may vary depending on the ingredients used.

Hair Care for Men

Using hair gel can cause buildup and dryness, so it's important to cleanse your hair properly and moisturize it regularly. Here are some tips for taking care of men's hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair after shampooing to restore moisture and prevent tangles.
  • Avoid using too much hair gel as it can cause flaking and buildup.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood flow and promote hair growth.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

By following these tips and making your own hair gel, you can achieve a stylish and healthy-looking hairstyle without breaking the bank.

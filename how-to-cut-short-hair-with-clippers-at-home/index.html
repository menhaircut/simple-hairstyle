How To Cut Short Hair With Clippers At Home

Written by Eveline Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to save some money on haircuts and learn how to cut short hair with clippers at home? Look no further! In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut short hair with clippers at home. We will also include tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect haircut. Let's get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Before starting the haircut, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a set of clippers, a comb, and scissors for any additional trimming.

1. Start by washing and drying your hair.

2. Comb your hair in the direction you want it to fall. This will help you determine the length you want to cut.

3. Attach the desired guard to the clippers. The guard determines the length of the hair that will be cut. For shorter hair, use a smaller guard and for longer hair use a larger guard.

4. Begin cutting your hair from the back of your head, moving towards the front. Use the clippers in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth. Take small sections of hair at a time and use the comb to guide the clippers in the right direction.

5. Once you have cut the back of your head, move to the sides. Use the same technique as before and take small sections at a time.

6. Finally, move to the top of your head. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. You can also use scissors to trim any additional hair that the clippers may have missed.

7. After you have finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check for any uneven spots or missed areas. Make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair with Clippers

- Invest in a good quality set of clippers to ensure a smoother and more precise cut.

- Use the comb to guide the clippers and maintain an even length throughout the haircut.

- Start with a longer guard first and work your way down to a shorter guard if you want a shorter haircut.

- Take breaks in between cutting to rest your arms and avoid any accidental cuts.

- If you're unsure about the length you want, start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard.

FAQs

Q: How often should I cut my hair with clippers?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want it to be. As a general rule, it's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I use clippers on wet hair?

A: It's not recommended to use clippers on wet hair as it can cause damage to the blades and skin.

Q: Should I use scissors to trim any additional hair?

A: Yes, scissors can be used to trim any additional hair that the clippers may have missed. However, make sure to use them carefully to avoid any accidental cuts.

Hair Care Tips

- Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

- Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

- Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type.

- Get regular haircuts to maintain the health and shape of your hair.

Now that you have learned how to cut short hair with clippers at home, you can save money and time by doing it yourself. Remember to follow our step-by-step guide, use the right tools, and take care of your hair to achieve the perfect haircut every time.

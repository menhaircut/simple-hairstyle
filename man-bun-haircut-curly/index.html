Man Bun Haircut Curly A Guide To The Perfect Look

Written by Aprilia Oct 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that’s both trendy and practical? Look no further than the man bun haircut curly! This style is perfect for those with naturally curly hair who want to keep their locks out of their face while still looking stylish. In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about getting the perfect man bun haircut curly. From how to cut and style your hair to tips for hair care, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The man bun haircut curly is a modern take on the classic bun hairstyle. It involves tying your curly hair into a neat bun on the top of your head, leaving some curls loose around your face. This style is perfect for those with curly hair who want to keep their hair out of their face while still looking stylish.

How To

To get the perfect man bun haircut curly, you’ll need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Grow Your Hair Out

The first step to getting a man bun haircut curly is to grow your hair out. You’ll need at least 6 inches of hair to be able to tie it into a bun. If your hair is already long enough, you can skip this step.

Step 2: Cut Your Hair

Once your hair is long enough, it’s time to cut it. We recommend going to a professional stylist who has experience cutting curly hair. Ask for a layered cut that will help your curls look more defined.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

To style your man bun, start by brushing your hair back into a ponytail. Then, twist your hair into a bun and secure it with a hair tie. Leave some curls loose around your face for a relaxed look.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect man bun haircut curly:

  1. Grow your hair out to at least 6 inches.
  2. Go to a professional stylist for a layered cut.
  3. Brush your hair back into a ponytail.
  4. Twist your hair into a bun and secure it with a hair tie.
  5. Leave some curls loose around your face for a relaxed look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect man bun haircut curly:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Try different hair ties to find one that works best for your hair.
  • Use a hair oil to help define your curls.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a half-up man bun or a top knot.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the man bun haircut curly:

What type of hair is best for a man bun?

The man bun works best on those with thick, curly hair.

How long does my hair need to be for a man bun?

You’ll need at least 6 inches of hair to be able to tie it into a bun.

How do I keep my man bun in place?

Use a strong hair tie and bobby pins to keep your man bun in place.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as a straightener or curling iron, as they can damage your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock the perfect man bun haircut curly in no time. Whether you’re going for a casual or formal look, this style is sure to turn heads.

What Is The Best Flat Iron For Thick Curly Hair In 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly, thick, and curly hair every morning? Do you struggle to find the right flat iron that can tackle your hair type without causing damage? Look no further! In this blog post, we'll discuss the best flat iron for thick curly hair in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Flat Iron for Thick Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide on How to Use a Flat Iron on Thick Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Description

When it comes to flat irons, not all are created equal. For those of us with thick and curly hair, finding the right tool can be a challenge. The best flat iron for thick curly hair should have high heat settings, ceramic or titanium plates, and be able to glide through your hair without snagging or pulling.

How to Choose the Best Flat Iron for Thick Curly Hair

When shopping for a flat iron, keep these factors in mind:

  • Plate Material: Ceramic or titanium plates are the best for thick curly hair. Ceramic heats up evenly and reduces frizz, while titanium is more durable and can handle high heat settings.
  • Heat Settings: Look for a flat iron with adjustable heat settings, with a maximum temperature of at least 400°F.
  • Plate Size: Choose a flat iron with wider plates (1-2 inches) to cover more hair in one pass.
  • Brand Reputation: Look for flat iron brands with a good reputation for quality and durability.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Use a Flat Iron on Thick Curly Hair

Follow these steps to achieve sleek, straight hair without damage:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from high heat.
  2. Section your hair into small sections, using hair clips to keep them separated.
  3. Starting at the nape of your neck, take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron at the root.
  4. Gently glide the flat iron down the length of the hair, stopping about an inch from the ends.
  5. Repeat on each section, working your way up towards the crown of your head.
  6. Once you've straightened all sections, apply a hair serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are some tips to keep your thick curly hair healthy and shiny:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as it can strip natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about flat irons for thick curly hair:

  • Q: Can I use a flat iron on wet hair?
    A: No, never use a flat iron on wet hair. Wet hair is more prone to damage and breakage.
  • Q: How often should I replace my flat iron?
    A: It depends on how often you use it and how well you take care of it. Generally, a flat iron should last 3-5 years.
  • Q: Can I use a flat iron on synthetic hair extensions?
    A: No, using a flat iron on synthetic hair extensions can melt and damage the hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for thick curly hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, which can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, which can snag and break your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know what to look for in a flat iron for thick curly hair, you can confidently shop for the right tool to help you achieve sleek and shiny locks. Remember to always take care of your hair and avoid using too much heat to prevent damage.

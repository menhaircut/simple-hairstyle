Easy Low Maintenance Short Curly Hairstyles

Written by Petter Nov 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending hours styling your short curly hair every morning? Do you want a hairstyle that is easy to maintain and will still look fabulous? Look no further than these easy low maintenance short curly hairstyles!

Description

Short curly hair can be a challenge to maintain, but with the right hairstyle, you can save yourself time and effort while still looking great. These easy low maintenance hairstyles are perfect for women who want to spend less time styling their hair and more time enjoying life.

How To

There are many easy low maintenance short curly hairstyles to choose from. Some popular options include:

1. The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short and sassy hairstyle that is perfect for curly hair. To style, simply apply a small amount of mousse or gel to damp hair and scrunch with your hands. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to speed up the process.

2. The Bob

The bob is a classic hairstyle that is easy to maintain and looks great on curly hair. To style, apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to damp hair and scrunch with your hands. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser.

3. The Half Up, Half Down

The half up, half down hairstyle is a simple and easy way to style your short curly hair. Simply pull the top half of your hair back and secure with a clip or elastic band. Let the bottom half of your hair hang loose.

Step by Step

Here are step-by-step instructions for styling the bob:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to damp hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Scrunch

Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your curls to form.

Step 4: Dry

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to speed up the process.

Step 5: Finish

Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and tame any frizz.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your easy low maintenance short curly hairstyle:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to help define your curls.
  • Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to avoid frizz.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my short curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair prefer to wash their hair every day, while others prefer to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: What is the best way to dry curly hair?

A: The best way to dry curly hair is to let it air dry or use a diffuser when blow drying. Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz.

Q: How can I prevent my curly hair from getting tangled?

A: To prevent your curly hair from getting tangled, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet. Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly hair looking its best, it is important to take good care of it. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to help define your curls, and use a diffuser when blow drying to avoid frizz. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.

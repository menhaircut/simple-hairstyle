How To Do Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Daniel Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old hairstyle every day? Do you want to experiment with different looks but don't know where to start? If you have shoulder length hair, you're in luck! There are many hairstyles you can try that are easy to do and will give you a fresh new look. In this article, we'll show you how to do hairstyles for shoulder length hair.

Table of Contents:

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we get started, there are a few things you'll need:

  • Brush or comb
  • Hair ties or bobby pins
  • Hairspray or styling gel (optional)

Once you have these items, you're ready to start experimenting with different hairstyles.

Step by Step

1. Half-Up Half-Down

This is a simple yet stylish hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a section of hair from each side of your head and pull it back to the middle of your head.
  3. Tie the two sections together with a hair tie.
  4. Fluff up the hair on top of your head to give it some volume.
  5. Optional: Spray with hairspray or apply styling gel to keep the hair in place.

2. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a quick and easy hairstyle that looks great on shoulder length hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Pull your hair back into a ponytail using a hair tie.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to form a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Use your fingers to pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.
  5. Optional: Spray with hairspray to keep the hair in place.

3. Side Braid

A side braid is a great hairstyle to wear if you want to keep your hair out of your face. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Part your hair to one side.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and divide it into three parts.
  4. Begin braiding the hair by crossing the right section over the middle section, then the left section over the middle section.
  5. As you continue braiding, add more hair from the side of your head to the braid.
  6. Once you reach the nape of your neck, finish the braid and tie it off with a hair tie.
  7. Optional: Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.

Tips

  • Experiment with different hair accessories like headbands or hair clips to add some variety to your hairstyles.
  • Don't be afraid to try new things! If a hairstyle doesn't turn out the way you wanted it to, just try again.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you're using hot tools like a curling iron or straightener.
  • Try different hair products like mousse, pomade, or texturizing spray to add some extra volume or texture to your hair.

FAQ

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, you can still try these hairstyles! Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add some extra volume to your hair.

What if I have curly hair?

These hairstyles can work for curly hair too! Just make sure to use a curl defining product to keep your curls looking their best.

Can I wear these hairstyles to work?

Yes! These hairstyles are appropriate for most workplaces, but if you're unsure, it's always best to check with your employer.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping away natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like a curling iron or straightener too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

With these tips and hairstyles, you'll be able to rock your shoulder length hair with confidence!

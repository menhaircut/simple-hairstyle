What Size Curling Iron To Use For Beach Waves A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jun 11, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for an effortless and stylish hairstyle, beach waves are the perfect choice. This popular hairstyle can be achieved using a curling iron, but what size curling iron should you use? In this guide, we'll help you choose the right size curling iron for beach waves and share some tips and tricks to achieve the perfect look.

Description

Beach waves are a relaxed and tousled look that resembles the natural waves you get after a day at the beach. This hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, from a casual day out to a formal event. Beach waves can be created on any hair length, texture, and color, making it a versatile hairstyle for everyone.

How to

The first step to creating beach waves is to choose the right size curling iron. The size of the curling iron you choose will depend on the length of your hair and the size of the waves you want to create. For shorter hair, a smaller curling iron would work best, while longer hair would require a larger curling iron.

Once you've chosen the right size curling iron, follow these steps to achieve perfect beach waves:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron.
  2. Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on the thickness of your hair.
  3. Take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, starting from the mid-length of your hair. Leave the ends free.
  4. Hold the curling iron in place for 5-10 seconds, then release the hair from the curling iron.
  5. Repeat the process with the rest of your hair until you've created waves all over your head.
  6. Use your fingers to gently tousle the waves and create a relaxed look.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect beach waves:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron.
  • Don't wrap the hair too tightly around the curling iron to create a natural and relaxed look.
  • For a more casual look, leave the ends of your hair out of the curling iron.
  • For a more polished look, curl all of your hair in the same direction.
  • Use a hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

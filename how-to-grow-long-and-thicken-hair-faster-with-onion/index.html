How To Grow Long And Thicken Hair Faster With Onion

If you're looking for a natural and inexpensive way to grow long and thick hair, then onions might just be the solution you've been searching for. Onions contain high amounts of sulfur, which is essential for hair growth and can help strengthen hair strands. In this article, we will show you how to grow long and thicken hair faster with onion.

How to Use Onion for Hair Growth

Onion can be used in different ways to promote hair growth. Some of the most common ways include:

  • Onion Juice
  • Onion Oil
  • Onion Hair Rinse

Each of these methods can help to promote hair growth and improve the overall health of your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Onion Juice

To make onion juice, follow these steps:

  1. Peel and chop one large onion into small pieces.
  2. Blend the onion pieces in a blender or food processor.
  3. Strain the mixture to extract the juice using a cheesecloth or strainer.
  4. Apply the juice to your scalp and hair, massaging it gently for a few minutes.
  5. Leave the juice on your hair for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo and cool water.

2. Onion Oil

To make onion oil, follow these steps:

  1. Peel and chop one large onion into small pieces.
  2. Heat one cup of coconut oil in a pan and add the onion pieces.
  3. Cook the mixture on low heat for 15 to 20 minutes.
  4. Strain the mixture to remove the onion pieces.
  5. Apply the oil to your scalp and hair, massaging it gently for a few minutes.
  6. Leave the oil on your hair for at least 30 minutes.
  7. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo and cool water.

3. Onion Hair Rinse

To make an onion hair rinse, follow these steps:

  1. Peel and chop one large onion into small pieces.
  2. Boil the onion pieces in two cups of water for 15 to 20 minutes.
  3. Strain the mixture to remove the onion pieces.
  4. Allow the mixture to cool and use it as a final rinse after shampooing your hair.

Tips for Using Onion for Hair Growth

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using onion for hair growth:

  • Always use a mild shampoo to wash your hair after using onion juice or oil.
  • Do not leave onion juice or oil on your hair for too long, as it can cause irritation and dryness.
  • Avoid using onion juice or oil if you have a sensitive scalp or skin.
  • Test a small patch of your skin before using onion juice or oil to make sure you're not allergic.
  • Use onion juice or oil once a week to see the best results.

FAQs

1. How long does it take to see results?

You may start seeing results after using onion juice or oil for a few weeks. However, it may take several months to see significant hair growth.

2. Can onion juice or oil be used on all hair types?

Yes, onion juice or oil can be used on all hair types, including curly, straight, thick, and thin hair.

3. Can onion juice or oil be used on colored or chemically treated hair?

Yes, onion juice or oil can be used on colored or chemically treated hair. However, it's always best to do a patch test first to make sure you're not allergic.

Hair Care Tips for Long and Thick Hair

In addition to using onion juice or oil, here are some tips to help you grow long and thick hair:

  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, such as curling irons and straighteners.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes and relaxers.

By following these tips and using onion juice or oil, you can grow long and thick hair naturally and without spending a lot of money.

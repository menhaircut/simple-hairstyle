Which Ghd Curler Is Best For Short Hair

Written by William Oct 14, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to styling short hair, curling can be a bit of a challenge. Not all curling tools can create the desired look, and using the wrong one can lead to disastrous results. If you have short hair and are looking to create beautiful curls, you might be wondering which GHD curler is best for you. In this article, we’ll take a look at the different GHD curlers available and help you choose the one that’s perfect for your short hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

GHD offers several curling tools, including the Classic Curl Tong, Soft Curl Tong, Creative Curl Wand, and Curve Classic Wave Wand. Each of these tools has its own unique features and benefits, but not all of them are ideal for short hair. For short hair, you need a curling tool that can create tight, defined curls that last all day without damaging your hair.

How to

The best GHD curler for short hair is the Classic Curl Tong. This curling iron has a medium-sized barrel that is perfect for creating tighter curls on short hair. The barrel is ceramic-coated, which means that it heats up evenly and reduces the risk of damaging your hair. The Classic Curl Tong also has a cool tip that makes it easy to handle and reduces the risk of burning your fingers.

Step by step

Here’s how to use the GHD Classic Curl Tong to create beautiful curls on short hair:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from the heat of the curling iron.
  2. Section your hair and clip it up, leaving a small section of hair down.
  3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the barrel of the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds, then release.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3, working your way up to the top of your head.
  5. Once you’ve curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a more natural look.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your GHD Classic Curl Tong:

  • Start with clean, dry hair – this will help your curls last longer.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from the heat of the curling iron.
  • Don’t wrap too much hair around the barrel – this will make it harder to create defined curls.
  • Hold the curling iron vertically – this will help you create more defined curls.
  • Use your fingers to separate the curls for a more natural look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using a GHD curler on short hair:

  • Can I use a GHD curler on short hair?
  • Which GHD curler is best for short hair?
  • How do I curl short hair with a GHD curler?
  • How do I make my curls last longer?

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is important, especially when using heat styling tools like a GHD curler. Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron.
  • Don’t use a curling iron on wet hair – this can damage your hair.
  • Don’t leave the curling iron on one section of hair for too long – this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With the right curling tool and a little bit of practice, you can create beautiful, defined curls on your short hair. The GHD Classic Curl Tong is the best curler for short hair, but make sure to follow our tips to get the most out of your styling tools and keep your hair healthy.

Is A Bob Cut Good For Thick Hair

Written by Eveline Oct 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can be voluminous and give you a full head of hair, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Finding the right haircut for thick hair can be a challenge, but have you considered a bob cut?

Description

A bob cut is a short to medium-length haircut that is typically cut straight around the head at about jaw-level. It can be styled with or without bangs and can be layered or blunt. This style has been popular for decades and has been seen on celebrities like Victoria Beckham, Rihanna, and Katie Holmes.

How to

If you're considering getting a bob cut for your thick hair, here's how to achieve the look:

Step 1:

Choose the length of your bob cut. You can go for a short bob that hits above the chin or a longer bob that hits just above the shoulders.

Step 2:

Decide if you want bangs or not. If you do, choose the length and style of your bangs.

Step 3:

Find a stylist who has experience cutting thick hair. This is important because thick hair requires special techniques to avoid a bulky and unflattering look.

Step 4:

Show your stylist a picture of the bob cut you want. This will give them a clear idea of what you're looking for.

Step 5:

After your haircut, style your bob with a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and volume. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to enhance your hair's thickness.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a bob cut for thick hair:

  • Ask your stylist to thin out your hair if it's too bulky.
  • Consider adding layers to add movement and texture to your hair.
  • Choose a bob cut that complements your face shape.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.

FAQ

Q: Will a bob cut work for all types of thick hair?

A: Yes, a bob cut can work for all types of thick hair. However, it's important to choose the right length and style that complements your hair texture and face shape.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a bob cut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a bob cut. You can try a half-up, half-down style or a low ponytail.

Q: How often should I get a trim with a bob cut?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your bob cut.

Hair Care

To keep your bob cut looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a volumizing shampoo.
  • Condition your hair with a lightweight conditioner to avoid weighing it down.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can make your hair appear greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot styling tools.
  • Sleep with a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

A bob cut can be a great option for those with thick hair. It's a low-maintenance style that can be versatile and stylish. Just remember to choose the right length and style for your hair texture and face shape, and keep up with regular trims to maintain your look.

Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 40

Having a round face can make it difficult to find the perfect hairstyle. As we age, it becomes even more challenging to find a hairstyle that flatters our face shape and complements our features. However, there are plenty of short hairstyles that suit round faces over 40. In this article, we will discuss some of the best short hairstyles for round faces over 40, including how to style them and how to take care of them.

Description

Round faces have wide cheekbones and a rounded chin, which can make the face appear fuller. As we age, our face shape changes, and we may start to notice more sagging in the cheeks and jawline. The key to finding a flattering hairstyle is to choose a style that adds height and length to the face, creating the illusion of a slimmer, more youthful appearance. Short hairstyles are a great option for round faces over 40, as they can add volume and texture while still framing the face.

How To

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape: Before choosing a hairstyle, it's essential to determine your face shape. Take a measuring tape and measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your jawline is round, you have a round face shape.

2. Choose a short hairstyle: Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for short hairstyles that flatter your features. Some of the best short hairstyles for round faces over 40 include pixie cuts, bobs, and layered cuts.

3. Consult with a stylist: Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it's a good idea to consult with a stylist. They can help you choose a style that suits your face shape and hair texture.

4. Style your hair: Once you have your new haircut, it's essential to know how to style it. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and volume to your hair. Use a round brush to create volume, and finish with a styling product to hold the style in place.

Tips

- Choose a hairstyle that adds height and length to your face.

- Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear wider.

- Use volumizing products to add texture and volume to your hair.

- Consult with a stylist to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture.

FAQ

Q: What are the best short hairstyles for round faces over 40?

A: The best short hairstyles for round faces over 40 include pixie cuts, bobs, and layered cuts.

Q: How do I know if I have a round face shape?

A: Measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your jawline is round, you have a round face shape.

Hair Care

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools too often. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

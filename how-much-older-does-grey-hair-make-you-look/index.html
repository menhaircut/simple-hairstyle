Why Do We Get Grey Hair?

Grey hair occurs when the melanin pigment in our hair follicles decreases, causing the hair to lose its color. This is a natural part of aging, and most people start to see grey hairs in their 30s or 40s. However, some people may go grey earlier or later depending on genetics, ethnicity, and other factors.

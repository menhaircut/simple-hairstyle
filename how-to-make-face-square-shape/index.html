How To Make Your Face Square Shape Tips Steps And Haircare

Written by Oliver Sep 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you want to make your face look more square-shaped? Having a square face shape is one of the most sought-after facial features nowadays. It is because a square-shaped face is often associated with a strong and confident personality.

In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make your face square shape, tips, and hair care that will help you achieve the look you desire. Whether you have a round, oval, or heart-shaped face, these tips are applicable to all face shapes.

What is a Square Face Shape?

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a square-shaped chin. The width of the forehead and the jawline is almost the same, and the face has a symmetrical appearance. Celebrities with a square face shape include Angelina Jolie, Olivia Wilde, and Chris Hemsworth.

How to Make Your Face Square Shape?

There are several ways to make your face look more square-shaped. Here are some tips:

Hairstyle

The easiest way to make your face look more square-shaped is by choosing the right hairstyle. A short and layered haircut with bangs that fall on the forehead can help you achieve a more symmetrical look. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight because they can make your face look rounder.

Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades to create shadows and lighter shades to highlight specific areas of the face. To make your face look more square-shaped, use a contouring powder or cream to create shadows along the jawline and the temples. Blend it well to make it look natural.

Facial Exercises

You can also do facial exercises to make your face look more square-shaped. One exercise is to clench your jawline and hold it for a few seconds, then relax. Do this several times a day to strengthen your jaw muscles. Another exercise is to smile and press your fingertips on the corners of your mouth. Hold it for a few seconds, then release. This exercise helps in defining the cheekbones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Make Your Face Square Shape

Here is a step-by-step guide to making your face look more square-shaped:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If the measurements are almost the same, then you have a square face shape.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that will make your face look more square-shaped. A short and layered haircut with bangs that fall on the forehead can help you achieve a more symmetrical look.

Step 3: Contour Your Face

Use a contouring powder or cream to create shadows along the jawline and the temples. Blend it well to make it look natural.

Step 4: Do Facial Exercises

You can also do facial exercises to make your face look more square-shaped. These exercises help in defining the jawline and cheekbones.

Step 5: Maintain a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help in maintaining a square-shaped face. Avoid foods that are high in sugar and salt because they can cause water retention, which can make your face look puffy.

Tips for Making Your Face Square Shape

Here are some additional tips for making your face look more square-shaped:

  • Wear accessories that are square-shaped, such as sunglasses or earrings.
  • Choose clothing that has a square neckline.
  • Use a matte foundation to avoid making your face look shiny.
  • Apply blush on the apples of your cheeks to create a more defined cheekbone.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Can I make my face look more square-shaped without makeup?

A: Yes, you can make your face look more square-shaped without makeup. Choosing the right hairstyle and doing facial exercises can help in defining your jawline and cheekbones.

Q: How long does it take to make my face look more square-shaped?

A: It depends on your facial features and the techniques you are using. With the right hairstyle and makeup, you can achieve a more square-shaped face instantly. However, doing facial exercises and maintaining a healthy diet can take several weeks or months to see significant changes.

Q: Is it possible to change the shape of my face permanently?

A: No, it is not possible to change the shape of your face permanently. However, you can use makeup and hairstyles to create the illusion of a different face shape.

Hair Care for Square Face Shape

Here are some hair care tips for square face shape:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair because it can damage it.
  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.

Now that you know how to make your face square shape, you can experiment with different hairstyles and makeup techniques to achieve the look you desire. Remember to be patient and consistent with your facial exercises and hair care routine to see significant changes.

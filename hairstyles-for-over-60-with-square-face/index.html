Hairstyles For Over 60 With Square Face The Ultimate Guide

If you're over 60 with a square face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task. However, with the right guidance and inspiration, you can have a hairstyle that flatters your facial features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, straight cheekbones, and a broad forehead. The goal of a hairstyle for a square face is to soften these features and create balance. Hairstyles that work best for square faces are those that add height to the crown, have soft layers, and feature side-swept bangs.

How To

The first step in finding a hairstyle that works for you is to determine your face shape. To do this, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are roughly the same, you have a square face.

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to start looking for inspiration. Look for pictures of hairstyles on women with square faces and save them to show your hairstylist. Your hairstylist can help you determine which styles will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step By Step

1. Determine your face shape.

2. Look for inspiration.

3. Show your hairstylist the pictures you've saved.

4. Listen to your hairstylist's advice.

5. Choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Tips

- Soft layers are your friend. They help to frame your face and add movement to your hair.

- Side-swept bangs are a great way to soften your features and create balance.

- Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make your jawline appear even more prominent.

- Don't be afraid to experiment with color. Highlights or lowlights can add dimension to your hair and enhance your facial features.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair short if I have a square face?

A: Absolutely! Just make sure to choose a style that has soft layers and adds height to the crown.

Q: Should I avoid bangs?

A: Not necessarily. Side-swept bangs can be very flattering on square faces. Just make sure they're not too blunt or heavy.

Hair Care

The key to maintaining a beautiful hairstyle is to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type, and avoid over-styling or using too much heat. Regular trims are also important to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Now that you have a better idea of what hairstyles work best for square faces, it's time to start experimenting! Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

How To Get Short Beach Waves For Hair

Written by Luffy Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a relaxed and effortless hairstyle that's perfect for the beach, then short beach waves might just be the right choice for you. Short beach waves are a classic style that never seems to go out of fashion. In this article, we will show you how to get short beach waves for hair, step by step. We'll also share tips on how to care for your hair and answer some frequently asked questions about this style.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Get Short Beach Waves for Hair

Description

Short beach waves are a relaxed and tousled hairstyle that looks effortless and natural. This style is perfect for those who want to look chic and stylish without putting in too much effort. Short beach waves are also a great way to add volume and texture to your hair, giving it a more lively and youthful appearance.

How to Get Short Beach Waves for Hair

To get short beach waves for hair, you will need a few basic hair styling tools and products. Here's what you need:

  • Hair curler
  • Hair spray
  • Texturizing spray
  • Heat protectant spray

Step by Step

Here's how to get short beach waves for hair step by step:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add volume to your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to avoid heat damage from the curler.
  3. Divide your hair into several sections and start curling each section using a hair curler. Curl each section away from your face to get that beachy wave look.
  4. Once you've curled all sections, use your fingers to gently comb through your hair to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair to add volume and texture.
  6. Finish off with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect short beach waves:

  • Choose the right size curler for your hair. For short hair, a smaller barrel size is recommended.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly. Loose curls create a more natural and relaxed look.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair. This will help create that beachy wave look.
  • Don't forget to apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using a curler. This will protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Use your fingers to gently comb through your hair after curling to create a more natural look.

FAQ

How long does it take to get short beach waves for hair?

It usually takes around 30 minutes to get short beach waves for hair, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Can I get short beach waves for hair if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can still get short beach waves for hair if you have straight hair. You just need to use a curler to create the waves.

How long do short beach waves for hair last?

Short beach waves for hair can last up to 2-3 days, depending on how well you maintain them.

Hair Care

To keep your short beach waves looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. Let your hair air dry whenever possible.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily get short beach waves for hair that look effortless and chic. So, grab your curler and start experimenting with this classic hairstyle today!

What Do Guys Think Of Naturally Curly Hair

Written by Daniel Jun 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair is beautiful, and it's time to embrace it. For years, women with naturally curly hair have been straightening their locks to fit society's beauty standards. However, in recent years, more and more women have been embracing their curls, and men are taking notice. In this article, we'll explore what guys think of naturally curly hair and provide tips on how to care for your curly locks.

Description

Curly hair is often seen as wild and unmanageable, but that doesn't mean it's not attractive. Many men love the way curls look and feel, and they appreciate the confidence that comes with embracing your natural hair. Curly hair can be sexy, playful, and fun, and it's time to stop hiding it behind straighteners and blow dryers. So, what do guys think of naturally curly hair? Let's find out.

How to Embrace Your Curls

If you're used to straightening your hair, it can be hard to know where to start when it comes to embracing your curls. Here are a few tips to get you started:

  • Invest in a good stylist who knows how to cut curly hair. A good cut can make all the difference.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair. Not all curly hair is the same, and what works for one person may not work for you.
  • Try different styling techniques, such as diffusing or plopping, to enhance your curls.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair as much as possible. Heat can damage your curls and cause frizz.
  • Be patient. Embracing your curls is a journey, and it may take some time to find what works best for you.

Step by Step: Caring for Your Curls

Caring for curly hair requires a different approach than caring for straight hair. Here's a step-by-step guide to caring for your curls:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.
  2. Apply a conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while the conditioner is still in.
  3. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water to help seal the cuticle and prevent frizz.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair, scrunching it in with your hands.
  5. Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently scrunch excess water out of your hair.
  6. Diffuse your hair on a low heat setting, or air dry if you have time.
  7. Once your hair is dry, scrunch out any crunchiness from the styling products by flipping your head over and gently shaking it out.
  8. Finish with a spritz of hairspray or a light oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Rocking Your Curls

Now that you know how to care for your curls, here are a few tips for rocking them:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair. Curly hair can be versatile, so don't be afraid to try new things.
  • Accessorize with headbands, scarves, or hair clips to add some flair to your curls.
  • Don't be afraid to embrace the frizz. Frizz is a natural part of curly hair, and it can add texture and volume to your locks.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the elements by wearing a hat or scarf in extreme weather conditions.

FAQ

Q: Do guys really like curly hair?

A: Yes! Many guys find curly hair attractive and appreciate the confidence that comes with embracing your natural hair.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair if I want to?

A: Of course! It's your hair, and you can style it however you like. However, it's important to remember that heat styling can damage your curls, so it's best to use heat sparingly or not at all.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. Some people with curly hair can go several days between washes, while others may need to wash every other day. Experiment to find what works best for your hair.

Round Faces With Short Haircuts Tips And Styles

Written by Robby Sep 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what short haircuts would suit you best. Short haircuts can be a great way to highlight your features and draw attention to your face. In this article, we'll go over some tips and styles for round faces with short haircuts.

Description

Round faces have soft features and a circular shape. The width and length of the face are roughly the same, with a rounded jawline and cheekbones. Short haircuts can help to elongate the face and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, it's important to consider the length and shape of the cut. A longer pixie cut or a bob that falls just below the chin can help to elongate the face. Avoid haircuts that are too short or too blunt, as they can make the face appear wider.

Another important factor to consider is the texture of the hair. Adding layers or volume can help to create the illusion of a longer face. Soft waves or curls can also help to add dimension and movement to the hair.

Step by Step

1. Start by consulting with your hairstylist. They can help you choose a haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture.

2. Consider a longer pixie cut or a bob that falls just below the chin.

3. Add layers or volume to create the illusion of a longer face.

4. Consider adding soft waves or curls to add dimension and movement to the hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing a short haircut for a round face:

  • Avoid haircuts that are too short or too blunt.
  • Consider a side part or bangs to help elongate the face.
  • Experiment with different textures and styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have a short haircut if I have a round face?

A: Yes! Short haircuts can be a great option for round faces. Just be sure to choose a length and shape that flatters your face.

Q: What kind of bangs should I get for a round face?

A: Side-swept bangs can help to elongate the face. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make the face appear wider.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care for short haircuts, it's important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and shiny. Regular trims can also help to keep your hair looking its best.

With these tips and styles, you can find the perfect short haircut for your round face. Experiment with different lengths and textures to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to try something new!

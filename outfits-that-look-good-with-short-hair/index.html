Description

Short hair is a timeless hairstyle that has been popular for decades. It is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it a great option for those who want a low-maintenance look. However, finding outfits that complement short hair can be challenging. In this post, we will provide you with a guide on the best outfits that look good with short hair in 2023.

How To

The key to finding outfits that look good with short hair is to keep it simple. You don't want to wear anything too busy or complicated, as this can clash with your hairstyle. Instead, opt for clean lines and classic pieces that complement your hair. Here are some tips for choosing the right outfits:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing outfits that look good with short hair, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a square face, for example, you may want to wear clothing that softens your features, such as flowy dresses and tops. If you have a round face, on the other hand, you may want to wear clothing that elongates your body, such as high-waisted pants and skirts.

2. Highlight Your Neckline

Short hair puts your neckline on full display, so it's important to choose outfits that highlight this feature. Scoop necks, V-necks, and off-the-shoulder tops are all great options as they draw attention to your collarbone and neck.

3. Play with Proportions

Short hair can be very playful and fun, so don't be afraid to play around with proportions when choosing your outfits. Try pairing a midi skirt with a cropped top or wearing oversized blazers with slim-fit pants.

Step By Step

Now that you know what to look for when choosing outfits that look good with short hair, here is a step-by-step guide on how to put together the perfect outfit:

Step 1: Choose a Focal Point

Start by choosing one focal point for your outfit. This could be a statement piece of clothing, like a bold blazer or a pair of patterned pants, or it could be a statement accessory, like a chunky necklace or a pair of statement earrings.

Step 2: Build Around Your Focal Point

Once you have your focal point, start building your outfit around it. Choose clothing pieces that complement your focal point and keep it simple. You don't want to wear anything too busy or complicated that will clash with your short hair.

Step 3: Add Accessories

Accessories are a great way to add some interest to your outfit. Choose accessories that complement your focal point and your short hair. For example, if you are wearing a bold blazer, you could add a simple necklace and a pair of stud earrings.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing outfits that look good with short hair:

  • Stick to neutral colors, such as black, white, and gray. These colors will complement your short hair and provide a classic look.
  • Avoid wearing clothing that is too baggy or loose, as this can make you look shapeless.
  • Invest in a few key pieces, such as a blazer, a pair of high-waisted pants, and a little black dress. These pieces can be mixed and matched to create a variety of different outfits.

FAQ

What should I avoid wearing with short hair?

Avoid wearing clothing that is too busy or complicated, as this can clash with your short hair. Additionally, avoid wearing clothing that is too baggy or loose, as this can make you look shapeless.

Can I wear patterned clothing with short hair?

Yes, you can wear patterned clothing with short hair. However, it's important to choose patterns that complement your hair and don't clash with it.

What accessories should I wear with short hair?

Accessories are a great way to add some interest to your outfit. Choose accessories that complement your short hair, such as simple necklaces, stud earrings, and delicate bracelets.

Hair Care

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and using our guide to choosing outfits that look good with short hair, you can rock this timeless hairstyle with confidence.

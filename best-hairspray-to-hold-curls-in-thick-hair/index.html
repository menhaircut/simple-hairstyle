Best Hairspray To Hold Curls In Thick Hair

Written by Robby Aug 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have thick hair and struggle to keep curls in place? We understand your pain. It can be frustrating to spend hours curling your hair, only to have it fall flat within minutes. That's why we've put together a list of the best hairsprays to hold curls in thick hair. Read on to find out more!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Hold Curls in Thick Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Holding Curls in Thick Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thick Hair

Description

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it's beautiful and voluminous, it can also be difficult to style. When it comes to holding curls in thick hair, you need a hairspray that's strong enough to keep them in place, but not so strong that it weighs your hair down or makes it look stiff.

The best hairsprays for holding curls in thick hair are ones that provide a strong hold without leaving any residue or causing frizz. They should also be lightweight and easy to brush out when you're ready to take your curls down.

How to Hold Curls in Thick Hair

Holding curls in thick hair can be challenging, but it's not impossible. Here are some tips to help you keep your curls in place:

  • Use a curling iron with a small barrel to create tight curls.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before curling it.
  • Wrap each curl around your finger and hold it in place for a few seconds before letting it go.
  • Allow your curls to cool completely before touching them.
  • Apply hairspray to each curl as you go to help them hold their shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you hold curls in thick hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections.
  4. Use a curling iron with a small barrel to curl each section of hair.
  5. Wrap each curl around your finger and hold it in place for a few seconds before letting it go.
  6. Apply hairspray to each curl as you go.
  7. Allow your curls to cool completely.
  8. Once your curls are cool, gently run your fingers through them to loosen them up.
  9. Apply a final coat of hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Holding Curls in Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you hold curls in thick hair:

  • Use a high-quality curling iron with a small barrel.
  • Apply hairspray to each curl as you go to help them hold their shape.
  • Don't touch your curls until they have completely cooled.
  • Use a lightweight hairspray that won't weigh your hair down.
  • Brush your hair out gently before bed to prevent tangles.

FAQs

Q: What is the best hairspray to hold curls in thick hair?

A: The best hairspray to hold curls in thick hair is one that provides a strong hold without leaving any residue or causing frizz. Some of the best options include L'Oreal Paris Elnett Satin Hairspray, Kenra Volume Spray, and TRESemmé Compressed Micro Mist Hairspray.

Q: How do I prevent my curls from falling out in thick hair?

A: To prevent your curls from falling out in thick hair, use a high-quality curling iron with a small barrel, apply a heat protectant spray before curling, and use a lightweight hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

When it comes to caring for thick hair, it's important to use products that nourish and strengthen your strands. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

With these tips and the right hairspray, you can hold curls in thick hair all day long. Try out some of our recommendations and see which one works best for you!

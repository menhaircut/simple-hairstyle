Male Fade Haircut Tutorial Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 06, 2023 ¬∑ 3 min read

Are you looking for a fresh new look? The male fade haircut may be just what you need. This style has been popular for years and is still going strong. We've put together a tutorial to help you achieve the perfect fade. Read on to learn how to get the look you want.

Description

The male fade haircut is a style that involves gradually cutting the hair shorter as it moves towards the bottom of the head. This creates a tapered effect, with the hair longest on top and shortest on the sides and back. The result is a clean, sharp look that can be styled in many different ways.

How to

Before you start cutting, you'll need some tools. You'll need clippers, guards, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Once you have these, you're ready to get started.

Step 1: Start with the sides

Use a guard to trim the sides of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard. This will create the fade effect. Make sure to blend the hair well as you move from one guard to the next.

Step 2: Move to the back

Next, move to the back of your head. Repeat the same process as you did on the sides. Use a longer guard at first and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard. Blend the hair well as you go.

Step 3: Trim the top

Finally, it's time to trim the top of your head. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. You can also use clippers with a guard if you prefer a shorter length.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect fade:

  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to create a gradual fade.
  • Make sure to blend the hair well as you move from one guard to the next.
  • Use scissors to trim the top of your head to the desired length.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the male fade haircut:

How long does a fade last?

A fade can last anywhere from a few days to a few weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

How often should I get a fade?

It's recommended to get a fade every 2-4 weeks to maintain the look.

Can I style a fade in different ways?

Yes, a fade can be styled in many different ways depending on the length of the hair on top.

Hair Care

To keep your fade looking sharp, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your fade.
  • Use a styling product to keep your hair in place.

With this tutorial, you're ready to achieve the perfect male fade haircut. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll have a stylish new look in no time.

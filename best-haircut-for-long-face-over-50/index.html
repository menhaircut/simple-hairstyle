Best Haircut For Long Face Over 50 How To Choose And Style

Written by Daniel Nov 07, 2023 · 4 min read

As we age, our face shape changes, and so should our haircut. Women over 50 with long faces often find it challenging to find a style that flatters their features. But fear not, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll share the best haircut for long face over 50, how to choose it, and how to style it. Read on to discover the perfect cut for you.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Long Face Over 50

Before choosing a haircut, it’s important to consider your face shape. Women with long faces have a lengthier forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. The goal is to find a style that narrows the face and adds volume to the sides. Here are some tips:

  • Opt for a haircut that adds width to the sides, like layers or waves.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume on top or at the crown, as they will elongate your face further.
  • Consider a side-swept or asymmetrical bang to shorten the forehead.
  • Choose a haircut that frames your face, like a bob or a shag.

Best Haircuts for Long Face Over 50

Now that you know what to look for, it’s time to choose the perfect haircut. These are some of the best haircuts for long face over 50:

Long Bob

A long bob, or lob, is a versatile haircut that flatters most face shapes, including long faces. It frames the face and adds volume to the sides, making your face appear wider. You can style it straight or wavy, and add layers for more texture.

Shag

A shag haircut is perfect for women over 50 who want a low-maintenance, stylish look. The layers add volume and texture to the sides, creating a more balanced face shape. You can style it messy or sleek, depending on your mood.

Side-Swept Bangs

A side-swept bang is a great way to shorten a long forehead and add volume to the sides. It’s also a low-commitment way to try a new look without cutting your hair too short. You can pair it with any of the haircuts above for a more flattering look.

How to Style Your Haircut

Once you’ve chosen your haircut, it’s time to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the sides.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create waves or curls.
  • Use a flat iron to create sleek and straight hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to find your most flattering look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

To maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to avoid split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or a UV protection spray.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for long face over 50:

What is the most flattering haircut for a long face?

The most flattering haircut for a long face is one that adds width to the sides, like a long bob or a shag.

Should I get bangs if I have a long face?

If you have a long forehead, you can consider getting side-swept or asymmetrical bangs to shorten it. However, if you have a long face, avoid getting blunt bangs, as they will elongate your face further.

Can I wear my hair long if I have a long face?

Yes, you can wear your hair long if you have a long face, as long as you add volume to the sides with layers or waves.

Hair Care Tips for Long Face Over 50

Here are some hair care tips for women over 50 with long faces:

  • Choose a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair and scalp.

In conclusion, the best haircut for long face over 50 is one that adds width to the sides and frames your face. Choose a cut that flatters your features and complements your style. With the right haircut and styling, you can look and feel your best at any age.

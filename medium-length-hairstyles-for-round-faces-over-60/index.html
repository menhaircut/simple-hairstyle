Medium Length Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 60

Written by Robby Dec 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're over 60 and have a round face, you may be wondering what hairstyles would be best for you. Medium length hairstyles are a great choice as they are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. In this article, we'll go over some of the best medium length hairstyles for round faces over 60, as well as tips and tricks for taking care of your hair.

Description

As we age, our hair tends to thin and lose its natural volume. This can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that flatters our face shape. Round faces, in particular, can be a challenge as they have a softer, fuller appearance. However, medium length hairstyles can help balance out the face and create a more youthful look.

How to

If you're considering a medium length hairstyle, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure the length falls somewhere between your chin and shoulders. This will help elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer jawline. Next, consider adding layers to your hair. Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, which can help create a more flattering look.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when styling your medium length hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair, then apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair.
  4. If you want to add curls or waves, use a curling iron or wand to create loose, beachy waves.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for taking care of your medium length hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and strengthen your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hairstyles for round faces over 60:

  1. What is the best length for medium length hair?

    2. The best length for medium length hair is between your chin and shoulders.

  2. What types of layers are best for round faces?

    3. Long layers or choppy layers can help create the illusion of a slimmer face.

  3. What are some easy hairstyles for medium length hair?

    4. Some easy hairstyles for medium length hair include a messy bun, a low ponytail, or loose waves.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is essential to maintaining a healthy and youthful appearance. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help nourish and protect your hair.
  • Limit your use of hair products, as too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, medium length hairstyles are a great choice for round faces over 60. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can create a flattering and youthful look that will make you feel confident and beautiful.

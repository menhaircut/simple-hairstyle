How To Do A 60 S Beehive Hairstyle

Written by Luffy Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

The 60's beehive hairstyle is a classic look that has stood the test of time. It's a great retro look that can be worn for special occasions or even for everyday wear. If you're looking to recreate this iconic hairstyle, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll go through step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect 60's beehive hairstyle.

Description

The 60's beehive hairstyle is characterized by its voluminous bouffant style that's perched on top of the head. It's a high-maintenance hairstyle that requires some skill and patience to achieve. It's a popular choice for events like weddings and proms, but it can also be worn casually with the right outfit.

How To

Here are the steps to follow in order to create the perfect 60's beehive hairstyle:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it until it's completely dry. Apply some volumizing mousse to your roots to give your hair some lift. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and create a smooth base.

Step 2: Tease Your Hair

Next, take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tease it using a fine-tooth comb. Tease the hair in small sections, working your way around the crown of your head. The more you tease your hair, the higher your beehive will be.

Step 3: Create the Beehive

Gather the teased hair into a bouffant shape and secure it with bobby pins. Smooth the top layer of hair over the bouffant and secure it with more bobby pins. Use a hairspray to keep the beehive in place.

Step 4: Style the Rest of Your Hair

Style the rest of your hair by curling it with a curling iron. You can either curl it all the way to the ends or leave the ends straight for a more modern look. Brush out the curls to create soft waves.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off the look with a final spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place. Use a light hold hairspray if you want a more natural look, or a strong hold hairspray if you want your beehive to stay put all night.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating your 60's beehive hairstyle:

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the 60's beehive hairstyle:

What hair length is best for a beehive?

The 60's beehive hairstyle works best on medium to long hair. If you have short hair, you can still create a mini beehive by teasing the hair at the crown of your head and securing it with bobby pins.

What type of hair works best for a beehive?

The 60's beehive hairstyle works best on hair that's thick and full. If you have thin hair, you can still create a beehive by using hair extensions or a hair donut to give your hair more volume.

How do I take care of my beehive?

To maintain your beehive, use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh. Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can flatten your beehive. Instead, use your fingers to gently smooth out any tangles.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to create the perfect 60's beehive hairstyle, it's time to give it a try. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to rock this iconic look like a pro.

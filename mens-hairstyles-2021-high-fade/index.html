Mens Hairstyles 2021 High Fade A Guide To Rocking The Trendy Look

Written by Robby Jun 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Mens hairstyles 2021 high fade is one of the most popular trends in the world of men's grooming. This hairstyle is characterized by a gradual fade from the sides to the top, leaving the hair longer at the crown. The high fade is versatile, stylish, and easy to maintain. In this article, we will provide a comprehensive guide on how to achieve and maintain the perfect high fade.

Description

The high fade is a modern take on the classic short back and sides haircut. It involves trimming the hair on the sides and back of the head gradually shorter towards the top, creating a fade effect. The hair on the top is left longer, allowing for more styling options. The high fade is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, textured style.

How to Achieve a High Fade

If you're interested in getting a high fade, here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the look:

Step 1: Choose the Right Barber

When it comes to getting a high fade, it's important to choose a skilled barber who has experience with this particular hairstyle. Look for a barber who specializes in men's grooming and has a portfolio of high fade haircuts.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Before your barber starts cutting, you'll need to decide on the length of your fade. A high fade typically starts just above the ear and gradually tapers upwards towards the crown. You can choose to have a skin fade, where the hair is shaved down to the skin, or a fade with some length left on the sides.

Step 3: Trim the Top

Once your barber has trimmed the sides and back of your head, it's time to focus on the top. The length of the hair on top will depend on your personal preference and styling needs. You can choose to leave the hair longer for a more textured look or keep it shorter for a more polished style.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

With the high fade, you can style your hair in many different ways. Some popular styling options include a slicked-back look, a messy, textured style, or a classic side part. Experiment with different products and techniques to find a style that works for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your High Fade

Now that you have a high fade, it's important to maintain the look. Here are some tips for keeping your high fade looking fresh:

  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Invest in a good styling product to keep your hair in place
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens hairstyles 2021 high fade:

Is the high fade suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the high fade can work with all hair types, from straight to curly to wavy.

How often should I visit the barber for touch-ups?

This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your fade starts to grow out. Most people will need to visit the barber every 2-3 weeks for touch-ups.

What styling products are best for a high fade?

This will depend on your hair type and the style you're going for. Some popular products for styling a high fade include pomade, wax, and gel.

Hair Care for Mens Hairstyles 2021 High Fade

Keeping your hair healthy is key to maintaining a high fade. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as this can weaken it
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth

Mens hairstyles 2021 high fade is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can work for any man. With the right barber, products, and care, you can maintain a high fade that looks fresh and stylish. Experiment with different styles and products to find the perfect look for you.

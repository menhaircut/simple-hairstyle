What Hairstyles Suit Heart Shaped Faces A Complete Guide

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles and never finding the perfect one? Well, if you have a heart-shaped face, you have come to the right place. In this guide, we will tell you everything you need to know about what hairstyles suit heart-shaped faces.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Hairstyle

Description

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead and a narrow, pointed chin. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile, as it can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, not all hairstyles will suit this face shape.

How to Determine if You Have a Heart-Shaped Face

To determine if you have a heart-shaped face, you need to follow these steps:

  1. Look in the mirror and examine the shape of your face.
  2. Measure the width of your forehead and the width of your jawline.
  3. If your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your chin is pointed, you have a heart-shaped face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Hairstyle

Follow these steps to find the perfect hairstyle for your heart-shaped face:

  1. Determine the length of your hair. Heart-shaped faces look good with any length of hair, but some styles may be more flattering than others.
  2. Consider your hair texture.
  3. Look for hairstyles that add width at the jawline.
  4. Avoid hairstyles that add height at the crown.
  5. Look for hairstyles that add volume at the chin.
  6. Consider getting bangs.
  7. Add layers to your hair.
  8. Consider getting a bob or a lob.
  9. Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or too severe.
  10. Experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips for styling your hair if you have a heart-shaped face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a round brush to add volume to your hair.
  • Use a curling iron to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly from the face.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories like headbands and hair clips.

FAQ

Q: Can heart-shaped faces pull off short hair?

A: Yes, heart-shaped faces can pull off short hair. However, you should avoid styles that are too severe or too slicked back.

Q: Can heart-shaped faces wear their hair straight?

A: Yes, heart-shaped faces can wear their hair straight. However, you should avoid hairstyles that add height at the crown.

Q: Can heart-shaped faces wear their hair in a ponytail?

A: Yes, heart-shaped faces can wear their hair in a ponytail. However, you should avoid styles that pull your hair back tightly from the face.

Hair Care for Heart-Shaped Faces

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Limit the use of heat styling tools to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

With these tips, you will be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your heart-shaped face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

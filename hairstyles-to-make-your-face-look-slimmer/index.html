Hairstyles To Make Your Face Look Slimmer

Written by William Nov 22, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to slim down your face without going under the knife, changing your hairstyle can make a big difference. With the right cut and style, you can create the illusion of a slimmer face. In this article, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles to make your face look slimmer and share tips on how to achieve them.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Face Look Slimmer with Hairstyles
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Slimming Hairstyles
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Healthy, Slimmer-Looking Hair

How to Make Your Face Look Slimmer with Hairstyles

The key to making your face look slimmer with a hairstyle is to create balance and proportion. Choosing the right cut and style can help elongate your face, draw attention away from problem areas, and create a more flattering shape.

One of the most effective ways to make your face look slimmer with a hairstyle is to add volume to the top of your head. This can be achieved with a layered cut or by styling your hair with a volumizing mousse or spray. Another option is to create a side part, which can help create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face.

Another great way to slim down your face with a hairstyle is to add length to your hair. Long, straight hair can help elongate your face and draw attention away from the cheeks and jawline. However, if you prefer short hair, a pixie cut or bob can also be slimming if styled correctly.

The Best Hairstyles to Make Your Face Look Slimmer

Here are some of the best hairstyles to make your face look slimmer:

  • Long, straight hair with a side part
  • Layered cut with volume at the crown
  • Pixie cut or bob with volume at the crown
  • Loose waves or curls with a side part
  • Top knot or high ponytail

Step-by-Step Guide to Slimming Hairstyles

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving some of the best hairstyles to make your face look slimmer:

Long, Straight Hair with a Side Part

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush to add volume at the crown.
  2. Create a side part using a comb.
  3. Straighten your hair with a flat iron.
  4. Finish with a texturizing spray to add definition and hold.

Layered Cut with Volume at the Crown

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush to add volume at the crown.
  2. Ask your stylist for a layered cut that frames your face.
  3. Style with a volumizing mousse or spray.
  4. Finish with a flexible hold hairspray.

Pixie Cut or Bob with Volume at the Crown

  1. Ask your stylist for a pixie cut or bob with layers that frame your face.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume at the crown.
  3. Style with a volumizing mousse or spray.
  4. Finish with a flexible hold hairspray.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips for choosing the right hairstyle to make your face look slimmer:

  • Consider your face shape – if you have a round face, choose a style that creates angles and lengthens your face.
  • Ask your stylist for advice – they can help you choose a style that flatters your features.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles – don't be afraid to try something new.
  • Use accessories like headbands, clips, and scarves to add interest and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

FAQs

What hairstyles make your face look slimmer?

Hairstyles that add volume at the crown or create length can make your face look slimmer. Some of the best styles include long, straight hair with a side part, a layered cut with volume at the crown, and a pixie cut or bob with volume at the crown.

What face shape is best for a pixie cut?

A pixie cut can be flattering for many face shapes, but it works best for those with oval or heart-shaped faces. If you have a round face, a longer pixie cut with layers can help create a more elongated look.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy, Slimmer-Looking Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat with a conditioning spray or serum.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

By choosing the right hairstyle and taking care of your hair, you can create the illusion of a slimmer face and feel confident and beautiful.

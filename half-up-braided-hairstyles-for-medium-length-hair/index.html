Half Up Braided Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have medium length hair, you know how versatile it can be. You can wear it up, down, or somewhere in between. One of the most popular hairstyles for medium length hair is the half up braided style. This look is perfect for any occasion, from a casual day out to a formal event. In this guide, we will show you how to create the perfect half up braided hairstyle for medium length hair, and give you tips and tricks for maintaining it.

Description

The half up braided hairstyle is a popular look that combines two different styles: a half updo and a braid. It is a versatile style that can be worn loose or tight, and can be dressed up or down. This style is perfect for medium length hair, as it allows you to show off your length while still keeping your hair out of your face.

How to

Creating a half up braided hairstyle for medium length hair is easy. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Take a section of hair from the top of your head, and divide it into three equal sections.
  3. Begin braiding the section of hair, adding in small sections of hair from the sides as you go.
  4. When you reach the nape of your neck, tie the braid off with a small hair tie.
  5. Take the remaining hair and pull it up into a half updo, securing it with bobby pins.
  6. If desired, add accessories such as flowers or clips to your hair to complete the look.

Step by step

Here is a step by step guide to creating the half up braided hairstyle:

Step 1: Brush your hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to braid your hair and create a smooth finish.

Step 2: Divide your hair

Take a section of hair from the top of your head, and divide it into three equal sections. This will be the start of your braid.

Step 3: Start braiding

Begin braiding the section of hair, adding in small sections of hair from the sides as you go. Keep braiding until you reach the nape of your neck.

Step 4: Tie off the braid

When you reach the nape of your neck, tie the braid off with a small hair tie. This will keep the braid in place and prevent it from unraveling.

Step 5: Create the half updo

Take the remaining hair and pull it up into a half updo, securing it with bobby pins. This will create the half up portion of the hairstyle.

Step 6: Add accessories

If desired, add accessories such as flowers or clips to your hair to complete the look. This will add a touch of personality and style to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect half up braided hairstyle for medium length hair:

  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair before braiding it.
  • Experiment with different types of braids, such as fishtail or Dutch braids, to add variety to your hairstyle.
  • If you have thick hair, use small hair ties or elastic bands to secure your braid, as larger bands may not hold your hair in place.
  • Use bobby pins that match the color of your hair to create a seamless finish.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the half up braided hairstyle for medium length hair:

Can I create this hairstyle on short hair?

This style is best suited for medium length hair, as shorter hair may not have enough length to create the half up portion of the hairstyle.

How long will this hairstyle last?

This style can last for several hours, or even a full day if properly secured with bobby pins and hair ties.

Can I create this hairstyle on curly hair?

Yes, this hairstyle can be created on curly hair. Simply brush your curls out first, then follow the steps above to create the braid and half updo.

Hair Care

Keep your hair healthy and strong by following these tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to give your hair an extra boost of moisture and nutrients.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and strong, and create the perfect half up braided hairstyle for medium length hair.

What Does A Long Layered Haircut Look Like A Complete Guide

Written by William Jun 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you considering getting a long layered haircut but not sure what it will look like? Look no further! In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about long layered haircuts, including what they look like, how to get them, and how to take care of them.

Description

A long layered haircut is a popular hairstyle that features longer hair with shorter layers throughout. This style is perfect for those who want to add volume, texture, and movement to their hair. Long layered haircuts can be customized to fit any face shape, hair type, and personal style.

How to Get a Long Layered Haircut

To get a long layered haircut, you should start by finding a reputable hairstylist who has experience cutting long hair. Be sure to bring in pictures of the style you want and communicate your desired length and layers. Your hairstylist will use a combination of cutting techniques, including point cutting, slicing, and texturizing, to create your desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a long layered haircut:

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair
  2. Part your hair down the middle
  3. Section your hair into four parts
  4. Tie up the top two sections of your hair with hair ties
  5. Start cutting the bottom sections of your hair, creating layers at the ends of each section
  6. Untie the top sections of your hair and repeat the previous step, creating layers throughout the top sections
  7. Blend the layers together by point cutting and texturizing
  8. Style your hair as desired

Tips

Here are some tips for getting and maintaining a long layered haircut:

  • Bring in pictures of the style you want to ensure you and your hairstylist are on the same page
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your haircut
  • Use hair products, such as texturizing spray and volumizing mousse, to enhance the texture and volume of your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long layered haircuts:

Q: What face shape is best for a long layered haircut?

A: Long layered haircuts can be customized to fit any face shape, but they tend to look best on oval or heart-shaped faces.

Q: Will a long layered haircut work for thin hair?

A: Yes, a long layered haircut can add volume and texture to thin hair.

Q: How often should I get a trim for my long layered haircut?

A: It is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.

Hair Care

To take care of your long layered haircut, be sure to:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair

And there you have it, everything you need to know about long layered haircuts! Whether you're looking to add volume, texture, or movement to your hair, a long layered haircut is a great option for any hair type, face shape, and personal style.

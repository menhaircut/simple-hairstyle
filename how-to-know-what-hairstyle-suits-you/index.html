How To Know What Hairstyle Suits You A Guide For 2023

Written by Bardi Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of your current hairstyle or want to try something new, it can be challenging to know where to start. With so many options available, how do you know what hairstyle will suit you best? In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to finding a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. This will help you narrow down which styles will be the most flattering for you. Some common face shapes include round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a photo of yourself facing forward. Then, compare your face to the following descriptions:

  • Round: Full cheeks and a rounded jawline
  • Oval: Balanced proportions with a slightly curved jawline
  • Square: Angular jawline and forehead
  • Heart: Wider forehead with a pointed chin
  • Diamond: Narrow forehead and jawline with wide cheekbones

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start to look for hairstyles that will complement your features.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

The next step is to consider your hair type. Some common hair types include straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Knowing your hair type will help you choose a style that works with your natural texture. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to avoid a style that requires a lot of heat styling, as this can damage your hair over time.

Step 3: Evaluate Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hairstyle, it's essential to consider your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, you may want a low-maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of styling time. On the other hand, if you have more time to devote to your hair, you may want to choose a more intricate style.

Step 4: Browse Hairstyles

Once you've determined your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can start to browse hairstyles. Look for styles that complement your face shape and work with your natural hair texture. You can find inspiration from magazines, websites, and social media. Consider creating a Pinterest board or a folder on your computer to save styles that you like.

Step 5: Consult with a Stylist

Finally, it's always a good idea to consult with a stylist before making a drastic change to your hair. A professional stylist can help you determine which styles will work best for your face shape and hair type. They can also offer advice on how to maintain your new style and recommend products to keep your hair healthy.

Tips for Finding the Right Hairstyle

  • Don't be afraid to try something new
  • Consider your personal style when choosing a new hairstyle
  • Bring photos of styles you like to your stylist
  • Be realistic about how much time you're willing to spend on your hair each day
  • Remember that your hair will grow back if you don't love your new style

FAQs

How often should I get my hair cut?

This will depend on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, most people will need a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep their hair looking healthy.

Can I dye my hair if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have curly hair. However, it's essential to choose a dye that is specifically formulated for curly hair, as this will help to prevent damage and dryness.

How can I make my hair grow faster?

There is no surefire way to make your hair grow faster, but you can promote healthy hair growth by eating a balanced diet, taking a daily multivitamin, and using gentle hair care products.

Hair Care for Your New Style

Once you've found your perfect hairstyle, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. This may include using a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, using a heat protectant when styling, and avoiding excessive heat styling. Your stylist can recommend products that will work best for your hair type and new style.

By following these steps and tips, you can find a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Remember to consult with a stylist before making any drastic changes to your hair, and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

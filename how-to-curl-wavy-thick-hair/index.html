How To Curl Wavy Thick Hair A Step By Step Guide In Relaxed English

Written by Eveline Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're blessed with wavy thick hair, you know that it can be both a blessing and a curse. While your hair can look effortlessly beautiful on some days, it can also be difficult to manage and style on others. One hairstyle that can take your wavy thick hair to the next level is curls. In this step-by-step guide, we will walk you through how to curl wavy thick hair in a relaxed English language.

How to Curl Wavy Thick Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the tools you'll need. You will need a curling iron or wand, heat protectant spray, hair clips, and hair spray. It's important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent your hair from getting damaged while curling.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Once your hair is dry, apply a heat protectant spray. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up. This will make it easier to curl your hair in sections.

Step 2: Heat up your curling iron or wand

Set your curling iron or wand to the desired temperature. If you have wavy thick hair, you may need to use a higher temperature to achieve the curls you want. However, be careful not to use too much heat as it can damage your hair.

Step 3: Start curling

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand. Hold the hair for 10-15 seconds, then release. Repeat this process for each section of hair. For a more natural look, alternate the direction of the curls.

Step 4: Finish with hair spray

Once you've curled all sections of your hair, apply hair spray to keep your curls in place. You can also use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your curls.

Tips for Curling Wavy Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when curling your wavy thick hair:

  • Don't use too much product before curling your hair as it can weigh it down.
  • Use a larger barrel curling iron or wand for looser curls.
  • For tighter curls, use a smaller barrel curling iron or wand.
  • For a more natural look, don't curl the ends of your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized and defined.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I curl my wavy thick hair without heat?

Yes, you can curl your hair without heat by using overnight curling methods such as braiding or twisting your hair. However, keep in mind that these methods may not give you the same results as using a curling iron or wand.

How often can I curl my hair?

It's best to limit heat styling to once or twice a week to prevent damage to your hair. You can use other styling methods such as braids or twists to switch up your hairstyle between curling sessions.

Hair Care for Wavy Thick Hair

Wavy thick hair requires special care to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using hair products with harsh chemicals that can dry out your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can now rock beautiful curls on your wavy thick hair. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid using too much heat to prevent damage. Happy curling!

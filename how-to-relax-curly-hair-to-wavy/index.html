How To Relax Curly Hair To Wavy

Written by Oliver Oct 07, 2023 · 3 min read

Do you have curly hair that you’d like to relax into a wavy style? Relaxing curly hair can be a daunting task, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the look you desire. In this post, we’ll walk you through the steps on how to relax curly hair to wavy.

How to Relax Curly Hair to Wavy

Before we begin, it’s important to note that relaxing curly hair can cause damage, so be sure to practice proper hair care techniques and use products designed for curly hair.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. Follow up with a moisturizing conditioner to hydrate your hair and prepare it for the relaxing process.

Step 2: Apply a Relaxer

Apply a relaxer to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Be sure to follow the instructions on the product carefully and use a relaxer designed for curly hair.

Step 3: Comb Through Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb through your hair, ensuring that the relaxer is evenly distributed. Be careful not to tug or pull on your hair, as this can cause damage.

Step 4: Rinse Your Hair

Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water to remove the relaxer. Follow up with a neutralizing shampoo to stop the relaxing process and restore your hair’s pH balance.

Step 5: Condition Your Hair

Apply a deep conditioner to your hair to restore moisture and prevent damage. Leave the conditioner on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out with warm water.

Tips for Relaxing Curly Hair to Wavy

- Use a heat protectant before using any heat tools on your hair
- Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy
- Be gentle when detangling your hair to avoid breakage
- Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to create defined waves
- Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

FAQs

Q: Can I relax my hair at home?
A: Yes, you can relax your hair at home, but it’s important to follow the instructions carefully and use products designed for curly hair.

Q: How often should I relax my hair?
A: It’s recommended to relax your hair no more than every 8-10 weeks to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Relaxed Hair

Once you’ve relaxed your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly to prevent damage and maintain your wavy style. Here are some tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils
- Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated
- Avoid using heat tools too frequently, as this can cause damage
- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up
- Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy

With these tips and techniques, you can relax your curly hair into a wavy style that’s both beautiful and healthy.

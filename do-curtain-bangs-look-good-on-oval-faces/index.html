Do Curtain Bangs Look Good On Oval Faces

Description

Are you thinking of getting curtain bangs but you're not sure if they'll look good on your oval face? Rest assured, curtain bangs can look great on oval faces! In fact, oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape, allowing for a variety of hairstyles to suit them.

How to

Before getting curtain bangs, it's important to consult with your hairstylist to determine if the style will suit your face shape and hair type. Once you've decided to go for it, here are some steps to consider:

1. Choose the right length

The length of your curtain bangs will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. For oval faces, shorter bangs that hit above the eyebrows can accentuate your features and add some dimension to your hair. Longer bangs that graze your cheekbones can also work well.

2. Consider your hair texture

If you have straight hair, curtain bangs can be easily styled with a round brush and blow dryer. If you have wavy or curly hair, you may need to use a flat iron to smooth out your bangs. It's important to use heat protectant products to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Choose the right part

Curtain bangs are characterized by their center part, which frames the face. However, depending on your face shape, you may want to consider a side part to balance out your features.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your curtain bangs:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling your bangs. Use a heat protectant spray if using heat tools.

2. Section off your hair

Separate your bangs from the rest of your hair using a comb or your fingers.

3. Style with a round brush

Using a round brush, blow dry your bangs, starting at the roots and working your way down. Repeat until your bangs are dry and styled.

Tips

Here are some tips to consider when getting curtain bangs:

  • Bring pictures of the style you want to your hairstylist for reference
  • Consider the maintenance involved in styling and trimming your bangs
  • Experiment with different lengths and partings to find what works best for you
  • Use hair accessories, such as bobby pins or headbands, to keep your bangs out of your face if needed

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs and oval faces:

Q: Will curtain bangs make my face look longer?

A: No, curtain bangs can actually shorten the appearance of your face by framing it.

Q: Can I wear my curtain bangs with a ponytail?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can be styled with a ponytail or updo for a chic look.

Q: Will curtain bangs work with thick hair?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can work with thick hair but may require more styling to keep them in place.

Hair Care

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it's important to maintain a healthy hair care routine. This includes:

  • Regular trims to keep your bangs at the desired length
  • Using heat protectant products when styling with heat tools
  • Using a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage
  • Avoiding excessive heat styling and chemical treatments

Overall, curtain bangs can look great on oval faces when styled and maintained properly. Consult with your hairstylist to determine if this style is right for you.

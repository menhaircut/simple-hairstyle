What To Wear When 40 Degrees Tips And Outfit Ideas

Written by Petter Jul 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Summer is here, and the temperature is soaring. When the mercury hits 40 degrees, it can be a challenge to stay cool and comfortable while still looking stylish. In this blog post, we'll share some tips and outfit ideas for what to wear when 40 degrees.

Description

When the temperature hits 40 degrees, it's essential to wear clothes that are lightweight, breathable, and comfortable. You want to avoid fabrics that trap heat or cling to your skin, as they can make you feel even hotter.

How to

Here are some tips for what to wear when 40 degrees:

Tip 1: Choose Lightweight Fabrics

Opt for lightweight fabrics like cotton, linen, and rayon. These materials are breathable and will help you stay cool in the heat. Avoid synthetic fabrics like polyester and nylon, as they trap heat and don't allow your skin to breathe.

Tip 2: Wear Loose-Fitting Clothes

Tight clothes can make you feel even hotter in the heat. Instead, choose loose-fitting clothes that allow air to circulate around your body. Flowy dresses, wide-leg pants, and oversized shirts are all great options.

Tip 3: Stick to Light Colors

Dark colors absorb more heat than light colors, making you feel hotter. Stick to light colors like white, pastels, and brights. These colors reflect the sun's rays and help keep you cool.

Tip 4: Protect Your Skin

When it's hot outside, you want to protect your skin from the sun's harmful rays. Wear a hat, sunglasses, and sunscreen to prevent sunburn and skin damage.

Step by Step

Here are some outfit ideas for what to wear when 40 degrees:

Outfit 1: Flowy Sundress

A flowy sundress is perfect for hot weather. Choose a lightweight fabric like cotton or rayon and opt for a bright or pastel color. Pair with sandals and a straw hat for a chic and comfortable outfit.

Outfit 2: Wide-Leg Pants and Tank Top

Wide-leg pants are comfortable and airy, making them perfect for hot weather. Pair with a lightweight tank top in a bright color for a summery look. Add sandals and a crossbody bag for a casual and chic outfit.

Outfit 3: Oversized Shirt and Shorts

An oversized shirt is comfortable and breezy, making it perfect for hot weather. Pair with denim shorts and sandals for a casual and effortless look. Add a statement necklace or earrings to dress up the outfit.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for what to wear when 40 degrees:

Tip 1: Stay Hydrated

When it's hot outside, it's essential to stay hydrated. Drink plenty of water throughout the day to prevent dehydration.

Tip 2: Avoid Heavy Makeup

Heavy makeup can feel suffocating in the heat. Opt for lightweight products like tinted moisturizer, lip balm, and waterproof mascara.

Tip 3: Wear Comfortable Shoes

When it's hot outside, your feet can swell, making tight shoes uncomfortable. Opt for sandals, flip-flops, or sneakers that are breathable and comfortable.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a maxi dress when it's 40 degrees?

A: Yes, you can wear a maxi dress when it's 40 degrees. Choose a lightweight fabric like cotton or rayon and opt for a bright or pastel color. Pair with sandals and a sun hat for a chic and comfortable outfit.

Q: What should I wear to a summer wedding when it's 40 degrees?

A: For a summer wedding when it's 40 degrees, opt for a lightweight dress in a bright or pastel color. Pair with strappy sandals and statement earrings for a chic and elegant look.

Hair Care

When it's hot outside, you want to keep your hair off your face and neck to stay cool. Opt for a high ponytail, messy bun, or braids. These hairstyles are stylish and practical for hot weather.

In conclusion, when it's 40 degrees, it's essential to wear lightweight, breathable, and comfortable clothes. Stick to light colors, protect your skin from the sun, and stay hydrated. With these tips and outfit ideas, you can stay cool and stylish all summer long!

How To Make Your Hair Look Short Like A Boy With A Hat

Written by Robby Nov 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's cute and trendy, why not try making your hair look short like a boy with a hat? This style is perfect for those who want to switch up their look without going all the way with a short haircut. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect boyish look!

Description

The "boy with a hat" hairstyle is a popular trend that involves making your hair look short without actually cutting it. It's a great option for those who want to experiment with a new look without committing to a drastic change. This style is perfect for anyone who wants a cute and trendy hairstyle that's easy to maintain.

How To

To achieve the "boy with a hat" hairstyle, you'll need a few things:

  • Texturizing spray
  • Hair gel
  • A hat

Follow these steps:

  1. Begin by washing and conditioning your hair as you normally would.
  2. Apply texturizing spray to your hair to add texture and volume. This will help your hair look shorter.
  3. Use a hair gel to slick your hair down and create a side part.
  4. Put on a hat of your choice, such as a beanie or a baseball cap. Make sure the hat fits snugly on your head.
  5. Adjust the hat so that it sits low on your forehead and covers most of your hair.
  6. Your hair should now look shorter, as if you had a short haircut.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Begin by washing and conditioning your hair as you normally would. Make sure your hair is clean and free of any tangles.

Step 2: Apply Texturizing Spray

Apply texturizing spray to your hair to add texture and volume. This will help your hair look shorter.

Step 3: Use Hair Gel

Use a hair gel to slick your hair down and create a side part. This will help your hair look even shorter.

Step 4: Put On a Hat

Put on a hat of your choice, such as a beanie or a baseball cap. Make sure the hat fits snugly on your head.

Step 5: Adjust the Hat

Adjust the hat so that it sits low on your forehead and covers most of your hair. Your hair should now look shorter, as if you had a short haircut.

Tips

  • Experiment with different hats to find the perfect one for you.
  • Use a hair gel that provides a strong hold to keep your hair in place.
  • Try using a hair dryer to add even more volume to your hair.

FAQ

Can I do this with any hair length?

Yes, this hairstyle can be done with any hair length.

Do I need to use texturizing spray?

No, you don't need to use texturizing spray, but it will help add volume and texture to your hair.

How long will this hairstyle last?

This hairstyle should last all day as long as you use a strong-hold hair gel.

Hair Care

To maintain this hairstyle, make sure to use a hair gel that provides a strong hold. You can also touch up your hair throughout the day by re-applying the hair gel and adjusting your hat. Remember to wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

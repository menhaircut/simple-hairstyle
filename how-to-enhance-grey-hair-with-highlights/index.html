How To Enhance Grey Hair With Highlights A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Nov 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to add some dimension to your grey hair? Highlights are a great way to add some depth and texture to your locks while embracing your natural silver strands. In this article, we'll walk you through how to enhance grey hair with highlights in a relaxed and accessible way. Let's get started!

How to Enhance Grey Hair with Highlights

Enhancing grey hair with highlights is a process that involves adding lighter tones to your hair. This can be done in a variety of ways, including foils, balayage, or babylights. Here's a step-by-step guide to enhancing your grey hair with highlights:

Step 1: Choose Your Highlighting Technique

Before you begin, you'll need to decide which highlighting technique you want to use. Foils involve sectioning off your hair and applying lightener to each section before wrapping it in foil. Balayage involves freehand painting lightener onto your hair to create a natural, sun-kissed look. Babylights involve creating very fine highlights throughout your hair to mimic the natural highlights children have. Choose the technique that works best for your hair and your desired look.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before you start highlighting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will ensure that the lightener adheres to your strands evenly. It's also a good idea to apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair a few days before highlighting to help protect it from damage.

Step 3: Mix Your Lightener

Follow the instructions on your lightener to mix it with developer. Be sure to wear gloves and mix the lightener in a well-ventilated area.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to apply the lightener evenly and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 5: Apply the Lightener

Using a brush, apply the lightener to the sections of hair you want to highlight. Be sure to start at the ends of your hair and work your way up, as the ends of your hair will lighten more quickly than the roots. If you're using foils, wrap each section in foil after applying the lightener.

Step 6: Process the Lightener

Follow the instructions on your lightener to determine how long to leave it on your hair. This will depend on the strength of the lightener and the desired level of lightening. Keep an eye on your hair as it processes to ensure that it doesn't lighten too much.

Step 7: Rinse and Tone

Once your hair has processed, rinse it thoroughly with cool water until the water runs clear. Apply a toner to your hair to neutralize any unwanted brassy tones and add shine. Follow the instructions on your toner to determine how long to leave it on your hair before rinsing.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is toned and rinsed, style it as desired. You can blow dry, straighten, or curl your hair to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Enhancing Grey Hair with Highlights

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when enhancing your grey hair with highlights:

  • Choose a highlighting technique that works best for your hair and your desired look.
  • Consider using a purple shampoo or conditioner to help counteract any brassiness in your hair.
  • Avoid over-processing your hair by keeping an eye on it as it lightens.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to help keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQs about Enhancing Grey Hair with Highlights

What is the best highlighting technique for grey hair?

The best highlighting technique for grey hair will depend on your desired look and the condition of your hair. Foils, balayage, and babylights are all great options to consider.

How often should I get my grey hair highlighted?

How often you get your grey hair highlighted will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. Most people get their hair highlighted every 8-12 weeks.

Hair Care for Enhanced Grey Hair

Enhancing your grey hair with highlights can be a great way to add some dimension and texture to your locks. However, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for caring for your enhanced grey hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help maintain your color and keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can enhance your grey hair with highlights and enjoy a beautiful, dimensional look that embraces your natural silver strands.

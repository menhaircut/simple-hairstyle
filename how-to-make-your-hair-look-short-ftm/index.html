How To Make Your Hair Look Short Ftm Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Sep 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you a trans man looking to make your hair look short? If so, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll provide you with tips and tricks on how to make your hair look short FTM. Whether you're looking to chop off your locks or simply style your hair in a way that gives the illusion of short hair, we've got you covered. So, without further ado, let's get started!

Description

For many trans men, cutting their hair short is a crucial step in their transition. Short hair can help them feel more masculine and confident. However, some trans men may not be ready to make the big chop just yet. Luckily, there are ways to make your hair look short without actually cutting it. With the right styling techniques and products, you can achieve the look you want.

How to Make Your Hair Look Short FTM

Step 1: Get the Right Cut

If you're ready to cut your hair short, it's important to get the right cut. A good barber or stylist will be able to help you choose a style that suits your face shape and hair texture. Some popular short haircuts for trans men include the buzz cut, crew cut, and undercut. These styles are low maintenance and easy to style.

Step 2: Use the Right Styling Products

If you're not ready to cut your hair, you can still make it look short by using the right styling products. Pomades, waxes, and gels can help you achieve a short, textured look. Apply the product to your hair and use your fingers to style it into the desired shape. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 3: Consider Hair Extensions

If you're not ready to cut your hair, but still want to achieve a short look, consider hair extensions. Short hair extensions can be clipped in or sewn in to add length and fullness to your hair. You can style the extensions just like your natural hair, giving you the short look you desire.

Tips

  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find the right short hairstyle for you.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and styling tools.
  • Visit a stylist or barber who has experience working with trans men.
  • Be patient with the growing out process if you decide to grow your hair out again.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my curly hair look short?

A: Yes, you can make your curly hair look short. Short haircuts like the buzz cut or curly undercut can work well with curly hair.

Q: How do I maintain my short hair?

A: Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape. It's important to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Styling products can also help you maintain your short look.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, whether it's short or long. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your hair look short FTM and embrace your true self. Remember to be patient and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you.

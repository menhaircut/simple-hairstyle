How to Choose the Right Long Haircut for a Round Face

Choosing the right long haircut for a round face requires a bit of knowledge about your face shape and hair texture. The goal is to create angles and length to elongate your face shape. Here are some things to consider when choosing a long haircut for your round face:

  • Layers: Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, which can help elongate your face. But be careful not to add too many layers, as this can create a rounder look.
  • Length: Longer hair can help create an elongated look, but be careful not to go too long, as this can also create a rounder look. Aim for a length that hits just below your collarbone.
  • Bangs: Bangs can be tricky for round faces, but if done correctly, they can help create angles and length. Consider a side-swept bang or long, layered bangs.
  • Texture: Adding texture to your hair, such as waves or curls, can help create angles and movement, which can help elongate your face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Long Haircuts for Round Faces 2020

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a long haircut for your round face, here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect long haircut:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist: Your hairstylist can help you choose a long haircut that works best for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Choose your desired length and layers: Work with your hairstylist to choose a length and layering style that will elongate your face shape.
  3. Add texture: Consider adding texture to your hair, such as waves or curls, to help create angles and movement.
  4. Consider bangs: If you want to add bangs, consider a side-swept bang or long, layered bangs.
  5. Maintain your haircut: Regular trims are important to maintain the shape and length of your long haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long Haircut for a Round Face

Once you've achieved your perfect long haircut for your round face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining your long haircut:

  • Regular trims: Regular trims are important to maintain the length and shape of your long haircut. Aim for a trim every six to eight weeks.
  • Use the right products: Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid heat damage: Use heat protectant products and avoid excessive heat styling to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: The sun can cause damage to your hair, so wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time outdoors.

FAQs

What are the best long haircuts for round faces 2020?

The best long haircuts for round faces 2020 are those that create angles and length to elongate the face shape. Consider layers, length, bangs, and texture when choosing a long haircut for your round face.

Can I still have long hair if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Long hair can be flattering on round faces, as long as it's styled correctly. Consider a long haircut that incorporates layers, length, and texture to elongate your face shape.

How often should I trim my long haircut?

Regular trims are important to maintain the length and shape of your long haircut. Aim for a trim every six to eight weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Long Haircuts for Round Faces 2020

Maintaining your long haircut for a round face requires proper hair care. Here are some tips for keeping your long locks healthy and shiny:

  • Use products designed for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling.
  • Protect your hair from the sun.
  • Regularly deep condition your hair.
  • Brush your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb.

With the right long haircut and proper hair care, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look for your round face. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve the perfect long haircut for round faces 2020.

How To Style Long Hair For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Nov 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair for guys has been in trend for quite some time now. However, styling it can be a challenge, especially for those who are new to it. In this guide, we will show you how to style long hair for guys, step-by-step. We'll also share some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Long Hair for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Long Hair for Guys

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's take a look at some popular long hair styles for guys:

  • Man Bun
  • Ponytail
  • Half-Up Half-Down
  • Long and Wavy
  • Slicked Back

Now that you have an idea of the different styles, let's move on to the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

To style long hair for guys, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Apply a styling product of your choice. If you want a natural look, go for a light hold product. For a more structured look, opt for a stronger hold product.
  4. For a man bun, gather all your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie. For a ponytail, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie. For a half-up half-down look, gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie.
  5. For a long and wavy look, use a curling iron or a hair straightener to create loose waves. You can also use a texturizing spray for added volume and texture.
  6. For a slicked-back look, apply a pomade or a gel and comb your hair back.
  7. Finish off with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

And there you have it! You've successfully styled your long hair.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect long hair look:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to prevent breakage.
  • Don't wash your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long hair for guys:

Q: Can guys with curly hair style their hair long?

A: Absolutely! In fact, long curly hair can look amazing when styled properly. Use a curling cream or mousse to define your curls and add volume.

Q: How do I prevent my long hair from getting tangled?

A: Use a leave-in conditioner or a detangler spray to prevent tangles. Brush your hair regularly and avoid sleeping with wet hair.

Q: Is it necessary to use a hairspray?

A: No, it's not necessary. However, if you want your hair to stay in place for longer, a hairspray can be helpful.

Hair Care Tips

Long hair requires extra care and attention. Here are some hair care tips to keep your long hair healthy:

  • Use a hair mask once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps as they can cause breakage.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to harsh chemicals and pollutants.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and strength.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style your long hair and keep it healthy and strong. So, go ahead and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best!

How To Get Wavy Hair Naturally Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you want to achieve the effortlessly cool and tousled look of wavy hair, but don't want to use heat or chemicals? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get wavy hair naturally guys.

Description

Wavy hair is a popular hairstyle for both men and women. While some people are born with naturally wavy hair, others may have straight or curly hair and want to achieve the wavy look without using heat or chemicals. Natural wavy hair is not only stylish but also healthier for your hair in the long run.

How to Get Wavy Hair Naturally Guys

Follow these steps to achieve wavy hair without using heat or chemicals:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply a Sea Salt Spray

Sea salt sprays are a great way to enhance your hair's natural waves. Spray the sea salt spray all over your damp hair and scrunch your hair with your hands.

Step 3: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Allow your hair to air dry naturally. Do not use a hairdryer or any heat styling tools.

Step 4: Apply a Texturizing Spray

Once your hair is fully dry, apply a texturizing spray to add more definition to your waves. Spray the product all over your hair and scrunch your hair with your hands.

Step 5: Finish with a Hairspray

Apply a hairspray to hold the waves in place. Make sure to choose a hairspray that provides a flexible hold, so your hair doesn't look stiff or crunchy.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair after washing it.
  • Do not towel-dry your hair too harshly, as this can cause frizz.
  • Do not touch your hair too much while it is drying, as this can disrupt the wave pattern.
  • For a more defined look, you can use a curling cream or mousse before applying the sea salt spray.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Can I use a diffuser to dry my hair?

While a diffuser can help enhance your natural waves, it still uses heat, which can damage your hair over time. It's best to let your hair air dry naturally.

Can I use a hair straightener to create waves?

While you can use a hair straightener to create waves, it's not a natural method and can cause damage to your hair. It's best to stick to natural methods to achieve wavy hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day or every few days, depending on your hair type. Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and damage.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair while it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve natural wavy hair without using heat or chemicals. Experiment with different products and methods to find what works best for your hair type. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

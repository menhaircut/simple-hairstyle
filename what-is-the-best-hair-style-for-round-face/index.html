What Is The Best Hair Style For Round Face

Written by Christine Jul 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hair style for your round face? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many people with round faces find it difficult to choose a hairstyle that complements their facial features. But fear not, as we've got you covered. In this article, we'll discuss the best hair style for round face, how to achieve it, and some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a soft jawline. The goal of choosing the best hair style for round face is to create balance and symmetry by elongating the face and minimizing the width. This can be achieved by selecting a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown of the head and creates vertical lines around the face.

How to

The best hair style for round face is one that creates the illusion of length and height. Here are some tips to achieve this:

1. Opt for a Long, Layered Cut

A long, layered cut is perfect for round faces. This style adds length to the face and creates vertical lines that elongate the face. The layers also add volume and movement to the hair, which is perfect for creating the illusion of height.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are great for round faces as they create an angled line across the face, which can help to elongate the face. They also add volume and texture to the hair, which is perfect for creating a more dramatic look.

3. Waves or Curls

Waves or curls add volume and texture to the hair, which is perfect for creating the illusion of height. They also create vertical lines around the face, which can help to elongate the face. Just be sure to avoid tight curls as they can add width to the face.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the best hair style for round face:

Step 1: Choose the Right Cut

Opt for a long, layered cut with side-swept bangs. This will create the illusion of length and height and add volume and texture to the hair.

Step 2: Add Waves or Curls

Add waves or curls to the hair to create volume and texture. This will also help to create vertical lines around the face, which can help to elongate the face.

Step 3: Style with Volume

Style the hair with volume by using a volumizing spray or mousse. This will add height to the crown of the head and help to create the illusion of length.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the look with hairspray to hold the style in place and add shine to the hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best hair style for round face:

1. Avoid Short Cuts

Avoid short cuts as they can make the face appear wider. Instead, opt for a longer cut that adds length to the face.

2. Avoid Blunt Bangs

Avoid blunt bangs as they can make the face appear rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that create an angled line across the face.

3. Use a Round Brush

Use a round brush when blow-drying the hair to add volume and create height at the crown of the head.

FAQ

What is the best hair length for round face?

The best hair length for round face is a long, layered cut. This style adds length to the face and creates vertical lines that elongate the face.

What hairstyles should be avoided for round faces?

Short cuts and blunt bangs should be avoided for round faces as they can make the face appear wider and rounder.

What is the best way to style hair for a round face?

The best way to style hair for a round face is to add volume and texture. This can be achieved by using a volumizing spray or mousse, and by adding waves or curls to the hair.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for achieving the best hair style for round face. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is free from harsh chemicals to avoid damaging the hair.

2. Condition Regularly

Condition the hair regularly to keep it hydrated and nourished.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage the hair and lead to breakage and split ends.

4. Trim Regularly

Trim the hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know what the best hair style for round face is, it's time to hit the salon and try out a new look! Remember, the key is to create balance and symmetry by elongating the face and minimizing the width. With the right cut, styling techniques, and hair care, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering look that complements your facial features.

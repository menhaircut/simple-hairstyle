If you're looking for a stylish and modern hairstyle that's easy to maintain, then you should consider getting a mens hairstyle long on top and shorter on the sides. This type of haircut has been popular for a few years now and is still going strong. In this article, we'll show you how to get this style, give you some tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions about this type of haircut.

How to Get Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Shorter Sides

The first step in getting mens hairstyles long on top shorter sides is to find a good hairstylist who can give you the look you want. You should look for a stylist who has experience cutting this type of hair and who can recommend the best products to use to maintain your style.

You'll also need to decide on the length of your hair. Mens hairstyles long on top shorter sides can vary in length, so it's important to find a length that works for you. Generally, the top should be at least 2-3 inches long, while the sides should be shorter, around 1-2 inches.

Finally, choose the style you want. You can go for a classic look with a side part or a more modern look with a messy fringe or slicked-back hair. Whatever style you choose, make sure it suits your face shape and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting mens hairstyles long on top shorter sides:

  1. Choose your stylist: Look for a stylist who has experience cutting this type of hair.
  2. Decide on the length: The top should be 2-3 inches long, while the sides should be 1-2 inches.
  3. Choose your style: Classic side part or modern messy fringe?
  4. Get your hair washed and conditioned.
  5. Section your hair: Use clips to section off the top and sides of your hair.
  6. Cut the sides: Use clippers to cut the sides to the desired length.
  7. Cut the top: Use scissors to cut the top to the desired length.
  8. Style your hair: Use products like pomade or wax to style your hair and give it texture.
  9. Finish with hairspray: Use hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you have your mens hairstyles long on top shorter sides, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims: To keep your hair looking neat and tidy, get regular trims every 4-6 weeks.
  • Use the right products: Use products like pomade or wax to style your hair and keep it in place.
  • Wash your hair regularly: Keep your hair clean by washing it every other day or as needed.
  • Don't over-style: Mens hairstyles long on top shorter sides look best when they're not over-styled. Keep it simple and natural-looking.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will mens hairstyles long on top shorter sides work for me?

A: Mens hairstyles long on top shorter sides can work for most men, but it's important to choose a style that suits your face shape and personal style.

Q: How do I know what length to get?

A: It's best to consult with your stylist to determine the best length for your hair and style.

Q: Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different styles and products to achieve different looks.

Hair Care for Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Shorter Sides

Mens hairstyles long on top shorter sides require some basic hair care to keep them looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too many products on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking sharp.

With these tips, you can keep your mens hairstyles long on top shorter sides looking great and stylish all year round.

Best Below Shoulder Length Haircuts

Written by Frank Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long hair but not ready for a short haircut? Below shoulder length haircuts might be the perfect solution for you! In this article, we will discuss the best below shoulder length haircuts for all hair types and face shapes.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Below shoulder length haircuts are perfect for those who want to keep some length without having to deal with the maintenance of long hair. These haircuts are also versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there is a below shoulder length haircut that will work for you.

How to

Before getting a below shoulder length haircut, it is important to choose the right style for your face shape and hair type. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which haircut will work best for you. Once you have chosen a style, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it is damp. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to keep each section separated.

Step 3: Start cutting

Starting with the back section, cut your hair to the desired length. Work your way around your head, cutting each section evenly.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once your hair is cut, style it using a blow dryer, curling iron, or straightener. Use hair products like mousse or hairspray to keep your style in place.

Step by step

Here are some popular below shoulder length haircuts and how to achieve them:

Shoulder-Length Bob

This classic haircut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style. The shoulder-length bob is cut straight across and can be styled with or without bangs. To achieve this look, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to shoulder length and add layers for volume.

Long Layers

Long layers are a great way to add movement and texture to your hair. To achieve this look, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to just below your shoulders and add long layers throughout. Style your hair with a curling iron or straightener to show off the layers.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to change up your look without cutting too much hair. To achieve this look, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to just below your shoulders and add side-swept bangs. Style your hair with a curling iron or straightener to show off the bangs.

Tips

  • Use heat protectant products when styling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

What face shapes look good with below shoulder length haircuts?

Below shoulder length haircuts work well with most face shapes. However, those with heart-shaped or oval faces may want to add layers to soften their features.

What hair types work well with below shoulder length haircuts?

Below shoulder length haircuts work well with all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your below shoulder length haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can rock a below shoulder length haircut with confidence. Whether you prefer a classic bob or long layers, there is a below shoulder length haircut that will work for you.

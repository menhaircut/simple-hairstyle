How To Use Thinning Shears On Wavy Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your wavy hair being too thick and unmanageable? Thinning shears might just be the solution you need! In this article, we will guide you through how to use thinning shears on wavy hair so that you can achieve a more manageable and stylish look.

Description

Thinning shears are a type of scissors that are used to remove bulk from hair. They have serrated blades that cut some strands of hair while leaving others, creating a layered effect. This tool is great for people with thick hair who want to make their hair more manageable by thinning it out.

How to Use Thinning Shears on Wavy Hair

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. It's important to ensure that your hair is completely dry before using thinning shears.

Step 2: Decide on the Section of Hair to Thin

Decide on the section of hair that you want to thin out. It's best to start with a small section of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.

Step 3: Hold the Section of Hair

Hold the section of hair that you want to thin out with your non-dominant hand. Make sure that you are holding the hair firmly so that it doesn't move while you are cutting.

Step 4: Use the Thinning Shears

Using your dominant hand, hold the thinning shears and cut through the section of hair. Start at the top of the section and work your way down to the bottom.

Step 5: Repeat as Needed

Repeat this process on other sections of hair that you want to thin out. Be sure to take your time and not cut too much hair at once.

Tips

  • Use thinning shears sparingly to avoid over-thinning your hair.
  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Make sure that your hair is completely dry before using thinning shears.
  • Don't cut too close to the scalp.

FAQ

Can thinning shears be used on all hair types?

Thinning shears are best suited for people with thick hair. They can be used on all hair types but may not be as effective on fine or thin hair.

Can thinning shears damage your hair?

Thinning shears can damage your hair if they are not used properly. It's important to use them sparingly and not cut too much hair at once.

How often should you use thinning shears on your hair?

It's best to use thinning shears on your hair once every few months to avoid over-thinning your hair.

Hair Care

After using thinning shears on your hair, it's important to take care of your hair properly. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant when using styling tools.

Thinning shears can be a great tool for managing thick and unmanageable wavy hair. By following these simple steps, you can achieve a more manageable and stylish look that you will love.

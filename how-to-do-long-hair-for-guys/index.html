How To Do Long Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long hair for guys is becoming more and more popular these days, but it can be tricky to know how to style it. If you're new to having long hair or just need some tips on how to keep it looking great, this article is for you. We'll go over everything from how to wash and condition your hair to different styling options and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in doing long hair for guys is to make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a good quality shampoo and conditioner, a brush or comb, and any styling products you want to use. Once you have all your tools, follow these steps:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo. Make sure to massage it into your scalp and work it through your hair before rinsing it out thoroughly. Follow up with a conditioner, focusing on the ends of your hair where it tends to be driest. Rinse out the conditioner completely.

2. Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a clean towel to gently blot your hair dry. Don't rub your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz. Leave your hair slightly damp for easier styling.

3. Apply Styling Products

If you want to use any styling products, now is the time to apply them. This could be anything from a leave-in conditioner to a styling cream or gel. Start with a small amount and work it through your hair evenly.

4. Style Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. This will depend on your personal preference and the occasion. You could leave your hair down and natural, pull it back into a ponytail or bun, or try a more elaborate style like a braid or half-up half-down look. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Step by Step

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to doing long hair for guys:

1. Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Start by wetting your hair in the shower or bath. Apply a quarter-sized amount of shampoo to your hair and massage it into your scalp. Rinse it out thoroughly and follow up with a quarter-sized amount of conditioner. Work it through your hair, focusing on the ends, and rinse it out completely.

2. Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a clean towel to gently blot your hair dry. Don't rub your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz. Leave your hair slightly damp for easier styling.

3. Apply Styling Products

If you want to use any styling products, now is the time to apply them. Start with a small amount and work it through your hair evenly. Some popular products for long hair include leave-in conditioner, styling cream, and hair oil.

4. Brush or Comb Your Hair

Use a brush or comb to detangle your hair and work out any knots. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots. Be gentle, as wet hair is more prone to breakage.

5. Style Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. This will depend on your personal preference and the occasion. Some popular styles for long hair include:

  • Leaving it down and natural
  • Pulling it back into a ponytail or bun
  • Trying a braid or half-up half-down look
  • Experimenting with different partings and hair accessories

Tips

Here are some tips for keeping your long hair looking great:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Invest in a good quality brush or comb
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often
  • Protect your hair from sun and chlorine damage by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing long hair for guys:

1. How often should I wash my long hair?

This will depend on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with long hair prefer to wash it every day, while others find that washing it every other day or even every few days works better for them. Experiment to find what works best for you.

2. Should I use a leave-in conditioner?

Leave-in conditioner can be a great way to keep your long hair hydrated and healthy. If you have dry or damaged hair, it's definitely worth trying. Just make sure to apply it evenly and not use too much, as this can make your hair look greasy.

3. Can I still wear hats with long hair?

Absolutely! Wearing a hat can be a great way to protect your hair from the sun and add some style to your look. Just make sure to choose a hat that fits well and won't damage your hair.

Hair Care

Long hair requires some extra care to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for taking care of your long hair:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often
  • Protect your hair from sun and chlorine damage by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF
  • Brush or comb your hair gently to avoid breakage

By following these tips and experimenting with different styles, you can keep your long hair looking great and feeling healthy. Remember to have fun with your hair and enjoy the freedom that comes with long locks!

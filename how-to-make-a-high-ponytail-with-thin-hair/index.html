How To Make A High Ponytail With Thin Hair

Written by William Aug 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of attempting a high ponytail with your thin hair only to have it fall flat and lifeless? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps to create a high ponytail that will stay put all day long.

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Make a High Ponytail with Thin Hair

The key to making a high ponytail with thin hair is to add volume and texture. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash your hair the night before, and allow it to air dry. Avoid using any heavy products that can weigh down your hair, such as oils or serums.

2. Use a volumizing spray.

Spray a volumizing spray at the roots of your hair. This will add texture and lift to your hair, making it easier to style into a high ponytail.

3. Tease your hair.

Take a comb and tease your hair at the crown of your head. This will add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style into a high ponytail.

4. Secure your hair into a ponytail.

Gather your hair into a high ponytail, using a hair tie to secure it in place. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.

5. Use a hairband.

Wrap a hairband around the base of your ponytail. This will help to keep your ponytail in place throughout the day.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to creating a high ponytail with thin hair:

  1. Wash your hair the night before, and allow it to air dry.
  2. Spray a volumizing spray at the roots of your hair.
  3. Tease your hair at the crown of your head.
  4. Gather your hair into a high ponytail, using a hair tie to secure it in place.
  5. Wrap a hairband around the base of your ponytail.

Tips for Maintaining Your High Ponytail

Here are some tips for maintaining your high ponytail throughout the day:

  • Use a hairband to keep your ponytail in place.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can cause your ponytail to fall flat.
  • If your hair starts to look oily, use a dry shampoo to refresh your roots.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use extensions to create a high ponytail?

Yes, you can use clip-in extensions to add volume and length to your ponytail.

How can I make my high ponytail look more polished?

Use a small amount of hair gel or hairspray to smooth down any flyaway hairs around your hairline.

Can I create a high ponytail with a part in my hair?

Yes, you can create a high ponytail with a center or side part in your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

Here are some hair care tips for thin hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing your hair when it's wet.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a high ponytail that will stay put all day long. Remember to use volumizing products, tease your hair, and secure your ponytail with a hairband for a polished and stylish look.

Best Styling Mousse For Curly Hair Uk

Written by Christine Oct 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. It's beautiful and unique, but it can also be difficult to manage. Finding the right styling products can be a challenge, and that's especially true for mousse. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing the best styling mousse for curly hair in the UK.

Description

Styling mousse is a popular product for curly hair because it adds volume and definition without weighing the hair down. It's also a great option for those who want to avoid using heavy gels or creams. However, not all mousses are created equal, and finding one that works well for curly hair can be a challenge.

How to Use Styling Mousse for Curly Hair

Using styling mousse for curly hair is easy, but there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  1. Start with damp hair. Mousses work best when applied to damp hair, so make sure your hair is towel-dried before you start.
  2. Apply the mousse evenly throughout your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the mousse evenly from roots to ends.
  3. Scrunch your hair. Scrunching your hair with your hands will help define your curls and create volume.
  4. Let your hair air dry or diffuse it. Mousses work best when allowed to air dry, but you can also use a diffuser to speed up the process.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Styling Mousse for Curly Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to using styling mousse for curly hair:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Apply a golf ball-sized amount of mousse to your palm.
  4. Rake the mousse through your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the ends.
  5. Scrunch your hair with your hands to help define your curls.
  6. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to speed up the process.

Tips for Using Styling Mousse for Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your styling mousse:

  • Use a mousse specifically designed for curly hair. These mousses are formulated to provide the right amount of hold and definition for curly hair.
  • Don't use too much mousse. Using too much can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different amounts of mousse to find what works best for your hair.
  • Use a diffuser to speed up the drying process and create more volume.
  • Don't touch your curls too much while they're drying. This can cause frizz and disrupt the curl pattern.

  • Cantu Shea Butter for Natural Hair Wave Whip Curling Mousse

Can I use styling mousse on dry hair?

Styling mousse is designed to be used on damp hair, but you can still use it on dry hair if you're in a pinch. Just make sure to use a small amount and distribute it evenly through your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are a few additional tips to help you keep your curly hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. Heat can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Don't brush your hair when it's dry. This can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern.
  • Avoid using products with sulfates and silicones, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know more about the best styling mousse for curly hair in the UK, you can find the perfect product to help you achieve beautiful, defined curls. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair, and don't forget to take care of your curls with regular conditioning treatments and gentle styling.

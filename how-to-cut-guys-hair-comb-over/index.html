How To Cut Guys Hair Comb Over A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 21, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to cut your own hair or your friend's hair, a comb over is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's sleek, sophisticated and perfect for any occasion. In this article, we're going to show you how to cut guys hair comb over in relaxed English language. We'll cover everything you need to know, including how to prepare for the cut, the tools you'll need, and the step-by-step process to achieve the perfect comb over. Let's get started!

How to Prepare for the Cut

Before you start cutting, it's important to prepare for the process. Here are a few things you should do:

  • Wash and dry your hair: Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting. This will make it easier to work with and ensure you get a more accurate cut.
  • Choose the right length: Decide how long you want your hair to be before you start cutting. If you're not sure, start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter.
  • Gather your tools: You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting a Comb Over

Step 1: Choose Your Part

The first step in cutting a comb over is to decide where you want your part to be. You can either choose a side part or a middle part. Use your comb to create a clean parting line.

Step 2: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair on the shorter side of your part. Use your clippers to create a fade from the bottom of your hairline up to your part. Gradually increase the length of your clippers as you move up your head.

Step 3: Blend Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting the shorter side of your hair, it's time to blend it in with the longer side. Use your scissors to trim the hair on the longer side, making sure to blend it in with the shorter side. You want to create a gradual transition from short to long.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Now it's time to cut the top of your hair. Use your scissors to trim the hair on the top of your head, starting from the front and working your way back. Make sure to cut your hair at an angle, so it blends in with the sides.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting, it's time to style your hair. Use your comb to create a sleek comb over, and use a hair product to keep it in place.

Tips for Cutting a Comb Over

  • Take your time: Cutting hair takes patience and practice. Don't rush the process.
  • Use the right tools: Make sure you have the right tools for the job, including clippers and scissors.
  • Start with a longer length: It's always better to start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter.
  • Blend your hair: Make sure to blend your hair in, so there are no harsh lines.

FAQ: Cutting a Comb Over

How long should a comb over be?

A comb over can be any length, depending on your personal preference. However, it's typically shorter on the sides and longer on the top.

What tools do I need to cut a comb over?

You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair. However, it's always better to have someone else do it for you, especially if you're not confident in your skills.

Hair Care Tips

  • Wash your hair regularly: Make sure to wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner: Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling can damage your hair, so try to avoid it as much as possible.
  • Get regular haircuts: Regular haircuts can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Cutting a comb over is a simple process that anyone can do with the right tools and a little patience. Follow our step-by-step guide and you'll have a perfect comb over in no time!

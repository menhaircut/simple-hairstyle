How To Make Wavy Hair Curly Naturally A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 18, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have wavy hair and you want to add some bounce and volume to your locks, you might be wondering how to make wavy hair curly naturally. The good news is that there are several ways to do this without resorting to harsh chemicals or heat styling tools. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to create gorgeous curls that will last all day.

Description

Wavy hair is an ideal canvas for creating curls. However, it can be challenging to make them last. By using natural techniques, you can create curls that are healthy and long-lasting. This guide will help you understand the best methods for making wavy hair curly naturally.

How to Make Wavy Hair Curly Naturally: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin any styling, it is essential to start with clean hair. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. The sulfate-free formula will help keep your hair healthy and prevent it from drying out.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancer

Apply a curl enhancer product to your hair. This product will help define your curls and keep them in place. You can use a mousse, gel, or cream. Apply the product to damp hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands while it is still damp. This will help define your curls and create volume. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.

Step 4: Air Dry Your Hair

Air dry your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools. You can also use a microfiber towel to gently squeeze out excess water from your hair.

Step 5: Add Volume

To add volume to your curls, flip your hair upside down and shake it out. You can also use a hair pick to gently lift your curls at the roots.

Tips for Making Wavy Hair Curly Naturally

Here are some additional tips for making wavy hair curly naturally:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to prevent frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and health.

FAQs

Can I make wavy hair curly naturally without using any products?

Yes, you can make wavy hair curly naturally without using any products. You can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer and scrunch your hair with your hands to create natural curls.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls will last all day as long as you don't touch them too much. Be careful not to brush or comb your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Wavy hair requires a little extra care to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for your wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make wavy hair curly naturally and enjoy gorgeous, healthy curls that will last all day.

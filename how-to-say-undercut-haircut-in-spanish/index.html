How To Say Undercut Haircut In Spanish

Written by Luffy Nov 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you planning to get an undercut haircut in a Spanish-speaking country? Or do you want to impress your Spanish-speaking hairdresser by using the right term for your desired haircut? Either way, learning how to say undercut haircut in Spanish is essential. In this article, we will guide you through the process of saying undercut haircut in Spanish in relaxed English language.

Description

An undercut is a popular hairstyle that involves shaving or trimming the hair on the sides and back of the head while leaving the hair on top long. The result is a sharp contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back.

How to Say Undercut Haircut in Spanish

The Spanish term for undercut haircut is "corte de pelo con degradado" or simply "degradado." You can use either of these terms to communicate with your Spanish-speaking hairdresser. Here's how to pronounce them:

  • "Corte de pelo con degradado" - kor-teh deh peh-loh kohn deh-grah-dah-doh
  • "Degradado" - deh-grah-dah-doh

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to say undercut haircut in Spanish:

  1. Learn the Spanish terms for undercut haircut: "corte de pelo con degradado" or "degradado."
  2. Practice pronouncing the terms until you feel confident saying them.
  3. When you go to a Spanish-speaking hair salon, use the term "corte de pelo con degradado" or "degradado" to request an undercut haircut.
  4. If you're not sure if the hairdresser understands what you mean, you can show them a picture of the hairstyle to clarify.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you communicate effectively with your Spanish-speaking hairdresser:

  • Bring pictures of the hairstyle you want to show the hairdresser.
  • Use simple language and speak slowly to ensure that the hairdresser understands you.
  • If you're not sure how to say something in Spanish, use a translation app or dictionary to help you.

Hair Care

After getting an undercut haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax to keep the hair on top in place.
  • Visit your hairdresser regularly to maintain the shape of the undercut.

Learning how to say undercut haircut in Spanish is a useful skill, especially if you're traveling to a Spanish-speaking country or living in one. With this guide, you can confidently communicate with your Spanish-speaking hairdresser and get the haircut you want. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain the style and look your best.

Which Haircut Is Best For Long Curly Hair

Written by Daniel Nov 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have long, curly hair, you may be wondering which haircut is best for your locks. There are many options out there, but not all of them will work for your unique hair type. In this article, we'll explore some of the best haircuts for long curly hair and provide tips on how to care for your curls.

Description

Long curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have voluminous, bouncy curls that can be styled in a variety of ways. On the other hand, you may struggle with frizz, tangles, and dryness. Finding the right haircut can make a big difference in how your curls look and feel.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Long Curly Hair

When choosing a haircut for long curly hair, there are a few factors to consider:

  • Face shape: Your face shape can play a role in which haircut will look best on you. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid short, curly haircuts that add width to your face.
  • Curl type: There are different types of curls, from loose waves to tight coils. The type of curls you have will affect how your hair falls and how much volume you have.
  • Hair texture: Curly hair can vary in texture from fine and silky to thick and coarse. The texture of your hair can affect how it holds a style and how much volume you have.
  • Lifestyle: Your lifestyle can also play a role in which haircut is best for you. If you're always on the go and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, you may want a low-maintenance cut.

Step-by-Step Guide for Getting a Haircut for Long Curly Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide for getting a haircut for long curly hair:

  1. Do some research: Look for inspiration photos of haircuts you like on people with similar face shapes and hair types as you.
  2. Find a stylist who specializes in curly hair: Not all stylists are trained in cutting and styling curly hair. Look for someone who has experience working with curls.
  3. Communicate your needs: Before your appointment, talk to your stylist about your hair goals and any concerns you have. They can recommend a haircut that will work for you.
  4. Cut your hair dry: Curly hair can look very different when wet vs. dry. Cutting your hair dry allows the stylist to see how your curls fall and make adjustments accordingly.
  5. Ask for layers: Layers can help add volume and movement to your curls.

Tips for Maintaining Long Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining long curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when they're wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define and hold your curls.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your curls looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for long curly hair?

The best haircut for long curly hair depends on your face shape, curl type, hair texture, and lifestyle. Some popular haircuts for long curly hair include layers, long bobs, and shag cuts.

How often should I get a haircut if I have long curly hair?

It's generally recommended to get a haircut every 8-12 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Can I straighten my long curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your long curly hair, but it's important to use heat protectant and avoid using heat tools too often, as this can cause damage and dryness.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a little extra TLC to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for long curly hair:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid towel-drying your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a cotton t-shirt or microfiber towel to blot your hair dry.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define and hold your curls.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your curls looking fresh and healthy.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, long curly hair can be a gorgeous, low-maintenance style. Whether you opt for layers, a long bob, or a shag cut, make sure to communicate your needs with your stylist and take good care of your curls at home.

Top 5 Men s Hairstyles For 2023 A Relaxed English Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 02, 2023 · 6 min read

When it comes to men's hairstyles, there are so many options to choose from. From classic cuts to modern styles, the choices can be overwhelming. That's why we've put together a list of the top 5 men's hairstyles for 2023. Whether you're looking for a new style or just want to stay up-to-date with the latest trends, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  1. Short Back and Sides
  2. The Quiff
  3. The Side Part
  4. The Buzz Cut
  5. The Slick Back

1. Short Back and Sides

The short back and sides is a classic men's hairstyle that never goes out of style. This cut features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with a slightly longer length on top. It's a versatile cut that can be styled in many different ways, from a messy look to a more polished style.

How to Get the Short Back and Sides

To get the short back and sides, ask your barber for a clipper cut on the sides and back, with a longer length on top. The length on top can vary, depending on your preference. You can also ask for a fade or taper on the sides and back for a more modern look.

Tips for Styling the Short Back and Sides

  • Use a matte hair product for a more natural look
  • Experiment with different partings for a new look
  • Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair on top

FAQs About the Short Back and Sides

Q: Can I wear this style if I have curly hair?
A: Yes, the short back and sides can work with curly hair. Just make sure your barber knows how to cut curly hair.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut to maintain this style?
A: You'll need to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the length on top looking fresh.

Hair Care for the Short Back and Sides

To keep your short back and sides looking its best, use a good quality shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a hair oil or serum to add shine and nourishment to your hair.


2. The Quiff

The quiff is a stylish men's haircut that's perfect for those who want a more polished look. This cut features short hair on the sides and back, with a longer length on top that's styled up and back.

How to Get the Quiff

To get the quiff, ask your barber for a clipper cut on the sides and back, with a longer length on top that's layered. Make sure to leave enough length on top to style it up and back.

Tips for Styling the Quiff

  • Use a strong hold pomade or wax to keep the hair in place
  • Blow dry the hair on top to add volume
  • Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair up and back

FAQs About the Quiff

Q: Can I wear this style if I have thin hair?
A: Yes, the quiff can work with thin hair. Just make sure to add volume with a blow dryer and use a strong hold product to keep the hair in place.

Q: How long does it take to style the quiff?
A: It depends on your hair type and the length of your hair. It can take anywhere from 5-15 minutes to style the quiff.

Hair Care for the Quiff

To keep your quiff looking its best, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a hair spray to keep the hair in place throughout the day.


3. The Side Part

The side part is a classic men's hairstyle that's perfect for those who want a more traditional look. This cut features short hair on the sides and back, with a longer length on top that's parted to one side.

How to Get the Side Part

To get the side part, ask your barber for a clipper cut on the sides and back, with a longer length on top that's layered. Make sure to leave enough length on top to create the part.

Tips for Styling the Side Part

  • Use a comb to create the part
  • Use a strong hold pomade or wax to keep the hair in place
  • Blow dry the hair on top to add volume

FAQs About the Side Part

Q: Can I wear this style if I have a receding hairline?
A: Yes, the side part can work with a receding hairline. Just make sure to keep the hair longer on top and use a strong hold product to keep it in place.

Q: Can I wear this style if I have curly hair?
A: Yes, the side part can work with curly hair. Just make sure to use a strong hold product to keep the hair in place.

Hair Care for the Side Part

To keep your side part looking its best, use a good quality shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a hair oil or serum to add shine and nourishment to your hair.


4. The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a low-maintenance men's haircut that's perfect for those who want a simple, clean look. This cut features short hair all over the head, with a uniform length.

How to Get the Buzz Cut

To get the buzz cut, ask your barber for a clipper cut all over the head, with a uniform length. The length can vary, depending on your preference.

Tips for Styling the Buzz Cut

  • Use a matte hair product for a more natural look
  • Experiment with different lengths for a new look
  • Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair on top

FAQs About the Buzz Cut

Q: Can I wear this style if I have a round face?
A: Yes, the buzz cut can work with a round face. It can help to create a more defined jawline.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut to maintain this style?
A: You'll need to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the hair looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care for the Buzz Cut

To keep your buzz cut looking its best, use a good quality shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a hair oil or serum to add shine and nourishment to your hair.


5. The Slick Back

The slick back is a stylish men's haircut that's perfect for those who want a polished, sophisticated look. This cut features short hair on the sides and back, with a longer length on top that's slicked back with a comb or brush.

How to Get the Slick Back

To get the slick back, ask your barber for a clipper cut on the sides and back, with a longer length on top that's layered. Make sure to leave enough length on top to slick it back.

Tips for Styling the Slick Back

  • Use a strong hold gel or pomade to keep the hair in place
  • Blow dry the hair on top to add volume
  • Use a comb or brush to slick the hair back

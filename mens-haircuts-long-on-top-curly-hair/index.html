Mens Haircuts Long On Top Curly Hair How To Get The Perfect Look

Written by Oliver Aug 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having the same old haircut for your curly hair? It's time to switch things up with mens haircuts long on top curly hair. This trendy style is perfect for men who have curly or wavy hair and want a more relaxed, effortless look. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to get this look, as well as some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQ

Description

Mens haircuts long on top curly hair is a popular hairstyle that features longer hair on the top and shorter sides and back. This style is perfect for men with curly or wavy hair, as the length on top allows for the curls to be accentuated, while the shorter sides keep the hair looking neat and tidy.

How to Get the Look

The first step in getting mens haircuts long on top curly hair is finding a good barber or stylist who has experience with curly hair. They will be able to advise you on what length and style will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Ask your barber or stylist to leave the hair on top longer than the sides and back.
  3. The length on top will depend on your personal preference, but a good rule of thumb is to have it at least 3 inches long.
  4. The sides and back can be buzzed or faded, depending on the look you're going for.
  5. If you want to add some texture to your curls, ask your stylist to use a razor or texturizing scissors on the ends.
  6. Style your hair with a curl-enhancing product, such as a curl cream or gel, to define your curls and keep them looking their best.
  7. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disrupting the curls.
  8. Finish off with a hairspray or styling spray to hold the look in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to keep your mens haircuts long on top curly hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls healthy and moisturized.
  • Don't overwash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet, as this will prevent breakage and frizz.
  • Invest in a good quality curl-enhancing product, such as a curl cream or gel, to keep your curls looking defined and frizz-free.
  • Try not to touch your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause the curls to become frizzy and lose their shape.

FAQ

What face shapes are best suited for mens haircuts long on top curly hair?

This style works well for most face shapes, but is particularly flattering for those with oval, square, or diamond-shaped faces.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Can I still get this style if my hair is not naturally curly?

Absolutely! You can use a curling iron or hot rollers to create curls or waves in your hair before following the step-by-step guide above.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your mens haircuts long on top curly hair looking its best. Here are some additional tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

Now that you know how to get mens haircuts long on top curly hair, it's time to head to the barber or stylist and try out this trendy style for yourself! Remember, with the right care and styling products, your curls can look their best every day.

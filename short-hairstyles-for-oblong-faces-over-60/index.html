Short Hairstyles For Oblong Faces Over 60

Written by Petter Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oblong face and are over 60, finding the perfect short hairstyle can be a challenge. However, there are many options that can flatter your face shape and make you look stylish and youthful. In this article, we will explore some of the best short hairstyles for oblong faces over 60, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips

Description

An oblong face is longer than it is wide, with a narrow chin and forehead. The goal when choosing a hairstyle is to create the illusion of width and balance out the proportions of the face. Short hairstyles are a great option as they can add volume and texture to the hair, making the face appear wider. Additionally, short hairstyles are low maintenance and easy to style, perfect for those over 60 who want to spend less time on their hair.

How To

When choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to consider your hair type and texture, as well as your personal style and preferences. Some popular short hairstyles for oblong faces over 60 include:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that can flatter an oblong face by adding volume and texture to the hair. This hairstyle is low maintenance and versatile, perfect for those on the go.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is another great option for oblong faces over 60. This hairstyle can be tailored to suit your face shape, with layers and angles to add volume and texture to the hair.

Short Shag

A short shag is a layered hairstyle that can add volume and texture to the hair, perfect for those with thin or fine hair. This hairstyle can be tailored to suit your face shape, with layers and angles to add volume and texture to the hair.

Step by Step

To style your short hair, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair.
  3. Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, lifting the roots to add volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and shape to your hair.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you choose and style the perfect short hairstyle:

  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best short hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.
  • Opt for layers and angles to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add extra body to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the perfect one for your hair type.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, clips, or scarves to add extra style and flair.

FAQ

What short hairstyles are best for oblong faces over 60?

Pixie cuts, bob cuts, and short shags are all great options for oblong faces over 60.

How do I style short hair?

Start with clean, dry hair and use a round brush and flat iron or curling iron to add texture and shape to your hair. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

How can I add volume to my short hair?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider adding layers and angles to your hairstyle to add volume and texture.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and healthy.

With these tips and ideas, you can choose a short hairstyle that flatters your oblong face and makes you look and feel your best. Whether you opt for a pixie cut, bob cut, or short shag, you can enjoy a low maintenance, stylish look that is perfect for those over 60.

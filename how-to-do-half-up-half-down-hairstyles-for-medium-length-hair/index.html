How To Do Half Up Half Down Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Robby Jul 31, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have medium length hair, you’re in luck! Half up half down hairstyles work great on medium length hair. This style can be dressed up or down and is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we’ll go over how to do half up half down hairstyles for medium length hair, step by step. We’ll also provide some tips and tricks to make your style last longer and answer some frequently asked questions about this popular hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Half Up Half Down Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to Do Half Up Half Down Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

To start, you’ll need a few basic tools. You’ll need a comb, some bobby pins, and some hair ties. You can also use a curling iron or straightener to add some waves or curls to your hair.

Begin by parting your hair down the middle. Take a section of hair from the front of your head on one side and twist it towards the back of your head. Secure it in place with a bobby pin. Repeat on the other side.

Next, take a section of hair from the back of your head and pull it up towards the crown of your head. Secure it in place with a hair tie. This is the “half up” part of the hairstyle.

Now, you can leave the rest of your hair down or you can add some curls or waves to it. If you want to add some waves, use a curling iron or straightener to create loose waves. If you want to keep your hair straight, you can just leave it as is.

Step by Step

1. Part your hair down the middle.

2. Take a section of hair from the front of your head on one side and twist it towards the back of your head. Secure it in place with a bobby pin.

3. Repeat step 2 on the other side.

4. Take a section of hair from the back of your head and pull it up towards the crown of your head. Secure it in place with a hair tie.

5. Leave the rest of your hair down or add some curls or waves to it.

Tips

- Use a comb to tease the hair at the crown of your head before pulling it back to create some volume.

- Use bobby pins that match the color of your hair to help them blend in.

- If you have thin or fine hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray to give your hair some extra body.

FAQ

Q: Can I do this hairstyle with short hair?

A: This hairstyle works best on medium length hair, but it can be adapted for shorter hair. Just make sure to leave some hair down in the back instead of pulling it all up.

Q: How can I make this hairstyle last longer?

A: Use hairspray to help keep your hair in place. You can also use a dry shampoo to absorb any oil and add some texture to your hair.

Q: Can I add accessories to this hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can add a headband or some hair clips to make the style more decorative.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to do half up half down hairstyles for medium length hair, you can experiment with different variations and find the perfect style for you. Have fun and enjoy your new look!

Short Hair For Long Face And Big Nose A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

If you have a long face and a big nose, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll explore some of the best short haircuts for long faces and big noses, as well as tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Hair
  3. Tips for Maintaining Short Hair
  4. FAQs About Short Hair for Long Face and Big Nose
  5. Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing a short hairstyle for a long face and big nose, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Look for styles that add volume to the sides of your face
  • Avoid styles that are too flat or straight
  • Consider bangs to draw attention away from your nose
  • Experiment with layers to add texture and movement

Some great short hairstyles for long faces and big noses include the pixie cut, bob, and layered bob. These styles are versatile and easy to maintain, making them perfect for busy women on the go.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Hair

If you're ready to take the plunge and cut your hair short, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Section off your hair into manageable sections
  3. Use a comb and scissors to cut your hair to your desired length
  4. Add layers and texture as desired
  5. Finish by styling your hair with a blow dryer or flat iron

Remember, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional stylist before making any major changes to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Once you've got your new short haircut, here are some tips for keeping it looking great:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat tools too often
  • Get regular trims to maintain your style
  • Consider using hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to switch up your look

FAQs About Short Hair for Long Face and Big Nose

Q: Will a short haircut make my nose look even bigger?

A: Not necessarily. By choosing the right style and adding volume to the sides of your face, you can create a balanced and flattering look.

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a short haircut?

A: Absolutely! Bangs can be a great way to draw attention away from your nose and add some extra style to your look.

Q: How often do I need to get my hair cut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, most short haircuts require a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Whether you have a long face, a big nose, or both, taking care of your hair is essential for keeping it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-drying
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors
  • Consider adding a hair serum or oil to your routine to add shine and prevent frizz

By following these tips and choosing the right short hairstyle, you can create a flattering and stylish look that enhances your natural beauty.

How To Cut Women s Curly Hair With Clippers At Home

Written by Christine Jun 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the salon? Do you want to learn how to cut your own curly hair with clippers at home? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you step by step on how to cut women's curly hair with clippers at home.

Description

Curly hair can be tricky to cut, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done at home. Clippers are a great tool to use for cutting curly hair because they can create a more even cut than scissors. However, it's important to be careful and take your time to avoid any mishaps.

How to Cut Women's Curly Hair with Clippers at Home

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need:

  • Clippers
  • Clipper guards of various sizes
  • Comb
  • Haircutting scissors
  • Sectioning clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair as you normally would. Make sure it's completely dry before you start cutting. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using sectioning clips. Start by separating your hair into two sections, one at the front and one at the back. Then, divide each section into smaller sections.

Step 4: Choose Your Guard Size

Choose the clipper guard size based on how much hair you want to cut. Start with a larger guard and work your way down to a smaller guard if necessary. It's always better to start with a larger guard and work your way down than to start with a smaller guard and accidentally cut too much hair.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair with the clippers, using the chosen guard size. Start at the back and work your way forward. Use the comb to lift the hair and run the clippers over it. Make sure to go slow and steady to avoid any mistakes.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

After you've finished cutting with the clippers, blend the hair using the scissors. This will give your hair a more natural look. Start at the back and work your way forward, cutting small pieces of hair at a time.

Step 7: Check for Any Unevenness

Check your hair for any unevenness. If you notice any, use the clippers with a smaller guard size or the scissors to fix it. It's important to take your time and make sure your hair is even.

Tips

  • Don't cut too much hair at once.
  • Take your time.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair before cutting.
  • Check for any unevenness as you go.
  • Blend the hair with scissors for a natural look.

FAQ

Can I cut my curly hair with scissors instead of clippers?

Yes, you can cut your curly hair with scissors, but clippers can create a more even cut.

Do I need to wet my hair before cutting?

No, you don't need to wet your hair, but it can make it easier to cut.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended to cut curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting your hair, make sure to take care of it properly. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. You can also use hair oil or a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls looking their best.

Now that you know how to cut women's curly hair with clippers at home, you can save money and time by doing it yourself. Just remember to take your time and be careful, and you'll have beautiful, even curls in no time!

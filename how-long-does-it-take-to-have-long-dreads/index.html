How Long Does It Take To Have Long Dreads

Written by Frank Aug 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you thinking of getting dreadlocks but wondering how long it will take for them to grow into long dreads? In this article, we'll answer that question and provide tips and advice for achieving and maintaining long dreadlocks.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get Long Dreads
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing Long Dreads
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Dreads

How to Get Long Dreads

Dreadlocks are a hairstyle that involves matting and twisting the hair into long, rope-like strands. To achieve long dreads, you'll need to let your hair grow to a certain length before starting the dreadlocking process. The length of time it takes to grow long dreads will depend on several factors, including:

  • Your hair texture
  • Your hair growth rate
  • Your hair care routine
  • The method you use to dreadlock your hair

Step-by-Step Guide to Growing Long Dreads

Here's a step-by-step guide to growing long dreads:

  1. Stop cutting your hair and let it grow to the desired length.
  2. Start washing your hair less frequently to allow natural oils to build up and help your hair lock up.
  3. Choose a method for dreadlocking your hair, such as the twist and rip method or the backcombing method.
  4. Divide your hair into sections and begin dreadlocking each section, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Maintain your dreads by washing them regularly with a residue-free shampoo, separating any sections that start to merge together, and palm rolling to keep them tight.
  6. Be patient and allow your dreads to grow and mature over time.

Tips for Growing Long Dreads

Here are some tips to help you grow long dreads:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Avoid using products that contain silicone, which can prevent the hair from locking up.
  • Protect your dreads from damage by wearing a hat or scarf when out in the sun or wind.
  • Avoid using heat tools and harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Be gentle when washing and handling your dreads to prevent breakage.

FAQs

How long does it take to grow long dreads?

The length of time it takes to grow long dreads will depend on several factors, including your hair texture, growth rate, and care routine. On average, it can take anywhere from 6 months to 2 years or more to grow long dreads.

Can I get long dreads if my hair is short?

If your hair is currently short, you'll need to wait until it reaches a certain length before you can start the dreadlocking process. However, you can use extensions or synthetic hair to create longer dreads if you don't want to wait for your natural hair to grow.

Hair Care for Long Dreads

Maintaining healthy, long dreadlocks requires a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips for caring for your long dreads:

  • Wash your dreads regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your dreads moisturized.
  • Palm roll your dreads regularly to keep them tight and prevent frizz.
  • Separate any sections that start to merge together to prevent them from combining into one large dread.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can leave residue in your dreads.

By following these tips and being patient, you can achieve and maintain long, healthy dreadlocks. Remember to enjoy the journey and embrace your unique style!

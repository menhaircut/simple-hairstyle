How To Thin Curtain Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your curtain bangs looking too thick and heavy? Thinning out your curtain bangs can help create a more natural and effortless look. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps on how to thin your curtain bangs at home.

Why Thin Your Curtain Bangs?

Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that can be worn by anyone with any hair texture. They are versatile, low-maintenance, and can be styled in various ways. However, curtain bangs that are too thick can overwhelm your face and make it look smaller. Thinning out your bangs can help create a more flattering and balanced look.

How to Thin Your Curtain Bangs

Here are the steps on how to thin out your curtain bangs:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair and bangs until they are completely dry.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section off your bangs from the rest of your hair.

Step 4: Use Thinning Shears

Using thinning shears, start at the ends of your bangs and work your way up towards the roots. Cut small sections at a time, making sure to blend the cut sections with the rest of your bangs.

Step 5: Check the Thickness

After cutting a few sections, check the thickness of your bangs by combing them down. If they still look too thick, continue thinning them out until you achieve your desired thickness.

Step 6: Style Your Bangs

Once you've thinned out your bangs, style them as desired. You can blow-dry them straight or use a curling iron to add some waves.

Tips for Thinning Your Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when thinning out your curtain bangs:

  • Start with small sections and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Always use thinning shears instead of regular scissors to avoid creating blunt edges.
  • Blend the cut sections with the rest of your bangs by combing them down.
  • Check the thickness of your bangs frequently to avoid over-thinning them.
  • Thinning out your bangs can make them shorter, so be careful not to cut off too much length.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about thinning out curtain bangs:

Can I thin out my bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still thin out your bangs if you have curly hair. However, it's best to do it when your hair is dry so you can see the natural curl pattern and avoid over-thinning.

Can I thin out my bangs if I have fine hair?

Yes, thinning out your bangs can actually help create the illusion of thicker hair. However, be careful not to over-thin your bangs as it can make your hair look stringy.

Do I need to go to a salon to thin out my bangs?

No, you can easily thin out your curtain bangs at home with the right tools and technique. However, if you're unsure or uncomfortable doing it yourself, it's best to seek the help of a professional stylist.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips on how to care for your curtain bangs:

  • Wash your hair and bangs regularly to avoid oil buildup.
  • Avoid using heavy products on your bangs as it can weigh them down.
  • Blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Thinning out your curtain bangs can help create a more natural and effortless look. With the right tools and technique, you can easily achieve your desired thickness and style. Remember to take your time and be careful not to over-thin your bangs. Happy styling!

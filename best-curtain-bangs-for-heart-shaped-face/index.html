Best Curtain Bangs For Heart Shaped Face

Written by Robby Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a cut that will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. One of the most popular hairstyles for heart-shaped faces is curtain bangs. This style is perfect for softening your facial features, highlighting your eyes, and creating a flattering frame for your face. In this article, we'll take a look at the best curtain bangs for heart-shaped faces, how to style them, and tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Get the Perfect Curtain Bangs
  3. Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Curtain bangs are long, wispy bangs that are parted down the middle, framing your face on either side. This style is perfect for heart-shaped faces because it draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones while softening your jawline. The key to the perfect curtain bangs is to make sure they are long enough to frame your face but not so long that they cover your eyes. A good rule of thumb is to have them end at the top of your cheekbones.

How to Get the Perfect Curtain Bangs

Getting the perfect curtain bangs for your heart-shaped face is all about finding the right length, thickness, and texture. You'll want to work with your stylist to find the perfect cut that will enhance your natural features and flatter your face shape. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a length that ends at the top of your cheekbones
  • Ask for a wispy texture that will soften your facial features
  • Make sure the bangs are thick enough to frame your face but not too thick that they overpower your features
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and volume

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow to get the perfect curtain bangs:

  1. Start by washing and blow-drying your hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle using a comb.
  3. Section off the hair you want to cut into bangs.
  4. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle, starting from the outside and working your way in.
  5. Repeat on the other side, making sure the length is even on both sides.
  6. Texturize the bangs using scissors or a razor to create a wispy, soft look.
  7. Style your bangs using a round brush and blow dryer, curling the ends away from your face.
  8. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curtain Bangs

Once you have your perfect curtain bangs, it's important to maintain them to keep them looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them at the right length.
  • Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your bangs away from your face.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh between washes.
  • Avoid using too much product on your bangs, as this can weigh them down and make them look greasy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

What face shape is best for curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs are perfect for heart-shaped faces, but they can also work well on oval and square faces.

What hair type is best for curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs can work well on all hair types, but they are especially flattering on fine to medium hair.

How do I style my curtain bangs?

Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your curtain bangs away from your face. You can also use a flat iron to create a sleek, straight look.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Here are some hair care tips to keep your curtain bangs looking great:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid over-washing and stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot tools on your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, if you have a heart-shaped face, curtain bangs are a great option for creating a flattering frame for your face. Keep these tips in mind when getting your cut and maintaining your new look, and you'll be sure to turn heads with your beautiful new hairstyle.

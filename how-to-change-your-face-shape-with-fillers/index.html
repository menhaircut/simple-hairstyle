How To Change Your Face Shape With Fillers

Written by Petter Jul 28, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

Are you unhappy with the shape of your face? Do you want to change it without undergoing surgery? You may be surprised to learn that fillers can be used to enhance or modify the shape of your face. In this article, we will discuss how to change your face shape with fillers, including the benefits, the process, and the potential risks.

Table of Contents

  1. Benefits of Using Fillers to Change Your Face Shape
  2. The Process of Changing Your Face Shape with Fillers
  3. Potential Risks of Using Fillers to Change Your Face Shape
  4. Tips for Getting the Best Results
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips After the Procedure

Benefits of Using Fillers to Change Your Face Shape

There are several benefits to using fillers to change your face shape:

  • Non-surgical: Unlike traditional plastic surgery, fillers do not require any incisions or general anesthesia. This means less downtime and a lower risk of complications.
  • Quick results: Fillers can provide immediate results, with some procedures taking as little as 15 minutes.
  • Customizable: Fillers can be tailored to your specific needs and desired outcomes.
  • Temporary: Most fillers are temporary, which means that if you do not like the results, they will eventually wear off.

The Process of Changing Your Face Shape with Fillers

The process of changing your face shape with fillers is relatively simple and straightforward:

  1. Consultation: Before the procedure, you will have a consultation with a qualified injector. During this consultation, you will discuss your desired outcomes and the best treatment options for achieving them.
  2. Preparation: On the day of the procedure, the injector will clean the treatment area and may apply a topical anesthetic to minimize discomfort.
  3. Injection: The injector will use a small needle to inject the filler into the targeted areas of your face. They may use a combination of different fillers to achieve the desired results.
  4. Aftercare: After the procedure, you may experience some swelling, bruising, and redness. You should avoid touching the treated areas and avoid strenuous exercise for at least 24 hours.

Potential Risks of Using Fillers to Change Your Face Shape

While fillers are generally safe, there are some potential risks to be aware of:

  • Bruising, swelling, and redness at the injection site
  • Allergic reaction
  • Infection
  • Asymmetry or uneven results
  • Migration of the filler

If you experience any of these symptoms, you should contact your injector immediately.

Tips for Getting the Best Results

To get the best results from your filler treatment, consider the following tips:

  • Choose a qualified, experienced injector
  • Be clear about your desired outcomes
  • Follow all pre- and post-treatment instructions carefully
  • Be patient and allow time for the filler to settle into place
  • Consider combining fillers with other treatments, such as Botox or laser therapy

FAQs

How long do fillers last?

The duration of fillers varies depending on the type of filler used and the individual patient. Most fillers last between 6 and 18 months.

Is the procedure painful?

Most patients report little to no pain during the procedure. However, a topical anesthetic may be used to minimize discomfort.

Can I return to work immediately after the procedure?

While you can return to work immediately after the procedure, you may experience some swelling, bruising, and redness. It is recommended that you take at least a day off to allow for recovery.

Hair Care Tips After the Procedure

After the procedure, it is important to take special care of your hair to avoid any irritation or infection:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours after the procedure
  • Avoid using any hair products that contain harsh chemicals or fragrances
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, for at least a week after the procedure
  • Avoid exposing your hair to sunlight or other sources of heat for at least a week after the procedure

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair stays healthy and beautiful while you recover from your filler treatment.

Conclusion

Fillers can be an effective and non-invasive way to change the shape of your face. By working with a qualified injector and following all pre- and post-treatment instructions, you can achieve the desired results safely and effectively. If you are considering fillers to change your face shape, be sure to discuss all of your options with your injector to determine the best treatment plan for you.

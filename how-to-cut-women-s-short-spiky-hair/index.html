How To Cut Women s Short Spiky Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short spiky hair is a great way to add an edgy and playful look to any woman's style. Whether you're into punk, alternative or just want to try something new, this hairstyle can be a great choice. However, cutting short spiky hair can be tricky and requires some specific techniques. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut women's short spiky hair.

How to Cut Women's Short Spiky Hair

If you're looking to cut women's short spiky hair, there are a few things you'll need to keep in mind. First, you'll need to choose the right tools, including a pair of sharp scissors, clippers, and a comb. You'll also need to have an idea of the style you want to create, as well as an understanding of how to use the tools to achieve that look.

Choose the Right Style

The first step in cutting women's short spiky hair is to choose the right style. There are many different styles to choose from, including a classic punk look, a messy layered style, or a sleek and modern cut. Once you've chosen a style, you'll need to gather the tools you'll need to achieve that look.

Section the Hair

The next step in cutting women's short spiky hair is to section the hair. You'll want to start at the back of the head and work your way forward, using a comb to create clean sections. This will help you keep the hair even and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Cut the Hair

Once you've sectioned the hair, it's time to start cutting. You can use scissors or clippers, depending on the style you're going for. If you're going for a messy or layered look, you can use scissors to create choppy layers. If you're going for a sleek and modern cut, you'll want to use clippers to create a clean, even cut.

Create the Spiky Look

The final step in cutting women's short spiky hair is to create the spiky look. You can do this by using a texturizing product, such as a hair wax or pomade. Apply the product to your fingers and run them through your hair, creating small spikes. You can also use a comb to create larger spikes, if you prefer.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut women's short spiky hair:

  1. Choose the right style
  2. Section the hair
  3. Cut the hair
  4. Create the spiky look

Step 1: Choose the Right Style

The first step in cutting women's short spiky hair is to choose the right style. You can do this by looking at pictures of different styles online, or by consulting with a hairstylist. Some popular styles include a classic punk look, a messy layered style, or a sleek and modern cut.

Step 2: Section the Hair

The next step is to section the hair. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward, using a comb to create clean sections. This will help you keep the hair even and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Once you've sectioned the hair, it's time to start cutting. You can use scissors or clippers, depending on the style you're going for. If you're going for a messy or layered look, you can use scissors to create choppy layers. If you're going for a sleek and modern cut, you'll want to use clippers to create a clean, even cut.

Step 4: Create the Spiky Look

The final step is to create the spiky look. You can do this by using a texturizing product, such as a hair wax or pomade. Apply the product to your fingers and run them through your hair, creating small spikes. You can also use a comb to create larger spikes, if you prefer.

Tips for Cutting Women's Short Spiky Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting women's short spiky hair:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Choose the right tools for the style you want to create
  • Be patient and take your time
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go
  • Experiment with different products to create different looks

FAQ about Cutting Women's Short Spiky Hair

How often should I cut my short spiky hair?

It's recommended to cut short spiky hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and keep the hair looking fresh.

Can I cut my own short spiky hair?

While it's possible to cut your own short spiky hair, it's recommended to visit a professional stylist for best results.

What products should I use to create the spiky look?

You can use a variety of products to create the spiky look, including hair wax, pomade, or gel.

Hair Care for Short Spiky Hair

Short spiky hair requires some specific hair care to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it
  • Use a texturizing product to create the spiky look
  • Visit a professional stylist for regular trims to maintain the style

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to cut women's short spiky hair like a pro. Whether you're looking for a classic punk look or a sleek and modern cut, this style is sure to turn heads and make a statement. So grab your tools and get ready to create a bold and edgy new look!

How To Tie Long Hair For Men A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Tying long hair for men can be challenging, especially if you're new to the long-hair game. But with a little bit of practice and some patience, you can create some fantastic hairstyles that will turn heads. In this article, we'll show you how to tie long hair for men in simple, easy-to-follow steps.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Tie Long Hair for Men
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Tying Long Hair for Men
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care Tips for Men with Long Hair

How to Tie Long Hair for Men

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the tools you'll need to tie long hair for men.

  • Hair ties or scrunchies
  • Bobby pins
  • Hair gel or pomade (optional)

Now that you have your tools ready, let's get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. If you have curly hair, you can use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage.

Step 2: Decide what type of hairstyle you want to create. Some popular options include a ponytail, bun, or half-up-half-down style.

Step 3: Gather your hair where you want to tie it. For a ponytail, this will be at the back of your head. For a bun, gather your hair into a high or low ponytail first.

Step 4: Use a hair tie or scrunchie to secure your hair in place. Make sure it's tight enough to hold your hair in place but not so tight that it's uncomfortable.

Step 5: If you're creating a bun, twist your ponytail around the hair tie and use bobby pins to secure it in place. For a half-up-half-down style, you can leave the bottom section of your hair down or tie it into a small ponytail.

Step 6: If you want to add some extra hold or shine to your hair, you can use a small amount of hair gel or pomade. Rub it between your hands and apply it to your hair, focusing on the ends.

Tips for Tying Long Hair for Men

Here are some additional tips to help you tie your long hair for men:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Don't tie your hair too tight, as this can cause breakage and discomfort.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands that may fall out of your hairstyle.
  • Try adding some texture to your hair by using a salt spray or texturizing paste.

FAQ

Q: How often should I tie my hair?

A: It's best to avoid tying your hair too often, as this can cause breakage and damage. Aim to tie your hair no more than once or twice a day.

Q: Can I tie my hair while it's wet?

A: It's best to avoid tying your hair while it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage. Wait until your hair is dry before tying it.

Q: Can I tie my hair if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can still tie your hair if you have thin hair. Just make sure to use a hair tie or scrunchie that's gentle on your hair and avoid tying it too tight.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Long Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for men with long hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners, as these can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to tie your long hair for men like a pro. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, there's a hairstyle that will work for you. So, start practicing and experiment with different styles to find your perfect look!

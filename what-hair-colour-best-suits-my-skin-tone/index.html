What Hair Colour Best Suits My Skin Tone

Written by Oliver Oct 30, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're thinking of changing your hair colour, it's important to know which shade will flatter your skin tone. Choosing the right hair colour can enhance your complexion and make you look more vibrant and youthful. In this article, we'll discuss how to determine your skin tone and which hair colours are best suited for each type.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair colour is to determine your skin tone. There are three main types of skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. Here's how to figure out which one you have:

Warm Skin Tone

If you have warm skin tone, your skin has a yellow or golden undertone. You may also have green veins on your wrist. The best hair colours for warm skin tones are rich shades of brown, copper, and red. These colours will bring out the warmth in your skin and make you look radiant.

Cool Skin Tone

If you have cool skin tone, your skin has a pink or blue undertone. You may also have blue veins on your wrist. The best hair colours for cool skin tones are ash brown, platinum blonde, and cool shades of red. These colours will complement your skin tone and make your features stand out.

Neutral Skin Tone

If you have neutral skin tone, your skin has a mix of warm and cool undertones. You may also have both blue and green veins on your wrist. The best hair colours for neutral skin tones are caramel, honey blonde, and warm shades of red. These colours will enhance your natural complexion and give you a healthy glow.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Now that you know your skin tone, here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the right hair colour:

  1. Determine your skin tone using the above guide
  2. Decide on the level of contrast you want between your hair and skin tone
  3. Consider your eye colour and natural hair colour
  4. Look at pictures of people with similar skin tone and hair colour
  5. Consult with a professional hairstylist

By following these steps, you'll be able to choose the perfect hair colour for your skin tone.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour

Once you've found the perfect hair colour, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair colour looking vibrant:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often

By following these tips, you'll be able to maintain your hair colour and keep it looking its best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair colour:

Q: Can I dye my hair a different colour if I have a warm skin tone?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair any colour you like. However, warm shades of brown, copper, and red will complement your skin tone the best.

Q: Can I dye my hair a different colour if I have a cool skin tone?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair any colour you like. However, ash brown, platinum blonde, and cool shades of red will complement your skin tone the best.

Q: Can I dye my hair a different colour if I have a neutral skin tone?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair any colour you like. However, caramel, honey blonde, and warm shades of red will enhance your natural complexion the best.

Hair Care for Coloured Hair

Coloured hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for taking care of your coloured hair:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often

By following these tips, you'll be able to keep your coloured hair looking its best.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. By determining your skin tone and following our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to choose the perfect hair colour for you. And by following our tips for maintaining and caring for coloured hair, you'll be able to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

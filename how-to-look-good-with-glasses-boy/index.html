How To Look Good With Glasses Boy A Guide For Fashionable Guys

Written by Bardi Sep 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a boy who wears glasses and wants to look your best? You're in luck because we've compiled some tips and tricks to help you rock your glasses and look stylish at the same time. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to choose the right frames to matching your glasses with your outfit. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Frames
  • Matching Your Glasses with Your Outfit
  • Hair Care Tips for Guys with Glasses
  • FAQ

How to Choose the Right Frames

The first step to looking good with glasses is choosing the right frames. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing frames, you want to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, go for square or rectangular frames to add some angles to your face. If you have a square face, try round or oval frames to soften your features.

Think About Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone can also play a role in choosing the right frames. If you have warm undertones, go for frames in brown, gold, or tortoise. If you have cool undertones, try frames in black, silver, or blue.

Choose Frames That Fit Your Personality

Ultimately, you want to choose frames that fit your personality and style. If you're more of a classic guy, go for wire frames or tortoise shell. If you like to make a statement, try bold frames in bright colors.

Matching Your Glasses with Your Outfit

Once you have the right frames, you want to make sure you're matching your glasses with your outfit. Here are some tips:

Match Your Glasses with Your Accessories

One way to tie your glasses into your outfit is to match them with your accessories. If you're wearing a brown belt, try brown frames. If you're wearing a silver watch, try silver frames.

Coordinate Your Colors

You can also coordinate your glasses with the colors in your outfit. If you're wearing a blue shirt, try blue frames. If you're wearing a green jacket, try green frames.

Keep It Simple

Remember, you don't want your glasses to overpower your outfit. Keep it simple and let your glasses be a subtle accessory.

Hair Care Tips for Guys with Glasses

Finally, you want to make sure your hair is on point when wearing glasses. Here are some tips:

Keep Your Hair Out of Your Face

If you have longer hair, make sure it's styled in a way that keeps it out of your face. This will prevent your hair from getting caught in your glasses or making them slip.

Choose a Hairstyle That Complements Your Frames

You also want to choose a hairstyle that complements your frames. If you have thicker frames, try a shorter, more polished hairstyle. If you have thinner frames, try a longer, messier hairstyle.

Experiment with Different Styles

Finally, don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to see what works best with your glasses. You might be surprised at what looks good!

FAQ

What are some popular glasses frames for guys?

Some popular glasses frames for guys include aviators, wayfarers, and round frames.

Can I wear glasses with a suit?

Absolutely! Glasses can be a stylish accessory to pair with a suit.

Should I wear my glasses all the time?

It's up to you! Some people choose to wear their glasses all the time, while others only wear them when they need them.

And there you have it - our guide to looking good with glasses for boys. Remember, the most important thing is to choose frames that fit your style and personality and to coordinate your glasses with your outfit. With these tips, you'll be a glasses-wearing fashion icon in no time!

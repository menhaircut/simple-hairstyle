How To Use Hair Thinning Scissors On Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short hair and want to add some texture to your style, hair thinning scissors can be a great tool to use. These scissors are designed to remove some of the bulk from your hair, creating a more layered and textured look. However, using thinning scissors on short hair can be tricky, so it's important to know how to use them properly. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of using hair thinning scissors on short hair, as well as provide some tips and answer some frequently asked questions about the process.

How to Use Hair Thinning Scissors on Short Hair

Before you get started, make sure you have a good pair of hair thinning scissors. These scissors have teeth on one blade, which allows them to remove hair without making a straight cut. You'll also need a comb, hair clips, and some patience. Here's how to use hair thinning scissors on short hair:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair as you normally would. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Use your comb to divide your hair into sections. Clip each section up and out of the way, leaving only the bottom section of hair loose.

Step 3: Use the Thinning Scissors

Take the thinning scissors and hold them parallel to the section of hair you want to thin out. Open and close the scissors as you move them down the length of the hair, removing some of the bulk as you go. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can create uneven layers.

Step 4: Repeat on the Remaining Sections

Release the next section of hair and repeat the process until you have thinned out all of the sections.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Use your regular scissors to blend the layers together, making sure that there are no harsh lines or uneven spots.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired, using a hair dryer, flat iron, or curling iron to create your desired look.

Tips for Using Hair Thinning Scissors on Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using hair thinning scissors on short hair:

  • Start small: It's better to remove too little hair than too much, so start with a small section and work your way up.
  • Use your fingers: Instead of using a comb to hold the hair, use your fingers to get a better feel for the hair and control over the scissors.
  • Be patient: Thinning out short hair can take some time, so be patient and don't rush the process.
  • Practice on a mannequin head: If you're new to using hair thinning scissors, practice on a mannequin head before trying it on your own hair.

FAQs About Using Hair Thinning Scissors on Short Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about using hair thinning scissors on short hair:

Can hair thinning scissors be used on all hair types?

Yes, hair thinning scissors can be used on all hair types, but they work best on thick or coarse hair.

How often should I use hair thinning scissors on my hair?

This depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows, but you can generally use hair thinning scissors every 6-8 weeks.

Do hair thinning scissors damage hair?

No, hair thinning scissors do not damage hair if used properly. However, if you use them too often or remove too much hair at once, you can create uneven layers or thin out your hair too much.

Hair Care After Using Thinning Scissors

After using hair thinning scissors on your hair, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as these can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair in good condition and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp healthy from the inside out.

Using hair thinning scissors on short hair can be a great way to add texture and dimension to your style. By following these steps and tips, you can use hair thinning scissors safely and effectively, and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Hairstyles For Over 60 With Double Chin

Written by Eveline Nov 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair and face shape change. One of the most common changes we experience is a double chin. A double chin can be caused by various factors such as genetics, weight gain, or simply aging. However, having a double chin doesn't mean you can't look fabulous! In this article, we'll be discussing some of the best hairstyles for over 60 with double chin. Whether you have short or long hair, straight or curly, there's a perfect hairstyle for you!

Description

Having a double chin can make you feel self-conscious, but there are many hairstyles that can minimize its appearance. The key is to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and draws attention away from your chin. In general, hairstyles with volume around the crown and length around the jawline are the most flattering for those with a double chin. However, there are many other factors to consider such as hair texture, thickness, and personal style.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are four basic face shapes: round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and minimizes the appearance of your double chin.

For those with a round face shape, a layered bob with volume at the crown and length around the jawline is a great option. This style creates the illusion of a longer face and draws attention away from the chin. Those with an oval face shape can pull off almost any hairstyle, but a shoulder-length cut with layers is particularly flattering. Square face shapes benefit from soft, wispy bangs and long layers that frame the face. Heart-shaped faces look great with side-swept bangs and chin-length bobs.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you have chosen the right hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the crown.
  4. Curl the ends of your hair using a curling iron or hot rollers.
  5. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and hold to your hairstyle.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you have the perfect hairstyle, you'll want to make sure it stays looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and smooth.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush and comb to prevent tangles and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if I have a double chin?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair long if you have a double chin. The key is to add volume at the crown and keep length around the jawline.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a double chin?

A: No, you don't need to avoid bangs if you have a double chin. Soft, wispy bangs can actually be quite flattering and draw attention away from the chin.

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have a double chin?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if you have a double chin. However, it's important to choose a hair color that flatters your skin tone and enhances your features.

Hair Care Tips for Over 60 with Double Chin

As we age, our hair and skin require more care and attention. Here are some hair care tips for those over 60 with a double chin:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and smooth.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair health.

With these hairstyles and hair care tips, you can look and feel your best at any age!

How To Cut Women s Curly Hair In Layers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to add dimension and movement to your curly hair, then layers are a great option. However, cutting curly hair in layers can be tricky. You don't want to end up with a choppy, uneven mess. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to cut women's curly hair in layers, so you can achieve a beautiful, bouncy look.

Table of Contents

  • Section 1: Preparation
  • Section 2: Cutting
  • Section 3: Styling
  • Section 4: Tips
  • Section 5: FAQ
  • Section 6: Hair Care

Section 1: Preparation

Before you start cutting, you'll need to prepare your hair. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Detangling comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle with water
  • Mirror

It's also a good idea to wash and condition your hair before cutting it. This will make it easier to detangle and cut.

Section 2: Cutting

Now it's time to start cutting. Follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair down the middle, and divide it into four sections: two in the front and two in the back.
  2. Starting with one of the back sections, use the detangling comb to comb through your hair. Then, take a small section of hair and twist it around your finger.
  3. Hold the twisted section between your fingers, and cut the ends with the scissors. Be sure to cut at an angle, so the layers blend together.
  4. Repeat this process with the rest of the sections, working your way up to the front.
  5. When you get to the front sections, you can decide how short you want the layers to be. You can either cut them at an angle, or you can use a technique called point cutting, where you hold the scissors vertically and cut into the hair at different lengths.

Section 3: Styling

Once you've finished cutting, it's time to style your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as mousse or gel.
  • Scrunch your hair with your hands, to encourage the curls to form.
  • Diffuse your hair with a hair dryer, using a low heat setting. This will help set the curls.
  • Finish with a light hairspray, to hold the style in place.

Section 4: Tips

Here are some additional tips for cutting women's curly hair in layers:

  • Start with small sections of hair, so you can control the length of the layers.
  • Don't cut too much off at once. You can always go back and cut more later.
  • When you're cutting the front sections, take your time and be careful. These sections are more visible, so mistakes will be more noticeable.

Section 5: FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting women's curly hair in layers:

Q: Can I cut my own hair in layers?

A: It's possible, but it can be difficult to achieve an even look. We recommend having a professional stylist cut your hair.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the layers lose their shape. We recommend getting a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Section 6: Hair Care

Now that you've cut your hair in layers, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as much as possible. These can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind, by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips, you can keep your curly hair looking beautiful and healthy.

Cutting women's curly hair in layers can be a bit intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a gorgeous, bouncy look. Remember to take your time, start with small sections of hair, and be careful when cutting the front sections. And don't forget to take care of your hair, so it stays healthy and beautiful.

How To Gel Men s Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Gel is one of the most popular hair products for men. It helps to create a sleek and polished look that can be easily styled into different hairstyles. However, gelling short hair can be a bit tricky, especially if you're new to using hair products. In this article, we'll provide you with some tips and tricks on how to gel men's short hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Gel Men's Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Gelling Men's Short Hair
  • FAQs

How to Gel Men's Short Hair

Gel is a versatile hair product that can be used to create a variety of hairstyles, from slicked-back looks to messy spikes. Here are the steps to follow when gelling men's short hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before applying gel, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Shampoo and condition your hair, then towel-dry it to remove any excess moisture. Avoid using a hairdryer, as it can damage your hair and make it harder to style.

Step 2: Choose the right type of gel

Not all gels are created equal, and some are better suited for short hair than others. Look for a gel that is lightweight and has a strong hold. Avoid gels that are too thick or heavy, as they can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Step 3: Apply the gel

Start by taking a small amount of gel and rubbing it between your palms to warm it up. Then, apply the gel evenly to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the tips. Use your fingers to style your hair as desired.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

To help your hairstyle stay in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will also help to add shine and control any flyaway hairs.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed, step-by-step guide on how to gel men's short hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair to remove any excess moisture. Avoid using a hairdryer, as it can damage your hair.
  3. Take a small amount of gel and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  4. Apply the gel evenly to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the tips. Use your fingers to style your hair as desired.
  5. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to help your hairstyle stay in place.

Tips for Gelling Men's Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when gelling men's short hair:

  • Use a small amount of gel to avoid weighing your hair down.
  • Start with a clean slate – avoid layering gel on top of old product buildup.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to use hairspray to help your hairstyle stay in place.

FAQs

Can I use gel on wet hair?

It's best to apply gel to dry hair, as wet hair can dilute the product and make it less effective. If your hair is still damp, use a towel to dry it before applying gel.

How often should I use gel on my hair?

It's best to use gel sparingly, as using too much can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy. Aim to use gel no more than once a day, and consider using a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hairdryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to gel men's short hair with ease and create a variety of stylish and polished looks. Remember to choose the right type of gel, apply it evenly, and finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place all day long.

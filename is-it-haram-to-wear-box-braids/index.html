Is It Haram To Wear Box Braids

Written by Bardi Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Box braids are a popular hairstyle among women of color. They are versatile, low maintenance, and can be styled in many different ways. However, there has been some debate in the Muslim community about whether or not box braids are haram. In this article, we will explore this topic in depth and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision about whether or not to wear box braids.

Description

Box braids are a type of protective hairstyle that involves braiding the hair into small, square-shaped sections. The braids can be made using synthetic hair or natural hair, and they can be styled in many different ways. Box braids are a popular hairstyle among women of color because they are low maintenance and can be worn for an extended period of time.

How to

If you are interested in getting box braids, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, it is important to find a stylist who is experienced in braiding hair. You should also make sure that your hair is clean and free of any products before getting your braids done. Finally, be sure to take good care of your braids by keeping them moisturized and avoiding any harsh chemicals or heat styling tools.

Step by Step

1. Find an experienced stylist who can braid your hair into box braids.

2. Make sure your hair is clean and free of any products before getting your braids done.

3. Sit in the stylist's chair and allow them to braid your hair into small, square-shaped sections.

4. Once your braids are done, take good care of them by keeping them moisturized and avoiding any harsh chemicals or heat styling tools.

Tips

If you are planning to wear box braids, there are a few tips you should keep in mind to ensure that your hair stays healthy and looks great:

- Keep your braids moisturized by using a leave-in conditioner and oil on a regular basis.

- Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your braids and cause damage to your hair.

- Be gentle when washing your hair to avoid frizz and breakage.

FAQ

1. Is it haram to wear box braids?

There is some debate in the Muslim community about whether or not box braids are haram. Some believe that the hairstyle is not in line with Islamic values, while others believe that it is permissible as long as the hair is covered.

2. Can I wear box braids if I am Muslim?

Ultimately, the decision to wear box braids is up to you. If you are comfortable wearing this hairstyle and believe that it is permissible under Islamic law, then there is no reason why you cannot wear box braids.

3. How can I keep my box braids looking great?

To keep your box braids looking great, it is important to take good care of them. This includes keeping them moisturized, avoiding tight hairstyles, and being gentle when washing your hair.

Hair Care

If you decide to wear box braids, it is important to take good care of your hair to avoid damage and breakage. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair healthy:

- Use a leave-in conditioner and oil to keep your hair moisturized.

- Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair.

- Be gentle when washing your hair to avoid frizz and breakage.

- Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your braids and cause damage to your hair.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there is no clear answer to whether or not box braids are haram. Ultimately, the decision to wear this hairstyle is up to you and should be based on your own personal beliefs and values. If you do decide to wear box braids, be sure to take good care of your hair to avoid damage and breakage.

How To Style Medium Choppy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Oct 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have medium choppy hair, you know that it can be a challenge to style. Whether you're going for a sleek and sophisticated look or a tousled, messy style, these tips and tricks will help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

Medium choppy hair is a popular hairstyle that features layers of various lengths, giving the hair a choppy, textured look. This style is versatile and can be worn straight, curled, or in messy, beachy waves. However, styling medium choppy hair can be tricky, as the choppy layers can make it difficult to achieve a polished look. With the right tools and techniques, though, you can create a variety of stunning styles that show off your choppy locks.

How to Style Medium Choppy Hair

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to styling medium choppy hair is to start with clean, well-conditioned locks. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Make sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid any product buildup that can weigh down your locks.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

If you plan on using heat styling tools, it's important to protect your hair from damage. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your damp hair before blow-drying or using a flat iron or curling iron. This will help prevent breakage and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to dry your hair, working in sections from the back of your head to the front. Use the brush to smooth your hair and add volume and body to your locks. If you want a sleek and sophisticated look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair after blow drying.

Step 4: Add Texture

To play up the choppy texture of your locks, use a texturizing spray or pomade. Rub a small amount of product between your palms and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends and the layers around your face. This will add definition and texture to your hair, giving it a tousled, messy look.

Step 5: Create Waves

If you want a beachy, wavy look, use a curling iron to create loose waves in your hair. Wrap sections of hair around the barrel of the curling iron and hold for a few seconds before releasing. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a relaxed, tousled look.

Tips for Styling Medium Choppy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your medium choppy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair after washing, as medium choppy hair can be prone to tangling.
  • If you have fine or thin hair, avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your locks.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the look that best suits you and your personality.
  • Visit a professional stylist for regular trims to keep your choppy layers looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

What is medium choppy hair?

Medium choppy hair is a layered hairstyle that features various lengths of hair to create a choppy, textured look.

How do I style my medium choppy hair?

To style your medium choppy hair, start with clean, well-conditioned locks. Apply a heat protectant and blow dry your hair with a round brush. Use a texturizing product to add definition and texture, and create loose waves with a curling iron for a relaxed, tousled look.

What are some tips for styling medium choppy hair?

Some tips for styling medium choppy hair include using a wide-tooth comb or fingers to detangle hair, avoiding heavy styling products for fine or thin hair, experimenting with different styles, and visiting a stylist for regular trims.

Hair Care for Medium Choppy Hair

To keep your medium choppy hair looking its best, it's important to follow a healthy hair care routine. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your locks hydrated and healthy. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant when you do. Visit a professional stylist regularly for trims and to maintain the health of your hair.

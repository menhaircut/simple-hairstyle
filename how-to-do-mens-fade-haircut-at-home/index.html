How To Do Men s Fade Haircut At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 23, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of spending a lot of money at the barbershop for a fade haircut? Or are you just looking for a way to maintain your haircut during quarantine? Whatever your reason is, you can now learn how to do a men's fade haircut at home. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect fade haircut without leaving your house.

How to

The first step in doing a fade haircut at home is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of clippers, different clipper guards, scissors, a comb, a mirror, and a towel. Once you have all your tools, you can start the process.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by washing your hair and drying it with a towel. Make sure it's completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Decide on the length of your fade. You can choose to have a low, mid, or high fade. The lower the fade, the longer the hair will be at the top, and the higher the fade, the shorter the hair will be at the top.

Step 3: Use the clipper guard to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. Start with the longest guard and work your way down to the shortest. Make sure you blend the hair as you go by using different guards.

Step 4: Once you've finished the sides and back, move on to the top. Use scissors to trim the hair to your desired length.

Step 5: Blend the top with the sides and back using the clippers and different guards.

Step 6: Create the fade by gradually decreasing the length of the hair as you move towards the neckline. Use a lower guard to trim the hair around the neckline.

Step 7: Clean up any stray hairs using the clippers and scissors.

Step 8: Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Tip 1: Take your time and be patient. A good fade takes time and practice to achieve.

Tip 2: Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.

Tip 3: Start with a higher guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.

FAQ

Q: What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

A: Don't panic. You can always fix it by blending the hair or trimming it to a shorter length.

Q: Can I use any type of clippers for a fade haircut?

A: It's recommended to use clippers specifically designed for fades to achieve the best results.

Hair Care

Now that you've learned how to do a men's fade haircut at home, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Make sure to wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it healthy and hydrated. You can also use hair styling products to keep your hair in place and looking good.

With this step-by-step guide, you can now achieve a perfect fade haircut without leaving your house. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun. Happy cutting!

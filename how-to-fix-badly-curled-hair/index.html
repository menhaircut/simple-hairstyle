How To Fix Badly Curled Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 27, 2023 · 4 min read
Table of Content: 1. Introduction 2. Causes of Badly Curled Hair 3. How to Fix Badly Curled Hair 4. Step-by-Step Guide to Fixing Badly Curled Hair 5. Tips for Fixing Badly Curled Hair 6. Frequently Asked Questions About Fixing Badly Curled Hair 7. Hair Care Tips to Prevent Badly Curled Hair 8. Conclusion

Introduction

Badly curled hair is a common problem that many people face. It can be frustrating when you spend hours curling your hair, only for it to fall flat or look frizzy. However, there are ways to fix badly curled hair and achieve the desired look. In this article, we will discuss the causes of badly curled hair, how to fix it, and provide some tips and hair care advice to prevent it from happening again.

Causes of Badly Curled Hair

Badly curled hair can be caused by several factors, including: 1. Using the wrong curling iron: If you use a curling iron that is too small or too large for your hair, it can lead to badly curled hair. 2. Using too much product: Using too much hairspray or mousse can weigh down your hair, causing your curls to fall flat. 3. Not using heat protectant: If you don't use heat protectant before curling your hair, it can damage your hair and cause it to look frizzy or limp. 4. Humidity: Humidity can cause your hair to lose its curl and become frizzy.

How to Fix Badly Curled Hair

If you have badly curled hair, don't worry, there are ways to fix it. Here are some methods to fix badly curled hair: 1. Use a straightener: If your curls are too tight or frizzy, you can use a straightener to smooth out your hair. Start at the roots and slowly glide the straightener down to the ends of your hair. 2. Use a curling iron: If your curls have fallen flat, you can use a curling iron to touch up your hair. Choose a curling iron that is the same size as your original curls. 3. Use a hairbrush: If your curls are too tight, you can use a hairbrush to loosen them up. Brush your hair gently from the roots to the ends.

Step-by-Step Guide to Fixing Badly Curled Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to fixing badly curled hair: 1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. 2. Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. 3. Use a straightener or curling iron to touch up your curls. 4. Apply a small amount of hairspray to hold your curls in place. 5. Use a hairbrush to gently loosen your curls. 6. Finish off with a light mist of hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Fixing Badly Curled Hair

Here are some tips to help you fix badly curled hair: 1. Use a heat protectant before curling your hair to prevent damage. 2. Choose the right size curling iron for your hair type. 3. Use a small amount of product to prevent your hair from becoming weighed down. 4. Don't overheat your hair with a curling iron or straightener.

Frequently Asked Questions About Fixing Badly Curled Hair

1. How can I prevent my hair from becoming badly curled in the first place? Answer: Use a heat protectant, choose the right size curling iron, and don't overuse product. 2. How often should I curl my hair to prevent damage? Answer: It's best to limit heat styling to once a week to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Badly Curled Hair

Here are some hair care tips to prevent badly curled hair: 1. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy. 2. Don't wash your hair every day to prevent it from becoming dry. 3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

Conclusion

Badly curled hair can be frustrating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be fixed. Use this guide to help you fix your badly curled hair and prevent it from happening in the future. Remember to take care of your hair with proper hair care techniques to keep it healthy and beautiful.

How To Style Short Hair Easily

Written by Oliver Nov 13, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have short hair, you might think that styling it is difficult. But with the right techniques and tools, you can easily create beautiful hairstyles that will make you look and feel great. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks for styling short hair easily.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Style Short Hair

The first step in styling short hair is to choose the right haircut. If you’re not sure which haircut is best for your face shape, consult with your hairstylist. Once you have the right haircut, you can start experimenting with different styles.

Some popular hairstyles for short hair include the pixie cut, the bob, and the lob. You can also try adding layers or bangs to your haircut to create more texture and volume.

Tools You’ll Need

Before you start styling your hair, make sure you have the right tools. Here are some tools you’ll need:

  • Hairbrush or comb
  • Hairspray
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Straightening iron
  • Curling iron
  • Bobby pins

Step by Step Guide

Now that you have the right tools, here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style short hair easily:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair.
  4. Use a straightening iron to create sleek, straight hair. Or, use a curling iron to create curls or waves.
  5. Add volume to your hair by teasing the roots with a comb.
  6. Create a messy bun or ponytail by pulling your hair back and securing it with bobby pins.
  7. Create a side part and add a hair accessory, such as a headband or hair clip.
  8. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Here are some tips to make styling short hair easier:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Add texture and volume to your hair by using a volumizing spray or mousse.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

What haircut is best for short hair?

The best haircut for short hair depends on your face shape and personal style. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for you.

How often should I wash my short hair?

You should wash your short hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from getting too oily.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily style your short hair and look your best every day.

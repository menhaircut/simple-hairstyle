How To Curl Short Hair Korean Style

Written by Bardi Jul 08, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have short hair and want to try something new, why not try the Korean style curls? This trendy hairstyle can make your hair look voluminous and bouncy, perfect for any occasion. In this article, we’ll show you how to curl short hair Korean style in relaxed English language. We’ll cover everything from the tools you need to the step-by-step process, tips, FAQs, and hair care. So, let’s get started!

  • Step-by-Step Process

Tools Needed

Before we start, you’ll need to have the right tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Curling iron or wand with a small barrel (around 1 inch)
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • Comb or brush
  • Sectioning clips

How to Curl Short Hair Korean Style

The Korean style curls are all about creating loose, natural-looking waves. Here’s how you can achieve this look:

Step-by-Step Process

Follow these steps to curl your short hair the Korean way:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the heat.
  2. Section your hair into small sections using clips. Start with the bottom layers and work your way up.
  3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand. Hold it for a few seconds, then release.
  4. Repeat step 3 for all sections of your hair. Make sure to curl away from your face for a natural look.
  5. Once all sections are curled, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a natural look.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Korean Style Curls

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect Korean style curls:

  • Use a small curling iron or wand for tighter curls.
  • Wrap your hair loosely around the curling iron for natural-looking curls.
  • Curl your hair away from your face for a natural look.
  • Use your fingers to separate the curls for a more natural look.
  • Finish with a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling short hair Korean style:

  • Can I curl my short hair without a curling iron? You can try using a straightener to curl your hair, or try using foam rollers overnight.
  • How long will the curls last? The curls can last for up to a day, but may loosen up over time.
  • How often should I curl my hair? It’s best to avoid curling your hair too often to prevent damage. Once a week is a good rule of thumb.

Hair Care for Korean Style Curls

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after curling it Korean style:

  • Use a nourishing hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent dryness.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray, as it can weigh down your hair.

That’s it! You now know how to curl short hair Korean style. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect natural-looking curls. Don’t forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful!

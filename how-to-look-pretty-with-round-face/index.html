How To Look Pretty With Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face, you might feel like it's hard to look pretty or elegant. But don't worry! There are many ways you can enhance your natural beauty and look your best every day. In this blog post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to look pretty with round face, including hairstyle ideas, makeup tips, and more.

Hair Care

First and foremost, taking care of your hair is essential to looking pretty and put-together. You don't need to have long, flowing locks to look beautiful - even a simple bob or pixie cut can look great on a round face. However, it's important to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained. This means washing your hair regularly, using a good conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible.

Regular trims are also important to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. If you have a round face, consider getting layers or side-swept bangs to add some dimension to your hair and frame your face nicely.

Understanding Your Face Shape

Before we dive into specific tips and tricks, it's important to understand your face shape. A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded jawline, and a face that is roughly the same width and length.

When it comes to makeup and hairstyles, the goal is typically to create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. This means adding height to your hair or using contouring techniques to create shadows and highlights on your face.

Makeup Tips

When it comes to makeup, there are a few tips and tricks you can use to enhance your natural beauty and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Here are a few ideas:

  • Use a dark contour shade to create shadows under your cheekbones and along your jawline. This will help to define your features and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.
  • Highlight the center of your face, including your forehead, nose, and chin. This will draw attention to the center of your face and create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face.
  • Avoid applying blush directly to the apples of your cheeks. Instead, sweep it up towards your temples to create a more lifted, elongated look.
  • Focus on your eyes and lips to draw attention away from your cheeks. Bold lipstick or eyeshadow can be a great way to make a statement and distract from any insecurities you may have about your face shape.

Hairstyle Ideas

When it comes to hairstyles, there are a few key things to keep in mind if you have a round face. Here are some ideas:

  • Consider getting layers or side-swept bangs to add some dimension and frame your face nicely.
  • Avoid blunt cuts or straight bangs, which can make your face appear even rounder.
  • Try a high ponytail or top knot to add height to your hair and create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension in your hair.

Clothing Tips

When it comes to clothing, there are a few tips you can use to flatter your round face. Here are some ideas:

  • Choose tops with v-necklines or scoop necklines to create the illusion of a longer neck and a more elongated face.
  • Avoid turtlenecks or high-necked tops, which can make your face appear even rounder.
  • Choose structured blazers or jackets to create definition in your upper body.
  • Consider wearing statement jewelry or accessories to draw attention away from your face.

Accessories

Accessories can be a great way to add some personality to your look and draw attention away from your face shape. Here are some ideas:

  • Wear statement earrings or necklaces to draw attention to other parts of your body.
  • Choose sunglasses with angular frames to create the illusion of a more angular face shape.
  • Avoid round hats or caps, which can make your face appear even rounder.

Confidence is Key

At the end of the day, the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin. Don't let your face shape hold you back - embrace your natural beauty and find the styles and looks that work best for you.

Best Hair Color For Over Sixty Tips And Recommendations

Written by Christine Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair naturally loses pigmentation and becomes gray or white. However, many women over the age of sixty choose to color their hair to enhance their appearance and boost their confidence. In this article, we will share some tips and recommendations for the best hair color for over sixty, including how to choose the right shade, how to apply the color, and how to care for your hair after coloring.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hair Color
  • Hair Color Recommendations for Over Sixty
  • How to Apply Hair Color
  • Hair Care Tips After Coloring
  • FAQs

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but it's essential to consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and personal preferences. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • Consider your skin tone: If you have warm-toned skin, choose warm hair colors like golden blonde, caramel, or copper. If you have cool-toned skin, go for cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum, or burgundy.
  • Choose a shade close to your natural hair color: If you're not ready to make a dramatic change, choose a shade that's close to your natural hair color. This will give you a natural-looking result and make the transition less noticeable.
  • Think about maintenance: If you're not willing to spend a lot of time and money on maintenance, choose a color that's close to your natural hair color or a low-maintenance option like highlights or balayage.

Hair Color Recommendations for Over Sixty

When it comes to hair color for over sixty, there are many options to choose from. Here are some recommendations:

  • Soft blonde: Soft blonde shades like honey or caramel can brighten up your complexion and make you look younger.
  • Rich brown: Rich brown shades like chocolate or chestnut can add depth and dimension to your hair and complement your skin tone.
  • Ash blonde: Ash blonde shades like platinum or silver can create a chic and sophisticated look and complement cool-toned skin.
  • Burgundy: Burgundy shades like wine or magenta can add a pop of color and create a bold and trendy look.

How to Apply Hair Color

If you're coloring your hair at home, it's essential to follow the instructions carefully and use the right tools. Here are some tips to help you apply hair color:

  • Wear gloves to protect your hands from staining.
  • Apply the color to dry hair and divide it into sections.
  • Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.
  • Leave the color on for the recommended time and rinse thoroughly.
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips After Coloring

After coloring your hair, it's essential to take care of it properly to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading and maintain the color.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent stripping the color.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs

1. Can I color my hair if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, but it's essential to choose a hair color that's free of harsh chemicals and irritants. Look for products that are labeled "gentle" or "natural" and do a patch test before applying the color to your hair.

2. How often should I color my hair?

It depends on the type of hair color and how fast your hair grows. Generally, permanent hair color should be touched up every four to six weeks, while semi-permanent hair color can last up to six to eight weeks.

3. Can I color my hair at home?

Yes, but it's essential to follow the instructions carefully and use the right tools. If you're not confident in your ability to color your hair at home, it's best to visit a professional stylist.

4. Can I color my hair if it's already been colored?

Yes, but it's essential to choose a hair color that's compatible with your current color and avoid overlapping the color. If you're not sure, it's best to visit a professional stylist.

Coloring your hair can be a fun and exciting way to enhance your appearance and boost your confidence. With these tips and recommendations for the best hair color for over sixty, you can find the perfect shade and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

How To Apply Product To 2C Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to apply product to 2c hair! If you have 2c hair, you know that it can be challenging to find the right hair products that work for your hair type. In this guide, we will walk you through the process of applying hair products to your 2c hair for the best results. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Apply Product to 2c Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Applying Product to 2c Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for 2c Hair

Description

2c hair is a type of curly hair that falls somewhere between wavy and curly. It has defined waves and curls, but they are looser and less defined than other curly hair types. 2c hair can be prone to frizz and dryness, so it’s important to use the right hair products to keep it looking healthy and defined.

How to Apply Product to 2c Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let’s talk about the types of hair products that work best for 2c hair. 2c hair needs hydration and moisture, so look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and glycerin. Here are some of the best types of hair products for 2c hair:

  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Curl defining cream
  • Gel
  • Hair oil

Step-by-Step Guide

Now let’s walk through the process of applying hair products to your 2c hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. You can either wash your hair or wet it with a spray bottle.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. Use a quarter-sized amount for shoulder-length hair and adjust the amount as needed for longer or shorter hair.
  3. Apply a curl defining cream to your hair. Use a dime-sized amount for shoulder-length hair and adjust the amount as needed for longer or shorter hair. Work the product into your hair from roots to ends.
  4. Scrunch your hair to enhance your curls. Do this by squeezing sections of your hair from the ends up to your roots. This will help to define your curls and create volume.
  5. If you want extra hold, apply a gel to your hair. Use a pea-sized amount for shoulder-length hair and adjust the amount as needed for longer or shorter hair. Work the gel into your hair from roots to ends.
  6. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser on low heat to dry your hair. Do not touch your hair while it is drying to avoid frizz.
  7. Once your hair is dry, you can use a hair oil to add shine and moisture. Use a pea-sized amount and work it into your hair from mid-length to ends.

Tips for Applying Product to 2c Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when applying hair products to your 2c hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before applying products.
  • Apply products to your hair in sections to ensure even distribution.
  • Do not use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and create a greasy look.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about applying product to 2c hair:

Q: How often should I apply product to my 2c hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and how often you wash your hair. Generally, you can apply products to your hair every 2-3 days or as needed to refresh your curls.

Q: Can I apply product to dry hair?

A: It’s best to apply hair products to damp hair, as this allows the product to penetrate your hair shaft and provide maximum hydration.

Q: Can I use the same products on my 2c hair that I use on other hair types?

A: It’s best to use products that are specifically designed for 2c hair, as this hair type requires specific ingredients to keep it healthy and defined.

Hair Care for 2c Hair

In addition to applying the right hair products, it’s important to practice good hair care habits to keep your 2c hair healthy and defined. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and using the right hair products, you can keep your 2c hair looking healthy, defined, and beautiful!

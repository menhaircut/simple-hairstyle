How To Do Bridal Carry A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're getting married soon, you might be wondering how to do bridal carry. This classic pose involves the groom carrying the bride in his arms, and it's a popular choice for wedding photos. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you and your partner nail the bridal carry pose.

Description

The bridal carry pose is a romantic and intimate pose that is often seen in wedding photos. The groom picks up the bride in his arms, and they look into each other's eyes as if they're the only two people in the world. It's a pose that requires some strength and coordination, but it's definitely worth it for the stunning photos you'll get.

How to Do Bridal Carry

Here's how to do bridal carry:

Step 1: Stand Facing Each Other

Start by standing facing each other. The groom should stand with his feet shoulder-width apart and the bride should stand in front of him.

Step 2: Pick Up the Bride

The groom should bend his knees slightly and reach his arms around the bride's waist. He should lift her up into his arms, cradling her against his chest. The bride should wrap her arms around the groom's neck and hold on tightly.

Step 3: Adjust Your Position

Once the groom has lifted the bride, he should adjust his position so that he's holding her securely. He should make sure his feet are firmly planted on the ground and that he has a good grip on the bride.

Step 4: Pose for Photos

Once you're both comfortable and secure in the bridal carry pose, it's time to pose for photos. Look into each other's eyes and smile, and don't forget to have fun!

Tips for Doing Bridal Carry

Here are some tips to help you and your partner nail the bridal carry pose:

  • Practice before the wedding day to make sure you're both comfortable with the pose.
  • The groom should use his legs to lift the bride, not just his arms.
  • The bride should hold on tightly to the groom's neck and keep her body close to his.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Bridal carry can be tiring, especially if you're wearing a heavy dress or suit.

FAQ About Bridal Carry

What is the Bridal Carry Pose?

The bridal carry pose is a classic pose in which the groom carries the bride in his arms. It's often used in wedding photos and is a romantic and intimate pose.

How Do You Do Bridal Carry?

To do bridal carry, the groom should stand facing the bride, bend his knees slightly, and lift her up into his arms. The bride should wrap her arms around the groom's neck and hold on tightly.

Is Bridal Carry Difficult?

Bridal carry can be difficult if you're not used to lifting heavy objects. However, with practice and proper form, it's a pose that can be done by most people.

Hair Care Tips for Bridal Carry

If you're planning to do the bridal carry pose on your wedding day, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking great:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to style your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair on the day of your wedding. Day-old hair is easier to style and holds curls better.
  • Consider using a hair accessory, like a headband or hairpin, to keep your hair in place during the bridal carry pose.
  • Use a hairspray or hair gel to keep your style in place throughout the day.

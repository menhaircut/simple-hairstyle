How To Wear Curly Hair Over 60 A Step By Step Guide

If you're over 60 and have curly hair, you might be wondering how to style it to look trendy and youthful. Fortunately, there are plenty of ways to wear curly hair that flatters mature women. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the process of styling your curly hair to enhance your natural beauty and confidence.

How to Wear Curly Hair Over 60

There are many ways to wear curly hair over 60. The key is to choose a style that works for your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some popular options:

Short Curly Bob

A short curly bob is a classic and chic hairstyle that flatters women of all ages. It's easy to maintain and style, making it perfect for busy seniors. To get this look, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair above the shoulders and add layers to create volume and movement. Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and add shine.

Long Curly Layers

If you prefer longer hair, consider adding layers to create dimension and texture. This style is perfect if you have thick hair that tends to get frizzy. To style, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

Cropped Curly Pixie

A cropped curly pixie is a bold and edgy hairstyle that looks great on mature women. It's low maintenance and highlights your facial features, making it a perfect option for those with a strong bone structure. To style, apply a curl-defining product to damp hair and scrunch your curls with your fingers.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the different options for wearing curly hair over 60, here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. Rinse thoroughly and gently blot your hair with a microfiber towel to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply a curl-enhancing product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair, such as a curl cream, mousse, or gel. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a diffuser attachment

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and set it to a low heat setting. Dry your hair by placing the diffuser near your scalp and using circular motions to lift and enhance your curls.

Step 4: Finish with a hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish with a light hairspray to hold your curls in place. Avoid using too much hairspray, as it can weigh down your curls and make them look stiff.

Tips for Curly Hair Over 60

Here are some additional tips to help you wear curly hair over 60:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and reduce frizz.

FAQs

Can I dye my curly hair over 60?

Yes, you can dye your curly hair at any age. However, be aware that hair dye can be drying and damaging to your hair. To minimize damage, use a deep conditioning treatment before and after dyeing your hair, and choose a dye that's formulated for curly hair.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once a week or every other week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. Use your judgment and listen to your hair's needs.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Over 60

In addition to styling your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can wear your curly hair over 60 with confidence and style. Embrace your natural beauty and enjoy your hair journey!

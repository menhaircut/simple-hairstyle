How To Get Messy Hairstyle A Complete Guide

Are you tired of perfectly coiffed hairstyles and looking to switch things up? Messy hairstyles are the perfect way to add some edge and texture to your look. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to get messy hairstyle. We will also cover some tips, FAQs, and hair care information to keep your locks healthy and looking great.

How to Get Messy Hairstyle

Getting a messy hairstyle is all about embracing imperfection. The key is to create a look that appears effortless, even if it takes a bit of work to achieve. Here are some basic steps to get started:

1. Start with Clean Hair

The first step to creating a messy hairstyle is to start with clean, dry hair. This will help your hair hold its shape and texture. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some extra lift and body to your locks.

2. Use a Texturizing Spray

Next, apply a texturizing spray to your hair. This will help create the gritty, lived-in look that is characteristic of messy hairstyles. Spray the product onto your roots and mid-lengths, then use your fingers to work it through your hair.

3. Add Some Waves

Creating some waves in your hair can add some much-needed texture to a messy hairstyle. You can use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or curls throughout your hair. Alternatively, you can braid your hair while it's damp and let it air dry for a more natural-looking wave.

4. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is an essential step in achieving a messy hairstyle. Use a fine-tooth comb to backcomb small sections of your hair at the crown. This will create some volume and texture at the roots.

5. Finish with Hairspray

To lock in your messy hairstyle, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Use a flexible hold hairspray to ensure that your hair retains its shape without looking stiff or crunchy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of creating a messy hairstyle, let's break down the process into a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add some body and texture to your locks.

Step 2: Apply Texturizing Spray

Next, spray a texturizing spray throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair.

Step 3: Create Some Waves

Use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or curls throughout your hair. Alternatively, you can braid your hair while it's damp and let it air dry for a more natural-looking wave.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Use a fine-tooth comb to backcomb small sections of your hair at the crown. This will create some volume and texture at the roots.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To lock in your messy hairstyle, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Use a flexible hold hairspray to ensure that your hair retains its shape without looking stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Messy Look

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect messy hairstyle:

1. Don't Overdo It

The key to a great messy hairstyle is to keep it looking effortless. Don't overdo it with the product or tease your hair too much, or you risk looking like you're trying too hard.

2. Embrace Flyaways

Flyaways are a natural part of a messy hairstyle. Don't try to tame them too much, as they add to the overall look and texture of your hair.

3. Use Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo can be a lifesaver when it comes to creating a messy hairstyle. Use it to absorb excess oil and add some extra texture to your hair.

FAQs

What hair types work best for messy hairstyles?

Messy hairstyles work well on a variety of hair types, from fine to thick hair. However, if you have very curly or coarse hair, you may need to use more product to achieve the desired look.

Can I achieve a messy hairstyle without using heat tools?

Absolutely! You can create waves in your hair by braiding it while it's damp and letting it air dry. You can also use a texturizing spray and scrunch your hair to create some volume and texture.

Hair Care Tips for Messy Hairstyles

To keep your messy hairstyle looking great, here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a Clarifying Shampoo

Because messy hairstyles often involve a lot of product, it's important to use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup from your hair.

2. Avoid Over-Washing

While it's important to start with clean hair when creating a messy hairstyle, over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and lead to dryness and damage. Try to limit your hair washing to every other day, or use dry shampoo in between washes.

3. Use a Heat Protectant

If you're using heat tools to create waves in your hair, it's important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect messy hairstyle in no time. Remember to have fun with it and embrace imperfection!

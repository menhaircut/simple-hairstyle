How To Find The Best Haircut For Your Face Shape

Written by Frank Jun 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a new haircut, it’s important to consider your face shape. The right haircut can enhance your features and make you feel confident and beautiful. In this article, we’ll show you how to find the best haircut for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Right Haircut
  • Tips for Choosing the Best Haircut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best haircut for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Here’s how to determine your face shape:

  • Round: Your face is about as wide as it is long, with full cheeks and a rounded jawline.
  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a slightly rounded jawline and a gently curved forehead.
  • Square: Your face is about as wide as it is long, with a strong, angular jawline.
  • Heart: Your face is wider at the forehead and temple area, narrowing down to a pointed chin.
  • Diamond: Your face is widest at the cheekbones, with a narrow forehead and chin.
  • Oblong: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a straight forehead and a long, narrow chin.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Right Haircut

Now that you know your face shape, it’s time to find the best haircut for you. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Research hairstyles: Look for hairstyles that are recommended for your face shape. You can find inspiration on social media, in magazines, or by asking your hairstylist for recommendations.
  2. Consider your hair type: Your hair type can also play a role in determining the best haircut for you. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to avoid a short, layered haircut that can be difficult to manage.
  3. Think about your lifestyle: Your lifestyle can also impact your haircut choice. If you’re a busy mom who doesn’t have a lot of time to style your hair, you may want to choose a low-maintenance haircut.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist: Your hairstylist can help you determine the best haircut for your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. They can also make recommendations based on what’s trending and what would look best on you.

Tips for Choosing the Best Haircut

Here are some additional tips for choosing the best haircut:

  • Consider your personality: Your haircut should reflect your personality and make you feel confident and beautiful.
  • Experiment with different styles: Don’t be afraid to try something new! You may be surprised at how a new haircut can transform your look.
  • Think about the long-term: If you’re considering a drastic haircut, think about how it will look as it grows out. Will you be happy with the style in six months?
  • Be open to feedback: Your hairstylist is an expert and can offer valuable feedback on what will look best on you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the best haircut for your face shape:

  • What is the most flattering haircut? The most flattering haircut is one that enhances your best features and makes you feel confident and beautiful. This can vary depending on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.
  • What is the best haircut for a round face? A layered haircut with long, face-framing layers can be flattering on a round face.
  • What is the best haircut for an oval face? An oval face can pull off a variety of haircuts, but a long bob or shoulder-length cut can be particularly flattering.
  • What is the best haircut for a square face? Soft, layered haircuts can help soften a strong jawline on a square face.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

Once you’ve found the perfect haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use the right products: Choose hair care products that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Protect your hair from heat: Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like a curling iron or straightener.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help keep your haircut looking fresh and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing: Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and brittle.

By following these tips and choosing a haircut that flatters your face shape, you can achieve a beautiful, confident look that you’ll love.

Best Hairstyle For Round Face Big Forehead How To Choose The Perfect One

Are you struggling to find the best hairstyle for your round face and big forehead? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this blog post, we'll discuss the best hairstyles that will flatter your face shape and make your forehead look smaller. We'll also give you some tips on how to choose the perfect hairstyle and take care of your hair. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Perfect Hairstyle?

When it comes to choosing the best hairstyle for your round face and big forehead, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Choose hairstyles that add height to your face, such as long layers or a side-swept fringe.
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face look rounder, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob.
  • Consider adding volume to the crown of your head, which will balance out a larger forehead.
  • Opt for hairstyles that frame your face, such as layers around the cheekbones or jawline.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what suits you best.

Best Hairstyles for Round Face Big Forehead

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, let's take a look at some of the best hairstyles for round face big forehead:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for round faces with big foreheads. They add height to your face and draw attention away from your forehead. You can wear your hair straight or add some loose waves for a more relaxed look.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for round faces with big foreheads. They cover your forehead and draw attention to your eyes. You can wear them long or short, depending on your preference.

Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

If you prefer a shorter hairstyle, a bob with side-swept bangs is a great option. The bangs cover your forehead while the bob adds volume to the crown of your head. You can wear your hair straight or add some waves for a more textured look.

High Ponytail

If you want to wear your hair up, a high ponytail is a great option. It adds height to your face and draws attention to your cheekbones. You can add some volume to the crown of your head for a more dramatic look.

Half-Up Half-Down

If you want a more relaxed look, a half-up half-down hairstyle is a great option. You can add some volume to the crown of your head and leave some loose waves around your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Style Your Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to style your hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.
  4. Straighten or curl your hair, depending on your preference.
  5. Add some hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips to Maintain Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to maintain your hairstyle:

  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to keep your hair fresh.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a round face with a big forehead?

The best haircut for a round face with a big forehead is one that adds height to your face and draws attention away from your forehead. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and a bob with side-swept bangs are some of the best options.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part with a round face and big forehead?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a middle part with a round face and big forehead. However, it's best to add some volume to the crown of your head to balance out your forehead.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. If you exercise regularly, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

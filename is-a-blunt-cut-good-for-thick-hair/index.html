Is A Blunt Cut Good For Thick Hair

Written by Christine Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing, but it can also be a curse. It can be difficult to manage, style, and maintain. One of the most popular haircuts for thick hair is the blunt cut. In this article, we will explore whether a blunt cut is good for thick hair or not.

Description

A blunt cut is a haircut in which all the hair is cut to the same length. This creates a sharp, straight line at the bottom of the hair. It can be done on any length of hair, from short to long. A blunt cut is a popular choice for those with thick hair because it creates a sleek, polished look.

How to

If you're considering a blunt cut for your thick hair, here's how to get it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Use a sharp pair of scissors to cut each section to the desired length.
  4. Blend the sections together by cutting at an angle.
  5. Use a comb to check for any uneven spots.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a blunt cut for your thick hair:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for thick hair. Towel dry your hair and let it air dry until it is about 80% dry.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into four equal sections: two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Use a sharp pair of scissors to cut your hair to the desired length. Start with the back sections and work your way to the front. Cut straight across each section, making sure to cut all the hair to the same length.

Step 4: Blend Your Hair

Blend the sections together by cutting at an angle. This will create a more natural look and prevent any harsh lines.

Step 5: Check for Uneven Spots

Use a comb to check for any uneven spots. If you find any, trim them with your scissors.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. You can blow dry it straight or add some waves with a curling iron. Finish off with a hair serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a blunt cut for your thick hair:

  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before cutting it.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if desired.
  • Ask your stylist to add some layers if you want more movement in your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a blunt cut for thick hair:

Q: Is a blunt cut good for thick hair?

A: Yes, a blunt cut is a great choice for thick hair because it creates a sleek, polished look.

Q: Will a blunt cut make my hair look thinner?

A: No, a blunt cut will not make your hair look thinner. It will create a sharp, straight line at the bottom of your hair, which can actually make your hair look thicker.

Q: How often do I need to get a blunt cut?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how long you want to keep your hair. On average, you should get a blunt cut every 8-12 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny after getting a blunt cut, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

If you have thick hair and are looking for a sleek, polished look, a blunt cut may be a great choice for you. It creates a sharp, straight line at the bottom of your hair and can make your hair look thicker. Just be sure to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.

Step by Step Guide

  3. Style your hair in a way that will keep it out of your face, such as a high ponytail, braids, or a bun.

  9. Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.

Tips for Playing Sports with Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for playing sports with curly hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling it.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and body hydrated.

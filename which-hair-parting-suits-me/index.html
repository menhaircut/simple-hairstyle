Which Hair Parting Suits Me A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Style

Written by Luffy Jun 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hair parting can be a game-changer for your overall look. It can accentuate your best features and elevate your style. But with so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to determine which hair parting suits you best. In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of finding your perfect hair parting, from determining your face shape to tips for maintaining your new look.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into specific hair partings, it’s important to determine your face shape. Knowing your face shape will help you choose a complementary hair parting that flatters your features. The four main face shapes are:

  • Round: Wide cheekbones and a narrow forehead and jawline.
  • Square: Strong jawline and forehead with equal width.
  • Oval: Balanced proportions with a slightly curved jawline.
  • Heart: Wider forehead and cheekbones with a narrow chin.

Take a selfie and trace the outline of your face on the photo to determine your face shape. Alternatively, you can measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline with a tape measure.

Which Hair Parting Suits Round Faces?

If you have a round face, a deep side parting can help elongate your face and create the illusion of length. Avoid middle partings as they can make your face appear wider. You can also experiment with a side swept fringe to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Which Hair Parting Suits Square Faces?

Square faces can benefit from a middle parting or a deep side parting. Soft, layered hairstyles can help soften the angles of a square face. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs that can make your face appear boxy.

Which Hair Parting Suits Oval Faces?

Oval faces can pull off any hair parting, so feel free to experiment with different styles. A center parting can create the illusion of symmetry, while a side parting can add volume to one side of your hair.

Which Hair Parting Suits Heart Faces?

If you have a heart-shaped face, a side parting with a sweeping fringe can balance out your forehead and narrow chin. Avoid middle partings that can accentuate the wideness of your forehead.

Step by Step Guide to Creating Your Hair Parting

Once you’ve determined your face shape and chosen your ideal hair parting, it’s time to create the look. Follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Using a tail comb, create your desired hair parting on either side of your head or in the center.
  4. Blow-dry your hair in sections, starting from the roots and moving towards the tips. Use a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Parting

Once you’ve created your perfect hair parting, you’ll want to maintain it for as long as possible. Here are some tips:

  • Refresh your hair parting daily by using a tail comb to redefine the parting.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently as it can cause your hair parting to shift.
  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make your parting appear flat.

FAQ: Which Hair Parting Suits Me?

Can I change my hair parting?

Yes, you can experiment with different hair partings to find your perfect style. However, be aware that changing your hair parting too frequently can cause hair breakage and damage.

Can I create a hair parting with curly hair?

Absolutely! Use a tail comb or your fingers to create your desired parting when your hair is wet. Let your curls air-dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to avoid disrupting your hair’s natural texture.

What if I have a short haircut?

Short haircuts can still have a hair parting. Use a comb to create your desired parting and style your hair accordingly.

Hair Care for Your Perfect Hair Parting

Now that you’ve found your perfect hair parting, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain your style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s tailored to your hair type and avoid using hot tools too frequently. Regular trims can help prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Choosing the right hair parting can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your style. With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect hair parting that complements your face shape and showcases your best features. Happy styling!

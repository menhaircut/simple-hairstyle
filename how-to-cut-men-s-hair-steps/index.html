How To Cut Men s Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 02, 2023 · 3 min read

When it comes to men's grooming, getting a haircut is an essential part of looking sharp and maintaining a neat appearance. However, with the ongoing pandemic, many men are opting to cut their hair at home to avoid crowded salons. If you're one of these men, don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll guide you through a step-by-step process on how to cut men's hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Men's Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Men's Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Men's Hair

How to Cut Men's Hair

Cutting men's hair is a skill that requires patience, practice, and the right tools. Before you start, make sure you have the following:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clip
  • Cape or towel
  • Spray bottle with water

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your tools, it's time to get started. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Start with dry hair. Comb through the hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Use the clippers to trim the sides and back of the hair. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard for a gradual fade.
  3. Blend the sides and back with the top. Use scissors to trim the top of the hair to the desired length.
  4. Use the comb to lift sections of the hair and trim any stray hairs with scissors.
  5. Trim the hairline around the ears and neck with clippers or scissors.
  6. Style the hair as desired using product.

Remember to take your time and work slowly to avoid any mistakes. It's always better to start with a longer length and gradually work your way down to the desired length.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a professional-looking haircut:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head.
  • Go slow and be patient.
  • Use a smaller guard when trimming around the ears and neck.
  • Use a spray bottle to keep the hair damp while cutting.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair:

Can I use regular scissors to cut hair?

No, it's best to use hair cutting scissors as they are designed to cut through hair smoothly and evenly.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on the individual. Some men prefer to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks, while others can go longer between cuts.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go shorter or visit a professional stylist to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care After Cutting Men's Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain a neat appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair product, such as gel or pomade, to style your hair.
  • Use a comb or brush to keep your hair looking neat.
  • Visit a professional stylist for regular trims to maintain your haircut.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair, you can save money and time by doing it at home. Just remember to be patient, use the right tools, and follow our step-by-step guide. Good luck!

Best Volumizing Mousse For Wavy Hair Achieve Fullness And Bounce In 2023

Written by Robby Sep 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of limp, lifeless waves? Do you want to add volume and bounce to your hair without weighing it down? Look no further than volumizing mousse!

What is Volumizing Mousse?

Volumizing mousse is a lightweight hair product that is applied to damp hair before styling. It works by adding volume and body to the hair, while also providing hold and control.

How Does Volumizing Mousse Work?

Volumizing mousse contains ingredients that coat the hair shaft, making it appear thicker and fuller. It also contains polymers that help to hold the hair in place, giving it added body and bounce.

How to Use Volumizing Mousse on Wavy Hair

Using volumizing mousse on wavy hair is easy! Follow these simple steps:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair, removing excess moisture.
  3. Shake the can of volumizing mousse well.
  4. Dispense a golf ball-sized amount of mousse into your palm.
  5. Apply the mousse evenly to your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths.
  6. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the mousse evenly.
  7. Scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance your waves.
  8. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat.
  9. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

And voila! You now have voluminous, bouncy waves.

Tips for Using Volumizing Mousse on Wavy Hair

Want to get the most out of your volumizing mousse? Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Apply mousse to damp hair to prevent frizz and enhance definition.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow drying to enhance your natural wave pattern.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while it's drying to prevent frizz.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to finish your style without weighing it down.

FAQ: Volumizing Mousse for Wavy Hair

Q: Can I use volumizing mousse on dry hair?

A: While it's best to use volumizing mousse on damp hair, you can also use it on dry hair to revive limp waves. Simply apply a small amount of mousse to your hands and scrunch it into your hair.

Q: Will volumizing mousse make my hair greasy?

A: Volumizing mousse should not make your hair greasy if you use the right amount and apply it evenly. If you're concerned about greasiness, start with a small amount of mousse and build up as needed.

Q: How often should I use volumizing mousse?

A: You can use volumizing mousse as often as you like, but it's best to avoid using it every day. Overuse of styling products can lead to buildup and weigh down your hair.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

In addition to using volumizing mousse, there are other things you can do to care for your wavy hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With the right care and styling products, you can achieve beautiful, voluminous waves that will turn heads wherever you go!

What Bangs Are Good For Long Faces A Guide For 2023

If you have a long face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But fear not, because bangs can work wonders to balance out your facial features. In this guide, we'll explore what bangs are good for long faces and provide tips on how to style and care for them.

What are bangs?

Bangs refer to the hair that falls across the forehead. They can be cut straight across, parted in the middle, or swept to the side. Bangs can dramatically change the look of a hairstyle, and they are a popular choice for those looking to switch up their look without making a drastic change.

What bangs are good for long faces?

For those with long faces, the goal is to add width to the forehead and shorten the appearance of the face. Here are some types of bangs that can achieve this:

Side-swept bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for long faces because they add width to the forehead without making the face appear longer. They can be styled to sweep to the left or right, depending on your preference.

Blunt bangs

Blunt bangs cut straight across the forehead can also work for long faces, as long as they are not too heavy. The goal is to add width to the forehead, so make sure the bangs are not too thick and don't cover too much of the forehead.

Choppy bangs

Choppy bangs that are cut at different lengths can also work for long faces. They add texture and dimension to the hair, which can help balance out the length of the face.

How to style bangs for long faces

Styling bangs for long faces is relatively simple, but it's important to keep in mind that the goal is to add width to the forehead. Here are some tips:

  • Blow-dry the bangs straight to add volume and width.
  • Use a round brush to style the bangs to the side for a more dramatic effect.
  • If you have curly hair, consider straightening your bangs to achieve a sleek look.

Step-by-step guide: How to cut bangs for long faces

If you're feeling brave and want to cut your own bangs, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape. The base of the triangle should be the width of your forehead.
  3. Hold the hair between your index and middle finger and twist it once or twice.
  4. Cut the hair straight across, making sure not to cut too much at once.
  5. Repeat on the other side, making sure the length and thickness of the bangs are even.

Tips for styling bangs for long faces

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling bangs for long faces:

  • Use a light-hold hairspray to keep the bangs in place without weighing them down.
  • If you're not ready to commit to cutting bangs, try clip-in bangs to see how they look on you.
  • Experiment with different parting and styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQs

Can bangs be too short for long faces?

Yes, bangs that are too short can make a long face appear even longer. Stick to bangs that hit at or below the eyebrows.

Are bangs high maintenance?

Bangs can be high maintenance, especially if you have oily hair or live in a humid climate. Plan to wash and style your bangs every day or every other day to keep them looking fresh.

Can bangs cause breakouts?

Yes, bangs can cause breakouts on the forehead. To prevent this, make sure to wash your bangs regularly and keep them off your forehead as much as possible.

Hair care tips for bangs

To keep your bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to prevent oil buildup.
  • Blow-dry your bangs straight to add volume and prevent them from sticking to your forehead.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs, as this can weigh them down.

With the right bangs and styling techniques, you can balance out your long face and achieve a flattering, chic look. Experiment with different styles and see what works best for you!

How To Use Clippers To Cut Women s Short Hair A Beginner s Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending hours in the salon to get a simple short haircut? Do you want to save time and money by cutting your own hair at home? If yes, then this guide is for you. In this article, we will discuss how to use clippers to cut women's short hair. We will cover everything from the tools you need to the step-by-step process of cutting your hair with clippers. So, let's get started!

Tools You Need

The first step in cutting your hair with clippers is to gather all the necessary tools. Here are the things you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Clipper guards
  • Comb
  • Scissors
  • Water spray bottle
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape

Once you have all the tools, you're ready to start cutting your hair.

Preparation

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to prepare your hair and your workspace. Here's what you should do:

  • Wash and dry your hair
  • Choose a well-lit area with a mirror
  • Wear old clothes or a cape to prevent hair from sticking to your clothes
  • Set up your tools within reach

Once you've prepared your workspace, you're ready to start cutting.

Sectioning Your Hair

Sectioning your hair is important to ensure that you cut your hair evenly. Here's how to section your hair:

  • Divide your hair into four sections using hair clips
  • Start at the nape of your neck and clip the top section of your hair away
  • Clip the side sections of your hair away

Once you've sectioned your hair, you're ready to start clipping.

Clipping Your Hair

Clipping your hair is the most important step in cutting your hair with clippers. Here's how to do it:

  • Attach the desired clipper guard to your clippers
  • Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up
  • Use the clippers to cut the hair in an upward motion
  • Repeat this process for the side sections of your hair
  • Remove the clipper guard and use the clippers to trim the hair around your ears and neckline

Once you've finished clipping your hair, you're ready to blend.

Blending Your Hair

Blending your hair is important to ensure that your haircut looks natural and even. Here's how to blend your hair:

  • Remove the clipper guard and use the clippers to trim the hair around your ears and neckline
  • Use the scissors to trim any longer hairs and blend the hair around your ears and neckline
  • Blend the hair between the top and side sections of your hair using the scissors

Once you've finished blending your hair, you're ready to clean your clippers.

Cleaning Your Clippers

Cleaning your clippers is important to ensure that they last long and work efficiently. Here's how to clean your clippers:

  • Remove the clipper guard and brush off any hair from the blades
  • Use a cleaning solution to clean the blades
  • Oil the blades to ensure they work smoothly

Once you've cleaned your clippers, you're done cutting your hair.

Tips for Cutting Women's Short Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down to a shorter one
  • Use a water spray bottle to dampen your hair before clipping
  • Take breaks to rest your arms and hands

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my hair with clippers if it's curly?

A: Yes, you can cut your curly hair with clippers, but it's important to choose the right clipper guard and avoid cutting your hair too short.

Q: Can I use clippers to cut my bangs?

A: Yes, you can use clippers to cut your bangs, but it's important to use a smaller clipper guard and work slowly and carefully.

Hair Care after Cutting with Clippers

After cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and looks great. Here are some tips for hair care after cutting with clippers:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape

Now that you know how to use clippers to cut women's short hair, you can save time and money by cutting your own hair at home. Just remember to be patient, take breaks when needed, and follow the tips we've provided. Happy cutting!

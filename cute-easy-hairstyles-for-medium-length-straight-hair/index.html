Cute Easy Hairstyles For Medium Length Straight Hair

If you have medium length straight hair, you might find yourself stuck in a rut when it comes to styling. But fear not! There are plenty of cute and easy hairstyles that you can try to switch up your look. In this post, we'll go over some of the best options for medium length straight hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Medium Length Straight Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Medium Length Straight Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Medium Length Straight Hair

How to Style Medium Length Straight Hair

Before we get into the specific hairstyles, let's go over some general tips for styling medium length straight hair. First and foremost, make sure your hair is clean and well-conditioned. This will make it easier to work with and help your style last longer.

Next, consider using some styling products to help tame your hair and keep it in place. A light hold hairspray, mousse, or pomade can all be great options depending on your hair type and the look you're going for.

Finally, invest in some quality hair tools. A good straightener or curling iron can make a huge difference in how your hair looks and how long your style lasts.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, let's dive into some specific hairstyles. Here are five cute and easy options for medium length straight hair:

1. Low Ponytail

Start by brushing your hair back into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Secure with a hair tie, and wrap a small section of hair around the base of the ponytail to hide the elastic. For a polished look, straighten any flyaways or frizz with a flat iron.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

Take the top half of your hair and pull it back into a small ponytail, leaving the bottom half loose. Secure with a hair tie, and add some texture or waves to the bottom half with a curling iron. This look is perfect for a casual day out or a night on the town.

3. Sleek and Straight

Straighten your hair using a flat iron, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place and prevent any flyaways. This classic look is perfect for any occasion.

4. Side Braid

Brush your hair to one side and create a loose braid. Secure with a hair tie, and gently pull at the braid to make it look more relaxed and effortless. This boho-inspired look is perfect for a festival or a day at the beach.

5. Messy Bun

Gather your hair into a high ponytail, and wrap it around itself to create a messy bun. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands, and pull out a few pieces around your face for a more relaxed look. This hairstyle is perfect for a lazy day or a workout.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Straight Hair

Here are some additional tips for getting the most out of your medium length straight hair:

  • Experiment with different partings to find the one that works best for your face shape.
  • Add some texture to your hair with a salt spray or texturizing powder.
  • Consider getting some layers to add some dimension and movement to your hair.
  • Invest in some cute hair accessories, like headbands or barrettes, to add some interest to your look.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about styling medium length straight hair:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, you should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, but some people may need to wash more frequently or less frequently.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from getting frizzy?

A: Use a good quality conditioner, avoid over-styling your hair, and consider using a smoothing serum or oil to tame any frizz.

Hair Care for Medium Length Straight Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your medium length straight hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling your hair too often, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these cute and easy hairstyles, you'll never be bored with your medium length straight hair again. Experiment with different looks and have fun!

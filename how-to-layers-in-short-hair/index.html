How To Layers In Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your short hair looking flat and lifeless? Adding layers can give your hair more dimension and movement. But how do you layer short hair without it looking choppy or uneven? In this guide, we’ll show you how to layers in short hair like a pro.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Layer Short Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Layering Short Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care After Layering Short Hair

How to Layer Short Hair

Layering short hair can be tricky, especially if you’re doing it yourself. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Short hair requires shorter layers. The longer your hair, the longer the layers can be.
  • Layering can add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Choose the right tool for the job. Scissors, razor blades, and thinning shears can all be used to create different types of layers.
  • Layering can be done on any type of short hair, from pixie cuts to bobs.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to layer your short hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair. Layering works best on clean, dry hair.
  2. Partition your hair into sections. Clip the top section of your hair away from the bottom section.
  3. Start at the back of your head. Take a small section of hair and hold it straight up. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle.
  4. Move to the sides of your head. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle, following the shape of your head.
  5. Layer the top section of your hair. Starting at the crown of your head, take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle. This will create volume and movement on the top of your head.
  6. Blend the layers. Use thinning shears or a razor blade to blend the layers together, creating a seamless look.
  7. Check for symmetry. Make sure the layers on both sides of your head are even.
  8. Style your hair. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to style your hair, accentuating the layers.

Tips for Layering Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect layered short hair:

  • Choose the right length for your layers. Shorter layers can add volume, while longer layers can create a softer look.
  • Use the right tools. Scissors, razor blades, and thinning shears all have different effects on your hair.
  • Don’t overdo it. Too many layers can make your hair look choppy and uneven.
  • Get a professional haircut. If you’re not confident in your layering skills, it’s best to leave it to the professionals.

FAQs

How often should I get my short hair layered?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the layers lose their shape. Generally, getting a trim every 6-8 weeks will keep your layers looking fresh.

Can I layer my short hair myself?

You can, but it’s not recommended unless you have experience cutting hair. It’s best to leave it to the professionals to avoid any mishaps.

Will layering my short hair make it look thinner?

No, layering can actually make your hair look fuller and more voluminous.

Hair Care After Layering Short Hair

After layering your short hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Don’t wash your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your layers.

Now that you know how to layers in short hair, you can give your hair the volume and movement it deserves. Just remember to take care of it properly to keep it looking its best.

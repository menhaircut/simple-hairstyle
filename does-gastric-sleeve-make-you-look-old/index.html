Does Gastric Sleeve Make You Look Old

Written by Petter Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering gastric sleeve surgery, you may have heard rumors that it can make you look older. This is a common concern among people considering weight loss surgery, but is there any truth to it? In this article, we'll explore the relationship between gastric sleeve surgery and aging, and answer some common questions you may have about the procedure.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Gastric sleeve surgery, also known as sleeve gastrectomy, is a weight loss surgery that involves removing a portion of the stomach to reduce its size. This helps patients feel full after eating smaller portions of food, leading to weight loss. While the surgery is effective for weight loss, some people have expressed concerns that it can make them look older.

There are a few reasons why people believe gastric sleeve surgery can make you look older. First, rapid weight loss can lead to loose skin, which can create a sagging or aged appearance. Second, some people experience hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, which can also contribute to an older look.

How to

The best way to avoid looking older after gastric sleeve surgery is to take care of yourself before and after the procedure. This includes eating a healthy diet, staying hydrated, and getting regular exercise. You may also want to consider working with a nutritionist or dietitian to develop a healthy meal plan that meets your nutritional needs.

After the surgery, it's important to follow your doctor's instructions for recovery. This may include taking medications as prescribed, attending follow-up appointments, and gradually increasing your physical activity. You may also want to work with a personal trainer or physical therapist to develop a safe and effective exercise routine.

Step by step

If you're considering gastric sleeve surgery, here are the general steps you can expect:

  1. Consultation with a bariatric surgeon to determine if you're a candidate for the procedure
  2. Pre-operative testing, including blood work, imaging, and other tests as needed
  3. Surgery (usually done laparoscopically, meaning small incisions are made in the abdomen)
  4. Recovery and follow-up appointments to monitor progress and address any issues that arise

Tips

Here are some tips to help you look and feel your best after gastric sleeve surgery:

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, fruits, and vegetables
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • Get regular exercise, starting with low-impact activities like walking or swimming
  • Take care of your skin by using sunscreen and moisturizer

FAQ

Q: Will gastric sleeve surgery make me look older?

A: It's possible that rapid weight loss can lead to loose skin and a sagging or aged appearance. However, this can be minimized by taking good care of yourself before and after the surgery.

Q: Will I experience hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: Hair loss is a potential side effect of gastric sleeve surgery, but it typically resolves on its own within a few months. Taking a biotin supplement and eating a healthy diet can also help promote hair regrowth.

Q: How much weight can I expect to lose after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: The amount of weight you lose will depend on a variety of factors, including your starting weight, your diet and exercise habits, and your adherence to your doctor's instructions. On average, patients can expect to lose between 50% and 70% of their excess weight within the first year after surgery.

Hair Care

If you do experience hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, there are a few things you can do to promote regrowth:

  • Take a biotin supplement
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein and vitamins
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair
  • Consider using a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for thinning hair

Overall, gastric sleeve surgery can be an effective tool for weight loss, but it's important to weigh the potential risks and benefits before deciding if it's right for you. By taking good care of yourself before and after the surgery, you can minimize the risk of looking older and enjoy the many health benefits of weight loss.

How To Curl Your Hair With A Straightener

Written by Aprilia Aug 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of using the same curling iron every time you want to add some curls to your hair? Well, you don’t have to rely on just one tool anymore. With the right technique, you can use your straightener to create gorgeous curls. In this article, we will show you how to do you curl your hair with a straightener and give you some tips on how to achieve the perfect look.

Description

Curling your hair with a straightener is a great way to create loose, natural-looking curls. This method is perfect for those who don’t want to invest in a separate curling iron or those who want to achieve a more relaxed, beachy vibe. The process is relatively simple, but it does take some practice to get the hang of it.

How to Curl Your Hair with a Straightener

Here are the steps to follow when curling your hair with a straightener:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent any damage to your hair. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles, and divide it into sections.

Step 2: Start Curling

Take a small section of hair and clamp the straightener around it near the roots. Twist the straightener away from your face, and then slowly glide it down the hair shaft while maintaining the twist. The slower you go, the tighter the curl will be.

Step 3: Repeat

Repeat the same process with each section of hair, alternating the direction you twist the straightener to create a more natural look. Take larger sections of hair for looser curls and smaller sections for tighter curls.

Step 4: Finish

Once you have curled all of your hair, use your fingers to loosen and separate the curls. Apply some hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when curling your hair with a straightener:

  • Use a high-quality straightener with ceramic plates for best results.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before curling to prevent any damage.
  • Start with a lower heat setting and gradually increase it if needed.
  • For tighter curls, wrap the hair around the straightener more tightly and glide it down slower.
  • For looser curls, wrap the hair around the straightener more loosely and glide it down faster.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling hair with a straightener:

Can I curl my hair with any straightener?

No, not all straighteners are suitable for curling hair. Look for a straightener with ceramic plates, as they distribute heat evenly and reduce the risk of damage to your hair.

How hot should the straightener be?

Start with a lower heat setting and gradually increase it if needed. The ideal temperature for curling hair with a straightener is between 300-350°F.

How do I prevent my hair from getting tangled in the straightener?

Make sure you’re taking small sections of hair and combing through each section before curling. This will help prevent tangles and ensure a smooth glide.

Hair Care

Remember to always use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair. To prevent damage, limit your use of hot tools and give your hair a break by air-drying it or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to curl your hair with a straightener, you can switch up your hairstyle and achieve a new look. Remember to practice and experiment with different techniques to find the perfect curls for you.

How To Make Body Wave Hair Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 10, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have body wave hair and you want to switch up your look, adding some curls can be a great way to do it. Luckily, it's not as difficult as it may seem! In this guide, we'll walk you through how to make body wave hair curly.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Body Wave Hair Curly
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Body Wave Hair Curly
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Body Wave Hair

How to Make Body Wave Hair Curly

There are a few different methods you can use to make body wave hair curly:

  • Using a curling iron
  • Using hot rollers
  • Using flexi rods

Each method has its pros and cons, so it's worth experimenting to find which one works best for you. In this guide, we'll be focusing on the curling iron method.

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow to make body wave hair curly using a curling iron:

  1. Start by thoroughly washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray.
  2. Blow dry your hair until it's completely dry.
  3. Separate your hair into sections, clipping each section up except for the one you're working on.
  4. Take a small section of hair and clamp the curling iron around it, starting at the root.
  5. Twist the curling iron away from your face, pulling it down the length of your hair as you go.
  6. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl.
  7. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.
  8. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create volume.
  9. Finish with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

And that's it! With a little practice, you'll be able to create beautiful curls in no time.

Tips for Making Body Wave Hair Curly

Here are some tips to keep in mind as you're making body wave hair curly:

  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start curling it. Wet hair is more prone to damage from heat styling.
  • Use a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel, as these materials distribute heat more evenly and cause less damage to your hair.
  • Don't hold the curling iron in place for too long – a few seconds should be enough to create a curl.
  • For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions and vary the size of your curls.

FAQs

Q: Can I make body wave hair curly without using heat?

A: Yes! You can use methods like braiding or twisting your hair while it's wet to create curls without heat.

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: This depends on your hair type and how well you maintain your curls. Generally, you can expect your curls to last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Body Wave Hair

Here are some tips to keep your curly body wave hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb when your hair is wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and keep them hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to create beautiful, curly hair with your body wave hair. Experiment with different methods and find what works best for you!

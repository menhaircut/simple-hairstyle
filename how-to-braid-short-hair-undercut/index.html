How To Braid Short Hair Undercut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an undercut and short hair, you may think that braiding is not possible. However, with the right technique and a few tricks, you can braid your hair and create a stylish and unique look. In this article, we will show you how to braid short hair undercut in a relaxed English language. We will cover everything from the basics to the final touches, so you can achieve the perfect braid every time.

How to Braid Short Hair Undercut

Braiding short hair undercut may seem complicated, but it's actually quite simple. The key is to have the right tools and technique. Here's what you will need:

  • Comb
  • Bobby pins
  • Elastic bands
  • Hair wax or gel

With these tools in hand, you're ready to start braiding.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to braid your short hair undercut:

  1. Part your hair on the side or in the middle. Use a comb to create a clean part.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and divide it into three equal parts.
  3. Begin braiding by crossing the right section over the middle section, then the left section over the middle section.
  4. Add hair to the right section and cross it over the middle section. Repeat on the left side.
  5. Continue adding hair to the right and left sections until you reach the nape of your neck.
  6. Once you reach the nape of your neck, braid the rest of your hair in a regular three-strand braid.
  7. Secure the end of your braid with an elastic band.
  8. Tuck the end of your braid under the rest of your hair and secure it with bobby pins.
  9. Apply hair wax or gel to the braid to smooth down any flyaways.

Congratulations! You've just braided your short hair undercut.

Tips for Braiding Short Hair Undercut

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect braid:

  • Use hair wax or gel to keep your hair in place while braiding.
  • Divide your hair into small sections to make braiding easier.
  • Don't worry if your braid is not perfect; a messy braid can look just as good.
  • Experiment with different braid styles, such as fishtail or Dutch braids.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about braiding short hair undercut:

Can I braid my hair if I have a shaved undercut?

Yes, you can still braid your hair if you have a shaved undercut. You may need to use more hair wax or gel to keep your hair in place.

How long does my hair need to be to braid it?

Your hair should be at least 2-3 inches long to braid it. If your hair is shorter than that, it may be difficult to create a braid.

Can I wear my braids for multiple days?

Yes, you can wear your braids for multiple days. However, it's important to take care of your hair by keeping it clean and moisturized.

Hair Care for Braided Short Hair Undercut

Here are some tips for taking care of your braided short hair undercut:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner at least once a week.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to your braids to keep them moisturized.
  • Cover your hair with a satin or silk scarf when sleeping to prevent frizz and breakage.

We hope this guide has helped you learn how to braid short hair undercut. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to create a variety of braided hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion.

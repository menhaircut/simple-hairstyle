How Long Does It Take To Grow Your Dreads

Written by Oliver Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking about getting dreadlocks, but wondering how long it will take before you can have the long, beautiful dreads of your dreams? The answer is not a simple one, as there are many factors that can affect how quickly your hair will grow and how long it will take to form into dreadlocks. In this article, we'll explore the question of how long it takes to grow dreads, and provide you with some tips and tricks for caring for your dreads along the way.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Grow Your Dreads
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing Dreads Faster
  • FAQs
  • Dread Care Tips

How to Grow Your Dreads

The first step in growing dreads is to stop cutting your hair. This may seem obvious, but it's important to remember that dreadlocks are formed from hair that has not been cut or trimmed for a certain length of time. The length of time it takes to grow your dreads will depend on your hair type, texture, and the method you choose to use.

There are several methods for growing dreads, including the twist and rip method, the backcombing method, and the neglect method. Each method has its own advantages and disadvantages, and the one that you choose will depend on your personal preferences and the texture of your hair.

Step by Step Guide

If you're new to growing dreads, here is a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Stop cutting your hair and let it grow out to the desired length.
  2. Choose a method for forming your dreads.
  3. Section your hair into small, even sections.
  4. Begin forming your dreads using your chosen method.
  5. Continue to maintain your dreads by washing and separating them regularly.

Tips for Growing Dreads Faster

If you're looking to speed up the process of growing your dreads, there are a few things you can do:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.
  • Use natural oils and products to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and slow down the growth process.
  • Avoid using chemical treatments or harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing dreadlocks:

Q: How long does it take to grow dreads?

A: The length of time it takes to grow dreads depends on your hair type, texture, and the method you choose to use. On average, it can take anywhere from 6 months to 2 years to grow fully formed dreadlocks.

Q: Can I grow dreads if I have fine or thin hair?

A: Yes, you can still grow dreads if you have fine or thin hair. However, it may take longer for your hair to form into dreadlocks, and you may need to use a different method than someone with thicker, coarser hair.

Dread Care Tips

Once you've grown your dreads, it's important to take good care of them to ensure they stay healthy and looking their best. Here are some tips for caring for your dreadlocks:

  • Wash your dreads regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Separate your dreads regularly to prevent them from growing together.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol or other harsh chemicals.
  • Use a natural oil, like coconut oil or jojoba oil, to keep your dreads moisturized.

Now that you know how long it takes to grow dreads and have some tips for caring for them, you're ready to start your journey to beautiful, healthy dreadlocks. Remember to be patient, as the process can take some time, but with the right care and maintenance, you'll have the dreads of your dreams in no time!

How To Make A Low Cut Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 20, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect low cut top and never finding one that fits you just right? Why not make one yourself? Not only will it fit you perfectly, but you can also customize it to your liking. In this step by step guide, we'll show you how to make a low cut top from scratch.

How to

Before we begin, gather all the materials you'll need:

  • Fabric (preferably stretchy)
  • Scissors
  • Measuring tape
  • Sewing machine
  • Thread
  • Pins
  • Iron

Step 1: Measure and Cut

Measure the length from your shoulders to your desired length of the top. Then, measure your bust and add 2 inches. Fold the fabric in half and cut out a rectangle that matches the two measurements you just took.

Step 2: Cut the Neckline

On the folded side of the fabric, measure and mark the center. From that center point, measure and mark the width of the neckline. Cut along the marks to create the neckline.

Step 3: Sew the Sides

With the fabric right sides together, sew the sides of the top together. Use a zigzag stitch to allow for stretch in the fabric.

Step 4: Hem the Edges

Using an iron, fold the edges of the top over twice and sew them down to create a neat hem.

Step 5: Create the Straps

Cut two long, thin strips of fabric to create the straps. Fold the strips in half and sew the edges together. Turn the straps right side out and press them with an iron.

Step 6: Attach the Straps

Pin the straps to the top, placing them where you want them. Sew the straps to the top, making sure to reinforce the stitching.

Tips

  • Use a stretchy fabric to ensure a comfortable fit
  • Choose a fabric that drapes nicely
  • Reinforce the straps with extra stitching to prevent them from coming loose
  • Customize your top by adding embellishments or a different neckline

FAQ

Q: What if I don't have a sewing machine?

A: You can hand sew the top instead, but it may take longer and require more effort.

Q: Can I make this top without straps?

A: Yes, you can make a strapless version of this top by skipping step 5 and 6.

Hair Care

While making your low cut top, it's important to take care of your hair as well. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your locks healthy and shiny. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and opt for air drying instead. If you want to add some extra volume, use a volumizing mousse or spray.

In conclusion, making your own low cut top can be a fun and rewarding project. With these simple steps, you can create a top that fits you perfectly and looks great. Don't be afraid to customize it to your liking and have fun with it!

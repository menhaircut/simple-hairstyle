How To Make Thick Nappy Hair Curly

Written by Daniel Jul 07, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

Do you have thick, nappy hair that you wish was curly? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks on how to make your hair curly. From how to prepare your hair to the best products to use, we've got you covered. So let's get started!

Description

Thick nappy hair can be difficult to style, especially if you want to achieve curls. But with the right techniques and products, you can make your hair curly and bouncy. In this article, we'll provide step-by-step instructions on how to make thick nappy hair curly, as well as tips on how to maintain your curls and keep your hair healthy.

How to

If you want to make your thick nappy hair curly, you'll need to start by preparing your hair. Here's how:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to soften your hair and make it more manageable.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

Step 3: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair. This can be a cream, gel, or mousse. Be sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair to ensure that your curls are uniform.

Step 4: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that your curls are evenly distributed.

Step 5: Use a Curling Iron

Use a curling iron to curl your hair. Start at the roots of your hair and work your way down to the ends. Be sure to hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds to ensure that your curls are set.

Step 6: Use a Diffuser

Once you've curled your hair, use a diffuser to dry your hair. This will help to set your curls and give your hair more volume.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make your thick nappy hair curly:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making thick nappy hair curly:

Can I make my hair curly without using heat?

Yes, you can make your hair curly without using heat. You can use roller sets, braids, or twist outs to achieve curls without using heat styling tools.

What products should I use to make my hair curly?

You can use curl enhancing creams, gels, or mousses to make your hair curly. Look for products that are specifically designed for thick, nappy hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and prevent damage. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

If you have thick nappy hair, you can still achieve beautiful, curly hair. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this article, you can make your hair curly and bouncy, while keeping it healthy and strong. So go ahead and try it out! You'll be amazed at the results.

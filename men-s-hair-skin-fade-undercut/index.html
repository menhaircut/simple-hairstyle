Men s Hair Skin Fade Undercut A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish yet low-maintenance haircut, then the men's hair skin fade undercut is the perfect choice for you. This haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years due to its versatility and easy-to-maintain nature. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about the men's hair skin fade undercut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The men's hair skin fade undercut is a type of haircut that features a very short back and sides with longer hair on top. The hair is usually cut to a length of 1/16th of an inch or shorter on the sides, gradually blending into longer hair on top. This haircut is called a "skin fade" because the hair is tapered to the point where it blends seamlessly into the skin. The "undercut" refers to the longer hair on top that is disconnected from the shorter hair on the sides.

How to Get a Men's Hair Skin Fade Undercut

Getting a men's hair skin fade undercut is relatively simple, but it requires the expertise of a skilled barber or hairstylist. Here's how to get the cut:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

Decide on the length you want for the hair on top. This can vary depending on your personal preference and the shape of your face. You can opt for a shorter length, such as 2 inches, or go for a longer length, such as 4 inches.

Step 2: Determine the Fade Length

The next step is to determine the length of the fade. The fade can be a skin fade, which is very short, or it can be a longer fade, such as a 1/4 inch or 1/2 inch fade. This will depend on your preference and the overall look you're going for.

Step 3: Choose Your Style

Once you've determined the length of the hair on top and the fade, you can choose your style. You can opt for a more classic look with a side part or go for a more modern, messy look with textured hair on top.

Step 4: Get the Cut

Finally, it's time to get the cut. Make sure you go to a barber or hairstylist who has experience with men's hair skin fade undercuts. The stylist will use clippers to create the fade and scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length.

Tips for Styling a Men's Hair Skin Fade Undercut

Styling a men's hair skin fade undercut is easy, but there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a matte styling product, such as a clay or wax, for a natural, textured look.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go to create volume and texture.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a slicked-back look or a messy, textured look, to find what works best for you.

FAQs

What is the difference between a skin fade and a regular fade?

A skin fade is a type of fade where the hair is tapered to the point where it blends seamlessly into the skin. A regular fade is a type of fade where the hair gradually gets shorter as it moves down the sides and back of the head.

How often do I need to get a men's hair skin fade undercut?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the cut. Generally, you should plan on getting a touch-up every 2-4 weeks.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your men's hair skin fade undercut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo every other day to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as a blow dryer or straightener, too often as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a stylish men's hair skin fade undercut with ease!

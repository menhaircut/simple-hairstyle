Easy Hairstyles For Short Hair To Do At Home Step By Step

Written by William Oct 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short hair, you may feel limited in terms of styling options. However, with a little creativity and some basic tools, there are plenty of easy hairstyles for short hair that you can do at home. In this article, we will go over step-by-step instructions for some of the best easy hairstyles for short hair.

Table of Contents

  • Pixie Cut
  • Bob Cut
  • Half Up Half Down
  • Messy Bun
  • French Braid

1. Pixie Cut

If you have a pixie cut, you can style it in a variety of ways. One easy option is to add some texture by using a texturizing spray or pomade. Simply apply the product to your hair and use your fingers to tousle it until you achieve the desired look.

Another option is to use a small amount of gel to slick your hair back for a more polished look. You can also add a hair accessory, such as a headband or barrette, for some extra flair.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. One easy option is to add some loose waves using a curling iron or wand. Simply wrap small sections of hair around the iron and hold for a few seconds before releasing.

You can also create a sleek and polished look by using a flat iron to straighten your hair. Apply a small amount of serum or oil to your hair before straightening to prevent damage and add shine.

3. Half Up Half Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a great option for short hair. Simply take the top section of your hair and use a hair tie or clip to secure it at the crown of your head. You can leave the bottom section of your hair down or add some loose waves for added texture.

4. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a classic hairstyle that can be achieved with short hair. Simply gather your hair at the crown of your head and twist it into a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place and pull out a few pieces of hair around your face for a more relaxed look.

5. French Braid

A French braid is another great option for short hair. Simply start at the top of your head and begin braiding your hair, adding small sections of hair as you go. When you reach the nape of your neck, secure the braid with a hair tie or clip.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Invest in a good set of styling tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, and barrettes.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to prevent breakage.

FAQs

1. Can I curl short hair?

Yes, you can curl short hair using a curling iron or wand. Simply wrap small sections of hair around the iron and hold for a few seconds before releasing.

2. How can I add volume to my short hair?

You can add volume to short hair by using a volumizing mousse or spray. Apply the product to your roots and use your fingers to tousle your hair until you achieve the desired volume.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Be sure to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage. Additionally, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

With these easy hairstyles for short hair, you can change up your look without having to visit the salon. Whether you have a pixie cut, bob cut, or something in between, there is a styling option that will work for you. So grab your styling tools and get creative!

Hairstyles Over 50 2021 Tips And Ideas

Written by William Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair changes along with our bodies. But that doesn't mean you have to sacrifice style. There are plenty of hairstyles over 50 2021 that can help you look chic and fashionable. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and ideas on how to style your hair if you're over 50.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle is important, no matter what your age is. However, as we age, our hair tends to lose its volume and thickness. So, it's important to choose a hairstyle that can work with your hair's natural texture and volume. Here are some tips:

Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume, such as a pixie cut or a bob. If you have a square face, you may want to choose a hairstyle that softens your features, such as long layers or a side-swept bang.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. If you're an active person who likes to exercise or swim, you may want to choose a hairstyle that's easy to maintain, such as a short bob or a pixie cut. If you're a busy professional who likes to look polished and put-together, you may want to choose a hairstyle that's easy to style, such as a sleek, straight bob.

Step by Step: How to Style Your Hair

Now that you've chosen a hairstyle, it's time to learn how to style it. Here are some step-by-step instructions:

Short Pixie Cut

If you have a short pixie cut, you can style it by applying a small amount of styling gel or pomade to your hair. Use your fingers to create a messy, textured look. You can also use a small amount of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

If you have a bob with side-swept bangs, you can style it by blow-drying your hair with a round brush. Use the brush to create volume at the roots and smooth out your hair. Finish with a small amount of hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips:

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, such as a flat iron or curling iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray. This will help prevent damage to your hair.

Condition Regularly

Conditioning your hair regularly can help keep it soft and moisturized. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for women over 50?

The best hairstyle for women over 50 is one that works with your natural hair texture and volume. Some popular hairstyles for women over 50 include short pixie cuts, bobs, and long layers.

What hair colors are popular for women over 50?

Some popular hair colors for women over 50 include shades of blonde, brown, and red. However, it's important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and personal style.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and lifestyle. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and leave it dry and brittle.

Conclusion

Styling your hair over 50 can be challenging, but with the right hairstyle and some simple tips, you can look chic and fashionable. Remember to choose a hairstyle that works with your natural hair texture and volume, and to maintain healthy hair with regular trims, conditioning, and heat protectant. With these tips, you'll be sure to have beautiful hair that complements your age and personal style.

How To Avoid Hair Fall Due To Stress Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jun 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Dealing with hair fall can be frustrating, especially when you're not sure what's causing it. One common cause of hair fall is stress. When you're stressed, your body releases hormones that can damage hair follicles, leading to hair fall. Luckily, there are ways to avoid hair fall due to stress. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you keep your hair healthy and strong, even during stressful times.

How to Avoid Hair Fall Due to Stress

1. Identify the Cause of Your Stress

The first step in avoiding hair fall due to stress is to identify the cause of your stress. Is it work-related stress? Relationship problems? Financial worries? Once you know what's causing your stress, you can take steps to address it. Talk to a therapist, find ways to relax, or make changes in your life to reduce stress.


2. Practice Self-Care

Self-care is essential for reducing stress and preventing hair fall. Take time each day to do something that makes you happy, whether it's reading a book, taking a walk, or practicing yoga. Make sure you're getting enough sleep, eating a healthy diet, and staying hydrated. All of these things will help keep your body and mind healthy, reducing your risk of hair fall due to stress.


3. Use the Right Hair Products

The products you use on your hair can also affect hair fall. Look for products that are gentle and nourishing, rather than those that contain harsh chemicals. Avoid using hot styling tools like straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and lead to hair fall.


4. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help improve blood circulation and reduce stress, both of which can help prevent hair fall. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day. You can also try using a scalp massager to make the process easier.


5. Take Supplements

There are a variety of supplements that can help prevent hair fall due to stress. Look for supplements that contain biotin, vitamins A, C, and E, and omega-3 fatty acids. These nutrients can help keep your hair healthy and strong.


6. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like braids, buns, and ponytails can put stress on your hair, leading to hair fall. Instead, opt for loose hairstyles that don't pull on your hair. If you must wear your hair up, use soft hair ties and avoid pulling your hair too tight.


Tips for Taking Care of Your Hair

Along with the tips above, there are a few things you can do to take care of your hair and prevent hair fall:


1. Wash your hair regularly

Regularly washing your hair can help keep it clean and healthy. However, be sure not to overwash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and lead to dryness and hair fall.


2. Use a conditioner

Conditioner helps keep your hair soft and hydrated, reducing the risk of hair fall. Look for a conditioner that's designed for your hair type and use it after every wash.


3. Avoid brushing wet hair

Wet hair is more fragile and prone to breakage, so avoid brushing it when it's wet. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb to gently detangle your hair.


Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can stress cause hair fall?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair fall. When you're stressed, your body releases hormones that can damage hair follicles, leading to hair fall.


Q: How can I reduce stress?

A: There are many ways to reduce stress, including exercise, meditation, therapy, and self-care. Find what works for you and make it a regular part of your routine.


Q: How long does it take for hair to grow back after hair fall?

A: It can take several months for hair to grow back after hair fall. However, if you're taking steps to prevent hair fall, you can help speed up the process.


Conclusion

Dealing with hair fall can be frustrating, but with the tips and tricks above, you can keep your hair healthy and strong, even during stressful times. Remember to take care of yourself, use gentle hair products, and avoid tight hairstyles. With a little effort, you can prevent hair fall and keep your hair looking its best.

Good Short Haircuts For Square Faces A Guide For 2023

Written by Robby Nov 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your square face shape and looking for a new haircut to flatter your features? Look no further as we have compiled a guide to good short haircuts for square faces. Read on to discover the perfect hairstyle for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular features. The goal of a good haircut for square faces is to soften the sharp angles and create a more rounded appearance. Short haircuts are an excellent option for square faces as they draw attention away from the jawline and towards the eyes and cheekbones.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for a square face, it's important to consider the length and style. Opt for haircuts that are chin-length or shorter as longer hair can make the face appear more angular. Layered haircuts are also a great option as they add movement and texture to the hair, creating a softer look.

Top Short Haircuts for Square Faces

There are several short haircuts that work well for square faces:

  • Pixie Cut
  • Bob
  • Short Shag
  • Layered Bob
  • Asymmetrical Bob

Step by Step

If you're unsure which haircut to choose, follow these steps to find the perfect style:

  1. Determine the length you're comfortable with
  2. Consider your hair texture and thickness
  3. Find inspiration from celebrities with square faces
  4. Consult with your hairstylist for their professional opinion

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut for a square face:

  • Avoid straight across bangs as they can make the face appear more angular
  • Opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs to soften the forehead
  • Add volume to the crown of the head to create a more rounded appearance
  • Consider highlights or lowlights to add dimension to the hair

FAQ

Q: Can square faces pull off a bob?

A: Yes, bobs work well for square faces as long as they are chin-length or shorter.

Q: What is the best haircut for a square face with thick hair?

A: Short, layered haircuts with texture work well for square faces with thick hair.

Q: Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a square face?

A: No, short haircuts can be very flattering for square faces as long as the length and style are chosen correctly.

Hair Care

To maintain your short haircut, keep up with regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the cut. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the crown of the head. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools.

In conclusion, there are many good short haircuts for square faces. Consider the length, style, and texture of your hair when choosing a haircut, and don't be afraid to consult with your hairstylist for their professional opinion. Follow these tips and maintain your haircut with proper hair care to keep your square face looking fabulous.

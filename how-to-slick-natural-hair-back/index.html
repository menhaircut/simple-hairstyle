How To Slick Natural Hair Back A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a sleek and sophisticated look, slicking your natural hair back is the way to go. This style can be achieved without any harsh chemicals or heat, making it a great option for those who want to maintain healthy hair. In this guide, we'll show you how to slick natural hair back in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Natural Hair Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Natural Hair Back
  • FAQs About Slicking Natural Hair Back

How to Slick Natural Hair Back

Slicking natural hair back is a simple process that can be done in just a few minutes. Here are the basic steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. You can either wash your hair or wet it down with a spray bottle.
  2. Apply a styling product. You can use a gel, pomade, or cream to help slick your hair back. Be sure to choose a product that works well with your hair texture.
  3. Use a comb to slick your hair back. Starting at the hairline, comb your hair straight back. Use a fine-tooth comb to create a smooth finish.
  4. Smooth down any flyaways. Use your fingers or a soft-bristle brush to smooth down any loose hairs.
  5. Secure your hair with a hair tie or bobby pins. If you have long hair, you may need to secure it with a hair tie or bobby pins to keep it in place.
  6. Finish with a hairspray. Use a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Slicking Natural Hair Back

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back look:

  • Choose the right product. Different products work better with different hair textures. If you have fine hair, choose a lightweight product. If you have thick hair, opt for a heavier product.
  • Be gentle when combing. Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair when combing it back. This can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different parts. You can slick your hair straight back or create a side part for a different look.
  • Use a satin scarf or bonnet at night. This will help preserve your slicked-back style and prevent frizz.

FAQs About Slicking Natural Hair Back

Here are some common questions people have about slicking natural hair back:

Can I slick my hair back if it's short?

Yes, you can slick back short hair. Use a lightweight product and a fine-tooth comb to create a smooth finish.

How often should I slick my hair back?

You can slick your hair back as often as you like, but be sure to give your hair a break between styles to prevent damage.

Can I slick my hair back without product?

You can slick your hair back without product, but it may not stay in place as well. Try using a little bit of water or leave-in conditioner to help smooth your hair back.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Slicked Back Hair

Here are a few tips to help you maintain your slicked-back style:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often. This can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly. This will help keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools. Heat can cause damage to your hair, so try to avoid using curling irons, flat irons, and blow dryers.
  • Protect your hair from the sun. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from UV damage.

Now that you know how to slick natural hair back, you can rock this sleek and sophisticated style with confidence. Remember to choose the right product for your hair texture, be gentle when combing, and experiment with different parts for a unique look.

Is Short Hair Suitable For Big Face

Written by Daniel Nov 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A big face can be a concern for many women when it comes to choosing a hairstyle. With so many options available, it can be challenging to find the perfect style for your face shape. Short hair is a popular choice for many women, but is it suitable for big faces? In this blog post, we will explore the answer to this question and provide you with expert advice on how to choose the best short hairstyle for your big face.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyle for Big Face
  • Step by Step Guide to Choosing Short Hairstyle for Big Face
  • Tips for Styling Short Hair on Big Face
  • FAQs

Description

The short answer is yes! Short hair can be suitable for big faces, but it's important to choose the right style that flatters your face shape. A big face is typically characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a broad jawline. The goal of choosing a short hairstyle for a big face is to create balance and symmetry while accentuating your best features.

How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyle for Big Face

When it comes to choosing a short hairstyle for a big face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your face shape and features. Look for a style that will balance out your facial features and create symmetry. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider.

Next, consider your hair texture and thickness. Short hair can be a great option for big faces, but it's important to choose a style that works with your hair type. If you have thick, coarse hair, a layered bob or pixie cut can help to thin out your hair and create a more balanced look.

Lastly, consider your lifestyle and personal style. Short hair can be low maintenance and easy to style, but it's important to choose a style that you feel comfortable and confident in. Look for inspiration from celebrities or consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect short hairstyle for your big face.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing Short Hairstyle for Big Face

Here is a step by step guide to choosing the best short hairstyle for your big face:

  1. Identify your face shape and features
  2. Research short hairstyles that flatter your face shape
  3. Consider your hair type and texture
  4. Consult with a hairstylist for expert advice and recommendations
  5. Choose a style that you feel comfortable and confident in

Tips for Styling Short Hair on Big Face

Styling short hair on a big face can be simple and effortless. Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Use a volumizing product to add height to your hair and create balance
  • Avoid adding volume to the sides of your face
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and definition to your hair
  • Accessorize with headbands or hair clips to add interest and draw attention away from your face shape

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about short hair and big faces:

Can short hair make a big face look smaller?

Yes, short hair can create balance and symmetry on a big face, making it appear smaller and more proportionate. Look for styles that add height and volume to your hair, while avoiding styles that add volume to the sides of your face.

What are some popular short hairstyles for big faces?

Popular short hairstyles for big faces include pixie cuts, layered bobs, and asymmetrical cuts. These styles help to create balance and symmetry while accentuating your best features.

Hair Care for Short Hair on Big Face

Short hair can be easy to maintain and style, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips for hair care for short hair on a big face:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping natural oils
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent damage
  • Get regular trims to maintain your style and prevent split ends

In conclusion, short hair can be suitable for big faces, but it's important to choose the right style that flatters your face shape and features. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a short hairstyle, and don't be afraid to consult with a hairstylist for expert advice and recommendations. With the right style and care, you can rock short hair on your big face with confidence!

How To Look Good With Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jul 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you one of those people with a round face and wondering how to look good? Don't worry! We have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss different hairstyles, makeup tips, and fashion styles that will suit your round face perfectly. So, let's dive right in!

How to:

If you have a round face, the key is to create an illusion of a longer face. You can do this by using different hairstyles, makeup, and fashion styles that complement your face shape. Here are some tips on how to look good with a round face:

Step by Step:

1. Hairstyles:

The right hairstyle can make all the difference. Long layers or a side-swept bang can elongate your face. Avoid blunt cuts and short bobs that make your face look rounder.

2. Makeup:

Contouring is a game-changer when it comes to makeup for round faces. Use a darker shade of powder or cream to contour your cheekbones, jawline, and forehead. This will create an illusion of a longer face. Also, avoid using highlighter on your cheeks as it can make your face look rounder.

3. Fashion:

When it comes to fashion, avoid oversized clothing as it can make you look bigger than you are. Instead, opt for fitted clothing that will accentuate your curves. Also, try to avoid horizontal stripes as they can make your face look wider.

Tips:

Here are some additional tips that will help you look good with a round face:

1. Accessorize:

Accessories like earrings, necklaces, and scarves can draw attention away from your face and towards your outfit.

2. Glasses:

If you wear glasses, opt for angular or rectangular frames that will balance out your face shape.

3. Hairstyling Tools:

Use hairstyling tools like a straightener or curling iron to create volume on the top of your head. This will elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

FAQ:

Q: Can I wear short hair with a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear short hair with a round face. However, opt for a layered or textured cut that will add volume on the top of your head.

Q: What kind of makeup should I avoid with a round face?

A: Avoid using highlighter on your cheeks and nose as it can make your face look rounder. Also, avoid using dark lipstick as it can make your lips look smaller.

Q: Can I wear stripes with a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear stripes with a round face. However, opt for vertical stripes as they can make your face look longer.

Hair Care:

It's essential to take care of your hair to ensure it looks healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo:

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Opt for a sulfate-free shampoo that will keep your hair moisturized.

2. Use a hair mask once a week:

A hair mask can nourish and strengthen your hair. Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools:

Heat styling tools can damage your hair, making it look dry and frizzy. Try to avoid using them as much as possible or use a heat protectant spray before using them.

By following these tips, you can look good with a round face. Remember, it's all about creating an illusion of a longer face using different hairstyles, makeup, and fashion styles. So, experiment with different looks and find what works for you!

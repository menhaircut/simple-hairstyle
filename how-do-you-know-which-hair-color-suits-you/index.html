How Do You Know Which Hair Color Suits You

Written by Daniel Oct 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you are not sure which shade will suit you. The right hair color can enhance your natural features and make you look and feel your best. In this article, we will discuss how to determine which hair color suits you best, and provide some tips on how to maintain your new hair color.

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining which hair color suits you is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow, golden, or peach undertones, while cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

You can determine your skin tone by holding up a white piece of paper to your face. If your skin appears yellowish or peachy, you likely have a warm skin tone. If your skin appears pink or bluish, you likely have a cool skin tone. If your skin appears to have a mix of warm and cool tones, you likely have a neutral skin tone.

Consider Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also play a role in determining which hair color suits you. If you have warm undertones in your hair, warm hair colors such as golden blonde, copper, and auburn will likely suit you. If you have cool undertones in your hair, cool hair colors such as ash blonde, platinum, and burgundy will likely suit you.

Understanding Hair Color Shades

Hair colors come in various shades, such as blonde, brown, red, and black. Each shade has warm and cool variations. For example, blonde hair colors can range from warm golden blonde to cool ash blonde. Brown hair colors can range from warm caramel to cool ash brown. Understanding the warm and cool variations of each shade can help you choose a hair color that suits you.

Choosing a Hair Color

When choosing a hair color, it is important to consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and the shade of hair color you prefer. If you are unsure which hair color to choose, consult with a hairstylist or colorist who can recommend a hair color that suits you.

Trying on Hair Colors

Before committing to a hair color, it is a good idea to try on different hair colors to see which one suits you best. You can try on hair colors virtually using apps or websites that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hair colors. You can also try on hair colors in person by using temporary hair color sprays or wigs.

Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you have chosen a hair color, it is important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair color from fading. Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause your hair color to fade faster. Protect your hair from the sun and heat damage by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray when using styling tools.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

  • Consider your skin tone and natural hair color when choosing a hair color.
  • Try on different hair colors virtually or in person before committing to a hair color.
  • Maintain your hair color by using color-safe shampoo and conditioner, avoiding washing your hair too frequently, and protecting your hair from the sun and heat damage.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair a completely different color?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair a completely different color. However, it is important to consider your skin tone and natural hair color when choosing a hair color to ensure that the hair color suits you.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, it is recommended that you consult with a hairstylist or colorist before doing so to ensure that you choose a hair color that suits you and that you follow the instructions on the hair dye package carefully.

Hair Care Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using styling tools.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause your hair color to fade faster.

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your skin tone, natural hair color, and the shade of hair color you prefer, you can choose a hair color that suits you and enhances your natural features. Remember to maintain your hair color by using color-safe shampoo and conditioner, avoiding washing your hair too frequently, and protecting your hair from the sun and heat damage.

How To Wear A Cowboy Hat With Long Hair Guys

Written by Oliver Sep 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a guy with long hair who loves cowboy hats but is not sure how to wear them? If so, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to wear a cowboy hat with long hair guys. We'll also provide tips on how to care for your hair while wearing a cowboy hat.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to wear a cowboy hat with long hair guys:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. This will help your hair lie flat under the cowboy hat.

Step 2: Pull Your Hair Back

Pull your hair back into a tight ponytail or bun at the nape of your neck. This will keep your hair from getting in your face and help it fit better under the cowboy hat.

Step 3: Put on the Cowboy Hat

Hold the cowboy hat by the brim and place it on your head. Tilt the hat slightly forward so that it sits comfortably on your head.

Step 4: Adjust Your Hair

Adjust your hair so that it lies flat under the cowboy hat. You can use bobby pins or hair clips to secure any loose strands.

Tips on Wearing a Cowboy Hat with Long Hair Guys

Consider these tips when wearing a cowboy hat with long hair guys:

Tip 1: Choose the Right Size Cowboy Hat

Make sure you choose a cowboy hat that fits your head properly. If the hat is too small, it will sit too high on your head and make your hair stick out. If it's too big, it will slide down and cover your eyes.

Tip 2: Use Bobby Pins or Hair Clips

Use bobby pins or hair clips to secure any loose strands of hair. This will keep your hair from getting in your face and help it fit better under the cowboy hat.

Tip 3: Avoid Overwhelming Your Hat

Try not to overwhelm your cowboy hat with too much hair. If you have thick, long hair, consider pulling it back into a bun rather than a ponytail.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a cowboy hat with long hair guys:

Q: Can I wear a cowboy hat with my hair down?

A: Yes, you can wear a cowboy hat with your hair down. However, it's best to keep your hair brushed and neat so that it fits comfortably under the hat.

Q: How do I keep my cowboy hat from falling off?

A: Make sure you choose a cowboy hat that fits your head properly. You can also use bobby pins or hair clips to secure the hat to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing a cowboy hat with long hair guys can cause some hair care challenges. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

Tip 1: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy. This will help prevent breakage and split ends.

Tip 2: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid wearing tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, such as tight braids or ponytails. This can cause hair breakage and damage.

Tip 3: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil buildup. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

Now that you know how to wear a cowboy hat with long hair guys, you're ready to rock this classic style. Remember to choose the right size cowboy hat, pull your hair back, and secure any loose strands with bobby pins or hair clips. And don't forget to take care of your hair with regular washing and conditioning.

Men s Hair Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide For 2023

Are you looking for a trendy hairstyle that will make you stand out? Look no further than the men's hair short sides long top style. This versatile and stylish haircut has been a popular choice for years and shows no signs of slowing down in 2023. In this ultimate guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips for styling, and hair care. Let's get started!

How To Achieve Men's Hair Short Sides Long Top

The men's hair short sides long top style is all about contrast. The sides and back are cut short, while the top is left longer for a more dramatic effect. This haircut can be achieved with any hair type and length, but it's best suited for those with thick, straight, or wavy hair. To achieve this look, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist who specializes in men's haircuts. Be sure to bring a picture of the style you want to ensure that your barber or stylist understands your vision.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving men's hair short sides long top:

  1. Visit a barber or stylist who specializes in men's haircuts.
  2. Show them a picture of the style you want.
  3. Ask them to cut the sides and back of your hair short, using clippers or scissors.
  4. Leave the top section of your hair longer, at least 3-4 inches in length.
  5. Ask your barber or stylist to blend the sides and top together for a seamless look.
  6. Style your hair using a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to create texture and hold.

Tips for Styling Men's Hair Short Sides Long Top

Once you've achieved the men's hair short sides long top style, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that suits your hair type and desired look.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as slicked back, messy, or textured.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to help you style your hair.
  • Consider getting a fade or undercut to add more contrast to your hairstyle.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style and keep it looking fresh.

FAQs

Is the men's hair short sides long top style suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle can be adapted to suit any face shape. Your barber or stylist can recommend the best variation of the style that will complement your features.

Can I still achieve the men's hair short sides long top style if I have curly hair?

Yes, curly hair can be styled into the men's hair short sides long top style. However, you may need to use more product to keep your hair in place and prevent frizz.

What styling product is best for the men's hair short sides long top style?

The best styling product for this hairstyle depends on your hair type and desired look. Pomade or wax are popular choices for creating texture and hold, while hairspray can be used to set the style in place.

Hair Care for Men's Hair Short Sides Long Top

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the men's hair short sides long top style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, which can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the style.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve and maintain the men's hair short sides long top style with ease. Whether you're going for a classic or modern look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident. Book your appointment with a barber or stylist today and get ready to rock this trendy haircut!

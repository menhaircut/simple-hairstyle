Best Short Haircuts For Oval Faces In 2023

Written by Aprilia Jul 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! Oval faces are considered the most versatile of all face shapes when it comes to hairstyles. There are many short haircuts that can accentuate your features and bring out the best in your face shape. In this article, we'll go through the best short haircuts for oval faces, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Oval faces are characterized by balanced proportions, with a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin and high cheekbones. This face shape is considered the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles because it can pull off almost any look. However, certain short haircuts can flatter your features and bring out the best in your face shape.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for an oval face, it's essential to consider your hair texture, thickness and personal style. You want a cut that will complement your features and lifestyle. Before going to the salon, do some research on the latest short haircuts for oval faces and bring some pictures to show your stylist. Be clear about your preferences and any concerns you may have.

Step by step

Here are some of the best short haircuts for oval faces:

1. Bob

The bob is a timeless classic that works well for oval faces. It can be styled in many ways and is suitable for all hair types. The length can be chin-length or shorter, depending on your preference. A blunt bob with no layers or a textured bob with choppy layers both work well for oval faces.

2. Pixie

The pixie cut is a bold and edgy short haircut that can bring out the best in an oval face. It's low maintenance and can be styled in many ways, from sleek and sophisticated to messy and tousled. The length can be short or medium, depending on your face shape and hair texture.

3. Shag

The shag is a layered haircut that works well for oval faces. It adds volume and texture to your hair and frames your face beautifully. The length can be short or medium, and the layers can be choppy or feathered, depending on your preference.

4. Lob

The lob is a longer version of the bob that works well for oval faces. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in many ways and suits all hair types. The length can be shoulder-length or slightly shorter, and the ends can be blunt or layered, depending on your preference.

Tips

When styling your short haircut for an oval face, keep these tips in mind:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Avoid heavy bangs or blunt cuts that can overwhelm your face
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair for a sleek and smooth finish
  • Accessorize with hair clips, headbands or scarves to add some personality to your haircut

FAQ

What short haircuts suit oval faces?

The best short haircuts for oval faces are the bob, pixie, shag and lob. These haircuts can flatter your features and bring out the best in your face shape.

How do I style my short haircut for an oval face?

Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume and texture to your hair. Avoid heavy bangs or blunt cuts that can overwhelm your face. Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair for a sleek and smooth finish. Accessorize with hair clips, headbands or scarves to add some personality to your haircut.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Follow these hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days with a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Condition your hair after every wash to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner

With the right short haircut and hair care routine, you can enhance your features and bring out the best in your oval face shape. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!

See What Colour Hair Suits Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hair color but unsure of what shade will best suit you? Look no further! This guide will help you determine what hair color will flatter your skin tone, eye color, and overall style. Keep reading to learn how to see what colour hair suits me.

Description

Choosing a new hair color can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be difficult to determine which shade will best suit your features. This guide will walk you through the process of discovering what hair color will complement your skin tone, eye color, and personal style.

How to Determine What Hair Color Suits You

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining what hair color will suit you is to identify your skin tone. There are three main categories of skin tone: cool, warm, and neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue-green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining what hair color will suit you. Generally, warm-toned hair colors like reds and golds complement warm-toned eyes like brown, hazel, and green. Cool-toned hair colors like ash blonde and platinum complement cool-toned eyes like blue and gray.

Step 3: Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style is an important factor in choosing a hair color. Consider your wardrobe and the colors you typically wear. If you prefer bold, vibrant colors, a bright hair color like red or purple may be a good choice. If you prefer a more natural look, a subtle change like highlights or lowlights may be a better option.

Tips for Choosing a Hair Color

  • Consider the maintenance required for different hair colors. Some shades may require more frequent touch-ups or special products.
  • Consult with a professional stylist for advice and guidance.
  • Try a temporary hair color or wig to test out different shades before committing to a permanent change.
  • Don't be afraid to step outside of your comfort zone and try something new!

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair color looks best on cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones typically look best with hair colors that have blue or violet undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum, or cherry red.

What hair color looks best on warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones typically look best with hair colors that have golden or copper undertones, such as honey blonde, auburn, or chestnut brown.

Can I dye my hair a drastically different color?

If you are considering a drastic change in hair color, it's important to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine the best approach and ensure the health of your hair.

Hair Care

After choosing a new hair color, it's important to take care of your locks to maintain the vibrancy and health of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for color-treated hair, and avoid excessive heat styling or chemical treatments. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and guidelines, you can confidently see what colour hair suits me and choose a new hair color that will flatter your features and complement your personal style. Happy coloring!

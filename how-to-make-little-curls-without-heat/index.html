How To Make Little Curls Without Heat

Written by Frank Oct 29, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of damaging your hair with heat tools to achieve curly hair? Well, you’re in luck! There are plenty of ways to make little curls without any heat. In this article, we will show you how to achieve cute little curls without damaging your hair.

How To:

1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it’s about 80% dry.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. This will help your curls last longer.

3. Section your hair into small pieces. The smaller the section, the tighter the curl.

4. Take each section and twist it tightly. The tighter the twist, the tighter the curl.

5. Once you’ve twisted all of your hair, pin each twist to your head with a bobby pin.

6. Leave your hair pinned for a few hours, or overnight. The longer you leave it, the tighter the curls will be.

7. Remove the bobby pins and gently unravel each twist. Use your fingers to separate the curls and create volume.

Tips:

- Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.

- Apply a small amount of hairspray to set the curls and prevent them from falling apart.

- If you want looser curls, twist your hair less tightly.

- If you want tighter curls, twist your hair more tightly.

FAQ:

Q: How long will the curls last?

A: The curls will last for a few days, depending on your hair type and how well you maintain them.

Q: Can I use this method on wet hair?

A: It’s best to use this method on damp hair, as wet hair will take longer to dry and may not hold the curls as well.

Hair Care:

It’s important to take care of your hair to ensure healthy, strong curls. Here are some tips:

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

- Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

- Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause breakage.

- Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

Now that you know how to make little curls without heat, you can enjoy beautiful, bouncy curls without damaging your hair. Happy styling!

How To Get Messy Hairstyle For Guys Tips Steps And Hair Care

Written by Daniel Jul 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring haircuts? Do you want a hairstyle that is edgy, stylish, and easy to maintain? Then a messy hairstyle might be just what you need. Messy hairstyles have become increasingly popular among men in recent years, and for good reason. They are versatile, low-maintenance, and give off a cool, laid-back vibe. In this article, we will show you how to get the perfect messy hairstyle for guys, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Messy Hair for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Messy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Messy Hair

How to Get Messy Hair for Guys

The first step in getting a messy hairstyle is to choose the right haircut. A messy hairstyle typically involves longer hair on top and shorter hair on the sides and back. You can get this look with a variety of haircuts, including a textured crop, a messy quiff, or a messy fringe.

Once you have the right haircut, the next step is to choose the right styling products. The key to a messy hairstyle is to use products that add texture and volume to your hair. Some popular products for messy hairstyles include:

  • Clay
  • Pomade
  • Sea salt spray
  • Mousse

Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect messy hairstyle:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair.
  3. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots and ends.
  4. Use a hairdryer on low heat to add volume and texture to your hair.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Remember, the key to a messy hairstyle is to make it look effortless. Don't worry about getting every strand of hair in place, and embrace the imperfections. The messier, the better!

Tips for Maintaining Messy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your messy hairstyle:

  • Don't wash your hair every day. Messy hairstyles look best with some natural oils in your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to help you style your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about messy hairstyles for guys:

Q. Can I get a messy hairstyle with short hair?

A. Yes, you can still achieve a messy hairstyle with shorter hair. Just focus on adding texture and volume with the right styling products.

Q. What is the best product for a messy hairstyle?

A. The best product for a messy hairstyle depends on your hair type and desired look. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.

Q. How often should I wash my hair for a messy hairstyle?

A. You should wash your hair every 2-3 days for a messy hairstyle. This will help maintain the natural oils in your hair that add texture and volume.

Hair Care Tips for Messy Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your messy hairstyle looking healthy and strong:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and steps, you can achieve the perfect messy hairstyle for guys. Remember to embrace the imperfections and make it look effortless. Happy styling!

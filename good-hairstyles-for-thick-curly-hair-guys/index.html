Good Hairstyles For Thick Curly Hair Guys

Written by William Sep 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Thick curly hair can be a challenge to manage, but it can also be an asset if you know how to style it properly. In this article, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for guys with thick curly hair.

Description

Thick curly hair is characterized by its volume, texture, and natural curls. It can be a blessing and a curse, as it requires more maintenance than other hair types but also offers a unique look that stands out from the crowd.

How to Style Thick Curly Hair

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it’s important to understand the basics of styling thick curly hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel, to your hair when it’s wet to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls and add volume.

Step-by-Step Guide to Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair Guys

1. The Classic Afro

The classic afro is a timeless hairstyle that looks great on guys with thick curly hair. To achieve this look, simply let your hair grow out and use a pick or comb to shape your hair into a rounded afro shape. You can also add some hair oil or leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

2. The High Top Fade

The high top fade is a popular hairstyle for guys with thick curly hair. This style involves shaving the sides and back of your head while leaving a longer strip of hair on top. You can style the top of your hair into curls or twists for added texture and volume.

3. The Short Curly Cut

If you prefer a shorter hairstyle, consider a curly cut. This style involves cutting your hair short on the sides and back while leaving the top longer and curly. You can use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and add some texture.

4. The Long Curly Style

If you’re not ready to part with your long locks, consider a long curly style. This style involves letting your hair grow out and using a curling wand or rollers to define your curls. You can also tie your hair up into a bun or ponytail for a more polished look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your curls.
  • Use a satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

What products should I use for my thick curly hair?

You should use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, as well as a curl-enhancing product such as mousse or gel. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls hydrated.

How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed depending on your activity level and hair type. Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.

How do I prevent frizz in my thick curly hair?

You can prevent frizz by using a curl-enhancing product, avoiding brushing your hair when it’s dry, and using a diffuser attachment when blow drying your hair. You can also tie your hair up in a satin scarf or bonnet when you sleep to reduce frizz.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Hair Guys

Thick curly hair requires more maintenance than other hair types, but it’s worth it to achieve a unique and stylish look. Here are some tips to keep your thick curly hair looking its best:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product when your hair is wet
  • Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage
  • Stay hydrated and eat a healthy diet

By following these tips and trying out some of the hairstyles we’ve suggested, you can rock your thick curly hair with confidence and style.

What Skin Tone Am I Test Understanding Your Skin Tone

Written by Aprilia Aug 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Knowing your skin tone is essential when it comes to choosing the right makeup, clothing, and hair color. But how do you determine your skin tone? That's where the "What Skin Tone Am I Test" comes in. In this article, we'll discuss how this test works, how to take it, and tips on what to do with the results.

Description

The "What Skin Tone Am I Test" is a quick and easy way to determine your skin tone. It involves looking at the color of your veins, the way your skin reacts to the sun, and the undertones in your skin. Once you know your skin tone, you can choose makeup, clothing, and hair colors that complement it.

How To Take The Test

Here are the steps to take the "What Skin Tone Am I Test":

Step 1: Look At Your Veins

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you may have neutral undertones.

Step 2: Observe Your Skin In Natural Light

Go outside and look at your skin in natural light. If your skin burns easily and doesn't tan, you likely have cool undertones. If your skin tans easily, you likely have warm undertones. If your skin burns then tans, you may have neutral undertones.

Step 3: Check Your Skin's Undertones

Look at the undertones in your skin. If you have cool undertones, your skin will have a pink or rosy tint. If you have warm undertones, your skin will have a yellow or golden tint. If you have neutral undertones, your skin will have a mix of both pink and yellow undertones.

Tips

Here are some tips for using the results of the "What Skin Tone Am I Test":

- If you have cool undertones, choose makeup, clothing, and hair colors with cool undertones like blue, purple, and silver. - If you have warm undertones, choose makeup, clothing, and hair colors with warm undertones like red, orange, and gold. - If you have neutral undertones, you can wear both cool and warm colors. - When choosing foundation, match it to your skin's undertones. - When choosing jewelry, silver complements cool undertones while gold complements warm undertones.

FAQ

What if I Can't Determine My Skin Tone?

If you can't determine your skin tone, you may have neutral undertones. Try wearing both warm and cool colors to see which complements your skin best.

What If I Have Different Undertones on Different Parts of My Body?

It's common to have different undertones on different parts of your body. Focus on the undertones on your face as that will be the most visible.

Hair Care

Knowing your skin tone can also help you choose the right hair color. If you have cool undertones, choose hair colors with cool undertones like ash blonde, platinum, and blue-black. If you have warm undertones, choose hair colors with warm undertones like caramel, honey, and copper. If you have neutral undertones, you can wear both cool and warm hair colors.

Now that you know how to determine your skin tone, you can confidently choose makeup, clothing, and hair colors that complement your skin. Remember to focus on your undertones and choose colors that match them.

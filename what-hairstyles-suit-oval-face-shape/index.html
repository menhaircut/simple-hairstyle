What Hairstyles Suit Oval Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Nov 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to decide which hairstyle to go for. In this article, we'll go over the best hairstyles for oval face shapes, how to achieve them, and some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead and jawline being almost equal in width. This face shape also has a slightly curved jawline and a gently rounded hairline. If you have an oval face shape, you can experiment with different hairstyles and lengths, as almost anything will look great on you.

How to

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a look at your face in the mirror. If your face is longer than it is wide, with a gently curved jawline and a slightly rounded hairline, you have an oval face shape.

Best Hairstyles for Oval Face Shapes

1. Long Layers: Long layers are great for oval face shapes as they accentuate the face's natural shape and add movement and texture to the hair.

2. Shoulder-Length Bob: A shoulder-length bob is a timeless classic that looks great on oval face shapes. It frames the face perfectly and can be worn straight or wavy.

3. Pixie Cut: If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut is a great option for oval face shapes. It shows off your face's natural shape and highlights your best features.

4. Side-Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs are perfect for oval face shapes as they add volume to the hair and create a flattering frame around the face.

Step by Step

1. Long Layers: To achieve long layers, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, blow-dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the ends to create a subtle curl. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold the curl in place.

2. Shoulder-Length Bob: To achieve a shoulder-length bob, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, blow-dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the ends to create a subtle curl. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold the curl in place.

3. Pixie Cut: To achieve a pixie cut, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, use a pair of scissors or clippers to cut your hair short, leaving the top slightly longer than the sides and back. Style with a light pomade or wax to add texture and hold.

4. Side-Swept Bangs: To achieve side-swept bangs, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, blow-dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the bangs to create volume. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks, and finish off with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

1. Experiment with different hairstyles and lengths to find what works best for you.

2. Use heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair to prevent damage.

3. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

4. Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

FAQ

Q: Can oval face shapes pull off bangs?

A: Yes, oval face shapes can pull off bangs, especially side-swept bangs. However, make sure to talk to your stylist about what type of bangs will work best for your face shape.

Q: Can oval face shapes wear short hair?

A: Yes, oval face shapes can wear short hair, such as a pixie cut. However, make sure to talk to your stylist about what length and style will work best for your face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited for your hair type. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup. If you have dry hair, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add moisture.

In addition to regular trims, make sure to get your hair colored or highlighted every few months to keep your hair looking fresh and vibrant. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair, and avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape and keep your hair looking its best.

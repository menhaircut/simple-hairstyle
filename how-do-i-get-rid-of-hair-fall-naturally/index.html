How Do I Get Rid Of Hair Fall Naturally

Written by Christine Oct 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you worried about hair fall? Do you want to know how to get rid of hair fall naturally? Hair fall is a common problem that affects both men and women. There are different reasons for hair fall, such as stress, poor diet, hormonal imbalances, and genetics. Fortunately, there are natural ways to prevent hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. In this article, we will discuss how to get rid of hair fall naturally.

How to Get Rid of Hair Fall Naturally

The following are natural remedies that can help you get rid of hair fall:

1. Amla

Amla or Indian gooseberry is rich in vitamin C and antioxidants that promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. You can use amla oil or amla powder to massage your scalp regularly.

2. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is a natural hair conditioner that nourishes your hair and prevents hair fall. Massage your scalp with warm coconut oil and leave it overnight. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo in the morning.

3. Onion Juice

Onion juice is a natural remedy that stimulates hair growth and prevents hair fall. Apply onion juice to your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes before washing your hair with a mild shampoo.

4. Fenugreek

Fenugreek or methi seeds are rich in proteins and nicotinic acid that promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. Soak fenugreek seeds overnight and grind them into a paste. Apply the paste to your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes before washing your hair with a mild shampoo.

5. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a natural hair conditioner that nourishes your hair and prevents hair fall. Apply aloe vera gel to your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes before washing your hair with a mild shampoo.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get rid of hair fall naturally:

Step 1: Massage Your Scalp

Massage your scalp with warm oil, such as coconut oil, olive oil, or almond oil. This will improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth.

Step 2: Use Natural Remedies

Use natural remedies, such as amla, coconut oil, onion juice, fenugreek, or aloe vera, to prevent hair fall and promote healthy hair growth.

Step 3: Follow a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins, minerals, and proteins. Include foods, such as eggs, fish, nuts, and leafy greens, in your diet to promote healthy hair growth.

Step 4: Manage Stress

Manage stress through yoga, meditation, or other relaxation techniques. Stress can cause hair fall, so it is important to manage stress effectively.

Tips for Preventing Hair Fall

Here are some tips for preventing hair fall:

1. Avoid Using Chemicals

Avoid using chemicals, such as hair dyes, hair straighteners, and hair sprays, as they can damage your hair and cause hair fall.

2. Use a Mild Shampoo

Use a mild shampoo that is free of sulfates and parabens. Sulfates and parabens can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause hair fall.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles, such as ponytails and braids, as they can pull on your hair and cause hair fall.

4. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Protect your hair from heat by using a heat protectant spray or serum before using hot styling tools, such as curling irons and straighteners.

FAQs

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress can cause hair fall. Stress can disrupt the hair growth cycle and cause hair to fall out prematurely.

Q. How often should I wash my hair?

A. You should wash your hair 2-3 times a week with a mild shampoo.

Q. Can diet affect hair fall?

A. Yes, a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins, minerals, and proteins can promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair fall.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

Here are some hair care tips for healthy hair:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

2. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair and prevent hair fall.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a scarf. The sun can damage your hair and cause hair fall.

4. Avoid Over-Brushing

Avoid over-brushing your hair as it can damage your hair and cause hair fall.

By following these natural remedies and hair care tips, you can get rid of hair fall naturally and promote healthy hair growth.

How To Thin Out Bangs Without Thinning Scissors

Written by Petter Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have bangs, you know how important it is to keep them looking neat and tidy. However, sometimes your bangs can start to feel too thick or heavy. The solution? Thin them out! While thinning scissors are the traditional tool for thinning out bangs, not everyone has access to them. That's why we've put together this guide on how to thin out bangs without thinning scissors.

How to Thin Out Bangs without Thinning Scissors

There are several techniques you can use to thin out your bangs without thinning scissors. Here are some of the most effective methods:

1. Twist and Cut

The twist and cut method is a simple but effective way to thin out your bangs. Here's how to do it:

  1. Twist a small section of your bangs tightly.
  2. Cut off the twisted section with a pair of sharp scissors.
  3. Repeat until you've thinned out your bangs to your desired level.

Remember to cut small sections at a time to avoid cutting too much off.

2. Razor Cut

A razor cut is another great way to thin out your bangs. Here's how to do it:

  1. Comb your bangs straight down.
  2. Using a razor blade, lightly shave the ends of your bangs in a downward motion.
  3. Repeat until you've thinned out your bangs to your desired level.

Note: Be careful not to shave too much off, as this can damage your hair.

3. Point Cutting

Point cutting is a technique used by hairdressers to create texture and movement in hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Hold a small section of your bangs between your fingers.
  2. Using a pair of scissors, cut into the ends of your bangs at an angle.
  3. Repeat until you've thinned out your bangs to your desired level.

Again, be careful not to cut too much off at once.

Step by Step Guide to Thinning Out Bangs

Now that you know the different techniques you can use to thin out your bangs, here's a step by step guide to help you get started:

  1. Choose the method you want to use to thin out your bangs.
  2. Gather your tools, including a pair of sharp scissors.
  3. Comb your bangs straight down.
  4. Start cutting small sections of your bangs.
  5. Check your progress frequently to avoid cutting too much off.
  6. Repeat until you've thinned out your bangs to your desired level.
  7. Style your bangs as usual.

Tips for Thinning Out Your Bangs

Thinning out your bangs can be a bit tricky, so here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start small and work your way up.
  • Use a pair of sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Be patient and take your time.
  • Check your progress frequently.
  • Don't cut too much off at once.

FAQ about Thinning Out Bangs without Thinning Scissors

Here are some frequently asked questions about thinning out bangs without thinning scissors:

Can I use regular scissors to thin out my bangs?

Yes, you can use regular scissors to thin out your bangs. Just make sure they're sharp and clean.

How much should I thin out my bangs?

This depends on your personal preference. Start small and work your way up until you're happy with the result.

Can I thin out my bangs if they're curly?

Yes, you can still thin out your bangs if they're curly. Just be careful not to cut too much off, as curly hair tends to shrink when it's cut.

Hair Care Tips for Thinning Out Bangs

Thinning out your bangs can be a great way to refresh your hairstyle, but it's important to take care of your hair afterwards to avoid damage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and treatments that can damage your hair.

Thinning out your bangs without thinning scissors is a great way to refresh your hairstyle and keep your bangs looking neat and tidy. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect look in no time!

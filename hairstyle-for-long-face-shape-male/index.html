Hairstyles For Long Face Shape Male The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Aug 26, 2023 · 3 min read

Description

Are you a male with a long face shape and struggling to find the right hairstyle to flatter your features? Look no further! This ultimate guide will provide you with a variety of hairstyle options that will complement your face shape and enhance your overall appearance.

How to Determine if You Have a Long Face Shape?

Before choosing a hairstyle, it's essential to determine if you have a long face shape. Here's how to do it: - Measure your face length from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin. - Measure the width of your cheekbones. - Measure the width of your jawline. If the length of your face is more significant than the width of your cheekbones and jawline, you have a long face shape.

Step by Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type First, determine your hair type. Is it straight, wavy, or curly? Knowing your hair type will help you choose a style that works best for you. Step 2: Research Hairstyles Do some research on hairstyles that flatter long face shapes. Look for inspiration from celebrities or models with similar face shapes. Step 3: Consult with a Hairstylist Consult with a hairstylist who can provide you with expert advice on which hairstyles will work best for your face shape and hair type. Step 4: Try Different Styles Don't be afraid to try different styles. Experiment with different hair lengths, textures, and styles until you find the perfect one for you.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

- Avoid hairstyles that add too much height to the top of your head. - Opt for styles that add fullness to the sides of your head to balance out your face shape. - Avoid hairstyles with a center part as they can make your face look longer. - Choose a hairstyle that complements your lifestyle and personality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can men with long face shapes have short haircuts? A: Yes, short haircuts can work well for men with long face shapes. Just make sure to avoid styles that add too much height to the top of your head. Q: What hairstyles should men with long face shapes avoid? A: Men with long face shapes should avoid hairstyles with a center part, sleek hairstyles that lay flat against the head, and hairstyles that add too much height to the top of the head.

Hair Care for Men with Long Face Shapes

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. - Use a hairbrush to distribute natural oils throughout your hair. - Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as they can dry out your hair. - Schedule regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and well-groomed.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle can do wonders for your appearance, especially if you have a long face shape. By following the tips and steps provided in this ultimate guide, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your overall look. Remember, don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.

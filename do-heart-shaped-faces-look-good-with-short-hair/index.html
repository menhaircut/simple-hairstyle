Do Heart Shaped Faces Look Good With Short Hair

Written by Robby Jul 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Short hair has been a popular trend for many years now, and it’s not going out of style anytime soon. However, some people may be hesitant to try out a short hairstyle if they have a heart-shaped face. It’s a common misconception that heart-shaped faces don’t look good with short hair, but this couldn’t be further from the truth. In this article, we’ll explore whether heart-shaped faces look good with short hair and provide some tips on how to rock this hairstyle.

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. If you have this face shape, you may be wondering if short hair will flatter your features. The good news is that heart-shaped faces can look great with short hair, as long as you choose the right cut.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Heart-Shaped Faces

When choosing a short hairstyle for a heart-shaped face, there are a few things to keep in mind. You want to create balance and proportion in your features, so avoid cuts that accentuate your forehead or make your chin look even narrower. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Go for a Bob

A bob is a great choice for heart-shaped faces because it adds volume to the chin area, creating balance in your features. You can opt for a chin-length bob or a longer bob that grazes your shoulders.

2. Avoid Short Pixie Cuts

While pixie cuts are trendy and fun, they may not be the best choice for heart-shaped faces. This cut can make your forehead look even wider and your chin look even narrower, creating an unbalanced look.

3. Try a Side-Swept Bang

If you want to keep your hair short but add some dimension to your features, consider adding a side-swept bang. This will draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, creating balance in your face.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Heart-Shaped Faces

Now that you’ve chosen the right cut for your face shape, it’s time to style your hair. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling short hair for heart-shaped faces:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry, or blow-dry it using a round brush to add volume to your hair.

2. Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair to help hold the style in place.

3. Use a Flat Iron or Curling Iron

Depending on the look you’re going for, use a flat iron to straighten your hair or a curling iron to add some waves or curls.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Heart-Shaped Faces

Now that you’ve styled your short hair, it’s important to maintain it so it looks its best. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair for heart-shaped faces:

1. Get Regular Trims

Short hair tends to grow out quickly, so it’s important to get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut.

2. Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you’re using heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can heart-shaped faces pull off a short pixie cut?

A: While it’s not the best choice for this face shape, some heart-shaped faces can pull off a short pixie cut. It’s all about finding the right cut and style for your unique features.

Q: What other hairstyles look good on heart-shaped faces?

A: Heart-shaped faces can also look great with long hair, especially if you add layers or volume around the chin area. Side-swept bangs are also a great choice for this face shape.

Hair Care for Heart-Shaped Faces with Short Hair

When it comes to hair care for heart-shaped faces with short hair, it’s important to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When you’re combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage and damage.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun’s harmful rays.

3. Deep Condition Your Hair

Once a week, deep condition your hair to keep it healthy and hydrated.

In conclusion, heart-shaped faces can look great with short hair as long as you choose the right cut and style for your features. Follow these tips and you’ll be rocking a chic and stylish short hairstyle in no time!

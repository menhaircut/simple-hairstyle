What Hairstyle Suits A Long Oval Face

Written by Petter Nov 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that don't suit your long oval face? Don't worry; we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the hairstyles that are perfect for long oval faces. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long oval face is characterized by its length and narrowness. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width, with the face being longer than it is wide. The best hairstyles for this face shape are those that add volume to the sides, which helps to balance out the length of the face.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your long oval face is to identify your hair type. Is it straight, wavy, or curly? Once you know your hair type, you can start looking at different hairstyles that will work best for you.

Step by step

Here are some steps to help you find the perfect hairstyle for your long oval face:

  1. Identify your hair type
  2. Research hairstyles that work well for long oval faces
  3. Try out different hairstyles and see which ones suit you best
  4. Consult with a hairstylist if needed

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your long oval face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head
  • Choose styles that add volume to the sides of your face
  • Consider bangs to shorten the appearance of your face
  • Opt for layers to add dimension and texture to your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for long oval faces:

  • What hairstyles should I avoid? You should avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as this will only elongate your face further.
  • What hairstyles work best for long oval faces? Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face work best for long oval faces.
  • Should I consider bangs? Bangs can be a great option for those with long oval faces, as they can help to shorten the appearance of the face.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use hair masks and treatments to keep your hair nourished

Now that you know what hairstyle suits a long oval face, it's time to try out some new looks! Remember to keep your hair type, face shape, and personal style in mind when choosing a hairstyle. Happy styling!

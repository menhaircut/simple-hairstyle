Do I Look Good With Short Hair Tips And Tricks To Pull Off A Short Haircut

Written by Robby Sep 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short haircuts have been in trend for quite some time now, and many women are considering chopping off their locks. However, before you make a drastic change, it's natural to wonder, "Do I look good with short hair?" This question can be daunting, but don't worry! This article will provide you with tips and tricks to help you pull off a short haircut with confidence.

How to Determine if Short Hair is Right for You

Before you book an appointment with your hairstylist, consider the following factors:

1. Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining the right haircut for you. Short haircuts suit oval, heart, and square-shaped faces. On the other hand, if you have a round or long face, avoid very short haircuts as they can accentuate the roundness or elongate your face.

2. Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another crucial factor to consider when deciding on a short haircut. If you have thick hair, a short, layered haircut can add volume and movement to your hair. However, if you have fine hair, a blunt bob or a pixie cut can make your hair look fuller.

3. Lifestyle

Your lifestyle also plays a role in choosing a short haircut. If you're always on the go and don't have much time to style your hair, a short haircut can be a low-maintenance option. However, if you enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles, you may want to stick to longer hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Pulling off a Short Haircut

If you've decided to go for a short haircut, here's a step-by-step guide to help you pull it off:

1. Find Inspiration

Look for inspiration by browsing through magazines or social media platforms. Save pictures of hairstyles that you like and show them to your hairstylist.

2. Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist about which short haircut would suit you the best. They will consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle before suggesting a haircut.

3. Take Care of Your Hair

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking sharp. Visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks to trim your hair and maintain the shape of your haircut.

4. Experiment with Styling

Short haircuts offer a lot of versatility when it comes to styling. Experiment with different products and styling tools to find a look that suits you.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for short hair, such as texturizing sprays, pomades, and waxes. These products can add volume and texture to your hair.

2. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.

3. Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every two to three days.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will a short haircut suit me?

If you have an oval, heart, or square-shaped face and the right hair texture, a short haircut can suit you.

2. How often should I trim my hair?

Visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks to keep your haircut looking sharp.

3. How do I style short hair?

Experiment with different products and styling tools to find a look that suits you.

Conclusion

So, do you look good with short hair? The answer is yes, if you choose the right haircut that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Remember to maintain your short haircut regularly and experiment with different styling techniques to find a look that suits you.

Should Chubby Faces Have Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Chubby faces can be a challenge when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. Short hair is often recommended for those with round faces, but is it a good option for those with chubby faces? In this article, we will discuss the pros and cons of short hair for chubby faces, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

Chubby faces are characterized by fuller cheeks and a rounded jawline. While this facial shape can be cute and youthful, it can also be difficult to find a flattering hairstyle. Short hair is often recommended for those with round faces, as it can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. However, it's important to consider your individual features and preferences before making a decision.

How to

If you're considering a short hairstyle for your chubby face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Consider your hair texture

The texture of your hair can play a big role in how a short haircut looks on you. If you have thick, curly hair, a short cut may require more maintenance to keep it looking neat and tidy. On the other hand, if you have fine, straight hair, a short cut can help to add volume and texture.

2. Choose the right length

Short hair can mean anything from a pixie cut to a bob, so it's important to choose a length that flatters your face shape. A bob that hits just below the chin can help to elongate the face, while a super-short pixie cut may make your face look rounder.

3. Consider your lifestyle

A short haircut can be a great option for those with a busy lifestyle, as it requires less time and effort to style. However, if you prefer to wear your hair up or have a formal event coming up, a short cut may limit your styling options.

Step by Step

If you've decided to go for a short haircut, here are some tips on how to style it:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Apply styling products

Apply a styling product such as mousse or gel to your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to add volume and hold your style in place.

3. Use a round brush

Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume. If you have fine hair, consider using a volumizing spray to give your hair extra lift.

4. Finish with hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling short hair on a chubby face:

1. Add layers

Layers can help to add texture and movement to your hair, making it look more voluminous and flattering.

2. Consider bangs

Bangs can help to frame your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. Consider a side-swept fringe or choppy bangs for a trendy look.

3. Experiment with accessories

Try adding a headband, hair clip, or scarf to your short hair to add some extra style and personality.

FAQ

1. Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

It depends on the length and style of the haircut. A super-short pixie cut may make your face look rounder, while a longer bob can help to elongate your face.

2. Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

Yes, but your styling options may be limited. Consider using bobby pins or a hair tie to create a half-up, half-down style.

3. How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to keep your style looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it's still important to take care of your locks. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine to your hair, and protect it from damage.

In conclusion, short hair can be a great option for those with chubby faces, but it's important to consider your individual features and preferences before making a decision. With the right length, styling, and care, you can rock a short haircut that flatters your face and boosts your confidence.

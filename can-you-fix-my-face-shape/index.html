Can You Fix My Face Shape

Written by Robby Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you feeling insecure about your face shape? Do you wish you could change it? If so, you're not alone. Many people are unhappy with the shape of their face, but the good news is that there are ways to fix it. In this article, we'll discuss how to fix your face shape and give you some tips on how to do it.

Description

Face shape is determined by the bones in your face, and it's largely determined by genetics. While you can't change your bone structure, there are ways to enhance your natural features and make subtle changes to your face shape. By using makeup, hairstyles, and other techniques, you can create the illusion of a different face shape.

How to Fix Your Face Shape

If you're looking to fix your face shape, there are a few things you can do. Here are some tips to get you started:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in fixing your face shape is to determine what shape it is. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you know your face shape, you can use makeup and hairstyles to create the illusion of a different shape.

2. Use Makeup

Makeup is a great way to enhance your natural features and create the illusion of a different face shape. For example, contouring can help you create the illusion of higher cheekbones, while highlighting can make your forehead appear larger.

3. Choose the Right Hairstyle

Hairstyles can also help you create the illusion of a different face shape. For example, a bob can help make a round face appear longer, while long layers can help make a square face appear softer.

4. Consider Non-Surgical Options

If you're looking for a more permanent solution, there are non-surgical options available. For example, Botox can help soften a square jawline, while fillers can help add volume to a small chin.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to fix your face shape:

1. Determine your face shape

Use a mirror and a measuring tape to determine your face shape. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Use these measurements to determine your face shape.

2. Use makeup to enhance your natural features

Use contouring and highlighting to create the illusion of a different face shape. For example, contouring can help you create higher cheekbones, while highlighting can make your forehead appear larger.

3. Choose the right hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape. For example, a bob can help make a round face appear longer, while long layers can help make a square face appear softer.

4. Consider non-surgical options

If you're looking for a more permanent solution, consider non-surgical options like Botox and fillers. These can help add volume to a small chin or soften a square jawline.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you fix your face shape:

1. Embrace your natural features

While it's possible to create the illusion of a different face shape, it's important to embrace your natural features.

2. Experiment with different makeup and hairstyles

Experiment with different makeup and hairstyles to find what works best for you.

3. Consult with a professional

If you're considering non-surgical options, consult with a professional to determine the best course of action.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fixing your face shape:

Q: Can I change my bone structure?

A: No, you can't change your bone structure. However, you can use makeup and hairstyles to create the illusion of a different face shape.

Q: How do I determine my face shape?

A: Use a mirror and a measuring tape to determine the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Use these measurements to determine your face shape.

Q: What non-surgical options are available?

A: Botox and fillers are two non-surgical options that can help enhance your natural features and create the illusion of a different face shape.

Hair Care

When fixing your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Condition regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and shiny.

3. Protect your hair from heat

Avoid using heat tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.

By following these tips, you can fix your face shape and take care of your hair at the same time.

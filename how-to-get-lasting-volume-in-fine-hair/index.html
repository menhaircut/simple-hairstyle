How To Get Lasting Volume In Fine Hair

Written by Christine Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have fine hair, you know how challenging it can be to get lasting volume. No matter how much product you use, your hair seems to fall flat within a few hours. But don't worry, there are plenty of tips and tricks to help you achieve lasting volume in your fine hair. In this article, we'll share our top tips for getting the volume you want and keeping it there all day.

How to Prep Your Hair for Volume

Before you start styling your hair for volume, it's important to prep your hair properly. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products are specially formulated to add volume to fine hair. After washing, gently towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or root-lifting spray. These products will help add volume to your hair from the roots.


Use the Right Products

Using the right products is crucial for getting lasting volume in fine hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for fine hair, such as volumizing mousse, root-lifting spray, or dry shampoo. These products will help add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style.


Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

One of the easiest ways to add volume to your hair is to blow dry it upside down. This technique will lift your roots and add volume to your hair. Start by flipping your head upside down and blow dry the roots of your hair. Once your roots are dry, flip your head back over and finish blow drying the rest of your hair. Use a round brush to add volume to the ends of your hair.


Tease Your Roots

Teasing your roots is another great way to add volume to your hair. Start by sectioning your hair and backcombing the roots of each section. Be sure to tease gently to avoid damaging your hair. Once you've teased all the sections, use a brush to smooth the top layer of your hair. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold the volume in place.


Set Your Hair with Hot Rollers

If you want long-lasting volume, try setting your hair with hot rollers. This technique will add volume and curl to your hair, making it look full and bouncy. Start by sectioning your hair and rolling each section onto a hot roller. Leave the rollers in for 15-20 minutes, then remove them and brush out your curls. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold the volume in place.


Tips for Maintaining Volume

Once you've achieved the volume you want, it's important to maintain it throughout the day. Here are a few tips to help you keep your volume all day:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause it to fall flat
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair and add volume throughout the day
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair

FAQs

Q: What is the best type of brush to use for adding volume to fine hair?

A: A round brush is the best type of brush to use for adding volume to fine hair. Use the brush to lift your roots while blow drying your hair.


Q: How often should I wash my hair to maintain volume?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day to maintain volume. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it harder to add volume.


Q: Can I use hot rollers on wet hair?

A: No, it's important to dry your hair completely before using hot rollers. Wet hair can't hold a curl, so your curls will fall flat if you try to use hot rollers on wet hair.


Final Thoughts

Getting lasting volume in fine hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, it's definitely achievable. Start by prepping your hair properly, using the right products, and styling your hair with techniques that add volume. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the volume you want and keep it there all day.

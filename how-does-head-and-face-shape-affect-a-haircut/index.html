How Does Head And Face Shape Affect A Haircut

Written by Frank Oct 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to getting a haircut, one of the most important factors to consider is your head and face shape. Your haircut can either enhance or detract from your best features depending on the shape of your head and face. In this article, we will explore how head and face shape affects a haircut and provide tips on how to choose the best haircut for your unique features.

Description

Your head and face shape play a significant role in the type of haircut that will look best on you. Just like how clothing is tailored to fit your body shape, your hair should also be cut to enhance your facial features. A good haircut will frame your face and accentuate your best features while minimizing any flaws.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the best haircut for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you have identified your face shape, you can choose a haircut that best complements your features.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you have balanced facial features and a slightly rounded hairline. You can pull off a variety of haircuts, including long layers, bobs, and pixie cuts. Avoid hairstyles that obscure your face shape, such as heavy bangs or long hair that covers your face.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, your face is as wide as it is long, with a rounded hairline and full cheeks. Choose a haircut that adds height to your face, such as a layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs. Avoid hairstyles that make your face look rounder, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width. Choose a haircut that softens your angular features, such as a long layered cut or a wavy bob. Avoid blunt cuts or straight bangs that accentuate your square jawline.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, your forehead is wider than your jawline, and you have a pointed chin. Choose a haircut that adds volume to your chin area, such as a long layered cut or a side-swept pixie cut. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your forehead, such as heavy bangs.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower. Choose a haircut that balances your features, such as a long layered cut or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your cheek area.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, your face is longer than it is wide, with a high forehead and prominent chin. Choose a haircut that adds width to your face, such as a shoulder-length cut with layers or a side-swept pixie cut. Avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer, such as long straight hair or a blunt bob.

Step by Step Tips for Choosing a Haircut

1. Determine your face shape.
2. Research haircuts that complement your face shape.
3. Consider your hair texture and lifestyle when choosing a haircut.
4. Bring pictures to your stylist to show them the type of haircut you want.
5. Ask your stylist for their professional opinion on what haircut will best suit your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

1. Keep up with regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
2. Use the right products for your hair type and texture.
3. Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools.
4. Protect your hair from sun damage with a hat or UV-protectant spray.
5. Book regular appointments with your stylist to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

Does hair length affect face shape?

Yes, hair length can affect the appearance of your face shape. Shorter haircuts tend to elongate the face, while longer hairstyles can make your face appear wider.

Can face shape change over time?

No, your face shape is determined by bone structure and does not change over time. However, weight gain and loss can affect the appearance of your face shape.

Can I change my face shape with a haircut?

A haircut can enhance or detract from your natural features, but it cannot change the shape of your face. However, certain hairstyles can create the illusion of a different face shape.

In conclusion, your head and face shape play an important role in choosing the right haircut. By following our tips and considering your unique features, you can find a haircut that enhances your best features and complements your face shape. Remember to take care of your hair with regular trims and the right products to maintain your haircut and keep it looking its best.

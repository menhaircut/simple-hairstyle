How To Control Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

Written by Christine Sep 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick curly frizzy hair, you know the struggle to tame it can be real. But fear not, with the right techniques and products, you can control your hair and rock your natural curls with confidence. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks on how to control thick curly frizzy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Control Thick Curly Frizzy Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep Your Hair Frizz-Free
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Routine for Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

How to Control Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

The first step to controlling thick curly frizzy hair is to use the right products. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for curly hair. These products will help to moisturize your hair, reduce frizz, and define your curls.

After washing your hair, use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and keep them in place. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair and scrunch it in to enhance your curls.

When styling your hair, avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons. These tools can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz. Instead, opt for air-drying your hair or using a diffuser on your blow dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to control thick curly frizzy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  3. Scrunch your hair to enhance your curls and distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  4. Air dry your hair or use a diffuser on your blow dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz.
  5. Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons.

Tips to Keep Your Hair Frizz-Free

Here are some additional tips to keep your curly hair frizz-free:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, detangle your hair when it’s wet using a wide-tooth comb.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It’s best to wash your curly hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my curly hair?

A: It’s best to use a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair, as these products will help to moisturize your hair and reduce frizz.

Hair Care Routine for Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

Here’s a hair care routine for thick curly frizzy hair:

  1. Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  3. Scrunch your hair to enhance your curls and distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  4. Air dry your hair or use a diffuser on your blow dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz.
  5. Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons.
  6. Detangle your hair when it’s wet using a wide-tooth comb.
  7. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  8. Avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.
  9. Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.
  10. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can control your thick curly frizzy hair and rock your natural curls with confidence. Remember, embrace your curls and love your hair!

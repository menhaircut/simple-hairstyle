How To Trim The Top Of Your Hair With Clippers

Written by Christine Aug 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the salon every time you need a haircut? Learning how to trim your own hair can save you time and money in the long run. In this article, we will be discussing how to trim the top of your hair with clippers. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced hair cutter, these tips and tricks will help you achieve a professional-looking haircut.

Description

Trimming the top of your hair with clippers is a relatively easy process that can be done at home. You will need a good set of clippers, a comb, and a mirror. Before starting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can clog the clippers, making them less effective.

How to

Step 1: Start by choosing the right guard size for your clippers. The guard size determines how much hair will be cut. If you’re unsure, start with a larger guard and work your way down gradually.

Step 2: Comb your hair in the direction it naturally falls. This will help you see any uneven areas that need to be trimmed.

Step 3: Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back. Use the clippers to trim the hair in a straight line, keeping the guard flat against your head.

Step 4: Move the clippers up your head, working your way towards the crown. Be sure to keep the clippers level with your head to avoid any uneven cuts.

Step 5: Once you’ve trimmed the top of your hair, move onto the sides and back. Use a smaller guard size for the sides and back to create a tapered look.

Tips

Tip 1: Always start with a larger guard size and work your way down gradually.

Tip 2: Keep the clippers level with your head to avoid any uneven cuts.

Tip 3: Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure everything is even.

Tip 4: Clean your clippers after each use to keep them in good working condition.

FAQ

Can I use scissors instead of clippers to trim the top of my hair?

While it’s possible to use scissors to trim the top of your hair, clippers are much easier to use and will give you a more even cut.

How often should I trim the top of my hair?

It’s recommended that you trim the top of your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important to keep your hair looking healthy and strong. Be sure to wash your hair regularly with a good shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. If you have any concerns about your hair, consult with a professional stylist or dermatologist.

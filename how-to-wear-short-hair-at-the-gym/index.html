How To Wear Short Hair At The Gym

Written by Oliver Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair can be a great way to stay cool during a workout, but it can also be a challenge to keep it in place. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for wearing short hair at the gym, including how to style it, what products to use, and how to care for your hair afterwards.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wear Short Hair at the Gym
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Wearing Short Hair at the Gym
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care After the Gym

How to Wear Short Hair at the Gym

When it comes to wearing short hair at the gym, the key is to find a style that is both functional and stylish. Here are some options to consider:

Ponytail

A classic ponytail is a great option for short hair. To do this, gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with an elastic band. If your hair is too short for a ponytail, try using bobby pins to secure it in place.

Braids

Braids are another great option for short hair. You can do a simple braid down the back of your head or try a more intricate style like a fishtail braid. Braids are great for keeping your hair out of your face and preventing it from getting tangled during your workout.

Headbands

If you prefer to wear your hair down, try using a headband to keep it out of your face. Look for a headband that is made of moisture-wicking material to help keep sweat from your face and hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for wearing short hair at the gym:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose a style that works best for your hair length and texture.
  3. If you are doing a ponytail, gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with an elastic band. If you are doing a braid, start at the top of your head and work your way down.
  4. Use bobby pins to secure any loose pieces of hair.
  5. If you are using a headband, put it on after you have styled your hair.
  6. Make sure your hair is secure and won't come loose during your workout.

Tips for Wearing Short Hair at the Gym

Here are some tips to help you wear short hair at the gym:

  • Use hair ties and bobby pins that won't slip out of your hair.
  • Choose a style that is comfortable and won't distract you during your workout.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality headband to keep your hair out of your face.
  • Don't forget to bring a hairbrush or comb to touch up your hair after your workout.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wear my hair down at the gym?

Yes, you can wear your hair down at the gym, but make sure to use a headband to keep it out of your face. If you have long hair, consider putting it in a braid or ponytail to prevent tangles.

What products should I use to style my short hair at the gym?

Use hair ties, bobby pins, and headbands to keep your hair in place. Consider using a dry shampoo or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care After the Gym

After your workout, make sure to rinse your hair with cool water to remove any sweat and dirt. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.

In conclusion, there are many ways to wear short hair at the gym. Whether you prefer a ponytail, braid, or headband, the key is to choose a style that is both functional and stylish. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to keep your hair in place during even the toughest workouts.

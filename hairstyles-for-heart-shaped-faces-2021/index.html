Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Faces 2021 The Ultimate Guide

In 2021, hairstyles for heart shaped faces are all about enhancing the natural beauty of your face shape. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there are plenty of styles that will flatter your features and highlight your best assets.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips

Description

Heart shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones that taper down to a narrow chin. The goal of hairstyles for heart shaped faces is to balance out these proportions and create a flattering, harmonious look. This can be achieved through a variety of haircuts, colors, and styling techniques.

How To

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and studying the shape of your face in the mirror. If your forehead is wider than your jawline and chin, and your cheekbones are prominent, you likely have a heart shaped face.

Once you've identified your face shape, it's time to start exploring different hairstyles that will complement your features. Some popular options for heart shaped faces include:

Short haircuts

Short haircuts can help to balance out the proportions of a heart shaped face by framing the forehead and drawing attention to the eyes. Pixie cuts, bob hairstyles, and layered crops are all great choices for this face shape. Consider adding bangs or side-swept fringe to further soften your features.

Medium hairstyles

Medium hairstyles are versatile and can be adapted to suit any face shape, but they are particularly flattering on heart shaped faces. Shoulder-length cuts, layered shags, and textured bobs are all great options. Experiment with different partings and styling products to find the look that best suits you.

Long hairstyles

Long hairstyles can be tricky for heart shaped faces, as they can sometimes accentuate the narrowness of the chin. However, with the right cut and styling, they can be incredibly flattering. Consider adding layers and volume to the crown of your head to balance out your features, or try a side-swept fringe to create the illusion of a wider forehead.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to finding the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face:

  1. Determine your face shape by studying your features in the mirror.
  2. Research different hairstyles that are suited to heart shaped faces.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best cut and color for your individual features.
  4. Experiment with different styling techniques and products to enhance the natural beauty of your face shape.
  5. Keep up with regular haircuts and maintenance to ensure your style always looks fresh and flattering.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your heart shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too heavy or flat on top, as these can make your forehead appear even wider.
  • Consider adding layers or volume to the crown of your head to balance out your features.
  • Experiment with different partings and fringe styles to find the look that best suits you.
  • Choose hair colors that complement your skin tone and enhance your natural features.

FAQ

What hairstyles are best for heart shaped faces?

Short haircuts, medium hairstyles, and layered long haircuts are all great options for heart shaped faces. Pixie cuts, bobs, and shoulder-length styles with lots of layers and texture are particularly flattering.

Should heart shaped faces have bangs?

Bangs can be a great way to soften the features of a heart shaped face and create a more harmonious look. Consider side-swept fringe or shorter, choppy bangs, which can help to balance out the proportions of your face.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to ensure it always looks healthy and vibrant. This means washing and conditioning regularly, using products that are suited to your hair type, and protecting your hair from heat damage with a heat protectant spray or serum. Regular trims are also essential to keep your style looking fresh and flattering.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face in 2021 and beyond. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there are plenty of options that will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty.

How To Make Extremely Thin Hair Look Thicker Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Oct 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have extremely thin hair, you understand how frustrating it can be. No matter what you do, it seems like your hair just won't look full and thick. Thankfully, there are a few tricks you can use to make your hair look thicker and more voluminous. In this post, we'll go over some of the best ways to make your thin hair look thicker and more voluminous.

How to Make Extremely Thin Hair Look Thicker

1. Use the Right Products

The right hair products can make all the difference when it comes to thin hair. Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners that are designed to add body and thickness to your hair. Additionally, consider using a root-lifting spray or mousse to give your hair more lift and volume. Be careful not to use too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

2. Cut Your Hair Strategically

The right haircut can make your hair look thicker and more voluminous. Consider getting layers or a shorter haircut to give your hair more body and bounce. Additionally, avoid getting a blunt cut, as this can make your hair look even thinner.

3. Use Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools like curling irons and flat irons can be your best friend when it comes to thin hair. Use a curling iron to add volume and texture to your hair, or use a flat iron to create sleek, voluminous styles. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

4. Try Hair Extensions

If you're looking for an instant fix for thin hair, hair extensions can be a great option. There are many different types of hair extensions available, from clip-ins to tape-ins to sew-ins. Talk to a professional stylist to find the right type of extensions for your hair and budget.

5. Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories like headbands, hair clips, and scrunchies can be a great way to add volume and texture to your thin hair. Try pulling your hair back into a messy bun or ponytail, and add a cute hair accessory for a fun and stylish look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Making Thin Hair Look Thicker

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your thin hair look thicker:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair with a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products will help add body and thickness to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Root-Lifting Spray or Mousse

Apply a root-lifting spray or mousse to your roots to give your hair more lift and volume.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow dry your hair upside down to add more volume and body to your hair. Use a round brush to add even more lift and texture.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron or Flat Iron

Use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture and volume to your hair. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Step 5: Use Hair Accessories

Finish off your look by adding some cute hair accessories like a headband or hair clip. These accessories can help add even more volume and texture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Thick-Looking Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining thick-looking hair:

1. Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it look thin and flat. Try to wash your hair every other day instead.

2. Use a Dry Shampoo

Use a dry shampoo in between washes to help absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair.

3. Consider a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to help nourish and strengthen your hair. Look for masks that are specifically designed for thin, fine hair.

4. Be Gentle with Your Hair

Avoid brushing or styling your hair too aggressively, as this can cause breakage and damage. Use a wide-tooth comb or a soft-bristled brush to detangle your hair gently.

Frequently Asked Questions About Thin Hair

Q: Is it possible to make thin hair look thicker?

A: Yes, it's definitely possible to make thin hair look thicker. By using the right hair products, getting the right haircut, and using heat styling tools, you can add volume and body to your hair.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that work well for thin hair?

A: Yes, there are many hairstyles that work well for thin hair. Consider getting layers or a shorter haircut to give your hair more body and bounce.

Q: Can hair extensions damage thin hair?

A: Hair extensions can potentially damage thin hair if they're not installed or removed properly. Be sure to talk to a professional stylist to find the right type of extensions for your hair and budget.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for thin hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently, as this can help prevent breakage and damage.

2. Avoid Using Hot Tools Too Often

Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and flat irons too often, as this can cause damage to your hair over time.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

4. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look thinner.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your thin hair look thicker and more voluminous. Remember to be gentle with your hair, use the right products, and get the right haircut to achieve the best results.

Men s Hairstyle Long Top Short Sides A Stylish Look For 2023

Written by Petter Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and fashionable hairstyle for men, the long top short sides haircut is a great option. This hairstyle is all about keeping the hair on top long and the sides short, creating a stylish and modern look that's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll take a look at how to achieve this hairstyle, provide tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions about this popular men's hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Men's Hairstyle Long Top Short Sides
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Look
  • Tips for Maintaining the Men's Hairstyle Long Top Short Sides
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Men's Hairstyle Long Top Short Sides

How to Get the Men's Hairstyle Long Top Short Sides

The long top short sides hairstyle is a versatile and popular haircut that can be achieved with a variety of hair types and lengths. Whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair, this hairstyle can work for you. To achieve the look, you'll need to keep the hair on top long, while trimming the sides and back short. This creates a contrast between the long and short hair, giving the hairstyle its signature look.

What You'll Need

To achieve the men's hairstyle long top short sides, you'll need a few items:

  • Scissors or clippers
  • Comb or brush
  • Hair gel or styling product

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Look

Follow these steps to achieve the men's hairstyle long top short sides:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using scissors or clippers, trim the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. For a short sides look, use a clipper with a #2 or #3 guard. For a more dramatic contrast between the top and sides, consider using a #1 or #0 guard.
  3. Leave the hair on top long, trimming it as needed to create the desired length and shape.
  4. Use a comb or brush to style the hair on top, using hair gel or styling product as needed to create a sleek and polished look.

Tips for Maintaining the Men's Hairstyle Long Top Short Sides

Here are some tips for maintaining the men's hairstyle long top short sides:

  • Get regular trims to keep the sides and back short and maintain the contrast with the long hair on top.
  • Use a high-quality hair gel or styling product to keep the hair on top in place and prevent flyaways.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find the look that works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's hairstyle long top short sides:

Can the long top short sides hairstyle work for curly or wavy hair?

Yes! The long top short sides hairstyle can work for a variety of hair types and lengths, including curly and wavy hair.

Is the long top short sides hairstyle low-maintenance?

The long top short sides hairstyle can be low-maintenance, but it does require regular trims to maintain the contrast between the long hair on top and the short hair on the sides and back.

Can I style the long hair on top in different ways?

Yes! The long hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or curly. Experiment with different styles to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care for Men's Hairstyle Long Top Short Sides

To keep your men's hairstyle long top short sides looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and clean.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it harder to style.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Overall, the men's hairstyle long top short sides is a stylish and modern look that's perfect for any occasion. With a little bit of maintenance and care, you can keep your hair looking great and feeling healthy.

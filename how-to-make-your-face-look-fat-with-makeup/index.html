How To Make Your Face Look Fat With Makeup

Are you tired of having a slim face and want to add some volume to it? Well, look no further because we have the solution for you! With the power of makeup, you can make your face look fuller and rounder. In this article, we will take you through the step-by-step process of how to make your face look fat with makeup. We'll also provide some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve that perfect, plump look.

How to Make Your Face Look Fat with Makeup

The first thing you need to do is to prepare your face by cleansing and moisturizing it. Use a primer to create a smooth base for your makeup.

Next, use a foundation that is one to two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply it to the sides of your forehead, temples, and jawline. Blend it well to avoid harsh lines.

Contouring is a crucial step in making your face appear fuller. Use a contouring stick or powder that is two to three shades darker than your skin tone. Apply it to the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline. Blend it well to create a natural-looking shadow.

Highlighting is the next step to make your face look fat with makeup. Use a highlighter that is one to two shades lighter than your skin tone. Apply it to the high points of your face, such as the cheekbones, the bridge of the nose, and the cupid's bow. Blend it well to create a natural glow.

Finally, use a setting powder to set your makeup in place. This will prevent your makeup from smudging or rubbing off throughout the day.

Step-by-Step Process

Step 1: Prepare Your Face

Cleanse and moisturize your face. Use a primer to create a smooth base for your makeup.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Apply a foundation that is one to two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply it to the sides of your forehead, temples, and jawline. Blend it well to avoid harsh lines.

Step 3: Contour Your Face

Use a contouring stick or powder that is two to three shades darker than your skin tone. Apply it to the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline. Blend it well to create a natural-looking shadow.

Step 4: Highlight Your Face

Use a highlighter that is one to two shades lighter than your skin tone. Apply it to the high points of your face, such as the cheekbones, the bridge of the nose, and the cupid's bow. Blend it well to create a natural glow.

Step 5: Set Your Makeup

Use a setting powder to set your makeup in place. This will prevent your makeup from smudging or rubbing off throughout the day.

Tips for Making Your Face Look Fat with Makeup

  • Use a cream or liquid contouring product for a more natural look.
  • Apply a blush to the apples of your cheeks to add some color to your face.
  • Use a volumizing mascara to make your eyes appear bigger.
  • Wear light-colored clothes to create the illusion of a fuller face.

FAQs

Can I use bronzer instead of contouring products?

Yes, you can use a bronzer to contour your face, but make sure that it is not too warm or orange-toned.

What kind of highlighter should I use?

You can use a powder, cream, or liquid highlighter, depending on your preference. Just make sure that it is not too glittery or shimmery.

Hair Care Tips to Complement Your Look

Having the right hairstyle can also help make your face appear fuller. Here are some hair care tips to complement your makeup:

  • Go for a layered haircut to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Create loose waves or curls to frame your face.
  • Add some highlights or lowlights to create dimension.
  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add some body to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your face look fat with makeup and achieve that perfect, plump look. Remember to experiment and have fun with your makeup to find the best look for you. Good luck!

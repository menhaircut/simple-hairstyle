Best Hairstyles For Oblong Face Woman

Written by Bardi Sep 17, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you one of those women who have an oblong or rectangular face shape? If yes, then you may have experienced the struggle of finding the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape. But worry no more, as we have compiled a list of the best hairstyles for oblong face woman that will enhance your beauty and make you feel confident. Let’s dive in!

Description

An oblong or rectangular face shape is characterized by having a longer face with a straight jawline and a high forehead. The goal of a good hairstyle for this face shape is to create an illusion of width and volume to balance out the face shape. You should avoid hairstyles that add length to the face and emphasize the straight lines of the jawline.

How to

Before choosing a hairstyle, you should consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal preferences. It is also recommended to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle that suits your face shape and enhances your features.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow for the perfect hairstyle:

  • Identify your face shape
  • Determine your hair texture
  • Consider your lifestyle and personal preferences
  • Consult with your hairstylist
  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape
  • Style your hair according to the chosen hairstyle

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add length to the face
  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume and width to the face
  • Choose hairstyles that have layers and texture to soften the straight lines of the jawline
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle to shorten the appearance of the face
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for oblong face woman:

What is the best hairstyle for an oblong face woman?

The best hairstyle for an oblong face woman is one that adds volume and width to the face. Some of the best hairstyles for this face shape include layered cuts, bobs, and hairstyles with bangs.

What hairstyles should an oblong face woman avoid?

An oblong face woman should avoid hairstyles that add length to the face and emphasize the straight lines of the jawline. Hairstyles such as long straight hair, center-parted hairstyles, and sleek ponytails should be avoided.

Can an oblong face woman have short hair?

Yes, an oblong face woman can have short hair. Short hairstyles such as bobs and pixie cuts can flatter this face shape if they add volume and width to the face.

Hair Care

After choosing a hairstyle, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and health

Now that you have learned about the best hairstyles for oblong face woman, it’s time to experiment and find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its beauty and health.

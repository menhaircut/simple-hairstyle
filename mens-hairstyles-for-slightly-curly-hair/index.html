Mens Hairstyles For Slightly Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 21, 2023 · 5 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse for men. While it adds texture and dimension to your hair, it can also be difficult to manage and style. If you have slightly curly hair and are looking for some hairstyle inspiration, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll give you some tips, tricks, and styles that will help you make the most of your locks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Slightly Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide for Styling Slightly Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Slightly Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions About Slightly Curly Hair

How to Style Slightly Curly Hair

The key to styling slightly curly hair is to embrace the natural texture and work with it, rather than against it. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or mousse, to damp hair to enhance your curls and add definition.
  • Blow-dry your hair on a low setting with a diffuser attachment to minimize frizz and enhance your curls.
  • Finish with a hairspray or pomade to hold your style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide for Styling Slightly Curly Hair

Ready to try out some new hairstyles for your slightly curly hair? Here's a step-by-step guide for three different looks:

1. The Classic Side Part

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or mousse, to enhance your curls.
  3. Use a comb to create a deep side part.
  4. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or pomade to hold your style in place.

2. The Messy Quiff

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to your hair.
  3. Use your fingers to create a messy quiff by pushing your hair up and back.
  4. Blow-dry your hair on a low setting with a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or pomade to hold your style in place.

3. The French Crop

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to your hair.
  3. Use a comb to create a short, textured fringe at the front of your hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair on a low setting with a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or pomade to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Slightly Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your slightly curly hair and keeping it looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily or dry.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, on your hair too often, as they can damage your curls.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your curls and keep them looking healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions About Slightly Curly Hair

Q: What is the best haircut for slightly curly hair?

A: The best haircut for slightly curly hair depends on your personal style and hair texture. Some popular haircuts for curly hair include the classic taper, the messy crop, and the curly fringe.

Q: Can I straighten my slightly curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your slightly curly hair using a flat iron or straightening brush. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid straightening your hair too often, as it can damage your curls.

Q: How do I prevent frizz in my slightly curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz in your slightly curly hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid brushing your hair, and use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair. You can also apply a small amount of anti-frizz serum to your hair before styling.

Hair Care Tips for Slightly Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your slightly curly hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, on your hair too often, as they can damage your curls.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your curls and keep them looking healthy.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat.

With these tips and hairstyles for slightly curly hair, you'll be able to embrace your natural texture and rock a stylish look that's all your own. Whether you prefer a classic side part or a messy quiff, there's a style out there that will work for you. So go ahead and experiment with different looks until you find the one that's perfect for you.

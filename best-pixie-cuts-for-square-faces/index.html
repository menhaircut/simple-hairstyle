Best Pixie Cuts For Square Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Sep 19, 2023 · 5 min read
Are you looking for the best pixie haircuts for your square face shape? Look no further, as we have rounded up the top options that will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the perfect pixie cut for your square face, along with tips and tricks to maintain and style your new look.

Description

Square faces have strong jawlines and angular features, which can make choosing a suitable hairstyle a challenge. Pixie cuts are a great option for square faces, as they can soften the strong lines and bring out the best in your features. The key is to choose a pixie cut that complements your face shape, and we are here to help you do just that.

How to Choose the Best Pixie Cut for Square Faces

When it comes to choosing a pixie cut for square faces, there are a few factors to consider:

1. Length

The length of your pixie cut will depend on your personal preference and face shape. For square faces, a longer pixie cut that falls just above the ears can help soften the jawline and balance out the face shape. Shorter pixie cuts can also work, but it's best to avoid styles that are too cropped or angular, as they can make your face look more square.

2. Texture

Adding texture to your pixie cut can create movement and soften the angles of your face. Consider adding layers, choppy ends, or a side-swept fringe to your cut. Avoid blunt and straight cuts, as they can emphasize the squareness of your face.

3. Color

Choosing the right hair color can also enhance your pixie cut and complement your square face. Consider warm tones like honey blonde or caramel, as they can soften the face and add warmth to your complexion. Avoid cool tones like platinum blonde or ash brown, as they can make your face look more angular.

Step by Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Pixie Cut

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a pixie cut for square faces, here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect cut:

Step 1: Find Inspiration

Browse through magazines, social media, and celebrity photos to find inspiration for your pixie cut. Look for styles that flatter your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

Schedule a consultation with your stylist to discuss your ideas and preferences. They can offer advice on which pixie cut will suit you best and create a customized look.

Step 3: Prepare for the Cut

Wash and dry your hair before heading to the salon. Avoid using any styling products or heat tools that can damage your hair or alter its natural texture.

Step 4: Cut and Style

Your stylist will cut your hair according to your chosen style, taking into account your face shape and hair texture. They will then style your hair using products that work best for your hair type and desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

To keep your pixie cut looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

1. Regular Trims

Schedule regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your pixie cut.

2. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair or make it look greasy.

3. Experiment with Styling

Try different styling techniques to create different looks with your pixie cut. Use a curling iron to add volume or a texturizing spray for a messy, undone look.

FAQ

What is the best pixie cut for square faces?

Longer pixie cuts that fall just above the ears and have added layers or texture are the best options for square faces.

Can square faces pull off short pixie cuts?

Yes, but it's best to avoid styles that are too cropped or angular, as they can make your face look more square.

What hair color is best for square faces?

Warm tones like honey blonde or caramel can soften the face and add warmth to your complexion.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

1. Wash and Condition Regularly

Wash and condition your hair regularly using products that are suitable for your hair type and texture.

2. Protect from Heat Damage

Use heat protectant sprays or serums before using heat tools like curling irons or straighteners.

3. Deep Condition Regularly

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

4. Protect from Sun Damage

Wear a hat or use hair products with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays. In conclusion, a pixie cut can be a great option for square faces when done right. By considering the length, texture, and color of your cut, along with following our tips and tricks for maintenance and hair care, you can rock a stylish and flattering pixie cut that enhances your natural beauty.

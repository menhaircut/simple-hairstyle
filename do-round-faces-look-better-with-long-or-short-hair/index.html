Do Round Faces Look Better With Long Or Short Hair

Written by Robby Jul 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

There has always been a debate on whether round faces look better with long or short hair. While some people believe that long hair is the way to go for round faces, others swear by short haircuts. So, what's the truth? Let's find out in this detailed blog post.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces have soft, circular features with a width and length that are approximately the same. As a result, they tend to lack definition and can appear wider than they actually are. Choosing the right hairstyle can help enhance the features of a round face and make it look more balanced and proportionate.

How to

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for a round face is to determine the face shape. This can be done by measuring the width of the forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If all three are roughly the same, then it is a round face.

Once you have determined the face shape, the next step is to consider the hair texture and thickness. Fine hair might not hold up well with long hair while thick hair might appear too bulky with short hair. This will help you choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair texture.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for choosing the best hairstyle for a round face:

  1. Measure the width of the forehead, cheeks, and jawline to determine the face shape.
  2. Consider the hair texture and thickness.
  3. Choose a hairstyle that complements the face shape and hair texture.
  4. Experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Long hair can make a round face appear longer and slimmer.
  • Short hair can add definition and angles to a round face.
  • Layered hairstyles can help create the illusion of length and angles.
  • Avoid middle parts and blunt cuts, as they can make a round face appear wider.
  • Consider adding bangs to break up the width of the forehead.

FAQ

Q. Can round faces pull off pixie cuts?

A. Yes, round faces can pull off pixie cuts. However, it's important to choose a style that adds height to the crown of the head to balance out the roundness of the face.

Q. Does hair color matter for round faces?

A. Hair color doesn't necessarily matter for round faces. However, lighter shades tend to reflect more light, which can make the face appear more angular and defined.

Hair Care

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

So, do round faces look better with long or short hair? The answer is that it depends on the individual's face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

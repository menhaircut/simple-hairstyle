What Hairstyle Suits A Chubby Face

Written by Aprilia Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that just don't seem to flatter your chubby face? Don't worry, you're not alone. Finding the right hairstyle can be a real struggle, especially when you have a round or chubby face. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding the perfect hairstyle that complements your chubby face.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle that suits a chubby face, the goal is to create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. This can be achieved by choosing a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown of the head while keeping the sides of the face sleek and tapered. The key is to avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as blunt bangs or heavy layers.

How To

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for a chubby face is to determine your face shape. This can be done by looking in the mirror and measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If these measurements are roughly equal, then you have a round or chubby face.

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to start looking for hairstyles that complement your features. The best hairstyles for chubby faces are those that add height and volume to the crown of the head while keeping the sides sleek and tapered. This can be achieved with a variety of hairstyles, including:

Short Bob

A short bob hairstyle can be a great option for a chubby face. The bob should be cut just above the chin and should be slightly longer in the front than in the back. This will help to create the illusion of a longer face. The hair should be styled with a deep side part and should be kept sleek and straight.

Long Layers

Long layers can also be a great option for a chubby face. The layers should be cut to frame the face and should be tapered towards the ends. This will help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. The hair can be styled with loose waves or kept straight.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold and stylish option for a chubby face. The hair should be cut short and layered to add height and volume to the crown of the head. The sides should be tapered and kept sleek. This will help to create the illusion of a longer face.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to finding the perfect hairstyle for a chubby face:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Research hairstyles that complement your features.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which hairstyle will work best for you.
  4. Choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown of the head while keeping the sides sleek and tapered.
  5. Style your hair according to the chosen hairstyle.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a chubby face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face.
  • Choose hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of the head.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle, as long as they are side-swept and tapered.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a chubby face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a chubby face. However, it's important to keep the ponytail high on the crown of the head to create the illusion of a longer face.

Q: Can I wear short bangs if I have a chubby face?

A: Short bangs can be tricky for a chubby face. It's best to opt for side-swept and tapered bangs that will complement your features and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for your chubby face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle for your chubby face, you can look and feel your best every day.

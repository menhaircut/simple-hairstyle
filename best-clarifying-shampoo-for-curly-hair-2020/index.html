Best Clarifying Shampoo For Curly Hair 2020

Are you tired of dealing with dry, frizzy curls that just won't cooperate? It could be time to switch up your hair care routine and invest in a clarifying shampoo specifically designed for curly hair. In this article, we'll take a look at the best clarifying shampoos for curly hair in 2020 and how they can help you achieve healthy, beautiful curls.

Description

Clarifying shampoos are specially formulated to remove product buildup, excess oil, and other impurities from the hair and scalp. This can be especially beneficial for curly hair types, which tend to be more prone to buildup and dryness. By using a clarifying shampoo once a week or as needed, you can help to restore your hair's natural balance and improve its overall health and appearance.

How to Use Clarifying Shampoo for Curly Hair

When using a clarifying shampoo for curly hair, it's important to follow a few simple steps to ensure that you get the best results:

  • Wet your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  • Apply a quarter-sized amount of clarifying shampoo to your scalp and massage it in gently.
  • Work the shampoo down to the ends of your hair, being careful not to tangle or knot your curls.
  • Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water, making sure to remove all traces of shampoo.
  • Follow up with a conditioner or deep conditioning treatment to restore moisture to your curls.

It's important to note that clarifying shampoos can be more drying than regular shampoos, so it's best to use them no more than once a week or as needed. Be sure to follow up with a moisturizing conditioner or hair mask to help restore moisture to your hair and prevent dryness and breakage.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Clarifying Shampoo for Curly Hair

Here's a more detailed breakdown of how to use clarifying shampoo for curly hair:

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  2. Apply a quarter-sized amount of clarifying shampoo to your palm.
  3. Massage the shampoo into your scalp, working it in gently with your fingertips.
  4. Work the shampoo down to the ends of your hair, being careful not to tangle or knot your curls.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water, making sure to remove all traces of shampoo.
  6. Apply a moisturizing conditioner or hair mask to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  7. Leave the conditioner or hair mask on for 3-5 minutes.
  8. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water to seal the cuticle and prevent frizz.
  9. Gently squeeze excess water out of your hair with a microfiber towel or t-shirt.
  10. Style your hair as usual, using a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls.

Tips for Choosing and Using Clarifying Shampoo for Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing and using clarifying shampoo for curly hair:

  • Look for a clarifying shampoo that is sulfate-free and gentle on your curls.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo no more than once a week or as needed to avoid over-drying your hair.
  • Follow up with a moisturizing conditioner or hair mask to restore moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz and breakage.

FAQs about Clarifying Shampoo for Curly Hair

Q: Can I use clarifying shampoo on color-treated hair?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose a clarifying shampoo that is safe for color-treated hair and won't cause fading or damage.

Q: Will clarifying shampoo dry out my hair?

A: Clarifying shampoos can be more drying than regular shampoos, so it's important to use them sparingly and follow up with a moisturizing conditioner or hair mask.

Q: How often should I use clarifying shampoo?

A: It's best to use clarifying shampoo no more than once a week or as needed to avoid over-drying your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Healthy Curls

Using a clarifying shampoo is just one part of a healthy hair care routine for curly hair. Here are a few other tips to keep your curls looking their best:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can cause damage and breakage to your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls, being gentle and careful not to pull or tug.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and using a clarifying shampoo for curly hair, you can achieve healthy, beautiful curls that are easy to manage and style.

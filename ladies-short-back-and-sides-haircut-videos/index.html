Ladies Short Back And Sides Haircut Videos The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Jul 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are looking for a trendy and daring haircut, the ladies short back and sides haircut may be just what you need. This haircut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look. And the good news is, there are plenty of ladies short back and sides haircut videos available online that can help you achieve the perfect look. In this guide, we will cover everything you need to know about this popular haircut, including how to do it yourself, tips, hair care, and more.

Description

The ladies short back and sides haircut is a bold and edgy style that involves shaving or cutting the hair very short on the sides and back, while leaving the top longer. It is a popular choice among women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish and modern. This haircut can be done in various lengths and styles, depending on your preference and face shape.

How to do it yourself

If you are feeling adventurous and want to try this haircut yourself, there are plenty of ladies short back and sides haircut videos online that can guide you through the process step by step. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Section off the top part of your hair and clip it away.
  3. Using clippers or scissors, cut the hair on the sides and back to your desired length. You can start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter length if you want a gradual fade.
  4. Blend the hair on the sides and back into the top section, using scissors or clippers.
  5. Trim the top section of your hair to your desired length and style it as you like.

Step by step

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to do the ladies short back and sides haircut:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry it until it is damp but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Section off the top part of your hair

Take a comb and create a parting from ear to ear, separating the top of your hair from the sides and back. Clip the top section away for now.

Step 3: Cut the sides and back

Using clippers or scissors, cut the hair on the sides and back to your desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter length if you want a gradual fade. Remember to keep the hairline natural and avoid cutting too high up the nape of the neck.

Step 4: Blend the hair into the top section

Use scissors or clippers to blend the hair on the sides and back into the top section. This will create a seamless transition and avoid any harsh lines.

Step 5: Trim the top section

Unclip the top section and trim it to your desired length. You can leave it longer or go for a shorter pixie cut. Style it as you like, using a hairdryer, brush, and styling products.

Tips

If you are new to the ladies short back and sides haircut, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way down gradually if you are not sure how short you want to go.
  • Consult with a hairstylist or watch plenty of ladies short back and sides haircut videos before attempting it yourself.
  • Invest in good quality clippers or scissors to avoid any mishaps.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Is the ladies short back and sides haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this haircut can be adapted to suit any face shape. A good hairstylist or a ladies short back and sides haircut video can help you choose the right length and style for your face.

Do I need to have a certain hair type for this haircut?

No, this haircut can be done on any hair type, although it may look different depending on your hair's texture and thickness.

How often do I need to get it trimmed?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. On average, you may need to get it trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape.

Hair Care

After getting the ladies short back and sides haircut, it is essential to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair and keep it moisturized.

And that's it! With this guide and plenty of ladies short back and sides haircut videos to choose from, you are ready to rock this daring and stylish haircut. Remember to have fun and experiment with different lengths and styles until you find the one that suits you best.

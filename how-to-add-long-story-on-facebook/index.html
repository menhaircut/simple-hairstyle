How To Add Long Story On Facebook A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to share a long story on Facebook? Maybe it’s a travelogue, a personal essay, or a fictional piece. Whatever the case, Facebook allows you to post long-form content on your profile, page, or group. In this article, we’ll show you how to add a long story on Facebook in just a few easy steps.

Table of Contents:

  1. Step 1: Log in to Facebook
  2. Step 2: Click on “Create Post”
  3. Step 3: Type Your Story
  4. Step 4: Add Formatting
  5. Step 5: Add Photos or Videos
  6. Step 6: Post Your Story
  7. Tips for Adding Long Stories on Facebook
  8. FAQs About Adding Long Stories on Facebook
  9. How to Maintain Healthy Hair While Using Facebook

Step 1: Log in to Facebook

The first step to adding a long story on Facebook is to log in to your account. If you don’t have an account, you can create one for free. Once you’re logged in, you’ll be taken to your newsfeed.

Step 2: Click on “Create Post”

Next, click on the “Create Post” button located at the top of your newsfeed. This will open up a new post window.

Step 3: Type Your Story

Now it’s time to type your story. You can either type it directly into the post window or copy and paste it from a document. Facebook allows you to post up to 63,206 characters, which is roughly equivalent to a 10,000-word document.

Step 4: Add Formatting

If you want to add formatting to your story, such as bold or italicized text, you can do so using the formatting tools located at the bottom of the post window. You can also add numbered lists, bulleted lists, and headings.

Step 5: Add Photos or Videos

If you want to add photos or videos to your story, you can do so by clicking on the camera or video icon located at the bottom of the post window. You can upload up to 10 photos or videos per post.

Step 6: Post Your Story

Once you’re happy with your story, click on the “Post” button located at the bottom right-hand corner of the post window. Your story will now be published on your profile, page, or group.

Tips for Adding Long Stories on Facebook

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when adding long stories on Facebook:

  • Break up your story into shorter paragraphs to make it easier to read.
  • Use headings and subheadings to organize your story.
  • Add photos or videos to break up the text and make it more visually appealing.
  • Edit your story for grammar and spelling errors before publishing.

FAQs About Adding Long Stories on Facebook

Q: Can I edit my story after I’ve published it?

A: Yes, you can edit your story by clicking on the three dots located in the top right-hand corner of the post and selecting “Edit Post” from the dropdown menu.

Q: Can I delete my story after I’ve published it?

A: Yes, you can delete your story by clicking on the three dots located in the top right-hand corner of the post and selecting “Delete Post” from the dropdown menu.

Q: Can I add links to my story?

A: Yes, you can add links to your story by copying and pasting the link into the post window. Facebook will automatically generate a preview of the link.

How to Maintain Healthy Hair While Using Facebook

While using Facebook, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Take breaks from using your computer or phone to reduce eye strain and prevent headaches.
  • Use a blue light filter to reduce the impact of blue light on your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can cause damage.

By following these tips, you can add long stories on Facebook while keeping your hair healthy and looking its best.

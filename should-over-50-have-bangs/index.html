Should Over 50 Have Bangs Pros And Cons

Written by Frank Oct 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair changes, and we may start to wonder if we should change our hairstyle. One question that comes up often is whether or not women over 50 should have bangs. In this article, we'll explore the pros and cons of having bangs at this age, and provide tips and advice for caring for your hair.

The Pros of Having Bangs

There are many benefits to having bangs, especially for women over 50:

1. Youthful Appearance

Bangs can make you look younger by covering forehead wrinkles and creating a more youthful appearance.

2. Easy Styling

Bangs are easy to style and can be a great way to change your look without committing to a full haircut.

3. Versatility

Bangs can be styled in many different ways, from blunt cuts to side-swept styles, making them a versatile option for any occasion.

The Cons of Having Bangs

While there are many benefits to having bangs, there are also some drawbacks to consider:

1. Maintenance

Bangs require regular trimming to maintain their shape and length, which can be time-consuming and costly.

2. Limited Hairstyles

Having bangs can limit your hairstyle options, as they may not work with certain updos or braids.

3. Hair Type

Not all hair types are suitable for bangs, as they may not stay in place or require too much styling to look good.

How to Decide if Bangs are Right for You

If you're considering getting bangs, there are a few factors to consider:

1. Face Shape

Your face shape can play a big role in determining whether or not bangs will look good on you. Oval and heart-shaped faces tend to look best with bangs, while square and round faces may want to avoid them.

2. Hair Type

Your hair type can also impact how bangs will look on you. Fine hair may not be able to hold bangs in place, while thick hair may require more maintenance to keep them looking good.

3. Lifestyle

Your lifestyle and daily routine can also play a role in your decision. If you don't have time for regular trims or styling, bangs may not be the best choice for you.

How to Care for Your Bangs

If you do decide to get bangs, it's important to take good care of them:

1. Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential to maintaining the shape and length of your bangs. Aim to get them trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

2. Proper Styling

Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your bangs, and avoid using too much heat or products that can weigh them down.

3. Washing and Drying

Wash your bangs separately from the rest of your hair, and be sure to dry them completely to avoid frizz and flyaways.

FAQs

Q: Will bangs make me look younger?

A: Bangs can create a more youthful appearance by covering forehead wrinkles and softening facial features.

Q: What face shape looks best with bangs?

A: Oval and heart-shaped faces tend to look best with bangs, while square and round faces may want to avoid them.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: Aim to get your bangs trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Final Thoughts

So, should women over 50 have bangs? Ultimately, the decision is up to you and what you feel most comfortable with. Consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle before making a decision, and be sure to take good care of your bangs if you do decide to get them. With the right care and styling, bangs can be a great way to change up your look and create a more youthful appearance.

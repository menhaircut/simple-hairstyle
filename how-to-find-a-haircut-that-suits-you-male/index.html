How To Find A Haircut That Suits You Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of getting haircuts that don't suit you? It's time to take matters into your own hands and find a haircut that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this guide, we'll show you how to find a haircut that suits you male, along with tips for hair care and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture
  • Step 3: Think About Your Personal Style
  • Tips for Finding the Perfect Haircut

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding a haircut that suits you is to determine your face shape. There are several face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has its own unique features, and certain haircuts can accentuate or detract from those features.

For example, if you have a round face, a haircut that adds volume to the top can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a haircut with shorter sides and a longer top can soften your features. If you're not sure what your face shape is, ask your hairstylist or use a face shape app to find out.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

The next step is to consider your hair texture. Do you have straight, wavy, curly, or coily hair? Different hair textures require different haircuts to look their best. For example, if you have curly hair, a longer haircut can help weigh down your curls and prevent frizz. If you have straight hair, a shorter haircut can add volume and texture.

Step 3: Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style is also an important factor in finding a haircut that suits you. Do you prefer a classic, clean-cut look or something more edgy and modern? Are you willing to spend time styling your hair every day, or do you want a low-maintenance haircut?

Consider your lifestyle, profession, and personal preferences when choosing a haircut. If you're unsure what style suits you best, ask your hairstylist for recommendations based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Tips for Finding the Perfect Haircut

Here are some additional tips for finding a haircut that suits you male:

  • Bring photos of haircuts you like to your hairstylist for inspiration.
  • Ask your hairstylist for their professional opinion on what will suit you best.
  • Consider your hairline and any bald spots when choosing a haircut.
  • Be willing to try something new and experiment with different styles.

FAQs About Men's Haircuts

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircuts:

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair growth rate and personal preference. Most men get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their style, but you may need more or less frequent haircuts depending on your hair type and style.

How do I know if a haircut suits me?

A good haircut should complement your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. If you feel confident and comfortable in your new haircut, it's likely a good fit for you.

What should I tell my hairstylist when getting a haircut?

Be clear about what you want and don't want in a haircut. Bring photos or examples of haircuts you like, and ask your hairstylist for their professional opinion on what will suit you best. Communicate any concerns or preferences you have, such as a desire for a low-maintenance haircut or a need to cover up bald spots.

Hair Care and Maintenance Tips

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care and maintenance tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Style your hair using products that suit your hair texture and personal style.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and steps, you'll be able to find a haircut that suits you male and maintain it for a healthy and stylish look.

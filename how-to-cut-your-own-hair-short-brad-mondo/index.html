How To Cut Your Own Hair Short Brad Mondo A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon and spending a fortune on haircuts? Do you want to try cutting your own hair at home? If you're looking to rock a short hairstyle, you're in luck. In this article, we'll show you how to cut your own hair short Brad Mondo style. Brad Mondo is a popular hairstylist on YouTube, known for his edgy and trendy haircuts. With our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to achieve a stylish and modern look from the comfort of your own home.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, especially if you're not a professional hairstylist. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut at home. In this article, we'll show you how to cut your own hair short Brad Mondo style. We'll cover everything from the tools you'll need to the actual cutting process. We'll also provide tips on how to maintain your new haircut and keep it looking fresh.

How to

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the right tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Spray bottle

Make sure you have all of these tools before you start cutting your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to wash and dry it. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your hair is clean. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid any damage to your hair. Once you've washed your hair, dry it completely using a towel or a blow dryer.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, you'll need to section your hair. Use hair ties to separate your hair into four sections: two on the sides, one on the top, and one at the back. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Now it's time to start cutting your hair. Start with the back section and use your clippers to trim the hair. Use a higher guard for a longer cut or a lower guard for a shorter cut. Move the clippers in an upward motion to create a layered effect. Repeat this process for the other sections, making sure to blend the hair evenly.

Step 5: Use Scissors to Trim

After you've used the clippers, it's time to use scissors to trim your hair. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle to create texture and movement. Use your comb to guide the scissors and make sure you're cutting evenly.

Step 6: Check Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, check it in the mirror to make sure it's even. If you notice any uneven spots, go back and trim them with your scissors.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if you want a shorter style.
  • Use your fingers to guide the clippers for a more natural look.
  • Take breaks if you need to. It's important to stay calm and focused when cutting your hair.
  • Always cut less than you think you need. You can always go back and trim more if you need to.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my hair without clippers?

A: Yes, you can use scissors to cut your hair. However, clippers are recommended for a more even cut.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how you want it to look. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

Now that you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid any damage. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair. Get regular trims to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. With our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to achieve a stylish and modern look from the comfort of your own home.

How To Make Your Face Not Look So Long

Written by Bardi Oct 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you struggle with a long face shape? While it might seem like there's nothing you can do to change your bone structure, there are actually plenty of tricks and tips you can use to create the illusion of a shorter, more balanced face. In this article, we'll explore some of the most effective ways to make your face not look so long, from hairstyles to makeup and beyond.

What Causes a Long Face Shape?

A long face shape is typically characterized by a high forehead, a prominent chin, and a narrow jawline. This type of face shape can be genetic or caused by factors such as aging or weight loss. While there's nothing inherently wrong with a long face shape, some people may feel self-conscious about it and want to find ways to make their face appear shorter or more balanced.

How to Make Your Face Not Look So Long: Tips and Tricks

1. Hairstyles

One of the most effective ways to make your face appear shorter is to choose a flattering hairstyle. Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face or that create the illusion of width can balance out a long face shape. Some hairstyles to consider include:

  • Layered cuts
  • Bangs
  • Bob haircuts
  • Shorter hairstyles
  • Updos with volume at the crown

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style. A professional hairstylist can help you find the perfect cut for your face shape.

2. Makeup

Makeup can also be a powerful tool for creating the illusion of a shorter face shape. Some makeup tips to try include:

  • Contouring: Use a matte bronzer or contour powder to shade the sides of your face and create the illusion of depth
  • Highlighting: Apply a highlighter to the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, and the center of your chin to draw attention away from the length of your face
  • Blush: Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks to create a more rounded, balanced look
  • Lipstick: Choose a bold lip color to draw attention to your mouth and away from your forehead and chin

Remember to blend your makeup well and choose colors that flatter your skin tone.

3. Accessories

Believe it or not, accessories can also play a role in the appearance of your face shape. Some accessories to try include:

  • Statement earrings: Bold earrings can draw attention to your face and add width
  • Hats: Choose hats with a wide brim to balance out a long face shape
  • Sunglasses: Look for sunglasses with a round or oval shape to soften your features
  • Necklaces: Long necklaces can create a vertical line that breaks up the length of your face

4. Posture

Believe it or not, your posture can also affect the appearance of your face shape. Standing up straight with your shoulders back can create the illusion of a shorter, more balanced face. Plus, good posture has numerous other health benefits!

FAQ:

What is the best hairstyle for a long face shape?

The best hairstyle for a long face shape is one that adds volume to the sides of your face or creates the illusion of width. Layered cuts, bangs, and bob haircuts are all great options.

Can makeup really change the appearance of my face shape?

Yes! Contouring, highlighting, and other makeup techniques can create the illusion of a shorter, more balanced face shape.

Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have a long face shape?

Hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, such as high ponytails or tall updos, can make your face appear even longer. Stick to styles that add width to your face instead.

Hair Care Tips for Long Face Shapes

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Some hair care tips for long face shapes include:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, which can damage your hair and make it appear limp
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Consider using a hair mask or other deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and keep it looking shiny and healthy

With these tips and tricks, you can create the illusion of a shorter, more balanced face shape and feel confident in your own skin. Remember to choose hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that flatter your features and make you feel your best!

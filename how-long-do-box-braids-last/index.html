How Long Do Box Braids Last A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Nov 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Box braids are a popular protective hairstyle among women of all ages and hair types. They are easy to maintain and can last for weeks if done correctly. However, you may be wondering, how long do box braids last? In this article, we will answer that question and provide you with tips on how to care for your box braids to ensure they last as long as possible.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Box Braids
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Box Braids
  • Tips for Maintaining Box Braids
  • FAQs About Box Braids
  • Hair Care Tips for Box Braids

How to Get Box Braids

Box braids are a type of protective hairstyle that involves braiding your hair into small, square-shaped sections. The braids can be done with natural hair or with extensions. If you want to get box braids, you can either do them yourself or have them done by a professional stylist.

If you decide to do them yourself, you will need to purchase braiding hair, a comb, and hair ties. You can find tutorials online or in hair care books to guide you through the process. If you decide to have them done by a professional, make sure to research the stylist beforehand and ask for pictures of their previous work.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Box Braids

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting box braids:

  1. Wash and condition your hair before braiding.
  2. Divide your hair into small, square-shaped sections.
  3. Use a comb to part your hair into the desired size of each section.
  4. Attach the braiding hair to each section using the desired braiding technique.
  5. Braid each section to the desired length.
  6. Seal the ends of the braids with hot water or a lighter.

Tips for Maintaining Box Braids

Here are some tips for maintaining your box braids:

  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet at night to prevent frizzing and tangling.
  • Oil your scalp every few days to keep it moisturized and prevent itching.
  • Wash your braids every two weeks with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Take breaks between braiding to give your hair time to rest and breathe.

FAQs About Box Braids

Here are some frequently asked questions about box braids:

How long do box braids last?

Box braids can last anywhere from two to eight weeks depending on how well you take care of them and the quality of the braiding hair.

Can I swim with box braids?

Yes, you can swim with box braids, but make sure to wear a swim cap and rinse your hair with clean water afterwards to remove any chlorine or saltwater.

Can I wash my box braids?

Yes, you can wash your box braids, but make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner and focus on cleansing your scalp.

Hair Care Tips for Box Braids

Here are some hair care tips for box braids:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your braids to prevent damage.
  • Keep your braids moisturized with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Trim any frayed or split ends to keep your braids looking neat.
  • Take breaks between braiding to give your hair time to rest and breathe.

Now that you know how long box braids can last and how to care for them, you can confidently rock this protective hairstyle all season long. Remember to take breaks between braiding and keep your scalp and braids moisturized to ensure they last as long as possible.

How To Make Thin Hair Look Thick In A Braid

Written by Aprilia Jul 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thin hair, you may feel like your braid never looks as thick as you want it to. But fear not, there are ways to make your braid look thicker and fuller. In this guide, we will give you tips and tricks on how to make thin hair look thick in a braid.

Description

Thin hair can be difficult to style, especially when it comes to braids. But with the right techniques and products, you can make your braid look thicker and fuller. Whether you have short or long hair, these tips will help you achieve the braid of your dreams.

How to

1. Use volumizing products

Before you start braiding your hair, make sure to use volumizing products. Products like mousse, root lifters, and volumizing sprays can help create volume and texture in your hair, making it easier to braid. Apply the product to your roots and work it through your hair, then blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.

2. Tease your hair

Teasing your hair can also help create volume and texture, making your braid look thicker. Use a fine-tooth comb to tease your hair at the crown of your head, then smooth the top layer with a brush. This will give your hair a lift and make it easier to braid.

3. Use hair extensions

If you want an instant solution to thin hair, hair extensions are a great option. Clip-in hair extensions can add length and volume to your hair, making it easier to braid. Make sure to choose extensions that match your hair color and texture for a natural look.

4. Start with a small braid

If you have thin hair, starting with a small braid can help make it look thicker. Begin with a three-strand braid, then gently pull on the strands to create a fuller braid. You can also pancake the braid by gently pulling on the edges to make it wider and fuller.

5. Add accessories

Adding accessories like headbands, hairpins, and scrunchies can also help make your braid look thicker. Use a thick headband to create a faux crown braid or add small hairpins throughout your braid to create the illusion of thickness.

Step by Step

Step 1:

Apply volumizing products to your hair and blow dry upside down to add volume.

Step 2:

Tease your hair at the crown of your head to create volume and texture.

Step 3:

Start with a small three-strand braid and gently pull on the strands to create a fuller braid.

Step 4:

Add accessories like headbands or hairpins to create the illusion of thickness.

Tips

  • Use a comb to gently pull on the edges of your braid to make it wider and fuller.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands in your braid.
  • Use a hairspray to set your braid in place.

FAQ

Can I make my braid look thicker without using products?

While using volumizing products can help make your braid look thicker, you can also try teasing your hair or using hair accessories to create the illusion of thickness.

Can I use a hairpiece instead of hair extensions?

Yes, you can use a hairpiece like a bun or ponytail holder to add volume to your hair. Make sure to choose a hairpiece that matches your hair color and texture for a natural look.

Hair Care

When it comes to thin hair, it's important to use products that won't weigh your hair down. Look for lightweight products like mousses and sprays that will add volume without making your hair greasy or heavy. Also, make sure to wash your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and shiny.

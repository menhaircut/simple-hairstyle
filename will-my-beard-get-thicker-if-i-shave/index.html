Will My Beard Get Thicker If I Shave

Written by Christine Nov 30, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're wondering whether shaving your beard will make it grow thicker, you're not alone. Many men believe that shaving their facial hair will lead to thicker, fuller growth, but is this really the case?

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Let's get one thing straight: shaving your beard will not make it grow thicker. This is a common myth that has been perpetuated for decades, but it has no scientific basis. The thickness and growth rate of your beard are determined by genetics, hormones, and other factors that are outside of your control.

So if you're hoping that shaving your beard will magically transform it into a thicker, fuller version of itself, you're out of luck.

How to

If you want to grow a thicker beard, there are a few things you can do to promote healthy growth. Start by taking care of your skin. Use a gentle cleanser to remove dirt and oil, and moisturize regularly to keep your skin hydrated.

You can also try using a beard oil or balm to nourish your facial hair and promote growth. These products contain natural ingredients like jojoba oil, argan oil, and shea butter, which can help to soften and strengthen your beard.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can take to promote healthy beard growth:

  1. Cleanse your skin with a gentle cleanser.
  2. Moisturize your skin regularly.
  3. Use a beard oil or balm to nourish your facial hair.
  4. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  5. Exercise regularly to boost circulation and promote hair growth.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you grow a thicker, healthier beard:

  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as these can inhibit hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your beard.
  • Consider taking a biotin supplement to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Be patient – it can take several months for your beard to grow in fully.

FAQ

Here are answers to some frequently asked questions about beard growth:

  • Will shaving my beard make it grow back thicker? No, this is a myth.
  • How can I make my beard grow faster? There is no guaranteed way to speed up beard growth, but taking care of your skin and eating a healthy diet can help.
  • What can I do if I have patchy facial hair? You can try using a beard oil or balm to promote growth, or consider consulting with a dermatologist about other treatment options.

Hair Care

In addition to taking care of your skin and using beard oil or balm, it's important to take care of your hair as well. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated, and avoid using hot tools or harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

By following these tips and taking good care of your beard, you can promote healthy growth and achieve the look you want. Remember, shaving your beard will not make it grow thicker – but with a little patience and care, you can achieve the beard of your dreams.

How To Cut Layers In Men s Long Length Hair

Written by Bardi Jun 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle and add some texture to your long locks, consider cutting layers into your hair. Layers can add volume, movement, and style to any long length hair, and it's a great way to switch things up without losing too much length. In this article, we'll go over how to cut layers in men's long length hair, step by step.

Description

Long hair can be challenging to manage, but layers can make all the difference. Layers can help to reduce weight, add volume, and give movement to long hair. Cutting layers in men's long length hair can be done with some basic tools and techniques. Whether you're a professional hairstylist or a DIY enthusiast, you can easily learn how to cut layers in men's long length hair.

How to Cut Layers in Men's Long Length Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to section and cut. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the crown, left side, right side, and back. Use hair clips to secure each section and keep it out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Crown Section

Starting with the crown section, take a small section of hair and hold it straight up. Use your scissors to cut a small amount of hair at an angle, moving towards the back of your head. Repeat this process, working your way around the crown section.

Step 4: Cut the Left and Right Side Sections

Next, move to the left and right side sections. Take a small section of hair from each side and hold it straight up. Use your scissors to cut a small amount of hair at an angle, moving towards the back of your head. Repeat this process, working your way around each side section.

Step 5: Cut the Back Section

Finally, move to the back section. Take a small section of hair and hold it straight up. Use your scissors to cut a small amount of hair at an angle, moving towards the top of your head. Repeat this process, working your way up the back section.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting layers in men's long length hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure clean cuts
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, start with small sections
  • Keep your fingers straight while cutting to ensure a consistent angle
  • Check your progress in a mirror as you go

FAQ

How long should my hair be to cut layers?

Your hair should be at least shoulder-length for layers to be effective. If your hair is shorter than that, consider adding some texture with a different cutting technique.

Can I cut layers in my own hair?

Yes, you can cut layers in your own hair. However, it's always best to have a professional hairstylist cut your hair if you're unsure about the technique.

Hair Care

After cutting layers into your hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your layers

Cutting layers in men's long length hair can be a great way to add some personality and style to your look. With some basic tools and techniques, you can easily learn how to cut layers in your own hair or someone else's. Remember to take good care of your hair after cutting layers to keep it looking healthy and stylish.

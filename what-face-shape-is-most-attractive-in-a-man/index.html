What Face Shape Is Most Attractive In A Man

Written by Petter Sep 13, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to attractiveness, the shape of a man's face is a prominent factor that can either make or break their appeal. In this article, we'll explore what face shape is most attractive in a man, how to determine your face shape, and how to style your hair to enhance your facial features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into what face shape is most attractive in a man, it's essential to know how to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, square, round, heart, diamond, and oblong.

The easiest way to determine your face shape is to stand in front of a mirror and trace your face's outline with a bar of soap or a washable marker. Alternatively, you can take a photo of yourself facing straight ahead and trace the outline of your face on the photo.

Once you have your face shape, you can start exploring what hairstyles and grooming techniques work best for you.

Why Oval Face Shape is Most Attractive in A Man

According to studies, an oval face shape is the most attractive in a man. An oval face shape has balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the jawline. Men with an oval face shape have a chiseled jawline and high cheekbones, making them look more masculine and attractive.

However, this doesn't mean that other face shapes are not attractive. With the right grooming techniques and hairstyle, men with other face shapes can enhance their facial features and look equally attractive.

Square Face Shape

Men with a square face shape have a broad forehead, a strong jawline, and a square chin. To enhance their facial features, they should opt for hairstyles that add volume on top and soften the angular jawline. A textured crop or a messy fringe can work well for this face shape.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape has soft angles and a circular shape. Men with a round face shape should avoid hairstyles that add volume on the sides and instead opt for styles that add height on top. A side-parted undercut or a quiff can work well for this face shape.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape has a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. Men with a heart face shape should opt for hairstyles that balance out their facial features, such as a textured fringe or a layered cut that adds volume on the sides.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin. Men with a diamond face shape should opt for hairstyles that add volume on the sides and create a more rounded look, such as a messy fringe or a layered cut.

Styling Tips for Different Face Shapes

When it comes to styling your hair, there are a few general tips that can work for different face shapes:

  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face look too long or too wide.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume on top and balance out your facial features.
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: What is the most attractive hairstyle for men?

A: There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question as different hairstyles work for different face shapes and hair types. However, a textured crop, a messy fringe, and a side-parted undercut are generally considered to be attractive hairstyles for men.

Q: Can facial hair enhance my facial features?

A: Yes, facial hair can enhance your facial features, but it should be groomed and styled according to your face shape. Men with an oval face shape can experiment with different beard styles, while men with a round face shape should avoid a full beard and opt for a well-groomed stubble.

Hair Care Tips for Different Face Shapes

Regardless of your face shape, taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining healthy hair and enhancing your facial features. Here are a few general hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and exploring what face shape is most attractive in a man, you can enhance your facial features and look your best.

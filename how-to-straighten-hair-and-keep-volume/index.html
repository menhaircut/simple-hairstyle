How To Straighten Hair And Keep Volume A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly or wavy hair and want to achieve a sleek, straight look, you might think that you have to sacrifice volume in the process. But fear not! With the right technique and products, you can straighten your hair and keep volume at the same time. Read on to learn how.

Table of Contents

  • How to Straighten Hair and Keep Volume
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Keeping Volume
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Routine

How to Straighten Hair and Keep Volume

The key to straightening hair without losing volume is to use the right tools and products. Here's what you'll need:

  • A high-quality flat iron with adjustable heat settings
  • A heat protectant spray or serum
  • A volumizing mousse or spray
  • A comb or brush

Before you start, make sure your hair is dry and detangled. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair, focusing on the ends. Then, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and mid-lengths. This will help to create volume and hold throughout the day.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to straighten your hair while keeping volume:

  1. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work through each section and ensure that you don't miss any areas.
  2. Starting with the bottom section, use your flat iron to straighten your hair. Hold the iron vertically and clamp down on a small section of hair near the roots. Then, rotate the iron 180 degrees and pull it down the length of your hair. Repeat this process until all of the hair in the section is straight.
  3. Once you have straightened all of the sections, use a comb or brush to gently tease your roots. This will help to create volume and prevent your hair from looking flat.
  4. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Keeping Volume

Here are some additional tips to help you keep volume in your straightened hair:

  • Avoid using heavy styling products or oils, as these can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Choose a flat iron with adjustable heat settings, and use a lower heat setting if your hair is fine or damaged.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to help distribute natural oils throughout your hair and create shine.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about straightening hair and keeping volume:

Can I straighten my hair without heat?

Yes, you can straighten your hair without heat using a straightening brush or a keratin treatment. However, these methods may not be as effective at creating volume.

How do I prevent my hair from getting frizzy?

Use a heat protectant spray or serum before straightening your hair, and avoid brushing your hair too much. You can also try using a smoothing serum or oil to tame flyaways.

How often should I straighten my hair?

It's best to limit heat styling to once or twice a week to avoid damaging your hair. You can also try using heat-free styling methods, such as braids or buns, to give your hair a break.

Hair Care Routine

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to have a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot water.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant product when you do.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth and strength.

By following these tips and using the right tools and products, you can straighten your hair and keep volume at the same time. Happy styling!

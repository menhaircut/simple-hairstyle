How To Make Different Hairstyles For Medium Hair At Home

Written by Frank Jul 04, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have medium hair, you're in luck. Medium hair is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. In this article, we'll show you how to make different hairstyles for medium hair at home. Whether you're heading to work, a party, or just want to switch up your look, we've got you covered. Read on for step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Different Hairstyles for Medium Hair at Home
  • Step by Step Instructions
  • Tips for Styling Medium Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Advice for Medium Hair

Step by Step Instructions

1. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are perfect for a relaxed, boho look. To achieve this style, start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow drying it on a low heat setting. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Run your fingers through your hair to separate the curls and spritz with a texturizing spray.

2. Half-Up, Half-Down

The half-up, half-down style is easy to achieve and perfect for any occasion. Start by brushing your hair and pulling the top half up into a ponytail. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie and fluff up the hair to create volume. You can leave the bottom half of your hair straight or add some curls for extra texture.

3. Braided Crown

A braided crown is a beautiful and elegant style for medium hair. Start by parting your hair down the middle and braiding each side. Once both braids are complete, wrap them around the top of your head and secure with bobby pins. You can leave the rest of your hair down or pull it back into a low ponytail.

4. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a quick and easy style for medium hair. Start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail and securing it with a hair tie. Twist the ponytail around the hair tie to create a bun and secure with bobby pins. Leave a few strands of hair out to frame your face and pull out a few strands from the bun to create a messy look.

Tips for Styling Medium Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your medium hair:

  • Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type (e.g., curly, straight, fine, thick, etc.)
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you
  • Don't be afraid to use accessories like hair clips, headbands, and scarves to add some extra flair to your style

FAQ

Q: Can I style my medium hair without heat?

A: Yes, there are plenty of heat-free styling options for medium hair. Try braiding your hair overnight to create waves, or use a texturizing spray to add some volume and texture.

Q: How often should I wash my medium hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and styling routine. If you use a lot of products or heat on your hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. However, if your hair is dry or damaged, you may want to wash it less often to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care Advice for Medium Hair

Here are some general tips for keeping your medium hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth

With these tips and styling ideas, you're sure to find a look that works for you. Have fun experimenting and trying out new styles!

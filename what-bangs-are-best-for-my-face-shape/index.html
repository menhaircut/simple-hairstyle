What Bangs Are Best For My Face Shape

Written by Eveline Nov 10, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you thinking about getting bangs but not sure which style will suit your face shape? Choosing the right bangs can enhance your features and give you a fresh, new look. In this article, we’ll guide you on what bangs are best for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Bangs
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Your Bangs

Description

Bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add a touch of style and personality to your look. Bangs come in different lengths and styles, and choosing the right one can be a daunting task. The key to finding the perfect bangs is to determine your face shape and choose a style that complements it.

How to Choose the Right Bangs

The first step in choosing the right bangs is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a bang style that will complement your features.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you’re in luck! This face shape looks good with almost any bang style. Side-swept bangs, blunt bangs, and wispy bangs all work well with oval faces.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, choose bangs that create angles and lengthen your face. Side-swept bangs that are longer on one side than the other, or choppy bangs that hit at the cheekbones, both work well with round faces.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, choose bangs that soften your angular features. Side-swept bangs that are longer on one side than the other, and wispy bangs that are longer at the sides, work well with square faces.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, choose bangs that balance the width of your forehead with the rest of your face. Side-swept bangs that are longer on one side than the other, and layered bangs that hit at the cheekbones, work well with heart faces.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, choose bangs that soften your angular features. Side-swept bangs that are longer on one side than the other, and wispy bangs that are longer at the sides, work well with diamond faces.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, choose bangs that create the illusion of width. Side-swept bangs that are longer on one side than the other, and blunt bangs that hit at the eyebrows, work well with oblong faces.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape and the types of bangs that work best with it, follow these steps to get the perfect bangs:

  1. Choose the right length – The length of your bangs should complement your face shape and features. If you have a high forehead, opt for longer bangs that hit at the brows. If you have a smaller forehead, choose shorter bangs that hit above the brows.
  2. Choose the right style – Once you’ve determined the right length, choose a bang style that complements your face shape. Consult with your stylist to get their expert opinion.
  3. Maintain your bangs – Keep your bangs trimmed and styled regularly to maintain their shape and length. Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your bangs as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

To keep your bangs looking their best, follow these tips:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their length and shape.
  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh between washes.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them oily and limp.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes! Curly hair can look great with bangs. Choose a bang style that complements your face shape and use a diffuser when blow-drying to maintain your curls.

Q: How do I know if I should get bangs?

A: Consult with your stylist to determine if bangs would work well with your hair type and face shape. They can help you choose the right style and length for your features.

Hair Care for Your Bangs

To keep your bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your bangs daily or every other day to keep them clean and fresh.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs, as this can weigh them down and make them look greasy.
  • Brush your bangs gently with a soft-bristled brush to avoid damaging them.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your bangs too often, as this can cause damage and split ends.

Now that you know what bangs are best for your face shape, it’s time to choose a style and make an appointment with your stylist. Whether you opt for side-swept bangs, blunt bangs, or wispy bangs, the right style can enhance your features and give you a fresh, new look.

Short Hairstyles For Over 60 Fine Hair 2019

Written by Frank Jul 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are over 60 with fine hair, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But, that doesn't mean you can't look stylish and trendy. In this blog post, we'll discuss some of the best short hairstyles for over 60 fine hair in 2019. We'll cover how to style them, tips for maintaining them, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

As we age, our hair naturally starts to thin out, making it more difficult to style. This is where short hairstyles come in. They are easier to manage, require less maintenance, and can make you look younger and more vibrant. There are several short hairstyles that work well for over 60 fine hair, from pixies to bobs to shags.

How to

Before you start styling your short hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to add body and texture. If you have a pixie cut, use a bit of styling wax to create a messy, tousled look. For a bob, use a straightening iron to create sleek, smooth locks. For a shag, use a curling iron to add some loose waves.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.
2. Apply volumizing mousse or spray.
3. Use styling wax for a pixie cut.
4. Use a straightening iron for a bob.
5. Use a curling iron for a shag.
6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

1. Don't be afraid to try something new.
2. Ask your stylist for recommendations.
3. Use products specifically designed for fine hair.
4. Experiment with different styling techniques.
5. Consider adding highlights or lowlights for added dimension.

FAQ

What is the best short hairstyle for fine hair?

The best short hairstyle for fine hair is one that adds volume and texture. Pixie cuts, bobs, and shags are all great options.

How often should I get my hair cut?

You should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and prevent split ends.

Can I color my fine hair?

Yes, you can color your fine hair. Just make sure to use products that are gentle and won't damage your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you do use heat, make sure to use a heat protectant spray. Additionally, try to limit the use of hair products, as they can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Overall, short hairstyles for over 60 fine hair in 2019 are all about adding volume and texture. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can look stylish and trendy at any age. So, go ahead and try something new!

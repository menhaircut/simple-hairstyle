This trendy and versatile hairstyle frames your face while still allowing you to see clearly. However, like any new haircut, curtain bangs require some training to look their best. How to Train Curtain Bangs

The first step in training your curtain bangs is to wash and dry your hair. Make sure your bangs are completely dry before you begin, as wet hair can be harder to work with.

Next, divide your bangs into two sections, one on either side of your part. Use hair clips to secure the sections in place.

Now comes the training part. Take one section of your bangs and hold it straight up. Using a round brush, roll the ends under and blow dry on high heat. Repeat this process with the other section of your bangs.

Once your bangs are dry, gently brush them out and part them down the middle. Your curtain bangs should now be framing your face and falling to either side of your part.

Step-by-Step Guide

To make it easier, here is a step-by-step breakdown of how to train your curtain bangs:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Divide your bangs into two sections using hair clips.
  3. Hold one section of your bangs straight up.
  4. Using a round brush, roll the ends under and blow dry on high heat.
  5. Repeat the previous step with the other section of your bangs.
  6. Once your bangs are dry, gently brush them out.
  7. Part your bangs down the middle.

Tips for Maintenance and Care

Now that your curtain bangs are trained, itâs important to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintenance and care:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them at the desired length.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh between washes.
  • Avoid touching or playing with your bangs too much, as this can cause them to become greasy or misshapen.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when blow drying or styling your bangs to prevent damage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

How long does it take to train curtain bangs?

Training your curtain bangs can take a few weeks to a few months, depending on how often you style them and how quickly your hair responds to the training.

Can I train my curtain bangs without a blow dryer?

A blow dryer is the most effective tool for training your curtain bangs, but you can also use a straightening iron or hot rollers to achieve the same effect.

Do curtain bangs work with all hair types?

Curtain bangs can work with all hair types, but may require more training or maintenance for those with curly or thick hair.

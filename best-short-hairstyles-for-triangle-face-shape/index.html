Description

Triangle face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and a wide jawline. If you have this face shape, you can enhance your features by choosing the right short hairstyle. In this article, we will guide you through the best short hairstyles for triangle face shape in 2023. You will learn how to determine your face shape, choose the right haircut, style it, and take care of it.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you choose a short hairstyle for triangle face shape, you need to determine your face shape. Here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Examine your face and look for the widest point.
  3. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  4. Compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

The four most common face shapes are round, square, heart, and triangle. If your jawline is wider than your forehead, you have a triangle face shape.

Step-by-step Guide to Choosing the Best Short Hairstyles for Triangle Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the best short hairstyles for triangle face shape. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate the width of your jawline.
  • Try a layered pixie cut with side-swept bangs.
  • Consider a bob with layers and volume at the crown.
  • Experiment with a short shaggy cut.

The key is to add volume and balance to your face shape. Avoid hairstyles that emphasize the narrowness of your forehead or the width of your jawline.

Tips for Styling Short Hair for Triangle Face Shape

Styling short hair for triangle face shape can be challenging, but with the right techniques, you can create a flattering look. Here are some tips:

  • Use volumizing products to add texture and lift to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to create volume at the crown.
  • Use a round brush to smooth your hair and create a side-swept bang.
  • Avoid center parts and sleek hairstyles that emphasize your jawline.
  • Add highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

With these tips, you can create a stylish and flattering short hairstyle for triangle face shape.

FAQs About Short Hairstyles for Triangle Face Shape

Q. Can I wear a bob with a triangle face shape?

A. Yes, you can wear a bob with a triangle face shape, but make sure it has layers and volume at the crown. Avoid a blunt bob that accentuates the width of your jawline.

Q. Should I avoid short haircuts with a triangle face shape?

A. No, you can wear short haircuts with a triangle face shape, but choose a style that adds volume to the top of your head and balances your features.

Q. Can I wear bangs with a triangle face shape?

A. Yes, you can wear bangs with a triangle face shape, but choose side-swept bangs that create a diagonal line across your face and add volume to your forehead.

Hair Care Tips for Triangle Face Shape

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and beautiful hairstyle for triangle face shape. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your short hairstyle for triangle face shape looking its best.

In conclusion, the best short hairstyles for triangle face shape in 2023 are those that add volume and balance to your features. With the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care, you can create a stylish and flattering look that enhances your natural beauty.

Medium Length Hairstyles For Curly Hair 2020 A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curls are beautiful, and they are in trend. If you have curly hair, then you should embrace it and flaunt your curls with pride. Medium length hairstyles for curly hair 2020 are the perfect way to showcase your curls. In this guide, we will provide you with all the information you need to know about medium length hairstyles for curly hair 2020.

Description

Medium length hairstyles for curly hair 2020 are all about embracing your natural curls. The hairstyles are designed to enhance the natural texture and volume of your curls. Unlike short hairstyles, medium length hairstyles provide more styling options, and they are perfect if you want to change your look without sacrificing the length of your hair.

How to

There are several ways to style medium length hairstyles for curly hair 2020. Here are some of the most popular ways:

1. Curly Bob

The curly bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, you should cut your hair to chin length and add layers to enhance the volume and texture of your curls. You can style your hair with a side part or a middle part, depending on your preference.

2. Curly Shag

The curly shag is a layered hairstyle that is perfect for medium length curly hair. The shag provides a lot of volume and texture to your curls, and it is easy to style. You can keep your curls loose, or you can use a diffuser to enhance the texture of your curls.

3. Curly Lob

The curly lob is a longer version of the bob. It is a great hairstyle for those who want to keep their hair longer but still want to showcase their curls. To achieve this look, you should cut your hair to shoulder length and add layers to enhance the volume and texture of your curls.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve medium length hairstyles for curly hair 2020:

1. Wash your hair

The first step is to wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to define your curls and reduce frizz.

2. Apply styling product

Apply a curl-enhancing styling product to your damp hair. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place throughout the day.

3. Diffuse your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance the texture of your curls. Diffusing your hair will also help to reduce frizz.

4. Style your hair

Style your hair according to the medium length hairstyle that you have chosen. You can use a curling iron to add more curls or use a straightener to straighten some sections of your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling medium length hairstyles for curly hair 2020:

  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to define your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance the texture of your curls.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair.

FAQ

Q. Can I still wear my hair up with medium length hairstyles for curly hair?

A. Yes, you can still wear your hair up with medium length hairstyles for curly hair. You can try a messy bun or a half-up-half-down style.

Q. Will medium length hairstyles for curly hair work for all curl types?

A. Yes, medium length hairstyles for curly hair can work for all curl types. However, the styling techniques may vary depending on your curl type.

Q. How often should I wash my curly hair?

A. It is recommended that you wash your curly hair every 2-3 days. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can cause frizz.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to take care of your curly hair:

  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry
  • Use a microfiber towel or a t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel

With these tips, you can keep your curly hair healthy and beautiful.

Conclusion

Medium length hairstyles for curly hair 2020 are a great way to showcase your natural curls. With the right styling techniques and hair care, you can achieve beautiful and healthy curly hair. Try out different medium length hairstyles and find the one that works best for you.

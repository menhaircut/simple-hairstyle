What Glasses Look Like On My Face A Guide To Choosing The Right Frames

Written by Bardi Sep 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you wear glasses, you know that finding the right frames can be a challenge. Glasses not only impact your vision, but they also play a significant role in your overall appearance. In this article, we'll explore what glasses look like on your face and how to choose the right frames for your features.

Description

Glasses come in all shapes and sizes, from round to square, thin to thick. The frames you choose will depend on your face shape, skin tone, and personal style. For example, if you have a round face, rectangular or square frames will help balance out your features. If you have a heart-shaped face, cat-eye or oval frames can help soften your look.

When choosing glasses, it's essential to consider not only the frames but also the lenses. You can choose from a variety of materials, including plastic, metal, and titanium, each with its own benefits and drawbacks.

How to Choose the Right Frames

Choosing the right frames can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect glasses:

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape is the most critical factor to consider when choosing frames. There are four basic face shapes: round, square, oval, and heart-shaped. Depending on your face's shape, certain frames will look better than others.

Think About Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone can also play a role in the frames you choose. If you have warm undertones, gold or brown frames will complement your complexion. If you have cool undertones, silver or black frames will look best.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your glasses should fit your lifestyle. If you're active or play sports, you might want to consider frames that are durable and lightweight. If you work in an office, you might want to choose frames that are more professional-looking.

Try Before You Buy

The best way to find the right frames is to try them on. Most eyewear stores will allow you to try on different frames before making a purchase. Take your time and try on as many options as possible to find the perfect pair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Frames

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the right frames:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Look in the mirror and determine your face shape. If you're not sure, ask a friend or family member for their opinion.

Step 2: Consider Your Skin Tone

Determine your skin tone. If you're not sure, hold a piece of white paper next to your face. If your skin looks yellowish, you have warm undertones. If your skin looks bluish, you have cool undertones.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle and the activities you participate in regularly. This will help you determine the type of frames you need.

Step 4: Try On Different Frames

Visit an eyewear store and try on different frames. Take your time and try on as many options as possible.

Tips for Wearing Glasses

Here are some tips for wearing glasses:

Take Care of Your Hair

Your hair can impact how your glasses look on your face. Keep your hair clean and styled, and avoid covering your frames with your hair.

Choose the Right Makeup

Choose makeup that complements your frames. If you wear thick frames, you might want to choose a bold lip color. If you wear thin frames, you might want to focus on your eyes.

Keep Your Glasses Clean

Keep your glasses clean to ensure they look their best. Use a microfiber cloth and lens cleaner to clean your lenses regularly.

FAQs

What glasses look best on a round face?

Rectangular or square frames are best for a round face. These shapes help balance out your features.

What glasses look best on a square face?

Round or oval frames are best for a square face. These shapes help soften your features.

What glasses look best on an oval face?

Most frame shapes look good on an oval face. However, rectangular or square frames can help balance out your features.

What glasses look best on a heart-shaped face?

Cat-eye or oval frames are best for a heart-shaped face. These shapes help soften your features.

Hair Care Tips for Glasses Wearers

If you wear glasses, you know that they can sometimes cause your hair to look flat or greasy. Here are some hair care tips for glasses wearers:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free from oil and product buildup. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Use Dry Shampoo

If you're short on time or can't wash your hair, use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair.

Style Your Hair Away From Your Face

Style your hair away from your face to avoid covering your frames. You can try a half-up, half-down style or a sleek ponytail.

Use a Volumizing Spray

If your hair looks flat, use a volumizing spray to add volume and texture. Spray the product at the roots and fluff your hair with your fingers.

In conclusion, glasses can make a significant impact on your appearance. It's essential to choose the right frames for your face shape, skin tone, and lifestyle. Try on different frames and take your time to find the perfect pair. And don't forget to take care of your hair to ensure you look and feel your best!

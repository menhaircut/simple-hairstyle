How To Find Out Which Hair Colour Suits You

Written by Daniel Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking about changing your hair colour but unsure of what will suit you best? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be providing you with a step-by-step guide on how to find out which hair colour suits you. We'll also be sharing some tips and answering frequently asked questions to ensure you make the best decision for your hair.

  • How to Find Out Which Hair Colour Suits You
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to find out which hair colour suits you best:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding the right hair colour is to determine your skin tone. This will help you choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. You can do this by examining the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you have difficulty determining your skin tone, you can always consult a professional hair stylist.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also play a role in determining which hair colour suits you best. If you have blue or green eyes, cool shades of blonde or brown will work well for you. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warm shades of brown or red will complement your eye colour.

Step 3: Take Your Natural Hair Colour into Consideration

If you're not looking for a drastic change, consider your natural hair colour when choosing a new shade. Going no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural hair colour will give you a subtle change that will still enhance your natural beauty.

Step 4: Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hair colour, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you lead an active lifestyle and spend a lot of time outdoors, a low-maintenance hair colour may be best for you. On the other hand, if you have a lot of free time and enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles, a high-maintenance hair colour may be a good option.

Step 5: Consult a Professional

If you're still unsure of which hair colour suits you best, consult a professional hair stylist. They can provide you with expert advice and help you choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips to consider when choosing a hair colour:

  • Consider your wardrobe and choose a hair colour that complements your clothing.
  • Think about the maintenance involved in maintaining your new hair colour.
  • Choose a shade that complements your personality and style.
  • Consider the health of your hair and choose a hair colour that won't cause damage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the right hair colour:

What's the best way to determine my skin tone?

Examining the veins on your wrist is the easiest way to determine your skin tone.

How do I know if a hair colour will suit me?

You can try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how a hair colour will look on you before making a decision.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it's possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend consulting a professional hair stylist to ensure the best results.

Hair Care Tips

After changing your hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

By following these steps and tips, you'll be able to find the perfect hair colour that enhances your natural beauty and suits your lifestyle.

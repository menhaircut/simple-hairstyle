Hairstyles For Long Face The Ultimate Guide In 2023

If you have a long face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. But fear not, because we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll show you the best hairstyles for long faces, as well as tips and tricks to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.

Table of Contents

  • Hairstyles for Long Face
  • Tips and Tricks

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we get into the best hairstyles for long faces, it's important to determine your face shape. The easiest way to do this is to stand in front of a mirror and analyze the shape of your face. Here are the most common face shapes:

  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded hairline and jawline.
  • Round: Your face is as wide as it is long, with a rounded hairline and jawline.
  • Square: Your face is as wide as it is long, with a strong, angular jawline.
  • Heart: Your face is wider at the temples and narrows at the jawline.
  • Long: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a high forehead and a narrow jawline.

If you have a long face shape, keep reading for the best hairstyles to flatter your features.

Hairstyles for Long Face

When it comes to hairstyles for long faces, the key is to add width and volume to the sides of your face. Here are some of our favorite hairstyles for long face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great choice for long face, as they add movement and volume to your hair. Ask your stylist for long layers that start at your chin or collarbone. This will create the illusion of width around your face.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for long face. They create a diagonal line across your forehead, which breaks up the length of your face. Plus, they're easy to style and can add volume to your hair.

3. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic choice for long face. It adds width to the sides of your face, while the length at the front elongates your neck. A chin-length bob is a great option, as it frames your face and adds volume.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a daring choice for long face, but it can also be incredibly flattering. A short, choppy pixie cut can add volume and texture to your hair, while the short length draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for styling your hair if you have a long face:

  • Try to avoid hairstyles that are too long or too straight, as they can elongate your face even more.
  • Use volumizing products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to add volume to your hair.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for your face shape.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new! Sometimes, the best hairstyles are the ones you never thought would look good on you.

FAQ

1. Can I wear my hair in a center part if I have a long face?

A center part can elongate your face even more, so it's best to avoid this style if you have a long face. Instead, try a side part or a diagonal part to add width to your face.

2. Should I get a fringe if I have a long face?

Yes, a fringe can be a great way to break up the length of your face. Side-swept bangs or a wispy fringe can add volume and texture to your hair.

3. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a long face?

A high ponytail can elongate your face, so it's best to avoid this style if you have a long face. Instead, try a low ponytail or a messy bun to add volume to your hair.

Hair Care

Finally, here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, such as a curling iron or straightener.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is tailored to your hair type.

With these tips and hairstyles for long face, you're sure to find the perfect look to flatter your features. Whether you opt for long layers or a chic pixie cut, remember to have fun and experiment with your hair!

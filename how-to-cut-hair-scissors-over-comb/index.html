How To Cut Hair Scissors Over Comb A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for an efficient way to cut hair that ensures precision and accuracy? Look no further than the scissors over comb technique. This technique involves using scissors and a comb to cut hair in a controlled manner, ensuring a consistent length throughout the hair. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to cut hair scissors over comb, providing you with tips and tricks along the way.

How To Cut Hair Scissors Over Comb

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before you begin cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Comb through the hair to remove any tangles or knots. You can also use a hair clip to separate sections of hair that you will be cutting.

Step 2: Choose the Right Comb and Scissors

Using the right tools is crucial for a successful scissors over comb haircut. Choose a comb that is sturdy and has fine teeth. For the scissors, opt for a pair that is sharp and comfortable to hold.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Hold the comb at a 45-degree angle to the head and use it to lift a section of hair. Hold the scissors parallel to the comb and cut the hair to the desired length. Repeat this process, working your way around the head and cutting in a consistent manner.

Step 4: Check for Balance

Once you have finished cutting, check for balance by running your fingers through the hair. If you notice any unevenness, make small adjustments by lifting and cutting the hair as needed.

FAQs

What is the benefit of cutting hair scissors over comb?

The scissors over comb technique provides an even and consistent cut, making it a great option for creating a clean and polished look. It is also a quick and efficient method of cutting hair, making it a popular choice for professional stylists.

Can I use scissors over comb on any hair type?

Yes! Scissors over comb can be used on any hair type, from fine and straight to thick and curly. It is a versatile method of cutting that can be tailored to meet the needs of each individual client.

Tips for Cutting Hair Scissors Over Comb

Here are some tips to help you master the scissors over comb technique:

  • Practice makes perfect! Don't be discouraged if it takes a few tries to get the hang of this technique.
  • Hold the comb and scissors with a firm grip to ensure control and precision.
  • Keep the comb and scissors at a consistent angle throughout the haircut to ensure an even length.
  • Work in small sections to ensure accuracy and prevent mistakes.
  • Don't forget to check for balance and make any necessary adjustments before finishing the cut.

Hair Care After Cutting Hair Scissors Over Comb

After cutting hair scissors over comb, it is important to take care of the hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can cause damage to the hair.
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the desired length.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to confidently cut hair scissors over comb and achieve a polished and professional look. So grab your comb and scissors and get started!

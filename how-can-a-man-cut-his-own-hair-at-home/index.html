How Can A Man Cut His Own Hair At Home

Written by Christine Jul 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of shelling out money for haircuts or want to try something new, cutting your own hair at home might be a great option for you. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a great haircut without ever leaving your house. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know to cut your own hair at home.

Table of Contents

  • How To Cut Your Own Hair At Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

How To Cut Your Own Hair At Home

The key to cutting your own hair at home is having patience, the right tools, and a good technique. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want to consider investing in a hair cutting cape or towel to keep hair off your clothes and a spray bottle to keep hair damp while cutting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own hair at home:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, it will be more challenging to cut, and you could end up cutting more than you intended.
  2. Use the clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair. Start with the longest guard and work your way down to the desired length. Remember to go slowly and use a mirror to check your progress.
  3. Use scissors to trim the top of your hair. Hold sections of hair between your fingers and cut the ends off with the scissors. Go slowly and take your time to avoid cutting too much off.
  4. Blend the sides and top together. Use the clippers without a guard to create a fade between the sides and top of your hair. You can also use scissors to blend the hair together.
  5. Trim the hair around your ears and neck. Use the clippers or scissors to trim any hair that hangs over your ears and neck.
  6. Check your work in the mirror. Turn your head from side to side and check for any uneven spots or missed areas. Touch up as needed.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting your own hair:

  • Start with a longer guard on the clippers and work your way down to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Use a spray bottle to keep your hair damp while cutting. This will make it easier to see the lines and prevent hair from flying around.
  • Trim a little at a time. It's better to take your time and make multiple passes than to cut too much off at once.
  • Watch tutorials online to get a better idea of technique and to see how others have cut their own hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair at home:

  • Can I cut my own hair with regular scissors? While it's possible to cut your own hair with regular scissors, it's best to use hair cutting scissors, as they're sharper and designed for cutting hair.
  • How often should I cut my own hair? It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how long you want it to be. As a general rule, most men get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks.
  • What if I mess up? If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always go to a professional stylist to fix the issue.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own hair, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a hair styling product, such as gel or wax, to style your hair as desired.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style.

Cutting your own hair at home can be a fun and cost-effective way to change up your look. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve great results. Just remember to take your time, start with a longer guard, and watch tutorials to get a better idea of technique. Happy cutting!

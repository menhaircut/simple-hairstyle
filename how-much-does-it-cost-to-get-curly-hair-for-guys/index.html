How Much Does It Cost To Get Curly Hair For Guys

If you're a guy looking to add some texture to your hair, you might be wondering how much it costs to get curly hair. The answer depends on a few factors, including the method you choose and the length of your hair. In this article, we'll break down the costs associated with getting curly hair for guys and provide some tips for maintaining your new look.

How to Get Curly Hair

Before we dive into the costs of getting curly hair, let's talk about the different methods for achieving this look. Here are a few ways you can get curly hair:

  • Use a curling iron or wand to create curls or waves
  • Get a perm, which involves chemically treating your hair to create curls
  • Use a curl enhancing product, such as mousse or gel, to enhance your natural curls or create waves

Each of these methods has its pros and cons, so it's important to do your research and choose the one that's best for you.

Methods for Getting Curly Hair

Let's take a closer look at the methods for getting curly hair:

Using a Curling Iron or Wand

If you have straight or slightly wavy hair, you can use a curling iron or wand to create curls. This method is relatively inexpensive, as you can purchase a basic curling iron for around $20. However, it can be time-consuming and may not be suitable for all hair types.

Getting a Perm

A perm is a chemical treatment that involves wrapping your hair around rods and applying a solution that breaks down the hair's natural structure, allowing it to reform into curls. This method is more expensive than using a curling iron, with prices ranging from $75 to $250 or more depending on your hair length and the salon you choose. It's also important to note that perms can be damaging to your hair and may require more maintenance than other methods.

Using a Curl Enhancing Product

If you have natural curls or want to enhance your waves, you can use a curl enhancing product such as mousse or gel. These products range in price from around $5 to $20 and can be found at most drugstores or beauty supply stores. This method is the least expensive of the three and can be a good option if you're on a budget.

Costs of Getting Curly Hair

Now that you know the different methods for getting curly hair, let's talk about the costs associated with each one:

  • A basic curling iron costs around $20
  • A perm can cost anywhere from $75 to $250 or more
  • Curl enhancing products range in price from around $5 to $20

It's important to note that these prices may vary depending on your location and the salon you choose. Additionally, if you opt for a perm, you may need to factor in additional costs for maintenance and touch-ups.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Once you've achieved your desired level of curliness, it's important to take care of your new 'do. Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your curls
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl defining cream to keep your curls moisturized and defined

FAQ

Can guys get perms?

Yes, guys can get perms. Perms are a popular way for men to add texture and volume to their hair.

How long does a perm last?

A perm typically lasts for around 3-6 months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

Can I get curly hair without using heat or chemicals?

Yes, you can enhance your natural curls or create waves using a curl enhancing product such as mousse or gel.

Hair Care

Now that you know how much it costs to get curly hair for guys and how to maintain your new look, it's time to start experimenting with different styles. Remember to be patient and take good care of your hair to ensure that your curls look their best.

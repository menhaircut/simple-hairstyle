Best Haircut For Oval Face With Glasses A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone with an oval face and glasses, struggling to find the perfect haircut that complements your face shape and glasses? Look no further, as we have put together the ultimate guide for the best haircut for oval face with glasses. In this article, we will cover everything from how to determine your face shape, to the most flattering haircuts for oval faces with glasses, and tips for maintaining your new haircut. Let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for oval faces with glasses, it's important to determine your face shape first. Here's how:

  1. Take a photo of yourself facing straight ahead, with your hair pulled back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face shape using a photo editing tool or by printing out the photo and tracing it on paper.
  3. Compare your traced face shape to the following shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. If your traced shape is longer than it is wide, you have an oval face shape.

The Best Haircuts for Oval Faces with Glasses

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's take a look at the most flattering haircuts for oval faces with glasses:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for oval faces with glasses, as they add movement and dimension to your hair without overwhelming your face. This haircut works well with all types of glasses, especially those with thicker frames.

2. Bob

A classic bob is another great option for oval faces with glasses. This haircut is versatile and can be customized to suit your personal style. Opt for a shorter bob if you have a smaller frame or a longer bob if you have a larger frame. This haircut works well with all types of glasses, but looks particularly chic with cat-eye frames.

3. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut is a great option for oval faces with glasses. This haircut is low-maintenance and perfect for those who want to make a statement. This haircut works well with all types of glasses, but looks particularly stylish with round frames.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

Now that you've found the perfect haircut for your oval face with glasses, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to maintain your haircut's shape.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products that are specifically formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools excessively, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective hair product.

FAQ

What glasses frames work best with oval faces?

Almost any type of glasses frames work well with oval faces, but square or rectangular frames can help balance out the natural curves of your face.

Can oval faces pull off bangs?

Yes! Oval faces can pull off almost any type of bangs, but side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are particularly flattering.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair is crucial in maintaining a healthy, shiny, and beautiful mane. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid damaging it and causing split ends.

And there you have it! Our guide to the best haircut for oval faces with glasses. We hope this article has helped you find the perfect haircut for your face shape and glasses. Remember to always consult with your stylist before making any drastic hair changes, and to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

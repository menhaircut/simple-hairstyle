How To Put Your Hair Up When You Have Short Hair

Written by Bardi Oct 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have short hair, you may feel limited in your styling options. But fear not, there are plenty of ways to put your hair up and create a stylish look. In this article, we will go over how to put your hair up when you have short hair, step by step.

Description

Short hair can be a challenge to style, but there are plenty of options for putting your hair up. From braids to buns, there are many different techniques to try. With a little practice, you can create a variety of stylish looks that will help you feel confident and put together.

How to

Follow these steps to put your short hair up:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. This will make it easier to work with and help your style hold up throughout the day.

Step 2: Choose your style

There are many different styles you can try, depending on the length and texture of your hair. Some popular options include:

  • Braids
  • Buns
  • Half-up hairstyles
  • Ponytails

Choose the style that works best for you and your hair type.

Step 3: Gather your supplies

Depending on the style you choose, you may need some supplies to help hold your hair in place. This could include bobby pins, hair ties, or hairspray. Make sure you have everything you need before you start styling.

Step 4: Section your hair

If you are doing a more complex style, you may need to section your hair. Use clips or hair ties to keep each section separate and make it easier to work with.

Step 5: Start styling

Now it's time to start styling your hair. Follow the steps for your chosen style, working carefully to create the desired look.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Once you have your hair styled the way you want it, finish off with a spritz of hairspray to help hold everything in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when putting your short hair up:

  • Use bobby pins and hair ties that match your hair color for a more natural look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.
  • Use hairspray sparingly to avoid a crunchy, stiff look.

FAQ

Q: Can I put my short hair up in a ponytail?

A: Yes, you can put short hair up in a ponytail. Use a hair tie that is small enough to hold your hair without slipping.

Q: How do I keep my short hair from falling out of a bun?

A: Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place. You can also try using a hair tie to create a base for your bun.

Hair Care

Putting your hair up can be a great way to give it a break from daily styling. However, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage.

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

With these tips and techniques, you can create a variety of stylish looks with your short hair. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!

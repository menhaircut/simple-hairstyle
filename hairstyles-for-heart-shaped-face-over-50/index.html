Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Face Over 50

Written by Bardi Sep 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart shaped face and are over 50, you may be wondering what hairstyles would suit you best. This article will give you a guide on hairstyles that are perfect for your face shape and age. Keep reading to learn more!

Description

A heart shaped face has a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The goal of any hairstyle for this face shape is to balance out the width of the forehead and the narrowness of the chin. Over 50, you may also want to consider hairstyles that are easy to maintain and do not require too much styling.

How to

Here are some hairstyles that are perfect for a heart shaped face over 50:

1. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for a heart shaped face. It can be a chin-length bob or a longer bob that falls just above the shoulders. This hairstyle is easy to maintain and can be styled with a side part or a middle part.

2. Pixie

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that can be styled in many ways. It is perfect for a heart shaped face because it draws attention to the eyes and the cheekbones. You can add layers or texture to this hairstyle to make it more interesting.

3. Shoulder-length layers

Shoulder-length layers are perfect for a heart shaped face because they balance out the width of the forehead and the narrowness of the chin. This hairstyle is easy to maintain and can be styled with a side part or a middle part. You can also add bangs to this hairstyle to frame the face.

Step by step

Here are some step by step instructions on how to style a bob:

1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.

Make sure your hair is clean before you start styling it. Towel dry your hair to remove excess moisture.

2. Apply a styling product.

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair to add volume and hold.

3. Blow dry your hair.

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Make sure to blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to fall.

4. Style your hair.

Style your hair by adding a side part or a middle part. You can also add some waves or curls to your hair to make it more interesting.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a heart shaped face over 50:

1. Consider your lifestyle.

Choose a hairstyle that suits your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, choose a hairstyle that is easy to maintain.

2. Add texture.

Add texture to your hair to make it more interesting. You can add layers or waves to your hair to create a more textured look.

3. Consider your hair type.

Choose a hairstyle that suits your hair type. If you have fine hair, choose a hairstyle that adds volume. If you have thick hair, choose a hairstyle that thins out your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for heart shaped face over 50:

1. Can I wear bangs with a heart shaped face?

Yes, you can wear bangs with a heart shaped face. Bangs can help balance out the width of the forehead and the narrowness of the chin.

2. Should I choose a short or long hairstyle?

You can choose either a short or long hairstyle, as long as it balances out the width of the forehead and the narrowness of the chin.

3. Can I style my hair with a side part or a middle part?

Yes, you can style your hair with a side part or a middle part. Choose the part that suits your face shape the best.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly.

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Use a conditioner.

Use a conditioner after shampooing your hair to keep it soft and manageable.

3. Protect your hair from heat.

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.

4. Get regular haircuts.

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and to maintain your hairstyle.

Now that you know which hairstyles are perfect for a heart shaped face over 50, it's time to choose the one that suits you the best. Remember to consider your lifestyle, hair type, and face shape when choosing a hairstyle. And don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful!

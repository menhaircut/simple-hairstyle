2 Guard Vs 3 Guard Haircut What s The Difference

Written by Frank Nov 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to get a new haircut but not sure which guard to use? A guard is the attachment that goes on a clipper to determine the length of the cut. The most common guards used are the 2 guard and the 3 guard. In this article, we will discuss the difference between the two and help you decide which one is best for you.

Description

The 2 guard is a 1/4 inch in length, while the 3 guard is a 3/8 inch in length. The difference may seem small, but it can make a big difference in the overall look of your haircut. The 2 guard will give you a shorter cut, while the 3 guard will leave your hair slightly longer.

How to

To achieve a 2 guard haircut, attach the 2 guard to your clippers and begin cutting your hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up, trimming the hair in even sections. For a 3 guard haircut, follow the same process but attach the 3 guard instead.

Step by Step

1. Decide which guard you want to use for your haircut.

2. Attach the guard to your clippers.

3. Start cutting your hair from the bottom and work your way up.

4. Trim the hair in even sections.

5. Repeat until you have achieved your desired length.

Tips

- Use a comb to help guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

- Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.

- Always cut in even sections to avoid uneven spots.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a 2 guard on longer hair?

A: It is not recommended to use a 2 guard on longer hair as it will result in a very short cut.

Q: Can I use a 3 guard on shorter hair?

A: Yes, you can use a 3 guard on shorter hair for a slightly longer cut.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your haircut. On average, most people get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After getting a haircut with a 2 or 3 guard, it is important to maintain your hair by washing it regularly and using conditioner to keep it healthy. You can also use styling products to create different looks and enhance your haircut.

In conclusion, the difference between a 2 guard and a 3 guard may seem small, but it can make a big difference in the overall look of your haircut. Consider your desired length and style before choosing which guard to use. Remember to maintain your hair with proper care and grooming to keep it looking its best.

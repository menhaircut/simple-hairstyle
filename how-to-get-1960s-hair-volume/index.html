How To Get 1960S Hair Volume A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 14, 2023 · 3 min read

Do you want to achieve the iconic voluminous hair of the 1960s? Look no further! In this guide, we will show you how to get 1960s hair volume with ease. From the proper hair care routine to the styling techniques, we’ve got you covered.

Description

The 1960s was a decade of bold fashion statements and big hair. Women back then were known for their voluminous and bouncy hairstyles that were achieved with a combination of techniques and products. Today, the retro look is making a comeback, and many women are looking to recreate the iconic 1960s hair volume.

How to Get 1960s Hair Volume

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to get 1960s hair volume:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to achieving 1960s hair volume is to start with clean hair. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more texture and body to your hair. Avoid using heavy conditioners that can weigh down your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Volumizing Mousse

After washing your hair, apply a volumizing mousse from the roots to the tips. This will help to add more volume and hold to your hair. You can also use a root-lifting spray to add more lift to your roots.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add more volume and bounce. Start by blow-drying the roots first and then work your way down to the tips. Use a diffuser attachment to avoid frizz and add more texture to your hair.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Tease your hair using a fine-tooth comb to add more volume and texture. Start by sectioning your hair and teasing the roots of each section. This will help to create the iconic 1960s bouffant hairstyle.

Step 5: Use Hot Rollers

Use hot rollers to set your hair and add more volume. Roll your hair from the roots to the tips and leave them in for at least 20 minutes. Once you remove the rollers, use a wide-tooth comb to gently brush out your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve 1960s hair volume:

  • Use a volumizing dry shampoo to add more texture and body to your hair.
  • Don’t be afraid to use hairspray to hold your hair in place.
  • Use a silk scarf to tie your hair up at night to prevent frizz and maintain the volume.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting 1960s hair volume:

What kind of hair works best for the 1960s hairstyle?

Any hair type can achieve the 1960s hairstyle with the right techniques and products. However, thicker hair tends to hold the volume better than finer hair.

Can I achieve 1960s hair volume with short hair?

Yes, you can achieve 1960s hair volume with short hair. Use a root-lifting spray and a volumizing mousse to add more lift to your roots.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to achieving 1960s hair volume. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heavy conditioners that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to gently brush out your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

With the right techniques and products, you can easily achieve 1960s hair volume. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to rock the retro look with ease.

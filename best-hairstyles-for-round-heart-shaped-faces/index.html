Best Hairstyles For Round Heart Shaped Faces

If you have a round heart shaped face, you know how difficult it can be to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features. Luckily, there are plenty of options that can accentuate your best features and make you feel confident and beautiful. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best hairstyles for round heart shaped faces, how to achieve them, and tips for maintaining your gorgeous locks.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for round heart shaped faces, it's important to determine whether or not you actually have this face shape. Here's a quick guide:

  • A round face is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin.
  • A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones that narrow down to a pointed chin.
  • A round heart shaped face is a combination of both of these, with a wider forehead and cheeks that taper down to a pointed chin.

If you're still unsure, ask your hair stylist for their professional opinion.

Best Hairstyles for Round Heart Shaped Faces

Now that you know you have a round heart shaped face, it's time to find the perfect hairstyle. Here are some great options:

Long Layers

Long layers can help to elongate your face, making it appear less round. Consider adding face-framing layers to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to shorten the appearance of your forehead, while drawing attention to your eyes.

Bob with Volume

A bob with volume can help to add height to your face, making it appear less round. Consider adding waves or curls to add even more volume.

Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down hairstyle can help to draw attention to your cheekbones, while keeping your hair out of your face.

Messy Updo

A messy updo can help to elongate your face, while keeping your hair out of your face. Consider adding braids or twists for added texture.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Your Perfect Hairstyle

Once you've chosen your perfect hairstyle, it's time to learn how to achieve it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using products that are suited for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair, being careful not to rub too hard and cause damage.
  3. Apply any styling products you'll be using, such as mousse or leave-in conditioner.
  4. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  5. If necessary, use a curling iron or straightener to add any additional texture or shape.
  6. Finish with hairspray or other styling products to hold your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Now that you've achieved your perfect hairstyle, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use products that are suited for your hair type, whether it's oily, dry, or somewhere in between.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

A. Absolutely! Just make sure to add some volume to the top of your head to elongate your face.

Q. Can I still have bangs?

A. Yes, but consider side-swept bangs instead of blunt bangs, as they can help to shorten the appearance of your forehead.

Q. What if I have curly hair?

A. No problem! Just make sure to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and makes you feel confident.

Hair Care for Round Heart Shaped Faces

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often, to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using too many styling products at once, as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Consider using a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.

With these tips and hairstyles, you'll be sure to find the perfect look for your round heart shaped face. Remember to always choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!

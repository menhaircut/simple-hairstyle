The Best Way To Use Hair Thinning Scissors For Perfectly Textured Hair

Written by Robby Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Hair thinning scissors are an essential tool for creating perfectly textured hair. Whether you want to add volume to fine hair or remove bulk from thick hair, these scissors are a game-changer. But, using hair thinning scissors can be intimidating, especially for beginners. In this blog post, we will guide you through the best way to use hair thinning scissors and provide tips for achieving the perfect textured look.

Description

Hair thinning scissors are designed to remove bulk from the hair without changing the overall length. They are also known as blending scissors, texturizing scissors, or thinning shears. These scissors have teeth on one blade and a sharp edge on the other, allowing the stylist to remove hair in a controlled manner.

How to Use Hair Thinning Scissors

Step 1: Choose the Right Scissors

Not all hair thinning scissors are created equal. The quality of the scissors can affect the final result. Invest in a high-quality pair of scissors that are specifically designed for thinning hair. Cheap scissors can damage the hair and create an uneven texture.

Step 2: Section Hair

Before you start cutting, section the hair you want to thin. This will make it easier to control the amount of hair you remove. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate.

Step 3: Hold the Scissors Correctly

Hold the scissors with your dominant hand and use your other hand to hold the hair. Hold the scissors with the teeth pointing upwards and the blade facing downwards. This will prevent the hair from sliding off the scissors and give you better control.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Start at the bottom of the hair section and work your way up. Take a small amount of hair between the blades and slide the scissors down the hair shaft, closing the blades as you go. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can create an uneven texture.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut the hair, use a comb to blend the cut section with the uncut hair. This will create a natural, textured look. Repeat this process on all the sections of hair you want to thin.

Tips for Using Hair Thinning Scissors

1. Use the scissors sparingly. Thin too much hair, and you risk creating an uneven texture.

2. Always start with a small amount of hair and work your way up slowly.

3. Use a comb to blend the cut sections with the uncut hair for a natural look.

4. Don't use thinning scissors on wet hair, as this can create a choppy texture.

5. Clean and oil your scissors regularly to keep them in good condition and prevent damage to the hair.

FAQs

Q: Can hair thinning scissors be used on all hair types?

A: Hair thinning scissors can be used on all hair types, but the amount of hair you thin will vary depending on the thickness of the hair.

Q: Will thinning scissors damage my hair?

A: If used correctly, hair thinning scissors will not damage your hair. However, using them too much or using them incorrectly can cause damage to the hair.

Hair Care

Using hair thinning scissors is just one way to create textured hair. It's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and avoiding heat styling can all help keep your hair in top condition.

In conclusion, using hair thinning scissors is an excellent way to create perfectly textured hair. By following the steps outlined in this post and using the right techniques, you can achieve a natural, textured look that will make you feel confident and stylish.

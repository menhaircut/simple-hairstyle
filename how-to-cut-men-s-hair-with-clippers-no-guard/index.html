How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers No Guard A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our step-by-step guide on how to cut men's hair with clippers no guard. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. This guide will provide you with all the information you need to achieve a perfect haircut using clippers no guard.

Description

Cutting hair with clippers no guard is a popular technique used by barbers and stylists. This technique involves using clippers without any guard to achieve a close cut. It's a great option for men who want a low-maintenance, stylish haircut without having to visit a salon. This technique is also useful for those who want to save money on haircuts or who don't have time to visit a salon.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers No Guard

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

The first step in cutting men's hair with clippers no guard is to prepare the hair. Start by washing the hair and towel-drying it. Make sure the hair is completely dry before you start cutting. Comb the hair in the direction it naturally falls to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clippers

Choosing the right clippers is crucial when cutting hair with no guard. Look for clippers that are specifically designed for hair cutting and have sharp blades. Make sure the clippers are well-oiled and cleaned before using them.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair from the back of the head and move forward. Use short, even strokes to cut the hair. Be careful not to cut too close to the scalp, as this can cause irritation and discomfort. Use the clippers to create a fade effect by going over the same area multiple times at different lengths.

Step 4: Trim the Sides and Back

Once you've finished cutting the top of the head, move on to the sides and back. Use the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length. Use short, even strokes and be careful not to cut too close to the scalp. Use a handheld mirror to check the back of the head and make any necessary adjustments.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Use the clippers to blend the hair on the sides and back of the head. Use a lower guard setting to create a fade effect. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using shorter and shorter guard settings. Use the clippers to blend the hairline at the nape of the neck.

Step 6: Finish the Cut

Once you've finished cutting and blending the hair, use scissors to trim any stray hairs. Use a comb and scissors to create a more natural look by trimming the ends of the hair. Use a handheld mirror to check the sides and back of the head for any uneven spots.

Tips

  • Use a spray bottle to wet the hair if it's too dry to cut.
  • Keep the clippers clean and oiled to maintain their performance.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of the head.
  • Start with a longer guard setting and work your way down to a shorter setting.
  • If you're not confident in your skills, start with a longer guard setting and gradually work your way down.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with clippers no guard?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers no guard. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow our step-by-step guide.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always use a longer guard setting to blend the hair and create a more even look.

How often should I cut my hair?

The frequency of haircuts depends on the individual. Some people may need to cut their hair every two weeks, while others may only need to cut it once a month.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, it's important to wash and condition it regularly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type. Avoid using hair styling products that contain alcohol or other harsh chemicals. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Cutting men's hair with clippers no guard is a simple and effective technique that can be done at home. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without having to visit a salon. Follow our step-by-step guide and enjoy a stylish, low-maintenance haircut.

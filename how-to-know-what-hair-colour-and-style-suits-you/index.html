How To Know What Hair Colour And Style Suits You A Comprehensive Guide

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

Choosing the right hair colour and style can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. However, it’s important to understand that choosing the right colour and style can make a significant difference in your overall appearance and confidence.

In this comprehensive guide, we’ll walk you through how to determine what hair colour and style suits you best. We’ll cover everything from how to assess your skin tone and face shape to tips for maintaining your hair once you’ve chosen your new look.

How to

The first step to determining what hair colour and style suits you is to assess your skin tone. Your skin tone plays a significant role in determining which hair colours will complement your natural features best. If you have warm undertones, you’ll want to opt for warmer colours, such as golden blonde or auburn. If you have cool undertones, cooler colours like ash blonde or jet black will look best.

Next, consider your face shape. Different face shapes lend themselves better to certain hairstyles. For example, if you have a round face, a long layered cut can help elongate your features. If you have a square face, a soft, layered bob can help soften your features.

Finally, consider your lifestyle and personal style preferences. If you’re someone who likes to keep things simple and low-maintenance, a shorter hairstyle may be best for you. If you like to experiment with different looks and have more time to devote to styling your hair, a longer hairstyle with layers may be a good fit.

Step by step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to determining what hair colour and style suits you:

  1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins in your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones.
  2. Consider your face shape. Use a mirror or take a photo of yourself to help determine your face shape.
  3. Research different hairstyles that complement your face shape.
  4. Consider your lifestyle and personal style preferences.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist to get their input and recommendations.
  6. Choose a hair colour and style that you feel confident and comfortable with.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colour and style:

  • Consider the maintenance required for your chosen style. If you’re someone who doesn’t have a lot of time to devote to styling your hair, a low-maintenance hairstyle may be best.
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new. Sometimes, stepping out of your comfort zone can lead to a look that you absolutely love.
  • Remember that hair colour can fade over time. Be prepared to schedule regular touch-ups to maintain your desired look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair colour and style:

How can I tell if a hair colour will look good on me?

Assessing your skin tone is key to determining which hair colours will complement your natural features best. You can also try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to get a better idea of how a certain colour will look on you.

What’s the best hairstyle for a round face?

A long layered cut can help elongate your features and make your face appear less round. Avoid blunt cuts or styles that add volume to the sides of your face.

How do I maintain my new hair colour and style?

Be sure to use products that are specifically formulated for coloured hair, and schedule regular touch-ups to maintain your desired look.

Hair Care

Once you’ve chosen your new hair colour and style, it’s important to take good care of your hair to maintain your look. Here are some tips:

  • Use products that are specifically formulated for your hair type and style.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Schedule regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a protective hair product.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and using our step-by-step guide, you can feel confident in choosing the right hair colour and style for you. Remember to consult with a hairstylist if you have any questions or concerns, and have fun experimenting with different looks!

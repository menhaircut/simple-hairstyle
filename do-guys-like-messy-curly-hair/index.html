Do Guys Like Messy Curly Hair

Written by Petter Sep 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair is a beautiful and unique feature that many women possess. However, it can be challenging to style and maintain, especially when it comes to messy curly hair. But the question is, do guys like messy curly hair? In this article, we will explore this topic in-depth and provide you with tips on how to embrace your natural curls.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Messy Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Messy Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Messy Curly Hair
  • FAQs About Messy Curly Hair
  • Hair Care for Messy Curly Hair

Description

Messy curly hair can be a turn-on for some guys. The tousled look can give off an effortless yet sexy vibe. However, it ultimately comes down to personal preference. Some guys may prefer neat and tidy hair, while others may find messy curly hair attractive.

It's important to note that you should always embrace your natural hair, no matter what others may think. If you feel confident and comfortable with your messy curly hair, that's all that matters.

How to Style Messy Curly Hair

Styling messy curly hair can be a bit tricky, but there are some tips and tricks you can use to achieve the look you desire. Here are some of our top tips:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural curls and add volume.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and scrunch with your hands to create texture.
  • Use a curling iron with a large barrel to create loose, effortless waves.
  • Embrace the messiness! Don't be afraid to let your curls do their thing and rock the tousled look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Messy Curly Hair

If you're new to styling messy curly hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the look:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on low heat, scrunching as you go.
  4. If desired, use a curling iron with a large barrel to create loose waves.
  5. Scrunch your hair again to create texture and volume.
  6. Finish off with a texturizing spray for added hold and definition.

Tips for Maintaining Messy Curly Hair

Messy curly hair can be high-maintenance, but there are some tips you can use to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Invest in a good deep conditioning treatment to keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent tangling and breakage while you sleep.

FAQs About Messy Curly Hair

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a messy bun or ponytail?

A: Absolutely! Messy buns and ponytails are great ways to show off your curls while keeping your hair out of your face.

Q: Will messy curly hair work for all hair types?

A: Yes! Messy curly hair can work for all hair types, from loose waves to tight coils.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my messy curly hair?

A: Try using a frizz-fighting product, such as a serum or oil, before styling your hair. Additionally, avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.

Hair Care for Messy Curly Hair

Messy curly hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Invest in a good leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and keep it moisturized throughout the day.

In conclusion, whether or not guys like messy curly hair ultimately comes down to personal preference. However, with the right styling tips and hair care routine, you can rock your natural curls with confidence and style.

Easy Updo Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair To Do At Home

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

If you have medium-length hair and you're looking for some quick and easy updo hairstyles to do at home, then you're in the right place. Updos are a great way to add some elegance to your look and they can be achieved in just a few simple steps. In this article, we'll show you some of the best updo hairstyles for medium-length hair that you can do at home.

How to

Before we get into the specific hairstyles, let's first go over some general tips for achieving the perfect updo:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use hair elastics and bobby pins to secure the updo
  • Don't be afraid to tease your hair to add volume
  • Use hairspray to hold the style in place

Step by Step

1. Low Bun

To achieve this look, start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail and wrap it around itself to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and hairspray.

2. Braided Crown

Part your hair down the middle and braid each side. Then, wrap the braids around the crown of your head and secure with bobby pins.

3. Half-Up Top Knot

Pull the top half of your hair back into a ponytail and then twist it into a knot. Secure the knot with bobby pins and hairspray.

4. French Twist

Pull your hair back into a low ponytail and twist it upwards, tucking the ends into the twist. Secure the twist with bobby pins and hairspray.

Tips

  • Experiment with different braids and knots to create different updo hairstyles
  • Add hair accessories like clips or headbands to dress up your updo
  • Use dry shampoo to add texture and help hold the style in place
  • Practice makes perfect – don't be discouraged if your updo doesn't turn out perfectly the first time

FAQ

What do I do if my hair is too slippery to hold an updo?

You can add some texture to your hair by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. This will help your hair hold the style better.

Can I do an updo on short hair?

While some updos may be more difficult to achieve with short hair, there are still plenty of styles that can be done. Look for updos that focus on the top half of your hair, like a half-up top knot, or try a braided crown.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and looks its best. Be sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray. Additionally, try to avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Now that you know how to achieve some easy updo hairstyles for medium-length hair, you can add some elegance to your look without having to go to a salon. With a little practice, you'll be able to master these styles in no time!

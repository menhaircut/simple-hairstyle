Best Hairstyle For Over 50 With Long Face A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 30, 2023 · 5 min read

If you’re over 50 and have a long face, finding the right hairstyle can be challenging. You want to look chic and stylish without trying too hard, and you want a cut that flatters your face shape. In this guide, we’ll show you the best hairstyles for over 50 with a long face, along with tips on how to style them and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Over 50 with Long Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Over 50s with Long Face

Description

As we age, our hair can lose volume, density, and shine. This is especially true for women over 50, who may experience thinning hair and hair loss. Choosing the right hairstyle can make a big difference in how you look and feel. If you have a long face, you want to avoid hairstyles that elongate your face even more. Instead, opt for cuts that add volume and width to your face.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Over 50 with Long Face

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. If you have a long face, you want to avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short. Instead, opt for cuts that add volume and width to your face. Here are some of the best hairstyles for over 50 with a long face:

1. Layered Lob

A layered lob is a great option for women over 50 with a long face. This cut adds volume and texture to your hair, while the layers frame your face and add width. You can style your lob straight or wavy, depending on your preference.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add dimension to your hair and frame your face. They’re especially flattering for women with a long face, as they add width to your forehead and shorten the length of your face.

3. Pixie Cut

If you’re feeling daring, a pixie cut can be a great option for women over 50 with a long face. This cut adds volume and texture to your hair, while also drawing attention to your eyes and cheekbones. Just make sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you’ve chosen the perfect hairstyle, it’s important to know how to style it. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you begin styling your hair, make sure it’s clean and dry. This will help your hair hold its shape and style better.

2. Apply Heat Protectant

Before using any heat tools on your hair, apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

3. Use a Curling Iron or Straightener

Depending on your hairstyle, you may want to use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair. If you’re going for a wavy look, use a curling iron to create loose waves. If you prefer a straighter look, use a straightener to create sleek, straight locks.

4. Finish with Hairspray or Styling Cream

Once you’ve styled your hair, finish with hairspray or styling cream to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these tips:

1. Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Invest in a quality shampoo and conditioner that’s specifically designed for your hair type. This will help keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

2. Get Regular Trims

To keep your hair looking fresh and healthy, get regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat tools on your hair, and avoid using high heat settings.

4. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and looks to find what works best for you.

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for over 50 with a long face?

The best hairstyle for over 50 with a long face is a layered lob, side-swept bangs, or a pixie cut.

How do I style my hair if I have a long face?

If you have a long face, you want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and width to your face. Use heat tools like curling iron or straightener to style your hair, and finish with hairspray or styling cream.

Hair Care Tips for Over 50s with Long Face

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

As we age, our hair can become more fragile and prone to breakage. Use a gentle shampoo that won’t strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to give your hair an extra boost.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun’s harmful rays.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy, balanced diet can help keep your hair looking its best. Make sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean protein.

5. Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair and scalp hydrated, which is essential for healthy hair.

Choosing the right hairstyle can make a big difference in how you look and feel. If you’re over 50 with a long face, try a layered lob, side-swept bangs, or a pixie cut to add volume and width to your face. Follow these tips for styling and caring for your hair, and you’ll be sure to look and feel your best.

How To Style Short Hair Over 60 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Sep 20, 2023 Â· 2 min read

Are you over 60 and struggling to find the right hairstyle for your short hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Short hair can be just as versatile and stylish as long hair, and with the right techniques, you can rock any look. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to style short hair over 60, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

  • How to Style Short Hair Over 60
  • Step-by-Step Instructions

How to Style Short Hair Over 60

First things first, it's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and hair texture. Some popular short hairstyles for women over 60 include pixie cuts, bobs, shags, and layered cuts. Once you've chosen the perfect style, it's time to start styling.

One of the easiest ways to style short hair is to use a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to add texture and volume. Apply the product to damp hair and use your fingers to tousle and shape your hair.

If you're looking for a more polished look, you can blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and smoothness. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and always aim the dryer downwards to avoid creating frizz.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these simple steps to style your short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a styling product to damp hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush.
  4. If desired, use a flat iron or curling iron to add extra texture or curl to your hair.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your short hair:

  • Invest in quality styling tools, such as a blow dryer, round brush, and flat iron.
  • Experiment with different techniques, such as twisting or pinning sections of hair to create volume or texture.
  • Don't be afraid to try new products, such as dry shampoo or texturizing spray, to enhance your style.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain your haircut and prevent split ends.
  • Consult with your stylist for personalized advice and recommendations.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short hair over 60:

Q: Can I still color my short hair?

A: Yes, you can still color your short hair. Just be sure to use a gentle, ammonia-free hair color and follow the instructions carefully.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may be able to go longer between washes.

Q: Can I wear my short hair curly or straight?

A: Yes, you can wear your short hair curly or straight. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance and care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too frequently to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can rock a stylish and flattering short haircut at any age. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a more textured and tousled style, there's a short haircut that's perfect for you. Just remember to take care of your hair and consult with your stylist for personalized advice and recommendations.

Can You Get An Undercut With Short Hair

Written by Daniel Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short hair and are considering getting an undercut, you might be wondering if it's possible. The answer is yes, you can get an undercut with short hair! In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about getting an undercut with short hair. We'll cover the basics, how to do it, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  1. The Basics
  2. How to Get an Undercut with Short Hair
  3. Tips for Getting an Undercut with Short Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care for Undercuts with Short Hair

The Basics

An undercut is a hairstyle where the sides and back of the hair are shaved or cut very short, while the top is left longer. It can be a great way to add some edge to your look and show off your unique style.

If you have short hair, you might be worried that you won't have enough hair to get an undercut. However, as long as you have at least an inch or two of hair on top, you can get an undercut. The key is to make sure that the top of your hair is long enough to cover the shaved sections on the sides and back.

How to Get an Undercut with Short Hair

The process of getting an undercut with short hair is similar to getting an undercut with longer hair. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Decide on the length and style of your undercut. You can opt for a very short buzz cut, or you can leave a bit more length on top.
  2. Choose where you want the shaved sections to be. You can have them start at the temples and go all the way down to the nape of your neck, or you can have them be more subtle.
  3. Visit a hair stylist or barber who is experienced in doing undercuts. They will be able to guide you on the best style and length for your hair.
  4. Have your hair shaved or cut to the desired length and style.

Once you have your undercut, you can style your hair in a variety of ways. You can slick it back, spike it up, or leave it messy and tousled.

Tips for Getting an Undercut with Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting an undercut with short hair:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consider the maintenance involved in keeping your undercut looking sharp. You will need to get it trimmed regularly to maintain the shape and length.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Be prepared to spend a bit more time styling your hair in the morning to achieve the desired look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get an undercut if I have very short hair?

Yes, you can get an undercut even if you have very short hair. As long as you have enough hair on top to cover the shaved sections, you can get an undercut.

Will an undercut make my hair look thinner?

An undercut can make your hair look thinner, especially if you have fine or thin hair. However, if you have thick hair, an undercut can actually make your hair look more voluminous.

How often do I need to get my undercut trimmed?

You will need to get your undercut trimmed every four to six weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Hair Care for Undercuts with Short Hair

Undercuts with short hair require some extra care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a styling product that works well with your hair type to achieve your desired look.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your undercut.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock an undercut with short hair and look amazing.

So, can you get an undercut with short hair? Absolutely! Just make sure to choose a style that works for you, find a skilled stylist, and take good care of your hair.

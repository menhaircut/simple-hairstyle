How To Cut Your Hair Short Diy

Written by Luffy Nov 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of long hair and want to switch up your look with a short haircut? Going to the salon can be expensive and time-consuming, but with a few tips and tricks, you can cut your hair short DIY at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting your hair short DIY, step by step. We will also provide you with tips, FAQ and hair care to ensure that you get the best results possible.

  • How to Cut Your Hair Short DIY
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Cut Your Hair Short DIY

The first step in cutting your hair short DIY is to decide on the length and style you want. Look for inspiration online or in magazines to find a style that suits you. Once you have decided on the length and style, gather the necessary tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb or brush
  • Clip or hair tie
  • Mirror

It is important to use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut. Dull scissors can damage your hair and create split ends. A comb or brush will help you section your hair and make it easier to cut. A clip or hair tie will help you keep the hair out of the way while you work. Lastly, make sure you have a mirror to check your progress and ensure that the cut is even.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your hair short DIY:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before you begin. Wet hair can be harder to cut and can lead to uneven results.
  2. Section your hair. Use a comb or brush to separate your hair into four sections: two at the front and two at the back. Use a clip or hair tie to secure each section.
  3. Start with the back. Take one of the back sections and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Repeat on the other back section.
  4. Move to the sides. Take one of the front sections and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut at an angle towards the back. Repeat on the other front section.
  5. Check your progress. Use a mirror to check that the cut is even. Make any necessary adjustments.
  6. Refine the cut. Use small scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven sections. Take your time and be gentle.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Short

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer length. It is easier to cut more hair off than to add it back on.
  • Take breaks. Cutting your hair can be tiring, so take breaks if you need to.
  • Be patient. Cutting your hair short DIY can take time, especially if it is your first time.
  • Ask for help. If you are unsure or nervous, ask a friend or family member to help you.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about cutting your hair short DIY:

  • Can I cut my hair short DIY if it is curly or wavy? Yes, but be aware that curly or wavy hair can shrink when it dries, so take this into account when deciding on the length.
  • What if I make a mistake? Don't panic. Hair grows back, and you can always go to a salon to fix any mistakes.
  • Can I use regular scissors? It is best to use scissors designed for cutting hair to ensure a clean cut.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair short DIY, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat tools such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Visit a salon every few months to trim your hair and maintain the shape.

Cutting your hair short DIY can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools, preparation and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and flattering look from the comfort of your own home. Just remember to take your time, be patient and have fun!

How To Do Small Curls Without Heat A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're tired of using heat on your hair to achieve small curls, you're in luck. There are many ways to achieve small curls without using any heat. In this article, we'll show you how to do small curls without heat using simple techniques that won't damage your hair. We'll cover everything from how to prepare your hair to how to maintain your curls.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Small Curls Without Heat
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Getting the Best Results
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

How to Do Small Curls Without Heat

There are several ways to achieve small curls without using heat. Here are some methods:

  • Using Flexi rods
  • Using Curlformers
  • Using Bantu knots
  • Using Twist-outs
  • Using Perm rods

Each method is effective in creating small curls, but you may prefer one over the others. Experiment with each method to find out which works best for you.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Using Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are a great way to create small curls without using heat. Here's how to use them:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or mousse.
  4. Take a small section of your hair and wrap it around a flexi rod.
  5. Secure the flexi rod in place by bending the ends together.
  6. Repeat this process until all of your hair is wrapped in flexi rods.
  7. Leave the flexi rods in your hair overnight, or until your hair is completely dry.
  8. Remove the flexi rods and style your hair as desired.

Using flexi rods can take some practice, but with a little bit of patience, you'll be able to achieve beautiful small curls.

Using Curlformers

Curlformers are another great way to create small curls without using heat. Here's how to use them:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or mousse.
  4. Insert the hook into the curlformer and pull it through your hair.
  5. Wrap your hair around the curlformer and secure it in place.
  6. Repeat this process until all of your hair is wrapped in curlformers.
  7. Leave the curlformers in your hair overnight, or until your hair is completely dry.
  8. Remove the curlformers and style your hair as desired.

Curlformers can take some practice to use, but once you get the hang of it, you'll be able to create beautiful small curls.

Using Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are a traditional African hairstyle that can create small curls without using heat. Here's how to use them:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or mousse.
  4. Take a small section of your hair and twist it tightly.
  5. Wrap the twisted hair around itself to create a knot.
  6. Secure the knot in place with a bobby pin.
  7. Repeat this process until all of your hair is in knots.
  8. Leave the knots in your hair overnight, or until your hair is completely dry.
  9. Remove the knots and style your hair as desired.

Bantu knots can be a little time-consuming, but the results are worth it.

Using Twist-Outs

Twist-outs are a popular way to create small curls without using heat. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or mousse.
  4. Take a small section of your hair and twist it tightly.
  5. Repeat this process until all of your hair is twisted.
  6. Leave the twists in your hair overnight, or until your hair is completely dry.
  7. Untwist the twists and style your hair as desired.

Twist-outs are easy to do and can create beautiful small curls.

Using Perm Rods

Perm rods are another way to create small curls without using heat. Here's how to use them:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or mousse.
  4. Take a small section of your hair and wrap it around a perm rod.
  5. Secure the perm rod in place by bending the ends together.
  6. Repeat this process until all of your hair is wrapped in perm rods.
  7. Leave the perm rods in your hair overnight, or until your hair is completely dry.
  8. Remove the perm rods and style your hair as desired.

Perm rods can take some practice to use, but they can create beautiful small curls.

Tips for Getting the Best Results

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when creating small curls without heat:

  • Use a styling product that's designed for curly hair.
  • Don't wrap too much hair around each rod or curlformer. It can prevent the hair from drying properly and create frizz.
  • Don't brush or comb your hair after removing the rods or knots. Use your fingers to separate the curls instead.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your curls from getting tangled while you sleep.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.

FAQs

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: The duration of your curls will depend on your hair type and the method you use to create them. Generally, small curls created without heat will last for a few days.

Q: Do I need to use a styling product?

A: Yes, using a styling product is important when creating small curls without heat. It will help your curls hold their shape and prevent frizz.

Q: Can I create small curls without using any products?

A: It's possible to create small curls without using any products, but your results may not be as good. Using a styling product will help your curls hold their shape and prevent frizz.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your curls from getting dry and frizzy.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair, as it can damage your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Don't brush or comb your hair too much, as this can cause your curls to lose their shape.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized throughout the day.

By following these tips, you'll be able to maintain your beautiful small curls without damaging your hair.

In conclusion, there are many ways to achieve small curls without using heat. Experiment with different methods and find the one that works best for you. By following the tips in this article, you'll be able to create beautiful small curls without damaging your hair.

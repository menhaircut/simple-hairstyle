Best Short Hair For Small Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a small face, finding the perfect short hairstyle can be a bit of a challenge. You don't want to overwhelm your delicate features, but at the same time, you don't want a style that's too simple or boring. Luckily, there are plenty of short haircuts that can help you achieve the perfect balance. In this article, we'll go over some of the best short haircuts for small faces, as well as tips on how to style and care for them.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Small Face

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Small Face

When it comes to finding the best short haircut for small faces, there are a few things to consider:

Face Shape

The first thing you should consider is your face shape. If you have a small face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that are too long or voluminous, as they can overwhelm your features. Instead, opt for a style that is shorter and more structured. A bob or a pixie cut can be a great option.

Hair Texture

Another thing to consider is your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid hairstyles that are too layered, as they can make your hair look even thinner. Instead, opt for a blunt cut or a style with minimal layering. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a layered cut to help thin it out and make it more manageable.

Lifestyle

Finally, think about your lifestyle. Are you someone who likes to spend a lot of time on their hair, or do you prefer something low-maintenance? If you're low-maintenance, you may want to opt for a shorter style that requires minimal styling. If you're someone who likes to experiment with different hairstyles, you may want to choose a style that is more versatile.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Small Face

Now that you've chosen the perfect short haircut for your small face, it's time to learn how to style it. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type. If you have fine hair, choose a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have thick hair, choose a smoothing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Gently towel dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too harshly. You want to avoid damaging your hair or creating frizz.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or a styling cream. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Blow Dry

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. This will help to add volume and shape to your hair.

Step 5: Style

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired. If you have a bob, you may want to use a flat iron to create a sleek and polished look. If you have a pixie cut, you may want to use a texturizing product to create a messy and tousled look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

Get Regular Trims

Short haircuts require more maintenance than longer styles, as they tend to lose their shape more quickly. Make sure to get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and polished.

Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools on your hair, such as a flat iron or a curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day, or even every third day, to keep it healthy and hydrated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs with a short haircut. However, you may want to opt for a shorter and more wispy bang, rather than a thick and heavy bang.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look even smaller?

A: No, a short haircut can actually help to balance out your features and make your face look more proportionate.

Q: How do I know if a short haircut is right for me?

A: The best way to know if a short haircut is right for you is to try it out. If you're hesitant to make a big chop, start with a slightly longer style, such as a lob, and work your way up to a shorter style.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for short hair:

Use a Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can help to prevent hair breakage and frizz, as it reduces friction between your hair and the pillowcase.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or restrictive, as they can cause damage to your hair and scalp.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Just like your skin, your hair can be damaged by the sun's UV rays. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun.

Final Thoughts

Choosing the best short haircut for your small face can be a bit of a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect balance. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a haircut, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. With regular maintenance and proper care, your short haircut will look polished and stylish for months to come.

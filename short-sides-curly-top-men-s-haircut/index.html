Short Sides Curly Top Men s Haircut A Stylish Look For 2023

Written by Eveline Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and easy-to-maintain haircut for men, then the short sides curly top men's haircut is a great choice. This haircut features short hair on the sides and back, with longer, curly hair on top. It's a versatile look that can be styled in many different ways, from a neat and tidy look to a more relaxed and messy style.

Description

The short sides curly top men's haircut is a popular hairstyle that has been around for many years. It's a stylish and low-maintenance look that is perfect for men who want to look good without spending a lot of time on their hair. The haircut features short hair on the sides and back, with longer, curly hair on top. The length of the hair on top can vary depending on your personal preference, but it's usually around 2-3 inches.

How to

If you're interested in getting a short sides curly top men's haircut, here's how to do it:

Step by Step

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Ask your barber to cut the hair on the sides and back short, using clippers or scissors.
  3. Leave the hair on top longer, around 2-3 inches.
  4. Ask your barber to cut the hair on top in a curly style.
  5. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or gel, to help define the curls.
  6. Style your hair as desired, using your fingers or a comb to create the look you want.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your short sides curly top men's haircut:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Consider getting a fade on the sides instead of a straight cut to add some extra style to your look.
  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to help keep your curls healthy and moisturized.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the short sides curly top men's haircut:

What face shapes does this haircut suit?

The short sides curly top men's haircut can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. Talk to your barber to find out if this haircut is right for you.

How often should I get this haircut?

You should get this haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy. If you prefer a more relaxed and messy look, you can wait a bit longer between haircuts.

What styling products should I use?

You can use a variety of styling products for this haircut, depending on your hair type and the look you want to achieve. Mousse, gel, and pomade are all good options for defining and holding the curls in place.

Hair Care

To keep your short sides curly top men's haircut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to help keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, as they can also damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to help promote healthy hair growth.

The short sides curly top men's haircut is a stylish and low-maintenance look that is perfect for men who want to look good without spending a lot of time on their hair. With a little bit of styling and care, you can keep your hair looking great all year round.

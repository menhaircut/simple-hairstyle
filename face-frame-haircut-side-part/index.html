Face Frame Haircut Side Part A Guide For A Perfect Look

Written by Frank Oct 29, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that gives you a fresh and stylish look? A face frame haircut side part might be the perfect choice for you! This type of haircut not only enhances your facial features but also adds volume and texture to your hair. In this article, we will guide you through the process of getting a face frame haircut side part, including how to style and care for it. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Face Frame Haircut Side Part
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling and Maintaining
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

A face frame haircut side part is a haircut that frames your face, emphasizing your best features. This haircut usually involves cutting layers around your face, so your hair falls in a flattering way. The side part adds a touch of elegance, making it a perfect look for both casual and formal occasions.

How to Get a Face Frame Haircut Side Part

Getting a face frame haircut side part is not complicated, but it requires some skills and knowledge. Here are some things you need to know before getting this haircut:

  • Choose a reputable hairstylist who has experience in cutting face frame haircuts.
  • Bring pictures of the haircut you want to show your hairstylist.
  • Communicate clearly with your hairstylist about the length, layers, and the side part you want.
  • Ask your hairstylist for advice on how to style and maintain your haircut.

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps to get a face frame haircut side part:

  1. Wash and dry your hair before going to the salon.
  2. Communicate with your hairstylist about the haircut you want.
  3. Section your hair and start cutting the layers around your face.
  4. Create a side part that suits your face shape.
  5. Blend the layers with the rest of your hair.
  6. Style your hair using a blow dryer and a round brush.
  7. Apply a styling product to add texture and volume to your hair.
  8. Finish your look by applying hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining

Here are some tips for styling and maintaining your face frame haircut side part:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  • Apply a small amount of hair serum to add shine and control frizz.
  • Use a hairspray to hold your hair in place and prevent flyaways.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut.

FAQs

How often should I get a face frame haircut side part?

You should get a face frame haircut side part every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut's shape and style.

Can I style my face frame haircut side part differently?

Yes, you can style your face frame haircut side part differently by changing the part's position or using different hair accessories.

Is a face frame haircut side part suitable for all face shapes?

A face frame haircut side part is suitable for most face shapes, but your hairstylist can advise you on the best haircut for your face shape.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like straighteners or curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes vitamins and minerals essential for hair health.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated and moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock your face frame haircut side part with confidence and style!

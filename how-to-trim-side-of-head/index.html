How To Trim Side Of Head Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Trimming the side of your head is an important aspect of maintaining your hairstyle. Whether you have a short or long haircut, it is necessary to trim the side of your head to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. In this article, we will guide you through the process of trimming the side of your head with ease. Follow our step by step guide and achieve a clean and polished look.

Description

Trimming the side of your head is a process of cutting off the excess hair from the side of your head. It is important to trim the side of your head regularly to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair looking neat and tidy. This process is easy and can be done at home with the right tools and techniques.

How to Trim Side of Head

Follow these simple steps to trim the side of your head:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Before you begin trimming the side of your head, it is important to wash and dry your hair. This will help you to see the natural growth pattern of your hair and make it easier to trim.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair by using a comb and separating the side of your head from the rest of your hair. Use hair clips to secure the hair in place to make it easier to trim.

Step 3: Choose the right trimmer

Choose the right trimmer for the job. A good quality clipper will make the process of trimming the side of your head much easier. Make sure the blades are sharp and clean before you begin.

Step 4: Start trimming

Start trimming the side of your head by using the clipper. Begin at the bottom and work your way up. Use the clipper to create a straight line along the side of your head. Use a larger guard if you want to leave more hair or a smaller guard if you want to take more off.

Step 5: Blend the hair

Blend the hair by using a blending comb. This will create a natural look and prevent any harsh lines. Use the clipper to blend the hair and create a seamless transition from the side of your head to the top.

Step 6: Repeat on the other side

Repeat the process on the other side of your head. Make sure to match the length and style of the other side for a symmetrical look.

Step 7: Check for any missed spots

Check for any missed spots and trim them accordingly. Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure everything is even.

Step 8: Clean up the hair

Clean up the hair by using a brush or vacuum. This will prevent any hair from getting caught in your clothes or on the floor.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you trim the side of your head with ease:

  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head
  • Start with a larger guard and work your way down
  • Don't rush the process
  • Use a blending comb to create a natural look
  • Trim your hair when it is dry

FAQ

How often should I trim the side of my head?

You should trim the side of your head every 2-4 weeks to maintain your hairstyle.

Can I trim the side of my head myself?

Yes, you can trim the side of your head yourself. Follow our step by step guide for best results.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, it's okay. Hair grows back. You can always go to a professional hair stylist to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care

Trimming the side of your head is an important aspect of maintaining your hairstyle. It is important to take care of your hair by washing it regularly, using a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and getting regular haircuts to prevent split ends. Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

By following our step by step guide and using our tips, you can easily trim the side of your head at home. Remember to take care of your hair and maintain a regular trimming schedule for best results. Happy trimming!

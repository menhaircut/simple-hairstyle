How To Tie Guys Hair Back A Relaxed Guide

Written by Christine Sep 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Whether you're growing out your hair or just want to keep it out of your face, tying it back is a simple and practical option. However, it can be tricky to find the right way to tie your hair back without it looking messy or uncomfortable. In this guide, we'll show you how to tie guys hair back in a relaxed way that looks great and feels comfortable.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tie Guys Hair Back
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Tying Guys Hair Back

How to Tie Guys Hair Back

Before we get started, it's important to note that there are many different ways to tie your hair back, and what works best for you may not work for someone else. That being said, here's a simple method that works for most guys:

What You'll Need:

  • A hair tie or elastic band
  • A mirror (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Brush or comb your hair back away from your face.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head, using your fingers or a comb to smooth out any bumps or tangles.
  3. Hold your hair in place with one hand and use the other hand to wrap the hair tie or elastic band around the base of the ponytail. Be sure to twist the band as you wrap it to ensure a secure hold.
  4. Once the band is in place, tug gently on the hair at the top of your head to create a little bit of volume.

That's it! You now have a sleek and secure ponytail that will keep your hair out of your face all day long.

Step by Step Guide

For those who prefer a more detailed explanation, here's a step by step guide:

Step 1: Brush or Comb Your Hair Back

Start by using a brush or comb to smooth your hair back away from your face. This will help you create a neat and tidy ponytail.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Once your hair is brushed back, use your fingers or a comb to gather it at the back of your head. Be sure to smooth out any bumps or tangles as you go.

Step 3: Secure with a Hair Tie

Hold your hair in place with one hand and use the other hand to wrap the hair tie or elastic band around the base of the ponytail. Twist the band as you wrap it to ensure a secure hold.

Step 4: Create Volume at the Top

After the band is in place, gently tug on the hair at the top of your head to create a little bit of volume. This will prevent your ponytail from looking too flat or tight.

Tips for Tying Guys Hair Back

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when tying your hair back:

  • Use a hair tie or elastic band that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • Avoid tying your hair back too tightly, as this can cause headaches and hair breakage over time.
  • If you have long hair, consider braiding it before tying it back to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • If you're not sure how to style your hair, look for inspiration online or ask your stylist for tips.

FAQs

Q: Can I tie my hair back if it's short?

A: Yes, even guys with short hair can tie it back. Just be sure to use a small hair tie or elastic band, and don't pull your hair too tightly.

Q: Can tying my hair back cause hair loss?

A: Tying your hair back too tightly or using the wrong type of hair tie can cause hair breakage and eventually lead to hair loss. However, if you tie your hair back properly and use a gentle hair tie, you should be fine.

Q: How often should I tie my hair back?

A: It's a good idea to give your hair a break from being tied back every once in a while to prevent breakage and promote healthy hair growth. Try not to tie your hair back every day if possible.

Hair Care for Tied Back Hair

If you tie your hair back frequently, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent breakage and promote healthy growth. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair, as these can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to moisturize and protect your hair.
  • Avoid wearing your hair tie in the same spot every day, as this can cause breakage and hair loss in that area.

Tying your hair back can be a practical and stylish way to keep it out of your face. With these tips and techniques, you can tie your hair back in a way that looks great and feels comfortable.

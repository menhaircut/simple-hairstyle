Short Bob Haircuts For Fine Hair Round Face

Written by Christine Aug 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone with fine hair and a round face? You might be wondering if a short bob haircut would suit you. The answer is yes! A short bob haircut can be a great option for those with fine hair and a round face. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the right short bob haircut for your face shape and hair type, as well as tips on how to maintain your new hairstyle.

Table of Content

  1. How to Choose the Right Short Bob Haircut
  2. How to Style a Short Bob Haircut
  3. FAQs
  4. Hair Care Tips for Short Bob Haircuts

How to Choose the Right Short Bob Haircut

When it comes to choosing the right short bob haircut for your face shape and hair type, there are a few things to consider. For those with a round face, a short bob haircut that is slightly longer in the front can help elongate the face. As for fine hair, a layered bob can add volume and texture to the hair.

If you have a round face and fine hair, a graduated bob with layers can be a great option. This type of bob features shorter layers in the back that gradually get longer towards the front. This creates an angled look that can help slim down the face while adding volume and texture to the hair.

Other Short Bob Haircut Options for Fine Hair Round Face

Aside from the graduated bob, there are other short bob haircuts that can work well for those with fine hair and a round face. These include:

  • A-line bob
  • Inverted bob
  • Blunt bob

When choosing one of these short bob haircuts, it's important to consult with your hairstylist to determine which one will work best for your face shape and hair type.

How to Style a Short Bob Haircut

Styling a short bob haircut can be quick and easy. To add volume and texture to fine hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or flat iron to add some waves or curls to your hair.

If you're short on time, simply use a texturizing spray to add some texture to your hair. You can also try a messy, tousled look by running your fingers through your hair and using a small amount of pomade to create a piecey look.

Other Short Bob Haircut Styling Tips

Here are some other tips for styling a short bob haircut:

  • Use a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and tame frizz
  • Try a deep side part for a more dramatic look
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or clips

FAQs

Q: Will a short bob haircut make my face look rounder?

A: No, a short bob haircut can actually help elongate a round face. Just make sure to choose a bob that is slightly longer in the front.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short bob haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short bob haircut. Try a half-up, half-down style or a messy bun.

Hair Care Tips for Short Bob Haircuts

Maintaining a short bob haircut is relatively easy. To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, be sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a hair mask once a week to add extra moisture to your hair.

To maintain your haircut, you will need to get it trimmed every 4-6 weeks. This will help keep your hair looking neat and tidy, as well as prevent split ends.

Other Hair Care Tips for Short Bob Haircuts

Here are some other tips for caring for your short bob haircut:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this is gentler on your hair than a brush
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils

Now that you know how to choose the right short bob haircut for your face shape and hair type, as well as how to style and care for it, you can confidently rock this trendy hairstyle. Just remember to consult with your hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair.

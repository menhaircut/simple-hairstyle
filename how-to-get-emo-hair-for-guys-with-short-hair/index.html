How To Get Emo Hair For Guys With Short Hair

Written by Robby Nov 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a guy with short hair who wants to rock the emo look? Emo hairstyles are all about expressing your individuality and standing out from the crowd. Whether you're into classic emo or more modern variations of the style, there are plenty of ways to get emo hair with short locks. In this article, we'll show you step-by-step how to achieve the emo hair look for guys with short hair.

How to

Step 1: Cut Your Hair

The first step to getting emo hair for guys with short hair is to get the right haircut. Emo haircuts are typically asymmetrical and feature long, sweeping bangs that cover one eye. If you already have short hair, you may not need to cut it much shorter. However, if your hair is longer, you'll need to get it cut to the appropriate length.

Step 2: Dye Your Hair

Emo hair is often associated with bold, bright colors. If you want to go all-out with your emo hair, consider dyeing it a vibrant shade of blue, green, pink, or purple. If you're not ready to commit to a full head of dye, you can try adding highlights or lowlights to your hair for a more subtle look.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you've got the right haircut and color, it's time to start styling your hair. Emo hairstyles often involve a lot of product, so you'll need to invest in some good quality hair gel, wax, or pomade. Apply the product to your hair and style it in the direction of your bangs, sweeping them to one side to cover your eye.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect emo hair look:

  • Invest in a good quality hair straightener to help you achieve sleek, straight hair.
  • Try using a hair dryer to add volume to your hair and create a more dramatic look.
  • Accessorize your hair with hair clips, hairbands, or even a beanie to add extra style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting emo hair for guys with short hair:

1. Can I get emo hair with naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can still get emo hair with naturally curly hair. However, you may need to use a hair straightener to achieve the sleek, straight look that is often associated with emo hairstyles.

2. Do I need to dye my hair to get emo hair?

No, you don't need to dye your hair to get emo hair. However, if you want to go all-out with the emo look, dyeing your hair a bold, bright color can help you stand out from the crowd.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair if you want to maintain your emo hairstyle. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. Use a heat protectant spray if you need to use a hair straightener or hair dryer.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to get the perfect emo hair look for guys with short hair. Whether you're going for a classic emo look or something more modern and edgy, remember to have fun and express your individuality through your hairstyle.

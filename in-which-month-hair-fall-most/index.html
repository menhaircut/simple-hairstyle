In Which Month Hair Fall Most Understanding The Science Behind It

Written by Bardi Jun 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you worried about losing hair in a particular season? Do you feel like you lose more hair in a specific month than others? If yes, then you are not alone. Many people have observed that their hair fall increases during a particular time of the year. In this post, we will explore the science behind the hair fall season and provide you with tips to minimize hair fall during this time.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Hair fall is a natural process that occurs throughout the year. However, many factors can impact the intensity of hair fall, including weather, stress, and hormonal changes. According to research, the most significant hair fall occurs in the autumn season. During this time, most people lose around 50-100 hair strands per day, compared to the standard 80-100 strands. This is because the hair growth cycle is affected by the change in seasons.

How to

The best way to prevent hair fall during this season is to be proactive and take care of your hair. Here are some tips to help you minimize hair fall:

Step by Step

  1. Keep your hair clean and wash it regularly with a mild shampoo.
  2. Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage the hair follicles.
  3. Massage your scalp with warm oil to improve blood circulation and strengthen hair roots.
  4. Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, iron, and vitamins to promote healthy hair growth.
  5. Avoid using harsh hair products that contain chemicals that can damage hair follicles.
  6. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  7. Avoid using hairdryers or other heating tools that can damage your hair.
  8. Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun, wind, and cold weather.
  9. Get enough sleep and manage stress levels to prevent hair fall due to a hormonal imbalance.

Tips

Here are some additional tips that can help you prevent hair fall during the autumn season:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull your hair tightly, such as braids and buns.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends, which can lead to hair fall.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to provide your hair with the necessary nutrients and hydration.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair fall:

Q. Is it normal to lose hair during a particular season?

A. Yes, it is normal to experience hair fall during a particular season due to the change in weather and environmental factors.

Q. How much hair fall is considered normal?

A. It is normal to lose around 80-100 hair strands per day. Anything more than that is considered excessive hair fall.

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress can lead to hair fall due to hormonal imbalances and inflammation.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair to prevent hair fall during the autumn season. Follow the tips mentioned above and be proactive in managing hair fall. Eat a balanced diet, keep your hair clean, and avoid using harsh hair products. If you experience excessive hair fall, consult a dermatologist to identify the underlying cause and get appropriate treatment.

By taking care of your hair, you can prevent hair fall and maintain healthy, luscious locks all year round.

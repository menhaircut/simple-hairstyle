Does Long Hair Make Your Face Look Bigger

Written by Luffy Jun 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long hair is a popular hairstyle among women. It adds beauty and elegance to their look. However, many women worry that having long hair might make their face look bigger. In this article, we will discuss the truth behind this belief and provide some tips on how to balance your hair length with your facial features.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

Many women have the belief that long hair makes their faces look bigger. This is because long hair can cover a significant portion of the face, making it difficult to see its true shape. However, this is not always the case. Long hair can also complement your facial features and make them appear more symmetrical and balanced.

To determine whether long hair makes your face look bigger or not, you need to consider your face shape and hair texture. Certain face shapes, such as round and square faces, may benefit from shorter haircuts that add angles and dimension to their features. On the other hand, oval and heart-shaped faces can pull off longer hairstyles that frame their face and create a soft, feminine look.

How to

If you want to know whether long hair makes your face look bigger or not, you can try the following steps:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Look in the mirror and identify your face shape. Is it round, square, oval, or heart-shaped? Knowing your face shape is crucial in determining the best hairstyle for you.

Step 2: Consider your hair texture

Is your hair thin or thick? Straight or curly? Knowing your hair texture will help you choose a hairstyle that suits your hair type and complements your facial features.

Step 3: Experiment with different hairstyles

Try different hairstyles, such as updos, braids, and ponytails, to see how they affect your face shape. Take pictures of yourself from different angles to get a better idea of how your hair length affects your overall appearance.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can take to balance your hair length with your facial features:

Step 1: Identify your face shape

Look at your face in the mirror and determine its shape. If you have a round face, consider a shorter hairstyle that adds angles and lengthens your features. If you have a square face, go for a layered haircut that softens your jawline. If you have an oval or heart-shaped face, longer hairstyles will complement your features.

Step 2: Determine your hair texture

Knowing your hair texture is essential in choosing the right hairstyle. If you have thin or fine hair, go for shorter hairstyles that add volume and texture. If you have thick or curly hair, longer hairstyles will complement your natural texture and create a more relaxed, effortless look.

Step 3: Experiment with different hairstyles

Try different hairstyles and see how they affect your face shape. You can try updos, braids, ponytails, and different hair accessories to add variety to your look. Take pictures of yourself from different angles to get a better idea of how your hair length complements your facial features.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you can dedicate to styling your hair.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair texture.
  • Use hair products that suit your hair type and help maintain your hairstyle.
  • Consider your personal style and how your hairstyle complements your overall look.

FAQ

Q: Does long hair make your face look bigger?

A: It depends on your face shape and hair texture. Long hair can complement your facial features and make them appear more symmetrical and balanced. However, certain face shapes may benefit from shorter haircuts that add angles and dimension to their features.

Q: What are the best hairstyles for a round face?

A: Shorter hairstyles that add angles and lengthen your features, such as a bob or a pixie cut, are ideal for a round face. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as they can make your face appear wider.

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have a square face?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a square face. However, make sure to choose a layered haircut that softens your jawline and adds texture to your hair.

Hair Care

Maintaining long hair requires proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hairdryers and straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to minimize damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Conclusion:

Long hair can complement your facial features and make them appear more symmetrical and balanced. However, it depends on your face shape and hair texture. By following the steps and tips provided in this article, you can determine whether long hair makes your face look bigger or not and choose the best hairstyle for your facial features.

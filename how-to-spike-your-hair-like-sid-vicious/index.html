How To Spike Your Hair Like Sid Vicious

Written by Luffy Nov 16, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking to rock a punk rock hairstyle, then spiking your hair like Sid Vicious is the way to go. Sid Vicious was the bassist for the iconic punk rock band, the Sex Pistols, and his signature hairstyle is still a favorite of many to this day. In this article, we'll show you how to spike your hair like Sid Vicious in a few easy steps.

Description

Sid Vicious was known for his messy, spiky hair that was styled to perfection. His hairstyle was a mix of punk rock and rebellion, and it's still a popular look today. Spiking your hair like Sid takes some time and effort, but the result is worth it.

How to Spike Your Hair Like Sid Vicious

Before you start, you'll need the right tools. You'll need a strong hold hair gel or wax, a blow dryer, and a comb. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or oil from your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair until it's completely dry. This will help to create volume and texture, making it easier to spike your hair.

Step 3: Apply Hair Gel or Wax

Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to your hands and work it through your hair. Make sure you get it evenly distributed throughout your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using a comb, style your hair into a spiky look. You can create spikes all over or just focus on the top of your head. Be sure to use the comb to create texture and volume in your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To make sure your spikes stay in place all day, finish with a light coat of hairspray. This will help to lock in the style and keep your hair looking great.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect spiked hair:

  • Use a strong hold hair gel or wax to keep your spikes in place.
  • Blow dry your hair before spiking it to create volume and texture.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and textures to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

How long does it take to spike your hair like Sid Vicious?

Spiking your hair like Sid Vicious can take anywhere from 10-30 minutes, depending on the length and thickness of your hair and the complexity of the style.

Do I need to use hairspray?

Yes, using hairspray is an important step in keeping your spikes in place all day.

Can I spike my hair if it's short?

Yes, you can still spike your hair even if it's short. Just focus on creating texture and volume in the hair you have.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by using a good quality shampoo and conditioner and avoiding heat styling tools too often. Over-styling and using too much product can damage your hair and make it harder to style in the future.

By following these tips and steps, you can achieve the perfect spiked hair like Sid Vicious. Rock on!

Men s Haircut Shorter Sides Longer Top A Trending Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Oliver Sep 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is both stylish and practical? Look no further than the men's haircut shorter sides longer top. This versatile haircut has become increasingly popular in recent years and is sure to be a hit in 2023.

Description

The men's haircut shorter sides longer top is exactly what it sounds like. The sides of the head are cut shorter than the top, creating a contrast in length that is both edgy and stylish. The length on the top can vary, from a short buzz cut to a longer, tousled style.

This haircut is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance style that still looks put-together. It can be worn in a variety of settings, from the office to a night out with friends.

How To

If you're interested in getting a men's haircut shorter sides longer top, the first step is to find a reputable barber or stylist. Once you've found someone you trust, follow these steps to achieve the perfect cut:

Step 1: Consultation

Before your barber starts cutting, they will likely ask you a few questions about the style you're looking for. Be sure to bring in a picture or two so they can get a better sense of what you want.

Step 2: Clipper Work

The barber will start by using clippers to trim the sides of your head. Depending on the length you're going for, they may use a few different clipper guards to achieve the perfect length.

Step 3: Scissors Work

After the sides are cut, your barber will move on to the top of your head. They will use scissors to cut the length you've requested, taking care to blend the sides and top together seamlessly.

Step 4: Styling

Once the cut is complete, your barber may add some styling product to the top of your hair to help it hold its shape. From there, you can style your hair however you like, whether it's slicked back or left messy.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind if you're considering a men's haircut shorter sides longer top:

- Be sure to communicate clearly with your barber about the length you want on the top of your head - This haircut can be worn with a fade or undercut on the sides for an even more dramatic look - Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type

FAQ

Here are some common questions people have about the men's haircut shorter sides longer top:

Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this haircut can work for a variety of face shapes. Your barber can help you determine the best length and style for your unique features.

How often should I get this haircut?

Depending on how fast your hair grows, you may need to get this haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Can I style this haircut in different ways?

Absolutely! The beauty of the men's haircut shorter sides longer top is that it can be styled in a variety of ways. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your men's haircut shorter sides longer top looking its best, be sure to follow these hair care tips:

- Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy - Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy - Get regular trims to maintain the length and shape of your haircut

With these tips in mind, you're sure to rock the men's haircut shorter sides longer top with style and confidence.

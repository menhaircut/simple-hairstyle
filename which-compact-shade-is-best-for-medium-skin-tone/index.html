Which Compact Shade Is Best For Medium Skin Tone

Written by Aprilia Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to finding the perfect compact shade for medium skin tone, it can be a bit challenging. With so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right shade that complements your skin tone. In this article, we will discuss the best compact shade for medium skin tone and help you find the perfect match for your skin.

Description

Medium skin tone falls between fair and dark skin tones. It has a warm undertone with a combination of olive, golden, or yellow undertones. Medium skin tone is the most common skin tone and can be found in people of different ethnicities. To find the perfect compact shade for medium skin tone, it is essential to understand your skin's undertone.

How to

Choosing the right compact shade for medium skin tone can be challenging. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect match:

Step 1: Determine your undertone

The first step in finding the right compact shade for medium skin tone is to determine your undertone. You can do this by looking at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear green, you have a warm undertone. If they appear blue, you have a cool undertone. If you cannot determine the color, you have a neutral undertone.

Step 2: Choose the right shade

Once you have determined your undertone, it's time to choose the right shade. For medium skin tone with a warm undertone, look for shades with golden or yellow undertones. For medium skin tone with a cool undertone, look for shades with pink or blue undertones. For medium skin tone with a neutral undertone, look for shades that are not too warm or cool.

Step 3: Test the shade

Before purchasing a compact shade, it's essential to test it first. Apply a small amount of product on your jawline and blend it in. The shade should blend seamlessly with your skin and not leave any visible lines or patches.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions to help you find the perfect compact shade for medium skin tone:

Step 1: Cleanse your face

Before testing any compact shade, it is essential to cleanse your face thoroughly. This will help remove any dirt or oil that may affect the shade's color.

Step 2: Determine your undertone

Using the vein test, determine your undertone to help you choose the right shade.

Step 3: Choose the right shade

Based on your undertone, choose the right shade that complements your skin tone.

Step 4: Test the shade

Apply a small amount of the shade on your jawline and blend it in. Check if the shade matches your skin tone and blends seamlessly.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you find the perfect compact shade for medium skin tone:

  • Always test the shade in natural light to ensure that it matches your skin tone.
  • If you have oily skin, choose a compact shade that provides a matte finish.
  • If you have dry skin, choose a compact shade that provides a dewy finish.
  • Choose a compact shade that provides coverage without clogging your pores.

FAQ

1. Can I use a shade that is slightly lighter or darker than my skin tone?

It is best to choose a shade that matches your skin tone perfectly. A shade that is slightly lighter or darker can look unnatural and may not complement your skin tone.

2. Can I use a compact shade with shimmer?

If you have oily skin, it is best to avoid compact shades with shimmer as they can make your skin look greasy. However, if you have dry skin, you can use a compact shade with shimmer to add a subtle glow to your skin.

3. Can I use a compact shade with SPF?

Yes, using a compact shade with SPF can help protect your skin from the harmful effects of the sun. However, it is essential to reapply the product every few hours to ensure maximum protection.

Hair Care

Choosing the right compact shade for medium skin tone is essential, but it's not the only thing that can enhance your overall look. Taking care of your hair is just as important. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with SPF.

With these tips, you can find the perfect compact shade for medium skin tone and take care of your hair to enhance your overall look.

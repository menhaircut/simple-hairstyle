Can Thick Curly Hair Be Layered

Written by Oliver Jul 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you may be wondering whether it can be layered. Layered haircuts can add volume, shape, and definition to curly hair, but they can also be tricky to achieve. In this article, we'll explore whether thick curly hair can be layered and provide tips on how to achieve the perfect layered look.

Description

Thick curly hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right haircut, it can look stunning. Layered haircuts can help to lighten the weight of thick curls, add movement and shape, and highlight your best features. However, not all hair types are suitable for layering, and it's essential to consider your hair's texture, length, and volume before making a decision.

How to Layer Thick Curly Hair

If you're looking to layer your thick curly hair, it's essential to find a stylist who has experience working with curly hair. Curly hair requires a different approach to cutting than straight hair, and a stylist who isn't familiar with curly hair may not achieve the desired results.

When layering thick curly hair, it's essential to avoid cutting too much hair at once. Cutting too much hair can cause your curls to lose their shape and bounce, leaving them flat and lifeless. Instead, opt for gradual layering, starting with a few layers around your face and gradually adding more layers as needed. This approach will help to maintain the integrity of your curls while providing shape and definition.

Step-by-Step Guide to Layering Thick Curly Hair

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Section your hair into four quadrants using hair clips.

3. Begin layering your hair by starting with the bottom section. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers at a 45-degree angle.

4. Cut the hair at the angle, then release it and check the length. If the length is too long, repeat the process until it's the desired length.

5. Move on to the next section and repeat the process until you've layered all four sections of your hair.

6. Use a diffuser to dry your hair gently, enhancing your curls' definition.

Tips for Layering Thick Curly Hair

1. Avoid cutting too much hair at once.

2. Gradual layering is key.

3. Find a stylist who has experience working with curly hair.

4. Use a diffuser to dry your hair gently.

FAQ

Can Layering Make My Thick Curly Hair Look Thinner?

No, layering can actually make your thick curly hair look fuller and more defined. However, it's important to avoid cutting too much hair at once, as this can cause your curls to lose their shape and bounce.

Can I Layer My Thick Curly Hair at Home?

We recommend going to a stylist who has experience working with curly hair to achieve the best results. However, if you're confident in your hair-cutting skills, you can try layering your hair at home. Just be sure to take your time and avoid cutting too much hair at once.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Hair

To keep your thick curly hair healthy and beautiful, follow these tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.

2. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.

4. Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz.

5. Avoid using hot styling tools, as these can damage your hair and cause it to lose its shape and bounce.

Conclusion

Thick curly hair can be layered, but it's essential to find a stylist who has experience working with curly hair and to avoid cutting too much hair at once. Gradual layering can help to maintain the integrity of your curls while providing shape and definition. Follow our hair care tips to keep your thick curly hair looking healthy and beautiful.

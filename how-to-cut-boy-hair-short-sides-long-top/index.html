How To Cut Boy Hair Short Sides Long Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to give your son a trendy new haircut? The short sides long top style has been popular for years and is a great option for boys. In this guide, we will show you how to cut boy hair short sides long top. With our step-by-step instructions and tips, you'll be able to give your son a stylish new cut in no time.

Description

The short sides long top haircut is a versatile and trendy style that has become increasingly popular in recent years. This cut is characterized by short hair on the sides and longer hair on top. It can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, swept to the side, or even spiked up. This style is perfect for boys who want a stylish and low maintenance haircut.

How to Cut Boy Hair Short Sides Long Top

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting boy hair short sides long top is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clippers with different length guards. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to help wet the hair and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, it's important to wash and dry the hair. This will make it easier to cut and give you a better idea of the natural texture and shape of the hair. Once the hair is dry, use a comb to part it in the middle.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Using your clippers with a #2 guard, start cutting the hair on the sides. Start at the bottom and work your way up, making sure to keep the clippers straight and parallel to the head. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Top

After you've finished cutting the sides, switch to a #4 guard and begin blending the hair on the sides with the hair on top. Use your clippers to create a smooth transition between the two lengths.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Using your scissors, start cutting the hair on top. Begin at the front and work your way to the back, cutting the hair to the desired length. You can use your fingers to guide the scissors and create texture.

Step 6: Style the Hair

Once you've finished cutting, it's time to style the hair. You can use a hair gel or pomade to create the desired look. Slick the hair back or to the side, or create a messy, textured look.

Tips

  • Start with longer guards and work your way down to a shorter length to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Take breaks if your child is getting restless or uncomfortable.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're not confident in your cutting skills.

FAQ

Is the short sides long top haircut easy to maintain?

Yes, this style is low maintenance and easy to maintain. A quick trim every few weeks will keep the hair looking fresh and stylish.

What if my child has curly hair?

The short sides long top style works well with curly hair. Just be sure to cut the hair when it's dry to avoid any surprises once it dries.

Hair Care

After cutting your child's hair, it's important to maintain it properly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. A regular trim every few weeks will help keep the hair healthy and looking its best.

Now that you know how to cut boy hair short sides long top, you can give your child a stylish new cut that they'll love. With our step-by-step instructions and tips, you'll be able to create a trendy and low maintenance look that will last for weeks. Happy cutting!

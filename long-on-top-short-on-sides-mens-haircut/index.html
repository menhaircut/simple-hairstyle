The Long On Top Short On Sides Men s Haircut

Written by Aprilia Oct 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, then the long on top short on sides men's haircut might be perfect for you. This popular haircut has been around for a few years now and is still going strong. This article will help guide you through the process of getting this haircut, from the description to the hair care tips.

Description

The long on top short on sides men's haircut is exactly what it sounds like. The hair on the top of your head is left longer while the hair on the sides and back is cut shorter. This creates a contrast between the two lengths and gives the haircut its unique look.

This haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can slick it back, comb it to the side, or leave it messy and tousled. The possibilities are endless.

How to

The long on top short on sides men's haircut can be achieved at any barbershop or salon. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get this haircut:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length you want for the top of your head. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. Consult with your stylist to find the perfect length for you.

Step 2: Trim the Sides

The next step is to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. This can be done with clippers or scissors, depending on the look you're going for. Make sure to blend the hair from the sides to the top for a seamless transition.

Step 3: Cut the Top

The final step is to cut the hair on the top of your head to the desired length. This can be done with scissors or a razor, depending on the texture of your hair. Your stylist will shape the top to fit your face and style it the way you want.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting the long on top short on sides men's haircut:

  • Bring a picture of the style you want to show your stylist.
  • Use a styling product to keep the hair on top in place.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

1. How often should I get this haircut?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut.

2. What kind of styling product should I use?

You can use a pomade, wax, or gel to keep the hair on top in place. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

3. Can this haircut work for any hair type?

Yes, the long on top short on sides men's haircut can work for any hair type, from straight to curly.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like blow dryers and straighteners, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Now that you know all about the long on top short on sides men's haircut, it's time to book your appointment and try it out for yourself. With the right styling and hair care, you can rock this trendy look with confidence.

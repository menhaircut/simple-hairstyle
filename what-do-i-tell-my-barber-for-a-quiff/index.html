What Do I Tell My Barber For A Quiff

Written by Frank Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to ask your barber for a quiff. This stylish and versatile hairstyle has been popular for decades, and it's not hard to see why. With a quiff, you can achieve a cool, classic look that's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll go over what you need to tell your barber to get the perfect quiff, as well as some tips for styling and maintaining your hair. So, let's get started!

Description

A quiff is a hairstyle that involves styling the hair at the front of your head upwards and back, while keeping the sides and back of your hair shorter. It's a great look for those who want a classic, timeless hairstyle that's both stylish and easy to maintain.

How To

When you go to your barber, the first thing you need to do is show them some pictures of the quiff you want. This will give them a better idea of what you're looking for, and they can advise you on what will work best for your hair type and face shape. You can find plenty of pictures of quiffs online, or you can ask your barber for some inspiration.

Next, you need to tell your barber how long you want your hair to be on the sides and back. For a classic quiff, the sides and back should be short, but not too short. You can ask for a fade or taper on the sides, which will gradually blend the hair from short to longer as it goes up your head.

Finally, you need to tell your barber how long you want the hair at the front of your head to be. This will depend on how high you want your quiff to be. If you want a high quiff, you'll need more hair at the front. If you want a more subtle quiff, you can ask for less hair at the front.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when getting a quiff:

Step 1: Show your barber pictures of the quiff you want

Bring some pictures of the quiff you want to your barber. This will help them understand exactly what you want.

Step 2: Decide on the length of the sides and back

Tell your barber how short you want the sides and back to be. A fade or taper can help create a smooth transition from short to longer hair.

Step 3: Decide on the length of the hair at the front

Tell your barber how long you want the hair at the front of your head to be. This will determine how high your quiff will be.

Step 4: Get the cut

Sit back and let your barber work their magic. They'll use clippers, scissors, and a comb to create the perfect quiff for you.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling and maintaining your quiff:

  • Use a high-quality pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upwards and back to create volume at the front.
  • Use a comb to style your quiff, starting at the roots and working your way up.
  • Have regular trims to keep your quiff looking sharp.

FAQ

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, a quiff can still work for you. Ask your barber to add some texture to your hair using thinning shears, and use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create a fuller look.

Can I still get a quiff if I have curly hair?

A quiff can look great on curly hair. Ask your barber to cut your hair slightly longer at the front to accommodate your curls.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day to keep it healthy and clean. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and damage.

Hair Care

To keep your quiff looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair when styling. Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

That's it for our guide on what to tell your barber for a quiff. With these tips and tricks, you'll be sporting a stylish and classic look in no time!

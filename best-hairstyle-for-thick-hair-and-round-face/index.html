Best Hairstyle For Thick Hair And Round Face

Written by Oliver Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles for your thick hair and round face? Do you find it difficult to choose the right hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair type? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyle for thick hair and round face that will make you look stunning and fabulous.

Description

Women with thick hair and round face often face difficulty in choosing the right hairstyle that suits their face shape and hair type. Thick hair can be a blessing but it can also be a curse if you don't know how to style it properly. Round face shape needs a hairstyle that can make it look slimmer and longer. In this article, we will provide you with the best hairstyle options that will enhance your features and make you look beautiful.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

Choosing the right hairstyle can be challenging but it is not impossible. Here are some tips that will help you choose the right hairstyle for your face shape and hair type:

  • Understand your face shape
  • Consider your hair type
  • Think about the length of your hair
  • Look for inspiration
  • Consult with a hairstylist

Best Hairstyles for Thick Hair and Round Face

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for women with thick hair and round face. This hairstyle adds movement and texture to your hair and makes your face look slimmer. You can also add side-swept bangs to this hairstyle to make it look more flattering.

2. Bob Hairstyle

A bob hairstyle is another great option for women with thick hair and round face. This hairstyle can be worn in different lengths and styles. You can opt for a short bob or a longer one depending on your preference. This hairstyle adds volume to your hair and makes your face look slimmer.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and edgy hairstyle that looks great on women with thick hair and round face. This hairstyle adds volume to your hair and makes your face look slimmer. You can also add layers or bangs to this hairstyle to make it look more stylish.

Step by Step Guide to Style Your Hair

Here is a step by step guide to style your hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush
  4. Curl your hair using a curling iron or a straightener
  5. Apply a hairspray to set your hair

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends
  • Avoid using too many hair products
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water

FAQ

Q. Can I wear a short hairstyle if I have a round face?

A. Yes, you can wear a short hairstyle if you have a round face. However, you should choose a hairstyle that adds volume to your hair and makes your face look slimmer.

Q. What hair color suits a round face?

A. Any hair color can suit a round face as long as it complements your skin tone and eye color. You can choose a hair color that adds depth and dimension to your hair and makes your features stand out.

Q. How often should I wash my hair?

A. You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. However, you can wash your hair more often if you have an oily scalp.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape and hair type can be challenging but it is not impossible. We hope that this article has provided you with some great options for the best hairstyle for thick hair and round face. Remember to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

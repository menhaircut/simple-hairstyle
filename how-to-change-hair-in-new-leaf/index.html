How To Change Hair In New Leaf A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to change hair in New Leaf! In this article, we’ll provide you with everything you need to know about changing your character’s hairstyle in this popular game. Whether you’re looking to update your look or simply want to experiment with different hairstyles, we’ve got you covered. Read on to learn more!

  • How to Change Hair in New Leaf
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

How to Change Hair in New Leaf

Changing your hair in New Leaf is a relatively simple process. All you need to do is visit Shampoodle, the local hair salon in your town. Here’s how:

Step 1: Unlock Shampoodle

In order to visit Shampoodle, you’ll need to have unlocked the salon first. To do this, you’ll need to meet the following requirements:

  • Have at least 10,000 Bells in your savings account
  • Have played for at least 7 days
  • Have had at least one haircut at the Able Sisters’ shop

Once you’ve met these requirements, Shampoodle will open up in your town.

Step 2: Visit Shampoodle

Head over to Shampoodle, which is located on the right side of Main Street. When you enter the salon, Harriet the poodle will greet you and ask what kind of hairstyle you’re looking for.

Step 3: Choose Your Hairstyle

Harriet will then show you a selection of hairstyles to choose from. You can choose from a variety of styles, including short, medium, and long haircuts, as well as different colors and highlights. Take your time choosing the right style for you.

Step 4: Confirm Your Choice

Once you’ve chosen your new hairstyle, Harriet will ask you to confirm your choice. If you’re happy with your selection, she’ll then take you to the stylist’s chair to get your hair done.

Step 5: Enjoy Your New Look

After your hair has been styled, Harriet will show you your new look. If you’re happy with your new hairstyle, you’re all set! If not, you can always come back to Shampoodle and choose a different style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a quick summary of the steps involved in changing your hair in New Leaf:

  1. Unlock Shampoodle by meeting the requirements
  2. Visit Shampoodle on Main Street
  3. Choose your new hairstyle from the selection provided
  4. Confirm your choice and get your hair done

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can make all the difference in how your character looks in the game. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Think about your character’s personality and style
  • Consider the season and weather conditions in your town
  • Look at hairstyles worn by other characters in the game for inspiration
  • Experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing hair in New Leaf:

Can I change my hair color?

Yes, you can choose from a variety of hair colors and highlights at Shampoodle.

Can I change my hair back to its original style?

Yes, you can always come back to Shampoodle and choose your original hairstyle again.

Can I change my hair multiple times?

Yes, you can change your hair as many times as you like. However, keep in mind that each visit to Shampoodle will cost you 3,000 Bells.

Hair Care in New Leaf

Once you’ve changed your hair, you’ll want to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Visit Shampoodle regularly to keep your hair styled
  • Avoid getting your hair wet in the rain or swimming
  • Use hair care products, such as shampoo and conditioner, to keep your hair healthy

That’s it for our guide on how to change hair in New Leaf! We hope you found this article helpful. Happy styling!

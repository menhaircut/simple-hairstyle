Best Hair Products For Fine Naturally Curly Hair

Written by Christine Jul 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have naturally curly hair that is also fine, it can be a challenge to find the right hair products that work for you. You want to enhance your curls without weighing them down or causing frizz. Luckily, there are many hair products on the market that cater specifically to fine, curly hair. In this article, we will go over the best hair products for fine naturally curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Shampoo
  • Conditioner

Shampoo

When it comes to shampoo for fine naturally curly hair, you want to look for a product that is gentle and moisturizing. A sulfate-free shampoo is ideal, as sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause frizz. Look for shampoos that contain ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, and argan oil, which will help to hydrate your hair and enhance your curls.

Recommended Products:

  • DevaCurl Low-Poo Delight Weightless Waves Mild Lather Cleanser: This sulfate-free shampoo is specifically designed for fine, curly hair. It is gentle and moisturizing, and will leave your hair feeling clean and refreshed without weighing it down.
  • Jessicurl Gentle Lather Shampoo: This gentle shampoo is sulfate-free and contains ingredients like chamomile and jojoba oil to hydrate and nourish your curls.

Conditioner

Conditioner is an essential part of any curly hair routine, but it can be especially important for fine hair. Look for a conditioner that is lightweight and won't weigh down your curls. You also want a product that will help to detangle your hair and provide moisture.

Recommended Products:

  • DevaCurl One Condition Delight Weightless Waves Conditioner: This conditioner is specifically designed for fine, curly hair. It is lightweight and won't weigh down your curls, but will still provide plenty of moisture and detangling power.
  • Ouidad VitalCurl+ Balancing Rinse Conditioner: This lightweight conditioner is great for fine curly hair. It contains ingredients like wheat protein and jojoba oil to help strengthen and nourish your curls.

Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can be a game changer for fine curly hair. It will provide extra moisture and help to define your curls without weighing them down. Look for a product that is lightweight and won't leave your hair feeling greasy or sticky.

Recommended Products:

  • DevaCurl B'Leave-In Miracle Curl Plumper: This leave-in conditioner is designed to enhance your curls and provide extra volume. It is lightweight and won't weigh down your hair, but will still provide plenty of moisture and definition.
  • SheaMoisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie: This leave-in conditioner is great for fine curly hair. It contains ingredients like coconut oil and hibiscus flower extract to help moisturize and define your curls.

Styling Products

When it comes to styling products for fine curly hair, you want to look for products that will enhance your curls without weighing them down. Look for lightweight mousses or gels that will provide hold without leaving your hair feeling crunchy or sticky.

Recommended Products:

  • DevaCurl Ultra Defining Gel: This lightweight gel is great for fine curly hair. It provides hold without leaving your hair feeling crunchy or sticky, and will enhance your curls without weighing them down.
  • Ouidad Advanced Climate Control Heat & Humidity Gel: This lightweight gel is designed to provide hold and humidity protection. It will enhance your curls and keep them looking great all day long.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to using the right hair products, there are a few other tips you can follow to keep your fine curly hair looking its best.

Tip #1: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair no more than two or three times a week.

Tip #2: Use a Microfiber Towel

A regular towel can be too rough on your curls and cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair.

Tip #3: Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and breakage. Silk is gentler on your hair than cotton, and will help to keep your curls looking great.

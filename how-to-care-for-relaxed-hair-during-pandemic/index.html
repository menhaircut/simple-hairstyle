How to Care for Relaxed Hair During Pandemic - Tips and Tricks

The global pandemic has changed the way we live our daily lives, and it has also affected our hair care routines. Relaxed hair needs extra care during this time to keep it healthy and strong. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to care for relaxed hair during pandemic.

Table of Contents

  • How to Care for Relaxed Hair During Pandemic
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Importance of Hair Care During Pandemic

How to Care for Relaxed Hair During Pandemic

Relaxed hair needs extra care during pandemic to keep it strong and healthy. Here are some tips to follow:

  1. Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  2. Deep condition your hair at least once a week to keep it moisturized.
  3. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  4. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  5. Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage.
  6. Protect your hair when going outside by wearing a hat or scarf.
  7. Use hair products that are specifically designed for relaxed hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to care for your relaxed hair during pandemic:

  1. Wet your hair with warm water.
  2. Apply sulfate-free shampoo to your hair and massage it into your scalp.
  3. Rinse the shampoo out with warm water.
  4. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Rinse the conditioner out with cool water.
  6. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  7. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  8. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to get rid of split ends.
  9. Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage.
  10. Protect your hair when going outside by wearing a hat or scarf.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips to keep your relaxed hair healthy during pandemic:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as it can dry out your hair.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your body and hair hydrated.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about caring for relaxed hair during pandemic:

  1. Can I still get my hair relaxed during pandemic?
    It is not recommended to get your hair relaxed during pandemic as it involves close contact with other people. It is best to wait until it is safe to do so.
  2. Is it safe to go to the hair salon during pandemic?
    It depends on your location and the safety measures put in place by the salon. It is important to wear a mask and practice social distancing when going to the hair salon.
  3. How often should I wash my relaxed hair during pandemic?
    It is recommended to wash your hair once a week to keep it clean and healthy.

Importance of Hair Care During Pandemic

During pandemic, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain your overall health and well-being. Stress and anxiety can cause hair loss and damage, so it is important to practice self-care and take the time to care for your hair. Additionally, social distancing and quarantine may limit your access to hair care services, so it is important to know how to care for your hair at home.

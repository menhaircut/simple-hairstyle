Why Am I Losing Hair On The Side Of My Head

Written by Robby Aug 25, 2023 · 3 min read

If you've noticed that your hair is thinning or falling out on the side of your head, you're not alone. Hair loss is a common problem that affects millions of people around the world. In this article, we'll explore some of the reasons why you may be losing hair on the side of your head and what you can do to address the problem.

Description

Hair loss on the side of the head can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, medical conditions, and lifestyle factors. It can manifest as thinning hair, bald patches, or receding hairlines, and can be temporary or permanent.

How to

If you're experiencing hair loss on the side of your head, it's important to identify the underlying cause in order to address the problem effectively. Here are some steps you can take:

Step 1: Consult a Doctor

If you're concerned about hair loss, the first step is to consult a doctor or dermatologist. They can examine your scalp, review your medical history, and perform any necessary tests to determine the cause of your hair loss.

Step 2: Address Underlying Health Issues

If your hair loss is caused by an underlying medical condition, such as thyroid issues or an autoimmune disorder, treating that condition may help to restore your hair growth. Be sure to follow your doctor's recommendations for treatment.

Step 3: Adjust Your Hair Care Routine

Your hair care routine may be contributing to your hair loss. Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as relaxers or dyes, and opt for gentle, natural products instead. Be gentle when brushing or styling your hair, and avoid pulling it too tightly.

Step 4: Make Lifestyle Changes

Your lifestyle habits may also be contributing to your hair loss. Stress, poor nutrition, and lack of sleep can all affect your hair growth. Make sure you're eating a balanced diet, getting enough sleep, and finding healthy ways to manage stress.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for addressing hair loss on the side of your head:

  • Consider using hair growth products, such as minoxidil or finasteride, under the guidance of a doctor or dermatologist
  • Try hair thickening treatments, such as scalp micropigmentation or hair extensions
  • Consider hair transplant surgery if you have significant hair loss

FAQ

Here are some common questions about hair loss on the side of the head:

Q: Is hair loss on the side of the head permanent?

A: It depends on the cause of the hair loss. Some types of hair loss, such as alopecia areata, may be temporary and can be treated. Others, such as male or female pattern baldness, may be permanent.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss on the side of the head?

A: Yes, stress can contribute to hair loss. Chronic stress can disrupt the hair growth cycle and lead to more shedding than usual.

Q: Are there any natural remedies for hair loss on the side of the head?

A: Some natural remedies that may help to promote hair growth include massaging the scalp with essential oils, taking supplements like biotin or saw palmetto, and using aloe vera or onion juice on the scalp.

Hair Care

Regardless of the cause of your hair loss, taking good care of your hair can help to promote healthy growth and prevent further damage. Be gentle when brushing or styling, avoid harsh chemicals, and nourish your hair with healthy foods and supplements.

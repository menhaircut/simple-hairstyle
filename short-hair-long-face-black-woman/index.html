Short Hair Long Face Black Woman A Guide To Styling And Caring For Your Hair

Written by Daniel Oct 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a long face and want to try out a short haircut, you may feel hesitant about whether it will suit you or not. However, short hair can look fantastic on long faces, especially on black women. In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the right hairstyle, maintaining it, and caring for your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant.

Description: Short Hair Long Face Black Woman

Black women with long faces have unique features that require special attention when it comes to hair styling. Short haircuts can help balance out the face shape and highlight your features. However, not every short hairstyle is suitable for long faces. It's essential to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Before you decide on a short hairstyle, it's crucial to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Identifying your face shape is the first step in choosing the right hairstyle. If you have a long face, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add length to your face. Short hairstyles that add volume and width to the sides of your face are ideal for long faces.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture also plays a significant role in determining the best hairstyle for you. If you have fine hair, a layered bob or a pixie cut can add volume and texture to your hair. If you have thick hair, a short bob or a curly pixie cut can help manage your hair and create a stylish look.

3. Think About Your Personal Style

Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and personal style. If you're a low-maintenance person, a short, easy-to-style haircut may be the best option for you. On the other hand, if you love experimenting with different styles, a layered bob or a pixie cut can give you plenty of styling options.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you create a chic and stylish look:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy. Use a towel to dry your hair and remove any excess moisture.

2. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product like mousse or gel to your hair to add texture and hold. Work the product through your hair with your fingers and comb it through to distribute it evenly.

3. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to style your hair. Start at the roots and work your way to the ends, using the brush to add volume and shape to your hair.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is dry, apply a hairspray to keep it in place. Hold the can about 6 inches away from your head and spray evenly over your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hair:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short haircuts tend to grow out quickly, so it's essential to visit your stylist regularly for a trim. Trimming your hair every 4-6 weeks will help maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to protect your hair from damage.

3. Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can make a significant difference in the health and appearance of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type and choose styling products that are designed for short hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will a short haircut suit me if I have a long face?

Yes, short hair can look fantastic on long faces, especially on black women. The key is to choose a style that complements your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.

2. How often should I wash my short hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every 2-3 days. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.

3. Can I style my short hair differently for different occasions?

Yes, you can experiment with different styling techniques to create different looks for different occasions. For example, you can use a flat iron to create a sleek and sophisticated look for a formal event or use a curling iron to create a playful and flirty look for a night out with friends.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair Long Face Black Woman

Black women with long faces can maintain healthy and vibrant hair by following these hair care tips:

1. Moisturize Your Hair

Black hair tends to be dry, so it's essential to moisturize your hair regularly. Use a moisturizing conditioner and leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Protect Your Hair at Night

Wear a silk or satin bonnet or use a silk or satin pillowcase to protect your hair while you sleep. This will prevent your hair from rubbing against rough surfaces and becoming frizzy and damaged.

3. Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals like relaxers and dyes on your hair, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage. Opt for natural hair care products that are gentle and free of harsh chemicals.

In conclusion, short hair can be a great option for black women with long faces. By choosing the right hairstyle, maintaining it, and caring for your hair, you can create a chic and stylish look that enhances your natural beauty. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve healthy, vibrant, and beautiful hair.

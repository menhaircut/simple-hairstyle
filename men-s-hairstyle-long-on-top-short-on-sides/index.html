Men s Hairstyle Long On Top Short On Sides A Trend That Will Never Die

Written by Eveline Aug 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is both stylish and easy to maintain? Look no further than the men's hairstyle long on top short on sides. This versatile look has been around for decades and shows no signs of going out of fashion. In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look, as well as tips on how to maintain it and answer some frequently asked questions about this hairstyle.

Description

The men's hairstyle long on top short on sides is a classic look that has been popular for many years. The hair on the top of the head is left longer, while the sides and back are cut short. This style can be achieved with a variety of hair types and lengths, making it a great choice for many men.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the men's hairstyle long on top short on sides, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The length of your hair on the top of your head should be longer than the sides and back. The exact length will depend on your personal preference and hair type. If you have thick hair, you may want to keep it longer on top to avoid a bulky look. If you have thin hair, you may want to keep it shorter on top to create the illusion of thickness.

Step 2: Get a Professional Haircut

To achieve the perfect men's hairstyle long on top short on sides, it's important to get a professional haircut. Your stylist will be able to create a balanced look that suits your face shape and hair type. Make sure to communicate your preferences and ask for advice if you're not sure what length or style will work best for you.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you've got your haircut, it's time to style your hair. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair and use your fingers to create a textured, messy look. You can also use a comb or brush to create a more polished look. Experiment with different styles until you find one that suits you.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

To keep your men's hairstyle long on top short on sides looking great, follow these tips:

- Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and length of your hair. - Use a high-quality styling product that works for your hair type. - Consider getting a fade or undercut to add some variation to your look. - Experiment with different styles and accessories, such as hats or headbands, to keep your look fresh and interesting.

FAQs

Q: Is the men's hairstyle long on top short on sides suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be adapted to suit any face shape. Your stylist will be able to create a look that complements your features.

Q: Can I achieve this look with curly hair?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can work well with curly hair. Just make sure to keep the length on top longer to avoid a bulky look.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut to maintain this look?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

- Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. - Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, too often. - Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or sunscreen. - Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals that support healthy hair growth.

The men's hairstyle long on top short on sides is a timeless look that can be adapted to suit any personal style. Follow our guide to achieve the perfect look and keep your hair healthy and looking great.

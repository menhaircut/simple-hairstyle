Shoulder Length Haircuts For Oblong Faces

Written by William Oct 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect haircut for your oblong face? Look no further! In this guide, we will be discussing the best shoulder length haircuts for oblong faces. We will cover everything from how to determine if you have an oblong face to tips on how to care for your new haircut.

What is an Oblong Face?

An oblong face is longer than it is wide and has a straight, elongated jawline. If you have an oblong face, you may also have a high forehead and a narrow chin. Some celebrities with oblong faces include Sarah Jessica Parker, Liv Tyler, and Ryan Gosling.

How to Determine if You Have an Oblong Face

If you're unsure whether you have an oblong face or not, there are a few key features to look out for:

  • A face that is longer than it is wide
  • A high forehead
  • A straight, elongated jawline

If you have these features, congratulations! You have an oblong face.

Best Shoulder Length Haircuts for Oblong Faces

Now that you know you have an oblong face, it's time to find the perfect shoulder length haircut. Here are a few options:

Long Bob

The long bob, also known as the "lob," is a great haircut for oblong faces. It falls just above or below the shoulders and adds volume to the sides of your face, making it appear wider. It also draws attention to your collarbone, which can be a great feature for those with oblong faces.

Layered Shoulder Length Haircut

A layered shoulder length haircut is another great option for oblong faces. The layers add volume to the sides of your face, making it appear wider. This style also works well with natural waves or curls, which can add even more volume and texture.

Blunt Shoulder Length Haircut

A blunt shoulder length haircut is a classic and chic option for oblong faces. This style falls straight across the shoulders and adds width to the sides of your face. It also draws attention to your collarbone, which can be a great feature for those with oblong faces.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Haircuts

Now that you've found the perfect shoulder length haircut for your oblong face, it's important to know how to care for it. Here are a few tips:

  • Regular trims: To keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy, make sure to get regular trims every 6-8 weeks.
  • Use heat protectant: If you use heat tools on your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.
  • Deep condition: Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQ

What other hairstyles work well for oblong faces?

In addition to shoulder length haircuts, other hairstyles that work well for oblong faces include long layers, side-swept bangs, and pixie cuts.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

Absolutely! Just make sure to add some volume to the sides of your face to balance out the length.

Conclusion

Now that you have all the information you need, it's time to find the perfect shoulder length haircut for your oblong face. Whether you choose a long bob, layered haircut, or blunt cut, make sure to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

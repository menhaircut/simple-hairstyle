How To Have Long Messy Hair Guys The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Nov 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy looking for a low-maintenance yet stylish hairstyle, you might want to consider growing out your hair and embracing the messy look. Long messy hair can give you a carefree and effortless vibe, and it's perfect for those who want to avoid spending too much time on grooming. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to have long messy hair guys, from growing it out to styling it. Read on to find out more!

Table of Content

  • How to Grow Out Your Hair
  • How to Style Long Messy Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Long Messy Hair
  • FAQ

How to Grow Out Your Hair

If you're starting from a short haircut, growing out your hair can take some time and patience. Here are some tips to help you along the way:

1. Have a plan

Decide on the length and style you want to achieve, and stick to it. This will help you avoid getting frustrated with the process and giving up halfway.

2. Be patient

Depending on your hair type and growth rate, it can take several months or even a year to grow out your hair. Don't get discouraged if it seems like it's taking too long.

3. Get regular trims

While it may seem counterintuitive, getting regular trims can actually help your hair grow faster and healthier. This is because split ends can cause breakage, which slows down the growth process.

How to Style Long Messy Hair

Once you've grown out your hair to your desired length, it's time to start styling it into a messy look. Here are some tips:

1. Use the right products

For a messy hairstyle, you'll want to use products that add texture and definition without weighing down your hair. Some good options include sea salt spray, texturizing paste, and hair wax.

2. Embrace the natural look

Don't worry too much about making your hair look perfect. The beauty of a messy hairstyle is in its imperfections. Let your hair fall naturally and embrace the relaxed vibe.

3. Experiment with different styles

There are many ways to style long messy hair, from a tousled bedhead look to a messy bun. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Maintaining Long Messy Hair

While long messy hair requires less maintenance than some other hairstyles, it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Even though messy hair is supposed to look, well, messy, you still want to keep it clean. Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to remove dirt and excess oil.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it harder to achieve a messy look. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible.

3. Protect your hair from the elements

Wind, sun, and other environmental factors can damage your hair and make it harder to style. Protect your hair with a hat or scarf when you're out in the elements.

FAQ

1. Can I have long messy hair if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Long messy hair looks great on all hair types, including curly hair. Just make sure to use products that enhance your curls and keep them defined.

2. Do I need to use conditioner?

Conditioner can help keep your hair soft and manageable, but it's not necessary for everyone. If you have oily hair, you may want to skip conditioner or only use it sparingly.

3. How often should I trim my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it gets damaged. As a general rule, you should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Long Messy Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep your long messy hair looking its best:

1. Brush your hair gently

Long messy hair can easily get tangled, so make sure to brush it gently with a wide-tooth comb or a soft-bristled brush.

2. Use a leave-in conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz. Apply it to damp hair and let it air dry.

3. Eat a healthy diet

Your hair needs nutrients to grow and stay healthy. Make sure to eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the long messy hair look with confidence. Remember to be patient, embrace the natural look, and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Good luck!

How To Cut Hair Short Youtube Video A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and looking for a change? Cutting your hair short is a great way to switch things up and create a fresh new look. But before you grab a pair of scissors and start snipping away, it's important to know the proper technique. Fortunately, there are plenty of YouTube videos available that can guide you through the process. In this article, we'll walk you through how to cut hair short using a YouTube video as your guide.

Description

YouTube is a fantastic resource for learning how to do just about anything, including cutting your hair. There are countless videos available that can guide you through the process, from basic trims to full-on haircuts. By following a YouTube video, you can get a visual guide to help you along the way and ensure that you get the look you want.

How To

First, find a YouTube video that demonstrates the type of haircut you want. Make sure the video is clear and easy to follow, with step-by-step instructions. Watch the video all the way through before you begin cutting your hair, so you have a good understanding of what you need to do. You may want to watch it a few times to make sure you're comfortable with the process.

Once you're ready to start cutting, gather all the tools you'll need. This will typically include a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you begin.

Section your hair into manageable sections using the clips. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, cutting small sections at a time. Be sure to follow the instructions in the video carefully, and take your time to ensure that each section is even.

When you've finished cutting, comb through your hair to check for any uneven spots. If you notice any areas that need adjusting, go back in and trim as needed. Once you're happy with the overall look, give your hair a final comb-through and style as desired.

Step by Step

1. Find a YouTube video that demonstrates the type of haircut you want.

2. Watch the video all the way through before you begin cutting your hair.

3. Gather all the tools you'll need, including a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

4. Section your hair into manageable sections using the clips.

5. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, cutting small sections at a time.

6. Follow the instructions in the video carefully, and take your time to ensure that each section is even.

7. Comb through your hair to check for any uneven spots.

8. If you notice any areas that need adjusting, go back in and trim as needed.

9. Once you're happy with the overall look, give your hair a final comb-through and style as desired.

Tips

- If you're unsure about cutting your hair short, start with a small trim before going for a full-on haircut.

- Always use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.

- If you're cutting your hair for the first time, consider consulting with a professional hairdresser beforehand.

FAQ

Q: Can I really cut my hair using a YouTube video?

A: Yes! YouTube is a great resource for learning how to cut your hair, as long as you choose a clear and easy-to-follow video.

Q: What if I make a mistake?

A: Don't panic! If you make a mistake, you can always go back in and trim the area as needed. Just take your time and be patient.

Q: What if I don't like the way my hair looks?

A: Hair grows back! If you're not happy with the way your hair looks after cutting it, give it time to grow out and try again later.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair short, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. This means using quality hair products, avoiding heat styling tools when possible, and getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. With the right care, your short hair will look great for months to come!

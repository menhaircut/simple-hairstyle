What Hair Colour Says About Personality

Written by Daniel Jul 12, 2023 · 6 min read

Have you ever wondered what your hair colour says about your personality? The shade of your locks can reveal a lot about your character, from your level of confidence to your creativity. In this article, we’ll explore the different hair colours and what they say about your personality.

Table of Contents

  • Blonde Hair
  • Brunette Hair
  • Red Hair
  • Black Hair
  • Grey Hair

Blonde Hair

Blonde hair is often associated with youthfulness, playfulness, and a carefree attitude. This shade of hair can indicate that you are outgoing, confident, and enjoy being the center of attention. People with blonde hair are often seen as bubbly and fun-loving, with a great sense of humor.

However, blonde hair can also be associated with a lack of intelligence or depth. This stereotype is outdated and untrue, but it’s important to be aware of it when choosing to dye your hair blonde.

How to Rock Blonde Hair

If you have blonde hair, embrace your fun-loving personality by wearing bright colours and bold patterns. Play up your sense of humor by wearing statement t-shirts or accessories with funny slogans. Don’t be afraid to be the center of attention!

Tips for Maintaining Blonde Hair

Blonde hair can be prone to brassiness, so invest in a purple shampoo to keep your locks looking bright and fresh. Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and leave it looking dry and damaged.

Brunette Hair

Brunette hair is often associated with intelligence, sophistication, and a grounded personality. This shade of hair can indicate that you are reliable, trustworthy, and down-to-earth. People with brunette hair are often seen as serious and responsible, with a strong work ethic.

However, brunette hair can also be associated with being boring or predictable. This stereotype is also outdated and untrue, but it’s important to be aware of it when choosing to dye your hair brunette.

How to Rock Brunette Hair

If you have brunette hair, embrace your sophistication by wearing classic, timeless pieces in neutral colours. Play up your intelligence by accessorizing with sophisticated jewellery or glasses. Don’t be afraid to take risks and try new things!

Tips for Maintaining Brunette Hair

Brunette hair can fade quickly, so invest in a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your locks looking fresh. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Red Hair

Red hair is often associated with creativity, passion, and a fiery personality. This shade of hair can indicate that you are independent, adventurous, and unafraid to take risks. People with red hair are often seen as unique and bold, with a strong sense of individuality.

However, red hair can also be associated with being hot-headed or temperamental. This stereotype is also outdated and untrue, but it’s important to be aware of it when choosing to dye your hair red.

How to Rock Red Hair

If you have red hair, embrace your creativity by wearing unique, statement pieces that reflect your personality. Play up your passion by accessorizing with bold colours and prints. Don’t be afraid to stand out from the crowd!

Tips for Maintaining Red Hair

Red hair can fade quickly, so invest in a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your locks looking vibrant. Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can cause it to become dry and brittle.

Black Hair

Black hair is often associated with mystery, elegance, and a sophisticated personality. This shade of hair can indicate that you are intelligent, confident, and unafraid to speak your mind. People with black hair are often seen as powerful and in control, with a strong sense of self.

However, black hair can also be associated with being cold or unapproachable. This stereotype is also outdated and untrue, but it’s important to be aware of it when choosing to dye your hair black.

How to Rock Black Hair

If you have black hair, embrace your sophistication by wearing sleek, tailored pieces in classic colours. Play up your intelligence by accessorizing with statement jewellery or glasses. Don’t be afraid to show your softer side!

Tips for Maintaining Black Hair

Black hair can show damage and breakage easily, so invest in a good quality hair oil to keep your locks looking healthy and shiny. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Grey Hair

Grey hair is often associated with wisdom, experience, and a mature personality. This shade of hair can indicate that you are confident, comfortable in your own skin, and unafraid to age gracefully. People with grey hair are often seen as wise and knowledgeable, with a great deal of life experience.

However, grey hair can also be associated with being old or past your prime. This stereotype is also outdated and untrue, but it’s important to be aware of it when choosing to embrace your grey hair.

How to Rock Grey Hair

If you have grey hair, embrace your wisdom by wearing classic, timeless pieces in neutral colours. Play up your experience by accessorizing with sophisticated jewellery or scarves. Don’t be afraid to embrace your age!

Tips for Maintaining Grey Hair

Grey hair can be prone to yellowing, so invest in a purple shampoo to keep your locks looking bright and fresh. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does hair colour really say anything about your personality?
  • While hair colour stereotypes are outdated and often untrue, the shade of your locks can indicate certain personality traits.

  • Can I change my hair colour to change my personality?
  • No, your personality is determined by a variety of factors, including genetics, upbringing, and life experiences.

  • Are hair colour stereotypes always negative?
  • No, some hair colour stereotypes are positive, such as blonde hair being associated with fun and playfulness.

  • Is it bad to dye my hair to fit a certain stereotype?
  • While it’s important to be aware of hair colour stereotypes, it’s more important to choose a hair colour that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Hair Care Tips for All Hair Colours

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your locks.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and leave it looking dry and damaged.

So, there you have it – what your hair colour says about your personality. Remember, while hair colour stereotypes are outdated and often untrue, the shade of your locks can indicate certain personality traits. Choose a hair colour that makes you feel confident and comfortable, and don’t be afraid to embrace your unique personality!

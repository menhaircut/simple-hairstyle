If you have a long face and you're over 60, you might be wondering what hairstyles would suit you best. It's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and complements your personality. In this article, we'll share some tips, ideas and a care guide for hairstyles for long faces over 60.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Long Face

When choosing a hairstyle for your long face, it's important to consider your hair type, lifestyle, and the occasion. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  • Short hairstyles can work well for long faces, but avoid styles that are too short or too severe, as they can make your face look longer.
  • Long, layered hairstyles can help to soften your face and create a more balanced look.
  • Bangs can also help to balance your face, but avoid blunt or straight-cut bangs, as they can make your face look longer.
  • Consider adding volume to the sides of your hair to help balance out your face shape.

It's also important to consider your hair type when choosing a hairstyle. If you have fine or thin hair, avoid heavy layers, as they can make your hair look flat. If you have thick hair, consider a hairstyle that will help to thin it out and make it more manageable.

Hairstyles for Long Faces Over 60

Here are some hairstyles that are perfect for long faces over 60:

  • Layered Bob: This classic hairstyle is perfect for long faces, as the layers help to add volume and dimension to your hair.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs can help to balance out your face and create a more youthful look.
  • Wavy Lob: This shoulder-length hairstyle is perfect for long faces, as the waves help to soften your features.
  • Short Pixie: If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle, a short pixie cut can be a great option.
  • Curly Shag: This layered hairstyle is perfect for those with curly hair, as the layers help to add volume and bounce.

Step-by-Step: How to Get the Perfect Hairstyle for Your Long Face

Here are some steps to follow to get the perfect hairstyle for your long face:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in hairstyles for long faces over 60. They can help you choose a hairstyle that will flatter your features and suit your lifestyle.
  2. Bring pictures of hairstyles that you like to your appointment. This will give your hairstylist a better idea of what you're looking for.
  3. Consider getting a haircut that will help to thin out your hair if you have thick hair. This will make it more manageable and easier to style.
  4. Ask your hairstylist for tips on how to style your hair at home. They can recommend products and tools that will help you achieve the perfect look.
  5. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find the perfect one for you!

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Invest in high-quality hair care products that are specifically designed for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use heat protectant products when styling your hair with heat tools, such as curling irons and flat irons.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage.
  • Consider getting a deep conditioning treatment at least once a month to keep your hair looking healthy and hydrated.

FAQs About Hairstyles for Long Faces Over 60

What are the best hairstyles for long faces over 60?

The best hairstyles for long faces over 60 are layered bobs, side-swept bangs, wavy lobs, short pixie cuts, and curly shags.

What should I consider when choosing a hairstyle for my long face?

You should consider your hair type, lifestyle, and the occasion when choosing a hairstyle for your long face.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Long Faces Over 60

Here are some hair care tips for long faces over 60:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid damaging your hair when brushing it.
  • Consider getting a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and tangling while you sleep.
  • Limit your use of heat tools, such as curling irons and flat irons, to prevent damage to your hair.

Choosing the right hairstyle for your long face is important for creating a flattering and balanced look. By following these tips and ideas, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your personality and lifestyle. Remember to take care of your hair with regular trims and high-quality hair care products to keep it looking healthy and shiny.

How To Do A Concave Haircut Step By Step Guide

Description

A concave haircut is a trendy and stylish haircut that can add volume and movement to your hair. It features layers that are shorter in the back and longer in the front, creating a graduated and curved shape. This haircut is suitable for all hair types and lengths, and it's easy to maintain and style.

How to Do a Concave Haircut

To achieve a perfect concave haircut, you need to follow these simple steps:

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties.
  3. Start at the back of your head and create a guide by cutting a straight line across the nape of your neck.
  4. Using the guide, cut the hair in the back section in a straight line, starting from the nape of your neck and moving towards the crown of your head.
  5. Gradually increase the elevation of your cutting angle as you move towards the crown, creating a curved shape.
  6. Continue cutting the hair in the back section until you reach the desired length.
  7. Release the next section of hair and use the previous section as a guide to cut the hair in the same way.
  8. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair, working your way towards the front.
  9. When you reach the front section, create a guide by pulling the hair straight down and cutting it in a straight line.
  10. Gradually increase the elevation of your cutting angle as you move towards the sides, creating a curved shape.
  11. Blend the front section with the back section by cutting a diagonal line from the shortest point in the back to the longest point in the front.
  12. Check for any unevenness or gaps and trim them as necessary.
  13. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for a Perfect Concave Haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure clean and precise cuts.
  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before cutting to avoid any surprises.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use a comb to help guide your cutting angle and ensure a smooth and even cut.
  • Take breaks and step back to assess your progress and make sure you're on track.

FAQ:

  • Q: Who can get a concave haircut?
  • A: A concave haircut is suitable for all hair types and lengths.
  • Q: How often should I get a concave haircut?
  • A: It depends on your hair growth and desired length, but generally every 6-8 weeks is recommended to maintain the shape and style.
  • Q: Can I style my concave haircut in different ways?
  • A: Yes, a concave haircut is versatile and can be styled in many ways, including straight, wavy, curly, or updo styles.

Hair Care after a Concave Haircut:

To keep your concave haircut looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.
  • Use hair products, such as mousse or hairspray, to add volume and hold to your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

Now that you know how to do a concave haircut, you can confidently try this stylish and trendy haircut at home or at the salon. With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a perfect concave shape that will add volume and movement to your hair.

How To Make My Hair Curly Again After Straightening

Written by Oliver Sep 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you miss your curly hair after straightening it? Don't worry, you can still get your curls back. In this article, we will guide you on how to make your hair curly again after straightening. Read on to know-how.

Description

There are times when we want to switch up our hairstyle, and straightening our hair is one of the most popular ways to do so. However, after a few days, you might want your curls back. The good news is that there are several ways to make your hair curly again after straightening it. Whether you have natural hair or relaxed hair, we have some tips that will work for you.

How to Make Your Hair Curly Again After Straightening

Here are some ways to make your hair curly again after straightening:

1. Use Curl-Enhancing Products

You can use curl-enhancing products such as curl creams, gels, and mousse to make your hair curly again. Apply the product to your damp hair and scrunch it with your fingers. Then, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry it.

Tips: Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type.

2. Use Flexi Rods

You can use flexi rods to create curls without using heat. Wrap small sections of your hair around the rod and secure it with a clip. Leave the rods in your hair overnight or until your hair is completely dry. Then, remove the rods, and you will have beautiful curls.

Tips: Use smaller rods for tighter curls and larger rods for looser curls.

3. Use Rollers

You can also use rollers to create curls without using heat. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair and then wrap small sections of hair around the roller. Secure the roller with a clip and let your hair dry completely. Remove the rollers, and you will have beautiful curls.

Tips: Use smaller rollers for tighter curls and larger rollers for looser curls.

4. Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair is another way to create curls without using heat. Divide your hair into small sections and braid each section. Leave the braids in your hair overnight or until your hair is completely dry. Then, remove the braids, and you will have beautiful curls.

Tips: The smaller the braids, the tighter the curls.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prepare it for styling.

Step 2: Apply Curl-Enhancing Products

Apply a curl-enhancing product such as curl cream or mousse to your damp hair. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your fingers to encourage curls to form. You can also use a microfiber towel to scrunch your hair.

Step 4: Use Flexi Rods

Wrap small sections of your hair around the flexi rods and secure them with a clip. Leave the rods in your hair overnight or until your hair is completely dry.

Step 5: Remove the Flexi Rods

Remove the flexi rods, and you will have beautiful curls. Use your fingers to fluff out the curls and create volume.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Do not use too much product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Do not use too much heat as it can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair at night by using a satin or silk bonnet or pillowcase.

FAQ

Can I Use Heat to Make My Hair Curly Again?

Yes, you can use heat to make your hair curly again, but it is not recommended as it can damage your hair. If you choose to use heat, use a heat protectant and a low heat setting.

How Long Will the Curls Last?

The longevity of the curls depends on your hair type and the method you used to create the curls. Generally, the curls can last from a few days to a week.

Can I Make My Hair Curly Again After Relaxing It?

Yes, you can make your hair curly again after relaxing it. However, it is recommended to wait for at least six weeks after relaxing your hair before using any heat or chemical treatments.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, it is important to follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water.

Now that you know how to make your hair curly again after straightening, you can switch up your hairstyle whenever you want. Try these tips and see which one works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty.

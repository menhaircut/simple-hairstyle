How To Thin Long Hair With Regular Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Thinning long hair can give it more movement and texture, making it easier to style. However, going to a salon to have your hair thinned can be expensive. If you're looking for a cost-effective way to thin your long hair, you can do it yourself with regular scissors.

Description

Thinning hair involves removing some of the bulk from your hair without altering its length. This technique is perfect for people with thick hair who want to reduce its weight and add more volume to their hair. The thinning process involves using regular scissors to cut some strands of hair shorter than others, creating a layered effect.

How to Thin Long Hair with Regular Scissors

If you want to thin your long hair with regular scissors, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Comb it to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair and securing it with a clip.

Step 3: Cut the Hair in the First Section

Take a small section of hair from the first section and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut some of the hair at an angle, making sure that the cut is not too harsh.

Step 4: Repeat the Process

Move on to the next section of hair and repeat the process. Continue until you have thinned out all the sections of your hair.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you have thinned out your hair, take a look at your work in the mirror. Make sure that the thickness of your hair is even and that there are no visible choppy areas.

Tips

If you're new to thinning hair, here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start by cutting a little bit of hair at a time. It's better to cut too little than too much.
  • Use professional hair cutting scissors if possible. They are sharper and will make the process easier.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair can be deceptive, and you may end up cutting more than you intended.
  • Take breaks in between sections to avoid cramping your hands.

FAQ

Can I use regular scissors to thin my hair?

Yes, you can use regular scissors to thin your hair. However, professional hair cutting scissors are sharper and will give you better results.

How much hair should I cut when thinning my hair?

Start by cutting a little bit of hair at a time. It's better to cut too little than too much. You can always go back and cut more if needed.

Can thinning hair damage it?

Thinning hair does not damage it. However, cutting too much hair or using the wrong technique can cause damage.

Hair Care

After you have thinned your hair, make sure to take good care of it. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can dry out your hair and cause damage.

Thinning long hair with regular scissors is a cost-effective and easy way to add more movement and texture to your hair. By following these simple steps and tips, you can achieve professional-looking results without leaving your home.

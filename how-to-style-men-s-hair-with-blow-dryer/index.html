How To Style Men s Hair With Blow Dryer A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 20, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle every day? Do you want to switch things up and try something new? One way to achieve a different look is by using a blow dryer to style your hair. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps on how to style men's hair with a blow dryer, as well as provide tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Men's Hair with Blow Dryer
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Men's Hair with Blow Dryer

Styling men's hair with a blow dryer can be a game-changer for your look. Not only can it add volume and texture, but it can also help you achieve a specific style that might not be possible with just a comb or brush. Here are the basic steps to follow when styling men's hair with a blow dryer:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair with a blow dryer, you need to make sure it is clean and conditioned. This will help your hair look and feel its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, and make sure to rinse thoroughly.

2. Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, use a towel to gently pat it dry. You don't want to rub your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz. Instead, use the towel to soak up excess water and leave your hair slightly damp.

3. Apply Styling Product

Once your hair is towel-dried, it's time to apply your styling product. This could be a mousse, gel, or pomade, depending on your hair type and the look you are trying to achieve. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to work it in from root to tip.

4. Use a Heat Protectant

Before you start blow-drying your hair, it's important to use a heat protectant. This will help prevent damage to your hair from the heat of the blow dryer. Spray the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, making sure to cover all areas.

5. Blow Dry Your Hair

Now it's time to start blow-drying your hair. Use a medium heat setting and a round brush to style your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the tips, using the brush to lift and shape your hair as you go. You can also use your fingers to tousle your hair and create texture.

6. Finish with a Cold Blast

Once you have styled your hair with the blow dryer, finish with a cold blast of air. This will help set your style in place and make it last longer. Use the cool shot button on your blow dryer to blast your hair with cold air for a few seconds.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to style men's hair with a blow dryer:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, making sure to work it in from root to tip. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a Heat Protectant

Spray a heat protectant onto your hair to prevent damage from the blow dryer.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer with a medium heat setting and a round brush to style your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the tips, using the brush to lift and shape your hair as you go.

Step 5: Finish with a Cold Blast

Finish with a cold blast of air to set your style in place.

Tips for Styling Men's Hair with Blow Dryer

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling men's hair with a blow dryer:

  • Use a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don't use too much heat, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a cold blast of air to set your style in place.

FAQs

Q: Can blow-drying your hair every day cause damage?

A: Yes, blow-drying your hair every day can cause damage if you use too much heat or don't use a heat protectant. It's best to limit the amount of heat styling you do to your hair and use a heat protectant when you do.

Q: What type of brush should I use when blow-drying my hair?

A: A round brush is best for blow-drying your hair, as it can help create volume and shape.

Hair Care for Men's Hair

When it comes to hair care for men, it's important to use products that are tailored to your hair type and style. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking and feeling its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

By following these tips and using a blow dryer to style your hair, you can achieve a variety of different looks and switch things up from time to time. Just remember to take care of your hair and use products that are suited to your hair type and style.

Is Pixie Cut Good For Long Face

Written by Frank Nov 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a long face and you're considering a pixie cut, you might be wondering whether it's a good idea or not. Pixie cuts are a popular hairstyle that can be a great choice for women with all face shapes, but will it suit those with long faces?

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is usually cut close to the head with layers or bangs. It's a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. Pixie cuts can be a great option for women with long faces because they help to balance out the face shape and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

How to

If you want to try a pixie cut but you're not sure how to get started, here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

Step by Step

  1. Find inspiration: Look for pictures of pixie cuts that you like and bring them to your hairstylist.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist: Ask your hairstylist if a pixie cut would suit your face shape and discuss the different styles available.
  3. Choose a style: Decide on a style that you like and that will suit your face shape.
  4. Make the cut: Get your hair cut into a pixie style by a professional hairstylist.
  5. Style your hair: Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find a look that you love.

Tips

If you're considering a pixie cut for your long face, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a style that will balance out your face shape, such as a pixie cut with layers or bangs.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find a look that suits you.
  • Consider the maintenance involved in keeping a pixie cut looking its best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie cuts for long faces:

Q: Will a pixie cut make my face look longer?
A: No, a pixie cut can actually help to balance out a long face shape by drawing attention to the eyes and cheekbones. Q: What type of pixie cut is best for a long face?
A: A pixie cut with layers or bangs can help to balance out a long face shape. Q: Will a pixie cut work with my hair texture?
A: Pixie cuts can work with many different hair textures, but it's important to talk to your hairstylist about what will work best for your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie cut looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to keep your pixie cut looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find a look that suits you.

In conclusion, a pixie cut can be a great choice for women with long faces. By choosing the right style and experimenting with different styling techniques, you can achieve a look that suits you and complements your face shape. Remember to take care of your hair with regular trims and gentle hair care products to keep your pixie cut looking its best.

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, then look no further than the mens haircuts shaved sides long on top. This popular hairstyle has been around for a while now and has become a go-to style for men who want to look fashionable and edgy. Description

The mens haircuts shaved sides long on top is a hairstyle that features short or shaved sides and a longer top. The length of the top can vary depending on personal preference, but it's typically longer than the sides. This style is often paired with a fade or undercut to create a sharp and clean look. It's a great option for men who want a low-maintenance but trendy haircut.

How to Get This Hairstyle

If you're interested in getting the mens haircuts shaved sides long on top, then you'll need to visit a professional hairstylist. They will be able to give you the perfect haircut based on your face shape and hair type. You can also bring in a picture of the style you want to ensure that you and your stylist are on the same page.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get the mens haircuts shaved sides long on top:

  1. Visit a professional hairstylist
  2. Show them a picture of the style you want
  3. Discuss any concerns or questions you may have
  4. Sit back and let the stylist work their magic
  5. Enjoy your new haircut!

Tips for Maintaining This Hairstyle

The mens haircuts shaved sides long on top is a relatively low-maintenance hairstyle, but there are still a few things you can do to keep it looking its best:

  • Regularly trim the sides to keep them short and clean
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the top healthy and shiny
  • Style the top with a light hold product to keep it in place
  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about the mens haircuts shaved sides long on top:

Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit any face shape. Your hairstylist will be able to recommend the best length and shape for your face.

Can I style this hairstyle differently for different occasions?

Yes, this hairstyle is very versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can slick the top back for a formal occasion or leave it messy for a casual look.

It's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy hair:

How To Cut And Style Short Black Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair can be a bold statement, and it's a popular choice among black women. However, many people are intimidated by the idea of cutting and styling their own hair. Don't worry - with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a chic and stylish look without leaving your home. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to cut and style short black hair.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • How to Cut Short Black Hair
  • How to Style Short Black Hair
  • Tips for Cutting and Styling Short Black Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Black Hair

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting and styling your hair, you'll need a few essential tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clipper or trimmer
  • Comb and brush
  • Hair gel or styling cream
  • Hair dryer

Make sure your tools are clean and sharp to ensure a precise cut.

How to Cut Short Black Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide for cutting short black hair:

  1. Wash your hair and let it dry naturally or use a hair dryer. Make sure your hair is tangle-free.
  2. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start with the bottom section and work your way up.
  3. Use clippers or a trimmer to trim the sides and back of your hair. Use the longest guard setting first and gradually work your way shorter until you reach your desired length.
  4. Use scissors to trim the top of your hair. Take small sections at a time and cut at an angle for a layered look.
  5. Blend the top and sides using scissors or clippers. Make sure the transition looks natural.
  6. Use a comb to check for any uneven areas and trim as needed.
  7. Remove any hair ties or clips and style your hair as desired.

How to Style Short Black Hair

Once you've cut your hair, it's time to style it. Here are some ideas:

  • Slick it back with hair gel or styling cream for a sleek and sophisticated look.
  • Add volume by blow-drying your hair upside down and using a round brush to lift the roots.
  • Create loose waves by using a curling iron or wand. Make sure to use a heat protectant first.
  • Add a pop of color with temporary hair dye or highlights.

Tips for Cutting and Styling Short Black Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting and styling short black hair:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the cut is even.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or family member.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I cut my short black hair?

We recommend getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

Can I cut my own short black hair?

Yes, but it's important to use the right tools and techniques to ensure a precise cut.

What products should I use to style my short black hair?

It depends on your hair type and desired style. Hair gel, styling cream, and hair spray are popular choices.

Hair Care Tips for Short Black Hair

Here are some tips to keep your short black hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or relaxers on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut and style your own short black hair. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!

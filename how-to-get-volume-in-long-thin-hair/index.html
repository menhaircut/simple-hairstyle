How To Get Volume In Long Thin Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having long, thin hair that lacks volume? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many people struggle with this issue, but luckily, there are ways to add volume to your hair. In this guide, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to get volume in long thin hair. From using the right products to trying new hairstyles, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Volume in Long Thin Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

How to Get Volume in Long Thin Hair

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, let's first talk about what causes thin hair. There are many factors that contribute to thin hair, including genetics, hormonal changes, and age. But regardless of the cause, there are ways to add volume to your hair.

The first step is to choose the right products. Look for products that are designed specifically for thin hair, such as volumizing shampoos and conditioners. These products will help to add volume to your hair without weighing it down.

Another great product to use is a volumizing mousse. Apply the mousse to damp hair and then blow dry your hair upside down. This will help to lift your roots and add volume to your hair.

Try a Layered Haircut

If you have long thin hair, consider getting a layered haircut. Layers can add texture and movement to your hair, which can help to create the illusion of volume. Talk to your hairstylist about the best type of layers for your hair type and face shape.

Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair may seem like an outdated technique, but it can actually be an effective way to add volume to your hair. To tease your hair, take a section of hair and backcomb it with a fine-toothed comb. This will create volume at the roots. Just be careful not to tease your hair too much, as this can cause damage.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to add volume to your long thin hair? Follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your damp hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair upside down, using your fingers to lift your roots as you dry.
  4. If you want to add even more volume, tease your hair at the crown.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you get volume in long thin hair:

  • Avoid heavy conditioners, as these can weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Try using a dry shampoo to add volume to your roots.

FAQs

How often should I wash my hair?

This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair every day. If you have dry hair, you may be able to go a few days without washing. Try to find a balance that works for your hair.

Can I use a volumizing spray instead of mousse?

Yes, you can use a volumizing spray instead of mousse. Just be sure to apply it evenly throughout your hair.

Hair Care for Long Thin Hair

In addition to using the right products and techniques, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your long thin hair healthy:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet, as this can help to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider taking a hair growth supplement.

With these tips and tricks, you can add volume to your long thin hair and achieve the full, voluminous look you've always wanted! Remember, it's all about choosing the right products and techniques, and taking good care of your hair.

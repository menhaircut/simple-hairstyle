How To Get A Spiky Afro A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a bold and stylish hairdo? Look no further than a spiky afro! This trendy hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's not hard to see why. With its unique texture and edgy spikes, a spiky afro is sure to turn heads wherever you go. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve this look and answer some common questions about maintenance and care. Let's get started!

How to Get a Spiky Afro

Getting a spiky afro is easier than you might think. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Moisturized Hair

Before you begin styling, you'll want to make sure your hair is clean and moisturized. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and hydrate your hair. Avoid products that contain sulfates or harsh chemicals, as these can dry out your hair and make it harder to style.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. There are many different options to choose from, including gels, pomades, and waxes. Look for a product that provides hold and definition without weighing down your hair. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to distribute it from root to tip.

Step 3: Use a Comb or Brush to Create Spikes

Using a comb or brush, begin to shape your hair into spikes. Depending on the length of your hair, you may need to use your fingers to help create the desired shape. Start at the roots and work your way up to the tips, using gentle pressure to create volume and texture.

Step 4: Set the Style with Heat

Once you've created the desired shape, use a blow dryer or flat iron to set the style. Apply heat evenly throughout your hair, using a low to medium heat setting to avoid damage. You can also use a diffuser attachment to add volume and definition to your spikes.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your spikes in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Look for a product that provides a strong hold without leaving your hair feeling crunchy or stiff. Hold the can about six inches away from your head and spray evenly, making sure to cover all of your spikes.

Tips for Maintaining a Spiky Afro

Once you've achieved the perfect spiky afro, you'll want to take steps to maintain your look. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it harder to style
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a spiky afro last?

A spiky afro can last anywhere from a few hours to a full day, depending on the products and techniques used to create it. To extend the life of your style, consider using a strong-hold hairspray and avoiding activities that could flatten your spikes (like wearing a hat).

Is a spiky afro suitable for all hair types?

While a spiky afro can be achieved with many different hair types, it may be more challenging for those with very fine or thin hair. In these cases, you may need to use additional products (like volumizing mousse) to achieve the desired shape and texture.

Can I create a spiky afro without heat?

Yes! While heat can help to set your spikes and add volume, it's possible to create a spiky afro without using any heat tools. Simply apply your styling product of choice to damp hair, use a comb or brush to create spikes, and allow your hair to air-dry. This method may take longer to achieve the desired look, but it's a great option for those who want to avoid heat damage.

Hair Care for a Spiky Afro

When it comes to caring for a spiky afro, it's important to use products that will keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for curly or textured hair, as these products will help to prevent dryness and breakage. You may also want to invest in a weekly deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair soft and manageable.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve a bold and stylish spiky afro in no time. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking and feeling its best!

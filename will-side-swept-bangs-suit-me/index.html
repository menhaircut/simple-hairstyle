Will Side Swept Bangs Suit Me A Guide To Find Out

Written by Christine Aug 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Side swept bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add a touch of elegance and sophistication to your look. However, before you decide to cut your hair, you may be wondering, "will side swept bangs suit me?" In this article, we will guide you through the process of determining whether side swept bangs will work for your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Description

Side swept bangs are a type of fringe that falls diagonally across your forehead, rather than straight across. This style can create a soft, feminine look, and can help to frame your face and accentuate your features. Side swept bangs come in various lengths and thicknesses, and can be customized to suit your individual preferences.

How to Determine if Side Swept Bangs Will Suit You

Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape

The first step in determining whether side swept bangs will suit you is to consider your face shape. Generally, side swept bangs are most flattering on those with oval, round, or heart-shaped faces. If you have a square or diamond-shaped face, you may want to opt for a different style of bangs.

Step 2: Assess Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also play a role in whether side swept bangs will suit you. If you have fine, straight hair, side swept bangs can add volume and movement to your look. However, if you have thick or curly hair, you may need to spend more time styling your bangs to get them to lay flat.

Step 3: Consider Your Personal Style

Lastly, it's important to consider your personal style when deciding whether side swept bangs will suit you. If you prefer a more low-maintenance look, you may want to avoid bangs altogether. However, if you enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles and are willing to put in the effort to style your bangs, side swept bangs may be a great option for you.

Tips for Styling Side Swept Bangs

If you've decided that side swept bangs are right for you, here are some tips for styling them:

  • Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz.
  • Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or hairspray, to keep your bangs in place.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your bangs.

FAQ

Q: Will side swept bangs make my face look wider?

A: No, side swept bangs can actually help to narrow the appearance of your face by creating a diagonal line that draws attention away from the width of your forehead.

Q: Can I still wear a center part with side swept bangs?

A: Yes, you can still wear a center part with side swept bangs. However, you may need to spend more time styling your hair to ensure that your bangs blend in seamlessly with the rest of your hair.

Hair Care Tips

When you have side swept bangs, it's important to take extra care of your hair to ensure that your bangs stay healthy and well-maintained. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup on your scalp.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent split ends.

Overall, side swept bangs can be a great way to update your look and add some personality to your hairstyle. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you can determine whether side swept bangs will suit you. With the right styling techniques and hair care routine, you can rock this trendy hairstyle with confidence!

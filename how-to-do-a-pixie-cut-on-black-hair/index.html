How To Do A Pixie Cut On Black Hair

Written by Frank Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Pixie cuts are a popular hairstyle that has been around for decades. They are a short, edgy haircut that is perfect for those who want to make a statement. The pixie cut looks great on all hair types, including black hair. If you are considering getting a pixie cut on your black hair, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss how to do a pixie cut on black hair, step by step.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is usually cut above the ears and around the nape of the neck. It is a low-maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for those who want to save time on styling their hair. Pixie cuts are also a great way to show off your facial features and bone structure. Black hair is versatile and can be styled in many ways, including a pixie cut.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and conditioning it. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use the comb to section your hair into four parts - two on the sides and two in the back. Clip each section out of the way.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start by cutting the hair at the nape of your neck. Use the scissors to cut the hair in a straight line. Move up to the sides and cut the hair in a diagonal line towards the back.

Step 4: Cut the Top Layer

Once you have cut the sides and back, move on to the top layer. Cut the hair in a diagonal line towards the front.

Step 5: Check for Balance

Check to make sure that the hair on both sides is balanced. If not, make adjustments until it is even.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it however you like. You can use a straightener or curling iron to add some texture.

Tips

- Start with a longer length and gradually cut it shorter until you achieve your desired length.

- Use hair clips to keep the sections of your hair separated.

- Cut small sections at a time to ensure precision.

FAQ

How often should I get a pixie cut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, you should get a pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length.

What face shape is best for a pixie cut?

Pixie cuts look great on all face shapes, but they are especially flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Can I color my hair after getting a pixie cut?

Yes, you can color your hair after getting a pixie cut. However, it is recommended to wait a few weeks before coloring to allow your hair to heal.

Hair Care

To maintain your pixie cut, it is important to use the right hair care products. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

In conclusion, a pixie cut is a great hairstyle for those who want a low-maintenance, edgy look. If you have black hair, you can easily achieve a pixie cut by following these simple steps. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right hair care products and getting regular trims.

How To Cut Long Curly Hair Mens A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Nov 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Cutting long curly hair can be a daunting task for many men. But with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily and effectively. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut long curly hair mens, step by step.

Description

Long curly hair is a popular hairstyle among men. However, it can be difficult to manage and style. To keep your long curly hair looking great, you need to cut it regularly. A good haircut can make a huge difference in how your hair looks and feels. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut long curly hair mens and give you some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great.

How to Cut Long Curly Hair Mens

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clippers. You may also want to invest in a hair cutting cape to keep hair off your clothes.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these step-by-step instructions to cut your long curly hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it. Wet hair is easier to cut and style.
  2. Comb your hair and separate it into sections. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.
  3. Start cutting your hair from the bottom up. Use your hair clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.
  4. Use your hair cutting scissors to trim the ends of your hair. Cut small sections at a time and make sure you’re cutting evenly.
  5. Take small sections of hair and twist them. Use your hair cutting scissors to trim any ends that stick out.
  6. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way up to the top of your head.
  7. Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, use your hair clippers to trim any stray hairs around your neck and ears.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Long Curly Hair Mens

Here are some tips for cutting long curly hair:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more hair, but you can’t put it back once it’s cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while you’re cutting.
  • Be patient. Cutting curly hair takes time and precision.
  • Consider going to a professional if you’re not confident in your ability to cut your own hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long curly hair mens:

Q: How often should I cut my long curly hair?

A: It’s recommended to cut long curly hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and fresh.

Q: Can I cut my own long curly hair?

A: Yes, you can cut your own long curly hair. However, it’s important to use the right tools and techniques to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

A: If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don’t panic. You can always go to a professional to fix it.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair Mens

Here are some tips for keeping your long curly hair healthy:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know how to cut long curly hair mens, you can keep your hair looking great all year round. Remember to use the right tools and techniques, and don’t be afraid to ask for help if you need it.

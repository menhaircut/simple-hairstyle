How To Cut Your Hair Into A Point

Written by Eveline Aug 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our hair cutting tutorial on how to cut your hair into a point. This is a trendy style that is becoming increasingly popular. The point is a unique style that can be done on any hair length. Keep reading to learn how to achieve this look.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The point is a haircut that has a sharp point at the back of your head. It is a style that can be done on any hair length, including short, medium, and long hair. The point is a trendy look that is perfect for those who want to add a unique touch to their hair.

How to

To cut your hair into a point, you will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clippers. If you are not confident in your hair cutting skills, we recommend that you visit a professional hair stylist.

Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Wash your hair and let it dry naturally. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Create a Middle Parting

Use a comb to create a middle parting down the back of your head. Make sure the parting is straight.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into two sections by creating a parting from your ears to the back of your head. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Section

Starting at the nape of your neck, take a small section of hair and cut it straight across. Continue to cut the middle section until you reach the top of your head.

Step 5: Cut the Side Sections

Starting at the nape of your neck, cut a small section of hair at an angle towards the middle section. Repeat this process on both sides until you reach the top of your head.

Step 6: Blend Your Hair

Use hair clippers to blend your hair on the sides and back. Make sure you blend your hair evenly to create a seamless look.

Step 7: Create the Point

Starting at the top of your head, take a small section of hair and cut it at an angle towards the middle section. Keep cutting your hair at an angle until you reach the nape of your neck. Make sure the point is sharp and defined.

Tips

  • Use hair cutting scissors that are sharp and designed for cutting hair.
  • Make sure you cut your hair in a well-lit area.
  • Start with small sections of hair and cut slowly to avoid making mistakes.
  • Always ask for help if you are not confident in your hair cutting skills.

FAQ

Can the point be done on any hair length?

Yes, the point can be done on any hair length, including short, medium, and long hair.

Do I need to use hair clippers?

Using hair clippers is recommended to blend your hair and create a seamless look. However, if you are not comfortable using clippers, you can use scissors instead.

Can I cut my hair into a point at home?

Yes, you can cut your hair into a point at home. However, if you are not confident in your hair cutting skills, we recommend that you visit a professional hair stylist.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair into a point, it is important to take good care of your hair to maintain the style. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. Regular trims are also important to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Cutting your hair into a point is a trendy style that is perfect for those who want to add a unique touch to their hair. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve this look at home. Remember to take good care of your hair to maintain the style and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

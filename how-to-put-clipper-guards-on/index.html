How To Put Clipper Guards On Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Clipper guards are essential tools for anyone who wants to trim their hair at home. They come in different sizes and can be used to achieve different hair lengths. However, putting clipper guards on can be a bit tricky, especially if you're new to hair cutting. In this guide, we'll show you how to put clipper guards on in a few easy steps.

How to Put Clipper Guards On: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Get Your Clippers Ready

Before you start, make sure your clippers are clean and oiled. This will ensure that they work smoothly and don't pull or tug at your hair. Also, make sure that the blade is properly aligned and tightened.

Step 2: Choose Your Guard Size

Clipper guards come in different sizes, usually numbered from 1 to 8. Each number corresponds to a specific hair length, with 1 being the shortest and 8 being the longest. Choose the guard size that corresponds to the hair length you want to achieve.

Step 3: Attach the Guard

Clipper guards are designed to fit over the blades of your clippers. To attach the guard, simply slide it onto the blade until it clicks into place. Make sure the guard is securely attached before you start cutting.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Once the guard is attached, you can start cutting your hair. Hold the clippers at a 90-degree angle to your head and move them in the direction of hair growth. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using long, smooth strokes. Be careful not to apply too much pressure, as this can cause the clippers to pull or tug at your hair.

Step 5: Clean Up

Once you've finished cutting your hair, clean up any stray hairs with a comb and a pair of scissors. You can also use the clippers without a guard to trim around your ears and neckline.

Tips for Putting Clipper Guards On

Here are some tips to help you put clipper guards on like a pro:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting.
  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure you're cutting evenly.
  • Be patient and take your time. Rushing can lead to uneven cuts and mistakes.

FAQs

Can I use clipper guards on any type of hair?

Clipper guards can be used on most hair types, including straight, curly, and thick hair. However, if you have very fine or thin hair, you should be careful not to apply too much pressure, as this can cause the clippers to pull or tug at your hair.

Can I use clipper guards to cut my own hair?

Yes, clipper guards are designed to be used by anyone, including those who want to cut their own hair at home. However, if you're new to hair cutting, it's a good idea to start with a longer guard size and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Hair Care After Using Clipper Guards

After using clipper guards, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Conclusion

Putting clipper guards on is a simple process that anyone can do at home. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without spending a lot of money at a salon. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and take care of your hair after using clipper guards.

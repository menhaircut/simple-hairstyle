How To Get Rid Of Red Hair Tones At Home A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have red hair tones that you want to get rid of? Whether you dyed your hair and it turned out too red, or your natural hair has red undertones that you want to tone down, this guide will help you achieve your desired hair color at home.

Description

Red hair tones can be stubborn to get rid of, but it's not impossible. This guide will provide you with step-by-step instructions, helpful tips, and useful information on how to remove red hair tones at home.

How to Get Rid of Red Hair Tones at Home

There are several ways to get rid of red hair tones at home, including using hair color removers, toners, and natural remedies. Here are some of the most effective methods:

Hair Color Removers

Hair color removers are specifically designed to remove artificial hair color, including red tones. They work by breaking down the color molecules in your hair and allowing them to be washed out. Some popular hair color removers include Color Oops, L'Oreal Effasol Color Remover, and Joico Color Intensity Eraser.

Toners

Toners are usually used to correct unwanted hair tones, such as yellow or orange. However, toners can also be used to neutralize red tones. Purple toners are particularly effective at neutralizing red and orange tones. Simply apply the toner to your hair and leave it on for the recommended amount of time.

Natural Remedies

If you prefer natural remedies, there are several options that can help reduce red hair tones. Chamomile tea, for example, can lighten hair and reduce redness. Simply brew a strong cup of chamomile tea, let it cool, and rinse your hair with it. Another option is to mix equal parts of apple cider vinegar and water and use it as a rinse after shampooing your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to get rid of red hair tones at home:

  1. Choose a hair color remover, toner, or natural remedy that suits your hair type and color.
  2. Read the instructions carefully and follow them exactly.
  3. Protect your skin and clothing by wearing gloves and an old shirt.
  4. Apply the product to your hair, making sure to cover all areas evenly.
  5. Leave the product on for the recommended amount of time.
  6. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  7. If you used a hair color remover, wait at least 24 hours before applying a new hair color.
  8. If you used a toner or natural remedy, repeat the process until you achieve your desired hair color.

Tips

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind when getting rid of red hair tones at home:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo before applying any hair color remover or toner.
  • If you have sensitive skin, do a patch test before using any product on your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when rinsing your hair, as it can open up the hair cuticle and cause the color to fade faster.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment after using a hair color remover or toner to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your new hair color.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting rid of red hair tones at home:

Can I use a hair color remover on natural red hair?

Yes, you can use a hair color remover on natural red hair. However, keep in mind that it may not completely remove the red tones and may result in a lighter, orangey color.

How often can I use a toner to get rid of red hair tones?

It's best to wait at least a week between toning sessions to avoid damaging your hair.

Can I use lemon juice to get rid of red hair tones?

Lemon juice can lighten hair, but it's not recommended for removing red hair tones as it can cause your hair to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after getting rid of red hair tones at home. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and methods, you can easily get rid of red hair tones at home without damaging your hair. Remember to always follow the instructions carefully and take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine.

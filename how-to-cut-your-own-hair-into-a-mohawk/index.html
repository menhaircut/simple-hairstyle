How To Cut Your Own Hair Into A Mohawk

Written by Robby Nov 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to get a new, edgy haircut but don't want to spend a fortune at the salon? Learning how to cut your own hair into a mohawk may be just the solution you need. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know to create a perfect mohawk haircut at home.

Description

A mohawk is a hairstyle that involves having a strip of hair down the center of your head, with the rest of the hair shaved or cut short. Mohawks can be styled in a variety of ways, from a tall, spiky look to a more subtle, understated style. This haircut has been popular for decades and has remained a favorite among people looking to make a strong fashion statement.

How to Cut Your Own Hair into a Mohawk

Before you start, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it dry completely. This will make it easier to work with and ensure a cleaner cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section off the hair you want to leave in the center of your head. Make sure it's straight and even, and use hair clips to keep it in place.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Using hair clippers, shave the sides of your head to the desired length. You can go for a short buzz cut or leave a little more length, depending on your preference.

Step 4: Cut the Top

With the center section of hair still clipped in place, use scissors to trim the top to the desired length. You can go for a shorter, spikier look or leave some length for a more subtle style.

Step 5: Style Your Mohawk

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's time to style your mohawk. Use styling gel or pomade to create the desired shape and hold your hair in place. You can also use hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

Cutting your own hair can be tricky, so here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more off until you achieve the desired style.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from all angles.
  • Take your time and be patient. Rushing can lead to mistakes.
  • Ask a friend to help if you're not confident doing it alone.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair into a mohawk if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can. However, it may be more difficult to achieve a straight, even line down the center of your head. Consider seeking the help of a professional stylist if you're unsure.

How often should I cut my mohawk?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the style loses its shape. Generally, you'll need to trim the sides every 2-3 weeks and the top every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your mohawk looking its best, make sure to take good care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid excessive heat styling or chemical treatments. Regular trims will also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Learning how to cut your own hair into a mohawk can be a fun and rewarding experience. With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a bold and stylish look that's all your own. Give it a try today!

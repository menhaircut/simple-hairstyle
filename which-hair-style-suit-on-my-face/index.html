Which Hair Style Suit On My Face

Welcome to our ultimate guide on choosing the perfect hairstyle that will perfectly suit your face shape! We understand that it can be a daunting task to choose the perfect hairstyle that will flatter your facial features. In this blog post, we will be discussing how to identify your face shape, the hairstyles that will suit your face shape, and hair care tips to maintain your new hairstyle.

  • Identifying Your Face Shape
  • Hairstyles for Your Face Shape

Identifying Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the perfect hairstyle is to identify your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To identify your face shape, you can stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face using a lipstick or an eyeliner pencil. You can also take a photo of yourself and trace the outline of your face on the photo.

Once you have identified your face shape, you can now move on to choosing the perfect hairstyle that will flatter your facial features.

Hairstyles for Your Face Shape

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky because you can pull off almost any hairstyle. You can try a pixie cut, a bob, or long layers. Avoid hairstyles with too much volume on top as it can make your face look longer.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you can try a long bob or a pixie cut with volume on top. Avoid blunt bangs and one-length haircuts as they can make your face look rounder.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you can try a layered haircut with soft, wavy curls. Avoid straight, one-length haircuts as they can make your face look boxy.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you can try a side-swept bangs or a long bob. Avoid short haircuts and heavy, blunt bangs as they can make your forehead look wider.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you can try a bob with side-swept bangs or a layered haircut. Avoid hairstyles with too much volume on the top and the sides as it can make your face look wider.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, you can try a long bob or a layered haircut with soft waves. Avoid straight, one-length haircuts as they can make your face look longer.

Tips for Your New Hairstyle

Now that you have chosen the perfect hairstyle that will suit your face shape, here are some tips to make sure that your hairstyle looks great:

  • Bring a photo of the hairstyle that you want to your hairstylist.
  • Ask your hairstylist for their opinion on whether the hairstyle will suit your face shape.
  • Be open to suggestions from your hairstylist.
  • Choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have your new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its healthy and shiny look:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

FAQs

Can I pull off a short haircut if I have a round face shape?

Yes, you can try a pixie cut or a bob with volume on top to elongate your face. Just make sure to avoid blunt bangs and one-length haircuts.

What is the best haircut for an oval face shape?

You can try a pixie cut, a bob, or long layers. Avoid hairstyles with too much volume on top as it can make your face look longer.

Can I change my hairstyle if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still change your hairstyle even if you have curly hair. Just make sure to choose a hairstyle that will suit your face shape and the texture of your hair.

We hope that this guide has helped you choose the perfect hairstyle that will suit your face shape. Remember, confidence is key to pulling off any hairstyle. Wear your new hairstyle with pride and rock it!

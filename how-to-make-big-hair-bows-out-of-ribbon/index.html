How To Make Big Hair Bows Out Of Ribbon A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our tutorial on how to make big hair bows out of ribbon! Hair bows are a fun accessory that can add a pop of color and style to any outfit. Plus, they're easy to make at home with just a few supplies. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to create your own big hair bows using ribbon. Let's get started!

How to Make Big Hair Bows Out of Ribbon

Here are the supplies you'll need:

  • 2.5 inch wide ribbon
  • Scissors
  • Hot glue gun
  • Needle and thread
  • Hair clip

Step 1: Cut the Ribbon

Start by cutting a piece of ribbon to the desired length. For a big hair bow, we recommend using a length of 18-20 inches.

Step 2: Make the Bow Loops

Take the ribbon and make a loop by bringing the two ends together. The loop should be about 5-6 inches long. Pinch the loop in the middle to create a crease.

Next, make a second loop on the other side of the crease, making sure it's the same size as the first loop. Pinch this loop in the middle as well.

Repeat this process, alternating sides, until you have 4-5 loops on each side.

Step 3: Secure the Bow Loops

Once you have all of your loops, pinch them together in the middle and sew them together with a needle and thread. This will hold the loops in place.

Step 4: Create the Bow Tails

Now it's time to make the bow tails. Cut two pieces of ribbon to the desired length for the tails. We recommend a length of 6-7 inches.

Take one piece of ribbon and fold it in half to create a crease. Then, fold the ends in towards the middle to create a pointy end. Repeat this process with the second piece of ribbon.

Step 5: Attach the Bow Tails

Take one of the bow tails and place it behind the bow loops, with the pointed end facing down. Use a hot glue gun to attach the tail to the back of the bow loops.

Repeat this process with the second bow tail, placing it on top of the first tail and gluing it in place.

Step 6: Add the Hair Clip

Finally, attach a hair clip to the back of the bow. Use a hot glue gun to glue the hair clip to the back of the bow loops and tails.

And there you have it! Your very own big hair bow made out of ribbon.

Tips for Making Big Hair Bows

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you make your big hair bows:

  • Use a high-quality ribbon to ensure your bow looks nice and holds its shape.
  • Experiment with different ribbon textures and patterns for a unique look.
  • Try layering different colors or patterns of ribbon for a more intricate bow.
  • Be careful when using a hot glue gun, as the glue can be hot and cause burns.

FAQ About Making Big Hair Bows

How do I attach the hair clip to the back of the bow?

Use a hot glue gun to glue the hair clip to the back of the bow loops and tails. Be sure to hold the clip in place until the glue dries to ensure it stays securely attached.

What size ribbon should I use for a big hair bow?

We recommend using a 2.5 inch wide ribbon for a big hair bow.

Can I make a big hair bow without a hair clip?

Yes! You can attach the bow to a headband or use it as a decoration on a purse or backpack.

Hair Care Tips When Wearing Big Hair Bows

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when wearing big hair bows:

  • Don't wear a hair bow that's too heavy for your hair, as it can cause damage or breakage.
  • Be gentle when removing the hair bow to avoid pulling or tangling your hair.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.

And that's it! We hope you found this tutorial helpful in making your own big hair bows out of ribbon. Happy crafting!

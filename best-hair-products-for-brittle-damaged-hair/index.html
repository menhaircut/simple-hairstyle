Description

Brittle damaged hair is hair that has been weakened due to over-processing, excessive heat styling, or exposure to harsh chemicals. This type of hair is prone to breakage and split ends, and can be difficult to manage. Fortunately, there are many hair products on the market that can help restore brittle damaged hair to its former glory.

Use products that are specifically designed for brittle damaged hair, and avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates. Apply products evenly throughout your hair, making sure to focus on the ends where damage is most likely to occur.

  • Shampoo: Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and contain ingredients like keratin, biotin, and argan oil. We recommend OGX Renewing + Argan Oil of Morocco Shampoo.

Step-by-Step Guide to Restoring Brittle Damaged Hair

Follow these simple steps to restore your brittle damaged hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo, like OGX Renewing + Argan Oil of Morocco Shampoo.
  2. Apply a deep conditioning treatment, like L'Oreal Paris Elvive Total Repair 5 Damage Erasing Balm, and leave it on for at least 10 minutes.
  3. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water to seal the cuticle and lock in moisture.
  4. Apply a leave-in treatment, like It's a 10 Miracle Leave-In Product, to protect your hair from further damage and promote healthy growth.
  5. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  6. Apply an oil treatment, like MoroccanOil Treatment, to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  7. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, and always use a heat protectant when you do.
  8. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy growth.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

In addition to using the right hair products, there are a few things you can do to maintain healthy hair:

  • Limit your use of heat styling tools, and always use a heat protectant when you do.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, like bleach or hair dyes.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals, like biotin and iron, which are essential for healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage, like tight braids or ponytails.

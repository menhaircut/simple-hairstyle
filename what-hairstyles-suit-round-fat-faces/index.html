What Hairstyles Suit Round Fat Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, one size does not fit all. People with round fat faces often struggle to find hairstyles that flatter their face shape. But worry not, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll share with you some of the best hairstyles that suit round fat faces along with tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round fat faces are characterized by a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded jawline. The goal of a good hairstyle for a round fat face is to create an illusion of length and slim the face. Hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head and have layers around the face are perfect for this face shape.

How To

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's talk about some general tips that will help you choose the right hairstyle for your round fat face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bobs or straight bangs.
  • Opt for hairstyles that have volume on top and are tapered at the chin.
  • Layered hairstyles that frame your face will help create a slimming effect.
  • Avoid center parts and opt for a side part instead.

Step By Step

Here are some of the best hairstyles that suit round fat faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers that start below the chin and frame the face will help create a slimming effect. Layers add movement and texture to your hair, making it look more voluminous.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs that are longer on one side will help create a diagonal line, which will make your face appear longer. Avoid straight bangs that cut across your forehead.

3. Bob with Volume

A bob that is tapered at the chin and has volume at the crown of the head will help create an illusion of length. Avoid blunt bobs that add width to your face.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut with volume on top will help elongate your face. Keep the sides short and the top longer to create height.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are slicked back or tightly pulled back, as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Accessorize your hair with clips or headbands to create height and add interest.
  • Consult with a hairstylist who has experience with round fat faces to find the best hairstyle for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round fat face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight, but make sure to add volume at the crown of the head to create an illusion of length.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round fat face?

A: Yes, but avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as it can make your face look rounder. Opt for a loose, messy ponytail instead.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, voluminous hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can damage the hair and make it appear flat.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Now that you know what hairstyles suit round fat faces, go ahead and try out some of these styles to find the perfect one for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking voluminous and healthy.

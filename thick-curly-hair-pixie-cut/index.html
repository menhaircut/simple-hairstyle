Thick Curly Hair Pixie Cut A Guide For 2023

Written by Frank Oct 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of dealing with your thick curly hair? Do you want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks great? Look no further than the pixie cut! In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about getting a thick curly hair pixie cut, including how to style it, tips for maintenance, and more. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that's perfect for those with thick curly hair. It's a low-maintenance style that's easy to style and looks great on everyone. The key to getting a great pixie cut is finding the right stylist who understands your hair type and can cut it in a way that flatters your face shape. With the right cut, you'll be able to rock your thick curly hair with confidence!

How to

Getting a thick curly hair pixie cut is easy, but you need to know what to ask for when you go to the salon. Here are some tips:

  • Bring pictures of the style you want to the salon.
  • Ask for a stylist who specializes in cutting thick curly hair.
  • Be prepared for your hair to be cut shorter than you're used to.
  • Discuss the best way to style your hair with your stylist.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a thick curly hair pixie cut:

  1. Find a salon and stylist who specializes in cutting thick curly hair.
  2. Bring pictures of the style you want to the salon.
  3. Discuss the best way to style your hair with your stylist.
  4. Get your hair cut to the desired length and style.
  5. Learn how to style your hair at home.
  6. Maintain your pixie cut with regular trims.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your thick curly hair pixie cut:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Invest in a good leave-in conditioner to help tame frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your natural curls.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about thick curly hair pixie cuts:

Will a pixie cut work for my face shape?

A good stylist will be able to cut your hair in a way that flatters your face shape. Be sure to discuss this with your stylist before getting your hair cut.

Will a pixie cut make my hair look even thicker?

Not necessarily. A good stylist will be able to cut your hair in a way that makes it look less bulky and more manageable.

How often do I need to get my pixie cut trimmed?

To maintain your pixie cut, you'll need to get it trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your thick curly hair:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Invest in a good leave-in conditioner to help tame frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your natural curls.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

With this guide, you should be well on your way to achieving the perfect thick curly hair pixie cut. Remember to find a stylist who understands your hair type and to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Good luck!

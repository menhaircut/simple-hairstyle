How To Get The Wet Long Hair Look For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you want to achieve that sleek and sexy wet hair look like your favorite male celebrities? You're in luck because we've got you covered! In this article, we will show you how to get the wet long hair look for guys in just a few easy steps. Read on to learn more.

How to Get the Wet Long Hair Look for Guys

If you want to achieve that hot-off-the-beach, wet and wild look, here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you style your hair, make sure it's squeaky clean. Wash your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it. Don't rub your hair too vigorously to avoid damaging it.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair while it's still damp. Use a styling gel, wax, or pomade to achieve that wet look. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair back. Start from the roots and comb through to the tips.

Step 5: Use a Hair Dryer

Use a hair dryer on low heat to dry your hair while combing it back. Make sure to keep the dryer at a safe distance from your hair to avoid damaging it.

Step 6: Finish with a Hairspray

To keep your hair in place, finish with a light hairspray. Hold the can about 10 inches away from your hair and spray lightly.

Tips for Achieving the Wet Long Hair Look for Guys

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Use a high-quality styling product to achieve that wet look.
  • Don't use too much product as it can make your hair look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Keep the hair dryer at a safe distance from your hair to prevent heat damage.
  • Use a light hairspray to keep your hair in place without making it look stiff.

FAQs about Getting the Wet Long Hair Look for Guys

Here are some common questions about achieving the wet hair look for guys:

Can I achieve the wet hair look without using a hair dryer?

Yes, you can air dry your hair to achieve the wet look. However, using a hair dryer can help you achieve the look faster and make your hair look more polished.

How long does the wet hair look last?

The wet hair look can last for a few hours or the entire day, depending on the styling product you use and the weather conditions.

Can I achieve the wet hair look with short hair?

Yes, you can achieve the wet look with short hair by using a styling gel or wax and combing your hair back.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as it can damage it.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

Follow these tips and you'll be sure to have healthy, gorgeous hair that's ready for any style!

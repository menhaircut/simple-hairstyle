How To Do Your Hair Like Fran Fine The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Jul 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a fan of the iconic TV show "The Nanny", you surely remember Fran Fine's signature hairstyle. With its voluminous curls, high ponytail, and wispy bangs, it's a look that never goes out of style. If you want to channel your inner Fran and learn how to do your hair like her, you're in luck. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect Fran Fine hairdo. Ready? Let's get started!

Description

Fran Fine's hair is all about volume, texture, and movement. It's a playful and flirty style that complements her bubbly personality and fashion-forward outfits. To achieve the Fran Fine look, you need to have long hair (or hair extensions), a curling iron, hairspray, and a few styling tools. You also need to be patient and willing to put in some effort, as this hairstyle requires some skill and practice.

How to

Step 1: Prep your hair

Before you start curling your hair, you need to prepare it by washing and conditioning it with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron. Then, blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume at the roots.

Step 2: Curl your hair

Next, divide your hair into sections and curl each one with a curling iron. Use a medium-sized barrel to create loose, bouncy curls that frame your face. Make sure to curl your hair away from your face for a more flattering effect. Once you've curled all your hair, run your fingers through it to separate the curls and create a more natural look.

Step 3: Create a high ponytail

Now, gather all your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a hair tie to secure it, but leave a small section of hair out to wrap around the base of the ponytail later. To create more volume at the crown, tease your hair with a comb or a teasing brush.

Step 4: Wrap the base of the ponytail

Take the section of hair you left out and wrap it around the base of the ponytail, covering the hair tie. Use bobby pins to secure it in place and create a more polished look. You can also tease the ponytail lightly to add more volume and texture.

Step 5: Style your bangs

If you have bangs, style them by blow-drying them with a round brush or a straightening iron. Fran Fine's bangs are wispy and feathered, so use a light touch and don't make them too straight or blunt. You can also use some hairspray to keep them in place.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Finally, spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep it in place and add shine. You can also use a shine spray or a serum to enhance the texture and movement of your curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect Fran Fine hairdo:

  • Use a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel to minimize heat damage and create smoother curls.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly, as it can look stiff and unnatural. Aim for loose, bouncy curls that have movement and texture.
  • Tease your hair lightly at the crown to create more volume and lift.
  • Use a hairspray with a flexible hold to keep your hair in place without making it stiff or crunchy.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, barrettes, and scrunchies, to add variety to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to do your hair like Fran Fine:

Q: Can I achieve the Fran Fine look with short hair?

A: It's possible to adapt the Fran Fine hairstyle to shorter hair by using hair extensions or a wig. However, the signature high ponytail may not be feasible with very short hair.

Q: How long does it take to do the Fran Fine hairstyle?

A: The time it takes to do the Fran Fine hairstyle depends on your hair length, thickness, and skill level. It can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour or more.

Q: What kind of products should I use for the Fran Fine hairstyle?

A: You should use volumizing shampoo and conditioner, heat protectant spray, curling iron, hairspray, and a shine spray or serum. You can also use a teasing brush or comb to create more volume at the roots.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use heat protectant products when you do.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet rich in vitamins and nutrients that promote hair growth and strength.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to do your hair like Fran Fine in no time. Whether you're dressing up for a special occasion or just want to add some glamour to your everyday look, the Fran Fine hairstyle is a timeless classic that never goes out of style. So, grab your curling iron and hairspray, and get ready to channel your inner Nanny!

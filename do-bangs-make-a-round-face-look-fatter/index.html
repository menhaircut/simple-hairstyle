Do Bangs Make A Round Face Look Fatter

Written by William Oct 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, there are a lot of factors to consider. Your hair texture, length, and face shape all play a role in determining which style will look best on you. For those with a round face shape, one question that often comes up is whether or not bangs make your face look fatter. In this article, we'll explore this topic in depth and provide you with some tips on how to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A round face shape is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a generally circular shape. Many people with this face shape worry that bangs will make their face look even rounder or fuller. While it's true that some bang styles can accentuate the roundness of your face, there are ways to make them work for you.

How to

When choosing bangs for a round face shape, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the length and thickness of the bangs. Short, choppy bangs that hit above the eyebrows can make your face look wider, while longer, side-swept bangs can elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Another important factor to consider is the shape of the bangs. Rounded bangs can mimic the shape of your face and make it appear even rounder, while angular or asymmetrical bangs can create the illusion of more angles and make your face look slimmer.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing bangs for a round face shape:

  1. Consider the length and thickness of the bangs.
  2. Opt for longer, side-swept bangs instead of short, choppy bangs.
  3. Choose angular or asymmetrical bangs instead of rounded bangs.
  4. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best bangs for your face shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making bangs work for a round face shape:

  • Try adding layers to your hair to create more movement and texture.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that can emphasize the roundness of your face.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to create the illusion of more angles.

FAQ

Do bangs make your face look fatter?

Some bang styles can accentuate the roundness of a face shape, but there are ways to choose bangs that flatter a round face. Opt for longer, side-swept bangs and angular or asymmetrical shapes to create the illusion of more angles and elongate the face.

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face shape is one that creates the illusion of more angles and elongates the face. This can be achieved with long layers, angular cuts, and longer, side-swept bangs.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining your bangs and keeping them looking great, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the right length.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your bangs and make them look greasy.
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your bangs between washes.

In conclusion, bangs can be a great way to add style and personality to your haircut, regardless of your face shape. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can make bangs work for you and create a look that's both flattering and stylish.

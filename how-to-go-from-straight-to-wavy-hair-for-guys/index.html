How To Go From Straight To Wavy Hair For Guys

Written by Eveline Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will be discussing how to go from straight to wavy hair for guys. If you are tired of your straight hair and want to switch things up, read on for our step-by-step guide to achieving wavy locks.

Description

Wavy hair is a popular hairstyle for men, and it's easy to see why. It's a low-maintenance style that looks effortlessly cool. If you have straight hair, it may seem like achieving wavy hair is impossible, but it's actually easier than you think. With the right tools and techniques, you can transform your straight hair into wavy locks.

How to

There are several methods you can use to achieve wavy hair, including using a curling iron or wand, braiding your hair, or using a sea salt spray. We will be focusing on using a sea salt spray, as it is the easiest and most natural-looking method.

Step 1: Choose a Sea Salt Spray

Choose a sea salt spray that works for your hair type. If you have fine hair, choose a spray that is lightweight and won't weigh your hair down. If you have thick hair, choose a spray that provides a strong hold.

Step 2: Apply the Spray

Apply the sea salt spray to damp hair, focusing on the mid-lengths to ends. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you apply the spray, to encourage the wave pattern.

Step 3: Let Your Hair Dry Naturally

Let your hair air dry naturally, or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting. Avoid using a high heat setting, as this can damage your hair and make it frizzy.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a natural-looking wave pattern. You can also use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and hold to your waves.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for achieving wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and frizz
  • Don't overuse sea salt spray, as this can dry out your hair and make it brittle
  • You can also use a hair straightener to create waves, by twisting your hair around the iron and pulling it downwards

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about going from straight to wavy hair for guys:

Can I use a sea salt spray on dry hair?

Yes, you can use a sea salt spray on dry hair, but it may not be as effective as using it on damp hair. Damp hair is more receptive to forming waves, as it can easily be molded into the desired shape.

How often should I use sea salt spray?

You should only use sea salt spray once or twice a week, as overuse can dry out your hair and make it brittle. It's important to use a hydrating conditioner to counteract the drying effects of the spray.

Can I use a sea salt spray on colored hair?

Yes, you can use a sea salt spray on colored hair, but it's important to choose a spray that is safe for color-treated hair. Look for a spray that is sulfate-free and contains hydrating ingredients.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day, or as needed
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp healthy

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve wavy hair that looks natural and effortless. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.

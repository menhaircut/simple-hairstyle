Why Is My Hair Thinning On The Sides Female Understanding The Causes And Finding Solutions

Written by Petter Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you noticing that your hair is getting thinner on the sides? This is a common problem for many women, and it can be caused by a variety of factors. In this article, we'll explore the reasons why your hair might be thinning on the sides and what you can do about it. We'll also provide some tips for taking care of your hair to prevent further thinning.

  • Causes of Hair Thinning on the Sides Female
  • Solutions for Hair Thinning on the Sides Female
  • Hair Care Tips to Prevent Further Thinning

Causes of Hair Thinning on the Sides Female

There are several possible causes of hair thinning on the sides for females:

Hormonal Changes

Changes in hormones can affect hair growth and cause thinning on the sides. This can happen during menopause or after giving birth, for example.

Genetics

Some women are genetically predisposed to hair thinning on the sides. If other women in your family have experienced this, it's possible that you will too.

Traction Alopecia

Traction alopecia is a type of hair loss that occurs when hair is pulled too tightly, causing damage to the hair follicles. This can happen if you frequently wear tight hairstyles like braids, weaves, or ponytails.

Aging

As we age, our hair naturally becomes thinner and weaker. This can be more noticeable on the sides of the head.

Nutritional Deficiencies

A lack of nutrients like iron, zinc, and vitamin D can lead to hair thinning on the sides. If you're not getting enough of these nutrients in your diet, consider taking supplements or adjusting your eating habits.

Solutions for Hair Thinning on the Sides Female

There are several things you can do to address hair thinning on the sides:

Change Your Hairstyle

If you've been wearing tight hairstyles, it's time to give your hair a break. Choose looser styles that don't pull on your hair, like loose braids or a low ponytail.

Use Hair Growth Products

There are many products on the market that claim to promote hair growth. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and caffeine, which are known to support healthy hair growth.

Try Platelet-Rich Plasma Therapy

Platelet-rich plasma therapy involves injecting your own platelet-rich plasma into your scalp to stimulate hair growth. This is a newer treatment that has shown promising results for some people.

Consider Hair Restoration Surgery

If other treatments haven't worked, you might consider hair restoration surgery. This involves transplanting hair from one part of your scalp to another to create a fuller look.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Further Thinning

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair to prevent further thinning:

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it more prone to thinning. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible, or use a heat protectant spray if you must use them.

Don't Brush Your Hair When It's Wet

Wet hair is more fragile than dry hair, so avoid brushing it when it's wet. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and make it more prone to thinning. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to shield your hair when you're outside.

FAQs

Can stress cause hair thinning on the sides?

Yes, stress can contribute to hair thinning on the sides. When you're stressed, your body produces hormones that can affect hair growth.

Will changing my diet help prevent hair thinning on the sides?

It's possible. If your hair thinning is caused by nutritional deficiencies, changing your diet can help. Make sure you're getting enough iron, zinc, and vitamin D, which are important for healthy hair growth.

How long does it take to see results from hair growth products?

Results vary depending on the product and the individual. Some products claim to produce results in as little as a few weeks, while others may take several months.

Overall, hair thinning on the sides female can have many different causes, but there are solutions available. By taking care of your hair and trying different treatments, you can achieve a fuller, healthier head of hair.

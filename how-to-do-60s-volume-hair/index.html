How To Do 60S Volume Hair In 2023

Written by Frank Jun 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to add some retro flair to your hairstyle, the 60s volume hair might just be what you need. This classic look from the past can be achieved with a few easy steps and some hair products. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the process of doing 60s volume hair and provide you with some useful tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do 60s Volume Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving the Best Results
  • FAQs

How to Do 60s Volume Hair

The key to achieving 60s volume hair is in the teasing and the use of volumizing products. Teasing the crown of your head will create height, while volumizing products will add thickness and fullness to your hair. Here are the steps to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and work it through with your fingers.
  2. Section off the top layer of your hair and clip it to the top of your head.
  3. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and backcomb it using a teasing comb. Start at the roots and work your way down. Repeat this step with other sections of hair until you've teased the entire crown of your head.
  4. Release the top layer of your hair and smooth it down over the teased section, creating a bump or pouf at the crown of your head. Secure it with bobby pins.
  5. Tease the sides of your hair to create volume and fullness.
  6. Use a hairspray to set the style in place.

Voila! You now have 60s volume hair.

Tips for Achieving the Best Results

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Use a teasing comb with fine teeth for better control.
  • Be gentle when teasing your hair to avoid damaging it.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you plan on using hot tools, such as a curling iron or straightener.
  • Use a light hand when applying volumizing products to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings and styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about 60s volume hair:

Q: Can I achieve 60s volume hair with short hair?

A: Yes, you can. You may need to use more volumizing products and tease your hair more to create the same effect.

Q: How long will my 60s volume hair last?

A: Your 60s volume hair should last for the day, provided you've used enough hairspray to set the style in place.

Q: Can I achieve 60s volume hair without teasing my hair?

A: It's possible, but teasing your hair is the most effective way to create volume and height.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep it looking healthy.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect 60s volume hair in no time. Whether you're going for a retro look or just want to add some volume and fullness to your hair, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.

How To Cut Hair Without Clipper Guards A Diy Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Cutting hair without clipper guards may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily and effectively. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps of cutting hair without clipper guards, provide tips and tricks, and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

How to Cut Hair Without Clipper Guards

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to section the hair.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Begin by sectioning the hair into manageable sections. Use your comb to create a straight part in the hair, then use your hair clips to clip the top section out of the way.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair in a straight line, using the length of your fingers as a guide. Continue cutting the hair in small sections, working your way around the head.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut all the hair to the desired length, use your scissors to blend the hair together. This will create a more natural look and prevent any harsh lines.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After you have finished cutting, comb through the hair and check for any uneven sections. If you notice any areas that need to be fixed, go back and trim as needed.

Tips for Cutting Hair Without Clipper Guards

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting hair without clipper guards:

  • Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts
  • Section the hair to make it easier to work with
  • Cut the hair in small sections to ensure accuracy
  • Blend the hair for a more natural look
  • Take breaks to rest your hands and avoid fatigue

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair without clipper guards?

Yes, you can cut your own hair without clipper guards. Just make sure you have the right tools and take your time.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and your desired length. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Can I use regular scissors to cut hair?

While regular scissors can technically be used to cut hair, it is not recommended. Haircutting scissors are specifically designed to make clean cuts and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth

Cutting hair without clipper guards may seem intimidating at first, but with practice, it can become a simple and rewarding task. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.

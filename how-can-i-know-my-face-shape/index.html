How Can I Know My Face Shape

Written by Christine Sep 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle, the first step is to determine your face shape. Knowing your face shape can help you pick the right haircut, makeup, and even eyewear that flatters your features. In this article, we will discuss how you can identify your face shape and offer some tips for hair care based on your face shape.

Table of Content

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Hair Care Based on Your Face Shape
  • FAQs

How to Determine Your Face Shape

There are six main face shapes, including round, square, heart, oval, diamond, and oblong. To identify your face shape, you need to look at your jawline, cheekbones, and forehead. Try pulling your hair back and standing in front of a mirror to get a clear view of your face. Here are some general characteristics of each face shape:

  • Round: Soft and curvy, with a rounded chin and a wide forehead.
  • Square: Strong and angular, with a square jawline and a broad forehead.
  • Heart: Wide at the forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow chin.
  • Oval: Balanced and symmetrical, with a gently curved jawline and a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin.
  • Diamond: Narrow at the forehead and jawline, with wide cheekbones.
  • Oblong: Long and narrow, with a high forehead and a long chin.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the general characteristics of each face shape, here is a step-by-step guide to determine your face shape:

  1. Take a photo of your face from the front, with your hair pulled back.
  2. Print the photo or open it on your computer.
  3. Draw a line across your forehead at the widest point.
  4. Draw a line from the top of your ear to the corner of your mouth on each side of your face.
  5. Draw a line across the bottom of your chin.
  6. Look at the shape that is formed by the lines. This is your face shape.

Remember that your face shape may not fit perfectly into one category. You may have a combination of two or more face shapes. Use your best judgment to determine which category your face shape falls into.

Tips for Hair Care Based on Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some tips for hair care based on your face shape:

  • Round: Choose a hairstyle that adds height and lengthens your face, such as a high bun or a long layered cut.
  • Square: Soften your features with a wavy or curly hairstyle and avoid straight cuts.
  • Heart: Go for a side-swept bang that covers your forehead and adds volume at the chin.
  • Oval: Lucky you! Almost any hairstyle will flatter your features, so have fun experimenting with different cuts and styles.
  • Diamond: Highlight your cheekbones with a short bob or a layered cut that frames your face.
  • Oblong: Add width to your face with a layered cut or a side-swept fringe that covers your forehead.

FAQs

Q. What is the most common face shape?

A. The most common face shape is oval, followed by round and square.

Q. Can I have a combination of two or more face shapes?

A. Yes, it is possible to have a combination of two or more face shapes. Use your best judgment to determine which category your face shape falls into.

Q. Can I change my face shape?

A. While you cannot change your bone structure, you can use makeup, hairstyles, and accessories to create the illusion of a different face shape.

Q. What are some other factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle?

A. Other factors to consider include your hair texture, thickness, and personal style. Consult with a hairstylist for personalized recommendations.

Conclusion

Knowing your face shape is the first step to choosing a hairstyle that flatters your features. Use the step-by-step guide and tips in this article to determine your face shape and take care of your hair accordingly. Remember to consult with a hairstylist for personalized recommendations.

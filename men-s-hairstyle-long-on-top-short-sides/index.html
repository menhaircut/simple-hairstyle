Men s Hairstyle Long On Top Short Sides

Written by Eveline Nov 08, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and trendy hairstyle, then the men's hairstyle long on top short sides might be the perfect one for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years and is now one of the most sought-after hairstyles in the fashion industry. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to style it, hair care tips, and more.

Description

As the name suggests, the men's hairstyle long on top short sides is a hairstyle that features long hair on the top and short hair on the sides. The hair on the top is usually styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, textured, or even curly, while the sides and back are typically buzzed or faded. This haircut is popular among men of all ages and is suitable for all hair types.

How to Style

The men's hairstyle long on top short sides can be styled in a variety of ways, depending on your preference and hair type. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt and oil from your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause frizz and damage to your hair.

Step 3: Apply Hair Product

Apply a small amount of hair product, such as wax, pomade, or gel, to your hair. This will help to add texture and hold to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now, it's time to style your hair. Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair on the top of your head, while using clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back. Experiment with different styles, such as a side part, slicked back, or messy, until you find a style that suits you.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your men's hairstyle long on top short sides:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles to find a look that suits you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's hairstyle long on top short sides:

1. Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

2. How often should I visit my hairstylist?

It's recommended to visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

3. How do I know which style suits me?

Experiment with different styles until you find a look that suits you. You can also ask your hairstylist for recommendations based on your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your men's hairstyle long on top short sides looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to deeply nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Conclusion

The men's hairstyle long on top short sides is a trendy and versatile haircut that is suitable for all hair types. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this article, you can achieve a stylish and modern look that will turn heads wherever you go. So, go ahead and experiment with different styles until you find a look that suits you!

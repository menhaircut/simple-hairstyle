How To Cut Top Of Hair For Comb Over A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Oct 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In this article, we will be discussing how to cut the top of your hair for a comb over hairstyle. This hairstyle has been trendy for years and is perfect for men who want a classic, clean-cut look. We will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to achieve this look at home.

Description

A comb over is a popular hairstyle that features a long top and short sides. The hair on the top of the head is combed over to one side, creating a sleek and sophisticated look. This style is perfect for men who want a professional, clean-cut look that is easy to maintain.

How to Cut Top of Hair for Comb Over

Before you start cutting your hair, you will need to gather the necessary tools. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It is also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to help you wet your hair as you work.

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step in cutting the top of your hair for a comb over is to decide on the length you want. If you are unsure, it is best to start with a longer length and gradually work your way down. Use your comb to separate the hair on the top of your head from the hair on the sides.

Step 2: Wet the Hair

Once you have decided on the length, it is time to wet your hair. Use your spray bottle to dampen the hair on the top of your head. This will make it easier to cut and will also help you see any uneven areas.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Starting at the front of your head, use your scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Work your way towards the back of your head, making sure to cut the hair evenly. Take your time and make sure that each section is even before moving on to the next.

Step 4: Comb the Hair

Once you have finished cutting the hair, use your comb to comb the hair into place. Comb the hair to one side, creating the comb over look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect comb over hairstyle:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way down
  • Take your time and cut the hair evenly
  • Use a mirror to check your progress
  • Wet the hair before cutting to make it easier to work with
  • Comb the hair to one side to create the comb over look

FAQ

Can I cut my hair for a comb over at home?

Yes, you can cut your hair for a comb over at home. Just make sure that you have the necessary tools and follow the steps carefully.

How often should I cut my hair for a comb over?

It is recommended that you cut your hair for a comb over every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it is important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish, whether you have a comb over or any other hairstyle.

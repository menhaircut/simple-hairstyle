How To Cut A Short Pixie Style A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that's both chic and edgy, a short pixie style might be just what you need. This trendy haircut has been a favorite of many women for years, and it's not hard to see why. Not only does it look great on almost every face shape, but it's also easy to style and maintain. In this article, we'll show you how to cut a short pixie style in relaxed English language, along with some tips and care advice to keep your hair looking its best.

Description of a Short Pixie Style

A short pixie style is a haircut that's typically cut very short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. The hair is usually cut close to the scalp, but there's enough length on top to add texture and style. This cut can be customized to suit your face shape and personal preferences, and it's a great option for anyone who wants a low-maintenance, edgy look.

How to Cut a Short Pixie Style

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to see the natural texture and shape of your hair, and it will also help you avoid any snags or tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into four parts: the top, sides, and back. Clip each section out of the way to make it easier to work with.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Starting with the sides, use a pair of scissors to cut the hair as short as you'd like. Use the comb to guide your scissors and make sure the cut is even. Repeat on both sides.

Step 4: Trim the Back

Next, move to the back of your head and use the same technique to trim the hair. Make sure to blend the hair from the back into the sides for a seamless look.

Step 5: Work on the Top

Finally, move to the top of your head and use scissors or a razor to add texture and layers. This is where you can get creative and customize the cut to suit your face shape and personal style.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting, style your hair using your favorite products. A short pixie style looks great with a little bit of texture and volume, so consider using a texturizing spray or a volumizing mousse.

Tips for Cutting a Short Pixie Style

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a short pixie style:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way shorter to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use a comb to guide your scissors and make sure your cuts are even.
  • Don't forget to blend the hair from the sides into the back for a seamless look.
  • Consider using a razor to add texture and layers to the top of your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut a short pixie style at home?

A: Yes, it's possible to cut a short pixie style at home, but it's important to have the right tools and technique. If you're not confident in your cutting abilities, it's best to leave it to a professional stylist.

Q: What face shape looks best with a short pixie style?

A: A short pixie style can look great on almost any face shape, but it's especially flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care for a Short Pixie Style

Here are some tips to keep your short pixie style looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and techniques, you're ready to cut a short pixie style at home. Remember to start with a longer length and work your way shorter, use a comb to guide your scissors, and style your hair with your favorite products. With a little bit of practice, you'll have a chic and edgy new look that's perfect for any occasion.

Can I Use Dog Clippers On Human Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're on a tight budget or simply want to save some money, you may be wondering if you can use dog clippers on human hair. After all, they're both hair, right? Well, the short answer is yes, you can use dog clippers on human hair, but there are some important things to keep in mind before you start buzzing away.

Description

Dog clippers are designed to cut through thick, coarse fur, which means they're typically more powerful than human hair clippers. They also tend to have wider blades and may not have the same attachments or guards as human hair clippers. While you can technically use dog clippers on human hair, you need to be very careful to avoid damaging your hair or injuring your scalp.

How to

If you're determined to use dog clippers on your hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Clean Your Clippers

Before you begin, make sure your clippers are clean and free of any dirt or debris. You can use a small brush or toothbrush to remove any hair or dust from the blades.

Step 2: Choose the Right Blade

As we mentioned earlier, dog clippers tend to have wider blades than human hair clippers. This means you'll need to be careful when selecting a blade to avoid cutting too much hair at once. A #10 blade is a good choice for most hair lengths, but you may need a different blade if your hair is very thick or curly.

Step 3: Use a Guard

Most dog clippers don't come with guards or attachments like human hair clippers do, so you'll need to be careful when cutting your hair. One option is to hold the clippers at an angle and use them to thin out your hair without cutting too much off.

Step 4: Go Slowly

When using dog clippers on human hair, it's important to go slowly and be very gentle. Start by trimming a small section of hair and gradually work your way around your head. Take breaks if your clippers get hot, and be sure to oil the blades regularly to keep them running smoothly.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you use dog clippers on human hair safely:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a mirror to check your progress
  • Don't cut too much hair at once
  • Be gentle around your ears and neckline

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using dog clippers on human hair:

Can I use dog clippers on my beard?

Yes, you can use dog clippers on your beard, but you need to be very careful. Use a guard to avoid cutting too much hair and go slowly to prevent nicks and cuts.

Can dog clippers cut human hair?

Yes, dog clippers can cut human hair, but they may not be as precise as human hair clippers. You'll need to be very careful to avoid damaging your hair or injuring your scalp.

Can I use human hair clippers on my dog?

Yes, you can use human hair clippers on your dog, but you need to be careful to avoid injuring your pet. Make sure you use the appropriate guard and go slowly to prevent nicks and cuts.

Hair Care Tips

Whether you're using dog clippers or human hair clippers, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your locks looking their best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth

So, there you have it - you can use dog clippers on human hair, but you need to be very careful. If you're not confident in your ability to use dog clippers safely, it's best to stick with human hair clippers or visit a professional stylist.

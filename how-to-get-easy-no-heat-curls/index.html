How To Get Easy No Heat Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools to style your hair? Heat damage can cause your hair to become dry, brittle, and prone to breakage. Luckily, there are ways to get beautiful curls without using heat. In this article, we will show you how to get easy no heat curls that will leave your hair looking stunning.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Easy No Heat Curls
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Perfect No Heat Curls
  • FAQs

How to Get Easy No Heat Curls

There are several ways to get no heat curls, depending on your hair type and the type of curl you want to achieve. Here are some of the most popular methods:

1. Overnight braids

Braiding your hair before bedtime is an easy way to wake up with beautiful curls. Simply braid your hair when it's damp, sleep on it, and undo the braids in the morning.

2. Twist and pin

Twist small sections of your hair and pin them in place with bobby pins. Leave them in until your hair is completely dry, then gently remove the pins to reveal bouncy curls.

3. Curlformers

Curlformers are a great way to get voluminous curls without using heat. Simply place the curlformers in your hair, leave them in for a few hours, and remove them to reveal beautiful curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get easy no heat curls using the overnight braids method:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start with clean, damp hair. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid any build-up that could weigh down your curls.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips. The number of sections will depend on the thickness and length of your hair.

Step 3: Braid your hair

Braid each section of your hair, making sure to keep the braids tight. Secure the ends with a hair elastic.

Step 4: Sleep on it

Go to bed and sleep on your braids. Make sure to use a satin pillowcase to avoid any friction that could cause frizz.

Step 5: Undo the braids

In the morning, undo the braids and gently separate the curls with your fingers. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Perfect No Heat Curls

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when styling your hair without heat:

1. Use the right products

Choose products that are designed to enhance your curls and add moisture to your hair. Look for products that are free from sulfates and parabens.

2. Don't overdo it

Don't braid your hair too tightly or twist it too much. This can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

3. Use the right tools

Using the right tools, such as wide-tooth combs and satin scrunchies, can help minimize damage to your hair.

FAQs

Q: How long do I need to leave in the braids?

A: You should leave the braids in overnight or for at least 6-8 hours.

Q: Can I use this method on short hair?

A: Yes, you can use this method on short hair. Just make sure to braid your hair tightly and secure the ends with a hair elastic.

Hair Care Tips for No Heat Curls

To keep your curls looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

1. Avoid heat

Avoid using heat tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can cause damage to your hair.

2. Moisturize regularly

Use deep conditioning treatments and moisturizing products regularly to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Protect your hair

Use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat tools, and always wear a hat or scarf when you're outside in the sun or wind.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve beautiful no heat curls that will leave your hair looking healthy and gorgeous.

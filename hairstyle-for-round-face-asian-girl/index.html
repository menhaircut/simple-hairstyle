Hairstyle For Round Face Asian Girl

Written by Robby Sep 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you an Asian girl with a round face and looking for the perfect hairstyle to flatter your features? Look no further! In this article, we will explore some of the best hairstyles for round face Asian girls that will not only enhance your features but also make you look trendy and fashionable.

Description

Having a round face can be challenging when it comes to finding the right hairstyle that complements your features. The key is to choose a style that adds height and length to your face while also creating an illusion of more defined cheekbones and jawline.

How To

Here are some trendy hairstyles for round face Asian girls that you can try:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for round face shapes as they add length to your face and create a slimming effect. You can also try adding side-swept bangs to create an asymmetrical look and draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another great hairstyle for round face Asian girls. Opt for a chin-length bob with layers that will create volume and add dimension to your hair. You can also try a short bob with side-swept bangs to create an angular look.

3. Pixie Cut

If you are feeling daring, a pixie cut is a perfect hairstyle for round face Asian girls. The short length of the hair draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones, creating an angular look. You can also try adding layers to create volume and texture.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving a long layered hairstyle:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

Gently towel dry your hair until it is damp. Avoid using a blow dryer as it can cause damage to your hair.

Step 3: Apply a volumizing mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair and distribute it evenly using a comb. This will help add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Blow dry your hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Start from the roots and work your way down to the ends. This will help create volume and add length to your hair.

Step 5: Add layers

Using a pair of scissors, add layers to your hair. This will help create movement and add texture to your hair.

Step 6: Style your hair

Finally, style your hair using a curling iron or straightener. You can also add some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for round face Asian girls:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height and length to your face
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a blunt bob
  • Try adding layers to create volume and texture
  • Opt for side-swept bangs to create an asymmetrical look
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits you best

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a pixie cut if you have a round face. The short length of the hair draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones, creating an angular look.

Q: Can I wear blunt bangs if I have a round face?

A: It is best to avoid blunt bangs if you have a round face as they can add width to your face. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs to create an asymmetrical look.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet to prevent breakage

With these hairstyle tips and hair care tips, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your round face and keep your hair healthy and shiny. Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful!

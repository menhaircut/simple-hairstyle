How To Cut Mullet Sides A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, you might want to consider getting a mullet. While the mullet is often associated with the 80s, it has made a comeback in recent years and is now a popular choice among both men and women. One of the key elements of a mullet is the long hair on the sides, which can be a bit tricky to cut. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut mullet sides like a pro.

Table of Contents

  • Materials You'll Need
  • Preparing for the Cut
  • Cutting the Mullet Sides
  • Finishing Touches

Materials You'll Need

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather a few materials. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Haircutting cape or towel

Preparing for the Cut

Before you start cutting, you'll need to prepare your hair. Here's what you should do:

  1. Wash and dry your hair: Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting. Wet hair can be harder to work with and may lead to uneven cuts.
  2. Brush your hair: Use a comb to brush your hair so that it's smooth and tangle-free. This will make it easier to cut.
  3. Section your hair: Use hair ties to section off the hair that you want to cut. For mullet sides, you'll want to focus on the hair on the sides of your head, just above your ears. You can leave the hair on top of your head longer if you want to keep a longer mullet style.
  4. Put on a haircutting cape or towel: This will help keep the hair off your clothes and make cleanup easier.

Cutting the Mullet Sides

Now that you're ready to cut your hair, it's time to start cutting the mullet sides. Here's how:

  1. Start with the longest length: Use your clippers to trim the hair on the sides of your head to the longest length you want. This will typically be around 2-3 inches, but you can adjust this depending on how long you want your mullet to be.
  2. Blend the hair: Use your scissors to blend the hair on the sides of your head with the hair on top of your head. You'll want to create a smooth transition between the shorter hair on the sides and the longer hair on top.
  3. Trim the hair around your ears: Use your scissors to trim any hair around your ears. You can either cut the hair straight across or angle it slightly downward. This will help create the classic mullet shape.
  4. Check for symmetry: Make sure that both sides of your hair are even and symmetrical. Use your scissors to make any necessary adjustments.

Finishing Touches

Once you've finished cutting the mullet sides, you can add some finishing touches to complete the look. Here are a few ideas:

  • Add some texture: Use your scissors to add some texture to the hair on the sides of your head. This can help give your mullet some edge and personality.
  • Style your hair: Use some hair gel, wax, or pomade to style your hair. You can create a messy, tousled look or a sleek and polished style.
  • Show off your new look: Take some selfies or show off your new mullet to your friends and family. You'll be sure to turn heads!

Tips for Cutting Mullet Sides

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting mullet sides:

  • Start with a shorter length: It's always easier to cut more hair off than to add it back on. Start with a shorter length and gradually work your way up if you want a longer mullet.
  • Use a mirror: Make sure you have a mirror in front of you and behind you so that you can see what you're doing from all angles.
  • Take your time: Cutting hair can be a bit nerve-wracking, especially if you're new to it. Take your time and don't rush the process.

FAQs

How long does a mullet typically last?

A mullet can last anywhere from several weeks to several months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

Can I cut a mullet myself?

Yes, you can cut a mullet yourself if you have the right tools and some basic hair cutting skills. However, if you're not confident in your abilities, it's always best to see a professional hairstylist.

Is the mullet still in style?

Yes, the mullet has made a comeback in recent years and is now considered a trendy and edgy hairstyle.

Hair Care for Mullet Sides

Now that you've cut your mullet sides, you'll want to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Use a gentle shampoo to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Condition your hair: Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Get regular trims: To keep your mullet looking fresh, you'll want to get regular trims every few weeks.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair, so try to avoid using them too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: If you're spending time outside, wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

Cutting mullet sides can be a bit intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and edgy look that you'll love. Good luck!

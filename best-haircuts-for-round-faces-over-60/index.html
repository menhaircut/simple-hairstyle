Best Haircuts For Round Faces Over 60 A Comprehensive Guide

As we age, our hair goes through various changes. It may become thinner, drier, and more prone to breakage. However, that doesn't mean we can't look our best. If you have a round face, finding the right haircut can be a bit of a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll discuss the best haircuts for round faces over 60.

Description

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved lines and a circular shape. If you have a round face, your cheekbones and jawline are likely to be the widest parts of your face. When it comes to haircuts, the goal is to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

If you're over 60, you may also be dealing with other hair-related issues such as thinning, graying, and dryness. That's why it's important to choose a haircut that not only flatters your face shape but also works with your hair texture and color.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to create the illusion of length. This can be achieved by adding layers, angles, and volume to the crown of the head. Second, you want to avoid styles that add width to the face. This includes blunt cuts, heavy bangs, and chin-length bobs.

If you have thinning hair, you may want to consider a shorter cut that adds volume and texture. Layers can also help create the illusion of thicker hair. If you have curly hair, you may want to avoid blunt cuts and instead opt for a layered style that enhances your natural texture.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces Over 60

1. Layered Bob

The layered bob is a classic haircut that works well for round faces. It adds volume to the crown of the head and frames the face without adding width. This is a great option for those with thinning hair or fine hair.

2. Long Layers

Long layers are another great option for round faces. They create the illusion of length and help to elongate the face. This is a great option for those with thick, curly hair.

3. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short, layered haircut that works well for round faces. It adds volume to the crown of the head and frames the face without adding width. This is a great option for those with thinning hair or fine hair.

4. Shaggy Bob

The shaggy bob is a layered haircut that works well for round faces. It adds volume to the crown of the head and frames the face without adding width. This is a great option for those with thinning hair or fine hair.

5. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for those with round faces. They create the illusion of length and help to elongate the face. This is a great option for those with thick, curly hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're considering a new haircut, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Research different haircuts that work well for round faces.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which cut will work best for your hair texture, color, and face shape.
  3. Schedule a haircut appointment.
  4. Bring a picture of the desired haircut to your appointment.
  5. Discuss any concerns or questions with your hairstylist.
  6. Sit back and relax while your hairstylist works their magic.
  7. Ask your hairstylist for tips on maintaining your new haircut.
  8. Enjoy your new look!

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of it to ensure it looks its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that works well with your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Schedule regular haircuts to keep your style looking fresh.
  • Consider using a volumizing spray or mousse to add texture and volume to your hair.

FAQs

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. A well-executed short haircut can actually elongate the face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline. It's all about finding the right cut for your face shape and hair texture.

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes! Long hair can look great on round faces as long as it's layered and styled in a way that adds length and definition to the face.

Hair Care Tips for Aging Hair

As we age, our hair goes through various changes. Here are a few tips for taking care of aging hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Consider using a volumizing spray or mousse to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Schedule regular haircuts to keep your style looking fresh.

In conclusion, finding the right haircut for a round face over 60 can be a bit of a challenge. However, with the right tips and advice, you can find a style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair texture and color. Whether you opt for a layered bob, long layers, pixie cut, shaggy bob, or side-swept bangs, the key is to create the illusion of length and avoid adding width to the face. With the right care and maintenance, your new haircut will look great for years to come.

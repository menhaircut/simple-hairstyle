What Hairstyle Suits My Body Shape Tips And Tricks For The Perfect Look

Written by William Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of trying out different hairstyles and not getting the desired look, it's time to consider your body shape! Your body shape can influence how certain hairstyles look on you, and it's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. In this blog post, we'll discuss how to determine your body shape and what hairstyles suit each body shape. Let's get started!

How to Determine Your Body Shape

Before you can choose the perfect hairstyle, you need to determine your body shape. The four main body shapes are:

  • Hourglass
  • Pear
  • Apple
  • Straight

The easiest way to determine your body shape is to measure your bust, waist, and hips. If your bust and hips are nearly equal in size, and your waist is smaller, you have an hourglass shape. If your hips are wider than your bust, you have a pear shape. If your bust is larger than your waist and hips, you have an apple shape. If your bust, waist, and hips are nearly equal in size, you have a straight shape.

Hairstyles for Hourglass Shapes

If you have an hourglass shape, you have a well-defined waist and curvy hips and shoulders. Hairstyles that look best on hourglass shapes are those that accentuate your curves. Long, wavy hair looks great on hourglass shapes because it adds volume to your hair and draws attention to your curves. Medium-length hair with layers is also a good choice because it adds texture and movement to your hair.

Hairstyles for Pear Shapes

If you have a pear shape, you have wider hips than shoulders and a smaller bust. Hairstyles that look best on pear shapes are those that add volume to your shoulders and draw attention away from your hips. Short, layered haircuts are a good choice because they add volume to your hair and give the illusion of wider shoulders. Shoulder-length hair with layers is also a good choice because it adds volume to your hair and balances out your proportions.

Hairstyles for Apple Shapes

If you have an apple shape, you carry most of your weight around your midsection. Hairstyles that look best on apple shapes are those that add volume to the top of your head and draw attention away from your midsection. Short, layered haircuts are a good choice because they add volume to your hair and give the illusion of a longer face. Long, straight hair with layers is also a good choice because it adds volume to your hair and draws attention to your face.

Hairstyles for Straight Shapes

If you have a straight shape, you have a similar bust, waist, and hip measurement. Hairstyles that look best on straight shapes are those that add volume and texture to your hair. Long, layered hair is a good choice because it adds movement and texture to your hair. Short, layered haircuts are also a good choice because they add volume to your hair and give the illusion of a longer face.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Now that you know what hairstyles suit your body shape, here are some tips for choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your face shape
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair
  • Consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion
  • Choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable

FAQs

Can I still wear any hairstyle I want?

Of course! These are just suggestions for hairstyles that flatter your body shape. Ultimately, it's up to you to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

What if I have a combination of body shapes?

Many people have a combination of body shapes. In this case, it's best to choose a hairstyle that flatters your most prominent features.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of your body shape, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth

Choosing the right hairstyle for your body shape is important for achieving the perfect look. By considering your body shape, face shape, and lifestyle, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and comfortable. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Happy hairstyling!

Medium Hairstyles For Curly Hair Over 50 Tips How To Faq

Written by Daniel Oct 29, 2023 · 3 min read

As we age, our hair changes with us. One of the most common changes is that our hair becomes curly. If you have curly hair and you’re over 50, you might be wondering what hairstyles will suit you best. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best medium hairstyles for curly hair over 50.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How-to
  • Step-by-step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium hairstyles are perfect for women over 50 because they’re versatile and easy to maintain. They can be styled in different ways, from casual to formal, and can be adjusted to suit your face shape and hair texture. For curly hair, medium hairstyles can help control frizz and add volume without being too heavy.

How-to

The first step in choosing a medium hairstyle for curly hair over 50 is to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a style that will flatter your features. Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can start looking at different hairstyles that will suit you.

Face Shapes

The most common face shapes are:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart

If you’re not sure what your face shape is, you can use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have these measurements, you can compare them to the different face shapes to determine which one you have.

Hairstyles

Some of the best medium hairstyles for curly hair over 50 include:

  • The layered bob
  • The shag
  • The curly pixie
  • The curly lob
  • The curly bangs

Step-by-step

Here’s how to create a curly lob:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment
  4. Curl your hair using a curling iron
  5. Brush out your curls
  6. Apply a texturizing spray

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining medium hairstyles for curly hair over 50:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent drying out your hair
  • Apply a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to avoid frizz
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent damage to your hair
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage

FAQ

What is the best haircut for curly hair over 50?

The best haircut for curly hair over 50 depends on your face shape and hair texture. Some of the best haircuts include the layered bob, the curly pixie, and the curly lob.

How can I style my curly hair without heat?

You can style your curly hair without heat by using a curl-enhancing product and letting your hair air-dry. You can also try braiding your hair while it’s damp to create loose waves.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry to prevent frizz
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair to prevent drying it out
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol to prevent drying out your hair

With these tips and hairstyles, you can rock your curly hair over 50 with confidence and style!

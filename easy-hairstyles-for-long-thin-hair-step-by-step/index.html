Easy Hairstyles For Long Thin Hair Step By Step A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Aug 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have long, thin hair, it can be challenging to find hairstyles that work for you. You might feel like your hair doesn't have enough volume or texture to create a variety of looks. But fear not! With a few simple tricks and techniques, you can create easy hairstyles that will make your hair look fuller and more voluminous. In this article, we'll share some step-by-step instructions for easy hairstyles for long thin hair.

How to Prepare Your Hair for Styling

Before you start styling your hair, it's essential to prepare it properly. Here are a few steps you can follow:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products will help give your hair more body and texture.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair while it's still damp. We recommend using a mousse or a volumizing spray. These products will help create volume and hold your hairstyle in place.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the roots. Make sure to lift your hair away from your scalp while you're blow-drying to create more volume.

Braided Hairstyles for Long Thin Hair

Braids are an excellent option for long, thin hair because they can create the illusion of thickness and volume. Here are a few braided hairstyles you can try:

The Classic French Braid

Start by dividing your hair into three sections. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the new middle section. Continue braiding in this pattern, adding more hair from the sides as you go. Secure the braid with an elastic band at the end.

The Dutch Braid

The Dutch braid is similar to the French braid, but it starts with the hair underneath the braid. Divide your hair into three sections, then cross the right section under the middle section, then the left section under the new middle section. Continue braiding in this pattern, adding more hair from the sides as you go. Secure the braid with an elastic band at the end.

Bun Hairstyles for Long Thin Hair

Buns are another great option for long, thin hair because they can create the illusion of volume and fullness. Here are a few bun hairstyles you can try:

The Messy Bun

Start by pulling your hair into a high ponytail. Twist the ponytail into a bun, then secure it with bobby pins. Pull a few strands of hair out of the bun to create a messy, effortless look.

The Low Bun

Start by pulling your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Twist the ponytail into a bun, then secure it with bobby pins. Pull a few strands of hair out of the bun to create a relaxed, casual look.

Ponytail Hairstyles for Long Thin Hair

Ponytails are a classic hairstyle that can work well for long, thin hair. Here are a few ponytail hairstyles you can try:

The High Ponytail

Start by pulling your hair into a high ponytail. Use a brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways. You can also add a hair tie or scrunchie to give your ponytail some extra volume.

The Braided Ponytail

Start by creating a classic French braid or Dutch braid (see above). Once you reach the nape of your neck, pull the remaining hair into a ponytail. Secure the ponytail with an elastic band.

How to Use Accessories to Enhance Your Hairstyle

Accessories can be an excellent way to enhance your hairstyle and add some extra flair. Here are a few accessories you can try:

Hair Clips

Use hair clips to pin back any loose strands or to add some extra volume to your hair. You can also use decorative hair clips to add some sparkle or color to your hairstyle.

Headbands

Headbands can be a great way to add some texture and volume to your hair. Choose a headband that matches your hair color for a subtle look, or choose a bold, colorful headband for a statement piece.

Tips for Styling Long Thin Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your long, thin hair:

  • Use volumizing products to create more body and texture.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the strands and make them look thinner.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.
  • Don't be afraid to use accessories to enhance your hairstyle and add some extra flair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I make my thin hair look thicker?

A: You can make your thin hair look thicker by using volumizing products, blow-drying your hair with a round brush, and experimenting with hairstyles that add volume and texture, such as braids and buns.

Q: Will using heat on my hair damage it?

A: Yes, using too much heat on your hair can damage the strands and make them look thinner. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

Hair Care Tips for Long Thin Hair

Here are a few hair care tips to keep your long, thin hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and thickness.

In conclusion, there are many easy hairstyles for long thin hair that you can try. By following these simple steps and tips, you can create beautiful, voluminous hairstyles that will make your hair look thicker and fuller. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories to find what works best for you. With a little creativity and patience, you can achieve the perfect look for your long, thin hair.

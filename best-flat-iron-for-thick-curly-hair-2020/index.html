Best Flat Iron For Thick Curly Hair 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how hard it can be to find a flat iron that can handle your mane. Luckily, we have compiled a list of the best flat irons for thick curly hair in 2020 to help you get the sleek, straight hair you desire. In this article, we will provide you with a guide on how to choose the best flat iron, step-by-step instructions on how to use it, tips to maintain healthy hair, and frequently asked questions.

How to Choose the Best Flat Iron for Thick Curly Hair

When choosing the best flat iron for thick curly hair, there are a few things to consider:

Plate Material:

The plate material is essential when it comes to choosing a flat iron. For thick curly hair, ceramic, titanium, and tourmaline plates are the best options. Ceramic plates distribute heat evenly, while titanium plates heat up quickly and are durable. Tourmaline plates emit negative ions that help reduce frizz and add shine to your hair.

Plate Size:

The plate size is also essential. For thick curly hair, choose a flat iron with plates that are at least 1.5 inches wide. This will help you straighten your hair faster.

Heat Settings:

Since thick curly hair requires higher heat to straighten, choose a flat iron with adjustable heat settings. This will allow you to control the temperature and prevent heat damage.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Use a Flat Iron

Now that you have chosen the best flat iron for your hair, here is how to use it:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before using a flat iron, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can cause damage when exposed to heat.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent heat damage.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair using clips. This will make it easier to straighten each section.

Step 4: Straighten Your Hair

Starting at the roots, glide the flat iron down each section of hair. Repeat until each section is straightened.

Step 5: Apply Serum

Apply a serum to your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.

Tips to Maintain Healthy Hair

Using a flat iron can cause damage to your hair if not used correctly. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant

Always use a heat protectant to protect your hair from heat damage.

Tip 2: Don't Use a Flat Iron on Wet Hair

Wet hair is more vulnerable to heat damage. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a flat iron.

Tip 3: Use a Lower Temperature

Using a lower temperature can prevent heat damage. Start with a lower temperature and gradually increase it until you find the right temperature for your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a flat iron on wet hair?

A: No, using a flat iron on wet hair can cause damage. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a flat iron.

Q: How often should I use a flat iron?

A: It is best to limit the use of a flat iron to once or twice a week to prevent heat damage.

Q: Can a flat iron cause hair breakage?

A: Yes, using a flat iron can cause breakage if not used correctly. Always use a heat protectant and avoid using high temperatures.

Hair Care

Using a flat iron can cause damage to your hair if not used correctly. To maintain healthy hair, make sure to use a heat protectant, avoid using a flat iron on wet hair, and limit the use of a flat iron to once or twice a week. Choosing the right flat iron for your hair type is also essential. Follow our guide to choose the best flat iron for thick curly hair in 2020, and you will have sleek, straight hair in no time!

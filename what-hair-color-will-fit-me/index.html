What Hair Color Will Fit Me

Written by Bardi Jun 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hair color, you may be wondering what shade will best complement your skin tone and features. With so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which color to go with. In this article, we'll explore how to determine what hair color will fit you best.

Description

Choosing the right hair color can enhance your natural beauty and boost your confidence. It's essential to take into account your skin tone, eye color, and lifestyle when deciding on a new shade. While there are no hard and fast rules, certain hair colors tend to look better on certain skin tones.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in figuring out what hair color will fit you is to determine your skin tone. There are three main categories: cool, warm, and neutral. Cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones, warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, and neutral skin tones have a mix of both.

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they look green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell, you likely have a neutral skin tone.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing a Hair Color

Once you've determined your skin tone, it's time to choose a hair color that complements it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

If you're looking for a subtle change, consider staying within two shades of your natural hair color. This will give you a more natural look and make maintenance easier.

Step 2: Determine Your Undertones

Now that you know your skin tone, it's important to determine your undertones. If you have cool undertones, consider shades like ash blonde, platinum, and cool brown. If you have warm undertones, try golden blonde, caramel, and warm brown. For neutral undertones, you can rock almost any shade.

Step 3: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in deciding on a hair color. If you have blue or green eyes, try shades like honey blonde or auburn. If you have brown eyes, go for rich shades like chocolate brown or deep red.

Step 4: Think About Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hair color. If you're always on the go, opt for a low-maintenance shade that doesn't require frequent touch-ups. If you work in a conservative environment, choose a classic shade like dark brown or black.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Once you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling tools
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hair color:

What hair color looks best on fair skin?

Blonde, light brown, and red tend to look best on fair skin tones.

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, but it's important to do a patch test first to ensure you're not allergic to the dye.

How often should I touch up my roots?

This depends on how quickly your hair grows, but most people need to touch up their roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care is Key

Choosing the right hair color can be a game-changer, but it's important to remember that hair care is key. By taking care of your hair and maintaining your new color, you can keep your locks looking healthy and beautiful.

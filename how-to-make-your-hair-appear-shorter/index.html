How To Make Your Hair Appear Shorter

Written by Eveline Jun 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of your long hair but don't want to commit to a dramatic haircut? Don't worry, there are ways to make your hair appear shorter without actually cutting it off. In this article, we will go over some tips and tricks to help you achieve the shorter hair look you desire.

Description

This article will provide you with different methods to make your hair look shorter without actually chopping off your locks. The methods shared here will help you achieve a shorter hair look and give you some hairstyle inspiration for your next hairdo. In addition, we will also share tips on how to care for your hair to keep it healthy and strong.

How to

1. Style Your Hair in a Bob

One of the easiest ways to make your hair appear shorter is by styling it in a bob. This classic hairstyle is perfect for those who want to achieve a shorter hair look without sacrificing too much length. You can opt for a sleek and straight bob or add some waves for a more relaxed look.

2. Tie Your Hair in a High Ponytail

Tying your hair in a high ponytail can also create the illusion of shorter hair. The key is to position the ponytail high up on your head, so it appears like you have a shorter bob hairstyle. You can also add some volume to the hair on top of your head to make the ponytail look more natural.

3. Get a Haircut with Layers

If you're willing to cut your hair, getting a haircut with layers can help make your hair look shorter. Layers can create movement and texture, which can make your hair look lighter and shorter. You can also ask your hairstylist to add some face-framing layers to accentuate your features.

4. Try a Messy Bun

A messy bun can also give the illusion of shorter hair, especially if you have long hair. The key is to position the bun at the nape of your neck, so it appears like you have a shorter hairstyle. You can also leave some hair strands loose to frame your face.

Step by Step

1. Style Your Hair in a Bob

To style your hair in a bob, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and bounce.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or a curling iron to add some waves.
  4. Apply some hairspray or styling cream to keep your hairstyle in place.

2. Tie Your Hair in a High Ponytail

To tie your hair in a high ponytail, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any knots and tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Use a comb to tease the hair on top of your head to add some volume.
  4. Wrap a hair strand around the hair tie to hide it and secure it with a bobby pin.

3. Get a Haircut with Layers

To get a haircut with layers, follow these steps:

  1. Find a hairstylist who specializes in layering techniques.
  2. Discuss your hair goals and preferences with your hairstylist.
  3. Let your hairstylist cut your hair using different lengths and angles to create movement and texture.
  4. Ask your hairstylist to add some face-framing layers to accentuate your features.

4. Try a Messy Bun

To try a messy bun, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any knots and tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Twist your hair around the hair tie to create a messy bun.
  4. Use bobby pins to secure the hair strands in place.

Tips

  • Use hair accessories to add some flair to your hairstyle, such as hair clips or headbands.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Visit a hairstylist regularly to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair in top condition.

FAQ

Q. Will tying my hair in a low ponytail make it look longer?

A. Yes, tying your hair in a low ponytail can make it look longer. If you want to create the illusion of shorter hair, opt for a high ponytail instead.

Q. Do layers work for all hair types?

A. Yes, layers can work for all hair types, but the type and amount of layers may vary depending on your hair texture and length.

Q. Can I still wear a messy bun if I have short hair?

A. Yes, you can still wear a messy bun if you have short hair. However, you may need to use some hair extensions or bobby pins to create the illusion of a fuller bun.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it's essential to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with UV protection.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your hair appear shorter without actually cutting it off. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong, no matter what hairstyle you choose.

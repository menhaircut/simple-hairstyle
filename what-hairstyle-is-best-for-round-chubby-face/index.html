What Hairstyle Is Best For Round Chubby Face

Written by Daniel Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect hairstyle that will flatter your round chubby face? Worry no more as we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles that will suit your face shape and enhance your beauty. Read on to know more about it.

Description

Round chubby face is a common face shape where the width and length of the face are almost equal, and the cheeks are fuller. Choosing the right hairstyle for this face shape can be tricky, as you need to create an illusion of a longer and slimmer face. The right hairstyle can enhance your features while hiding the flaws, making you look more attractive and confident.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

The right hairstyle can make or break your look, so it's essential to choose the one that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personality. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, you need to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing them. If they are almost equal, then you have a round face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture plays a crucial role in choosing the right hairstyle. If you have thin hair, then you should avoid hairstyles that require a lot of volume, whereas if you have thick hair, you can opt for layered hairstyles.

Step 3: Decide on the Length of Your Hair

The length of your hair also matters when choosing a hairstyle. Short haircuts can make your face look wider, while long hair can create an illusion of a slimmer face. So, decide on the length of your hair accordingly.

Best Hairstyles for Round Chubby Face

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle let's discuss the best hairstyles for round chubby face.

1. Bob Haircut

The Bob Haircut is a classic hairstyle that suits almost all face shapes, including round chubby face. This hairstyle is short at the back and longer at the front, creating an illusion of a longer face. You can opt for a chin-length bob or a longer one depending on your preference.

2. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is another great option for round chubby face. This hairstyle adds volume to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker. You can opt for short or long layers depending on the length of your hair.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add some dimension to your hair and create an illusion of a longer face. You can opt for a wispy or blunt bang depending on your preference.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short haircut that is perfect for round chubby face. This hairstyle adds volume to your hair and creates an illusion of a longer face. You can opt for a messy or sleek pixie cut depending on your preference.

5. Long Layered Hair

Long layered hair is another great option for round chubby face. This hairstyle adds volume to your hair and creates an illusion of a slimmer face. You can opt for face-framing layers or layers throughout your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you have chosen the right hairstyle, you need to maintain it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too many styling products as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

1. Can I opt for a center parting with a round chubby face?

It's best to avoid a center parting with a round chubby face as it can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for a side parting that can create an illusion of a longer face.

2. Can I opt for a curly hairstyle with a round chubby face?

Yes, you can opt for a curly hairstyle with a round chubby face. Curly hair can add volume to your hair and create an illusion of a slimmer face. You can opt for loose curls or tight curls depending on your preference.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle for round chubby face can be tricky, but it's not impossible. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your beauty and boost your confidence. So, choose the hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personality, and maintain it well to keep it looking great.

Remember, your hair is your crown, so wear it with pride!

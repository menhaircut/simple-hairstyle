Good Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair Male

Written by Petter Jul 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we'll be discussing some good hairstyles for men with thick wavy hair. We'll cover everything from how to style your hair, to tips on how to care for it, and more. So, whether you're looking for a new hairstyle or just want to learn more about taking care of your thick wavy hair, keep reading!

Description

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse for men. On one hand, it's full of volume and texture, which can make for some seriously stylish looks. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style, especially if you don't know what you're doing.

How to Style

When it comes to styling thick wavy hair, the key is to find a style that works with your natural texture. Here are a few good options:

1. The Messy Quiff

The messy quiff is a great option for guys with thick wavy hair. To achieve this look, simply apply a small amount of styling cream to your hair and use your fingers to create a messy, textured look. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair.

2. The Side Part

The side part is a classic look that works well with thick wavy hair. To achieve this look, simply apply a pomade or styling cream to your hair and use a comb to create a defined side part. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.

3. The Undercut

The undercut is a popular choice for guys with thick wavy hair. To achieve this look, simply shave the sides and back of your head and leave the top long and wavy. You can also use a styling cream or pomade to add texture and definition to your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your thick wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for thick, wavy hair. This will help to moisturize and soften your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths. This will help to add texture and definition to your hair.

Step 3: Style your hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into your desired look. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Finish your look with a spritz of hairspray to help hold your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your thick wavy hair:

  • Use products designed for thick, wavy hair
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause frizz
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you

FAQ

What's the best haircut for thick wavy hair?

The best haircut for thick wavy hair is one that works with your natural texture. A layered cut can help to add movement and texture to your hair, while a short, textured cut can help to tame unruly waves.

How do I prevent frizz in my thick wavy hair?

To prevent frizz in your thick wavy hair, avoid using brushes and instead use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. You can also apply a small amount of anti-frizz serum to your hair before styling.

How often should I wash my thick wavy hair?

It's best to wash your thick wavy hair every 2-3 days, as washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care

To keep your thick wavy hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use products designed for thick, wavy hair
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to moisturize and nourish your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair

In conclusion, there are plenty of good hairstyles for men with thick wavy hair. Whether you prefer a messy quiff, a side part, or an undercut, there's a style out there that will work for you. Just remember to take good care of your hair, and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you!

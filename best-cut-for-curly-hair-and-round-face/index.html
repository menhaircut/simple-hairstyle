The Best Cut For Curly Hair And Round Face In 2023

Written by Christine Sep 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your curly hair and round face? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll provide you with the best cut for curly hair and round face in 2023. We’ll also give you some tips on how to style and take care of your curly hair.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The best cut for curly hair and round face is a long layered haircut. It helps to elongate your face and add movement to your curls. The layers also help to reduce the bulkiness of your hair, which is common with curly hair. The key is to have the layers start at your chin or below, so they don't add width to your face. A side-swept fringe or bangs can also help to balance out a round face shape.

How to

When you’re at the salon, ask your stylist for a long layered haircut. Show them pictures of haircuts that you like and make sure to mention that you have curly hair and a round face. They’ll be able to customize the haircut to suit your specific needs. You can also ask for a side-swept fringe or bangs, if you want to add some extra dimension to your face.

Step by step

The steps for getting a long layered haircut for curly hair and round face are as follows:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry or diffuse it with a blow dryer.
  2. Go to a reputable salon and show your stylist pictures of the haircut you want.
  3. Ask them to customize the haircut to suit your specific needs, taking into account your curly hair and round face.
  4. After the haircut, style your hair with a curl-enhancing product, such as mousse or gel.
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair or let it air dry.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long layered haircut for curly hair and round face:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curly hair, as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as it can damage your curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best cut for curly hair and round face:

What if I don’t want a long haircut?

You can still get a layered haircut for curly hair and round face, but make sure the layers start at your chin or below. Avoid getting a blunt cut, as it can add width to your face.

Can I still get a side-swept fringe or bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can! Just make sure to ask your stylist to cut them when your hair is dry, so they don’t shrink up too much.

How often should I get a haircut?

It’s recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Here are some additional hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Get a deep conditioning treatment every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

By following these tips and getting a long layered haircut, you’ll have beautiful and healthy curly hair that complements your round face shape. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you. With the right care and attention, you can have stunning curly hair that you’ll love!

How To Wear A Ponytail Over 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 50 and wondering how to wear a ponytail? Don't worry, you're not alone! Wearing a ponytail can be a quick and easy hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you're running errands, going to work, or attending a formal event, a ponytail can be a versatile and stylish option. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to wear a ponytail over 50.

How to Wear a Ponytail

Wearing a ponytail is easy and straightforward. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Gather your hair

Start by gathering your hair at the back of your head. Use a comb or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or knots.

Step 2: Secure your ponytail

Use a hair elastic to secure your ponytail. Make sure it's tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it hurts. If you have thick hair, you may need to use two hair elastics.

Step 3: Style your ponytail

Now it's time to style your ponytail. You can leave it simple and sleek, or you can add some volume and texture by teasing the hair at the crown of your head. You can also add accessories like a headband or hair clip.

Tips for Wearing a Ponytail Over 50

Here are some tips to help you wear a ponytail over 50:

Tip 1: Choose the right elastic

Choose a hair elastic that is gentle on your hair. Avoid elastics with metal clasps or rubber bands that can cause breakage and damage.

Tip 2: Add volume

If you have thin or fine hair, add some volume by teasing your hair at the crown of your head. This will give your ponytail a fuller look.

Tip 3: Use hair accessories

Add some interest to your ponytail by using hair accessories like a headband or hair clip. This will add some sparkle and shine to your hairstyle.

Tip 4: Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different ponytail styles. You can wear your ponytail high or low, to the side or in the middle, and with different textures and accessories.

FAQ About Wearing a Ponytail Over 50

Can I wear a ponytail if I have short hair?

Yes, you can wear a ponytail with short hair. Use bobby pins to secure any short or stray hairs.

Can I wear a ponytail to a formal event?

Yes, you can wear a ponytail to a formal event. Just make sure to style it in a sleek and polished way.

How do I prevent my ponytail from slipping?

Use a hair elastic that is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it hurts. You can also use bobby pins to secure any stray hairs.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing a Ponytail Over 50

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when wearing a ponytail:

Tip 1: Don't wear a ponytail too often

Wearing a ponytail too often can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Try to alternate between wearing your hair down and wearing a ponytail.

Tip 2: Use a hair mask

Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. This will prevent breakage and damage from wearing a ponytail.

Tip 3: Avoid using heat tools

Avoid using heat tools like curling irons and straighteners. These tools can cause damage to your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

Now that you know how to wear a ponytail over 50, you can rock this easy and versatile hairstyle with confidence. Just remember to choose the right elastic, add volume, experiment with different styles, and take care of your hair. Happy styling!

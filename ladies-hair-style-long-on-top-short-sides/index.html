Ladies Hair Style Long On Top Short Sides A Trendy And Chic Look

Written by Aprilia Jul 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle to something trendy and chic, the ladies hair style long on top short sides is an excellent choice. This hairstyle features longer hair on the top and shorter sides, creating a unique and edgy look that's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to achieve it, styling tips, and hair care advice.

How to Achieve the Ladies Hair Style Long on Top Short Sides

The first step in achieving the ladies hair style long on top short sides is to find a skilled hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. You'll need to explain exactly what you're looking for, including the length and style you want on the top and sides of your head.

Once you've found a hairstylist you trust, they'll typically start by cutting the sides of your hair short, usually with clippers. They'll then gradually blend the sides into the longer hair on the top of your head, creating a smooth and seamless transition.

Depending on your personal style preferences, you may choose to leave the top of your hair straight and sleek, or you may opt for some texture and volume. Your hairstylist can help you achieve the perfect look for your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling the Ladies Hair Style Long on Top Short Sides

To style your ladies hair style long on top short sides, you'll need a few key products and tools, including a good quality shampoo and conditioner, a hairbrush, a blow dryer, and a styling product such as a hair gel or mousse.

Follow these steps to style your hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, which is especially important if you're using heat styling tools.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture on the top of your head.
  3. Apply a styling product such as a hair gel or mousse to the longer hair on the top of your head, working it through with your fingers to create your desired style.
  4. Use a hairbrush or your fingers to smooth the shorter hair on the sides of your head, blending it seamlessly into the longer hair on the top.
  5. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Your Ladies Hair Style Long on Top Short Sides

To keep your ladies hair style long on top short sides looking great, there are a few key tips to keep in mind:

  • Regular trims are essential to maintain the shape and style of your hair. Aim to visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Invest in good quality hair care products, including a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, which is essential for maintaining your style.
  • Use heat styling tools sparingly, as excess heat can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style. A good styling product can make all the difference in achieving the perfect look.

Frequently Asked Questions About the Ladies Hair Style Long on Top Short Sides

What face shapes suit the ladies hair style long on top short sides?

The ladies hair style long on top short sides is a versatile hairstyle that can suit a range of face shapes. However, it's particularly flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces, as it can help to balance out the proportions of the face.

Can I achieve the ladies hair style long on top short sides with curly hair?

Absolutely! The ladies hair style long on top short sides looks great on all hair types, including curly hair. Your hairstylist can help you achieve the perfect length and style for your curly hair, taking into account factors such as curl pattern and texture.

How do I know if the ladies hair style long on top short sides is right for me?

The best way to determine if the ladies hair style long on top short sides is right for you is to consult with a skilled hairstylist. They can help you assess your face shape, hair type, and personal style to determine if this hairstyle is a good fit for you.

Hair Care Advice for the Ladies Hair Style Long on Top Short Sides

Maintaining healthy and hydrated hair is essential for keeping your ladies hair style long on top short sides looking its best. Here are a few key hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools excessively, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb to help detangle your hair without causing damage.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to help keep your hair shiny and healthy-looking.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the ladies hair style long on top short sides with confidence and style. Whether you're headed to the office or out for a night on the town, this trendy and chic hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make a statement.

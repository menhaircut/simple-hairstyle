How To Style A Short Pixie Bob Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jun 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance yet stylish haircut, a short pixie bob might be the perfect choice for you. This versatile cut can be worn in many different ways, from sleek and polished to messy and edgy. In this article, we'll show you how to style a short pixie bob from start to finish, including tips, tricks, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style a Short Pixie Bob

Before we dive into the styling process, it's important to choose the right cut for your face shape and hair type. A short pixie bob typically ranges from 2 to 4 inches in length and can be customized to suit your unique features. Once you have the right cut, follow these steps to style your short pixie bob:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a towel to remove excess moisture, but don't rub your hair vigorously as this can cause breakage. Instead, gently pat your hair dry.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product that suits your hair type and desired look. If you want a sleek, polished style, use a smoothing serum or cream. For a messy, textured look, use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray. Work the product through your hair from roots to tips, using your fingers to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer and a round brush, start blow-drying your hair from the roots. Use the brush to lift and shape your hair as you dry it, aiming the dryer nozzle down to prevent frizz. Once your hair is mostly dry, switch to a cool setting to set the style.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

If you have bangs, use a flat iron to straighten them or a curling iron to create soft waves. You can also use a texturizing spray to add some volume and movement.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your style in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Choose a formula that provides hold without stiffness, and avoid spraying too close to your scalp to prevent buildup.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed breakdown of the styling process for a short pixie bob:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry it gently.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product that suits your hair type and desired look. Use a dime-sized amount for short hair, and work it through your hair with your fingers.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down. Use the brush to lift and shape your hair as you dry it, and switch to a cool setting once your hair is mostly dry.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

If you have bangs, use a flat iron or a curling iron to style them. You can also use a texturizing spray to add some volume and movement.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Spray a light mist of hairspray over your hair to keep your style in place all day.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your short pixie bob:

  • Invest in a good quality blow dryer and round brush for the best results.
  • Try different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as twisting and pinning your hair to create volume and texture.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, and other hair accessories to add interest to your style.

FAQ

Q: Is a short pixie bob suitable for all face shapes?

A: While a short pixie bob can look great on many different face shapes, it's important to choose a style that flatters your features. Talk to your hairstylist about the best options for your face shape.

Q: Can I style a short pixie bob without heat?

A: Yes, you can use a texturizing spray or a styling cream to create a messy, tousled look without heat styling. Simply work the product through your hair with your fingers and scrunch it for added texture.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your short pixie bob looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a protective styling product.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your short pixie bob with confidence and ease. Remember to choose the right cut for your face shape and hair type, and experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the perfect look for you. Happy styling!

How To Grow Long Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of short, lackluster hair? Do you dream of having long, luscious curls that cascade down your back? If so, you're in luck. In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks for growing long curly hair. We'll cover everything from hair care to lifestyle changes that can help you achieve your dream hair. So, grab a cup of tea and let's get started.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. Diet
  3. Lifestyle Changes
  4. FAQ

Hair Care

The first step to growing long curly hair is taking care of the hair you already have. Curly hair is prone to dryness and requires extra moisture to stay healthy. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so you want to avoid using a shampoo that strips your hair of its natural oils. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo that is designed for curly hair.

2. Condition regularly

Conditioning is key to keeping curly hair healthy and moisturized. Use a conditioner that is designed for curly hair and leave it in for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb

Curly hair is more prone to tangles than straight hair, so you want to use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

Diet

The food you eat can also affect the health of your hair. Here are some foods that can promote hair growth:

1. Protein

Protein is essential for hair growth. Make sure you're getting enough protein in your diet by eating foods like chicken, fish, eggs, and beans.

2. Iron

Iron helps the body produce red blood cells, which carry oxygen to the hair follicles. Foods that are high in iron include spinach, lentils, and red meat.

3. Vitamin C

Vitamin C helps the body absorb iron, so it's important to eat foods that are high in vitamin C, such as oranges, strawberries, and broccoli.

Lifestyle Changes

In addition to taking care of your hair and eating a healthy diet, there are some lifestyle changes you can make to promote hair growth:

1. Reduce stress

Stress can affect the health of your hair. Try to reduce stress by practicing relaxation techniques like yoga or meditation.

2. Get enough sleep

Sleep is important for overall health, including the health of your hair. Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep per night.

3. Exercise regularly

Exercise improves circulation, which can promote hair growth. Aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise per day.

FAQ

How long does it take to grow long curly hair?

The rate at which hair grows varies from person to person. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month. So, growing long curly hair can take anywhere from a few months to a few years.

Can I still use heat styling tools?

Heat styling tools can damage curly hair, so it's best to limit their use. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature on the lowest setting possible.

Do I need to trim my hair regularly?

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Conclusion

Growing long curly hair takes time and effort, but it's worth it in the end. By taking care of your hair, eating a healthy diet, and making lifestyle changes, you can achieve the long, luscious curls of your dreams. So, go ahead and start taking care of your hair today.

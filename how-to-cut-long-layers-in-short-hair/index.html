How To Cut Long Layers In Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to add some volume, texture, and movement to your short hair, cutting long layers may be the perfect solution. The layers will help break up any bulk and add dimension to your locks. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps on how to cut long layers in short hair.

Description

Long layers in short hair are a great way to add some personality and style to your hair. The layers will give your hair some volume, texture, and movement. This style is perfect for those who have short hair but want to add some dimension and movement to their locks.

How to Cut Long Layers in Short Hair

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Clip each section of hair to keep it out of the way while you work on the other sections.

Step 3: Start cutting

Start cutting the layers in the back section of your hair. Take a small section of hair and hold it straight up. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the bottom and moving upward. Repeat this process for each section of hair in the back.

Once you've finished cutting the back section, move on to the front sections. Take a small section of hair from the front and hold it straight up. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the bottom and moving upward. Repeat this process for each section of hair in the front.

Step 4: Blend the layers

Blend the layers by taking a comb and running it through your hair from the bottom up. This will help blend the layers and give your hair a more natural look.

Step 5: Style your hair

Style your hair as desired. You can add some mousse or hair spray to give your hair some extra hold and volume.

Tips

- Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts.

- Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.

- Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut less and then go back and cut more if needed.

FAQ

How often should you cut your hair?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Can you cut layers in curly hair?

Yes, you can cut layers in curly hair. However, it's recommended to go to a professional stylist who has experience cutting curly hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.

Additionally, make sure to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

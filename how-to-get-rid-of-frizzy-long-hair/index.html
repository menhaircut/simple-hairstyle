How To Get Rid Of Frizzy Long Hair

Written by Aprilia Jun 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have long hair, you know the struggle of dealing with frizz. Whether it's due to humidity, static, or just naturally unruly hair, frizz can be a pain to deal with. But fear not, there are simple steps you can take to get rid of frizzy long hair and achieve smooth, shiny locks. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about combating frizz, from how to prevent it to the best products to use.

Description

Frizzy hair can be caused by a number of factors, such as humidity, genetics, hair damage, or styling tools. Whether you have naturally curly hair or straight hair, frizz can happen to anyone. It's important to understand the root cause of your frizz in order to effectively combat it. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this article, you can say goodbye to frizzy long hair and hello to silky, smooth locks.

How to Get Rid of Frizzy Long Hair: Step by Step

Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

Start with the basics by using a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for frizzy hair. Look for products that contain hydrating ingredients like coconut oil or argan oil to help smooth and moisturize your hair. Avoid products with sulfates or alcohol as they can strip your hair of its natural oils and exacerbate frizz.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel as this can cause friction and lead to more frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot your hair dry.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

To further hydrate and protect your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil. These products can help smooth hair cuticles and prevent frizz. Apply a small amount to the ends of your hair and work your way up, avoiding the roots.

Step 4: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush. This will help prevent breakage and frizz. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up, gently combing through any tangles.

Step 5: Blow Dry Your Hair

If you need to blow dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment and set it on a low heat setting. This will help distribute heat evenly and minimize frizz. Avoid using a brush while blow drying as this can cause static and frizz. Instead, use your fingers to gently lift and separate your hair.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

If you're styling your hair with heat tools like a straightener or curling iron, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and frizz. Apply the spray to your hair before using any heat tools, and make sure to use a lower heat setting to avoid frying your hair.

Tips for Getting Rid of Frizzy Long Hair

Here are a few additional tips to help you get rid of frizzy long hair:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to deeply hydrate and nourish your hair.
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day as this can transfer oils and cause frizz.

FAQs About Getting Rid of Frizzy Long Hair

Q: Can I use a hair mask instead of conditioner?

A: While a hair mask can provide deep hydration and nourishment to your hair, it's not a substitute for conditioner. You should still use a conditioner after shampooing to help detangle and smooth your hair.

Q: Will cutting my hair help get rid of frizz?

A: While getting a trim can help get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth, it won't necessarily get rid of frizz. You need to address the root cause of your frizz and follow a consistent hair care routine to see results.

Q: Can I use hair spray to prevent frizz?

A: While hair spray can provide hold and control, it won't necessarily prevent frizz. Look for products that are specifically formulated to combat frizz, like a smoothing serum or hair oil.

Hair Care for Frizzy Long Hair

When it comes to getting rid of frizzy long hair, consistency is key. Follow these tips and techniques on a regular basis to help maintain smooth, shiny locks:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair.
  • Towel dry your hair gently.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week.

With these tips and techniques, you can say goodbye to frizzy long hair and hello to silky smooth locks.

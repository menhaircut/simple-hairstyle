How To Use The Revlon Pro Collection One Step Hair Dryer And Volumizer

Written by William Jul 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a way to dry and style your hair quickly and easily, the Revlon Pro Collection One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is a great tool to have. This hair dryer and volumizer combo can help you achieve salon-quality results at home without the need for multiple styling tools.

Description

The Revlon Pro Collection One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is designed to help you dry, smooth, and style your hair in one easy step. It features a unique oval brush design with tufted bristles that help to detangle hair while adding volume and shine. The brush is also designed with airflow vents to help reduce drying time.

The hair dryer and volumizer combo has three heat/speed settings, including a cool setting to help set your style. It also has a 6-foot swivel cord for ease of use.

How to

To use the Revlon Pro Collection One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to help minimize damage from the heat.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using hair clips.

Step 4: Turn On the Hair Dryer

Plug in the hair dryer and turn it on to your desired heat/speed setting.

Step 5: Start Styling

Take one section of hair and place the brush at the root. Slowly move the brush down the length of the hair, pulling it taut. As you reach the end of the hair, rotate the brush away from your head to create a slight curl. Repeat this process with each section of hair until your entire head is styled.

Step 6: Finish with Cool Air

Once you've finished styling your hair, use the cool setting to help set your style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your Revlon Pro Collection One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to help minimize damage from the heat.
  • Section your hair into manageable sections to make styling easier.
  • Start at the root and slowly move the brush down the length of your hair for best results.
  • Use the cool setting to help set your style.
  • Clean the brush regularly to prevent buildup.

FAQ

Q: Can I use the Revlon Pro Collection One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on wet hair?

A: Yes, you can use the hair dryer and volumizer combo on wet hair.

Q: Can I adjust the heat/speed settings?

A: Yes, the hair dryer and volumizer has three heat/speed settings.

Q: Is the brush detachable?

A: No, the brush is not detachable.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and health.

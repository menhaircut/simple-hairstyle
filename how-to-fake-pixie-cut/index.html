How To Fake Pixie Cut A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jun 15, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're feeling adventurous and want to try a shorter hairstyle without actually cutting your hair, then you're in luck. With a few simple tricks and some hair products, you can fake a pixie cut that will have everyone fooled. In this guide, we'll show you how to fake a pixie cut in easy steps.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that typically features short layers and a tapered back. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, edgy look. However, if you're not ready to commit to a pixie cut, you can still enjoy the look without cutting your hair.

How to Fake Pixie Cut

Step 1: Start with clean and dry hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help the products stick to your hair and stay in place.

Step 2: Apply texturizing spray

Apply a texturizing spray to your hair to give it some grip and texture. This will help create the illusion of a shorter haircut.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into two parts: the top and the bottom. Use a hair clip to hold the top section in place.

Step 4: Twist and pin the bottom section

Twist the bottom section of your hair and pin it to the back of your head using bobby pins. This will create the illusion of a shorter haircut.

Step 5: Tease the top section

Tease the top section of your hair to create volume and texture. This will also help hide the fact that you've pinned the bottom section of your hair.

Step 6: Style your hair

Use your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape. You can create a messy, textured look or a sleek, polished look.

Step 7: Finish with hairspray

Finish off your faux pixie cut with a spritz of hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips

- Use a hair gel or pomade to create a more defined look.

- Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

- Don't be afraid to use hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to enhance your faux pixie cut.

FAQ

Can I fake a pixie cut with long hair?

Yes, you can. However, the longer your hair is, the more difficult it will be to create the illusion of a shorter haircut.

How long does a faux pixie cut last?

A faux pixie cut can last for a few hours to a full day, depending on how well you've secured your hair and the type of products you've used.

Can I wash my hair after creating a faux pixie cut?

Yes, you can. However, you may need to reapply some of the products to recreate the look.

Hair Care

Make sure to take care of your hair, even if you're faking a pixie cut. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Overall, faking a pixie cut is a fun and easy way to experiment with shorter hairstyles without committing to a haircut. With these simple steps and tips, you can create a faux pixie cut that looks just as good as the real thing.

How To Get Big Hair With Rollers

Written by Aprilia Sep 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of flat, lifeless hair? Do you want to add some volume and oomph to your locks? Look no further than rollers! With the right technique and some helpful tips, you can achieve big, beautiful hair that will turn heads. Read on for our step-by-step guide on how to get big hair with rollers.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Big Hair with Rollers
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Helpful Tips

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these easy steps to achieve big hair with rollers:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before using rollers. If your hair is damp or wet, the rollers won't be able to set properly, and you won't get the desired volume.

2. Choose the right rollers

The type of rollers you use will depend on your hair type and desired look. Velcro rollers are great for adding volume, while hot rollers are ideal for creating bouncy curls. You can also use foam rollers for a more natural, wavy look.

3. Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to roll each section and ensure that all of your hair gets equal treatment.

4. Roll your hair

Starting at the ends of each section, roll your hair up towards your scalp. Make sure the roller is snug against your head and secure in place. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

5. Let the rollers set

Depending on the type of rollers you're using, you may need to wait anywhere from 15 minutes to an hour for them to fully set. Check the instructions on your rollers for specific timing.

6. Remove the rollers

Once the rollers have set, carefully remove them from your hair. Start at the top of your head and work your way down, gently unraveling each roller.

7. Style your hair

Use your fingers or a brush to gently comb through your hair and style it as desired. You can also use hairspray or other styling products to help hold the volume and shape.

Helpful Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve big hair with rollers:

  • For maximum volume, use larger rollers at the top of your head and smaller rollers towards the bottom.
  • If you have fine or thin hair, use hot rollers to add volume without weighing your hair down.
  • Don't roll your hair too tightly or you risk creating unwanted curls instead of volume.
  • For a more natural look, use foam rollers and wrap your hair around them in a spiral pattern instead of rolling it straight up.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about getting big hair with rollers:

What type of rollers should I use?

The type of rollers you use will depend on your hair type and desired look. Velcro rollers are great for adding volume, while hot rollers are ideal for creating bouncy curls. You can also use foam rollers for a more natural, wavy look.

How long should I leave the rollers in?

Depending on the type of rollers you're using, you may need to wait anywhere from 15 minutes to an hour for them to fully set. Check the instructions on your rollers for specific timing.

How can I make my big hair last all day?

Use hairspray or other styling products to help hold the volume and shape. You can also try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help preserve the volume overnight.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and looking their best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using excessive heat from styling tools like blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve big, beautiful hair with rollers. Happy styling!

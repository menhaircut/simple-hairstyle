What Is A Straight Across Haircut Called

Written by Daniel Sep 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering what a straight across haircut is called in relaxed English language? You're not alone! This type of haircut can have different names depending on where you are in the world, but in the United States it is commonly referred to as a blunt cut. In this article, we will dive into what a blunt cut is, how to achieve it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

Description

A blunt cut is a haircut that is done straight across with no layers or angles. This creates a sharp, clean line that can be worn at any length. It is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous.

How to Achieve a Blunt Cut

To achieve a blunt cut, you can either go to a salon or do it yourself at home. If you're going to a salon, be sure to bring a picture of the style you want so your stylist can understand your vision. If you're doing it at home, make sure you have sharp scissors and a steady hand.

Here are the steps to achieve a blunt cut:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start with clean, dry hair. This will help you see the natural fall of your hair and make it easier to cut straight across.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into four sections: front left, front right, back left, and back right. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut your hair

Starting with the back sections, take a 1-inch section of hair and comb it straight down. Use your scissors to cut straight across. Continue this process for each section of hair until you have cut all of it.

Step 4: Check for evenness

Once you have cut your hair, check to make sure it is even. Stand in front of a mirror and look at your hair from all angles. If you notice any unevenness, trim the offending sections until it's even.

Tips for Maintaining a Blunt Cut

Now that you have achieved your blunt cut, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking sharp and fresh. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with heat tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Is a blunt cut good for thin hair?

Yes, a blunt cut can actually make thin hair look thicker and more voluminous. By cutting straight across, you create the illusion of fullness.

Can you style a blunt cut in different ways?

Absolutely! A blunt cut is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many ways. You can wear it sleek and straight, curly and voluminous, or somewhere in between.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is important for maintaining any haircut, including a blunt cut. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Now that you know what a straight across haircut is called and how to achieve it, you can confidently go to the salon or try it at home. Remember to maintain your haircut properly to keep it looking fresh and sharp. Happy styling!

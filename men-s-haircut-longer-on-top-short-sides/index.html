Men s Haircut Longer On Top Short Sides A Relaxed Guide

Written by Robby Jun 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Looking for a trendy men's haircut that's easy to style and maintain? Look no further than the longer on top, short sides haircut. This versatile style is perfect for guys who want to keep their hair neat and tidy without sacrificing style.

What is a Men's Haircut Longer on Top Short Sides?

A men's haircut longer on top short sides is a hairstyle where the hair on top is kept longer than the hair on the sides and back. This style can be customized to suit any hair type, length, and face shape. The longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, textured, or messy, while the shorter sides and back provide a clean and polished look.

How to Get a Men's Haircut Longer on Top Short Sides

Getting a men's haircut longer on top short sides is easy. Simply visit your local barber or hairstylist and ask for this specific style. Make sure to bring a photo or reference to show your stylist what you're looking for. Your stylist will use clippers or scissors to cut the sides and back short, while leaving the hair on top longer. Depending on your hair type and desired style, your stylist may also add layers or texture to the longer hair on top.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style a men's haircut longer on top short sides:

  1. Wash and towel dry your hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as pomade, wax, or gel, to the longer hair on top.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style the longer hair on top into your desired look.
  4. Use clippers or scissors to trim the sides and back to your desired length.
  5. Use a razor or trimmer to clean up the edges around your hairline and neck.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or finishing product to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining a Men's Haircut Longer on Top Short Sides

Here are some tips for maintaining your men's haircut longer on top short sides:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Apply a styling product to damp hair for best results.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircut longer on top short sides:

  • What hair types work best with this style?
  • Can I still wear a hat with this style?
  • How often should I wash my hair?
  • What if I don't like the way my stylist cuts my hair?

For answers to these questions and more, consult with your hairstylist or barber.

Hair Care for Men's Haircut Longer on Top Short Sides

Maintaining healthy hair is key to pulling off a men's haircut longer on top short sides. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or straighteners, too often.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Visit your hairstylist or barber regularly for trims and maintenance.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking a men's haircut longer on top short sides with ease. Whether you prefer a slicked-back look or a messy, textured style, this versatile haircut is sure to turn heads.

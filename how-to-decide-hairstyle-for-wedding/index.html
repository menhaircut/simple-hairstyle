How To Decide Hairstyle For Wedding

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to preparing for your wedding day, choosing the perfect hairstyle is one of the most important decisions you'll make. Your hairstyle can set the tone for your entire look, and it's important to get it right. In this article, we'll discuss how to decide on the perfect hairstyle for your wedding day.

  • How to Decide on the Perfect Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Your Wedding Hairstyle

How to Decide on the Perfect Hairstyle

Choosing the perfect hairstyle for your wedding day can seem overwhelming, but with a little bit of guidance, you can make the right decision. Here are some things to consider:

1. Dress Style

Your wedding dress style will play a big role in determining your hairstyle. A formal dress may call for an updo, while a more casual dress may work better with loose waves.

2. Venue

The venue you choose can also influence your hairstyle. For example, an outdoor wedding may require a hairstyle that can withstand wind and humidity, while an indoor wedding may allow for more flexibility.

3. Face Shape

Your face shape can also play a role in determining the best hairstyle for you. For example, a round face may look better with an updo, while a heart-shaped face may look better with long, loose curls.

4. Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when choosing a wedding hairstyle. Fine hair may look better with an updo, while thick hair may work better with loose waves.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Your Wedding Hairstyle

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the perfect wedding hairstyle:

Step 1: Research

Start by researching different wedding hairstyles online. Look for styles that complement your dress, venue, face shape, and hair texture.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

Once you have a few styles in mind, consult with your hairstylist. They can help you determine which styles will work best for you and your hair.

Step 3: Schedule a Trial

Schedule a trial run with your stylist to try out your chosen hairstyle. This will give you an idea of how the style will look on your wedding day.

Step 4: Make Adjustments

If you're not happy with your trial run, don't be afraid to make adjustments. Work with your stylist to make changes until you're completely satisfied with your hairstyle.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Wedding Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect wedding hairstyle:

1. Consider Your Personal Style

Your wedding day is a chance to showcase your personal style, so choose a hairstyle that reflects who you are.

2. Keep It Simple

While it's tempting to go all out on your wedding day, sometimes simple is best. Choose a hairstyle that is elegant and timeless.

3. Don't Forget Your Accessories

Your hairstyle can be enhanced with the right accessories. Consider adding a veil, tiara, or flowers to complete your look.

FAQs About Choosing a Wedding Hairstyle

Q: Should I choose a hairstyle that is trendy or classic?

A: It's best to choose a hairstyle that is classic and timeless. Trends come and go, but your wedding photos will last a lifetime.

Q: How soon before my wedding should I choose my hairstyle?

A: It's best to choose your hairstyle at least 3-4 months before your wedding day. This will give you plenty of time to schedule a trial run and make any necessary adjustments.

Q: Should I wash my hair on my wedding day?

A: It's best to wash your hair the day before your wedding. This will give your hair time to settle and make it easier to style on your wedding day.

Hair Care Tips for Your Wedding Day

Here are some hair care tips to help you look your best on your wedding day:

1. Start with Healthy Hair

Healthy hair will look better on your wedding day. In the months leading up to your wedding, take care of your hair by eating a healthy diet and using high-quality hair care products.

2. Don't Overdo It

On your wedding day, it's tempting to use a lot of hair products to keep your hair in place. However, too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

3. Keep it Simple

When it comes to styling your hair on your wedding day, simplicity is key. Choose a hairstyle that is elegant and timeless, and avoid trying anything too complicated.

4. Pack Touch-Up Supplies

Finally, be sure to pack touch-up supplies in case your hair needs a quick fix throughout the day. Bring along bobby pins, hairspray, and a comb to keep your hair looking its best.

Choosing the perfect hairstyle for your wedding day can be stressful, but with these tips, you'll be sure to find a style that complements your dress, venue, and personal style. Remember to start early, schedule a trial run, and keep it simple on your big day. Congratulations and best wishes!

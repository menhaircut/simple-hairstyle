How To See If Bangs Would Look Good On You

Written by Oliver Nov 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering getting bangs, you may be wondering if they will look good on you. Bangs can be a great way to change up your hairstyle and add some personality to your look. However, they're not for everyone. In this article, we'll show you how to see if bangs would look good on you.

Hair Type

The first thing to consider when deciding if bangs would look good on you is your hair type. Bangs work best on hair that is fine to medium in thickness. If you have thick hair, bangs may be difficult to style and manage. If you have curly hair, bangs can be challenging to maintain and may require more styling time.

Face Shape

Your face shape is another important factor to consider. Bangs can be flattering on most face shapes, but the style and length of the bangs can vary depending on your face shape. If you have a round face, consider longer, side-swept bangs to elongate your face. If you have a square face, opt for softer, wispy bangs to soften your features.

Forehead Size

Your forehead size is also something to consider when deciding if bangs would look good on you. If you have a tall forehead, bangs can help balance out your features. If you have a smaller forehead, shorter bangs may be more flattering.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also affect how bangs will look. If you have straight hair, blunt bangs can be a great option. If you have wavy or curly hair, consider getting longer, side-swept bangs. It's also important to consider how your hair will react to humidity and other weather conditions.

Hair Growth Pattern

The way your hair grows can also affect how bangs will look. If you have a cowlick or a natural part in your hair, it may be difficult to style bangs. If your hair grows in a straight line, bangs may be easier to manage.

Face Features

Consider your other facial features when deciding if bangs would look good on you. If you have prominent cheekbones or a strong jawline, bangs can help balance out your features. If you have a small nose or eyes, shorter bangs may be more flattering.

Try Virtual Bangs

If you're still not sure if bangs would look good on you, try using a virtual hairstyle website or app. Upload a photo of yourself and try on different styles of bangs to see how they would look. This can be a great way to get a feel for how you would look with bangs without committing to a haircut.

Consult a Professional

If you're still unsure if bangs would look good on you, consider consulting a professional hairstylist. They can assess your hair type, face shape, and other factors to help determine if bangs would be a good fit for you. They can also recommend specific styles of bangs that would be most flattering.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs

If you decide to get bangs, here are some tips for maintaining them:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to add volume and shape
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them greasy and limp
  • Use dry shampoo to extend the life of your bangs between washes

FAQs

Can anyone pull off bangs?

Bangs can be flattering on most people, but the style and length of the bangs can vary depending on your hair type, face shape, and other factors.

What are some good styles of bangs?

Some popular styles of bangs include blunt, side-swept, wispy, and curtain bangs.

How often should I trim my bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Will bangs work with curly hair?

Yes, but longer, side-swept bangs tend to work best with curly hair.

Can I style my own bangs?

Yes, but it can be challenging to get them to look just right. Consider visiting a professional hairstylist for your first bangs trim.

Overall, deciding if bangs would look good on you requires consideration of several factors, including your hair type, face shape, forehead size, hair texture, hair growth pattern, and other facial features. Try virtual bangs or consult a professional hairstylist to help you make the best decision for your unique look.

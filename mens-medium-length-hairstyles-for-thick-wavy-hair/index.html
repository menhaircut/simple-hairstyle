Mens Medium Length Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Petter Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling with your thick wavy hair? Don't worry, we have got you covered! In this article, we will be discussing some of the best mens medium length hairstyles for thick wavy hair. These hairstyles will not only make you stand out in the crowd but also help you manage your hair with ease. So, let's get started!

Description

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing or a curse depending on how you manage it. While it looks great, it can be challenging to style and maintain. However, with the right hairstyle, you can make the most out of your hair. Medium length hairstyles are perfect for men with thick wavy hair as they help in managing the hair while keeping it stylish.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's first understand how to maintain your thick wavy hair. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use the right products

Using the right products is crucial when it comes to managing your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for thick wavy hair. Also, use a styling product that suits your hair type.

Tip 2: Don't over-wash your hair

Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and frizzy. Instead, wash your hair two to three times a week and use a dry shampoo in between washes.

Tip 3: Get regular haircuts

Getting regular haircuts will not only keep your hair in shape but also help in managing it. A trim every six to eight weeks is ideal.

Step by Step

Now that you know how to maintain your hair let's move on to the hairstyles:

Hairstyle 1: The Classic Side Part

The classic side part is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. It is perfect for men with thick wavy hair as it helps in managing the hair while keeping it stylish. Here's how to achieve this look:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product of your choice.
  3. Use a comb to create a side part.
  4. Blow-dry your hair while combing it to the side.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to keep the style in place.

Hairstyle 2: The Messy Quiff

The messy quiff is a popular hairstyle among men with thick wavy hair. It gives a messy yet stylish look that is perfect for any occasion. Here's how to achieve this look:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product of your choice.
  3. Use your fingers to create a messy quiff on the top of your head.
  4. Blow-dry your hair while combing it upwards.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips that will help you manage your thick wavy hair:

Tip 1: Don't overuse heat styling tools

Using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons can damage your hair. Use them sparingly and always use a heat protectant before styling.

Tip 2: Experiment with different hairstyles

Don't be afraid to try out different hairstyles until you find the one that suits you the best.

Tip 3: Get a good quality comb

A good quality comb will help you manage your hair better and prevent breakage and damage.

FAQ

Q1: Can I use any styling product on my thick wavy hair?

Ans: No, it is important to use a styling product that is specifically designed for thick wavy hair.

Q2: Will blow-drying my hair damage it?

Ans: Blow-drying your hair can cause damage if not done correctly. Always use a heat protectant and keep the dryer at a safe distance from your hair.

Q3: Can I achieve these hairstyles on short hair?

Ans: These hairstyles are best suited for medium length hair. However, you can try them on short hair with the help of a professional stylist.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair is crucial to maintain its health and manageability. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Eat a healthy diet

A healthy diet can do wonders for your hair. Include foods that are rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.

Tip 2: Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Use a hat or a scarf to protect your hair when you're out in the sun.

Tip 3: Don't skip conditioner

Conditioner helps in keeping your hair moisturized and manageable. Use a conditioner every time you wash your hair.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can now manage your thick wavy hair with ease. So, go ahead and try these hairstyles and let us know which one worked for you!

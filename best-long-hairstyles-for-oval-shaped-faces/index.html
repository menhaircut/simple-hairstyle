Description

Oval shaped faces are considered to be the most versatile face shape when it comes to hairstyles. With an oval face, you have the freedom to experiment with different hairstyles and lengths. Long hair, in particular, accentuates the features of an oval face, making it appear longer and more defined. In this article, we will help you choose the best long hairstyles for your oval shaped face, and provide tips on how to care for your long hair.

How to Choose the Best Long Hairstyles for Oval Shaped Faces

When choosing a long hairstyle for your oval face, you want to create balance and proportion. You can achieve this by adding layers, bangs, or waves to your hair. Here are some of the best long hairstyles for oval shaped faces:

Layered Haircuts

Layered haircuts work well for oval shaped faces because they add volume and texture to the hair. You can opt for long layers or short layers depending on your preference. Layers also frame the face, creating a balanced look.

Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are a great option for oval faces because they add depth and dimension to the hair. They also draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones, making them appear more defined.

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a popular long hairstyle for oval shaped faces because they add texture and movement to the hair. They also create a relaxed, effortless look that complements the oval face shape.

Straight Hair with Layers

For those who prefer straight hair, adding layers can create a flattering look for oval shaped faces. The layers add volume and texture to the hair, while the straight style elongates the face.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Oval Shaped Faces

Now that you know the best long hairstyles for oval shaped faces, here is a step by step guide to styling your long hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair
  3. Section your hair into 2-3 inch sections
  4. Use a curling iron or straightener to create waves or straighten your hair
  5. Apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add volume and texture to your hair
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair for Oval Shaped Faces

Here are some tips for maintaining your long hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an oval shaped face?

An oval shaped face is characterized by balanced proportions and a slightly curved jawline. It is considered to be the most versatile face shape when it comes to hairstyles.

What are the best long hairstyles for oval shaped faces?

The best long hairstyles for oval shaped faces include layered haircuts, side swept bangs, beachy waves, and straight hair with layers.

How do I care for my long hair?

To care for your long hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, trim your hair regularly, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and protect your hair from the sun.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for long hair:

  • Brush your hair gently to avoid breakage
  • Avoid using tight hair ties that can cause damage to your hair
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent stripping your hair of natural oils
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth

Conclusion

With an oval shaped face, you have the freedom to experiment with different long hairstyles. Whether you opt for layered haircuts, side swept bangs, beachy waves, or straight hair with layers, the key is to create balance and proportion. By following our tips on how to care for your long hair, you can keep your locks healthy and beautiful.

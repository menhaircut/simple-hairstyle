How To Get Curly Hair Without A Perm Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 25, 2023 · 3 min read

For many guys, curly hair is a highly sought-after look. However, not everyone wants to go through the chemical process of getting a perm. Luckily, there are natural ways to achieve curly hair without resorting to perms. In this article, we’ll show you how to get curly hair without a perm guys, and give you some tips to care for your new locks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curly Hair Without a Perm Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Get Curly Hair Without a Perm Guys

There are several ways to get curly hair without a perm:

  • Twist and curl method
  • Braid method
  • Finger coils method
  • Rod set method
  • Roller set method

In this article, we’ll focus on the twist and curl method, which is one of the easiest and most effective ways to achieve curly hair without a perm.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry your hair until it’s damp but not wet.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product. Use a curl-enhancing product like a mousse, gel, or cream to help define your curls.
  3. Section your hair. Divide your hair into sections, starting at the back of your head and working your way up to the front.
  4. Twist each section. Take a small section of hair and twist it tightly from the root to the end. Repeat this for each section of hair.
  5. Curl each twist. Once each section is twisted, wrap it around your finger to create a curl. Secure each curl with a bobby pin or hair clip.
  6. Let your hair dry. Wait until your hair is completely dry before removing the bobby pins or hair clips.
  7. Unravel each twist. Gently unravel each twist to reveal your new curls.
  8. Fluff your curls. Use your fingers to gently fluff your curls and create volume.

Voila! You now have curly hair without a perm.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your new curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to prevent frizz and maintain your curls’ shape.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your curls from getting tangled and frizzy overnight.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting curly hair without a perm:

Can I use this method on short hair?

Yes, this method works on all hair lengths. Just make sure to twist smaller sections of hair for shorter hair.

How long will the curls last?

The duration of the curls depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, the curls can last anywhere from a few days to a week.

Can I use this method on all hair types?

Yes, this method works on all hair types, but the results may vary depending on your hair texture.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve curly hair without a perm and keep it looking great.

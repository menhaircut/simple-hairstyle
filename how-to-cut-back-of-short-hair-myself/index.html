How To Cut Back Of Short Hair Myself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Cutting the back of your short hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're doing it for the first time. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy cut from the comfort of your own home. In this post, we'll guide you through the process of cutting the back of your short hair yourself, from start to finish.

Description

Cutting your own hair can save you time and money, but it can also be nerve-wracking if you're not sure where to start. The back of your head can be particularly tricky to cut, as you can't see what you're doing. But with some practice and patience, you can master the art of cutting the back of your short hair yourself.

How to Cut Back of Short Hair Myself: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools at hand. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to section off your hair.

Step 2: Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair before you start cutting. This will make it easier to work with and ensure a smoother cut.

Step 3: Section Off Your Hair

Use hair clips to section off your hair, starting from the nape of your neck and working your way up. The sections should be about an inch wide.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Take the first section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way up to the crown of your head.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting, use a mirror to check the back of your head. Make any necessary adjustments to even out the length.

Tips for Cutting Back of Short Hair Myself

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting the back of your short hair yourself:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as this can result in an uneven cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to, to avoid fatigue or strain on your arms.

FAQs

Can I cut the back of my short hair myself?

Yes, you can cut the back of your short hair yourself, but it may take some practice to get the hang of it.

What tools do I need to cut the back of my short hair?

You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to section off your hair.

How do I avoid cutting too much hair?

Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections. This will help you avoid cutting too much hair at once.

Hair Care After Cutting Back of Short Hair Myself

After cutting the back of your short hair yourself, it's important to take good care of your hair. This means washing and conditioning it regularly, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. You can also use hair masks to nourish and strengthen your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to cut the back of your short hair yourself like a pro. Remember to take your time, be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!

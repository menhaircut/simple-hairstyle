How To Start Dreads With Short Hair Sponge

Written by Aprilia Jun 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Starting dreadlocks with short hair sponge can be a challenging task, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the desired results. In this article, we will guide you through the process of starting dreads with short hair sponge, step by step.

Description

A hair sponge is a popular tool used for creating and maintaining dreadlocks. It is made of foam material with small holes, which helps in creating the perfect texture for dreads. With a hair sponge, you can start your dreadlocks even if you have short hair. The process involves twisting and knotting your hair, which eventually forms dreadlocks.

How to Start Dreads with Short Hair Sponge

Creating dreadlocks with short hair sponge requires a few essential tools and techniques. Follow the steps below to start your dreadlocks with a hair sponge.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step is to wash your hair thoroughly with a clarifying shampoo. This will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair, making it easier to work with.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, use a towel to remove excess water. Your hair should be damp, but not soaking wet.

Step 3: Apply Hair Twist Gel

Apply hair twist gel to your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help in creating the perfect texture for your dreadlocks.

Step 4: Use Hair Sponge

Take the hair sponge and hold it against your scalp. Twist the sponge in a circular motion, moving it in different directions. This will create knots and tangles in your hair, which will eventually form dreadlocks.

Step 5: Repeat the Process

Continue twisting and knotting your hair with the hair sponge until you have created the desired number of dreadlocks.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when starting dreads with short hair sponge.

  • Use a good quality hair twist gel for better results.
  • Keep your hair damp throughout the process.
  • Use a hair sponge with smaller holes for tighter dreadlocks.
  • Be patient, as it may take some time to form perfect dreadlocks.

FAQ

How long does it take to start dreadlocks with short hair sponge?

The time it takes to start dreadlocks with short hair sponge varies depending on the length and thickness of your hair. It may take several hours or even days to create perfect dreadlocks.

Can I start dreadlocks with short hair?

Yes, you can start dreadlocks with short hair using a hair sponge.

How do I maintain my dreadlocks?

To maintain your dreadlocks, you should wash them regularly with a residue-free shampoo, and use a hair oil or spray to keep them moisturized.

Hair Care

Dreadlocks require special care to keep them healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips for dreadlocks.

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Use a hair oil or spray to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heavy products or oils that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz.

Starting dreadlocks with short hair sponge is a great way to achieve a unique and stylish look. With the right tools and techniques, you can create perfect dreadlocks that will last for years. Follow the steps and tips in this article to start your dreadlocks with short hair sponge.

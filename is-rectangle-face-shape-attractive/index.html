Is Rectangle Face Shape Attractive

Written by Daniel Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering if a rectangle face shape is attractive? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about a rectangle face shape, including how to enhance your features and hair care tips. Let's dive in!

Description

A rectangle face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape with a strong jawline and forehead. This face shape is often compared to an oval face shape but with more defined features. People with a rectangle face shape typically have a straight hairline and a square chin.

How To Enhance Your Features

While beauty is subjective, there are ways to enhance your features if you have a rectangle face shape. Here are some tips:

Hairstyles

When it comes to hairstyles, the goal is to soften your features. Soft layers, waves, and curls can help balance out your strong jawline and forehead. Avoid straight and blunt hairstyles, as they can make your face appear more angular. A side-swept bang can also help create the illusion of a shorter forehead.

Makeup

Soft and natural makeup can work well for rectangle face shapes. Focus on highlighting your cheekbones with a blush or bronzer. A nude lip and a subtle eye look can also complement your features. Avoid harsh contouring and heavy eye makeup, as it can make your face appear more angular.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to enhance your features if you have a rectangle face shape:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can enhance your features, you need to determine your face shape. Look in the mirror and assess the length and width of your face. If your face is longer than it is wide and you have a strong jawline and forehead, you likely have a rectangle face shape.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that softens your features, such as soft layers, waves, and curls. Avoid straight and blunt hairstyles, as they can make your face appear more angular. A side-swept bang can also help create the illusion of a shorter forehead.

Step 3: Apply Soft Makeup

Apply soft and natural makeup, such as a blush or bronzer to highlight your cheekbones. A nude lip and a subtle eye look can also complement your features. Avoid harsh contouring and heavy eye makeup, as it can make your face appear more angular.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for enhancing your features if you have a rectangle face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can elongate your face.
  • Experiment with different partings, such as a middle part or a deep side part, to see what works best for you.
  • Choose glasses and sunglasses that have rounded frames to soften your features.
  • Consider facial hair, such as a beard or goatee, to add definition to your face.

FAQ

What celebrities have a rectangle face shape?

Some celebrities with a rectangle face shape include Angelina Jolie, Olivia Wilde, and Rihanna.

Can I still have short hair if I have a rectangle face shape?

Yes, you can still have short hair if you have a rectangle face shape. Just make sure to avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can elongate your face.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your hair's health and shape.
  • Consider using hair oils or leave-in treatments to keep your hair moisturized.

Conclusion

In conclusion, a rectangle face shape can be just as attractive as any other face shape. By choosing the right hairstyle, makeup, and hair care routine, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your own skin. Remember, beauty comes in all shapes and sizes!

How To Find A Hairstylist On Instagram

Written by Christine Nov 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you in the market for a new hairstylist but don't know where to start? Look no further than Instagram! With over 1 billion active users, Instagram is a great platform for finding talented hairstylists in your area. In this blog post, we'll show you how to find a hairstylist on Instagram and provide tips for choosing the perfect one.

How to Find a Hairstylist on Instagram

1. Use relevant hashtags - The easiest way to find a hairstylist on Instagram is by using hashtags. Try searching for hashtags like #hairstylist or #haircut and see what comes up. You can also add your location to the hashtag search to find local hairstylists.

2. Explore Instagram's 'Discover' page - Instagram's Discover page uses an algorithm to show you content that you may be interested in based on your activity on the app. You may find hairstylists in your area under the 'Beauty' or 'Style' categories.

3. Look for local salon pages - Many salons have their own Instagram pages where they showcase their work and tag their hairstylists. This is a great way to find a hairstylist who works in a reputable salon.

Step by Step Guide

1. Open your Instagram app and go to the search bar.

2. Type in relevant hashtags like #hairstylist or #haircut.

3. Browse through the results and check out the profiles of hairstylists that catch your eye.

4. Look for local salon pages and check out their tagged photos to see the work of their hairstylists.

5. Once you've found a few hairstylists that you're interested in, take a closer look at their profile to get a better idea of their work and style.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstylist

1. Check their portfolio - Make sure to look at their portfolio to see their previous work. This will give you an idea of their style and skill level.

2. Read reviews - Check out their reviews on Instagram or other platforms like Yelp to see what other clients have to say about their experience.

3. Schedule a consultation - If you're unsure about a hairstylist, schedule a consultation before making an appointment. This will give you a chance to discuss your needs and expectations and see if you're a good fit.

FAQ

Q: How do I know if a hairstylist is licensed?

A: You can check if a hairstylist is licensed by contacting your state's cosmetology board or checking their website.

Q: How much should I expect to pay for a haircut?

A: Prices can vary depending on the hairstylist and location. It's always best to check with the hairstylist or salon beforehand.

Hair Care

Remember, finding the right hairstylist is just one part of maintaining healthy hair. Make sure to take care of your hair at home by using quality products and avoiding heat damage. Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can also help keep your hair looking its best.

With these tips, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstylist on Instagram in no time. Happy hair hunting!

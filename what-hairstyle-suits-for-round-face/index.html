What Hairstyle Suits For Round Face A Complete Guide

In 2023, finding the perfect hairstyle for your round face is easier than ever. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. But don't worry, we've got you covered. Read on to discover the best hairstyles for round faces, how to achieve them, and hair care tips to maintain your gorgeous locks.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Description

A round face has full cheeks and a rounded chin, with the width and length of the face being almost equal. If you have a round face, you want to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face and make it appear less round. The best hairstyles for round faces are those that add volume at the crown and lengthen the face.

How To

The first step in achieving the perfect hairstyle for your round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing the two. If they are almost equal, you have a round face.

Next, consider the features of your face that you want to enhance or downplay. If you have a round face and a short neck, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds length to your neck. If you have a round face and a prominent forehead, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the crown of your head.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of the best hairstyles for round faces:

1. Long Layers

- Start with clean, dry hair.

- Create a center part.

- Use a curling iron to add loose waves to the ends of your hair.

- Apply a volumizing spray to the roots of your hair.

- Tease the crown of your head to add volume.

- Finish with a light hold hairspray.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

- Start with clean, dry hair.

- Create a deep side part.

- Blow-dry your hair straight.

- Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.

- Cut side-swept bangs that hit just below your eyebrows.

- Finish with a light hold hairspray.

3. Pixie Cut

- Start with clean, dry hair.

- Create a deep side part.

- Use a razor to cut your hair into a short, choppy pixie cut.

- Apply a styling cream to the roots of your hair.

- Use your fingers to create texture and volume.

- Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your round face:

- Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides of your face, as this will make your face appear wider. - Choose hairstyles that add height at the crown of your head, as this will elongate your face. - Experiment with different hairstyles and lengths to find what works best for you. - Consider the texture and thickness of your hair when choosing a hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for round faces:

1. What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides of your face, such as blunt bobs and blunt bangs. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear wider.

2. What hairstyles are best for a round face?

The best hairstyles for a round face are those that add height at the crown of your head and lengthen your face. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and pixie cuts are all great options.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy, beautiful hair, follow these hair care tips:

- Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils. - Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. - Apply a weekly hair mask to nourish and hydrate your hair. - Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible. - Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know what hairstyle suits for round face, you can confidently choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel beautiful. Remember to consider your face shape, features, and hair texture when choosing a hairstyle, and follow our hair care tips to keep your locks looking their best.

Best Short Bob Haircuts For Round Faces

Written by Aprilia Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may find it challenging to find the right haircut that flatters your features. However, a short bob is the perfect haircut for round faces as it can make your face look slimmer and more defined. In this post, we will be discussing the best short bob haircuts for round faces that you can try in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Short Bob Haircut for Your Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Short Bob Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Bob Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Bob Haircuts

How to Choose the Right Short Bob Haircut for Your Round Face

When it comes to choosing the right short bob haircut for your round face, there are a few things to consider. The first thing to consider is the length of your bob. A shorter bob that falls above your chin can help elongate your face, making it appear slimmer. On the other hand, a longer bob that falls below your chin can make your face look wider.

The second thing to consider is the shape of your bob. A bob with layers can help add texture and movement to your hair, while a blunt cut can make your face look rounder. Additionally, a bob with side-swept bangs can help add length to your face.

Lastly, consider your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, a shorter bob can help add volume to your hair. If you have thick hair, a longer bob can help weigh down your hair and prevent it from looking too bulky.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Short Bob Haircut

Styling your short bob haircut is easy and can be done in just a few steps.

  1. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the roots to add volume.
  3. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and movement to your hair.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Bob Haircut

Maintaining your short bob haircut is important to ensure that it always looks its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape of your bob and keep it looking fresh.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove any buildup from hair products.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

FAQs

Q: What is the best short bob haircut for a round face?

A: The best short bob haircut for a round face is a chin-length bob with layers and side-swept bangs.

Q: Can I wear a short bob if I have a double chin?

A: Yes, you can wear a short bob if you have a double chin. However, consider opting for a longer bob that falls below your chin to help elongate your face.

Q: How often should I trim my short bob?

A: It is recommended to trim your short bob every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Short Bob Haircuts

To keep your short bob looking healthy and shiny, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet to prevent breakage.

In conclusion, a short bob is the perfect haircut for round faces as it can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. By following the tips and advice in this post, you can find the perfect short bob haircut for your face shape and maintain it for a healthy and beautiful look.

Hairstyles For Round Faces 2021 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Oct 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles but none of them seem to suit your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll show you the best hairstyles for round faces in 2021 that will flatter your features and enhance your beauty. From short to long, curly to straight, we've got a hairstyle for every preference. So, let's get started!

Description

Round faces have soft features and a circular shape, which can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that complements them. However, with the right cut and styling, you can highlight your best features and create a balanced look. In this guide, we'll explore hairstyles that can help elongate your face, add volume to your hair, and create a slimming effect.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Round Face

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's take a look at how to choose the right one for your face shape.

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Firstly, you need to determine whether you have a round face or not. To do this, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are roughly the same, you have a round face shape.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture and Length

Your hair texture and length can also affect how a hairstyle looks on you. For example, if you have thin hair, you may want to avoid very short hairstyles that can make your face look rounder. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you can experiment with more voluminous styles.

3. Look for Styles That Create Height and Length

Hairstyles that add height and length to your face can help balance out your features. This can be achieved through layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and hairstyles with volume at the crown.

Top Hairstyles for Round Faces 2021

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle, let's take a look at some of the best hairstyles for round faces in 2021.

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help create a slimming effect on your face and add movement to your hair. You can wear your hair straight or add loose waves for a more relaxed look.

2. Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob with layers can also help elongate your face while adding volume to your hair. You can style it straight or add some texture with loose curls.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help create a diagonal line across your face, which can help make it appear longer. They can be paired with any hairstyle, from long layers to a short pixie cut.

4. Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut can also be flattering for round faces, especially if it has longer layers on top. This can help create height and add dimension to your hair.

5. High Ponytail

A high ponytail can help create the illusion of a longer face. You can also add some volume at the crown to balance out your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for a Round Face

Now that you have some hairstyle ideas, let's take a look at how to style them.

1. Long Layers

To style long layers, start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair. Then, use a straightening iron to create loose waves. You can also use a curling iron for a more defined look. Finish with a light hairspray.

2. Shoulder-Length Bob

To style a shoulder-length bob, start by applying a volumizing mousse to your hair. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and smoothness. You can also add some loose curls with a curling iron for a more textured look. Finish with a medium hold hairspray.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

To style side-swept bangs, start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush. Then, use a flat iron to straighten your bangs and create a diagonal line across your face. Finish with a light hairspray.

4. Pixie Cut

To style a pixie cut, start by applying a styling cream to your hair. Use your fingers to create texture and add volume at the crown. You can also use a flat iron to add some definition to your hair. Finish with a light hairspray.

5. High Ponytail

To style a high ponytail, start by applying a volumizing mousse to your hair. Pull your hair back into a high ponytail, securing it with an elastic band. You can also add some volume at the crown by teasing your hair with a comb. Finish with a medium hold hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking great:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid over-drying
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from environmental damage
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

You should avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs, short bobs, and hairstyles with too much volume at the sides.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face. However, make sure to add some volume at the crown to balance out your features.

Can I wear a center part with a round face?

Yes, you can wear a center part with a round face. However, make sure to add some texture and volume to your hair to create a more balanced look.

Conclusion

There you have it, the ultimate guide to hairstyles for round faces in 2021. We hope this guide has given you some inspiration and ideas for your next hairstyle. Remember to choose a style that flatters your features and suits your hair texture and length. With the right cut and styling, you can enhance your beauty and feel confident in your look.

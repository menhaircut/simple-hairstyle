What To Say For A Two Block Haircut A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Oct 26, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you thinking of getting a two block haircut but don't know what to say to your barber? Look no further! In this article, we'll provide you with a complete guide on what to say for a two block haircut. From descriptions to tips and hair care, we've got you covered.

Description

A two block haircut is a popular Korean hairstyle that features short sides and back with longer hair on top. The longer hair on top is usually styled upwards or slicked back. It's a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to Get a Two Block Haircut

Before you go to the barber, it's important to have a clear idea of what you want. Here's what to say for a two block haircut:

Step 1: Describe the Length

The first step is to describe the length of the hair on top. Do you want it short, medium, or long? Keep in mind that the length should be longer than the sides and back.

Step 2: Specify the Style

Next, specify the style you want. Do you want your hair styled upwards or slicked back? This will help your barber understand how to cut and style your hair.

Step 3: Discuss the Sides and Back

Lastly, discuss the length of the sides and back. The sides and back should be short, but how short is up to you. You can ask for a fade, taper, or even a buzz cut.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a two block haircut:

  • Bring a picture: If you have a specific style in mind, bring a picture to show your barber.
  • Communicate clearly: Make sure to communicate clearly with your barber to avoid any misunderstandings.
  • Maintain your haircut: To keep your two block haircut looking fresh, make sure to get regular trims and use hair products to style it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about two block haircuts:

How often should I get a two block haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the sides and back start to grow out. On average, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Can I style my two block haircut differently?

Yes! Two block haircuts are versatile and can be styled in different ways. You can try combing it to the side, adding texture, or even wearing it messy.

Hair Care

To keep your two block haircut looking fresh, make sure to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use hair products, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

In conclusion, getting a two block haircut is easy as long as you know what to say to your barber. By following our guide, you'll be able to describe the style and length you want, and communicate clearly with your barber. Remember to maintain your haircut and take care of your hair to keep it looking fresh.

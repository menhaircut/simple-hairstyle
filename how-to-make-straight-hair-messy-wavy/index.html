How To Make Straight Hair Messy Wavy A Step By Step Guide

How to Make Straight Hair Messy Wavy

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first understand how to make straight hair messy wavy. Messy waves are achieved by creating loose, undone waves that look effortless and natural. The key is to use the right styling tools, products, and techniques to create the perfect balance between volume, texture, and movement.

Tools and Products You Will Need

You will need the following tools and products to get started:

  • 1-inch curling iron or wand
  • Hairspray
  • Texturizing spray
  • Sea salt spray
  • Heat protectant spray or serum
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Clips or bobby pins

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make straight hair messy wavy:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  2. Using a wide-tooth comb, detangle your hair to remove any knots or tangles. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up.
  3. Take a 1-inch section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand, leaving the ends out. Hold for 10-15 seconds and release.
  4. Repeat step 3 with the remaining sections, alternating the direction of the curls to create a more natural, messy look.
  5. Once all the sections are curled, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create a more undone look.
  6. Spray a texturizing spray or sea salt spray onto your hair to add volume, texture, and hold.
  7. Flip your hair upside down and scrunch it up to create more movement and volume.
  8. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the waves in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Messy Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating messy wavy hair:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a 1-inch barrel for a more natural and relaxed look.
  • Alternate the direction of the curls to create more texture and movement.
  • Use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add volume, texture, and hold.
  • Flip your hair upside down and scrunch it up to create more volume and movement.
  • Don't overdo it with the hairspray, as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

FAQs

How long does messy wavy hair last?

Messy wavy hair can last for up to 2-3 days if you use the right products and techniques to maintain it. You can refresh your waves with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray on the second or third day.

Can I create messy wavy hair without heat?

Yes, you can create messy wavy hair without heat by braiding your hair overnight and unraveling it in the morning. You can also use a headband to create loose waves or twist your hair into a bun for a more relaxed look.

Hair Care for Messy Wavy Hair

Messy wavy hair requires proper care and maintenance to keep it healthy, shiny, and voluminous. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too often, as it can break your curls and create frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent tangles and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily make your straight hair messy wavy and achieve a relaxed, effortless, and natural look. So, go ahead and try it out!

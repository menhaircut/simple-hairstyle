How To Make Long Hair Look Short Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Sep 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have long hair, you may have considered cutting it short at some point. However, you may not be ready to commit to a drastic change. The good news is that you can fake a short hair look without actually cutting your hair. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to make long hair look short.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Long Hair Look Short
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

How to Make Long Hair Look Short

There are several ways to make long hair look short:

  • Use hair accessories
  • Create a faux bob
  • Tie your hair in a high ponytail
  • Style your hair in a messy bun
  • Try a half-up, half-down hairstyle

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to make long hair look short:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. If you want to create a faux bob, start by curling your hair with a curling iron. Then, tie your hair in a low ponytail and tuck it under to create a bob-like shape. Secure with bobby pins.
  3. If you want to tie your hair in a high ponytail, start by brushing your hair back and securing it with an elastic band. Then, use bobby pins to create a messy bun on top of your head.
  4. If you want to style your hair in a messy bun, start by tying your hair in a high ponytail. Then, twist your hair and wrap it around the elastic band. Secure with bobby pins.
  5. If you want to try a half-up, half-down hairstyle, start by parting your hair in the middle. Then, tie the top half of your hair in a ponytail and leave the bottom half loose.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make long hair look short:

  • Use hair accessories like headbands, scarves, and barrettes to create a shorter-looking hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to see which one looks best on you.
  • Use hair products like texturizing spray and dry shampoo to add volume to your hair.
  • Consider getting a shorter haircut if you're ready for a change.

FAQ

Q. Will these hairstyles damage my hair?

A. No, these hairstyles are gentle on your hair and won't cause damage.

Q. How long do these hairstyles last?

A. These hairstyles can last all day with the help of bobby pins and hairspray.

Q. Can I create these hairstyles on curly hair?

A. Yes, you can create these hairstyles on curly hair. Just make sure to use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls.

Hair Care Tips

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Now that you know how to make long hair look short, you can experiment with different hairstyles and see which one you like best. Remember to take care of your hair by washing it regularly and getting regular trims.

Can You Start Dreadlocks With Relaxed Hair

Written by Bardi Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a person who loves changing up their hairstyle, you may have wondered if you can start dreadlocks with relaxed hair. Fortunately, the answer is yes! In this article, we'll discuss how to start dreadlocks with relaxed hair, step by step guide, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

Description:

Dreadlocks are a popular hairstyle that has been around for centuries. They are a fun and unique way to express your personality and style. However, starting dreadlocks can be a bit tricky, especially if you have relaxed hair.

How to Start Dreadlocks with Relaxed Hair:

Step 1: Decide on the Size of Your Dreadlocks

The first step in starting dreadlocks with relaxed hair is deciding on the size you want them to be. You can choose thin or thick dreadlocks, depending on your preference.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before starting your dreadlocks, you need to wash your hair thoroughly. Clean hair will make the process easier and prevent any dirt or oil from getting trapped in your new dreadlocks.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, you need to section your hair into equal parts. Use a comb to create neat, even sections. This will make it easier to create and maintain your dreadlocks.

Step 4: Apply Dreadlock Wax or Gel

To start your dreadlocks, you'll need to apply dreadlock wax or gel to each section of your hair. This will help to hold the hair together and create a firm hold.

Step 5: Begin Twisting Your Hair

Once you've applied the wax or gel, you can start twisting your hair into dreadlocks. Twist each section of hair in a clockwise motion until it starts to form a dreadlock. Continue this process until all sections of hair are twisted into dreadlocks.

Step 6: Use a Hair Tie

After twisting each section of hair, use a hair tie to secure it in place. This will help to keep the hair in place and prevent it from unraveling.

Step 7: Repeat the Process

Continue this process until all sections of hair are twisted and tied. It may take several hours to complete, depending on the size and thickness of your dreadlocks.

Tips:

Starting dreadlocks with relaxed hair can be a challenging process. Here are some tips to help you along the way:

  • Use a comb to create even sections of hair
  • Apply dreadlock wax or gel to each section of hair
  • Twist each section of hair in a clockwise motion
  • Use a hair tie to secure each dreadlock in place
  • Be patient â the process can take several hours

FAQ:

Can you start dreadlocks with relaxed hair?

Yes, you can start dreadlocks with relaxed hair. Follow the steps above to create your new hairstyle.

How long does it take to start dreadlocks with relaxed hair?

The time it takes to start dreadlocks with relaxed hair depends on the size and thickness of your hair. It can take several hours to complete the process.

How do you maintain dreadlocks with relaxed hair?

To maintain your dreadlocks with relaxed hair, you should wash them regularly and use a dreadlock wax or gel to keep them in place. You can also visit a stylist to have them retwisted and maintained.

Hair Care:

Starting dreadlocks with relaxed hair requires proper hair care to maintain. Here are some tips to keep your dreadlocks healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a dreadlock wax or gel to keep them in place
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your hair
  • Visit a stylist for regular maintenance

Starting dreadlocks with relaxed hair is a fun and unique way to express your style. By following the steps above and taking care of your hair, you can maintain your new hairstyle for years to come.

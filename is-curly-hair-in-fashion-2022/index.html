Is Curly Hair In Fashion 2022 A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jul 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you're in luck! Curly hair is definitely in fashion in 2022, and it's a trend that's set to continue in 2023. Whether you're a natural curly girl or you're looking to create curls, this guide will give you all the information you need to rock the curly hair trend.

Description

Curly hair has been in fashion for years, but it's becoming even more popular in 2022. From tight ringlets to loose beachy waves, there are so many different ways to wear curly hair. Celebrities like Zendaya, Tracee Ellis Ross, and Solange Knowles have all been seen sporting their natural curls on the red carpet, and it's a trend that's trickling down to everyday women.

How to Get Curly Hair

If you don't have naturally curly hair, there are plenty of ways to create curls. Here are a few methods:

1. Curling Iron

A curling iron is a classic tool for creating curls. To use it, divide your hair into sections, and wrap each section around the curling iron barrel. Hold for a few seconds, then release. Repeat until all your hair is curled.

2. Curling Wand

A curling wand is similar to a curling iron, but it doesn't have a clamp. To use it, wrap your hair around the wand and hold for a few seconds, then release. Repeat until all your hair is curled.

3. Rollers

Rollers come in all shapes and sizes, and they're a great way to create curls without heat. To use them, wrap sections of damp hair around the rollers and secure with clips. Leave them in for a few hours or overnight, then remove the rollers and enjoy your curls.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair

Styling curly hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right techniques, you can achieve gorgeous, defined curls. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair. Work it through your hair from roots to tips, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser up to your scalp and move it around in a circular motion to dry your roots. Then, move the diffuser down to the ends of your hair, scrunching as you go.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, scrunch it with your hands to encourage your curls. You can also use a small amount of curl-defining cream or gel to redefine any curls that have fallen flat.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a little extra TLC to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Curly hair tends to be dry, so you don't need to wash it as often as straight hair. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or even less if your hair is particularly dry.

2. Use a Satin Pillowcase

Cotton pillowcases can cause friction and lead to frizz. Switch to a satin pillowcase to keep your curls looking smooth.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and lead to frizz. Try to avoid using heat tools as much as possible, or use a heat protectant spray if you do need to use them.

FAQs

Is curly hair in fashion in 2022?

Yes, curly hair is definitely in fashion in 2022, and it's a trend that's set to continue in 2023.

What are some popular curly hairstyles?

Some popular curly hairstyles include curly bobs, long loose curls, and afros.

How do I keep my curls looking defined?

Use a curl-defining cream or gel, and avoid overwashing your hair. Scrunch your curls with your hands to encourage them.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a little extra care to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are a few tips:

1. Use a Deep Conditioner

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it needs extra moisture. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your curls hydrated.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Curly hair is prone to split ends, so it's important to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair, so wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your curls.

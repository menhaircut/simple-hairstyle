How Do I Get My Curls Back After Straightening

Written by Bardi Jul 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have recently straightened your curly hair and are wondering how to get those curls back, you're in the right place! In this article, we will go over some tips and tricks to help you regain your natural curls after straightening. We will cover everything from how to care for your hair to specific styling techniques that can help you achieve those curls.

Description

Straightening your hair can be a fun way to switch up your look, but it can also be damaging to your curls. Heat styling tools like flat irons and blow dryers can cause breakage, frizz, and dryness, all of which can make it difficult to get your curls back. However, with some patience and care, you can revive your curls and get them looking their best again.

How to Get Your Curls Back

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in getting your curls back is to wash your hair. Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Follow up with a nourishing conditioner to help restore moisture and hydration to your curls.

Step 2: Deep Condition

After washing your hair, use a deep conditioning treatment to help repair any damage caused by heat styling. Apply the treatment to your hair and leave it on for at least 10-15 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling

To prevent further damage to your curls, avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and blow dryers. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz.

Step 4: Use a Curl-Enhancing Product

To help define your curls and keep them looking their best, use a curl-enhancing product like a curl cream or gel. Apply the product to your hair while it's still damp, scrunching it in from the ends to the roots.

Step 5: Try a Protective Style

If you want to give your hair a break from heat styling and protect your curls, try a protective style like braids or twists. These styles can help prevent breakage and keep your curls looking healthy and hydrated.

Tips for Getting Your Curls Back

Here are some additional tips to help you get your curls back:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach or relaxers on your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls to prevent breakage

FAQ

How long does it take to get your curls back after straightening?

The time it takes to get your curls back after straightening will vary depending on the individual. Some people may see their curls return immediately, while others may take a few weeks or even months to fully regain their curls.

Can you get your curls back after heat damage?

Yes, it is possible to get your curls back after heat damage, but it may take some time and effort. Follow the steps outlined in this article to help repair your hair and revive your curls.

What products should I use to get my curls back?

Use a sulfate-free shampoo, a nourishing conditioner, and a curl-enhancing product like a curl cream or gel. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair and avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or alcohol.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can keep your curls looking their best. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than once or twice a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls while they're wet
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and blow dryers
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz

By following these tips and techniques, you can keep your curls looking healthy, hydrated, and beautiful!

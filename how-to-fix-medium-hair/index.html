How To Fix Medium Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Medium hair can be both a blessing and a curse. It's long enough to experiment with different styles, yet short enough to avoid damage from over-styling. However, finding the right style and fixing it can be challenging. In this post, we'll show you how to fix medium hair and give you some tips on how to maintain it.

  1. How to Fix Medium Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Medium Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Medium Hair

How to Fix Medium Hair

Before you start fixing your medium hair, you need to determine your hair type and face shape. Knowing your hair type will help you choose the right products, while understanding your face shape will help you pick the most flattering hairstyle.

Once you've determined your hair type and face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle. There are several hairstyles that work well with medium hair, including:

  • Bob
  • Layered
  • Wavy
  • Straight
  • Bangs

Choose the one that suits your face shape and personality. Once you've decided on a hairstyle, it's time to fix your medium hair.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to fix your medium hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to avoid damage from heat styling.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  5. Straighten or curl your hair using a flat iron or curling iron, depending on the style you want.
  6. Apply a finishing spray to hold your style in place.

Remember not to over-style your hair, as this can damage it. Use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat styling tools every day.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium hair:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQs

Q: Can I fix my medium hair without using heat styling tools?

A: Yes, you can. There are several no-heat styling methods you can try, such as braiding or twisting your hair overnight to create curls.

Q: How do I choose a hairstyle for my face shape?

A: There are several online guides that can help you determine your face shape and suggest the most flattering hairstyles.

Hair Care for Medium Hair

To keep your medium hair healthy and strong, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and cause buildup.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can fix your medium hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

What Hairstyle Suits Me A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Look

Written by William Jul 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Do you want to find a hairstyle that suits your face shape, personality, and lifestyle? Look no further, because in this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect hairstyle that suits you. From different face shapes to hair textures, we will cover everything you need to know to find your ideal look. So, let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back from your face and look in the mirror. You can also trace the outline of your face on a piece of paper and measure the length and width of your face.

If your face is oval, you have a balanced face shape with a slightly rounded forehead and jawline. If your face is round, you have a soft, circular shape with no angles. If your face is square, you have a strong jawline and forehead with a square shape. If your face is heart-shaped, you have a wider forehead and a pointed chin. If your face is diamond-shaped, you have a narrow forehead and jawline with wide cheekbones. If your face is oblong, you have a long, narrow shape with a high forehead and narrow jawline.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find your perfect hairstyle. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Consider Your Hair Texture

The first thing to consider is your hair texture. Do you have straight, wavy, or curly hair? This will determine what hairstyles will work best for you. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to avoid short, choppy cuts that will make your hair look frizzy. Instead, opt for longer, layered cuts that will enhance your curls.

Step 2: Look for Inspiration

Next, look for inspiration. Browse through magazines, Pinterest boards, and Instagram pages to find hairstyles that you like. Save or bookmark your favorite looks to show to your hairstylist.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your hairstyle should also reflect your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance cut that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles, you may want to choose a more versatile cut.

Step 4: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Finally, consult with your hairstylist. They will be able to help you determine what hairstyles will work best for your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. They may also suggest different cuts and styles that you haven't considered before.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found your perfect hairstyle, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use high-quality hair products that are suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the perfect hairstyle:

What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you don't like your new hairstyle, don't worry. Hair grows back, and you can always try a different cut or style. However, give your new hairstyle some time to grow on you before making any drastic changes.

Can I pull off a short hairstyle?

Short hairstyles can be flattering on many different face shapes and hair types. Consult with your hairstylist to determine what length and style will work best for you.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care: Final Thoughts

Finding the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance and inspiration, you can find a look that suits you. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a new cut or style. And don't forget to maintain your hair with regular trims and high-quality products. With these tips, you'll be well on your way to finding your perfect hairstyle!

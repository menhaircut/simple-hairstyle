What To Do With Thick Curly Hair Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thick curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it can be voluminous and eye-catching. On the other hand, it can be unruly and hard to manage. If you're a male with thick curly hair, you may be wondering what to do with it. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you tame your curls and make the most of your hair.

How to Style Thick Curly Hair

When it comes to styling thick curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you'll want to choose a haircut that complements your curls. A layered cut can help to control volume and add definition to your curls. You may also want to consider getting your hair thinned out to reduce bulk.

Once you have the right cut, you can experiment with different styling techniques. Here are a few ideas:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product, such as mousse or gel, to define your curls.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Create a messy, tousled look by applying a texturizing spray and scrunching your hair.
  • For a sleeker look, use a straightening iron to smooth out your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to styling your thick curly hair, here's a step-by-step guide to get you started:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently, being careful not to rub too hard and cause frizz.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as mousse or gel, to your damp hair.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your hair with your hands as you go.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray to create a messy, tousled look.
  6. Alternatively, use a straightening iron to smooth out your curls for a sleeker look.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Managing Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you manage your thick curly hair:

  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause frizz.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush or comb with wide teeth to detangle your hair without causing breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straightening irons, too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about managing thick curly hair:

Should I brush or comb my curly hair?

You should use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it's wet, and a hairbrush with natural bristles to smooth it when it's dry.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause frizz.

What products should I use on my curly hair?

You should use products designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing mousse or gel, a leave-in conditioner, and a texturizing spray.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Caring for your thick curly hair is important for keeping it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as this can cause frizz. Instead, gently squeeze out excess water with a microfiber towel.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straightening irons, too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

With these tips and tricks, you can make the most of your thick curly hair and keep it looking healthy and stylish.

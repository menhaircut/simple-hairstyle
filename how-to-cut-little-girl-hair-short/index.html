How To Cut Little Girl Hair Short A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Cutting a little girl’s hair short can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a breeze. In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of cutting your little girl’s hair short, step-by-step. We’ll also provide tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect cut.

Table of Contents

  • Tools Needed
  • Prepping for the Cut
  • Cutting the Hair
  • Styling the Hair
  • Tips for Cutting Little Girl Hair Short
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

Tools Needed

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools on hand. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Prepping for the Cut

Before you start cutting, make sure your little girl’s hair is clean and dry. Comb out any tangles and part the hair as desired. Use hair clips to section off the hair you’re not cutting.

Step 1: Wet the Hair

Use a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair. This will make it easier to cut and will also help you see any uneven areas.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Use hair clips to section off the hair you’re not cutting. This will help you see and cut each section more easily.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Take a small section of hair and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it with sharp scissors. Cut in a straight line, following the shape of the head. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way around the head.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After you’ve cut all the sections, check for evenness. If you notice any uneven areas, trim them with scissors until the hair is even.

Styling the Hair

Once you’ve cut the hair, you can style it as desired. For a simple, classic look, comb the hair straight and add a hair accessory like a bow or headband. For a more modern look, add layers or texture with scissors or a razor.

Tips for Cutting Little Girl Hair Short

Cutting little girl hair short can be challenging, but with these tips, you can make it easier:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way around the head.
  • Use a comb to hold the hair in place as you cut.
  • Don’t cut too much at once – it’s better to cut a little bit and then trim as needed.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid pulling or damaging the hair.
  • If your little girl has curly hair, be sure to cut it while it’s dry to avoid shrinkage.

FAQs

How often should I cut my little girl’s hair?

It depends on how fast her hair grows and the style you’re going for. Generally, a haircut every 4-6 weeks is recommended to maintain the style.

Can I cut my little girl’s hair myself?

Yes, you can cut your little girl’s hair yourself with the right tools and techniques. However, if you’re unsure or inexperienced, it may be best to visit a professional stylist.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires less maintenance than longer hair, but it’s still important to keep it healthy and hydrated. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools like curling irons or straighteners too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your little girl’s hair short and achieve the perfect style. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and check for evenness throughout the process. Happy cutting!

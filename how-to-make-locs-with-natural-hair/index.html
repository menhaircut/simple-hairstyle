How To Make Locs With Natural Hair In 2023

Written by William Jun 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of constantly maintaining your natural hair and looking for a protective style that will last for months? Look no further than locs! Locs are a popular and timeless hairstyle that can be created with natural hair. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to make locs with natural hair, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Make Locs with Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Locs with Natural Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Locs

How to Make Locs with Natural Hair

The process of making locs involves allowing your natural hair to mat and tangle over time. There are several methods to start locs, including the comb coil method, two-strand twist method, and freeform method. The comb coil method involves using a small comb to twist small sections of hair into coils. The two-strand twist method involves twisting two sections of hair together until they start to mat. The freeform method involves allowing your hair to naturally tangle and mat without any manipulation.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Choose the method you want to use to start your locs.

Step 2: Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. Dry your hair thoroughly.

Step 3: Section your hair into small, even sections using a rattail comb.

Step 4: Take one section of hair and apply a small amount of locking gel or wax to the roots.

Step 5: Use your chosen method (comb coil, two-strand twist, or freeform) to start your locs. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 6: Once all sections of hair are twisted or coiled, cover your hair with a satin or silk scarf and allow it to dry overnight.

Step 7: In the morning, remove the scarf and style your locs as desired.

Tips for Making Locs with Natural Hair:

  • Be patient! Locs take time and patience to develop.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Consider getting professional help to start your locs.

FAQs:

Q: Can locs be undone?

A: Yes, locs can be undone with the help of a professional. However, the process can be lengthy and may result in significant hair loss.

Q: Can I wash my hair with locs?

A: Yes, it is important to regularly wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove buildup and debris.

Hair Care for Locs:

Once your locs are established, it is important to maintain them properly to prevent breakage and promote healthy hair growth. Here are some tips for caring for your locs:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a clarifying shampoo.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a satin or silk scarf to protect your hair while sleeping.
  • Trim your locs regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Moisturize your scalp with natural oils to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and a bit of patience, you can make beautiful locs with your natural hair. Whether you choose the comb coil, two-strand twist, or freeform method, you can enjoy a low-maintenance hairstyle that will last for months to come.

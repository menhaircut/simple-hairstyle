Description

If you have a heart shaped face, then you know how tricky it can be to find the perfect hairstyle. Your face shape is characterized by a wide forehead and a narrow chin, which means that you need to find a hairstyle that balances out your features. Long hairstyles are perfect for heart shaped faces because they help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the forehead. In this article, we will be discussing some of the best long hairstyles for heart shaped faces.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to know how to determine if you have a heart shaped face. Take a look in the mirror and see if your forehead is wider than your jawline. If it is, then you have a heart shaped face.

Step by step

Now that you know your face shape, let's look at some of the best long hairstyles for heart shaped faces.

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to soften the angles of a heart shaped face. Ask your stylist for long layers that start at your chin and continue down to the ends of your hair.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can help to balance out a wide forehead. Ask your stylist for long, side swept bangs that start at the crown of your head.

3. Beach Waves

Beach waves can add volume to the bottom half of your face. Use a curling iron or a flat iron to create loose waves.

Tips

When styling your hair, avoid adding volume to the top of your head. This can make your forehead appear larger. Instead, focus on adding volume to the bottom half of your hair.

FAQ

What if I don't want long hair?

If you don't want long hair, you can still use the same principles to find a flattering hairstyle. Look for hairstyles that add volume to the bottom half of your face and avoid adding volume to the top of your head.

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, you can still rock long hairstyles. Ask your stylist for long layers that will add volume to your hair.

Hair Care

In order to keep your long hair looking healthy, it's important to take care of it. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and trying out some of these long hairstyles, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face.

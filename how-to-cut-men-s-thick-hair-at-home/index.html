How To Cut Men s Thick Hair At Home

Written by Eveline Jul 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending money on expensive haircuts for your thick hair? Cutting your own hair at home can save you time and money. However, cutting men's thick hair can be challenging since it requires proper technique and tools. In this article, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut men's thick hair at home.

How to

Step 1: Gather the Right Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, hair clippers, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner and then dry it thoroughly. Wet hair is difficult to cut, and it's essential to have dry hair to see the actual length.

Step 3: Trim the Sides and Back

Start with the sides and back of your hair using hair clippers. Choose the desired length of your hair and start cutting from the bottom up. Use a comb to guide the clippers and ensure even cutting.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Use scissors to cut the top of your hair. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair between the top and sides using scissors or clippers. This step ensures a smooth transition between the different sections of your hair.

Step 6: Check for Any Uneven Spots

Check your hair for any uneven spots and make necessary adjustments.

Step 7: Clean Up

Clean up any loose hair using a comb and scissors.

Tips

Here are some tips to consider when cutting men's thick hair at home:

  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid mistakes.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your hair.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair, start with small cuts and gradually increase the length.
  • Use a spray bottle to keep your hair damp and easier to cut.
  • Consider seeking the help of a professional if you're not confident in your cutting skills.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

While regular scissors will work, it's better to use hairdressing scissors. They are sharper and designed for cutting hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and your desired length. It's recommended to cut your hair every four to six weeks.

Can I cut my hair wet?

It's best to cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair appears longer and may result in cutting off too much hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's essential to take good care of it. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Regular trims can also keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these steps and tips, you can successfully cut men's thick hair at home. Remember to take your time, start small, and seek help if needed.

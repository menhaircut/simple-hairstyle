What Kind Of Hair Is Used For Butterfly Locs

Welcome to our blog post for 2023, where we will be discussing what kind of hair is used for butterfly locs. Butterfly locs are a popular protective hairstyle that has been gaining popularity in recent years. This hairstyle involves creating small, twisted locs that are usually styled in a wavy or curly pattern. If you are interested in getting butterfly locs, it is important to understand what type of hair is best suited for this style.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Create Butterfly Locs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Butterfly Locs

Description

Butterfly locs are typically created using synthetic hair. The most popular type of synthetic hair used for creating butterfly locs is Kanekalon hair. This type of hair is made from a type of synthetic fiber that is designed to look and feel like natural hair. Kanekalon hair is also lightweight, which makes it ideal for creating long, flowing locs that won't weigh down your head.

Another type of synthetic hair that can be used for butterfly locs is Toyokalon hair. This hair is similar to Kanekalon hair in that it is lightweight and designed to look and feel like natural hair. Toyokalon hair is also heat-resistant, which means that it can be curled or straightened using a flat iron or curling wand.

How to Create Butterfly Locs

Creating butterfly locs is a complex process that involves twisting small sections of hair around synthetic hair extensions. This process can take several hours to complete, so it is important to have patience and take breaks as needed. Here are the basic steps for creating butterfly locs:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing your hair and detangling it using a wide-toothed comb.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections and clip each section out of the way.
  3. Take a small section of Kanekalon or Toyokalon hair and fold it in half.
  4. Place the folded end of the hair against your scalp and use a crochet hook to pull the two loose ends through the looped end.
  5. Take a small section of your natural hair and twist it tightly around the synthetic hair extension.
  6. Continue twisting your natural hair around the synthetic hair extension until you reach the end of the hair.
  7. Seal the end of the loc by dipping it in hot water or using a lighter to melt the ends together.
  8. Repeat this process for each section of hair until all of your hair is twisted into butterfly locs.

Tips for Maintaining Butterfly Locs

Once your butterfly locs are complete, it is important to take good care of them to ensure that they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your butterfly locs:

  • Wash your locs every 1-2 weeks using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy products on your locs, such as oils or gels, as these can weigh down your hair.
  • Wrap your hair at night using a satin or silk scarf to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as this can damage your natural hair and the synthetic hair extensions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about butterfly locs:

How long do butterfly locs last?

Butterfly locs can last anywhere from 2-3 months with proper care and maintenance.

Can you swim with butterfly locs?

Yes, you can swim with butterfly locs, but it is important to rinse your hair thoroughly with fresh water after swimming in chlorinated or saltwater pools.

Can you style butterfly locs?

Yes, you can style butterfly locs in a variety of ways, including updos, half-up styles, and braids.

Hair Care for Butterfly Locs

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful butterfly locs. Here are some tips for caring for your hair while wearing butterfly locs:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly using a lightweight oil, such as jojoba or almond oil.
  • Avoid using heavy products on your hair, as these can weigh down your locs and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf when spending time outdoors.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair, as this can cause your locs to unravel.

In conclusion, butterfly locs are a beautiful and trendy protective hairstyle that can be created using synthetic hair extensions such as Kanekalon or Toyokalon hair. To ensure that your butterfly locs last as long as possible, it is important to take good care of them by washing and moisturizing them regularly, avoiding heavy products, and protecting them from the sun and other sources of damage.

Does Cutting Your Hair Short Make It Look Thicker

Written by Frank Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've ever felt like your hair is thin and lifeless, you've probably wondered if cutting it short could help. After all, shorter hair looks fuller, right? But does cutting your hair short actually make it look thicker? In this article, we'll explore the answer to that question and provide tips for getting the most out of short haircuts.

How to Cut Your Hair Short to Make It Look Thicker

The short answer to this question is yes, cutting your hair short can make it look thicker. When you have long hair, the weight of the hair can pull it down and make it look limp and lifeless. Cutting off some length can help to make it look fuller and more voluminous.

However, the key to making your hair look thicker with a short haircut is to choose the right style. Some short haircuts can actually make your hair look even thinner, so it's important to consult with a stylist who can recommend the best cut for your hair type and face shape.

Best Short Haircuts for Thicker-Looking Hair

Some of the best short haircuts for making your hair look thicker include:

  • Pixie cuts
  • Bobs
  • Layered cuts
  • Cropped cuts

These haircuts work by removing some of the weight from your hair and adding layers that create movement and texture. This can make your hair look fuller and more voluminous, especially if you have thin or fine hair.

Step-by-Step Guide for Cutting Your Hair Short

If you're ready to take the plunge and cut your hair short, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Consult with a stylist to determine the best short haircut for your hair type and face shape.
  2. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly.
  3. Section your hair into four parts using clips or hair ties.
  4. Starting at the nape of your neck, use shears to cut your hair to the desired length.
  5. Continue cutting the rest of your hair, section by section.
  6. Create layers by holding your hair at a 45-degree angle and making short, snipping cuts.
  7. Blend the layers together by using shears to create texture.
  8. Style your hair as desired using a blow dryer, straightener, or curling iron.

Tips for Maintaining Thicker-Looking Short Hair

Once you've cut your hair short, there are a few things you can do to maintain a fuller, thicker look:

  • Use volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair body and lift.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add movement and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

FAQ: Answering Common Questions About Short Haircuts and Hair Thickness

Will cutting my hair short make it grow thicker?

No, cutting your hair short will not change the thickness of your hair. Hair thickness is determined by genetics and cannot be altered by cutting your hair.

Can I still have long hair if I want my hair to look thicker?

Yes, you can still have long hair and make it look thicker by getting regular trims to remove split ends and using volumizing products to add body and lift.

What should I do if I don't like my short haircut?

If you don't like your short haircut, don't worry! Hair grows back, and you can always try a different style in the meantime. Consult with your stylist to see if there are any adjustments that can be made to improve the look of your haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Thicker-Looking Short Hair

In addition to using the tips and techniques outlined above, there are a few other things you can do to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dull and lifeless.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushing can cause breakage and damage.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and getting the right short haircut for your hair type and face shape, you can achieve a fuller, thicker look that will have you feeling confident and stylish.

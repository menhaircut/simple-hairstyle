How To Revive A Synthetic Wig Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Sep 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have a synthetic wig that looks dull and lifeless? Don’t worry! With a few simple steps, you can revive your wig and make it look as good as new. In this article, we’ll walk you through the process of reviving a synthetic wig, including tips and tricks to keep it looking its best.

Description

Synthetic wigs are a popular choice for those who want to change their look without damaging their natural hair. However, over time, synthetic wigs can lose their shine and become tangled and frizzy. This is especially true if you wear your wig every day or if you don’t take proper care of it. Reviving your synthetic wig is an easy way to extend its lifespan and keep it looking great.

How to Revive a Synthetic Wig

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to revive a synthetic wig:

Step 1: Detangle Your Wig

The first step in reviving a synthetic wig is to detangle it. Use a wide-tooth comb or a wig brush to gently comb through the wig, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be sure to be gentle and avoid pulling or tugging on the hair.

Step 2: Wash Your Wig

Next, wash your wig in cool water with a wig shampoo. Be sure to follow the instructions on the shampoo bottle and avoid using hot water, which can damage the fibers. Rinse the wig thoroughly and gently squeeze out any excess water.

Step 3: Condition Your Wig

After washing your wig, apply a wig conditioner to the hair. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes, then rinse it out with cool water. This will help to soften the hair and reduce frizz.

Step 4: Dry Your Wig

Once you’ve washed and conditioned your wig, gently pat it dry with a towel. Do not rub or wring the hair, as this can damage the fibers. You can also let the wig air dry on a wig stand or mannequin head.

Step 5: Style Your Wig

After your wig is dry, you can style it as desired. Use a wig brush or comb to style the hair, and avoid using heat tools like curling irons or straighteners, which can damage synthetic fibers. Instead, use a low-heat setting on a hair dryer to style your wig.

Tips for Reviving a Synthetic Wig

Here are some additional tips for reviving a synthetic wig:

  • Use a wig conditioner to help reduce frizz and keep the hair soft.
  • Avoid using hot water or heat tools on your wig, as this can damage the fibers.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help maintain its shape.
  • Use a wig brush or comb to gently detangle the hair.
  • Avoid sleeping or showering in your wig, as this can cause tangling and damage.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about reviving a synthetic wig:

Can you revive a synthetic wig?

Yes, you can revive a synthetic wig by washing it with a wig shampoo and conditioner, and gently detangling and styling it.

How often should you revive a synthetic wig?

You should revive your synthetic wig whenever it starts to look dull or frizzy, or after every 10-12 wears.

How do you maintain a synthetic wig?

To maintain a synthetic wig, you should store it on a wig stand or mannequin head, avoid using hot water or heat tools, and gently detangle and style the hair.

Hair Care for Synthetic Wigs

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the quality and lifespan of your synthetic wig. Here are some tips for taking care of your wig:

  • Use a wig shampoo and conditioner to clean your wig.
  • Avoid using hot water or heat tools on your wig.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help maintain its shape.
  • Use a wig brush or comb to gently detangle the hair.
  • Do not sleep or shower in your wig.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily revive your synthetic wig and keep it looking great for years to come.

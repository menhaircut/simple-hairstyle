Best Hairstyles For 50 Year Old Woman 2018 A Timeless Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Oct 08, 2023 · 6 min read

As a woman in her 50s, you may be looking for a new hairstyle that flatters your age and personality. You may have searched for “best hairstyles for 50 year old woman 2018” in the past, but now it’s 2023 and we have updated this guide to help you find a timeless hairstyle that suits you. In this article, we will provide you with tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle for your age.

Description

As you age, your hair may become thinner, drier, and more prone to breakage. However, this doesn’t mean that you can’t have a stylish and trendy haircut. It’s important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. A good haircut can enhance your natural beauty, boost your confidence, and make you feel younger.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for 50 Year Old Woman

When choosing a hairstyle, you should consider the following factors:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair texture
  • Your lifestyle
  • Your personal style
  • Your hair color

Your face shape is an important factor to consider because it can determine which hairstyles will flatter your features. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head and elongates your face. If you have a square face, you may want to choose a hairstyle that softens your jawline and adds movement to your hair.

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider because it can determine how much time and effort you need to style your hair. If you have fine hair, you may want to choose a short haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a medium-length haircut that can be styled in different ways.

Your lifestyle is also an important factor to consider because it can determine how much time and effort you can spend on styling your hair. If you have a busy schedule, you may want to choose a low-maintenance haircut that can be styled quickly and easily. If you have more free time, you may want to choose a more elaborate haircut that requires more styling.

Your personal style is another important factor to consider because it can determine which hairstyles you feel comfortable wearing. If you prefer a classic and elegant style, you may want to choose a sleek and polished haircut. If you prefer a more edgy and trendy style, you may want to choose a haircut that has layers and texture.

Your hair color is also an important factor to consider because it can determine how your hair looks in different lighting conditions. If you have gray hair, you may want to choose a haircut that enhances your natural color and adds shine to your hair. If you have colored hair, you may want to choose a haircut that complements your hair color and highlights your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Best Hairstyle for 50 Year Old Woman

Here are some step-by-step guides to help you achieve the best hairstyle for 50 year old woman:

1. Determine your face shape

Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, compare the measurements to the following face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Pear

Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

2. Choose a haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture

If you have a round face, you may want to choose a short haircut that adds volume to the top of your head and elongates your face. If you have a square face, you may want to choose a medium-length haircut that softens your jawline and adds movement to your hair. If you have fine hair, you may want to choose a layered haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a blunt haircut that adds weight to your hair.

3. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone

If you have warm undertones, you may want to choose a hair color that has golden or reddish tones. If you have cool undertones, you may want to choose a hair color that has ashy or cool tones. If you have neutral undertones, you may want to choose a hair color that has both warm and cool tones.

4. Choose a styling product that suits your hair texture

If you have fine hair, you may want to choose a volumizing mousse or a texturizing spray that adds volume and texture to your hair. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a smoothing serum or a leave-in conditioner that tames frizz and adds shine to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

FAQ

Q: Can I still have a long haircut in my 50s?

A: Yes, you can still have a long haircut in your 50s. However, you may want to choose a haircut that has layers and texture to add volume and movement to your hair.

Q: Can I dye my hair in my 50s?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair in your 50s. However, you may want to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty.

Q: How often should I wash my hair in my 50s?

A: You should wash your hair as often as needed to keep it clean and healthy. However, you may want to avoid washing your hair every day to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated

With these tips, you can achieve the best hairstyle for 50 year old woman 2018 that suits your age and personality. Remember to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, and maintain your hair with proper hair care.

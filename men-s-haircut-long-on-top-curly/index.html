Men s Haircut Long On Top Curly How To Achieve The Look

Written by Oliver Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that showcases your curly locks? Try a men's haircut long on top curly! This hairstyle is perfect for those with natural curls and is easy to maintain. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look, tips on how to style it, and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How To Achieve The Look
  • Tips On How To Style It
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How To Achieve The Look

The men's haircut long on top curly is a versatile hairstyle that can be achieved with a few simple steps. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls healthy and bouncy.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. Choose a product that works well with your hair type and provides hold and definition to your curls.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help define your curls and prevent frizz.
  4. Use a curling iron to touch up any loose curls or to add more definition to your hair.
  5. Trim the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. The length of the top should be longer than the sides and back.
  6. Style your hair using a pomade or wax. Apply a small amount to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the top.

And voila! You now have a men's haircut long on top curly.

Tips On How To Style It

Once you've achieved the men's haircut long on top curly, here are some tips to keep it looking great:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it's damp.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair while it's damp to help define your curls.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to prevent frizz.
  • Trim the sides and back of your hair regularly to maintain the shape of the style.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of hair works best with this hairstyle?

This hairstyle works best with natural curls or wavy hair. If you have straight hair, you can still achieve this look with a curling iron and the right products.

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim the sides and back of your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the style.

What products should I use?

Use a curl-enhancing product, such as a curl cream, mousse, or gel, to help define your curls. You can also use a pomade or wax to style the top of your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your curly hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls healthy and bouncy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often as this can damage your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it's damp.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a men's haircut long on top curly and keep your curls looking healthy and bouncy. Don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you. Happy styling!

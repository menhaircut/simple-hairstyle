Best Curly Hairstyles For Diamond Shaped Faces

Written by Eveline Oct 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone with a diamond-shaped face and curly hair? If you are, then you know how challenging it can be to find the right hairstyle that complements your face shape. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will be discussing the best curly hairstyles for diamond-shaped faces.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Diamond-shaped faces are characterized by a narrow forehead, sharp cheekbones, and a pointed chin. The key to finding the perfect curly hairstyle for this face shape is to balance out the sharpness of the cheeks and chin while creating volume at the crown. It's essential to choose a style that complements the angles of the face, rather than accentuating them.

How To

When it comes to finding the right curly hairstyle for a diamond-shaped face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to create volume at the crown to balance out the sharpness of the cheekbones and chin. Second, you want to choose a style that softens the angles of the face. Lastly, you want to avoid styles that add too much width to the cheeks.

Consider Layers

Layered haircuts are perfect for diamond-shaped faces as they add volume at the crown and soften the angles of the face. Opt for curly layers that start at the jawline and gradually get longer towards the back. This will help to create a balanced and harmonious look.

Try Bangs

Bangs are a great way to balance out the sharpness of the cheekbones and chin. Side-swept bangs work well for diamond-shaped faces as they create a diagonal line that softens the angles of the face. You can also try curly bangs that start higher on the forehead and gradually get longer towards the sides.

Consider Length

When it comes to length, mid-length haircuts work well for diamond-shaped faces. Avoid styles that are too short as they can make the face appear wider. Instead, go for a style that falls just below the shoulders. This will help to elongate the face and create a balanced look.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating the perfect curly hairstyle for a diamond-shaped face:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a curl-defining shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a curl-defining cream or mousse.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural curls.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any loose curls or waves.
  5. Finish off the look with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when wet to avoid breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls healthy and hydrated.
  • Invest in a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a diamond-shaped face?

Avoid hairstyles that add too much width to the cheeks, such as blunt bobs or short pixie cuts. These styles can make the face appear wider and accentuate the sharpness of the cheekbones.

Can I wear my hair in an updo if I have a diamond-shaped face?

Absolutely! Updos work well for diamond-shaped faces as they help to elongate the face and create balance. Opt for a loose, messy bun or a low ponytail to soften the angles of the face.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for curly hair, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are sulfate-free and contain ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil to help hydrate your curls. Also, make sure to deep condition your hair once a week to keep your curls healthy and bouncy.

In conclusion, there are plenty of curly hairstyles that work well for diamond-shaped faces. Whether you opt for layered curls or curly bangs, the key is to create balance and soften the angles of the face. With a little bit of care and styling, you can rock any curly hairstyle with confidence!

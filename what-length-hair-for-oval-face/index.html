What Length Hair For Oval Face A Complete Guide

Written by William Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, finding the right length of hair that flatters your face shape can still be a daunting task. In this article, we will guide you on what length hair works best for oval face shapes.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide on Choosing the Right Length of Hair
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Oval Face Shapes

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin. If you have an oval face shape, you have a wide range of hairstyles to choose from. However, it's important to choose a length that complements your face shape and highlights your best features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right length of hair for your oval face shape is to determine your face shape. There are a few simple steps you can follow to do this:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Examine the shape of your face.
  3. Compare your face to the following face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.
  4. Determine which shape is closest to yours.

Characteristics of an Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, your face will have the following characteristics:

  • Your face will be longer than it is wide.
  • Your forehead will be slightly wider than your chin.
  • Your jawline will be slightly rounded.

Step-by-Step Guide on Choosing the Right Length of Hair

Once you've determined that you have an oval face shape, the next step is to choose the right length of hair. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Consider your lifestyle. Choose a length of hair that is easy to maintain and fits your lifestyle.
  2. Think about your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a shorter length may work better. If you have thick hair, a longer length may be more appropriate.
  3. Consider your features. If you have a long neck, a shorter length of hair may accentuate your neck. If you have high cheekbones, a longer length may complement your features.
  4. Choose a length that balances your face shape. For an oval face shape, a length that hits at or below the collarbone is ideal. This length will balance your face shape and highlight your best features.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep in mind when choosing the right length of hair for an oval face shape:

  • Layers can add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Longer hair can elongate your face shape.
  • Shorter hair can highlight your cheekbones.
  • Consider bangs to add dimension to your face shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can oval face shapes pull off any length of hair?

While oval face shapes can pull off almost any length of hair, it's important to choose a length that complements your face shape and highlights your best features.

Is there a certain length of hair that is best for oval face shapes?

For an oval face shape, a length that hits at or below the collarbone is ideal. This length will balance your face shape and highlight your best features.

What hairstyles should oval face shapes avoid?

Oval face shapes can pull off almost any hairstyle, but it's best to avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the crown of your head or hairstyles that are too short and choppy.

Hair Care for Oval Face Shapes

No matter what length of hair you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for oval face shapes:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and free of split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Choosing the right length of hair for an oval face shape can be a fun and exciting experience. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a length that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features.

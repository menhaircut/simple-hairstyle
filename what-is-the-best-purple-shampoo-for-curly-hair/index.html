What Is The Best Purple Shampoo For Curly Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

When it comes to maintaining curly hair, it can be challenging to find the right products that work for your hair type. If you have blonde or silver curly hair, you may have heard about purple shampoo. But what is the best purple shampoo for curly hair? In this post, we will discuss everything you need to know about purple shampoo for curly hair and recommend some of the best products on the market.

Description

Purple shampoo is a type of shampoo that is designed for blonde or silver hair. It contains purple pigments that help to neutralize brassy tones in the hair. When used on curly hair, purple shampoo can help to enhance and brighten your curls while also reducing brassiness.

How to Use Purple Shampoo for Curly Hair

When using purple shampoo for curly hair, it is essential to follow the instructions on the product label. Here are some general tips:

  • Wet your hair thoroughly.
  • Apply a small amount of purple shampoo to your hair.
  • Massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair.
  • Leave the shampoo on your hair for 2-5 minutes.
  • Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  • Follow up with a conditioner or hair mask.

Step by Step Guide

If you are new to using purple shampoo for curly hair, here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly.
  2. Apply a small amount of purple shampoo to your hair, focusing on the roots and areas with the most brassiness.
  3. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair for 2-5 minutes.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  5. Follow up with a conditioner or hair mask.

Tips for Using Purple Shampoo for Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your purple shampoo:

  • Use purple shampoo once or twice a week to avoid over-drying your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or conditioner after using purple shampoo to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can cause dryness and damage.
  • Dry your hair gently with a microfiber towel or t-shirt to reduce frizz.

FAQ

Can I use purple shampoo on all hair types?

No, purple shampoo is designed for blonde or silver hair. If you have darker hair, you can still use purple shampoo, but you may need to dilute it with regular shampoo to avoid staining your hair.

How often should I use purple shampoo for curly hair?

You should use purple shampoo once or twice a week, depending on how often you wash your hair. Using it too often can cause dryness and damage.

Will purple shampoo make my hair purple?

No, if you follow the instructions and rinse your hair thoroughly, purple shampoo should not make your hair purple. However, if you have porous hair or leave the shampoo on for too long, it may cause a purple tint.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Using the right products is just one part of maintaining healthy curly hair. Here are some other tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know what the best purple shampoo for curly hair is, you can give your curls the TLC they deserve. Remember to use it in moderation and follow up with a conditioner or hair mask to prevent dryness and damage. Happy styling!

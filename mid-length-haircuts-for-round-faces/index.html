Mid Length Haircuts For Round Faces

Written by Eveline Oct 22, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have a round face, choosing the right haircut can make a big difference in how you look and feel. Mid length haircuts are a great option for round faces because they can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. In this article, we'll explore some of the best mid length haircuts for round faces, as well as some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle.

Description

Mid length haircuts are typically defined as hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. This length is great for round faces because it can help to create the illusion of length and balance out the face. There are many different mid length haircuts to choose from, including layered cuts, blunt cuts, and angled cuts.

How to

If you're interested in getting a mid length haircut for your round face, the first step is to find a hairstylist who has experience working with round faces. They can help you choose a haircut that will flatter your features and complement your personal style.

Once you've found a hairstylist you trust, it's time to start thinking about the specific haircut you want. Some popular mid length haircuts for round faces include:

Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great option for round faces because it can help to create the illusion of length and add some dimension to your hair. This haircut typically features layers that start at the chin and angle down towards the front of the face.

Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, long layers can be a great way to add some movement and texture to your locks. This haircut typically features layers that start at the chin and gradually get longer towards the back of the head.

Shoulder-Length Blunt Cut

A shoulder-length blunt cut is a classic hairstyle that can work well for round faces. This cut features straight, blunt ends that fall just above the shoulders.

Step by Step

Once you've chosen your ideal mid length haircut, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for caring for your new haircut:

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right hair care products can make a big difference in how your mid length haircut looks and feels. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture, and avoid using too much product to prevent weighing down your hair.

2. Get Regular Trims

To keep your mid length haircut looking fresh and healthy, it's important to get regular trims. Your hairstylist can help you determine how often you should get a trim based on your hair type and the specific haircut you have.

3. Experiment with Styling

Mid length haircuts are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a mid length haircut for your round face:

1. Consider Face Shape

When choosing a mid length haircut, it's important to consider your face shape as well as your personal style. Some haircuts may work better for round faces than others, so be sure to talk to your hairstylist about your options.

2. Embrace Texture

Adding texture to your mid length haircut can help to create a more interesting and dynamic look. Consider adding layers, bangs, or other elements to add some texture to your hair.

3. Don't Be Afraid to Experiment

Mid length haircuts are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

What is the best mid length haircut for a round face?

There are many different mid length haircuts that can work well for round faces, including layered bobs, long layers, and shoulder-length blunt cuts. The best haircut for you will depend on your personal style and preferences.

How often should I get a trim for my mid length haircut?

It's generally recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your mid length haircut looking its best. However, your hairstylist may recommend a different schedule based on your hair type and the specific haircut you have.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your mid length haircut looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Choosing the right hair care products can make a big difference in the health and appearance of your hair. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture, and avoid using too much product to prevent weighing down your hair.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Excessive heat can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using heat styling tools, and try to limit your use of these tools as much as possible.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet is essential for maintaining healthy hair. Be sure to eat a well-balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to support healthy hair growth.

Overall, mid length haircuts can be a great option for round faces. With the right cut, styling, and maintenance, you can achieve a beautiful, flattering look that highlights your best features.

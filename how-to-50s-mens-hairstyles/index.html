Description

The 1950s was a time of great fashion and style, and men's hairstyles were no exception. The 50s saw a rise in popularity of slicked-back hair, pompadours, and crew cuts. These hairstyles are still popular today and can be a great choice for men who want a classic, timeless look. In this article, we'll show you how to get 50s mens hairstyles in 2023, including step-by-step instructions and tips for maintaining your new look.

How to Get 50s Mens Hairstyles

Getting a 50s mens hairstyle is easier than you might think. There are a few different styles to choose from, including the slicked-back look, the pompadour, and the crew cut. To get started, you'll need to decide which style you want to go for and then gather the necessary tools and products.

Tools and Products

To achieve a 50s mens hairstyle, you'll need the following tools and products:

  • Clippers or scissors
  • Comb or brush
  • Hair gel or pomade
  • Hairspray (optional)

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a 50s mens hairstyle:

  1. Decide on the style you want to go for (slicked-back, pompadour, or crew cut).
  2. Use clippers or scissors to trim your hair to the desired length.
  3. Depending on the style you want, you may need to leave more length on top.
  4. Apply hair gel or pomade to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.
  5. Use a comb or brush to slick your hair back or create the desired style (e.g. a pomp).
  6. If you want extra hold, you can finish with a spritz of hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining 50s Mens Hairstyles

Once you've achieved your 50s mens hairstyle, you'll want to make sure it looks great for as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your new look:

  • Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.
  • Consider using a hairspray for extra hold.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a comb or brush to keep your hairstyle looking sharp.
  • If you have a pompadour, consider using a hair dryer to help set the style.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve a 50s mens hairstyle with long hair?

A: While most 50s mens hairstyles are shorter, you can still achieve a similar look with longer hair. Consider styling your hair into a pompadour or slicked-back style, but leave more length on top.

Q: What kind of hair gel or pomade should I use?

A: Look for a high-quality hair gel or pomade that provides a strong hold and doesn't leave your hair feeling greasy or sticky. You may need to experiment with a few different products to find the right one for your hair type.

Hair Care for 50s Mens Hairstyles

Proper hair care is important for maintaining your 50s mens hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can lead to breakage and damage.

By following these tips and using the right tools and products, you can achieve a classic 50s mens hairstyle that looks great and is easy to maintain. Whether you opt for a slicked-back look, a pompadour, or a crew cut, you'll be sure to turn heads with your timeless style.

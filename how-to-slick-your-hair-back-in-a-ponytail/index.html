How To Slick Your Hair Back In A Ponytail A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 12, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

If you're looking for a simple yet stylish way to wear your hair, slicking it back in a ponytail might be just the look you need. This classic hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, whether you're heading to the gym or attending a formal event. In this article, we'll show you how to slick your hair back in a ponytail in just a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Your Hair Back in a Ponytail
  • Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back in a Ponytail
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Slicked-Back Ponytails

How to Slick Your Hair Back in a Ponytail

Follow these simple steps to achieve a sleek and stylish ponytail:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

The key to a slicked-back ponytail is to start with clean, dry hair. This will help you achieve a smooth and polished look. If your hair is wet or damp, it will be more difficult to style and may not hold the shape of the ponytail.

Step 2: Apply Styling Gel

Using a styling gel or pomade will help to hold your hair in place and create a sleek look. Apply a small amount of product to your hands and work it through your hair, focusing on the roots and the hairline.

Step 3: Brush Your Hair Back

Using a fine-tooth comb, brush your hair back from your forehead and temples, smoothing out any bumps or flyaways. Continue to brush your hair back until you reach the crown of your head.

Step 4: Secure Your Hair with a Hair Tie

Once your hair is brushed back, use a hair tie to secure it in a ponytail. Make sure the hair tie is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To ensure that your ponytail stays in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold your hair in place and prevent any frizz or flyaways.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back in a Ponytail

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back ponytail:

  • Use a comb instead of a brush for a smoother, more polished look.
  • Apply styling gel or pomade sparingly to avoid creating a greasy or oily appearance.
  • Experiment with different hair ties to find one that works best for your hair type and thickness.
  • If you have shorter hair, you may need to use bobby pins or hair clips to secure any loose strands.

FAQs

Can I slick my hair back in a ponytail if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Slicked-back ponytails work well with all hair types, including curly hair. You may need to use a bit more styling product to keep your curls in place, but the end result will be just as stylish and polished.

How high or low should I position my ponytail?

The position of your ponytail is a matter of personal preference. Some people prefer to wear their ponytail high on the crown of their head, while others prefer a lower, more relaxed look. Experiment with different positions to find the one that works best for you.

Can I wear a slicked-back ponytail to a formal event?

Yes! A slicked-back ponytail can be a chic and sophisticated look for a formal event, especially if you dress it up with statement jewelry or a bold lipstick.

Hair Care Tips for Slicked-Back Ponytails

While slicked-back ponytails are a great way to style your hair, they can also cause damage if you're not careful. Here are a few tips to help you care for your hair while wearing a slicked-back ponytail:

  • Avoid wearing a tight ponytail for extended periods of time, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair before and after wearing a slicked-back ponytail.
  • Give your hair a break from slicked-back ponytails every once in a while to allow your scalp and hair to breathe.
  • Use a nourishing hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a sleek and stylish slicked-back ponytail in no time. Whether you're heading to the office or out for a night on the town, this classic hairstyle is sure to turn heads.

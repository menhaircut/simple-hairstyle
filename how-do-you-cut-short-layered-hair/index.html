How Do You Cut Short Layered Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and stylish haircut, short layered hair might be the perfect option for you. Layered haircuts can add volume, texture, and movement to your hair, making it look more dynamic and modern. However, cutting short layered hair can be a bit tricky, especially if you don't have any experience with hair cutting. In this article, we'll show you how to cut short layered hair, step by step, and provide you with some useful tips and hair care advice.

Description

Short layered hair is a popular haircut that involves cutting the hair in layers of different lengths, creating a textured and voluminous look. This type of haircut is suitable for many hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. Short layered haircuts can be customized to suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, making them a versatile and trendy option for any season.

How to Cut Short Layered Hair

To cut short layered hair, you'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and some hair clips. Follow these steps to achieve a perfect layered haircut:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Before cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner and let it air dry or blow-dry it. It's essential to work with clean, tangle-free hair to ensure an even cut.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. You'll need to create three sections: the top, the sides, and the back. Start by separating the top section of your hair from the rest and clip it away.

Step 3: Cut the back section

Start by cutting the back section of your hair. Use the comb to comb your hair straight down, and then cut the hair at a slight angle, creating a diagonal line. Cut the hair in small sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

Step 4: Cut the side sections

Release the side sections of your hair and cut them in the same way you cut the back section. Hold the hair at a 45-degree angle and cut it in small sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

Step 5: Cut the top section

Finally, release the top section of your hair and cut it in the same way you cut the side and back sections. Hold the hair at a 90-degree angle and cut it in small sections, starting from the crown of your head and working your way down.

Step 6: Blend the layers

Use the scissors to blend the layers together, removing any harsh lines or unevenness. Use the comb to comb your hair and check for any uneven spots.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short layered haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean and even cut.
  • Cut your hair when it's dry to see the exact length and texture.
  • Start cutting your hair from the bottom and work your way up to create layers.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the layers are even.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid making mistakes.

FAQ

How often should I cut my short layered hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of your layered haircut.

Can I cut my short layered hair myself?

It's possible to cut your short layered hair yourself, but it's recommended to visit a professional hairdresser if you're not confident in your skills.

How do I style my short layered hair?

Short layered hair can be styled in many ways, including straight, wavy, and curly styles. Use a styling product that suits your hair type and personal preference, such as mousse, gel, or hairspray.

Hair Care

To keep your short layered hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

Cutting short layered hair might seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and modern haircut that suits your personal style. Follow our step-by-step guide and use our tips and hair care advice to keep your short layered hair looking beautiful and healthy.

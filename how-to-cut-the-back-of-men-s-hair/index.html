How To Cut The Back Of Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting men's hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're just starting out. One of the most challenging areas to tackle is the back of the head. However, with the right tools and techniques, anyone can learn how to cut the back of men's hair like a pro. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to do just that.

Description

The back of the head is a tricky area to cut because it's difficult to see what you're doing. This is why it's crucial to have the right tools and techniques to ensure a clean and even cut. With the right approach, cutting the back of men's hair can be a breeze.

How to Cut the Back of Men's Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Clipper or trimmer
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror

Once you have all the necessary tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with the Clippers

Begin by using the clippers or trimmers to cut the back of the head. Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up. Use a guard that's appropriate for the length of hair you want to cut. Make sure to go slowly and use even strokes.

Step 2: Blend the Sides

Once you've finished cutting the back, blend the sides using scissors. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. Blend the sides and back together using a scissor-over-comb technique.

Step 3: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check the back of the head for any uneven areas or missed spots. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the trimmers to clean up the neckline. Shave any hair below the natural hairline to create a clean and defined look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting the back of men's hair:

  • Use a mirror to check your work
  • Go slowly and use even strokes
  • Blend the sides and back together using a scissor-over-comb technique
  • Use a guard that's appropriate for the length of hair you want to cut
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it

FAQ

1. How often should I cut the back of my hair?

It's recommended that men get their hair cut every 3-4 weeks to maintain their style and keep it looking fresh.

2. Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair, but it's important to have the right tools and techniques. If you're not confident in your abilities, it's best to seek the help of a professional.

3. What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always make adjustments or seek the help of a professional if necessary.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and looks great. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy

Cutting the back of men's hair doesn't have to be a difficult task. With the right tools and techniques, anyone can learn how to do it like a pro. Remember to go slowly, use even strokes, and blend the sides and back together using a scissor-over-comb technique. If you're not confident in your abilities, don't be afraid to seek the help of a professional.

How To Add Volume To Guys Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jul 03, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of your hair looking flat and lifeless? Do you want to add some volume and texture to your locks? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll show you step-by-step how to add volume to guys’ hair. From tips and tricks to frequently asked questions, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Add Volume to Guys Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Adding Volume to Guys Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Volume and Texture

How to Add Volume to Guys Hair

Adding volume to guys’ hair is relatively easy and can be achieved with a few simple techniques. Here are some of the best ways to add volume to your locks:

  • Blow-dry your hair upside down
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Add layers to your hair
  • Use a hair mousse or root lifter
  • Tease your hair at the roots
  • Style your hair with a round brush

By using these techniques, you can create volume and texture in your hair that will last all day.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to add volume to guys’ hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a root lifter or mousse to your roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair upside down using a round brush.
  4. Tease your hair at the roots to create volume.
  5. Use a hairspray to set your style in place.

These steps will help you achieve a voluminous hairstyle that will turn heads.

Tips for Adding Volume to Guys Hair

Here are some additional tips for adding volume to guys’ hair:

  • Try a new haircut with layers to add volume and texture.
  • Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo to add volume and texture between washes.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, like using a diffuser or air-drying your hair.

By following these tips, you can create a hairstyle that is full of volume and texture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about adding volume to guys’ hair:

Can I add volume to my hair without blow-drying it?

Yes, you can add volume to your hair without blow-drying it. Try using a root lifter or mousse and then air-drying your hair.

What is the best product for adding volume to guys’ hair?

The best product for adding volume to guys’ hair is a root lifter or mousse. Look for products that are lightweight and won’t weigh down your hair.

How often should I wash my hair to maintain volume?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to maintain volume. Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, which can cause it to become flat and lifeless.

Hair Care for Volume and Texture

Maintaining volume and texture in your hair requires proper hair care. Here are some tips for keeping your locks healthy and full of life:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won’t strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can maintain voluminous and textured hair that looks great every day.

Conclusion

Adding volume to guys’ hair is easy and can be achieved with a few simple techniques. By following our step-by-step guide and using our tips and tricks, you can create a hairstyle that is full of volume and texture. Remember to take care of your hair with proper hair care to maintain healthy locks that look great every day.

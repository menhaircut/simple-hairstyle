Are Bangs Good For A Square Face Tips How To And Hair Care Faq

If you have a square face, you may be wondering if bangs are a good option for you. Bangs can be a great way to change up your look and add some dimension to your face, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs to flatter your face shape. In this article, we'll explore whether bangs are good for a square face, how to choose the right type of bangs, and hair care tips to keep your bangs looking great.

What is a Square Face?

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a square chin. This face shape can be angular and striking, but it can also be a challenge to find hairstyles that flatter it. Some celebrities with square faces include Angelina Jolie, Keira Knightley, and Olivia Wilde.

Are Bangs Good for a Square Face?

The answer is yes! Bangs can be a great way to soften the angles of a square face and add some dimension to the forehead. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs to flatter your face shape. Some bangs styles that work well for square faces include:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long, wispy bangs
  • Curved or rounded bangs

Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as these can accentuate the squareness of your face.

How to Choose the Right Type of Bangs for a Square Face

When choosing bangs for a square face, it's important to consider the length, thickness, and shape of the bangs. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Length: Side-swept or long, wispy bangs that hit at or below the eyebrows are a good option for square faces.
  • Thickness: Thin, wispy bangs are more flattering than thick, heavy bangs that can make the face appear wider.
  • Shape: Curved or rounded bangs can help soften the angles of a square face.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Cut Your Own Bangs

If you're feeling brave and want to cut your own bangs, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with dry, styled hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape.
  3. Comb the hair straight down and hold it between your fingers where you want to make the cut.
  4. Cut the hair in a straight line, being careful not to cut too much at once.
  5. Continue cutting small sections until the desired length is achieved.
  6. Trim any uneven pieces or stray hairs.

Remember, it's always better to start with a longer length and cut more if needed. You can always trim more later, but you can't add length back once it's cut!

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Once you have bangs, it's important to take good care of them to keep them looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your bangs frequently to avoid oil buildup.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a small amount of styling product, such as hairspray or mousse, to keep your bangs in place.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the length and shape of your bangs.

FAQ: Common Questions About Bangs and Square Faces

Q: Can I wear bangs if I have a square face?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs to flatter your face shape. Side-swept or long, wispy bangs are a good option for square faces.

Q: Should I cut my own bangs?

A: It's always best to go to a professional stylist for a haircut. However, if you're feeling confident and want to cut your own bangs, be sure to follow a step-by-step guide and start with a longer length.

Q: How often should I get my bangs trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your bangs trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock bangs with a square face and keep them looking great. Remember to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape, take good care of your hair, and get regular trims to maintain your bangs' length and shape.

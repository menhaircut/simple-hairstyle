Best Hairstyle For Coarse Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Are you tired of struggling to style your coarse wavy hair? Do you find it difficult to achieve the perfect hairstyle that suits your hair type? Look no further, as we have compiled a complete guide on the best hairstyle for coarse wavy hair that will make you look stunning and feel confident.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

Coarse wavy hair is thick, voluminous, and has a natural wave pattern. It can be challenging to manage and style due to its texture, but it can also be a blessing as it creates a unique and striking look. The best hairstyle for coarse wavy hair is one that emphasizes its natural waves without making it look frizzy or unkempt.

How to

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for coarse wavy hair is to determine your face shape. Different hairstyles suit different face shapes, and it's essential to choose a style that complements your features. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that suit your hair type.

Face Shapes

The most common face shapes are round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Here's a quick guide on how to identify your face shape:

  • Round: Your face is as wide as it is long, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.
  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead is slightly wider than your chin.
  • Square: Your face is as wide as it is long, and your jawline is square.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your cheeks and chin, and your chin comes to a point.
  • Diamond: Your face is widest at the cheekbones, and your forehead and jawline are narrow.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can follow to achieve the best hairstyle for coarse wavy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Coarse hair can be dry and brittle, so it's essential to keep it hydrated.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your damp hair to help detangle and protect it from heat damage.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves. Use a low heat setting and avoid touching your hair too much to prevent frizz.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define your waves further. You can curl all of your hair or just the sections that need more definition.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray or styling cream to hold your waves in place and add shine.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling coarse wavy hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle it when it's damp.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils and make it even drier. Aim to wash it once or twice a week, depending on your hair type.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized. You can also use a hair mask or oil to nourish it.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it and make it look frizzy. Use a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools, and try to air-dry your hair as much as possible.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for coarse wavy hair?

The best haircut for coarse wavy hair is one that suits your face shape and hair texture. Some popular options include long layers, a bob, or a lob (long bob). Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make your hair look bulky and heavy.

How do I style coarse wavy hair without heat?

You can style coarse wavy hair without heat by using a styling cream or mousse to define your waves. Apply the product to damp hair and scrunch it with your hands to enhance the waves. You can also braid your hair overnight and undo the braids in the morning for soft, natural waves.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful coarse wavy hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can make it dry and brittle. Use lukewarm water instead.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, the best hairstyle for coarse wavy hair is one that embraces your natural waves and enhances them with the right products and styling tools. With the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can achieve a stunning look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Best Bathing Suit Top For Big Chest How To Choose And Style

Written by Frank Nov 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Summer is here, and it’s time to hit the beach! But for those of us with a big chest, finding the right bathing suit top can be a challenge. You want something that offers support, coverage, and style. In this post, we’ll help you find the best bathing suit top for your big chest, with tips on how to choose and style it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Bathing Suit Top for Big Chest
  • Styles of Bathing Suit Tops for Big Chests
  • Tips for Styling Your Bathing Suit Top
  • FAQs About Bathing Suit Tops for Big Chests
  • Hair Care Tips for the Beach

How to Choose the Best Bathing Suit Top for Big Chest

When shopping for a bathing suit top, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Support: Look for tops with underwire, molded cups, or adjustable straps to provide support for your chest.
  • Coverage: If you prefer more coverage, consider a halter or tankini top.
  • Style: There are many styles of bathing suit tops that work well for big chests, from bandeaus to balconettes.

It’s also important to try on bathing suit tops before buying them, to ensure a proper fit. Don’t be afraid to ask a sales associate for help in finding the right size and style for your body.


Styles of Bathing Suit Tops for Big Chests

Here are some styles of bathing suit tops that work well for big chests:

  • Bandeau: A strapless top with a straight neckline that can be worn with or without straps.
  • Halter: A top with straps that tie behind the neck, providing support and lift for the chest.
  • Bralette: A soft-cup top with a more relaxed fit, ideal for those who want a more comfortable and casual look.
  • Balconette: A top with underwire and molded cups that provides lift and support for the chest.

Tips for Styling Your Bathing Suit Top

Once you’ve found the perfect bathing suit top for your big chest, here are some tips for styling it:

  • Pair your top with high-waisted bottoms for a retro look that also provides more coverage.
  • Add a cover-up, like a sarong or kaftan, for a stylish and practical way to protect your skin from the sun.
  • Accessorize with statement jewelry or a wide-brimmed hat to elevate your beach look.

FAQs About Bathing Suit Tops for Big Chests

Here are some frequently asked questions about bathing suit tops for big chests:

Q: Can I wear a triangle top with a big chest?

A: Yes, but look for a triangle top with thicker straps and molded cups for support.

Q: What colors and prints are best for a big chest?

A: Darker colors and small prints can help minimize the appearance of a big chest, while brighter colors and bold prints can draw attention to it.

Q: Can I wear a bandeau top with a big chest?

A: Yes, but look for a bandeau top with underwire or molded cups for support, and consider wearing it with straps for added security.


Hair Care Tips for the Beach

Don’t forget to take care of your hair while you’re at the beach! Here are some tips:

  • Wear a hat or scarf to protect your scalp and hair from the sun.
  • Rinse your hair with fresh water before and after swimming to remove salt and chlorine.
  • Apply leave-in conditioner or hair oil to protect your hair from the sun and salt.

With these tips, you’ll be able to find and style the best bathing suit top for your big chest, and enjoy a fun and fashionable day at the beach!

How To Cut A Man s Long Hair With Clippers Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 25, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune for a haircut every few weeks? Want to learn how to cut your man's long hair at home with clippers? It's easier than you think! Follow this step-by-step guide to get a professional-looking haircut at home.

How to Cut a Man's Long Hair with Clippers: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, hair-cutting scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a towel.

Step 2: Prepare Your Workspace

Find a well-lit area with enough space to move around freely. Cover the floor with a towel to catch stray hairs.

Step 3: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash the hair with shampoo and conditioner, then dry it thoroughly with a towel or hairdryer.

Step 4: Section the Hair

Use hair clips to divide the hair into sections. Start with the back of the head and work your way forward.

Step 5: Choose the Right Guard

Select the right guard for the length of hair you want. Start with a higher guard and work your way down gradually.

Step 6: Start Cutting

Start cutting from the back of the head, moving the clippers in an upward motion towards the crown. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.

Step 7: Trim the Sides

Use the same guard to trim the sides of the head. Start from the bottom and work your way up.

Step 8: Blend the Hair

Use a lower guard to blend the hair on the sides and back of the head. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs.

Step 9: Cut the Top

Use the scissors to cut the hair on the top of the head. Cut it evenly and in small sections.

Step 10: Finish the Haircut

Brush off any stray hairs and use a towel to clean the neck and face. Style the hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting a Man's Long Hair with Clippers

  • Start with a higher guard and work your way down gradually.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.
  • Always cut the hair in small sections.
  • Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs.
  • Brush off any stray hairs and use a towel to clean the neck and face.

FAQ

Can I cut my man's long hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your man's long hair with clippers. Follow this step-by-step guide to get a professional-looking haircut at home.

What tools do I need to cut my man's long hair with clippers?

You'll need a pair of clippers, hair-cutting scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a towel.

Hair Care after Cutting

After cutting your man's long hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Wash and condition the hair regularly, and use hair oil to keep it moisturized. Trim the hair every few weeks to maintain the style.

With these simple steps, you can easily cut your man's long hair at home with clippers. Give it a try and save on expensive haircuts!

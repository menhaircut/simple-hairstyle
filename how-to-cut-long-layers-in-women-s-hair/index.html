How To Cut Long Layers In Women s Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Nov 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle with some long layers but don't want to spend a fortune at the salon? Cutting long layers in women's hair can be done at home with the right tools and technique. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to cut long layers in women's hair step-by-step.

How to

Before you begin, make sure you have the following tools: a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

Step 1:

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before you begin cutting.

Step 2:

Part your hair down the middle and divide it into two equal sections. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3:

Begin cutting the first layer. Take a small section of hair from the bottom layer and hold it straight up with your fingers. Use the comb to comb through the hair, and then cut the ends in a straight line.

Step 4:

Repeat step 3 for the entire bottom layer, cutting the hair to your desired length.

Step 5:

Release the top layer of hair and repeat steps 3 and 4, cutting the hair to the same length as the bottom layer.

Step 6:

Starting with the bottom layer, take a small section of hair and comb it straight up. Use your fingers to measure the length of the hair you want to cut, and then cut the ends at an angle. Repeat this technique for the entire bottom layer.

Step 7:

Repeat step 6 for the top layer of hair, cutting it at an angle to match the bottom layer.

Step 8:

Blend the layers by taking small sections of hair from both layers and cutting them at an angle. This will create a seamless transition between the two layers.

Step 9:

Check your work in the mirror, making sure the layers are even and blended.

Step 10:

Style your hair as desired and enjoy your new long layers!

Tips

- Start by cutting less hair than you think you need. You can always go back and cut more if needed.

- Use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut.

- Take breaks if you need to. Cutting long layers can be time-consuming, so don't rush the process.

FAQ

How often should I cut my long layers?

It's recommended to cut your long layers every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Can I cut long layers in curly hair?

Yes, you can cut long layers in curly hair. However, it's important to cut the hair when it's dry to ensure the layers are even.

Is it better to cut long layers when your hair is wet or dry?

It's best to cut long layers when your hair is dry since wet hair can stretch and appear longer than it actually is.

Hair Care

After cutting your long layers, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shape. Here are some tips for hair care:

- Use a heat protectant before styling with hot tools.

- Avoid using excessive heat on your hair.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

Cutting long layers in women's hair can seem intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done at home. Follow our step-by-step guide and enjoy your new hairstyle!

How To Wear A Beanie With A Round Face Tips And Tricks

Beanies are a popular winter accessory that can add style and warmth to any outfit. However, if you have a round face, you may be hesitant to wear a beanie because you're not sure how to pull it off without looking silly. Fear not! In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks on how to wear a beanie with a round face.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Beanie with a Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

How to Wear a Beanie with a Round Face

Firstly, it's important to choose the right style of beanie for your face shape. Look for a beanie that sits snugly on your head without being too tight or too loose. Avoid beanies that are too bulky or oversized, as they can make your face look rounder.

Next, consider the material of the beanie. Opt for a beanie made from a soft and stretchy material, such as wool or acrylic, as it will conform to the shape of your head and won't add any extra bulk.

When it comes to wearing the beanie, there are a few different styles to choose from. One option is to wear the beanie slightly back on your head, so that it sits just above your forehead. This will create a slouchy, relaxed look that can help to balance out your face shape.

Another option is to wear the beanie pulled down over your forehead and ears, with a small amount of hair showing at the front. This can help to elongate your face and create a more flattering look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to wear a beanie with a round face:

  1. Choose a beanie that fits snugly on your head without being too tight or too loose.
  2. Opt for a soft and stretchy material, such as wool or acrylic.
  3. Decide on the style you want to go for - slouchy or pulled down over your forehead.
  4. If you're going for the slouchy look, position the beanie slightly back on your head, so that it sits just above your forehead.
  5. If you're pulling the beanie down over your forehead, make sure to leave a small amount of hair showing at the front.
  6. Adjust the beanie as needed to ensure a comfortable and flattering fit.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for wearing a beanie with a round face:

  • Choose a beanie in a dark, neutral color, such as black or navy, as it will create a slimming effect.
  • Pair your beanie with a scarf or jacket that has a V-neckline, as it will help to elongate your face.
  • Try wearing your hair in a side part or a low ponytail, as it can help to balance out your face shape.
  • Avoid wearing your beanie with a high ponytail, as it can make your face look rounder.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a beanie with a round face:

Can I wear a beanie with bangs?

Yes, you can wear a beanie with bangs. Just make sure to leave a small amount of hair showing at the front to help elongate your face.

What if the beanie is too tight?

If the beanie is too tight, it can make your face look rounder. Try stretching it out by pulling it over a large ball or vase overnight. Alternatively, you can opt for a beanie made from a stretchy material that will conform to the shape of your head.

Can I wear a beanie with glasses?

Yes, you can wear a beanie with glasses. Just make sure to position the beanie slightly back on your head so that it doesn't interfere with your glasses.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind when wearing a beanie:

  • Avoid wearing a beanie on wet hair, as it can cause your hair to become frizzy and tangled.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to keep your hair looking fresh and clean between washes.
  • Don't wear a beanie for extended periods of time, as it can cause your hair to become flat and matted.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a beanie with confidence and style, no matter what your face shape may be!

Are you tired of trying different hair care products that don't seem to work on your black hair? Look no further than As I Am, a brand that caters specifically to the needs of black hair. In this article, we will explore how As I Am can benefit your relaxed black hair, and provide tips and tricks for optimal hair care.

Description

As I Am is a hair care brand that caters specifically to the needs of black hair. They offer a wide range of products that are formulated with natural ingredients and are free from sulfates and parabens. As I Am products are designed to promote healthy hair growth, reduce breakage, and enhance your natural curls and coils.

How To Use As I Am For Black Hair

The first step in using As I Am on your black hair is to identify your hair type. As I Am offers products for all hair types, from 3a to 4c. Once you have identified your hair type, you can choose the products that are best suited for your hair.

The next step is to incorporate As I Am into your hair care routine. As I Am offers a range of products, including shampoos, conditioners, leave-in treatments, and styling products. Here's how you can use As I Am for optimal results:

Shampoo

Start by wetting your hair thoroughly. Apply a small amount of As I Am shampoo to your scalp and massage gently. Rinse thoroughly with warm water.

Conditioner

After shampooing, apply As I Am conditioner to your hair. Focus on the ends of your hair, as this is the most prone to breakage. Leave the conditioner on for 3-5 minutes, then rinse thoroughly with cool water.

Leave-In Treatment

For added moisture, apply As I Am leave-in treatment to your hair. This will help to detangle your hair and reduce breakage. Apply a small amount to your hair and distribute evenly with your fingers or a wide-tooth comb.

Styling Products

As I Am offers a range of styling products, including gels, creams, and butters. Choose the product that is best suited for your hair type and style as desired.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to using As I Am for black hair:

  1. Identify your hair type
  2. Choose the As I Am products that are best suited for your hair type
  3. Wet your hair thoroughly
  4. Apply As I Am shampoo to your scalp and massage gently
  5. Rinse thoroughly with warm water
  6. Apply As I Am conditioner to your hair
  7. Leave the conditioner on for 3-5 minutes, then rinse thoroughly with cool water
  8. Apply As I Am leave-in treatment to your hair
  9. Distribute evenly with your fingers or a wide-tooth comb
  10. Style your hair as desired using As I Am styling products

Tips for Optimal Results

Here are some tips for using As I Am on your black hair:

  • Use As I Am products consistently for best results
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • For added moisture, use As I Am leave-in treatment on damp hair
  • Protect your hair at night with a satin or silk scarf or pillowcase
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can cause damage

FAQ

Q: Can As I Am be used on natural hair?

A: Yes, As I Am products are suitable for natural hair as well as relaxed hair.

Q: Are As I Am products sulfate-free?

A: Yes, As I Am products are free from sulfates and parabens.

Q: Can As I Am products be used on all hair types?

A: As I Am offers products for all hair types, from 3a to 4c.

Hair Care Routine

Here's a sample hair care routine using As I Am for black hair:

  • Wash your hair once a week with As I Am shampoo
  • Deep condition your hair once a month
  • Apply As I Am leave-in treatment to your hair after washing
  • Style your hair using As I Am styling products
  • Protect your hair at night with a satin or silk scarf or pillowcase

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve healthy, moisturized, and defined black hair with As I Am. Incorporate As I Am into your hair care routine for optimal results.

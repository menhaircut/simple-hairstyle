How To Cut Your Own Hair Men s Medium Length A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 26, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts and want to learn how to cut your own hair? If you have medium length hair and are looking for a new style or just want to trim your ends, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut your own hair, tips for a successful cut, and answer frequently asked questions about cutting men's medium length hair.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Men's Medium Length

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and knowledge, it can be a breeze. Here are the steps you need to follow to cut your own hair:

1. Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to gather the right tools. You will need:

  • Clippers with guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Haircutting cape or old shirt

2. Decide on Your Style

Before you start cutting, decide on the style you want. Look at pictures online or in magazines for inspiration. You can also consult with a professional hairstylist for advice.

3. Start with Clean Hair

Wash and dry your hair before you start cutting. This will make it easier to see the length and texture of your hair.

4. Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair into four parts: top, left side, right side, and back.

5. Start Cutting

Start with the back section of your hair. Use your clippers with a guard to trim the ends to the desired length. Use the scissors to trim any longer pieces.

6. Move to the Sides

Next, move to the sides of your head. Use the same technique as the back section, using your clippers with a guard and scissors to trim any longer pieces.

7. Trim the Top

Finally, trim the top of your hair. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length.

8. Check Your Work

Once you have finished cutting, use your mirror to check your work. Make any final adjustments as needed.

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to cut your own hair men's medium length:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to see the length and texture of your hair.

Finally, trim the top of your hair. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length.

Tips for a Successful Cut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a successful cut:

  • Start with a longer guard on your clippers and work your way down to a shorter guard.
  • Use a comb to ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush.
  • Check your work frequently in the mirror.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, consider consulting with a professional hairstylist.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I cut my own hair?

You should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. It's just hair and it will grow back. You can always make adjustments or consult with a professional hairstylist for help.

What if I don't have clippers or scissors?

If you don't have clippers or scissors, consider purchasing them or borrowing them from a friend or family member. It's important to use the right tools for a successful cut.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and knowledge, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to achieve a successful cut and keep your hair looking great.

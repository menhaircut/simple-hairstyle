Most Attractive Face Shape Everything You Need To Know

Written by Aprilia Sep 01, 2023 · 4 min read

In this article, we will discuss what the most attractive face shape is, how to determine your face shape, and tips on how to enhance your features. We will also answer some frequently asked questions and give you some hair care tips to complement your face shape. So, let's dive in!

What is the Most Attractive Face Shape?

According to a study conducted by cosmetic surgeon Dr. Julian De Silva, the most attractive face shape is the oval face shape. The oval face shape has a balanced proportion, with the forehead and jawline being slightly narrower than the cheekbones. This face shape also has a harmonious symmetry with the features complementing each other.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape can be done by following these simple steps:

  1. Pull your hair back and take a picture of your face.
  2. Trace the outline of your face on the picture using a marker or pen.
  3. Identify the shape of your face by comparing it to the following face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

The Oval Face Shape

The oval face shape has a length that is one and a half times the width of the face. The forehead and jawline are slightly narrower than the cheekbones, creating a balanced proportion. This face shape is versatile and can pull off any hairstyle.

The Round Face Shape

The round face shape is characterized by a face that is as wide as it is long. The forehead and jawline are rounded, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face. This face shape benefits from hairstyles that add height and length to the face.

The Square Face Shape

The square face shape has a strong jawline and forehead that are almost the same width. The cheekbones are also prominent, creating a square shape. This face shape looks great with hairstyles that soften the angles of the face.

The Heart Face Shape

The heart face shape has a wide forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow jawline that comes to a point. This face shape benefits from hairstyles that add volume to the chin area.

The Diamond Face Shape

The diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and jawline, with wide cheekbones that are the widest part of the face. This face shape looks great with hairstyles that add volume to the forehead and chin area.

How to Enhance Your Features

Once you have determined your face shape, you can enhance your features by following these tips:

  • Choose hairstyles that flatter your face shape.
  • Use makeup to highlight your best features.
  • Wear accessories that complement your face shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can face shape change over time?

No, your face shape is determined by your bone structure, which does not change over time.

2. Can hairstyles change the shape of my face?

No, hairstyles cannot change the shape of your face, but they can enhance your features and complement your face shape.

3. Can plastic surgery change my face shape?

Yes, plastic surgery can change the shape of your face, but it is a permanent and costly solution.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape and how to enhance your features, here are some hair care tips to complement your face shape:

  • For oval face shape: any hairstyle works
  • For round face shape: add height and length to the face
  • For square face shape: soften the angles of the face
  • For heart face shape: add volume to the chin area
  • For diamond face shape: add volume to the forehead and chin area

We hope this article has helped you determine your face shape and provided you with tips on how to enhance your features. Remember, the most attractive face shape is the one that is confident and happy. Embrace your unique features and be proud of who you are!

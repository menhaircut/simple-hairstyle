Are you over 50 and have a round face? Are you looking for short hairstyles that can flatter your face and make you look younger? If yes, then you have come to the right place. How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Round Face Over 50

Choosing the right hairstyle for your round face can be a daunting task, especially if you are over 50. However, with the right guidance, you can easily find a style that flatters your face and makes you look younger. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  • Look for hairstyles that add height to your face, such as a pixie cut or a short bob.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as a blunt bob or a chin-length cut.
  • Consider hairstyles with layers or angled cuts that can create an illusion of a longer face.
  • Choose hairstyles that have side-swept bangs or a side part, as they can help to elongate your face.

Short Hairstyles for Round Face Over 50

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle for your round face over 50, let's take a look at some of the best short hairstyles:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. It is a short, layered cut that can add height to your face and make you look younger. It is also easy to maintain and style, making it perfect for busy women.

Short Bob

A short bob is another great hairstyle for round faces over 50. It is a classic cut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. You can choose a blunt bob or a layered bob, depending on your preference.

Asymmetrical Bob

The asymmetrical bob is a modern take on the classic bob. It is a short, angled cut that can create an illusion of a longer face. You can choose an asymmetrical bob with long or short layers, depending on your style.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add dimension to your short hairstyle. They can help to elongate your face and draw attention to your eyes. You can choose thick or wispy bangs, depending on your preference.

Step-by-step Guide to Short Hairstyles for Round Face Over 50

Now that you have chosen your short hairstyle, here is a step-by-step guide on how to style it:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting your hair at the roots to create volume.
  • Style your hair using a flat iron or a curling iron, depending on your preference.
  • Finish off with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Round Face Over 50

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hairstyle:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I dye my hair if I have a round face over 50?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have a round face over 50. However, it is important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and highlights your best features.

2. Can I wear bangs if I have a round face over 50?

Yes, you can wear bangs if you have a round face over 50. However, it is important to choose the right type of bangs that flatters your face shape. Side-swept bangs are a great option for round faces.

3. How do I know which short hairstyle is right for me?

You can consult with your hairstylist to determine which short hairstyle is right for you. They can take into account your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle to recommend a style that suits you.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Round Face Over 50

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools as it can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with SPF.

Now that you have read our guide to short hairstyles for round face over 50, you can choose a style that flatters your face and makes you look younger. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. If you have any questions, feel free to consult with your hairstylist.

What To Wear In 60 To 70 Degree Weather Tips And Advice

Written by Luffy Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering what to wear in 60 to 70 degree weather? Whether you're going for a hike, heading to work, or just running errands, dressing appropriately for the weather is important for both comfort and style. In this blog post, we'll provide you with some tips and advice on what to wear in 60 to 70 degree weather, including clothing options, accessories, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • Clothing Options
  • Accessories
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQ

Clothing Options

When it comes to dressing for 60 to 70 degree weather, it's all about layers. The temperature can fluctuate throughout the day, so it's important to be able to adjust your clothing accordingly. Here are some clothing options to consider:

1. Light Jacket or Cardigan

A light jacket or cardigan is perfect for this temperature range. It provides just enough warmth in the morning or evening, but can easily be removed if it gets too hot during the day. Opt for a denim jacket or a lightweight cardigan in a neutral color.

2. Long-Sleeved Shirt

A long-sleeved shirt is a great option for layering. Wear it under a light jacket or cardigan, or pair it with a scarf for added warmth. Look for shirts made of lightweight materials like cotton or linen.

3. Pants or Jeans

Pants or jeans are a must for 60 to 70 degree weather. Opt for lighter colors like beige or white, or go with classic denim. If you prefer shorts, make sure to pair them with a long-sleeved shirt or light jacket.

4. Comfortable Shoes

Comfortable shoes are a must for any weather, but especially for 60 to 70 degree weather. Sneakers or loafers are great options, and you can also opt for ankle boots if it's a bit cooler outside.

Accessories

Accessories can add the perfect finishing touch to any outfit. Here are some options to consider:

1. Scarf

A scarf is a great accessory for 60 to 70 degree weather. It can add warmth to your outfit, and can easily be removed if it gets too hot. Look for scarves made of lightweight materials like cotton or silk.

2. Sunglasses

Sunglasses are a must for any sunny day, and can also add a stylish touch to your outfit. Look for a classic pair of aviators or wayfarers.

3. Hat

A hat can add both style and functionality to your outfit. Opt for a baseball cap or fedora for a casual look, or a wide-brimmed hat for sun protection.

Hair Care Tips

When it comes to hair care in 60 to 70 degree weather, it's important to protect your hair from the sun and keep it hydrated. Here are some tips:

1. Wear a Hat

As mentioned earlier, wearing a hat can protect your hair from the sun's rays. Opt for a wide-brimmed hat for maximum sun protection.

2. Use a Heat Protectant

If you're using heat styling tools on your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

3. Hydrate Your Hair

Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized in the dry weather. You can also use a hair mask once a week for extra hydration.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear shorts in 60 to 70 degree weather?

A: Yes, you can wear shorts in 60 to 70 degree weather, but make sure to pair them with a long-sleeved shirt or light jacket.

Q: What kind of shoes should I wear in 60 to 70 degree weather?

A: Comfortable shoes like sneakers or loafers are great options for 60 to 70 degree weather.

Q: What colors should I wear in 60 to 70 degree weather?

A: Lighter colors like beige or white are great options for 60 to 70 degree weather.

By following these tips and advice, you'll be able to dress appropriately and stylishly for 60 to 70 degree weather. Remember to layer your clothing, accessorize appropriately, and take care of your hair in the dry weather.

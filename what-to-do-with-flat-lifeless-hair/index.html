What To Do With Flat Lifeless Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Aug 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Flat, lifeless hair can be frustrating, especially if you've tried everything from volumizing shampoos to teasing and hair sprays. But don't worry, there are still plenty of ways to add some life and volume to your hair without resorting to drastic measures like cutting it all off. In this post, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you breathe new life into your flat hair.

How to Add Volume to Flat Hair

If you're looking for ways to add volume to your hair, there are several things you can try:

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step to adding volume to your hair is to use the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are designed to add volume and lift to your hair. These products usually contain ingredients like keratin, collagen, and biotin, which can help strengthen and thicken your hair.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Another trick to add volume to your hair is to blow dry it upside down. This helps lift your hair at the roots and create more volume. To do this, flip your head over and blow dry your hair from the roots to the ends. Use a round brush to create even more lift and volume.

3. Use a Volumizing Mousse or Spray

If you're looking for a quick fix, try using a volumizing mousse or spray. These products can help add volume and lift to your hair without weighing it down. Apply the product to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers or a brush.

4. Tease Your Hair

If all else fails, you can always tease your hair. This involves backcombing your hair at the roots to create more volume. To do this, take a small section of hair and backcomb it with a teasing brush or comb. Repeat this process until you've teased all the sections of your hair that you want to add volume to.

Step-by-Step Guide to Adding Volume to Flat Hair

If you're not sure where to start, here's a step-by-step guide to adding volume to your flat hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add volume and lift to your hair from the very beginning.

Step 2: Apply a Volumizing Product

After washing your hair, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots. Work the product through your hair with your fingers or a brush.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Flip your head over and blow dry your hair from the roots to the ends. Use a round brush to create more lift and volume.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

If you still need more volume, you can tease your hair at the roots. Use a teasing brush or comb to create more volume and lift.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look with a light hairspray to hold your hair in place and add some shine.

Tips for Maintaining Volume in Your Hair

Once you've added volume to your hair, you'll want to make sure it stays that way. Here are some tips for maintaining volume in your hair:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it look flat and lifeless. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days to keep it looking healthy and voluminous.

2. Use Dry Shampoo

If your hair starts to look oily between washes, try using a dry shampoo. This can help absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair at the same time.

3. Avoid Heavy Products

Using heavy products like oils and serums can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Stick to light, volumizing products to keep your hair looking lifted and full.

FAQs About Flat Lifeless Hair

Q: Can cutting your hair add volume?

A: Yes, cutting your hair can add volume, especially if you get layers or a shorter haircut. This can help create more movement and lift in your hair.

Q: Can a hair stylist help add volume to my hair?

A: Yes, a hair stylist can recommend products and hairstyles that can help add volume to your hair. They may also suggest a haircut that can help create more lift and movement in your hair.

Q: Can heat damage make my hair look flat?

A: Yes, using too much heat on your hair can damage it and make it look flat and lifeless. Try to limit your use of hot tools and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

Hair Care Tips for Flat Lifeless Hair

Finally, here are some additional hair care tips to help you keep your hair looking healthy and voluminous:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss. Make sure you're getting enough protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet to keep your hair looking its best.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair and scalp hydrated, which can promote healthy hair growth and prevent dryness and breakage.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

By following these tips and tricks, you can add volume and lift to your flat, lifeless hair and keep it looking healthy and voluminous.

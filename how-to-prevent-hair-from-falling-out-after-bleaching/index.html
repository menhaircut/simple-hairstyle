How To Prevent Hair From Falling Out After Bleaching Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Sep 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you worried about losing your hair after bleaching? Bleaching can be a tricky process that can cause a lot of damage to your hair. While it can give you a beautiful look, it is important to take proper care of your hair to avoid hair loss. In this article, we will provide you with tips on how to prevent hair from falling out after bleaching.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Prevent Hair from Falling Out After Bleaching
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Prevent Hair from Falling Out After Bleaching

If you want to prevent your hair from falling out after bleaching, it is important to follow a few simple steps:

  • Use a good quality bleach that is gentle on your hair
  • Avoid over-bleaching your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment after bleaching
  • Avoid heat styling tools that can further damage your hair
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to prevent hair from falling out after bleaching:

  1. Choose a good quality bleach that is gentle on your hair.
  2. Avoid over-bleaching your hair, as this can cause damage and hair loss.
  3. Use a deep conditioning treatment after bleaching to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  4. Avoid heat styling tools that can further damage your hair, such as flat irons and curling wands.
  5. Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes and relaxers.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks to help you prevent hair loss after bleaching:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this can help prevent breakage.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause tension and breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and biotin, which can help keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about preventing hair loss after bleaching:

Q. How long does it take for hair to stop falling out after bleaching?

A. It can take several weeks or even months for your hair to stop falling out after bleaching. This is because the damage caused by bleaching can take time to repair.

Q. Can hair grow back after falling out from bleaching?

A. Yes, hair can grow back after falling out from bleaching. However, it may take some time for your hair to grow back to its original thickness and length.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes and relaxers.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and biotin, which can help keep your hair healthy and strong.

By following these tips and tricks, you can prevent hair loss after bleaching and keep your hair healthy and strong.

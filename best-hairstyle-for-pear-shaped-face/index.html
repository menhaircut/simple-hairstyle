Best Hairstyle For Pear Shaped Face A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of experimenting with different hairstyles that don't complement your pear-shaped face? Well, you're not alone. Finding the perfect hairstyle for your face shape can be a challenging task. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll guide you through the best hairstyle for pear shaped face, how to style it, tips, and hair care. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

Before we dive into the best hairstyle for pear shaped face, let's first understand what it means to have a pear-shaped face. A pear-shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead, wide cheekbones, and a prominent jawline. The goal is to create a balance between the upper and lower half of your face. The best way to achieve this is by opting for hairstyles that add volume to your hair's crown and create width around your forehead.

How to

The best hairstyle for pear shaped face is one that adds volume to the crown area, creates width around the forehead, and draws attention away from the jawline. Here are some popular hairstyles that you can try:

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that looks great on most face shapes, including pear-shaped. Opt for a chin-length or shoulder-length bob cut that adds volume to the crown area and creates width around the forehead. You can also add layers to create movement and texture to your hair.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and daring hairstyle that can accentuate your facial features. Opt for a side-swept pixie cut that adds volume to the crown area and creates width around the forehead. You can also add layers to create texture and movement to your hair.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to create width around the forehead and draw attention away from the jawline. Opt for long side-swept bangs that cover your forehead and blend well with the rest of your hair.

Step by Step

Here's how you can style your hair using the best hairstyle for pear shaped face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that suit your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the crown area.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown area and create width around the forehead.
  4. If you're opting for a bob cut or pixie cut, use a flat iron to create texture and movement to your hair.
  5. Finish off by applying a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while styling your hair using the best hairstyle for pear shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as it can make your face look wider.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown area and create width around the forehead.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits your style and personality.

FAQ

Q. Can I opt for a middle parting hairstyle if I have a pear-shaped face?

A. Middle parting hairstyle can make your face look longer and accentuate your jawline. It's best to opt for hairstyles that create width around the forehead and add volume to the crown area.

Q. Can I opt for a short bob cut if I have a pear-shaped face?

A. Yes, you can opt for a short bob cut that adds volume to the crown area and creates width around the forehead. It's best to avoid a bob cut that ends on your chin, as it can accentuate your jawline.

Q. Can I opt for a ponytail if I have a pear-shaped face?

A. Yes, you can opt for a ponytail, but make sure to create volume at the crown area and avoid tying your hair too tight. You can also opt for a side ponytail that adds volume to the crown area and creates width around the forehead.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind while styling your hair using the best hairstyle for pear shaped face:

  • Wash your hair regularly using products that suit your hair type.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as it can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote hair growth.

That's it! We hope this guide helps you find the best hairstyle for pear shaped face. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits your style and personality. Remember, confidence is the key to rock any hairstyle!

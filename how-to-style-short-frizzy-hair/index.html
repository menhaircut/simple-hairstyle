How To Style Short Frizzy Hair

Written by Daniel Oct 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short frizzy hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a sleek and polished look. In this article, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to style short frizzy hair, as well as tips and tricks to help you maintain your hairstyle throughout the day.

Description

Frizz can be caused by a variety of factors, including humidity, heat damage, and genetics. Short hair can be particularly prone to frizz, as it doesn't have the weight of longer hair to keep it in place. However, with the right styling products and techniques, you can tame your frizz and create a smooth and polished look.

How to Style Short Frizzy Hair

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

The key to styling short frizzy hair is to start with a clean slate. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, and then towel dry your hair gently.

Step 2: Apply a Smoothing Product

Apply a smoothing product, such as a serum or cream, to your hair. This will help to tame frizz and create a smooth surface for styling. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer with a concentrator nozzle, blow dry your hair in sections. Use a round brush to smooth your hair as you blow dry, and be sure to direct the airflow down the hair shaft to prevent frizz.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to further smooth your hair. Take small sections of hair and run the flat iron over them, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 5: Finish with a Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hairspray to keep your hair in place. Be sure to hold the hairspray bottle at least six inches away from your head to prevent a sticky or stiff look.

Tips for Styling Short Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style short frizzy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Avoid using a brush on wet hair, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Choose a smoothing product that is specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.

FAQ

Q: Can I style short frizzy hair without heat?

A: Yes, you can style short frizzy hair without heat by using techniques such as braiding, twisting, or pinning your hair. You can also try using a curl enhancing product to create soft, natural waves.

Q: How often should I wash my short frizzy hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people prefer to wash their hair daily, while others may only wash their hair once or twice a week. If you have particularly dry hair, you may want to limit washing to once or twice a week to prevent further damage.

Hair Care for Short Frizzy Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, frizz-free hair. Here are some tips to keep your short frizzy hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too frequently.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, on your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and polished look for your short frizzy hair. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and styling methods until you find what works best for you.

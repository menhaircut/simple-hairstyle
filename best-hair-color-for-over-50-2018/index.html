Best Hair Color For Over 50 2018 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for the best hair color to suit your personality and style? Look no further! In this article, we are going to provide you with all the information you need to choose the best hair color for over 50 in 2018. From descriptions to tips and FAQs, we've got you covered.

Description

As we get older, our hair tends to lose its natural pigment, which leads to gray hair. However, this doesn't mean that we have to stick to gray hair. In fact, there are many different hair colors that can complement our skin tone and make us look younger.

The best hair color for over 50 in 2018 is one that complements your skin tone, eye color, and personality. Whether you prefer a natural look or something more adventurous, there's a hair color out there that's perfect for you.

How To

Choosing the best hair color for over 50 in 2018 requires some research and experimentation. Here's how to do it:

  1. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, go for golden or honey shades. If you have cool undertones, opt for ash or platinum shades.
  2. Consider your eye color. Blue eyes look great with warm shades, while green eyes look great with cool shades.
  3. Think about your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, a low-maintenance hair color is best.
  4. Experiment with different shades. Don't be afraid to try something new!

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the best hair color for over 50 in 2018:

  1. Determine your skin tone and eye color.
  2. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color.
  3. Consider your lifestyle and choose a low-maintenance hair color if necessary.
  4. Experiment with different shades until you find the perfect one.
  5. Visit a professional stylist to ensure that the hair color is applied correctly.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hair color for over 50 in 2018:

  • Consider your natural hair color when choosing a shade.
  • Choose a hair color that will enhance your features.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different shades.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.
  • Visit a professional stylist for the best results.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair color for over 50 in 2018:

Q: What is the best hair color for over 50?

A: The best hair color for over 50 is one that complements your skin tone, eye color, and personality. Some popular shades include blonde, brown, and red.

Q: Should I choose a natural or bold hair color?

A: It depends on your personality and style. If you prefer a natural look, choose a hair color that's close to your natural shade. If you're feeling adventurous, go for a bold hair color.

Q: How often do I need to touch up my hair color?

A: It depends on the hair color and your hair growth rate. Generally, you should touch up your hair color every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After you've chosen the best hair color for over 50 in 2018, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for colored hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip away the color.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage the hair and fade the color.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips, you'll be able to maintain your new hair color and look fabulous at any age.

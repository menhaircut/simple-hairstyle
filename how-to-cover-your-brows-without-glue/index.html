How To Cover Your Brows Without Glue

Written by William Aug 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of using glue to cover your brows before applying makeup? Glue can be harsh on your skin and cause irritation. Fortunately, there are alternative methods to cover your brows without glue. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps of how to cover your brows without glue.

How to Cover Your Brows Without Glue

Step 1: Brush Your Brows

Before you start covering your brows, make sure to brush them upwards with a spoolie brush. This will help you see the shape of your brows and make it easier to cover them.

Step 2: Apply Concealer

Using a small brush, apply a concealer that matches your skin tone over your brows. Make sure to blend it out evenly. This will help to neutralize the color of your brows and make it easier to cover them with foundation.

Step 3: Apply Foundation

Apply a full-coverage foundation over your brows using a flat brush. Make sure to blend it out evenly and cover the entire brow area.

Step 4: Set with Powder

Using a translucent powder, set your foundation by lightly dusting it over your brows. This will help to keep your brows covered and prevent any smudging.

Step 5: Fill in Your Brows

Using a brow pencil or powder, fill in your brows as desired. Make sure to blend it out evenly and create a natural-looking shape.

Tips for Covering Your Brows Without Glue

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect brow cover without glue:

  • Use a concealer that matches your skin tone to help neutralize the color of your brows.
  • Make sure to blend out your foundation and powder evenly to prevent any harsh lines.
  • Use a brow pencil or powder to fill in your brows after covering them to create a natural-looking shape.
  • Practice makes perfect - it may take a few tries to get the hang of covering your brows without glue.

FAQ

Can I use this method for all skin types?

Yes, this method can be used for all skin types. However, if you have sensitive skin, make sure to use a gentle concealer and foundation to prevent any irritation.

How long will my brows stay covered?

Your brows should stay covered throughout the day as long as you set your foundation with powder. If you notice any smudging, simply touch up with a brow pencil or powder.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your brows after covering them. Make sure to gently wash your face at night and avoid rubbing the brow area. You can also apply a brow serum or oil to promote healthy hair growth.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to cover your brows without glue, you can achieve the perfect brow cover without any harsh chemicals. Remember to practice and be patient - it may take a few tries to get the hang of it. With these tips and steps, you'll be on your way to flawless brows in no time.

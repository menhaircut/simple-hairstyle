Mens Haircuts Short Sides Long Top A Complete Guide

In recent years, mens haircuts short sides long top have become very popular among men of all ages. This style is versatile, low maintenance, and can be worn in a variety of ways. Whether you want a classic, slicked back look or a messy, textured style, the short sides long top haircut can be tailored to your individual taste.

Description

The mens haircuts short sides long top is characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on the top. The length of the hair on top can vary from a few inches to several inches, depending on the desired look. This style is often paired with a fade or undercut on the sides, which adds contrast and definition to the haircut.

How To

If you want to achieve the mens haircuts short sides long top, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it's important to find a skilled barber or stylist who can give you the right cut. Bring in photos of the style you want and be clear about the length and texture you're looking for.

To maintain the style, you'll need to trim the sides and back every few weeks to keep the length in check. The top can be styled with a variety of products, including pomade, wax, or clay. Experiment with different products to find the right one for your hair type and desired style.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the mens haircuts short sides long top:

  1. Consult with a barber or stylist to determine the right length and texture for your hair.
  2. Ask for a fade or undercut on the sides and back, leaving the hair longer on top.
  3. Use a comb or brush to style the hair on top into the desired shape.
  4. Apply a small amount of product to the hair on top to add texture and hold.
  5. Trim the sides and back every few weeks to maintain the length.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting the mens haircuts short sides long top:

  • Bring in photos of the style you want to show your barber or stylist.
  • Experiment with different products to find the right one for your hair type and desired style.
  • Consider adding a beard or mustache to complement the haircut.
  • Try different styling techniques, such as slicking the hair back or creating a messy, textured look.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for adjustments or modifications to the style if you're not happy with the initial cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircuts short sides long top:

What face shapes does this style suit?

The mens haircuts short sides long top can work well on a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces.

What hair types does this style work best for?

This style works well on most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, it may not be suitable for very fine or thinning hair.

Is this style easy to maintain?

Yes, the mens haircuts short sides long top is relatively low maintenance. You'll need to trim the sides and back every few weeks, but the top can be styled in a variety of ways with minimal effort.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircuts short sides long top looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers or straighteners too often, as this can damage the hair.

Regular trims will also help to keep the hair in good condition and prevent split ends. If you're experiencing any hair loss or thinning, talk to your doctor or a hair loss specialist about treatment options.

By following these tips, you can enjoy the mens haircuts short sides long top and keep your hair looking great.

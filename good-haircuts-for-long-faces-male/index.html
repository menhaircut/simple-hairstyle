Good Haircuts For Long Faces Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 07, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to haircuts, choosing the right one can make all the difference. If you have a long face, it can be challenging to find a style that complements your features. However, with the right haircut, you can balance out your proportions and enhance your facial features. In this article, we will discuss some of the best haircuts for long faces male and how to style them.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long faces are characterized by a high forehead, narrow cheeks, and a prominent chin. The goal of a good haircut for a long face is to create the illusion of width and balance out your features. This can be achieved by adding volume to the sides of your head while keeping the top and back of your hair relatively short. The ideal haircut for a long face should also complement your personal style and be easy to maintain.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a long face, there are several factors to consider. Firstly, consider your hair type and texture. The thickness and coarseness of your hair will affect how it falls and how much volume it can hold. Secondly, consider your personal style and the image you want to project. A good haircut should reflect your personality and enhance your overall appearance. Lastly, consider your face shape and features. The right haircut can highlight your best features and balance out any asymmetry.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for getting a good haircut for a long face:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist who can assess your hair type, face shape, and personal style.
  2. Choose a haircut that complements your features and enhances your personal style.
  3. Ask your stylist to add volume to the sides of your head while keeping the top and back of your hair relatively short.
  4. Style your hair using a volumizing product to create the illusion of width and balance.
  5. Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims and using high-quality hair care products.

Tips

Here are some tips for getting the best haircut for a long face:

  • Choose a haircut that adds volume to the sides of your head, such as a side part or layered cut.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat, as they can make your face appear even longer.
  • Use a volumizing product, such as mousse or hairspray, to add texture and height to your hair.
  • Consider getting a fringe or bangs to shorten the appearance of your forehead.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for long faces male:

What is the best haircut for a long face?

The best haircut for a long face is one that adds volume to the sides of your head while keeping the top and back of your hair relatively short. This can be achieved with a side part, layered cut, or textured crop.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat, as they can make your face appear even longer. Also, avoid center parts, slicked-back styles, and hairstyles with too much volume on top.

Can I still have long hair if I have a long face?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a long face. However, it is important to add layers and texture to your hair to create the illusion of width and balance.

Hair Care

To maintain your haircut and keep your hair looking its best, it is important to use high-quality hair care products. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your haircut.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut for a long face can be a challenge, but with the right guidance, it is possible to find a style that complements your features and enhances your personal style. By considering your hair type, personal style, and face shape, you can choose a haircut that creates the illusion of width and balance while reflecting your unique personality.

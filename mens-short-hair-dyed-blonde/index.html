Mens Short Hair Dyed Blonde How To Achieve The Look In 2023

Blonde hair has always been popular among women, but it has also been gaining popularity among men in recent years. Mens short hair dyed blonde can give you a fresh and edgy look that is sure to turn heads. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve the look, step by step, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description

Mens short hair dyed blonde has become a popular trend in the last few years, with many men opting for the bold and edgy look. The style is versatile and can be tailored to your preference, whether you want a subtle, natural look, or a bold and bright statement. Blonde hair can also make you look younger and more energetic, which is why it has become a go-to style for many men in 2023.

How to Dye Mens Short Hair Blonde

Dyeing your hair blonde is a relatively simple process, but it does require some preparation and caution. Here are the steps to follow:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Choose a shade of blonde that suits your skin tone and natural hair color. It's essential to pick the right shade to avoid looking washed out or too brassy.
  2. Prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. This will ensure that the dye will be evenly applied and absorbed by your hair.
  3. Apply petroleum jelly or a similar product around your hairline, ears, and neck to prevent the dye from staining your skin.
  4. Put on gloves to protect your hands from the dye.
  5. Follow the instructions on the hair dye package to mix the dye and developer. Make sure to wear an old shirt or towel to protect your clothes from any drips or spills.
  6. Start applying the dye to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the dye evenly and ensure that all strands are covered.
  7. Leave the dye on for the recommended time, usually around 30 minutes, depending on the brand and shade.
  8. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until the water runs clear.
  9. Apply a color-safe conditioner to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off.
  10. Style your hair as desired, and enjoy your new look!

Tips for Maintaining Mens Short Hair Dyed Blonde

Once you have achieved the perfect shade of blonde, you will want to maintain it as long as possible. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking vibrant and healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for color-treated hair to prevent fading and dryness.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as this can damage your hair and cause the color to fade faster.
  • Avoid swimming in chlorinated water, as this can cause the color to fade and turn green.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

FAQs

Is bleaching required to achieve the blonde look?

Yes, bleaching is required to lighten your hair enough to achieve a blonde shade. However, the number of times you need to bleach your hair will depend on your natural hair color and the shade of blonde you want to achieve.

Can I dye my hair blonde at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair blonde at home. However, it's essential to follow the instructions carefully and take precautions to avoid damaging your hair or staining your skin.

How often do I need to touch up my roots?

This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how noticeable the roots are. On average, you may need to touch up your roots every four to six weeks.

Hair Care for Mens Short Hair Dyed Blonde

Maintaining healthy and vibrant hair is essential for any hair color, not just blonde. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and use a heat protectant spray when styling with heat tools.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Overall, mens short hair dyed blonde is a bold and edgy look that can be customized to your preference. Whether you opt for a subtle, natural look or a bright and bold statement, blonde hair is a great way to switch up your style and turn heads. Just remember to take proper precautions when dyeing your hair and maintain it with the right hair care routine.

Are you looking for a trendy and versatile haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the guys haircuts short sides long top. Description

The guys haircuts short sides long top is exactly what it sounds like - short hair on the sides and longer hair on top. This style can be customized to suit your preferences, with the length on top ranging from a few inches to several inches. The sides are usually buzzed or faded, creating a sharp contrast with the longer hair on top. This cut is popular among men of all ages and is suitable for all hair types.

How to Get the Cut

If you're interested in getting a guys haircuts short sides long top, the first step is to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who can give you the look you want. Before your appointment, it's a good idea to bring in pictures of the style you're after. This will give your stylist a clear idea of what you're looking for and ensure that you get the cut you want.

During your appointment, your stylist will use clippers or scissors to trim the sides of your hair, leaving the top longer. They may also use a razor to create a fade or taper effect, depending on your preference. Once the sides are trimmed, your stylist will cut and style the top to your desired length and shape.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the guys haircuts short sides long top:

  1. Find a skilled barber or hairstylist who can give you the look you want.
  2. Buzz or fade the sides of your hair to your desired length.
  3. Cut and style the top to your desired length and shape.
  4. Use styling products like pomade or wax to create your desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you rock your guys haircuts short sides long top:

  • Experiment with different lengths and shapes on top to find the style that suits you best.
  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep your hair in place and add shine.
  • Maintain your cut by visiting your stylist every 2-4 weeks for a trim.
  • Invest in a good quality pair of clippers if you want to maintain your cut at home.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the guys haircuts short sides long top:

Is this cut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the guys haircuts short sides long top can be customized to suit all hair types, from straight to curly to wavy.

Can I style this cut in different ways?

Yes, the longer hair on top allows for a variety of styling options, from slicked back to messy to textured.

How often do I need to get this cut?

You should visit your stylist every 2-4 weeks for a trim to maintain the shape of the cut and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

To keep your guys haircuts short sides long top looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.

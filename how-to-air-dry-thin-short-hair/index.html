How To Air Dry Thin Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have thin short hair, you know that drying it can be a challenge. Blow-drying can cause damage and frizz, while air drying can leave your hair looking limp and lifeless. But don't worry - with the right techniques and products, you can air dry your thin short hair and still have it looking full and voluminous.

How to Air Dry Thin Short Hair: Step by Step

Follow these simple steps for the best air-dried hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add body and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Gently towel-dry your hair, removing as much moisture as possible. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply a lightweight styling product, such as a texturizing spray or mousse, to your damp hair. This will help add volume and hold to your hair.

Step 4: Part Your Hair

Use a comb to part your hair in the desired style.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Allow your hair to air dry naturally, without using any heat tools.

Step 6: Fluff and Style

Once your hair is completely dry, use your fingers to fluff and style your hair.

Tips for Air Drying Thin Short Hair

Here are some additional tips for air drying thin short hair:

Use the Right Products

Look for lightweight styling products that won't weigh down your hair. Texturizing sprays, mousses, and volumizing powders are all great options.

Don't Touch Your Hair

Avoid touching your hair too much while it's drying, as this can cause frizz and damage.

Sleep on Silk

If you have trouble with bedhead, try sleeping on a silk pillowcase. This will help prevent tangles and frizz.

FAQ: Air Drying Thin Short Hair

Can I air dry my thin short hair overnight?

Yes, you can air dry your hair overnight. Just make sure to use a silk pillowcase to prevent tangles and frizz.

How long does it take for thin short hair to air dry?

The amount of time it takes for your hair to air dry depends on its length and thickness. Generally, it should take about 2-3 hours for thin short hair to air dry completely.

Hair Care for Thin Short Hair

To keep your thin short hair looking its best, make sure to follow these hair care tips:

Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Look for products that are specifically designed to add volume and body to your hair.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can cause damage and breakage, so it's best to avoid it as much as possible.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to air dry your thin short hair with confidence. Say goodbye to heat damage and hello to voluminous, healthy-looking hair!

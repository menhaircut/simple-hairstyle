How Do I Know If I Look Better With Dark Or Light Hair

Written by Bardi Jul 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hair color, you may be wondering if you should go for a darker or lighter shade. Deciding on a hair color can be a daunting task, but there are a few things you can do to help you make the right choice. In this blog post, we'll go over how to determine if you look better with dark or light hair.

Table of Contents

  • How To Determine If You Look Better with Dark or Light Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • FAQs

How To Determine If You Look Better with Dark or Light Hair

There are a few things you can do to determine if you look better with dark or light hair:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone can play a big role in determining which hair color will suit you best. If you have a warm skin tone, you may look better with darker hair colors like chocolate brown or auburn. If you have a cool skin tone, lighter shades like platinum blonde or ash brown may be more flattering.

Think About Your Natural Hair Color

If you're unsure about which hair color to choose, think about your natural hair color. If you have naturally dark hair, going too light may not look natural. Similarly, if you have naturally light hair, going too dark may not suit you.

Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining which hair color will suit you best. For example, if you have blue eyes, a warmer hair color like copper or honey blonde may bring out the blue in your eyes.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to help you determine if you look better with dark or light hair:

  1. Determine your skin tone. Are you warm or cool?
  2. Think about your natural hair color. Will going too light or too dark look natural?
  3. Consider your eye color. Will a warm or cool hair color bring out the color in your eyes?
  4. Try on wigs or use virtual hair color apps to see what you look like with different hair colors.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist to get their opinion on what hair color will suit you best.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle. Will a certain hair color require more maintenance than you're willing to commit to?
  • Think about your wardrobe. Will a certain hair color clash with your favorite outfits?
  • Take into account the season. Certain hair colors may be more suitable for different seasons.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new! Hair color can always be changed if you're not happy with the results.

FAQs

What hair color is best for warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with darker hair colors like chocolate brown or auburn.

What hair color is best for cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with lighter shades like platinum blonde or ash brown.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it is possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend consulting with a professional hairstylist for the best results.

Hair Care Tips

After you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your hair color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

By following these tips and taking the time to determine which hair color suits you best, you'll be able to achieve a beautiful new look that you'll love.

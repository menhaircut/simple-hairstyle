How To Hide Crown Balding Female Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Oct 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you experiencing crown balding as a female? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many women experience hair thinning or balding in the crown area, which can be caused by various factors such as genetics, hormonal changes, stress, and even certain hairstyles. While there may not be a permanent solution to this problem, there are ways to hide crown balding and boost your confidence. In this article, we'll show you how to hide crown balding female with some tips and tricks.

How to Hide Crown Balding Female: Tips

Before we dive into the tips, let's first understand what crown balding is. Crown balding is hair thinning or balding in the crown area, which is the top back part of your head. Now, let's explore some ways to hide crown balding:

1. Change Your Hairstyle

One of the easiest ways to hide crown balding is by changing your hairstyle. Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair tightly, such as tight ponytails, braids, or buns, as they can cause further hair loss. Instead, opt for loose hairstyles that add volume to your hair, such as soft waves, curls, or layers. You can also try wearing a side-swept bang or a deep side part to cover the thinning area.

2. Use Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are tiny hair-like particles that attach to your existing hair, adding volume and thickness. They come in different colors and can be easily applied with a spray or a pump. Simply sprinkle the fibers on the thinning area and pat gently to blend with your hair. Hair fibers are a temporary solution and can be easily washed off with shampoo.

3. Try Hair Extensions

Hair extensions are a great way to add length and volume to your hair, especially if you have thinning hair. You can opt for clip-in extensions, which are easy to apply and remove, or go for more permanent options such as tape-in or sew-in extensions. Make sure to choose extensions that match your hair color and texture for a natural look.

4. Use Hair Accessories

Another way to hide crown balding is by using hair accessories. You can try wearing a headband, a scarf, or a hat to cover the thinning area. You can also experiment with different hair clips, barrettes, or pins to create a new hairstyle that conceals the balding area.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to hide crown balding female:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and thickness to your hair.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots. Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause further hair loss.

Step 3: Apply Hair Fibers

If you're using hair fibers, apply them on the thinning area and gently pat to blend with your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair in a way that conceals the balding area, such as a side-swept bang or a loose updo. You can also try using hair accessories to add more volume and cover the thinning area.

Step 5: Maintain Your Hair

Take good care of your hair by avoiding harsh chemicals, using a wide-tooth comb, and eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals. Consult with a dermatologist or a hair specialist if you're experiencing severe hair loss.

Tips for Hair Care

Here are some tips for hair care to prevent further hair loss:

1. Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach, hair dyes, or relaxers, as they can damage your hair and cause further hair loss. Use natural hair care products or consult with a hair specialist for the best products for your hair.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause breakage and hair loss. Start combing from the ends and work your way up to avoid pulling on your hair.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals such as iron, vitamin C, and biotin, which are essential for healthy hair growth. You can also take hair supplements or consult with a nutritionist for a customized diet plan.

FAQs

Q: Can crown balding be reversed?

A: Unfortunately, there's no permanent solution to crown balding. However, there are ways to hide it and boost your confidence.

Q: Can wearing a wig help hide crown balding?

A: Yes, wearing a wig is another option to hide crown balding. Make sure to choose a wig that matches your hair color and texture for a natural look.

Q: Can stress cause crown balding?

A: Yes, chronic stress can cause hair loss, including crown balding. Try to manage your stress levels through meditation, yoga, or other relaxation techniques.

Conclusion

Crown balding can be a distressing experience for women, but there are ways to hide it and boost your confidence. By following the tips and tricks mentioned in this article, you can create a new hairstyle that conceals the balding area and take good care of your hair to prevent further hair loss. Remember to consult with a hair specialist if you're experiencing severe hair loss.

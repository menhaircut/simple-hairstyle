How To Wear Long Hair With A Suit A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have long hair and are wondering how to wear it with a suit, you're not alone. Many men struggle with this issue, as they want to look sharp and professional, but also want to show off their long locks. Luckily, there are several ways to style long hair with a suit that can help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wear Long Hair with a Suit
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Wearing Long Hair with a Suit
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

How to Wear Long Hair with a Suit

When it comes to wearing long hair with a suit, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape
  • Make sure your hair is clean and well-groomed
  • Consider using hair products to keep your hair in place
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too messy or casual

By following these simple tips, you can create a polished, professional look that is sure to impress.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to wearing long hair with a suit:

  1. Clean and condition your hair to make sure it's smooth and sleek.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape. For example, if you have a square face, consider a side-parted style. If you have a round face, try a slicked-back style.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as gel or pomade, to keep your hair in place.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  5. Style your hair into your desired look, using a comb or your fingers to create texture.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to keep your hair in place.

With these steps, you can create a sleek, polished look that is perfect for any formal occasion.

Tips for Wearing Long Hair with a Suit

Here are some additional tips to help you wear long hair with a suit:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too messy or unkempt.
  • Consider getting a haircut or trim to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on the best haircut and styling for your face shape and hair type.

By following these tips, you can create a polished, professional look that is sure to impress.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing long hair with a suit:

Q: Can I wear my hair down with a suit?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair down with a suit, as long as it is clean, well-groomed, and styled in a way that complements your face shape.

Q: Can I wear a ponytail with a suit?

A: It depends on the occasion and the dress code. In general, a ponytail can be too casual for a formal event, but it may be appropriate for a more relaxed occasion.

Q: What if I have curly hair?

A: Curly hair can be a great asset when wearing a suit. Consider using a styling product to enhance your curls and create a polished, professional look.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for maintaining healthy, long hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and treatments, such as coloring and perming, if possible.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can keep your long hair healthy and looking great, whether you're wearing a suit or not.

In conclusion, wearing long hair with a suit can be challenging, but it's definitely doable. By choosing the right hairstyle, keeping your hair clean and well-groomed, and using the right products, you can create a polished, professional look that is sure to impress.

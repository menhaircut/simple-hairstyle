Best Short Haircut For Oblong Face Female

Are you tired of your long hair and looking for a change? Do you have an oblong face and want a short haircut that will flatter your features? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short haircut for oblong face female and provide you with tips on how to maintain and style it.

Description

An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide and has a straighter jawline. The best short haircut for oblong face female is one that adds width to the face and balances out the length. A short haircut that has volume on the sides and top will help to create the illusion of a wider face.

How to

Before you get a short haircut, it is important to consider your lifestyle and hair type. Short haircuts require more maintenance and styling than long hair, so make sure you are willing to commit to it. It is also important to consult with your hairstylist to find a haircut that suits your face shape and features.

Step by step

Here are the steps to get the best short haircut for oblong face female:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to find a short haircut that suits your face shape and features.
  2. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment.
  3. Bring a picture of the haircut you want to show your hairstylist.
  4. Sit back and relax while your hairstylist works their magic.
  5. Style your new haircut with a volumizing mousse or hairspray to add volume to the sides and top of your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain and style your new short haircut:

  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a round brush to blow dry your hair and add volume to the roots.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and waves to your hair.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and control frizz.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best short haircut for oblong face female:

What is the best short haircut for oblong face female?

The best short haircut for oblong face female is one that adds volume to the sides and top of the hair to create the illusion of a wider face.

Will a short haircut make my face look longer?

No, a short haircut can actually help to balance out the length of an oblong face shape by adding width to the sides and top of the hair.

How do I maintain a short haircut?

You can maintain a short haircut by trimming it every 4-6 weeks and using styling products to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-drying.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, the best short haircut for oblong face female is one that adds volume to the sides and top of the hair to create the illusion of a wider face. Consult with your hairstylist to find a haircut that suits your face shape and features, and maintain it with regular trims and styling products.

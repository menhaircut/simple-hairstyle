How Can I Cut The Back Of My Hair Straight

Written by Daniel Nov 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who loves to cut their own hair at home, you may have noticed that it can be a challenge to cut the back of your hair straight. In this blog post, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to cut the back of your hair straight, so you can achieve salon results from the comfort of your own home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut the Back of Your Hair Straight
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting the Back of Your Hair
  • FAQs

How to Cut the Back of Your Hair Straight

The key to cutting the back of your hair straight is to use a mirror that allows you to see the back of your head. You'll also need to invest in some good quality hair cutting scissors, as regular scissors can damage your hair and cause split ends.

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. It's also a good idea to comb your hair thoroughly to ensure there are no tangles or knots.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut the back of your hair straight:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and hold another mirror behind your head. This will allow you to see the back of your head.
  2. Section your hair by dividing it into two parts from ear to ear.
  3. Use a hair clip to secure the top part of your hair and focus on the bottom part first.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the back and hold it straight between your fingers.
  5. Use your hair cutting scissors to cut the hair in a straight line, making sure to cut in a downward motion.
  6. Repeat this process on the other side, making sure to keep the sections of hair even.
  7. Release the top section of your hair and repeat the same process, working from the back to the front.
  8. Once you've cut all your hair, use a mirror to check the back and make any necessary adjustments.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting the Back of Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting the back of your hair:

  • Start with small sections of hair to ensure accuracy.
  • Don't cut your hair too short, as it will be difficult to fix any mistakes.
  • Use hair cutting scissors instead of regular scissors.
  • If you're struggling to cut the back of your hair straight, ask a friend or family member to help.
  • If you're not confident in your cutting skills, it's always a good idea to visit a professional hair stylist.

FAQs

Can I cut the back of my hair straight without a mirror?

No, it's essential to use a mirror to see the back of your head when cutting your hair. You can use a hand-held mirror or a larger mirror that's placed behind you.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, regular scissors can damage your hair and cause split ends. It's best to invest in a good quality pair of hair cutting scissors.

How often should I cut the back of my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips

After cutting your hair, it's important to take good care of it to maintain its health and shine. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut the back of your hair straight like a pro. Remember to take your time and use caution when cutting your hair, and always seek the help of a professional if you're not confident in your cutting skills.

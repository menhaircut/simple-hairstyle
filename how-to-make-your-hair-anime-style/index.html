How To Make Your Hair Anime Style A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 03, 2023 · 4 min read
If you're a fan of anime, you've probably noticed how cool and unique the characters' hairstyles can be. But have you ever wondered how to achieve that look in real life? In this article, we'll teach you how to make your hair anime style, from the basics to more advanced techniques. Whether you're cosplaying or just want to switch up your everyday look, this guide is for you.

Description

Anime hairstyles are often characterized by bold colors, gravity-defying shapes, and intricate designs. They can be inspired by a variety of sources, including traditional Japanese hairstyles, pop culture icons, and fantasy creatures. While some anime hairstyles may seem impossible to recreate in real life, with a little creativity and some hair styling tools, you can achieve a similar look.

How to

To make your hair anime style, you'll need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Inspiration

The first step in making your hair anime style is to decide on your inspiration. Do you want to recreate the hairstyle of your favorite anime character, or create a unique look that embodies the spirit of anime? Look for reference images online, and take note of the colors, shapes, and textures that stand out to you.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start styling, you'll need to prepare your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, and then blow dry it until it's completely dry. If your hair is naturally curly or wavy, you may need to straighten it using a flat iron.

Step 3: Add Volume

Anime hairstyles often have a lot of volume, so you'll need to add some extra oomph to your hair. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and lift to your roots. You can also tease your hair with a comb to create more volume.

Step 4: Create the Shape

Once you've added volume, it's time to create the shape of your anime hairstyle. Depending on the style you're going for, you may need to use hair clips, bobby pins, or hair ties to create the desired shape. Use a hairspray or hair wax to keep the shape in place.

Step 5: Add Color

Anime hairstyles often feature bold and bright colors, so don't be afraid to experiment with hair dye. You can use temporary hair dye or hair chalk to add color to your hair without committing to a permanent change.

Tips

- Practice makes perfect! Don't be discouraged if your first attempt at an anime hairstyle doesn't turn out the way you want it to. Keep trying and experimenting until you find the perfect look. - Invest in good hair styling tools, such as a quality hair dryer, flat iron, and curling iron. These tools will make it easier to achieve the desired shape and texture. - Be gentle with your hair. Anime hairstyles can be very intricate, but don't pull or tug on your hair too much as this can cause damage. - Consider consulting with a professional hairstylist if you're struggling to achieve the desired look. They can offer expert advice and help you achieve the perfect anime hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: What products do I need to make my hair anime style?
A: You'll need a volumizing mousse or spray, hairspray, hair wax, and potentially hair dye or hair chalk. Q: Can I make my hair anime style without using hair dye?
A: Yes, you can achieve an anime-inspired hairstyle without using hair dye. Focus on creating the right shape and texture, and use hair accessories to add pops of color. Q: How long does it take to make my hair anime style?
A: The time it takes to make your hair anime style will depend on the complexity of the hairstyle and how much practice you have. Some styles can be achieved in as little as 20 minutes, while others may take several hours.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair when making it anime style. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Finally, consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking its best. Making your hair anime style can be a fun and creative way to express your love for anime. With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect anime-inspired hairstyle in no time. So grab your hair styling tools and start experimenting!

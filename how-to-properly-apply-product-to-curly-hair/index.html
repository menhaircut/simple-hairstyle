How To Properly Apply Product To Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 14, 2023 · 5 min read

Curly hair is a beautiful and unique hair type that requires special care and attention. One of the most important aspects of curly hair care is using the right products and applying them correctly. In this article, we will discuss how to properly apply product to curly hair to achieve healthy, defined, and bouncy curls.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Apply Product to Curly Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Applying Product to Curly Hair

Description

Curly hair is prone to dryness, frizz, and tangles, which is why it is essential to use the right products and apply them properly. Proper application of hair products helps to define curls, reduce frizz, and increase moisture retention. Whether you have wavy, curly, or coily hair, following the right hair care routine and using the right products can make a significant difference in your curls' health and appearance.

How to Apply Product to Curly Hair

Applying product to curly hair is not always straightforward, but it is essential to get the best results. Here are some general tips to follow when applying product to curly hair:

  • Start with clean and wet hair.
  • Section your hair to ensure that every strand gets coated with product.
  • Apply product in a downward motion to encourage curl formation and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid applying too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and leave them greasy.
  • Scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation and reduce excess water and product.
  • Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser to dry your curls gently.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the general tips let's go over the step-by-step guide to applying product to curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair: Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for curly hair. Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  2. Detangle your hair: After washing your hair, detangle it using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. This step is crucial to ensure that every strand of hair gets coated with product.
  3. Section your hair: Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This step ensures that every strand of hair gets adequately coated with product.
  4. Apply leave-in conditioner: Apply a leave-in conditioner to each section of your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be sure to apply the product in a downward motion to reduce frizz and encourage curl formation.
  5. Apply curl cream or gel: Apply a curl cream or gel to each section of your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be sure to apply the product in a downward motion to reduce frizz and encourage curl formation.
  6. Scrunch your hair: Scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation and reduce excess water and product. Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to scrunch your hair gently.
  7. Diffuse your hair: Use a diffuser to dry your curls gently. Be sure to dry your hair on low heat to avoid damage and frizz. Alternatively, you can allow your hair to air dry.

Tips for Applying Product to Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best results when applying product to curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Apply products to wet hair to ensure maximum absorption.
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates, parabens, or silicones.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Try using a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and protect your curls while you sleep.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about applying product to curly hair:

Q: How much product should I use?
A: Use a small amount of product, about the size of a quarter, for each section of your hair. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and leave them greasy. Q: How often should I apply product to my hair?
A: It depends on your hair's needs and the products you are using. Generally, it is best to apply products to your hair every 2-3 days or as needed. Q: Can I apply product to dry hair?
A: It is best to apply products to wet hair to ensure maximum absorption. However, you can apply products to dry hair for touch-ups or to refresh your curls between washes.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful curls. Here are some tips for taking care of curly hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your curls.
  • Protect your curls from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.

Follow these tips for applying product to curly hair and taking care of your curls, and you will have beautiful, defined, and healthy curls that you will love to show off!

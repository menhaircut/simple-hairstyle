What Is The Best Hairstyle For A 60 Year Old Woman With Thick Hair

Written by Frank Jul 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and more brittle. However, some women are lucky enough to have thick hair even in their 60s. If you are one of them, you might be wondering what is the best hairstyle for you. In this blog post, we will give you some tips and ideas on how to style your thick hair to look fabulous at 60!

Description

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. It's great to have a lot of hair, but it can also be difficult to manage and style. When you are in your 60s, you want a hairstyle that is age-appropriate, easy to maintain, and stylish. You also want a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and enhances your features.

How to

The best hairstyle for a 60 year old woman with thick hair is one that is layered and textured. Layers will add movement and dimension to your hair, while texture will make it look more youthful and modern.

Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Consult a Hairstylist

Before you decide on a hairstyle, it's important to consult a hairstylist. They will be able to recommend a style that suits your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. They will also be able to give you tips on how to maintain your hair and keep it healthy.

Step 2: Choose a Layered Cut

A layered cut will add volume and movement to your hair. It will also make it easier to style. You can opt for a long layered cut or a shoulder-length layered cut, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Add Texture

Texture is important to make your hair look more youthful and modern. You can add texture by getting a perm or using a texturizing spray. You can also use a curling iron or a flat iron to create waves or curls.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you have to style your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new and experiment with different styles.
  • Invest in good quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for 60 year old women with thick hair:

Q: Should I get bangs?

A: Bangs can be a great way to frame your face and add a youthful touch to your hairstyle. However, they require regular maintenance and can be difficult to style.

Q: Can I still wear my hair long?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair long if you have thick hair. However, make sure to get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, make sure to follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals.

By following these tips and ideas, you can find the best hairstyle for a 60 year old woman with thick hair that suits your personality and lifestyle. Remember, age is just a number, and you can still look fabulous at any age!

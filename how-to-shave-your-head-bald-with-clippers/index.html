How To Shave Your Head Bald With Clippers The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Sep 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to shave your head bald with clippers, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll take you through the step-by-step process of how to achieve that perfect bald head look. We'll also give you some tips on how to take care of your scalp after the shave, and answer some frequently asked questions about bald heads.

How to Shave Your Head Bald with Clippers

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, a mirror, a towel or cloth, and shaving cream or gel. You may also want to use a pre-shave oil or lotion to help soften your hair and skin.

Step 1: Trim Your Hair

Before you start the actual shave, you'll want to trim your hair as short as possible. This will make it easier to shave with the clippers. Use the clippers with the lowest guard to trim your hair as close to the scalp as possible.

Step 2: Apply Shaving Cream or Gel

Apply a generous amount of shaving cream or gel to your head. Make sure the cream or gel covers all the areas you want to shave. This will help the clippers glide smoothly over your scalp, and also help prevent irritation.

Step 3: Begin Shaving

Start shaving with the clippers, using a smooth and even motion. Start at the front of your head and work your way towards the back. Be sure to go over each area several times to ensure a smooth and even shave.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Once you've finished shaving, check your work in the mirror. Make sure you haven't missed any spots, and that your head looks even all around. If you see any areas that need touch-up, go over them with the clippers again.

Step 5: Rinse and Moisturize

After you've finished shaving, rinse your head with warm water to remove any remaining shaving cream or gel. Pat your head dry with a towel or cloth. Then, apply a moisturizer or aftershave lotion to your scalp to help soothe any irritation and keep your scalp moisturized.

Tips for Shaving Your Head Bald with Clippers

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect bald head look:

  • Use a new pair of clippers for the cleanest shave
  • Take your time and go over each area several times
  • Shave in the direction of hair growth for a smoother shave
  • Use a pre-shave oil or lotion to help soften your hair and skin
  • Use a moisturizer or aftershave lotion to soothe your scalp and keep it moisturized

FAQ: Bald Heads

Q: Will my hair grow back after shaving my head?

A: Yes, your hair will grow back after shaving your head. However, it may take some time for it to grow back to its original length.

Q: What should I do if I experience irritation after shaving my head?

A: If you experience irritation after shaving your head, apply aloe vera or a soothing lotion to help calm the skin. If the irritation persists, consult a dermatologist.

Hair Care for Bald Heads

Just because you have a bald head doesn't mean you can neglect your hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep your scalp healthy and moisturized:

  • Wash your scalp regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Apply a moisturizer or sunscreen to your scalp to protect it from the sun
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or straighteners on your scalp
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote blood flow and keep it healthy

Now that you know how to shave your head bald with clippers, you can confidently rock that bald head look. Remember to take care of your scalp and keep it moisturized, and you'll be sure to turn heads wherever you go.

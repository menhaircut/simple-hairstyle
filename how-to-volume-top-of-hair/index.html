How to Volume Top of Hair - Step by Step Guide

In this year 2023, having voluminous hair is a trend that never goes out of style. But achieving volume on the top of your hair can be a bit challenging, especially if you have flat or thin hair. In this step by step guide, we will show you how to volume top of hair easily and without damaging your hair.

  • How to Volume Top of Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Maintain Volume

How to Volume Top of Hair

There are several ways to add volume to the top of your hair. Here are some popular methods:

1. Teasing

Teasing or backcombing is the most popular method for adding volume to the top of your hair. Take a section of your hair, hold it straight up, and tease it with a comb from the tips to the roots. Repeat this process until you achieve the desired volume.

2. Blow-drying

Blow-drying your hair upside down can also add volume to the top. Start by applying a volumizing mousse and flip your head upside down. Blow-dry your hair while scrunching it with your fingers. Once your hair is dry, flip your head back up and style as desired.

3. Hair Rollers

Using hair rollers can also add volume to the top of your hair. Roll your hair in large rollers and leave them in for at least 30 minutes. Once you remove the rollers, use a volumizing hairspray to set the style.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to volume top of hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply Volumizing Mousse

Apply a generous amount of volumizing mousse to your hair from root to tip. This will help to add volume and hold to your hair.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Upside Down

Flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair while scrunching it with your fingers. Once your hair is 80% dry, flip your head back up and continue blow-drying until your hair is completely dry.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Take a section of your hair, hold it straight up, and tease it with a comb from the tips to the roots. Repeat this process until you achieve the desired volume.

Step 5: Set with Hairspray

Once you have achieved the desired volume, set your hair with a volumizing hairspray. This will help to hold the style in place and prevent it from falling flat.

Tips to Maintain Volume

Here are some tips to help you maintain volume on the top of your hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh your hair down
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and flatten
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair as it can cause damage and flatten your hair

FAQs

Q: Can I add volume to the top of my hair if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can add volume to the top of your hair even if you have thin hair. Follow the steps mentioned in this guide and use volumizing hair products to achieve the desired volume.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener to add volume to the top of my hair?

A: No, using a hair straightener can cause damage to your hair and flatten it. It is best to use a round brush and blow-dry your hair to add volume.

Hair Care for Voluminous Hair

Here are some tips to take care of your voluminous hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to avoid product buildup and oiliness
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair to avoid breakage
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently to prevent damage to your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and breakage

That's it! Follow these steps and tips to volume top of hair and achieve a voluminous hairstyle. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty.

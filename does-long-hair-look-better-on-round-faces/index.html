Does Long Hair Look Better On Round Faces

Written by Petter Sep 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair has always been considered a symbol of beauty and femininity. However, for women with round faces, the question arises - does long hair look better on round faces? In this article, we will delve deeper into this question and provide you with tips and tricks on how to style long hair if you have a round face.

Description

Round faces are those that have a circular shape, with the width and length of the face being almost equal. This face shape is characterized by soft features, such as a rounded chin and full cheeks. Women with round faces often struggle with finding hairstyles that complement their facial structure.

How To

If you have a round face and are contemplating growing out your hair, here are some tips on how to style it:

1. Add Layers

Adding layers to your long hair can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, making your face appear more angular.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. When getting your bangs cut, ask your stylist to cut them at an angle to create a diagonal line across your forehead.

3. Avoid Blunt Cuts

Blunt cuts can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for layered cuts or soft waves that can help create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style long hair if you have a round face:

1. Blow Dry Your Hair

Start by blow-drying your hair straight. Use a round brush to add volume and movement to your hair.

2. Add Layers

Next, add layers to your hair. Ask your stylist to add layers that start at your jawline and continue down to the ends of your hair.

3. Style Your Bangs

If you have side-swept bangs, style them by blow-drying them to the side. If you don't have bangs, consider getting them cut at an angle to create a diagonal line across your forehead.

4. Curl Your Hair

Finally, use a curling iron to add soft waves to your hair. Make sure to curl your hair away from your face to help create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to style long hair if you have a round face:

1. Avoid Middle Parts

Avoid middle parts, as they can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for a side part to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

2. Experiment with Updos

Experiment with updos, such as a high ponytail or a messy bun. Updos can help elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

3. Consider Highlights

Consider adding highlights to your hair. Highlights can help create depth and dimension to your hair, making your face appear more angular.

FAQ

1. What are the best hairstyles for round faces?

The best hairstyles for round faces are those that create the illusion of a slimmer face. These include layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and soft waves.

2. Can short hair look good on round faces?

Yes, short hair can look good on round faces. However, it is important to choose a hairstyle that complements your facial structure. Avoid blunt cuts and opt for layered cuts or soft waves.

Hair Care

If you have long hair, it is important to take care of it to ensure it looks healthy and shiny. Here are some tips on how to care for your long hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is free of sulfates and parabens. These harsh chemicals can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle.

2. Condition Your Hair

Use a conditioner to help hydrate and nourish your hair. Apply conditioner from the mid-lengths to the ends of your hair, avoiding your roots.

3. Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron, use a heat protectant spray to help protect your hair from damage.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, long hair can look great on round faces if styled correctly. By adding layers, side-swept bangs, and soft waves, you can create the illusion of a slimmer face. Remember to take care of your hair by using a gentle shampoo, conditioning regularly, using a heat protectant, and trimming your hair regularly.

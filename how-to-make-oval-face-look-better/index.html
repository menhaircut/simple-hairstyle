How To Make Oval Face Look Better Tips And Tricks

Written by William Sep 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky because it is considered to be the most versatile of all face shapes. However, there are ways to enhance your features and make your face look even better. In this article, we will give you some tips and tricks on how to make oval face look better.

Description of Oval Face Shape

An oval face is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded jawline and a forehead that is wider than the chin. The cheekbones are typically the widest part of the face, and the face tapers slightly towards the chin. This face shape is considered to be the most balanced and proportional, with no prominent features.

How to Make Oval Face Look Better

If you want to enhance your features and make your oval face look even better, here are some tips and tricks to follow:

1. Choose the Right Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can make a big difference in how your face looks. If you have an oval face, you can pull off most hairstyles. However, a layered haircut with volume on top can make your face look more proportionate. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can elongate or widen your face.

2. Highlight Your Cheekbones

Your cheekbones are one of the most prominent features of your face. Highlighting them can make your face look more defined and sculpted. Use a bronzer or a highlighter to contour your cheekbones and make them stand out.

3. Use the Right Makeup Techniques

Makeup can help enhance your features and make your face look even better. Use light-reflecting foundation and concealer to brighten up your face. Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks to create a natural-looking flush. Use a lip color that complements your skin tone to bring attention to your lips.

4. Wear the Right Accessories

Accessories can help balance out your face and make it look more proportionate. Wear earrings that are round or square-shaped to add width to your face. Avoid long and dangly earrings that can elongate your face. Choose sunglasses that are wider than your face to balance out your features.

Step by Step Guide to Make Oval Face Look Better

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make oval face look better:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can enhance your features, you need to determine your face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and look at the shape of your face.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choose a layered haircut with volume on top to make your face look more proportionate.

Step 3: Highlight Your Cheekbones

Use a bronzer or a highlighter to contour your cheekbones and make them stand out.

Step 4: Use the Right Makeup Techniques

Use light-reflecting foundation and concealer to brighten up your face. Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks to create a natural-looking flush. Use a lip color that complements your skin tone to bring attention to your lips.

Step 5: Wear the Right Accessories

Wear earrings that are round or square-shaped to add width to your face. Choose sunglasses that are wider than your face to balance out your features.

Tips for Making Oval Face Look Better

Here are some additional tips for making oval face look better:

1. Avoid Too Much Volume on Top

While volume on top can make your face look more proportionate, too much volume can make your face look wider. Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous.

2. Use a Matte Bronzer

Using a matte bronzer can create a more natural-looking contour than a shimmery one.

3. Use a Lip Liner

Using a lip liner can help define your lips and make them look fuller.

FAQs on How to Make Oval Face Look Better

1. Can oval face shape look bad?

No, oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile of all face shapes, and it can look good with most hairstyles and makeup looks.

2. Can oval face shape wear bangs?

Yes, oval face shape can wear bangs, but it is better to choose side-swept bangs instead of straight across bangs.

3. Can oval face shape wear oversized sunglasses?

Yes, oval face shape can wear oversized sunglasses, but it is better to choose ones that are wider than your face to balance out your features.

Hair Care for Oval Face Shape

To keep your hair healthy and looking good, follow these hair care tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Use Heat Protectant

Before using hot styling tools, use a heat protectant to protect your hair from heat damage.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can enhance your features and make your oval face look even better.

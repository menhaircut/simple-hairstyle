Male Hair Long Top Short Sides The Perfect Hairstyle For Modern Men

Written by Eveline Sep 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a modern man looking for a versatile and stylish hairstyle, then the male hair long top short sides is the perfect option for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years thanks to its easy maintenance, flexibility, and subtle sophistication. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this popular hairstyle, including how to achieve it, hair care tips, and more.

Description

The male hair long top short sides is a haircut that features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides. The length of the hair on the top can vary, depending on personal preference, but it's usually long enough to be styled in different ways. The sides are typically buzzed or faded, creating a sharp contrast between the top and sides of the head. This hairstyle can be worn with or without a beard, making it a versatile option for different facial hair styles.

How to Achieve the Male Hair Long Top Short Sides

To achieve the male hair long top short sides, you'll need to visit a professional hairstylist who can help you create the perfect cut for your face shape and hair type. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this hairstyle:

Step 1: Choose the Length of the Hair on Top

Decide how long you want your hair to be on the top. This will depend on your personal style and the shape of your face. If you have a round face, consider keeping the hair on top shorter. If you have a square face, you can go for longer hair on top.

Step 2: Decide on the Type of Fade

You can choose between a high, mid, or low fade for the sides of your head. The type of fade you choose will depend on the length of your hair on top and your personal preference.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have the right cut, you can style your hair in different ways. You can slick it back, comb it to the side, or even create a messy, textured look. Use a good hair product to keep your hair in place and add shine.

Tips for Maintaining the Male Hair Long Top Short Sides

Here are some tips for maintaining your male hair long top short sides:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your hair looking fresh and well-maintained.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Apply a hair product that suits your hair type to keep your hair in place and add shine.
  • If you have a beard, make sure to trim it regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

Can I wear the male hair long top short sides if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! The male hair long top short sides works well with different hair types, including curly hair. Talk to your hairstylist about the best way to achieve this hairstyle with your hair type.

Do I need to use a hair product for the male hair long top short sides?

Yes, you'll need to use a hair product to keep your hair in place and add shine. Choose a product that suits your hair type and gives you the desired look.

Can I wear the male hair long top short sides to a formal event?

Yes, the male hair long top short sides is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn to a wide range of events, including formal occasions. You can slick your hair back for a more formal look or create a textured, messy look for a casual event.

Hair Care for Male Hair Long Top Short Sides

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the male hair long top short sides. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair as it can dry it out and cause damage
  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type to keep your hair in place and add shine
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and well-maintained

In conclusion, the male hair long top short sides is a versatile and stylish hairstyle that is perfect for modern men. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this hairstyle can be styled in different ways to suit your personal style. Take care of your hair to keep it healthy and well-maintained, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles.

Best Parting For Your Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Choosing the right parting for your hair can make a huge difference in how your face looks. Whether you have a round, square, oval, or heart-shaped face, the way you part your hair can accentuate your best features and balance out any asymmetry. Read on to discover the best parting for your face shape and how to achieve it.

Description

Parting your hair is simply separating it into two sections, either in the middle or to one side. Your hairline and the shape of your face will determine the most flattering parting for you. A good parting can make your face look slimmer, balance out any asymmetry, and draw attention to your eyes or cheekbones.

How to

Follow these steps to find the best parting for your face shape:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Look in the mirror and determine whether your face is round, square, oval, or heart-shaped. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step 2: Find your natural parting

Comb your hair straight back and see where it naturally falls. This will show you where your hair naturally wants to part.

Step 3: Experiment with different partings

Try parting your hair in different ways to see how it affects your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, try a deep side parting to create more length and angles.

Step 4: Consider your hair type

Your hair type can also affect the best parting for your face shape. If you have thin or fine hair, a middle parting can make your hair look fuller. If you have thick or curly hair, a side parting can help tame your hair and showcase your curls.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best parting for your face shape:

  • Use a tail comb to create a precise parting
  • Try a zig-zag parting for a more edgy look
  • Use a volumizing spray to add lift to your roots
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids or updos, to change up your parting

FAQ

What is the best parting for a round face?

A deep side parting can help create more length and angles in a round face.

What is the best parting for a square face?

A middle parting can help soften the angles of a square face.

What is the best parting for an oval face?

An oval face can pull off any parting, so experiment to find what works best for you.

What is the best parting for a heart-shaped face?

A side parting can help balance out a heart-shaped face and draw attention to your cheekbones.

Hair Care

Parting your hair in the same place every day can cause breakage and damage to your hair. To avoid this, switch up your parting every few days and use a heat protectant when styling with heat tools. Additionally, make sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.

In conclusion, choosing the best parting for your face shape can make a huge difference in how you look. Follow these tips and experiment to find the perfect parting for you.

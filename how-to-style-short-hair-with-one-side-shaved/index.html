How To Style Short Hair With One Side Shaved

Written by Luffy Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have decided to chop off your long locks and rock a short haircut with one side shaved, congratulations! You are making a bold statement and embracing your edgy side. However, styling short hair can be challenging, especially when you have a buzz cut on one side. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will show you how to style short hair with one side shaved, step-by-step.

Description

Short hair with one side shaved is a trendy and daring hairstyle that has been embraced by many women in recent years. It involves shaving one side of your head while leaving the other side longer. This hairstyle is perfect for women who want to make a statement and stand out from the crowd. However, styling short hair with one side shaved can be tricky, especially if you are new to this hairstyle. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks to help you style your hair with ease.

How to Style Short Hair with One Side Shaved

Step 1: Wash your hair

The first step in styling short hair with one side shaved is to wash your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly and pat dry with a towel. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel as this can cause breakage and damage.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product to your hair to add texture and hold. You can use a mousse, gel, or wax to achieve the desired look. Apply the product to your hair evenly, focusing on the longer side of your hair.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Blow-dry the longer side of your hair first, and then move on to the shaved side. Use a small amount of product to style the shaved side of your hair.

Step 4: Style your hair

Now that your hair is dry, it's time to style it. You can part your hair on the longer side and tuck the shaved side behind your ear, or you can create a side-swept style by sweeping your hair to one side. You can also add accessories such as headbands, clips, or barrettes to add some flair to your look.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before washing.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

FAQ

Can I style short hair with one side shaved without using any products?

While it is possible to style short hair with one side shaved without using any products, using a styling product can help add texture and hold to your hair, making it easier to style and maintain throughout the day.

How often should I wash my hair if I have short hair with one side shaved?

It is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days if you have short hair with one side shaved. However, this may vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle. If your hair gets oily quickly, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Hair Care

Short hair with one side shaved requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers and flat irons too often, as they can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.

Now that you know how to style short hair with one side shaved, you can rock this edgy and bold hairstyle with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and following a regular hair care routine. With these tips and tricks, you can maintain your short hair and keep it looking fabulous.

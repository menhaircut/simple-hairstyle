How To Trim Your Own Short Natural Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Aug 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Trimming your own short natural hair can be a daunting task, but it's an essential part of maintaining healthy hair. In this guide, we will take you through the process of how to trim your own short natural hair step by step. We'll also provide you with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to ensure you get the best results possible.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Trim Your Own Short Natural Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Trimming Short Natural Hair

How to Trim Your Own Short Natural Hair

Trimming your own short natural hair can seem challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Here are the things you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • A mirror

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to trim your own short natural hair:

  1. Wash and detangle your hair.
  2. Section your hair into small parts using sectioning clips.
  3. Start at the back of your head and take one section of hair.
  4. Comb through the hair and trim the ends using your scissors.
  5. Continue trimming each section of hair until you've trimmed all of your hair.
  6. Use your mirror to check that the hair is even on both sides.
  7. Remove any tangles or knots that you may have missed.
  8. Style as desired.

Tips for Trimming Short Natural Hair

Here are some tips to make trimming your short natural hair easier:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Trim your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can appear longer than it actually is, which can lead to uneven cutting.
  • Take your time and be patient.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to take small sections and trim a little at a time.
  • Use a mirror to check that the hair is even on both sides.

FAQs

How often should I trim my short natural hair?

You should trim your short natural hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair. This will also help to prevent split ends and breakage.

Can I trim my own hair if it's very short?

Yes, you can trim your own hair even if it's very short. Just be sure to use a mirror and take your time.

Do I need to use special scissors to trim my hair?

It's best to use sharp scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair. This will ensure a clean cut and prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care Advice

Trimming your hair is just one part of maintaining healthy hair. Here are some additional hair care tips:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a silk or satin bonnet or using a silk or satin pillowcase.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals.
  • Reduce stress, as stress can lead to hair loss and breakage.

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy, beautiful hair that looks great whether you've just trimmed it or not.

Trimming your own short natural hair may seem intimidating at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and follow the steps we've outlined in this guide. With a little practice, you'll be able to trim your own hair like a pro!

Best Men s Haircut For Thinning Hair On Crown

Written by Robby Nov 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thinning hair is a common problem among men. It can be due to aging, genetics, or other factors. However, having thinning hair on the crown can be frustrating and embarrassing. Fortunately, there are several different haircuts that can help men with thinning hair on the crown look their best. In this article, we will discuss the best men's haircut for thinning hair on crown and provide tips on how to care for thinning hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Men with thinning hair on the crown should look for haircuts that add volume and texture to their hair. A good haircut can make thinning hair appear thicker and fuller. The best men's haircut for thinning hair on crown is a short haircut with a textured top. The sides and back should be shorter than the top to create the illusion of more volume on top. This type of haircut is easy to style and maintain, making it a great choice for men who are busy or don't want to spend a lot of time on their hair.

How to

If you're looking for a new haircut to help with thinning hair on the crown, here's what you should do:

  1. Consult with a professional hairstylist who has experience cutting hair for men with thinning hair.
  2. Show them pictures of haircuts you like and ask for their opinion on what will work best for your hair type and face shape.
  3. Choose a haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair, such as a short haircut with a textured top.
  4. Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and full.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the best men's haircut for thinning hair on crown:

  1. Choose a professional hairstylist who specializes in men's haircuts.
  2. Show them pictures of haircuts you like and ask for their opinion on what will work best for your hair type and face shape.
  3. Ask the hairstylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving the hair on top longer.
  4. Ask the hairstylist to add texture to the top of your hair to create the illusion of more volume.
  5. Use styling products, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair and create the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips for caring for thinning hair on the crown:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and clean.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume to your hair and make it appear fuller.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, such as gel or hairspray, as they can weigh down your hair and make it appear thinner.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best men's haircut for thinning hair on crown:

1. Will a short haircut make my thinning hair look worse?

No, a short haircut can actually make thinning hair appear thicker and fuller. The key is to choose a haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair.

2. How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

3. Can I style my hair with a hairdryer?

Avoid using a hair dryer on high heat, as it can damage your hair. Instead, use a low heat setting or air dry your hair.

Hair Care

Caring for thinning hair on the crown requires a gentle and consistent hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for thinning hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume to your hair and make it appear fuller.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, such as gel or hairspray, as they can weigh down your hair and make it appear thinner.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  • If you're experiencing significant hair loss, consider talking to a doctor or dermatologist about treatment options.

In conclusion, the best men's haircut for thinning hair on crown is a short haircut with a textured top. This type of haircut adds volume and texture to your hair, making it appear thicker and fuller. To care for thinning hair, use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoid hot styling tools, and use styling products that add volume and texture. By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and full.

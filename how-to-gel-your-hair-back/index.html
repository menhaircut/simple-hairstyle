How To Gel Your Hair Back A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Gel has been a popular hair styling product for decades. It is a quick and easy way to style your hair and achieve a sleek, polished look. If you are looking to gel your hair back, you've come to the right place. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect gelled back hairstyle.

Description

Gel is a styling product that is used to hold hair in place. It is typically applied to wet or damp hair and then combed through to distribute the product evenly. Gel can be used to create a variety of hairstyles, including the gelled back look.

How to Gel Your Hair Back

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

The first step in achieving a gelled back hairstyle is to start with clean, damp hair. This will help the gel to spread evenly throughout your hair and provide a strong hold.

Step 2: Apply the gel

Take a small amount of gel and rub it between your palms. Then, apply the gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. Be sure to distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair to ensure a strong hold.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a fine-tooth comb, comb your hair back away from your face. This will create the gelled back look and ensure that your hair stays in place throughout the day.

Step 4: Allow the gel to dry

Once you have combed your hair back, allow the gel to dry completely. This will ensure that your hair stays in place and maintains its shape throughout the day.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray (optional)

If you want to ensure that your gelled back hairstyle stays in place all day, you can finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will provide an extra layer of hold and ensure that your hair stays in place even in windy conditions.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect gelled back hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality gel for the best results
  • Start with a small amount of gel and add more as needed
  • Use a fine-tooth comb for the most precise styling
  • Allow the gel to dry completely before touching your hair
  • Experiment with different amounts of gel to achieve your desired level of hold

FAQ

Q: How much gel should I use?

A: It depends on the length and thickness of your hair. Start with a small amount of gel and add more as needed until you achieve your desired level of hold.

Q: Will gel make my hair look greasy?

A: If you use too much gel or apply it to dry hair, it can make your hair look greasy. Be sure to start with a small amount of gel and apply it to damp hair for the best results.

Q: How long will my gelled back hairstyle last?

A: If you allow the gel to dry completely and finish with hairspray (optional), your gelled back hairstyle should last all day.

Hair Care

Using gel on a regular basis can dry out your hair and scalp. To keep your hair healthy, be sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and limit your use of gel to a few times a week.

Gel can be a great styling product for achieving the gelled back look. By following these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a sleek, polished hairstyle that will last all day.

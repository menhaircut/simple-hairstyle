How To Style Short Pixie Cut Video A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and thinking of trying something new? A short pixie cut might be the perfect option for you! Not only is it trendy and fashionable, but it's also low maintenance and easy to style. In this article, we'll show you how to style short pixie cut video and provide you with some tips and tricks to make your short haircut look fabulous.

Description

A short pixie cut is a super short hairstyle that typically features cropped layers and a tapered neckline. It's perfect for those who want to showcase their facial features and make a bold statement. The best part about a short pixie cut is that it can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and sophisticated to edgy and textured.

How to Style Short Pixie Cut Video

If you're new to the world of short haircuts, styling your hair might seem a bit daunting. But fear not! With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a chic and stylish look in no time. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style short pixie cut video:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step in styling your short pixie cut is to wash and condition your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to add texture and hold. Depending on your hair type and the style you want to achieve, you can use a mousse, gel, or wax. Rub the product between your palms and apply it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using your fingers to shape it. If you want a sleek and smooth look, use a round brush to smooth out your hair as you blow dry it. If you want a more textured and tousled look, use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair as you blow dry it.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, it's time to style it. Depending on the look you want to achieve, you can use a flat iron to straighten your hair or a curling iron to add some waves or curls. You can also use your fingers to tousle and shape your hair to create a more natural and effortless look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your short pixie cut:

  • Use a high-quality styling product to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair while using your fingers to shape it for a more natural look.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to add some color to your hair to make your short pixie cut stand out.

FAQ

What face shape is best for a short pixie cut?

A short pixie cut works well for most face shapes, but it's especially flattering for those with round or heart-shaped faces.

How often should I get my short pixie cut trimmed?

You should get your short pixie cut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care

Short haircuts like the pixie cut require minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for taking care of your short pixie cut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your short pixie cut's shape and style.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

Now that you know how to style short pixie cut video, it's time to give it a try! With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a chic and stylish look that's perfect for any occasion. So go ahead and make the chop - you won't regret it!

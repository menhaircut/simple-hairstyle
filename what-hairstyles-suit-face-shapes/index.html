What Hairstyles Suit Face Shapes The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. Not all hairstyles suit every face shape, so it's important to find the right one that accentuates your features. In this article, we'll be discussing what hairstyles suit face shapes and how to choose the perfect one for you.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped
  • Diamond-shaped
  • Long

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Take a good look at the shape of your face and compare it to the descriptions above. Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that suit your features.

Hairstyles for Oval Faces

Oval faces are considered the most versatile of all the face shapes. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any hairstyle. Some popular hairstyles for oval faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob cuts
  • Updos

One thing to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for an oval face is to avoid hairstyles that hide your face shape. You want to show off your features and accentuate your natural beauty.

Hairstyles for Round Faces

If you have a round face, you want to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a more oval shape. Some popular hairstyles for round faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Choppy cuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Pixie cuts

One thing to avoid when choosing a hairstyle for a round face is blunt cuts that end at the chin. This can make your face appear even rounder.

Hairstyles for Square Faces

If you have a square face, you want to soften your features and create more roundness. Some popular hairstyles for square faces include:

  • Soft layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Wavy hair
  • Bob cuts

One thing to avoid when choosing a hairstyle for a square face is blunt cuts that end at the jawline. This can make your face appear even more square.

Hairstyles for Heart-Shaped Faces

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to draw attention away from your forehead and create balance between your chin and cheeks. Some popular hairstyles for heart-shaped faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob cuts
  • Pixie cuts

One thing to avoid when choosing a hairstyle for a heart-shaped face is adding volume to the top of your head. This can make your forehead appear even larger.

Hairstyles for Diamond-Shaped Faces

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you want to create balance between your forehead and chin. Some popular hairstyles for diamond-shaped faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob cuts
  • Updos

One thing to avoid when choosing a hairstyle for a diamond-shaped face is adding too much volume to the sides of your head. This can make your cheekbones appear even wider.

Hairstyles for Long Faces

If you have a long face, you want to create the illusion of a wider face. Some popular hairstyles for long faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob cuts
  • Pixie cuts

One thing to avoid when choosing a hairstyle for a long face is adding too much length. This can make your face appear even longer.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Now that you know what hairstyles suit face shapes, here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair type and texture
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair
  • Get inspiration from celebrities or Pinterest
  • Consult with a hairstylist for professional advice

FAQs

Can I still wear a hairstyle that doesn't suit my face shape?

Of course! At the end of the day, you should wear a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable. These guidelines are simply suggestions to help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

Most people have a combination of face shapes, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your most prominent features. For example, if you have a heart-shaped face with a prominent chin, you may want to choose a hairstyle that balances out your chin.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine

By following these hair care tips and choosing a hairstyle that suits your face shape, you'll have healthy, beautiful hair that makes you feel confident and fabulous!

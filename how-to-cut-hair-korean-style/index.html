How To Cut Hair Korean Style A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, why not try a Korean-inspired look? Korean hairstyles are known for their trendy and modern styles that suit all face shapes and hair types. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to cut hair Korean style so you can achieve that coveted look.

Description:

Korean hairstyles are popular all around the world for their stylish and chic look. They range from short to long haircuts, with bangs or without, and suit all hair types. Korean haircuts are known for their soft, flowy layers and natural-looking hair color. If you're looking to achieve a trendy and modern look, then Korean haircuts are the way to go.

How to Cut Hair Korean Style:

1. Gather your tools:

To cut hair Korean style, you'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a spray bottle filled with water. Make sure your scissors are of good quality and are sharp enough to cut through hair easily.

2. Part your hair:

Start by parting your hair in the middle or to the side, depending on the look you're going for. Use a comb to create a clean and straight parting.

3. Section your hair:

Clip the top section of your hair away from the bottom section so you can work on one section at a time. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly and avoid any mistakes.

4. Cut the bottom section:

Starting with the bottom section, comb your hair straight and cut it to your desired length. You can choose to cut your hair straight across or add some layers for a softer look.

5. Cut the top section:

Next, unclip the top section and comb it straight. Cut your hair to your desired length, making sure to blend it with the bottom section. You can add layers to the top section for a more textured look.

6. Add bangs:

If you want to add bangs to your Korean haircut, part your hair in the middle and comb it straight. Cut your bangs to your desired length and style them to the side.

7. Check your work:

After you've finished cutting your hair, check your work in the mirror to make sure it's even and looks the way you want it to. Make any necessary adjustments or touch-ups.

8. Style your hair:

Once you're happy with your haircut, style your hair using a hairbrush, hair dryer, and styling products. Korean hairstyles are known for their natural and effortless look, so don't overdo it with the styling products.

Tips:

-Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting too much hair at once.

-Take your time and work slowly to avoid making mistakes.

-If you're not sure about cutting your own hair, consider getting it done by a professional stylist.

FAQ:

1. Can I cut my own hair Korean style?

Yes, you can cut your own hair Korean style using the step-by-step guide above. Just make sure to follow the instructions carefully and take your time.

2. What are some popular Korean hairstyles?

Some popular Korean hairstyles include the bob, layered haircut, and the wavy perm. You can also add bangs to any of these haircuts for a more Korean-inspired look.

Hair Care:

To keep your Korean haircut looking its best, make sure to take care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.

By following this step-by-step guide, you can achieve a trendy and modern Korean-inspired haircut. Remember to take your time, follow the instructions carefully, and have fun with your new look!

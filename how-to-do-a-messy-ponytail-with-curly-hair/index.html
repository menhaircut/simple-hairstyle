How To Do A Messy Ponytail With Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Oct 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful, it can be difficult to style. One of the easiest ways to style curly hair is to put it up in a messy ponytail. Not only is it effortless, but it's also a great way to keep your hair out of your face. Here's how to do a messy ponytail with curly hair.

Description

A messy ponytail is a casual hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. It's effortless, chic, and stylish. It's a great way to keep your hair out of your face in the summer months, and it's also a great way to add some volume to your hair. A messy ponytail with curly hair is perfect for those who want to embrace their natural texture.

How to

Follow these steps to achieve a messy ponytail with curly hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Begin by washing and conditioning your hair. Once your hair is clean, towel dry it and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product to your hair to help define your curls and add some hold. You can use a mousse, gel, or cream. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.

Step 3: Gather your hair into a ponytail

Gather your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head. Use your fingers to lightly comb through your hair and smooth out any bumps or tangles.

Step 4: Secure your ponytail

Wrap a hair tie around your ponytail to secure it in place. Don't worry about making it too tight or neat – a messy ponytail should look effortless.

Step 5: Mess it up

Use your fingers to gently pull on the hair at the crown of your head to create some volume. You can also pull on some strands of hair around your face to create a relaxed, messy look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy ponytail with curly hair:

  • Use a silk or satin scrunchie instead of a regular hair tie to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to embrace the frizz – it adds to the messy look.
  • Add some texture to your hair by using a salt spray or dry shampoo.
  • If you have shorter hair, you can still achieve a messy ponytail by pulling your hair back into a half-up, half-down style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing a messy ponytail with curly hair:

Can I do a messy ponytail with wet hair?

No, it's best to start with dry hair. Wet hair is more fragile and can be easily damaged when pulled into a ponytail.

How do I prevent my curls from getting tangled?

Apply a leave-in conditioner or detangler to your hair before styling it. This will help to prevent tangles and make it easier to comb through your curls.

Can I wear a messy ponytail to a formal event?

Yes, a messy ponytail can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Simply add some accessories, such as a sparkly hair clip or headband, to make it more formal.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires a lot of care and attention to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can break your curls.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a messy ponytail with curly hair in no time. Remember, the key to a perfect messy ponytail is to keep it effortless and relaxed. Embrace your natural texture and have fun with your hair!

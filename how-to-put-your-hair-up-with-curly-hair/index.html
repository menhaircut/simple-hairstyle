How To Put Your Hair Up With Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you have curly hair and struggle to put it up in a way that looks stylish and stays put? Don't worry, you're not alone! Putting curly hair up can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a beautiful and secure style. In this article, we'll guide you through how to put your hair up with curly hair, including some tips and tricks for maintaining healthy curls along the way.

How to Put Your Hair Up with Curly Hair

When putting curly hair up, it's important to choose the right style and products to ensure a secure hold and prevent damage to your curls. Here are some tips for putting your curly hair up:

  • Choose a style that works with your curls. Some styles, like tight ponytails or braids, can put too much tension on your curls and cause them to lose their shape or even break off. Opt for looser styles that allow your curls to move and breathe.
  • Use products that provide hold without stiffness. Look for gels, creams, or mousses that are formulated for curly hair and provide a flexible hold. Avoid products that contain alcohol or other drying ingredients, as these can make your curls brittle and prone to breakage.
  • Don't put your hair up when it's wet. Wet hair is more fragile and prone to breakage, so wait until your hair is dry or almost dry before putting it up.
  • Use gentle hair ties or clips. Avoid using rubber bands or other harsh materials that can snag or break your curls. Instead, opt for soft fabric hair ties or clips that won't damage your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to put your curly hair up? Follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If you need to refresh your curls, use a spray bottle of water or a leave-in conditioner to spritz your hair and revive your curls.
  2. Choose your style. Whether you're going for a messy bun, a half-up style, or a sleek ponytail, make sure the style you choose is gentle on your curls.
  3. Apply a styling product. Use a curl-enhancing product like a curl cream or gel to define your curls and provide hold. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the ends and any frizz-prone areas.
  4. Gather your hair into a ponytail or bun. Use your fingers to gently gather your hair into the desired style, keeping in mind that loose styles are better for curly hair. Secure your hair with a soft hair tie or clip.
  5. Smooth any flyaways. Use a small amount of styling product or a toothbrush to smooth any flyaways or frizz near your hairline or around your ears.
  6. Spray with hairspray (optional). If you want extra hold, spray your hair with a light mist of hairspray. Be sure to choose a hairspray that won't make your hair stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Putting Curly Hair Up

Here are some additional tips for putting curly hair up:

  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for your curls. Some people find that braids or twists are gentler on their curls than ponytails or buns.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.
  • If you have shorter layers, use bobby pins to secure them in place and prevent them from popping out of your style.
  • If you're putting your hair up for a workout or other physical activity, consider using a sweatband or headband to help absorb sweat and prevent frizz.

FAQs

Can I put my curly hair up when it's wet?

No, it's best to wait until your hair is dry or almost dry before putting it up. Wet hair is more fragile and prone to breakage, so putting it up can cause damage to your curls.

What kind of hair ties should I use for curly hair?

Soft fabric hair ties are best for curly hair, as they won't snag or break your curls. Avoid using rubber bands or other harsh materials.

How can I prevent my curls from losing their shape when I put my hair up?

Choose gentle styles that don't put too much tension on your curls, and use products that provide hold without stiffness. You can also try using a diffuser to dry your hair after putting it up to help maintain your curl pattern.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Putting your curly hair up is just one part of maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for curly hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your curls.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your curls from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf when you're outside.

By following these tips and techniques, you can put your curly hair up in a way that's stylish and healthy for your curls. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice!

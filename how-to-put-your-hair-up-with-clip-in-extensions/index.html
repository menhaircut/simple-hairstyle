How To Put Your Hair Up With Clip In Extensions A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Clip in hair extensions are a great way to add length and volume to your hair. They are easy to use and can be styled in many different ways. However, putting your hair up with clip in extensions can be a bit tricky. In this article, we will show you how to put your hair up with clip in extensions in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Put Your Hair Up with Clip in Extensions
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Put Your Hair Up with Clip in Extensions

Putting your hair up with clip in extensions is not difficult, but it does require a bit of practice. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Brush your hair to remove any tangles and make sure it is smooth.

Step 2: Section your hair into two parts: the top and the bottom. Use a clip to hold the top section in place.

Step 3: Take a small section of hair from the bottom and tease it with a comb. This will create a base for the clip in extension to rest on.

Step 4: Open the clips on the extension and place it on the teased section of hair. Snap the clips shut to secure the extension in place.

Step 5: Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you have added all the clip in extensions you want.

Step 6: Release the top section of hair and brush it back over the extensions.

Step 7: Gather your hair into a ponytail or bun, making sure to include the clip in extensions. Use an elastic or hair tie to secure the style in place.

Step 8: Use bobby pins to further secure the style and hide any visible clips.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you put your hair up with clip in extensions:

  • Tease the roots of your hair before applying the clip in extensions. This will create a better base for the clips to hold onto.
  • Apply hair spray to the teased sections of hair to make them easier to work with.
  • Use bobby pins to hide any visible clips and create a more natural look.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I put my hair up with clip in extensions?

A: Yes, you can! Follow the steps outlined above to put your hair up with clip in extensions.

Q: How many clip in extensions do I need?

A: It depends on how much volume and length you want to add to your hair. Generally, 6-8 wefts are enough for a full head of extensions.

Q: How long do clip in extensions last?

A: Clip in extensions can last for several months with proper care and maintenance.

Hair Care Tips

Clip in extensions require proper care and maintenance to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Brush your hair and extensions regularly to prevent tangles.
  • Wash your hair and extensions separately to avoid damage.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your extensions, as this can damage them.
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when you're not using them.

With these tips and tricks, you should be able to easily put your hair up with clip in extensions. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your new look!

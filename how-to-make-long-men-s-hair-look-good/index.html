How To Make Long Men s Hair Look Good

Written by Frank Jul 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a man with long hair, you may be wondering how to style it in a way that doesn't make you look like a lost member of a 70s rock band. Fear not, there are plenty of ways to make long men's hair look good without resorting to a mullet or a ponytail. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for styling long hair on men.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Long Men's Hair
  2. Step by Step Instructions
  3. Tips for Styling Long Hair on Men
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care for Long Men's Hair

How to Style Long Men's Hair

The first step to making long men's hair look good is to find a style that works for you. There are plenty of options, from messy and textured to sleek and polished. Some popular styles for long men's hair include:

  • Man bun
  • Top knot
  • Half-up half-down
  • Low ponytail
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Layered and textured
  • Slicked back

The key to making any of these styles work is to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained. This means regular trims to prevent split ends, using a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible.

Step by Step Instructions

Here's how to create two popular long men's hair styles:

Man Bun

  1. Brush your hair back and gather it into a ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around itself to create a bun shape.
  3. Secure the bun in place with bobby pins or a hair tie.
  4. Use your fingers to loosen up the bun and create a relaxed, messy look.

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as a pomade or wax.
  3. Using a comb, create a deep side part on one side of your head.
  4. Comb your hair over to the opposite side, sweeping your bangs across your forehead.
  5. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a relaxed, textured look.

Tips for Styling Long Hair on Men

Here are some additional tips for making long men's hair look good:

  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and experiment with different looks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions men with long hair may have:

Can I wear my hair down if it's long?

Absolutely! Wearing your hair down can look great, especially if it's layered or textured. Just make sure to keep it brushed and clean.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

What should I do if my hair is thinning or receding?

If you're experiencing hair loss or thinning, talk to your doctor or a hair specialist. There are treatments available that can help slow down or even reverse hair loss.

Hair Care for Long Men's Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your long men's hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for long hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons as much as possible, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Try not to wear tight hats or hairstyles that pull on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

By following these tips and experimenting with different styles, you can make your long men's hair look good and feel confident in your appearance.

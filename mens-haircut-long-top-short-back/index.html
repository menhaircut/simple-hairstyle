Mens Haircut Long Top Short Back The Ultimate Guide To Relaxed Style

Written by Frank Jun 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance yet stylish haircut, the mens haircut long top short back is the perfect choice for you. This style has been trending in recent years and has become one of the most popular hairstyles for men. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about this style, including how to get it, tips for styling, and hair care advice.

Description

The mens haircut long top short back is a versatile hairstyle that features longer hair on top and shorter hair at the back and sides. This style is perfect for men who want to keep their hair relatively short but still want some length to work with. The long top can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or even in a pompadour style. The short back and sides give the style a clean and polished look.

How to Get the Mens Haircut Long Top Short Back

The first step to getting the mens haircut long top short back is to find a good barber or stylist who is experienced in this style. Once you've found a professional you trust, communicate your desired length and style to them. It's important to be clear about what you want so that the stylist can give you the best possible cut.

The stylist will typically start by cutting the back and sides of your hair short, using clippers. They will then move on to the top of your hair, using scissors to create the desired length and shape. The length of the top will depend on your personal preference, but it's typically between 2-4 inches long.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling the Mens Haircut Long Top Short Back

Styling the mens haircut long top short back is relatively easy and requires minimal effort. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Once you've washed your hair, dry it using a towel or a hairdryer. Make sure your hair is completely dry before moving on to the next step.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as pomade or wax. Rub the product between your palms and then apply it to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the tips. Make sure you distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Using your fingers, style your hair into the desired shape. You can slick it back, create a messy look, or even style it into a pompadour. Experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

To ensure your style lasts all day, finish with a light spritz of hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling flat.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Haircut Long Top Short Back

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mens haircut long top short back:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to keep your hair in good condition and maintain the length and shape.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find the ones that work best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

What face shapes suit the mens haircut long top short back?

The mens haircut long top short back is a versatile style that can suit a variety of face shapes. It works particularly well for men with oval or square-shaped faces.

Can I style the long top in different ways?

Absolutely! The long top of the mens haircut long top short back can be styled in a variety of ways, depending on your personal preference. You can slick it back, create a messy look, or even style it into a pompadour.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, as rough handling can cause breakage and split ends.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the mens haircut long top short back, it's time to head to the barber and try it out for yourself. With the right cut and styling, you'll be able to achieve a relaxed yet stylish look that's perfect for any occasion.

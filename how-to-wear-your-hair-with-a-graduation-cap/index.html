How To Wear Your Hair With A Graduation Cap

Written by Daniel Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Graduation season is just around the corner, and it’s time to start thinking about what to wear under your graduation cap. If you’re wondering how to wear your hair with a graduation cap, don’t worry, we’ve got you covered. In this blog post, we’ll share some tips and tricks to help you look your best on graduation day.

Description

Wearing a graduation cap can be tricky, especially when it comes to your hair. You want to look your best on your big day, but you also want to make sure your hair doesn’t get in the way of your graduation cap. In this article, we’ll share some tips on how to wear your hair with a graduation cap, so you can look and feel confident on your special day.

How to

Here are some steps to follow when wearing your hair with a graduation cap:

Step 1: Choose your hairstyle

Before you even think about putting on your graduation cap, you need to decide on a hairstyle that works with your cap. Some popular hairstyles for graduation include updos, braids, and loose waves. Choose a style that you feel comfortable with and that won’t get in the way of your cap.

Step 2: Prep your hair

Once you’ve chosen your hairstyle, it’s time to prep your hair. Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you style it. You may also want to use some hairspray or other styling products to help your hair stay in place.

Step 3: Put on your graduation cap

Now it’s time to put on your graduation cap. Place the cap on your head so that the mortarboard is level and the tassel is on the right-hand side. If you have long hair, you may need to adjust the cap slightly to ensure your hair is tucked underneath.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once your cap is in place, you can style your hair as desired. Make sure to tuck any loose strands of hair underneath the cap, so they don’t get in the way of your photos.

Step 5: Secure your cap

Finally, make sure your graduation cap is secured in place. Use bobby pins or hair clips to secure the cap to your hair, so it doesn’t fall off during the ceremony.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you wear your hair with a graduation cap:

  • Choose a hairstyle that you feel comfortable with and that won’t get in the way of your cap.
  • Use hairspray or other styling products to help your hair stay in place.
  • Tuck any loose strands of hair underneath the cap, so they don’t get in the way of your photos.
  • Use bobby pins or hair clips to secure the cap to your hair, so it doesn’t fall off during the ceremony.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing your hair with a graduation cap:

Can I wear my hair down with a graduation cap?

Yes, you can wear your hair down with a graduation cap. However, make sure to tuck any loose strands of hair underneath the cap, so they don’t get in the way of your photos.

What if I have long hair?

If you have long hair, you may need to adjust the cap slightly to ensure your hair is tucked underneath. You can also use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair in place.

What if my cap falls off?

If your cap falls off during the ceremony, don’t panic. Simply pick it up and put it back on your head. If it keeps falling off, use more bobby pins or hair clips to secure it to your hair.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair before and after graduation day. Make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a heat protectant if you plan on styling it with hot tools. You may also want to consider getting a trim or a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to wear your hair with a graduation cap, you can focus on enjoying your special day. Congratulations on your achievement, and best of luck in your future endeavors!

Black Hairstyles For Round Faces 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Oct 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what hairstyles will suit you best. Don't worry; we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing the best black hairstyles for round faces in 2020. We have compiled a list of styles that will flatter your face shape and make you look stunning.

Description

Round faces have full cheeks, and the width and length of the face are nearly equal. If you have a round face, you should avoid hairstyles that add width to your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that create the illusion of length and slim down your face.

How to

The first step in choosing a hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Once you know that you have a round face, you can use this guide to choose the best hairstyle for you.

Tips

When choosing a hairstyle, keep in mind that what suits one person may not suit another. Take into consideration your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style.

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face
  • Choose hairstyles that create the illusion of length
  • Consider face-framing layers
  • Avoid blunt cuts
  • Avoid center parts
  • Consider side-swept bangs

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for round faces?

The best hairstyles for round faces are those that create the illusion of length and slim down your face. Examples include long layers, side-swept bangs, and angled bobs.

Can I wear a pixie cut if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear a pixie cut if you have a round face. However, it's important to choose a pixie cut with longer layers on top to create the illusion of length.

What haircuts should I avoid if I have a round face?

If you have a round face, you should avoid haircuts that add width to your face. Examples include blunt cuts, heavy bangs, and center parts.

Hair Care

Once you have chosen a hairstyle, it's important to maintain it with proper hair care. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture.

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Deep condition your hair once a week

Now that you know the best black hairstyles for round faces in 2020, you can choose a style that suits you best. Remember to take into consideration your personal style, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle.

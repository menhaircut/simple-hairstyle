How To Do Cute Hairstyles For Medium Hair In 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Medium hair length is perfect for trying out different hairstyles. Whether you want to go for a casual look or a formal one, there are numerous options to choose from. In this article, we will be discussing how to do cute hairstyles for medium hair in 2023. From braids to buns, we have got you covered.

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, here are a few things to consider:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before styling.
  • Invest in good quality hair tools like a curling iron, straightener, and hairbrush.
  • Use hair products like mousse or hairspray to keep your hairstyle intact.

Braids

Braids are a great way to add some texture to your hair. Here are a few braided hairstyles for medium hair:

  • Fishtail braid
  • Dutch braid
  • French braid

Buns

Buns are perfect for a formal occasion or if you want to keep your hair out of your face. Here are a few bun hairstyles for medium hair:

  • Messy bun
  • Low bun
  • Braided bun

Curls

Curls can add volume and texture to your hair. Here are a few curly hairstyles for medium hair:

  • Beachy waves
  • Tight curls
  • Loose curls

Step by Step

Fishtail Braid

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into two sections.
  3. Take a small piece of hair from the outside of the left section and cross it over to the right section.
  4. Repeat step 3 with the right section.
  5. Keep alternating between the left and right sections until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Tie the braid with a hair tie.

Messy Bun

  1. Tease the crown of your hair to add volume.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  3. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few pieces of hair from the bun to create a messy look.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hair tools to avoid hair damage.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories like headbands or hair clips.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match different hairstyles.

FAQ

Q: Can I do these hairstyles on curly hair?

A: Yes, you can definitely do these hairstyles on curly hair. However, you may need to straighten your hair first if you want a more defined look.

Q: How long does it take to do these hairstyles?

A: The time it takes to do these hairstyles depends on your skill level and the complexity of the hairstyle. However, most of these hairstyles can be done in under 15 minutes.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for healthy and beautiful hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like a curling iron or straightener every day.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth.

Now that we have covered different hairstyles for medium hair, it's time to try them out. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you.

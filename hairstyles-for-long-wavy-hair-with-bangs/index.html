Hairstyles for Long Wavy Hair with Bangs | Hair Care Tips

Are you tired of wearing the same hairstyle every day? If you have long wavy hair with bangs, there are many hairstyles you can try to change up your look. In this article, we will share some of our favorite hairstyles for long wavy hair with bangs, along with tips for hair care and styling.

How to Style Long Wavy Hair with Bangs

Before we get into specific hairstyles, it's important to know how to style long wavy hair with bangs. Here are some general tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet, then let it air dry or blow dry with a diffuser to enhance your waves.
  • Apply a styling cream or mousse to add definition and hold to your waves.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to create more defined waves or curls, or straighten your hair with a flat iron for a sleek look.
  • Part your bangs to the side or straight down the middle, or sweep them to the side with a bobby pin or clip.

Step-by-Step Guide for Hairstyles

1. Beachy Waves

To achieve beachy waves, apply a salt spray to damp hair and scrunch it with your hands. Let it air dry or blow dry with a diffuser. Use a curling iron or wand to add more waves if needed.

2. Half-Up Bun

Take the top half of your hair and tie it into a bun at the crown of your head. Leave some strands of hair out to frame your face. This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out.

3. Side Braid

Sweep your bangs to the side and braid the rest of your hair over your shoulder. You can also add some loose waves to your hair for a more romantic look.

4. High Ponytail

Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. Use a curling iron or wand to add some waves to your ponytail.

Tips for Maintaining Long Wavy Hair with Bangs

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too often, as it can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

FAQs about Hairstyles for Long Wavy Hair with Bangs

Q: Can I wear my bangs straight with wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can wear your bangs straight or side-swept with wavy hair. It's all about personal preference and what looks best on you.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a tight bun or ponytail?

A: It's best to avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, as it can cause breakage and damage. If you want to wear your hair up, opt for a loose bun or ponytail.

Hair Care Tips for Long Wavy Hair with Bangs

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these hairstyles and tips, you can keep your long wavy hair with bangs looking beautiful and healthy. Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you!

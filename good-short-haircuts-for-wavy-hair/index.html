Good Short Haircuts For Wavy Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have wavy hair, you know that finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. You want something that will show off your natural texture, but also be easy to manage and style. That's why we've put together this guide to the best short haircuts for wavy hair. Whether you're looking for a pixie cut, a bob, or something in between, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts are a great option for wavy hair because they can help to reduce frizz and make your hair easier to manage. Plus, they can be super cute and stylish. Some of the best short haircuts for wavy hair include the pixie cut, the bob, and the lob (long bob).

How To

Before you head to the salon, it's important to have an idea of what you want your new haircut to look like. You can browse through magazines or online for inspiration, or talk to your stylist about what would work best for your face shape and hair texture.

When it comes to cutting wavy hair, it's important to work with the natural texture of your hair. Your stylist may use thinning shears or texturizing techniques to help reduce bulk and create more movement in your hair. They may also recommend a specific styling product or technique to help enhance your natural waves.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a great short haircut for wavy hair:

  1. Choose a haircut that works well with your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Bring pictures or examples to your stylist to help them understand what you're looking for.
  3. Ask your stylist to cut your hair while it's dry, so they can see how your natural waves fall.
  4. Be open to texturizing techniques that can help reduce bulk and add movement to your hair.
  5. Ask your stylist for styling tips and product recommendations to help you maintain your new haircut.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short haircut for wavy hair:

  • Choose a haircut that works with your natural texture.
  • Ask your stylist for tips on how to style your hair at home.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products that work well with wavy hair.
  • Consider getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for wavy hair:

What is the best short haircut for wavy hair?

The best short haircut for wavy hair depends on your personal style and face shape. Some popular options include the pixie cut, the bob, and the lob.

How do I style my short wavy hair?

Styling short wavy hair can be easy with the right products and techniques. Try scrunching your hair with a curl-enhancing mousse or cream, or blow-drying with a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.

How often should I get a trim?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your short wavy hair, it's important to use products that work well with your natural texture. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically formulated for wavy hair, and consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help reduce frizz and add moisture.

Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, short wavy hair can be easy to manage and style. Whether you're going for a chic pixie cut or a trendy bob, embrace your natural texture and have fun experimenting with different styles and products.

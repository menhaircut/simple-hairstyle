Description

Permed hair can be a lot of fun to style, but it can also be a challenge, especially in the morning. If you have recently gotten a perm and are struggling to find ways to style it, this article is for you. We will provide you with tips, tricks, and a step-by-step guide on how to style permed hair in the morning, as well as answer some frequently asked questions and give you some hair care tips to maintain your perm.

How to Style Permed Hair in the Morning

Styling permed hair in the morning can be a breeze if you follow a few simple steps. First, it is important to use the right products. Look for products that are specifically designed for permed hair, such as mousse, curl enhancing spray, or hair serum. These products will help define your curls and keep them in place throughout the day.

Next, consider using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to dry your hair without causing frizz, which can be a problem for permed hair. Use the diffuser on low heat and low speed, and scrunch your hair as you dry it to help define your curls.

Finally, be sure to use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to style your hair. Avoid using a brush, as this can cause your curls to frizz and lose their shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style permed hair in the morning:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for permed hair. This will help to keep your curls looking their best.
  2. Apply a curl enhancing spray or mousse to your damp hair, focusing on the ends.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat and low speed, scrunching your hair as you go to help define your curls.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers or a wide-toothed comb to style your hair. Avoid using a brush.
  5. Finish with a hair serum to add shine and hold your curls in place throughout the day.

Tips for Styling Permed Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your permed hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can cause your curls to become limp and lose their shape. Instead, try washing every other day or using dry shampoo in between washes.
  • Consider getting a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as braids, buns, or half-up half-down styles, to keep your look fresh and versatile.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling permed hair:

How long should I wait before styling my newly permed hair?

You should wait at least 24 hours after getting a perm before styling your hair. This will give your hair time to set and prevent any damage or breakage.

Can I use a hair straightener on permed hair?

Avoid using a hair straightener on permed hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. If you want to straighten your hair, consider using a blow dryer with a round brush to smooth out your curls.

How do I keep my curls looking their best throughout the day?

Use a hair serum or hairspray to hold your curls in place and prevent frizz. You can also touch up your curls throughout the day with a curling iron or curling wand.

Hair Care for Permed Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your permed hair:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can cause your curls to become limp and lose their shape.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow drying, curling, or straightening your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

With these tips, tricks, and care, you can style your permed hair with confidence every morning. Remember to use the right products, avoid using a brush, and experiment with different styles to keep your look fresh and versatile.

How To Cut Your Own Hair Long Layers Youtube

Written by William Jul 21, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to save money on haircuts or just want to try something new, cutting your own hair into long layers is a great option. Thanks to YouTube, there are plenty of tutorials available to help you achieve the perfect haircut at home. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to cut your own hair long layers YouTube style.

Description

Long layers are a versatile and low-maintenance haircut that can work for a variety of hair types and face shapes. This style involves cutting the hair into different lengths, with the longest layers framing the face and the shortest layers at the back. By following a few simple steps and using the right tools, you can achieve a salon-quality haircut at home.

How To

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and clips to section your hair. You'll also want to make sure your hair is clean and dry, and that you're working in a well-lit area.

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps to cut your own hair long layers YouTube style:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back. Use clips to hold each section in place.

Step 2: Cut the First Layer

Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle, starting from the chin and working your way towards the back of your head. Repeat this process on the other side.

Step 3: Cut the Second Layer

Take another small section of hair from the front and comb it straight down, holding it between your fingers. This time, cut the hair at a slightly steeper angle than the first layer. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Cut the Back Layers

Unclip the back sections of your hair and comb them straight down. Hold a section between your fingers and cut it at an angle, starting from the back of your head and working your way towards the front. Repeat this process on all the sections of your hair, making sure to cut the hair at a slightly steeper angle with each layer.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut all the layers, use your scissors to blend the hair together, removing any harsh lines or uneven edges. Comb your hair to check for any unevenness, and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results when cutting your own hair long layers YouTube style:

  • Start with a small amount of hair and work your way up, rather than trying to cut a large section all at once.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure clean cuts and avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting your own hair can be tricky, but with practice, you'll get better at it.
  • Consider watching multiple YouTube tutorials to get a variety of techniques and tips.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair long layers YouTube style if I have curly or thick hair?

Yes, you can still cut your own hair into long layers if you have curly or thick hair. However, you may need to adjust the angle of the layers to suit your hair type, or seek the advice of a professional stylist.

How often should I cut my hair if I have long layers?

It depends on your hair growth rate and how quickly your hair loses its shape. Generally, you should aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. Wait for your hair to grow out a bit, or consider seeking the advice of a professional stylist to correct the mistake.

Hair Care

To keep your long layers looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Now that you know how to cut your own hair long layers YouTube style, you can give it a try and see how it works for you. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun with it!

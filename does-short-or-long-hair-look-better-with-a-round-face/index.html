Does Short Or Long Hair Look Better With A Round Face

Written by William Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to decide whether to go for short or long hair with your round face? If you're unsure which style would suit you best, you're not alone. Many people with round faces find it difficult to choose the right hairstyle. There are pros and cons to both short and long hair, and it all depends on what you're looking for.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by soft curves and a lack of defined angles. Hairstyles that work well with this face shape are those that add angles and make the face appear longer. Short hair can do this by adding volume and height to the crown of the head. Long hair can also work well by adding length and creating the illusion of a longer face.

How To

The first step in deciding whether short or long hair looks better with a round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If they are all roughly the same width, you have a round face. Once you've determined your face shape, you can start exploring different hairstyles.

Step By Step

If you're considering short hair, you can opt for a pixie cut, a bob, or a lob. A pixie cut can add volume to the crown of the head and elongate the face. A bob can also work well by adding angles to the face. A lob, which is a longer version of a bob, can add length to the face and create a more balanced look.

On the other hand, if you're considering long hair, you can opt for layers, waves, or curls. Layers can add volume and texture to the hair, while waves and curls can create the illusion of a longer face by adding movement and bounce. You can also try side-swept bangs, which can add angles to the face and create a more elongated look.

Tips

When deciding on a hairstyle, it's important to consider your hair texture and lifestyle. Short hair can be easier to maintain and style, while long hair may require more time and effort. It's also important to consider your personality and personal style. Your hair should reflect who you are and make you feel confident and comfortable.

FAQ

Q. Can round faces pull off short hair?

A. Yes, round faces can pull off short hair. It's all about finding the right cut and style that works for you.

Q. Can round faces pull off long hair?

A. Yes, round faces can also pull off long hair. Again, it's all about finding the right cut and style that works for you.

Q. What are some hairstyles to avoid with a round face?

A. Hairstyles to avoid with a round face include blunt bangs, middle parts, and hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face.

Hair Care

Whether you opt for short or long hair, it's important to take care of your hair. Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type and texture, and avoid over-styling with heat tools. Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Ultimately, the decision of whether short or long hair looks better with a round face is up to you. Experiment with different cuts and styles to find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful. With the right hairstyle, you'll be sure to turn heads and feel your best.

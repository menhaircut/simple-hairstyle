How To Cut Your Own Hair In Long Layers Easy Haircut Tutorial

Written by Christine Nov 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon every few months to get your hair cut? Do you want to save money and time by cutting your own hair at home? If so, this easy haircut tutorial on how to cut your own hair in long layers is perfect for you! In this article, we will provide step-by-step instructions on how to achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

Cutting your own hair in long layers is an easy and affordable way to keep your hair looking healthy and styled. The layers will add volume and movement to your hair, giving it a natural and effortless look. By following this tutorial, you'll be able to achieve a salon-worthy haircut without leaving your house.

How To:

To cut your own hair in long layers, you will need a few tools:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror

Before you start cutting, it's important to have a clear idea of the style you want to achieve. You can find inspiration from magazines, social media, or by consulting with a professional hairstylist.

Step-by-Step:

Step 1: Divide Your Hair

Start by dividing your hair into sections. Create a middle part and divide your hair into two equal parts. Then, divide each half into two more sections, creating four equal parts. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 2: Cut the Bottom Layer

Take the first section of hair from the bottom layer and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut off the desired length. Make sure to cut straight across and avoid cutting at an angle. Repeat this step for each section of the bottom layer.

Step 3: Cut the Middle Layer

Release the next section of hair from the middle layer and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut off the desired length, making sure to leave some length from the bottom layer. Repeat this step for each section of the middle layer.

Step 4: Cut the Top Layer

Release the next section of hair from the top layer and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut off the desired length, making sure to leave some length from the middle layer. Repeat this step for each section of the top layer.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut all the layers, it's time to blend them together. Comb your hair straight down and look for any noticeable lines between the layers. Use your scissors to cut into the ends of the layers, creating a softer and more blended look.

Tips:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair before cutting. This will make it easier to cut and style.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair tends to look longer than it actually is, and you may end up cutting too much off.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your hair and make sure the layers are even.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring and time-consuming, so don't rush it.

FAQ:

Can I cut my hair in long layers if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your hair in long layers if it's curly. Keep in mind that curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so cut your hair a little longer than you want it to be.

Do I need to wash my hair before cutting it?

No, you don't need to wash your hair before cutting it. In fact, it's better to cut your hair when it's dry so you can see the length and texture more clearly.

Hair Care:

After cutting your hair, it's important to take good care of it to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid pulling it too much.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Cutting your own hair in long layers is an easy and affordable way to maintain your hairstyle at home. By following this tutorial and using the right tools and techniques, you'll be able to achieve a professional-looking haircut in no time. Remember to take your time, start with small cuts, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles!

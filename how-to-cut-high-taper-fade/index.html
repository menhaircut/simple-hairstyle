How To Cut High Taper Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that combines sleekness and edge? Look no further than the high taper fade. This popular men's haircut involves a gradual fade from long hair on top to shorter hair on the sides and back. With a bit of practice and the right tools, you can easily learn how to cut a high taper fade at home. Follow these step-by-step instructions to achieve a fresh, modern look.

Description:

The high taper fade is a stylish haircut that involves a gradual reduction in hair length from the top of the head to the sides and back. This cut creates a sleek, edgy look that is perfect for men who want a modern, trendy hairstyle. The high taper fade is also versatile and can be customized to meet your individual preferences. Whether you prefer a short, tight fade or a longer, more gradual fade, this cut can be adjusted to suit your needs.

How to Cut High Taper Fade:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin cutting your hair, you will need to gather some essential tools. These include:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair trimmer
  • Barber cape or towel
  • Clipper guards
  • Mirror

Make sure you have all of these tools on hand before you begin cutting your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, it is important to wash and dry it thoroughly. This will help you achieve a clean, even cut. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and condition your hair. Then, use a towel or blow dryer to dry your hair completely. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 3: Create a Guide

Using your scissors, create a guide for the taper fade. Start at the crown of your head and cut the hair to the desired length. Then, use your clippers to create a line around the sides and back of your head. This will be your starting point for the taper fade.

Step 4: Start the Fade

Use your clippers to start the fade. Begin with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard. Start at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up to the guide you created in step 3. Be sure to blend the hair carefully to create a smooth, even fade.

Step 5: Create a Seamless Transition

Once you have created the initial fade, use your hair trimmer to create a seamless transition between the shorter hair on the sides and the longer hair on top. Use a comb to guide the hair and trim away any excess hair that may be left.

Step 6: Finish the Cut

Use your scissors to trim any remaining hair on top of your head. Be sure to blend the hair carefully to create a seamless transition between the top and the sides. Use your clippers to touch up any areas that may need additional attention.

Tips:

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high taper fade:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to create a more gradual fade.
  • Use your comb to guide the hair and create a seamless transition between the top and the sides.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting a high taper fade requires precision and attention to detail.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find the perfect high taper fade for you.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about the high taper fade:

  • What is a high taper fade?

    • A high taper fade is a men's haircut that involves a gradual reduction in hair length from the top of the head to the sides and back.

  • How do you maintain a high taper fade?

    • To maintain a high taper fade, you will need to get regular haircuts every 3-4 weeks. You can also use hair products such as pomade or wax to style your hair and keep it looking sleek and polished.

  • Can you cut a high taper fade at home?

    • Yes, with the right tools and a bit of practice, you can easily learn how to cut a high taper fade at home.

  • What are some variations of the high taper fade?

    • Some variations of the high taper fade include the low taper fade, the skin fade, and the bald fade.

Hair Care:

Here are some hair care tips to keep your high taper fade looking fresh and healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straightening irons.
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair gently.
  • Apply hair products sparingly to avoid buildup and damage to your hair.

Now that you know how to cut a high taper fade, you can achieve a sleek, modern look at home. With a bit of practice and some patience, you can master this stylish men's haircut and customize it to meet your individual needs. So why wait? Start experimenting with different lengths and styles today and find the perfect high taper fade for you.

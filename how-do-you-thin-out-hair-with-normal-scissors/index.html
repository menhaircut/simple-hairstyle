How Do You Thin Out Hair With Normal Scissors

Written by Daniel Aug 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Thinning out your hair can give your hair a lighter and softer look. It can also help manage thick and unmanageable hair. However, you don't always need specialized tools to thin out your hair. You can use normal scissors to achieve the desired result. Here's how to thin out your hair with normal scissors.

Description:

Thinning out hair with normal scissors is a simple process of cutting the hair strands at an angle, which creates a "thinning" effect. This method is an ideal option for those who do not have access to specialized thinning scissors or want to save money on salon appointments. Thinning out hair with normal scissors can be done at home, but it requires patience and precision.

How to Thin Out Hair with Normal Scissors:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly. This will ensure that your hair is clean and tangle-free, which will make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into several sections using hair clips. This will help you work on one section at a time without getting confused.

Step 3: Hold the Scissors at an Angle

Hold the scissors at an angle, with the point facing upwards. This angle will create a "thinning" effect on your hair, without removing too much hair at once.

Step 4: Cut the Hair Strands

Start cutting the hair strands at an angle, taking small sections at a time. Hold the section of hair with your fingers and start cutting the hair strands at the tips. Cut only a small amount of hair at a time, and repeat the process until you achieve the desired level of thinning.

Step 5: Repeat the Process on All Sections

Repeat the process on all sections of your hair until you have achieved the desired level of thinning. Make sure to cut the hair strands at an angle to create a natural-looking "thinning" effect.

Tips:

- Always start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting off too much hair at once.

- Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.

- Do not cut your hair when it is wet, as it can result in uneven cutting.

- Take breaks between sections to avoid making mistakes due to fatigue.

FAQ:

Can I use normal scissors to thin out my hair?

Yes, you can use normal scissors to thin out your hair. However, it requires patience and precision to achieve the desired result.

How often should I thin out my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast your hair grows. Generally, thinning out your hair once every 6-8 weeks is recommended.

Can I thin out my hair by myself?

Yes, you can thin out your hair by yourself. However, it is recommended to seek professional help for the first time to avoid making mistakes.

Hair Care:

Thinning out your hair can cause damage if you are not careful. Make sure to use sharp scissors and take small sections at a time to avoid damaging your hair. After thinning out your hair, make sure to use a deep conditioner to nourish your hair and prevent damage.

Thinning out your hair with normal scissors is an easy and cost-effective way to achieve a natural-looking "thinning" effect. With patience and precision, you can achieve the desired result without going to a salon. Follow these simple steps and enjoy your new look!

Haircut For Curly Hair With Round Face Tips How To And Styles

Written by Luffy Oct 19, 2023 · 6 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse, especially when you have a round face. You want a haircut that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural curls. But with so many hairstyles to choose from, it can be overwhelming to find the right one. Fear not, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll discuss tips and tricks for the perfect haircut for curly hair with a round face. We’ll also provide step-by-step instructions, styling tips, and hair care advice. Let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  1. Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut
  2. How-to: Cutting Curly Hair with a Round Face
  3. Styles for Curly Hair with a Round Face
  4. Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

When it comes to choosing the right haircut for curly hair with a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Embrace Your Curls

Curly hair can be beautiful and unique, so it’s important to find a haircut that accentuates your natural curls. Avoid straightening your hair or opting for a sleek, blunt cut that can make your face appear rounder. Instead, embrace your curls and choose a style that enhances their natural shape.

2. Consider the Length

The length of your hair can play a significant role in how it flatters your face shape. For instance, long hair can elongate your face, while short hair can make it appear wider. Opt for a length that complements your features and enhances your curls.

3. Layer It Up

Layering is an excellent way to add texture, volume and shape to curly hair. Layers can create a flattering frame for your face and help balance out the roundness. When getting your hair cut, ask your stylist for layers that start at your cheekbones and work their way down.

4. Avoid Heavy Bangs

Heavy, blunt bangs can add weight to your face and make it appear rounder. If you want to add some bangs to your haircut, opt for lighter, side-swept bangs that can create a slimming effect.

How-to: Cutting Curly Hair with a Round Face

Cutting curly hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right technique, you can achieve a beautiful, flattering haircut. Here’s how:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for curly hair. This will help hydrate and define your curls, making them easier to cut.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair. Be careful not to pull or break your curls.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into four sections: top, bottom, left, and right. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start by cutting the bottom section of your hair. Use a pair of sharp scissors and cut small sections of hair at a time. Cut at an angle to create layers that will frame your face.

Step 5: Move to the Top Section

Once you’ve finished cutting the bottom section, move on to the top section. Cut small sections of hair at a time, making sure to cut at an angle to create layers that will frame your face.

Step 6: Cut the Sides

Finally, move on to the sides of your hair. Cut small sections of hair at a time, making sure to cut at an angle to create layers that will frame your face.

Styles for Curly Hair with a Round Face

Now that you know how to cut your curly hair, it’s time to choose a style that flatters your round face. Here are a few styles to consider:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can elongate your face and create a slimming effect. Ask your stylist for layers that start at your cheekbones and work their way down.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can create a slimming effect and draw attention away from your round cheeks. Ask your stylist for light, wispy bangs that start at your cheekbones and work their way down.

3. Bob with Layers

A bob with layers can create a flattering frame for your face and enhance your natural curls. Ask your stylist for a chin-length bob with layers that start at your cheekbones and work their way down.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are a few tips:

1. Use Products Designed for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires products that are designed to hydrate and define your curls. Look for shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are specifically formulated for curly hair.

2. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and leave it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair no more than two or three times a week.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently. Avoid using a brush, as it can break and damage your curls.

4. Dry Your Hair Gently

After washing your hair, gently squeeze out the excess water with a towel. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as it can cause frizz and breakage. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry it gently.

FAQs

Q: What is the best haircut for curly hair with a round face?

A: The best haircut for curly hair with a round face is one that flatters your features and enhances your natural curls. Consider a long layered cut, a bob with layers, or side-swept bangs.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: Curly hair should be washed no more than two or three times a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: How do I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz in curly hair, avoid over-washing it, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle it gently, and dry it gently with a towel or diffuser.

There you have it, tips, how-to, styles, and care advice for the perfect haircut for curly hair with a round face. Follow these tips, and you’ll have a beautiful, flattering hairstyle that enhances your natural curls and flatters your face shape.

How To Know What Hairstyle Suits Me Male A Comprehensive Guide

If you're a male looking to switch up your hairstyle, but have no idea what style would suit you best, you're in the right place! In this article, we'll provide you with tips and guidelines on how to know what hairstyle suits you male. We'll cover everything from face shapes to hair types and styling preferences. So, let's get started!

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in finding your perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.

Once you have your measurements, compare them to the following:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the jawline, and the face length is greater than the width of the cheekbones.
  • Round: Cheekbones and face length have a similar measurement, and the forehead and jawline are also similar in width.
  • Square: All measurements are fairly similar, and the angle of the jaw is sharp.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones and jawline, and the chin is pointed.
  • Diamond: Face length is the largest measurement, and the forehead and jawline are narrower than the cheekbones.
  • Oblong: Face length is the largest measurement, and the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are similar in width.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement it.

Understanding Your Hair Type

The next step in finding your perfect hairstyle is to understand your hair type. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and kinky. Straight hair is the easiest to style, while curly and kinky hair require more maintenance. Wavy hair falls somewhere in between.

Knowing your hair type is important because certain hairstyles work better with certain hair types. For example, if you have curly hair, a short buzz cut might not be the best option. Instead, you might want to consider a longer style that will allow your curls to shine.

Considering Your Styling Preferences

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your styling preferences. Do you like to spend a lot of time styling your hair, or do you prefer a low-maintenance style? Do you like to use products like gel or mousse, or do you prefer a more natural look?

These are all important questions to ask yourself when choosing a hairstyle. If you prefer a low-maintenance style, you might want to consider a shorter cut that doesn't require a lot of styling. If you like to use products, you might want to consider a longer style that allows you to play around with different looks.

Popular Hairstyles for Men

Now that you've determined your face shape, hair type, and styling preferences, it's time to start looking for a hairstyle that suits you. Here are some popular hairstyles for men:

  • Buzz cut
  • Crew cut
  • Side part
  • Quiff
  • Slick back
  • Undercut
  • Man bun
  • Top knot

Remember, just because a hairstyle is popular doesn't mean it will necessarily suit you. Take into account your face shape, hair type, and styling preferences when choosing a style.

Tips for Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you find your perfect hairstyle:

  • Bring a picture to your barber or stylist to show them the style you want.
  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a style. If you have an active job or play sports, you might want to choose a shorter, low-maintenance cut.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. It's just hair, and it will grow back!

FAQs

How often should I get my hair cut?

It depends on the style you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, you should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

Don't panic! Remember, it's just hair, and it will grow back. If you don't like your new style, talk to your barber or stylist about how you can change it.

Can I still style my hair if it's thinning or balding?

Yes! There are plenty of great hairstyles for men with thinning or balding hair. Talk to your barber or stylist about the best options for you.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found your perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use products like gel or mousse sparingly. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often. Heat can damage your hair and make it brittle.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and guidelines, you should now have a better idea of how to know what hairstyle suits you male. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Good luck!

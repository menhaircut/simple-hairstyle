Best Hair Style For Round Face Girl

Written by Eveline Jun 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a round-faced girl looking for the best hair style that will enhance your facial features? Look no further because we've got you covered! In this article, we will be discussing the best hair styles for round face girls. We will go over how to achieve these styles, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-By-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

A round face is characterized by a softer, circular shape with wider cheekbones and a rounded jawline. The best hair styles for round face girls are those that elongate the face and add volume to the crown while slimming down the cheeks. It's important to note that the best hair style for you ultimately depends on your personal style, hair texture, and length.

How To

Before we dive into the best hair styles for round face girls, it's important to figure out your face shape. Here's how to do it:

  1. Take a full-face photo of yourself head-on.
  2. Measure the length and width of your face from the widest points.
  3. Compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

Once you've determined that you have a round face shape, it's time to choose the best hair style for you.

Step-By-Step

1. Long Layers

Long layers that graze the cheeks and frame the face are perfect for round face girls. This style adds volume to the crown while slimming down the cheeks.

Steps:

  1. Part your hair in the center and section it off.
  2. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, cutting your hair into long layers.
  3. Once you've finished, style as desired.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for round face girls because they elongate the face and add depth. This style also draws attention away from the cheeks.

Steps:

  1. Section off your bangs.
  2. Cut them at an angle, starting from the middle of your forehead and angling down.
  3. Once you've finished, style as desired.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and edgy style that works well on round face girls. This style elongates the face and adds volume to the crown.

Steps:

  1. Section off your hair.
  2. Cut your hair into a short, choppy style.
  3. Once you've finished, style as desired.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hair style for round face girls:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the cheekbone.
  • Choose styles that add volume to the crown and slim down the cheeks.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can short hair work on round face girls?

A: Yes, short hair can work on round face girls. The key is to choose a style that elongates the face and adds volume to the crown.

Q: Should I avoid bangs?

A: No, you don't have to avoid bangs. Side-swept bangs can actually work well on round face girls.

Q: Can I still have long hair?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair as a round face girl. Just make sure to add layers that slim down the cheeks.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain your chosen style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know the best hair styles for round face girls, it's time to choose the style that works for you. Remember to consider your personal style, hair texture, and length before making a decision. Happy styling!

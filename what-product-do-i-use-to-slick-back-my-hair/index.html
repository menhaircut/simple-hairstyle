What Product Do I Use To Slick Back My Hair

Written by Aprilia Aug 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for the perfect product to slick back your hair, you've come to the right place. Whether you're trying to achieve a sleek, sophisticated look or just want to keep your hair out of your face, there are a variety of products available that can help you achieve your desired style. In this article, we'll take a closer look at some of the best products on the market for slicking back your hair.

Description

Slicked back hair is a timeless style that never goes out of fashion. It's a classic look that can be worn by anyone, regardless of hair type or length. The key to achieving this look is finding the right product that will hold your hair in place without making it look greasy or stiff. There are a variety of products available that can help you achieve this look, including gels, pomades, and waxes.

How to Slick Back Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, you'll want to make sure it's clean and dry. If your hair is wet, it will be harder to style and will not hold its shape as well. Once your hair is dry, you can start applying your product of choice.

Gel

Gel is a great option for those who want a strong hold that will last all day. To use gel, simply apply a small amount to your hair and comb it through from front to back. Be sure to distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair to avoid any clumps or buildup.

Pomade

Pomade is a popular choice for those who want a slick, shiny look. To use pomade, scoop a small amount onto your finger and rub it between your hands to warm it up. Then, apply it to your hair and comb it through from front to back. Pomade can be used to achieve a variety of looks, from a sleek, shiny finish to a more textured, matte look.

Wax

Wax is a great option for those with thicker hair who want a strong hold that will last all day. To use wax, simply apply a small amount to your hair and work it through from front to back. Be sure to distribute the wax evenly throughout your hair to avoid any clumps or buildup.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair

2. Apply a small amount of your chosen product to your hair

3. Comb the product through your hair from front to back

4. Repeat until you achieve your desired look

5. Use a hairdryer to set the style in place if needed

Tips

- Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed

- Use a comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair

- Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look

FAQ

Q: How long will my slicked back hair last?

A: The length of time your slicked back hair will last depends on the product you use and your hair type. Gel and wax tend to have a stronger hold and will last longer than pomade.

Q: Can I slick back my hair if it's short?

A: Yes, you can still achieve a slicked back look with short hair. Just be sure to use a product that isn't too heavy or greasy, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're using styling products on a regular basis. To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, be sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You should also consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know what product to use to slick back your hair, it's time to experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve a sleek, sophisticated look that's perfect for any occasion.

How To Get New Haircuts In Gta 5

Written by William Aug 16, 2023 Â· 2 min read

Are you looking to give your character in GTA 5 a new look? One of the easiest ways to do this is by getting a new haircut. In this article, we'll go over the steps you need to take to get a new haircut in GTA 5.

How to

Getting a new haircut in GTA 5 is a simple process. You'll need to find a hair salon, which can be done by looking on your map for a pair of scissors icon. Once you've found a hair salon, follow these steps:

Step by Step

1. Walk into the hair salon

2. Approach the barber chair

3. Choose your desired haircut from the menu

4. Confirm your choice

5. Wait for the haircut to be completed

6. Pay the barber for their services

And that's it! You now have a fresh new haircut in GTA 5.

Tips

- Make sure to save your game before getting a new haircut, just in case you don't like the way it looks.

- Some hair salons may offer different hairstyles depending on the character you're playing as, so make sure to check all of them.

- If you're running low on cash, you can always rob a store or complete a mission to earn some extra money.

FAQ

Q: Can I change my haircut again after getting one?

A: Yes, you can change your haircut as many times as you want.

Q: Do I need to have a certain level or amount of money to get a new haircut?

A: No, anyone can get a new haircut regardless of their level or amount of money.

Hair Care

While getting a new haircut in GTA 5 is fun, it's important to take care of your hair in real life too. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

- Wash your hair regularly

- Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair (such as blow dryers or straighteners)

- Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you'll have healthy, great-looking hair in both GTA 5 and real life.

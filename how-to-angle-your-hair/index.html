How To Angle Your Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 16, 2023 · 3 min read

Angled haircuts are a popular hairstyle trend that can add dimension and movement to your hair. Whether you have short or long locks, angled hair can give you a fresh and modern look. In this article, we’ll go over how to angle your hair and some tips for maintaining this style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Angle Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Angled Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Angled Hair

How to Angle Your Hair

Angled hair refers to a haircut where the hair is cut at an angle, creating a graduated look. This style is also known as an A-line haircut. Angled haircuts can be short, medium, or long, and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type.

Before you decide to angle your hair, it’s important to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best angle for your face shape and hair type. They can help you decide on the perfect length and angle for your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to angle your hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts – two in the front and two in the back.
  3. Starting at the back, cut your hair at an angle, using the shortest point as your guide.
  4. Move to the front sections of your hair and repeat the process, cutting at the same angle as the back.
  5. Blend the back and front sections together by cutting at a slight angle.
  6. Trim any excess hair to create a seamless transition from the shortest point to the longest.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

It’s important to note that cutting angled hair requires precision and skill, so it’s best to leave it to a professional hairstylist.

Tips for Maintaining Angled Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your angled hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the angle and shape.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

FAQ

Q: Can angled hair work for any hair type?

A: Yes, angled haircuts can be customized to suit any hair type, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How often should I wash my angled hair?

A: It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry or oily.

Q: Will angled hair make my face look slimmer?

A: Yes, angled haircuts can create the illusion of a slimmer face by drawing attention away from the jawline and towards the cheekbones.

Hair Care for Angled Hair

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for keeping your angled haircut looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as heat can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair to keep it moisturized and frizz-free.
  • Avoid tying your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can enjoy a beautiful, angled haircut that’s easy to maintain and style.

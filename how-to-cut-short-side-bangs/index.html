How To Cut Short Side Bangs Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 17, 2023 · 3 min read

Cutting your own bangs can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and chic look. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut short side bangs in a relaxed English language. We’ll cover everything from the tools you’ll need, to the step-by-step process of cutting your bangs, and finally, some tips on how to care for your new hairstyle.

Table of Content

  2. How to Cut Short Side Bangs
  3. Step-by-Step Guide

Tools

Before you start cutting your bangs, you’ll need the right tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A fine-toothed comb
  • A handheld mirror
  • Hair clips

How to Cut Short Side Bangs

Short side bangs are a trendy and chic style that can give your face a fresh and youthful look. Here’s how to cut short side bangs:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any tangles.
  2. Part your hair in the middle or on one side, depending on your preference.
  3. Take a section of hair from the front of your head, where you want your bangs to start. Make sure the section is about 1-2 inches wide.
  4. Comb the section of hair forward and down, making sure it’s straight and even.
  5. Take your scissors and hold them vertically, with the blades pointing up.
  6. Starting at the tip of the hair, cut vertically into the hair, making small snips along the way. This will create a textured, choppy look.
  7. Continue cutting until you’ve reached the desired length.
  8. Repeat on the other side, making sure to match the length and texture of the first side.
  9. Trim any longer pieces until you have a straight, even line.
  10. Style your new bangs as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own short side bangs:

  • Start with less hair, you can always cut more.
  • Take your time and cut slowly to avoid mistakes.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don’t cut your bangs too short, you can always go back and trim more if necessary.
  • When in doubt, seek the help of a professional stylist.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short side bangs:

  • Can I cut my own bangs?

    • Yes, it’s possible to cut your own bangs. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the step-by-step guide above.

  • How often should I trim my bangs?

    • You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

  • What type of face shape is best for short side bangs?

    • Short side bangs can work for many face shapes, but they’re particularly flattering on heart-shaped and oval faces.

Hair Care

Now that you have your new short side bangs, it’s important to take care of them. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you’re using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your bangs.
  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • If you’re not happy with your bangs, don’t panic. They’ll grow out eventually!

Now that you know how to cut short side bangs, you can try this trendy and stylish look at home. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow the step-by-step guide above. Happy cutting!

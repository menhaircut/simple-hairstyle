How To Cut Bangs Face Frame Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 28, 2023 · 3 min read

Cutting bangs can be a great way to change up your hairstyle and add a unique touch to your look. However, cutting bangs can be intimidating, especially if you’re not sure where to start. In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to cut bangs face frame, step-by-step, and provide some helpful tips and advice.

Description

A face-framing bang is a great option if you want to add some definition to your face without committing to a full fringe. This style of bangs is cut to frame the face, drawing attention to your eyes and cheekbones. Face-framing bangs can work with any hair length and texture, and they’re easy to style and maintain.

How to Cut Bangs Face Frame

Step 1: Prep

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles or knots, and section off the hair you want to cut into bangs.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Use a comb to section off the hair you want to cut into bangs. Hold the section of hair at the front of your head and determine how long you want your bangs to be. Use your fingers to hold the hair at the desired length, and then cut across the section of hair with sharp scissors.

Step 3: Add Texture

To add some texture and movement to your bangs, hold the scissors vertically and make small, quick snips into the ends of your bangs. This will create a choppy, layered look that adds dimension to your hair.

Step 4: Blend

Once you’ve cut your bangs to the desired length and texture, use a razor or thinning shears to blend the bangs into the rest of your hair. This will create a more natural, seamless look.

Tips

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind when cutting bangs face frame:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up. You can always cut more hair, but you can’t put it back on once it’s cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut. Dull scissors can lead to uneven or frayed ends.
  • Cut your bangs when your hair is dry. Wet hair can appear longer than it actually is, and you may end up cutting too much.
  • Take breaks and step back to assess your progress. It’s easy to get caught up in the moment and cut too much hair.

FAQ

Can I cut my bangs myself?

Yes, cutting your own bangs is possible, but it can be tricky. It’s important to take your time and start with small snips. If you’re unsure about cutting your own bangs, it’s always best to go to a professional stylist.

How often should I trim my bangs?

It’s best to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Hair Care

To keep your bangs looking their best, it’s important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Now that you know how to cut bangs face frame, you can add a new look to your style with ease. Just remember to take your time, start with small snips, and follow our tips and advice.

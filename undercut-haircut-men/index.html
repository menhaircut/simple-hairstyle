Undercut Haircut Men A Complete Guide For 2023

Written by William Jul 23, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a stylish haircut that’s versatile and easy to maintain, the undercut haircut for men could be the perfect choice for you. This classic hairstyle has been popular for decades and continues to be a favorite among men of all ages. In this guide, we’ll take a closer look at the undercut haircut for men, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to care for your hair.

Description

The undercut haircut for men is a short back and sides haircut that’s longer on top. The hair on the sides and back is usually cut very short or shaved, while the hair on top is left long enough to be styled in a variety of ways. This versatile hairstyle can be styled into a quiff, pompadour, slick back, or messy fringe, making it a great choice for men who want a haircut that can be styled in different ways.

How to Get an Undercut Haircut Men

If you’re interested in getting an undercut haircut for men, the first step is to find a good barber or hairdresser who has experience cutting this type of hairstyle. You can show them a picture of the style you want, or ask for their advice on the best way to cut and style your hair.

When getting an undercut haircut, the sides and back of your head will be shaved or cut very short, while the hair on top will be left longer. The length of your hair on top will depend on the style you want to achieve, but it’s usually between 2 and 6 inches long.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting an Undercut Haircut Men

Here’s a step-by-step guide to cutting an undercut haircut for men:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties.
  3. Cut the sides and back of your hair with clippers, starting with a longer guard and gradually decreasing the length until you reach your desired length.
  4. Cut the hair on top of your head with scissors, leaving it longer than the sides and back.
  5. Blend the hair on top with the sides and back using scissors or clippers.
  6. Style your hair using a hair product and a comb or your fingers.

Tips for Styling Your Undercut Haircut Men

Here are some tips for styling your undercut haircut:

  • Use a hair product that works well with your hair type and the style you want to achieve.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair, depending on the look you want to achieve.
  • Apply hair product to damp hair for best results.

FAQs

How often should I get my undercut haircut trimmed?

You should get your undercut haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Can I style my undercut haircut differently for different occasions?

Yes, the great thing about the undercut haircut for men is that it can be styled in different ways for different occasions. You can style it into a slick back for a formal event, or a messy fringe for a casual night out.

What hair products should I use for my undercut haircut?

The best hair products for your undercut haircut will depend on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Talk to your barber or hairdresser for advice on the best products for your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Undercut Haircut

Here are some hair care tips for men with an undercut haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hairdryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a good quality conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weigh it down.
  • Get regular trims to keep your undercut haircut looking sharp and stylish.

With this guide, you should now have all the information you need to get an undercut haircut for men, style it, and care for your hair. Whether you’re looking for a classic or modern look, the undercut haircut is a great choice for men who want a versatile and stylish haircut.

How To Determine If Bangs Will Look Good On You

Written by Luffy Nov 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but not sure if they will suit you or not? Bangs can be a great way to switch up your look and add some character to your hair. However, before you take the plunge, it's important to determine if bangs will look good on you. In this blog post, we'll discuss how to determine if bangs will suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Table of Contents

  1. Determining Your Face Shape
  2. Considering Your Hair Type
  3. Assessing Your Personal Style
  4. Tips for Getting Bangs
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Bangs

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in determining if bangs will look good on you is to assess your face shape. Bangs can work well for most face shapes, but some styles may be more suited to certain shapes than others. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round face: Side-swept or asymmetrical bangs can help elongate the face.
  • Square face: Soft, wispy bangs can help soften angular features.
  • Heart-shaped face: Side-swept or curtain bangs can balance out a wider forehead.
  • Oval face: Most styles of bangs can work well with an oval face shape.

If you're not sure what your face shape is, you can use an online face shape calculator or consult with a hairstylist.

Considering Your Hair Type

In addition to face shape, your hair type is also an important factor to consider when getting bangs. Here are some tips for different hair types:

  • Straight hair: Most styles of bangs work well with straight hair, but blunt or choppy bangs can be particularly flattering.
  • Curly hair: Side-swept or curtain bangs can work well with curly hair, but avoid blunt or heavy bangs that can be difficult to manage.
  • Thick hair: Blunt or choppy bangs can help thin out the appearance of thick hair.
  • Thin hair: Avoid heavy or blunt bangs that can make thin hair look even thinner; opt for wispy or side-swept bangs instead.

Assessing Your Personal Style

Your personal style is also an important factor to consider when getting bangs. Do you prefer a classic, polished look, or something more edgy and unique? Here are some style considerations:

  • Classic: Blunt, straight-across bangs can be a timeless look that works well with a classic style.
  • Edgy: Choppy or asymmetrical bangs can add some edge to your look.
  • Boho: Wispy or curtain bangs can give off a more bohemian vibe.
  • Professional: Side-swept or polished bangs can be a great choice for a professional look.

Tips for Getting Bangs

Once you've determined that bangs will look good on you, it's important to keep a few things in mind:

  • Communicate clearly with your hairstylist about the type of bangs you want, including length and thickness.
  • Consider the upkeep involved with bangs, including regular trims and styling.
  • Experiment with different styles of bangs to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about getting bangs:

Will bangs work with my glasses?

Yes! Bangs can work well with glasses, but it's important to choose a style that complements your frames. Consider trying on different styles of glasses with your new bangs to ensure a cohesive look.

How often do I need to trim my bangs?

Bangs typically need to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and prevent them from getting too long.

Can I style my bangs differently?

Yes! There are many ways to style bangs, including blow-drying them straight, curling them under, or pinning them back. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the one that works best for you.

Hair Care for Bangs

Finally, it's important to take care of your bangs to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to prevent oil buildup.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too frequently, as this can transfer oils from your hands.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to help them maintain their shape.

By following these tips, you can determine if bangs will look good on you and maintain them for a flawless look. Remember, bangs can be a great way to switch up your look and add some personality to your hair!

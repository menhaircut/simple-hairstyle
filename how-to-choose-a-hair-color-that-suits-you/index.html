How To Choose A Hair Color That Suits You A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Choosing a hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what will suit you best. With so many shades and tones available, it's important to take a step back and consider what will work best for your skin tone, eye color, and personal style. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to choose a hair color that suits you, along with some tips and FAQs to help you make the best decision for your hair.

  2. Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing a hair color that suits you is to determine your skin tone. This will help you determine which shades will look best on you. There are three main skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral.

If you have a cool skin tone, you'll have blue or pink undertones in your skin. Cool-toned people look best in shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, and black. If you have a warm skin tone, you'll have yellow or golden undertones in your skin. Warm-toned people look best in shades like honey blonde, golden brown, and auburn. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can pull off both warm and cool shades.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in choosing a hair color that suits you. If you have blue or green eyes, warm-toned shades like honey blonde or copper can help bring out the color in your eyes. If you have brown eyes, cool-toned shades like ash brown or platinum blonde can help make your eyes pop.

Step 3: Look at Your Natural Hair Color

When choosing a hair color, it's important to consider your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, going too light may not suit you. Similarly, if you have light hair, going too dark may not look natural. Consider staying within two shades of your natural hair color for a more natural look.

Step 4: Decide on a Shade

Once you've determined your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color, it's time to choose a shade. Consider whether you want to go lighter or darker, as well as the undertones of the shade. For example, if you have warm undertones, consider choosing a shade with warm undertones as well.

Step 5: Choose a Hair Dye

When choosing a hair dye, consider whether you want a permanent or semi-permanent option. Permanent dyes will last longer, but they can be more damaging to your hair. Semi-permanent dyes will fade over time, but they can be gentler on your hair. Additionally, consider whether you want to use a box dye or go to a salon for a professional application.

Tips for Choosing a Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color that suits you:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance your hair color will require.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual hair color apps to see how different shades will look on you.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hair color:

How often should I touch up my roots?

This will depend on the hair dye you use and how fast your hair grows. Typically, roots should be touched up every 4-6 weeks.

Can I dye my hair if it's damaged?

Dyeing your hair can make it more damaged, so it's important to take care of your hair before and after dyeing. Consider using a deep conditioning treatment before dyeing, and use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help prevent further damage.

What should I do if I don't like my new hair color?

If you don't like your new hair color, don't panic. You can either wait for it to fade or go to a salon for a color correction. It's important to avoid dyeing your hair again too soon, as this can cause further damage.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Once you've chosen a hair color that suits you, it's important to take care of your colored hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your hair color.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can cause the color to fade faster.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as these can cause damage to colored hair.

Choosing a hair color that suits you can be a fun and exciting process. By following these steps and tips, you can find the perfect shade for your skin tone, eye color, and personal style.

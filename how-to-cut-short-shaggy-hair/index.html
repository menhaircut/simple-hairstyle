How To Cut Short Shaggy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Oct 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both edgy and low-maintenance, you might want to consider a short shaggy haircut. This trendy hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add texture and volume to their hair without sacrificing length.

If you're interested in learning how to cut short shaggy hair, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this hairstyle, as well as some tips on how to maintain it.

How to Cut Short Shaggy Hair

Before we get started, it's important to note that cutting hair can be tricky, especially if you're not a professional. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great result.

What You'll Need:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Hair clips

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before you start cutting.

2. Section your hair into four parts: top, left side, right side, and back. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

3. Start with the back section. Use your clippers to trim the hair at the nape of your neck, leaving about half an inch of hair. Use your scissors to create layers by cutting the hair at an angle.

4. Move on to the side sections. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, starting at the bottom and working your way up. This will create layers and texture.

5. Finally, move on to the top section. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, starting at the crown and working your way down. This will create volume and texture.

6. Once you've finished cutting your hair, use your comb to check for any uneven spots. Make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Maintaining Short Shaggy Hair

Now that you've learned how to cut short shaggy hair, it's important to know how to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking fresh.
  • Use a texturizing product, such as sea salt spray, to enhance the texture of your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Can I cut short shaggy hair at home?

Yes, you can. However, it's important to be careful and use the right tools and techniques.

What face shape is best for short shaggy hair?

Short shaggy hair can work with any face shape, but it's especially flattering on those with round or square faces.

How long does short shaggy hair last?

Short shaggy hair can last up to 6-8 weeks before it needs to be trimmed.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth.

With this step-by-step guide, you can achieve a trendy short shaggy hairstyle at home. Remember to be careful and use the right tools and techniques. With proper maintenance, your hair will look great for weeks to come!

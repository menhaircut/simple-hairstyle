How To Style Really Frizzy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jul 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have really frizzy hair, you know how difficult it can be to manage it on a daily basis. It can be frustrating to have hair that always seems to have a mind of its own. However, with the right techniques and products, you can tame your frizzy hair and create a beautiful style that you'll love. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for styling really frizzy hair.

How to

Step 1: Start with clean, moisturized hair

The key to styling frizzy hair is to start with a good foundation. Make sure your hair is clean and moisturized before you begin styling it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for frizzy hair.

Step 2: Use a leave-in conditioner

After you've washed your hair, use a leave-in conditioner to add extra moisture and help tame frizz. Apply the conditioner to your hair while it's still damp, and then comb it through with a wide-tooth comb.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair

Use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair. This will help to reduce frizz and create a more defined curl pattern. Use a low heat setting and avoid touching your hair too much as you dry it.

Step 4: Use a curling iron or straightener

Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling iron or straightener to create your desired style. If you're using a curling iron, wrap small sections of hair around the barrel and hold for a few seconds before releasing. If you're using a straightener, run it over small sections of hair, pulling the hair straight as you go.

Step 5: Finish with a serum

To help keep your hair looking smooth and shiny, finish with a serum. Apply a small amount to your hands and then run them through your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help to lock in moisture and reduce frizz.

Tips

- Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and make frizz worse.

- Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz while you sleep.

- Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

- Don't be afraid to embrace your natural texture and work with it, rather than against it.

FAQ

Q: What are some good products for taming frizzy hair?

A: Look for products that are designed specifically for frizzy hair, such as leave-in conditioners, serums, and creams. Some popular options include Moroccanoil Treatment, Living Proof No Frizz Nourishing Styling Cream, and Bumble and Bumble Hairdresser's Invisible Oil.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have frizzy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people with frizzy hair can go several days between washes, while others may need to wash their hair every day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is key to reducing frizz and creating beautiful styles. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and hydrated:

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add extra moisture to your hair.

- Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and make frizz worse.

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, rather than a brush, as this will cause less breakage.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your frizzy hair with ease and confidence. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.

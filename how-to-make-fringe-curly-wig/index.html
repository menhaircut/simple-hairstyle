How To Make A Fringe Curly Wig Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your natural hair and need to switch things up? Do you want a new look without damaging your hair? If you answered yes to these questions, then you should consider making a fringe curly wig. In this article, we will guide you on how to make a fringe curly wig that will transform your look.

Description

A fringe curly wig is a perfect hairstyle that gives you the freedom to switch up your look without damaging your natural hair. It is a wig with a curly fringe that is perfect for any occasion. Making a fringe curly wig is not as complicated as it seems. It requires a few materials and tools, and you can make your own wig at home.

How to Make a Fringe Curly Wig

Step 1: Gather Materials

The first step in making a fringe curly wig is to gather all the necessary materials. You will need a wig cap, hair bundles, scissors, needle, thread, and a wig stand.

Step 2: Measure and Cut

Measure the wig cap on your head and mark where you want the fringe to be. Cut the hair bundles into small sections that are about 2-3 inches long.

Step 3: Sew the Wefts

Using the needle and thread, sew the hair wefts onto the wig cap. Start at the back and work your way to the front. After sewing the wefts, cut the excess hair and sew the fringe onto the wig cap.

Step 4: Style the Wig

After sewing the wig, style it to your desired look. You can use curling irons or flexi rods to curl the hair. You can also use a flat iron to straighten the hair.

Tips

  • Choose high-quality hair bundles that match your hair color and texture.
  • Use a wig stand to hold the wig as you sew the wefts.
  • Always measure your head before sewing the wig cap.
  • Use a wig brush to style the wig.

FAQ

How long does it take to make a fringe curly wig?

The time it takes to make a fringe curly wig depends on your skill level and the complexity of the wig. It can take anywhere between 3-8 hours.

Can I wash my fringe curly wig?

Yes, you can wash your fringe curly wig. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner and let the wig air dry.

How long does a fringe curly wig last?

The lifespan of a fringe curly wig depends on how well you take care of it. With proper care, it can last up to six months or longer.

Hair Care

To keep your fringe curly wig looking fresh, you need to take care of it. Use a wig brush to comb the hair, and avoid using heat styling tools frequently. Store the wig on a wig stand to avoid tangling and shedding.

In conclusion, making a fringe curly wig is a great way to switch up your look without damaging your natural hair. With the right materials and tools, you can make your own wig at home. Follow these steps and tips to make your own fringe curly wig and enjoy your new look.

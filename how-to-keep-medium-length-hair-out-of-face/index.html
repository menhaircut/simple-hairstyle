How To Keep Medium Length Hair Out Of Face Tips And Tricks

Written by William Oct 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Medium length hair can be a great style to have, but it can be frustrating when strands constantly fall in your face. Whether you’re at work, working out, or just trying to look good, having hair constantly in your face can be a hassle. In this article, we’ll be discussing some tips and tricks on how to keep medium length hair out of face.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Keep Medium Length Hair Out of Face
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

How to Keep Medium Length Hair Out of Face

There are several ways to keep medium length hair out of your face. Here are some popular methods:

  • Using hair accessories such as headbands, hair clips, and bobby pins
  • Trying out different hairstyles such as braids, buns, and ponytails
  • Using products such as hairspray or gel to keep hair in place

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to keep medium length hair out of your face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair to make it more manageable.
  2. Apply a hair product such as gel or hairspray to keep hair in place.
  3. Try out different hairstyles such as braids, buns, or ponytails to see what works best for you.
  4. If you’re using hair accessories, make sure they’re comfortable and don’t cause any pain.
  5. Experiment with different colors and styles of hair accessories to add some variety to your look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind when trying to keep medium length hair out of your face:

  • Use hair accessories that match your outfit or personal style.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products that won’t damage your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to keep things interesting.
  • If you’re using bobby pins, try crisscrossing them for extra hold.
  • If you’re using a headband, choose one with grips on the inside to keep it in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping medium length hair out of your face:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much you sweat. Generally, it’s recommended to wash your hair every two to three days.

Q: Can I use regular bobby pins to keep my hair in place?

A: Yes, regular bobby pins work fine. However, if you have thicker hair, you may want to invest in stronger pins.

Q: What’s the best way to keep a ponytail from falling out?

A: Use a hair tie that’s tight and secure. You can also use bobby pins to hold any loose strands in place.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your medium length hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your medium length hair out of your face and looking great. Whether you’re at work, working out, or just trying to look good, having hair constantly in your face can be a hassle. So, try out some of these methods and see what works best for you!

Will Bangs Suit Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but worried if they will suit your round face? Well, worry no more because we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss how to determine if bangs will suit your round face, how to choose the right type of bangs, and how to take care of your bangs.

Description

Around face shape is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin, which can make it challenging to find the right hairstyle. Bangs can be a great way to frame your face and add some dimension to your hair. However, not all types of bangs suit round faces, and you need to be careful when choosing one.

How to Determine If Bangs Will Suit Your Round Face

Before deciding to get bangs, you need to determine if they will suit your round face. The best way to do this is to look at your face shape and see if you have a prominent forehead and a defined jawline. If you do, then bangs will most likely suit you. However, if you have a round forehead and a soft jawline, then bangs may not be the best choice for you.

How to Choose the Right Type of Bangs

Once you have determined that bangs will suit your round face, the next step is to choose the right type of bangs. The most flattering types of bangs for round faces are side-swept, long, and layered. These types of bangs help to create the illusion of an elongated face and draw attention away from the roundness of your cheeks.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great choice for round faces as they help to create the illusion of length. Sweep your bangs to one side and keep them longer than the rest of your hair. This will help to create a diagonal line that will make your face appear longer.

Long Bangs

Long bangs that graze the eyebrows are also a good choice for round faces. These bangs help to draw attention to your eyes and create a vertical line that lengthens your face.

Layered Bangs

Layered bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle are also a good choice for round faces. These bangs add texture and movement to your hair and help to create the illusion of an elongated face.

Step by Step: How to Cut Bangs for a Round Face

If you have decided to get bangs, here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut them for a round face:

Step 1: Determine the Type of Bangs

Determine the type of bangs that will suit your round face. Side-swept, long, and layered bangs are the most flattering for round faces.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into a triangle shape at the front of your head. The base of the triangle should be at your hairline, and the point should be at the top of your head.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Cut your bangs to the desired length, starting from the middle and working your way outwards. Cut your bangs longer on the sides and shorter in the middle if you want layered bangs.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Style your bangs by blow-drying them with a round brush. Use a straightening iron to smooth out any kinks or curls. Apply some hair serum to add shine and keep your bangs in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Now that you have bangs, it is essential to take care of them to keep them looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your bangs:

Trim Them Regularly

Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the right length. You can do this yourself or go to a salon.

Wash Them Often

Wash your bangs often, as they tend to get oily faster than the rest of your hair.

Blow-Dry Them After Washing

Blow-dry your bangs after washing to keep them in place. Use a round brush to create volume and a straightening iron to smooth out any kinks.

FAQs

1. Can I get bangs if I have a round face?

Yes, you can get bangs if you have a round face. However, you need to choose the right type of bangs that will flatter your face shape.

2. What type of bangs suit round faces?

The most flattering types of bangs for round faces are side-swept, long, and layered. These types of bangs help to create the illusion of an elongated face.

3. How do I style my bangs?

You can style your bangs by blow-drying them with a round brush and using a straightening iron to smooth out any kinks. Apply some hair serum to add shine and keep your bangs in place.

Hair Care for Bangs

Bangs require extra care to keep them looking great. Here are some hair care tips for bangs:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo to wash your hair and bangs. Harsh shampoos can strip your hair of its natural oils and make your bangs dry and brittle.

2. Use a Conditioner

Use a conditioner to keep your hair and bangs moisturized. Apply the conditioner to the ends of your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners on your bangs. Heat can damage your hair and make your bangs dry and frizzy.

4. Protect Your Bangs From the Sun

Protect your bangs from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray. The sun can damage your hair and make your bangs dry and brittle.

Now that you know all about bangs for round faces, you can confidently choose the right type of bangs and take care of them to keep them looking great. Follow the tips and tricks in this article, and you will have beautiful, flattering bangs in no time!

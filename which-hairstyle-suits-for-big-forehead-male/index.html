Which Hairstyle Suits For Big Forehead Male

Written by Frank Jun 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Big forehead can be a source of insecurity for some men. However, with the right hairstyle, it can be transformed into a stylish feature. In this article, we will discuss which hairstyles suit for big forehead male and provide tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

Men with big foreheads often struggle to find the right hairstyle that flatters their facial features. The key is to choose a hairstyle that balances your forehead with the rest of your face. The right hairstyle can help to make your face appear more proportionate and enhance your overall appearance.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a big forehead, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. Extremely short haircuts can make your forehead appear larger, while long haircuts can draw attention away from your face. Secondly, choose a hairstyle that adds volume to your hair on top of your head. This will help to balance out your forehead and make it appear smaller. Lastly, avoid hairstyles that are slicked back or flat, as they can accentuate your forehead.

Step by Step Guide

1. Determine your face shape. Knowing your face shape will help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

2. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to your hair on top of your head. This can be achieved with layered haircuts or haircuts with bangs.

3. Avoid hairstyles that are slicked back or flat. Instead, opt for hairstyles with texture and movement.

4. Consider using hair products such as mousse or pomade to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips

1. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

2. Visit a professional hairstylist to get a haircut that flatters your facial features.

3. Use hair products to enhance your hairstyle and add volume to your hair.

4. Maintain your hairstyle by trimming your hair regularly and using hair care products that are suitable for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have a buzz cut if I have a big forehead?

A: It is not recommended to have a buzz cut if you have a big forehead as it can make your forehead appear larger.

Q: Can I have long hair if I have a big forehead?

A: While long hair can draw attention away from your forehead, it is not recommended for men with big foreheads. Instead, opt for medium-length hairstyles that add volume to your hair on top of your head.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and stylish appearance. Use hair care products that are suitable for your hair type, such as shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot styling tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons too frequently as they can damage your hair. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyles that suit for big forehead male. The key is to choose a hairstyle that balances your forehead with the rest of your face and adds volume to your hair on top of your head. Experiment with different hairstyles and hair products to find the one that suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and stylish appearance.

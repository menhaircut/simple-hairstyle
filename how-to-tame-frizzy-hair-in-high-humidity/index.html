How To Tame Frizzy Hair In High Humidity

Written by Daniel Nov 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you struggle with frizzy hair on a humid day? You’re not alone! High humidity can wreak havoc on your hair, leaving it frizzy, unruly, and downright frustrating. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll share our top tips for taming frizzy hair in high humidity.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Tame Frizzy Hair in High Humidity
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Taming Frizzy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Frizzy Hair

How to Tame Frizzy Hair in High Humidity:

The key to taming frizzy hair in high humidity is to keep your hair moisturized and protected from the moisture in the air. Here’s how:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain ingredients like glycerin, panthenol, and coconut oil, which can help to hydrate and smooth your hair. Avoid products that contain sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it even more prone to frizz.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized throughout the day. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, jojoba oil, and argan oil, which can help to smooth and defrizz your hair.

3. Use a Heat Protectant

If you’re going to be using heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray first. This will help to protect your hair from the heat and prevent further damage, which can lead to even more frizz.

4. Avoid Touching Your Hair

When your hair is frizzy, it can be tempting to keep touching it and trying to smooth it down. But the more you touch your hair, the more you’ll activate the frizz. Try to avoid touching your hair as much as possible, and use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to gently detangle it instead.

5. Use a Hair Oil or Serum

Finally, use a hair oil or serum to help smooth down any remaining frizz. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, or grapeseed oil, which can help to hydrate and smooth your hair without weighing it down.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Here’s a step-by-step guide to taming frizzy hair in high humidity:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized
  3. Use a heat protectant if you’re going to be using heat styling tools
  4. Avoid touching your hair as much as possible
  5. Use a hair oil or serum to smooth down any remaining frizz

Tips for Taming Frizzy Hair:

Here are some additional tips for taming frizzy hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause even more frizz
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to help prevent frizz while you sleep
  • Try using a hair mask once a week to help keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or other styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Consider getting regular trims to help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can lead to frizz

FAQs:

Q: Is it better to air dry or blow dry frizzy hair?

A: It’s generally better to air dry frizzy hair, as blow drying can cause even more frizz. If you do need to blow dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment and keep the heat on low.

Q: Can I use a straightening iron to tame frizzy hair?

A: Yes, you can use a straightening iron to help smooth down frizzy hair. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray first, and don’t use the straightener on wet hair.

Hair Care for Frizzy Hair:

If you have naturally frizzy hair, it’s important to take good care of it to help keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it even more prone to frizz
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot water when you wash your hair, as this can also strip it of its natural oils
  • When you do wash your hair, be sure to rinse it thoroughly to remove any product buildup
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look limp

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to tame frizzy hair in even the highest humidity. Don’t let frizz ruin your day – take control of your hair and show it who’s boss!

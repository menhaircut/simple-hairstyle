How To Shave Your Head Buzz Cut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of dealing with hair maintenance or just want to try something new, shaving your head into a buzz cut can be a great option. Not only is it low maintenance, but it can also give you a sleek and sharp look. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to shave your head buzz cut style.

Description

A buzz cut is a short hairstyle where the hair is cut very close to the scalp. It's a popular look among men and women alike, as it's easy to maintain and gives off a cool and confident vibe.

How to

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, a mirror, and a towel to wipe away excess hair. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Choose your guard

The guard on your clippers determines how close the hair will be cut. For a buzz cut, choose a guard that's no larger than a #4. If you want an even closer cut, you can go down to a #2 or #3.

Step 2: Trim your hair

If your hair is long, start by trimming it down with scissors. This will make it easier to cut with clippers. Once your hair is trimmed, take your clippers and start at the base of your neck, moving upwards towards the crown of your head. Use smooth and even strokes to avoid leaving any patches.

Step 3: Check for evenness

After you've gone over your entire head, check for any spots you may have missed or areas that need to be touched up. Use your clippers to go over those areas until you achieve an even cut.

Step 4: Clean up

Once you're finished, wipe away any excess hair with a towel and take a shower to wash away any hair that may have fallen on your body. You may also want to apply lotion to your scalp to prevent dryness.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when shaving your head into a buzz cut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one if needed
  • Don't rush, take your time to make sure you don't miss any spots
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head
  • Consider asking a friend or family member for help, especially if you're nervous about doing it yourself
  • Invest in a good pair of clippers to make the process easier and smoother

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaving your head into a buzz cut:

How often should I shave my head?

It depends on your preference, but most people shave their heads once a week or every two weeks to maintain the look.

Can I shave my head with a razor?

While it's possible to shave your head with a razor, clippers are recommended as they're easier to use and less likely to cause irritation or cuts.

Will shaving my head make my hair grow back thicker?

No, shaving your head won't affect the thickness or texture of your hair. It may appear thicker initially, but that's just because the hair is shorter and hasn't had time to grow.

Hair Care

Now that you've shaved your head into a buzz cut, it's important to take care of your scalp and hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your scalp regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Apply sunscreen to your scalp when outdoors
  • Moisturize your scalp with a lightweight lotion or oil
  • Avoid using hair products that can clog pores or cause buildup
  • Consider wearing a hat or scarf to protect your scalp from the sun

With these tips, you'll be able to maintain your buzz cut and keep your scalp healthy and happy.

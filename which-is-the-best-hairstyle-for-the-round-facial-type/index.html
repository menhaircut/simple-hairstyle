Which Is The Best Hairstyle For The Round Facial Type

Written by Christine Oct 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having a round face can be both a blessing and a curse when it comes to choosing the perfect hairstyle. While a round face is often associated with youthfulness and femininity, it can also make you look chubby or baby-faced if you don't choose the right hairstyle.

Description

In this blog post, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for round facial types. Whether you have short, medium, or long hair, we've got you covered. We will also provide tips on how to style your hair for different occasions and how to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Round Facial Type

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for round faces, it's essential to understand what a round face shape looks like. A round face has soft, curved edges, and the width and length are approximately the same. To create the illusion of a longer face, you want to add height and volume to your hairstyle.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing the right hairstyle for a round face:

  • Measure the width and length of your face
  • Look for hairstyles that will add height and volume to your hair
  • Avoid hairstyles that will make your face look even rounder, such as a blunt bob or a middle part
  • Consider getting layers or bangs to frame your face

Best Hairstyles for Round Facial Type

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle for a round face, let's explore some of the best hairstyles for this facial type:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for round faces because they create the illusion of length and add volume to your hair. You can wear your hair straight or wavy, and it will look great either way. Just make sure to avoid blunt cuts, as they can make your face look rounder.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another excellent option for round faces because they create the illusion of length and add dimension to your face. You can wear them with long, medium, or short hair, and they look great with both straight and wavy hair.

3. Beach Waves

Beach waves are perfect for round faces because they add volume and texture to your hair. You can achieve this look with a curling iron or by braiding your hair and leaving it overnight. Just make sure to avoid tight curls, as they can make your face look rounder.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Now that you know the best hairstyles for round faces let's discuss some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add height and volume to your hair
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and texture
  • Use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or curls to your hair
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a bob with a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a bob with a round face, but make sure to add layers and texture to your hair to create the illusion of length.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part with a round face?

A: It's best to avoid a middle part with a round face because it can make your face look even rounder. Instead, try a side part or side-swept bangs.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you know how to style your hair let's discuss some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, as it can damage your hair

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle for your round face, you can accentuate your features and look your best every day.

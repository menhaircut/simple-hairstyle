How To Get Loose Curls With Curly Hair

Are you tired of your tight curls and looking to switch things up? Loose curls are a great way to add some variety to your curly hair. In this article, we'll go over how to get loose curls with curly hair and provide some helpful tips and advice for maintaining your new look.

How to Get Loose Curls with Curly Hair

Getting loose curls with curly hair can be done in a few different ways. Here are some methods to try:

Method 1: Braid-Out

A braid-out is a great way to achieve loose curls with curly hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section, starting from the roots and working your way down.
  4. Once all sections are braided, allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser on a low heat setting.
  5. When your hair is completely dry, gently undo the braids and use your fingers to separate the curls.

This method works well for those with medium to long hair.

Method 2: Twist-Out

A twist-out is similar to a braid-out but uses twists instead of braids. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section, starting from the roots and working your way down.
  4. Once all sections are twisted, allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser on a low heat setting.
  5. When your hair is completely dry, gently undo the twists and use your fingers to separate the curls.

This method works well for those with short to medium hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide for getting loose curls with curly hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp hair.
  3. Choose a method for achieving loose curls (braid-out or twist-out).
  4. Divide your hair into sections and braid or twist each section.
  5. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser on a low heat setting.
  6. Once your hair is completely dry, gently undo the braids or twists and use your fingers to separate the curls.
  7. Apply a light hold hairspray to set the curls in place.

Tips for Getting and Maintaining Loose Curls

Here are some tips for getting and maintaining loose curls:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using a diffuser or other heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and maintain your curls overnight.
  • Refresh your curls by spritzing them with water and applying a small amount of curl cream or leave-in conditioner.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get loose curls with naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can achieve loose curls with naturally curly hair using the braid-out or twist-out methods.

How long will my loose curls last?

Your loose curls should last at least a few days, depending on your hair type and the products you use.

What products should I use to get loose curls?

You can use a leave-in conditioner, curl cream, or styling gel to achieve loose curls. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a daily basis.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

Now that you know how to get loose curls with curly hair, it's time to try it out for yourself! Remember to be patient and experiment with different methods and products to find what works best for your hair type.

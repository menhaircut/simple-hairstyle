How To Do Short Back And Sides Haircut At Home

Written by Bardi Jun 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new and fresh look for your hair? Do you want to try a short back and sides haircut but don't want to spend a fortune at a salon? Well, you're in luck! With a few simple steps, you can easily achieve this classic and stylish haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll show you how to do a short back and sides haircut at home in just a few easy steps.

Description

A short back and sides haircut is a classic men's hairstyle that has been around for decades. It is a simple yet stylish cut that involves trimming the hair on the sides and back of the head short while leaving the hair on top longer. This creates a neat and tidy look that is perfect for any occasion. The great thing about this haircut is that it is easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways.

How to

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

The first step in doing a short back and sides haircut at home is to gather all the necessary supplies. You will need a pair of hair clippers, a comb, scissors, and a mirror. It is also a good idea to have a towel or cape to protect your clothes from hair clippings.

Step 2: Start with the Sides

Using the hair clippers, start trimming the hair on the sides of your head. Begin at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to lift the hair as you go. Be sure to use a guard attachment to ensure that you trim the hair evenly. You can choose the length of the guard depending on how short you want the hair to be. Keep trimming until you have reached your desired length.

Step 3: Move to the Back

Next, move to the back of your head and repeat the same process. Use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim it to the desired length. Be sure to check the mirror periodically to ensure that the hair is being trimmed evenly.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Back

Once you have finished trimming the sides and back, it's time to blend the two together. Use the clippers without a guard attachment to create a gradual transition from the shorter hair on the sides to the longer hair on top. Be sure to blend the hair evenly on both sides.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Finally, it's time to trim the hair on top. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length, being sure to cut it evenly. You can choose to leave the hair longer on top for a more dramatic look or trim it shorter for a more classic style.

Tips

  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go.
  • Start with a longer guard attachment and work your way down to a shorter length if necessary.
  • Be patient and take your time to ensure that the haircut is even.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair, ask a friend or family member for help.

FAQ

Can I do a short back and sides haircut on myself?

Yes, you can do a short back and sides haircut on yourself. However, it may be easier to ask a friend or family member for help.

What length should I use for the guard attachment?

The length of the guard attachment will depend on how short you want the hair to be. Start with a longer length and work your way down if necessary.

Do I need to use scissors to trim the hair on top?

Yes, scissors are necessary to trim the hair on top. They allow for more precision and control than clippers.

Hair Care

After you have finished your short back and sides haircut, be sure to maintain it properly. Regularly trim the sides and back to keep the hair looking neat and tidy. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and moisturized. You can also use a styling product to achieve your desired look.

Now that you know how to do a short back and sides haircut at home, it's time to give it a try! With a little practice, you can achieve a stylish and classic look that is perfect for any occasion. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!

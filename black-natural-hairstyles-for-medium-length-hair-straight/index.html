Black Natural Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair Straight A Comprehensive Guide In 2023

Written by Aprilia Jul 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for some new hairstyle ideas for your medium length straight hair? As a black woman, it can be challenging to find hairstyles that suit our hair texture and length. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing some of the best black natural hairstyles for medium length hair straight that you can try out in 2023. From twists and braids to updos and buns, we've got something for everyone. So, let's dive in!

Description

Medium length straight hair is a versatile texture, which means there are endless possibilities when it comes to styling it. However, black women have unique hair needs, and it's essential to find hairstyles that cater to those needs. In this article, we'll be focusing on natural hairstyles that are easy to maintain, protect your hair, and look fabulous.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's crucial to know how to take care of your hair. Natural hair requires a bit of TLC, and there are a few things you should do regularly to keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly keeps it clean and healthy. However, over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, so it's essential to find a balance. We recommend washing your hair once a week or every other week.

2. Moisturize your hair

Natural hair needs moisture, and it's vital to keep it hydrated. Use a leave-in conditioner, oil, or moisturizer to keep your hair soft and moisturized.

3. Protect your hair at night

Wear a satin bonnet or scarf to protect your hair at night. This prevents breakage, tangling, and frizz.

4. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and cause breakage. Try to avoid using heat styling tools or limit their use.

Black Natural Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair Straight

Now that we've covered some hair care tips let's move on to the hairstyles. Here are some of the best black natural hairstyles for medium length hair straight:

1. Twist Out

A twist-out is a classic natural hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's easy to do and looks great on medium length straight hair. Simply twist your hair in sections, leave it to dry overnight, and unravel the twists in the morning.

2. Braids

Braids are a protective hairstyle that looks great on medium length hair. You can try different braid styles, such as cornrows, box braids, or Senegalese twists. Braids are also low maintenance and can last for several weeks.

3. Updos

Updos are a chic and elegant hairstyle that looks great on medium length straight hair. You can try a high bun, a low bun, or a braided updo. Updos are perfect for formal occasions or when you want to switch up your look.

4. Afro

An Afro is a bold and beautiful hairstyle that looks great on medium length straight hair. It's a simple style that requires minimal maintenance. You can use a pick to fluff out your hair and create a voluminous Afro.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

1. Use the right products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. Look for products that are specifically designed for natural hair.

2. Don't over-manipulate your hair

Over-manipulating your hair can cause breakage and damage. Try to limit how often you style your hair and be gentle when handling it.

3. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to try new hairstyles and experiment with different looks. You might discover a new favorite style!

FAQ

1. Can I straighten my natural hair?

Yes, you can straighten your natural hair, but it's essential to use heat protectant and avoid excessive heat. Too much heat can damage your hair and cause breakage.

2. How often should I wash my hair?

We recommend washing your hair once a week or every other week.

3. How can I keep my hair moisturized?

Use a leave-in conditioner, oil, or moisturizer to keep your hair soft and moisturized. You can also try the LOC or LCO method to lock in moisture.

Hair Care

Finally, taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some additional hair care tips:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Trimming your hair regularly helps to prevent split ends and breakage. We recommend trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks.

2. Avoid harsh chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as relaxers or dyes. These can damage your hair and cause breakage.

3. Eat a healthy diet

Eating a healthy diet can improve the health of your hair. Make sure to eat a balanced diet with plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you're ready to rock your medium length straight hair with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair, experiment with different styles, and have fun!

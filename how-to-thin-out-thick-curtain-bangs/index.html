How To Thin Out Thick Curtain Bangs

Written by Bardi Oct 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have thick curtain bangs, you know how hard it can be to style them. They tend to look bulky and can overwhelm your face. The solution to this problem is to thin them out. In this article, we will give you step-by-step instructions on how to thin out thick curtain bangs.

Description

Thick curtain bangs can be challenging to style, especially if you have a small forehead or a round face. Thinning them out can help you achieve a more natural look that is easier to manage. Thinning out your bangs will also create more movement and texture, giving you more styling options.

How to

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure your bangs are dry before you start thinning them out.

Step 2: Section Your Bangs

Section your bangs from the rest of your hair. Use a comb to separate the section you want to thin out.

Step 3: Hold Your Scissors Correctly

Hold your scissors correctly. Hold the scissors vertically, with the tips pointing upwards.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Cut your bangs with the scissors. Cut the hair at an angle, starting at the middle of your bangs and working your way outwards.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

Check your progress by releasing the section of hair you have just cut. Check to see if the section looks thinner and more manageable.

Step 6: Repeat the Process

Repeat the process on the rest of your bangs. Remember to cut at an angle and work your way outwards.

Tips

When cutting your bangs, start with a small amount of hair. You can always cut more if needed.

Always hold your scissors vertically. This will help you achieve a natural-looking cut.

Don't cut your bangs straight across. This will create a harsh line that looks unnatural.

FAQ

How often should I thin out my curtain bangs?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs become thick again. A good rule of thumb is to thin them out every six to eight weeks.

Can I thin out my bangs at home?

Yes, you can thin out your bangs at home. However, if you are not confident in your ability to do so, it is best to leave it to a professional.

Hair Care

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, make sure you wash them regularly and apply a light conditioner. Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your bangs and make them look flat.

In conclusion, thinning out thick curtain bangs can be done at home with the right tools and techniques. Follow our step-by-step instructions, and you'll have natural-looking, manageable bangs in no time.

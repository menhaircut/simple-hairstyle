How To Get The Best Red Hair Color A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Nov 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to add a fiery touch to your hair? Red hair color is a bold and beautiful choice, but it can be tricky to achieve the perfect shade. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps on how to get the best red hair color. From choosing the right shade to maintaining your color, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Choosing the Right Shade
  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Application Process
  • Maintenance Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Red Hair

Choosing the Right Shade

The first step in getting the best red hair color is choosing the right shade. There are many different shades of red, ranging from a subtle auburn to a bright, bold red. It's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and natural hair color.

If you have fair skin, a cool-toned red like strawberry blonde or copper would look great on you. For medium skin tones, a warm-toned red like auburn or cinnamon would be a good choice. If you have dark skin, a deeper red like burgundy or mahogany would look stunning.

Prepping Your Hair

Before you apply the red hair dye, it's important to prep your hair. This will ensure that the color adheres evenly and lasts longer. Here are some prepping tips:

  • Wash your hair a day or two before coloring, but don't condition it. This will help the color stick to your hair better.
  • Brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots.
  • Apply a barrier cream or petroleum jelly around your hairline and ears to prevent staining.

Application Process

Now that your hair is prepped, it's time to apply the red hair dye. Follow these steps:

  • Put on gloves to protect your hands from staining.
  • Mix the hair dye according to the instructions on the box.
  • Divide your hair into small sections and apply the dye from roots to tips.
  • Leave the dye on for the recommended time on the box.
  • Rinse the dye out thoroughly with lukewarm water until the water runs clear.
  • Apply conditioner and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Maintenance Tips

After you've achieved your desired shade of red, it's important to maintain your color. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the color.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs

How long does red hair dye last?

Red hair dye typically lasts between four and six weeks. However, this can vary depending on your hair type, how often you wash your hair, and how well you maintain your color.

Can I dye my hair red if I have dark hair?

Yes, you can dye your hair red if you have dark hair. However, you may need to bleach your hair first to achieve a brighter shade of red.

Will red hair dye stain my clothes?

Yes, red hair dye can stain your clothes. It's important to wear old clothes that you don't mind getting stained or use a towel to protect your clothes.

Hair Care for Red Hair

Red hair can be more prone to dryness and damage than other hair colors. Here are some tips for caring for red hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these steps, you can achieve the best red hair color and keep it looking vibrant and beautiful for weeks to come. Happy coloring!

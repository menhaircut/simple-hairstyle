What Is The Best Short Haircut For A Square Face

Written by Daniel Jul 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square face shape, you may be wondering what the best short haircut is for you. Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline and a forehead that is roughly the same width as the jawline. The goal of a short haircut for a square face is to soften the angles and create balance. In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for a square face, including a description, how-to, step-by-step, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

Description

The best short haircut for a square face is one that adds softness and femininity to the strong, angular features. Some examples of short haircuts that work well for square faces include pixie cuts, bob haircuts, and short layered haircuts. These haircuts are all versatile and can be customized to suit your unique facial features and personal style.

How-To

If you are considering a short haircut for your square face, the first step is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine which haircut will work best for your face shape and hair type. Once you have decided on a haircut, your hairstylist will begin by washing and conditioning your hair. They will then use scissors or clippers to cut your hair to the desired length and shape. After the haircut is complete, they may use styling products and tools to create a finished, polished look.

Step-by-Step

1. Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best short haircut for your square face shape and hair type.
2. Wash and condition your hair before the haircut.
3. Sit in the hairstylist's chair and let them work their magic with scissors or clippers.
4. After the haircut is complete, your hairstylist may use styling products and tools to create a finished look.

Tips

When choosing a short haircut for a square face, keep in mind that the goal is to soften the angles and create balance. Some tips to keep in mind include:
- Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make the forehead appear wider.
- Consider adding layers to create movement and texture.
- Opt for a side part or a deep side part to create asymmetry.
- Experiment with different styling products and tools to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear a pixie cut if I have a square face?
A: Yes! Pixie cuts can work well for square faces, especially if they are styled with layers and texture to soften the angles.

Q: Should I avoid bob haircuts if I have a square face?
A: No! Bob haircuts can work well for square faces, especially if they are styled with layers and asymmetry to create balance.

Hair Care

After you have your new short haircut, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Some tips for hair care include:
- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
- Trim your hair regularly to maintain your short haircut and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, the best short haircut for a square face is one that adds softness and femininity to the strong, angular features. Some examples of short haircuts that work well for square faces include pixie cuts, bob haircuts, and short layered haircuts. When choosing a short haircut for a square face, keep in mind that the goal is to soften the angles and create balance. Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you, and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.

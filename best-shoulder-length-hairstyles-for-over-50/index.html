Best Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Over 50

Written by Aprilia Nov 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for a new hairstyle? Shoulder length hair is a great option for mature women. It's versatile, easy to maintain, and can work with any hair texture. In this article, we'll be discussing the best shoulder length hairstyles for over 50.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a flattering and versatile length for women over 50. It's not too short, nor too long, making it the perfect length to frame your face and highlight your best features. Shoulder length hair is also easy to style and maintain, making it a great option for women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle.

How to

When it comes to styling shoulder length hair, there are many options to choose from. Here are some of the best shoulder length hairstyles for over 50:

1. Bob

A bob is a classic and timeless hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. It's a great option for women over 50 who want a low-maintenance and easy-to-style haircut. A bob can be worn straight or wavy, and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture.

2. Lob

A lob, or long bob, is a great option for women who want a slightly longer haircut. It's a great option for women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish and put-together. A lob can be worn straight or wavy, and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture.

3. Shag

A shag is a great option for women who want a more textured and layered haircut. It's a great option for women with thin or fine hair, as it adds volume and dimension to the hair. A shag can be worn straight or wavy, and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling your shoulder length hair:

1. Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type and texture.

2. Apply styling products

Apply styling products to your hair, such as a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

3. Use a round brush

Use a round brush to blow dry your hair. This will help to add volume and create a smooth and polished finish.

4. Style your hair

Once your hair is dry, style it using a curling iron or straightener. You can also add accessories, such as hair clips or headbands, to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.

5. Finish with hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light hairspray to hold it in place and add some extra shine.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your shoulder length hair:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

2. Use heat protectant

Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair with hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners. This will help to protect your hair from damage.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

What is the best shoulder length hairstyle for over 50?

The best shoulder length hairstyle for over 50 depends on your face shape and hair texture. Some great options include a bob, lob, or shag.

Is shoulder length hair easy to maintain?

Yes, shoulder length hair is easy to maintain and style. It's a great option for women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle.

What styling products should I use for shoulder length hair?

You can use a variety of styling products for shoulder length hair, depending on your hair type and texture. Some great options include volumizing mousse, texturizing spray, and hairspray.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your shoulder length hair, it's important to use the right products and techniques. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and hydrated:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Use a shampoo that is gentle and suited to your hair type and texture. Avoid shampoos that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.

2. Condition regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy. Use a conditioner that is suited to your hair type and texture.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

In conclusion, shoulder length hair is a great option for women over 50. It's versatile, easy to maintain, and can work with any hair texture. Whether you prefer a bob, lob, or shag, there's a shoulder length hairstyle that will suit your face shape and hair texture. Follow these tips and techniques to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

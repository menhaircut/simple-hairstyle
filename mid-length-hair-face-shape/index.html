Discover The Best Mid Length Hair For Your Face Shape

Written by William Oct 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that flatters your face shape? Look no further than mid length hair! This versatile hair length looks great on just about everyone, but there are certain styles that work best for different face shapes. In this blog post, we'll explore the best mid length haircuts for various face shapes, and provide tips for keeping your hair healthy and beautiful.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best mid length hairstyles for your face shape, it's important to determine what your face shape actually is. Here are the basic face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Rectangle

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the shape of your jawline, cheekbones, and forehead. You may also want to measure your face to get a more accurate assessment. Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for the best mid length hairstyle for you.

Best Mid Length Haircuts for Different Face Shapes

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a mid length haircut that adds height and volume to the top of your head. Layers can also be flattering, as they help to elongate your face. Consider a mid length bob with layers or a shaggy mid length cut.

Oval Face Shape

Lucky you! If you have an oval face shape, just about any mid length hairstyle will flatter you. Consider a blunt mid length cut or a wavy mid length style.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your features with a mid length haircut that has layers or texture. Consider a mid length cut with side-swept bangs, or a wavy mid length style.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you'll want to choose a mid length haircut that adds volume around your jawline. Layers can also be flattering, as they help to balance out your face shape. Consider a mid length cut with choppy layers or a textured mid length bob.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you'll want to choose a mid length haircut that adds width to your forehead and chin. Consider a mid length cut with face-framing layers or a wavy mid length style.

Rectangle Face Shape

If you have a rectangle face shape, you'll want to choose a mid length haircut that adds softness to your features. Layers and waves can be flattering, as they help to create the illusion of width. Consider a mid length cut with face-framing layers or a textured mid length bob.

Mid Length Hair Care Tips

No matter what mid length haircut you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for mid length hair care:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is considered mid length hair?

Mid length hair typically falls between the chin and shoulders.

Can mid length hair be styled in different ways?

Absolutely! Mid length hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including straight, wavy, curly, and updos.

What face shapes are best suited for mid length hair?

Mid length hair can be flattering on just about any face shape, but there are certain styles that work best for different face shapes. See above for our recommendations!

How often should I wash my mid length hair?

This will depend on your hair type and personal preference, but most people with mid length hair can get away with washing it every other day or a few times a week.

Final Thoughts

Mid length hair is a great choice for anyone looking for a versatile and flattering hairstyle. By choosing a mid length cut that flatters your face shape, and taking care of your hair with regular trims and moisturizing products, you can have healthy, beautiful hair that looks great no matter how you style it. Happy hairstyling!

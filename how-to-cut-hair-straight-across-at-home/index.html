How To Cut Hair Straight Across At Home

Written by Petter Nov 13, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts at the salon? With a little practice and the right tools, you can easily cut your hair straight across at home. In this article, we’ll show you how to do it step by step, as well as provide tips and hair care advice to ensure your haircut looks great.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cut Hair Straight Across
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Hair Straight Across
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care After Cutting Hair Straight Across

How to Cut Hair Straight Across

Cutting your hair straight across is easier than you might think. All you need is a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here is a quick overview of the steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see the natural fall of your hair and ensure an even cut.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair Straight Down

Using a fine-toothed comb, comb your hair straight down over your forehead and ears. Make sure it is straight and even.

Step 3: Cut Across in a Straight Line

Using your scissors, cut across your hair in a straight line. Start at one end and work your way to the other. Be sure to cut small sections at a time to ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After you have cut all of your hair, check for evenness. Use a mirror to check the back and sides of your head. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure a straight and even cut.

Step by Step Guide

Now let’s dive into the step by step process for cutting your hair straight across:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, gather all the tools you will need. This includes a pair of sharp scissors, a fine-toothed comb, and a mirror.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to see the natural fall of your hair and ensure an even cut.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Straight Down

Using a fine-toothed comb, comb your hair straight down over your forehead and ears. Make sure it is straight and even.

Step 4: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Using your comb, divide your hair into sections. Start by dividing it into two sections, one on top and one on the bottom. Then, divide each section into smaller sections. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 5: Cut the Bottom Section

Start with the bottom section of your hair. Using your scissors, cut across in a straight line. Be sure to cut small sections at a time to ensure an even cut.

Step 6: Cut the Top Section

After you have cut the bottom section of your hair, move on to the top section. Using the same technique, cut across in a straight line.

Step 7: Check for Evenness

Once you have cut all of your hair, use a mirror to check for evenness. Check the back and sides of your head to make sure it is straight and even. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure a perfect cut.

Tips for Cutting Hair Straight Across

Here are a few tips to ensure your haircut looks great:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Start with small sections and cut slowly to avoid mistakes.
  • Check for evenness regularly to avoid uneven haircuts.
  • When in doubt, cut less rather than more. You can always cut more hair later, but you can’t uncut it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my hair straight across if it’s curly?

Yes, you can cut your hair straight across if it’s curly. However, keep in mind that curly hair tends to bounce up when cut, so you may need to cut it slightly longer than you think.

Do I need to wet my hair before cutting it?

No, you do not need to wet your hair before cutting it. In fact, cutting dry hair can be easier because you can see the natural fall of your hair more clearly.

How often should I cut my hair?

It’s recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Hair Care After Cutting Hair Straight Across

After cutting your hair straight across, it’s important to take good care of it to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy look.

With these tips and step by step guide, you can easily cut your hair straight across at home. Not only will you save money on salon visits, but you’ll also have the satisfaction of knowing you did it yourself!

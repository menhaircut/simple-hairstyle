How To Make Your Hair Look Fluffy With A Straightener

Written by Robby Sep 08, 2023 · 4 min read
How to Make Your Hair Look Fluffy with a Straightener | Hair Care Tips

Do you want to make your hair look fluffy with a straightener? If so, you’ve come to the right place! In this blog post, we will share with you some tips and tricks on how to achieve a fluffy hairstyle using a straightener. Read on to learn more!

Description

Having flat and lifeless hair can be a real bummer, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a voluminous and fluffy hairstyle. One of the best tools for this job is a straightener. With a straightener, you can create waves and curls that add volume and texture to your hair.

How to

Here are the steps to make your hair look fluffy with a straightener:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, it’s important to prep it properly. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the straightener.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections, starting from the bottom. Clip the top sections out of the way so you can work on the lower sections first.

Step 3: Heat Up Your Straightener

Turn on your straightener and let it heat up to the desired temperature. For fluffy hair, use a lower heat setting to avoid flattening your hair.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and clamp it with the straightener near the roots. Twist the straightener away from your face and pull it down to the ends of your hair. This should create a loose wave. Repeat this technique on all the sections of your hair until you’ve curled everything.

Step 5: Add Volume

Once you’ve curled your hair, flip your head upside down and gently scrunch your hair with your fingers. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a fluffy hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying your hair to add extra volume.
  • When curling your hair with a straightener, be sure to twist the straightener away from your face for a more natural look.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add more texture and hold to your hair.
  • Don’t overdo it with the heat. Use a lower heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQ

Can I make my hair look fluffy with a straightener?

Yes, you can! Using a straightener to create loose waves and curls can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fluffy and full.

What kind of straightener should I use?

Any straightener can be used to create fluffy hair. However, a straightener with adjustable heat settings is ideal so that you can control the temperature and avoid damaging your hair.

How long will my fluffy hairstyle last?

Your fluffy hairstyle can last for up to 24 hours with the right products and techniques. Use a hairspray to set your style and keep it in place.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Excessive heat can damage your hair, making it dry and brittle. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Now that you know how to make your hair look fluffy with a straightener, you can try this technique at home and achieve a voluminous and stylish look. Have fun experimenting with different styles and products and find what works best for you!

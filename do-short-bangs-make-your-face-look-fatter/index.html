Do Short Bangs Make Your Face Look Fatter

Description

Short bangs have been a trend in the fashion industry for years now. While they can look great on some people, many wonder if short bangs make your face look fatter. The answer is not a simple yes or no. Short bangs can make your face look fatter or thinner, depending on how they are styled and what type of face shape you have.

How to

Here are some tips on how to style short bangs to make them work for your face shape:

Cut

The first thing to consider is the cut of your bangs. Short bangs that are blunt and cut straight across your forehead can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for bangs that are cut at an angle or have some texture. These types of bangs can create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Length

The length of your bangs is also important. Bangs that are too short can make your face look wider, while bangs that are too long can hide your features and make your face look rounder. The best length for short bangs is just above your eyebrows.

Style

When styling your short bangs, avoid pulling them straight back or parting them down the middle. Instead, try sweeping them to the side or adding some volume to create a more flattering look.

Step by step

Here's how to style short bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to go.
  3. If you want more volume, use a round brush to lift your bangs at the roots while blow drying.
  4. Once your bangs are dry, use a flat iron to smooth them out.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and tame any flyaways.
  6. If you want to add some texture, use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to create definition.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling short bangs:

  • Consider your face shape when deciding on the length and style of your bangs.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using a flat iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Try different styling techniques until you find what works best for you.
  • Visit a professional stylist to get the perfect cut and style for your face shape.

FAQ

Do short bangs make your face look fatter?

Short bangs can make your face look fatter or thinner, depending on how they are styled and what type of face shape you have.

What face shape is best for short bangs?

Short bangs can work for a variety of face shapes, but they look best on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

How often should you trim your bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

Hair Care

To keep your short bangs looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

In conclusion, short bangs can make your face look fatter or thinner, depending on how they are styled and what type of face shape you have. With the right cut, length, and styling techniques, you can make short bangs work for you and create a flattering look that enhances your features.

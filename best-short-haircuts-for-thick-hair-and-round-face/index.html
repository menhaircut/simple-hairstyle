Best Short Haircuts For Thick Hair And Round Face A Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Aug 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your thick hair and round face? Look no further! In this guide, we will be discussing the best short haircuts for thick hair and round face in 2023. From bobs to pixie cuts, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts for thick hair and round face can be a tricky combination to find. You want a cut that will flatter your face shape and show off your beautiful thick hair. But with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to know where to start.

In this guide, we will be focusing on short haircuts for thick hair and round face in 2023. These cuts are not only trendy but also easy to maintain and style. Whether you’re looking for a bold new look or a subtle change, we’ve got you covered.

How to

Before we dive into the best short haircuts for thick hair and round face, it’s important to know what to look for in a haircut. First and foremost, you want a cut that will flatter your face shape. For round faces, a cut with layers or angles can help to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline.

When it comes to thick hair, you want a cut that will help to thin out the hair and reduce bulk. Look for cuts with layers or texturizing to help achieve this. And finally, consider your personal style and how much time you’re willing to spend styling your hair each day.

Step by Step

Now, let’s dive into the best short haircuts for thick hair and round face in 2023:

1. Bob

The bob is a classic cut that never goes out of style. For round faces, consider a long bob that falls just below the chin. This will help to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline. Adding layers to the bob can also help to thin out thick hair and reduce bulk.

2. Pixie Cut

If you’re looking for a bold new look, consider a pixie cut. This cut is great for round faces as it helps to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline. It’s also a great option for thick hair as it can help to thin out the hair and reduce bulk.

3. Shag

The shag is a great option for those with thick hair and round faces. This cut features long layers and choppy ends, giving it a messy, textured look. The layers help to thin out the hair and reduce bulk, while the choppy ends add texture and movement.

Tips

When it comes to styling short haircuts for thick hair and round face, there are a few tips to keep in mind. First, consider using a texturizing spray or pomade to add texture and volume to your hair. This can help to create a more defined look and reduce any bulk.

Second, experiment with different partings to find what works best for your face shape. For round faces, a middle part can help to elongate the face, while a side part can create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

FAQ

What short haircut is best for a round face?

For round faces, a cut with layers or angles can help to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline. Consider a long bob or a pixie cut to flatter your face shape.

How do I style a short haircut for thick hair?

Consider using a texturizing spray or pomade to add texture and volume to your hair. Experiment with different partings to find what works best for your face shape.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your short haircut for thick hair and round face, it’s important to use the right products. Look for products that are designed for thick hair, such as volumizing shampoos and texturizing sprays.

Be sure to also schedule regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy. And finally, consider investing in a high-quality blow dryer and styling tools to achieve the perfect look.

In conclusion, finding the perfect short haircut for thick hair and round face can be a challenge, but with the right information, it’s possible to achieve a flattering and stylish look. Consider the tips and haircuts discussed in this guide to find the perfect cut for you.

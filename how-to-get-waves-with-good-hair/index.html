How To Get Waves With Good Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 17, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to achieve waves with good hair, you've come to the right place! This guide will take you through the steps to achieve the perfect wave pattern in your hair, and give you tips on how to maintain your waves. Whether you have straight or curly hair, you can still achieve waves with the right technique and products.

How to Get Waves

Getting waves in your hair is all about creating a consistent wave pattern. Here are the steps to achieve this:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and free of any product buildup. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any excess oils or dirt.

Step 2: Apply Wave Cream

Apply a wave cream to your hair to help define your wave pattern. Use a dime-sized amount and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Brush Your Hair

Using a wave brush, brush your hair in a circular motion to create a wave pattern. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down to the nape of your neck.

Step 4: Tie Your Hair Down

Once you've created your wave pattern, use a durag or wave cap to tie your hair down. This will help your waves set and maintain their shape.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Set

Leave your hair tied down for at least 30 minutes to let your waves set. For best results, leave your hair tied down overnight.

Tips for Maintaining Your Waves

Once you've achieved your waves, you'll want to maintain them for as long as possible. Here are some tips to keep your waves looking great:

Tip 1: Use a Satin Pillowcase

Using a satin pillowcase will help reduce friction on your hair while you sleep, preventing your waves from getting messed up.

Tip 2: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizziness. Try to limit washing your hair to once or twice a week.

Tip 3: Wear a Durag or Wave Cap

Wearing a durag or wave cap will help maintain your wave pattern and prevent it from getting messed up.

FAQ

How long does it take to get waves?

It can take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months to achieve waves, depending on your hair type and how often you brush your hair.

Can you get waves with curly hair?

Yes, you can still achieve waves with curly hair. However, it may take longer to train your curls to form a wave pattern.

Can you get waves with short hair?

Yes, you can still achieve waves with short hair. However, it may be more difficult to create a consistent wave pattern.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain healthy waves. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo

Use a moisturizing shampoo to prevent your hair from drying out and becoming brittle.

Tip 2: Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning your hair once a week will help keep it moisturized and prevent breakage.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling your hair can cause damage and dryness. Try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.

By following these steps and tips, you can achieve and maintain great waves with good hair. Remember to be patient and consistent, and soon you'll have the perfect wave pattern!
